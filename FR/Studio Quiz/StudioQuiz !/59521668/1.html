Hey les SQuizzers,

[ENG] As you may all know, SQ is going through a real’ tough phase recently and we can confidently say that it’s not doing very well due to the latest updates. The number of players has massively shrunk, and it has made it really hard for us to play. I gotta admit that we are quite powerless regarding this situation, unless you’re one of SQ’s current developers (in that case, please help our poor souls with your magic ).
So I thought for a certain number of years, concerted with a certain number of local experts, and we came up with an option : one thing we can improve by ourselves is the communication that’s been cruelly taken off from us. The communication about each and everyone’s availability to play a game or two, and also the communication in-between the questions, the opportunity to simply make a match more pleasant and enjoyable, the opportunity to get to know each other and make some new nerdy friends!
Okay so here’s our brilliant idea: creating a Discord group chat! If you don’t know what it is… Google it? Jk It’s an upgraded version of Skype, without the video, only audio and/or messages.
So if you’re on board, let us know by replying to this thread. If you’re not, let us also know by replying to this thread.

TLDR: let’s create a damn cool group chat on Discord in order to avoid being in a game all by oneself and wait for it to start until you completely forget about it… Not that it has ever happened to me before, huh…

[FR] J’m’excuse sincèrement de ne pas savoir comment m’exprimer proprement en fr, j’ai préféré utiliser les bons mots en anglais … En gros, pour améliorer la situation du jeu, on devrait avoir un truc qui permettrait de savoir qui est dispo/actif/présent à telle heure précise pour faire une petite partie en direct. Et l’application Discord serait pas mal pour ça (pour ceux qui ne connaissent pas, c’est un peu comme Skype, mais sans la webcam, on n’est pas obligé de parler vocalement, y’a un chat, c’est gratuit, et populaire en ce moment).

Donc voilà, dites-moi ce que vous en pensez ?
