Even without the Eleet mode you can use keyboard commands for the viruses (virii?). Just press the number shown at the bottom of each virus' field. Or for going up a directory, press Space. For scrolling within a directory use Page Up/Down.

But I recommend enabling the Eleet mode, it's not really harder because you can still use the mouse, only thing you can't do anymore is clicking on the viruses to activcate them. But you can define alias commands, so for example to destroy an antivirus I target it with the mouse, then type dm (for medium damage) twice and once ds -> destroyed.


And a thought about the lvl 5 and higher missions. Do they have any advantage over the lvl 4 missions? Ok, mostly you get a higher reward, but then again you won't collect as many fichiers valeur or credit card numbers. So when having to choose between a 1900 lvl 5 and a 1700 lvl 4 in terms of money it may be wiser to choose the lvl 4.

Um, there is no "unshield" virus (at least not in level 5). To remove a lock, you use Decodage. To remove a shield, you kill the paladins. Right ?No, not the Paladin and neither the lock. In level 6 there are "shield" anti-viruses, they reduce the damage all other anti-virus take by 3 points each.

So if there are 2 shield anti-viruses as in that mission I mentioned each attack gets reduced by 6 points. That means a medium damage causes zero damage. And that means you can't destroy any anti-virus incl the two "Shields" -> mission can't be solved.

That's where the unshield virus comes into play. It allows you to remove the shielding from the targeted anti-virus so it can be destroyed.


And regarding the locks, in lvl 6 missions you often need Decode v2 or v3. So if you play those missions w/o such a virus you're doomed. I found it best to carry the v1 and the v3 Decode on one deck. v3 takes lots of so you can't use it all the time thus the need for the v1. So the v2 isn't really needed and the money can be saved.
