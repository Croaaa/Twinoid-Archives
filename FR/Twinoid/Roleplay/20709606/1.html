For the interested ones, I have undertook to write a RP in english, I invite you to participate. Enjoy

A young boy called Geronimo suffered from a tumour which was detected too late. He was in Andromed Hospital, Grand City, capital city of Deimos Universis between life and death. We could read on the regular-acute-beep-machine that his heart was no longer mighty. His body was weakened but his mind was begging for his survival. To young to be dead. His eyes were closed, his skinny body was wholly blistered. His life is only kept by medical machines and pipes.
The doctor entered the gloomy and cold room. She was Doctor Madonna, a woman being about fourty. Then, the doctor analyzed the young boy whom could have been her boy. She knew that the young boy life was into her hands. No failure was admitted now. Nevertheless, analyses showed that his metabolism was no longer reactive to the different treatments.
Three days later, Geronimo died from his tumour which was definitively gnawing on. The doctor was deeply disappointed. Despite it, Geronimo's parents decided to send her to trial. The government was drastic, they would punish all the failures in order to spread the fear on the population. She was found guilty and she was condamned to being jailed for ten years bad treatments leading up the boy's death. Madonna was worn the orange prisonner uniform and was sent to a humid and empty cell. In her sorrow, she decided to write her story to explain how these events happened.
(Flashback one year ago in Grand City)
