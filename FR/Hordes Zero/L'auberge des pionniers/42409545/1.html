Comme vous le savez, l'alpha a eu lieu le mois dernier pendant une semaine.

buzzard est un administrateur de la Motion Twin, il me semble qu'il s'occupe de la communauté anglophone de Dead Cells, ou en tout cas il communique avec elle.

Il y a deux semaines, alors que nos amis anglophones se demandaient pourquoi ils n'avaient pas eu le droit à une alpha mais que nous autres francophones si, buzzard leur a apporté une réponse : Ok so. I've been putting off telling you all about this...

But for the moment there will be no English alpha.

As I Mentioned in a less official post, they waited till I went on holidays and then snuck it out for the Frenchies!

After that first outing deepnight and Carduus decided that the alpha was so bug ridden and stinky that they would end it after less than a week, as they already had enough work to do for at least 2 months (that's the idea of an alpha after all).

In the meantime we've had some more reshuffling here at MT. Meaning that certain people who were working on projects have to be replaced and their replacements are busy with other things. This all boils down to, you guys waiting for the beta (sorry I tried, I really did).

The beta is looking like it will be in mid december given the things that have to be done by then.

I'll keep you all posted. Don't hesitate to ask questions, rage, go on strike, throw things, you know... Let the devs know how you feel!
