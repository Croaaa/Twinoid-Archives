If you are lost, don't forget that in the Settings of the game, you can activate options to make the game easier (such as leading you to the right location for your quest or even though highlight the action to do if you are at the site)

I guess you have the whole game in French so if you want to do that :
-Go to "Votre page personnelle" aka the little house under your character.
-In the top-right hand corner : "Paramètres compte"
-Activate the first two lines of "Préférences" that are :
Mettre en surbrillance la prochaine action à effectuer pour accomplir les quêtes
Mettre en surbrillance le chemin vers le prochain lieu des quêtes sur la carte
