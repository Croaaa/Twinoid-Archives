Hello there! After not playing Naturalchimie in years, I somehow started thinking about it and wanted to play some games out of nostalgia. However, the game I fondly remember from my childhood seems to have grown, transforming into Naturalchimie 2. While this seems a much more elaborate, deep, interesting, story-driven game, I don't speak french so I can't enjoy most of it. Given that I couldn't find the old game anywhere online, I decided to code up my own version of it, which you can play right now on knexator.itch.io/naturalchimie-classic ! It's made on Pico-8, a retro fantasy console, which fits the nostalgia theme quite well. If you play it, let me know what you think of it

Cheers!
