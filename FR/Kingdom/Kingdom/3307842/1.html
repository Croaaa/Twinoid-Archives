Hi mehrunes

You haven't play your turns and the grid counter reach the maximum
Between 100 and the automatic turns there is a security, you can spend some turn
the max number you can spend is behind the grid counter, move your mouse on it you will see the second counter for it

You are in "automatic turns" : each 30 minutes you spend gold and food as a normal turn , but no production is done
Your food and gold reserve vanish
When you have no more gold to pay troops they leave you
and when there will be no more food the people in your city will die

The solution is : play your turns regularly

This is an automatic security to avoid too many inactive players staying in the map
It kill the one who forgot the game, dont want to continue, are in holidays ...

I hope this will help you
