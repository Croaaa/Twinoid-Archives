It's been 4 days that the Mountain of Putrefaction hideaway has been infected.Everyone that lived in this peaceful haven knew it was a great community. All those died, womens to childrens. It was a massive butchery.

The hardest part was the last survivors of the attack, all they could do was watching their newly family being eaten by zombies.

But today, everyone is way happier, they're now part of the horde... aiming for new cities to eat their brains! It will be a massive carnage.

Every night, during the normal Die2Nite attack, you will attack a newly chosen peaceful, not for long, village. ! Everyday, the horde will attack stronger and stronger cities, with a higher defense. The number of defense points of the city will be determined by consecutive d100 throws. With all the zombies, it's hard to communicate and coordinate. Both zombie leaders Shini and TheLion will propose a plan for each attack. Every zombie player will be able to kill between 1 and 10 humans by throwing a d10.
Every player with a Dead Cells related avatar will be able to throw a d12 instead of the normal d10!There will also be restrictions to the attack further on, in harder cities...
If the horde can slaughter more than 5 cities, the most valuable zombies will receive rewards from the Animaction (thanks to Shini that brought up the idea and rewards)This animation involves all 3 servers (FR/EN/ES) and all their participation will be brought up together as one. The attack will be thrown on the FR forum, then will flow down other forums the minutes after. Message Shini and TheLion if you don't understand, or leave a message below!
