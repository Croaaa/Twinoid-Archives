pickupsticks

easiest way to keep track is probably to add subfolders for bookmarks to your favourites list.
army link, then folder of "level me up now" recruits, then folder of "at a good level, but still worth developing" recruits, then folder of "on the backburner" recruits, then folder of "sub-recruits" - simple, but organized enough and everything is easy to find when you need it.

lists and trees require too much work. folders are nice and easy to check in a hurry (open-all-in-tabs is useful) and you can drag a recruit army from one folder to another much quicker than reworking a list or tree.
