Wait for smart troopers buddy, I found out that smart troopers are the best trooper one should get at the start. Maybe your first four troopers should be all smart (When they troopers are still cheap). These smart troopers will be your primary back bone of the army, being offered 3 skill per level means that they are the most flexible troopers and can very well adapt to the changes your army will undergo later on. (Additional bonus, your army can resist the "sabotage" more in early battles (4-5 troopers) where it only takes two lvl. 6 saboteurs to output 100%)Level these smart troopers up until you have a good grasp of what your army will concretely looks like, it is in this moment that you can pump in the frenetic/on-point/twinoid/whatever-you-want troopers.
As of now, in all my new army I am playing this kind of concept: I treat every wave as a battalion. Each battalion should have a general (in the form of smart trooper). Each general should have a trusty and powerful soldiers/pawns (in the form of frenetic/on-point/sprinter etc.) This way, power distribution is equal or is in middle ground
