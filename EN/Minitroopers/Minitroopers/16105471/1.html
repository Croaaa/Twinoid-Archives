I don't know man...
Double Scavenger with a Saboteur is great.
Your Twinoid Soldier looks ok.
There are a some little things like... the Comms Officer should have a long range weapon and not a Talky-Walky (there will be no more troops after the Comms Officer).

Having both a Comms Officer and a Saboteur with just 4 troopers is really, really, good. Most armies don't get that lucky,
...but by level 7 you should have some initiative skill on someone.

I know that the Saboteur is incredible but as far as I'm concerned this game is about spies and anti-spies.
By now you should either have a spy or have some anti-spy skills. Preferably On point, Suspiciousor or a Motorcycle (or at least Vendetta or Adrenaline). Dodger is ok, it's also anti-spy.

Right now if someone was to infiltrate that army what would happen?
Firstly your Saboteur 'may' take out his weapon (Great!)
Secondly the spy will do as he likes (as you have no initiative skills)
Then, thirdly, if you are still alive you get to do 'something'.
What are your options here...

Either the weapons can not fire at close range or will cause friendly fire, the only weapon you could use is Desert Eagle.
Other options... Faceboot, Scavenger, Scavenger, Twinoid.

The Twinoid is on a trooper with only Semi-Auto Shotgun and Assault Rifle. So thats not a good anti-spy.
Faceboot is ok but it's not a reliable knockdown (unlike Wrestler).
The only safe and reliable anti-spy option would be the two scavengers.

If you could get an initiative skill like On point for one of your guys with Scavenger (say for Murder your Saboteur) then this would start to look like an army. It will be slow going from here as they are already up to level 7.
Thats my 2 cents.
- Pause
