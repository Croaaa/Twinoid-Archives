SilentPinoy

You have to exceed the deployment costs of any vehicle in order to deploy, but it will only cost you that many units. I.E. you need a 7 deployment but it will cost you only 6 units on deployment for a Heavy Tank. I've confirmed this because I've seen an army with two tanks and a deployment of 5 deploy both tanks at once(http://herakles.minitroopers.com/b/fv...), while myself with a deployment of 4 with 3 tanks have never double deployed tanks. Single Light Tanks and Helicopters will always deploy when able because of how we automatically exceed the deployment cost. For me also I have times in raid where I have a Heavy Tank and a Light Tank deploy together since I have a wave deployment of exactly 10(I made my early recruits into scouts and kept it to a minimum due to learning the system early).

Yes it is a strange rule.
