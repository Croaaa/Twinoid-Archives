I laugh when I see troopers with lucky charm going down to 1 hit from Dual Pistols.

Giving a spy a shotgun is awesome but keep the following in mind.
There is a trick that top players know where you intentionally have a smaller army which lowers the number of enemy spys that can infiltratie.
With 3 spys your last spy will only infiltratie when fighting against a 12 trooper army (not less).
Against an 11 trooper army spy number 3 will just walk in on your side with your friendlys (shotgun in hand!).
Friendly fire is a big deal in this game and only your first spy is totally reliable.

If you pick a shotgun for a spy (which is a great idea) just know that you are dedicating them to being your first spy in the list for 'Command my troops'.
Any second spy might go for... say... Knife or Minigun. Somehting less likely for Friendly fire then a scattergun.
Also keep in mind that Motorcycle on a spy will also stop them from infiltrating and can result in Friendly fire when fighting spy vs spy.
