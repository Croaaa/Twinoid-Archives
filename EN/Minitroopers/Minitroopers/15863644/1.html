I'm thinking about starting a few threads up, in the style of the King Of The Hill thread.
One person will be the "belt holder" and anyone else can challenge them as many times as they like, until they win and take the belt becoming the new champion.
Now, the KotH thread has no entry limits, but I was thinking of creating several new competitions for armies of different power. You can think of this as the weight classes used in other competitions such as boxing.
Sounds good?

So, this is where the question come in. Where are the best places to draw the line?
I'm thinking of using power-rating only, rather than number of troopers.
(If the belt holder upgrades and is no longer in their weight class then anyone can post in the thread and take their place...)

I was thinking of the following divisions.
For each, the number represents the maximum power allowed to compete. It would be possible to hold more than one belt, if you have a very effective low-power army.

Flyweight: 25

Bantamweight: 50

Featherweight: 75

Lightweight: 100

Welterweight: 125

Middleweight: 150

Light Heavyweight: 175

Heavyweight: unlimited (this is just the KotH, really. But I might make a new thread just for show!)

So, what do you think... reasonable numbers?
