Back when I used to play LSN, a long time ago, there was a very popular competition. Matches would be played, but beforehand the punters would bet their imaginary money on the result.
The banker would set out spread betting options and allow people to buy or sell. There would also be side bets, such as the time the first unit got killed... that kinda thing.

So this thread is for expressions of interest. How many of you would want to open a virtual string of credit and take part, if I were to start something like this up on here using Minitroopers matches?
Play it as a Belt kinda competition but before each match everyone taking part gets to bet. Whoever wins the most imaginary money each time gets to be the next challenger.

If we get, say, eight people who want to take part then it would be worthwhile and I'll make it happen. People should be aware though that there could be days between matches as we wait for everyone to place their bets... so it's more of a slow, thoughtful competion than a fast paced extravaganza. Signing up means at the very least several weeks of competition. Probably more, until my study timetable gets to be too much and I have to step down - at which point we'd either crown a winner for most money won, or someone else could take over the admin duties and keep things going.

So! Anyone interested?
