How does everyone feel about leveling their units?

Do you...
-Balance the levels between all units?
-Improve an already strong unit to become stronger?
-Improve your weakest/least useful unit until it becomes decent?
-Put priority into maxing the number of units?
*If you do this, when do you find it right to level up another unit?

I personally balance all my levels, but I put my stronger units first in line towards the beginning then in the middle my weaker units and finally my average units last.

This is how I feel who needs to level first.
-Smart units.
-Units with 0 or 1 equipment, I try to minimize getting max equipment but I always hope for a good piece.
-Units with a specific plan in mind. For me I put priority in my game changing units. Spies, speed units, and Doctors without Savior all with hopes to make my army effective with minimal levels.
-Pilots without vehicles, I firmly am against having any Pilots, but their usefulness basically relies on obtaining a vehicle.
-Comms. and Sabs. until they reach comfortable numbers in their points.
-Units with limited game plans. Units with few weapons or abilities that make them stand out.
-Soldiers and Spies where levels improve their passive abilities.
-Doctors and Scouts in hopes to gain their class specific abilities (Savior, Amphetamine Shot, Binoculars).
-Everyone else.

I basically go down the list and combine each of the descriptions to see which is the most appealing of my units to level.

I'm curious if anyone agrees or has a different way of looking at this.
