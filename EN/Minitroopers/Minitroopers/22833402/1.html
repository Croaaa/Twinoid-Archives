Kompera

And an old thread in the 'other forums' which has a lot of discussion on Exterminate missions and might be worth a read.

What I've had the best success with is a mix of rifles and machine guns, and specifically the fast CK-Magellan rifle and the fast and accurate machine guns such as the Minigun and the Comanche Auto. I've also tried to avoid skills with only a limited amount of uses per battle (grenades, some shells, etc.) in favor of 'always on' skills, since Exterminate missions run very long.

Having at least one Munitions officer is key, or you'll just run out of ammunition against the mass hoard of rats, and a Comms. officer is also nice for the special orders every 400 ticks on the clock. And certain skills such as Frenetic are just as useful against rats as they are against other armies.

Saboteurs and Spies are less useful, since the rats have no weapons to sabotage and going anywhere near melee (such as a Spy deployment in the enemy line) against rats is a bad idea. But these are great officer types for fights with other armies, so if you avoid them you'll be better against rats but less effective against other armies.

Good luck!
