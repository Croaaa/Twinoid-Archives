This is a team competition. Each team will consist of three different armies.
The competition will have several phases.

PHASE ONE - SIGN UP
Everyone who wants to take part can post in this thread with a link to their army. Also they need to roll a d100 like this: 27
Each player may only enter ONE army to this competition.
Sign-ups will continue until 24 armies are listed.
When sign-ups are complete the eight players with the highest rolls will become the team captains.

PHASE TWO - CHOOSING SIDES
The team captains will then take it in turns to select from the pool of armies which are signed up, until each team has three armies listed.

PHASE THREE - FIXTURE DRAW
There will be a random draw for knockout fixtures, team versus team. There will be three rounds of single-elimination matches.

PHASE FOUR - FIGHT!
For each fixture, the team captains will Rock/Paper/Scissors against each other to see who has to declare their army first. Then each team can discuss their choice of army to fight with. The captain's decision is final.
The fight will be a single challenge, winner takes all, sent by the player listed first in the fixture draw. As usual, MISS or BAT fights may not be counted.
Each army may ONLY be used once during the competition.
Deadlines will be in place for declaration of army used and also for sending of challenges. Matches will be every two days.
