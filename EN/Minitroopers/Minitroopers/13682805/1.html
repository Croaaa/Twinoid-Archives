Situation One:
/hq link is posted.
Someone who just wants the recruit clicks it.

Situation Two:

Someone who just wants the recruit has to click on the minitroopers banner to bring up the hq page.

pickupsticks...check anyone other than just the recruit immediately preceding theirs. But does anyone do that? I do that. I look over the new pages in RotH almost every day, some days I randomly check links if I can not see other suspicious behaviour. I try to check all links from players with bad history.

I like Situation Two, /ranks.

I think that both situation one and two are talking about the same workload but it is reassigning that workload.
Situation One: one person does a larger amount of work.
Situation Two: many people do a little more work.

With situation two (/ranks) I could check more links in the same time.

Here is another reason why I like situation two...
Talking to offenders I sometimes get the impression that they thought that I could not check the link.
That is, they did not know about the /ranks trick.
If we use /ranks links all of the time then new players would know about it, they would know that we can check, they would know that they would be caught and so they would not try to cheat.

education = prevention
prevention > policing (or punishment)

The need for policing will go down using /ranks links.


My only concern is that players may feel less free.
I like to think that the players here feel free, some people post 'You:' other post 'above:' or '^:' It's all fine.

So if we do use /ranks I think it should only be a recommendation and not a rule.
