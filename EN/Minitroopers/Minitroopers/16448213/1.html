Just a nub here, so my opinions are not based on any experience or knowledge in the slightest.

Murder - Saboteur is best hat. I'm really not sure what you want to do with him, though; he's got some long-ranged weapons and then a lot of close-range skills; Faceboot and Huge Calves are hardly what you want on a sniper/rocket guy.
Suicide - Having a Doctor is always fun, but he's the same as Murder. You've got a bazooka and a decent sidearm alongside a shotgun and close-combat skills. I never thought Vicious was useful in the slightest, doubly so on rockets, and Sprinter is a pretty much useless choice.
Death - It's always good to have a Comms Officer for all his fancy buffs, but I'm pretty sure that once he dies they stop triggering, so having him be your close combat guy is a bit risky. Other than that, though, he seems to be a decent guy; Pump Action shotguns were always my favorite. But... Talky-Walky? You only have four guys...
Kill - This is a classic shock trooper here, probably your strongest link. Twinoids are amazing, and with boss HP (he should be pushing 20, right?) and decent dodge, he should be doing his job pretty damn well.
