On august 11, 2012

The numbers of troopers per wave is 4, if you get a scout the number of troopers that can deploy in a wave increases to 5, for every scout you get, 1 more trooper will be able to join the fight. BTW if you get a trooper with Out of Bound that trooper will be able to deploy with the others without the need of a scout in your team.
