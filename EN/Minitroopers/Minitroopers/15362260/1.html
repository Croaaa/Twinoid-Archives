I would like to share my thoughts about skills, some changes and new ones. This is just my sketch, feel free for comment and share your ideas, and PLEASE, help me (:

CHANGES

Like Wife Beater, other skills like Camouflage and Bait could have a visual change in the uniform. (Bait could have a target in the chest, or shine like in the description)

Talking about Wife Beater, why this skill uses two slots, when the description says: "I feel much more free in my vest."?
In my opinion, this skill could increases two slots, using one.
Example: A trooper have Laser Sights, Grenade and Paralysing Shells. At level 7 he pick Wife Beater, then, he still have a free slot, having a total of 5 slots.

Armor-piercing should null killshot penalties (Full Metal Baclava, Bullet Proof Vest and Helmets)

Barrel Extension should add +1 maximum damage from all weapons, instead of give 10% aiming bonus, without changing the range bonus.
Example: (Default) Pistol, without Barrel Extension: 2~3 dmg. With Barrel Extension: 2~4 dmg.

Rucksack is not used at the same time of munitions, preserving it for another moment .

Doctors can auto-heal. Why not ?

Some yellow skills don't affect the movement skill, but the action speed. For make it clear, when these skills are activated, they became a buff (speeding).
- Thermos Coffee: Passive buff of 25% speeding.
- Adrenaline: Every miss add 5% of speeding for 500 turns, making this skill like Amphetamine Shot.
- Enthusiastic: 25% of speeding as long as <trooper> isn't wounded.
- Survival Instincts: 50% of speeding when the life poins are halved.
- Amphetamine Shot: 100% of speeding when used, for 500 turns.
- Wife Beater: Passive buff of 30% speeding.
- Hyperactif: Passive buff of 50% speeding.

The M16 Assault RIfle is a wronged weapon. The M16 have a good capacity, but the damage is the same of default Assault RIfle. For having 100 recovery, this weapon should have better damage. My guess is about 2~4 or 3~4 dmg.
The possibility of changing the order of recruits (which doesn't exist) could change the performance in raids, winning 1 or 2 more raids every day.

NEW SKILL

Helmets:

Engineer: Have a gear in the helmet
- Ability: Fix ally vehicles, Additional +1 fix with each 3 level-up. Always is deployed togheter with a vehicle and without deployment cost (like Out of Bounds). Increases the army chances of a vehicle deployment. Gain cover bonus from vehicles.
- Used: When a ally vehicle have the life points halved.
- Quote: "Oh my... My tank gonna blow! Relax Bob, I can fix to you"

Veteran: Use a army cap, and is smoking a cigarette.
- Ability: Doubles the general speed (+100% speeding). When any trooper moves, <trooper> reloads the clip of the weapon which he is using.
- Used: Always (Passive)
- Quote: "Watch and learn, boys".
