RULEBOOK:

Obviously, only solo armies are allowed. Anyone may join, and the seeds will be determined by a "power-rank system".

Format will be BO3-double elimination. In the case of bugged replays, the "space feature" will be applied; if not possible, the match may be played again.

Only fights where you sent the challenge (are on the left) are allowed. Only direct challenge fights are allowed. Fights that end in BAT1/BAT2/BAT3 or MISS1/MISS2/MISS3 are not eligible.

Anyone can challenge his/her enemy before the deadline.

Prizes will be as follows:

1st: 4 recruits with 250 gold each
2nd: 3 recruits with 250 gold each
3rd: 2 recruits with 250 gold each
4th: 1 recruit with 250 gold each
5th/6th: 1 recruit with 100 gold each

No form of hacking/cheating/exploitation is allowed. If found to be doing such, the army will be disqualified from the tournament.

One army per account only. It is considered unethical for the same person to use different accounts.

Everyone is expected to RESPECT each other before, during, and after the match, game, and the tournament.
