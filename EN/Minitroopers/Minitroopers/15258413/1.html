The shotgun is a decent weapon in it's own right. Granted, it's outclassed by other shotguns, but for the fact that it's the starting weapon, it can do alot of damage.

Assault Rifle, while not keen on damage, has the fact that it's the only 1 of the starter weapons that actually shoots more than 1 shot per volley going for it. And with a few upgrades, even it can be a dangerous weapon.

Sniper, while I am reluctant to say it, isn't too bad. Granted, you'll want to replace it, but as it is, it can deliver a killshot at a crucial time, even if fire time and reload times are poor.

SilentPinoy

Shotgun > Assault Rifle > Pistol = Sniper

Basically:
Shotgun best,
+Knockdown
+Spread Damage
+Fastest Aim/Shoot/Reload
-Shortest Range
Assault Rifle next,
+Good Ammo Stock
+Fastest Aim/Shoot
-Slowest Reload
-Lowest Accuracy
-Average Range
-Average Damage, but what else do you expect from a starter
Try to ignore the others.
Pistol
+Good Range
+Good Accuracy
-Poor Damage
Sniper
+Best Range
+Highest Accuracy/1 Hit KO potential(Considering they're starter weapons)
-Poor Damage
-Slowest to Aim/Fire/Reload basically damage output
-Ruins Setups, mostly when dealing with short range built shock troopers
