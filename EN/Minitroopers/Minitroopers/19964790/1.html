Remember those Twinoid's adversements?? How about earn T's intead them??
I was thinking about an old poll on MT wikia, which asks about what would be added on the game. The most voted is one that would be added ways to make money...

What about Duels, I mean, 1x1??

My first though was select one trooper from your army then battle. I made a few classifications for battles: Range of Weapons, Level, Helmet battle...

Then I though: what about create other new troopers every day? The rules would be quite simple:
- There will be three Duels at one day, likely Battles and Missions.
- Likely Raids, a dead Trooper cannot fight again. You must create another one, being three per day... If all of them fall, only tomorrow my friend.
- There will be only three levels (modalities) avaliable: 5, 10 and 15. You must reach those levels on your army troopers to unlock those levels on Duel.
- You would pay about 3 to 5 T's for every trooper (3 troopers per day), with random starter Weapon and starter Skill (being Red, Yellow or Blue).
- After that, you will pay for skills, selecting by the colors. Obviously, Red, Blue and Yellow Skills will be cheaper and easier to get than Green and Cyan, for example.
- The following modalities will have 5 more upgrades than the level (5 [10] and 10 [15]). Obs: those 5 more upgrades are optional. There won't be Skills that need a superior level to reach, I mean, in modality 5, won't be allowed Helmets; and goes on.
- After picking the best Skills, the trooper would be ready to challenge another one in the same modality and level.
- The base of winning money is win (of course), and the loser would be pay back.

I didn't explain it clearly , though, then I will give some examples:

A normal day, after 'all' done in my army, I've three Duels to battle. Fine.
I've one free Trooper Duel slot, too. Let me create a random Trooper lvl 1: Pistol and Dodger (Blue Skill) It costs 3~5T
After that, I'll have to choose between a random Red, Blue or Yellow Skill.

All of them costs 1~3T
I've chosen Red, and I received an Ak-47. (Trooper is lvl 2)
I've chosen Yellow, and I received Nimble Fingers. (Trooper is lvl 3)
I've chosen Yellow, and I received Zig-Zag (Trooper is lvl 4)
I've chosen Blue, and I received Bullet Proof Vest (Trooper is lvl 5)

Now, I can change that preffered target, and stuff...
He's ready to Duel ! But, I want let him better... I've 5 other choices to pay.
Now, Green, Cyan and Orange skills are enabled. Fine.

Red, Blue and Yellow costs now 3~5T
I've chosen Yellow, and I received Enthusiastic (Trooper is lvl 5)
I've chosen Red, and I received Fragmentation Grenade (Trooper is lvl 5)

Green, Cyan and Orange skills costs 5~7T
I've chosen Green, and I received Battle Ready (Trooper is lvl 5)

Fine, I already spent 18~34T on my daily Duel trooper... LOL

Now, I must choose between those skills, I mean, only six skills will be allowed
(I don't know if starter Weapon and Skill will be bind with the trooper)

Let's choose: Pistol, Dodger, Ak-47, Battle Ready, Enthusiastic and Frag Grenade

Fine. He finaly will Duel. There will be three ends:
- You win! (Your trooper didn't die, you earn the money that your oponent spent with him Duel Trooper... Yes, ~30T for example )
- You lost D: (Your trooper died, you received the money that you spent with him, and your oponent received the money that you spent with your guy)
- If a Trooper died and his enemy is poisoned, he will must survive ~50 on the clock for win, if both die, both die

If your Trooper survives, he can Duel again and again, being alive for days
How I already said, there will be only three Duels per day, then, if your Trooper dies on the 1st Duel, you will earn the money that you spent on him, and your 1st Duel is lost. If your Trooper dies on the 2nd Due, you will earn the money that you spent on him, and the 2nd Duel is lost. Boooooooooring If your Trooper dies AGAIN, (poor bastard) you will earn the money that you spent on him, and the last Duel is lost.

BUT, if you win the 1st Duel, the 2nd and the 3rd with the same trooper, he will be alive for the next day, and goes on.

Duel system is great because you will never lost money, you will only don't earn it

There are some ideas of only using Skills that you have on your troopers.

For enable daily Duels, you should pay around ~100T (like 5T for Mission, but a little more)

I really don't know how it would work, but is something fun that I would like to share with you guys (:
