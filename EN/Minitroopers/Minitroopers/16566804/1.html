Welcome to the first Minitroopers Scavenger Hunt!

Now is your chance to meet interesting armies from all over the world, and kill them.

WHO CAN TAKE PART
Anyone with an army on the English server can take part. There are no power restrictions or trooper limits for who may enter. A player is only allowed to use ONE army to take part.

HOW DO I WIN?
Below are a list of a dozen weapons and skills. Scavengers try to "collect" each of the items on the list. The first to collect them all is the winner!

WHAT DO I WIN?
Well... not very much, to be honest. Bragging rights I suppose.

HOW DO I "COLLECT" AN ITEM?
You kill people! Fun, right? But before you can kill them, you must find them. Find an army that has the skill or weapon you want to claim for, and defeat them.

WHICH ARMIES CAN I FIGHT?
There are two rules here that are very important.
1) The NO SOFT PLAY rule. You can only claim by defeating an army that has the same power rating as you, or higher.
2) The NO REPEATS rule. If someone else has already claimed any item using a particular opponent then you can't use them too: you have to find a different army to defeat.

HOW DO I CLAIM?
Beat the army using an infiltrate mission (no BAT or MISS fights are allowed) and post the replay in this thread.
Then copy and paste the item list, and add your army name underneath the item that you have claimed for, along with who you fought.

e.g.
Fred's army defeats Pete's army to claim the Twinoid item.
Fred would make a post like this, adding his name to whoever is already on the list:

http://www.Fred-Army.minitroopers.com...

TWINOID
Charlie: beat Roger's army
Fred's Army: beat Pete's army

OTHER ITEM
Steve: beat Alan

AND ANOTHER ITEM
Alan: beat Charlie

Make sense so far? If you've got any questions then feel free to ask and I'll try to answer them as best I can.
I won't be able to check every replay so hopefully you lot can double check to make sure no mistakes are made. The List that's copied each time should make it easy to see who has claimed what, and which armies have been used (and can't be used again)

WHEN CAN WE START?
Right now! Here's the list of items you need to look for. Some of them may be more tricky to find than others. Good luck!
