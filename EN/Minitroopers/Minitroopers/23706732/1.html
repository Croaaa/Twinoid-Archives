What is a theme army?
A theme army is an army where ever trooper in the army starts with the same skill.
It's a great way of testing that skill with a variety of other skill combinations.

Two well known examples of theme armes...
Twinoid all troopers started with the Twinoid skill.
Dr.Frenetic all troopers started with the Frenetic skill.

Do you know any more?
