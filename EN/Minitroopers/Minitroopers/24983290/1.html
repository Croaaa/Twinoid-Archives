Based on this battle http://971marsu.minitroopers.com/b/fv... here is my suggestion:

On the subject of the Pilot

1. Try putting your munitions Georgater in the first wave and set him to target Pilots, since he have Covering Fire if he manages to aim at the Pilot first then his CF skill would trigger killing the Pilot.

2. Somehow you would need to take out your own Spy from the first wave since he is contributing to the speed of the Suspicious Pilot and he just ends up as fodder to his rampage. Since you have 2 spies I don't know how you would be able to do that.


The next problem is the Spy

1. The most effective anti-spy I saw that you have are Georgater and Maxieber the Sab(if he deploys from the bike). I suggest putting them both in the first wave.

2. Try leveling those two trooper and see if they get offered speed boosters.


As of now just try upgrading your troopers that you need to combat the Pilot and Spy of Marsu you'll eventually catch up with him in the level department.
