Hello all! I'm making a new championship, called The Credit Cup! This game will be somewhat like the Continous Championship, in the sense that it will have a list of players playing.

Here are the rules:

- You must have more than 250 credits AT THE MOMENT to join.
- Your army can have any number of troopers.
- You will have to fight and win against the army on top of you to advance.
- You must post your winning replay.
