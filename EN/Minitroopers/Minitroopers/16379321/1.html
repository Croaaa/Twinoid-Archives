"Rate My Army!"Ok, Maanam has been thoroughly teased. Now some love.

My rating will assuming a comparison with a similar level trooper, similar size army.

1. - Saboteur is great in a 4 man army. Decent weapon load. I'll assume he is set to use either the Bazooka or a MOS, and is a long range support trooper. No initiative enhancers. Neither shotgun or Faceboot are likely to be used unless the enemy closes. Scavenger has a decent range.

2. - Again assuming that Bazooka M25 is default. Desert Eagle is a good backup with good range. Don't really need Sprinter if you're a support trooper. No initiative enhancers.

3. - Weapons load is for an assault trooper, but you've got no way to get there fast. And a Comms. Officer on assault isn't so ideal because you'll lose your orders when he dies. Talky-Walky is useless until you get another trooper since you'll fully deploy your 4 troopers. Biped is a marginal equipment item. No initiative enhancers.

4. - Here's your best build IMO. An assault troop with the speed to get there fast, the skills to keep going, and the health of a Soldier. Bait is an interesting selection, but assault troops are often targeted first just by virtue of being close by, so you're trading being hit more often for 8 health. How has that worked for you?

I'll give you 5/10.

You need initiative, especially on the two troopers with launchers. Fire those first and you might just end the engagement. Either On Point (preferable) or Twinoid would help. Frenetic also, when you can get multiple kills with a launcher. Look to get Kill a PAS or dual pistols. Frenetic would keep him killing.
