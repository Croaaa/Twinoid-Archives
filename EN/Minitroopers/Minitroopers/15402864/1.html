RULEBOOK:

1. Everyone from Europe can join the competition, including Turkey, Azerbaijan, Kazakhstan, Georgia, Armenia, and Russia.
2. There is no minimum power rating (PR) limit. However, it is suggested that one has at least 30 PR before joining.
3. Format will be BO3-double elimination. In the case of bugged replays, the "space feature" will be applied; if not possible, the match may be played again.
4. The slots for the World Finals will be as follows:

ASIA=6 (reduced to 2 for participation reasons) (DONE)
EU=12
NA/SA=6
FINAL CALL=8 (increased to 12 to enable more people to get in)

for a total of 32 slots.

5. Only fights where you sent the challenge (are on the left) are allowed. Only direct challenge fights are allowed. Fights that end in BAT1/BAT2/BAT3 or MISS1/MISS2/MISS3 are not eligible.
6. Anyone can challenge his/her enemy before the deadline.
7. Prizes will be as follows:

OPEN CLASS:

1st: 4 recruits with 250 gold each+ Qualifying Slot
2nd: 3 recruits with 250 gold each+ Qualifying Slot
3rd: 2 recruits with 250 gold each+ Qualifying Slot
4th: 1 recruit with 250 gold each+ Qualifying Slot
5th/6th: 1 recruit with 100 gold each+ Qualifying Slot

Of course, there will be consolation prizes:

For armies that are below 61 PR:

Best seed=4 recruits; 2nd seed=3 recruits, 3rd seed=2 recruits, 4th seed=1 recruit

For armies that are below 121 PR:

Best seed=8 recruits; 2nd seed=6 recruits, 3rd seed=4 recruits, 4th seed=2 recruits

8. Please allow 7-30 days for the total delivery of prizes.
9. No form of hacking/cheating/exploitation is allowed. If found to be doing such, the army will be disqualified from the tournament.
10. One army per account only. It is considered unethical for the same person to use different accounts.
11. Everyone is expected to RESPECT each other before, during, and after the match, game, and the tournament.

NOTE:

Last day of registration is 00:00 CET (Central EU time, GMT+1), September 17, 2012.

QUALIFIERS are as follows:

Last week of August=Asian qualifiers (Admin: Lost In Dreams)
Last week of September=EU qualifiers (Admin: TBA)
Last week of October=NA/SA qualifiers (Admin: TBA)
Last week of November=Global Call (free-for-all) (Admin: TBA)
Anytime in December=World Championships (Admins: TBA)

For questions, please post in the comments below!

GOOD LUCK and HAVE FUN!
