I try to avoid skills that benefit PvP battles and try to choose only those that benefit rat missions. Doesn't always work of course.

My favourite skills are Nimble Fingers and Cold Blooded and will chose them over Frenetic or On Point. And if I have a Frenetic trooper I try to get him Survival Instinct as well.

Also the deployment setup of my army is quite peculiar. I start the mission with 5 troopers and get 3 reinforcement waves of 2 troopers each during the battle + a reinforcement of a spy.

The average mission looks like this:

5 troopers in the first wave: Main force - 2 munitions troopers and 1 saboteur (2 snipers and 1 rocket launcher). Support force - 1 doctor and 1 soldier that act as a barrier to protect the main force.

When the doctor and soldier die a second support force comes in - 1 munitions and 1 scout. This is the second wave and also the second barrier.

After the second barrier dies a third comes in - 1 munitions and 1 saboteur.

They also die and a fourth wave and a fourth barrier comes in - 2 comms. troopers (both snipers).

Somewhere in between of all this a lone spy shows up and usually gets killed instantly. He is pretty useless so he acts as a decoy.

With this setup I have 4-5 troopers on the field for about halfway through the mission. After that it's just the main force.
