It's been at least five minutes since I've run a competition on here, so how about another random-draw knockout cup?

I'm going to try something a little different this time.

PARTICIPANTS
The first 32 people to post "I sign up" and a link to their army will play. There are no restrictions to power or trooper number for the entrants. Each player may only enter ONE army.

THE DRAW
I'll make a random draw and post the fixtures here. There won't be any seeding or grouping according to power, just the luck of the RNG.

TIMEFRAME
The competition will start a couple of days after the sign-up sheet is filled, to give everyone a fair chance not to miss the beginning.
Each round will last for one day. If any player in each round does not post their fights by the end of the day then they will be eliminated by default.

THE FIGHTS
In each fixture, both opponents challenge their opponent three times.
Scoring will be by total number of dead enemies, with the highest total bodycount for all six matches combined being the winner.
Only units which are "dead" at the end of the fight will score. The following do not score: spies which have not deployed, units which have been removed from the field with saviour, units which have used survivor and are still standing.

REPORTING
Post a link to replays for all three fights in this thread, along with a note as to the bodycount for both armies in each fight. You may put this note in spoiler tags if you wish, but it MUST be included in the post. Only "numbered" direct-challenge fights count. Fights that end MISS1/MISS2/MISS3 or BAT1/BAT2/BAT3 do not count. If you have more than three fights per day available to your army then only the first three may be used.
When the second player posts their replays they should also include the totals from ALL SIX fights, with a note as to who won the match.

OTHER RULES
In the event of a ruling being required, my decision will be final. Questions or suggestions may be posted in this thread, but I reserve the right to ignore them all!

Sign ups are now open.
