Ok before I go HAM all on this, this is just another idea. I need input on scoring (how much for each value and additional criteria). Once again I will also be in charge of organizing, however this idea will require tons less work from people, but probably more thought input.

So yeah many of my ideas are convoluted and require lots of work. But this one I think everyone can appeal to.

So for anyone who doesn't know what a Fantasy League is basically take different members of different teams and create a team. Depending on the sport, statistics of each player are gathered and evaluated, then converted into a total point value for the created team.

I've wondered a little bit which members of which teams statistically are better, of course we won't know 100% and team synergy does play a lot of role in a successful army, but in this way we can see a little how in our eyes which units together would make the strongest MT team.

The only effort I need from people is to post replays from the other game threads into a single forum with participating troopers so I can organize data and creating their own draft picks. It's better this way so that people fighting each other end up fighting armies around their skill level and have a chance to showcase units(most of the time).

Here's how I would organize this:

Team Draft

-Each participant selects units from active forum players' armies. An active army must have a posted replay on the forums within at least a week of their last replay.
-Each player will compose their team of...

An "active" Comms.
An "active" Sab.
An "active" Doctor
A Spy
4 non-spy units

-"Active" units will have an additional individual scoring card while all 8 units will have a universal scoring. These individual scores will add points based on additional things these units can do.
-Each participant can only select up to 2 units from an individual army.
-Keep in mind that things such as Sab% and Comms. Points, while communal among whole armies, will still count as an individual score. I will justify this because the units put forth their contribution. Keep this in mind when selecting these units from teams.
-Use of Vehicles is something I'm unsure of still. I'm more inclined to include a special scoring for vehicle use, or null and void replays involving vehicles for all participation.

How the league will work:

After drafts have been made participants will be organized into a tournament or round robin bracket(depending on interest).
