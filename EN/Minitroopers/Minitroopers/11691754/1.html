PREVIEW:
Knife Fighter goes for the “knife-themed” build which involves speed and timing. In addition, he has equipped Loader on two of his troopers, so that in case the knives don’t work, they would be able to use their weapons efficiently even at close range. A possible weakness would be the “shotgun-HMG strat”, which involves having lots of shotguns and firepower to take down the otherwise melee-based army.
In the meanwhile, Dr. Frenetic goes for the rather standard “Spy+Speed+HMG” and “Launcher+HMG Abuse” strategies, which work because of the intense firepower and area of effect (AoE) damage at hand. A notable thing to note is that he has speed buffs on his army; maybe he wants to engage his opponent quickly. He should be vulnerable to the "Spy+Twinoid+HMG+On Point" combo.
Both sides have Poison Grenades in addition to standard ones; although this will not have much of an impact on the game (as Knife Fighters has only one Munitions trooper). On the other side, both of them have Saboteurs which will surely make an impact in this game...In what way will it be? Let's find out together!
Will the knives win? Or will the classic “metal rain” take this game? Let’s find out!

Bomba Samers throws a poison grenade which hits all of Knife Fighter’s troopers except Bull; the Red side has its hands full right now.
Both sides start to shoot at each other; Zen Warrior rushes in with his shotgun and reduces Burterg Jr’s HP by half, and Keverg throws a grenade that sends Rabbit flying into the air…dead.

He dies after a few more blows, and the tanky Comms. Officer has started to wreak havoc all over the place. All this time, Zen Warrior has suffered from the poison, and he quickly dies hereafter.
It took two revolvers and some fists to knock down Bull, and all of a sudden, only one remains: Pinpoint. While he comes into the field, Frenetic’s Doctor healed Burterg Jr, and it just means more trouble for the Red side: With weapons and fists at the ready, they can overwhelm the Comms. Officer in a matter of moments!
But then, a surprise happens: Pinpoint activates his solo mission, and Keverg goes down after three shots! Burterg follows thereafter, and only the brave doctor remained…Moments later, he died, and it was GG for Dr. Frenetic.
POSTVIEW:
Man of the Match: Bull (for helping Knife Troopers maintain a presence throughout the game)
Flop of the Match: Yull (since his HMG got sabotage, he wasn’t able to do his work. And it is well-known that Frenetic’s spies are deadly when they do their job.)
Breaking Point: When Rickiham missed that Infernal Tube shot, he lost a chance to secure a very large advantage.
Final Notes: Even with Yull’s failure and Bull’s presence, the match could have gone Dr. Frenetic’s way. However, it seemed that the Violet side’s firepower did not come into play in this game, which meant that he has lost control of an important part of the game.
The replay was fun, so I’m giving 8/10. Lots of things to learn too from this game.

Overall, 7/10. Great match, great viewing value, and certain things to learn:
1. Tanks should be taken out ASAP if they exist in an enemy’s army.
2. Twinoid does not work 100% all the time, thanks to the not-so-smart behavior of the troops.
3. They are examples of how an army should be: NO friendly fire!
That’s all guys! Hope you enjoy!!!
