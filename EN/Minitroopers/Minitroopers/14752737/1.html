Some time ago there was a discussion on Speed & Initiative and how heavy weapons impacted it.

After watching for some time, I conclude that heavy weapons do impact movement speed cumulatively, but do not impact Initiative or firing speed at all.

Here is a Trooper with three heavy weapons and no Sturdy skill. The effect on his movement is easy to see, but he still fires as rapidly as he would if he had the Sturdy skill:
