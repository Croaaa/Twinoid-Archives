Then the shooting starts once again, and then Holsten takes the Twinoid too. In the meantime, Campbell sends Aizen to the hands of Death and attempts to kill the Violet doctor, but two Tuck and Rolls have saved his life!

Final Wave:

Violet: Kevers, Ichigo, Micha, Toussen, Zommaru Rure (survivor)

Otherwise, his troops performed really well in the match!
