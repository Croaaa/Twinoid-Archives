Here's a fun game. Imagine that you had 6 themed armies. Each army's troopers all have the same weapon.

The first army's troopers all have one specific weapon of the Pistol family (Pistol, Revolver, Beretta, Desert Eagle, Dual Pistols).
The second army's troopers all have one specific weapon of the Shotgun family (Shotgun, Semi-Auto Shotgun, Double-Barrelled Shotgun, Pump-Action Shotgun, Scattergun).
The third army's troopers all have one specific weapon of the Assault Rifle family (Assault Rifle, M16, AK47, FAMAS, Thompson).
The fourth army's troopers all have one specific weapon of the Machine Gun family (Heavy Machine Gun, Gatling Gun, Minigun, Comanche Auto).
The fifth army's troopers all have one specific weapon of the Sniper Rifle family (Sniper, MOS-TECK, Lizaro Jungle, Sparrowhawk, CK Magellan).
Finally, the last army's troopers all have one specific Launcher (Bazooka M1, Bazooka M25, Rocket Launcher, Infernal Tube).

Which weapons would you pick to theme your armies with?
