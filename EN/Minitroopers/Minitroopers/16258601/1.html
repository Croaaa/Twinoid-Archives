I think how it works is that troopers have a certain zone in front of them (similar to grenades having an AoE and there being a deployment 'zone') which is their line of sight.

Troopers getting in front of one another can mess with this line of sight and cause misses and friendly fire, which Blind Fury negates (it counters flashbangs too?).

Brick sh*thouse is similar, if a trooper with it is in front of another trooper, bullets fired are more likely to hit him and not the trooper behind him.
