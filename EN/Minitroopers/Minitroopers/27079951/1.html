I thought of a new competition idea we could do in the future. I need to know how many people are interested as well as some suggestions on some issues. It's a rough idea and a long wall of text, so bare with me.

---
This continuous ladder will test your ability to work with what you got. Here are the rules.

To sign up, participants are to make a new army w/ password and add a link to the HQ link w/ army name and player name besides it. From here on, usual ladder rules applies to this competition.
What make this ladder different from all other ladders is you do not have control over what upgrades your troopers receive in the army you signed up.

How this will work is each participant will have a partner. This partner is in charge of the upgrades their partner's army receive (Example: Person A and Person B are partners. Person A control Upgrades of Person B, and vice versa.) To do this, both partners will post the upgrades choice of each trooper they upgrade via PM. The other partner will choose the upgrade for the other person, in which that person has to follow through, even if it's a terrible choice.

This gives the potential for the other person to give your troopers poor upgrades. This is intentional and allowed, as the point of this ladder is to climb to the top with what you get from the other player. Besides, you get to do the same back should it happen.


This is only an idea right now, and I need to know how many people are interested. I also need suggestions on certain things.

1.How would we pair off the participants? Would it be by choice, by numerical order, or by RNG?
2. What if there was an odd number of people participating? How would we give them a partner?
3.Since this is a ladder, how would we add new players to the ladder since you need a partner in order for have the sense of no control over the army you are competing with.

Thank you if you took the time to read this wall of text.
Additional suggestions are always welcome, should anyone be interested enough to make one.
