Hello all, and welcome to my 5-Color Tourney! This competition will have 6 teams, and each team will 3 members. This tourney will be different, because each team MUST have 3 armies of the same color suit. For example: Robo's Slaves, SABR's Loyalists, and Drape go together, because they are all members of The Wretched Individuals. Each team will go together with a kind of army.

You will have to join one of the 6 teams, and I will pair you up with a member of another team. You will have to fight each other, and post your battle scene here. The winners will move on to the finals, and the winners will win 2 recruits from me, and receive the title of the Championship King!

There will be 3 members on each team, so please post the team you want to be on. There can only be 10 troopers or less on each player's army.

To be the leader of the team, each player will have to roll a 100 sided dice. This can be found under the RP Gadgets section. To make it a little different, the player with the LOWEST number will be chosen as the leader.

You cannot choose the name of your team; it will be chosen by default as the name of the faction. Ex: Team Name: Wretched Individuals.

I will also be participating in this tourney.

Thanks for reading, and please join!
