The Minitroopers Wiki

If you're new to Minitroopers or if your a seasoned veteran, if you would like to view all of the trooper skills available or maybe you would like to share something new that you have discovered about the game, then the Minitroopers Wiki can be of help.

Link: The Minitroopers Wiki

The wiki is a ever growing and changing place fulled with information.
The information is not always correct or complete but it is always getting better.

The Minitroopers Wiki is a community project, it is not owned by anyone here. It is created through the donated time and effort of the community for the community.

For players with questions the wiki can be a good place to start.
For repeating questions the wiki maybe the best place to post up the answers.

If you would like to help us build a better wiki please feel free to contribute in anyway that you can. This could be as simple as pointing out spelling mistakes or as complicated as running tests on theories.
Thanks for your time.

Minitroopers Technical Problems

Are you having account problems?
Some things you may want to try...

Reloading the web page.
To reload a page hit the F5 key or try holding the Control key and then hit R. Link

Resetting your browser window size.
To do this hold down the Control key (Ctrl) and then hit the number 0 on the keyboard.

Updating or changing your browser.
Chrome and Firefox are both fast free browsers.

For any browser to be working properly all of these next things need to be true.
The browser...

...is accepting cookies,
...has javascript turned on,
...is not in "private browsing" mode and
...preferably is not using a proxy.

Also your Flash settings should be set to allow websites to save information on your computer.
To do this right click on a trooper on the side of your HQ page, and click on "Global Settings". Look for the option to allow all sites to store information on your computer.

If you are still having technical problems you may start a new forum thread asking for help or send a private message to coro, doomys or myself.

Please try to be patient and do not disturb the community.
Some problems can not be fixed the same day and some problems can not be fixed at all.
