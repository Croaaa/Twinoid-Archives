How about a tournament for themed army? This is only a rough draft.

My idea is each participant will choose a theme for his army.
Only 3 armies can register under each theme having a level 1 trooper with the themed skill.
For example:
A] Adrenaline-themed Army
1. Participant#1
2. Participant#2
3. Participant#3

B] On-point-themed Army
1. Participant#1
2. Participant#2
3. Participant#3

C] Camouflage-themed Army
1. Participant#1
2. Participant#2
3. Participant#3

Themes may vary depending on the suggestion of the interested participants. Smart and Frenetic are not advised.
Registration phase will start on MM/DD/YY
After registration, you can collect credits with your daily fights and doing missions.
But you are not allowed to recruit yet.
After all the slot for each theme has been occupied the Recruitment and Adding of Troopers phase will commence.
Each army will be limited to 34 Recruits only.This will prevent overpowered armies. It will also maximized the starting credits.
The tournament will only start if all the registered armies have 6 level 1 troopers having the themed skill.
A thread will be created to update the armies about their progress on adding a trooper.

As soon as all the army have 6 troopers each, the tournament will start the next day.
Everyday each team will fight the 3 armies under a team for 1 month.
For example: Day 1
Team A attacks Team B
Team B attacks Team A
Team C attacks Team A

Day 2
Team A attacks Team C
Team B attacks Team C
Team C attacks Team B

Each win = 2 points and each loss = 1 point
Team points will be added and updated at the end of the day.
Upgrading troopers is allowed only after you have fought all the other teams.
Adding the 7th trooper is allowed only if all the troopers reach level 6.
The team who accumulated the highest points after a month will win the
Themed Army Warlord title.
Most Valuable Army will be recognized.

Chadow, are you still interested in starting this competition up?

If so, I'll suggest the following rules, cribbed heavily from the one man army rules:
===
THEMED ARMY LADDER COMPETITION

Rules

1. Your army must have one skill (weapons and equipment are skills) in common for all Troopers. Be sure to protect your army with a password so it stays that way! If your army cannot meet this condition it is disqualified from competition.

2. To join, place your Army Name, linked to your army URL, followed by your player name, at the bottom of the list of participants.

Example: Rocky III / Kompera

3. To move up the rank list, beat the person above you (using 'Infiltrate!' in the Pick a Fight section) and post the replay with the updated list.
It is possible to not realize you weren't at the last page, and use an old version of the list. If this happens, please edit your post to fix it! If you incorrectly fight an army which is not immediately above you in the ladder that fight is not counted.

4. You are responsible for the accuracy of your posts! If you screw up the order of the list and someone posts after you, your fights will be invalidated, not those of the person who went after you.

5. Each player can have up to two armies in the list. You don't fight your own army— you skip over them.

6. If you want to drop out of competition or replace one of your two armies with another, put [RETIRED] next to your retiring army in the list— also, strikethrough the link.

Example: Rocky III / Kompera [RETIRED]
Then if you wish you may add your new army at the bottom of the list.
===
