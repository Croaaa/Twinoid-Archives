Fixing flash (sort of)
1. On your computer, open Chrome.
2. At the top right, click More and then Settings.
3. At the top left, click Settings again.
4. At the bottom, click Advanced.
5. Click "Privacy and security,"
6. Under "Privacy and security," click Content settings.
7. Click Flash.
8. Click Add.
9. Copy and paste your army link.
