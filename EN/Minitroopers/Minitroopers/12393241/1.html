SilentPinoy

In case people don't know.

At least 1 Comms. Point
-Solo Mission
-Commando
-Locked

10 Comms. Points
-Retreat
-Support

20 Comms. Points
-Propaganda

My personal favorite of these is Retreat, for 1 specific scenario.

The interesting thing about Retreat is that it fully resets the units affected, places them last in your line-up, and your incoming wave will have a minor initiative advantage (barred by Restless skilled opponents). My Doctor Unit "Cleric" has the ability Savior that carries a dead unit back into the reserves.

So here is the optimal scenario to exploit this command.

-30 Comms. Points between 3 Comms. Units each with 10 points.
-Only 1 of them needs to be alive for Retreat to activate.
-Cleric "Saviors" my unit Bard who was killed.
-Fighter (or Ranger) who has the skill "Survivor" is killed.
-Rest of wave defeats opponent before 400 initiative, Fighter comes back to life.
-On 400 Cleric and Fighter are the only units alive, Retreat is activated.
-Incoming waves continue as normal.
-If battle drags on and I do not win under normal units, Cleric, Fighter, and Bard come in as an additional wave.
-Cleric can "Savior" another unit, in most cases it will be Bard again or my last unit Monk into the reserves, creating another wave.
-If this wave dies Fighter can use "Survivor" again and join the "Savior'd" unit.

This scenario is possible. I've experienced up to the first created wave, not letting my see the second created wave. I've lost the replay which the idea has worked out and doubt I can replicate it on command.

What this essentially does is make my currently 11 unit army into a potential 18 unit army.
11 Base
2 Savior'd
2 Retreated
3 Survivor (Even with 1 HP I still consider them as a re-deploy since they do not always interfere with the incoming wave deployment count)
+X possibilities for additional Retreats or Propaganda.

Aside from the numbers, the main advantage is the small initiative bonus each incoming wave provides. 4-5 Waves are created in this case rather than my typical 2-3.
