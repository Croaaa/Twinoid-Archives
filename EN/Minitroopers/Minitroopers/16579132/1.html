Testing out an idea here.
Continuous Championship seems to have gotten a bit bloaty: lots of inactive yet strong armies low down making things painful for the active players.

I am considering starting a new Championship each month. At the end of the month the winner would be crowned and then the list would be emptied out, ready to start again from scratch.
That should take care of the inactivity problems, and keep things fresh for those that want to play.

Another possibility I am considering is that each month there would be a theme or condition, such as a power cap (minimum or maximum) or some other restriction. I propose that the winner each month selects the condition for the following month (and makes the new thread to get things going). Hopefully whoever wins will choose something interesting that doesn't upset all the other players

Which brings me to this month. It's the 16th now so we're already half way through. So perhaps Caine and SilentP' will only be half as annoyed at me for my choice of first condition?

Let's give it a try and see how it works.

_ _ _

1) Only armies without a SPY may take part this month. Any army that gets a Spy trooper will be removed from the listing

2) To sign up add a link to your HQ page (tinyurl is allowed to help with forum formatting issues) and army name then playername afterwards. This is to make it as easy as possible for everyone else to see which army name to use to fight.
e.g. http://tinyurl.com/knife-fighters Knife Fighters / pickupsticks
LINK then ARMY NAME then / PLAYER NAME
3) To move up the ranking, beat the person above you then post a link to the replay here. Copy and paste the list, adjusting your position, and re-post it.

4) MAKE SURE you use the correct list from the last post in the thread. MAKE SURE nobody else has posted at the same time as you. If this happens, then edit your post to correct it. Anyone that messes up the list regularly may be excluded from the following month so pay attention.

5) Only fights where you sent the challenge (are on the left) are allowed. Only direct challenge fights are allowed. Fights that end in BAT1/BAT2/BAT3 or MISS1/MISS2/MISS3 are not eligible.

6) Players may NOT go "inactive" however you may choose to remove yourself from the competition at any time.

7) Only one army per player is allowed.

The last correct post to this thread with the forum timestamp of September will be the ranking used to decide the winner.
