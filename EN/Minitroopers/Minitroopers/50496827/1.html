Guidelines for the recruit of the heart.

Welcome to the recruit of the heart
If you follow this pattern you will earn a new recruit.

For this to work you must create a new recruit for the last player who posted here.
Later you will receive your new recruit from the next player to post here.

Each player must show that they have made a new recruit by posting a link to that recruit.
This 'new recruit link' will be the first line in the post.

The whole post will look something like this...

You: http://new-recruit.minitroopers.com/hq
Me: http://my-army.minitroopers.com

The second line is your army link.

Before posting please look at the other people using this thread, try to follow their pattern.
If you are still unsure then feel free to send a private message to coro or myself.

Failure to follow this pattern may have repercussions.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
Other things to know...

Make sure that you are looking at the last page of this thread.
Make sure that you are looking at the last post (refresh your browser).
Do not use this thread if your army does not have a password.
Remember, you can only create two new troops each day.

Being Skipped
If anyone is skipped do not repost.
By reposting you risk skipping other people and this can be just as bad as the original problem.
If someone does make a mistake then it should be between the moderators and them. You may want to send one of us a private message explaining the problem.
Please do not 'play the sheriff'.

Try not to start conversations in this thread.
If you are confused about this thread please do not post here, this thread is not for questions.
All questions, even questions about this thread, should be posted elsewhere (such as the 'Minitroopers hangout').

This thread will become derailed from time to time. When this happens please be patient and do not express your annoyance here.
Send a private message to coro or myself and we will do what we can.

Thanks
