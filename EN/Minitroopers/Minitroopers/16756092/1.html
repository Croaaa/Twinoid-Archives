Lots of useful discussion in this thread, so figured I'd add some input:

All my spies used to be deployed in phases 4-9, and were dead by the time I really need them. I've been able to increase spy survivability by setting some to Defender (only those with assault rifles or sprint), so that they run towards the bulk of my troops before fighting.

Also, if any of my raiders get rocket launchers, I don't set them as their favorite weapon until higher levels (when they are deployed at later raid phases). This way, they are less likely to kill my spies with friendly fire, and have more launcher support skills (aim, initiative/speed, etc).

Thanks to this, my spy deaths to friendly fire have decreased dramatically at lower raid phases
