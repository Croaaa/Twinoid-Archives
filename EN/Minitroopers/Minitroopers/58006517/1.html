Okies, gonna post a manual.

1) You MUST have dynamic IP conncetion type (it won't work with Static)

2) You need to open any replay or start a new battle/mission/raid

3) [Optional] Pause the battle (click left mouse button on battle screen)

4) Open flash settings (click right mouse button on battle screen) and choose "Settings" option in pop-up menu.

https://s19.postimg.org/c9ufmbs2b/mtr...

5) Go to Adobe Flash Player tab and disable local data storage for data.minitroopers.com and close settings tab.

https://s19.postimg.org/o08d3pkur/mtr...
^ back then, you had to do that by changing the setting of local storage size (from 100kb [default] to 0kb)

6) Reconnect to Interent (Close-open VPN connetcion; not sure how it works if you connect via Router... looks like you can only reboot your router... didn't check other ways)

7) Create 2 recruits for an army

8) back to "7)"

Notes :

1. When you disable local data storage for data.minitroopers.com (see "5)") then you WON'T BE ABLE to set a password for a new army!! So you need to enable local data storage if you wanna create your armies that are protecteced with password.

2. When you reconnect ot Internet, it's better to NOT DO that too fast. I used to wait from 5 to 10 seconds before I oppened VPN connection again.

3. Sometimes you may not be able to create new recruits after reconnect. In this case, just reconnect again.

P.S. Last time I added recruits like that in Dec 2013 or so.
