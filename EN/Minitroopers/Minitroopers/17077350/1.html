Before we go back to MT Worlds, there will be another final tourney! Welcome to the MT Swiss Wars, where the battles will be determined using a Swiss-ranking system!

RULEBOOK:

All NON-SPY based armies are allowed. Anyone may join, and the seeds will be determined by a "power-rank system".

In the case of bugged replays, the "space feature" will be applied; if not possible, both sides will receive a draw for that match!

Only fights where you send the challenge (are on the left) are allowed. Only direct challenge fights are allowed. Fights that end in BAT1/BAT2/BAT3 or MISS1/MISS2/MISS3 are not eligible.

Anyone can challenge his/her enemy starting from 00:00 CET to 21:00 CET (that's French time, for your information...and that's also GMT+1, so anyone participating from Asia should check the times accordingly!)

Prizes will be as follows:

1st: 4 recruits with 250 gold each
2nd: 3 recruits with 250 gold each
3rd: 2 recruits with 250 gold each
4th: 1 recruit with 250 gold each

NOTE: Joint placings are POSSIBLE! As usual, expect 7-30 days for the delivery of recruits...

No form of hacking/cheating/exploitation is allowed. If found to be doing such, the army will be disqualified from the tournament.

One army per account only. It is considered unethical for the same person to use different accounts.

Everyone is expected to RESPECT each other before, during, and after the match, game, and the tournament.

Both sides will attack each OTHER.

E.G. Lost In Dreams vs. Pedped

Lost In Dreams (3-0)+replays
Pedped (2-1)+replays

Final Score for Round 1, Match 1: 4-2

Then scores will be calculated in a fair manner (we will come to that).

ROCK AND ROLL!

THE POINT SYSTEM IS AS FOLLOWS:

3.0 points per match win (this applies to the winner of the six-game round)
1.5 points per match tie (3-3 score. OBVIOUS?)
1.0 points per game/set win (there are six games per match, so max of 6 points)
0.5 points per game/set tie (in case only of totally bugged replays)
0.0 points per bye (That's obvious!)

E.G. Caine wins all 6 games (replays). So he'll get 3 points for the match, and only 3 out of 6 points for the game wins (in the first two rounds).

If you agree to this compromise, then I'm opening the competition up for EVERYONE, spy or NO SPY!
