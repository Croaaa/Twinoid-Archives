Because there is a distinct lack of active tournaments in the forums, I figured I would start one.

ABOUT
This tournament is for armies with thirty points or less.
NOTE: I will be ignoring all challenges and requests to join posted inside this thread! Because it is easy to miss posts, tournament business will be handled purely through PMs. Use this thread for any discussions you feel like having.

TO JOIN
Send me a PM with a link to the army you wish to use. One army per user.
NOTE: I will immediately remove any army from the tournament if it either goes above thirty points or I do not receive any PMs from its owner in a month. In the latter case, this can be easily remedied by rejoining the game with your army again.

PVP
There is a ranked list of armies on the last few pages of this thread; I will post periodic updates. New armies begin at the bottom of the list. If you wish to rise through the ranks, send me a PM with a link that includes you defeating an army in that list. Your army will then be placed directly above the defeated army in the rankings.
NOTE: You do not need to challenge the army directly above you; this is to prevent stagnation.
NOTE: I will update the list only infrequently, although it should be no later than once every two days. If you want to find the latest list without digging through the thread, do a search for posts with the keyword 'asdlkj' and made by myself.
NOTE: I would like to join this tournament once a few other people do so. To prevent abuse, I will be posting my own victories alongside the updated list; if I go power-mad and place myself on the top you can always leave and let me wallow in my shallow, meaningless victory.
