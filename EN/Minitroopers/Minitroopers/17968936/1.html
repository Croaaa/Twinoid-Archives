Link: MiniTroopers Gaming Forum
The gaming forum is the place for tournaments, championships and any minitroopers games.


Right now you are in the Minitroopers Main Forum.
The main forum is the place for lengthy discussions, recruiting and any other threads.

If you visit the gaming forum a lot than you may want to use a twinoid shortcut.

Adding a shortcut.
1. At the very top of this page, on the right side, you should see your username. Hover your mouse over your username and a menu will appear.
2. Look for the word 'Shortcuts' and click.
3. On the next webpage move to the very bottom, on the right side look for a button named 'Add'.
4. Just to the left of this button there is a text field.
Copy this line into the text field...
http://twinoid.com/tid/forum#!view/47420
...and then click the add button.

By doing this you will see a new shortcut at the top of your page.
You may want to repeat this for the Main Forum.
For the Main Forum add this line without the space...
http://twinoid.com/en/tid/forum#! view/30466

Hope that helps

Minitroopers Technical Problems

Are you having problems with missions?
Do you get an error message that starts something like this...
"SELECT * FROM User WHERE battleScore<91593..." etc, etc.

We have seen this error a number of times.
To fix it you need to have another army attack you.
This could be any other army, just use one of your own recruits to attack your main army or you could ask someone here in the forum to attack you.


Other account problems?
Some things you may want to try...

Reloading the web page.
To reload a page hit the F5 key or try holding the Control key (Ctrl)and then hit R. Link

Resetting your browser window size.
To do this hold down the Control key and then hit the number 0 on the keyboard.

Updating or changing your browser.
Chrome and Firefox are both fast free browsers.

For any browser to be working properly all of these next things need to be true.
The browser...

...is accepting cookies,
...has javascript turned on,
...is not in "private browsing" mode and
...preferably is not using a proxy.

Also your Flash settings should be set to allow websites to save information on your computer.
To do this right click on a trooper on the side of your HQ page, and click on "Global Settings". Look for the option to allow all sites to store information on your computer.

If you are still having technical problems you may start a new forum thread asking for help or send a private message to coro, doomys or myself.

Please try to be patient and do not disturb the community.
Some problems can not be fixed the same day and some problems can not be fixed at all.
Thanks for your understanding
