pickupsticks

I have an idea. Or maybe two.

I was thinking to have a belt where whenever there is a title fight, the challenger gets to choose the deployment order of the holder's army. The holder would then change their army to the selected order (with free control over all the other options, of course), send the challenge and report whether the title changes hands or not.
I was thinking of having it where the holder could also nominate the challenger's deployment order, but this could lead to having armies set up in strange ways for long periods of time and would be open to abuse for the other ranking tables. By having the holder send the challenge, they can then change everything back immediately afterwards without this being an issue.
Also, this way the challenger has the advantage which should keep things a bit lively as far as the belt changing hands. A belt should be difficult to hold.
Anyone see any other problems with this? If not then I might just go ahead and make the thread.

The other idea was to have a knockout cup where before each round a random deployment order is chosen and posted up, and ALL armies have to put their troopers into that order (with free control of other options of course) before the round begins.
I won't have time to admin this so someone else would have to take over if there was any interest.

The idea behind both of these, obviously, is to provoke variety and get people to think more about how to make their troopers work well together. I'm fairly sure that most people settle on a deployment order that they consider optimal and then won't change this very often until they get significant upgrades that change the balance of their troopers. In these competitions the deployment order would be set for you and you would have the pleasure of using your brain to figure out the best way to work with what you've got.

So, what do you think? Would anyone be interested in this sort of a competition?
