Changing your IP number
Some players can change their IP number, if you are going to try this then you will need to delete the history again just before hand.
First delete your browsers history and close the browser, then turn off your router, wait for a bit and then turn it back on.

Different makes of router work differently, and this can be tricky.
Some routers need to be turned off AND unplugged from the wall, these routers may also need to stay off and unplugged for about 2 minutes to power down the capacitors.
You will need to look at your router and play about with it a bit when no one else in the house is using it.
