The link to your army page looks something like this:
http://zen-warriors.minitroopers.com/hq

If you take the /hq off the end then it becomes the recruitment link, like this:
http://zen-warriors.minitroopers.com/

Anyone visiting has the chance to make their own army. If they make the army on YOUR recruitment link then the first soldier in their army will become one of your recruits, and able to take part in raids and epic missions for you.

So, yes: send them your link and ask them to create an army from the page that opens.

(you might like to look in the "recruit of the..." threads pinned at the top of this forum, where people trade recruits with each other. There are strict rules for how it works though, so be sure to read them before you join in)
