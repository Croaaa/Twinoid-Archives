It's not that they are afraid of changing the game. They have changed several of their games (and the ones I am aware of all have a pay option, while Minitroopers does not). Their business model seems to be to release a game and move on to the next. Released games get bug fix attention only, and then only if it is an egregious bug. Many bugs live on forever, especially if they are only encountered in fringe cases.

Spending time on feature requests for already released games takes developer time away from releasing more new games. Setting up a player poll on potential feature requests takes developer time away from releasing more new games. They simply do not care to do this.
