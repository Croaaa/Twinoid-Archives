Hi guys,
this forum is really, really, busy and I love it that way
In fact it's the busiest forum on the English server.
...but...I think we could make it even more busy.

To help with this we mods have been playing with the idea of expanding this Minitroopers forum into the old Muxxu forum as well. The Muxxu forum would become a Minitroopers sub-forum.
Right now the old Muxxu forum is closed and not being used for anything.

My view right now is that one forum could be for discussions (asking for help or bragging etc) and the second forum could be for the structured, ongoing threats.
By ongoing threats I mean any recruiting or competing threads, things such as RotH, Specialized Recruiting, the Continuous Championship, etc.

If you would prefer something else let us know.

We do have some restrictions.
The forum link from the minitroopers page gos to this forum.
This forum is newer then the Muxxu forum. The old Muxxu forum is no good for sticky threads and we can not run polls there.
Also we have mods in both forums but they are different people, so we will be needing some help from Coro again.
There may also be other restrictions that I am still unaware of.

Having a sub-forum is just an idea.
There maybe a risk that this could somehow lower the activity in total.

Before we do anything I would like to hear from the Minitroopers community.
Any opinions, objections or general feelings are all welcome.
