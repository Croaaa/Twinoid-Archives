To seriously help you with your problem, make sure that all of these things are true

  • You are accepting cookies
  • You aren't using a browser's "private browsing" mode
  • You aren't using a proxy
  • Your IP stays the same throughout the process of registering and setting the password
  • Your Flash settings are set to allow websites to save information on your computer
    • Right click on a trooper on the side of your HQ page, and click on "Global Settings". There should be an option to allow all sites to store information without prompting you
    • I think MiniTroopers stores data under "data.minitroopers.com". If you don't see that in the list of websites, then something went wrong, and you can't set a password
