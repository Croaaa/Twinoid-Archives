I've heard that this game isn't getting updated anymore, but if thought I'd still share some stuff that I'd think be cool to add to Minitroopers.

1: Landmine: The solider throws down a landmine that if stepped on by an enemy, will explode with the force of a regular grenade.

2: Armor piecing bullets: Deals triple (Or more) damage to vehicles and ignores the opponent's armor.

3: Unstoppable: The trooper cannot be knocked down or paralyzed.

4: Avenger: When an allied troopers dies, your troopers gains X initiative (May or may not stack each time an ally dies)

5: Return gift: The troopers can throw back grenades that are thrown by the enemy and are heading close enough to the trooper.

6: Mortar: The mortar fires just as commonly as the rocket launcher, but take a few seconds to land after firing. It carries more rounds than the rocket launcher and reloads faster. However the trooper cannot move while using it and will be forced to abandon it if an enemy is too close.

7: Riot Shield: The trooper gets out his riot shield, he uses it to get into range to attack his target while the shield absorbs at least 10 points of damage before being destroyed. When he is within range he will discard the shield and pull out a weapon. While using this the trooper cannot be instakilled like a head shot for example because the shield is of course covering him. However the shield only protects his front half so if an opponent is behind him he will be able to directly hurt the trooper.

8: Sunglasses: When equipped the trooper will be immune to the effects of the flash-bang grenade.

So, what do you think?
