SaviorOfViennaGood job designers, good job. Retards.There are plenty of reasons to curse the MT developers. They code many bugs, and are very slow to fix or even acknowledge them. But I do not believe this is one of those times.

There is no way, even were it desirable, which I do not believe it is, to code the diverse skills they allow Troopers to gain to be exactly equal. If they were somehow magically all equal, than any army with one more Trooper than you, or a few more levels than you, would constantly beat you. And then you'd be here, bitching about that.

Were you the guy with the On Point, Frenetic, Explosive Shells Trooper (wonderful combo, BTW) I doubt you'd be frothing at the mouth about it.
