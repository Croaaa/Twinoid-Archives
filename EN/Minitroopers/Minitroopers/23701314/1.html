Hey all,
hows life?

I'v been thinking about the recruit of the heart thread and wondering about how we can make it better.

One of the problems with the current systems is that it requires ongoing policing. This is organising time that could be spent elsewhere (wiki, ESC or whatever).
Also by the time we mods have find the problem in the thread the damage is usually done.

so... what if the thread becomes self policing?
We mods will still be here, by private message players could let us know about things if/when we are needed ...but the thread could become mostly self policing.

Here are some changes I'm thinking about...

Firstly.
For easy checking each post in the recruitment threads shows the recruit link ending in '/ranks' (not /hq).

Secondly.
Every player who uses the recruiting threads is responsible for checking that the last player's post is correct.
If the link from the last player is not correct then do not create a recruit for them.
You must use one of the other recruiting threads...

...or alternatively...
...you may use that thread but you must find, and create a recruit for, the last player who did use a correct recruit link in their post.
When you find the last post that was correct you may use that post and ignore everyone in between.

When doing this use the word 'JUMP:' and add the name of the player who you are jumping from (the last correct post).

Thirdly.
Like now any player who misuses the thread will risk repercussions.
Adding to this any player who creates a recruit for a player who is misusing the thread will also receive a warning (or other repercussion if already warned).


Hows that sound?
If we can get this to work then we can stop the damage from happening. Right now I'm just playing clean up after the damage is done.

I was hopping the two types of recruiting threads, Specialized recruiting and Recruit of the heart/mind, could be opposites and extremes.

Specialized recruiting would be a rigid, unforgiving framework.
Recruit of the heart would be a more free flowing place, good for new players.

Because of this I do not consider the name (the name of the account or of the trooper) to be a necessary requirement when using the recruit of the heart.
For this thread I overlook everything, I just need to know that the recruit is a new recruit and that it is going to the right army.

We can always create more recruiting threads if we want to, new ones with new rules, but I would like to keep the recruit of the heart as simple as possible for the players who are new or maybe have English as a second language.

...that said...
I feel that making it more complicated is becoming more necessary.
I don't want to do this but I feel that using '/ranks' links with self policing is the only way that the community can protect itself from malicious trolling.
Even numerous, super active mods could still never be as good as an aware community.
