Skills we don't talk about ...but are actually pretty significant.


I'v got to say Restless for one.
When I'm losing a fight I take comfort knowing that my next wave will come out fresh and ready to go. It's a real facepalm moment when they start dropping weapons or falling over because of some enemy trooper with a pump action shotgun and restless. I remember a lot of players talking about the value of On Point but I think that Restless deserves a lot of credit too.

Some times I think that the Binoculars are undervalued.
I know that having a scout can be like losing an extra trooper in the wave but with Binoculars... well... sometime it's like I'm wiping out an extra 2 enemy troopers.
There is also value in bringing out the enemy's medic before the survivors can get up
