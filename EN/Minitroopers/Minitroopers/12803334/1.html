A spy will deploy based on how many units your opponent has.

I won't go into the logic right now cause I've done it dozens of times, but for him to deploy you need to fight an opponent with at least 4 units... He will deploy at an 80% chance. Not because he won't be summoned via special spy deployment, but if he places himself last on your opponent's roster then he won't have a wave to come in with.

What I mean, that how a spy works by inserting itself randomly into your opponent's team order and the unit's get deployed normally as if they had the same number in there deployment count, it's just that your unit takes up 1 of their deployment count.


For best results fight someone with 5 units minimum.
