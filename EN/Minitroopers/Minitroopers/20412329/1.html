No need to hope for a tank since if a pilot has a motorcycle and an other vehicle, he will have 1/2 as much chance of deploying with the other vehicle. Here is why:


Disclaimer: I didn't invent this theory about vehicle usage but I agree that this is the way vehicle deployment works. Keep in mind that this is only a theory and the subject is still very controversial. This is only the one I choose to believe in).


-When a trooper has 2 vehicles, he can only use one in a battle.

-Before every battle, the game rolls a dice to see if he will "spawn" with his vehicle.

-Obviously a trooper cannot spawn inside 2 vehicles. That would just not make any sense.

-so if both vehicles dice rolls end up positive (trooper will deploy with vehicle) then the game has to roll an other "two-sided dice" to determine which vehicle will deploy.

-Same principle is applied to troopers with 3 vehicles except that the system uses a "3-sided dice".

-I guess to resume this, the reason I say other vehicles have 50% less chance of deployment if a pilot has a motorcycle and that vehicle is because since the motorcycle has 100% deployment rates after level 8 (pilots only), so his dice roll will always end up positive for the motorcycle, therefore forcing a 2-sided dice roll each time an other vehicle's dice roll ends up positive.
