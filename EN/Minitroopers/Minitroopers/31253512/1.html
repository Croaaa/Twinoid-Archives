Which skill do you think is better for a starting trooper? I know I'm having a hard time deciding.

Smart gives your trooper more options when leveling up and counters propaganda and clown grenades. It also halves the chance of getting sabbed, making it a good long-term investment.

Meanwhile, the motorcycle is all around an awesome vehicle. It takes your trooper within striking distance immediately and gives them a nice initiative boost. It turns any trooper into a deadly shock trooper.

So, what do you think? Smart or motorcycle?

Smart.
With the smart skill your more likely to be offered the motorcycle as you level up. In the end you have both.
Motorcycle is good at any level, you can just get it later.
Smart however is only really powerful if you can get it before level 6, and the chances of getting it on one of the first level ups is just too low.
It's Smart that will get you both the Motorcycle and the pilot skill to go with it (or spy, Motorcycle + spy is better).
