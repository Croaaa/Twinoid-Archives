Personally, I like the impression of solitude it gives. Besides, the number of times I fall off bridges into the water, I'm glad no-one can see me!

Although you can't see the other players, you will see kubes around you vanish if someone else picks them up, or appear out of thin air if someone else puts them down.

A good way to see if there are other players nearby - although not recommended - is to drop one of your rarer kubes. If it's still there after you've blinked, you're probably alone.
