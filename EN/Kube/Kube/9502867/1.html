norby89
All the swimming, crossing bridges, hopping around to get to the place where you left off can sure get tiring. In order to help explorers cover large distances in the shortest amount of time possible I have started building what I like to call a Megabridge. Basically it's a bridge 2 kubes wide that starts from the origin point [0][0] and goes East towards [x][0].

At this moment it's 10+ zones long but with your help we can make it reach 100 or even 1000 zones in length. Explorers can then create sub-bridges from it to other islands and it will make traveling a whole lot easier. If we gather enough people we could make a bridge in all 4 directions but for now we should concentrate on making this one as long as possible.

How can you help?

Extend it with a few kubes each day, you don't have to use all your moves although you're free to do so. With enough people, if each of us contributes with 10 kubes a day and we'll get far in now time and we'll have enough moves left for our daily activites.
Use common kubes. This reduces the chance of newbies destroying the bridge, find the most common kubes on the nearest island.
If you see a hole in the bridge fix it by placing a kube in it (preferably of the same type that was previously there).
Do not leave gaps! Some people tend to place 10 kubes, leave 10 spaces, place another 10 and so on. This slows traveling down and that's exactly the opposite of what we'd like to achieve.
Don't destroy the bridge! This one should be pretty obvious but if a kube has been placed on the bridge, do not take it unless you are going to replace it with another kube.
Last but not least, try to build a nice looking bridge. This rule isn't all that important but try using a bunch of kubes of the same type instead of randomly placing whatever kubes you have in your inventory, if you can.
