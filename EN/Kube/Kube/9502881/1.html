When I first read that, I interpreted it the same way I think you are: as a good way to make . But...

You don't have a zone to start with. If you want one, you have to rent one, which means paying tokens to own it for a limited period. You can't buy a zone, only rent it, so if you want to keep it, you will need to renew the rent (i.e. pay more tokens) when it's about to run out. Also you can only rent a zone if it is next to another zone which someone else is renting.

As for the bit about renting it to other players, I don't understand that at all. It sounds like you can charge players tokens to access your zone but I'm not sure if it does mean that. Why would anyone want to pay to access my zone rather than paying for their own zone, especially if mine has to be "empty" in order for me to rent it out?

Unless someone else can shed light on it, I'm assuming something got lost in translation in that part of the help.
