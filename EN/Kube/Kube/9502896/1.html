A thread for the Bridgebuilders...

Well, I don't know why the Megabridge topic was locked, but I'd personally like to see that the people who are dedicated to creating and maintaining these structures have a place to get together, talk, and coordinate.

I am personally working on the bridge leading from [0,0] to the East, and of late, I have been working on the connection to the swamp island north of [31, 0]. The reason for this is that there are a lot of swamp islands near the center, from what I've seen, and I think it means that those blocks, especially swamp leaves, would hopefully be less valuable to wandering players, and thus less likely to be looted off our bridges. so maybe we can concentrate on using those kinds of bricks for the bridges (or at least for the eastern bridge I'm working on).

I'd also like to address the bridge building technique. I agree that a 2 block bridge is better than a 1 block, and less stamina draining than a 3 block, but I propose that the 2 blocks be offset like this: Image What this will accomplish is that you can face your character into the crook of the L and you will not have to spend any time at all keeping yourself on the bridge. It will still be only 2 blocks, but one of them will be raised, and this works because the engine does not require the raised blocks be supported by anything, just that they connect to another block at that level initially. I have found that with a 3 block bridge with 2 raised, that I could point myself at one side and slide down the bridge easily without anything more than pressing the up arrow, and I tested if the third block was necessary by removing it, and the effect stands with only 2 blocks.

Now, perhaps in the long run, we could redo the exiting bridges as well, but I am more concerned with the possibility of doing this going forward. To start, this might require using a second block, next to the first, so that you can put the raised block on top of it, but from that point forward, the raised blocks can be attached to the existing raised rail.

So. What do you think? Good idea? Bad? And also, what are you working on, and how can we help. I feel that our forces of construction need to be more organized than the forces of destruction, if we are going to make any progress at all on making the game maximally enjoyable. The French servers are nicely organized and decorated. Let's show them the English speaking servers can do just as well.
