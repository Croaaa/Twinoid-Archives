Erem
If you don't have a compass and find yourself getting lost in the world of Kube, you can easily tell which direction you are facing by observing the ocean currents.
The currents flow south to north, so:
- if the water is flowing away from you, you are facing north;
- if it's flowing towards you, you're facing south;
- if it flows left, you're looking to the east;
- and if it flows to the right, you're looking west.
