BIG LAVA TIP
If you run into lava, or get stuck in a crevase that will take a TON of energy to get out of, don't panic!

Click the play button as soon as you hit the lava or land in the water in a pit.
Hitting the play button reverses you last couple of steps, therefore taking you out of the lava, or out of the crevase.
