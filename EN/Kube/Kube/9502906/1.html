I'm stuck in [230] [-100]
How are you stuck? You get a limited amount of energy each day. Moving takes a little bit of energy; picking up and putting down blocks take a big bit. When your energy is all gone you cannot move. You get a message saying you have run out of energy. The white gauge at the top of the right hand panel shows how much energy you have left. It is refilled at server reset

If you want an owned zone, start at the Swamp zone and go up one zone - the name of a zone is usually 'empty' but a zone can be renamed by someone renting it
