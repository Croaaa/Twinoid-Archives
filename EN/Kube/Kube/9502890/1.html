Unfortunately, any game of this type is vulnerable to people who want to run around being mean. Call them bullies or call them trolls, the end result is that they exist, and they will show up. The best way to get rid of a troll is to ignore him. Leave the area and go do something else, and hope they get bored. In Internet language, don't feed the troll.

The ultimate problem is that you can't be given freedom without being given freedom. Free to create. Free to destroy. Free to have fun. Free to be an @$$hole. As long as we want the game as it is, we have to accept that it's an unfortunate part of life, and it's something we all have to put up with.

That being said, the meanies who are carpeting the public zones with lava are, in fact, making the game unplayable for others, and that does need to be stopped. I would personally say that a wiping of the blocks in just those zones would be beneficial, and possibly a wipe of the glitched zones would fix THAT problem too (while I'm on the subject), but the Kube team would have to announce it, and make sure that everybody knew and agreed to the wiping of the glitched non-public zones.
