Fortify
Cubes placed on the first floor could be fortified if the players have bought the upgrade. It would cost demolishers more energy to destroy bridges or the base floor of buildings.
Fortify 1 -> taking one fortified block costs 5 energy
Fortify 2 -> taking one fortified block costs 15 energy
Fortify 3 -> taking one fortified block costs 25 energy

Float
Instead of endless jumping to get on the top of some building of some flying structures you get lighter and can see the world from above. Limitation should be 36 blocks in one direction to prevent cheating.

Gravity canon
Takes all flying cubes of a zone down. Only useable in zones with no other players. Very useful for flying structures and jungle islands

Gills
You live along all swimming creatures. You need no air to breath you have gills.
Endless swimming upgrade

Energy doubler
You mastered your energy. Instead of taking one block or putting one down you can do two with your energy
