Did you find a stack of three dark kubes? That's a dolmen (also called a portal, though I'm not sure why), not a teleporter.

Personally, I would find the game far more enjoyable if it did work the way you thought it would, with you being able to select your starting point from any of the dolmen/portals you've discovered, but sadly it doesn't.

All the dolmen/portal does is display the 5x5 area around it permanently on your map. It helps you see where you've been but doesn't help you get back there.

For now we only have the two starting points, the Crowded Wood and the Distant Swamps, and they are so close to each other that it doesn't take long to travel between them.

Apparently, it is possible to increase the number of starting points, but I don't know the details. See this thread:
http://muxxu.com/forum/thread/724399
