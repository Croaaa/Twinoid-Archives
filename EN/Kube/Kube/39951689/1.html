In the final zones of long bridges you might often see kubes 2 tiles high in front and in one of the sides. Specially in bridges that have walls.

The idea is this: You calculate more or less the time it takes to reach your desired zone. Point yourself straight, put a weight in your keyboard and go drink a coffee, talk in the phone, take an air, etc. If you forget to come back, the road black will impede you from drowning.
