Hello, I'm Azuron from the Spanish server.
I think you can build a lot of awesome things! The pyramid that Norby89 showed is a very good starting point.
First we could only build with common cubes. Spanish players built castles and pyramids with basic cubes and the constructions were impressive. Then we started to create exploration groups: the groups explored and built together on a zone.
We have just made a building contest and the project that Epoq showed was the winner. You can see the others projects here (I hope this link works for you ). Perhaps a contest could help you to motivate, it worked for us.

Tips:
You can make exploration groups. In these groups, all players are important, even if they do not buy , because every player is 125 daily cubes more for your bridge. And rare cubes are for all the players - don't forget that them all will be on your group zone.
Do not waste energy on building things in public zones, they will be destroyed... Better to make bridges.

It's very general but I hope it will help you, and I'm sure that you will have a lot of beautiful constructions soon.
Greetings! and Merry Christmas.
