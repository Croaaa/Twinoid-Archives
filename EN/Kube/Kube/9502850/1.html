Sorry but I will spoil that one about teleports :
A megakube of teleport kubes will rent the zone it is transmuted on for 100 days without need for bordering a rented/public zone, instead it only needs to be within 299 zones distance on each coordinate. If that distance is exceeded, nothing happens except a message.

Furthermore that rental mode generates no message on the player's wall or general fluxx (when not logged in), allowing for secret, private, distant bases with fresh zones nearby.
