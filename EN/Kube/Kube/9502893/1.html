As many of you know, Kube doesn't use the 3D Card in your PC, as it is limited by Flash Player.

With the new beta version of Flash Player 11 this is now possible! We have brought the game bang up to date to let you enjoy higher quality viewing of your already impressive constructions!

Download and install the beta version of Flash Player 11. Select the appropriate version for your browser (Internet Explorer/Other) and operating system (32/64 bit).
Head over to "My Page" in Kube to activate the 3D functionality!

Furthermore, youcan activate Fullscreen Mode using the "Top" or "Home" button on your keyboard. This function also works with the "normal" version of Kube.
