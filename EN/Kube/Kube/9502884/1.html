Q1: What are treasure chests and how can I find them?

Q2: What are megakubes?
On august 12, 2011
norby89
A1: Treasure chests are special kubes found on undiscovered islands, when opened they can reveal the following types of kube: Dark, Gold, Spring, Forum, Case, Rubix

A2: Megakubes are created by placing kubes of the same type together so they form a large 3x3x3 kube. There are special kubes that transmute once you do this and form new types of kubes. For a full list read the FAQ.
