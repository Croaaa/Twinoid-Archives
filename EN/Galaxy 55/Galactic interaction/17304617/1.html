Sites and Tools

The Galaxy55 Wiki
www.galaxy55wiki.comUpdated by the English Galaxy55 community, this is the ultimate source of information about the game. Find information about planets, kubes, crafting, ships... Just think of something related to Galaxy55 and it's on the wiki!

Official thread - Discuss everything about the wiki!
quick links: Blueprints | Quick Start Guide | Version History | Ship | Mining Lasers

The Galaxy55 IRC
http://webchat.esper.net/?channels=Ga... (bookmark it!)Once in a while, it's time to take a break from the game. That's when the Galaxy55 IRC kicks in, because it's never time to take a break from the awesome community! Chat with your fellow Galaxy55ians here about the game, ask questions (we're there to help you) and even talk about other unrelated stuff!

Official thread - How to join + IRC discussion

The (Unofficial) G55 Client
http://bit.ly/Rqi4j3 (this link is updated automatically with every new version)Always wanted to play Galaxy55 outside your browser? To be able to play it by just double-clicking an icon on your desktop? You're in luck, because our fellow player tikker created a special Galaxy55 client! This combines the best of the community and the best of the game!

Official thread - Latest updates and feature requests


Forum Topics

Show your creations!
http://galaxy55.com/tid/forum#!view/9...Built something awesome that everybody needs to see? Show it in this thread!
Tip: use camera links for big creations
this thread enables players with no free licences to look at your creations without having to join the planet!

Show Your Ship
Show Your ShipBeing an adventurer, having a good-looking ship is really important. Some people think it's all about style, some people think it's all about speed.
Show your ship in this thread to make sure everybody knows how you're traveling through Galaxy55

Planet Structures
http://galaxy55.com/tid/forum#!view/9...A topic where you can post screenshots of awesome things you have found on your planet! Strange-looking rocks, rare artifacts, crashed ships...
If you find something special, post it there!

Art - Structures and Monuments
http://galaxy55.com/tid/forum#!view/9...Art is a concept which is hard to understand, but often wonderful to behold. Built anything nice to look at? Post the screenshot in this thread.
Make sure it fits into the catagory 'art'

Post Your Camera URLs Here
http://galaxy55.com/tid/forum#!view/9...Busy with something that's still work in progress?
Build a camera and post the camera url here so people can see it changing every day!
