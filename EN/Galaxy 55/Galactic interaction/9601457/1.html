Since I can't find any sort of bug reporter, I'm just posting this here.

The problems aren't so much with the game as with the game page, so they really should be 10 second fixes.

1.) Listed in Alpha 3, you wrote "exchange resources and kubes avec les autres joueurs" Since this is the English version, you should change that to "Exchange resources and kubes with other players."

2.) In Beta 3, in point one: "explore the ruins of ancient civilisations," "civilisations" should be spelled "civilizations"

3.) In Beta 3, in point two: "find plans and rare artefacts," "artefacts" should be spelled "artifacts."

4.) In your exposition about the game, under "A Minecraft-esque adventure in outer space!" and "Explore Galaxy 55," in the second paragraph, on the third line, after "trade," you forgot to put "and."

Apart from those, the other things to fix are open to interpretation; they aren't technically wrong, but they make the page look less organized. First of all, in "The future of Galaxy 55" "future" is not capitalized. Generally, all important words in a title are capitalized, so (in my personal opinion) you should change that. But of course, it is your game, so that decision lies with you.

Secondly: in the version itinerary, you similarly did not capitalize "progress" in "In progress!" "operations" in "Advanced operations," "monsters" in "Play as monsters!" and neither "advanced," nor "worlds" in "Aliens and advanced worlds." You may want to capitalize those words as well.

Lastly: in the coming version summary, all of the bullet points start with lower case letters except for "Set out on multiplayer, real time exploration missions!" and "Grrrrrrrrrrrr!!!" You may either want to change the capital bullets to lower case, or capitalize all the other bullets, so they all match.

.
That's all. I'd like to see this again further down the line of development. But farewell for now, from this friendly wandering grammar nazi. ^_-
