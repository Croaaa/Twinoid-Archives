If you have a Windows machine Paint will work fine (found in the Accessories folder).

You just need to hit PrtScn with your browser open on your construction (note: if you have a laptop you might have to press an additional key to pick that function if your PrtScn key has more than one option on it), open Paint and then paste (right click and pick paste, hit ctrl-v, or use edit menu and paste... all of those will paste) & your whole screen will be there (then you just need to use Paint to pick only what you want to show/resize).

When you have the picture how you'd like it save the image (but make sure to change the "save as type" dropdown under where you type the name of the image from bitmap to something else like .png if you want to upload it and easily share it). Good luck!
