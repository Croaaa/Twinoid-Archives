Yes we know we are late, but we lost Epoq somewhere in space during this new adventure... Wait a moment! We have a message from him... Thanks Franz24 for decoding it!

Our exploration began when we realised that we could take off using the Ship's Computer! This means we can explore new solar systems and planets. Only one sector is available at the moment, but engine technology is improving - others should be available soon!

Let's swim!

It was a shock when we fell into the water at first! There are now real underwater worlds that can be explored! Be careful not to end up drowning though, it would be a shame to spoil a great vacation.

New types of planets

During our explorations, we have discovered three new types of planets over and above the two we knew already. And that's not all: newly discovered planets are sometimes sprinkled with surprises... amazing!

Lots of new kubes!

According to our flora and fauna expert, over a hundred new Kubes can be discovered! We're starting to wonder whether a chest to store all this would be practical...

We found some beautiful waterfalls and lava, which we could even bring home. Be careful not to burn in the lava (or not to drown in a waterfall, but that should really be st...)

Making surveillance cameras is fun

You can even send pictures of your planets to your friends!


Let's explore! Beta version, here we come!
