                    This is the official Moon 55 thread

What is Moon 55?
On the 30th of August 2012 we started with Earth 55, the first real multiplayer planet on G55. Now we continue our work with our second planet, Moon 55.
This is all part of Project 55, which means creating a new planet every update until the final game is released.
When the project is finished, we'll have something to show to new players and while waiting for more updates, we can have some fun!


The Planet Rules
Do not grief. It's easy, it's clear. Let's just have some fun!
Wanna join? Send me a PM where you tell me why you want to join!
Help each other with projects.
Try to maintain some style, we want a good-looking planet.


This forum thread is for...
Discussing new projects, or projects we're working on
New ideas for Moon 55, camera links, etc.
Suggestions how to make things better


This forum thread is NOT for...
People who want to join
Ads for other multiplayer planets


That's it, let's make Moon 55 the greatest multiplayer planet ever!
