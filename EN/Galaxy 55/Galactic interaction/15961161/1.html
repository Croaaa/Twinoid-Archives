Rivaan

Okay, here's the tour. Please keep hands inside the vehicle, and no flash photography, please.

I don't know if guests will arrive via the beam, but if they do, they would first find themselves in the transport chamber of the spaceport. Passing through the door, a look behind will reveal the view from the other direction.

Traveling further, through the corridor, you find yourself on the skybridge. A long winding staircase provides safe passage down to the surface.

At the bottom, a quick look up reveals a more challenging method of traversing the tower. Nearby, a small dock sits empty. At the front, two towers stand guard.

An arbor-mounted camera provides views of the area just outside. One of the striking images is that of a crudely-hewn figure with a torch. Another is that of an old temple.

The settlement consists mainly of small residences. At the center, is the moon gate, which received visitors before the spaceport was built. There is also a well that provides fresh water.

At the northern edge, just past the gate is an old watermill, now empty. Not far from there, is a squat mine, that seems to disappear into the ground. A look through the glass ceiling provides a view not for the faint of heart.

A strange sight among the old-world structures is that of this modern-styled building, with lush interior, and spacious top floor.

One of the great wonders of this world is the hanging gardens, that also feature a labyrinth, several sub-levels (1 & 2), and a giant tree, with passages within to reach the sky.

Past the east gate are a few landmarks, including a greenhouse, a chapel, and an Oozing Inn (D2N players might get the reference).

To the west, is a lower bridge that leads to the foundations of a palatial mansion.

Directly south of the town, is the massive storage complex, with many materials for building. Further south is an old ship assembly area and a nursery with many strange specimens of alien flora.

That's it so far. Not shown are the subterranean passages, the modern suburb(still in the planning stage), and a few other "secrets." Future plans include massive stone carvings in the mountains, rebuilding the forests, reclaiming the flooded caves, hollowing out the kubes between the surface & the core, and of course the previously mentioned simulated biomes.
