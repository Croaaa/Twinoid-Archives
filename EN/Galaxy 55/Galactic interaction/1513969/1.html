Yes, indeed. Limits for z (height) are 1 (cannot dig the red "lava" which is evidentally 1 block size) and 127 (cannot put a block on the top of this block with z=127). Coordinates x and y are cyclic - from 0 to 255, then instead of 256 goes 0 once more. Measure units are just blocks.
