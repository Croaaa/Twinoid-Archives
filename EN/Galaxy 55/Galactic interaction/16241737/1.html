                    This is the official Earth 55 thread

What is Earth 55?
Earth 55 is the first real multiplayer Galaxy55 planet. We started on the 30th of August 2012 and we have a around 30 different people visiting and building on the planet!

Our goal is having a great community and having an even greater planet that we build together.


The Planet Rules
Do not grief. It's easy, it's clear. Let's just have some fun!
If you want to join, I need two things:
         A PM where you tell me why you want to join.
         A recommendation from another active member of Earth 55.
Help each other with projects.
Try to maintain some style, we want a good-looking planet.

This forum thread is for...
Discussing current projects
New ideas for buildings/projects
Suggestions how to make things better

This forum thread is NOT for...
People who want to join
Ads for other multiplayer planets


That's it, let's make Earth 55 the best multiplayer planet ever!
