Actually the world isn't round, it's kind of a Tore, impossible to obtain in real life...
To be clear ; The world is a 256x256x128 kubes wide block...if you reach an extremity, you get back to its opposite on the map, like on this diagram... if you follow any direction, you'll get through the numbered parts in order, get it? (C => C2 => C3 => C4)
There is just an optical effect to make you think the world is round but it isnt, else a "road" going around the world wouldn't be the same lenght at the lowest and highest altitudes (try it, it's true!)...and the Kubes couldn't be kubes since it wouls need at least one triangle somewhere for the junction!
So when you dig too far you'll find a layer of unbreakable rocks, just press "F" and check the coordinates to see that (Z=0 => You're at the bottom, Z = 127 you're at the top)

Oh and you can not carry 8 Kubes (with a K), but 8 different types of kubes, in a limited number depending on which type it is (54 lunar rocks max but 92 lunar sand on one inventory spot)

As for returning the ship, if you click the button, it's normal, cause it's just an emergency button for when you're stuck somewhere and really can't get out otherwise, but makes you lose your equipment to make it remain an emergency button, not an instant teleport...next time just find your teleport beam (it's yellow and shines in the sky right under your ship, hard to miss) and simply step into it...
