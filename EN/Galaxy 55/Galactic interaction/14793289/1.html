Ever seen an awesome-looking block and wondered what it's called? Here's how to find out:

1. Press F to open debug info.
2. Point at the block and take note of the name that appears in the debug info.
3. Translate the name if it's in French using Google Translate.

This works on blocks that are destroyed by the mining laser, such as healing desert plants and blocks that drop resources.
