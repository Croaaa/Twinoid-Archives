If you can get there, just go to your ship, hover your cursor over the ships computer, press E. Then select the planet you want to go to. The moon should be in that list IF you have it. (Though if you just made an account, you probably don't.)

Stronger lasers? If you find fruit (in the trees or on the ground), mine them and they will give you yellow charges. Blue charges come from Etherium, blue crystal like blocks in mountains.
