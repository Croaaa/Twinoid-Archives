On october 7, 2012
[139783]

You'll need yellow charges to break strong blocks.
These come from "Old Citronox and Citronox cubes".
The old ones can be found on the ground in your red planet. They look like "yellow gift boxes". Once broken, they will give you 1-5 yellow charges.
The cubes can be found on the top of trees, in your red planet - have you ever looked up and seen glowing lamps hanging from trees? Well those are actually fruit, which can be crafted to give 25 yellow charges.

The charges stack to 99, and can be used for yellow laser.
