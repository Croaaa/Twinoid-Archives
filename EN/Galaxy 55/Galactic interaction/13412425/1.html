Since some employee of motion twin will come to deliver the news of Alpha 3 to these forums I thought I might be able to write an idea and maybe they'd notice when they'd come. 19% chance of that happening...

The style of fighting involves how people can fight with their spaceships against others or space pirates (like I've suggested before).

It is a style of fighting where people choose the move they will make and the turn will commence (such as Toribash or Steambirds)
You can google those games to get an idea of what I'm talking about.

People can choose their direction the ship will move before the turn begins and the action they will take. Health is determined by the number of Aluminum and non-Aluminum kubes there are, +5 for every Aluminum type kube and +1 for every non-Aluminum kube. When a ship runs out of health it will blow up and anything too close to it will be damaged by 20. When the ship blows up 50% of the material the ship is made of will be destroyed and 50% of the items the player was carrying (or computer) will be destroyed, the rest can be salvaged.

If the creators haven't already made another way for people to discover planets I have another idea. If the computer of the ship hasn't blown up the player has a 25% chance that they can examine it and learn the coordinates of one planet or moon the player had listed. This is a one time event, once the player has been killed on that planet or left it will no longer be visible and they can not return. The computer can be taken from a player's ship or a pirate's.

Any questions ask, the battle style is similar to steambirds mostly.
