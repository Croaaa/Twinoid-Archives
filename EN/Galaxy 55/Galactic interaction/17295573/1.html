Welcome to Galaxy55. A game where you can explore breathtaking planets, build breathtaking structures and work together on the biggest projects!

When you arrive here, it is obvious that you have a lot of questions regarding the game. To make sure we don't get topics about the same question over and over, here is a quick guide to the game and the forum.

First of all...
Read the forum rules here
Submit bugs, and see solutions to them here
Check out some useful Galaxy55 links here
Read the G55wiki Quick Start Guide, this can help you a lot!
If the FAQ doesn't answer your question, you can give it a try in the IRC

Now you're ready to venture into the world of Galaxy55!
Most of the game speaks for itself, but there are some problems anybody can run into. Look for a solution here, or read it beforehand and make sure you'll never run into the problem in the first place.


Basic Problems

I'm trapped in a cave!
Don't panic (yet). The easiest way to get out of a cave, is mining some of the rocks around you, and using those blocks to mine up. Just jump and place the block below you to go up.
One problem: You need yellow charges to do this.

I'm trapped in a cave and I don't have any yellow charges!
There is only one way out in that case, and that's the ship button on the bottom-right corner of the screen. Using it will teleport you to your ship, but you will lose everything from your inventory...

My mine is flooded!
There is not much to do about this. Best thing you can do is isolate the flooded area and continue playing somewhere else.
You can also try to fill every block of it, and remove the blocks again to remove the water.
Tip: Use vines to do this as quick as possible!

I died and I can't find my EScorp Box!
When you die you leave an EScorp box with your inventory.
EScorp Boxes remain in the same place for a few days so don't worry if you can't find it immediately.
However, on multiplayer planets other players can also pick up your EScorp boxes! The best thing to do is quickly press F when you're dying to see your coordinates. Just remember the X and the Y location to get back to your box as soon as possible!


Miscellaneous Questions
What is the goal of this game?
At the moment Galaxy55 is still in a test phase. That means the game doesn't have a real goal yet, but you can make your own goals like building something awesome!

How do I take a screenshot?
There are multiple ways to do it:
1) Press F2 during the game and save the image that pops up in a new window.
2) Press the Prt Scr (PrintScreen) button on your keyboard. Open an image viewing/editing program (eg. paint, irfanview, gimp, etc). Paste the image there and save it.

Now to share it here, upload the image to an image hosting site like Imgur or Imageshack and post the link you get on the forums.

I can't drill a certain block...
There are four levels of blocks:

Level 1 blocks (like dirt) can always be mined.
Level 2 blocks (like rock) can only be mined with a Yellow Laser.
To get this you need to get Yellow Charges. Get them by destroying the yellow fruits that are on the ground in the forest, or by crafting the yellow fruits that grow on trees. They can also be found under snow patches in the Winter Peaks planets!
Level 3 blocks (like HardRoq) can only be mined with a Blue Laser.
To get a Blue Laser you have to mine Blue Mineral blocks, they can be found near Cracked Rock.
Level 4 blocks (like Fossils) can only be mined with a Purple Laser.
Purple Charges are extremely rare and look like little purple flowers!

What are the green charges/diamonds?
They don't have any purpose yet, but it is advised to collect as many as possible because they're going to be very useful in a later version.


Planets & Licenses
I would like to create a new planet!
Strictly speaking, it is impossible to create a new world. However, you can colonize an existing planet if you have enough licences, just 'scan' it and you're done!

How does the licencing system work?
It is possible to colonize 3 different planets at the same time (so 3 licenses) but this limit may rise to 4 if you receive a planet invitation while already having 3 used licences.

Warning: When you have 4 licenses in use, you will have to delete 2 of them to be able to colonize a new planet!

How can I join a multiplayer planet?
You have to get invited by the creator of the planet. To find a planet, look in the official List of Multiplayer planets thread.


Crafting
How do I craft new blocks?
Place the blocks you want to craft on the crafting panels in your ship. Look on the wiki (link below) to see the recipes.
