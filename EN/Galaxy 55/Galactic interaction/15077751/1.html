I would consider "minecraft style" to be digging tunnels into the earth. Mine shafts, strip mines, that sort of thing. With minecraft's world gen, anywhere you dig, you will eventually hit ore of some kind. (unless you're really unlucky) G55 isn't the same way. Ore is generated in specific locations, not scattered through the rock itself, so tunneling won't really get you anywhere...except wherever the tunnel goes I guess. But no ore. At least in my experience.
