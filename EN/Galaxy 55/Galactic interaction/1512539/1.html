It was working fine until I finally got to the deep ocean, where every square of water is 8 or more deep, and that trench is so wide and deep my ~64 flowers are useless. Anyway, an interesting thing I noticed: When Water is only ~4 deep, you can face down and keep laying blocks on the sea floor until they reach the surface. That is a huge time saver.

The danger of using flowers to repel water is getting stuck under water in a 2 flower tall bubble. I've lost about 22 flowers to such accidents... but if you have enough resources, such traps are usually escapable.
