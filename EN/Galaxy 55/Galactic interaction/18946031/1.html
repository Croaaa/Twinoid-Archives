The Explorer's Guild
The explorer's guild is a guild for anyone who loves to explore the g55 world, we have no official planets for we are explorers. We separate ourselves from our peers by diving into the deep water, climbing the tallest mountains, going into the darkest caves, and finding items others refuse to go find. We take whatever measures we can to take the items we must find, whenever it involves freezing the sea itself, or diving into the depths of quicksand.
How to join
To join the explorer's guild, one must prove themselves as a true explorer. Provide some details on your latest exploration, and a picture. When that is finished, we will send you a message with more instructions
