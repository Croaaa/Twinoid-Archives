I know some of you here use a sliding network. Now, I found a way to make an instant elevator.

1. Add a new tube to the network.

2. Put a block where I put one.

3. Put a block below that and remove the block you put before.

4. Stand on the block you put down in step 3, go one block behind it onto the "completed" tube and stack bricks where I did.

5. Get into the network, turn that way, and see the magic happen!
