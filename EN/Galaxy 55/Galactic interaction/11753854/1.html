Kube construction technology has advanced enormously in Alpha 2! From now on, every player-explorer will use their personal synthesizer found aboard their ship

Activate the Synthesizer to craft Planks. Note: to activate it, simply fire on it (E or CAPS).

Try to discover "blueprints" using other kubes, or check out the forums for more help
