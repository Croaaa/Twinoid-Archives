When I want to get higher, I use blocks in my inventory to build a small pillar just under myself. You just look under your feet, jump and hold "Space"+"E" until blocks in your current "pocket" (1 of 6) will expire, then, if you still need get higher, switch to another pocket and continue building. (Just do not move, or you'll fall from this pillar and will have to build another one to get rid of both.) After you've made your job on that height, switch to an empty pocket, look under your feet again and hold "E" until you gather your pillar back to the pocket. It's faster than stationary ladders, but needs more accuracy and free blocks in pockets.
