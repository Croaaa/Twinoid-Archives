Well, if you're on the ground, and your teleporter beam is in the canopy (as mine was) I'd say climb up there (probably be mining your way about a tree trunk, and search for it while jumping from tree to tree. This would probably be the easiest way to find it if the beam is on the ground even. I found the tree tops to be much safer than the ground.
Just place an object, pick a direction, and keep walking until you find the object again. If that doesn't work, pick a different direction and repeat.
