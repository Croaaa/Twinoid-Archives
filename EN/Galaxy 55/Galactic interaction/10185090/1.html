Galaxy55 Alpha-1 is now online!

Loads of new content:

  • You can now use your laser to extract kubes
  • to excavate certain types of rock you'll need special charges
  • you can see a yellow halo which represents the future access point for your ship!
  • once you are in your ship, you can produce new kubes!
  • you can also sell your minerals to ESCorp to earn galactic credits!
  • if you fall from a great height, you can lose health. If you fall from even higher, forget it!
  • if you die, or use the button to return to your ship, all your kubes and charges will be left where you were until you go back and recover them (don't hang arif you ever want to see them again!)

If you already have a planet from the previous version, you can create a new one using the interface to be found underneath the game!

Enjoy the game!
