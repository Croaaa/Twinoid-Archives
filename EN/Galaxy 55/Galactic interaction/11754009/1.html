The Alpha 2 of Galaxy 55 is finally here!

It comes fully-loaded with new features:

  • your spacecraft is now visible in the sky! You can beam up to your ship using the glowing column of light
  • activate the ship's computer to be able to modify your spacecraft in the astroport hangar!
  • the hull must be entirely made up of hull plating (kubes made from Aluminium) to avoid air leaks during space travel. Remember that there is nothing to stop you from adding other types of kube on top!
  • to produce new kubes you should now use the kube synthesizer which you'll find aboard your ship! (as explained in this forum)
  • if you register before Alpha 3, you can now create a new type of planet! (we'll let you discover it for yourselves!)

To finish up, we have made some changes to our plans so that you don't have to wait any longer. It is now possible to return to planets you have previously created using the ship's computer, however you cannot visit/discover new ones yet.

That's coming shortly in Alpha 3.

Enjoy!
