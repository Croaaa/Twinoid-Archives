There are four levels of blocks:

Level 1 blocks (like dirt) can always be mined.
Level 2 blocks (like rock) can only be mined with a Yellow Laser.
To get this you need to get Yellow Charges. Get them by destroying the yellow fruits that are on the ground in the forest, or by crafting the yellow fruits that grow on trees. They can also be found under snow patches in the Winter Peaks planets!
Level 3 blocks (like HardRoq) can only be mined with a Blue Laser.
To get a Blue Laser you have to mine Blue Mineral blocks, they can be found near Cracked Rock.
Level 4 blocks (like Fossils) can only be mined with a Purple Laser.
Purple Charges are extremely rare and look like little purple flowers!

What are the green charges/diamonds?
They don't have any purpose yet, but it is advised to collect as many as possible because they're going to be very useful in a later version.
