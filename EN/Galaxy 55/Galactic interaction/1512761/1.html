Get a body of water.
1. One cube away, dig a hole 3 cubes deep.
2. Put 2 vines in that hole, stacked.
3. Mine the cube preventing the water from going into that hole.
4. Jump in that hole.
5. You're now trapped, just refresh to escape.

2: http://i.imgur.com/80eL1.png
5: http://i.imgur.com/JAW8u.png

I just did this because I was bored, and to point out a glitch, you can't jetpack through that water, and can't pick up any blocks.

This also works with purple flowers, and using that you can get trapped underwater, and the water's somewhat invisible.

This can allow you to make underwater tunnels, and see the depth of the water, though it's somewhat hard to do: http://i.imgur.com/fpEmP.png
