It seems flash player blocks data storage. Try this:
1. Right click in game, a menu should pop up containing: Settings, General Settings, Information about flash player x.x.xxx
2. Click on General Settings
3. In the window that pops up, go to the tab "Storage" if not already there and select "Allow sites to store information on my computer" or "Ask me before new sites store information on my computer"
