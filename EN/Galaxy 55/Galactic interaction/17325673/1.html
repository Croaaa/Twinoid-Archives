This is the perfect thread for you!


How to submit your planet

  • Name: The name of your planet.
  • Type: What kind of planet is it?
    • Red Earth/Barren Sands/Winter Peaks/Dark Rocks/Acid Forest
  • Summary: A short summary where you tell something about your planet
    • For instance, say something about the theme (if there is one) or what you want to achieve with the planet.
  • Rules: A short version of the planet rules.
    • Most important: How to join the planet. (PM, a post in the thread, etc)
  • Link: A link to the forum thread about the planet.
