The concept of the alien planet and being in a space suit or something is an interesting one. To me, though, the controls feel VERY clunky and slow. The look around speed is too sensitive, and too slow, simultaneously. The block placement cursor is very fiddly; it likes to jump around when you are trying to place things. Also, I'm not particularly afraid of enclosed spaces, but the borders on the window, while representative of a helmet, make me feel rather boxed in and nervous. Maybe make them smaller, less obtrusive, or just translucent? Not being able to look directly up or down is a bit annoying too - makes me feel like I am walking around in some big mech suit.

Also, the slow movement speed isn't too terrible a thing, but maybe boosting it a little would be nice. Not a big issue; just aesthetic really.

I do like the world curvature, but it does make things a little disorienting - perhaps it could be made a little more gradual?

Aside from that, it's a very interesting little game so far, but I do encourage a really different track for it; stick with the sci-fi, definitely. Otherwise you run the risk of Mojang deciding that it's lawsuit time...
