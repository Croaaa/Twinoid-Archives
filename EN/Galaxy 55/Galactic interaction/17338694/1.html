Welcome to the English Galaxy55 Forum! Please respect the Twinoid Terms and Conditions and follow the forum rules:


I. Search before posting.


  • Before asking a question, use the Search button to ensure the question hasn't been asked before.
  • If you cannot find it by searching, go to the FAQ thread and look for it there.
  • If your question also isn't in the FAQ, post it there.
  • The same counts for BUG reports or similar threads.

II. Use correct English and be polite.


  • Refrain from using SMS language, too many smileys, CAPS LOCK, etc.
  • Try to be as polite as possible, this will make your posts more enjoyable to read for everybody.
  • Refrain from swearing or use of offensive language.

III. Think before posting.


  • In general, avoid flooding, off-topic posts, etc.
  • Do not bump without a reason. Posts like 'bump' and 'up' will receive an official warning!
  • If an issue is resolved, don't post in the thread without a reason.
  • Don't try to contact a mod by posting here.
    • Use this online form
    • Or PM one of the mods

IV. Leave moderation to the moderators.


  • Don't pretend to be the police. In case of trouble, report the post and/or contact a moderator.
  • Note that moderation is here to make the forum better. You can request locking, moving or editing for threads and a mod will do it as soon as possible. Similarly, if you have ideas for the forum, let the mods know and they'll assist you.
