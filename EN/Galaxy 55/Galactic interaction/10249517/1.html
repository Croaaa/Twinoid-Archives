Tips-
When searching for specific minerals, whether it'd be Blue mineral in Cracked stone or Pyrite in HardRoq you can easily get what you want without wasting too much energy. When mining and you are in a not so dark, but not so lit place you may notice a glow in a cube's corner. This means that there is Blue Minerals or Pyrite emiting light close to that cube. Light will always come from a corner not a single side, so if you see this that means that a block emiting light is next to it (from 2 sides), to its corner, and above or below that corner.
