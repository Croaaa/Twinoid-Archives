Hear ye, hear ye! Universal citizens of Galaxy 55!

You can now share your planetary licences with other players. They can then come to your planet and explore and build with you.

To make the magic happen, simply press T and you can chat to all your friends on the planet to discuss strategy and communicate more effectively.

We appreciate your patience with regard to any technical problems and specific models for the explorers. We are working on that right now, and will soon have even more new content and updates for your favourite Alpha
