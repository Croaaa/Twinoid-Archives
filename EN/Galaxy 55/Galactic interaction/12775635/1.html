The game is still in the alpha phase, which is usually not played by us but rather tested by interns of MotionTwin until it's good enough to become a Beta, playable by a limited group of persons (closed beta) or by anyone....
In galaxy 55 's case, the devs thought it would be better for the players to participate in the testing and development of the game since the very first steps...
So the goals varies, and we still don't know much about the final release and its goal...
Until then, just have fun!

Let's have a short history of the thing, though :
-Technical demo : No real goal, but "gold" kubes to mine so almost everyone collected them...
-Alpha-1 : GAthering pyrite and selling it for space credits
-Alpha-2 : Pyrite is now aluminium ore, you can craft it to build a ship
-Alpha-3 : Not yet, but i guess the goal will be to gather fuel to go explore new planets, to infinity and beyond!
and so forth...
