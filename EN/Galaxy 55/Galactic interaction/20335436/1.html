Here is my attempt at translating the announcement from French to English (with the help of Google Translate and manual editing) :

Galaxy 55 is a game developed in 2011-2012 by a small team at Motion Twin. Its goal at that time was rather clear: move away from the Minecraft model as far as possible by providing all that, we believe, was missing from it. Global multiplayer, exploration and general gameplay (here: mining, refining resources and industrial production).

Several problems have arisen at that time:

  • the game was launched early to everyone in open alpha. First observation, the public did not really come and the game did not generate the enthusiasm that we were hoping for. It had at the time federated a community of passionate players but had completely failed to touch anyone outside of our existing communities.
  • Galaxy 55 has undergone several internal rearrangements in terms of game design. From a game management / industrial production of resources, it passed to a pure exploration play, then regularly fluctuated between exploration / management without ever finding its equilibrium. After several months of development, the game always seemed a little "hollow", despite some already considerable content.
  • Minecraft has finally caught up proposing finally a decent Adventure mode and content that went beyond the sandbox. It became therefore difficult to bring something really new to the initial model (which, ironically, was not displeasing to us as fans of Minecraft )

All this added together eventually melted our motivation for Galaxy 55. After several months, it became clear that the project lacked a true identity (other than visual) and surpassing Minecraft in terms of depth of play became an exhausting race.

Between working on a new original project or continuing to develop Galaxy 55 under the shadow of the new Minecraft, the choice was quickly made.

At the moment, we have no plans to reboot the game in its current form. The genre of Minecraft-like (because yes, it is now clearly a genre as was the Doom-like at the time) has spawned many quality titles and we do not have the habit of staying too long on a beaten track. All our energy is now focused on more original projects and we hope you will like them

This translation of the announcement is not "official" but is my best effort at making it understandable to those who do not speak French.
