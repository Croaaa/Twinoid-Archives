Motion Twin is offering you all the chance to take a sneak peek at our latest project: Galaxy 55!

We decided to develop this project a little differently. Traditionally, a game is developed, then released as a beta for a few weeks or months before finally being made available to the public.

For Galaxy 55, we're going to allow as many players as possible to test the game throughout its development, even before the alpha or beta versions! The game will regularly be updated and enhanced with new features and functions and we will be advancing in leaps and bounds. The timeline for these updates can be seen on the game website, at the bottom of the page.

We look forward to hearing your feedback and what you think of the project
