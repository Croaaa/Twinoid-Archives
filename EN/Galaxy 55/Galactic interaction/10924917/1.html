Also a neat trick i discovered (it worked in Demo, not sure if it works now).
Find a nice mining spot. Move into your "mining position", or spot, or how you wish to call it
Switch window focus (or just move to another tab) for a couple of minutes. Google/Wikipedia surfing is great for this step.
Put your fingers on the next needed mining key (shift or E, i think).
Now, be fast. Move focus to the game window (or tab) and start mining.

If it still works, you will mine at least a couple of blocks at an astounding rate. If it doesn't, nothing will happen.

Also a way to test it: move focus away, wait, move back and press W (to move forward). If it works, you'll know
