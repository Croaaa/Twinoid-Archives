On may 9, 2013
MotionTwit

Add to the list FAFI 360. What an annoying game.

It's Interwheel v2.0, but much harder and unforgiving.

Break a 10-pass combo and you've just lost yourself over 1000 pts.

There are so many variables that affect your performance:
- The number of +20 second time bonuses (if none appear, forget about getting a good score)
- The number of 2000-, 1500- and 1000-point powerups that appear
- The strengths and weaknesses of the CPU players (if you get an opponent team with really fast players or a really good goalie, you can't rack up a good combo)
- The positioning of the players & land mines (to be fair, this resets after every goal)
