TradeFederation
Franz24I enjoyed it, though the turrets seem kinda useless in the first levels

Your very first play is free, and you can try as many times as you have to to win, if you have no tokens, make sure you take advantage of this and start this when you are done for the day, and intentionally lose as much as possible in your first play in level up mode to practice the game and collect lots of bonus . The turrets are like the Borderlands turrets, they distract and create a physical barrier. You can place them in doorways to just slaughter the enemies while you loot. They are much better in league mode, where you want to loot as fast as possible. Since both you and your turrets are on auto-shoot mode, you basically run from the enemies, place turrets, reload when necessary, and position yourself so your shot types can hit. For example, the grenades only hit a certain area away from you, so you need to herd your enemies into the kill zone if you have them.
