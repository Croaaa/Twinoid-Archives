MotionTwit

Every time you successfully pass the ball to a teammate (or the goal), you earn a score bonus of MIN(50*N, 500) points for your Nth consecutive pass.

Every time you lose the ball to the opposing team, or kick a ball out of bounds, your multiplier resets.

Watch Machades' replay and notice how on a few occasions, he passes to out-of-the-way teammates for additional points.
