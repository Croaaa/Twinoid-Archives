I can't think of any guides, however I can give some basic tips as someone who has a 36,400 pb.


The goal for this game is to score goals fast, while maintaining a high consistant pass rate. As once you pass 10 times in a row each pass is worth 500 points (when passed to a glowing player).


What I usually do (seems others do too) is try to get the pass rate up higher in between your first two goals, that way when the game gets more challenging (their goalie arrives, more players spawn for them, mines, etc) you can be extra careful while passing and still get a good amount of points.


I'm not sure how your score is calculated based on the amount of goals you have, but obviously more is better. I personally try not to worry much about the beer bonuses, yes they do give you points but my main priority is trying to maintain my high pass rate. I also usually take an extra few seconds to see if I can see any clocks on the field.

If you're able to score 5 goals and get around 25 passes completed I'd be surprised if you were under 25,000.

Hope I helped, if you have any specific questions I'd be happy to try an answer them.
