Next season begins as soon as the competent M-T admins kick of the process/scripts to do the period change.

What will happen is
- Level-Up Mode: All of your levels will be reset to 1.

- Mission Mode: Your mission will reset to 1.

However, for every jackpot you earn (LV5 and LV20), your base jackpot level increases by 1 in the next period:

- If you finished a game between LV5 and LV19, you'll have to reach LV6 in the next period to win your first jackpot.

- If you won a LV20 jackpot, you'll have to reach LV7 in the next period to win your first jackpot.
