Season 3 Overview:
The mission reward has been cut from 255 s to 205 s per mission. Payouts for the other two modes are comparable with those of the previous season. This makes grinding missions a less appealing option.

Season 3 Objectives:
1. Win all LV5 jackpots in descending order of payouts. Skip Cataclismo and Animoz.
2. Play just enough in League Mode to get promoted.
3. Spend any remaining s (in multiples of 6 s) on Missions.

Allocations per Mode:
Level-Up: 9 games * 5 lvls/game - (Trigo LV1) = 44 s
Missions: 1 mission * 6 s/mission = 6 s
League: 11 games * 1 /game = 11 s
Screw-Ups: 9 s allocated for ill luck and/or crappy RNGs.
TOTAL : 5 free s/day * 14 days = 70 free s
(If things go extremely well, I can squeeze in a 2nd mission or an Animoz jackpot, and allocate only 2–3 s for screw-ups, but now that I'm in I expect stronger competition)

Anticipated Earnings:
Level-Up: 9 LV5 jackpots = 1 823 s
League: 863–935 s () or 1227–1370 s (Gold)
Missions: 1 mission * 205 s/mission = 205 s
Points obtained from games: 70 s * ~20 s/ (average) = 1400 s
TOTAL : 4200–4800 s

Footnotes:

- With all due respect to Calib's suggestion, I do not think it is a good idea to shoot for LV 20 jackpots. Notice that the LV 20 jackpot is always 4X the LV 5 jackpot (and of course, 20 = 4*5.) In other words, the payout rises linearly, while the difficulty rises exponentially. This makes it a poor tradeoff. Moreover, if you complete levels beyond LV 5, there is a chance that your base jackpot level will be increased for the following period.

- I've noticed that my s seem to decrease when I play the same game over and over again, even if I'm completing objectives and making progress. For example, my earnings from Nosushi Levels 1–5 were +31, +27, +24, +22 and +21, respectively. I always end the games as late as possible to fish for s. I don't want to start up conspiracy theories, but if M-T is manipulating payouts this way, I will try scrambling my plays between different games to see if the same thing happens.

- Why are Cataclismo s payouts so horrible for Levels 1 through 5? I've never picked up any more than 5 s in-game. Taking this into account, the first 5 levels of Animoz will earn you more s than Cataclismo despite the lower jackpot.
