These 3 emoticons are free and are available to you as soon as you register for Arkadeo:

Each game unlocks at least 4 Twinoid achievements and at least 2 emoticons via Level-Up Mode:

+2 pts and an emoticon for winning your first LV5 jackpot
+4.5 pts for winning your second "base level" jackpot (LV5/6/7)
+2 pts and an emoticon for winning your first LV20 jackpot
+2 pts for winning your second LV20 jackpot

You unlock when you register for League Mode. Each tier above Bronze unlocks at least 3 Twinoid achievements and at least 3 emoticons:

+21.5 pts and for your first season in the Silver League
+0 pts and link for your third season in the Silver League
+0(?) pts and link for your 5th/8th/10th(?) season in the Silver League

The point values for reaching the Gold and Platinum tiers for the first time are +42.5 pts and +64 pts, respectively.

That's everything known so far. With 13 games including Pulsar and Gauntlet, there are at least 45 emoticons and at least 67 Twinoid achievements.
