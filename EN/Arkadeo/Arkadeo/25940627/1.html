Welcome to the English Arkadeo Forum! Please respect the Twinoid Terms and Conditions and follow the forum rules:


I. Search before posting.

  • Before posting a question, use the Search button to make sure somebody else didn't already ask it.
  • Also check the FAQ (coming soon), your question could already be answered there.
  • If you still don't find it, use the Questions/Answers (coming soon) thread.
  • If you want to discuss a particular subject, make sure there are no threads already talking about it.

II. Use correct English and be polite.


  • Refrain from using SMS language, too many smileys, CAPS LOCK, etc.
  • Try to be as polite as possible, this will make your posts more enjoyable to read for everybody.
  • Refrain from swearing or use of offensive language.

III. Think before posting.


  • In general, avoid flooding, off-topic posts, etc.
  • Do not bump without a reason. Posts like 'bump' and 'up' will receive an official warning!
  • If an issue is resolved, don't post in the thread without a reason.
  • Don't try to contact a mod by posting here.
    • Use this online form
    • Or PM one of the mods

IV. Leave moderation to the moderators.


  • Don't pretend to be the police. In case of trouble, report the post and/or contact a moderator.
  • Note that moderation is here to make the forum better. You can request locking, moving or editing for threads and a mod will do it as soon as possible. Similarly, if you have ideas for the forum, let the mods know and they'll assist you.
  • Do not use the report button without a valid reason.

V. Respect the players and staff.

  • Although your opinion can be different from others, it is very important to have respect for others and other opinions. Be polite to other players, moderators and administrators.
  • As such, teasing, flaming, trolling, personal attacks, challenges to sanctions or other similar events are not allowed on the forum. However, critique, positive or negative, is always welcome as long as it is argued in peace.
  • Any discussion held by PM must remain private.

If you don't follow the Terms and Conditions and/or these forum rules you will be strictly punished. Penalties range from warnings, a permanent ban from the forums to deleting your account.


Enjoy your stay on the forums!
