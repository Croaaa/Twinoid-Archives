What I'm wondering is do we as a clan lose points when our attack is defended? I guess not but there is confusion about that in our clan.
On july 15, 2014
coro

No points are lost when an attack is defended, or rather, no points are exchanged between the two clans


On a related note, you might see things in the log that look like this:
user (Clan) took 0pts from us in their attack.This doesn't mean that the attacking clan earned 0 points
If a clan has no points to give, the system generates points
