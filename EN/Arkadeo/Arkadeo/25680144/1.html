I had same trouble before but I found out the way how to slove it.
I used to make first move at the first lvl (in league mode) with mouse (cuz keyboard wasn't active), then I used keyboard.

But now, I open the game in the new window (as I did before), click the screen once (when you see "Click to play", you have to click here), click on Play button (or just press Enter) and no mouse clicks after! I just wait 5-6 seconds and start the first level with active keyboard (it could save 5+ seconds).

If you make mouse click somewhere during the game, then you may lose keyboard control and you will have to make any move with mouse to activate keyboard.
