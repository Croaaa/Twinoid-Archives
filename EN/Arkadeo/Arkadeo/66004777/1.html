Everest Base Camp Trek is one of the most popular and renowned treks in the world that thousands embark on every year. Leading you to the exquisite Sherpa villages tucked beneath the giant Himalayas, the Everest base camp trek is the best adventure one can intake during their visit to Nepal. From the rhododendron forests at a lower elevation to the highest viewpoint of the world, Kala Patthar, the trek is a total package of nature, culture, and adventure in one journey.
