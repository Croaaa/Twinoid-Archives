How are the points in the league table calculated? Is it based on actual scores in each game in the 'league' section? Is it based on your position in each of those games? Something else?
On april 13, 2013
npfan

It is based on your rank of each game relative to everyone in the league you're in. 1st place in a game gives you 60 points, 2nd is 57, and every lower rank is worth 3 points less until 6th for 45. After 6th place, every lower rank is worth 1 point less.
