Multilingual games made after Twinoid launched respond to the "lang" query

Examples:

http://arkadeo.com/tid/forum?lang=es#... (english forum with spanish set as active language)
http://arkadeo.com/user/sponsor?lang=fr (french version of referrals page)
http://arkadeo.com/tid/forum?lang=fr#... (third page of the Coffee Shop with french set as active language (notice that there's two question marks in the URL, which is probably incredibly confusing to normal people))
