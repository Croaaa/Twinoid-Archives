SiiseliAnd no euros was used.

Of course he achieved all this without buying s!

I hope I'm not making people jealous when I say this, but in an act of generosity that I truly did not expect from M-T, beta testers essentially had access to unlimited s during the beta period. Immediately, I knew that was the ideal time to practice like crazy, so that when the time came for Arkadeo to go out of beta, I would be able to take advantage of every good RNG I came across with my free daily s...

LV 20 jackpots took me anywhere between 30-40 s (for a game that hasn't yet been released to the public, although I only failed 2 levels, one of which took a dozen tries) to 200+ s for Nosushi.

As for the League, here are some examples:
79k Nosushi took me about 150-200 tries
73k Rock Faller took me about 40-50 tries
33k Capman took me about 20-30 tries
6300-6700 (forgot exact score) Cataclismo took me about 20-30 tries

These numbers were all after I had already become proficient at the games, so I was basically brute-forcing them for favourable RNGs... If I had counted the number of s I used while practicing and getting familiar with the games, the numbers I provided above would probably be understated by about a factor of 10.

So I'd say he definitely used over 500 s to get to where he's at right now. This would be a conservative estimate...
