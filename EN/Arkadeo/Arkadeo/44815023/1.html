I've gotten myself into a tight spot because I picked a few "upgrades" that have made the game significantly harder.

"Upgrade" is a misnomer (but is consistent with the game's terminology.) What I'm referring to are the options to increase the game's difficulty. You have to pick one at the beginning of each level.

One of the worst is "cellulions split into two." Not only do you have a ton of enemies to defeat (because cellulions are the most common enemy) the split ones hurl themselves at you, so it's very easy to get hit.

I really hope I don't have to restart this for a more favorable gameplay environment, as I've failed more games as of Level 11 than it took me to clear all 20 levels the first time.
