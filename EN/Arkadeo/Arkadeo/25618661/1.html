MotionTwit

The objective of the game is to get the coal into the pipes. Any gems blocking the way will disappear when they're arranged in groups of 4+ identically-coloured stones.

You start out the game with 10 turns. After you run out of turns, the game ends.

There are two types of pipes: the gold ones, which increase your remaining turns, and the pink ones, which award one-time point bonuses.

Gold pipes are essential to making a high score. If pink pipes keep appearing instead of gold, you're getting screwed over by bad luck. All of the Pantheon scores have near-endless supplies of gold pipes. Your priority should be getting the coal into gold pipes as rapidly as possible.

Other than that, there's really not much more to say. You should mentally visualise each move in your head before you commit. Remember that you're trying to group identical colours together, so try to minimise scattering.

This is one of the most heavily luck-based games on Arkadeo, so don't feel too bad.
