If you're wanting to join a clan, let people know by posting here!

Include as much detail as you can, so that clan recruiters can more easily determine if you're a good fit for their clan.

Here are some rules that you should follow in this thread:

Do not post here if you are recruiting for a clan.
Please post an update if you've been accepted into a clan.
Don't post here if you are currently in a clan, unless you plan to leave your existing clan very soon.
Do not make any comments on the presentations made by others.

I'm just wanting to prevent a problem before it starts, while also setting up something that should have been here since clans were available to the general public
