You also need to quit attacking enemy enclosures when you're close to clearing a level and there's still neutral enclosures. At that point, you're just giving up free points for no reason

Also, you don't always need to save small enclosures if a big attack is coming to it. I personally use small enclosures to bait out an attack from either the enemy's original spawning enclosure or the biggest one they control. The point of baiting out an attack is to halve the number of units there, so then you have an easier time taking it over (but this technique is less effective if the enemy has a cluster of enclosures to move units around and absorb your attack)
