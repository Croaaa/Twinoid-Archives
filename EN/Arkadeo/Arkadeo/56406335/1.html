Here's a replay of GW if you want to improve http://arkadeo.com/r/4ZAQ0w

Tips:
- Try to stick with the same way over and over again so you can keep track of the princes without having to check the map.
- The best way to kill the enemies is with the burning turret; when you're halfway, make sure that the turret is full (3 turns).
- Around 200K, always choose the lightning weapon; It can kill the hard enemies in 2 shots ( Ghost, Red monster) and with one shot the purple monsters; Again, make sure you don't run out ammo
- 2nd best turret is the shield; use it to escape right onto where the burning turret is placed

The best maps are those where the burning turret and lightning weapon are placed in the middle of the map.
