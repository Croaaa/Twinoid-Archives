Long story short:
Try out league and level up mode, don't rely on the games themselves to get .

Long story long:
Not sure what my top three games are for earning are, but I can tell you that I'm gaining an average of 1,000 a season (other players, feel free to compare using this page). Note I am using the VIP 5 games a day, so your results may vary.

Now, if you want to rely on that alone, it'd take you just about a year to earn one MTPass. You'd honestly be better off playing Carapass.

What I'd suggest is try to raise your rank in League mode or find a game you really like and try from some Level up mode jackpots. My preference is League mode and once you get the hang of most of the games, you shouldn't have trouble making it up to gold or platinum (not sure, I haven't been following the league numbers). They offer seasonal "paychecks" as well as promotional "paychecks" as well. For example, in two seasons if you make it up to gold, you'll be probably ~1,300 richer from league alone.

If you can find a clan, that would also be good. Try to find one that prioritizes mission mode as that will give you gifts no matter how you rank. You may have to raise your league though so people know that you deserve to be in their clan.

All in all, good luck and have fun!

If you're a very patient person, Rock Faller is good. It will pretty much give you max per game as long as you make the best moves. Of course, you're screwed no matter what if you get a terrible starting board.
