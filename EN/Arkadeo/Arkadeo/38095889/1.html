Have we got a smorgasbord of changes for you!

On the menu:

LevelUp Mode just got much more fun
- It's now possible for games to have up to 100 levels! A real challenge, if you can handle it...
- A Jackpot is waiting for all players on the final level, complete the level to strike it rich!
- Your records here to stay, they'll no longer be reset. You no longer need to restart each LevelUp challenge each season. You decide if and when you restart the adventure!
- The jokers you previously accumulated are no longer useful, they'll be swapped for some cool bonuses... We'll tell you more about that on the forums real soon.


More spice to the seasons
- Seasons now last for 3 weeks, which allows all players to make the most of League, Clan and Mission modes. Everyone can play at their own speed!
- Scores for all your games, including Mission and Tactical mode, are now taken into account for your overall ranking.

Pixel rich prizes
- For over a year now, 75% of prize points have been used to play more games. In order to align ourselves with this tendancy, we have decided to modify the products we offer in the prize section.
- You asked for it, we made it happen: The MTPass is here, you can now use your prize points to buy anything you like on your favourite Twinoid sites.
- Amazon gift vouchers will, however, be removed from Arkadeo. 20$ and 65$ vouchers will remain in the game until the end of the current season.

Clan beta test
- This mode will gradually open to players during the beta test and during the season.
- The twisted Tactical Mode allows clans to face off against one another.
- A cooperative Mission mode which works in collaboration with LevelUp Mode. United we are stronger!


A new heavyweight game
- All our VIP Level 2 players can now test Brutal Teenage Crisis!
- 100 levels of authentic pixel flailing mayhem.


Add a generous sprinkling of general upgrades and improvements to the mix, and voil√†!.

We hope you enjoy this update, we'll ba keen to read your reactions and suggestions in the forums over the coming days.
