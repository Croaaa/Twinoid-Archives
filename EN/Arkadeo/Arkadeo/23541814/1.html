The following strategy will earn you 4000-5000 s with 70 free s :

1. Win LV5 jackpot for Nosushi (230 s)
2. Win LV5 jackpot for Mel (224 s)
3. Play just enough in League Mode to get promoted.
4. Use any remaining s on missions (255 s per 6 s)

allocations per mode:
Level-Up: 2 games * 5 lvls/game = 10 s
League: 10 games * 1 /game = 10 s
Missions: 8 missions * 6 s/mission = 48 s
Screw-Ups: I've allocated 2 s for failed levels or bad scores due to ill luck and/or crappy RNGs.
TOTAL : 5 free s/day * 14 days = 70 free s

s earned:
Level-Up: 230 s (Nosushi LV5) + 224 s (Mel LV5) = 454 s
League: 198-238 s (Bronze) or 897-936 s (Silver)
Missions: 8 missions * 255 s/mission = 2040 s
Points obtained from games: 70 s * ~22 s/ (average) = 1540 s
TOTAL : Between 4200 s (Bronze) and 5000 s (Silver)
