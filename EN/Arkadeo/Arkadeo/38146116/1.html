Loyehcycling? please explain

Basically, exploiting the game for "infinite" s at no monetary cost.

Procedure:
1. Play X sectors to earn
2. Use those to buy Y s from in-game merchants, where Y > X

So effectively, you net Y - X "free" s.

This is possible because s regenerate every 200 s, so if your mining sequence exceeds 200 sectors, you can loop through it again and again and again...

In reality, the people who can best take advantage are those who've already "beaten" the game, as most -rich areas are scattered in deep space, and Generator V7 and Radar V3 require landing on Terre. They also need to be good enough to beat all those sectors without too many game overs, in order to make the "loop" profitable.

Even in a best case scenario, we're looking at hours upon hours of work for 300-350 net s, or about $20's worth if you buy from M-T directly. Not necessarily the best use of time depending on your opportunity costs.
