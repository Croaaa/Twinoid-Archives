The easiest way to earn s is to

(1) reach a League tier that's relatively easy to enter and stay in, but hard to fall out of (in the past, I would have said , but after the admins raised the demotion quota from ~3 to ~13 players per period, I'm not so sure anymore)

(2) level each game to 5 (no more, no less) in descending order by jackpot size.

If you do this properly, you'll end up with around 4000 s (0.86 USD) per period. This is about the most you can earn without paying for s, which is guaranteed to put you at a net loss financially, even if you're an elite player. You earn 9000 s (1.95 USD) for being #1 in Elite, and the cheapest payment method already costs more than that.

That said, it's extremely boring, and it's likely you will lose interest within a few months tops. However, if you care about Twinoid points and that kind of thing, you'll probably have to earn at least 20X jackpots on each game to unlock all possible achievements.

Outside of one period when the mission rewards were unusually high, the entire mode is not worth the trouble.
