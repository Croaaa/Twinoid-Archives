TL;DR: Do League Mode

Well, the obvious answer is just keep playing. It's going to take awhile, but eventually it'll start all adding up.

There's different modes that you can do as well for "bonus" prizes:

Level-up mode: Probably wouldn't recommend this to start with if you're new, as you need to be really good at the game to do well. You need to play the game X times (and win!) and once you reach that number (different for every game) you get the jackpot.

League Mode: You're currently in a league with 150 people. You can play league games to compare your scores with others, and you earn "league points" by placing above others. If you're top 25 in your league, you'll go up a rank and earn 500 which is essentially double your current. I'd strongly recommend League as you don't have to be good at every game to rank up. As you go up in league ranks, you're against more skilled people, but the rewards are greater for ranking up and placing well.

Mission Mode: Never was a big fan of this mode, but if you complete the required objectives you earn a small jackpot. As you complete more objectives the difficulty goes up, but you earn more. Try it if you want.

Clan Mode: If you want to try your hand at Clans, you can try to join a clan by posting in the "players clan recruitment" thread. If you don't get any offers, don't be discouraged, it's just because you're new and don't have as much experience as others. Clans do attack and defenses, or they have their own personal mission mode.

Hopefully this isn't too confusing. Any questions, feel free to ask.
