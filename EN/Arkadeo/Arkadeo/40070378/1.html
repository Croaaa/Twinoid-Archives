shadowlake-screws with your 'hit box' {how the heck did i hit that saw?}

What happens is that your hitbox actually shifts to the side when you do a cling

So say you cling to the edge of a platform
For a split second Mel is effectively standing on the edge of that platform
So if a saw is within a character width to the edge you're probably going to get hurt

Take a look at this to see what I mean: http://arkadeo.com/r/_LLT
The very end could just be a graphical glitch but I'm willing to believe that it's just showing an actual frame of animation that you normally can't see
