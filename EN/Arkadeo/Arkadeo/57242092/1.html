Anglophone

There's only 4 patterns you have to learn, basically. 2 (look for colours where there's just 2, directly opposite one another) 3 (may be different colours but usually not), 4 (same as 3) and 6 (may be multiple colours).

Figuring out which one it is: look at the inner ring. If it's 2, just line them up (watch for stone). If it's 3 you'll notice the same colour evenly split 3 times. Same with 4. If six, it'll be very full usually or it will be the same colour alternating around the wheel - very obvious.
