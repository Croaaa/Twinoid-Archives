coro

You get 100 points for decreasing a neutral enclosure's number by 1

Also, there's a point bonus for capturing a neutral enclosure in one attack (you get 30 points times the number the enclosure had once you began capturing it). You can tell when you can get a bonus by the little sheep icon next to the enclosure's number. The bonus doesn't go away if the enemy begins to capture an enclosure, only when you have an attack that doesn't completely take over a neutral enclosure

If you send multiple attacks to the same neutral enclosure at the same time, make sure the larger attack can complete before any other ones, or the smaller attack(s) will cause your bonus to go away


For a scoring example, say you have a neutral enclosure with a 53 on it. If you haven't touched it yet, you get 6890 points from capturing it in a single attack

(53 * 100) + (53 * 30)

Otherwise, you would only be able to get a maximum of 5300

tips by coro:

coro

How I play BS (starting from the very start of a game):


  • look for neutral enclosures with single digit numbers on them, and send sheep immediately to them
  • try to find the largest enclosure that I can put a building on, take it over, and put a motel on it
  • bide my time with neutral enclosures that need a lot of units to take over
  • wait for the enemy to attempt to take it over
  • wait for your unit count in one enclosure to be high enough to take it over easily (2x the value shown is the minimum, wait till you have 3x or more)
  • attempt to gain control of a section of the map before starting to take over enemy enclosures
  • turtle like a champ until I can fully humiliate my enemy

Other tips:




  • never build the pesticide (I think that's what it's called) on a large enclosure, as you handicap your ability to generate units
  • turtling is only good if you have control of most of the larger enclosures (otherwise the computer will be able to out-turtle you)
  • on that note, try to get control of large enclosures, even at the expense of one of your smaller enclosures (including your initial enclosure!)
  • if you can afford it, send sheep to take over enemy enclosures that just sent units to attack you elsewhere
  • if the enemy starts off with a large enclosure, while you have a small one, you will immediately lose if you cannot find an easily-captured large enclosure (don't get too mad about this )
  • don't play BS in LevelUp mode if you only have 1 token left (even if you feel like you're good at the game)

The final result is that you "exchange" the enclosure with the enemy, but he's at loss, because he's giving up a big one for a small/medium one.
