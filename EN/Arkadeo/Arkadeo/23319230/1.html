Well, you can go directly to the page that lets you buy Amazon.es gift cards: http://arkadeo.com/shop/?cat=3

As for the site's language, you can get into spanish mode by adding a "lang" value of "es" to your query string .w.

An example: http://arkadeo.com/?lang=es

Doing so will change your language until you get logged out, then it will be set to your account's primary language
