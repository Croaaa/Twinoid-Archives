Well at least I can actually give generic advice for 61 (unlike some other levels where I felt like I had to break locks in a specific order)

On the left side, you need to knock enemies off into the abyss before they reach the top platform with the exit chain. The reason you need to do it early is because that top platform is longer, so you have to push the enemies a longer distance. Yes you risk falling off with less time to recover, but you'll just have to get used to it

On the right side, you need to knock the enemies into that gap to the left of the other exit chain. This buys a bit more time than just knocking them down one level, and avoids wasting time where you're stuck in the right corner due to falling down on top of a clump of enemies that can't go anywhere
