- Grouping

Three categories, 32 players per categorie:
Easy - Bronze/Silver Leagues
Medium - Gold/Platinum Leagues
Hard - Master/Elite Leagues
You should register to a competition rank suitable with your higher level reached in Arkadeo so far. If you try to enlist for a lower rank you will be automatically disqualified.
The minimum number of players for the event to start is 32.
 Player vs. Player

The matches will use FAFI League version, one player against the other (but against the machine as well).

Wins the one who scored more goals. If there is a draw then the one who scored more points is the winner.

Matches

You have just one day to submit a replay with your match. You can play how many times you want but you can only send one replay per match.
- Rules

Only replays from the day of the event will be valid.

If you send me 2 or more replays I will choose the worst of them.

If you fail to send a replay you lose.

Any mis-behaviour or foul-play against other players/organizers will be severely punished.
