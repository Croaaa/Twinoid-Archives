List of known bugs:
Arkadeo

  • Translation Errors
    Bug: A lot of texts are still in French
    Status: Will be translated in time. The admins are currently focusing on more important issues. Use Google Translate in the meantime
  • No titles or rewards
    Bug: Impossible to get any special titles or icons
    Solution: You need to get a Jackpot to win an icon or title.
  • Red Loading Screen
    Bug: The loading screen in the Preview game is red, the game won't even start
    Extra information: Preview games are not complete, so expect a bit of glitchiness.

Iceway

  • Impossible level
    Bug: Impossible level in Iceway, no solution possible
    Status: Needs evidence (screenshots, replays)
    Extra info: Atlanteine in KadoKado had the same bug.
  • Keyboard not working
    Bug: Impossible to control Iceway with a keyboard
    Solution: You need to click once in the game area at the beginning of level 1

BattleSheep

  • Force disconnect
    Bug: While playing BattleSheep you get the message "This action requires you to be logged in to Arkadeo" and you lose the game and your token.
    Status: Not fixed yet

Pulsar

  • Replays end prematurely
    Bug: Most (if not all) Pulsar replays end with a score lower than the actual score achieved.
    Status: Not fixed yet
  • No slime
    Bug: Cellions don't drop slime anymore.
    Occurrence: When playing in low quality, or when the game automatically lowers the quality to keep the performance high
    Status: Not fixed yet
  • Enemy upgrades not working
    Bug: Enemy upgrades don't work in Mission mode if it's LEVEL type of Pulsar mission
    Occurrence: Every time
    Example: If you need to Complete level 20 Pulsar in Mission mode then actually you have to pass level 1
