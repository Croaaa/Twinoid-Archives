Hello everybody in new year 2015. In many reflexions associated with the new year, I wonder what is wrong with me, or maybe with these Arkadeo games. In many games today I play much better than few or more seasons ago, but... my results do not improve at all Best examples are CQ and Mel, in which I have problem to cross results I've done for a first time about 26th season
I am "old generation player" - I remember times of local arcade clubs in early 90', where the games had always the same setups, all equipments always lie at the same places, and if you were good at the game, you could reach good scores almost at every attempt. Here in Arkadeo I can strong feeling that the games in paralel with my evolution gives me setups from worse and worse cart, and the main aim of the system is to take more and more tokens I bought. This are my feelings, I would like to believe this not works in that way
The second fact is, that the games are simply dishonest, especially in clan mission mode. I would like to see, how Twonoid creators do 55k in Nosushi, without any bonuses except explosions??? Sometimes there are 10 consecutive games in which even a world champion have no chances Impossible to do setups are an ordinary extortion of tokens.
Independently of these problems, I like Arkadeo and I wish a happy New Year for all players
