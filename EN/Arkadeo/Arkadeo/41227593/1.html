Lately there have been clans emerging that have very few members (let's say up to 5).
They do nothing or very few attacks until end of the season and then launch several attacks (usualy using one game).

So they easily rank over place 50, meaning a reward of 5.5k or if they rank over place 25, it's 9k ... They spend only few tokens and rank really high and get up to 2k reward for doing almost nothing.

I find it really frustrating if your clan ranks the whole season at rank 20 (and has to do lot of attacks/defends) or higher and then it's shot down by this tactic.
Currently there is no reason to play tactical mode until last few days before season end.

This could be avoided if some "overall rank bonus" was implemented.
For example every day you get bonus points: 51 - your_current_rank
This bonus will be cumulated until end of the season and then a season average will be added to your final points.

So if you end up with 80 points and cumulate bonus 630 points (which averages to 30 points), your final score will be 80 + (630 / 21) = 110 points.

(Formulas are just for illustration, i hope you got the idea)
