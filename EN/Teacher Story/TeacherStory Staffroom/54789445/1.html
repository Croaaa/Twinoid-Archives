I could not really find a thread like this, so I decided to make a new one. The purpose of this thread is to share tips & tricks (certain combos) with other teachers. Maybe you're already using my tricks, but in case you didn't I think it's good to share them. I'm curious to see your tricks as well!

Wisdom Curveball + Big Brain
Pick a student with 4 and as much as possible. Use the big brain student attack on him (making him highly attentive) and then defeating him with wisdom curveball, bypassing all and usually dealing around 6~8 damage, far more effective than pop quiz. Instead of big brain you could also use twinoid if you really want.

Meditation + Pay attention
A pretty obvious one. Pay attention almost never removes all 10~15 that it could technically do. If you're going to use pay attention anyway (or any -removing skill), why not use meditation first, to get 2 free control points back.

Solid explanation + Twinoid
Solid explanation is a pretty good skill in my opinion. It is easy to use in the end-game, defeating pupils with only a few left and also getting back a few recovery points, but what makes the skill even better is that you can combine it with twinoid to deal upto 5 damage and also get 5 self control points back.

Interesting anecdote + Twinoid
It's like the wisdom curveball + big brain combo, only much more situational, but if used correctly it might be able to instantly defeat 2 pupils, doing over 15 damage.

I will add more, when I think of them.
