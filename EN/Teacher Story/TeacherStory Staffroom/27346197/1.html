Fudgeawesome sheriff hat! Sheriff Norray's Deputy = 100 one-shot?

No, you get it for advancing to Grade 2.

To reach Grade 2 you need to level up to level 12, then you get an option to go on vacation. While on vacation you research your family history and when you come back to class the following school year you have a new hat (can cycle hats and no hat by pressing 'C') and also a new portrait in your home (you can see the little portrait icon on the wall in your/someone elses home, but you can only view them in your own home on the "play" page when James Norray is physically at home, if that makes sense).

You go back to level 1, but are now a Grade 2: Teacher, you get to keep one of your previously unlocked skills to add to your permanent skill set, and you start a new mission and class immediately.
