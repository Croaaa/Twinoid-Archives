TradeFederation

1. Good Memory

WIN/get. More attack options just makes everything else better, powers up all your attacks by making sure you get you your Boredom destroying attacks early, and your power hits later, and lessens the chance of being hosed by the RNG. Some people pay real money for this edge, and it also stacks with the real money unlock, so you can hit em with all 4 barrels . I'm not sure why this isn't on every list.


2. Rigged Clock Lev 1,2

If you ace every class, then these might just be +1 XP each, but you get more than that. A large number of attacks are on a turn timer, and more turns makes their window of effectiveness larger. If you don't ace every class, this will help you get there, and each unneeded turn also reduces the cost of immediately playing another class. If you can beat a class with 4 turns remaining, it becomes quite affordable to play again, even if you are not buying budget coins with real money.


3. Brainwash and Burst of Knowledge

The top level area of effect attacks. They just do a ton of damage to lots of students. These make it much easier to take out the whole class fast, no subtlety here.


4. Individual Work

Takes out a student slowly. The damage is good, somewhat situational, its here because it also stops most negative statuses, on top of its direct damage.


5. Solid Explanation

Instant damage + healing, whats not to like ?

jdawg011

My pick for top 3 passive and top actives.

Passive:
-Good memory: Ever have those games when you get Pay Attention and Call to order on your last turn? This will end that!
-Rigged clock: Makes it easier to win classes, or get more xp per class.
-Good rest: Extra health, can never complain about that.

Actives:
-Front of the Class: I haven't had it, but come on. Use on an extremely bored student and you'll make quick work of them.
-Uber Concentration: An upgraded for of the already great skill: Pay attention.
-Excercise: Hit's every student in the class (as long as they are able to be hit, ie. not KO). This can do a ton of damage overall.
-Burst of knowledge: Can potentially hit 5 students, depending on class size.
-Individual work: This is great in situations where you get it early. High health kid? Bam he's done and won't cause problems.
-Solid explanation: Accompanies with 'highly attentive' this skill can heal a ton, while doing a fair bit of damage.
-Let's get serious: This one is excellent for killing those high-boredom , yet low health students.

The upgraded teach/teach harder are also great for that extra damage per student.
