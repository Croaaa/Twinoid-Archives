Front row gives +2, middle row +1 and back row as was said previously gives +0

There are player skills+teacher's pet skills that gives more score such as the teacher skill 'educational flick' that gives +1.5 points if you defeat the student with this skill, theres also a teacher's pet skill called antiswag that gives the pupil a +1 when he is defeated

Naturally you can choose to grade papers in the break room or home to give every defeated student from last class +0.5
