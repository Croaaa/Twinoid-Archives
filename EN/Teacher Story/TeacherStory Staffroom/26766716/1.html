Miner101Xerm... not sure if this is the right post but does anyone know what the effect soft in the head does (caused by the teacher's pet's ability: tranqualiser darts)

Soft in the Head reduces the damage that student can inflict with their random actions, such as screeching noise, spitballs, or what have you. With that, they can only hurt you for one damage every turn.
