No? The pupils in your class are the people who sent most recently and reliably. This way you signal that you're not blocked, without having to go to the forum or PMing. Then if they're full up, you move down your friends list or check the forum.

Instead of worrying about where you'll get your pupils, ensure that you're open to receive pupils - and your friends know you're online and playing the game because you just sent them a pupil. Likewise, your queue is not that large, which means the current pupils in your class are far more likely to be active players. And if not, then as I said, go down your list.

It's hard to picture with wall-text, but 2 pupils in your class, 3 pupils to send each day, several days per class - who's getting left out? Newbs who send pupils are automatically dealt in, even.

This beats going through every single friend and then the next day wondering if you missed someone. Or checking each and every person's nexus? I am less likely to keep banging my head on a full-up queue if I can't tell if you're active or abandoned the game. The "last 3 days" status applies across all of MT, not only TS.
