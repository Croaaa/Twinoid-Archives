LoveVaseyWhat's the point of even having this option if its simply going to take a class away?

No. If you lose all your SC you also lose many hours recovering just some of your SC. If you voluntarily take leave you gain more SC and lose just a single class.

Since almost all missions are able to be beaten in under the number of classes you are allocated, taking leave is a valid option for players who find themselves low on SC and on a mission which they will be able to complete in at least one class under those allocated for the mission.

It's a gift. Accept it as such and move on.
