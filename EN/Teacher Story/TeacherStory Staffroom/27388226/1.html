Heya, if you're student has the Stoopid status applied to them, they take 1 less damage from all sources, and unlike other things in the game I found out it has -no- minimum damage taken threshold. If you deal 1 damage, it will take 0 due to this skill.

I tried putting a bomb on a student (1 dmg over 3 turns) and the student gained stoopid on the turn I applied it, and took no damage. I then attached attentive to the student (+1 damage on next damage source), and the student still took zero damage. Finally I had to remove the stoopid status on the final turn so the bomb would go off (3 turns of actions just to remove one point of damage, eek!)

Just a fair warning to all of you out there with bombs or, possibly individual work? If that skill doesn't wipe debuffs on application.
