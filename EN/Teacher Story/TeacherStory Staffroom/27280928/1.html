You're at the beach, on holiday, chilling... James Norray though, is never idle!

We have been thinking about him a lot from our caba√±a on the beach, and have decided to make his day-to-day life with these frightful creatures they call pupils a little more bearable.

Loads of changes have appeared in Teacher Story:

  • There is a new system for progressing to the next class: the price is reduced based on the time and whether or not you had a perfect class.
  • The waiting time between 2 classes has been reduced from 7 to 5 hours.
  • You can now only send pupils to your friends
  • Every bonus pupil received will give an additional class in which to complete a mission before the deadline.
  • A pupil can be sent along with a gift object
  • New inter-class action: Go on sick leave. If you have less than half of your self-control remaining, you can cancel the following class in favour of going back to bed to recover.

All the details of this update pack can be found on this forum thread.

Enjoy your next class on Teacher Story!
