To date, 1099 out of 1101 points have been accounted for. Where are the last 2 points?

I. Objects Confiscated : 588 pts

I.A Individual Objects: 268 pts
- 5 rare objects * 10 pts each = 50 pts (Kick-ass black pacifier, nebulous spinning top, titanium spinning top, demonic black flea. turbo frog)
- 3 uncommon objects * 5 pts each = 15pts (Compass, set square, luminous spinning top)
- 203 other objects * 1 pt each = 203 pts

I.B. Sets : 320 pts
- 2 "milestones" * 10 pts each = 20 pts (Professionneur for 101+ objects, Kleptoman for 174?+ objects)
- 2 common sets * 5 pts each = 10 pts (My Little Pony, Pink objects)
- 29 other sets * 10 pts each = 290 pts

II. Grades : 420 pts
- 50 pts each for Grades 2, 3, ..., 8 = 350 pts
- 70 pts for reaching Grade 9 for the first time

III. Teacher's Pet Actions : 42 pts
- 2 pts for each of the 21 actions : 1 pt for x3 and 1 pt for x300

IV. Assistants : 28 pts
- 1 pt each for the first 3 assistants
- 5 pts each for the last 5 assistants

V. Mission objectives : 18 pts
- One shots: 1 pt for x1 , 2 pts for x50 , 3 pts for x500 , 3 pts for x5000
- Bonus objectives: 1 pt for x1 , 2 pts for x10 , 3 pts for x200 , 3 pts for x1000

VI. Holiday hats: 3 pts
- 3 hats, 1 pt each

VII. Unknown : 2 pts

TOTAL : 1101 pts, of which 1099 are confirmed
