Big @$$ clock is a timer. When it hits zero, you can play your next class again. Nice and easy, and don't have to question whether it's server time or local time.

Little clock when you mouse-over is the actual time your next class will start (i.e. the actual time the timer will hit zero during), so you can get a peek and know in advance what actual time you can come back to the game (unless you decide to buy off the timer). Results may vary, but this clock appears in my local time to me.
