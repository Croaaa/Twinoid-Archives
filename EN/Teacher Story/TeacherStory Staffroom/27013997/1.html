The XP curve has been documented here.


You'll need at least
- 1 477 XP to reach Grade 2.
- 2 761 more XP to reach Grade 3, or 4 238 XP total.
- 4 712 more XP to reach Grade 4, or 8 950 XP total.
- 7 628 more XP to reach Grade 5, or 16 578 XP total.
- 11 561 more XP to reach Grade 6, or 28 139 XP total.
- approx. 18 903 more XP to reach Grade 7, or 47 042 XP total.


If I increase my average daily earnings to 40 XP, it will take over 3 years to reach Grade 7 without paying. And there are two more grades after that, on exponentially-increasing scales for both the number of XP needed to ascend levels, and the number of levels needed to ascend grades.
