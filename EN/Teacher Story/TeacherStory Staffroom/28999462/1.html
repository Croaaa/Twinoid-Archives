Hacksaw

Class ends after you make your attack on the last turn. Nothing procs after that (whether beneficial, like Exercise or Academic Bomb, or detrimental, such as Uncontrollable Laughter or Chewing), and no student makes any attacks.

Whether this is a good design or not is debatable. On the one hand it sucks when your timed attack doesn't proc and kill your last student. On the other hand, I think it would suck more to be tagged with 3 or 4 laughing students on the last turn as well. And I think something like laughing/chewing/humming students is likely to happen more often than a ticking Academic Bomb.
