There's a hidden gain with the bird. See, it restores you to 3 SC no matter how deep into the hole you are reduced to, so if you are hit with an 8 point attack when you only have 1 or 2 SC, the gain is more like 10 SC, not 3 SC, making the bird cheaper than a ferrier. Mind that A: this is most likely to happen at the higher levels when the attacks are more deadly, and B: still likely on rarer occasions.

On the other hand, having that buffer might buy you that one or two extra turns to get the last student or two up to the minimum requirements to complete the mission before your nervous breakdown is cemented, letting the mission complete (on time) when the breakdown ends. For some people, that buffer is priceless.

As for Eddy, his protection is immeasurable. Used him one class and had 4 students break out in uncontrollable laughter. He blocked roughly a third of the damage they dealt on top of several other direct and indirect attacks throughout the class. The amount of SC he protected me from losing was definitely greater than the amount a single ferrier would have provided me.

Another time I used him, he didn't protect enough damage to equal the gain from a single ferrier, but he did block just enough damage for me to finish the mission and level up to grade 4 without using a ferrier, saving me the 25 cost for a glass at the cost of hiring him for 10 and letting me reach the free SC refill for grading up.
