SoItBegins

Good question. Typically, check how many classes your students need to complete the mission (or finish a secondary objective). You need two SEPARATE +.5s to get any use out of it, so only use it if those two will send you over the top and cut a class off it.

(more than two usually isn't worth it).


If correcting papers isn't useful in that regard, then rest. If your SC is (nearly) full and you don't need to correct papers— or if you just need that teeeeeeny bit more to level up— then share the experience.


TLDR:
1. Correct papers if two sessions (no more) will shave a class off your completion time.
2. If not, rest.
3. If you're full up or about to level up, share!
