Finish a mission as soon as possible, or take a few classes more to get all the bonus pupils' objectives?

Usually I try to finish missions asap, as it seems that gives faster xp (in which case bonus pupils are still usefull as they generally have a higher score). But I would like to hear your opinion on this!

My opinion is as follows:
A) it's a bit of an apples and oranges situation, since most of the time, bonus pupils don't give exp, but rather gold or items for mission completions. If you need/want more items, therefore, they are very useful, but if you care about leveling it might be different.
B) bonus pupils can sabotage your mission (see: no students lower than X, which inevitably gives you a really bad student), or save it (1 B, then recieve a 12/20 student right off the bat).
C) bonus pupils do give bonus turns and targets and classes. if one more turn per class lets you land one more learner, a bonus pupil can add one xp for that, not counting any mission progress you would get through that. on the other hand, if one more target means you can't finish early, it might make you miss out on several exp and a skip discount.

In short, if you want more income/items, bonus students are always useful. otherwise, it's debatable and situational.
