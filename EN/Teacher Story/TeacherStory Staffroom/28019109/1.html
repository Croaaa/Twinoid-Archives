Sorry if there's a thread on this already, couldn't find.

I've been listing all the status and respective skill unlocks with Teacher's Pet.
Here's what I've got so far (help to improve the list):


Clown -> Synergy: The targeted pupil loses 2 (any) if they are defeated, you earn 1 additional XP


Rip Van Winkle -> Iron Will: You gain 1 point of SC


Insolent -> Exclusion: The pupil is excluded. The class can finish without them


Dreamer -> Valium: The targeted pupil becomes harmless


Wimp -> Valium: The targeted pupil becomes harmless


Slow -> Meditation: The targeted pupil gains 1 (shield), you gain 2 SC points


Greedy -> Solace: You gain one SC point every turn. Lasts 3 turns.


Boredom -> Tranquilizer dart gun: The targeted pupils becomes 'soft in the head'.

Clever -> Explanations: The targeted pupil loses 1 (any)


Curious -> Big Bran: The targeted pupils becomes "Highly Attentive'.
