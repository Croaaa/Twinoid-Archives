This game is fairly simple, and is missing a lot of things that I would like, like a budget screen that showed what your income and outflow was, and an inventory that you could load up with cheap farriers. That being said, I actually don't use any items very often. As long as you use solace and rest every class, you can almost break even on self control. The Pet powers are hugely unbalanced, with some being essential, some being so situational that it would be a miracle to be able to activate one at the right time, and annoying to try to get rid of when you are done, and some being massively underpowered, like Iron Will. I would never use that power normally, unless I had no meditate or solace, and even then id be checking the others that are available.
