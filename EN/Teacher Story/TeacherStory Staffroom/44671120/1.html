Any damage buffs only apply to direct attacks (i.e Teach Harder, Solid Explanation, Brainwash, etc.) and unfortunately Exercise and its awesome counterpart Academic Bomb do not count as direct damage. Don't fret if you use them after taking a Twinoid, you keep the buff until you use a direct damage attack.

Also you cannot buff boredom reduction. If Pay Attention! only does 5 Boredom, a pupil with 6 will have one left over, regardless of Twinoid or Mr. Nuke helping. However, something that attacks BOTH like Front of the Classroom (2 direct Stupidity damage, all Boredom removed) or bypasses Boredom can still be buffed to do extra stupidity damage.

Somewhat-easy-thing-to-remember of how the "end-turn-priority-order"-cycles goes something like this:
Player Action / Attack -> !!!End Turn!!! -> Between Turn-effect / Attack ->Student Attack / Action ( This also includes the "Questions". And "pick-up-item / yoink! " gets the priority ) -> New Turn

So in other words, Direct-Player-Action will instantly end the turn, unless there's anything else saying you're allowed to have re-do / continue your turn (like item items, switching seats, etc.).
