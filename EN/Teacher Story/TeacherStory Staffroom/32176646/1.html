Hacksaw

SC does not naturally recover between classes. Only way to recover SC between classes is by either Drinking a Ferrier or by choosing the "take a rest" after class option.

The only exception is Sick Leave (either by taking the option as your after class option or running out of SC during a class) where SC regenerates a certain amount after a set number of hours (both values I don't know). Keeping the window open during this time does not change the outcome of the sick leave, but buying off the timer might.

TradeFederation

There are numerous attacks that prevent SC loss, or regain SC. There are also passive skills that boost SC recovery. If you have and use 2-3 of them, you will be able to 'naturally' recover your SC without budget/sick leave.


Attacks


Life Transfer : +1 SC per turn, for 4 turns, for each student active at the target's table
Solid Explanation : +1 SC for each stupidity damage the attack does, base damage is 3, but twinoids/attentive/highly attentive boost this directly
Language Assistant : Prevents the loss of SC, as long as it is active


Pet Attacks


Meditation : +2 SC, +1 boredom on target
Iron Will : +1 SC
Solace : + 1 SC for 3 turns


Passive Abilities

Fin gourmet level 1,2 : Small powerup of Ferriers, but it stacks with...
Upgraded healing potion level 1 , 2 : Large powerup of Ferriers
Experienced Buyer 1, 2 : makes Ferriers cheaper, which can stack with upgraded Ferriers to save you piggies per SC recovered with Ferriers


Improved rest 1,2,3 : Gives you a lot more SC back each rest between classes


Another thing to note is that attacks that stop negative effects protect your SC, as do attacks that 'lock down' negative statuses, like Individual Work, and avoiding hitting students with 'distracted' status. Exclusion can get rid of students with negative statuses.
