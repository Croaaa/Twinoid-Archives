Hacksaw

Didn't see Synergy at all during grade 1. Had it almost all the time during grade 2. Have had it about 30% of the time so far in grade 3.

As far as the best, they're all situational, some just way more than others.

Usually I'll use synergy, elbow smash, explanations, or big brain when available to deal damage faster. I'll use anticheat almost every time it's available. Exclusion I'll use when I only need to defeat one or two pupils and some high HP punks are just getting in the way. Removal I'll also try and use when it's available and I actually have desks in position to be moved up so that I can fit more students in the first or second row for more score boosts in the class.

If all those options aren't available, then I'll start looking at attacks that either restore SC or limit/neutralize damage received. Also, when available, I'll consider Alzheimer's, since it resets the option to move students between desks (can still only use it once per turn, but it eliminates the 2-turn cool down); also sometimes I don't get an attack I want, and since I have 5 attack options unlocked there's a good chance a quick reset will give it to me.
