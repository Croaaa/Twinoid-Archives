Hacksaw

It's a known effect. If you have a Lovestruck student in your class and they Love Up with another student, when one of them is defeated (or has the Loved Up status removed, IIRC), the other will become Heartbroken, and that state will persist until removed, including being on them at the start of the next class and every class after that until removed.

Not sure if the Call to Order attack will remove it (or The Big Clean Up), but the Cattleprod pet ability will, so long as you have a student with that ability AND it isn't the one Heartbroken. And, as you already know, you can use a sponge.
