Update
  • Every bonus pupil received will give an additional class in which to complete a mission before the deadline.

When you chose your next mission you only see the basic number of classes before the deadline.
As soon as the first lesson begins, the bonus class you get from sent pupils will be included in the total of classes.
If you get another pupil in your second class of the mission you'll gain another class before time runs out.
