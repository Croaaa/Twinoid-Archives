Demonic appears after you reached a certain high level (the last one to appear if I'm not mistaken) but that applies to others as well i.e. you won't see Lovestruck when you're still at level 5. His most powerful attack is written in French, which takes half of your current SC and can't be reduced/resisted (Valium, Soft in the Head, Resist, it doesn't matter; it still gives full damage).

Even though you don't memorize their traits, it' pretty obvious based on their name alone i.e. Violent, Demonic, Noisy are some of the more dangerous pupils compared to Rip Can Winkle, Curious, Super-serious.
