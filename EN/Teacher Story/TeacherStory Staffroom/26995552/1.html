Kompera

What you lose:
Your level, you start again at L1;
All of your skills, although you get to select one of them to start with in the next grade.

What you gain:
A Grade advance;
A gold distinction;
A hat.

You can continue playing in your current Grade, you are not forced to advance to the next Grade. This could be handy if there is a specific skill you'd like to pick up to bring with you to the next Grade but you do not have it yet. Otherwise I'm not sure I see the point, as Grade 2 doesn't seem to be any harder than Grade 1 was.

Kompera

Rigged Clock is probably the best skill around.

It's a win/win.

You either beat the class in the normal amount of time, and therefore gain a +1 EXP for the additional turn in the rewards phase, or you have one more turn to beat an additional student which gains you an additional +1 EXP plus the advancement (+2, +1, +0) of that student.

Win/win
