The closer a student is sitting to the front of the class, the more points they will receive when defeated. A front-row student will get +2, middle-row students get +1, and back-row students get nothing.

So to get your student from 5 to 11 points in the minimum amount of classes, you would want to have them in the front row when defeated, whenever possible.
