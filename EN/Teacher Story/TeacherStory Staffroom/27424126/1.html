Well, for example, if your mission is to get Grade "C" x 5 (that is, getting five pupils scoring 12/20 or better) and you're given 3 classes to accomplish it for 40 , if you don't accomplish your objective by the third class you can keep playing until you accomplish the objective, but your reward is halved to 20 starting from the fourth class.

I'm not exactly sure how the sick leave works after the last update, but if you have a nervous breakdown (SC reaches zero), you need to wait 15 hours before being able to go to a new class. So you can theoretically play without ever using items, but you'll advance more slowly. Which explains why I started playing before you and you have more points and stuff than me by now >_>.
