My approach is to make sure i have at least 5-10 Ferrier drinks (try to get first aid / upgraded healing potion when you can). Then have another 250 coins saved up for the next ferrier buy Or if you feel pretty confident .. start making sure you have 10 sponges and 10 twinoid as well. I find if your smart with your strategy .. you hardly need to use sponges and twinoids. Ideally getting to at least 5-10 for all 3 items. If you stay ahead in ferrier, then you can use XP boost after each class (again preferably if you have the improved rest upgrade)


Now, I use the remaining coins to do more classes per day, preferably at 35 to 45 coins each instead of the usual 50. you have to clear all students in a round to get a reduction usually.

I usually spend not more than 1 Ferrier drink per game. So in order not to reach Nervous Breakdown, I try to make sure that I always able to buy drinks any time I need. I always buy 10 of them as it is the most efficient. "Eperienced Buyer" skill also good here. Also I usually try to preserve 500-1000 coins.

Personally I usually do not use twinoid, and use sponges occasionally. But if you buy it, I would recommend to buy stack of 10 as well.

Also I spend coins for extra classes if it costs 25 or less. If it costs more it is not efficient as per my observations. But it is up you how many coins spend on it.
