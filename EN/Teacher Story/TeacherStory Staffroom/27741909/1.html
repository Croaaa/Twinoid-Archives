Hacksaw

I've held off completing a mission because I knew I could complete a secondary objective with it in the next class. Whether or not it was worth it is debatable, though, since another class means more SC loss which means whatever budget or item you get from taking the extra class is probably not enough to balance out the cost of replenishing the SC.

On the one hand, you get a distinction point for every side objective you complete, so as far as unlocking titles/icons goes, worth it to complete the side objective. On the other hand, the mission XP is the biggest boost you get in the game, so completing missions faster levels you up faster.

So the question in the end is whether you want to level up faster or get all the titles and icons for defeating side objectives faster.
