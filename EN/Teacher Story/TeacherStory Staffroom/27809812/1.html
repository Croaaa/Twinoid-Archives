1. Add gameplay mechanics that are only available to senior players. As the game currently stands, the gameplay is the exact same from Grade 1 to Grade 8, and this gets tedious real quickly. Your missions don't get any harder (the difficulty level is random, and I got my first 4-devil mission in Gr1-Lv3), and your powerups and collect-a-thon objects are also randomly generated. The only difference is that at higher levels, you'll have more SC and a larger library of skills to be randomly drawn from on each turn. NaturalChimie 2 is a tedious multi-year grind, but long-time players are rewarded with teleporters, exclusive recipes, and access to special game modes. If this weren't the case, I would have stopped playing long ago.

2. Real home customization. I'm not expecting Animal Crossing levels of customization, but at least expand this gameplay mechanic so that there are more than ~15 upgrades, they don't arrive in a fixed order, and there are more parameters than simply wall and floor colour. Except for the colours, everyone's home ends up looking the same, with the vast majority of upgrades already received in Grade 1.

Any other ideas?
