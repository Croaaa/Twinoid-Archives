My strategy is to be patient and not pay to get past timers, and abuse things that heal SC. Between classes, I tend to do things like this

  • Active class, have a decent amount of defeated students - Grade
  • Active class, low amount of defeated students or grades are fine - Relax if needed, otherwise XP bonus
  • Between classes - XP bonus, or relax if I'm at less than 8 SC

The reason why you should care about XP bonuses is that you can begin to collect upgrades to fit your playstyle, as you level up. Take a look at the upgrades that the top players have, to see what's available



Having said all that, it's smart to find students with teacher pet actions that help regain SC. Some actions have downsides, and you have to try not to defeat your teacher pet, but those SC bonuses help a lot
