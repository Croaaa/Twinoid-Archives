Jerry000How can i gain more coins? Because every mission i get 1 or 2 breakdowns. And noone wants to register with my referal. Is there any way to get more coins? Beside paying

The best way is to beat the classes fast, so you get some coins for each class survived, and you don't get hurt too much. If you have a breakdown, you will not get any budget for that class. Students sent by friends also can give coin, usually 20-30 each, which can add up.


Another passive option that nets more coin is to choose level up powers that boost your SC, or boost your Ferriers, or recover SC, so you can survive more classes. The more classes you survive, the more coin you will earn normally. Having a breakdown costs you that classes coins, and some time, but you can recover your SC without spending coins, so you should eventually net some coin just plowing through classes, even if you get taken out every so often.


Don't buy the timer, or buy twinoids or sponges. By buying only the Ferriers in 10 packs, you will save up coins eventually, because you should be able to afford the Farriers you need with just the per class coins, and still have some extra.
