Each grade has a cap level (for grade 1, the cap is 12, for example). When you reach that cap level a button will appear below the mission window that'll allow you to go on vacation and begin a new school year.

As for the pupils, as there is no option to decline all pupils (yet), only way to keep from getting them is to go into your settings (as noted above) and set them to friends only, and possibly unfriend any "friends" who play this game and send you pupils.
