On losing all SC control. First, congratulations on never getting to that point! I did it twice before I really "caught on" to the game.

Losing all Self Control (SC) results in a nervous breakdown. There is a 7 hour delay in between classes normally. If you suffer a breakdown you will have to wait 13 hours, during which you will regain 1 SC per hour.

Both the normal timer and the breakdown timer can be skipped by paying coins (called budget).

==

If you refuse a request to go to the bathroom the student becomes Angry. Angry causes them to viciously attack you in the next turn. I haven't allowed a student to do this to me, my guess would be that they will do more damage with that attack.
