I think it's highly dependent on your current abilities, I did some quick math and Farriers seem to be up to 1.5 times more effective, considering you maxed out their healing effectiveness and cost reduction. Without putting any points into those abilities it's probably more or less the same.

However the main reason I think this method is bad is because of the attack that halves your SC points. You could potentially lose 30+ right after coming out of sick leave so it's advised to keep your SC points as low as possible (not to the point it becomes dangerous) and that's where Farriers come in handy, since they don't heal you full. Not to mention you could use the gold you save this way to skip classes.

Still it's an interesting experiment and congratz for reaching the end this way.
