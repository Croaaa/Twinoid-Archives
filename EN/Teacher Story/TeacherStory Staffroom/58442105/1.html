Hiring Tippex gives a decent boost to Critical Hit chance (To roughly a 1/6 chance, if no other boosts are in play), but he only works one row at a time and moves each turn (FrontMidBackFront). I'd still only recommend it on when students don't need a specific setup (Outside of being in a particular row.)

More usefully, Eureka status guarantees a critical on the next hit on that student, but is only obtained if the option appears when the student raises his/her hand.
