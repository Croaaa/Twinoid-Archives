Example:

Score 'A' x 1
50

Student A 11/20
Student B 10/20
Student C 10/20
...blah blah blah

Class 1 ends:
Student A 11/20 -> 13/20
Use +0.5 option
Student A 13/20 -> 13.5/20

Class 2 ends:
Student A 13.5/20 -> 15.5/20
Use +0.5 option
Student A 15.5/20 -> 16/20

Mission complete!
Gain +4 SC or +4

And you now have gained 50 in 2 classes. You could have chosen not to use the +0.5 option and gain more SC/XP but you also end up taking another class to finish the mission and thus lower XP/class. Of course, if you don't care about maximising XP/class or you're really low on Farriers and budget, it might be better to rest instead and take the extra class. However, this is a (common) example of how the +0.5 option could be useful.

Also, if you have Helping Hand or Educational Flick, it makes it possible where you only need to use the +0.5 option once and you would literally have nothing to lose.
