Flick a student in the back row: +1.5 score
Flick a student in the middle row: +2.5 score
Flick a student in the front row: +3.5 score

Bonuses from anticheat, raising their hand, and/or Mr. Dwane also apply and can boost the score up to +6.5 in total.
