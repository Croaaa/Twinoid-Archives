I think that Teacher Story is a good game but the game really needs balancing. I understand you are making these games to make money but what I am going to say will actually improve user interaction than reduce it.

Before I get into the core of what I am going to say I would like to point out that you are monetizing the wrong. When designing a game you should develop the core game first and then add on payment stuff afterwards. Right from the get go the core of the game is about making money and the game afterwards with the lack of HP recovery, long waiting periods and locked skills.

I understand that the in game cash system kinda fixes some of these below but I believe it is not enough to make the game what it can really be.

1. Waiting period: The first thing I would like to point out is that the time limit for each class is wayyy too high. I read in one of your logs it used to be 7 hours and that totally kills user interaction. I would suggest dropping it down to at least 1 hour. This way people can play more in the day and would do stuff like be forced to buy healing items and unlock slots or go on sick leave. Getting more fun for a shorter time to get them hooked.

If someone just plays a round and its like "oh you need to wait 5 hours" most people will go away and forget about it.

2. Healing / Damage: Right now it feels like I take too much damage or I am not healing enough after a class. Having the ability to only heal 4 hp after a class is really bad and forces people to buy a Glass of Farries (as much as I know that is your goal). Its not the fact that in the end we would need to buy that Farrie but the fact there is no way to sustain unless we were really really lucky and got that bonus to resting recovery.

3. Downtime: Right now there is a clear lack of things to do during downtime. You should put in some kind of event to do while we are waiting for our next class because that will retain people's attention and give them a change from the normal game. I would also like to point out that there is little reason to do anything else but rest because of the reason above (not able to rest enough) that you can never take a turn to do any of the other actions.

4. Battle system: When i played the battle the first time I really enjoyed it. It is a mashup of a RPG and stategy game. I would like to see a bit more into the mix like a few new skill (for both myself and for the kids) and some other kind of elements that can happen like maybe a thunderstorm where you need to move the kids away from the window or it is raining and the roof is leaking.

That is all for now, please give the devs your feedback and let them know if you agree with me or not!
