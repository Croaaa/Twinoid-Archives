Kompera

Ok, pupil sent. No need to send one back, but be aware that you can send 3 per day, so why not use that resource? If you need people to swap pupils with, check out the sticky thread "Send me pupils! v1". There you will find people who both need pupils and who can send them back to you.

Just be aware that they can be a mixed blessing. You will earn +1 EXP for beating the additional pupil, but the additional pupil can prevent you from beating the entire class.

They will come with a Teacher's Pet ability which might be very useful, but they can also attack you (but if it wasn't them, it would be some other pupil).

They can help a "X number of Y score pupils" mission by coming in with a high starting score, or they can hurt a minimum score mission by coming in with a low starting score.

All in all I think the advantages well outweigh the disadvantages, but that's just my opinion.
