Leapfrog

When you start a new grade you begin again from level one, and you can relearn old skills when they're available to choose from.

In addition, when you finish a grade you are allowed to choose one skill (out of those you have learned) to keep permanently. This will remain with you forever, no matter how many times you advance to a new grade and revert in level.
