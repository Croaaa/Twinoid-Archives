Clear your flash cache and see if that works for you.

Typically they are under:

C:\Users\<userID>\AppData\Roaming\Macromedia\Flash Player\macromedia.com\support\flashplayer\

C:\Users\<userID>\AppData\Roaming\Macromedia\Flash Player\

Note that those may not be your exact paths. You can use the Find function to help you locate where these caches exist on your computer. Search for 'flash' or 'flashplayer', or flash player'.

Good luck if you're not reasonably computer literate. A reboot might help you in that case, or it might not. Sorry to be vague, but that's the situation as it stands.
