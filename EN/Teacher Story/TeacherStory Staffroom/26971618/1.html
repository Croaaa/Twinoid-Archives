Another way to not lose coins is to not pay to win. You actually don't need to use the coin items besides the Farrier, and even that is only needed to avoid an occasional breakdown, which only costs you an additional 6 hours to recover, if you don't use the Farrier. This game is fairly easy, but it does have a huge random element that can throw off even a paying player. If you don't get upset at getting half EXP on some of the missions, you likely don't need to worry about coin at all.


If you want to play continuously, you will probably have to pay real money to skip the timers, no matter how good you are, because the per class payoff is too low to cover the cost, even with some bonus student quests.
