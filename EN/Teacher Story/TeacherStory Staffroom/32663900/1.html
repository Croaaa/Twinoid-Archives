Kompera

k0l3 listed the primary benefits and drawbacks. Less impacting are:

Benefit
- The extra EXP for defeating more pupils
- Slightly greater chance to have one of the better Teacher's Pet abilities such as Anticheat
- If you complete the friends' pupil mini-mission you earn extra items/EXP/Budget

Drawback
- High potential to take more turns to beat the entire classroom, which costs EXP and clock time (or Budget if you choose to skip the clock), and more turns means more attacks, which costs Self Control
- Slight chance to bring in a Pupil with one of the more annoying attacks
- This is entirely choice based, but you might expend more Sponges/Ferrier/Twinoid if you have a large number of pupils in the class


Like k0 I tend to select missions which are of the '3 X Score pupils' type rather than minimum score missions. And I rarely have an issue defeating the entire class. So I prefer the additional pupils.

Kompera

I have one level of Rigged Clock, so I have an extra turn to beat the last pupil. I don't often beat the class by more than 3 turns, and very often it's on the very last turn.

I try to plan the class out in advance, including TP ability uses (and I prefer those that do damage, I have 2 levels of Improved Rest for SC recovery) and their cool downs, swap seat uses, and item uses. This planning lets me use an item tactically to inflict a lot of damage where it counts early, such as a Twinoid plus Teach Harder, and the column has 3 pupils with 4 or less , or a Twinoid plus Let's Get Serious at a table with 2 pupils with 5 or less . I think this results in less frantic item use near the end of the class when the clock is expiring and the pupils are all scattered about, and less item use overall for the same result.

And while at the beginning of this Grade I was only using an item every 1-3 classes or so (I don't keep a log), now that I'm on L17 it's more like an item per class.

But I just finished a class with 2 Lovestruck pupils who never triggered, so I considered that a huge win for me, since when that happens it requires heavy Sponge use to beat the class. Both have Anticheat and I find it faster to select one as the TP, use Anticheat, beat that pupil, and then the cool down to select a new TP is shorter than the cool down for Anticheat.
