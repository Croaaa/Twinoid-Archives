When a pupil raises their hand there is always a chance you can regain SC from it. Sometimes it's one of the options avaiable from the "question" they ask and sometimes it's the only option.

Way it works when it's the only option is one of two ways:

  • If you aren't wearing a hat they'll say, "You're my favorite teacher."
  • If you are wearing a hat they'll say, "I like your hat."
