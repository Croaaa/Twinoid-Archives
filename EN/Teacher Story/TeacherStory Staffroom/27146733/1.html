Hacksaw

It's possible to get a score of 20/20 on a student with a main objective of 16/20 x 1, but it requres a specific skill set to do it.

First you need to get the pupil up to a score of 15.5/20 in a prior class.
In the final class, you need to defeat the pupil in the front row for +2.
You'll also need the pet ability Antiwag cast on the pupil for +1.
Finally, you have two available options:
Option A: the pupil needs to raise their hand and "understand" for +1 as well as defeat the pupil with the level-up skill "Helping Hand" for +0.5 or "Flick" for +1.5 to get a total +4.5 or +5.5 respectively for the class( and in the case of "Flick" you could have the pupil raised to only 14.5/20 from a prior class)
Option B: you must have "flick" unlocked and defeat the pupil with it for the +1.5 point bonus and the grand total of +4.5 for the class.

So, yeah, it's unlikely, but not impossible.

I've never had a problem with the extra students. Feel free to "attack" me with additional students at any time!

I usually select a mission which is to get a small amount of students to a fairly high score, and the player sent students usually require a fairly high score to win their secondary quest reward, so there's a synergy there. And for whatever random reason, those missions have always been the highest EXP payoff, so they are more attractive even without additional students.

If you keep your queue filled up then the player sent students arrive in the second turn of the first and second classes. If your queue isn't full then I guess you can be surprised by a student arriving on the last class, but I've never had that happen to me. There's an easy solution there, just make some friends and keep your queue full. Three students sent per day should get you enough return students to keep the queue at maximum.
