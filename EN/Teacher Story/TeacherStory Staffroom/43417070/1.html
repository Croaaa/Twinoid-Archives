npfan

Not missions early, classes early. Normally, it costs 50 to skip the 5-hour wait and start a new class. If you defeat the class with turns leftover, skipping the timer costs 5 less for every turn leftover from the previous class. This discount stacks with the 5 discount you get for every hour of the 5-hour wait you waited for. Skipping the timer can never cost less than 10 no matter how many turns you had leftover and how many hours of the 5-hour wait you waited. Don't go too crazy spending on classes, or you may not have enough to buy farriers or other items when you need them!

norby89

Anything that conserves SC. Out of active skills Life Transfer is the best, Language Assistant maybe Solid Explanation. Also try to get Lv. 3 in Improved Rest, with it you won't need to use as many Farriers unless you get unlucky with the student attacks. Upgraded Healing potion is also great, optionally you could get some points in Upgraded Resistance and/or First Aid, Experienced Buyer. They don't help that much but it's still better than nothing.

Other than that try to beat the class as fast as possible, not only will it cost lest to skip the timer allowing you to play more turns but you will have to endure less student attacks. Sometimes it's better to end the class as quickly as possible so you can skip the timer and play an extra turn than to wait for that Finger Flick to end the mission faster. More turns = more gold / XP.
