Update of 18/07/2013

Proceeding to the next class:
* Waiting time has been reduced from 7 to 5 hours.
* Every hour of waiting time reduces the cost of proceeding to the next class immediately by 5 .
* If you teach a perfect class, the prices of progressing to the next class immediately is reduced by 5 for every unused turn, with a minimum price of 10 .


Sending pupils:
* Pupils can only be sent to your friends.
* In addition to an extra turn in classes, every bonus pupil gives you an additional class to help complete your mission in time.
* When you send a pupil it is also possible to send an object from your inventory.


Sick Leave and Nervous Breakdowns:
* Sick leave generally has a fixed duration of 15 hours, instead of 1 hour per self-control point required (in order to reduce the waiting time for teachers with lots of self-control).
* If you have half of your self-control or less remaining between classes, you can also "take sick leave": Your next class will be replaced by sick leave so that you can regain your self-control points little by little.
Sick leave can be stopped at any time, allowing you to go to the next class, with the self-control you've already recovered. The cost of proceeding to the next class is reduced in the usual way during the first 5 hours ( 5 per hour).
* In the event of a nervous breakdown, it is possible to "Get up" to restore all your self-control.

Update of 09/07/13:

Assistants:

* Need a hand in class? The school have staff to help: discover 8 assistants which can be unlocked by collecting objects.
* Before each class, choose 1 assistant from the available staff. Every assistant comes with you to class and lets you use their special power!


Collectible Objects:

* The pupils are back to school with their pockets stuffed with all-new trendy objects. it's up to you to confiscate them all.
* More than 70 new objects to discover!
* Your friends can help you to discover new rare objects!


Pupil sending system redesign:

* Pupils can once again be sent to anyone you choose, and
* We've added the option to only allow your Twinoid friends to send you them.
* You have a search box which will allow you to find your friends quickly.


And more still:
* New titles, new smileys, new icons to collect! Also, loads of prefixes and suffixes are there to be discovered throughout the year.
* Some new furniture to fulfil your home decoration needs!
* Loads of in-game texts have been added - you might even learn a thing or two!
* New option: It's now possible to start your school year again from scratch
* Lots of reported bugs have been fixed.
