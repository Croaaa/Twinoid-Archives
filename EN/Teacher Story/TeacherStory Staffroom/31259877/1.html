Thanks for the clarification.

I think your research is missing something that you can never really know. The EXP you gain from completing a mission should not be listed as a simple +EXP figure. That ignores the fact that a high EXP mission which takes a long time to complete actually earns you less EXP over time than a low EXP mission which you can knock out quickly.

As an example:

If it takes you 6 turns to complete a 60 EXP mission, you've earned 10 EXP per turn. If it takes you 2 turns to complete a 40 EXP mission, you've earned 20 EXP per turn. Double the EXP over time, even though the mission completion figure is 33% lower.

And there's no real way to determine how many turns it would have taken you to complete the missions you bypassed in your selection process.
