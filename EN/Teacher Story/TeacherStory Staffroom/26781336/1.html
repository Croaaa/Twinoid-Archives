Kaderie

Students defeated at the front row gain +2, middle row +1 and back row gains nothing. Also there's at least one skill I know of that is extremely useful for this called 'Educational Flick' it does 1 dmg but if the pupil is defeated by it it gains +1.5 scores regardless where he sits, ie. front row students defeated by this gain +3.5 total score at the end of that class.

I think there are also negative status effects that prevent students from gaining scores at all, but that's as simple as removing them before defeating the student

Hacksaw

There's also a skill called Helping Hand you can unlock on level up that deals 3 damage and raises the student's score by +0.5 if the attack defeats them, and I know I've seen some status effects pop up on a student that will give them +1 score when defeated.

Also, if you correct papers between classes every student you defeated last class gets +0.5 score.
