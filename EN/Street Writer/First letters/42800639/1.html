What does "recharge" power actually do?
On december 2, 2014
SummerMcLovin

As its description says, it automatically recharges once you have finished that game. All the other powers either have to be recharged (all three uses) with gold or through the slot machine (only one use) at the end of another game. Even if you have no "real" powers available, you can always have three free games going.
