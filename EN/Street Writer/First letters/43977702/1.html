Sometimes it is irritating that letters are too random, and a might get a hand with awful " Z Q W Y V V I I " and best words someone can get either WIZ or IVY.

so I thought why not having a space of letters that the game draws from, to skip some complications and make stuff easier I think a space of 1000 letters would be resonable, even though a game would not use more than 200 + few jokers, but there is recycle that might get in the way...

so to decide how much each letters gets, I wiki-ed at "Letter frequency"

and got this result for a space of the 1000:
E= 127, T= 90, A= 81, O= 75, I= 69, N= 67, S= 63, H= 60, R= 59, D= 42, L= 40, C= 27, U= 27, M= 24, W= 23, F= 22, G= 20, Y= 19, P= 19, B= 14, V= 9, K= 7, J= 1, X= 1, Q= 1
Z= 1, the total here is less than 1000 since mostly skipped rounding.

for rounding and grouping similar frequent letters this result could be better:
E = 130
T = 90
A = 80
O, I= 70
N, S = 65
H, R = 60
D, L = 40
C, U, M, W, F = 25
G, Y, P, B = 20
V, K = 10
J, X, Q, Z = 2

the joker probably should not be taking from this letter space, however, the recycle would return letter from hand and draw randomly from the letter space, also theft from someone who finished the game, would substitute the hand of the player who finished with 8 random letter from the letter space -as it does now with random letters, or last hand not sure- (even thought I believe that theft should not work with people with 0 letters on hand no mater if they finish or did not finish the game).

hopefully this would be helpful in lowering the chance of getting some terrible hands, and increase the chance of writing long words .
