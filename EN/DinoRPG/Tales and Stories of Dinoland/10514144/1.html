Welcome to the section "Dinomal Planet", where zoologists -I mean dinologists- tell to the world the marvelous types of dinoz categorized by behavior, structure, physiology, classification, and distribution.

If you want to share the studies you have made by analyzing the nature of your dinoz while traveling along the different places that the Dinoland Planet offers, here you can publish those discoveries that have made you an expert in dinology. Simply perform the following steps:

1- In the forum's section "Tales and Stories of Dinoland" create a new topic by the name of the dinoz you want to start talking following by the mark " - Dinomal Planet" e.g. Wanwan - Dinomal Planet

2- Then start the topic by coping and pasting the dinoz's description given by the game. You can take them from here: Dinodex
e.g. Wanwan were once considered man's best friend, before they returned to the wild. Wanwan are vivacious, able to make multi-assaults, and their long, sharp teeth are deadly. Their fur conducts electricity, enabling them to store lightning and discharge when they choose.

3- Your done now, and thank you for participating ; now let's wait until someone else share his/her knowledge about the dinoz, in this case wanwans.


4- You can post again after some other master has posted i.e. no double posting (and please don't use more than 3 lines per post, don't make it too long). e.g.

Master A : Wanwas were man's best friend until they got along with Gorilloz. These two kinds of dinoz formed an excellent team for traveling to undiscovered places like the Grumhel Forest.

Master B: Once being in the forest, wanwas became wild and started to grow agile bodies for hunting purposes.

Master A: Wanwas couldn't survive much longer in the forest due to an abrupt regimen that Gorilloz constituted to conquer the Grumhel Forest just for them.

5- Last but not least, Have fun reading, composing, and posting
