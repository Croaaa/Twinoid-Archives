Greetings Dinoz Masters!

The Cult of Gorgoth is accepting new sacr--supplicants into its ranks! From our humble home deep in the Grumhel Forest, we operate an underground network of mischievous Korgons that steal your underpants, cookies and milk. Come be a part of the treachery today!

CoG is a medium-tier competitive clan. We emphasize friendliness and maturity above all. Event participation is a main focus and we pursue success through strategy, ingenuity and well-trained coordination.

For a bit of history, we are a relatively new clan founded around Halloween of last year. Our roots run a bit deeper, though, since CoG is a fusion between Death's Children and the d'Irma Initiative, two of the older clans on the server. Drawing from this background, we have the good fortune of sharing a distinctive vision and philosophy, and an experienced membership that contributes to a fun and progressive clan atmosphere.

CoG was created as a fresh start with the knowledge gained from our previous incarnations in mind. We wanted to create a clan name and theme that had something to do with the game, and would be recognizable to most players. Thus, the cult of Grumhel Forest was born.

So if you'd like to try your luck with this tongue-in-cheek/occasionally-serious troop of demon worshipers, check out our pages today and see if you'd fit with our theme, aspirations and atmosphere!

Minimum requirements to join include at least 3 well-trained dinoz at level 35 or above. All inquiries should be directed to recruitment leader Sithis.
