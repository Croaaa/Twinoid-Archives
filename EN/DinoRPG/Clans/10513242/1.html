Once again, Mystery of Angels is rebuilding. We have a good Treasury, and will soon complete the Clinic. We will use those assets for the enjoyment of our members.

We welcome applications from anyone who wants the opportunity to take part in the Championship of the Clans or the Clan Wars. To give our members the best access to those, we will set our ambitions, and the level that we play at, so that as many can take part as possible.

We recognise that there are people who cannot log in every day, or who do not wish to spend money, but who want to have fun and still make good progress. We do not make claims that we will be the strongest Clan; we would like to offer advice and help you to develop.

We would like you to be active in our Clan Forum, and join in the discussions over the future of the Clan. If you have the Stamps, you can send mails to other members, but it is not essential. You choose how active you wish to be.

Check out our Clan Page (we are changing those ) or mail me if you have any questions.
