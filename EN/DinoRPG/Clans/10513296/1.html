First of all here are our new requirements. If you dont meet all of these, dont worry, just message Rusty or Myself and im sure we can work something out for you.

-Must be an active member (gets on at least once every 3 days) Non-active members will be dismissed, unless they have a reason e.g going on a holiday.

-Must participate in clan wars and events of such

-45+ points

-At least one lv15 dino

-Must be active on our clan forums

-No double accounts


As a member of The Rangers, you will get privileges that must other clans dont give you.
For example, everyone in our clan can:
-Modify the announcements
-Add Clan pages
-Modify Private pages

Thats not to mention the other privileges that loyal rangers unlock over time.

Also feel free to benefit from a fully complete clinic, Our Irma potion test results and our Dojo stratigys.

Think thats pretty cool? It gets better. If you do well in a war or COTC then you can have your name immortalized in our Hall of Fame and the best get a line art made by our clan leader!
