Are you a young stockbreeder? do you want to create a clan? here some councils:

the clan:

- Before creating your clan, ensure that some people will be able to join you! (without creating topic of recruitment, since the clan does not exist yet)
- Choose a name runs, avoid putting a sentence like name of clan. It is necessary that the name is easy to retain. Do not choose a name which has a bond with a movement of mode, because the modes do not last a long time! But choose a certain topic all the same
.
- At the beginning of your clan, do not put conditions of too high entries, wait to be more advanced in the classification for that

the decoration

- Ensure that a rather good stockbreeder in decoration will be able to join you, because the decoration is something of very important to recruit!
- Not to overload not your pages of small images, that can be very irritating.
- remain in a rather sober decoration, do not put
too many colors.

Presentation and pages:

- If your clan have a “history”, do not put too much text after, punctuate the account of some images to illustrate your remarks.
- Do not copy the pages of other clans, especially if they did not give you the authorization! it is very badly seen.
- Do not create too many pages, try to gather them when the subjects are dependant.
- Made a simple presentation, but which explains the maximum of things. conditions of recruitment, chiefs, ranks etc…
- Do not create or do not put pages basing itself on are delirious personal, little of people will understand and that could straightforwardly be heavy.
- A bit of Role Play adds a more serious key to the clan.
- remain in the topic of your clan, do not make irrelevant subjects!

The communication:

The image of the clan on the forum of Dinorpg and the internal forums of the clans is very important:

- When your clan is created, decorated etc… made a topic with recruitment in the part “Clan” of the forum. Make a rather long message (but not too) summarizing the conditions of entries in the clan, environment, the ranks, made the maximum to satisfy the reader!
- Made speak about your clan, try alliances with known clans, that will allow you to make known yourselves.
- Be always polite on the forum and by private Message, because a clan having an impolite chief/member will have a bad reputation!
- Certainly, recruitment by private message is rather effective, but do not go until badgering with the stockbreeders, if they said not, it is not!

this topic was created to help the young creators of clan who do not have much experiment in the field.
