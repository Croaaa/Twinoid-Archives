8. English speaking
It's an english version of Dinorpg, so the forum has to be in english.

Posts written in other languages will be removed, please use the private messages (via the section "Mail") or your clan's forum for other languages.

The sanctions for using other languages are:
3 days.
2nd offence: 1 week
3rd offence: 2 weeks
subsequent offences: 1 month.
