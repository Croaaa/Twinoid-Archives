Vengeance is an active, medium-tier, English clan. We placed in the Top 20 of the last Mana War and reached the Amateur League finals in the last CotC.

Our requirements for prospective members are listed below:
-At least one Dinoz with the Blacksylva Key
-At least one team of three Dinoz around Level 30 or higher
-Be willing to help fill the Treasury for use in events
-Be willing to participate in clan events
-Be able to read and write in English to a reasonable level
If you are experienced but new to this server and are building your account, exceptions can definitely be made.

If you meet these requirements and would like to join, put in an application and send a PM to SummerMcLovin.taken from our Welcome Page

Although we are an English-speaking clan, our members come from many different countries and backgrounds so you could easily have a lot in common with someone interesting.

We produced some good results in the two events we actively took part in and aim to continue doing so in the future. If you have teams of Dinoz raring for some clan event action, consider us as your home. There are many more teams with friendly masters ready to fight beside you. Dedicated gatherer Dinoz are also appreciated, as a Mana War can't be won without a considerable Treasury.

As for clan assets, we have a completed Clinic and Karimbao Tower for easy access to Nimbao.
