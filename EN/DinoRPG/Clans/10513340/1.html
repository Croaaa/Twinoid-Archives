Sanctuary - The Legend -

Once upon a time, there was a sanctuary...
A sanctuary, a peaceful place where dino's would go to feel comfort, to memorize their lost friends and to take shelter in times of war.
The Sanctuary was a holy place, believed to build by the Holy Epoq himself. It possessed many defensive charms to protect all who were inside from anyone malevolent.
Until one day...
There was an evil creature, Xyst, said to have come straight from the Abyss. This creature was known for its unsatisfiable bloodlust, killing everything on its path. Every hero that tried to fight him found a horrible death.
One day, this creature and its army reached the Sanctuary. Evil as it was, Xyst was eager to bring down this Sanctuary, and as he wasn't able to break through the defensive charms his intrigue in it became even bigger. How was it possible that he could be stopped? What power would be great enough to stop him? And most interesting: How to use this power.
He called for his Shaman Thade to undo this magic. Thade tried everything he knew, but nothing worked.
Except...
There was one curse, a curse so dangerous that Thade never dared to perform it before. But Xyst forced him to do it.
Thade gathered the ingredients needed for this curse and in the middle of the night, under the bright shining full moon, he casted the spell.
Flames in the forms of demonic creatures escaped his bowl and for a few seconds the light was so bright that it was day again. The power of this curse could be felt all over dinoland and the noise was so heavy that many thought the Big All Hot was about to erupt.
The flames destroyed the Sanctuary's shield and then took control over everything inside.
It is said that Xyst begged for his life as an infant possessed dino viciously murdered him.
Since that day hardly anyone has entered the Sanctuary again. Those who did came back insane, though people say you have to be insane to even enter there to begin with.
It is said that the power of the former Sanctuary is still somewhere inside these ruins, but the place is haunted by evil. The screams of the dino's that were once massacred echo through every hall.
But still...
It seems some clan is brave... or rather insane enough to try and find this hidden power.
Now led by a lunatic called Luiaard this clan resides in the ruins of this once great building.
They claim that except for some small but unexplainable events they don't really feel anything odd.
But each day, the bloodlust of Xyst is seen more and more in their eyes, a hunger for sacrifices that has been seen on their battlefields, and a general insanity that distinguishes them from any other clan in dinoland.
So... next to all other questions one most important remains:
Are you brave enough to enter the Sanctuary?
