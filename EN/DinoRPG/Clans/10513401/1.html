Hey there

I didn´t find a topic about us here so I decided to create one!

Now that the great Mana War is over, we, the German Dino Jungle
are recruiting new members again!

We´re known for our hard but fair fights in all the Events epoq throws at us. We already got some hardcore gamers in our rows. Examples are Bengeldorn, Sotho and many others just look at our members list, you might recognize some familiar names.

We are a strong community, doing a lot of internal stuff like clan intern events, helping each other talking about everything related and not related to Dino-RPG and other games in our clan chat and I´m sure, if you decide to join us you might find some new friends in our rows.

Our requirements are still not high, considering our position in the current and former event rankings because we rely on the development of our members. Therefore the requirements to join us for now are still the following:

1) We are a german speaking clan, therefore you should have the ability to read and write at least some german.
(beeing from Wisconsin might count as well )

2) Login and play regularly

3) Have the ability to donate 1000 of ingredients monthly.

4) Have at least 5 Dinoz and 70 Points

5) Participate in our clan life. Take part in clan events, enjoy server events together with us, talk with us in our forum and the clan chat and most important of all:

6) HAVE FUN PLAYING DINO-RPG!

Well, I think that should be enough for now!


If you´re interested in joining us, just send a message to: Monaly, Dennomon, Bengeldorn, Sotho or me.
We´re looking forward to having you here!
