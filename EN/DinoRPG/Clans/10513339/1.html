dont waste the levels lol...there are many tiers in this game...the first tier is 3 skills, 2nd tier is 6 skills, 3rd tier is 12 skills at the very LEAST.

I made the mistake with my first dinoz to follow one tier for strong skills first (like vacuum disc), but once the skill is reached I then have to move back to another tier and level again.

This is am example: agility, (unlock), dodge, (unlock), vacuum disc, mistral, (unlock), tornado, (unlock), vaporous form ....following this example I used 10 levels in air to reach this point...

Same example: agility, mistral, (unlock), tornado, dodge, (unlock), vacuum disk, vaporous form....following this example I used 8 levels of air to reach this point...

Simply said...get ALL skills from the lower teirs before moving onto a higher tier..if you do not then you only end up wasting unlocking upper tiers with useless leveling...
