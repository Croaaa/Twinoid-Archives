It takes some legwork because you have to track down dino numbers from individuals' account pages, but once you've got those links saved up, you can disable scales at no cost.

There is a possible mitigation, which is to un-equip your scales between fights and only equip them immediately prior to using them. Also, if magic items are not allowed in IDT qualification fights for the upcoming tournament, one presumes that this exploit will not be possible during that time.
