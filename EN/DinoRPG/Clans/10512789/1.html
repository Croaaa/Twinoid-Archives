There are skills for dinoz that enables them to collect ingredients in some places of Dinoland.

This ingredient can be sold to the Itinerant Merchant or given to your clan.

After that, the clan keep in his treasure all the donated ingredients. With a certain amount of them, the clan will built the castle, and improvements for it.
