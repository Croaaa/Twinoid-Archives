The Jedi Order is opening our Temple doors for recruitment. We have some simple requirements, but the most important quality we look for in a potential member is regular activity within our clan forum and participation in clan events, and, of course, regular training and improvement of your dinoz.

http://en.dinorpg.com/clan/520#?page=455 6

We are a long established clan with many friendly and knowledgeable players of many different ages and backgrounds. Whether you are an experienced player of high skill or a relatively newer player who is still learning the nuances of the game, this is a clan that can suit you. So, will you join the Jedi Order and help bring balance to the Force, and peace to the galaxy? We hope that you will! To join our clan, visit our page and apply! If you have any questions, or are not sure if you meet the requirements, please feel free to contact one of the members of our esteemed High Council, Bomb11 or Bubbles9, or our fearless leader, the powerful Jedi AylaSecura.
