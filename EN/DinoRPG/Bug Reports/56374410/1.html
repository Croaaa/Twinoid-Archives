Those of you, that have a Cube, accidently equipped a magic item on the wrong dinoz, or equipped the wrong magic item on a dinoz, know the pain of waiting 5 days to use the dinoz or magic item as intended.

I was wondering about, how we keep the 5 days rule, but have an option to circumvent it at a cost.

See here is my idea:
Somewhere in Dinoland you have a new or allready existing NPC that unequips a magic item of the dinoz, that starts the dialog with that NPC.
Unequipping a magic item shouldn't be free, and I was thinking about having the cost based on the remaining time, the dinoz would have to wait until the magic item could be unequipped.
Possible formulas for could be:
a) 1000 gp × days left [hours rounded up] - max cost would be 5000 gp
b) hours left × 50 gp [minutes rounded up] - max cost would be 6000 gp
c) minutes left × 1 gp [second rounded up] - max cost would be 7200 gp

For example, you have you have 75h 25m (and some seconds) left, before you can unequipp a magic item from a dinoz:
a) 4 days × 1000 gp = 4000 gp
b) 76 h × 50 gp = 3800 gp
c) 4526 m × 1 gp = 4526 gp

That way you have to decide for yourself, if you're willing to pay for the option to be able to unequipp now or wait a some time to unequip for a cheaper price, or don't pay at all and wait the full 5 days.

Now you might wonder how the option to unequip magic items sooner as it is now could effect events?

Since your dino would have to move to the NPC and start a dialog, Mana-War wouldn't be affected very much.
If you loose a dino during a fight, you won't be able to do anything with it until the war is ended.
On the other hand, if you loose dinoz from your main attack team, you could have the option, to adjust the magic items of your remaining dinoz at a cost.

If I remember correctly, when your dinoz dies during CotC, you can revive it directly, but not field it on the battle field anymore.
Of course those, that would be willing to pay the costs would have an advantage.
To prevent this, the NPC cold deny its service, as long as the battle is ongoing.

The third event we have is Inter Dojo Tournament. Here the service could be denied to dinoz that participate in the tournament.

I believe having this option as I described it, with its restrictions during events, wouldn't effect the game balance too much the way.

The NPC or the option could be unlocked by a quest, so that we also could have some new content.

I know suggestions from this server aren't much looked on by the devs, so if you have a french account and like the idea, you could share it with the french community.
