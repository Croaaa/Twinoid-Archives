Sometimes you have to look around and you could have seen what you ask is already existing...

There's skills raising how many health points you can get by resting per hour, like Cocoon, Regeneration and Priest, the latter being a U-skill. That means your dinoz could have from 2 to 4hp/h rest.

There's also 3rd spherical skill Phoenix Heart, which raise maximum hp gain through rest up to 65%.

But frankly, you only need to group your dinoz (3-man), to buy more dinoz (up to 18/21) to get more gold and rest normally, don't buy any items either. And then you will see you can save gold.
