I have played this game since the first of July 2009. Many clan wars, two cotc and a lot of idt.

All form of PvP is nice, but the total randomness in PvP fights need to change. It not that Im whining (mby a little) but the luck factor in the PvP fights are way to high to even be considered as fighting system at all. Sure, luck goes both ways. Sometime Im lucky while my opponent is unlucky and vice versa.

I had a little experiment to see how high the luck factor is. So I had 30 fights vs another clan member. I used a lvl 39 Rocky and battled vs a well built lvl 25 Rocky. If there was another mechanic based more on lvl, skills and spheres the outcome should be that the higher lvl Rocky with more powerful skills and spheres should win at least 95% of the fights.

So 30 fights lvl 39 Rocky vs lvl 25 Rocky ended in 11 wins for the lvl 25 Rocky. So much lower lvld Rocky with less skils and less spheres almost won half the fights due to a high luck factor or a high bad luck for the lvl 39 Rocky.

The luck factor should not have such a high impact on the game. Some fights are like flipping a coin. Just a little thought to make the game more solid
