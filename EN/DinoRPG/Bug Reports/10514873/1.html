I agree, the mechanics are a bit difficult. Us mid-smaller level clans have to overcome many disadvantages. While the stronger clans can go after dependable mid-level targets and afford to skip clans that may potentially go to 0 rep (since they can grab them quickly at the end when needed), a smaller clan might be only left with those weaker targets. They are also forced to start the war earlier since taking down 50+ walls requires a lot of resources and time commitment for them.

There may be faulty mechanics that can be improved, but they are also part of the challenges within the system, either intended or unintended. Managing limited resources and making decisions based on longterm strategy can help overcome those challenges. And indirect tactics are sometimes necessary when direct confrontation would result in destruction.
