With the new update, we did get new missions (Chen / Gulom).

Unfortunatelly Gulom's missions confront players with the problem, that it's very time consuming completing them within a group.

Even before the new update, you had to leave dinoz in certain areas to provide them with necessary items during WARs or CotCs, because the maximum usually isn't sufficient for the whole event.

Now comming to my request:

I'd like to have something like a repository, where you can store a certain total ammount (50-100)* of items and/or ingridients.

Putting items/ingridients in the repository, or taking them out of it could use a similar interface like compiling an offer at the Tamer Market.
This feature could be unlocked by a quest/epic item, just like Illustrated Mission Guidebook or Diamantite Pebble.


* Example:
Max. Total = 50
Items/Ingridients in Repository:
• 3 x Fire Energy
• 5 x Air Energy
• 10 x Battle Ration
• 5 x Relaxative Pill

Remaining space = 27
