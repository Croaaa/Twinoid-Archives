The fight with the 3 Wanwans are initiated by moving there.

At the Whistler Syphon, see if any paths to the West are open. If there are, that means you've already started the quest. If the path isn't open, that means that either your account is glitched, your computer doesn't like Rocky Quests, or you have already fought the Wanwans and you still have to talk to the Rocky King.

It might be a problem with your computer (yes really). I can't turn off Cold Shower on my Winks from one of my laptops, which was pretty annoying when I was at the Steppes, but I had no issues with my brother's computer.
