That's a very negative and polarised way of looking at it. You are correct, of course, that there will always be dinos and skills that are more powerful than others, that's pretty much a given. You are also correct that 'good' players who work hard and do their homework and play the odds will make better builds of the same dinos than players who don't. This is the way it should be.

But this doesn't mean that the developers shouldn't work to narrow the gap between the good dinos/elements and the bad ones and to curtail the very best skills to bring them a little down to earth. For example, right now the tree is so much better than the others and the tree so much worse. If you buffed the tree a little (either give it some more skills, buff some existing ones or make the Collosus path actually useful) and nerfed the path slightly (by nerfing some of the best skills and/or reducing the importance of speed/initiative) then it would still be the case that the best players would make better builds and almost certainly that Rockies are still better than Gorilloz, but there would be a bit more variety out there and people would could actually enjoy levelling up dinos other than Rockies, Wanwans and Winks a bit more. Even if only 3-4 more species became viable at level 50 it would be better than nothing.

Same goes for Bubble, you could reduce its power while still keeping it as a very powerful skill. Just not head, shoulders and most of the torso above every other double skill in the game. And poison, you could keep it as a viable option, just not *so* good that people need to equip Poisonites on every single dino (without LB or SC) in PvP and auto-lose in Dojo qualification.

tl;dr version - Instead of thinking of rebalancing as trying to make the game 'perfectly balanced' just think of it as making it better than it is now.

2. Slow lighting Dinoz' consumed initiative by 30%, making them less overpowered.

3. Workaround the Monochromatic-used-by Lightning-Dinoz problem, as at the moment a monochromatic used by a lightning dinoz with Adrenaline achieves, that all his attacks cost 50% initiative.
