We're all looking forward to level 80 coming out soon, but once it does, the elemental potion skills will be less potent because there will be more dinoz that will be immune to their debuffs. So I have one simple suggestion and that is to increase the minimum level from 10 to 15.

I also think they should have positive effects for dinoz that have the respective element higher than 30. Here are my additional buff suggestions for each:

Ember - +15% counter attack chance for dinoz.
Amazon - dinoz heal 5% of their max each turn.
Abyss - 25% increased damage for assaults and spells for all dinoz.
St elma's fire - bonus 8 initiative for dinoz and +25% dodge chance.
Uvavu - immaterial status and +50% movement speed for all dinoz.
