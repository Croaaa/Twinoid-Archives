I got Mud Wall last night and it looks severely bugged. OP-style bugged.

I tried refreshing my dojo last night to see the skill in action, but it never triggered, so I assumed it did what it said it did and hoped I would see it in action in the fight against McHalls. I did not trigger then either. Today I wanted to see Deto on some new dinoz and finally it triggered in my testdojo.

First of all, it is not an A or E skill, it triggers when enemy dinoz attack (like vaporous). You can still lose init, petri, slowed etc. behind the Mud Wall. It looks like KSB does not do damage, but cancels the Mud Wall right away. Primal State does not affect it.

The bug is this: after it triggers, it triggers every single time the dino gets attacked (spells or physical). So Mud Wall will absorb up to 30 damage, disappear, then on the next attack it will come back. I had a multi hit combo of 12 against it and the Mud Wall disappeared and came back 3 times during that combo.

In the test dojo my Casti (Mud Wall dino) took down 3 L60+ sphered dinoz with almost full health all by himself, because he is almost invulnerable.

I have of course only tested this in dojo, but I assume this is how it works. Any other players with Mud Wall?

I will post this in Bug section.
