If the topic you're looking for is too old, you can search it with Google. Since the links of all the old topics have changed, they're not directly available but you can display them using the "cache" function of Google (put the mouse on the arrows of the fast sight function of Google and select the link "cached"). There isn't a cached copy of each page but knowing the date, you can search it in the forum by yourself.
