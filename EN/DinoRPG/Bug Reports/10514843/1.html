Hi all

I really don't like the so called tactics of some clans stopping to rebuild their castle during a war.
So I'd like to make a suggestion to minimize the time, clans want to have their walls down.

On the castle page of a clan you could read:

Your treasury, currently valued at ... is protected by the castle.

So wouldn't it be right, that the treasury is no longer protected if the walls are down?

Maybe a system similar to the reputation loss (which is ok and should be kept, as it is today) would be nice.

If the walls of a clan are down longer than one day, he would lose a percentage of his ingredients each day.

As I don't want to burn away those ingredients, they could be sent to the clans that destroyed the castle the last time.

The percentage could be really low in the first few days but should raise after some days to avoid hiding behind broken walls.

As a clan loses a percentage instead of a fixed amount, this should be no big issue for smaller clans who cannot afford to rebuild the castle.
Or maybe the ingredients drain could stop if a clan hasn't enough ingredients to rebuild the castle. I don't want to punish small clans with low income.

So what do you think?

Although i like the idea that there should be an end to hiding behind broken walls, i think that ur solution is not the right one.

We should look at the reasons, why clans are hiding behind walls.

Reason#1)
Big Clans hide in the end to not gain losses and save their points until the end or to not get a retaliation of another equal big clan.

Reason#2)
Mid clans hide as long as they can so that the time-span for bigger clans is rly small to achieve a win against them. Also the time span for smaller clans to steal a 30dmg win while bigger clans attack is smaller.



Your solution is to increase the punishment for broken walls. But imho the ingredients should not be touched, as this will hurt some clans more then others even if its procentual.


My solution would be a combo with increasing the punishment and weakening a part of reason to keep broken walls down.
Part 1)
Higher Punishment:
Make the Repu loss for destroyed castle per day an exponentional curve. It'll b like this then:
Castle Down for 1 day: -1 Repu
Castle Down for 2 days: -4 Repu
Castle Down for 3 days: -9 Repu
Castle Down for 4 days: -16 Repu

Part 2)
Implement a better system to prevent "steal wins" of smaller clans. There are enough proposals, like raise the minimum dmg needed to win
or split the win reward equal to all clans attacking,
or split the win reward equal to the dmg a clan did.

With no fear of smaller clans stealing ur points a part of a reason to keep the walls destroyed would fall apart and thus making this strat less attractive.




Also another solution could be that the castle can be repaired within 3 days, but is autorepaired after 3 days. If there are no ingredients for repair the clan suffers an additional 10 Reputation loss after 6 day additional 20 etc.
This solution is not elegant as it is forcing the clan repair onto the clan.


But imo its still better then the hiding strategies that are now common.

Broken Walls is a shame and thus the reputation should drop faster.
Dont want to blame anyone, we are using this strategy, too. But we would like to NOT use it if no one would or if it would not pay off.
