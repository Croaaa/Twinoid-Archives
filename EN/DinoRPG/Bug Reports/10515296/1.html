Pigov is right. Such ideas are de-balancing. It would also discourage people who work long and hard on there dinoz, and discourage people who do pay.

It would also encourage people to multiaccount which is illegal. Opening up useless accounts just to create and sell a single high level dinoz in the marketplace would be a huge problem, and make the admins' job that much harder.

You don't need to be competitive with the uberplayers and uberclans to enjoy dinorpg. There are a lot of smaller players and smaller clans that enjoy the game as much or more than the big guys. Being diligent and persevering in your own progress is part of the game. Dinorpg is a game of patience. Trying to change that fact is trying to make dinorpg into something else.

The Dojo has a remarkable two tier prize system. The first tier, with its Toufufu Eggs, is a realistic goal, even for relatively new players. The second tier, the IDT, can also be a good source of notes for relatively new players. But to expect everyone of every skill level to be competitive to the end of the IDT, would ruin what the Dojo has to offer for the bigger well established players. The Dojo currently has something for everyone, and it should be kept that way.
