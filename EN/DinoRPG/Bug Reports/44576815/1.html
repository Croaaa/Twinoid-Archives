That's because of the way damage is calculated. As you may already have noticed, the same attack doesn't always do the same damage when hitting the same opponent. So your observation may be "wrong".

+20 defense means the normal attack should drop by 3 points in damage (pvp armor means devided by sth around 7). But there is a luck-factor added that gives sth like +-25% to the damage. That can easily be above those 3 points you just saved.

I'd advice you to test it in your dojo. Ofc that may take a while. Just let your pigmous fight against each other. One only with the invo turned on and the other one with only fireball turned on. You should notice a difference when observing a bigger amount of fights.
