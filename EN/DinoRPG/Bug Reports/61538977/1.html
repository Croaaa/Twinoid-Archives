Just leaving a note here in case there's anyone else with my specific situation.

The problem I had been having since the update to Firefox is that the fight animation would freeze and blank out every few seconds. You would have to mouse over a link to get it to show again, but that would only last a few seconds as well.

Turns out that it was an interaction with the add-on 'Tree Style Tabs'. Something they do with saving the tab state causes the fight to freeze and blank out as though the browser had lost focus.

My solution has been to run the flash games in a separate window with the Tree Style Tabs hidden. The fight animation now runs without issue.
