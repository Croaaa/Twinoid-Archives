As a reminder you can read the DinoRPG Forum Rules

Be aware Motion Twin may or may not use your suggestions, and some of your ideas may already be an existing feature in the game.

As devs already announced what they will do for DinoRPG and what they won't, things can be listed as such:

Can't suggest on: New skills/dinoz/monsters/quests/missions, interface customization (music/sounds/adding new elements), new mode of events.
Can suggest on: slight skills change, current events rules tweaks.

Before posting a bug report, please follow these guidelines first. It may resolve your problems.

Motion Twin will never put advertisement links, nor flash ones. If you have them it means a (toolbar) plugin(s) installed them and you need to remove it.
Simply refresh the page (F5). If it doesn't work, do a forced refresh (CTRL+F5) (for OSX users: cmd+shift+R).
Verify that you have the latest flash version.
Close your browser and then reopen it.
Clear your browser cache.
Try different browsers, Motion Twin games are developed on FireFox browser.
Turn off your computer, wait a around one minute and turn it back on. This will clear your computer cache.
Clear your Flash cache.
