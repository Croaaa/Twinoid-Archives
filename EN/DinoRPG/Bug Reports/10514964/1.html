The clan wars should keep the current duration. Why? Because the smaller clans will be dominated by the bigger ones as soon as they enter. By having a clan war which lasts 2 months, it encourages small clans to have some fun before the big clans arrive and, if Epoq follows Bwig in having a very small clan warning time (which was only 2 days compared to 10 days in the previous wars), it allows some clans to prepare themselves during the war if they are not ready.

Usually, small clans have about 3 weeks to enjoy themselves in the wars before the big clans arrive and trot on their fun. By having a month long war, the small clans will have their castles destroyed instantly and frequently and this means less clans will join the wars.

So what's it going to be:
we have a month long war which is heavily focused on the medium and big clans only so they don't have to wait to join the war, thus discouraging small clans to join the war as they would only have about a week of having fun before the clan wars are dominated by the big clans.
We stick with the current duration which is fair for all clans. Small clans will have enough time to enjoy the wars and big clans will have enough time to mobilise by simply waiting, making for a more interesting war.
