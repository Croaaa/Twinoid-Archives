I have a suggestion that could possibly resolve some of the exploitable aspects of clan wars. Before a clan war starts, there should be a 10 days time frame for clan registration for those wishing to join the clan war. Only the clans who have registered within the 10 days can participate in the clan war. This could possibly stop 100 rep ghost clans from being created near the end of war.

Furthermore, when the clan register to enter the clan war, all the members of that clan will also register to their clan. This will make it so that players can only attack or defend in the clan that they originally registered in. This will stop clan hopping to reduce others' rep or their own war costs and various tricks.

Lastly, if wars only last 1 month, there can be an additional rule of having all registered clan castles automatically built on the third day of war. (This will give some time to put up defenders and stop multi accounts from registering to participate in clan war and popping up near the end of the war. If their castle are forced to be built, then if their castle is continuously destroyed, they will no longer be a fresh "100 rep clan")

Feel free to give some feedbacks.
