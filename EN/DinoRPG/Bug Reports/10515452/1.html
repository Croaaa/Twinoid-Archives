Thanks for clarifying. I was going on the assumption that it was common knowledge that no player could join a clan in the last 15 days of this Mana War.

Clearly I need to give some rationale for extending this restriction to the entire time of the War Event.

First, what is the point? To get a few powerful dinos into a different clan to help that clan for the first 2/3 of the War? Why? The only thing this does is artificially inflate the position of the weaker clan. Add the rule that this clan does not have to attack daily and no penalty for NOT rebuilding their Castle and this Clan can just sit out the war once the big dinos leave.

Second, this practice can only discourage mid to small clans from competing, if they know they are going to meet unusually strong dinos and be defeated by clans they should beat. Less competition, less interest in the game, less players.

Last, I understand alliances. If you want to team up and fight from your proper Clan so be it (I see this as part of the game). Apparently MT saw the need to restrict the changing of Clans in the last 15 days, I saw that was a good start but incomplete. Use the thinking that promted this decision and apply it to the entire war.
