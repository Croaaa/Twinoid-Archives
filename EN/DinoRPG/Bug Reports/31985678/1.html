Main point of suggestion - low-level clans protection by some emulating of league system in common event.

so, Castle level affect 2 things:

1. max lvl of dinos which can attack and defend castle
2. amount of mana production
___________________

Now more detail:

1. Castle level = max lvl of dinos which can attack and defend castle

example: we have 3 clans with different castles lvls in such ranking:
rank 1. clan A - 60 lvl
rank 2. clan B - 30 lvl
rank 3. clan C - 40 lvl

according the 1st rule, if clan A attacks clan B then:
clan A can use 30 lvl dinoz in attack & 60 lvl dinoz in defend
clan B can use 60 lvl dinoz in counterattack & 30 lvl dinoz in defend

*** there can be variant, when clan B can counter with no lvl limits

this rule must have 2 exclusions

exclusion 1: if lower-lvl castle attacks higher-lvl castle - then higher-lvl castle can counter with it's full force

in our examle: if clan B attacks clan A then
clan B can use 60 lvl dinoz in attack & 30 lvl dinoz in defend
clan A can use 60 lvl dinoz in counterattack & 60 lvl dinoz in defend

*** there can be variant, when "full force" mean no lvl limits
*** possible must work only if higher-lvl castle has higher rank in manawar

exclusion 2: if lower-lvl castle has higher rank in manawar than higher-lvl castle - then higher-lvl castle can attack lower-lvl castle with it's full force

in our examle: if clan C attacks clan B then
clan C can use 40 lvl dinoz in attack & 40 lvl dinoz in defend
clan B can use 40 lvl dinoz in counterattack & 30 lvl dinoz in defend

*** there can be variant, when "full force" mean no lvl limits

2. Castle level determines amount of mana production

i think formula of mana production per hour must be something like
mana/h = 200 + castle lvl

*** imho Mana should be accrued not every hour, but every 10 minutes + some amount with castle building

castle lvl can be 30-70. (may be even 20 for minimum?)
numbers can go in steps (30 35 40 45 ..., or 30 40 50 ...) or can be any

also there must be a rule which wil not allow to change castle lvl too often.

discuss?
