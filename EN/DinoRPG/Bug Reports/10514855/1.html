Wouldn't it be interesting if, when you built your castle, you could choose how sturdy to make it?

On building a castle, you could choose whether to have a 50, 100, 150, 200, 250 or 300 point castle. It would still be possible to add plates to diminish damage and other extras.

If you mount a good defence then it might still take several clans to co-operate in order to defeat you but only one of them would actually be able to claim a victory (or maybe two if the first attacker leaves a couple of points and the second actually destroys the castle)


This would greatly increase the strategy and diplomacy required for wars.
