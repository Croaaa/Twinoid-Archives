The idea of decreasing the size of clans has popped up quite often recently. It's a suggested solution to increase the activity of the server, which is something that I think everyone can agree as a huge issue regarding the future of this game most of us love or at least like.

Before this idea has been shot down by a rather large constituency of the forums going member-base but perhaps the times are a-changin' and people are ready to reconsider this? The main argument for clan size decrease is that the server as it is does not maintain a large enough playerbase to support interesting events and cutting the size of clans would create around twice the amount of active, able and ardent clans or at least it would free up a lot of members to try their wings elsewhere.

Those who oppose the idea do not buy into this idealism and think it's unfair to break close Clan communities simply because MT is struggling to bring new players in. It can even be questioned if the mass divide of clan members would actually help the game or just drive more people away and create some un-needed bad blood? Is it really the players job to take the fall when Motion Twin fails to promote DinoRPG and make it more approachable for new players?

Mengo9999Hmmm... How much of a reduction would it be?? From 50 members to..??

That's a really good question.

Although I'm somewhat against the idea we still shouldn't make a strawman out of it by proposing something ludicrous like only 15 member clans. That would require 100% activity during events and I think that's a pipe dream for most clans. It's only a flash game after all, people have lives and priorities and I'm sure they'd be quick to find something better to do if involvement in events meant spending all your free time here to help the clan.

The golden middle here is the point where the decrease in clan size causes a surge of active players without a clan large enough to actually support the formation of new clans. If the size is decreased too little, say to 40 members, I doubt most of the people getting the boot, so to say, would bother with the game but the same rings true if the clan sizes are decreased too much. If the vast majority of event participating people of the server are suddenly and quite groundlessly cut from the social ties they've built I think that would cause a lot of bitterness and resentment toward the game aswell.

Of course I'm just guessing here. The worst case scenario could be that instead of 20 active clans with give or take 50 members, we'd have the same 20 active clans with just 15 members.
