I think it'd be cool if there was a sort of PvP event where the level of the master's dinoz had NO effect/outcome on all of the battles... So I had an idea:

The Create-Your-Own-Team Tournament
All participating players would "create" 3 level 50 dinoz, choosing all the skills, 3 starting elements, 5 spheres of their choice, and the dinoz (cosmetic purposes only).
A dinoz cannot have more than 30 skills+unlocks in a single element
The tournament would be held in a similar fashion to the Inter-Dojo Tournament. However, no items are allowed, Black Hole is not allowed, and Hypnosis is not allowed. 3 v 3 fights.
The qualification stage would be the previous month's dojo qualification. The 8 days following the final's of the inter-dojo would be the "create-your-own-dinoz" tourny.
Only people in the last 32 get prizes (you don't spend any extra money qualifying anyways)


This way, it would be a competition of who can create the best team, rather than who has the best level 40+ fully sphered demon/reincarnated team. There's also no advantages in being farther in the game other than a vaster knowledge of what skills/builds are superior to others.

I hope this isn't a silly suggestion.
