To cut to the chase: we meagre players have no control over the randomness of urma eggs. I'm not saying that should be removed entirely. Instead, Urma should also sell bingo tickets worth 750.

You get a grid that shows all the items/ingredients you can get from an urma egg confined into a 5x5 grid and each ticket = a random announcement of an item. Multiple tickets will never announce same item. That is then highlighted onto your bingo grid and if you get 5 in a row, diagonal or column, you get a choice to do the following:

you can bolster the chances of getting one certain item. The exact boost depends on the value of the item with an inverse correlation between item value and exact % chance boost. The lower the value, the higher the boost. On average, the chance boost is about double per stack (max 3 stacks, may diminish with boosts for other items).
You can eliminate the chances of getting one certain item

Benefits:
Whenever you get an urma egg, the chance boosts are permanent on that account and server.
Item chance eliminations are temporary, around 2-3 years. So if you get a bingo and one of the items is a goblin costume and you want to eliminate it from the randomness in urma eggs, you can do that and you will never get that item from urma eggs for 2-3 years.

Here are the following limits:
You can only choose an item out of the 5 in the row/diagonal/column.
Upon getting a bingo, your bingo card resets and if there are more 25 items that you can get from an urma egg, some items may not be highlighted initially but may appear in subsequent grids.
You can only block 2 items per the type of shop they come from. So if you don't want any items from forges, you can't do that.
You can boost the chances of getting same item maximum 3 times.
when boosting chances, you won't have control over the indirect limit of chances of getting other items. However, if you got 2 items that you have boosted chances of getting, the formula will prioritize reducing chances of other items more and the other boosted item much less. So stacking boosts in multiple items may diminish their effectiveness. This enables you to be constantly juggling what you want.
You can only block item chances from urmas up to 7 at a time.
Again, chance blocks/eliminations are temporary.
