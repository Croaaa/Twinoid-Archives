Right now the stats are 4 stats:

-attacks initiated
-Moves
-damage done
-gold won

The damage done includes all damage done by your entire team if you initiated the attack. You don't get points for defending or joining in on an attack.

Maybe it would be better/more interesting to list the damage done by the individual players dinoz in all fights (offense, defense). In addition a statistic "hit points lost" can be added, similar to the clan wars "hit points lost while defending".

Finally, in addition to the score above the battle field, a counter "dinoz killed" would be interesting.

Problem with Damage done is the heals that would screw the system up a little and make it hard to code while the current system is very easy to code.

Example:
Two dinos of two different players attack one Wanwan with lets say 100 HP.
The first players dino deals 40 damage and the second players dino 25 damage. After that the Wanwan heals 40 HP. After that it doesnt heal again and player ones dino deals 40 dmg again and the other one 35.
resulting player damage would be:
player 1 = 80
player 2 = 60
kinda irritating, given the Wanwan only had 100 HP. Those value could even be far greater because of many rations and in fights with many dinos and Leafy Dawn etc.
If you want to want those two values to add up to 100 you would need to substract the heals from it. But then again its hard to decide which damage got healed away. Ofc you could just do something like this: 80/(80+60) * 100 = 57 etc.


Other than that I would also like more statistics, especially for the damage the dinos dealt while they were not the initiators or at least a counter which tells you the total participations of one's dinos.
