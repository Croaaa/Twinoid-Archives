@B2OB

The two of us trying to help you have both done exactly what you are trying to do. There is no need to be rude just because you cannot follow simple instructions.

If you do not get the popup message saying "all page modifications will be lost" then your image is being added as an image and NOT as a background. You cannot preview backgrounds - you have to apply them and see if you got everything lined up properly.


As another example:
Take this presentation page: Merchant

It has a background image:
http://imgup.motion-twin.com/dinorpg/4/6 /d33df9c8_730156.jpg

Background images are not shown in the page text.
Any @blahblah@ is an inline image displayed within the page.
The text for the example page just says (with the correct brackets):
.
{g}Waikiki Island{/g}

{g}22nd Feb 2010{/g}


.

Note that you need to pad the text out with newlines and terminate with some non-whitespace character in order to get the whole background image to be displayed if the image is longer than the text (as in this case)
