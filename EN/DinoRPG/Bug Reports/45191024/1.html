Here are some suggestions. I would implant these if I coulp Totally for free! (Or not ? )

Hades is broken? It is currently useless? No problem! to fix it, we just need to chage it to this: it will be unique and interesting.

Hades marks ONE enemy with a big debuff (so yes it would be blocked by self control)
I was thinking the debuff could be a filter, like petrification, but darker, and with an icon looking like a scythe, or maybe a sword of Damocles on top of the dinoz.
Here is how the debuff works :
-It slows the dinoz for 50% like the other slows
-No matter what, the Santaz can only attack this dinoz
-It remebers the sum of all damage delt to this dinoz and the Casting Santaz (maybe a static counter could be nice? It could use dark numbers instead of the red and green one already in use, and it would grow in size without disapearing)
-It lasts for 10 turns of the casting Santaz, OR until the Santaz dies, OR until it is removed (by something like primal state for exemple.) At that moment the Santaz casts "Call of the Grave" : the debuff explodes, dealing all the damage stored to the debuffed dinoz + 25 of the casting Santaz if the skill is activated by his death.

Why is this a very good idea?
It gives enormous strategic diversity to the santaz, gives meaning to its slow speed, or it's low overall HP, creates very interesting combos with other single target invocations, and the assassination theme fits the god "Hades" and the grim reaper image. I mean heck we might start seeing kamikaze Santaz !


Gel-> rename to Frost
Inflicts 5 water damage and slows an opponent. If the opponent is already slowed or petrified, the skill turns into Permafrost: inflicts 10 water damage that goes through bubble (makes sense, since Ice is solid)

Combustion : it is a nearly useless skill at the moment, here's an idea to revamp it : Combustion reaplies its damage everytime fire damage is apllied up to three times (idea of fire "digging" into the ennemies)

Benediction: same thing: useless skill at the moment. Here's an idea: Changing the Blessed Status, since it's a benediction, it should be able to smite ennemies and heal the allies. So the buff would be to sometimes (rarely) heal by 10 hp and sometimes (rarely) smite an attacking ennemy, dealing 10 true damage to dinoz and 100 true damage to monsters.

Hermetic Aura should be like the feross passive, since armor is useless. In fact in my opinion all armor skills: carapace (that should be named shell) basalt armor, faroe heritage, and Armor plated shell should be like that, increasing in the likeliness of blocking as armor increases, just like dodging basically. In that case what i would personally do is:
Carapace : 5% blocking
Faroe : 5% blocking
Basalt armor: 10% blocking
Feross passive: 10%
Armor plated shell: 15 %
Hermetic aura: Temporary 5%

stacking multiplicatively for a maximum of 60% blocking, for a Feross only with hermetic aura on. This would give feross its uniqueness, and reinforce it's "main tank" feel.

Hospital porter is an unsatisfying skill: make it able to revive dead allies: idea of a rescue

Finally, Achilles heel, Diamond fangs, Warlord (and possibly strength and fury and martial arts, although they are tolerable) are all useless skills: with the advent of forcecontrol, these skills are pure uselessness, whereas they could be strategic options worth exploring.

Here is my idea for Warlord: it would be a passive that only activates on the dinoz with highest Fire element in the team, and it would be a permanent fire based "focus" or "plunging attack" depending on what is more balanced, on all assaults, but using that dinoz's fire element. So basically one dinoz empowers all the others including itself fire fire 1 or fire 2 on all assaults.

Here is my idea for Achilles heal: deals 2 extra TRUE damage on each assault, but 10% of the time, when receiving an assault, receives 5 or 10 extra damage, depending or what is more balanced.

Diamond fangs: Deals one extra true damage on all assaults. The way i picture this is the extra damge applies after the first. Like for example, dinoz A attacks dinoz B, deals 15 damage, and after that dinoz b receives 1 extra damage (without the attack animation occuring again)

Also the defence invocations need some love, maybe they could go up to +30 defence ? And + 15 for Buddha?

My suggestions for the skills OP (original post) mentioned:

Hades: Keep the group slow (-50%) that pierces SC, just as fuijin is immune to primal state. Remove poison.
Gel: leave it as is.
Combustion: double the damage based on amount. Highest combustion damage I've seen is around 7 or 8. Double it and you got up to 16 damage with an E skill against heavy dinoz.
Benediction: +50% assault damage bonus seems ok.
Armor: Instead of functioning as a reduction in pve damage, since it does reduce smashroom damage from 2 to 1, maybe armor reduces all incoming assault damage by 5% per armor. So that 10 armor for -20% speed will give you around a -50% assault damage reduction. EDIT: that might seem too strong, so maybe armor stacks diminishingly or speed penalty is higher for that skill. So it scales mostly with higher damage, but doesn't negate normal hits and reduction is rounded down so 1 damage hits don't become 0 damage hits. FC will pierce armor though.
Hospital Porter: I like OP's idea. revives 1 dino from death per fight at the end of the battle, but you must win the fight and the porter dino must not die.
All the useless defence and attack skills OP mentioned in old tree: I strongly agree: they do need to be buffed.
Defence invocations: Maybe double their current bonuses. That should be decent enough.
