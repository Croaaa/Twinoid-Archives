Ok, so whenever I sacrifice a dino, I lose the levels he had when I'm calculating my position in the individual rankings. Whenever you freeze a dino, you keep counting him, but he doesn't count towards your limit. This leads to some players (like me) being under rated due to sacrificing high level dinoz, and some being over rated, such as those that have 87 dinoz frozen.

Here's a simple, easy fix that would take about 10 minutes to implement. Instead of counting the levels of all dinoz active + all dinoz frozen and ignoring sacrificed dinoz, why not merely calculate someone's ranking points by adding up the values of their top 21 dinoz, whether active, frozen, or sacrificed? This would give a MUCH more accurate ranking of who has the most levels.
