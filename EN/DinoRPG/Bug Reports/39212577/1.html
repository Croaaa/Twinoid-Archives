Talking to a clanmate and this idea comes up because I felt like dinorpg could be more "interactive": A feature that would allow me to group dinoz with another clan mates, example would be my lack of a third dinoz for nimbao so I invite a clanmate. Whenever we want to move a new action appears saying, "XSp3c1aL requests that the group move to BrutForce, do you accept?"
(The below part is an example of the idea, it could also be a limit of 5 or 10 irma's per ally if 20 is to much)
Irma's can be shared with a limit of 20 per ally dinoz(Ally can only help 3dinoz per day, this applies to all clanmates so they must choose), resets at 24:00. This means that the group can use their new action to move once, and then both player's can share 20 Irma's with each other for a maximum 41 moves per day. Wealthy clanmates would be able to help newcomers, but the drawback of accepting this help is exp boosting skills such as professor, intelligence and the magic item encyclopedia would be turned off.
