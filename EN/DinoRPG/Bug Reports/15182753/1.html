mtp85

If all the Dinoz in the group have a move AND the group leader is the one starting the fight with the Gluon, all of the Dinoz in the group will join the fight.

That mission doesn't force the requested monster to appear (unlike the Giant and Chicken missions), so it can take a while to finish. It's probably best to try to get it out of the way early, when Gluons appear more often (as in, before Giants appear at level 11).

It sounds like your only option to complete it with your current Dinoz is to wait and eventually find one, but maybe you can adjust your plan on future ones to make it easier.
