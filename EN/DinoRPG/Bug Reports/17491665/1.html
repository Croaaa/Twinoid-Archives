There are some good skills above lvl 50, but there are also quite a bit of negative skills or skills with large penalties.

For example, there are skills that give negative endurance, negative initiative, negative speed. Is it really justified to have high end skills like these to have such large penalties with not so much benefits? Additional battery gives +50hp, but -15 initiative. Compared to the old skill tree, Sumo gives +100 hp without any penalties. A regular skills like Hazard only gives +7 initiative. I think skills should be appealing and have actual benefits. If you think that the skill gives too much hp, you can just lower the amount of HP given, instead of giving it big penalties. We level to lvl 50+, with only 10 additional levels to gain new skills, we should be given more useful skills instead of a lot of skills with lots of penalties.

Just my 2 cents on the new skills. Hope the developers will take it into consideration.
