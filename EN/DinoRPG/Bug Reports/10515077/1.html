How to make a more newbie-friendly game...

Well, I guess you know that half the people who plays this DinoRPG posts a raging thread in the forum about having to wait a day to do one single thing and ragequits.So, here is how this could be fixed...of all these ways, there HAS to be a doable one.

1-Cheaper starting Dinoz
The starting capital is 20.000 coins, but there are no starting Dinoz under 16.000...which means one would need to rack up 12000 more cash just to purchase a second dino without spending real cash.If starting Dinoz were perhaps less powerful, but a bit cheaper (8000-15000 would be an ideal price range) people could grab a couple weak dinoz to start with, and later save money for a stronger one.
2-Encountering and capturing dinoz
If our Dinoz could randomly (even a 1 in 50 chance would be fair) encounter a Dinoz in battle and, in case of victory, add the Dinoz to the master's stables, it would be easier to get more than 1 dinoz, even if they were much weaker than bought dinoz and not able to be frozen- this way a master could just throw away the weaker ones once he hits the maximum monster cap.
3-Have a Dinoz as a starter's quest reward
Either chosen randomly, or chosen by the player as reward, having another low-tier dinoz would really help newcomers having something better to do than just move once, see a fight and coming back tomorrow
4-Free PVP with rewards
If people could battle each other's dinoz, even just a set amount of times a day like Mybrute, and earn some coins or useful items (for instance, healing) both in case of win and loss
4-Give out free items everyday
This kind of explains itself...for example, 3 extra turn potions would mean a lot to who only has one Dinoz, but wouldn't be of much use to a player that already has 5-6.Or, these item giveaways could only be given out to players below a certain ranking.
