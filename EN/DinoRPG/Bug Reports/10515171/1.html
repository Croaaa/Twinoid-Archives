On january 31, 2011
mtp85
The totals at the end of the battle only report the total damage dealt and don't account for any recovered (Nap, Leafy Dawn, etc.), but the outcome is determined by who has the highest % of health remaining. If you can re-watch that battle, you'll probably see your opponent recover enough health to win the battle.
