I think there should be a rapid transit option to simplify movement and cut down on the amount of time needed for some particularly repetitive movement-based quests (I'm glaring at you, Elemental Master):

Have on the group leader a "mass move" button. You would click it, pull down a destination, and *presto* you appear there, mostly intact, with the corresponding irmas vanished from your inventory and a summary of all the HP lost and gold earned. The destination would always be chosen by the shortest route somewhere, so it may require a couple hops to avoid going a direction you don't want to go.

This wouldn't be without its perils, of course. The team would plow ahead, whether dead comrades were being dragged along, or not. Better know where you are going and if you are safely able to do so!

Upside:
1)Saves lots of time, particularly on phones and tablets that can be hard to do repetitive small clicks with.
2)Saves a lot of resources on server as there are no page refreshes. This could speed up latency for everyone!
3)Easy to blow a lot of cash quickly.

Downside:
1)Could kill dinoz if they make a mistake and go the wrong way. Some WTF? factor there.
2)No gathering along the way.
3)Easy to blow a lot of cash quickly.

Perhaps this option could be enabled in account settings or something to keep the newbies from clicking to their doom or early irma depletion without full disclosure.
