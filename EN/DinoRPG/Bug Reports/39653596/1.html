This magic item is an alternative for people who don’t have a Dinoz with Aphrodisiac, Sympathetic and Charisma for a group of six. It isn’t as strong as Sympathetic because it weakens the holder dinoz to join the fight even with maximum group space but it’s still useful.

Etherwasp Honey
Cost: 5 Napodinoz
- Allows this dinoz to follow a full group.
- The dinoz is weakened in combat from the negative effects of the Honey if following a leader with a “group” skill.
- WARNING: ONLY one dinoz can be holding this magic item in the group!

If following a leader with 1 skill like charisma, -1 to all elements.
Initiative reducing skills i.e Vines, Flood etc have their effects reduced by 20%
All multi hits and counter attacks are reduced by 20%
This dinoz maximum hp is reduced by 10.

2 skills, charisma + sympathetic, -2 to all elements.
Initiative reducing skills i.e Vines, Flood etc have their effects reduced by 30%
All multi hits and counter attacks are reduced by 30%
This dinoz maximum hp is reduced by 20.

(This dinoz is more negatively affected in a group of 7.)
3 skills, charisma + sympathetic + aphrodisiac, -3 to all elements.
Initiative reducing skills i.e Vines, Flood etc have their effects reduced by 50%
All multi hits and counter attacks are reduced by 60%
This dinoz maximum hp is reduced by 25%.
