Yes, another "Defending Dinoz" request/idea. Just slightly different.

As a clan leader, or General, Colonel, Major, or Majordomo (whatever your title may be), If you are charged with the responsibility of setting up the defense of your castle then you will understand the frustration of getting the skill sets of your defenders from their masters. It's huge pain in the backside. Yes they can post the info on the internal forum. Yes they can post it externally. Yes they can post it in a PM. Yes they can put the info out on an external site somewhere. But all of these things are inefficient and cumbersome. Some moreso than others.

Wouldn't it be easier for anyone charged with castle defense/defenders find it a much better solution if when you moused over a defender a pop-up box appeared with it's skills? This would be restricted to only dinoz defending during a clan war, and only to people authorized to move/remove dinoz on the defense.

Any comments?
