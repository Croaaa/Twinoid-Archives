I don't know how others feel about it, but I don't like scale. Sending half-dead dinoz with scale into a defense is not what this game should look like. People should fight "fair". It's dinoz-rpg, not lemming-rpg or suicide-bombing-rpg.

Here is a suggestion: scale could always target the weakest enemy (that was already present at the beginning of the fight).

If an attacker dies, he was obviously the weakest in battle at that moment, so it's only fair to kill the weakest defender. More importantly, a smashroom cage would protect the defenders against such kamikaze-bombers. If somebody attacks fair and square, the smashroom will be long dead the moment the first scale triggers. If not, scale was the sole purpose of the attack.


P.S.: Relaxative pills and Monochromatics are also overpowered, at least one can do something against the latter.
