I don't know how many of you are familiar with the sporting company "Accuscore" but the service they provide to ESPN and others is cool and would be a great addition to the IDT results.

Accuscore uses metrics to simulate two sporting teams against one another one thousand times and predicts results based on percentage of times per fictional 1000 fights that each side won a game.

This process would be easy to translate for this game especially. Each IDT match would process and result only the first fight for everyone to watch, just as it does now. But on top of the 1 fight that counts, the computer could easily crunch 100 or even 1000 simulated fights between the same two masters and give an expected victory margin. This could be great to show whether results that actually happened were anything like the expected outcome based on those two masters and the dinoz involved. It would lead to identifying odds-defying matches and (hopefully) fun and insightful debate.

Anyway... its information easily obtained to make a fun addition to the IDT. There it is.
