I think it would be a lot of fun to have some more statistics to go with Dojos. I think most anyone with Dinoz in the dojo would like to know some fun facts such as:

1) Times your dinoz was challenged by other masters and the win/loss percentage

2) Percentage of the time "special objective" was met (this would help us know the true cost of the dojo for ourselves)

3) Total damage dealt per dinoz

4) Number of challenges against your dinoz that other masters cancelled.

Maybe even some leaderboards would be fun. Who doesn't like to see what kinds of dinoz could be at the top? Since the preliminary stages of the tournament look really different than the actual tournament, I think it would be fun to have some ideas of "best dinoz" out there that may be very different than the "best team of 3 using items" dinoz.

What else would you want to see? I would think that most of the data above is already being generated and compiled. Some statistics is being kept on CW and CoTC events, so I'm hoping it isn't too big a request for some further enjoyment of the dojo experience.
