Stinkymeat
We have a lot of problems with our various events in that we can't form proper leagues and the power concentration at the top 3 clans has been immense and worsening. I understand (and am in a top 3 without the sphere/demon credentials normally present) why, because people want rewards and gathering together to monopolize talent makes sense to get the highest chance at rewards.

However, I think it also might be in the best interest of the game to cap members in the clans for a little while, or maybe forever: it will depend upon what people do in response.

My suggestion would be to limit the clan size to 35 or 40. That still gives a chance to play with friends (more than most people would normally play a game like this with, I would think) and would disperse talent out of the top 3 into some other clans. It would instantly render the top 3 into the top 4 or 5 and would, I am sure, form some all new clans that had high quality, low-membership, that would be competitive. It would open up the possibility of meaningful competitions and would also encourage the 35 members to all be more active to be competitive. I also think it could be a lot more fun!

Unfortunately, most English speakers here are going to come from an American perspective that values max/min decision making with no regard or desire to have true competitive balance. We can't help ourselves. As long as it is possible to load the deck and win, win, win! people will do that. This could "force" some of this behavior out of the game in some ways and "force" us to have some fun!
