I think mods should definitely sign off on posts they edit or delete. It's just simple courtesy so the player knows who is addressing them, rather than just have a punishment or warning delivered to them from some faceless entity. It also helps in the case that a player does need to write to the mod in question about any dispute they may have.

In theory yes, the moderation team should all be on the same page as each other, and they should all make the same decisions because they all enforce the same rules. But I don't think it's any secret that mods can disagree with each other, and some mods may be stricter than others. I'm sure I've seen at least one post by a mod in the past even admitting that some mods are more relaxed than others. This isn't a criticism, it's just a simple fact that people have different opinions and the mods are no different in this regard.

Squamph, I think I've agreed with everything you've ever said on this forum, but you're wrong in this case. We're not asking for this just so we can send nasty hate mail to mods. In any case, I think those players who would send hate mail will do so regardless. It's just that at the moment they might not send it to the right mod. At least if every mod signed their name the abuse wouldn't go to a random mod who might have nothing to do with it.

Again, it's just simple courtesy to make it clear who is moderating what. It's not a big deal, and I can live without it, but I really don't think it's asking too much.
