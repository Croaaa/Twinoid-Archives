As most of us know, rpg games usually give you full regeneration of health and mana amongst curing curses, new skills, ups on defenses or other things as well.
And I understand dinoRPG gives up new skills for leveling, better defenses, but also possibly some tougher monsters. And tougher monsters wear down our health. Now, it's important to keep health in this game very sacred, through letting it regenerate itself, very slowly, 1hp per hour or to spend some of that gold of ours.
But I'd like to suggest giving us some hp (10-30) when we level because it would be super for the following reasons (any others of course):

- People new to dinoRPG won't be turned off by the burdens of a sacred hp system way too soon.
- Players would be excited to level up for a whole new reason.
- People who can't pay for gold and only rest for health won't be bored prematurely and leave.
- We only level once in a long time when we get to higher levels.
- Players who kill a powerful monster and gain a lot of xp, but lost a lot of hp would be nicely rewarded as they level up.
- Dying right after leveling isn't fun at all, especially to a new monster.
- People who realize the scarcity of hp would really appreciate it and much reverence would be achieved in general.

There are more reasons and specific ones too when it comes to generously giving us some hp (10-30) when we level. In the hardcore rpg world, leveling is a major achievement and since most games do it, i'm pretty sure there are strong fundatmental reasons. It's good to learn from our brothers and sisters. And if giving hp every level isn't a choice for you guys, maybe consider perks for reaching nice round levels like 10 or 20 or 50 etc.

Thanks for reading. Happy Considering!

Hello Koool, as I check your account page, I've seen you are a new player. You didn't yet discover all the things this game provided for the purpose of healing your own dinozs.

Here's some tips to help you:
- Have the Pearl of the Fountain. Owning this epic item would give you 5hp (after midnight server update) at the Fountain of Youth for each dinoz stationned there.
- Group your dinozs. You can group up to three dinozs together (two others following one). There will be more monsters but your dinoz would recieve less damage in a sense.
- Aim for the U-Skill Priest. It will give you an additional 1hp in rest mode, so 2hp per hour.
- At level 20 do Strange Prowler' Dark Dinoz mission to win a unique Tik Bracelet, that give 10hp to the bearer at midnight CEST.

Well there's other things, but I would let you discorver it by yourself. The game isn't meant to be easy and beat it in one go.
