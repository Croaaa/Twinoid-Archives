Hello, MermaidAmy. Welcome to the game. DinoRPG is not for everyone, but once you adjust to the playstyle it can become a very enjoyable experience. The game takes a lot of patience and planning. It's important not to approach it the same way you might approach a more mainstream game. Once you take your turn on DinoRPG, it's best to simply close your window and go do something else. Treat it almost the same way you might treat checking your favorite daily websites, blogs, or your email. Later on you can participate in the forums and enjoy the experience of clan wars, but first allow yourself time to learn the game and the ways you might enjoy it simply by yourself.

Do you like:
-planning and managing virtual pets?
-cute/funny graphics, characters and storylines?
-the idea of a journey that might take months or even years?

If you answered yes to those questions, this game might be for you. Later on, you might enjoy the game enough to buy some gold and help move yourself along, but I advise that you get a bit more familiar with the game (and its various pitfalls) before doing so. There are various guides on several clan pages. You can read this one for a beginner's introduction to the game. Best of luck.
