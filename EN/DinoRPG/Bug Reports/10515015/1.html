Gathering ingredients is supposed to introduce some kind of gambling "booo, I found nothing" - "Yay, I found something"...

If you already know that there are exactly 3 energies in the grid at Brute Force, that gambling fun part is lost. My personal experience is that I no longer care what I find. I know that my two elemental fission dinoz will give me 3 in 18 days. It doesn't matter if I find them at the beginning or at the end of that period.

Maybe it would be a good idea _not_ to set the number of per grid constant but give each field a 1/12 chance to have air. On average nothing will change, but searching a grid would become much more thrilling.

The same holds true for the other element's gathering skills - a sharpened flint could be 2% per grid point, etc.
