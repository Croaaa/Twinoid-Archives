Something that has been stuck in my head for sometime and how I think it could work not that I care.

How it works:
- Gender is determined by talking to Professor Eugene. Once complete a male/female status symbol will appear.
- An item can be used to raise or lower the chance to from 50/50 to 85/15.
- Dinoz can only be crossbred if they share the same main elements. This is similar to Fear Factor.
Things that breeding does:
- Breeding gives +2 to a random element, example breeding a Quetzu with a Winks increases the chance of getting a Quetzu with +1 in lightning for invocation. So +3 water +1 fire +1 lightning could occur.
- Bred dinoz earn the "Tiny" status which lowers 1 of their main elements by 1.
- Costs 1 move from both dinoz to complete the action.
- Very small chance (about 1%) that the egg will be a rare dinoz.
Limitations:
- Every hour there is a small chance of the action appearing(like Yakuzi)
- Costs 20,000 gold.
- Egg cannot be sold to other players.
- Limited amount of mystery eggs allowed in inventory(3).
- Can only be performed once every week.
