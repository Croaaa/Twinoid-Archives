We've all heard the rumor about a level 70, and i was wondering if players feel that the Brave ability should stay like it is, or if some form of adjustement should be made.

The ability was clearly not created with the idea that a dinoz would still have to gain 20 levels of experience solo.

Currently, just leveling after 50 is really really hard, since you get almost no experience, there is close to no quest, and the dark dinoz will kill you without survival (and using lighting sphere just to give survival to a torch dinoz makes no sense).

It is to the point that i think masters should wait on Quetzu and Moueffe until lvl 55 to get it and grab other stuff. Softpig master who usually run out of fire ability should just skip it and compensate with the new stuff. A level 60 fire dinoz is probably better then a Brave one stuck at 51...


So, here are a few ideas:

1. Leave it as it is, people just have to skip it, and screw masters who have it already.

2. Change it so that a master can turn it on or off, only ONCE per week to avoid abuse.

3. Maybe give Ana Tomie at the clinic the ability to, once per month per account, vaccinate a dinoz against Brave for 30 days. (turn it off for a month). Irreversible for that month.

4. Put an exact copy of the Brave skill deep into the new fire tree.

5. Keep it like it is, but add to the game a special NPC: The Taskmaster. This special NPC only adresses Brave dinoz, and gives out an unlimited amount of "tasks", which are short missions: either a delivery of 3 to 5 moves away, or a kill 10 of X monsters. Max 3 tasks can be done on a single day. Completing a task gives a small XP bonus that puts the dinoz a little closer to the XP he would get in a group.


So: do you think Brave should change before lvl 70? And if so, how?
