This is something that occurred to me recently when I was about to go on holiday. I only had around half an hour until my next turn, but I had to leave to the airport! Wouldn't it have been great if there was a potion that cut down your New Turn wait by an hour?

Function: Reduces wait for New Turn by 1 hour.

Cost: Similar to normal Irma.

If you think about it, the normal potion cuts 23 hours from a single dinoz' wait. This will cut a total of 21 hours. Usually one would want to use the normal Irma to focus resources on a single dino, but there are cases where you want to change the actual clock time (going on holiday, or making the new turn at a more manageable hour).

What do you think?
