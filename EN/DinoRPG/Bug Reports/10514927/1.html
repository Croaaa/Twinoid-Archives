The points system could do with a total overhuall.

It's much easier to reach the top (ignoring demons and re-incarnations for a moment) by having lot's and lot's of level 20 dinos, rather than spending a lot (A LOT) more time or gold getting less dinos but to a higher level, say level 40 (level 50 eventually) dinos.

So instead, the points system should take into consideration the effort spent making dinos a higher level. Just like in the demon note trading. The demon note trading must have the correct math behind it to reasonably represent the extra effort/cost of building a higher level dino. So I may as well use that in my example. If you want to see a full list of what each dino would be worth http://en.dinorpg.com/clan/41?page=3239

Level 1 = 1 ranking points
Level 2 = 2 ranking points
Level 3 = 3 ranking points
Level 4 = 4 ranking points
Level 5 = 5 ranking points
Level 6 = 7 ranking points
Level 7 = 8 ranking points
Level 8 = 10 ranking points
Level 9 = 13 ranking points
Level 10 = 17 ranking points
...
Level 50 = 608 ranking points

This way as apposed to 5 level 10's being equal in points to one level 50, it would take 36 of them. Which sounds more like it to me.

Demon dinos influence on ranking is easily solved, everytime a user sacrafices a dino, their ranking points shouldn't change, as the dino still exists in the demon shop (you can buy it back) this way no ranking points are lost from sacraficing dinoz. So the "demon effect" no longer exists. There only remains one tweak, a demon starts at level 10. And as you don't loose any ranking points from sacraficing anymore this is basically giving a user 17 free ranking points. Simple solution a demon's contribution to ranking points should have 17 taken away from it.

Now the only thing that needs to be adressed is re-incarnation. Very simple again, when a dino is re-incarnated its current ranking points are frozen and a new tally is started and added to the original from level 1.
So if you decided to re-incarnate a level 50 dino (to save me looking up what points a level 40 gives ) It would have 608 + 1 points at level 1. (This is actually giving a free point to a master so technically it should have a 1 taken away from it ^^)
At level 2 it will have 608 + 2 and so on.

So I propose, the new formulae for working out a users total points should be a total of all the dinoz from this forumale.

"Current Demon Note Sacrafice Value" + "Previous Demon Note Sacrafice Value Before Reincarnation - 1, or 0 If Not Re-Incarnated" - "17 if Demon, or 0 if Not"

And Also that Sacraficing Dinos doesn't removed them from the "total of all dinoz points" aka "ranking"

Simple.

Then the leader of the ranking WILL BE the leader of the game thus far in highest points atleast. He could still be bested by better trained dinoz masters.

....

This would mean that the top of the leader board would actually be the top of the leader board. And not the person who has re-incarnated and sacraficed the least. Although that is currently not the case on our server due to every big money spender sacraficing or re-incarnating. But if you look at the top of the french leader board. You can see that the person to least sacrafice and re-incarnate wins out.
