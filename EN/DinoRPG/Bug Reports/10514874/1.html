Sanjar
Morale isn't an upgrade
Which is why I put "upgrades" in quotations.

I agree morale boosts don't seem to effectively address the shortcomings of the current system, but they do encourage trying to maintain defenses for as long as possible.

Defending comes at a disadvantage at the moment, and many clans simply skip defending in favor of other tactics.

Danger of multi-clan attacks
One clan attacks and others start to join to take advantage, or they plan 4-5 clans in advance. Holding off one clan is manageable, 4-5 clans is next to impossible.

Low experience
The XP gained to HP lost ratio is terrible. Most clans tend to skip defending in favor of constantly attacking and taking back the victories they gave up.

Attackers have the initiative
Attackers can choose when, how and in what numbers they attack the defenses. This could be at a time when defenders are likely asleep. Coordinating 5 waves to hit a defense at once would take care of most defensive lines, especially if the attack is unexpected. In war, initiative is everything.

"Losing" immunizes your clan
A clan can choose never to defend and give victories freely. Once an opposing clan is given victory, the "loser" clan is immune to any further attacks (until 200+ rep, of course). Then they have the assured safety to pick and plan when and how they'll take each of those defeats back at the most favorable moments.

So yes, fix the defense system. It is an enjoyable aspect of the game that unfortunately suffers from too many flaws to utilize often.
