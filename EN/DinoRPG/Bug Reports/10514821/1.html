Hi there!

Im sure you guys are tired of having to click all the talking over and over..
Yup, the merguez seller. Hes a nice guy, we all like free stuff, but he talks to much.

But thats not where the problem lies.
Having to click on the merguez to equip it on every single dino is boring and exhaustive.

And you know, some people just skip it, since it takes to much time.
It can take about fourty minutes or even more, depending on the internet connection.
So much clicking for little benefit is no fun at all.

So what i am suggesting is a button that will make our problem go away.
A single button that would fill EVERY free slot with merguez! How cool is that!?
Every single dino would instantly have their empty equipment slots filled with merguez, and your own bag too!
this way we would only need to talk with the Merguez once in a while.

But wait! there is more!
Someone could argue that it would be a bad idea, since having your bag full of merguez makes it impossible to remove merguez equiped on dinoz..
And you wouldn't be able to equip a battle ration, or any other desirable item.

Well, this would be no problem if we could remove/destroy a currently equiped item to free up a slot. Instead of giving an error message, clicking an equiped merguez with the bags full would just make it disapear.
Simple! ^^

Imagine the following option when talking with the Merguez seller:
"Please, can you fill this bag with merguez?"
And ta daa!, bags full, dinos happy.

What do you think?
"It's good for the business!"
