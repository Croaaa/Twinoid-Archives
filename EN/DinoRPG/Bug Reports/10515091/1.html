Not to be picky, but this should be in the suggestions forum, as you are suggesting a new skill.

(on topic) I agree that speed and initiative are quite a gamebreaker. I suggest a battle turn cap. For example, with this cap, the really fast dinoz full of initiative won't be able to use more than about 7 actions in a row. Once they reach their cap, even if they activate a detonation, they won't have another attack.

Problems with this suggestion:
Enemies that are vined, stumped or flooded would have to attack somehow, because the dinoz would have more initiative as soon as they finish their 7th attack. Solution: After the dinoz makes his 7th attack, he loses an amount of initiative equal to the difference between his initial initiative and the enemy dinoz initiative (vined or not).

I know there would be more problems, so add to this list if you are interested in or critical of this idea, in case this "new skill" is not implemented, let alone considered.
