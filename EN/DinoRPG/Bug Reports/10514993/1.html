To have Hazard, you must have Lightning Attack.
A Rocky almost certainly has Lightning as the primary element and therefore Lightning Attack will be important in determining the timing of the second action.
The Winks probably has more Water than Lightning and therefore it slower to get to the second attack.

In any case, INITIATIVE determines the FIRST action.
In discussing second and subsequent actions, SPEED is much more relevant.
