MadamX's Mission with the BriefCase will always get you one Gluon, so just make a Group (2 or 3) with the Leader doing MadamX's BriefCase/Gluon Mission and the Follower the one that Needs to Kill a Gluon. Make sure all in the Group have a Turn available when your Leader opens the BriefCase and the Group Fights the Gluon that Pops out. This solution will work every time you Needs to Kill a Gluon.
