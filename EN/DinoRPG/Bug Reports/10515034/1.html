I am sure that many players would like to see their gameplay time streamlined to require fewer refreshes of the same screens for every dinoz in a group. I couldn't find a suggestion similar, so if I am reposting something, forgive me:

My suggestion is that there would be a toggle added to the group leader's screen that would allow for actions to be applied to an entire group at once. It could either be separate or just a built-in function of any of the dialogue or action toggles the leader could otherwise use.

For instance: Rather than wading through the dialogue (particularly cumbersome on Elemental Master, but others apply) in a group of 4 or 5, the group leader would plow through that dialogue and every dinoz who has the exact same sequence as the leader would have it finished for them. Additionally, any time there is a "pick up some object on the ground" objective, all dinoz would pick it up at the same time.

I even wouldn't be opposed to having a "harvest" button on the leader's screen that would incorporate all of the group's search efforts into 1 search screen. Anything to reduce screen loads and refreshes of redundant actions is what I am after. Some with duel skills for harvesting may not want to use that if they are going to start or arrive at a different harvesting point than others in the group, but they could individually click just like they do now in those circumstances.

Anyway, the reason, in particular, I think this suggestion is useful is because at times, when I travel, I end up playing on computers that are crushingly slow at refresh. I am sure many others are playing on phones or netbooks, or whatever, that just doesn't have all that much bandwidth or GPU power. Every time you click something, it has to reload all the images of the dinoz and there are 5-10 second delays every time you hit a dialogue toggle or click anything. This makes playing very slow at times. Additionally, the reloading of objects by the server takes up quite a bit more bandwidth, I would imagine. This could help save resources for MT, also, and wouldn't adversely affect anything.

Hopefully this is something that can be considered!

Respectfully,
