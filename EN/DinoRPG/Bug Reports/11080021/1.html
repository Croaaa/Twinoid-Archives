Hi folks,

like everybody on the English server, we argued about the pros and cons of the existing CotC Systems. After a lot of talking, I had the idea about a system, which could provide nearly balanced fights.

It's based on the original powerlimit. First all of the clans who want to participate get a week to register their dinos, creating their powerlevel. There will be a powerlevel ranking afterwards.
Now u create groups of eight clans, starting with the most powerful. e.g. clans 1-8 in Group 1, clans 9-16 in Group 2 etc.

The powerlimit for each group is the powerlimit of the weakest clan. So for Group 1 it's the powerlimit of the clan ranked 8th. The clans get a second week to register their dinos again, using the new powerlimit.

So the weaker clans get the chance to use their best dinos, while the top clans in each group have to overthink their strategy. Fights should get much more exciting this way.

Battles will be the fights against the 7 other clans of each group.

Some open points: Matches will be harder so maybe there should be some fixes:
- change prizes: not only for 4-7 wins, but also for over 200 points in each battle or for more than 2k movements?
- power limit: on the battlefield useful?

I would like to see you guys discussing this idea. Maybe adding some details, or pointing at some more open points.

Ndeline
