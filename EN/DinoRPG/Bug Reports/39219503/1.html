This thread is just about new invocations for new dino that MT might introduce. Here are some of my own:


Medusa: Attacks all enemies with a power of 10 . Petrifies all enemies.
Amun-Ra: Attacks all enemies with a power of 10 . Blinds all enemies.
Grendel: Grants a 20% counter-attack bonus to all allies.
Gyges: Grants immaterial status to all allies.
Hecate: Summons all deceased allies (including dead summoned units like clones) to 25% of their (ignores beer) and attacks all enemies, draining 15% of their total .
Hephaestus: Increases on the caster by 40 but also reduces speed by 25% as well. Dino also shields the weakest ally with the lowest HP (excluding non-dinoz).
