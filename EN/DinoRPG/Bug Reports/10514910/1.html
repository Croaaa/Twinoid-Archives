Since you have one dinoz with the skully bone, you are able to sacrifice even a low level dinoz. Just let him drop under 11 hit points at the frish port, then group him with your dinoz with the skully bone and let them walk together to the cemetary.

Even if he dies in this fight, you might be able to resurrect him with a potion and then sacrifice him. Only the higher level dinoz with the bone has to survive to get both dinoz to the cemetary.
