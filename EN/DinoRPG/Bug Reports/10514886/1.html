Something like this could be useful for beginners who get discouraged with the game due to poorly leveled early dinoz. While the option to reincarnate exists for dinoz that make it to level 40 (and have the necessary lightning levelups), no such option exists for smaller dinoz. Part of the enjoyment of this game comes from raising dinoz you grow to care about. We fall in love with a dinoz due to colors, traits, overall looks. It's a sad fate that many of these dinoz end up frozen or sacrificed, or that a beginner quits the game because the first dinoz they picked turned out to be horrible.

On the other hand, bad luck and sad fates seem to be a part of DinoRPG, and the few masters that succeed are the ones that keep going regardless of such setbacks. However, an option such as this would make for a friendlier game, and one that might retain more customers in the long run.

Reincarnation would still remain as an advantage due to the added element levels.

On a final note, imagine a demon dinoz that hits bad levelups and doesn't have enough lightning to go for reincarnation.
