I'm not suggesting to cut the conversations out. What I mean is to have a selective lite mode that is only in effect during conversations with NPCs. By lite mode, I mean the mode that turns off all the dino icons (the little pictures of the dinos that appear next to their name in the dino list to the left of the screen) and that replaces the "Buy Coins," "Shop," and "My Clan," buttons with small icons. Personally, I have a hard time finding a specific dino when that mode is on, so I don't like to leave it on while making my moves. However, when it's turned on, it cuts the load time of each conversation screen down to about a fourth of what it is when I don't have the lite mode turned on. That's why I was suggesting having another option to only have the lite mode be in effect during conversations, when you don't need the dino pictures or page buttons to be active anyway.
