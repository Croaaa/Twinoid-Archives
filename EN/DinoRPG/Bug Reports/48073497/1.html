Would it maybe be possible to make it so that the one selling can set the time that the order remains available? That way when someone asks to buy something and someone is willing to sell they don't have to wait 3 days before the deal is done.

I understand that similar suggestions have been made in the past, like setting a price at which the offer would be sold immediately to circumvent sniping, but those were never implemented due to a risk of abuse (as i understand it). To reduce that risk of abuse a minimum time (of 2 hours for instance) could be a solution.
