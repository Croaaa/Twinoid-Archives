In topic about community growth I wrote a little aboout how to improve participation rate in Clan Wars.

This topic I created to clarify my idea and improve it in some way
(even though it may never be released).

So, how I see a new system:

1.Clan size should be decreased to 30. Instead new Alliance System should be added. So clans may organise alliances with it's own names and additional alliance forums. Also it should be added Alliance Rankings based on sum of participants points.

2.League System should be added in Clan Wars(Mana Wars etc.). For example Little leagues(1-25 lvl), Amateur League(25-45 lvl) and Pro League(45-60 lvl). After adding lvl 70 it may be changed to 1-30, 30-50 and 60-70.
I used those numbers because original league system is quite unbalanced in my opinion.

3.Prizes should be divided in 2 groups.

First groups is prizes in each league where ranking is based on clan own position. Prizes in that group should be like 1 napo to top 5 clans in Pro league and 1 napo to 1-2 clans in Amateur league plus smaller prizes based on league(DT+TN in Pro, TN in Amateur and Gold/TN in Little).

Second group is prizes to alliances. Those prizes should be greater. For example 2-3 napos to top 3, 1-2 napos to top 10 and 1 napo to top 20.
As additional prizes dinoz eggs or other usefull items(dinoz remodeling or ability to convertate N gold to TN like in master pack, Tickets to different grids etc.) may be given.

4.Alliances should be limited. For example in each Alliance may be only one clan in Pro League, one-two clans in Amateur League and 2-3 clans in Little League. Points may be calculated as 1.0 to Pro league clans, 0.75 to Amateur league clans and 0.5 to Little league Clans. So alliance from 3 Little league clans may get more points in Alliance Rankings then alone Pro clan.

Firstly it will force bigger clans to make alliances with smaller clans. This will particularly solve forum problems because most of active players from smaller clans woulf be able to ask veterans some questions on Alliance forum.

Secondly it will let newbies to get almost as much napos as top players (difference would be 1 napo rewarded to Pro and Amateur leagues). Also Pro clans will become more elite because of fewer free space so it will be worthless to accept weak players into them. At the same time it will make Amateur and Little clans to be much more viable choices.

5.How the system will work during Clan Wars.

A) Little(and Amateur) league clans would be able to ask their ally clans from little, amateur and pro leagues for a help in cost of some points awarded for the win.
BUT(it's most important thing here), those Pro and Amateur players would be able to use only dinoz that fit Little league and wouldn't be able to defend allies castle. Cost of ally involving should be higher depending on league of ally(higher from Little to Pro), but not TOO high to make it still worthy.
Players from any league can involve allies only from equal or higher leagues (it means Pro clan can't involve allies).
Both atacking and defending clan can involve allies.

B)Clans from each league can declare a war only to clans from the same league.
War can't be declared to a clan which is already in war with other Clan. But even during the War clan can help his allies if they ask(asking should be an in-game decision, like a button "ask ally for a help"). So it is impossible to declar a war to several clans, but possible to accept some requests of allies plus declaring one war(or defending from one enemy).

Also it may be an option to declar Huge War to the whole Alliance in much higher cost but with all the dinoz involved. Then you will need to atack not Clan Castle but Alliance Castle with more hp(I think double or even triple) and maybe some improved defence. Also all ally clans can defend Alliance Castle with all their dinoz without any restriction. Clans losing Alliance war won't be able to declar wars during the whole next day. It may be an option to make declaring Alliance war possible when both Alliances agreed to prevent bullying from Stronger Alliances. Also I think Alliance Wars between to Clans should be possible only one time during the whole Clan War. But it should be an option of revenge while the clan-winner can't refuse. So the maximum number between two clans is actually 2. Of course after second lose it will be another day of Peace(unable to declare the war) to losing clan so it prevent silly moves.
Huge war can't be declared while one of a clans is in war with different clan.(For example two alliances want to organise a Huge war, but one of a clans is in war with clan from third Alliance).

C)Players from clans inside one Alliance should be able to freely change clans during the whole war in cost of 1000 gold, but won't be able to change Alliance during the war(they may quit it but won't be able to join it or any other Alliance again). Also they can't be dismissed from clan until war is over. Insted they may be banned temporarily by their clan leader, so all their dinoz can't participate in war(to prevent breaking defence combination by inactive players etc.). Between clan events swapping clans and alliances is free(but still cost 1000 gold).
It will make some strategic maneuvrs possible but prevent abusing.
In addition, players who wasn't part of the clan when the war started should be able to join any clan they want until the war is nearly ended.

D)What about prizes, I had an idea to make the whole prize on the clan instead of priize to each player depending on number of players. But it also should be a minimal prize to each player which is half of usual prize. It will make possible awarding players who participated the most and lowering prize to those who did nothing. Of cource, players who don't like this may create their own clan and make more democratic rules.

E)In addition to point D it may be a clan ranking system when up to 10 members are equal to clan leader and they will decide how to share prizes. Let's call them Lords but each clan may call them as they want(like original status system). They will all be elected by clan leader but after that their votes are equal to leaders votes. It will help to make decisions and improve their status but won't make them much more rights than usual clan administrators(just prestigious status inside clan and maybe twinoid icon).

F)The last but not least, what about how Alliances created. There should be a leading clan who will be able to start Alliance wars and to invite new clans to Alliance. That's why "bigger" Alliances may absorb "little" alliances(for example Alliance from 1 Pro clan may absorb Alliance from 2 Amateur and 3 Little clans even though last is bigger in number). Also decisions about joining alliances, sending invitation or dismissing clan should be accepted by ALL clan leaders or Lords(point E) (one vote from each clan needed, it means that even 1 lord can accept the decision). Clans can't be dismissed from alliance during the war, so you should chose wisely who you will invite in your alliance. Leader clan can't be dismissed.

If all the clans will originally be part of the same alliance(just after the system launch), for example Rakshasa will originally be part of Alliance called Rakshasa etc., such mechanic of joining Alliances will make linking all the clans to global Alliance ranking significally easier. So each clan will automatically be a part of Alliance and if Leader clan leaves alliance it should firstly chose a new leader so the name of alliance will automatically change. And immediately after leaving this clan will join newly created alliance with the same name as this clan.

Also at the beginning of the war Leading clans of alliances fully filled with clans may change the name of alliance and after the war those alliances will get special golden status and may be saved in Hall of Fame if they do well. But it is not important and don't have too much sence, only forcing people to create full alliances.

G)Also it may be a good idea to change cost of creating new clan to 50000 to make it worthy to join other clans instead of creating own(it will help to improve more clans especially in Little league). Joining an alliance may also be paid from clans treasury(for example 10000 cooins). But it may be accepted only after leading clan of alliance you want to join sent you invitation.


In conclusion I need to say that this idea is totally raw and need a lot of improvement(it came to my had just after I read some posts from community grows thread, all ideas I invented today in a few hours, so I never saw how they work). But that's why I posted it here(if it was perfect and totally done I would immediately sent it to Epoq). I hope for your feedback and maybe one day it will appear on our or even on other servers.
