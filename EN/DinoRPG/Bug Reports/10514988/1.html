11. Spoilers

Spoilers for content on the other servers will be deleted. Newly released content for this server may not have solutions posted until after one week from the release. Those who post spoilers will receive sanctions.

The sanctions:
1st offense: warning
2nd offense: 1 day
3rd offense: 3 days
Subsequent offenses: 1 week.

We are delighted to see how you feel about the game and we are honored to see the motivation that drives you when it comes to suggestions.

However:

- Suggestions on adding content (new dinoz, worlds, quests ...) will not be considered by MT.

- Suggestions for changes to the interface can be considered if they do not complicate the basic interface.

- Suggestions for improving the game as it currently exists will be considered.

Suggestions for new content will be closed by moderators, but other suggestions could be discussed (if a negative return is made on the
following suggestions in this regard, they will also be closed).
