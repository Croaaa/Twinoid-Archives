infernal claws is the 3rd level Quetzu (only) skill (on the Strength path)

It makes your Quetzu to ignite an opponent with its assault: the target dinoz will burn with black flames for a short time. During this
- it will suffer damage based on the numbers of its own fire elements
- it will do 1 damage to anythig, that damages it with an assault

so it works like a reversed torch : some damage to the burning dinoz, 1 damage to the attackers (torch does 1 damage to the burning dinoz and about 1/4 fire element dam to other dinoz)
