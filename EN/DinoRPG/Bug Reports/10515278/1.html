For security in probably all MT games, it would be better to have publicly displayed player names, and the actual player login names be different. This would make account hacking/hijacking much harder, and targeting a specific player essentially impossible. A generic login error wouldn't reveal 'whether there was even an account with an attempted the login name', or 'whether there is an account and the password was just incorrect'.

The only way to determine if a login name was already in use would be by attempting to register a new account in that name... which could flag that ip for comparisons with future incorrect password entries on the account with that login name.
