First of all, do not venture into areas with strong monsters without a group. That is how the game works, you are supposed to group your dinos or suffer from strong monsters.

2nd, you are not forced to go to the islands hat level 11. It would be wise to kill green giants until you reach a higher level. You can as well go to the big all hot. Cobraseros don't appear till level 17.

With level 18 you can kill giant chickens in dinoland, which give very good XP till level 22 (and still good XP after that). And with that level borgs will spawn more frequently in the islands.


If you plan accordingly you can avoid Eeloz almost entirely.
