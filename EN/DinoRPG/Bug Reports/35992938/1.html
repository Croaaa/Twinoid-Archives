I like this topic! Here are my takes on them:


4: Smoky Haze - A skill - high endurance cost: all enemies get sun blindness. (Sendos' skill)
5: Death of the Phoenix - P skill: On death the dinoz deals damage with power equal to his x10 to all enemies. Activates every time the dinoz dies.


4: Roots of Amazonia - A skill - high energy cost: All enemies are entangled in roots (Petrified status) for 4 turns. Flying opponents are immune.
5: Nature's Retribution - P skill: Every time an ally Korgon dies a Gorilloz Spirit is summoned.


4: Fog - A skill - medium energy cost: Each ally dinoz gains +10% dodge for 5 turns. Mistral, Tornado and Vacuum Disk removes this effect.
5: Maelstrom - A skill - very high energy cost: Activates two random skills of level 3 or lower (they can be from any element). This skill's energy cost pays the energy cost of the other two skills.


4: Dance of Death - P skill: Adds two more assaults to Lightning Dance skill.
5: Seance - A skill - very high energy cost: Summons the spirit of a dead ally. It has equal to the summoner's element. It is Immaterial and can use skills as the original dinoz. Can be used only once on each ally.


4: Eagle Strike - P skill: element assaults and assaults with Plunging Attack can't be dodged.
5: Armor of Slashing Winds - P skill: Works like Torch. When deactivated by Primal State it is reactivated with the use of Tornado skill.
