This thread is a contribution against Lorie Custome (later denoted as LC). Please read this thread and let me explain why LC makes the game less enjoyable for some players.

I start with the comparison of Lorie Custome and Sorcerer's Wand (SW). The 1st item used by most of the players during the IDT. The 2nd one is an item from the magic shop that costs 7 napodinos. It destroys 30 percent of a random dino's . Its quite obvious this item is worthwhile against dinos with big like dinos with brave and sumo or colossus skills. Just to mention a few of them; softpig, moueffe, quetzu. So a player with a SW against these dinos has a good chance to inflict a huge amount of damage in case of the activation of SW with the aid of some luck to choose the right target.

Even so LC is much more effective against dinos with these skills. Let me explain why;
Mostly dinos with these skills aren't capable of aquiring high initiative. So the opponents of these dinos can use many LC before their turn comes. If it happens they will inflict big damage with their main element to each other. And thats not all. Most of the damage comes after this. In case of softpig and moueffe (even some type of quetzu) there is a big chance to attack back with counter attack to each other with their main element as well. Quetzu has another big disadvantage against LC. This is its 'Infernal claws' skill. It makes its own teammates to burn and receive damage in every turn. Dinos with 'Torch' skill suffer great losses against LC too. In these cases dinos receive huge amount of damage just from one type of item.
There are other side effects as well. Like dinos cancels its teammate's vaporus form if it has the ability to do so.

My conclusion is that there was already a magic item costed 7 napodinos that was made against a special type of opponents but during the developing of the game LC was released. Now LC totally prevents the opportunity to use that kind of teams.

LC ruines the game experience for players trying something new and special. I ask the Developers to rethink or remove LC from the game to aid the viability of special and unique teams!
