You should not have multiple dinoz with different levels of wood-search skills searching in the same grid.

Other than that, wood grids contain a lot less ingredients compared to other element grids (but the ingredients are of higher value). I think This Voodoo page shows the best and most up to date information about probabilities of ingredients in grids.
