I was wondering, perhaps we could develop a system of guilds for all the clans. Here's how it goes:
You only choose one guild
The guild will provide you a special power in a certain area during the wars
3 levels (1st is free, next 2 are quite expensive)
You can choose to abolish the guild, however, the next guild you choose will cost ingredients

Types of guilds:

Builder's guild:
Provides a total of 33% ingredients discount on upgrading
Castle repairs are free
Decreased building time of upgrades by 50%

Soldier's guild:
When defending your castle, your dinoz gains additional 3 armor.
Castle damage increased by 25%

Element Master's guild:
When attacking, special A or E skills (e.g. lightning, cold shower, burning breath etc.) deal 20% more damage
When attacking or defending, dinoz gain 33% poison damage resistance
Status buffs like acupuncture, torch, hermetic aura, etc, are 50% more effective

Intellect's guild:
When attacking your dinoz gains 25% more
When defending your dinoz gains 100% more

Pirate's guild:
When attacking your dinoz gain 200% more at the end of a fight
Enables your clan to steal some ingredients from your enemy after defeating them

Ninja's guild:
When attacking or defending, dinoz gain 15% more chance to multi-hit, counter attack and dodge. However, A or E skills deal 33% less damage.

Well, what do you think, guys?
