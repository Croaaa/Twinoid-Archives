Meeting them at lvl 4 is just bad luck, nothing else.
Your dinoz is no weakling, lvl 4s almost always die against glouns, especially if its a multielemental dino or a rocky, which lacks 1 additional starting element.
Thats one of the reasons, to level dinoz in groups ( teams of 3 are best grouped at lvl 2). I know that its hard to afford 3 dinoz at once, if you dont got enough dinoz to generate enough gold, but you should aim for that.
If thats not possible, there ARE some workarounds, like doing missions with scripted/triggered fights to level up, since you avoid meeting gluons while doing them.
Those would be:
Joes: Hills smashrooms, Wolf hunting, and stamps. This should be enough to get to lvl 5 or 6, until then, you can even consider equiping a refridgerated shield/ration and go for the elemental early, to get another level up (be at 50% health for that, of course). Afterwards, gluons should be no problem anymore.
