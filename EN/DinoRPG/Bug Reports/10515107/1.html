The CotC is over now for most of us, so lets begin to talk about the new war system.

My toughts:

The war system should be made more clan oriented and should not allow individual members defeat a clan alone. With the old system if a clan had one member with 5 realy strong dinoz, he can destroy almost any castle alone. If they would change the waiting time between 2 attacks from 10 minutes to 1 hour, then one master would need to stand at the walls for 6-7 hours to destroy it, giving time to the defending clan to put in more defenders etc. This would push clans to make organized attacks with more members participation.

Another thing which would make the war better in my oppinion, if it would be some time, lets say one hour, what should be elapsed between the declaration of the war against a clan and the first attack on there walls. This would prevent the gang ups on one clan and would gave some time to the defenders to prepare.
