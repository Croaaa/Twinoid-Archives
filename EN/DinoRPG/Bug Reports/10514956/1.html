Yes, I am aware that MT won't give a damn in implementing them, it's just for people to think about and consider them. These ideas for based on the original castle upgrades. They are 2 levels after some upgrades. I don't know what the price for them would be but let's say that they are double the price of level 3.

Here is the list of ideas:

Active defence:
Cage large smashroom: a large smashroom from Dinoplaza defends the castle. Takes 40 hours to build and is twice the cost of a cage korgon.
Cage dark smashroom: a dark smashroom defends the castle. Takes 50 hours to build and is 5 times the cost of a cage korgon.

Attacks deceleration:
Wetland: attackers take 40 minutes to recover from an attack. All attacking dinoz suffer -3 initiative penalty. Takes 150 hours to build and costs twice as much as marsh.
Quicksand: attackers take 50 minutes to recover from an attack. All attacking dinoz suffer -5 initiative penalty and -33% speed. Takes 250 hours to build and costs 6 times as much as marsh.

Passive defence:
Thorny bushes: attackers receive 1 - 4 contact damage when attacking the castle. Takes 5 hours to build.
wooden stakes: attackers receive 4 - 7 contact damage when attacking the castle. Takes 15 hours to build.
Metal spikes: Attackers receive 7 - 10 contact damage when attacking the castle. Takes 40 hours to rebuild.

I'm certain more castle upgrades would be introduced sooner and later.
