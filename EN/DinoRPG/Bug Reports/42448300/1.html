The first section are small changes that are possible and the second is just things that I think should happen that probably won't. Not posting for a week or two, thank you for reading!
Old Skills:
Stealth P - Increases Air, Water, Lightning defense by 2
Elasticity P - Increases the strength of Air attacks by 10 and Air defense by 3.
Achilles' Heel P - Increases the strength of all assaults by 2
Awakening P - Decreases the speed of Air attacks by 20%
Lonely meditation P - Increases Air defense by 3. Decreases the speed of air assaults by 50%
Transcendental Meditation P - Increases air defense by 6. decreases the speed of air assaults by 50%
Force Control S - Dinoz deals at least 10 damage on an opponent with assault.
Tireless P - Increases all defenses by 2.(Requires Aqueous Clones)

Changes to Skills:
Reasons:
- Slight buff to air dinoz, especially Kabukis which roll more air than needed.
- Air heavy dinoz lack HP and may be slower.
- Small nerf to Force Control.
- Tireless is a useless skill.
Stealth P - Increases Air defense by 5, Lightning defense by 10 and Water defense by 15.
Elasticity P - Increases the strength of Air attacks by 10 and Air defense by 5.
Achilles' Heel P - Increases the strength of all assaults by 3.
Awakening P - Decreases the speed of Air attacks by 10%.
Lonely meditation P - Increases Air defense by 10. Decreases the speed of air assaults by 20%.
Transcendental Meditation P - Increases air defense by 15. decreases the speed of air assaults by 30%.
Force Control S - Dinoz deals at least 8 damage on an opponent with assault.
Tireless P - Increases all defenses by 8.(Requires Aqueous Clones)

Unlikely changes that I'd want to see:
-These bottom suggestions are unlikely because numbers are easier to change(I agree, no one wants to rewrite code with the chance of making more bugs.)
