I feel like there is currently no enough game mechanics to promote community.
I mean, beeing in a clan gives you, of course, a "team" to participate in PvP events like wars and championships.
But aside from that, there is very little advantages.
- A clinic that is so out of the way that it's barely used.
- Some people to help with the portal (but now you can skip that with Golum's help and 10k)
- Some clan pages and forums (not really necessary with the fan sites...)
- and basically a community that is completely based on the players.


Other online games usually have a lot more guild benefits, like housing, craft table, bonus, etc...

In this game, you can't even give a fellow clanmate an item!


So i figure, what if there was some "clan items" that could be purchased with the clan treasury or certain special player donations, that would give bonus to the entire clan? You could have some small things, and some really expensive ones that clans could aim for. This would promote recruiting and joining clans.
Exemples of things to buy:


Clan Library ( bonus)

Level 1: Book room. Cost: 300000 from treasury. All clan Dinoz gain a 3% bonus.
Level 2: Book Hall. Cost: 600000 from treasury. Must have book room. All Clan Dinoz now gain 3% more (total 6%)
Level 3: Book Tower. Cost 1 million from Treasury. Must have book hall. Raise the bonus to 9%


Demonic Sigil (demon tickets discount)

Any clan members can donate demon tickets toward acquiring sigils.
Level 1: Lesser Sigil. Cost: 1000 demon tickets. 10% reduction of Demon prices.
Level 2: Great Sigil. Cost: 2500 demon tickets. 15% reduction of Demon Price. Bonus 10% tickets gained from sacrifices.
Level 3: Greater Sigil. Cost 5000 demon tickets. 20% reduction of Demon Price. Bonus 15% tickets gained from sacrifices.


Elemental Master Privilege
Clan Members are now offered 2 sphere choices and must pick 1 when completing the EM missions.
Cost: 100 spheres (players can donate sphere toward this cost).

Boon of health ( regen bonus)
Cost: 250000 from fish ingredients only. Effect: all clan dinoz regenerate 1 extra when resting per hour.

Storage unit
each master has 1 more dinoz slot.
Cost: 4 millions in treasury.
If a member leaves the clan, the last dinoz on his list will be automatically frozen.


Ok, these are just exemples, an far from perfect. It's not about the exemples, it's about the IDEA of having more goals for clans, and more benefits for players.
