Well I got the idea a bit from the Die2Nite bank. Every person in the clan can donate coins and objects to their clan, (much like the treasury but without ingredients). All this stuff goes into the clan "Vault". The clan leader can distribute these items, and maybe a few others that have the right, (like being able to accept and refuse members). It would be a great way to stockpile items like irmas and warm breads for wars.

Of coarse there would have to be an anti-abuse system on it, like a clan member can only be given items and gold up to the amount of 5000 per day. If its calculated like the market place, it shouldn't be that hard.

It would increase the benefits of being in a clan, and would help to build up smaller members and make the clan stronger overall.

The only problem might be people making multiple accounts to get more money. But there are limits on joining a clan, and as this is already an existing problem, there wouldnt be that much difference.

Well thats my idea. Does anyone have any ideas or ways of improving this system?
