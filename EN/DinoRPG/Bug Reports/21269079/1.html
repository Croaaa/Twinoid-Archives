[8847]

I've read a lot of different ideas about TM, and I think it SHOULD be changed a bit.
Most of statemens have a good ideas inside, but there are some problems.

1.TM shouldn't block any spherical spells.
This is a very nice idea, since there are just too much things that counter spheres(for now the only speheres that are really useful is lightning+3water, all other are slightly useless now). It will be honest to leave sphere skills unaffecte by TM, but there are just one problem. For some dinoz Liquid stumps and Detonation are just much more powerful than for others and they have no real counters(like any other ini spells). I think a perfect decision will be just making an additional type of spells for spherical spells so TM won't block it completely but just weaken(there are not that much spherical spells so it shouldn't be too hard).

2.It should help more the owner rather than both players.
I completely disagree with that. TM together with 50+ spells opened a HUGE variety of possible builds for nearly EVERY dino in the game. Even though classic builds are still much stronger w/o TM, it helps dinoz with alternative builds to win them. Before TM and energy appeared such spells as vines, aqueos clones, cold shower, and ESPECIALLY spherical spells were too much overpowered. Because it granted tons of free damage on enemy team and vines or liquid stumps were just too much disbalanced. So every dino SHOULD have got them. Now with TM you can chose between two ways of training dino: classic way, wich is still much stronger w/o TM or TM way, wich grant bonuses against classis builds when using TM. And what about 60 or 80% blocking chance, it breaks the whole idea of TM, because if in this 20% it will be cold shower for example, there is no difference, but if it would be liquid stumps or vines it may be deadly for TM owner. And when you have proper build, there is just no IMPORTANT e-spells to use, so you don't need this 60% chance, you need 100% for the enemy.
This is also a reason why classic spells such as primal state shouldn't be blocked. It is powerful enough for the fights w/o TM, because it grants an advantage to non-TM dinoz, and nerfing it will break more than half of the advantage(since for some dinoz(torch ones) other e-spells such as vines are not so deadly).

3.Make TM work only on Primal state.
It is not good idea at all, because there are a lot of builds for TM for non-Torch dinoz. Also solution can be just rebalancing Primal state and Magic Resistance/Saving puree. Maybe Primal should be as it is, since it is usefull in non-TM fights. Magic Resistance can become and A-spell that will works just like tricky hits but instead will remove positive effects from dino. Saving puree can work just like Primal but being A-spell as well. It will make those spells much more viable and particularly help to counter TM.
About idea of recasting torch as E or A spell and making TM work like nimbao potions. Torch can be recasted with lighter. If it will work only for 3 turns it will be useless.

4.Little suggestion about beer and passive damage from Kantid.
I totally agree that beer should nerf heal instead of total removing(make it for example -75%(50 is too low) rounded up(so acupuncture still will be blocked). It will make occasional use of healing items not so dreadful and will still help to prevent OP healing with leafy dawn.

5.TM shouldn't affect summons.
It might be a good idea, but there are just 1 slot for magic item on 1 dino. For classic TM team must have items are TM+Beer+Antimono. Most teams nowadays consist of 3 dino. And even if you have more than 3 dinoz in the team, there are much better choices like mask, fireband(since TM-teams mostly include softpigs) or maybe antidote for winks. So making an additional important item will be bad idea and hurt TM-teams hard.
Also banishment have some advantages as it also blocks bambooz(as I expect), summon items and hunting glove monsters. So it still may be used in builds where you don't want to block e-spells, but just summons.
That's why I don't like idea to prevent TM blocking summons, but I think Tear of Life should break through TM since it is also magic item and it has the same(or higher?) cost. But as well it should be countered as banishment(since it has a power to block summons, so it is MADE for this, not like TM), so it will be a really usefull coutering item.

6.Idea about torching item.
I don't like it at all as we already have lighters and removing TM will just block A LOT of different variants of builds. Also you can just get this items and save few lvlups for torch.

Conclusion: 1. I think TM shouldn't be removed or changed hard because:
1)it works perfectly preventing such an OP spells as Vines, Liquid Stumps, Primal State and others
2)it also grants a lot of different build for EACH dino(I have some pretty decent TM builds even for gorillos(!))).
3)it actually weakens classic builds against TM-builds, but if(for example) TM-carry dies, classic team will have MUCH higher chanes to win. And while just weakening old and completely discovered builds, it also adds a lot of new builds, which is totally a good side.
2. I think there should be some spells that can go through TM and help against TM dinoz(as I suggested, it will be nice to change Magic resistance or Saving Puree).
3. The last problems is spherical spells but I think it may be solved by just adding to them 50% resistance for TM(so the spell will be just 50% weaker). By the way, for medium players it is also a good side of TM, since they don't need to buy all the spheres to be competitive.
