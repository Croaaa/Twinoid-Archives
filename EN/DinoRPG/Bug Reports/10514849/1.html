This one has been something - that at least personally - appears to be in slight in balance from the rest of the game.

A good example of this would be when my clan was attacked by BBC. During our wars against them I know that I fought at one time against Gunja's attack team and walked away with close to 400, however when he attacked us, and no one on our defense died those five dino only cleared 3 each.

This is unfair to those who dedicate dinos to defense during a war, and does not sit well in the delicate balance that the game has strives for.

In another thread I have made two suggestions on how to adjust this issue.

1) - Straight up increase in the amount of XP that defending dinos gain for each successful repelled attack.

or

2) - A method of xp gain for each "time frame" or attack survived that the defending dino goes through.

Either one I think will also be incentive for alot clans to put up a defensive line which is what makes the wars fun
