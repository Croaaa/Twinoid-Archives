Unlike epic fights between wanwans, rockies and mouffetes, filled with fire, explosions, lightnings and meteors, where one of the sides gets completely destroyed in a couple of minutes after an intense and furious fight which is awesome to watch, fights against (and gods forbid, among) bubbled dinoz are a deathly boring exchange of 1-dmg-hits and 0-dmg-hits (to vaporous dinoz), typically ending when the timer runs out and not when one side actually wins.
