What you're asking for already exist...

U-skills benefits to your whole account, U-skills are only needed once, and thus having more than one dinoz having the same U-skills doesn't stack (U stands for Unique after all). Here's the list:

- Savagery -> Tenacity -> Charisma -> Leader: Increase maximum dinoz amount by 3. You can have 21 dinoz instead of 18.
- Savagery -> Korgon Reinforcement -> Wild Instinct -> Engineer: All Dinoz can be equipped with an additional combat gear.
- Water Cannon -> Cold Shower -> Sapper -> Storekeeper: Increase maximum storable items by 50% in your inventory.
- Mutation -> Ventral Pouch -> Sumo -> Cooker: Increases the life gain of the items by 10%.
- Focus -> Regeneration -> Saving Puree -> Benediction -> Priest: Increases by 1 the number of life gain by resting.
- Intelligence -> Lightning Conductor -> Elemental Fission -> Merchant: Reduces by 10% the prices in the Dinoland Flying Shop.
- Mistral -> Tai-Chi -> Awakening -> Professor: Add 5% of experience at end of battle for each dinoz.

You can only sell at the dinoz market (at Market Place). Flying Dinoland Shop items cannot be sold at that market. Your selling have to reach a minimum of 5.000 value and the minimum price the motor is setting can't be reduced by any means.

There's already epics gained by doing quests/missions. Please play the game before suggesting...

U-skills benefits to your whole account, U-skills are only needed once, and thus having more than one dinoz having the same U-skills doesn't stack (U stands for Unique after all).
Although U Skills do not Stack with themselves, they do Stack for a given Dinoz with other Skills or Items that the Dinoz might have equipped, eg. Professor (U Skill) Stacks with Intelligence ( Skill) and an equipped Encyclopedia (Magic Item) giving an boost of +5% +5% +15%).
