On may 5, 2010
AndySQdancer
Which I believe works out that your Dinoz gets a turn every two days as well as defend. It's better than no turns at all. Also, if you have several Dinoz defending, you can do it with all of them--just remember to 1st take them all off defense, then perform the Fight for all of the defenders, press New Turn, then put all the Dinoz back on defense. Only real bad thing about doing this is that the Dinoz always return defending at the end of the defenders list and have to be moved up if you want them somewhere else in the list of defenders.
