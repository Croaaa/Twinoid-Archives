Zephides
5) The Askpouk Conspiracy
-> Go To LavaPit
-> Go To Ashpouk Ruins and wait 10 hours
-> Go To Deep Gorges
-> Find the right place (LavaPit) and fight with the suspect
-> Go To BruteForce
-> Find the right place (Ashpouk Ruins) and wait 5 hours
-> Go To LavaPit and fight with 3 suspects
-> Go To BrutForce and Validate Mission


You will need the Lantern to get to the Deep Gorges.

So, you will need to get a Shovel from the Coral Miner, dig at the Sticky Swamp, Fountain of Youth and Basalt Slopes (remember repair after you dig each time). Then get to the Underwater Dome (with your Scrobscuba caller), fight Earth Elemental. Talk to Master Hydargol and get Waterlilly Pads. Enter Grumhell Forest, go to Haunted Hills and talk the to guy up there and then... finally... you have your Lantern. Tho you will need to go all the way back via Islands, Dinotown, Volcane and such...
