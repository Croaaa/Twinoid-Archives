To acces you'll need the Zors Gloves and the Waterlilly Pads. But to get get the Zors Gloves you will need a Scorbscuba Caller first. Here's how to get it:

Scorpscuba Caller :
- Go to the Mutant Falls and talk to the Atlantean Guard
- Go to Frish Port and talk to Joveboze
- Fight a big fish and return to the Atlantean Guard

Zor's Gloves :
- Get a shovel from Coral Mine and dig up 3 items.
You can find these at:
The Sticky Swamp
Fountain Of Youth
Basalt Slopes
- Move to the Underwater Dome
- Talk to the ArchSage and fight the earth elemental
Remember you will need to repair your Shovel every time you dug something up

Waterlilly Pads :
- Go to Mutant Falls and talk to Master Hydragol

Lantern :
- Go to the Grumhel Forest and then the Haunted Hills
- Talk to the Strange Man and fight a korgon
Makes you able to go to the Deep Gorges
