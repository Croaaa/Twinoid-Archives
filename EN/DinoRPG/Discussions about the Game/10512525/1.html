Thursday 27 October 2011 at 17:30

Hello everyone!

From the 1st to the 15th of November at 14h server time, take part in the Dinotown festival of film, this event will show who is the most talenter producer/director in all of Dinoland!

For this event and until the 15th of November, Spileberk, the director, has made some additional resources available: The scenery, monsters and objects from the Dark World will be available for the duration of this event!


The best director will be awarded the golden film.
and a bonus of 100 000 gold coins!
The 2nd best director will win 50 000 gold coins.
The 3rd best director will win 30 000 gold coins.
The 4th best director will win 20 000 gold coins.
The 5th best director will win 10 000 gold coins.
From 6th to 10th place, the directors will receive 5 000 gold coins.
Please note! All films created during the event will be entered for the Festival, but once the film has been created, you will have the option of removing your film from the Festival entries should you just want to make a normal film.

Edit: Dinoz masters will be responsible for nominating the top 10 films, at which point the moderation team will take over, and work out the ranking of each film!

Lights, cameras, ACTION! And may the best film win!
