"all the other dinoz" means exactly that -- all the -other- dinoz in the group, including friendly reinforcements (e.g. korgons, gorilloz spirit), but not the dino who activates the skill.

Generally a dino cannot be healed for more damage than it has taken in the current battle. So if a friendly dino has taken 5 damage, and then a 7 dino activates Precocious Spring, the amount healed would be 5, not 7.
