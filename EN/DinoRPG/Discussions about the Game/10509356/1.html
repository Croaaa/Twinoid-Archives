Here is the quick guide, only the location:

1. Forest warden: jummin river, sticky swamp, fountain of youth and deep gorge

2. The Green: jummin river, Edge of the jungle

3. Permit to cut: sinister path

4. King of the jungle: haunted hill, edge of the forest (forest edge), haunted hill.

5. make your wish: mutant falls, edge of the forest, sinister path, jummin reiver, wild of the jungle. lots of kill monsters on this mission.

6. jack of the forest: Ashpouk ruin

7. Currency monkey: nowhere.

Good Luck...
