This is an observation I've made regarding attack order.

On the details screen of your pet page when you put your mouse over the HELP box for Assaults, it states, "While your dinoz attacks a creature, he uses these assault values: the strongest first, then the weakest."

In actuality, your pet's attack order is not based on the assault values. Rather, the order of attacks is based on the value of each element you have.

For example, I have two of the fire element and three of the wood element. Because of the passive skill Burning Claws, my fire assault value is 17 (2x5+7). However, my wood element is only 15 (3x5). According to the help tip, fire should go first; the fire assault value is higher than the wood assault value.

But in battles, wood goes before fire, likely due to the fact that my wood element is of a higher value than my fire element.

Again, I'm not complaining; this is just an observation.
