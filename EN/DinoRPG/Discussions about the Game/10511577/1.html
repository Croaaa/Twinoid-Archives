LS_and_berli
The advice about the U skills is good.

Look at Priest (for healing while resting) and Merchant (for cost of buying irmas and health items later - wait until you have more dinoz) as the first two U-skills to get (and a glid is good for both of these).
Then consider Leader (to increase maximum number of active dinoz), Professor (to increase experience gain in fights) and
Cooker (healing items give more ).

So this could mean possibly
Gorilloz
Sirain
Cloudoz or Pteroz (more costly - stick with cloud I would suggest)

If I were starting over again I might just get them in exactly that order.
