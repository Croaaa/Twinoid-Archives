AndySQdancer
I did not know the trick by that name either, Fiera. Reason it does not work on other servers, is that on the other servers you get your New Turn at 0:00 server time--time the New Turn Clock Timer is reset every 24 hours. Thus you have to take your turn within a 24 hour period or lose it.

On our, English server, you get your New Turn whenever you push the New Turn Button and restart the Timer Clock for the next New Turn (23 hours or more later). Thus you can save your current turn longer than 24 hours as long as you have not pressed the New Turn Button. Therefore while resting, you can rest more than 24 hours and still have your current turn preserved for when you stop resting. Which puts you in the situation of having the current turn available for the dinoz and also having the New Turn Button available to take the next turn. On the other servers, there is no New Turn Button, system automatically presses it for you at 0:00 serve time.

Really not a trick, just a feature of the game on the English server.
