On november 6, 2015
kiroukou
Grand Contaminateur

Hi all Dinoz Masters !

First of all, as you certainly know, english isn't my primary language, so please keep it in mind while reading

For those who don't know me yet, I'm the lead developer of DinoRPG for many years now.
And as you recently learned, Gyhyom who is the main visual artist of DinoRPG has left the company. This means I'm the only one leading the future of your favorite game !

I wanted to come here and share with you what to expect in the near future from the team, because, the game is far from being abandoned or over !

an API system, which will allow devs among you to develop new tools. That may be simple Dino management tools, or app to levelup or even to organize your clan during events.
This will come quite rapidly and will be improved with first feedbacks.

a new system to translate our content ! As you may understand, translating a game is something complex, because there's so much specific vocabulary.
So far, we had Epoq who learned all our specifics tools, and knew the game universe.
The transition for Buzzard is quite hard on that aspect, and he also have much more responsabilities.

So how to make the new content available to other servers, with quality and correct delays? That's the question which is in our mind for the latest weeks.

The option we have in mind, is to develop a full system, a brand new one, which would allow community, you, to translate as easily as possible. Because no one will know the game as you guys do.

events, quests etc will still continue to exists and evolve. The only thing that will change is is graphic artist style.


So I really hope you master do not panic or anything The knowledge is still here, the team loves that game (8 years !) and is ready to continue make your Dinos even more Badass !

So, I'm wishing you all a very nice week-end and be sure Buzzard will come to you pretty soon with some news and a better English
