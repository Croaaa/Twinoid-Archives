Ratushebarl

On average, the number of draws needed to get 1 special item out of a bag of 100 is 50.5.

Snowplough tickets average 5 t-notes apiece on the market, so if you bought them all the average cost of getting a rare kabuki that way would be about 250 notes. In practice you get 10 or so tickets free (thanks devs!!), so it's really only about 200 notes.

So if all you care about is the kabuki, and you can pay less than 200 for it on the market, it's a good deal.

Of course, that way you don't get all the other schwag those 50 tickets would have turned up! The rare santaz alone is worth 60-80 notes. Which jibes with the fact that the actual price of a rare kabuki egg on the market is 120-180. The invisible hand has worked its magic.

On the other hand, if you're only going to do this once, buying the egg gives you a 100% chance of getting it, rather than the 50% shot that you get by buying tickets.

Please carry on. Just thinking out loud.
