Nope, it can be argued that the defination of Rest:
If your Dinoz has very few health points, he can Rest. Resting enables him to recover health points over time; however, he cannot make any action while he rests.

Initially, your Dinoz recovers 1 per hour, but it is possible to improve the rate of recovery with specialized skills.

You can stop resting at any time, but your Dinoz can't recover more than half of his health points by resting.

So it states that "however, he cannot make any action while he rests." and use of the Pearl or Tik Bracelet could be considered another action. It also states that "but your Dinoz can't recover more than half of his health points by resting." so as long as your dinoz is in Rest he cannot go over 50% health.
