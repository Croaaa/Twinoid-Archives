What does the tik bracelet do?
On october 16, 2010
Zephides
It gives 10 HP at 0:00 midnight (server time) to the Dinoz who is equipped with it.
On october 16, 2010
[891599]
Every day, at 0:00 server time, it heals 10HP of the dino that is currently wearing it in its inventory.
On october 16, 2010
AndySQdancer
However, as long as a Dinoz is in a Resting State, the Max. recovered is 50% of Full and overrides recovery of via Tik Bracelet or Fountain. So if wearing the Tik Bracelet and Resting, remember to Stop Resting at 50% if later you want to use the powers of the Tik Bracelet or Fountain to heal beyond 50% of Full at server time 00:00.
