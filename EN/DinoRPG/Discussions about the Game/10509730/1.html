The earliest I would group a dino is level 2.

If they are 4 or lower, never group them with a dyno level 5 or higher. The chance of a gluon spawning and doing serious damage to the lower level dino is too great.

The more dinos you have, the quicker you can afford a new dino, the less of a gap there will be, eventually.

Now, I get a new dino when my latest one gets around level 3.

In a group, I make the lowest level dyno the leader, and make movement decisions based on what the leader needs for a quest. Any followers will do quests as they can while following the leader. Also, I carefully pick which quests to do in what order to try and make the dinos in a group have the same quest, if at all possible, at least some of the time.
