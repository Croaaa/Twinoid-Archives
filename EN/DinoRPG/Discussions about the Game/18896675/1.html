At first, you need a Dojo. You'll get that at Bruteforce from Master Zenith. Build it anywhere
You'll get a "dojo"-button next to your "my clan" button. Every 8th of each month the dojo-qualification starts. you can chose some of your dinos and fight against dinos of other masters. i've you win, you get some points and the 256 masters with the most points take part in the tournament.

TIP: don't buy and build it untill you have your 21 dinos, they are more important. you won't be able to win a single fight untill you reach lvl 50 either way.
