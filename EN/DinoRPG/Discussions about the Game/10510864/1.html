The 5 Goblin Merguez go in your Inventory. Equip them on all your Dinoz. Each time they fight they have a chance to eat one; it gives 1 to 4 (usually 1 or 2) hp back. If you equip all 5, just ask for more, they are free. Any Dinoz at the Ruins can collect them for any Dinoz to use them.

Make sure you keep 3 in your inventory when you do the quest.
