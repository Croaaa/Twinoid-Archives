From FR rules:

Starting1st Castle building fees: 10.000.
Starts with 100.
Attacks generate around 10% of opponent mana pool. But you gain that mana only after winning the war, in which you add 5% steal.
As long as you castle is standing you gain 1 every two minutes.WarsDuration: 40 hours.
You can declare on clans with even or less mana than yours.
An attacked clan can counter-attack on same war duration.
You can change clans once, up to before the last 25+X days of war.
The war finish as soon as your or their castle is destroyed.
Building the castle isn't automatic anymore.
You cannot forfeit a war.
You have to wait 10 minutes after declaring before being able to attack.
Counter-attacks gives less mana than a true war.
If no one won, no mana.
You can buy spells with ingredients.
Absolute Terror can only be used once per day.The CastleYou need to be at least 10 members in your clan to build your castle.
You can park, up to 50, dinoz to repair the castle, at a rate of 1hp/h (starting from the time you send it to repair the walls).
Unlimited number of defenders.
Addendum: no castles in west-dinoland. Even if it's said so.DeclaringTo be able to declare, you need to not have done 3 wars in a same day (server time), to have your castle built, enough to pay the fee, no other war declared, and enough honour.
Declaring fees depends on your mana pool.HonourYour clan begins at 100 honour points and max at 200.
Each day (following day of your first building), the taxman will come collect a tax paid in ingredients (depends on your mana pool). If you pay, you will receive directly 5 honour points, if you don't, he will come back at midnight (server time) he takes 5% of your mana (cumulative).
You can attack a clan if you have more honour or equal amount to target clan.
If attacked clan is 5 ranks above yours, you will win +1 honour, and every 5 ranks, up to +10.
If attacked clan is 5 ranks below yours, you will lose -1 honour, and every 5 ranks, down to infinite losses (until you reach 0 honour).
Declaring a clan which is already in war, will make you lose honour. Same goes if you declared clan was attacked by you within 4 days, and even more on same day.
Gains or loss are shown on declaring page. Honour is gained only if the war is won, while loss is subtracted directly on declaration.FightsFights are to death, no time limit.
Survivors will have same health points from entering to exiting one fight. Please note defenders have a limited health recovery.
You can reequip survivors.
Damage dealt on wall by dinoz are 10 and from other 5.
30s between attacks, 2 minutes cooldown for your own team.
Defenders are at least 2, 3 with Defensive Reinforcements, and temporary more with Mercenaries.
Dead dinoz will come back at full health after a war is done, spawning back at your castle or in defence for defenders.Defence
There can't only be 2 dinoz from a same master in one fight for defence.
Each defender got 4 health recovery points, which is lost at each fight won by defending.
When those points reach 0, the recovery rate diminish by 10% at each fight won.
Your dinoz recover those points every 2 minutes without a fight within one minute.
If you take your dinoz out of defence, you need 10 minutes before putting it back in.

Here's the list of changes from FR (version number is from FR too):
Features kept from MW3.2 (or 2nd-EN-MW) You can reduce the castle repair cost by putting repairers to repair your castle basement.
You can see the tax collector approximative time even if you're destroyed.New features presented in the gazette, MW4.0 You can check the magic item equipped by hovering over a dinoz name on the castle defense tab.
A rebuilt castle has an hour immunity before being target-able. Your immunity is cancelled if your clan declare within this hour.
One player can't move more than 3 times from clan to clan for the whole war period, and recruitment will be locked on the last 15 days of the event.
Allies will share their gains if they attack together one target by 50/50 (numbers by default, can be changed).
Heroics and spells return.
Counter-attack gives now 50% (default).
Unpaid tax, will now remove -5% mana per day, and is cumulative.Details added in the rules, MW4.1aStealing is now scaled down by the numbers of attacks made:
Less than 20 attacks: 5% opponent clan mana theft, more than 120: 0%.
To build a castle you need a minimum of 10 players in the clan.
Minimum defenders is 2 instead of 3.
Honour loss while declaring on a clan already in war depends on the number of attacks made on it.Spells and cost, MW4.1aCost are in gold sum, paid with the castle treasury by exchanging an equal (or more) value in ingredients.
Permanent spells are removed as soon as the castle is destroyed.
Spells are (using the support list as a base for names):
Mercenaries (10.000): Increases the minimum number of defenders for the next attack by two.
Defensive Reinforcements (30.000, permanent): Increases the minimum number of defenders for all attacks by one.
Thousand-year Drought (5.000): During the next battle, the water element of all fighters will be reset to zero.
High Voltage (5.000): During the next battle, the lightning element of all fighters will be reset to zero.
Time Shield (20.000, once per day): Protects your castle from enemy attacks for one hour.
Red Respray (20.000, permanent): Give your castle a red tint. It doesn't do much but at least it looks good.
Black Beauty (50.000, permanent): Give your castle a dark tint. It doesn't do much but at least it looks good.
Morale Boost (40.000, permanent): Doubles the morale point gain after a battle.
Abomination (5.000): Prepares a special welcome for the next attackers!
Absolute Terror (5.000, once per day): An attacker will flee the battlefield at the start of the next combat (as long as they are not alone).
Mirrored Shield (5.000): Instead of hypnotising an enemy Dinoz, the next Dinoz to use Hypnosis will join the opposing team.Some spells can stack, some not.
