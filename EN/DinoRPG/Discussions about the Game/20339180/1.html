The 2nd Great Mana War is over!

...and to the victor go the spoils. Once more, this victor is a clan who know no fear, a clan formed in 2011 which counts amongst its members some of the wisest and most feared dinoz masters ever, a clan with a notorious leader in the legendary Rjun74... Rākshasa!!!

Rākshasa narrowly beat LOoT (The Last Order of Transylvania) for the top spot in what has to be one of the tightest finishes in the history of the server with the ever-present Magyar Királyság rounding out the top 3!

Peace (or something like it) has returned. How long it will last is anyone's guess... but at least for now everything can get back to almost-normal!

Well well Dinoz Masters! That was a war which sparked a lot of conversation and controvery... but at the end of the day, it comes down to the numbers, and here they are!
