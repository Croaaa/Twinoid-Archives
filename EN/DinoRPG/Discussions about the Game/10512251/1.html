For a First Dino, I would suggest not a Gorilloz or Castivorous. Winks, Softpigs and Wanwans are more expensive... so most people would say wait on those.

I am inclined to think a Sirain is a good starting Dinoz... they are fairly easy to level, and they can hold their own. My current Sirain is leveling wonderfully, taking Wrath, Carapace, Celerity, and Mistral(for the eventual chance at Bubble) and all Water Branches. Even if a Sirain levels poorly, it can become a good candidate for the two water U-Skills as a fall back plan.

HugoMVP
My Sirain was my second dino and it turned out an ok fighter, without me knowing much of the game. The only big mistake that makes me wish to hit my head on the wall, is the path, since I have enough level ups in there to have bubble. I also didn't follow much of the tricky hits path, but besides that I have everything important and he does great in PVP: Sumo, Aqueous Clone, Kar sheer beam. It is a slowing master with petrification, gel, marsh. Is also a pain to kill by assaults because of acupuncture and acid blood, which is why bubble would be so great on it. So, yeah, Sirain is a great and easy dino to start with, and he can already be a good fighter at around level 25.

Cloudoz is the 16k dino that has the most potential, but you have to know what to do, he is harder to level up...

But of course, when you already have some dinoz, you should invest in a softpig, a winks, rockys and a couple of wanwans
