AndySQdancer
Chaitanya, depends on what you want out of the dinoz. I would say that you already have wasted one unlock, since you have 2 Level 3 skills, several level 2 skills, and one level 1 skills to choose from. You should have developed a plan from the very first skill selected and not wait until now to develope one. In general you should take all the level 1 skills you plan on getting before the first unlock. Then take all the level 2 skills you plan on getting before the second unlock, and so on. This method uses the least level-ups to reach your desired goal.

But, you can learn from this and use this dinoz to get some of the U skills and then freeze him to make a better monkey the next time. That being the case, I would take the higher level skills leading to U skills--but no unlocks until you get the rest of the already unlocked skills you plan on before unlocking higher level skills. That's what I would do.

Use your turn to rest, after 23 hours you would have rested +23 hp, but instead of stopping your rest and clicking new turn, go away and come back in 22 hours, at this point it will be 1 hour before your next new turn, at this point you have rested +45 hp (without any benefits), stop resting, click new turn (this is from 22 hours ago) take your turn, it will now read "new turn in 1 hour" on your dino, wait 1 hour, take the new turn. You have successfully healed over 2 days and not lost any turns (except the one you used to rest to begin with, most people do this via Irma.)
