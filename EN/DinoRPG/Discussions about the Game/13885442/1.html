General info:
0. Magic items are items that are sold in the Magic Shop, located in Dinotown. You need napodinoz (napos) to buy these items and you need to have at least one napo in your inventory to be able to see the shop. The magic items use a combat gear slot and they are permanent (they will not disappear after use in battle). Some of them start working randomly, some under certain conditions, and some work from the beginning of the fight until the end of the fight - or until the death of the holder.
1. Some magic items work during the entire fight, even if dinoz that holds it dies.
2. After equipping a magic item on a dinoz you can not remove it for next 5 days.
3. You can equip only one magic item per dinoz
4. Bambooz is considered a magic item and the only one that is not sold in Magic Shop at Dinotown, but instead is a reward for finishing a Quest.

Antichromatic (antimono)
Costs 4 napos.
Effect Prevents using of monochromatics (monos) by enemy, but allows using of monos by your team.
Works if holder dies? yes

Antidote
Costs 5 napos.
Effect Permanently immunises Dinoz against poisons. Works only on holder.
Works if holder dies? no

Bambooz Friend
Costs 100 Devourer seeds
Effect A Bambooz Friend will help your Dinoz during battles! You can not buy this item in Magic Shop, but get as a quest reward from Jasper K. Banishment doesn’t work against the Bambooz Friend. It has 30 HP.
Works if holder dies? yes


Banishment
Costs 3 napos.
Effect Prevent calling of reinforcement from both sides such as korgons, Gorilloz spirits, clones and monsters from capturing glove. Do not prevent reinforcement that summoned threw items like pirhanos and flamelets and bambooz friend. Calling reinforcements triggers still, so it is wise to shut down all reinforcement skills that your dinoz have, if you know that in coming fight banishment will be used by one of the sides.
Works if holder dies? no

Battle grade boxer shorts
Costs 6 napos.
Effect Your team is imprevious to black hole and sylphs skills. Shorts also block Dimensional powder item. If shorts holder hypnoed it still protects dinoz of own team at least. Shorts doesn't protect summons of the holder team.
Works if holder dies? yes
Questions if shorts holder hypnoed does he protect also enemy dinoz?


Beer
Costs 3 napos.
Effect Prevent healing from all items (BR, survival kits) and skills.
Beer only works on dino's, not reinforcements like clones, korgons, spirits, flamelets etc. So triggered leafies and such do heal them. Countered by chen potion Strong Tea. Beer affects phoenix feather and survival skills.
Works if holder dies? yes

Battering Ram
Costs 5 napos.
Effect Dinoz attacks your opponent's castle twice if you are victorious. Works only on holder.
Works if holder dies? no

Curse Locker
Costs 4 napos.
Effect Restricts a random enemy to using their weakest element for 3 turns. Triggers once at the beginning of the fight. Cursed dinoz can use A- and E- skills of any Element, only assaults are affected. Specialist has no affect on item anymore. Self Control doesn't protect vs this item. Works on dark dinoz.

Cuzcussian mask (Mask)
Costs 8 napos.
Effect Protects team members against Hypnosis. Doesn't help vs Mirrored shield spell.
Works if holder dies? No

Dimensional Powder:
Costs 6 napos.
Effect As soon as a Dinoz's life falls below 10 HP, it will be engulfed by a black hole and disappears from the fight. Do not work in Dojo. It affect both sides in fight. If the last hit makes more then 10 points of damage, dinoz dies. Team that has shorts is protected from DP effect. Dinoz that was teleported from fight by DP had a bigger cooldown for next attack during mana war (10 minutes vs 3 minutes)
Works if holder dies? no

Dinoz Cube (Cube)
Costs 9 napos.
Effect Allows you to re-roll the element selected when levelling up and allows you to do so all the way until level 10 (inclusive it means rol lform 10 to 11 also covered by cube ). Having CP and Cube gives you only one reroll possibility as if you have only CP.
Works if holder dies? no

Encyclopedia
Costs 6 napos.
Effect Increases experience points received at the end of the fight by 15% only for one dinoz, that has encyclopedia equipped. 2 encyclopaedias equipped on wasp do not produce a double bonus to xp.
Works if holder dies? no

Enchanted Steroid
Costs 6 napos.
Effect Protects the carrier against any endurance penalties. Beware only their maximum endurance points cannot be reduced. The additional that come from ether skills can be reduced.
Works if holder dies? no

Fear factor
Costs 8 napos.
How Fear Factor (FF) works:
A dinoz with the Brave skill, which carries FF can now form groups as leader. It can also follow another dinoz if it has at least one same starting element as the group leader. Elements that were get upon reincarnation doesn’t count as starting elements. Taking FF off the brave group leader doesn’t break group, but from that point of time group size can not be increased. You can't take off FF from the brave follower.

Works if holder dies? no

Firebrand
Costs 5 napos.
Effect Increases the fire assaults of all Dinoz from both sides by 30%. It is reported here it also affects clone.
Works if holder dies? yes


Friendly Whistle:
Costs 8 napos.
Effect When your Dinoz attacks another Dinoz, Dinoz from your group attack that dinoz as well. It works for all assaults excluding the ones where the opponent dodge the last assault (in case of double hits or lightning dance). The dinoz who attacks because of the whistle can't double hit iirc, but the opponent can counter and also deals passive damage. Friendly whistle assault strength is reduced. For now non FC dinoz do 1 point of damge even on the whistle attack with main element. Source: Druid2K laar Video provided by Laar
Force control and Tenacity affect damage from whistle. It is also possible to use more than one whistle in team, but whistle holder doesn't follow other whistle holders in assaults.
If holder hypnoed enemy dinoz start follow whistle, but not your own until he returns.
Works if holder dies? no
Known bugs if holder hit a team mate becasue of lorie other dinoz hit team mate also. If holder triggers leafy, after that other dinoz attack own team.

Life Stealer
Costs: CotC Reward
Effect: If holder drops below 20 hp, it heals 30 hp, and opponent gets 30 damage.
- The healing effect is blocked by beer, the damage effect still applies though.
- It triggers only once per fight.
- The item doesn't trigger, when passive damage (torch damage or poison or acid blood or Life Source) drops the hp below 20, but it can trigger, if it is attacked again.
- It doesn't trigger, if the attack kills the holder unless survival was used before that.
- It can trigger after using survival, not immediately but next turn, when you get hit again.
- It can trigger while dodging, or during an opposing counter attack.
- It sometimes triggers during an opposing multiattack.
- It only triggers once in a fight.
- It also triggers against clones, taking away their 1 HP, but adding 30 HP to the carrier.
- It also triggers against dark dinoz.
- It triggers vs usual monsters but not triggered vs Fire Elemental f.e.
- DD prevents HP loss from LSr. HP still added to the holder

Scale:
Costs 7 napos.
Effect An enemy Dinoz will be killed if your Dinoz dies during the fight. Can be used only once per day (it means id your dinoz used it, this dinoz or another dinoz to that you equip scale will be able to use it again after the next server reset (00 00) server time)
There are 2 different opinion on the question if scale works on summons as well or only on dinoz.
Some players say yes (it works on clones but no confirmed info on korgons and spirits) (Rafaelss; Teeerex)
Some players say no (Druid2K)
No proof video was provided in this thread still
Works if holder dies? Works only if holder dies.

Sorcerer's Wand
Costs 7 napos.
Effect Randomly decreases a Dinoz HP by 30% (it can also decrease HP of your own dinoz). Using this item replaces the Dinoz' attack. Life Breath does not protect vs Sorcerer's Wand. Do not forget to take it off during regular moving, or your dinoz will lose a lot of HP in usual pvm fights.
Works if holder dies? no

Tear of Life (Tears)
Costs 6 napos.
Effect Clones get 10% of the life from the maximal hp of dino which launch it. Works only on dinoz that has tear equipped. If holder dies nothing happens to clones (they will not die and hp will not be reduced to 1).
Works if holder dies? no

Temporary Manipulator (TM)
Costs 5 napos.
Effect Prevent using of E-skills from both sides. Countered by chen potion Temporal Stabiliser.
Works if holder dies? yes

Temportal Reduction
Costs 5 napos.
Effect Reduced initiative bonuses and penalties on the equipped dinoz by 50%. It works for bonuses/penalties of Blowtorch palms, Vines, Detonation, etc. and does not affect the bonuses from permanent abilities like: warm blood, hazard, sprint, etc.
Works if holder dies? no

General info:
0. Magic items are items that sold in a Magic Shop located in Dinotown. You need napodinoz (napos) to buy this items and you need to have at least one napo in your inventory to be able to see the shop. The magic items using a combat gear slot and they are permanent, they will not disappear after using. Some of them start working randomly, some under certain conditions and some working form the beginning of the fight till the end of the fight or till the death of the holder.
1. Some magic items work during the whole fight even if dinoz that holds it dies.
2. After equipping a magic item on a dinoz you can not remove it for next 5 days.
3. You can equip only one magic item at one dinoz
4. Bambooz is an magic item and the only one that is not sold in Magic Shop at dinotown, but is a reward for finishing a Quest.

Antichromatic (antimono)
Costs 4 napos.
Effect Prevents using of monochromatics (monos) by enemy, but allows using of monos by your team.
Works if holder dies? yes

Antidote
Costs 4 napos.
Effect Permanently immunises Dinoz against poisons. Works only on holder.
Works if holder dies? no

Banishment
Costs 3 napos.
Effect Prevent calling of reinforcement from both sides such as korgons, Gorilloz spirits, clones and monsters from capturing glove. Do not prevent reinforcement that summoned threw items like pirhanos and flamelets and bambooz friend. Calling reinforcements triggers still, so it is wise to shut down all reinforcement skills that your dinoz have, if you now that in coming fight banishment will be used by one of the sides.
Works if holder dies? no

Beer
Costs 3 napos.
Effect Prevent healing from all items (BR, survival kits) and skills.
Beer only works on dino's, not reinforcements like clones, korgons, spirits, flamelets etc. So triggered leafies and such do heal them.
Works if holder dies? yes

Battering Ram
Costs 5 napos.
Effect Dinoz attacks your opponent's castle twice if you are victorious. Works only on holder.
Works if holder dies? no

Black Hole:
Costs 6 napos.
Effect As soon as a Dinoz's life falls below 10 HP, it will be engulfed by a black hole and disappears from the fight. Do not work in Dojo. If the last hit makes more then 10 points of damage, dinoz dies.
Works if holder dies? no

Cuzcussian mask (Mask)
Costs 8 napos.
Effect Protects team members against Hypnosis.
Works if holder dies? ?

Dinoz Cube (Cube)
Costs 9 napos.
Effect Allows you to re-roll the element selected when levelling up and allows you to do so all the way until level 10 (inclusive).
Works if holder dies? no
Questions Is it possible to use cube and career plan (Do you get the second roll?).

Encyclopedia
Costs 5 napos.
Effect Increases experience points received at the end of the fight by 15% only for one dinoz, that has encyclopedia equipped.
Works if holder dies? no

Firebrand
Costs 5 napos.
Effect Increases the fire assaults of all Dinoz from both sides by 30%.
Works if holder dies? ?

Friendly Whistle:
Costs 8 napos.
Effect When your Dinoz attacks another Dinoz, Dinoz from your group attack that dinoz as well. Friendly whistle gives only assaults from your other dinoz and the assault strength is reduced. They use about 40-50% of their original assault value and it is unknown if it is a fix value or a random number. Source: Druid2K laar Video provided by Laar
Works if holder dies? no

Scale:
Costs 7 napos.
Effect An enemy Dinoz will be killed if your Dinoz dies during the fight. Can be used only once per day (it means id your dinoz used it, this dinoz or another dinoz to that you equip scale will be able to use it again after the next server reset (00 00) server time)
There are 2 different opinion on the question if scale works on summons as well or only on dinoz.
Some players say yes (it works on clones but no confirmed info on korgons and spirits) (Rafaelss; Teeerex)
Some players say no (Druid2K)
No proof video was provided in this thread still
Works if holder dies? Works only if holder dies.

Sorceror's Wand
Costs 7 napos.
Effect Randomly decreases a Dinoz HP by 30% (it can also decrease HP of your own dinoz). Using this item replaces the Dinoz' attack. Life Breath does not protect vs Sorceror's Wand.
Works if holder dies? no

Tear of Life (Tears)
Costs 6 napos.
Effect Clones get 10% of the life from the maximal hp of dino which launch it. Works only on dinoz that has tear equipped. If holder dies nothing happens to clones (they will not die and hp will not be reduced to 1).
Works if holder dies? no

Temportal Reduction
Costs 6 napos.
Effect Reduced initiative bonuses and penalties on the equipped dinoz by 50%. It is believed (stil lwaiting confirmation from Epoq) it works for bonuses/penalties such Blowtorch palms, Vines, Detonation, etc. and does not affect the bonuses from permanent abilities like: warm blood, hazard, sprint, etc.
Works if holder dies? no

Temporary Manipulator (TM)
Costs 5 napos.
Effect Prevent using of E-skills from both sides. They are not triggering so no need to shut them down.
Works if holder dies? yes

Any additions and improvements (improvements of grammar as well) are welcome.

General info:
0. Magic items are items that are sold in the Magic Shop, located in Dinotown. You need napodinoz (napos) to buy these items and you need to have at least one napo in your inventory to be able to see the shop. The magic items use a combat gear slot and they are permanent (they will not disappear after use in battle). Some of them start working randomly, some under certain conditions, and some work from the beginning of the fight until the end of the fight - or until the death of the holder.
1. Some magic items work during the entire fight, even if dinoz that holds it dies.
2. After equipping a magic item on a dinoz you can not remove it for next 5 days.
3. You can equip only one magic item per dinoz
4. Bambooz is considered a magic item and the only one that is not sold in Magic Shop at Dinotown, but instead is a reward for finishing a Quest.

Effect Prevents using of monochromatics (monos) by enemy, but allows using of monos by your team.

How Fear Factor (FF) works:
A dinoz with the Brave skill, which carries FF can now form groups as leader. It can also follow another dinoz if it has at least one same starting element as the group leader. Elements that were get upon reincarnation doesn’t count as starting elements. Taking FF off the brave group leader doesn’t break group, but from that point of time group size can not be increased. You can't take off FF from the brave follower.

It means you can form any number of groups with only one FF and have a brave leader in each group, but you need 2 FF to get 2 brave dinoz into the same group and 3 FF to get 3 brave dinoz into one group and so on.
