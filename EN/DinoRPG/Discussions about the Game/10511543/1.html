@ CaptainRex: Sorry, in my haste to keep the post short, I did a bad job of summarising the previous posts. It did look right when I wrote it, but is not what I meant.

To the point of taking over both Abandoned Clans and Inactive ones (your definition), I am against both. Abandoned Clans can never be recovered, Inactive ones depend on how long anyone defines as inactive (and could become active again after a long while).

With all the controls in the hands of the leader all members take the risk that their clan might become inactive, and it might be better if more members had more power. Unfortunately, only the leader can pass control to someone else. We would need changes to dismiss the leader and take control, but this would open the doors to all sorts of possibilities where a clan is 'stolen' in a takeover move. I don't see an easy way of resolving this.

The treasury has been donated by existing members and those now left. Some of those donations may have been used up. I don't see an easy formula for deciding on a 'fair' way to split the treasury.

Of course, the original post was about Abandoned Clans, not Inactive ones.
