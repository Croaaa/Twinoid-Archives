I would suggest to buy a Gorilloz first. It has 65% to get wood. In wood there are 2 Unique skills the first lets you have 21 active dinoz instead of 18. The skill is called Leader. The second one gives all your dinoz +1 equipment slot it's called Engineer. The second one is not crucial for beginers but will be useful later. Here is the path how you can reach them:
Savagery, unlock, Korgon Reinforcement, Tenacity, unlock, Wild Instinct, Tenacity, unlock, Leader, Engineer

During the moves with your Gorilloz you will earn money to buy any other dinoz. Cloudoz is the closest to what you are searching. Kabukis are hard to get (there is a quest to obtain 3 kabuki eggs and they cost 50000 each beyond the first).

I suggest you to join a clan after the Mana War (it lasts until 20th of June). Most clans have detailed guides and lots of experience so they will help you grow faster (at first this game is really slow). If you contact with a clan leader now he will help you for sure.
