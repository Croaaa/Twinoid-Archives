Follow Loamoat's strategy though:
Each dinoz you have will provide you around 200 each turn, then there are random bonuses of up to around 2500 (so far it has occurred with my first two dinoz). Do grandpa Joe's missions. They are very helpful. Check a clan's guide on his missions. You should only do the missions that pay , and not just . The second mission, the lost dog, even though it doesn't pay , is still a good mission for angel potion. That way, if you bump into a early gluon, you have a handy angel potion with you.

If you want to do Madam x's missions, well, judging by the guide on them, they're not so good. One mission only offers you 20. That should be changed to 200. The only one I'd suggest is the black briefcase one. You can get a easy 300.

Get a harvest/hunt skill quickly, you can make quick money finding worthy objects (actually quick is a bit of an understatement as you need to find the merchant).

That would be my strategy for quick gold and missions: do the ones that pay .
