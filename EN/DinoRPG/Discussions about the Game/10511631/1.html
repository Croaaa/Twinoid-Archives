You just have to defeat the Water Element at The University (Talk to Professor Eugene and chose "learn", "swimming buoy" or something like that) to get a status item, which allows you to go to the islands from Frish Port.. There you will find Sticky Swamp and other nice places
Be aware of the Eeloz there, they are pretty tough for low level dinos (you will encounter them from level 12 on)

Best wishes and good luck
