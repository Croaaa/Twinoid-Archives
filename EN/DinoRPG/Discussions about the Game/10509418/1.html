I think there are mostly 2 ways to go:
1) Softpig
- upside: very strong in fire, high chance of one-hit kill so you don't lose many HPs every fight
- downside: costs 20k, so it will take a little longer before you get a second dinoz
2) as said above, take a 16k dinoz with 2 points in one element. Gorilloz or Sirain are probably best because they have a higher chance of getting their element
- upside: fairly strong in 1 element, cheaper, so you will get your second dinoz faster
- downside: you may have to rest a little more often than softpig

In both cases, you have a dinoz that is really strong in 1 element, so you shouldn't have too much trouble against the gluons. Dinoz that have more spread out skill distribution die a little too often against gluons at levels less than 7 or 8 if they're not in groups, so I wouldn't recommend them as a first dinoz.
