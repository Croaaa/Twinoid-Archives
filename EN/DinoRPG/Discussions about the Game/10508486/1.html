Before anything, think to try to empty the cache of your Internet browser! This easy operation can solve many technical problems.

On Internet Explorer 7 :

* On the "command bar"
* Open drop-down menu for "Tools"
* Select "Delete Browsing History..."
* From the menu, select "Delete Files..." next to "Temporary Internet Files"
* Validate, close Internet Explorer, and restart the site

On Firefox :

* From the menu "Tools" choose "Clear Private Data"
* Select "Cache" and then click on "Clear Private Data Now". Re-load the site.
