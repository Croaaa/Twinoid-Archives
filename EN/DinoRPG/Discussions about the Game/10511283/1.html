You cannot remove him. You must either sell him, sacrifice or freeze him.

To sell, go to the marketplace. Open up a bid and sell it. This is the quickest way.

To sacrifice, you will need to come out alive from the katatombs with an epic item which enables you to sacrifice dinoz. However, this will take very long and is very hard.

To freeze, get the lantern and go to the gorges in BAH. Talk to the man there and he'll freeze it for you.

There is no way to simply remove a single Dinoz but you have two ways to take him from the active part of your Dinoz's list.
One way is to fight your way through the catacombs which isn't that easy and takes some time. I doubt that you could make it with the Dinoz you own at the moment anyway.
The other way is to freeze your Dinoz in the Deep Gorges. You only need the status item Lantern to go there. Quote by Lolwhut:

How to get the Lantern?
First, you'll need to dig up 3 items:
- Swamp Mud at the Sticky Swamp
- Pure Water at the Fountain of Youth
- Basalt Shard at the Basalt Slopes
Remember to repair your shovel at the Coral Mines when used!

When you have these 3 ingredients, you move to the Underwater Dome (Scrobscuba Caller needed!). Talk to the Archsage and fight the Earth Elemental. After this you'll receive the Zor's Gloves.

Move to the Mutant Falls and talk to Master Hydragol. He will give you Waterlilly Pads, wich makes you able to enter the Grumhel Forest.

Enter the Grumhel Forest and head to the Haunted Hills. Talk to the Strange Man and fight a Korgon (this is really easy!). After this, you got yourself the Lantern wich makes you able to travel to the Deep Gorges.

Now you know how you could get rid of your Dinoz. Nevertheless I'd recommend to you to keep it since there is no need to clear a spot right now. No matter how weak your Dinoz is he can still earn money. A good way not to die is grouping your Dinoz which also results in more and Gold at all.
You should buy more Dinoz until you reach the maximum of 18 and at this point you can seperate the usefull Dinoz from the rather useless ones.

George + a couple of others could do it but it would cost more in Irmas for a group.

The majority of the monsters you meet (around level 20 anyway) are Green Giants, Wolves and Giant Chicken. Sometimes you meet the skeletal brigade and then you will lose quite a lot of health, especially if you cannot jump or fly and don't have Primal State.

If you send George in alone, he will make good progress and, when he gets low on health, just walk back out and rest in the Cemetary. Next day, walk back in to where you were and carry on - the monsters in the Katatombs do not respawn.

You should be done within a week if you focus on it.
