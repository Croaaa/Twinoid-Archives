How do I know if anyone wrote down my username as a player that intoduced them to this game?
You will get a message telling you that you have sponsor tickets waiting for you in the market.

And does the money-to-referring player still work? Especially if anyone I've introduced to this game purchases coins or notes?
The only way to get the referral bonus (sponsor tickests) is if the person you referred buys gold with real money. Once you get them to do so, and you go to the market, you'll have a chance to use your tickets to get gold, ingredients, or the chance of a rare dinoz called the Feross.
It is said that the more tickets you have, the better your chances at getting a Feross, since you start with a 1 in 100 chance.
