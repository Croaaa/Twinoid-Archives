While grouping will not reduce the Xp earned per dinoz, if the amount from X level to the next level are close, then the total Xp gained from the group will be divided. Afterwhich the remaining will go to any others in the group that are not close to leveling.

Ex. lvl 6 100/105, lvl 6 40/105 After battle of gain 40Xp lvl 7 0/110, lvl 6 75/105 (the rest of the Xp rolls over to the other dinoz in the same group)

Ive no proof to comfirm this, however have studied the effects with the groups to give an example of the outcome of grouping in critical situations.
