I will explain it for you why FR server get always things first and why they have better prizes.

Quoting the Q&A:
Why does new content always come out on the French server first and we have to wait for the same things in English?
The French version of DinoRPG is the "primary" (figuratively speaking) version , we don't deny it! the reason for this is very straightforward and logical.

Motion Twin is a French company consisting of a small team of less than 20 people.

In France, the programmer has total control of "their" game and it is in their own language. They don't need any intermediaries. They then get feedback from the players, correct bugs, even up imbalances in the game design as the game is played. This affords the programmer more independance, saves time and avoids headaches.

If we released updates for all 4 versions of DinoRPG at the same time, not only would it be logistically complicated (coordinating at least 4 people who are all already extremely busy ), it would technically be very complicated too.

There is a logic to this: first of all we "test" the update in France, then when it is confirmed as stable, it is released at an opportune moment for the other language versions.
FR server got numerous more active players, more active clans. So don't be surprised if only the top50 recieved prizes instead of top256. To compare, 1st ENG CotC have something like 150 clans (with one fourth less than 10 members), 4th FR CotC have 600 clans participating, 450 LL only.

For Dojo, FR got 14.000 players, ENG 2.500... Epoq said on CotC that prizes would be scaled by the number of truly participating clans and players.
