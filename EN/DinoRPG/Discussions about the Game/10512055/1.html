from the support page:

To freeze a Dinoz you have to take it to the Deep Gorges (at the Big All-Hot Volcano), and once there, you must speak to Popotholeler to make the freeze option appear. Bear in mind that the freezing action is instantaneous, and space for a new Dinoz will be available immediately.

Later, as Popotholeler explains, you can thaw your Dinoz at any time as long as you have a free space (which starts at 18, unless you have the 'Leader' skill which makes it 21). The thawing process will last for about a day, and will not cause any change in the experience or health points of the Dinoz, nor will it affect any progress made on any mission that was in progress when the Dinoz was frozen.

Note: You can see your frozen Dinoz in My Account.

Note: If your Dinoz is following another Dinoz, you should UNFOLLOW before freezing.

You can own an unlimited numbers of dinoz, though only 18 or 21 (with Leader skill) could be active. The others would have to be frozen.

Demon have one special skill the normal ones from their own species don't have. You need to own the Demon Scroll (an epic item), found in the Catacombs, to be able to see the Demon Shop, then sacrifice your own dinozs to have Demon notes, then buy one Demon.
