Just completed it. You have to kill 2 wolves in each of these zones: BrutForce, Fountain of Youth, Dinotown, and the university.

Actually, all my encounters were from 1 wolf when I was doing the mission. If you have 1/2 or 0/2 killed in the area just use the fight action next time, until the quest tells you to move on.
