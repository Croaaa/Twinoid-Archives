There are French, Spanish and German servers where you do get 1 turn per day at midnight server time. The English server has a different turn system... you get a turn, wait, then get another turn when you click 'New Turn'. The wait from your first turn to your second is 0.5 hour, the next wait is 1 hour, the next 1.5 hour. You do get 8 or 9 on day 1 (if you start early in the morning!) then 4 or 3, then 3 or 2.

This is much better than the single turn per day. If you buy a 16000 Dinoz and save ALL your money you should get a second after about 30 - 35 turns, a third after 25 - 30 turns, a fourth after 20, and so on.

The game is designed to need a short time per day, although after 10 Dinoz it can take a while to move them all.

The turn system will not change. Play it as it is or go play something else (perhaps after reading the 1000+ other messages complaining about it).
