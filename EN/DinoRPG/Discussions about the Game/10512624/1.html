Softpigs will have the highest torch damage and highest counter damage on their first assault. Downsides would be that torch can be turned off (not a concern with TM), and they don't have a strong second assault for their counters.

Moueffes will do slightly less torch damage (depending on level it might even be the same), but can get vendetta, so more counters for them. Although counters can be hurtful to you, if the other guy brings lories. Your first dino attacks your own with a multihit, your attacked dino counters with a multi counter and so on. The chance for that increases with vendetta as well.

Quetzus can get pretty much all the passive damage skills. Torch, counter, acid blood, acupuncture but no chance for vendetta. They will always return damage when hit by an assault, no matter what.
