This thread is meant to inform the players about the contents of the 4 special grids that are found in the Dinotown area. Some of them have been reset several times, and to be honest I have lost track a bit of what is where. So everyone is welcome to contribute, especially during and after promotion events. Here we can sum up the info to give players a better idea what can be found. Basicly it's good to know what kind of rare and special items can be found (like dinoz eggs). If someone clears a whole grid it would be nice to note down all the contents in it.


Digging in Grandpa Joe's Garden

Location: Grandpa Joe's House, Action: Excavate,
Item needed: Tac Ticket, Grid Size: 6x6, Resets: None

It seems this grid has never been reset. I could confirm the same contents on 3 different servers, on EN not too long ago.

Contents:
22x Empty Square
1x Fur Tuft
4x Sharpened Flint
2x Lightning Energy
3x Figonicia Roots
3x 500 Gold Pieces
1x Baby Mahamuti


Snowplough Game

Location: Dinotown, Action: Snowplough,
Item needed: Xmas Ticket, Grid Size: 10x10, Resets: Several

This Grid started out with 3 Santaz Eggs I think, then Rare Santaz were added with a reset, and there has been at least 1 reset more after that.

Contents:
Not sure what special stuff is in it now. The normal things include Fur Tufts, Sharpened Flints, Lujidanee Groupers, Air Energies, and Pelinae Leaves.


Fireworks Display

Location: Dinotown, Action: Fireworks,
Item needed: Magic Fuse, Grid Size: 10x10, Resets: Unknown

Not sure how often this got reset (or not).

Contents:
At some point there was a Rare Softpig Egg in it. No idea about other Eggs right now. The normal things include Fur Tufts, Chocolate Horns, Lightning Energies, Air Energies, Fire Energies, Wood Energies, White Phalisk Boletus, Sadiquae Mordicus, Figonicia Roots, Tac Tickets and piles of 2000 and 1000 Gold Pieces.


Sandcastle Game

Location: Frish Port, Action: Sandcastle Game,
Item needed: Birthday Ticket, Grid Size: 10x10, Resets: Unknown

Not sure how often this got reset (or not).

Contents:
At the moment I have no info on what rare and common items can be found here.
