Gloves + Waterlily pad = Access to the south part of Ghrumel Jungle/Forest.

If you want the waterlily, show the gloves to Master Hidargol (old man in the mutant falls).

If you dig in the "Ashpouk ruins", you will obtain the "old stone". Show the stone to Professor Eugene (university), he will "clean" the stone and give you the Totem Ashpouk ( grants you access to the Forcebrut tournament).

Good luck.
