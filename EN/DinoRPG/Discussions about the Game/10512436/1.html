These types of topics tend to pop out every once in a while so if you flip back few pages on the forum I'm sure you'll find a lot of useful information by opening the threads that sound the most useful. I do congratulate you for skipping one of the biggest newbie mistakes and not spending all of your gold on items but saving them for more dinos. That is definitely the smartest way to advance in the game early on.

There are no real "killer combinations" when it comes to grouping dinos. It all depends on the skills, levels and what area they are exploring. I would tend to agree with Tusika about the importance of getting a Gorilloz pretty early, because of their easy access to U-skill LEADER. I'm not sure how much you've read about these already but U-skills are skills that work outside of combat giving you small advantages like more inventory slots and cheaper shop prices. Plenty of clans have sweet guides for these, such as this one.
