So for all Dinoz with base 16, 19, 20 and 28k for that we have data the formulas are ok.

1-10: base price (2000 * lvl)
11-15: price of lvl 10. + (15000 * (lvl-10))
16-50: price of 15. + (20000 * (lvl-15))

Assuming Wooby gived the confirmed base price for Tofufu 34k I tried to use this formulas for getting the price.

We have the confirmed price of level 12 Tofufu 100k.

Tofufu of level 10 should cost = 34k + 10*2k = 54k

Tofufu of level 12 should cost = 54k + (12-10)*15k = 84k but confirmed price was 100k. So we have 3 options the confirmed price of tofufu at level 12 is wrong, the base price is wrong or the formulas for such types of dinoz are wrong.
