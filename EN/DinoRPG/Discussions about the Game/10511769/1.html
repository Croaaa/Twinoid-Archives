Hoghot: first you need to buy a second Dinoz, who might be a bit weak for fighting the Gluon even with your Cloudoz. You need to give the Gluon 35 damage. If your Cloudoz, when healed, cannot do that, I would recommend cancelling that mission and returning to Grandpa Joe.

Gluons are a pain. Have you done the "Hills Smashrooms", "Wolf Hunting" and "The Stamps" missions? They restrict the monsters you get to Wolves and Smashrooms.

Do not spend lots of coins on healing or Irmas or Angel potions; focus on keeping as many of those coins as you can so you can buy another Dinoz. You will then have 2 Dinoz gaining coins and (nearly) double the coins you are getting. It IS worth it.
