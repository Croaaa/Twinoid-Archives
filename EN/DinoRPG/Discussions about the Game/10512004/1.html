AndySQdancer
If you want to get Max Experience from the Brut Force Tournament, you always need to arrange the Fights so that your Opponent's Level is Higher than your Attacking Dinoz Level. However, you can also expect to Die a lot. Also, this goes against the Advantage of saving the Brut Force Tournament until your Dinoz is a higher Level and really needs the 30 or so to reach the next Level. So I would save the Brut Force Tournament until the Dinoz doing it is a higher Level and has the Intellegence Skill, you have the U Professor Skill, and Ideally the Dinoz doing the Tournament is equiped with the Encyclopaedia. It's also nice to do the Brut Force Tournament with a Dinoz that is Close to Leveling to hasen the Leveling, just don't waste by doing the final Leveling Fight at the Tournament.

First there are different ways to approach this tournament. You must identify if the dinoz to enter is to be sacrificed for demon tickets, frozen or is your permanent playable dinoz.

Case 1 A dinoz to be frozen. Really there is no need to even start this tournament.

Case 3 A dinoz to be played as a prermanent fixture. Since points are fixed the later the better (Probably best to do it when your dinoz is well into it 40 levels).
