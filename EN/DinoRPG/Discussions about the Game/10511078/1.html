I think I am seeing a "dirty" tactic of abandoning clans during the war.

Members of the clan focus on attacking strong and competitive clans in the beginning of the war. Then all the members leave the castle destroyed and abandon the clan to join a new clan. This handcuffs the clans the original clan attacked since the attacked clans cannot counterattack. This affects the attacked clans' rep since there is no way to erase their previous defeats.

I do not wish to sound like I am whining about the defeats, but I think all the players expect a certain level of sportsmanship and the evidence speaks for itself.

As a solution to this problem I propose that game programmers do not allow a player who leaves his clan during the war to rejoin another clan until after the war. Recruiting new members who were not clan members when the war started is not affected. Also members are free to join and leave clans when there is no war as normal.
