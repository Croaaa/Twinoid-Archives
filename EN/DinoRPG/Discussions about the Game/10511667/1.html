Zephides
And if you're lazy:

- First, you need 15 points, you can find your total points on your account page.
- Secondly, you'll need 20.000
- Then, you go to the Clans Tab, which can be found upper right corner.

There will be a button here to create your own clan. You fill in your Clan name and you must tell something about your clan.

After that you can add your own pages and change your homepage etc.
