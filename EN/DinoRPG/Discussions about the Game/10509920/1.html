Cloudoz are often cited as good gatherers because they can relatively easily get the , and gathering skills. The gathering skill is in particularly high demand, and the cloudoz has a higher chance of getting than any other dinoz except kabuki and santaz, and is cheaper or much easier to obtain than either of those.

There's really no need to have more than 1 gatherer dinoz with each gathering skill. If you ever need a large quantity of something, you can just get your gatherer to the right spot and use the collecting skill repeatedly using Irma's potions.
