some missions require you to fight only in one place.

Yes. If I am on a mission with one of my lower-level Dinos who needs to kill 6 wolves and/or smashrooms in the Precipitous Hills, then it would not be a good idea to move him to the University and then head back to the Hills...

That would not only waste a turn, it would also expose my Dino to a fight that could injure him, and wouldn't help me complete his mission.

Read the mission requirements carefully, and make a point of reading the hints at the top of your Dinos' sheets before acting rashly... simply because you, "Want to play!"

At many times, the best Tactic is to wait, study, and learn... Running away from an important battle can often cost you more than standing and fighting!
