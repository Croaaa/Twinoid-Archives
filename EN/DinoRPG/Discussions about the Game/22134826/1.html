Most Loyal Dinoz Masters!

After an unhealthy delay, we are proud to officially announce the winner of Inter-Dojo Tournament XXV: Mengo9999!
Mengo9999 has previously demonstrated his skill in destroying all his opponent's teams with his unbelievably potent, feral Cloudoz in IDT22 and he has done it once again! Accompanied by the Winks Paras, the fearsome trio of level 60 Cloudoz, Boreas (the obsidian fume always watching you), Notus (the one always looking forward to the fight) and Zefiro (the brains behind the formation) didn't leave Raynor's team any time to grasp for breath as his dinoz were destroyed one by one with the killer combo of a high multiple hit chance and Without Mercy! An action Without Mercy, so to speak! The unstoppable trio of cumuli lay waste to none other than Sjefke, Venar and Loppermann while Raynor had to fight his way through such giants of our time as B1onic, John0452 and Insanedar! What an incredible feat from both masters!

The finals saw poison, extreme amounts of multihits and vaporous forms and took a long time before the last of Raynor's creatures fell to the ground only leaving the deathly trio of nebulae and a weakened, but still standing
crustacean on the battlefield!

Once again, congratulations to the winner of the XXV International Dojo Tournament, Mengo9999!
