On march 6, 2009
Husaurus
Take the Element Score under Information at the top of the Dinoz's sheet. Multiply that number by the number given. So my 5th level Sirain with a Water of 4 has a Water Cannon of 6x4 = 24.

Since his Assault Score for Water is only 20, since he hasn't got Perception yet... Water Canon is better than his normal Water attack... and all his other attacks too.

If any of his Attacks were higher than 24, he might have a problem, since Water Canon might randomly replace that better Attack.
