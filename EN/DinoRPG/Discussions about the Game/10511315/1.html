Yes, as noted
* they get bigger from each change from levels 1 to 10,
* at level 7 some change specific details (softpigs with bumps grow horn, feross horn increases more than just the size growth etc.),
* at level 10 they reach their 'final' look.

Then, as noted, if their max HP increases then you will see them look larger in fights as well, but not on the dinoz master page or your account page.
