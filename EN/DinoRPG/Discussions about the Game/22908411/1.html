HugoMVP

Hi, welcome. Builds can be different from player to player, and usually are shared within clans.

For Cloudoz basic guidelines are to get Sumo, Career Plan, Bubble, Fujin, and then you can set for whichever you think are best or fit better the level ups you get, from a wide range of choices like acid blood, life guard, overload, electrolysis, leafy dawn, you just have to select which ones you prefer bearing in mind you can't take them all, you have to plan to finish the build around level 50-55 (or more, if you don't wish any of the new skills, but I wouldn't suggest for that).

Poison is not very needed, not too many times you are able to poison. But poisoned claws is a requisite to acid blood, a very nice skill. Petrification is on the other hand very useful to have as it works much more often (only dinoz with self control can avoid it), it really favors you. And is also a requisite to a very good skill.

Tricky Hits and Fatal Hits don't work as described and for a cloudoz aren't of much use. Tricky Hits could be taken on other dinoz more water heavy though, on the way to Life Guard.

Immaterial is not worth it as a simple level 1 water skill (perception) can take it away, and being a level 1 skill you can imagine how many dinoz have it. And the requisite skills simply prejudice your dino too much in PVP (player vs player) fights. But in PVE (player vs environment), your dinoz is invincible since monsters don't have perception, and you stay immaterial forever. But you should focus your dinoz into PVP, not PVE.

Well, thats a matter of taste. With oracle, its almost guaranteed to cast fujin, which is good. On the downside, the meta got away from speed>everything, due to endurance and stronger skills ether skills in fire and wood. Additionally, your bubble will be pretty weak, so be aware that your cloud wont be anything more than an empty fujin casting vessel, that contributes not much to the fight, especially when it comes to damage. It comes down to the team you will face then. If it isnt a full bubble team, your lightning (and sineous) will become decent damage dealing skills. Against non bubble dinoz, they are still more or less useless (add potential vap.form triggers to the minimized damage). Still, fujin remains an awesome skill and guaranteeing an early trigger is something good to have, so its up to you. I'd still say take warm blood for now, lightning (skill) wont make a huge difference ( imagine some worst cases... target a vap form dinoz... target a korg/clone... and afterwards: lack endurance to cast fujin next round). Then just watch what you are rolling once in the new tree. 3 or even 4 water early on before rolling lightning and you should change your plans and go for MV. Or play even smarter, and go for both (MV and oracle/love hertz) if you feel lucky, because lvl 80 is confirmed...

(there's a reason why most people turn lightning off on winks, some even on wanwans)

edit: even if i take vendetta into consideration, iam not sure wether to go for HP or oracle. Lasting longer due to more hp and having a stronger bubble will lead to more succesful counters. Pimping lightning will lead to less wasted counters with damage less what you recieve in response due to torch, ls, and acid. Pretty much a coin toss. The more i think about it, the more i tend to suggest the "smart" but "dangerous" way to go for both, mv and oracle, in regards of lvl 80.That way, you wont end up with one tree only in case your cloud messes up, or is "too perfect" (rolling only lightning until lvl 80 comes and then you get stuck in other trees in the middle of nowhere). Thats probably the smartest thing to avoid that such a great dinoz gets stuck later on.
