After much speculation and debate, here is the UPDATED setup for the Championship of the Clans starting this afternoon:

3 leagues:
Pro league: 1-60,
Amateur league: 1-40
Starter league: 1-25

48 hour battles (44 hour pause between battles)

Max 15 AP
Move every 60 minutes

*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*

And now to the prizes:

PRO League: Battles take place at Dinotown, Big-All-Hot and the Islands

Winner: Epic Reward + 3 Napoz + 200 notes + 150 demon tickets
Runner-Up: Epic Reward + 3 Napoz + 100 notes + 100 demon tickets
Semi-Final: Epic Reward + 3 Napoz + 50 notes
Quarter-Final: Epic Reward + 2 Napoz + 50 notes
Last 16: Epic Reward + 1 Napoz + 50 notes

Clans in the Pro league must have at least 10 members and 45 dinoz lvl 40 or higher.

AMATEUR League: Battles take place at Dinotown

Winner: Epic Reward + 2 Napoz + 100 notes + 100 demon tickets
Runner-Up: Epic Reward + 1 Napoz + 100 notes + 100 demon tickets
Semi-Final: Epic Reward + 1 Napoz + 100 notes
Quarter Final: Epic Reward + 1 Napoz + 50 notes
Last 16: Epic Reward + 150.000 coins

STARTER League: Battles take place at Dinotown

Winner: Epic Reward + 100 Treasury Notes
Runner-Up: Epic Reward + 80 Treasury Notes
Semi-Final: Epic Reward + 50 Treasury Notes
Quarter Final: Epic Reward

Well I also said it before, for me 8 unbeatable clans are just as wrong as 2 unbeatable clans.

It basically comes down to the american vs. european sports mentality.

In Europe, you have a few strong teams, which win almost each time. Every good player from the smaller teams wants to get into these big teams, because that is where you get big money and win titles. So basically this hierarchy stands forever, unless outside factors interfere (for ex. a millionaire taking over a small team, turning it into a big one).

In the USA, you have salary caps, you have drafts, etc. This ensures that most teams will only have 1-2 star players, good young players go to the smaller teams, etc. This way you ensure that most teams get at least a few good runs now and then.

- with DC in it's final days, when we were a never group and very active, but weak. I fought a lot, ended up without a napo
