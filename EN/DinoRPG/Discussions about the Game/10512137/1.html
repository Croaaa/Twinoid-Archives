Simple. When:
i * ΔX / (X1*Xg) > ΔH * Bc/Bh

where:
X1= gained solo
Xg= gained in group
ΔX=X1-Xg
ΔH= lost solo - lost group

i=irma cost (900 base)
Bc=bread cost (6000 base)
Bh=bread (100 base)

Doesn't account for differences in from fights.
