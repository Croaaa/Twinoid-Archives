Sorry for the double post - just realized I didn't explain why it might be better to keep them.

Once a dino gets below 50% health, a new option becomes available - Rest. You can choose to rest instead of fighting/moving. If you do this, you will recover 1hp per hour, up to 50% of your health. That means that if you have a 23 hour wait between turns, you could get back up to 46 hp by resting (more on that in a moment).

I recently learned (thanks to the wise ones here on the forum) that you can use a potion to rest after you've taken a turn - this is probably a better use of the potions than simply fighting again.

For example:

Your dino fights and is at 10hp after the battle. That's low enough that you probably don't want to fight again until you rest - but the option to rest won't be available until you have a new turn. If you take an Irma's potion, that one dino gets another turn, which they can use to rest... by the time your other dinos are ready for a new turn, you should have recovered quite a few hp (up to 23 if that's your time between turns).

You can actually get more, though... if you start your new turn with a different dino, you can leave the hurt dino resting. Then, before you start your next turn, stop resting. You will have a turn available to you immediately, and then can start your new turn on another dino.

It really is a personal thing. The potion gives you another turn, whether you need to wait 1 hour or 23. Using them just because you are impatient only saves you time.

To really gain from the potions, do something you can't get by waiting.
* If you are 1 fight short of having enough gold to buy a nice Dinoz from your shop, use a potion to get that gold before the Dinoz disappears the next day.
* Use it to get into the Sticky Swamp without fighting (unless you want the fight to get xp and gold) i.e. Wednesday / Sunday.
* Take an extra turn to catch the Merchant before he moves away (i.e. before Sunday) or stops buying your ingredients.
* Take an extra turn to get your Dinoz together so they can follow.
* Use a potion to heal / repair during the Clan War.

In the end, it is your choice.
