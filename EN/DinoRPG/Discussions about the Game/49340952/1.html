Original Text and where to post (in French only). Following is rather a summary than a translation.

Kiroukou have made some IDT presets which are selected through randomization. There's only a few presets right now and there may be some other repeats to come by.

KiroukouSo here's the idea, you can create your own presets!

Though, you need to post them on the topic which is first linked above.

For those who only want to vote, it's basically +1/-1, with some arguments (still in French, though they may accept English under spoilers tag only).

For those who want to post their own ideas, it may be tricky. You need to follow the topic, write in comprehensible French (still English under spoilers tag may work, but you need both. "Google French" is out of question).

  • Please precise the races, the number of dinoz per team etc...
  • Please follow the same restrictions already existing.
  • Magic items / Consumables could be used or not.
  • A small text about what's the fun about your idea.
  • Please use C+n to number your proposition(s) like on Q&A topic (C states for config).
    • A player can post a maximum of three propositions, but only under one post, and only one.

Topic will stay open two weeks, until Sunday 25th October.
On a side note Server time zone will go from CEST to CET that day.

The work will be then transmitted to the Amphitheatre (a group of people dedicated to work and think of what is best for DinoRPG within their own possibilities, which may be a bit small though...).

Thereafter things will be processed and a synthesis will reach the hands of Kiroukou.

Then everything is up to you
