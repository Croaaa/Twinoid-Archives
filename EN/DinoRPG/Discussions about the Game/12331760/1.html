I don't know by rules what you meant.


Pro league, any dinoz level. Amateur league, up to level 30.
Board have obstacles on, in asymmetrical view. It's a 20*20 grid.
48h match, 45 minutes Action Point tick, max 15 AP stackable. 24h pause.

Your clan put 45 dinoz on the board. 3 per master at the same time max.
Dinoz on the board cannot be healed, unless the Tik Bracelet. Or else you need to move it out, heal and move it in back from your 1st column side. You can re-equip it all you want (magic items still have the 5d unmovable).

A dead dinoz on board isn't auto-healed back. A revived dinoz cannot re-enter on the board.

When you entered on the board you have automatically 1AP.
You score points as how many column you pushed your line per hour. The farthest your line is, the more points you score. You beat your opponent if you get the biggest score at the end of the 48h or your opponent forfeited. In case of score tie... head or tails?

Groups are randomly made, groups will be between 8 to 15 clans, first pairing will be random, then winners vs winners, losers vs losers at the condition that clan never encounter another clan more than once, withing 6 qualifying matches.

Top4 of a group will be qualified. If several clans are tied, they have bonus points you will be able to see them by hovering your mouse on a clan name in a group. If they are still tied, MT have a secret way to see which is in or not.

Finals is knock-out fashion. If you lose, it's the end of the road...
