about the 2nd question:

for example, you want fire in your moufee. the chance of not getting it is 50% at the first time. If you dont get fire, then you can use career plan and you have 50% chances again.

so, the chance of not getting fire is 50%x50%=25% with career plan, and so, you have 75% instead of only 50%!

lets say you wanted . the chance of not getting wood is 80%. but with career plan is 80%x80%=64%.
