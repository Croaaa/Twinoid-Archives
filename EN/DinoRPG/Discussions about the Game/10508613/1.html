focus add damage bonus to an assault of any element.

strategy will make the better use of 'A' skill. 'A' skill replaces any regular attack to that kind of attack such as water cannon. So it can replace your strong water attack with it which is no fun... since your regular attack usually much stronger. With strategy, the use of water cannon will be in better timing (replacing your weak attack element).
