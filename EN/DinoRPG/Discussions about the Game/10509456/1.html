Different Dinoz for different jobs. You've got your resource gathering Dinoz for "times of peace" and then your fighting Dinoz for Clan Wars... eventually, you'll have about 20 of those if you do the whole 4 groups of 5.

Also, you can level a Dinoz up *just* for the purpose of getting a [U] skill and then freeze them because you don't need to play them anymore. This way you don't have to waste that skill point on one of your main fighting Dinoz.

Finally, there's the whole Dinoz sacrifice thing for Demon Dinoz - in the future, that is. Freezing your less satisfying Dinoz and saving them to be sacrificed for towards buying Demon Dinoz isn't a bad idea... You can always try to raise a "better" Dinoz. Eventually, you get luck and end up with the perfect combo of element hits that you wanted.
