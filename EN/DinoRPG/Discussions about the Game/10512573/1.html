epoq
So there you have it, Twinoid has been added to DinoRPG.

We just wanted to tell you a little bit about what has been added already and what else is coming your way!

Your Twinoid Passport has been added to the My Account page. The design leaves a little to be desired, but we'll work on that! As you know, players will have the opportunity to change their nickname, so it is important that we can see clearly that a player is who we think it is, before getting in touch.

That's what the Twinoid Passport is there to do. It is a highly visible feature which shows the Twinoid details, separate from those of DinoRPG.

The Messaging System.
We are planning a system involving folders or tags so that you can manage your messages more effectively. This has not been put in place yet, but all being well, this will be added soon!

The time! Yes, it's missing, and we know about it. We'll make sure to get it added before the next clan event.

The welcome page. Yes, the news articles are displayed in the old style. This is a limitation of Twinoid, but we count on changing this in the future.

To make up for the missing news articles, we will be making another Twinoid data feed which will keep you updated of all your messages and news articles via your Twinoid bar.

More information coming soon, and in the mean time feel free to add your comments and thoughts, and if you have any problems, please send us as much detail as possible.

All@DinoRPG
