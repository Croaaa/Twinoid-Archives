Teckna, before you can sacrifice Dinoz to get Demon Notes you need to finish the Katacombs located at the Sunset Cemetary and before you can freeze Dinoz you need to get Zor's Gloves and the Lantern. Needless to say you have a lot of game before you before you can do these things. If the Dinoz you want to get rid of is poorly leveled, you might think about letting him collect items available to skills in his/hers major element. Dinoz that collect items do not have to be as strong as Castle Attacking and/or Defending Dinoz--they just have to be able to stay alive fighting the normally encountered monsters. Plus you get extra gold and/or notes when you sell the ingredients. Clans are always looking for menbers who can donate ingredients too to the Clan, sepecially Dinoz who possess the Sharp-Eye Collecting Skill
