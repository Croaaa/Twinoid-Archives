@ Asm

You can get double skills on your dinoz when they are older.

For the double skill Bubble you need
Sapper
Vaporous Form

You can get these on a mouffe with some luck.

You need to take these skill paths

Mistral/Tornado/Vaporous Form
Water Cannon/Cold Shower/Sapper

Vaporous Form and Sapper will both be useful however the level 1 + level 2 skills from this path will do hardly any damage if your main element is fire. Your mistral does so little damage because you have hardly any elemental points in it, so your enemies defences reduce the damage to 0. So yes it is better to turn these skills off with the Strategy Book. But they are not "stupid" skills to take because they can lead to a wonderful double skill, but that all depends on how patient you are, it will take a long time to get and is only really useful in PVP.
