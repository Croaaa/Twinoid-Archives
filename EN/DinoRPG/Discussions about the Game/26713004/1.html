Next in the hall of eternal fame, we are proud to officially announce the winner of Inter-Dojo Tournament XXVIII: Goma!

Goma has accomplished the feat of defeating the swiss army knife of the DinoRPG playerbase, he took down Venar! Venar sent his long-trusted soldiers, Mustafar, Hoth and Bling into the fray and they once again did well, crushing Meischner, ICEY and RJun74, but when they glimpsed at the immense frame of Patricia, the not-so-gentle giant they knew the fight of their life was coming. Especially that Patricia was accompanied by the writhing snake-entity Rakshasa and Lui, a winks with fearsome, crushing pincers. These warriors have sent many great ones to the grave before, among them Mengo9999, SOTHO and Insanedar and they were ready to take another player for his last trip.

Without any idleness, the finals' participants jumped into the middle of the carnage and after Patricia's crucial move of disabling Mustafar's Torch, Venar suffered major casualties from a Flood, Sinuous combo. The bloodshed was followed by the snake-god going nuts on Bling and scraping half his life-force off. Not much later, Bling gave in to his fate and that's when Mustafar ignited his rocket thrusters, frenzied by his brother's passing he did everything possible for vengeance.

Meteorites, rains of fire, charges trampling others underfoot and counterattacks were executed, but in the end, the overwhelming pressure of three versus one was just too much for the frenzied warrior and he drew his final breath. Standing their ground, Goma's warriors enjoyed the cheers of the crowd and thus we once again pronounce Goma as the winner of Inter-Dojo Tournament XXVIII!
