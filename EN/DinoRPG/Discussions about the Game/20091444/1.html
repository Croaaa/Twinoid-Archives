XP for all Bosses apart of Venerable can be found here

Venerable
First Encounter
Beware it is 1 vs 1 fight.
Location: Venerable's lair
Immune to Petrification
Not immune to poison and infernal talons, but infernal talons do zero damage.
Attack aoe and it is a direct attack so danger detectors are not working. This attack removes immaterial status dealing 1 damage and penetrates bubble doing full damage.
Hints equip 3 refrigerated shield (Forges Shop) and 2 BR or survival kits. Switch off all A- , E- skills with strength under 90 or under strength of your strongest assault.
Switch off Detonation, Stumbs, Good Whack and Flood if it does small damage, all those skill eat your energy out very fast in solo fight and you get few attacks from Venerable in row after that.
Low level dinoz can try to use monos and pills. Not forget to switch off other not needed skills that can eat your energy like primal state.
Best time to kill: Better to do this before lvl 51 cause it was reported the xp drops a lot. It is also a good way to get additional lvl on dinoz that are supposed to be sacrificed or on dinoz that should be reincarnated.
XP XP for Venerable
Second Encounter
During the King's quest
This fight can be done in group (group leader and other dinoz should have an active turn and group leader initiate the fight).
XP low (under 100)

Morg
You will encounter Morg 2 or 3 times depending on if you finished Quetzu Quest before you started Chen's missions or not.
First encounter
Summit of Everwest. During the first part of Nimbao quest.
This fight can be done in group (group leader and other dinoz should have an active turn and group leader initiate the fight).
Attack usual, St Elmas Fire (aoe), doesn't remove immaterial status
Immune to petri
Not poison immune, not immune to slow.
Hints Switch off all A- , E- skills with strength under 90 or under strength of your strongest assault. Since he has usual attack it is a good idea to equip up to 4 flamelets and one BR. Usually group of 3 lvl 43 dinoz has no problems with Morg. Do not forget to switch off other not needed skills that can eat your energy like primal state.
Best time to kill As soon as you hit lvl 43, when you stop grinding on Summit and change to upper Nimbao.
Best possible group size unknown


Second encounter (Mutated Morg) Path to the palace. During the second part of Nimbao quest (Quetzu Quest). This fight can be done in group (group leader and other dinoz should have an active turn and group leader initiate the fight).
Attack aoe
Immune to Petri
Not poison immune not immune to slow.
Hints equip 3 BR or survival kits and pill. Mono if you have problems, fight in group. Switch off all A- , E- skills with strength under 90 or under strength of your strongest assault. Vines helps. Do not forget to switch off other not needed skills that can eat your energy like primal state.
Best time to kill Unknown, maybe before you go to Caushemesh. At lvl 45-48. Or earlier if you want to buy quetzu.
Best possible group size unknown

The Giant Demyon
This fight can be done in group (group leader and other dinoz should have an active turn and group leader initiate the fight).
Location end of Cuzcous Ruins

Encounters with Dark Dinoz
During CS quest and also during missions of Cassandra.
- This fight can be done in group (group leader and other dinoz should have an active turn and group leader initiate the fight).

Fight info for Scorpious/Scorpus:
HP 1200-1214, likely 1200.

Fight tips: -ini skills are great (flood, vines, stumps) as always in boss fights.
