sjefke

It's no mistake that you bought a gorilloz, but you should get the skills 'leader' and 'engineer' on it. Leader allows you to have 3 additional dinoz, so 21 instead of 18. Engineer gives all dinoz an additional combat gear slot.

The glidwings you probably will use to collect energies, can easily get the 'merchant' skill too. That will lower the prices of stuff you buy in the dinoland shop by 10%.

The next very important U-skill you should get is 'priest'. This will give your dinoz an extra health point every hour when it's resting.

You will need some patience to get these skills though.
