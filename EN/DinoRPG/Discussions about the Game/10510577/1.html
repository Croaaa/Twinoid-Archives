You get one turn per Dinoz every 23 hours.
If you do not take the turn then you lose it - they are not stacked.

If you rest the Dinoz on its turn then it will not get another turn for 23 hours.
After 23 hours, one of your other Dinoz can select "new turn" and move, rest or fight. This resets the turn timer for your account.

You can leave the Dinoz resting for another 22 hours to gain health. If you then stop resting, that Dinoz can take a turn and it will get another turn in only an hour.
