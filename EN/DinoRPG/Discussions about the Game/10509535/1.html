I decided to resurrect this topic since everyone's talking about it these days...

From what I can gather on the French Server News Page, here is what we can expect in the future:

The Magnetic Steppes were rolled out during the 1st Clans War, so we should expect this one next. This didn't come until after the start of the 2nd Clans War on the Spanish server, though.

The alien missions became available during the 2nd Clans War. This came after the Kabuki and during the 1st Clans War on the Spanish server.

About one month later, the Kabuki became available. On the Spanish server, this was available before the Strange Prowler was introduced (before there even WAS a clans war!).

The Elementary Teacher missions came available during the 3rd Clans War. On the Spanish Server, this came after the alien missions, and during the 1st Clans War.

Also, the Cinema Paradino contest came available soon after. This happened during the 2nd Clans War on the Spanish server.

During the 4th Clans War, the Magnettic Caverns came available. On the Spanish Server, it came available during the 3rd Clans War.

During the 5th Clans War, the wolf hunting contest was launched.

You can check the Spanish Server News Page for updates and clues as well.

I actually cannot find any news info about Demon Dinoz, so sorry about that...! Maybe someone who plays on those servers can verify that this is the latest expansion on those servers...?
