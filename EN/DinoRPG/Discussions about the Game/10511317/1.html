Not quite, Veren, but you're about right. You've forgotten that the first 100 turns or so are between 0-23 hours, so perhaps just less than that.

FYI: for a non-paying member, it takes about a year to get to level 23 if you don't plan your dinoz well. If you group them up, they level up quickly, therefore reaching level 40 will be quicker.

Here are some boosters that you could consider:
lavapit training center
Brutforce tourney.
Last 2 of Grandpa's missions, Bao Bob's second mission and huge hunt, Prowler's second mission (they offer 100+ )
Clan war attacking
Group fights (depending on level and monsters present, e.g. a group of level 18+ dinoz will meet chickens and will gain more there than fighting solo).

Tip: make sure each fight you have provides you 10 at least.
