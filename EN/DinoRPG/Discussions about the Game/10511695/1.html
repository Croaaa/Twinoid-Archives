Spheres teach skills.
If you use a sphere, the sphere is consumed but you get a skill.

If you reincarnate a Dinoz, he loses all skills
--- including those gained from any Spheres.

He can however, go and get another sphere since all the missions are reset for the reincarnated Dinoz.
