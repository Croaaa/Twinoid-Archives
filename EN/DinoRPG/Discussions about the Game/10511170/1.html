[None]
Before you get to do any of the missions with the Soft Shaman, you must first speak with him. You must first listen to him speak of Big-All Hot's history and tourism, and then you may select choices between speaking to him about the Vulnerable, and something else. Avoid speaking to him about the Vulnerable unless you have already talked to the dragon, and are planning to receive the Fire Charm.
