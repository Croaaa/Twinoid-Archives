The hostilities begin at 15h on the 6th of November 2013!

Prepare your dinoz, sharpen their claws and check their teeth! Reunite the clans, schedule a meeting of your war council, and maybe it's not too late to persuade a powerful master to join your clan...

The Mana War has undergone a few major changes for this event!

The King of Dinoville has brought about the following changes:

  • XP gain from battles boosted heavily.
  • Mana gains will be proportionally distributed amongst clans depending on their contribution to the war effort at the moment when the castle is destroyed.
  • A clan will gain mana every hour that their castle is not destroyed.
  • It will not be possible to change clan after day 2 of this war.

Here are the Mana War prizes:

  • Winning clan: Winner's Epic Reward + 3 Napodinos + 150 Treasury Notes
  • 2nd + 3rd clan: Epic Reward + 3 Napodinos
  • 4th - 6th clan: Epic Reward + 2 Napodinos +100 Treasury Notes
  • 7th - 10th clan: Epic Reward + 2 Napodinos
  • 11th - 15th clan: Epic Reward + 1 Napodinos + 100 Treasury Notes
  • 16th - 20th clan: Participation Reward + 1 Napodinos + 25 Treasury Notes
  • 21st - 25th clan: Participation Reward + 100 Treasury Notes
  • 26th - 30th clan: Participation Reward + 50 Treasury Notes

The war will finish on Wednesday the 11th of December at 15h00 server time!

Over and above these war system changes, it will no longer be possible to group a dinoz below level 10 who is due to level up.
