I suggest that you get the pearl as soon as possible. Too bad your highest dino is level 12 because if you take him to the islands to get the pearl, he will encounter the Eeloz, and take much damage because of them. The pearl is very helpfull in keeping your young dinoz close to full health. They recover 5 at the Fountain at 00:00 server time. A lot of missions can involve traveling through the fountain and getting 5 to 10 everytime you pass through sure decreases the time between rests. Also, by timing when you arrive and leave the Fountain, you can gain 10 in one turn just passing through the Fountain.
