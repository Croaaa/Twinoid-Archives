Do your dinoz physically have to be in a specific place during the fight or can they continue moving and doing quests?

Do they lose health during fights?
On november 12, 2009
w3ko
Each battle will take place at a random place (depending on the league), just like castles, and will change every battle. Your dinoz need to be at that place during the entire battle.
A player can place up to 3 dinoz on the board, and if one die, you can replace it with another one but you can't put the dead dinoz again during the rest of the battle.

Do they lose health during fights?
Sure they do. You CAN'T heal a dinoz that is already on the board, so heal them before entering the battle (you can equip stuff whenever you want).
