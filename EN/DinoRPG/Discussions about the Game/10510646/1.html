To get the charm you have to tell him that you were following a Korgon and then ask him to make you stronger*. He will then send you back to the Soft Shaman, who will then give you the charm. As for the Membership card, after talking to him you have to go back and talk to the wounded soldier in Dinotown and give him some bread.

* I can't remember the exact conversation path, but that's the gist of it.
