Grouping gives you more and less .
You save as well. It's definitely worth to group dinoz. Unless you have a dinoz that can handle enemies (example: a lvl 17 winks moving around dinotown kingdom, facing only green giants, pretty easy for the winks to move around without being bothered) you should group them.
Places like Atlanteid Islands and Grumhel Forest are such where you should walk in groups, as the enemies are quite dangerous and can give a single dinoz a hard time.
