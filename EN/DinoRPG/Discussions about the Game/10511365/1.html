Hi, Im a newbie (my softpig is level 15) so my advice might not be the most profesional. Ill tell you what I think tho.

I would not unlock the 1 skill. If you want to unlock another skill later, you have to 'waste' a whole levelup on 1 unlock again. I would try to unlock as much as possible at once.

I wouldnt look so much at the skills you can train at this moment, but also at skills that you can unlock in the future. Im planning on training my softpig Meteors (very strong) and Torch. Therefor I have to get Combustion and Incandescent Aura which I dont like that much..., then I have a levelup to unlock both Meteors and Torch and that will be worth it

So in your case I would train Fury to eventually get to Meteors. Or you can train Burning Heart first, it doesnt unlock any other skills (I think) but is a decent skill. My last option would be unlocking the 1 skill self-control....Good luck.
