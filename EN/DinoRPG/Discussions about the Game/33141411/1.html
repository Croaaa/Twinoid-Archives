DinoRPG has evolved once more.

From now on, dinoz will be able to travel their path to maximum potential much more quickly!

  • XP distribution re-evaluated, combats against monsters will earn between 20% and 90% more experience depending on the level of your dinoz!
  • New ultra-powerful dinoz from the dark world have started to appear all across Dinoland!
    Fortunately, they'll only be inclined to fight veteran dinoz! Will you be up to the challenge? Will you take them down?
  • The first mission of the Sage Rhubarbabao'riley is now only available to dinoz having reached level 30, the second is available to level 40 and the other missions to level 50+.
  • We take this opportunity to remind you all about dinoz under level 10, who have a free action every x hours, corresponding to their level. They are obliged to level-up when this action is offered. These extra turns are a helping hand to prepare your dinoz for battle!
