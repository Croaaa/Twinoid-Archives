Not all fights initiated within a discussion can be fought with more than one Dinoz. Only fights where the Leader starts the discussion at the 1st of his turn where all the Dinoz in the group have a turn/move will help the Leader when the fight starts after pushing the 1st discussion button. Fights started after pushing a 2nd or subsequent discussion button will be fought alone.
