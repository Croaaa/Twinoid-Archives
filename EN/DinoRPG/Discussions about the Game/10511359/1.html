You get items by digging in specific spots. For example, if you dig in the area "Fountain of Youth" you will get the item "pure water". You could check some Clan Pages to find other items gotten by digging. You're only able to dig once though, then the Shovel breaks and you'll have to go back to the Coal Miner to repair it.

To be able to dig you need to go to the Coral Miner first. Talk to him and he will give you a shovel. He is located at the Coral Mines in the Atlanteid Islands (need the Buoy to reach that map).

There are 4 items you can dig up:
1. Swamp Mud at Sticky Swamp (Atlanteid Islands)
2. Pure Water at the Fountain of Youth (Dinotown Kingdom)
3. Basalt Shard at the Basalt Slopes (Mount Big All-Hot)
4. Old Stone at Ashpouk Ruins (Mount Big All-Hot)

You just go to the right place and press the 'Dig' option in your Menu.

Each time you've dug up something, go back to the Coral Mines to repair your shovel at the Coral Miner.

Hope this helps

Note: To go to the Mount Big All-Hot map, you'll need the Climbing Gear!
