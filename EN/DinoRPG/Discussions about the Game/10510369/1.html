Running full time:

7 days to second dinoz

28 days total to third dinoz

45 days total to fourth. (this is the first one that is all 23 hour turns)

58 days total to fifth.

68 days total to sixth.

...And times continuing to get shorter.

This assumes 16000 for each dinoz, using all potions in first week, 23 hour turns being 23 hours apart, not 24, only spending on dinoz, 250gold per fight, doing the gold missions, and being there to take your turn any time of day or night. Also it doesn't factor in resting, and rounds off the days, but it gives a (very) rough estimate.

In reality, figure on getting the fourth dinoz at about 7 weeks or more.

Jaaay9: I think you have misunderstood the turns system. When you start with your first Dinoz you have to wait 1/2 hour for the second turn. Then 1 hour for the next, then 1 1/2 hour... increasing by 1/2 hour each turn, until it gets to 23 hours. It then stays at 23 hours for ever. It does not matter if you have one or twenty Dinoz at this point, the turns are the same length; they do not reset.
