Broken down in to points for easy browsing.

Inn- This is a great idea. Although I don't think a half-hour rest time is very helpful with the current turn system, even just an increased rate of healing while at the inn would be great. If possible, one inn per map would be great.

Bank-Also a great idea. If it was only accessible from one place (again, I'd suggest one bank per map) it would add an interesting bit of planning to the game. You'd have to account for shop purchases before depositing money or else you might find yourself in a tight spot.

New elements-This I don't think is a very good idea. While it's certainly interesting, it isn;t very fair to older players who's dinos won't have the same chance to unlock the new skills.

A new dino-This is a fine idea too. I like the idea of having to build one over a loing period of time. Maybe after doing the starting event (a quest or similar) all enemies of a chance of dropping a certain type of bone and you need x number of each type to build the new dino? This would mean you could go other places while collecting pieces and only in the end need to focus on an area with a particular enemy in order to get all the parts.

A new thing every month-I don't like this idea much either. While I would like to see new things more often, setting rigid deadlines without a plan of what to produce risks leading to poorly made, poorly implemented updates.

More turns-I'm in favor of the stacking turns idea. If turns stacked, you wouldn't miss turns while asleep or travelling. You could take a pleasant break, knowing that you'll have moves when you get back. Also, I wouldn't mind seeing a times during my current move of how long a wait I'll have once I submit my action. Sometimes I forget where my timer is and I'm not sure if I should stop and rest because I'll be asleep for my next turn or just keep going.

Optional ads-I know ads are a touchy subject, which is why I think they should be optional. People who don't want ads don't have to see them and people who don't mind them can maybe get a little bonus for them. They could either be an option box to turn them on for all pages or maybe just one page with ads where for every ad you watch you get a bit of gold or an extra turn or something.

More dinos per account: like this idea too. I think it should be semi-infinite, with some kind of goal needing to be reached to get more dinos. Maybe a certain in-game quest or reaching a certain level with a dinos (Maybe 20 or 30?) in order to unlock additional slots. This way people won't buy up tons of dinos in the beginning but will be able to have a mix of high-level and low-level dinos to play.
A spelling/grammar checker: It would be great if you could find two or three people to play through the game from the start and poin t out where there are errors. The translation is pretty rough and I know we have a thread for this, but I think getting some people to officially go through and clean it up would be great. If you go with this, you might want to make it something like a sub-staff position, screened and without direct correcting access so that things should move along effectively.
