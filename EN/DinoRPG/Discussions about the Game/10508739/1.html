Traveling in a group will save you some turn time too.
If you travel with each dino alone, they will add 30 mins to your waiting time each of them. If they are in a group, your waiting time for the next turn will only rise by 30 mins for the group together.

-> this is how it took me about 3 weeks to reach the maximum waiting time 23 hours, and I have had 3 Dinos almost from the start.
