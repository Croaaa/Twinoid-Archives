Loxagon

Its pretty much like Stinkymeat said. In mixed groups the higher level ones get a bit more XP than they would with a group of the same level and the lower level ones will get a bit less than they would earn normally in a group of only their level. It's just a few pinots with a small level difference, but grows larger the bigger the level gap is.

An example for a low level gap can be found here: http://twd.io/e/d98O0g/108

Example for a larger level gap:
Three dinos with level 41/65/65 get 413/538/538 base XP for adult dwarf archelion.
Three dinos with level 39/65/65 get 404/544/544 base XP for adult dwarf archelion.

You see that the larger gap gives the high level dinos more XP.
