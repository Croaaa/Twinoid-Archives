1. You must get the Demon Scroll inside the Katakombs (guide here), which can be found in the Cemetary (you need either your dinoz to have less than 10 or the Skully Souvenir (status item, you can get after completing the 3rd Skully Mission - guide here).

2. Teach it the Reicarnation Skill, from Celerity Path and once you reach lvl 40 you have the option to reicarnate it. You can do it anytime after lvl 40.

3. They always be at lvl 10 and have all 3 skills of their main elements and some random distribution from the weaker elements, all you gotta do is wait and choose for the one you find the best.

4. You can only reincarnate the dinoz once.

5. Well, there's no best moment actually, some people just sacrifice dinoz that have bad leveling. There's some information that states lvl 10 is the best time to sacrifice dinoz, money wise. In my opinion, sacrificing dinoz between lvl 24-28 is the best, so you don't have to get rid of a lot of them.

6. Yes, you can. The reincarnated demon will drop to lvl 1 after the process, then you can lvl it again as before.
