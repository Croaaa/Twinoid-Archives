I don't think it's true that groups get more , ceteris paribus. Of course, they get more collectively, but on a per dino basis it always seems to be about the same. I guess it is perceived that groups get more for 2 reasons:

1. People tend to notice 'big numbers'. So if their group of 3 dinos get 1800 gold they notice the nice four-digit number and are pleased, but if their solo dinos gets 600 it doesn't really register, even though it is basically the same.

2. A lot of the time groups of dinos get better XP (per dino) than solo dinos and seeing as most experienced players believe that is tied to XP, obviously this also means that these groups will get more on average too.
