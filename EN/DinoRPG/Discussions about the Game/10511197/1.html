Their is no conspiracy, the gold amounts have not been changed.

XP affects gold gained.
Grouping affects gold gained.
Randomness affects gold gained.
A slight random chance really affects gold gained. (The "Bonus" Gold Amounts)

And that is that.

If you think your not getting as much gold as you used to it will be because of one of the above reasons, generally its just "as a dino gets higher in level, they get less XP rewards...." but that does depend where you level them. You can get decent XP on each level of your dino if you know where to get it.
