My personal choice:

: reroll it if you have career plan, take Wrath, then Burning Claws if you don't.

: I see 2 viable paths:
- Savagery - Korgon Reinforcement - Acrobat / Wild Instinct: gives a slightly faster dinoz that can summon help
- Carapace - Vines - Precocious Spring / Primal State: gives a dinoz that can slow down enemies and help the group
I personally think that the second option is better.

: I think the most important skills here are Sumo, Aqueous Clone, Petrifiction and Sher-Kaar Ray. After that, if you have a LOT of extra points, you could try to go for Master Swimmer for the extra hit points and added power to your water attacks, or you could go for Marsh to get the double skill.

: Since your winks shouldn't get many or , I'm not convinced anything in the Focus path would be that advantageous, and they're probably better left to a rocky or glidwings (except perhaps for Focus itself). I would definitely get everything in the Celerity path, except perhaps Reincarnation. Adrenaline can be interesting if you want to get the double skill, and you can put your extra points either in Hospital Porter to help the group or maybe in Concentration or Regeneration.

: As in , I see 2 possibilities here:
- Strategy - Analysis - Specialist
- Agility - Dodge - Elasticity
The advantage of the first path is that Specialist will make you drop your attack, which will be far weaker than the others. The second path enables you to get the Overload skill ( double skill: +15% of multiple hit).
The thing to note here is that with only 10% chance of getting , you are not guaranteed to get the level 3 skill. If you knew you would get the level 3 skill, then Specialist is probably superior. However, if you don't, with the first path, you're stuck with 2 possibly useless skills (nobody really seems to know what Strategy and Analysis do), while with the second path, Agility and Dodge are useful regardless of what happens.
I guess this one depends on what you're willing to risk.
