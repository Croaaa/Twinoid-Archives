That's the idea of randomness. Each turn they have a choice between several actions and each time that action is pretty much randomly chosen. The reason why you feel like all actions your dinoz do are idiotic is because of 2 things:
1. In each situation there is usually only one best course of action and if there are several actions to choose from the chance of doing the wrong thing is rather high.
2. If a dinoz does the right thing you tend to take it for granted, whereas if your dinoz does the thing you absolutely don't want it to do you get frustrated and overthink it. It's quite a bit of psychology that makes it feel like your dinoz does all the wrong things much more often than you'd expect on basis of chance, while in fact it really does not.

I'm not really surprised by your story. The early stages of this game are very annoying indeed and the only solution against such unfortunate fights is to group your dinoz (no more than 3 per group, otherwise you gain less per fight.

Another factor involves builds. Most new players never see the elemental tree so I recommend viewing that (clan pages or rianon's homepage). The next thing is to decide what skills you need and what skills you don't need. If you have a question about a skill, ask it in the general questions thread. It takes a while to get a decent build. I've seen quite a lot of weird fights where, for example, a wanwan has fireball or a non- dino has water cannon.

You have already progressed quite a bit. So it's essential for you to focus on these 2 factors if you do wish to continue. I am not one of those who pays real cash in this game and believe me, it takes 3 years (maybe less) to become as strong as I am. It's very important to take heed of advice from veterans.

As for mybrute, I quit that game as soon as I found this game. I had similar reasons: random levelups, takes ages to level up, only 3 fights a day. I am surprised that my bro recommended mybrute to me 3 years ago, but I am glad I found this game via an advertisement of it.
