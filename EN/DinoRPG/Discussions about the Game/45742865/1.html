A U-skill / collector is not a bad idea. Yet, nowadays level 10 or 11 dinoz recharge two turns per 24h day. So that means more collecting on these dinoz so you should get those eventually.
Thus the U skillers are best Frozen as suggested Rianon info on freezing dinoz. In this way their U-skill will be active on your account, but you are not using a dinoz spot. You can have 18 dinoz active at one time, and 21 with the Leader U-skill.
In your starting phase, you should buy more dinoz as soon as possible, and fill the active-dinoz positions. Because more dinoz = more gold income from moving/fighting each day = grow faster.

Regarding the skills you leveled; I dont think there is anyone around that got it right from the start^^ You can always sacrifice (or freeze) dinoz and start over so its not a problem. Exp skills dont do enough and they wont help you in fights, so I personally dont bother with Intelligence (unless I need Overload). Speed on the other hand is still very important. Initiative + speed were the killers from before, now with the introduction of 'endurance' (every skill costs a certain amount of endurance to cast, blue bars in fights) they became slightly less important, but still very important imo.
You should also know that you can train ether skills from level 50+. This will probably influence the builds for your dinoz.

I would advice you to join a starter clan, there are usually people around that can answer your questions + it can be fun.
