Currently, there are many things to do! Speaking with every character usually results in doing missions

List of Missions: [in random order]
- Grandpa Joe at Grandpa Joe's House in Dinotown Kingdom
- Madam X at Brutforce in Dinotown Kingdom
- Skully at the Twilight Cemetery in Dinotown Kingdom

- Nurse Anna Tomie at the Clinic in Dinoplaza.

- Mr. Bao Bob at Bao Bob's House in the Atlantean Islands

- Soft Shaman at Lavapit at the Mount Big All-Hot
- Strange Prowler at the Forges of the Big All-Hot at the Mount Big All-Hot
- Elemental Master at Lavapit at the Mount Big All-Hot

- Nicolas Mulot at the Edge of the Forest in Grumhel Forest
- Diane Korgsey at the Korgon Camp in Grumhel Forest
- Forest Warden at Blacksylva Door in Grumhel Forest

- Request Office at the Rocky Outpost in the Magnetic Steppes

List of Quests: [in random order]
- Kabuki Quest starts once spoken with the Mysterious Punk at Frish Port
- Strange Creature Quest starts once spoken with the Strange Creature at the Fountain of Youth
- Rocky King Quest starts once spoken with the Citadel Guard at the King's Citadel
- Bambooz Quests starts once you have spoken to Jasper K. at the Steppes Borders

You also need to talk to other characters in order to receive items or 'unlock' other features!
