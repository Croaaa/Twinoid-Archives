bace135

Since this is your first dinoz, it's not uncommon to make some mistakes. We all did it. Your build is ok, because as your dino fights, it needs to stay alive too, it doesn't have a team. And you will have your glids for some time, so it will get those other skils you want for it too.

With that being said, you should use all your money to buy more dinoz, and don't spend on anything else. The more dinoz you have, the more money you earn each day. Eventually you will have 18 dinoz and you will be able to level your dinoz much quicker.

Also, it is wise to think about what you want each dinoz to be and what kind of skills you want for it before it picks any levels. Then you can make use of your level ups most efficiently. Good luck.

Nuke

I think you might have misunderstood what they meant.
The common way of training U-skillers, is to rush to the U-skill(s) and then freeze the dinoz, thus getting the bonus from the skill, without having it count towards your Dinoz limit.

My advice would be to get a new Glidwings, rush it to Merchant and then freeze it/use it to collect for a while. Meanwhile, train your current Glids to be a potent fighter.

Maybe I'm just being silly, though. Maybe you understood perfectly, I just thought it was important to make sure you didn't misunderstand, since that could cause your account some serious annoyances.
