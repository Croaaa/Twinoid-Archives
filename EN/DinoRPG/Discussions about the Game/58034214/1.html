Try to build groups (of around the same level) when you move around.
Use the rest action when you are low on health.

To make a group, your dinos have to be at the same spot an both must have the move/fight action available. Use the follow action and pick the dino the selected dinos should follow.

Before buying Irma's potions, save the gold to get more dinos.
