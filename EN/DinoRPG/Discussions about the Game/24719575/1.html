Afreet

Lightning
Hermetic Aura and Benediction are not very useful PvP skill. Hence should be excluded. Saving Puree could be excluded too, since you're going for Primal State in wood anyway. Both Archangels should be added instead. Wans are multielement dinoz, they need those additional boosts.


Water
Everything is ok, but remember that if you're not under TM or Banishment and all your team members doesn't have Without mercy, you need to turn off that skill.


Wood
1. Cocoon is useless in PvP.
2. Tenacity has much better analog in lvl50+ skill tree (named Force Control) and can be skipped (with Magic Resistance, which is also not very useful)
3. Primal State is a must on Wanwans (very useful in no item environment). Also it's prerequisite for the Shock skill, which will add a good boost to your assaults. As I already mentioned, Wans are multielement dinoz and they need all possible boosts.
4. Taking Growth is a matter of taste. Some people prefer to not waste ups on them, making more offensive dinoz, by taking more Lightning ups instead.


Air
It is also matter of taste, whether to go for 5 ups in air or take only 3 (Dodge). There are 3 main strategies:
1. Overload (also works well with Force Control)
2. Specialist/Achilles heel (+Vendetta)
3. Earth Tremor
Bonus: (very questionable!) some people think that Vaporous form could be good on a wan.


Fire
1. Leave Burning Heart on the very last up. I mean, take if after key skill (Self-Control) will be taken. Also consider not taking Burning heart for the sake of stronger Lightning element.
2. If you're going for Specialist/Vendetta, add Fury and Vengeance to your plan.

From my point of view, perception is a must have skill for any dino, it is especially essential for dinoz doing multi hits. A wanwan, especially a multi-hit one, is crap without it if you ask me.

Other things:
- you do not need saving puree or magic resistance, take primal state instead (as a bonus you get vines, very helpfull skill in non-TM enviroments and very usefull during levelling your dinoz)
- you do not need jump: flying dinoz are seldom, and you have other ways to attack them or get them to the ground
- you do not need hermetic aura => useless bonus
- you do not need benediction => useless bonus
- you do not need fury => i would say this even hurts, as 2 defense points are more important then 3 attack points
- you should definintly go for the archangel skills, they give a great boost
- shock is also a stong skill for a wanwan, since it uses it's 2 most powerfull elements

As for sumo, there are really 2 schools of thought here. Some say it is a mandatory skill for any dino, other say that a wanwan can live without it, and can compensate the missing HPs by using the extra 4 skills to boost his attack. All in all, sumo is a though skill to get with only one water element in the grid, so if you get lost underway, you wasted the skills for nothing.

As a final tip, I would suggest you join a clan, they usually have good build guides, which they will not share here. Also, in my opinion, the moderators are way to eager to lock such basic discussion threads, and pointing you to the general questions thread, so it is hard to have a good discussion on the public forum about skills on a certain dino.
