1. Spend gold sparingly. Hold on to your starter items for as long as possible, especially the Irma's potions. Don't use them just because you want to play more.

2. Keep track of your move time. If you make a move shrotly before going to sleep, use the rest option if your health it low. THis way you'll sleep through the resting period and be able to move when you wake up.

3. If you think your dino is strong enough, do the missions The HIlls Smashrooms and Wolf Hunting as soon as possible. Both have gold rewards. Doing the walking missions later might mean you get to encounter stronger enemies worht mroe experience.

4. Keep track of where you are and what your doing. Avoid making accidental moves and going to the wrong location.

5. If you have a long wait time, don't just sit around staring at the timer on the move page. Go off and get invovled with something else. Write down the time of your next move if you think you'll forget and come back then.

6. Spend lots of time planning out the future of your account. Look up your Dinoz level-up sheet and see what types of skills your likely to get. Go look up the skills and see how oyu want to train your dinoz. Get all the skills? Fast-track your way to certain ones?

7. Try your hand at writing or drawing related to your account. I can waste a good 20-30 minutes on what I consider a short writing excerize.
