On august 8, 2011
loam
You can try selling them if you'd like, but if they aren't raised well they may not sell.
Freezing them is a good option.

Another option would be to do the katacombs quest to get a demon. There, you'd sacrifice a lot of your dinoz and you could buy a demon from them. I recommend this for a team of dinoz ~level 25.

I think a lot of people (including me) start out not knowing what they're doing and end up with some poorly raised dinoz. My chosen method was to sacrifice them.
Remember, you can always unfreeze dinoz if you have room on your account. You can also unsacrifice them if you have not bought a demon yet. Both take 24 hours to undo (if I remember correctly).
