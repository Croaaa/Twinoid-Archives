their is no "maximum", you will not be able to get all the double skills, simply not enough levels, nor would you want to get every single double skill. Generally it consists of going down a path you would otherwise not go down to obtain one, if every single path your dinoz chose was one you don't like it would be a silly dinoz.

Also, realistically you will only get enough levels to obtain a double skill where at least 1 of it's elements is in your main element, i.e bubble skill, will mostly if not entirely be seen on dinoz which have a high water or air chance or both.

Thus the notion of obtaining bubble on a dinoz with hardly any water or air, such as a wanwan or rocky is ridiculous not that's saying its impossible, just unlikely.

1 double skill is easy, 2 is entirely possible, 3 is pushing it as this would require six level 3 skills of various elements. You may be able to get more than 3 but its unlikely anyone would ever bother.
