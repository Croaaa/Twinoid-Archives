I got few request about leveling guide last time. So I suppose it is time to create one.

I will provide a leveling guide for non paying members, any paying member can also provide here one for paying members. I will be also glad to any improvements and suggestions especially about Caushemesh part, where I don't have enough data.

Bla bla part: What is leveling guide, it is the best order of picking missions and changing regions in order to get maximum xp and grow up fast. Unfortunately game is balanced so, that most missions are simple not needed and you get less xp doing missions, than simple grinding (grinding is fighting at one/two locations without taking missions), of course it is also depend on type of your team at what point you should change region or location. You can have teams with a lot of A-skills or rather assault/healing teams. Another reason why we need a guide is because monster spawn at the same location can change with growing level of dinoz, also the income from daily moves depends on level of the dinoz in group. Beware it is better with irmas always keep the levels of all dinoz in group at the same level. For fights between lvl 30-35 on Nimbao XP loss for mixed groups was reported (for the same enemies you get in total less xp)

It is Levellign Guide for fight dinoz. (Adjustments for gatherers and nimbao gatherers will follow).

(1) We start with Grandpa Joe missions:
- Do the 1,3,4 mission.
- Start 5 and with active 5 mission kill water elemental (rest and use 2 flamelets and 1 br) get buoy. You can try to kill fire elemental also.

(2) Now you will try to get lantern and pearl.
The reason of getting lantern so early is that you get it before lvl 12 and hence not face HP consuming Eeloz in Atlanteid Island region. If you will have serious problems wit elementals and/or scorpscuba you can get lantern much later simple finish Mission 5 and then 2 of Grandpa Joe skip (2) and go to Lavapit to do Shaman missions (see (3)). If you still will have problems with elemental continue with rest of Grandpa Joe missions till you will be able to kill fire elemental.

- Go to Mutant Falls (Atlanteid Islands region). Talk to atlantean guard about his friend Jovebose. If it is your very first group of dinoz speak to Master Hydargol.
- Go to Coral Mines (Atlanteid Islands region) and get Lucky Shovel.
- Kill scorpscuba when you will pass by Frish Port (rest or even heal to 100% and use 2 flamelets and 1 br, beware Scorpscuba gives 100 base xp on low levels so better kill it when you have at least 100 xp till next lvl). You can also kill it later you will pass by Frish port at least 3 more times. If it is your very first group of dinoz speak to Monk and get Waterlily pad.
- Go to university kill fire elemental if you still didnt' do this.
- Go to Basalt Slopes (Big All-Hot region) get basalt.
- Back to Coral Mшnes fixing shovel then move to Fountain and get water.
- Back to Coral Mшnes fixing shovel then move to Sticky Swamp to get mud.
- Go to Underwaterdome. Get Zor's Glove (heal and use 2 falmelet and 1 BR on Earth elemental, beware it gives around 80 xp)
- Go to Mutant Falls. If it is your very first group of dinoz speak to Master Hydargol and give him waterlily pad. You get the epic Fountain Pearl as reward (allow any you dinoz that is at Fountain of Youth at 00 00 restore 5 hp). Speak to Master Hydargol again and get waterlily pad.
- Go to Haunted Hills (Grumhel Forest region) get lantern.

(3) Soft Shaman and Elemental Master
- Go to Lavapit (Big All-Hot). Speak to Soft Shaman and do 1,2,3,4,6,7,9,10,11 missions.
If you skipped (2) you will need to get lantern as soon as you start mission 9 (Hieroglyphs). Simple do what described in (2) then continue with mission.
- After finishing mission 11 of Shaman, another npc Elemental Master (EM) will become visible at Lava pit.
- Do all missions of EM and get sphere. Usually your dinoz is around lvl 20-23 at that point. And you can estimate if you keep this dinoz for further pushing with irmas (if it looks very promising), or you sacrifice (freeze) it for now/always (if it is U-dinoz or you see it screwed) or you continue to push him with daily moves (if it is not clear if it becomes goo or not). You can also sell spheres for getting more coins for pushing or collect them in sets for your future dream team.

(4) Dian Korgsey
Go to Korgon Camp (Grumhel Forest access via Deep Gorges)
Do first Diana's mission and get status item fins.

(5) Dark World
You are around lvl 23. If XP HP ration is not great you cna try to get out without key and jump to (6) if you also have problems in Forest you can go back to Grandpa Joe and finish 10 and 11 mission first, then try Grumhel forest or Dark World again.

If you don't use immaterial dinoz for clearing catacombs to Monster Island (MI) region, you can go out with a gorup of lvl 25 dinoz without black sylva key and send them to catacombs of MI. But it is realyl better to do this wtih imamterial dinoz or some high levelled brave jumping dinoz like pig or mouffe. MI isn't really needed for non paying members imho

- Got to Bao Bob (Atlanteid Islands) and focus 7 dinoz with your clan mates or alone.
- Grind in dark world until you reach lvl 29 (when xp for amepenne drops).
- Get status item blacksylva key and go out.

(6) Nicolas Mulot
If you see that your dinoz will turn out good most probably you can do Mulot missions, since additional combat gear is always good in pvpv, but if you are still nto sure and you rather need more levels to see how it turns out, jump to (7)
- Go to Edge of the Forest (Grumhel Forest)
- Do all Mulot's missions get back pack status item.

(7) Nimbao
If you experience big problems with your lvl 29-30 Dinoz go to Grandpa Joe House, do the Mahamuti Quest for Al Zaimeur do his first mission and then take 2 and rotate it among dinoz of your group without validating. Till you get 1-2 levels then try Nimbao again.

- Get access to Nimbao region (see Nimbao quest) and send your dinoz there.
- Start grinding on Gate to the upper level (Nimbao), continue grinding till lvl 35.
- At lvl 35 try to switch to Summit of Everwest (Nimbao) and check if pink gromster spawn there or only lapoufs, check how much XP you get and how much HP you lose. If it is not so good as at gates get back to gates (as option try Prison at that point). Anyway at lvl 36 you need to go to Summit. Grind there til lvl 42 (at that point it is good to kill Morg).

Starting from here different ways of pushing possible depending on if Caushesehs region is already available for you and if you have some groups there. We assume that Caushemesh is still not available. Check for alternatives at (10)

- After killing Morg or simple at level 42 switch to Upper Nimbao Subregion (see Nimbao Quest part 2 or Quetzu Quest for unlocking Upper Nimbao). Grind at Archdorogon Palace or Path to Archdorogon Palace or at Sewers location (look for location with the highest spawn rate for double dorogons, form my observation all this 3 location give around the same spawn rate for double dorogons mixed team and double dorowolfs). It is also a good time point for killing Mutated Morg (see Quetzu Quest). Grind there till lvl 48. Go to Venerable as soon as you hit lvl 48 and kill it.

( 8 ) Preparation for Caushemesh
Start Caushemesh Quest and get Access to Caushemesh. A good way to do Cassandras missions with one of your group dinoz and then continue with Caushemesh Quest till the point where you set Colossuses free, but do nto do this, grind (try Gravity Rock if it will be too hard then Ethereal Well) till you hit lvl 50 and unlock lvl 60 on your dinoz (or even switch to ether skills) then colelct enough coins (300k) and set Colossuses free and kill them fast using irmas.

(9)Caushemesh
Grind till lvl 55 at Pyramid Interior or Gravity rock (they gives the same enemies). When you xp drops there are few options:
Brave dinoz with Cursed Locker can try Dark Dinoz at Extraction complex or Demons skull island (if they can hunt). Usual groups can also try Dark dinoz at Extraction complex (you will have problems if you don't have any fast ini dinoz in group). Fighting Dark dinoz you can use lories or change nubmer of dinoz in group, also it can be useful to hold your group on 1 hp. If you are not happy with outcome of fighting with dark dinoz you can try Entryway to Pyramid after lvl 55.

(10) Adjustments
If Caushemesh available some ppl switch there earlier (lvl 42+) and report better XP/HP ratio (Depends on team, I suppose) I hope we will be able to collect more info on subject here. Also using mixing groups of 50+ and 40+ dinoz can be good at Caushemesh.
If you finished Caushemesh Quest and Sofia is at villa a possible option is to stay at Nibmao till lvl 49, then go kill venerable, use Brut force tournament and Misisons 10, 11 of Joe and/or Strange Prowler missions. To hit lvl 50 and unlock lvl 60 at villa. Then jump to (9). Unfortunately if you don't switch to ether skills at lvl 50 you will need to go back to villa to unlock them later.


So having a good account structure and using all coins from ingredients and daily moves only for pushing main team will help to get a high leveled team pretty fast (3x50 in 1,5-2 year) without using real money. More on good account structures will follow someday in another thread, for now you can get it from me (PM) as link to collected by axians info for beginners.
