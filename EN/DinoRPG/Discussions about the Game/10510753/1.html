How does my dino die?
Your Dino will die when his bar reaches 0.

How can I stop it from dieing?
You can let your Dino rest till 50% of his or give him a Cloudburger or Meatpie. You can buy food in the shop.

Can I save my dino while it is dead?
There are 2 options: Revive normally or use an Angel Potion.
If you revive normally, your Dino will lose some and will return to Dinotown.
If you use an Angel Potion, your Dino won't lose any and it will stay where it died.
