Ok so the overall atmosphere is pretty neat and (almost) original, but one thing does get on my nerves, well actually two things:

1 - you have to wait like 3 hours for each turn (I've noticed that it varies, dunno on what it depends though)

2 - If you die you get 1 hp PER HOUR (wtf?)

These 2 things really kill the game for me, cause, I registered this as in one of those "break from work" kind of games and thus I only play it 2 turns per day! (3 + 3 = 6 hours, 8/8:30 hours of work per day is what I usually do, so I never get to the 3rd turn). That's like, not very clever if you ask me. Yes I know the point is for me to pay, but these games are more lucrative if you get somekind of ad-sense banner campaign to support the costs I think, because not many people will pay just for those extra turns, they'll just play another game.

Anyway, just felt like sharing this with you and know if someone else agrees with me.
