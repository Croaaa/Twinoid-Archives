Er, you seem to be a little mixed up here.

a) Specialist removes the lowest element (based on the number of time you've rolled that element at levelups.

b) The first element to be used is nothing to do with attack vs defense etc on your opponent. Your Dinoz will ALWAYS attack first with the highest element (ie has been rolled most times at levelups).

So, a Wanwan with 8 7 3 3 2 (or whatever) will lose to Specialist, and always attack with first, even if ends up doing more damage to a -weak opponent.
