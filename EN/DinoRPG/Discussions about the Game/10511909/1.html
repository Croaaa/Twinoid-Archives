Buy more dinoz - more dinoz means more received every day. You can have up to 18 dinoz (or eventually 21). Plus it is pretty hard to play with solo dinoz - better group them into groups of 3 (or 4 or 5). Generally, groups get more and than solo dinoz and you lose less .

For more missions, see Bao Bob (on the Islands) and Soft Shaman in the Lavapit (Volcano).

Also, try to join a clan - the game is much more fun this way!
