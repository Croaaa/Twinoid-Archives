In general, I wait to finish the grandpa joe's missions, before to try to fight any elemental.
(so it's something like level 7 or 8, depending...)

About how to choose the better elemental to fight with first : you can go in the details part of your dinoz, and check its defenses : if its defences are better in fire, I would choose to learn rock climbing, with the fire elemental, if its better defenses are in water, the water elemental...

The good point if you wait to have a good level, it's that you don't need any meat pie or cloud burger : you just need to rest until you are at the half of your HP.

But everybody can have a different strategy.
