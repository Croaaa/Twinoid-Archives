No. Don't get your Dinoz to follow. Move your Cloudoz to Grandpa Joes and do some missions. You should soon get another Dinoz. Do the same with that one. Leave Hills Smashrooms and Wolf Hunting until you are nearly level 4, then you should avoid the Gluon and just get Smashrooms and Wolves.

To follow, Dinoz must be in the same place, with a move left. A new button appears "Follow". It does not matter which one leads, but it is that one who moves and fights. Make sure all Dinoz do the talking, looking, etc.

Search the Forum for more about Following.
