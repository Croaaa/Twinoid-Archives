U-Skillers (team of 4 dinos which the gor going for leader can lead later on), which get their U-Skills, while leveling relatively save in a group of 4 (since they rush the U-skills they aren't that strong in fights); I used a gor for Leader/Engineer, a Glid for Priest (and Prof, if it gets much air), a Cloud for Merchant and Professor (alternatively go for prof only and get career plan with the lightning-ups and get merchant with your gathering dinos) and a Sirain for Storekeeper and Cooker.
