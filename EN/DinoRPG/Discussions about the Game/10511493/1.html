There have been plenty of threads explaining initiative and speed. If you search the forumn you will find very detailed explanations which may help.

I will not go into massive detail giving examples and what not, I have already done this in other threads on the matter.

But suffice to say, it is easy to work out if you picture an untold dinorpg mechanism which is time. Time however is not measured in seconds or minutes, but in attacks, rounds and turns, and all this affects the combat.

Initiative usually only plays a part at the start of a fight, but thanks to certain skills it can be re-introduced in the fight.

Now imagine this timeline in the fight, Dinoz A with a high initiative will (probably due to dinorpgs random factor) move first and is at the begging of the timeline. Dino B with less initiative is further down the timeline.
In between Dino A and Dino B is a void, so after Dino A attacks it would be Dino B's turn. But this may not be the case, because as soon as Dino A attacks his next attack is put into the time line. This is a constant figure, but it is affected by Speed. If he has a high speed his attack will be closer in the timeline compared to his first attack than if he had a low speed. So depending on the size of the void will determine how many of these speed affected attacks will be made.
If a skill changes a dinoz initiative, then thier place for thier next attack on the time line is altered. Say detonation will increase the void between thier attack they are about to make and next scheduled dinoz attack. And alternatly vines will cause another Dino to move down the Timeline.

Hopefully this helps you understand a little better, I said I wasn't going to use an example, I guess I lied. I like examples. If your still unclear on the matter thier are very concise explanations in the forum.

The base speed is 1.00
If a Wanwan has Acrobat and Celerity and any one Dinoz in the group has Electrolysis then the Wanwan starts with a speed across all attacks of 1.00*1.15*1.15*1.05 = 1.388625

It will almost certainly has Lightning Attack and maybe Adrenaline so that the speed when using Lightning is 1.388625*1.50*1.40 = 2.9161125

In the posted video, the first action is to pop a monochromatic meaning that ALL attacks are Lightning. The second action pops a Relaxative pill which doubles speed so the Wanwan then has a speed of 5.832225

It also uses the Demon ability Mass Frenzy which grants the Accelerated status (which doubles speed) so the Wanwan now has a speed of 11.66445

With that speed, it will get 14 attacks just in the initiative boost granted by Reflex and Hazard. In addition to that, it will get 11 attacks to every one that a Dinoz with an unboosted speed would get.

Factor in the 5 x attacks from Lightning Dance and the various multi-hits and the effect increases even more.
