What can i get when i join Clan?
On march 2, 2009
[None]
At the beginning, not to much.

Just to be with a bunch of other player in the game, talk with them in a private forum.

The clan can create pages: private pages only the clan members can access, and public pages all dinoRPG players can see.

When the first war will be launched, the clan leader will be able to create a "castle", which one you will have to defend against other clan.

At the end of the war, the bests clans will receive some special rewards.

To access some hidden part of the map (I don't know if it already exists in the english version), you will have to bring your dinoz somewhere with other dinoz of the same clan.


But for now, I think that the clan is more about sharing your feelings, your questions and your experiences about DinoRPG with other players in a smaller community, even with some real friends if they also play this game.
