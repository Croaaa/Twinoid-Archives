Plus the community got a lot of information via the free dojo pvp fights, including statistics. For example you can turn off all skills but one and inspect how often it activates. Then you can do that with another skill, then both of them to see how they affect each other. Or how defense and armor affects damage taken. Or many others.

Almost everything can be inspected via dojo (for free) if you have enough time, without sacrificing an otherwise winnable battle (noone would've turned on combustion in a fight in the old days just to see how much damage it causes, and you still wouldn't know the opponents wood power to get useful data).
