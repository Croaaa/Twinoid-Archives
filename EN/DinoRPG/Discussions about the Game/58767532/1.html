Bengeldorn

The problem is that we have too few clans participating for various reasons.

Especially new clans, or clans who don't have players with strong dinos (yet), lack targets to attack, and their sole purpose is to pay the starting fee and get the prizes.

In addition mana war drains you clan treasuary a lot for building up the castle, starting a war and paying tax.

The top clans allready have few clans to choose from, and once attacked, they can't be attacked for 4 days again, because the honor penalty is too big to do it anyways.

Lower ranked clans have the same problem, because attacking a stronger clans usually results in loosing more than gaining.

In my opinion the first step for improvement would be to reduce the increment of the honor penalty/bonus for declaring lower/higher ranked clans by one or two, while also decreasing the duration of the honor penalty after decleration of war (currently 4 days).

To motivate and provide fun for new or less strong clans, introducing 2 NPC-Clans* could help to get more clans participating.

*NPC-Clans
An NPC-Clan would be virtual clan, that allways have less mana, than the lowest ranked clan. It never starts wars or counters when attacked. The castle is filled with strong monsters or monsters that are hard to kill, but provide good xp when defeated (Dark Dinos, Monster Hunt creatures or Leeches/Spagetthi for example). The castle might allways be in the same location or change everytime after it was defeated.
Honor penalty after war decleration and the duration for that are the same as for declaring normal clan.

But maybe that's a dumb idea, which won't come anyways.
