Unfortunately this game is a "few minutes a day" game, or fortunately, depends on your perspective.. you can get ridicliously massive stacks of gold by buying with money or just saving it up, and play for a long time all at once. But yeah its not a game that sucks up a ton of time unless you want it too. You can buy gold with real money and spend it little by little, sticking to 10-20 minutes a day game play.

Suffice to say though, when you get more dinoz you will have a lot more to do, even if not daily, sometimes my daily turn is 5 mins long, and sometims I have gold to spend lol, but in the long run, more dinoz means more goals and more to do, more gold generating, more gold that needs spending, and in general more fun. Just stick it out, and save gold for more dinos. Look through as much info as you can to learn about the game and thus what dinos to get. If you wish to speed things up a bit, not massively, but if you buy one of the smaller gold amounts, 5 Euro's for 30,000 gold, that will give you enough with a little gold from your savings ingame to buy 2 16k dinoz, with 3 dino's you have triple (actually a little bit more thanks to group earnings) the income. It will speed things up considerably.

The best tip used these days as well as don't buy food and irmas too early is simply get as many dinoz as possible. At your 12th or 13th dinoz, start thinking about getting U skills, especially leader, so you can get another 3 dinoz once you reach 18 dinoz. Averagely, you'll get 200 per dinoz, however this average may drop if you keep your dinoz in the same area for way too long, such as keeping a level 30+ dinoz in Dinoland as you will generate very little and if you do that. Your average you receive from all your dinoz will go up if you do missions as well as random bonuses such as what Sithis got. However, don't rely on bonuses to come, unless you have the maximum account of dinoz.

the second tip is open a credit account for this game and buy like crazy. If you do this, then your priority will be leveling up quickly.

This game is a combination of free MMORPG and money-required MMORPG. (refering to those who really have the money and can advance right through the game. The game is generally free for all.) So you can choose what you want to do.
