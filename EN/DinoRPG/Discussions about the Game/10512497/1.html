Wobby
Wrath, Focus, and Good Whack only apply to assaults, and not A skills (with the exception of Lightning Dance).

An interesting thing to note is that if you use any of those 3 E skills followed by an A skill that doesn't work with the E skill, it will carry over until you use an assault or Lightning Dance. For example, I saw Lolwhut's Demon Wanwan use Good Whack, and then an A-Skill that wasn't LDance. Then is used Good Whack again the next turn and did an assault for 20 and 24 damage. 400% assault boost for the win.
