Pyxel smashes the Monster Hunt out the park! In a rare feat, one Dinoz master made an early break from the pack and maintained it until the very end, assuring fame, fortune, and the all-important bragging rights for this event. Pyxel's trophy is winging its way to the trophy cabinet as we speak!

With a massive 109 masters claiming a gold Napodino and almost 600 masters claiming prizes in this event, we can be sure that competitors are well equipped for the COTC!

The top ten hunters were: Pyxel, Targetn, Ghenya, Goma Sininchi, Gigasinner, Henrique04, Condordog, Mengo9999, and Jaaa66. Congratulations to everyone who participated and scooped some loot! To check out the final rankings, click: Monster Hunt Results

After a gruelling monster hunt, Dinoz masters look forward to a well-earned rest. However, that'll need to wait as there are far more pressing matters - the Championship of the Clans is here!
