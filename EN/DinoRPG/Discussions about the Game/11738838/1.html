[895738]

Ok, your options are the following:
Reset your account and get a single elemental dino that will have the chance of taking out a gluon in 1 punch. I recommend a sirain, because water cannon + more will really help. Since you are new, this shouldn't be too costly, but you'll lose the newbie boost items though.
Keep going and go to joe's and do the wolf mission until you level up again. As said earlier, when doing the wolf mission, you won't encounter gluons whilst following the instructions but when you complete the mission, there's a chance that you will encounter a gluon. The gluons specialise in so if you have a higher defence, their absorption skill will be less effective. Other than that, they can hit hard with any element that your dinoz is weakest with in terms of defence. When you get more dinoz, make sure you group them, but I don't recommend large level gaps within the groups. It distorts the distribution in favour of the higher level dino.
