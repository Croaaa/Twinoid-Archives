Facts to know (I actually leveled up one to try it)

1 - It doesn't stick. You start with it, but once it goes it goes.
2 - The speed it looses is worse than that of a gorilloz with collosus
3 - In pvp the counter attack skills (acid blood, accupuncture, torch) will remove it...
4 - Any element damage done by the fauna in the game will also remove it.
