To answer the question in detail:
Renaissance is the status item to indicate a rebirthed dino.
Rebirth is similar to Reincarnated, but with some differences.
Unlike Reincarnated, you don't need a skill to rebirth your dino.
Instead of 5 random element points, rebirth gives your dino 10 random element points.

To rebirth a dino, it must be level 80 and dead (0 ).
You then have the option to rebirth it, in addition to revive it as normal.
When you rebirth the dino, you can choose any one skill, the dino has obtained, and keep it after rebirth. The dino starts with enough to immediatelly to level 10.
The skill you choose is immediatlly available for your dino, but to get any skills after the kept skill, you first have to get the skills before the kept one.

For example:
You rebirth with Double Hit. You won't be able to get Career Plan until you get Celerity first.

When you rebirth a dino, you also get the Reincarnated status item, thus you cannot reincarnate a rebirthed dino.
You also cannot rebirth twice, but you can rebirth a Reincarnated dino, but the extra elements from Reincarnated will be overwritten by those from rebirth.

I believe that's all, or did I miss something?
