Just to ensure that all the causality relations are clear:
- to go to the deep gorges, you will need the lantern, which you obtain from the strange man at the haunted hills
- to go to the haunted hills, you need the waterlily pad in order to cross to the Grumhel jungle, which you obtain from Master Hydargol at the Mutant falls
- to obtain the waterlily pad, you need the zors glove, which it seems you already have.
