You cant "use" a battle ration to fill your health before a fight, they can only be used in battle to restore hp that have been lost during the fight. Also, as was mentioned above, they restore "up to" 20 hp. It is not uncommon for them to restore only 5 or 10, and yes sometimes only 1 hp.
Since battle rations can not be used to fill up hp before a fight the items are priced to benefit buying larger ticket items:

10 hp - 700 (70 per hp)
30 hp - 2000 (67 per hp)
100 hp - 6000 (60 per hp)

Battle rations can range anywhere from 50-1000 but cannot be used outside of fights.
