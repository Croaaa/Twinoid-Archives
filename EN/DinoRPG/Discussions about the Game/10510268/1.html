Ah btw u got a mistake in ur
"Requirements to reach level:"
actually its always +7,5%
The exact formula is
100*1.075^(x-2) where x is the lvl to reach starting from 2.
Important: Be sure NOT to round up or down, just to cut off the numbers behind the coma.

And ull get sth like that
100 = 100 for lvl 2
107,5 = 107 for lvl 3
115,5625 = 115 for lvl 4
124,2296875 = 124 for lvl 5
etc

I figured that out some time ago to calculate what level i must be before i start doing missions for my high end dinoz.
