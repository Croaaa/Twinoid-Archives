Zephides
Fetith Breath poisones the opponent with each element he uses + the amount of dmg is based on amount of air.

Poisoned Claws only works with a water attack and gives 14 dmg each time.

So Fetith Breath covers all elements and gives more dmg to your opponent. Therefore, Poisoned Claws would be a waste of a lvl up.
