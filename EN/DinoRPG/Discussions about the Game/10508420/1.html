Unless you want to spend money (and I see from your posts that you are unable to) this is a game of patience. I suggest planning ahead. Always remember where your final destination for any given trip is; write it down if you have to. Learn the map well, to minimize getting lost and making a turn-wasting move. Also, read all mission direction carefully and read one of the mission guides posted on the forums if you feel the need. These suggestions will help keep you from making unnecessary moves.

If you haven't checked it out yet, look up a skills guide on the forum. With your Dinoz type, you're going to be getting primarily fire type level-ups and skills.

Also, I think the post about 'Intelligence' was referring to the in-game skill; it's a level 1 Lightning type skill that increase EXP gained in battle by +5%. If you happen to get a Lightning level up, I highly recommend it, especially since your Dinoz species is unlikely to be able to get Lightning moves often so there isn't much point in trying to build up to the strong lightning moves.

Hope this helps!
