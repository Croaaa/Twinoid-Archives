...could be something like 100-500 gold that allows you to rest without waiting for a new turn, and maybe 1000-5000 "bed and breakfast" that would allow you to rest above 50%....

Good, but...
Right now, 6000 will heal 100 with no time or turn lost (Bread). If you're going to do this via a boosted rest function, it will take time.

Rest costs 1 action, 1 day, for 24. 1 turn gets you about 210, so you can say that's the cost of resting (this is, after all, a 1-turn-a-day game).

An extra turn costs 900 (Irma).

900 Irma + 2 * 210 turn = 1320 for 48 in 48 hours.
(That's the turn you could have taken with the Irma, plus the turn you spent waiting after that turn, plus the cost of the Irma).

If you have more than 100, it gets cheaper relatively: 900 + 560 (64 hours, if you have 130 ) = 1460 for 64, only 23 per 1, so let's just go with 100.

So the first 48 is cheaper than a Pie that heals 30 for 2000.

*ahem*
If the Inn takes time, call that a penalty of 9 per hour, or per 1 if the healing isn't accelerated. That's 450 for the 50 hours of over-50% healing. The lowest food cost is 3400 for 50 with no turn or time spent. Pie+burger+burger saves you the time, so 2950 time-adjusted. (If it doesn't take time, it may as well be food.)

1320...2950... I think I'm putting the value of an Inn stay, if it is unlimited over-50 rest, at about 2200, higher than normal rest but lower than food.

Of course, there could be major flaws in there, I should have been sleeping the past few hours....
