Short new still news.

First of all, Danger Detector threshold will or has already been changed, triggering from an indirect damage of 25 or more hp, instead of 10.

From FR Gazette:
New monsters from next map update will be perhaps catchable. The new zone will give more experience points according to zone levels. But since the new zone is still in pre-production they still don't know when it will come out.
There won't be any kind of global changes on the metagame, only skills could see some slight* changes. Though they could also add new items, same as Strong Tea and Temporal Stabilizer (which could be modified as well).
There won't be any kind of napo refund in any foreseen future.Slight means: effect won't change, only numbers can be changed.
