Double Skills

The only other possible couple I could get. Is Sprint and Overload but these require completing 5 level up's in 1 tree I dont need or want for Sprint and 10 level up's I dont want or need for Overload... So unless something horrible happens I doubt I will get anything other than shock.

Additional Points
This is a very nice build with LOTS of lightning and wood. It should be a very good fighter, even better if it can get the extra HP level's and Self Control (Very Good for Avoiding Petrification/Posion's and any other nasty surprises) And the extra HP should make it last longer, its two healing abilities should hopefully activate often enough in PVP to keep himself and his team alive ^>^

I think what is essential in this build is getting carear plan early by wasting 2 level up's. And most importantly getting very lucky. I brought this wanwan at level 8. It had Celerity>>Double Hit>>Carear Plan and Carapace/Savagery. 2 wood level up's and 5 in lightning. Now that is very lucky. I brought it for a steal at 65k gold. Although having all wood/lightning is very lucky indeed at level 8, perhaps if you were slightly unlucky getting 1 water and a couple fire you could still make an excellent build.

This build was designed to include sympathetic and charisma. So it could lead a team of 5. IF you don't need it to lead then you can take out Tenacity>>Charisma. This will give you 3 level up's back. If you don't waste level up's on wood too like me, you should find it quite easy to take all the wood skills you need.
