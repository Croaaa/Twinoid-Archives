1st place: 1 Etherwasp Egg + Epic Reward + 100 Treasury Notes
2nd - 5th: 1 Feross Egg + 60 Treasury Notes
6th - 10th: 1 Kabuki egg + 30 Treasury Notes
11th - 20th: 5 Tac Tickets + 10 Treasury Notes
21st - 50th: 50 Treasury Notes
51st - 100th: 25 Treasury Notes

And that's not all!

Each time you pass a new level in your personal rankings you'll unblock:

500 points: 5 Madam Irma's Potions
1000 points: 2 Authentic Warm Bread
1500 points: 2 Authentic Warm Bread + 3 Angel Potions
2000 points: 1 Pack of Nimbao island objects
3000 points: 2 Tac Tickets
4000 points: 200 Demon Tickets
5000 points: 100 Treasury Notes
6000 points: 1 Gold Napodino

So if anyone is interested I can give my statistics for the MH I did on FR server. I suppose except for the length of the MH everything else will be the same here on EN.

I raised an immaterial Santaz from level 37 to 50 in Grumhel Forest (at Sinister Path). Needed to buy a total of 803 Irmas to reach 6k points. If you subtract the gold won by fighting and by selling the found ingredients the total cost was 353k. This does not include the prizes you get (like the 100 TN).

It was a fair deal this time I would say.

On the average I got 19 items per 100 fights (with a single dino). The drop rate seemed to stay pretty much constant no matter what level my dino was.

DW might be a better place, but it would likely go with no or at least lower gold from gathering to repay part of the Irma costs.
