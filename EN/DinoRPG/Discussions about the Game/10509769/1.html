Honestly, the Kabuki quest shouldn't be on your radar right now... you should be concentrating on building up your existing team and adding more dinoz to it. In that vein, a Pteroz is probably not the best dinoz to spend your hard earned on... a cheaper dinoz could be working for you right now, earning you more money. Pteroz (like the other exotics) are cool, but probably not worth the premium cost compared to starter-type dinoz.

If you really have your mind set on getting one, I'd suggest you make sure your Softpig is well prepared. The wild Pteroz is a tough opponent for L9 guys. If you haven't already, read the Fighting Pteroz thread.
