I hope it helps...

To get the pearl first you have to combat against the Water Elemental in the university and kill him, you will win a status, with that status you will be able to cross swiming from the port to the Islands.

There you have to go to the "Mutants Falls" (is an island but I´m not soure about the correct translation here) there you have to talk with Hydragol the Master, he will ask you about something, then you have to go back to the port and talk with Padamonje, he will give you the Neufar Leaves (another status). You return to the Mutants Falls and give the Neaufar Leaves to Hydragol finally he will give you the pearl.

With the pearl all your Dinoz recover 5 at 00:00 time of the server, if they are in the fountain of youth.
