Husaurus
Primarily single element dinoz will complete a single branch/path way too fast to stick to a single path in their main element. So basically, this leads to little more than wasting 3 level ups on early unlocks... unless the dinoz are designated U-Skillers or Collectors.

Three Sirains would get two U-Skillers and a Collector... which aren't bad short term builds. But they won't complement each other in battle.

Three Gorilloz would get a U-Skiller, a Archaeologist without Planner, and 'Planner' without Archaeologist. The Archaeologist can easily get Planner late, but the 'Planner' would be mostly three wasted unlocks on a longer term build. But they won't complement each other in battle.

Three Lightning Dinoz would get two U-Skillers, and a Dinoz that can be eventually Reincarnated after the fun wears off. But they won't complement each other in battle.

Three Air Dinoz would get a Collector, a U-Skiller, and a one with three wasted unlocks on a longer term build. But they won't complement each other in battle.

Three Fire Dinoz would get Collector, and two be with three (or four) wasted unlocks on longer term builds. But they won't complement each other in battle.

If I had to do this, I'd go with Sirains... because two U-Skillers and a Collector is not a complete waste. Three Rockies would be my second choice because of good U-Skills and the Reincarnation option... but level 40 is a long way away.

If you want dinoz that don't max out their branch too soon, then you are stuck with Hippoclamps... which have no main element... but that would be a whole other kettle of fish... since they probably won't dominantly level in the same elements anyway.
