I do apologize for a possible double post - I remember reading someone else's question about this issue:

When considering getting a new dinoz, should I get one at a time as soon as I can get one or save to get two at once?
Here's my answer, for what it's worth:

I will get two the next time I get dinoz. I will then group them for at least their first batch of missions. I feel that the benefits of having two initial blows for over 10 far outweigh whatever gain there might be in raising them more "individually".

Rather than losing 5 or 7 from a single smashroom, now each dinoz gets and only loses .5 (they seem to trade hit receiving)...

This allows them to level up faster and require less resting/food, allowing me to save up more to repeat the process!
