[None]
You do not dig for ingredients.
You have to teach your Dinoz the COLLECTOR (C) skills.
Once they have such a skill, they are able to use them to collect ingredients - they may harvest plants or perhaps go hunting depending on the skill that you teach them.

Check the Clan pages for details of the ingredients, the required skill and the locations where each ingredient can be found.
