Loxagon

For a while I have been watching the same. In groups with mixed levels the higher level dinos get a bit more XP than when paired with dinos of same level, and the lower levels in a group get a bit less XP than they would if fighting with group members of the same level. Sometimes this modification does not make a single XP, sometimes it does, and sometimes the difference is even bigger.

For example a group of mine with levels 37/39/37 fought 1 Pink Gromster & 1 Lapouf
in Nimbao and got 45/59/45 base XP for it (meaning without int/prof modifier).
Next fight the first dino leveled up to 38.
The fight after that the group met Pink Gromster & Lapouf again, this time getting for level 38/39/37 a base XP of 45/59/44.

So the 3rd dino got 1 XP less based on the fact that the 1st dino now had a higher level.

Things like this have been noted ever since before the XP boost already. I'm convinced this is some kind of mechanism installed by MT to avoid high level dinos 'dragging' lower level dinos through high XP locations to power level them.

I know that this situation was a problem for some online games where a low level character could get the level ups he would normally need weeks for in a matter of days, just by joining a group of high level characters.
