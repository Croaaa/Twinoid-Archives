He needs to take the dino in question to the marketplace and put it up for sale. You then need to buy the appropriate amount of to buy the dino (either with real money or by exchanging a boatload of ingredients witht he Shady Man) then go to the marketplace and place a bid. You may get outbid by another player of course, but unless the dino in question is hihg level and/or very well bult this shouldn't be an issue.
