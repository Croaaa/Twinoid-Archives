The time gap goes up in half hour intervals up to 23 hours and then stops, from what I've read. The idea is that you get a turn every day and aren't limited by GMT+1 midnight.

I think the main reason for this turn-system was to give starting players more turns, to combat the slow start dino-rpg would have otherwise.
