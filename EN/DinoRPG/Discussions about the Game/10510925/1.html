A lower level E skill (or A skill) doesn't replace as far as we can tell a higher level E skill (Or A skill respectively).

It merely decreases the chance of using the base action. Attacks in the case of A skills and Battle Gear in the case of E skills.

So if you are very fond of using battle rations for example, Wrath will activate and replace instances where you would use a battle ration. It also can replace other level 1 E skills, which I believe is only Focus, which is better if your a lightning based dino I think. Those are the only reasons you would ever remove wrath.
