The champion returns! druid2k has fought hard to take away first place once again! Check out the epic fight here.


In other news... We're pleased to announce the new rules for this and the upcoming tournaments!

Take on the ultimate challenge until the 1st of September and qualify for the finals.

From now on the tournaments will be bimonthly, equal and synchronized across the English, French and Spanish servers! This way everyone is on the same level...

New Rules
  • During the qualification round: No equipped items!.
  • You need to have four Dinoz, each of a different race to make up your team.
  • During the finals you will have the chance to equip you Dinoz with consumable and magic items!
Heaps of goodies to get your hands on:

  • Champion: The Zen Medal will go to the Master of Masters + 3 Napodinos + 100 Treasury Notes + 100 Demon Tickets **
  • Runner-Up: 3 Napodinos + 80 Treasury Notes + 100 Demon Tickets
  • Semi final: 2 Napodinos + 80 Treasury Notes + 100 Demon Tickets
  • Quarter final: 1 Napodino + 80 Treasury Notes + 100 Demon Tickets
  • Last 16: 1 Napodino + 80 Treasury Notes + 50 Demon Tickets
  • Last 32: 1 Napodino + 80 Treasury Notes
  • Last 64: 1 Napodino + 50 Treasury Notes
  • Last 128: 1 Napodino + 30 Treasury Notes
  • Last 256: 1 Napodino + 10 Treasury Notes

Remember, the players with the Top 50 Dojos at the end of the qualification phase will win a Baby Tofufu.

Good Luck to you all, dinoz masters!

War is brewing...

The next Mana War will commence in the middle of September!

Train up your Dinos in preparation for this bloody battle!
