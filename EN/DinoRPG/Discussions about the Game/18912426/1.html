I have been enjoying this game for 3 and a half years. Before I used to play Dinoparc english server where I was one of the top 10 players so when I came to this game, I had a good idea where this game would lead to.

Early in the game I did the Kabuki quest and sold as quickly as I could those 3 Kabuki to earn coins.

Now I see a chance to have Kabukis in my account, demon ones without the 3 limit that the original ones had.

But now, I realize that my early Kabuki sales seem to be a great deal. Each one cost 50K and I sold them for 150K to 250K so that was a decent profit. Although many things/ideas from Dinoparc helped me to play this game I never thought that I would see such a disparity between a good deal and bad deal.

So I present to you the good deal and bad deal.

Good deal buy a Demon Kabuki for just 800 notes vs a Demon Wanawan that costs 900 Demon notes.

Bad deal purchase a Kabuki for 50K or even more on the market vs a regular Wanwan for 20K.

This oddity can be regulated by increasing the price of a demon Kabuki to possibly double 1600 demon notes. This change will make better sense in this game.
