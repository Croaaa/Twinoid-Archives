As one who is trying to get the Pearl - you need skills rather than levels.

One of the monsters you meet keeps going immaterial, Kazadine, so you are better if you have Perception. These are accompanied by smaller beasts that use flight. The flight is not too much of a problem, but you need attacks that kill them quickly or several at once (piranhoz, 5 ), that is one mass attack or your low strength attacks being enough to kill them.

They don't hit for much, 1-2, but they can hit often. Assume you lose 10 , or more, each fight. Thats why you need to rest.

Oh, and plan your moves through the sticky swamp!
