There's now a new feature next to the Share and Quote button, it's a red exclamation mark. If people remembered about the Alert button, it's basically the same function, with better explanation of what it does.

So if you use it, a pop-up windows will prompt up with two options Block and Cancel. By blocking you will remove the post from your own eyes, set an alert on a secret menu we got.

In case you find the post really over-offensive, as a breach of the Terms and Conditions, you can report illicit message. By doing so, you will chose in which category the offence is, then by continuing you will be able to write a full report which will be sent to Support.

Though, pay in mind not everything is offensive and so it isn't a tool for your own convenience but something to help moderators in their duties (alerters names are shown).
