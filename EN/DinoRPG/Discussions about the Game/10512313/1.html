Koshchei
It's normally CET (Central European Time) which is GMT +1. It changes to CEST (Central European Summer Time) which is GMT +2 in Summer (Northern hemisphere). People who compare their time to Britain might not notice the change because Britain also changes to BST (British Summer Time) which is GMT +1.

All of which is totally academic to the game - look at the server time at the top of your screen - now look at your clock. If you have daylight saving time in the northern hemisphere then the gap will stay the same give or take a week (providing you only change 1 hour - if you change more then go invade a country with proper timekeeping ). I'm sure everyone in Southern hemisphere is used to making all the adjustments to play on northern servers .
