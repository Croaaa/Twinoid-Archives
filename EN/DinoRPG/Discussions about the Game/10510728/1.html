For a 5 dino team, there are a lot of combos to go with.
Depending what the objectives of the team are, you could try to be heavy in particular elements/skills, or have an even spread.

2x Softpig or Moueffe
2x Gorilloz
2x Sirain
2x Glidwings
3x Pteroz

Then again, for a heavy dinoz, a Rocky may be preferred to a Glidwings.

I've had multi element dinoz like Cloudozes and Wanwans on a few teams, and so far they're pretty crappy (at least in the teen levels where my single element dinoz are still more badass). For some reason every Winks I get seems to do really well even next to single element dinoz....maybe it's just luck. My Moueffes and Gorilloz 3 dino team always rocks far more than any of my teams with multi-elements dinoz though.
