One thing I found about the rest action that is not obvious is this:

If you stop resting before your turn timer is up, you will still have to wait the balance of the turn timer. Also you cannot restart resting until you get your new action at the expiration of your turn timer.

eg: your turn timer is 12 hours. You click on the Rest button, and rested for 10 hours. Then you click on the Stop Resting button. You will now have to wait for another two hours before you can click on new action. You cannot move, fight or restart your rest during that time.
