- if your dinoz has both the required 3rd level skills (fe. sapper AND vaporous form

- then talking to madam X gives him a skill, named "Double Skill"

- after that when the dinoz levels up from any of the two necessary elements ( or ) , and choose to unlock

- then he will have the 4th level double skill (fe. "bubble" ) unlocked

- the next time he will levels from (again) any of the two elements, he can choose to get the double skill

so, briefly, in an example

...
level 30 sapper
level 31 vaporous form
-> talk to madame X, get free Double Skill
level 32 or unlock
level 33 or bubble
