I confirm this bug. The start button is indeed hidden underneath 5th listed dino and the current solution is to go to View > page style > no page style, then click start fight.

1. Go to dojo and select your team.
2. Pick an enemy to fight and a dinoz to fight it.
3. Go to View in your browser meny.
4. Select Page style and pick No Page style.

You will now get a freaking ugly page view without all the overlays. Scroll down until you can see the picture of the enemy dinoz you picked to fight. Under that is a blue sentence saying "Start the battle"
Click that and then go to the browser View menu again and set it for Norma page view again and the fight will start.

You have to repeat this process with all your fights until MT fix this bug.
