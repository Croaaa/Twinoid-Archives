No, the Dinoz will not have 5 extra abilities, but extra elements 5, ie if you reincarnate a Pigmou, that normally in the level 1 have 2 points in Fire, may have up to 7 points in Fire, with lucky.
These points will increase the attack and defense of the Dinoz but the Dinoz is not taught any skill.
It is also a good option if your Dino was poorly trained or has fallen, to raise their levels, in elements very unevenly and therefore its power is too low for your level.
Remember that the 5 extra elements are distributed randomly, so that every elements can fall into the same element, which falls once in each element... Among several possibilities.
