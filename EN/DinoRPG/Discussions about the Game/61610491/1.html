Loxagon

A319: While up to level 10 you need to level up to be able to move/fight normally, starting at level 10 levelling up is not mandatory. While levelling up is the 'normal' way to develop your dinos and your account there is one advantage leaving dinos at level 10. Instead of getting 1 turn per day (starting at level 20) they can instead move or fight twice a day. Also having a gathering skill they can gather twice a day. If done in the right area this can increase your daily income of fighting money and money from selling the ingredients. You can use this money to 'push' your other dinos (by buying them irmas/extra moves). Especially if you concentrate on one team at a time this will speed up their development considerably for the cost of leaving others at level 10/11.
