To clarify the debate about GREEN and YELLOW Cloudoz, I analyzed the actual colors used in the screenshot provided by Sanjar.

The YELLOW Cloudoz in the screenshot is using #FFE9B7 as its base color, which consists of 255 red, 233 green and 183 blue. This means that technically it's more of an orange color than yellow, since the amount of red is more than green.

The GREEN Cloudoz in the screenshot is using #EFEFB0 as its base color, which consists of 239 red, 239 green and 176 blue. This means that technically it's actually the more yellow color of the two, but in comparison it will seem more green than the orange Cloudoz.

So this screenshot does not prove that there are green Cloudoz, but it is enough to prove that there are different shades of yellow, one of which might seem greener than the other.
