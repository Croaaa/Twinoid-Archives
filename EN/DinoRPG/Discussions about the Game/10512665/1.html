3) Dinoz less than 25

It is their choice, I do not know what would be most fun for them.

We had some nice discussion about Leagues and problems of forming them.
Everyone is afraid to be stuck in a group or league where they don't have any chance of succeeding. So what if we were to make a pact? We make and agreement with top16+ clans that they will show up in League A to fight. Mitigating the damage of Raksha, Loot and other monsters.

Let's say we have 3 Leagues:
League A - everything counts,
League B - everything below 40 counts,
League C - everything below 30 counts.

We can easily find some 16+ clans that would like to participate in the 40+ group as long as they are not there to just fight the TOP5. And more importantly, if there were good rewards even for winning just their equals.

If we got the prizes per win reward system for qualification phase. Where we got 2 groups in League A with 8 clans in each with the following rewards:

1st win +10 Tnotes = 10 Tnotes total
2nd win +15 Tnotes = 25 Tnotes total
3rd win +20 Tnotes = 45 Tnotes total
4th win +1 Napo = 45 Tnotes + Napo total
5th win +20 Dtickets = 45 Tnotes + Napo + 20 Dtickets total
6th win +50 Tnotes = 95 Tnotes + Napo + 20 Dtickets total
7th win +30 Dtickets = 95 Tnotes + Napo + 50 Dtickets total

This would mean that you only need to win 4 other clans to secure a Napo. As there are 15 possible opponents and you get to fight 7 of them. You can surely tell me that you would not be up for the challenge? It means you can lose to 3 clans. Even if you are stuck in a group with Raksha, Loot and Cookies or MK or VA - you can still lose them if you want and win the others. And the other group would even had a blast as they would not even get to fight the big monsters before elimination phase - and even then maybe not. Sure, you will not take home the cake for winning in League A, but you will still get your rewards and you will still get to fight equal and winnable matches.

Why will this work?

Because you can set different rewards for other Leagues:
League B:
1st win +10 Tnotes = 10 Tnotes total
2nd win +15 Tnotes = 25 Tnotes total
3rd win +20 Tnotes = 45 Tnotes total
4th win +25 Tnotes = 70 Tnotes total
5th win +30 Tnotes = 100 Tnotes total
6th win +1 Napo = 100 Tnotes + Napo total
7th win +25 Dtickets = 100 Tnotes + Napo + 25 Dtickets totalLeague C:
1st win +10 Tnotes = 10 Tnotes total
2nd win +15 Tnotes = 25 Tnotes total
3rd win +20 Tnotes = 45 Tnotes total
4th win +25 Tnotes = 70 Tnotes total
5th win +30 Tnotes = 100 Tnotes total
6th win +35 Tnotes = 135 Tnotes total
7th win +25 Dtickets = 135 Tnotes + 25 Dtickets totalWhich would mean that you would have to win 6/7 matches in League B while keeping in mind that you cannot use your 40+ levels. Just to get the same Napo from qualification.

But that is not all, there are more rewards on my mind. For the elimination rounds:
League A:
WON Round 1 +Napo
WON Round 2 +50 Dtickets
WON Round 3 +NapoLeague B:
WON Round 1 +25 Tnotes
WON Round 2 +Napo
WON Round 3 +40 Dtickets
WON Round 4 +NapoLeague C:
WON Round 1 +20 Tnotes
WON Round 2 +Napo
WON Round 3 +30 Dtickets
WON Round 4 +NapoSo this means that you either take a Napo for doing very well in your group (except for the rookie league), or you take a Napo for winning someone other who also did OK in their group. Sure, you can get very bad pairing with group and then again with Round1, but with 2 Leagues the gap will be even more wast, so we must try our hardest to organize 3 Leagues where everyone are more or less equal and they get good rewards for at least winning their equals.

p.s. the prize amounts are up for tweaking but the important thing in my proposed system is that any TOP16 clan should be able to get a Napo if they fight in League A - so there should not be incentive for them to under-register. And this would not only be good for the League A which will finally have a decent amount of contestants, but also good for other Leagues who will be able to fight even closer matches.

And my final thing.. What if we got 24 clans in League A !? Just think of the awesomeness. There would be even better chances on getting those 4 wins.
