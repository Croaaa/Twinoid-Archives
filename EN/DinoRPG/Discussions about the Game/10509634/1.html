AndySQdancer
I don't think it's a bug. The from one turn from a mission will carry over during a level up if it is on the same turn. But, if you get to level up on one turn--don't take the level up on that turn--then complete a mission (that turn or the next) and then on the next turn take the level up, you will lose the for that mission on the level up. That is you lose from a mission or a level up between two or more turns, but do not lose it for a mission and level up on the same turn.
