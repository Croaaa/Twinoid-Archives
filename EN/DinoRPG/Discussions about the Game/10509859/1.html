I came across this game after it was mentioned on BBC's Click programme. My first impressions were good as I love RPGs and I like the idea of playing an online game away from the consoles, but there's something lacking IMHO.

If anyone played DataClash back when WAP was new, you'd know how addictive that was, fast turns quick but exciting battles with tactics. Or some PlayByMail games like it's a crime - slow turns - but you had a lot to plan and consider. But this is neither - long turns for fast battles; the coins I purchased quickly went 900 for extra turns goes quickly). I don't want to play it all day - but I want more than a minute or two!

I think the game needs a rethink... get me to spend my coins on new monsters and equipment - not to play - cuz it's just not worth it.
