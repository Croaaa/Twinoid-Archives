As I have seen there has been a lot of discussion on the topic of the time in the official thread.

As they have mentioned and they advice you well the slow road is to get the missions that get you gold rewards, first. And save for more dinoz.

The fast way, will be to buy 100,000 or 350,000 in gold from the get coins option.
Once you've done that, you can buy several dinos, and think how will you train them and group them.
Make groups of 3 at first and make them do the same missions, and get (more) gold and other rewards; A group gives more money that a single dino.

When you get to the limit of 18 dinos, or near that limit, then you can use the gold you get from your dinos to get potions, and get them to level up faster, for example.

I cite Cache:
i get a full heal daily and i don't spend any coins on healing items.
A higher level player can get items and abbilities for a dino to get its health back quickly, like 3 per hour, and 5 or 10 extra a day.
