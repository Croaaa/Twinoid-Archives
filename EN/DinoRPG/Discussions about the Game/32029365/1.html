Nights are getting colder, darkness is closing in, and evil is afoot. Creatures from the dark world have invaded parts of Dinoland once again. We've managed to contain the monsters so that they won't trouble our younger masters, but be aware, the further travelled of you will encounter the most evil of monsters, but you will also be handsomely rewarded!

Although this hunt will only last until the 4th of November at 15h, but there is a boatload of XP to be earned just in time for some last-minute levelling up if you're participating in the Mana War!

Prizes are announced below, and placing in the Top 3 internationally ranked players will earn further fame and fortune!

Oh! We should also mention that there may or may not be hidden prizes over and above those stated below... and there may be a hint in this post!

Stay tuned for updates! Good luck!



The monsters have been restricted from areas frequented by new masters. Hunting will only be possible in the Dark World, Grumhel Forest, the Steppes, Nimbao and Caushemesh!


The Top Hunters will earn:

1st place: 1 Etherwasp Egg + Epic Reward + 100 Treasury Notes
2nd - 5th: 1 Feross Egg + 60 Treasury Notes
6th - 10th: 1 Kabuki Egg + 30 Treasury Notes
11th - 20th: 5 Tac Tickets + 10 Treasury Notes
21st - 50th: 50 Treasury Notes
51st - 100th: 25 Treasury Notes


And that's not all!
Every time you break into a new level in the personal rankings, you will receive:

500 points: 5 Irma's Potions
1000 points: 2 Hot Breads
1500 points: 2 Hot Breads + 3 Angel Potions
2000 points: 1 Pack of Nimbao objects
3000 points: 2 Tac Tickets
4000 points: 200 Demon Tickets
5000 points: 100 Treasury Notes
6000 points: 1 Gold Napodino
