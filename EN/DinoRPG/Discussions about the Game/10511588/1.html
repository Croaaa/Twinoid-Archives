Piemaster
(Note, this is not a suggestion, just a general musing about what is wrong with the clan wars at the moment. Maybe someone more creative than me can actually come up with something that will fix this problem)

The fundamental problem with the clan war as I see it is that there isn't much incentive to actually fight! Let's take my clan for example, we're a borderline top 20 clan what a lot of you might call 'average'. If we fight anybody in the top 10 we will lose, pure and simple so we don't want to attack them (obviously) and if they attack us, the best thing we can do is remove as many defenders as possible before they get slaughtered. Obviously this suits the big clan as well as they use less Irmas getting their win, so it's quite a happy equilibrium that no actual fighting takes place.

Fighting smaller clans (lets say rank >30) is fine and dandy as it's easy points, but many of these clans don't defend their castles anyway and those that do it's usually a token defence force that they would just be better off just removing before they get slaughtered. Again, no actual fighting is good for all sides.

So that just leaves maybe 15 or so clans that are actually a fair fight for us. If they attack us it is in our interest to defend hard because we might hold them off and if we attack them we would expect the same. But still, actual fighting is not often in anybody's interest. If we attack a clan of equal power to our own, the most likely outcome is a lot of carnage and a ton of Irmas, Angel potions, Battle rations and such being used. If we can't break through, both sides have spent a whole bunch of gold for no outcome. If we do break through and win then great, but all that will leave us with is a victory that came at a high price and could easily be taken back later in the war. Of course, we could try to win against the clan when their defences are down, or they us, but that's rather cheesy and once again has given us a best-case scenario of no actual fighting taking place.

So really, although it's called a clan war, most clans of our level (and probably other levels too) are in a situation where actual fighting is not really advantageous. The easiest way to get ahead in the rankings is to get as many easy wins as possible, plus some cheeky 'tag along' wins against equal and better opponents when their defences are down while trying to minimise the number of clans who can do the same to you. That's not to say that all clans do this, many clearly enjoy a fair fight even if they do spend a ton of gold for not a lot of result. But the way clan wars are designed, it seems to just reward behaviour that most people consider cheesy, unsportsmanlike or boring, so it's hardly surprising that a lot of such behaviour takes place.
