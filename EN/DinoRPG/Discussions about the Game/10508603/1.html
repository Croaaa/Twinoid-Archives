Rose Bush is probably better done early in the dinoz's life.

If you do Fresh Fish, Rose Bush, Recipe Book, and Lost Dog, all of them just carrying things, first, you'll face smaller enemies and be stronger for the next ones.

Hill's Smashrooms, Wolf Hunting, Stamps, and Strange Monster are all about killing specific enemies. If you are at a higher level, you are more likely to get multiple enemies or bigger enemies, so you can finish them faster with less injury.
