There are the baobob missions and soft shaman missions (volcano) that a dinoz your level might be able to do. The baobob missions get much more difficult when your dinoz reaches level 12 (eeloz start appearing), so do those now or much later.

Don't worry about killing the hippo for now, it's not like it's worth buying one at this stage for you, anyway. Kill him when you can easily do so and happen to be at that place.
