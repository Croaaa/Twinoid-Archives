You have to gather a certain amount of basic ingredients (4 furs/4 lightning energies/6 flints and I'm not sure about groupers and leaves right now, because no dinoz at the market) and go to the market place. There you will find a guy who will offer you treasury notes. Talk trough the conversation and in the end you will be able to buy them.

Another option is pvp events. You can get TNs from the dojo, but nowadays it's a little less, cause it's all about the napo. If you want serious amount, you have to fight in wars/cotc.

Last option I can think of this fast is quests. The bambooz quest is very hard and only the best players around have finished it, but it gives a lot of TNs, then the Sage's missions give out a lot of TNs. You can check here Guide, there is a lot of imformation and you may find more ways of getting TNs there
