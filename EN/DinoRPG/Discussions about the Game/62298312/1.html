For Quests status, just check your account. There is a button "My Quests" in the pane right above your epics.

Click on it, and every quests progress bar is shown.

If you are looking for individual dinoz' missions, use your Illustrated Mission Guidebook. Left of your screen scroll down to bottom of your list of dinoz and click "My Dinoz' Missions'.

All you need to know will be in these 2 locations.
