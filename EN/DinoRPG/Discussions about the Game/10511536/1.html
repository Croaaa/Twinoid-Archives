w3ko
No, it doesn't hurt, you actually save your time by skipping those long, boring battles. But if you want to skip them and want to know the results first, do this trick (only firefox, dunno if it works with other browsers):

Go to View> Page Style> No Style
Scroll to the bottom and the fight's results should be there (works with lvl ups, too). Also, if you want to re-watch the battle because it was so cool or your dinoz did so well, you can by going to View> Page Style> Basic Page Style (you have to be on the No Style page first), flash battle window should be loading again.

Anyway, I skip fights when I can't spend that much time on the computer and only watch them for training purposes or if bosses are involved.
