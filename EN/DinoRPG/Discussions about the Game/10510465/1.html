It depends on the mission and on your own opinion I would say.

For example, if you do The Stamps in a group of 4 Dinoz and you get 4 Smashrooms each time then it will take 16 turns of fighting and say 1 of resting.
If you do the same mission with each Dinoz alone and you get 2 Smashrooms each time it will take only 8 turns of fighting and maybe one of resting.
Is time important? How do the XP or Gold vary between those two scenarios? You decide which you prefer...


If you want to do the "deliver a letter" type quests though, a group will mean lower health loss for all to complete the mission.

Maybe you choose to travel to place as a group and then split up and fight separately...

Hippoclamps actually start out quite strong. This makes intuitive sense, as having a point in each element means that each attack (for a L1) is for a base of 5 damage, and its defenses start out better than any other dinoz. The balanced nature starts taking its toll quickly, though, and they become inefficient as fighters by the time you get to intermediate levels. Flippity's analysis showed that you can turn them into effective high-level fighters, but most players probably won't want to go to the trouble.
