You can accept one mission with every single dinoz. So if your lvl 9 dinoz already finished the first Grandpa Joe mission he cannot do it again. He could join your weaker dinoz anyway when the smaller one is doing the mission but would not really benefit from this. It is not recommended to group dinoz with such a huge level difference.
