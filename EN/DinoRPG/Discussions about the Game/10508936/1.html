Let's take Water Cannon since you picked it...your assault in Water was zero, which means you are going to do horrible damage with Water Cannnon since the damage is BASED on your water assault.

So yes, if you decide to take a skill where you have ZERO assault...yup you will do virtually no damage. It acts as a multiplier of your element, so again, lets take Water Cannon which attacks with a Water power of 6, with a 5 you will do (before defense of the enemy) 30 damage.

So yes, 10 damage sure does suck at high levels, but you are not going to be doing paltry damage with such skills as I listed unless 1) you have next to nothing in that element and 2) the mob you're attacking has high defense against said element.
