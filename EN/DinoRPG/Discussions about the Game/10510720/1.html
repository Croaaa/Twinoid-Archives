From what I heard the formula went like this:

attack - defence = damage

This formula is clearly incorrect or at least incomplete.
Attack and Defence are fixed values calculated from the Element values and modified by skills.

If there was a constant involved then the damage would also be constant and this certainly does not appear to be the case.
The different damage values (e.g. observed 92, 101, 97, 100) suggest that there is a random factor within the formula.
