I've experimented around with Speed and Initiative a bit, and here are my findings:

Initiative:
You need a certain amount of initiative to perform an attack, and the dinoz with higher initiative attacks first. I don't know the exact amount, but here is an example:

A battle between Dinoz A and Dinoz B. Dinoz A has 55 initiative and Dinoz B has 50 initiative. Let's say you need 10 initiative to make an attack. Dinoz A attacks first, and used 10 initiative. Now Dinoz A has 45 Initiative and Dinoz B has 50 initiative, so Dinoz B gets to attack. After that B drops to 40 initiative. However, Dinoz also recover initiative during battle, and that is where speed comes in.

Speed:
Speed is the rate at which dinoz recover initiative. The higher the speed, the more initiative they recover during each round of battle. Let's say Dinoz A has 115%(+15% from celerity) speed and Dinoz B has 80%(-20% from Giant) speed. So after a round of battle, Dinoz A recovers 11.5 initiative, and Dinoz B only recovers 8 initiative. After the round of battle, Dinoz A now has 45+11.5=56.5 initiative, and Dinoz B has 40+8=48 initiative. After two rounds of battle, Dinoz A would have about 10 more initiative than B, so Dinoz A would get to attack twice. Let's say Dinoz A has 62 initiative and Dinoz B has 50 initiative. Dinoz A would attack B once using 10 initiative, but Dinoz A still got 52, which is greater than Dinoz B's 50 initiative, so A attack again using another 10 initiative. Now Dinoz A has 40 and B has 50, so Dinoz B now get to attack after taking two hits.


This is just my hypothesis of how initiative and speed work based on my experiments. And the numbers are all made up.

Oh boy, have i got a treasure trove of information for you all!

Translated French Server Avis Praeda clan page

This one clan page has responses from the developer about both the questions i have posted about today.

The bit that's relevant to this thread:

Each fighter has a value of time. The fighter with the smallest value performs an attack, then we add 10 / Speed at the time. So a fighter with 2.0 speed in attack twice more than 1.0 as fighting with each action will cost him 5 points of time instead of 10.

The Initiative will reduce the value of time at the beginning of combat. It therefore allows both to perform the first action, but also saves time over other fighters: a 10 DINOZE initiative with fighting a DINOZE with -10 will be almost certain to carry out two attacks before the other (since 20 points separate the time).

--- ---
L'équipe DinoRPG The team DinoRPG

Initiative represents an initial quantity that determines who goes first. Speed is the amount added to the quantity after each attack. The combatant with lowest total value goes next at each step of combat.
