It depedends on what you want to do with the dojo.

If you are just interested to test your dinoz against other dinoz from your clanmembers there isn't a minimum level. Looking at your account, your should be spend on more dinoz first.

If you want to qualify for the tournament, the best strategy is to create missmatches so that you have at least 3 certain wins (for example: level 3 vs. level 1), while doing only the certain win fights. Again, I recommend, that you get more dinoz first.

If you want to get far during the tournament, you'll properbly need at least Level 45 Dinoz, though it very much depends on the drawings. Before you start pushing your current dinoz, you should get more Dinoz first.

To sum it up: Get more Dinoz first!
