Most Loyal Dinoz Masters!

This was the first time in the history of DinoRPG when diversity was the rule and the whole server was allowed to participate. The IDT players united in one goal, to present to the public a team of different races of dinoz, but brought together for one purpose: to reach above everyone else and to grab the trophy of trophies! We are proud to officially announce the winner of Inter-Dojo Tournament XXVI: Meischner! He has done it on the german server and now, he has done it right here as well! Combining the forces of an almighty striped Softpig, Drill, a kabuki of great power, Hagakure and a rare winks, gifted with a seemingly unpenetrable shell, comparable only to those of heavy battle tanks, Pearl, he stood up to the challenge! After destroying the teams of HyperFREAK, Muccosauro and Raynor, he guided his army against that of B1oniC, who by grounding Rjun74, DarkShogun and MohnKahn promised to be an almighty foe.

A furious battle erupted, Life Sources draining vitality from both sides, hits raining from the immense beast of a Softpig, comrades falling on both sides, but in the end, the adamantium-solid armour of the silver Pearl guaranteed that there can be only one, and one alone, standing. The fury of battle subsided, the dust settled and one master came through victorious! Thus we are proudly announcing the winner of the XXVI. International Dojo Tournament: Meischner! Congratulations!
