As far as I see, the only difference is that when you pay from a certain country you can also send a sms or make a phone call while there is no such option for international payments. You have to use either a credit card or Paypal.

I can only recommend what I'm using - Paypal + a debit card I got specifically for online payments. Issuance and maintenance of debit cards are free of charge (or at least mine is and I know several other BG banks that do not charge either, I don't know about other countries) and you can control the money there, you get charged only if you use an ATM (which I don't, at least not with this card

Payment by sms is possible if MT has agreements with the respective mobile operators of the country. This is why it is more expensive than the other types of payment (but on the other hand, it's easier

If you're underage and can't get a bank card, find a friend who has. I have a friend who helped me before I got my card so if you really want, you can always find a way
