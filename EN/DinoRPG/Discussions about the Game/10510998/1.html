Its easier for your sanity if you make sure all your dino's have the same XP. And if you wonder'd why your winks got less XP when fighting alone against 2 smashrooms it is simple.
The XP dino's get is based upon monsters fought. If you were getting 4xp per smashroom. Based on a solo fight vs 1 smashroom. (Thats the easy way to figure out your XP per monster) If you faced these solo you'd get +4xp for each smashroom, so 3 smashrooms give you 12xp.
However when In a group, their is an additional calculation, you still get the XP per monster so if your group of 2 or 3 dinoz was fighting against 4 smashroom's, they would get 4x4xp each = 16xp BUT, their is a penalty for using a group, which is you get only 75% of the XP you should get (thats for a group of 2-3, 4 dino group = 60% ish, and 5 dino group = 50%) . So 16xp becomes 12xp. Then all the single dinoz XP is tallied and shown as XP at the end of the fight. So if you had 2 in the group for that fight it would show 24xp.
As for different level dino's, as the other fellow said, dinoz levels and monster levels play a part in who gets the most XP, e.g if it's a level 20 and a level 2, and they fight a chicken, the chicken is a level 20 enemy, so he will get most of the XP. If they fought a smashroom it goes the other way around. Some say using a low level dino is a good way to encourage glouns to fight a group with higher level dino's in it who haven't yet done the mission.
