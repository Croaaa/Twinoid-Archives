Agree with Bace here. Newbies on this forum often get bombarded with information about getting a gatherer, getting this or that U-skill, getting specific species of dinoz, doing (or not doing) certain missions in a certain order, etc etc.

But to be honest there are only two rules of making good progress as a newbie:

1. Buy as many dinoz as you can as quickly as possible.
2. Make sure you thoroughly understand the importance of rule #1

Anything else you can argue this way and that, but essentially its gravy. It all pales in comparison to the above.

hkmaly
With your dinoz, I would recommend Sirian as a next dinoz. But the important part is to not spend any on anything but more dinoz until you have 18 of them (repetition is the mother of learning).

Grouping is good but it would probably delay you to much to wait for buying two dinoz at once now so either leave next two or three dinoz separate or make a group with uneven levels.

About skill trees: you will probably level up your first dinoz incorrectly anyway but you should start to learn. With main elements it's good to wait long with opening, with non-main elements on the other hand you probably skip some subtree entirely to reach one or two usefull advanced skills ...
