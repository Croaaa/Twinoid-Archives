Even tho Andy described it on a mysterious way, I'll give you the full version.

To get to the Deep Gorges you will need the Lantern. You can get that by going to the Grumhell Forest and moving up to the Haunted Hills (check FAQ for the maps if needed). At the Haunted Hills, there's a guy where you can talk to. After defeaded the big mean Korgon *cough*, you will recieve the Lantern.

Remember you will have to walk all the way back, because you don't have the Fins to cross the Jumin River.

How to get to Grumhell Forest?
You will need:
- the Buoy (Prof. Eugene)
- Scorbscuba Caller (Atlantean Guard + Joveboze)
- Zor's Gloves (Dig up 3 items and talk to Archsage at Underwater Dome)
- Waterlilly Pads (Master Hydragol)
