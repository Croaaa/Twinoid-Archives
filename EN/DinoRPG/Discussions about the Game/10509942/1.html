I would say that going to the islands to get the pearl is the best idea, but you have to be aware of some things..

A goriloz is strong aganist water, but in the islands you will mostly encounter immaterial enemies and groups of piranhoz, witch can be hard if you dont have perception and an area attack(or many small hits).
might take a while, but if you feel ready, go to the university and fight the water elemental to get the buoy

If you want (and i recomend), you can do the first 2 missions of madam x. veeeery easy ones.
in the second one you get a battle ration that may be usefull aganist the wild hippoclamp, for example.
Many will say that its not worth it, but in those two you fight addicional mission battles that dont cost turns, so esencialy you get more and
(for example, in the merchants quest, i got 1500 and 650 and 5 from each merchant ^^)
