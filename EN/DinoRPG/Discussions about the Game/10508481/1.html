Speed:
When a dino has more speed than the another dino or monster, that dino attack first .
When a dino has less speed than the another dino or monster, that dino attack second .


Initiative:
When a dino has more initiative than the another dino or monster, that dino attack more times than the another dino or monster.
When a dino has less initiative than the another dino or monster, that dino attack more times than the another dino or monster.
