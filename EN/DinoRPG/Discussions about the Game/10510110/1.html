Esacially when you got several dinoz I would recommend resting instead of buying food.
When your dinoz needs to recover you make a normal turn and then use a Madam Irms's potion. With the new turn you start resting. Until the next turn your dinoz restores at least 23. Instead of cancelling resting you let it go on, maybe even wait with the other dinoz before starting a new turn. Between this turn and the following you have again 23+ hours. Your dinoz will rest shortly before you can make a new turn and then makes his turn in good condition.
By using a Madam Irma's potion which costs 900 you lost a single turn but you recovered over 46.
A Meat Pie recovers 30 but costs 2000 which is more than twice as much.
Authentic Warm Bread would recover 100 but costs 6000 which is a huge load of Gold.

I don't buy any food. When there is going to be a tough fight I use Battle ration that is a reward in one of Madam X's missions.
