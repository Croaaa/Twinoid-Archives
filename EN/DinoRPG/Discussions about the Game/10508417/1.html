It doesn't go by assault, but rather by the actual element proficiency. You are saying that you have 15 wood asault and 10 lightning, which means you probably have 2 in lightning and 2 in wood, but you picked that tallent that increases wood assault by 5. The game, however, considers your wood and air strengths to be equal (2 points in each), so you will randomly start with either wood or lightning for now, even though your wood packs more damage due to tallent.
