The 6th Mana war is over!

They say history repeats itself, but this time German Connection has learnt from the past and moved up the rankings by one place to claim victory in the 6th Mana War! Congratulations!
Equal praise should be given to The Last Order of Transylvania, who've climbed from 4th place to second! A valiant effort!
And last but not least, in 3rd place are The Cookies strongly represented by their outspoken leader Loppermann!

We're also looking to the clans who've known the taste of victory before but just missed out this time... Congratulations to you all and good luck for the next war, maybe you can even recapture the glory lost...

You can check out the full ranking for the war here.


Now here are the prizes:

  • Epic Reward of the Victor!, a glowing doll...
  • **Epic reward of the almost victor. A flaming doll...
  • Epic reward of participation, a dripping doll...

Congratulations to everyone who took part in the War, we hope that you all enjoyed yourselves and that the fighting was fierce.

I'd like to take a moment to acknowledge the technical difficulties that lead to certain teams having a hard time. We're not entirely sure how we're going to proceed in addressing the problem at the moment, but rest assured justice will be done (eventually). For any of you that aren't happy with the results, please voice you concerns here. Don't forget that much discussion was also had here: http://en.dinorpg.com/tid/forum#!view...
