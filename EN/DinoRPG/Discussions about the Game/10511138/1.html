Leafy Dawn and Precious Spring work in pretty much the same way... Only 3 Differences.

Leafy Dawn (A) heals all your dinoz in your group up to (wood element*2 + lightning element*2).

Precious Spring (E) heals all other dinoz in your group up to (wood element)

Well if your not very good at "spot the difference" it is simple..

1.
Leafy Dawn skill is much more powerful, giving a possible "2 times wood and 2 times lightning" elements, to the Precoious Springs mere "up to wood element"

2.
Sticking with the current theme of being more powerful, Leafy Dawn also heals the caster (the dinoz that used the skill) Precoious Spring on the other hand does not, It simply heals the rest of the group.

3.
Leafy Dawn's only draw back however is that it is an A skill where as Precoious Spring is an E skill. This mean's Leafy Dawn can use up vital attacks where you could have killed your enemies. It also probably has less chance of activating, though that is merely a guess.

The great thing is you can take both of them and use both of them on the same turn even, so they are both great skills to take, Wood/Lightning Dinoz for the win... Wanwan's king of the wood and light
