As for an on-topic comment,

It's a matter of opinion and well what your plans are for your dinoz. Whether or not "bad" levelup's are infact a good thing or a bad thing. Suffice to say, all levelup's can be good if planned correctly. But if you only plan on getting 1-3 air in 50 level up's you obviously won't choose a path that leads to good level 3 skills or good double skills, but a path with good level 1/2 skills, such as agility/dodge as apposed to strategy/anazlyse/achilies heel which leads to vendetta, or mistral/tornado/vaprous form which leads to
bubble. You can't always go for double skills in all secondary elements, if you practically have no chance of getting enough elements it is better to take the useful lower skills.

However if you get "bad" levelups, those in elements not in your main element, and you get more than you expect, if you took the gamble on a path leading to useful double skills as apposed to the safer paths with useful level 1/2 skills. A really good dinoz can be made.

If however you don't take the gamble, you will just regret every single level
