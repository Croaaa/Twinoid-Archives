Defenders are chosen by the players. When you have dinoz at the site of your clan's castle, you can set them to defend.

Up to 15 dinoz can be inside a castle at any one time. Only 5 dinoz at a time, however, will actually take part in fights against attacking dinoz.

While players can chose which dinoz are inside the castle, the clan leaders are the ones that choose which dinoz defend first. ((not sure about this))

Dinoz are not grouped while inside a castle, nor do they receive turns in the same way.
