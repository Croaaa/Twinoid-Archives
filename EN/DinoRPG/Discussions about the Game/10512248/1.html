well, Im not a big expert either,cause I have played just about a month but Ive learned so i can somehow answer to you.

1. There arent really classes, but its a term used for special dinoz. Every dinoz does quests to level up a little faster to get skills. and yes, gatherer dinoz usually stay at specific place and gather stuff until the specific place is depleted. Fighter dinoz are meant to be... well fighters. They are meant to do harder quests and fights for special stuff. they are also the guys who you use at clan wars, dojo and other events. and U-skillers are dinoz who are meant to get special U-skills. You can see them at some clan pages.

2. Well, its usually to get space for other dinoz, because maximum dinoz that you can have active is 18 (except if one of your dinoz has U-skill leader when its 21). Usually U-skillers are freezed because U-skill trees arent very good to use to being fighter, and you still have the U-skill in use even though they are frozen.

and 3. Fighter. You need money and fighter is the fastest way (Other than buy it yourself) to get it. you should have atleast a few of them before you start getting other classes though.

Hope this helps you.
