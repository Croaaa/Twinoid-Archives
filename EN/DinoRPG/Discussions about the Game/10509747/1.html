Good advice, z. Remember that there is no compulsion to fight the elementals. They will wait there for you as long as you want to take... and there's no need to fight them unless you want to go to the area unlocked by the statuses they grant. Usually, after finishing Joe's missions (1-9), you'll want to hit the islands before venturing up the volcano anyway.

If you're getting two-shotted, a rule of thumb might be to wait for two level-ups before trying again. One good skill (one level-up) and a boost from healing items might be enough in some cases.
