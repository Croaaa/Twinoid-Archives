once you click the 'new action' button on any dino the wait time is started, so every dino has the same timer. Other dinos has no more 'new action' button.

Following Dino does the same as the leader, so forfeits the movement. They can be left behind though, for example learning/fighting the elemental at the univ which only for 1 dino at a time. or going to the location that required special item for access and only some dino in the group has it.
