Right. If you started with a cheap ( 16000 ) Dinoz, didn't spend any money on anything, worked on the missions, and fought the elementals near the end of a week (they don't take up turns, but could cause heavy damage), you might then have 16000, or close to it, in that week. Maybe 8 days.

Since you have a Winks, you'll need a little extra time. One of the high level missions from Grandpa Joe, or the areas unlocked by beating the elementals, might get you the rest of the money you need.

Getting the third Dinoz might take longer, as the turns are farther apart at that time, and even those who started with cheap dinoz used up the extra they had. For the fourth, it will start to get faster.

With turns slowed down to just one each day, the Masters with 20 Dinoz can get enough for a new one in about 3 days even without mission rewards. 21 is the max, though, so they'll find other things to spend on.
