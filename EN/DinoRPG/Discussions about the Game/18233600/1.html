[admin]Here is the "exhaustive list of what can me modified for the Manawar (without excess coding)[/admin]

defenderLevels="1000"
Max: 1.000 total points for defending a castle.

defenderMax="5"
Max: 5 Dinoz defending a castle at once.

defenderTotal="15"
Max: 15 Dinoz in the queue to defend a castle.

defenderAutoLevels="1.3"
Max defence = 1.3x the attacking power

moreWarsPossibleWithReput="1"
If active, a clan can be defeated several times, depending on their reputation (100 = 1 time, 200 = 2 times, etc.)

attackTime="80"
castle attack duration (80 by default)

enableBuildings="1"
If active, building castles is possible

enableHeroicActions="1"
If active, defenders' morale increases and allows them to use heroic actions

cumulativeReputLoss="1"
If active, reputaion points are lost incrementally when the castle is destroyed

cantAttackIfCastleDestroyed="1"
If active, a clan cannot attack when their castle is destroyed

minimumDamagesToWinWar="30"
If active, when a castle is destroyed, all clans having inflicted at least 30 points of damage, win the war

enableMana="1"
If active, this allows castles to become enchanted and activates spells

clanCategories="x:y:z"defines limits for clan levels participating

categoryBattleTimeCoef="x:y:z:w" (requires: clanCategories)
Allows different war durations for different levels.
For example, a coefficient of 1.0 means the attack will last the usual amount of time as stated in attackTime.
A coefficient of 2.0 means the war will last twice as long

categoryAttackLimit="x:y:z:w" (requires: clanCategories)
Allows limits to be enforced for the power of groups of attacking dinoz in the different levels.
For example, if x = 50, a player can only attack a castle on the first level with a group of dinoz if the total of their levels is over 50.
