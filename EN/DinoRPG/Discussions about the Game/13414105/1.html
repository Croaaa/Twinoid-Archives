CornDuck

While it seems excessively hard to reach Skully alive, you only really need to do this with a few dinos until you get her souvenir.

Then those same dinos can finish her missions, and only ONE dino needs to do her last one for the pebble.

After you have the souvenir on a dino, you can use that dino with full health to guide dinos with <=10hp into the cemetery. Yes sometimes they still get killed, but they go with the still living guide and end up in the cemetery.
This is much less frustrating than having to use an Angel potion at Frish Port only to die again your next attempt.
