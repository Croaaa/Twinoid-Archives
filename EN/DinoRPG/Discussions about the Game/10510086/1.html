You can only have 18 active dinoz normally, if you own one dinoz with Leader skill you can have 21. There are already two ways to deactivae dinoz:
1) Freezing:
The Dino needs to get the lantern and go to deep gorges, there he can be frozen. U skills of frozen dinoz are still active, but they can't do anything else. As far as i know you can always unfreeze dinoz, as long as you are not at the limit of active dinoz (so, you have to freeze or sacrifice (see 2) a dino to make space for another dino to be unfrozen). After unfreezing the dino you can only use him after 24h, to prevent any abuse of this function i guess.
2) Sacrifice:
A sacrificed dino will give you demon notes. He will then appear in the demon shop for the same amount (but i dont know if he stays there for unlimited time), you can buy him back (giving a 24h couldown), or buy demon dinoz when you have enough demon notes.

I dont think there is a limit to how many frozen dinoz you can have.

MT won't add anything else to deactivate Dinoz i guess, because there are already these two options.
