Click on the My Account button on the Right side menu. Under your name (Master yournamehere), you will see a little button called Modify My Information. Reset Your Account is the next to the last option in the menu that appears.

Save your money to increase your stable of dinoz. That's more important than spending it on items early in the game. A related point: Be miserly with the Inventory you get to start the game. You don't want to spend money to replace them, so it's good to make them last as long as you can. Save the healing stuff to buff your guys up before a big fight. Try to keep your dinoz from getting too low in by resting when they are weak... that will keep them from dying so you won't have to use Angel Potions. And try to save the Irmas for when your wait time between turns is very long, to maximize the time return on them.
