The 5th Mana War has officially ended.

The undisputed lords of destruction are Magyar Királyság! Congratulations to them and commiserations to their enemies. The competition for the first runner up was fierce, but it was German Connection who snuck ahead and claimed their spot on the podium! Luna park weren't far behind and deserve a place on the honneur role for coming in third!

We also say felicitari to The Last Order of Transylvania as a special mention and very well done to Rākshasa for being right there behind them.

You can checkout the complete rankings from the war here.


And here are the prizes:

  • Prize for the winner, a mini statue of an all powerful deity.
  • Team prize, a mini statue of an imposing divine being.
  • Team prize for participation (first 30 clans), mini statue of a terrifying serpent.

Congratulations to everyone who took part! We hope that the new rules and changes shook things up a little.


Don't hesitate to let your moderators know of any problems with prizes and I'll do my best to get them fixed up.
