people always tend to forget how the speed is determined exactly

its not +5% ,its *5% .

In in certain cases, those 5% are from incredible worth

maxed out wanwan on lightning attack with pill :
100% ( base ) * 1.15 ( celerity ) * 1.15 ( acrobat ) * 1.15 ( brave ... dont laugh, iam going to give mine brave eventually, so take care whom you are going to attack )* 1.50 ( adrenalin ) * 1.40 ( lightning attack ) * 2.00 ( pill )
= 638,7657 % speed , compared to a non boosted attack .
adding those 5% from electrolysis makes 670,7 , so taken solely, nearly 40% extra. This CAN make a difference. Despite that ... you dont need to transcribe everything into numbers.
if your teams is as fast as the enemys, but you HAVE electrolysis additonaly, your enemy not, those 5% will lead to an additional attack eventuelly, and if this is the deciding one, you wont care anymore if 5% are much, or not.
