Use ours.

we have a seperate page for each tree as well as a seperate page for double skill's, and many other things, study them.

As for how things work.

When you unlock, you only unlock in the element the unlock is performed, so If you unlock on a lightning element on a level up, it won't do anything to say your water skills, marsh (not march btw, game typo) for example is a water skill, to get it you must look here
http://en.dinorpg.com/clan/41?page=1643
It is again called march, but as I said, it's a typo on the game.

Also when you unlock it unlocks all skills that can be unlocked in the element, so If you take both focus and celerity, you will unlock 4 skills not just 2. Those being /concentration/regeneration/double hit/lightning attack/

Unless you do not wish to take any further skills in a "branch" of an element tree, you should take the lowest skill in the branch before using an unlock. This is because if you later wish to obtain skills in that branch you will need to do another level up to unlock it, and as you said, in this game, that can be dire. Sometimes it can be beneficial to unlock early on just one branch this is so you can quicker gain access to a higher level powerful skill.

If you wished to gain double skills, then you almost have it correct,

1. You need both corresponding level 3 skills, i.e for Electrolysis it's Marsh and Lightning. This involves lot's of levels in two element's, not a very easy task.

2. You need to visit Madam X, talk to her, you don't need to do any missions, if you have both the needed Level 3 skills to unlock a double skill, simply talking to her will unlock it for you to take upon your next level up, or it will be in your list of skills to unlock when you next unlock, either one.

Another question, looking at the charts on this page
http://en.dinorpg.com/clan/9?page=665Those are just suggestions, based on the probabilitys to get each element on those dinos. Every dino "could" get every skill, if he got enough level ups in that element.
It's important to make plans like that for your dinos so you dont waste lvl ups when unlocking skills on the one hand and that you dont take to many low lvl skills and never reach the more powerfull skills on the other hand.
