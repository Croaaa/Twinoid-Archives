Support Q&A:
Why is there a limit to payment?

As a result of many cases of fraud with bank card payments, we have established limits to users, in order to limit improper use.
In addition, the limits to a bank card also apply if the same card is used by several users or on several of our sites.

The amount and the number of transactions are limited. If you think you may want to make several payments, then the best solution is to buy less frequently but to consider making bigger payments (between 15 and 50 €).
You can make:

1 payment every 10 minutes
2 payments every 24 hours
4 payments every 7 days
8 payments every 30 days

You can make a payment of:

30 € in the last 24 hours
80 € in the last 7 days
200 € in the last 30 days
