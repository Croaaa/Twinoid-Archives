Kayin

Ahem... errr... well, free rotten tomatoes for you! I'm not sure I'm the right target .

There's some changes within Motion-Twin since epoq left, and more precisely for DinoRPG EN, and so I'm here to announce them to you.
First of all, buzzard, our new EN administrator isn't in charge of communication (outside newspage). Since I'm already working with gyhyom (one of the developer managing DinoRPG), as a FR Gazette editor, I got appointed as your Comm' Officer for the meantime.

So what does that mean? In case we got the same problems like last Mana War rewards given off wrong, I will report them directly to dev. Only server-wide problems are being understood there.

The bug around Air Dinoz has been fixed by kiroukou (the other dev). No compensation, nor reset have been announced on that point (yet or never).

You have several ways to report bugs, either on the Bug Reports forum section or the sticky topic, but if you feel it leads to cheating, you have to report it to moderators (listed on the Forum Rules).

Afreet, after one year of service, Pigov, after two and me, after three, have already left the moderation team. To replace the missing European mods, sjefke and Ndeline have joined them.

Now for the last news but most important of all.

To avoid problems generated by new tweaks (and devs availability), the EN calendar will now follow the FR calendar for the main PvP events. Two MW will be followed by one CotC, then followed back by two MW etc.

Right now, I'm still discussing with devs about EN MW rules tweaking. If rules are being copy/pasted from FR event or we still keep our custom rules.
