calorifere
If your choice is necessarily between a cloudoz and a wanwan, then I would choose the cloudoz for two reasons
1) you possibly plan to make it a collector. It's not a bad idea early on to have a collector to increase your cash flow.
2) the cloudoz is cheaper than the wanwan, so you'll be able to buy your next dinoz faster.

For the reason above, you may want to reconsider buying a softpig. They can be really strong, but the additional 4k they cost slow down your progress in the game (you probably know how long those 4k take to collect early on in the game...). The moueffe or glidwings can also make pretty strong fighters when raised well, and sooner or later, you'll need a gorilloz to get the U-skills Leader and Engineer, so you may want to consider that one as well.

When you 'unlock' you unlock all skills you have the prerequisites for and no others. That means that yes, if you rush Sumo you will have to unlock again after taking Gel to get Petrification. This is why rushing skills in your primary element is not usually recommended.
