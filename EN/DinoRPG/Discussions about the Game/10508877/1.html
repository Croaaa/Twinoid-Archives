These are the special skills the demon dinoz have. They are available only on the French version, and in order to get one you have to sacrifice several dinoz to get one. This info was found on a French Clan by Dinno, on the Spanish server.
Demon Gorilos
Special skill: Increases all assaults by 5
Demon Wan-wan
Special skill: Gives the status Acelerated to all the dinoz in the group
Demon Winks
Special skill: Increases all defenses by 6
Demon Pigmou
Special skill: Attacks one enemy with a fire power 5 plus wood power 3
