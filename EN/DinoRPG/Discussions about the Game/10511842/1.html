The wanwan definitely wants leafy dawn and possibly shock. Where are you getting cocoon from? It is in the endurance tree and doesn't do anything for PvP anyways.

In general your final builds are good, but the order of the skills can be different. Ie, a rocky that is planning to learn corrosive archangel and not overload, doesn't need to learn intelligence until after career plan. It will still unlock adrenaline at the same time as corrosive archangel without wasting an unlock.

The winks on the other hand to achieve that build might want to waste one unlock to rush career plan and avoid lightning level ups. Then it can unlock hazard and electrolysis later. This gives you 10 lightning level ups, 5 of them after career plan. You do have 1 wasted unlock this way (the other extra over your 8 is learning electrolysis in lightning instead of water), but you have 5 skills to learn in lightning after career plan. Only 2 skills to learn in lightning after career plan, I don't think is doable. You will hit lightning again after career planning more than that. Having to take lightning dance is the same as a wasted unlock as you should definitely turn it off. Lightning dancing a torched dino for 5 damage to him and 35-50 damage to yourself is pretty bad. Getting career plan sooner also helps to ensure you pick up warm blood and vaporous form.

Also like Poquemon2 said, you aren't showing where you are learning the double skills. You need to pick which element you will unlock and learn the skill in.

Husaurus
These builds seem to ignore the issue of needing both Double Skill prerequisites before a 4th tier unlock. Even with Career Plan, this can be very unlikely if one of the skills is in an off element. Hence, these builds are likely to require extra unlocks, wasting levels.

I would avoid taking unfavorable branches in off elements just for a chance at a second or third Double Skill. The multiple skills missed in the favorable branches are often more useful than the Double Skills.

My builds are fast becoming flow charts... with conditionals based upon the elements gained on previous levels, and the presence of Career Plan... all to maintain the timing of Double Skill prerequisites as well, as general optimizing considerations.
