Some tips for Newbies you might find useful:


1. Don't buy items for healing, rest instead. Spend your money on new Dinoz.

2. Look at clan page maps to see the fastest route to a specific destination.

3. Don't ever do the "Find the right place" parts of missions own your own, check the clan pages for that. You'll just be wasting turns if you search by yourself.

4. Don't blindly pick whichever skill sounds the best, check clan pages to see where each skill leads where. Plan the future of your Dinoz accordingly.

5.Chose you Dinoz carefully, you should think of what kind of role the Dino would play before you buy it. A fighter? A Gatherer? One that goes for a specific U skill?

6. Powerful monsters start to appear at certain levels of every area. Be prepared to either leave or group your Dinoz together before they arrive. Clan Pages.
