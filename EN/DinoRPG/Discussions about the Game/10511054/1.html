insanedar
There has to be a constant of damage reduction involved. If it is straight calculation of defense, then there would be an extremely huge difference between attacks like Meteor vs Burning Breath.

For example, if your fire element is 20, meteor would be at 240 fire dmg, and burning breath would be at 100 fire dmg. Let's say a Dinoz' fire defense is 70. The difference between the two is 240-60=180, and 100-60=40. The difference is many factors greater. In actual pvp combat, you see that if a Dinoz do about 30 dmg in meteor, their burning breath will do about 18 or so, which is a much smaller factor of difference.
