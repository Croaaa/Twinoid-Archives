A bunch of changes to announce for DinoRPG:

A goodbye:

First and foremost, I’d like to take the time to announce the departure of our Dino Dev kiroukou.. After 6 wonderful years at Motion Twin, the talented multi-instrumentalist engineering type is taking off on a new adventure.

As THE guy who’s been looking after Dino since he arrived, he’s going to miss the community that he helped create and maintain over the years. He’s been an influential figure in the game, controversial at times, but always working to make sure that the game keeps on keeping on, and that all the players have access to their little piece of fun when they want to relax.

In anticipation of his departure he’s been hard at work adding a bunch of improvements to the game. Some of these you’ve already seen… You now have the calendar of events, which lets you plan which events you’d like to take part in and train up just the right team of Dinoz for the event. He’s also been busy training up skool as his replacement and making sure that he’s correctly commented his code (we know where he lives, so he had to do it). But that’s not all he’s been up to...

To level 80 and beyond?

We’re happy to announce the long awaited (well at least from the French side) arrival of level 80! This will be the last level rise for your Dinoz. Master Zenith should be able to help you find out a little more about the training you’ll need to undertake in order to reach the ultimate level… But don’t think that level 80 is the end. You might be surprised what you find once you get there.

Because of the addition of the new levels we’ve also looked over the XP distribution algorithm, tweaking it so that you’re not going to have to grind for too long to reach level 80. We feel that this should make the game more enjoyable overall and allow newer players to reach the ultimate level without struggling to get past level 70.

This comes hand in hand with other updates to the game that should leave us with a better system and less bugs. For example:

  • Certain bugs linked to battles and objects have been fixed.
  • We’ve added a page that allows you to check up on the progress of your quests.
  • You can now synchronise your wins and achievements that might not have made it across to Twinoid from your profile.
  • The public API has been fleshed out and improved so that any developer types among you can get their hands on the data of the game and make some new and interesting tools.
Translations:

As I mentioned in a previous post, I’m going to be delivering the new quest. Although it seems I spoke too soon when I said the translation was done (as I discovered a few more files with waaay more text in them). In any case, I’m still working on it and will be getting the quest out to you ASAP.

As for the other updates we’ve announced today they’re all translated and ready to go.

Special Offers:

On top of all the changes and the departure of our kungfu kiroukou (yeah he actually does kungfu), we’re rolling out some “special offers” that should put a smile on your dial. You might find an unbeatable offer coming your way over the next few days, but I’ll let you guys find out more about that as you play. Just know that this won’t be the only time that you see such a thing...

Have fun with the new updates and stay tuned for the new quest!

buzzard.
