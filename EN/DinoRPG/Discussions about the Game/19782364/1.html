This war it's a bit more complicated. To not lose mana you need to pay the taxman every day after the first day you build in this war. It was planned that you need your castle to be up to do that, but a bug now makes it possible that you can even pay him when your castle is down. You can see on your castle page after reset at what time the taxman can arrive for that day.
Another reason to lose mana is losing a war to another clan. They will get 5% of your mana pool. But that is something you most likely know already.
