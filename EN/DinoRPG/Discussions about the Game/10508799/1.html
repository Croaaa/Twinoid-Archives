Well, since the thread about the Game Future was closed, I thought I would leave my suggestion here instead.

I know that Phantom is not a developer of Motion Twin, but he can voice in suggestions.

I think Double Skills are very, very interesting; although, I do not know if I know all of them. I would still like to make a suggestion for the English Version's future.

My Suggestion:

From what I've searched up, Double Skills are comprised of two different level three skills from different elements. So, I was thinking, "So, that's what would make Hippoclamps even more interesting!" Yet, I think the idea could be even better, because not only are Hippoclamps the most expensive to purchase in the shop, it has to be obtained through a trophy, and it's description says it is an "Elite" Dino. Of course, they do not have a special trait like Soft Pigs or Winks, but I think a way to make them even more playable without a trait, is to add Triple Skill or Quadruple Skill. It is what the names imply, the bonding of different skills of different levels and elements to form a new synthetic skill. It's not always guaranteed a Hippoclamp will get the strongest skills in an element, is it? Because they have a random chance to get any element, so I thought it would make them more interesting!
