Guess what Guys, the game already has an item that gives you all that and more. It's called the PDA and you get it after finishing all of Skully's missions. It gives you a one page list of all of your Dinoz in the same order as the list on the left.

The two major Columns are Dinoz and Elements. The Dinoz Columns is subdivided into the following 3 columns (one row for each Dinoz): Picture of your dinoz; Level; and Dinoz Name, Yellow Health Bar, and Violet Experience Bar. If you mouse over this last colum/row you get a picture of all the status items that that dinoz has acquired.

For each dinoz the Elements Column is broken down into the 5 Elements ( 4 6 2 12 2) and the number of each element with the highest element being highlighted.

This PDA also allows you to change the order of all your dinoz too. If you mouse over one of the rows of your dinoz, you get an up and down arrow at the end of the row. If you click on either of the arrows, you move your dinoz either up or down in the list of dinoz. This changes the order of your dinoz on the left list and also the order of your dinoz in the My account page.
