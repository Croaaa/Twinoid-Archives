Well, a good start would be going directlly with grandpa Joe, finish all of his missions (exept the last two), then fight the water elemental to gain the lifesaver [Your dino lvl should be like 9 by now] , go to the islands and get the pearl, and do some of Mr. Bao Bao missions if you want. By that time you will have like 6 (or even more) dinoz or so (if you dont buy irma potions). Remember to use rest instead of buying food. Don't worry about the quests, Im sure there are masters working on guides already.

The only modification to w3ko's advice would be that if your Dinoz has an "A" skill in a weak element, like Water Canon with a very low ability (as listed under information at the top of the Dinoz's sheet - not under Details/Characteristics [BTW, Caracteristics is a misspelling]), then your Dinoz might better go north to the fire area and do the first two quests there before bothering with the water area.
