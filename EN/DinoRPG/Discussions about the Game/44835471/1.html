Some calculations since its more easy to compare numbers:
Using teams of 5 for these calculations.

Before nerf:
1 whistle: 1x4 = 40 extra damage
2 whistles 2x4 = 80
3 whistles 3x4 = 120
4 whistles 4x4 = 160
5 whistles 5x4 = 200

After nerf:
1 whistle: 1x4 = 40 extra damage
2 whistles 2x3 = 60
3 whistles 3x2 = 60
4 whistles 4x1 = 40
5 whistles 5x0 = 0

So for two whistles your extra damage will drop from 80 to 60 now. Thats not totally horrible, still good.
And the nerf is an excellent method to prevent the 200 (250 for 6man team) extra damage which is just kinda outrageous
