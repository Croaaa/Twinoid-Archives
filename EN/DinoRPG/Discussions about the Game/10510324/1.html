The selection of skillz doesnt seem so catastrophic to me. You probly just wasted some levelups on unecessary/too early unlocks. The wanwan may not be elite warrior, but it can be a good dinoz still. Just use it as any other dinoz now. As several people mentioned, unlock u-skill Merchant with it, get Elemental Fission along the way. Goblin Mergues supplier is a good idea, however note that its most advantageous to use dinoz who has wood based collection skill for that (Search, Archeologist, Detective), because you can use those in Ashopuk Ruins. Wanwans are fairly strong in wood as well, so if you are going to get lots of wood level ups later on, get those skills and station him in Ashopuk Ruins as Goblin Merguez dealer.

There is no such thing as "supposed to" when it comes to picking the skills if this is a roleplaying game - you just pick the skills you like or want.

However, one needs to consider how one wants their Dino to evolve and choose skills to further that plan.
Some of your choices will clearly depend on the Dino race and the role you want him to take (e.g. a gatherer compared to a psychotic killing machine)

Since I am not familiar with the skill progressions, I have been looking at resources made available by some of the clans in order to help me to decide which skills to choose.
