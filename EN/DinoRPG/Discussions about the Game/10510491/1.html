The Tamers Market (at Market Place (Dinotown Kingdom) is the auction house. You can buy dinoz, ingredients, eggs, a few items (amnestic rice for example), and any combination of these from other players. And you can also sell these there.
Buyers pay in Treasury Notes (you get these for real money or for low level ingredients at the Market Place from the Shady Man), and the seller receives the price in gold

The Itinerant Merchant only buys ingredients (and only ingredients!) , different kinds on different days (check the top clan's page for more information). His location is randomly re-placed on every week.
He gives only cash (gold) for your ingredients.
