Remember the IDT presets topic? Well Amphitheatre asked devs what IDT could do and analyzed what you gave them, then they made this poll (on FR forums) for you.

Here's a summary:

There will be 12 IDT along the year, the propositions where put inside this sheet. 1st column list all dinoz, 1st row the IDT type. So whenever there's one IDT type, dinoz with a 1 on its cell should where it has to appear (since hippos are wildcards, they were deemed to appear in every editions).

12 propositions1. vs , 3vs3, Several, Magic items, no consumables.
2. vs , 3vs3, Several, Magic items, no consumables.
3. vs , 3vs3, Several, Magic items, no consumables.
4. vs , 3vs3, Several, Magic items, no consumables.
5. Monochromatique (heavy one main element), 5vs5, Only one, Magic items, no consumables.
6. Pikachu ( oriented), 3vs3, Only one, Magic items, no consumables.
7. Bulle = Bubble races, 3vs3, Several, Magic items, no consumables.
8. Jungle ( oriented), 4vs4, Only one, no Magic items, no consumables.
9. Délaissés = Unused, 4vs4, Several, Magic items, no consumables.
10. Duo populaire = Two most popular, 2vs2, Several, Magic items, no consumables.
11. All-in3, 3vs3, Several, Magic items, no consumables.
12. All-in5, 5vs5, Only one, no Magic items, no consumables.

Poll translation:1. Yes, I like it.
2. Yes, I think we could change some things, but it's better than the current settings.
3. No, I think we have to change the settings, but not in this way.
4. No, do not change anything, I like the way it is currently.
5. I don't know / I don't care / I only want to see the results.
