You can have maximal 21 unfrozen Dinoz at the same time, with that Dinoz you can make moves. You can not use frozen Dinoz till you unfreeze it. And you must freeze or sacrifice one of your maximal number of active dinoz to unfreeze one of the frozen.

There are many reason for having frozen dinoz:
1. You can freeze Dinoz with U-Skills they will continue to give benefits to your acc.
2. You can freeze Dinoz that you want ot sacrifice later if you had bad luck in rolls.
3. You can freeze some kinds of gatherers if you don't need this kind of ingredients at this time and unfreeze them during CW or smthng like that.
4. etc.
