richiedude, you can transfer One Free Dinoz from Dino-Fight anywhere from Level 5 to Level 20 ready to level up to Level 21. In general, Masters that already have the Max Number of Dinoz should wait for their Dinoz to be Level 20, ready to level to Level 21, while Masters without the Max Number of Dinoz can transfer anytime within the 2 levels, depending on what they want to accomplish. Transfer just at Level 5 to have another Working Dinoz earning Daily Gold sonner to get more Dinoz sooner. Transfer at any other level to have a Dinoz to make a group of 2 or 3 Dinoz that are the same Level or close to it or without having to pay for the upgrade, or to have a Level 21 Dinoz without having to pay for the Upgrade.
