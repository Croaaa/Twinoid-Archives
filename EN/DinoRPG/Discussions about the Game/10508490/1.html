It's called wolf hunting, and here are all of Grandpa Joe's quests, and their rewards.

1- Fresh Fish

Go to Frish Port and speak with Fish Merchant
Go to DinoTown and speak with Madame Squyd
Go to Grandpa joe's Home and validate mission
Prize: 20

2- The Lost Dog

Go to Precipitous Hills and Speak with Madame Squyd
Go to Frish Port and Speak with Poodle
Go to Precipitous Hills and Speak with Madame Squyd
Go To Grandpa Joe's Home and validate mission
Prize: 15 and 1 angel potion.

3- The Hills Smashrooms

Go To Precipitous Hills and kill six Wolves or Smashrooms
Go to Grandpa Joe's Home and validate mission
Prize: 30 and 500

4- Wolf Hunting

Go to BrutForce and Kill 2 Wolves
Go to The Fountain Of Youth and Kill 2 Wolves
Go to DinoTown and Kill 2 Wolves
Go to The University and Kill 2 Wolves
Go to Grandpa Joe's Home and validate mission
Prize: 30 and 2.000

5- The Rose Bush in danger

Go to The Fountain of Youth and Take Some Water
Go to DinoTown and Speak with Madame Squyd
Go To Grandpa Joe's Home and validate mission
Prize: 20

6- The Recipe Book

Go to The University and Take the Book
Go to DinoTown and Speak with Madame Squyd
Go to Grandpa Joe's Home and Validate Mission
Prize: 20

7- The Stamps

Go to The Fountain Of Youth, kill 15 Smashrooms and Take Stamps
Go to Grandpa Joe's Home and validate mission
Prize: 20 and The Stamps

8- The Confidential Letter

Go to DinoTown and Speak with Madame Squyd
Go To Grampa Joe's Home and validate mission
Prize: 20

9- A Strange Monster

Kill 1 Gluon( is not necessary move )
Validate Mision
Prize: 30 and 500

10- The Giants
You need lv 11 Minimun

Kill 12 Giants( is not necessary move )
Validate Mision
Prize: 100 and 5.000

11- The Month Exploit
You Need lv 18 Minimum

Kill 20 Giant chickens
Validate Mission
Prize: 200 and 8.000
