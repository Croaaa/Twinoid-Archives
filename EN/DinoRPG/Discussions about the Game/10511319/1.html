Sparti
What do you guys do when there is no war??

Well, I can only talk about my clan, since I don't know what's going on in other clans. But I would guess that things are similar there.

* We share knowledge and information and help each other grow . There are a couple of people specializing in different things and if you need an answer to any of these they help.
* We celebrate members achievements.
* We tell each other interesting things that happened to us in the game.
* We talk about other things. After a while you get to know people in you clan and their interests.

For me the game is completely different with and without a clan. Let's face it: this game without a clan is a game where you log in and press 6-7 buttons every day. Your dinoz move and that's about it. If you are in a nice clan, you log in to see how your friends around the world are doing, and by the way move those dinoz.

I would have been so bored in this game by now if I didn't know anybody. But different people enjoy different things.
