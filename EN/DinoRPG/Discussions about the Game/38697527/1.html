Another good thing would be damage dealt per dino as different masters attacked with different size of a group.

And I strongly disagree with the ideas to change the % of mana gained while attacking a weaker clan or an idea where the percentage is depreciated basing on the honour spent for declaring a war.
The whole reason for a war system is to get an adequate ranking at the end of the event.
Strongest 1st, weakest last.

If you mess the numbers then how can you tell clan A is stronger than clan B if, for example, clan B stayed low for the most of the war just to lose small amounts of mana and then, at the end, skyrocketed.

With static % of mana gained the clans are properly ordered in the ranking.

Although I agree that smaller clans could have their treasury costs lowered, making it easier for them to participate in the event.
