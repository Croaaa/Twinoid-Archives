You may be asking, but what are the implications, well I will explain.

Lets assume for simplicity that the only skills you have, or have "on" are water cannon and mistral, and that both have a 25% activation chance.

If water cannon activates first this is how it will affect mistral.

In 100 attacks on average water cannon will activate 25 times.
You might think that then out of the 100 attacks mistral will also activate 25 times.... but no, you would be wrong. This is because you don't have 100 attacks to try and activate mistral, you only have 75 because water cannon gets checked first and steals 25 of the attacks from water cannon.

So out of 75 attacks Mistral would activate

75 * 25% = 18 (on average)

So with water cannon getting checked first with both skills having equal %'s, in 100 attacks, the results are as follows.

25 Water Cannon
17 Mistral

Now while this isn't a bad thing if water is your main element, what if things don't work out how I say, what if you only have 5 elements i.e 1 mistral damage, and then mistral gets checked first eh. Or theirs another example of implication, when skills that aren't very good but have a high level and high activation chance such as Magic Resistance, which completely destroy more useful activations of vines/korgon for example.

As far as thinking about skills like tornado and mistral, the lower level worse skill never interferes with the higher level ones so no worries there, also no problems with much more powerful skills such as tornado or meteors or w.e replacing the infantile Mistral.
