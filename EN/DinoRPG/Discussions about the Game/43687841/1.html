My guess is that MT places the teams in order of favorites (most likely to win to least likely to win). Then they are paired 1st favorite vs 8th favorite, 2nd favorite vs 7th favorite etc etc etc.

The 2nd round is more challenging winners vs winners and losers vs losers. We will have 4 winners and four losers in order of favorites. 1st favorite winner vs 4th favorite winner and 2nd favorite winner vs 3rd favorite winner. While the 5th favorite loser will face the 8th favorite loser and 6th favorite loser will face the 7th favorite loser.

The 3rd round will have the only 2 undefeated clans meet one another and the only 2 time losing clans meet each other. The other clans with one win and one loss will face each other starting from the most favorite playing a clan not faced before.

The 4th round starts with the only undefeated clan facing the highest favorite clan that has not been previously fought. Now the currently 2nd place clan faces the next highest rated clan that has not been faced before.

The fifth round uses the same philosophy as the previous round, however MT does reserve the right to always change their original format.
