[ http://twinoid.com/en/article/3748/the-age-of-caushemesh-v1-1 ]

The arrival of the Age of Caushemesh has reignited passion in the community, so in order to make the dinoz masters even happier, we have made a few corrections:

  • It is now possible to return to the Steppes after a certain stage in the scenario from the Emmemma Camp.
  • You can activate new actions for all Dinoz at once by using the button located next to the flying shop button.
  • The time remaining until the next free action is displayed next to the map/inventory/details tabs.
  • Navigation between dinoz has been fixed.
  • After level 20, You will receive a free action every 24h (at midnight)
  • New Dinoz management using the PDA is possible, allowing you to organize your dinoz more easily!
  • The issue with free actions in dungeons and harvesting grids has been corrected.
  • New account setting, you can now activate SmartGroup, which regroups your dinoz intelligently.
  • A failed invocation (if called twice) doesn't use up any energy.
  • Ingredient donations are available again!

Masters! To your Dinoz!
May the lands of Caushemesh tremble beneath the feet of all the dinoz destined to arrive there shortly!
