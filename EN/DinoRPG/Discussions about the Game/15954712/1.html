Blank

I don't quite fit into this club as i have spent some money on the game but the small amount i spent helped alot. I started the game with 100,000 gold that i bought and advice from my brother who already played the game. This pretty much ment i had a 6 month jump start compare to a normal "newbies". Even though i started the game with a plan already set up it was not perfect however, and in the end switched my Number 1 team to 3 young Rockies. If my plan had worked out better i could have reached 50 a few months ago, instead im 48,48,49 (close ).

Generally for people to reach lvl50 without spending money they need to follow these key tips:
Concentrate on just 1 team of 3 dinos, all gold earned spent on Irma potions for this team
Plan out a balanced account, Pick some teams of fighting dinoz, U-skillers and Gathering dinos. so that once all your dinos have matured your happy with you account.
Avoid buying new dinoz and spending time and money on them (this sets you account back), If you really want/need to buy new dinoz either don't spend money on them or wait until you main team has hit lvl 50 first.
Qualifying for the Dojo, Selling ingrediants, Spheres and even demons in the tamer market can all be ways of boosting you income. This gold can be spent on irma's to lvl you team up quicker.

Generally this is harsh journey for a beginner and in all likelyhood will go awry. But once you have your stong team of 3 then you can begin with raising all sorts of other dinos and build's and make your account your own.
