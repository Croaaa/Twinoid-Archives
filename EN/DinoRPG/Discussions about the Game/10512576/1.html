hkmaly
Laar is 184th, how much more experienced masters you expect?

Remember that you will need at least 14 dinoz (at level 20) to sacrifice for one demon. So don't worry about screewing some.

Now, the most important non-PvP skill on lighting dinoz is regeneration. You really should get it. It may be different if you plan PvP dinoz but you shouldn't worry about those yet. Otherwise, as Laar already said, do as few unlocking as possible. One unlock will always open all possible path for that element, so for example in lighting you shouldn't unlock before geting Celerity, Focus AND Intelligence unless you plan to NEVER have one of these on that dino (which will be the case for dinoz with low chance for that element). Pteroz have three , so getting both Celerity and Focus and then unlock may be good strategy.

And about grouping: problem with grouping over big level gap is that the younger dinoz will meet monster it would never meet when alone. You should consult bestiary and disband the group or go to other area when the risk is too big.
