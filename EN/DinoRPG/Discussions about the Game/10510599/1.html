First of all the dinoz you want to fight in the tournament must go to the islands and get the shovel. Then he must go to the Ashpouk Ruins on the mountain and dig with the shovel. He should find an old stone. Go to the university and show the stone to Professor Eugune who would recognize it as an Ashpouk Totem or something. The totem is a relic which will allow you entry into the tournament.

Once you're in the tournament yes if you lose your dinoz dies and you have to revive him and let him rest up. Each time you fight in the tournament you use a turn or an irma potion. I think the first opponent is a level 8 Wan Wan from memory. Each opponent gets progressively harder.
