My thoughts on this...
----------------------------------------
1- Easy and simple, Another server, the speed server
The GOOD (ideas)...

THE BAD (ideas)...

SUMMARY
Its just a test speed server so newcomers have an idea of what the indepth normal server is like, so they decide if they are gonna put time on the game or ditch it
The game eventually grows a PvP element, and the 1-a-day turns are so no one gains a huge advantage, as would happen with faster turns, as some players could check in repeatedly, while others can't. If you could play through the whole game (there really isn't too much content), most people wouldn't want to replay it more slowly. It would have to be different content, which would be better placed within the game.
Really the new turn system is there to give new players an idea of what the game is, so this is already being addressed. By slowing it down a little at a time, you don't have a situation where you're playing quickly until you reach a certain point, where it suddenly changes to 1-a-day.
----------------------------------------
2- Another Currency...
There's already gold and notes. Another would just make things harder, giving fewer ways to use each kind.
----------------------------------------
3- Newbie Trainer
Just some helper pet that acts as if you had a second pet, fights and help you do quests
lasts 3? Days, if you don't have a 2nd pet by then, then you are on your own again.

They really don't seem to be needed, since the things you fight on early levels aren't too tough (other than the Gluon, which you shouldn't see many of before you can fight them. There are Angel Potions you get, both a free starter one and at least one for an early mission, in case you do run into something you can't handle.
