If your dino's defence is below 26, it will suffer more than 1 damage from a cobrassero. If your dino's defence is below 13, it will suffer about 13-15 damage. When korgons are attacked by cobras, they receive up to 25 damage.

NB: the cobra also has a strong attack and can deal about 8 damage against dinoz with low resistance (e.g, moueeffes, softpigs, santaz).
