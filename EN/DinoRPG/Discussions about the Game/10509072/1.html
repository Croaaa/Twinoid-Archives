I've searched loads on the forum, but just can't find any guide for this. Also I don't wanna open a topic everytime I'm stuck. So I wanna make a lil Skully Guide topic here. The first few 3 are done. If anyone else knows how the rest goes, we don't have to open a topic anymore for the Skully Missions.

So, here it goes! Note: You have to be 10 or below to go to The Cementary and talk to Skully. (Skully is on the right of Frish Port)

MISSION 1: Preliminary Information
Find the right place (Grandnpa Joe's Home)
Speak with Grandpa Little Joe
Use 3 "Goblin Merguez"
Speak with Grandpa Little Joe Go to The Fountain of Youth
Read the Message Validate the mission
10 experience points
MISSION 2: On The Trail of Moulder
Find the right place (Sticky Swamp)
Speak with A Stuck Tourist
A.E.A. Assembly (Fight 2 Eeloz)
 Find the right place (Mutant Falls)
Speak with Fisher
Piranhoz Bay (Fight 6 Piranhoz)
Speak with Fisher
 Go to the Sticky Swamp
Signboard
 Validate the mission
20 experience points
1000 coins
MISSION 3: Miss Bao
Go to Mr Bao Bob's House
Speak with Miss Bao???
 Go to the Venerable's Lair
Speak with Miss Bao
Speak with Bao Babe
Kill 10 Goblins
Speak with Bao Babe
Speak with Bao Babe
 Validate the mission
10 experience points
500 coins
Skully Souvenir
MISSION 4: The Annoying Tourists
Find the right place (Ashpouk Ruins)
Speak with Merguez Seller
Visit the Ruins (Fight Merchant)
 Go to the Frish Port
Search a Shady Boat
Enter in the Boat
Speak with Freed Man
 Find the right place (Ashpouk Ruins)
Speak with Merguez Seller
 Validate the mission
40 experience points
2000 coins
MISSION 5: Napalm Shrimps
Speak with Skully
 Go to the Underwater Dome
Speak with Shrimps Merchant
Use 3 "Treasury Note"
Speak with Shrimps Merchant
 Go to the Twilight Cemetary
Speak with Skully
 Go to Lavapit
Speak with Soft Shaman
Use 5 "Rescue Flamelet"
Speak with Soft Shaman
 Go to the Twilight Cemetary
Speak with Skully
 Search for a Korgon (Venerable's Lair)
Search for a Korgon
 Go to the Twilight Cemetary
Speak with Skully
 Validate the Mission
30 experience points
5000 coins
MISSION 6: The Reunion
Go to the Ashpouk Ruins
Speak with Merguez Seller
Rest of the mission waiting... (Speak with Moulder)
 Validate the mission
10 experience points Talk to Skully
Pebble in Diamantite Agglomerated
