I think I finally have the answer as to why people have different experiences. Maybe you all knew this already but it is new to me.

I have been able to fight both the water elemental and fire elemental at age 5 but not for the same dinoz. No matter which elemental I fight first, when I then try to immediately fight the second I get the following

Hilariously enough, my 6-year old dinoz got the same response so it seems that the good Professor cannot distinguish between a 5-year old and a 6-year old.
