+6 defense is very good against normal monster. They do 6 less damage per hit after all. The problem is that steppe, forest, monster island and nimbao monsters arent "normal". They do and they recieve PvP damage. In PvP +6 defence negates just ~1,2 points of damage. The second attack of the worm would have done 9 dmg without hermetic aura. Thats all the skill does in PvP.

Armor works differently and much much better against monsters. It reduces assault damage by 1 per point of armor and monster always assault you as they dont have range skills but guardians. The second advantage of armor is it doesnt matter if it is PvP or "normal" damage. Its always working as it should. Thats why everybody skills carapace but nobody skills hermetic aura.
