Hello all. I'm breaking my forum hiatus to talk and launch the discussion about what I believe that can be beneficial for the growth of this server. I also play in french and spanish servers, and what I see in there is a much bigger community than ours. This sounds odd to me, since english is the most spoken language in the world. This can be because of marketing reasons where only MT can help themselves. But I believe there is some we can do, and today I want to speak about what we can lobby to happen and help this community grow. This is just my opinion about just 2 matters, but please feel free to add more, if you feel like it.

First and foremost: wars. What I'm currently seeing in spanish server is a total dedication by most clans that are participating in the mana war, even if they are weaker and don't have much chance for a "napodino prize" position. I'm talking of about at lest 50-60 clans actively participating in the war. To be sure, our server doesn't have this numbers of clans (or players) to be equal to this, but I wonder of how many clans have been destroyed for the rules of our wars, where only top20 is properly rewarded with napos. This forces players that outgrow their clan to leave their weaker clan in order to keep evolving, this forces clans to merge together, I'm sure we all know examples of this, but bottom line, we are reducing our "war community" to 25-30 clans (cotc may have some more), and I believe this is forced right away by the long-term prizes structure that we have. Big clans keep getting bigger, and weak clans remain weak, unless a big effort (€€€) is done to change that. Some clans could still be operating if they had a better growth perspective than they have at the present, but instead they were forced to merge with some other clan or just get absorbed by another. And players that left their weaker clans so they could keep growing, would all still do that if they could get a decent prize in their clan?

So, I think we can lobby to change this. Even if only top30 at first, even if the clans that do get a top30/top40 seem "unworthy" of a napo, Rome wasn't built on a day and I think we can call it a long term plan, to give time for clans to be created and grow and have a better long term perspective. There are more ideas that can be given for there to be more clans (reducing number of members, change some war rules, etc), but I believe this wouldn't be one of the easiest and most effective ways.


Secondly, I would like to ask the moderators to rethink the forum they have "created". I see a much more participated forum in the other servers, and again, more players obviously means more participation, but in my opinion you also are responsible for the forum, and you keep locking newbies threads and telling them to use other threads with pages and pages of information that they may not understand or need at all. And some do use them, but not all, or else that page would be overcrowded and impossible to follow. Why don't just let people create their own threads and get their own personal attention? Why is that so bad? If they ask a thing that has been questioned a thousand times before, well, they have the doubts everyone had, what is the big deal? Just let someone answer it for the thousandth time and incentive them to put up more questions they might have, not locking them out and telling them to use a page that is overcrowded with discussions which half of newbies won't get. People need to start with baby steps in a new game, and you're just shutting them up, and in my opinion that is not good. And it's not only them you're shutting up, but also other people, moving and editing their threads, locking because from your perspective isn't worth being open... Well, I say any discussion is better than none and what isn't interesting to you can be to someone else. I prefer a more lively forum with some "disorganization", than having everything "perfectly" placed, but no one paying them much attention. And then there are bans, bans, and bans, bans for everyone and everything, and I can just feel that you do it because you just feel that because you can then you have to. No. Don't drive people away from the forum.
