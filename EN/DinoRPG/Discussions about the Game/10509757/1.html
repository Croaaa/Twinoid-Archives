In fights, only one dino gets credit for a kill. So, if each dino needs to kill 3 wolves, the group needs to kill 6.

(This is still better sometimes, since the group will take less HP damage. And if the group is high enough level, it'll get attacked by more enemies.)

The other option, of course, is to ungroup when you get to your combat area, fight your monsters, then regroup again for movement.
