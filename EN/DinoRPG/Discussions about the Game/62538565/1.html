Yes, that's what the piece of dark orb is for

As a follow up: You can enter with the dino with piece of dark orb in their status items and any dino that is following at the time. If you rebirth or reincarnate the dino or you'd want to enter with any dino, who does not have the item ie. didn't finish all of Gulom's missions, you'll have to use the elixir again and finish the missions to gain access to dark world trough the falls again.

A tip: watch out when going to the forrest with the dino with dark orb, you can missclick and loose a lot of time going to the dark world
