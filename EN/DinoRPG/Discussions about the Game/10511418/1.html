Well actually the thing about defence is that it's calculated based on your other 5 elements defence, so an increase in defense will actually increase your defence...
This is not true.
Void defence is based on the total of your elements adjusted by skills which either boost ALL defences or increase element values.

Increasing defence has no effect on
Increase element by 1 will affect void defence and also all other defences since they other five defences are interconnected (but based on element rather than defence values)
