TO THE PLAYERS OF DINORPG

While the recent April Fools Joke proves that our new Admins have a sense of humor, I personally did not find it very amusing.
In fact, it made me start thinking again about the vulnerability and lack of power we all have here.

I know how much real money I have spent here, and considering this is a 'free' browser-based Flash side-game, it is a rather obscene amount.

Yet, I know others who have spent much much more.

All this time and real money, and we have no guarantee, no security whatsoever.

The LLC Motion Twin could fold tomorrow, and none of us would receive any compensation.
MT could reset our accounts tomorrow, a bug or virus could wipe the Servers clean..you get the idea.

Who else here feel just a little uneasy about that? If you have spent even 5 Euros here, you deserve some security regarding your Account, and some say regarding the future of this game.

If you have spent over 500 Euros, then surely you must agree. How would you like to wake up one day and log on, only to find that your Account has been reset, all the dinoz you worked so hard on have been eliminated, all the items you have accumulated have vanished from your Inventory, and all the time and money you spent here has been for naught?

All those Euros you spend pay the salary's of MT's Staff, which as we all know is at a bare minimum right now, keep MT's Servers running--not only here but also for the other games under the MT umbrella--and allow MT to turn a profit and stay in the Black.
In addition, the amount of daily traffic we provide to this site allows it to be ranked and valued at over $700,000 according to several Online Website Analysis Systems.

In return, we expect more than just a flawed and mediocre PvP system, a flawed and unfair Market/Auction System, an under-staffed Administration that is unable to handle it's workload in a timely manner (through no fault of their own) and perhaps most importantly, we deserve the safety and security of knowing that our accounts will never be 'reset,' 'deleted,' or otherwise altered in any way without our express written consent.
We would also like to be more involved in the decisions made which affect us all, and which determine the present and future state of this game.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
And now, to wit, this 4th Day of April, 2010 come the Members of the Official DinoRPG Players Union, who demand safety,security,assurance, flexibility, the hiring of more Admins and other qualified Staff, and a more direct role in the daily decisions made about the present and future of this game.
Let it be known that any arbitrary deletion of any part of any players Account is wholly and completely unacceptable.

Let it be known that we want to be informed of plans for the future of this game, in advance, not a day before such changes might occur.

Let it be known that we demand that Motion Twin LLC hire a proper Staff of Developers, Administrators and moderators, in numbers sufficient to handle the workload to which they are assigned, and with the ability and qualifications to handle said duties.

Let it be known that we desire a more efficient, fun and fair PvP system, without loop-holes, exploits and cheats, and which includes a broader, more inclusive and more intricate method of Player vs Player Content.

let it be known that we are unhappy with the present Market-Place/Auction System, and that we would like to see some of the ideas that have been mentioned in previous threads implemented, and we would like the current system to be tweaked to be more fair. Some ideas that are to be considered are a Timer, the removal of the 3-day automatic waiting system, the removal of a minimum price,the ability to sell directly to friends and clan-mates at prices agreed to prior to any actual sale,a SELL NOW option for Masters who wish to accept an offer before the 3-day waiting period ends, and a silent auction-style system.
Yes,some of these ideas would allow for multi-account abuse, but security measures could be implemented to help avoid this.

Let it be known that we demand a guarantee of security of our Accounts,and that such a guarantee should include but not be limited to
A. Extra back-up Servers to preserve all data in the event of a virus, system failure or other malicious attempt to manipulate data stored on MT Servers
B. A written promise from Motion-Twin LLC , stating that accounts will never be intentionally, accidentally or arbitrarily reset, deleted, changed, manipulated or altered in any way without the express written consent of each individual master

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Any attempt to block the creation of this Union or censor this Thread in any way, including Auto-Lock, shall be considered a hostile act, and possible grounds for self-deletion of accounts and cancellation of subscription.

There are many 'free' online games out there,for which spending real money gives no real unfair advantage.But we love DinoRPG, and that's why we are still here, despite some of the issues that need to be addressed.

I personally believe that all the time and money so many of us have already spent here warrants at the very least, permission and support of a Union created by and for the players, to be used as a tool to ensure a fun,safe, secure and satisfactory environment for all.


Anyone that has ever spent real-money here, or that feels they deserve more security, inclusiveness, power and rights, speak here and now, by signing this petition to start an Official DinoRPG Players Union.
Positions within this democratic union, which seeks only fairness and security, shall be assigned at a later date.

For now, just Bump this Thread with an 'Aye,' and a brief message, expressing your willingness and desire to join the Official DinRPG Players Union, which shall herein be known as the ODRPU.
