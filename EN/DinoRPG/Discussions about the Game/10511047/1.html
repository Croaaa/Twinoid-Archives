AndySQdancer
Owyndaw's solution will not work, then battle with the gluon is solo

But, I believe there is a trick so that it will work and you fight the gluon as a group instead of solo by doing the following:

1) Make the leader of the group the one that has the mission of doing the briefcase thing.

2) Make the one that has to kill the gluon a follower.

3) When you arrive at Madam X's and have the button to click to open the briefcase to let the gluon out, before you click it, click the group Irma pot button to give the group a new turn. Then click the button to open the briefcase to let the gluon out. The whole group should fight the gluon, not solo, and the follower gets the credit for killing a gluon.

I have not tried this for this specific case, but I have for other fights that were supposed to be solo and was able to fight as a group instead.
