Yeah, getting more dinoz is the way to go at first. More dinoz lets you make groups (safer combat)... but it also means you earn more money each "turn".

Once you have a good number of dinoz, you can start spending your income on stuff like Irma potions, and concentrating on leveling up your favorite (or most useful) dinoz.

No, every dinoz can act once in each Turn.

Only effect multiple dinoz has is during the starter period when turns happen faster than every 23 hours. In that case, each dino that acts will increase the "next turn" timer. But once you're at the maximum time between turns (23 hrs), it doesn't make a difference.
