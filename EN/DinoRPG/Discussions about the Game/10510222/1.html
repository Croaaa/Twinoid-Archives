I actually tracked all my stats for the first few dinoz (did it on spanish server, then killed the acct because 1 is enough).
The average gold was over 200, but under 300 (near 250). There is no relation to the dinoz level or enemy number (my lv 15 is still doing the same as my level 2). It is random, but weighted for the low end of the range.
Adjusted for the English turn system, a second Dinoz takes about 8 days, if you can be awake at the PC and take all the turns as they come up.
That assumes doing the money missions, using the free Irma's potions and food, not buying any more, and not needing to rest at all for that period.
The next couple take longer, as the turns reach 23 hours, then they start to get shorter again with more Dinoz collecting gold.

The actual stats are on my old PC, so I can't post them right now. There is an old post, if it still exists....
