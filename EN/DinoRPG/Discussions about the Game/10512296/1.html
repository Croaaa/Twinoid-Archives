Kayin
Well for resting.

- When you can, go for the Pearl of the Fountain (Epic item). You will be able to gain +5 at midnight update when your dinoz are standing at the Fountain of Youth.
- Until you get level 20 and be able to do a Strange Prowler mission, the "Dark Dinoz" one, you will be rewarded with the Tik Bracelet, which can give to the equipped dinoz with it +10 at midnight too.
- Then if you join a clan, if that clan got or will got the clinic, you can rest one dinoz fully to his maximum life for one hour. Clinic will be available 20 hours later.
- U-skill Priest will add +1 on the one hour rest tick for all your dinoz account. There's other skills but these will benefit only the one that learn them.
- If you group dinoz (I know you have only one), each individual will recieve less damage, then less resting.
