Here are the reports you've all been waiting for! Inter-Dojo Tournament XX and XXI - take it away Druid2k!

IDT XX

The format was 5 vs 5, with all items. With lots of Temporal Manipulators in use the vines-detonate-mass frenzy type initiative locks are not working. So it is time for some slower dinoz or dinoz with moderate initiative. Those who can survive longer will be victorious, without doubt.
Venar's team is the perfect choice for this format. 3 cloudoz with the all powerful, stacking Fujin at hand, an uber-powerful perfectly built castivorous with another powerful invocation, Werewolf, in its pocket, and a good old "I can deal heavy damage" Softpig for dessert! This team had many ways to defeat every kind of opponent! With specialist and without mercy, its assault damage is above average and concentrated! With fujins, the dinoz accelerate to be faster than anything, and with Werewolf every opponent gets a good 50+ damage!
This group of five had style, performed perfectly, and showed that its master knows every detail about how to make good builds, and how to assemble the best teams around! Congratulations to Venar on winning the champion's title with a team including a Castivorous - a thing that was unthinkable in the past!

IDT XXI

This dojo had the old-favorite 3 vs 3, all items format.
It was quite the mixture of the popular and powerful builds, including sumo Wanwans, bubble Winks and brave Rockies, and the new breed the fujin Cloudoz!
Almost every battle included Temporary Manipulator plus Beer, and the masters used tons of Lorie Costumes to defeat their opponents.
In the semi final Perrbear defeated Mohnkahn's full bubble team no problem, his Rockies dealing too much damage, and using Lories with great experience. On the other side, two Luso-Brasilian Team members fought each other, and Meng9999 defeated his clan's leader with his 3 Cloudoz. The battle seemed to be even, but after Mengo invoked 2 fujins in a row, it was decided who would fight for the title of champion.
Lots of players waited for the final with anticipation. It was the battle of the new and very powerful Fujin against experience, and the "good old ways" of the brave Rockies. The battle was very exciting, both sides giving and taking and everybody waited for the fujins to be invoked! After the boost came, the Cloudoz showed what speed means! Perrbear's rockies just stand, like a rock wall (more preciselly like a flaming rock wall), and inflicted tons of passive damage to the buzzing clouds! Mengo's strength finally depleted, and the surviving rocky finished the exhausted clouds.
And finally, a little about our champion. Ladies and gentleman, winning the Dojo Championship FIVE times (out of 10 attempts! Yes, he won HALF of the championships!) is an achievement that is truly EPIC. Perrbear, in doing so, has proven his knowledge of the game, his ability to adapt to the environment, and that he has the luck of the gods on his side!

We'd like to say a big thank you to Druid2K for his invaluable help and insight in putting these reports together!

Stay tuned for our COTC Report, coming soon!
