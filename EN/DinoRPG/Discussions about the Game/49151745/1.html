[895738]
edubrWatching those fights only made me realised I will need years playing this game to reach a remote chance to compete with those groups...

You've started since June this year and (assuming you have either spent no real money or a bit of it) it looks like you've made some very good progress. You just need more dinoz to maximize revenue and then find a clan to access steppes more efficiently. You can still access it on your own without a clan, but clan will provide you with other benefits like clinic and other project features in some quests.

I don't mean to give an "old man" lecture "back in the day", but the current turn system was not in place since about 2-3 years ago. I was also like you having similar thoughts when I started and I advanced using the old turn system which placed no incentive on young dinoz. The timer just kept ticking up to 23 hours from 0 mins (you started with 2 turns). I wouldn't really say I'm one of the most powerful, but I've definitely come a long way in 6 years.

The point as a non-paying member is this: are you going to commit to the game for the next several years or not to become as strong as us? It's a decision best made early.

If so, that's great. We have a wonderful community here who will help you with development. You'll know the best builds and tips for quests and the best way to acquire and .

If not, all is not lost. You don't have to compete with the strongest, so long as you have a good time and have fun. Although the IDT is in a sense reserved for the best players, the rewards for qualification are decent and the napos will help you develop. The clan events are now set up in a way where smaller clans will fight each other in mana wars usually and in COTC, each league is divided by level.

If you are still unsatisfied, don't feel forced to continue and then feel you made a bad choice in playing this game. I found this game via mybrute and considered it to be far better than mybrute. If I never found that dinorpg ad, I obviously would not be here and I would have ragequitted out of mybrute, regretting the several months I put into that game leveling my character up. Then again, it doesn't take up so much time each day.

I'm sure you'll enjoy this game in the years ahead and soon you'll become a formidable player. Go at your preferred pace.
