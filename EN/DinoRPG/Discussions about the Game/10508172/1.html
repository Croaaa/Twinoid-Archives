The starting Dinoz that are the most geared toward a single element are:
Gorilloz 13/20 Wood
Softpig 12/20 Fire
Sirain 11/20 Water

Gorilloz lack special attacks, since Wood doesn't have "A" Skills.
Softpigs are more expensive but have a lot of good Skills to choose.
Sirains have a lot of good Skills and aren't more expensive... so I chose Sirain.
