Piemaster
You don't need much money for the Kabuki quest. Just 10,000, plus some ingredients (which you can collect yourself if you make some collecting dinos). So put all you r money in more dinos and that will earn you more money in the long run anyway.

As for groupings, people talk about this and that dino working well together, but in my opinion it really doesn't matter that much. Pretty much any dino can work well with any other dino really. Personally I like to have all dinos in a group strong in either or defence so I can concentrate on either the volcano or islands with them. But other people go the other way and like the dinos compensating for each others weaknesses.

In short, just pick some dinos you like, chuck them in a group together and it will probably work
