The Dark Rocky does have 150. But he is located at the Mutant Falls and you encounter him and his other 2 Dark Dinoz (Pteroz and Hippoclamp) in the 2nd Prowler MISSION:
#2: The Dark Dinoz (must be level 20 to do the mission)
- Go to the Mutant Falls - Fight the Dark Dinoz
- Go to the Forges - Validate Mission
250 and 5.000 and Tik Bracelet

Be careful when doing this mission. After Accepting this mission, you encounter the three Dark Dinoz as soon as you move to Mutant Falls. Best to do it with a group of 5 Dinoz--all doing the mission. This way all 5 fight the 3 Dark Dinoz, and if successful in killing them, all 5 get credit for the kill, so only fight them once--it's not a separate selection button you push, you fight them when you arrive at the Mutant Falls. However, you only get one Tik Bracelet, but you do get 250 points for all the Dinoz doing the mission.
