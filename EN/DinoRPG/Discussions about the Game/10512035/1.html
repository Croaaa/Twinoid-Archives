[939518]
The problem is, that we didn't get the rules for the 130% system, just the info it will be in effect, that is why so many players don't understand how things are work.

The maximum sum of the defender levels is maximized in 130% of the sum attacker levels.
Automaticaly the first in the line of the defense will be choosen to defend, except if the attacker level is too small(lv30 attacking alone and the first defennder is lv40 or more)
In this case all the defenders in row will be checked and the first who is in the limit will be choosen. If all the defenders are above the maximum, then the lowest level dinoz will be choosen to defend, because the minimum number of defenders is 1.

The Defensive Reinforcements raise this minimum to 2, so if you attack with a lv1 dino alone, the 2 lowest level dinoz will defend even if they are both above lv40.

Defensive Reinforcements has been made because without it Brave Rockys would be the attackers
