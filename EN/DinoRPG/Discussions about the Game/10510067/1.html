I'll try to sum these guys up and make it as clear as possible.

Example:
-You join a clan.
-Timer starts counting for 10 days.
-Untill the timer is done with counting to 10 days, you can't join another clan but you can leave your current clan.
-Timer is done counting, you can join a new clan.
-Repeat.
