I suggest making a focus team (so you would spend irmas and gold on these guys) of 3 dinoz to fight in PvP events and level up as fast as possible. There are leveling guides everywhere. Regarding races, kabuki are decent, so you could use the egg you got for PvP. The other two are up to you. There are some good rare dinoz, but some like the santaz (debatable because of revealed 50+ skills) and feross are hard to train or not that great. I suggest a fujin cloudoz and for the 3rd type of dinoz either a strong passive damage/counter (softpig or quetzu, maybe even pteroz) or a bubble-penetrator (casti are good, mahamuti if you want to make a show). For the other dinoz, hunting is one of the best collecting skills in the game, so I suggest buying maybe 9 or so softpigs and rushing Dragon Hunter, and keep them collecting and fighting below level 12 so that you can use them twice a day.

Personally, i´d only start spending my hard earned coins to grind a "focus team" if you already got 18 (21) dinoz and have a merchant.
Maybe its tempting to get your hands on some of these rare dinoz early on, but in the long run, its smarter to just buy any other random dino (U-skiller, gatherer) and freeze/sacrifice them, once you got the infrastructure of your account running. Putting all your coins into a focused pvp team would be a waste, until then. Still, i wouldnt designate all your dinoz for support rules, theres nothing wrong with having 1 or 2 teams designated as future pvp ones, since you can gain experience with what works and what doesnt.
One of the most important lessons i learned is: Know your aims. If you freeze every dinoz just because he has 1 bad level up, you will probably never have a decent team as a non or semi-paying member. On the other hand: If you got a really bad dinoz, dont fall in love with him and continue to level him "just because".

(And one last thing: Its hard to level "efficient" in the early game, since you cant properly group your dinoz. Teams of 3 are the most efficient way, so since you got 60k right, i´d suggest buying 3 clouds at once. Go for gatherer skills, increase your income and head for some the air and water u-skills, maybe even lightning too, since you want to get fission anyways. It´ll take a while, but the income is worth it. Iam just assuming that your rocky and gor are already designated to head for some u skills, so cloudz would be the next logical choice, but in the end its up to you and what you prefer )

In that case, its probably better if you consult this thread first:
Advice for Beginners
Most of your questions will be answered there

But in a nutshell: Gathering skills are skills which allow you to gather ingredients. You can still move and/or fight, so this comes as an additional action. Ingredients are used for quests, can be sold at the market/merchant for extra income, or can be exchanged for treasury notes. So gatherers are a must, to gain more coins and fulfill certain quests (like the kabuki-quest)

U-Skills (Unique-skills) are skills which grant you an overall bonus.
Leader (wood) increases your active dinoz cap from 18 to 21.
Merchant (lightning)reduces item prices by 10%
Priest (lightning) lets your dinoz recover 1 more hp per round.
Other u-skills, skilltrees, where to gather, a.s.o. is explained in the beginners guide.
Further informations for your own research can be found on various clanpages, like Void Alliance´s Clanpage, or on rianons drpg-page http://dinorpg.novasoftware.pl/

This should be enough, regarding the basics, i hope it helps. If not, there are various other threads like yours which you could find via forum-search . If you got other short questions, you can also ask them in the Q&A thread.
