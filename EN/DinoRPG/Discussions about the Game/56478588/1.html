Thought I'd make a topic about this already, since not everyone has noticed yet probably Group Therapy has gotten a buff since some months. Either that, or it's a bug, but I like to believe the first one, bubble has been getting the short end of the stick with 50+ anyway...

It used to work only on enemy healing, but as of some time it also works on friendly healing, including your own! I noticed this when my Sirain was triggering Group Therapy a lot while fighting 1v1 against a dino with no accu, meaning my Sirain copied its own accu healing.
Only note here: You can only use Group Therapy once per "turn". So it's not possible to get a whole chain of Group Therapy healing, once you triggered it you can't trigger it right after again. Either the whole turn has to end, or a new dino needs to be able to make a move. Bit hard to explain, but if you watch some fights with GT dino's (like in this IDT) you'll get to understand.

Now a bit of background information of what GT can copy:
EVERY healing. No exceptions. So: Other GT's, accupuncture, LS healing, leafy healing from any dino on the opponent team, item healing (like rations), magic item healing (like Life Stealer). And so this goes for your own team now as well, and your own dino. So you can for instance trigger Life Stealer or LS, and then trigger Group Therapy on your own heal.

So also used that a little bit this IDT by putting Nap on and letting my teammates share the heals a bit
So not sure if bug or buff, but at least it's something to get bubblers a little back in tier again and make up for the bad init loss of Gamma Radiation.
