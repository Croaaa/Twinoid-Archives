Actually, I have an idea!

It's a story by T200.

The elements of Arcane:

Weaken
This is a level 1 E skill. It lowers an enemy's defense in all stats by 1.

Telepathic Attack
This is a level 2A skill which stems after Weaken. The dino tosses an opponent in the air and deals an arcane damage of 6.


Arcane Blast
This is a level 3 A skill that deals an arcane damage of 9.

Super Weaken
This is a level 4 A skill that stems after Arcane Blast. The dino weakens all the enemies' defenses by 5 in all elements.

Sacrifice
This is a level 3 P skill that stems after telepathic attack. This skill increases the arcane attack by 10 points, but decreases the speed by 20%.

Tyranny
This is a level 2 S skill that stems after weaken. The dino lands the strongest attack on the strongest opponent in the enemy group (by level).

Reflect
This is a level 3 S skill that stems after Tyranny. All negative stats are reflected back at the inflictor.

Encouragement
This is a level 4 A skill that stems after Refelct. When used, all the dinoz in the group gain +1 defense point in all elements.

Revolt
This is a level 3 P skill. The dino always attacks the strongest enemy (by level) in the enemy group. This skill is different from Tyranny, as Tyranny only makes the dino use its most powerful element on the strongest opponent.

Mind's Eye
This is a level 1 E skill. The dino predicts the next move of an opponent and prevents him from using an A or E skill on the following move.

6th Sense
This is a level 2 C skill that stems after Mind's Eye. It allows the dino to intercept common arcane energy in some places.

Epiphany
This is a level 3 C skill that stems after 6th Sense. It allows the Dino to intercept rare arcane energy in some places.

Ghostly Perception
This is a level 4 C skill that stems after Epiphany. It allows the dino to intercept ultimate arcane energy.

Ultimate Arcanist
This is a level 5 E skill that stems after Revelation. When used, the dino turns into a demigod: it increases the arcane attack power by 20 and increases all defenses by 10, but the dino loses 33% of its current hp. This is a rarely used E skill.

Lucky Day
This is a level 3 C skill that stems after 6th Sense. It increases the chance of intercepting arcane energy.

Meditation
This is a level 2 S skill that stems after Mind's Eye. With it, the dino gains +1 point bonus in any attack or defense whenever it levels up.

Teleport
This is a level 3 P skill that stems after Meditation. It increases the evasion chance by 10%; when the dino evades an attack, it teleports to a different location on the battle field.

Deceleration Beam
This is a level 3 A skill that stems after Meditation. The dino fires a beam that decreases an enemy's speed by 15%; it can only be used once in a battle.

Elfin Life
This is a level 1 P skill. It increases the maximum hp by 20.

Force Field
This is a level 2 P skil that stems after Elfin Life. It increases all defense by two points.

Nine Lives
This is a level 3 P skil that stems after Force Field. It increases the maximum hp by 50 points.

Recover
This is a level 4 A skill that stems after Nine Lives. The dino recovers up to 20 hp during a battle.

Blood Ties
This is a level 5 U skill that stems after Recover. All dinoz gain 5 points in every attack element.

Ghastly Presence
This is a level 4 S skill that stems after Time Control. When the dino is in battle, the enemies' defense drops by 5 points in all elements.

Repel
This is a level 2 P skill that stems after Elfin Life. the dino becaomes a less favorite target if in a group.

Arcane Mirror
This is a level 3 S skill that stems after Repel. A third of the damage that the dino receives is inflicted back at the attacker.

Manipulation
This is a level 4 A skill that stems after arcane mirror. The dino takes control over an enemy. It's similar to the Hypnosis skill, except that the dino that used Manipulation cannot fight until the manipulated dino dies.

Cat's Eyes
This is a level 3 P skill. It allows the dino to attack immaterial enemies.

What do I know? Nimbao is at (cold) war with Carushemesh. Since the people Carushemesh seem able to attack Nimbao, this hints that there could be lots of flying of dinoz up there.

It's very much likely to be a snow biome. Think of it as something like BAH but cold rather than hot.

Gathering prospects
2 places to get energies (perhaps even double chances of getting them).
2 places to get plants (some ultra-rare).
2 places to hunt
0-1 place to fish
2-3 places to excavate (chances of finding items which could be used in battle and/or ultra rare chance of finding a napo)

There could also be a few shops. Some would specialise in reducing assault damage at expense of speed or vice versa, others might involve cloak devices. There could also be another magical shop where there are new magic items.

IMO, the monsters will specialise in a mixture of and/or (or ether as the prowler puts it). Expect lots of immaterial, flying and maybe even speed buffs and debuffs. Species of monsters might involve:
Mountain Stealth ninja bandits (immaterial + steal + multihit + dodge + counterattack). Leaders of those bandits could be equipped with an enhanced version of the tic bracelet which enables them to regenerate 10 each turn during a battle.
Wild dark santaz + kabukis or dark dinoz in general. Could be resistant to A or E skills like borgs.
Yetis, maybe, with the ability to stun (petrify) and deal about 15 damage. Could be immune to damage, which instead increases the damage of their next hit by 50%.
Dark smashrooms (in larger groups)
Boss monster might be something similar to a tower guardian but instead they have more and they don't suffer 30 damage for each hit.
Other monsters with ether ability. Ether defence dependent on ( defence + defence divided by 3).

In conclusion, when going to Carushmesh, have a plan that would be similar to both fighting monsters in nimbao and fighting santaz and kabukis in pvp, or any dodgy dino/monster.
