Rights which can be distributed among the members are :

Can change members' options
Can change members' Title
Can accept and refuse new members
Can dismiss members
Can edit the public pages
Can edit the private pages
Can add pages
Can delete pages
Can declare wars
Can rebuild the castle
Can delete messages from the forum
Can remove defenders
Can build castle improvements
Can modify the Clan announcement
Can produce and transform mana

ingredients: you cant "get" ingredients... once you donate them, they are and stay in the treasury, until you spend them for spells, declarations or castlerepairs.

"chatting":
There is an internal forum, once you create your clan. Since the official drpg channel is situated on mibbit.com, which is browserbased ( so members can join the chat, even if they dont want to install an irc-app, you can still join with mirc or other apps), you can easily create another chat and create a link in your announcement.
