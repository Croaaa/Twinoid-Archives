Apprentice Fisher skill will give you Lujidanee Grouper in any fishing spots.
Skilled Fisher skill will give you Avenger-Fish in any fishing spots too.
And Master Fisher will give you one of the three rare fishes, depending on what map you're fishing on.
Though each square isn't a 100% catch. But the statistics are somehow something like that: 18 Groupers, 6 avengers, one rare. Those numbers could be more or less.
