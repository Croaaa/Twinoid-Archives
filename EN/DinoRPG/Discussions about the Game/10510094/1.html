The Strange Prowler (at the Forges) gives some Amnesic Rice as a reward for completing his first mission. That stuff makes your dinoz forget its name (as well as any experience earned since its last level-up), so you can rename it. Your Gorilloz might be able to do that mission, but it would be tough, and will take a fairly long time. You might find the Rice for sale at the Market, but it is usually kind of expensive. Other than that, you're stuck with the misnamed guy, unless you want to try and sell him
