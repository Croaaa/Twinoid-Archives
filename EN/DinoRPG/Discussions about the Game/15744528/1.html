Well... For teams you get more , and you usually lose less per fight.

Individual dinoz have an advantage when you have to fight against several normal monsters in missions. For example in Grandpa Joes mission "The hills smashrooms", it can be an advantage to split up groups to receive more opponents, even if you receive less and from the individual fights.

EDIT: maybe I'm remembering it wrong, but the loss shouldn't be too high. If you find that it is, you should wait with using that strategy until later levels and later missions.
