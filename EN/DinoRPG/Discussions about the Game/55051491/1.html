Ok, gonna make some rules, coz suggestions have limits with the new(ish) dev we got.

Don't create completely new stuff out of nowhere.
Be sensible when you give your ideas, they need to be within your previous DinoRPG experience, in terms of CotC/MW.

In the end, Dev may or may not use your proposals.

exampleLast time I analysed CotC worst starting time and medium match duration (way before they closed, so not necessarily the current results). As a grand general, starting time could be around 10:00AM to 02:00AM, and duration, something 5 hours.

As our server host a grand number of nationalities around the globe, a fixed starting time is the worst thing to do, it means only a few of us could participate on the current CotC edition, so I thought starting time could change by +1h, starting first match at 10:00 and last match be at 21:00 (or 22:00 server time, depending on the maximum number of matches).

Well I didn't thought I didn't have time to negotiate something more in sync with our server, due to kiroukou's leaving the week before CotC started, and new dev only available one day before CotC start (and I was myself in vacations without any computer access)...

As you can see, it's nothing new, it's within doable since different starting time have already been done before. Nothing revolutionary.
