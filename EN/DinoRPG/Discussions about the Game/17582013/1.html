Attracted by the big prizes and the top quality opposition, a solid 18 clans registered for the Pro League of the 3rd Championship of the Clans, meaning this would be the most hotly contested Pro League we've ever seen here on DinoRPG!

The Pro League had a lot of amazing fights. Masters had their favorites, some of which were The Last Order of Transsylvania, The Cookies, and Rakshasa.

However, the qualifying round was like the EuroCup, other clans that weren't favorites and clans which people never thought would do so well surprised everyone by showing their best at this stage.

Luso Brasilian Team was one of these clans. Some of the clans beaten by LBT were the wise and spirited Big Boys Club and the brave and experienced Void Alliance. They won every match in their group with GREAT strategy and activity! They set up rock-hard defensive lines, and countered every attempt!

Another clan who surprised everyone was Cult of Gorgoth, they demonstrated great strategic skills in this CoTC. However, they did not make it to the final round. BBC defeated them in one of the most amazing battles of this COTC to deny them a place in the finals...

In Pool 1 there were some good fights too. LOoT got a firm reign on their group after winning all their matches. GC vs Cookies was one of the most disputed matches in the first group, with dinoz' corpses discarded on both sides.

The whole community was expecting the bloodiest fights to come from 1st pool; however some clans prefered to forfeit quickly and save resources, making the 2nd pool more interesting to follow.

After the fierce qualifying rounds the survivors, Last Order of Transylvania, The Cookies, Big Boys Club, German Connection, Rakshasa, Luso-Brassilian Team, Void Alliance and Voodoo, made it to the finals.
Every fight was amazing, though Voodoo forfeited a bit too quick for the popcorn eatering enthusiasts, but then again - they were facing the all-mighty LOoT. The BBC-Rak was a fight to the death, giving the chills to the Big Boys until their last man, but Rak was stronger on the day, and won.

In the first round The Cookies finally managed to breach the LBT's diamond hard defense! Until this point LBT had won every battle.

The most interesting battle of this quarter-finals was the match between VA vs GC, the proud members of VA cleaning up GC's side. However GC didn't retreat and marched forward with a new line. After titanic fights, the Germans were the ones left standing over the carnage. They worked very hard for this victory, Void is a formiddable opponent!
Now we enter the semi finals, German Connection encountered the Cookies, those sweet cakes smashed their opponents off their plate, and they didn't leave any crumbs. The Cookie Jar was clearly untouched by German hands.

Two possible winners of the championship clashed in the other semi final in a giantic battle. LOOT took up an early lead, they begin to accumulate points upon points every hour. After a few failed attempts, Rakshasa eliminated the northern defensive lines of LOoT in an unstappable assault, but after a few hours, LOOT made another wawe of counter-attack which even the strongest members of the server couldn't stop, so LOOT went on to the final.

The long-await final to decide who would be crowned the 3rd CotC champion wasn't what we expected. Tired from all the fights, The Cookies prefered to set up a pleasant tea party and opened their jar for their opponents, allowing them to rest as well. It wasn't fear, but wisdom above all, they realized there was no chance to win or else they would have been processed into granola bars. Instead, the clans organized some private battles for fun.

That gave the Pro League Championship crown to the Last Order of Transylvania. Last Order of Transylvania were clearly the best clan in the CoTC from day 1.

Congrats to LOoT for winning the PRO League of the Championship of the Clans.



We want to congratulate all clans who gave their best on this CoTC too, specially to runners-up The Cookies and the other 6 finalist German Connection, Rakshasa, LBT, VA, Big Boys Club and Voodoo.
If you did not win what you wanted... Don't feel bad. Just keep giving your best and maybe you will be the champions next time.

Congratulations to Russia Dark Star, winners of Cotc 3-Amateur League!

This year's edition of the COTC´s amateur league surprised us in a lot of ways.

Among the unbelievable 54 participating clans were well known ones with familiar names, like R.O.o.T., Les Froggiez and Cloak & Dagger. Everyone aimed for the Napodinoz, and ultimately, for the win.

We could see a lot of exciting fights, thrilling situations, and unexpected outcomes. Not only did they manage to steal the show from some of the Pro League fights, they even proved that there are a lot of developments going on in most of these clans.


For those who already forgot, since it has been a while, the quarter finals were:

-Crimson Unicorn´s Forest vs Guerreros de Elite
-Clinicbuilders vs Hun Horda
-Russian Dark Star vs Dinorampage
-Les Froggiez vs R.O.o.T.

Every one of those clans got their hands on a well deserved napo and thus, shouldn't stay unmentioned.

In the end, none of those could stop RDS´ march to victory. Incredible activity, well skilled dinoz and sharp minded situation-dependent strategies lead to their success. Especially the latter was showcased during the final against CUF and secured their win on that trench-like map.

Use your 2 Napos wisely! Hopefully, they will help you to defend your title next time.

Honorable mention should go to Little Italy as well, who lost against CUF in the round of top 16. The former streak-7 "winners" of Cotc 2 put up an awesome fight, with just 12 active members, inspiring a lot of players with their fighting spirit and earned the community's respect. Some comments from master tamers:

Afreet: Frankly speaking, I would like to see Little Italy - RDS final. It's ridiculous that one of the strongest CotC clans didn't even get a napo

ZealMund: Little Italy was actually the phenomenon of this league I think. You did more than an awesome job

Ramza: Good job Italians! You've fought well!
