they work together . Question is : does it make sense ?
analyze and no mercy is tactically the same : you will always attack the weakest enemy , so turn off if you dont like to waste your 100+x first strike on a korgon , flamelet , or piranho Its nonetheless nice , to kill off a halfdead defender early in the attack , BUT as soon as there is some reinforcement like a korgon or clone , you will attack that one , unless you have conentration .

Strategy only applies the best element according to the enemeys defense .

so if water would be your next assault on a certain enemy , because you have 10 elements in it , but your lightning with 9 elements would do more damage : lightning is choosen before water .

concentration is a nice thing to have in pvp , to assure that your lightning dinoz wont scatter his damage , but kill off one dinoz once and for all . Just hope that he doesnt have acid blood or torch .

my thought : If you have a high ini glidwing or maybe rocky , with concentration and analyze ( because lot of poeple like to get specialist with lightning dinoz ) : let them on . Chances are high that he attacks before there is any korgon or clone on field , so he attacks the weakest one , is the fastest one by itsself , and sticks to him because of conentration , even if there are spawns coming after your first attack .
