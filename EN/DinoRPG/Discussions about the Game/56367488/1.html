You may have read it on the FR forums, but there's some kind of training method, which hasn't been judged as an exploit, but still excessive.

You can use it with your own free will (though you need lots of time to kill and rather have a big enthusiasm to not be bored doing so), I'm putting it under spoilers:

- Have finished Smog Quest
- Have a pair of two Dragon Hunters with Artemis, blocked at Lv70.
- The trained dinoz can be at any level, it will follow one of the 70s.
- At Bao, fight & hunt, until you finished the 15 grids per day limit.
(Maybe not only at Bao, perhaps it works also on Joe or Hills).
- Do it on Thursday and Saturday.
- If you do it everyday, stock your ingredients in Marketplace.

So since what's hidden here has been judged as excessive by dev, he said he will put a limit on ingredient storage and ingredient sales at the merchant.

As for ingredient storage, it could be the old tolerated bug of stocking above the maximum through the market, being fixed. As for the ingredients sales, it sounds as if it's not about prices.
