Any monsters you killed in the tombs will stay dead - regardless of how long you wait before you return.

If you find the Demon Scroll and leave the tombs, then you can visit the Demon shop. See the Shops page for more information.

You need to collect Demon tickets by sacrificing Dinoz to the shop. Once you have enough (minimum of 700) then you can buy one of the Demon available each day.
A Demon starts at level 10 - it is a level 1 Dinoz with 9 pre-chosen level ups, elements and skills already selected. You can view the skills the Demon has before buying - each Demon has a special Demon skill in addition to any racial skill (e.g. demon Winks has Shell and Hard-Boiled)
