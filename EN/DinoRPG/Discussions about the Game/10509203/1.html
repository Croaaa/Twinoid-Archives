Hi,

My opinions:

1. There isn't an ideal amount of dinoz to have, since that's a personal perspective, some players like to max their dinoz limit as soon as possible, others like to keep a few dinoz.
Since you're the "grow-attached" kind of player, 6 would be a good number to own at first, as mentioned by yourself. It all comes down to your personal liking really. Me, I like the current number of dinoz I got at the moment (12), not so many but also not a few either.
You might also plan on getting the now unreleased dinoz (Santaz and Kabuki) in the future, so it would be a good choice to hold some space for them. I don't believe you would be very pleased to freeze your dinoz. That's what I'm planning on doing, so I'm not going to max my dinoz limit anytime soon.
It's true that more dinoz = more income, but with less dinoz, you can reach higher levels and grow them stronger faster.

2. I really don't think it makes any difference. With more dinoz, you have more chances of getting high rewards (I've got 10k and 9k a few times with 3 dinoz groups). 5 or 3, it doesn't really matter.
With 5 dinoz tho, you're gonna have to use 5 irma potions to move the whole crew, so it really blows in the end. 3 would be better, if you ask me.
Since you feel like owning 6 dinoz, why not making 2 groups of 3 dinoz?

Bonus answer:
When your dinoz collects an ingredient, it goes to your ingredients list (you can access it through the menu to the right of your screen).
If you're in a clan, you can donate the ingredients you collected to increase the clan's treasury, to help the clan to improving and rebuilding the castle, declaring war, among other things.
Or you can either sell them to the itinerant merchant, a npc who wanders through the dinoz rpg world and set shop weekly at a random fixed spot, or sell the ingredients at the Tamer Market, where other players can buy them.

You can move the group normally once each day, just like you move a single dinoz. But if you wanna move them again, instead of 1 irma potion, you'll need the number of irma potions corresponding to the number of dinoz in that group.

With 6 dinoz, you could also make 3 groups of 2, check this thread for more details: http://en.dinorpg.com/forum/thread/34127
