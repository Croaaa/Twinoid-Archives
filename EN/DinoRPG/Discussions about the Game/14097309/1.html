You do not have to choose between the 2.

Get dinoz, and train them until ~lev20. Do the elemental master missions.

After that, decide for each dino if you want to train it further, or sacrifice it.

For the ones you want to keep, wait until you have 3 you want to train further (freeze 1 or 2 if necesarry), and keep training them.
Sacrifice the rest.
