When you say "dark" dino do you mean the ones with blueish hue? If so those are not dark dino's. They're dino's frozen at the deep gorge. They can have many frozen dino's as they don't count towards the dino limit per account. But if the player/master already has 21 dino's then they cannot defrost one unless they freeze one in it's place since the limit for any able fighting dino is 21 (with the skill leader). Which I hope answered your question for the limit of 21 dino's.

Now for demon dino's you would need to jump through some hoops... First you need a dino capable of surviving the catacombs (where you'll find the demon note). With that AND 50 demon notes (you receive demon notes by sacrificing a dino)you can SEE the demon dino's in the demon shop. In order to buy a demon dino you would need to sacrifice A LOT of dino's. For example a level 30 regular dino only gets you 120 demon notes. The "cheapest" demon dino is 700 demon notes. You can do the math here ...

The 4 races of demon dino's are: wanwan, softpig, winks, and a gorilloz. If you'd like to see a wanwan you can check my account.
