Hello everyone!

Since the next FR Gazette edition is incoming, you're welcomed to ask questions to our developers. Please use this kind of format:

Question number X (or QX): **Short and precise question in bold**

//Additional commentaries in italics.//

If you're agreeing with anything posted, quote only the questions (and not the arguments) and add a +1 underneath. If you're disagreeing, you can still add a -1 and argue.

Then at some point, we (FR mods) will choose the best of them out of FR, EN and ES questions, and submit them to devs through a Gazette article.

Anything related to a suggestion won't be selected.
Anything already answered or previously void of answers won't be selected. It's mainly due to the "test it yourself first" MT policy.
Anything "I want", "Give me" or anything like those statements won't be selected either.
Anything related to game mechanics formula or calculation won't be selected too.
