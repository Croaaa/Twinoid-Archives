Looking at your account profile, you could do the following:

fountain pearl quest will be very helpful to save up some free each day at FOY.
You have room for 9 more dinoz, 12 once you get the leader U skill. Good to see that you are getting U skill dinoz early so you could now consider what sort of team you want to build. It is essential to max that limit early so right now you'll be able to double your daily earnings.
acquire access to the grumhel forest to be able to do the Element Master's missions once you have done the soft shaman's missions.
Unlock clinic quest and then join a clan that has built it (pretty much all of the active clans have done it), so you don't have to build the clinic all by yourself.
Gain access to steppes. You may need to join a clan to make this process easier for you if you prefer. Otherwise you need 7 dinoz to gain access to dark world to get the key to unlock the blacksylva door.
and of course, keep doing all the missions that provide good and .
Do not grind your dinoz yet until you have finally decided on your personal combat team.

There's a lot to do when you're new to the game. Once you've done all the main quests and missions, it's mainly events and dojo to further strengthen yourself by getting napos which is a rare unique currency which the magic shop only accepts if you wish to buy magic items.

Have fun playing DinoRPG.

How do you define 'tons of cash'? A well-developed account produces at least 10-12000 gold a day just for good measures. You have only 9 dinoz (the limit of active dinoz is 24, plus the ones you freeze), and even the highest level is only lvl16. The level limit is currently 70, and it will go up to 80.

Even if you only play pve (though I strongly recommend to join a clan, this game is 1000 times more fun that way), you've been only in the 3 basic regions: dinoland, the vulcano and the island. There is a forest, a desert, a flying island, a very distant mountain, a monster island, the dark world, the monster levels on these varies between lvl 20 and 50. Above level 50 you can find dark dinoz in varius places with lvls of 50-70.
The higher level dinoz are exponentially stronger, you can even have 750 hp dinoz (around 3-400 is the normal these days for fighters)!

You can't even buy all dinoz yet, there is 20 different races currently (some of them you can only win via lottery, pvp dojo events, specific high-level quests etc), with 7 demon variations, almost every one of them have a rare variation too.

I haven't written these to lecture you, just to give a sight how much can you still evolve in this game from your current state
