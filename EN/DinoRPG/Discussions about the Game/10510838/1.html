When you unlock, you unlock everything that is eligable to be unlocked in that element. All paths and all levels.

So let's say you picked up Celerity and unlocked, then took Double Hit, then backtracked and picked up Intelligence. If you now unlocked you would unlock the two level 2 skills opened up by intelligence, plus the two level 3 skills unlocked by Double Hit.
