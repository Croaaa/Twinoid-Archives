[ http://twinoid.com/en/article/4391/international-monster-hunt-beg[...] ]

An international monster hunt will begin today the 22nd of August at 15h00 and will end on August 29th at 15h00

Every dinoz master throughout the world will take part in this hunt should they wish to and the top hunters will claim the finest rewards and the greatest honours ever bestowed upon citizens of Dinoland. Over and above the English server rankings, the top 3 hunters from all servers combined will earn all new gold, silver and bronze medals!

The Hunt is reserved for the elite. It will be contested in the Dark World, Grumhel Forest, the Steppes, Nimbao and Caushemesh!


The Top Hunters will earn:

1st place: 1 Etherwasp Egg + Epic Reward + 50 Treasury Notes
2nd - 5th: 1 Feross Egg + 30 Treasury Notes
6th - 10th: 1 Kabuki Egg + 20 Treasury Notes
11th - 20th: 5 Tac Tickets
21st - 50th: 50 Treasury Notes
51st - 100th: 25 Treasury Notes


And that's not all!

Every time you break into a new level in the personal rankings, you will receive:

500 points: 5 Irma's Potions
1000 points: 2 Hot Breads
1500 points: 2 Hot Breads + 3 Angel Potions
2000 points: 1 Pack of Nimbao objects
3000 points: 2 Tac Tickets
4000 points: 200 Demon Tickets
5000 points: 100 Treasury Notes
6000 points: 1 Gold Napodino

Will ANYONE beat the score of the mighty Pyxel who scored 89740 last time around?
