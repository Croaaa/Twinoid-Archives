Bengeldorn

Copy the url of the link behind the icon and paste it into bookmark.

The syntax of the link is like this:
en.dinorpg.com/dino/<DINO-ID>/inventory/24884/use?sk=<SESSION-ID>

<DINO-ID> must be equal to the ID of one of your Dinoz, and <SESSION-ID> changes each time you log on.

Once you have the bookmark, you can click on it several times, each time you cklicked it, you change 1 to 1000 .

Note: Remember that the bookmark only works for the current session!
