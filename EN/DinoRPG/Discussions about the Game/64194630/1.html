Since it allready has Flood, you might consider to go for Battle Cry in ether,
but don't forget to turn off Hypnosis, because if Battle Cry triggers, it will trigger as the next attack - not very usefull in a Dojo Setting.

The obvious choices are Burning Fever, Phoenix Fether for fire.
In water I personally would try to go for Marine Vitality and avoid the lower path, but going for Cleptomaniac might also be an Option.

If you want to go the safe route in Wood, you could go for Life Source, going for Force Control is a Little more risky, but possible since it allready has Career Plan. Getting both will be hard, and you might also go for River of Life instead.

I was somehow lucky enough to get one of my Quetzus to Force Control, which I kept for rebirth and was then able to get River of Life.

I don't have any good suggestions for Lighting, except Maybe getting Hazard before switching to ether skills.
