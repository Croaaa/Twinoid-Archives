Loxagon

You can't change the name in Dinofight, but its possible to change the name of a dino here in DinoRPG by the use of an item called "Amnesiac Rice ". This item you can get on a later mission.

You will have all missions available for your transfered dino in RPG.

Best level to transfer depends. If you are a starter and want to grow as quick as possible you might want to transfer as soon as possible to get more gold per day through fighting. Minimum level to transfer is 5.
Reasons to transfer later are: you will level the dino faster in Dinofight than in DinoRPG (without Irmas). You can chose 1 of 3 dinos to transfer and pick the one you like best.

For some more info on Dinofight you can use this dinofight guide.
