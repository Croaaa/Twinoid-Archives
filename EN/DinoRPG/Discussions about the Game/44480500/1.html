They exist. It is almost undeniable at this point. Although, i hope to demonstrate that a few minor tweaks could change those frustratingly useless skills into new, interesting, potential strategic decisions for players.

I'd like to kick things off with Armor, the most notoriously useless stat in Dinorpg.
I think that the way to fix Armor is already in the game, in the form of the Feross passive, as in a way to completely negate damage with certain odds. Simply put, i think that replacing armor with a new stat, called blocking, that does just that, is the way to go. But hang on a second Nandato that would be unfair, surely more powerful asaults need to be harder to block!
Indeed! Well i beleive i have a simple fix for that too: Blocking could work as follows: Lets say Dinoz A, that has 40% blocking is being attacked by dinoz B. If Dinoz B is about to deal 23 damage to dinoz A after applying defence, then, dinoz A has a 40-23 =17% chance to block that attack. Of course this would only work on assaults, not ranged attacks.

So here is what i would personally do :

Carapace : 5% Blocking
Faroe Heritage : 5% Blocking
Basalt armor : 10% Blocking
Cuirass : 10% Blocking
Armor plated shell : 15 % Blocking
Hermetic aura : Temporary 5% Blocking

Those skills would stack multiplicatively for a maximum of 60% blocking on a Feross with all these skills with hermetic aura on. I think that the feross needs some love anyway, and this would add to its "main tank " feel. I for one beleive that this is almost balanced, because only the Feross can reach high blocking.

I want to give Gel some love. I mean yeah, water is a great element and all, but i think it would be awesome to introduce a synergy between Marsh and Gel, instead of having those skills conflict and waste energy. I have always been frustrated with the fact that reapplying an effect such as slowness was a waste. Since water is basically the element that focuses the most on crowd control, here is my suggestion, bold as it may be, I dare say it will feel like an exciting buff for most masters .

Gel -> renamed to Frost
Inflicts 5 water damage and slows an opponent. If the opponent is already slowed or petrified, the skill turns into
Permafrost (as in the actual in-fight skill anouncementwould be permafrost) : inflicts neutral damage equal to 9 water damage.

This means this damage goes through bubble, which i think is great for several reasons:
-It makes sense since ice is solid.
-It rewards self control users who can't get slowed or petrified
-It adds the strategic aspect of water, replacing conflicts with synergies, overall making more sense.

Combustion : it is a nearly useless skill at the moment, here's an idea to revamp it : Combustion reaplies its damage everytime fire damage is apllied up to three times (idea of fire "digging" into the ennemies)

Benediction : same thing: useless skill at the moment. Here's an idea: Changing the Blessed Status, since it's a benediction, it should be able to smite ennemies and heal the allies. So the buff would be to sometimes (rarely) heal by 10 hp and sometimes (rarely) smite an attacking ennemy, dealing 10 true damage to dinoz and 100 true damage to monsters. And just to make sense, it could also remove infernal talons's dark flames.

Hospital porter is an unsatisfying skill: make it able to revive dead allies. This would add to the idea of an actual rescue dinoz . Yes, this would be a very valuable skill for caushemesh upping, but it is an investment of ups for convenience.

Achilles heel , Diamond fangs , Warlord (and possibly Strength and Fury and Martial Arts, although they are tolerable) are all useless skills: with the advent of Force Control, and even with Berserk, these skills are not useful at all, whereas they could be strategic options worth exploring.

Here is my idea for Warlord : it would be a passive that only activates on the dinoz with highest Fire element in the team, and it would be a permanent fire based " focus " or " plunging attack " depending on what is more balanced, on all assaults, but using that dinoz's fire element.
So basically one dinoz empowers all the others including itself with fire 1 or fire 2 on all assaults.

Here is my idea for Achilles heel : deals 3 extra TRUE damage on each assault, but 10% of the time, when receiving an assault, receives 10 extra TRUE damage. This would make the skill's name make a little more sense in my opinion, and it would synergyze with Vandetta .

Diamond fangs : Deals one extra true damage on all assaults.

The way i picture this extra damage is that it applies after the initial damage. Like for example, dinoz A attacks dinoz B, deals 15 damage, and after that dinoz B receives 1 extra damage (without the attack animation occuring again)

Perhaps Tireless could reduce energy recovery and energy debuffs, But i'm really not sure about that one. I don't know whether it would just make the etherwasp a more complete "energy master" type of dinoz, or just make all wood dinoz even more OP, especially Castis.


These are just some Ideas I'm throwing out there for fun. I know I'm not an extremely experienced player, but i hope these suggestions can be of use. I know there are some skills i did not mention, I just had nothing to say about them.
