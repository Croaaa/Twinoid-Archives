This was more like a question than a decision.

Basically there's 3-4 ways to change the starting time.

  • Either stretch the pause, by adding 2 to 10 hours, the starting time will move forward on the time line.
    • if 1st starts at 18:00, if added 2h, 2nd will be at 20:00, 3rd at 22:00 [...], Finals will be at 14:00/16:00.
  • Stretch the pause further, by adding 14 to 22 hours, the starting time will move backward.
    • if 1st starts at 18:00, if added 22h, 2nd will be at 16:00, 3rd at 14:00 [...], Finals will be at 22:00/20:00
    • Stretching further would cause the event duration to be way longer than usual, so on this aspect, maybe shortening the pause could also be fine, but that depends on you.
  • There's also the choice to add 12h too.
    Last time we had 6h match, 66h pause, and +1h. We began at 15:00 and finished the Elite final at 2:00.
