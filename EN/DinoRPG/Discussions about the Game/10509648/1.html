I will not specify 100%, but I'm going to explain so that you understand:

If one Dinoz of greater element is fire element, attack other Dinoz of wood, the damage will be multiplied by two. If this Dinoz attack other Dinoz of water, the damage will be multiplied by 1.5 (or something like that, I do not know the exact measurements but it is something so). If this Dinoz attack other Dinoz of fire, the damage will be multiplied by 1, ie, the Dinoz that attack cause the damage normal. If the fire Dinoz attack to one air Dinoz, the damage is multiplied by 0'3 (less cash) and if this Dinoz attack to one Dinoz of ligthning, the damage multiplied for 0.5 (by course is not the exact xD).
