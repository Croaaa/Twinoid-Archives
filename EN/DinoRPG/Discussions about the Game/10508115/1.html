For those of us who have been playing the French version and wondered about multi-account rules in regards to playing both the French and English versions of DinoRPG. There is no conflict since the two versions are not connected. We can play both versions, one account on each.

It's possible to play and have an account on both game without breaking the multi-accounts rules as you are free to play in french on the french version and in english on the english one and as having an account on both game version will not give a special advantage that normal player on one version will not have.
