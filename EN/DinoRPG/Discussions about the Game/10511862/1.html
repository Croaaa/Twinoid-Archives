As you already know, CotC qualifying round will be finished when all groups have done its 6th match, and final phases will begin directly after the same pause you got between each matches.

As it's already written, the top 4 of each group will be qualified, and be in final phases, with knock out matches.

This is an example if your league have 8 groups. It's following some charts, so you can also know which will be your next opponent (by guessing the winner though) in the future.

- 1st Group 1 vs. 3rd Group 2,
- 2nd Group 3 vs. 4th Group 4,
- 1st Group 5 vs. 3rd Group 6,
- 2nd Group 7 vs. 4th Group 8,
- 1st Group 2 vs. 3rd Group 3,
- 2nd Group 4 vs. 4th Group 5,
- 1st Group 6 vs. 3rd Group 7,
- 2nd Group 8 vs. 4th Group 1,
- 1st Group 3 vs. 3rd Group 4,
- 2nd Group 5 vs. 4th Group 6,
- 1st Group 7 vs. 3rd Group 8,
- 2nd Group 1 vs. 4th Group 2,
- 1st Group 4 vs. 3rd Group 5,
- 2nd Group 6 vs. 4th Group 7,
