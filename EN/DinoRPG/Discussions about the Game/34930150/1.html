Another massive IDT is over and the dust is beginning to settle. As ever, one dinoz master is left standing. One of the most modest, sporting and undeniably talented masters playing right now. Dominating the dojo with a castivore and hippoclamp would have been thought impossible not so long ago... but this master put a team together which did just that. Ladies and gentlemen, I present your IDT37 Champion, ANSKAR!

"First of all, thank you all for nice feedback and support, I really appreciate it. And thanks to all those I fought for nice matches. I really feel my team was quite mediocre: hard hitters with no Perception, multi hitters against enemies with lots of passive damage, only two bubbles, no Primal State, Marsh, Leafy, etc. I relied heavily on 50+ skills and invocations: I brought on the glidwings and cloud purely for their invocations, the casti is all about 50+ skills, and the hippo needs reincarnation and 69 level ups to get some element points

After 50+ skills came, it is all about getting the good skills after L50. If you want good dinoz, you need to get the good 50+ skills. My casti has all the nice wood skills: damage, hp, init and passive. My hippo has Burning Fever and last level up could be Force Control (yeah, right ). My cloud, glid and quetzu hasn't got any skills over tier 11 in the new tree. So my win was pretty much an effort by the casti and hippo, supported by the invos of the cloud and glid. Quetzu mostly just burned.

I really haven't got any good advice for building dinoz, because I just do what I think is fun; one of the hippos I got Acupuncture instead of a superior skill because I liked the sparkling things, my casti got Ka-Sher-Beam and I'm always surprised when I level up my cloud and find out that he doesn't have Career Plan And, of course, i build hippos because I think they're fun Next IDT I'll bring in my Overload, Burning Fever, Force Control, Bubble hippo for you all to play with "

Good insight from our latest champion there, thanks Anskar!

Qualification is underway for the finals of IDT38, will you be there? Good Luck dinoz master!
