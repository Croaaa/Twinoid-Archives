Ramza
Here's mine entry for the Haloween contest. It took a while to write, but I think it came out quite good in the end.

Have fun



Some time ago in a green land there was this mysterious valley
With unique culture, people and animals, some angry, some smiling.
The fearsome dinoz roamed the hills, swam in the seas and flew in the skies.
All kinds of species, colours and shapes. I couldn't believe my own eyes!
Most people were scared to death. They were no slayers or busters,
But still some stood firm - known all over the land as Dinoz Masters!
Their pure bravery, strength and skills allowed them to tame the beasts.
They made them obedient, often with love, but sometimes also with fists.

Life of all in Dinoland for a long time was peaceful and steady.
Nobody knew what was coming. Nobody foresaw the disastrous tragedy.
It was the 31st of October, a special day - All Hallows Eve.
This day the dead were returning to haunt them, that's what they believed.
Masters knew what to do. It was no use to pray.
They dressed up as monsters to scare the hollows away.
But the trick which had worked for so many years before,
Has failed them this time. All thought they were doomed for sure.

Some strongest dinoz of the best masters were taken over, lost their sanity,
They were stripped of their pride and injected with fury, dark heart and vanity.
People called them demons and believe me, the name was justified.
All hell broke loose. Screams, gore and fire. Many fine masters died.
The preachers spoke of the old legend and ancient monsters which came to life.
A punishment from gods for mankind's sins, wars, lies, mischief and strife.

Best known names in Dinoland picked up their swords to fight.
They had a firm resolve to crush the enemy with all their might.
Mr Bao Bob with his water dinoz, Soft Shaman with fire, charms and spells,
Forest Warden with guardians, Anna with healing water from Fountain's wells.
Many others as well, the list was impressive and long.
They picked up the battle field and prepared to stop the demons throng.
They stood with strong resolve to win, holding bare swords and knives,
But their regiments were shattered and both masters and dinoz ran for their lives.

It was the biggest battle ever in all Dinoland history,
Unfortunately lost... oh how much they all needed that one, single victory.
When the dust settled and the setting sun bathed the battle field in red,
The skies have cried over the fallen, rivers of tears were shed.
The land went into chaos and the soil was soaked with blood and tears
There was only one, last, tiny beacon of hope for the upcoming years.
A hundred-year-legend said that this time of despair would come,
But the world will be saved by one man and his dino, the chosen one.

And there he was, injured, trying to grasp his breath,
Searching for place to hide, hoping to survive and fool the Death.
He's left the battle as last, fighting until the end,
And now he was wondering how will his last moments be spend.
With last ounce of strength he's reached a strange forest hut.
He went inside knowing that this was the end for this young, little brat.
He didn't care if there was a friend inside, or a foe,
But believe it or not, he met there an old man - Grandpa Joe.
The man was sitting by the table, studying some ancient scrolls.
"Ah, the young dinoz Master, the man of many skills and flaws.
I have a task for you, boy. You are the one, I'm sure it's true.
You'll bring them down to their knees, here's what you need to do..."
And Grandpa Joe told him with details what had to be done.
There was only one thing which could stop the biggest army under this sun.
Only one chance, and to lose... he couldn't afford.
His mission was to slay the army's leader - Great Demon Lord.
"I'm seriously injured, impossible, that can not be done."
But Grandpa Joe healed him and asked to trust his brilliant plan.

The following morning the young one left, leaving his dinoz behind,
Except a cute, little wanwan, dark with pink socks, would you mind.
And he went like nothing into the very own middle of demons nest,
With his single, little dino and a mysterious sack on his chest.
Grandpa's advice was simple: demons are vain, use it if you can.
So he shouted out loud: "Come out Great Demon Lord! Be a man!
I'm here to stop you and your minions, if you will!
I'm sure you are bored and have some time to kill!
I'm willing to bet that this little wanwan I have with me,
Will crush your best fighter, don't you want to see?"
The land has shaken, the ground has breken and the air became so tense,
The Great Lord appeared from nowhere, saying "Boy, you must be dense!
That little cat? Beat my strongest beast? You have a death-wish, or what?"
"I came to teach you a lesson, Lord, the one that needs to be taught.
Let's make a bet. If I win the fight you will leave this land forever.
If I lose we will stop fighting and become your slaves. Be clever."
"First I thought you're brave, now I think you're just stupid, boy.
I like seeing people die. I'll accept the bet with joy!"
And they shook hands, the Great Demon Lord and the Chosen one,
A nasty Wanwan demon was summoned and a fight started in the last rays of the sun.

The difference in strength and skills was huge, the small cat was retreating,
But the young one knew beforehand that he was going to get the beating,
So he reached into the sack given to him by Grandpa Joe,
And took out the spheres which were supposed to change the battle flow.
At least this is what grandpa said, giving him the whole set.
"This, my boy, is the triumph card that will allow you to win that bet!
There are many mysterious forces in this universe to be feared,
But nothing can compare to a dino which is fully sphered!"
And the words spoke the truth, as they changed the tides of war.
The Great Demon Lord has never seen such tremendous power before.
Flood after flood, stumps, sinuous lightnings and detonations stack,
The demon wanwan was loosing badly, he's received a really good whack.
Soon the demon cat was dead but the Lord was still smirking.
"Demons don't keep their words, boy. What were you thinking?!"
"Maybe they don't, but your words have spoken the truth.
You've sealed the deal by shaking my hand with this magic Dorogon's Tooth.
It's a simple charm mostly used for stupid children's games,
But it's powerful enough to enforce the rules of our bet and my claims."
The Lord got pale like a vampire and then red like flames of fury.
He jumped towards the boy with intention to kill, but suddenly got blurry.
An enormous portal appeared, right behind his back,
And started to suck every demon around, each of them disappearing with a crack.
The Great Demon Lord struggled, he gave it his best,
But as all other demons he too was transferred by the portal to his demon's nest.

The Dinoland was free and the mission was finally done,
Thanks to all brave warriors, Grandpa Joe and the chosen one.
To celebrate the victory, as all of you know,
Each year now in Dinoland there is the Great Fireworks Show.

Remember the fallen warriors, admire the brave,
The great Dinoz Masters are here for you, to serve!
