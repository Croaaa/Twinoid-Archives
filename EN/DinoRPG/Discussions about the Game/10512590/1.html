Uh, ok? Then do that. You seem to know the basics already, building a U-skill dino is not exactly rocket science. Your only question here is whether to pick Career Plan or not to ward off unlucky element rolls later on, this of course comes with a disadvantage of probably getting the U-skills later on, especially if you plan on getting both Lightning skills on the same dino. Main weakness of a Lightning U-skiller is that you will miss all the good skills the Element has to offer if you do a straight rush, making training the dino bit of a pain, especially if you're not buying gold.

As for your next dino I would pick Gorilla for Leader (and Engineer), it's never too early to aim for the pivotal U-skills.
