Dark Dinos are generally very tough.
Since you don't have a high Ini-dino (a dino with River of Life), the best you can do is, to have dinos with as much Ini as possible, that have a good main attack.
In addition the magic item curse locker, will reduce the first 3 hits of one of those dark dinos.

Also watch you fights and take a look on which A-/E-Skills don't do much damage and turn them off.
