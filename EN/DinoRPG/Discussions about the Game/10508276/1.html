I am in the BrutForce tournament, and realizing how usefull those items for a long battle. For regular battle, don't even bother. However for big battle and long tournament battle, they are excellent.

Forge shop items:
1. Ice shield - fire protection
2. helmet - armor
3. lighter - this is cool... lights up the dino so when attacked the attacker will get fire damage (just like counter attack). Last very long too, perfect for tournament battle.
4. small pepper - add damage to next attack
5. rescue flamelet - flamelet reinforcement.

If you are in the tournament I would recommended to use the lighter. Burn baby burn...
