Well actually I decided it is time to make another info tab for our clan and I want to collect all possible info on clones and also post some questions that we have.

Clone is summoned with 1 hp unless holder has tear equipped.
It is reported Firebrand affect clones.
There are controversial reports on if the scale can hit clone or not.

It is believed P-skills are inherited by clones and S-Skills do not inherited by clones.

P-skills that are inherited by clones for sure:
- Multihits: Double hit
- Evasion: Dodge; Love Hertz (checked on rocky without dodge)
- Countering: Vengeance and waikikido (only if clone not killed by attack so if it has dodged ro has mroe than 1 hp due to tear).
- Other: Force control, Tenacity, Jump, Perception

What can hit clone:
- Hypno
- BH
- any A and E skill

What can't hit a clone:
- Scale (most probably)

But there are some P-skills that reported as not inherited by clones:

1. Double P-skills: Vendetta and Overload reported as not working on clones. At the same time some master claim that overload do work. We could try to add this question to list of questions to the admin, unless somebody has overload clones dinoz without double hit

2. Increasing energy ether P-skills (Muscular Oxygenation, Dinoz Proteine, etc).

S-skills that do not inherited by clones for sure:
- Specialist
- Torch
- Self Control
- Life breath
- Accupuncture

Is there any known to you S-skill that is inherited by clones?
Is there additional P-skills that do not affect clones?
Can sylphs hit clone as BH?
Does burning fever affect clones?
