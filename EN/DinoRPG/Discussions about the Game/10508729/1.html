As far as I know, it is unnecessary to move/fight to start the timer for the next turn. It starts as soon as you press the 'New Action' button, but it is hidden until one of your Dinoz moves/fights, and then it shows up for that Dinoz (and any Dinoz thereafter after they move/fight).

To verify this, on your next turn, press 'New Action' and wait 15 minutes before moving or fighting, then see if the timer starts at 22h 59m or 22h 44m once you do (assuming you're at the 23 hour timer).

The main reason for no autoreset is so players can decide for themselves when their reset will be:

- You can play more at the beginning and so enjoy this time.
