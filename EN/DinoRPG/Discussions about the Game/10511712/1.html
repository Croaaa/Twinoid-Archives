U-skills comes from U like Unique . They are some kind of specials addin' Your account few boosts. They are:
Leader - additional +3 to maximum of active dinoz,
Engineer - additional slot to equip Your dinoz with an item,
Cooker - +10% when healin' Your dinoz with any addin' item,
Storekeeper - +50% to maximum storage in inventory,
Professor - +5% ,
Priest - +1/hour for restin' dinoz,
Merchant - -10% for items in Dinoland Flying Shop.

Once You get one of them on any dinoz its bonus will work for every dinoz on Your account. Believe me, they are important and, in my opinion, at least Leader and both skills are a "must have". You can't buy rocky so that's why i suggested glidwings .

If You care for pretty dinoz then wait with one meant to get U-skill/-s. If You care for good account go for monkey + bat ^^.
