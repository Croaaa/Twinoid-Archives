You'll need the Blacksylva Key first. How?
- Bring 7 Dinoz to Mr Bao Bob's Place (from yourself and/or clanmates)
- Talk to Bao and Focus, once 7 are focussed, enter the portal
- Defeat the Tower Guardian in the Dark World
- You'll be teleported back to the Sticky Swamp.

All Dinoz that have defeated the Tower Guardian will have the Blacksylva Key.

Go to Blacksylva Door and enter the Steppes.

can i just move any 7 dinos there or do all 7 dinos have any special requirements or prerequisites? are they the same prerequisites for all 7 or does 1 dino need anything special/different?
The prerequisite for the Dinoz to focus is that they have to have the Korgon Fins status item.

If they have the Fins then Bao Bob believes that the Dinoz might survive there.... just...
If not, he will not assist that Dinoz to focus on the portal.

You need seven Dinoz from anyone in the same Clan to be focusing to open the portal. The first seven go through so you need to plan the focusing with your Clanmates or you may not get your group through as you expect.
