They just updated the whole turn system yesterday, look at the new threads and the message in the news.

Each level takes an extra hour per level.

Level 1 = 1 turn per hour
Level 6 = 1 turn per 6 hours

all the way up to a max of 20 hours per turn. But you must click the new turn button before the time for your next new turn will start.

By the way before you only got a turn once every 23 hours, so they did just make it faster
