You have one dinoz almost ready to enter. The one with the glove must talk nicely to the monk at mutant falls. If you are nice to him he will give you the water lilly pad that allows you to enter the forest from there.

Oh, this glove and pad will be needed by each and every dinoz that wants to enter. If you are grouped and the one with the pad is not the leader you will not be able to enter.

If the dinoz is the leader you will see the entrance appear, but if you move forward only the glove and pad carriers will enter and the rest of the group left behind at Mutant Falls.
