Of course, most of this discussion is about 'cheating or abusing the rules'. If you want to change the rules, think properly about the changes you are suggesting.

Whether a clan is an ally or a 'wing' of another clan does not make any difference - they both have their members and separate turns. Does the abuse come in moving members between them? Don't block the active member moving to an active clan when their clan is not performing well. Don't stop a popular clan having a wing, then moving all the active members into the top clan as a reward. Perhaps stop ALL clan movements in the last week / 10 days.

Don't ban new clan creation during a war - all the new players who want a test of the war side of the game would be denied this and put off playing. Perhaps change the initial ranking score, so that it gets less as you get nearer to the end of the war (so that a clan losing reputation because of a destroyed castle will have zero at the end of the war).

Whatever the change proposed, we need to make sure the ways the rules can be abused are considered, and the ways in which it removes fun or challenge. Don't destroy Dino RPG
