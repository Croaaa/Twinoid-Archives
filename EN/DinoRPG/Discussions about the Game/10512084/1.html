slyfox
I personally think you are a bit premature on the sacrifice thing, and you cannot sacrifice them yet anyways. First you need the demon scroll, which means you need dinoz that can manage the katacombs (maps found http://en.dinorpg.com/clan/125#?page=347 6 ). I am sure you can figure out how to expand that map and read it. I did it with three dinoz leveled about 28 and they died once. I re-healed them and tried again, and made it through. Word of caution, you cannot heal your dinoz while they are in the Katacombs, but if you die you only start at the beginning (you do not have to fight opponents you have already defeated until you get to the point in the Katacombs where you initially died).

Good news is that items like battle rations and goblin marquez can be equipped on your dinoz...so basically keep a healthy supply at all times. I would recommend getting the spheres as already mentioned and trying to get your dinoz up to a slightly higher level before doing Katacombs and before sacrificing. I would like to mention that, to get to the Katacombs you must have 10 or below while at Frish Port and move to the twilight cemetery.

Once your ready to sacrifice the important thing to remember is that they all must be taken to the twilight cemetery, so be prepare to move them from the frish port with 10 or less. The next thing to remember is never sacrifice a dinoz with a U skill unless another dinoz has that same skill (IE: merchant). These skills are so valuable that most people will level dinoz JUST to get these skills. Next I would only sacrifice after a dinoz has recently leveled. If one is close to leveling up, then level them first. Finally, if your team is too big you can always freeze dinoz instead of sacrificing them. This way you can continue playing until you do have a team skilled enough to play the Katacombs. Then you simply unfreeze your dinoz and sacrifice them. (Freeze them past the tunnel under the breath)
