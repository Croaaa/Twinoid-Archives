Recently a change occurred on this potions: before you could put different potions on your dinoz without a problem, but now they cancel each other out. You use an Ember for example, and if you have a St Elma in another dino, he can use it before Ember ends (it can be even on the turn right after).

I'm posting this, because first it looks like a bug (the name of the second potion doesn't appear, the background simply changes and the turn of the dino with the potion goes after), and second, because I don't think this change was needed, this potions are expensive and to use them only for a turn removes strategy from the fights.

So, I'm posting in hope more people agree and maybe we can ask the admin to speak with the devs to change it back.
