yepp probably. These transfers have been declared more or less as legit, some years ago. Some members help out others in their clan after events, or for whatever reasons. Major reasons are
a) members who didnt contribute as much as others during an event often make gifts to players in their clan who spent more, to say "thanks"
b) sometime, deals are made between 2 people, to sell something below minimum prize. Lets say a dino has a minimum prize 550, but the seller wants to give him away for 200. The buyer buys him for 550, places a rice on the market and the one who sold the dinoz buys the rice for 300, so in sum, the dino was sold for 250.

Its still good to keep an eye out for some more suspsicious transactions, like a master with no clan paying 250 for a single rice, those bids could be multiaccountrelated. Pretty hard to get 250 notes without a clan though Still possible.
