You started with a 16000 Dinoz, so you have an advantage of quite a few turns over others. If your second is also 16000, you need to earn 12000 to get enough.

If you're doing the missions, you get 3000 more in rewards (assuming you didn't get to the last 2 Grandpa Joe's missions, or leave the starting area).

So, 9000 might be 36 turns. To convert that to time, subtract the 10 free Irma's Potions and the 2 University lessons, all of which can be done between turns. Add 2 full days rest to be safe.

Old system > 1 turn a day > 26 days.

New system > slowing turns > just under 8 days.

By that time turns are almost half a day each, so it could be less. Bigger prizes could knock off a day. Since the fight prizes are random, you can't be sure, but "a week" seems a good estimate.
