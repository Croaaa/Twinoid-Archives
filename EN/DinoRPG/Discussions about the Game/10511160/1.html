Defences on all the elements are calcualted depending on what you have in elements.

I.e 1 wood element gives
1 defence

I believe thats the right way round, but anyway, one defence figure is not affected if you change another defence figure. I.e if you took the skill,
Elasticity
It increase's Air defence by 3. This would only affect Air defence, no other defence is affected by any change in other defences.

As such this non-interaction means when you use fury or tireless the results are very simple. The change is singular.

Fury
-2
-2
-2

-2
-2

Tireless
+2
+2
+2
+2
+2
+2

The only additional defence manipulation involved in taking the skill is that you will get the defence points you normally gain from leveling up because you have +1 on an element.
