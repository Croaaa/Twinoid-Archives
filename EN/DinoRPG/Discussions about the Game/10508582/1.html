[None]
Angel Potion restores the Dinoz to life, and no more. That means it will be at one (1) HP.

Resting will not get you past 50% of the Dinoz's total HP. Resting will stop when the Dinoz reaches that point, and you can only rest when below that.

Resting will not stop when it is time for a new turn. You can rest through several turns, depending on the length of the turns, the HP needed, and the speed of healing.

You can stop resting any time you like.

You are probably referring to mention of resting strategy with multiple Dinoz. That would go like:
A turn begins.
Dinoz 1 moves.
Dinoz 2 is injured, so rests.
After some time, a new turn is available.
Dinoz 1 moves again.
Dinoz 2 continues resting from the previous turn.
More time passes, and you are about to start yet another turn.
Dinoz 2 stops resting, and can still take the second turn that isn't over yet.
Now you start the third turn.
Dinoz 1 moves.
Dinoz 2 moves.
