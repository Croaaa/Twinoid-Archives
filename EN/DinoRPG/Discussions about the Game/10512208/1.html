Kayin
Only one potion in your inventory of each type, only dinoz with 10 or more in the required element can equip one of those. Here's the (unofficial) description:

- Saint Elme's Fire: All dinoz which possess less than 10 element will lose 5% within the caster's 3 turns.
- Abyss: All dinoz which possess less than 10 have all their attacks and assaults dimished by 25% within the caster's 3 turns.
- Amazon: All dinoz which possess less than 10 won't be able to move within the caster's 3 turns unless they were dealt more than 10 damage.
- Ember: All dinoz which possess less than 10 couldn't use A and E-skills within the caster's 3 turns.
- Uvava: All dinoz which possess less than 10 have their speed diminished by 50% within the caster's 3 turns.
