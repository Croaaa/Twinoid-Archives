With the "Strategy Guide" from a mission given by the "Soft Shaman" at "The Lavapit" at the "Volcanic mountain" you are able to turn on and of A-skills, E-skills and some S-skills.

MISSION #1: The Testing Ordeal

- Go to the Ashpouk Ruins - Inspect the Inscriptions
- Find the Dictionary (University) - Read the Ashpouk Dictionary
- Go to the Ashpouk Ruins - Decipher the Inscriptions
- Go to the Lavapit - Validate the mission
Reward: 20

MISSION #2: The Archelionscarer

- Go to the Venerable's Lair - Pick up the Claws
- Go to the Forges of the Big All-Hot - Speak with Smith - Kill 6 monsters
-Speak with Forges Guard - Kill 6 monsters
-Go to the Lavapit - Validate the mission
Reward: 60 and the strategy guide
