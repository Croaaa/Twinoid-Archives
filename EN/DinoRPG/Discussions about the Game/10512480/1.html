Hello everyone,

Here is the full breakdown of the voting in the recent survey. Thanks to you all for taking the time to give us your feedback, and as you'll see, we intend to put it to good use.

------------------

I would prefer the Inter Dojo Tournament XII & XIII FINALS to be:
2 vs 2: 38.2% (470 votes)
4 vs 4: 15.6% (192 votes)
5 vs 5: 29.5% (364 votes)
I don't mind, I'll destroy them all anyway!: 16.5% (204 votes)

I would like the IDT XII and XIII FINALS to allow?
Both temporary objects and permanent/magic items: 36.8% (453 votes)
Temporary objects but no permanent/magic items: 17.4% (215 votes)
No temporary objects but allow permanent/magic items: 8.9% (110 votes)
Neither temporary objects nor permanent/magic items: 36.7% (452 votes)

**Note**: As the voting was so close on this subject, the finals of IDT XIV and XV will allow neither temporary objects nor permanent/magic items.

I'd like the next big clan event to start:
NOW! What are we waiting for?: 13.9% (172 votes)
On the 1st of November: 8.6% (107 votes)
On the 15th of November: 10% (123 votes)
On the 1st of December: 20.4% (251 votes)
After a mini-event (Beauty Contest/Cinema Paradino): 9.3% (115 votes)
After a mini-event AND a few weeks to relax...: 25.6% (316 votes)
Whenever - I was born ready for battle...: 11.8% (146 votes)

I thought Clan War VI was:
Great! I can't wait for the next one!: 23.7% (292 votes)
Much better than the last war: 19.7% (243 votes)
About the same as previous wars: 19.3% (238 votes)
Worse than the last war: 9.2% (114 votes)
I didn't participate: 27.8% (343 votes)

The next Clan War should last:
1 month: 47.8% (588 votes)
5 weeks: 11% (136 votes)
6 weeks: 11.2% (138 votes)
2 months: 13% (160 votes)
I don't mind: 16.9% (208 votes)

------------------

Thanks again everyone!
Epoq
