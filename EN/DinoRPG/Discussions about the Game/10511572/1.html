What do I get if I join a Clan?
You get a bunch of other people to advise you and socialise with and a group with which you can engage in certain restricted activities such as Wars and (hopefully) soon-to-be released other stuff.

What do I have to do?
That varies from clan to clan but generally you would be expected to socialise with the clan, donate ingredients to the clan and fight in wars etc..


What clan should I be in?
You need to decide that for yourself.
Look at the clan pages, the Clan recruitment topic, their requirements and their members. Maybe read their members posts on the public forums to see whether they share similar interests or languages to you.
These things will help you decide which clan you want to join and then you just need to meet any requirements and convince them that you are the one they have been waiting for...
