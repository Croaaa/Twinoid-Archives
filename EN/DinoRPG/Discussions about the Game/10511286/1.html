no, not exactly.
You can rest ~48h without losing a turn, when another dino starts the new turn. When he is resting while you click new turn, he still recives that turn, so you only should unrest bevore clicking new turn again.
If you unrest bevore the first new turn, your dino wouldn't have a turn, and you waste some time your dino could be resting instead.
