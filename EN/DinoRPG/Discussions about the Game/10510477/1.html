If all schools in the County use the same Firewall then they will almost certainly also use the same IP Address.

It has been implied that the registration of IP Address is per account and is permanent.

If that is the case, then in order to be able to trade with your schoolmates, ever, then you will need to completely delete your account (from either home or school) and create a new one from home which you only use at home. If your friends ever access their account from your home then the ban will be reapplied and if you share an ISP then there is a chance that you may share IP address at some point randomly in the future assuming that the ISP uses dynamic addressing.

This pretty much boils down to no trading with your real-life friends.

Note that this is based upon information provided and experienced in using MT sites.

In a business or a school, the Firewall changes the IP Address.
A business may have 150,000+ addresses used on its internal systems but the Firewall will use only three addresses to allow all 150,000 systems to access the Internet.

In fact, since the protocol and port are always the same, it may use only one address externally.

MT will only ever see the IP address that the Firewall allows them to see - that is what Firewalls do
