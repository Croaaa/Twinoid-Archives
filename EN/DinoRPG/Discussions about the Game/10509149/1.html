That's right, you'd have to talk to the Venerable first about Korgons, then he tells you that the Shaman can help make your dinoz stronger. But don't go to the Shaman right away, heading back to Dinotown instead. There you'll meet the solider, and if you help him, he'll give you his membership card.

And yeah, the training center is a total scam. It's like trading for , but you take on real damage. Even if you factor in that it doesn't count against your turns, the amount of paid + damages ain't worth the gain. Plus, it stops working after your dinoz hit level 30.

So I'll get the membership card as a collection (which is expensive as it is), but will not use the training center.
