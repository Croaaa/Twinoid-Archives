Value for me is like this:
1st place: Water spheres (all skills useful on all dinos)
2nd place: Lightning sphere (+ Init is great, also the metagame is pretty lightning-dominated, thus making it my second best)
3rd place: Air spheres (Hypnosis is great if it triggers, sting and smelly aura is unimportant, since no influence on the damage done vs good enemies (all poison immune or equiped with spheres, sting only does decent damage on santaz and on other dinos it can overtrigger other, better E-skills))
Fire spheres also on third place (on certain builds it can gain more importance and even place 2nd with together lightning spheres; not so precious because you only need 2 spheres to get the skill you want from those spheres)
5th wood sphere (not much influence at all, Good Whack ofc can increase your dinos damage noticably but it just doesn't have that much influene on fights as the other spheres)
