The problem is, nobody said you would get those 2 skills automatically after you level up.

After your Dino learned Water Canon, you spent 1 level-up on unlocking the following skills: Gel and Cold Shower.
That means, when you next got at level-up, you can finally learn one of those 2 skills. Without "wasting" that one "unlocking" level-up, you would not be able to learn the higher skills.

Look here for the Water skill tree - you'll always have to spend "unlocking levels" before you can learn higher skills.
