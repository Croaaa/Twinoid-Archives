Xp is worked out for each dinoz individually.

But if you are grouped then the XP you get is reduced (per monster you kill) so if you group and kill more monsters generally you get more XP.

Group of 2-3 = 75% of Solo XP
Group of 4 = 62.5% of Solo XP
Group of 5 = 50% of Solo XP

Generally you will see best XP results in a group of 3 dinoz from level 2-30

At level 1, being solo gives a little more XP,
and when your dinoz gets strong enough (level 30 ish) to get maximum enemies in every area (and be able to kill them) you should go back to solo.
