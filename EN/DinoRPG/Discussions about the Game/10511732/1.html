When you level up, the element is picked randomly from a Board. The amount of different elements on the board(and therefore their % chance to get picked) depend on the race of the Dino. SO much you should now
When you level your Dino you can EITHER level all the Tier 1 skills of one element first and then unlock a lot of new skills with only one level up.
OR you can train only one Tier 1 and unlock the Tier 2 skills depending only on this skill with the next level up.

The first option is recommended for the main element of a Dino (The element with the most pictures on the level board ) As here for the Sirain, it would be Since a Sirain has a 55% of picking and will (most probably) get enough during its levelling process to unlock ALL essential Waterskills if you do not waste Level ups for "single unlocks"

The second option is for the elements which have a small percentage of being picked during a level up, for a Sirain it´s e.g. or which have only 10% chance of being picked. You surely could just learn all weak Tier 1 options, but this doesn´t necessarily bring huge benefits for your Dino in the long run and in higher levels. E.g. the skill "Vines" or the skill"Double Hit" ar far more effective than having all three Tier 1 skills but also need only three level ups if you select their respective Tier 1 skill, unlock the Tier 2 skill with the 2nd pick in this element and skill the skill with your 3rd

And while you are right that you cannot influence the Element you get when you level up (the skill "Career Plan" aside), you can still mess up by choosing the wrong skills.

Now the author of this thread just used the first option for the low percentage elements and thats the fault he did, I hope you understand now Indetrucks
