You need the Latern (status item) on your Dinoz first before you can enter there.

How to?
- Go to the Coral Mines and get a Shovel from the Coral Miner
- Dig at 3 places: Sticky Swamp, Fountain of Youth, Basalt Slopes
Remember: Shovel breaks after each use, so you need to repair it at the Coral Miner again
- Talk to the Atlantean Guard at Mutant Falls
- Talk to Joveboze and fight a Scorpscuba
- Move to the Underwater Dome and talk to the Archsage. Fight the Earth Elemental.
- Move to Mutant Falls and talk to Master Hydragol
- Move to Grumhel Forest towards the Haunted Hills
- Talk to the Strange Man and fight a Korgon.

You now have the Lantern, but you can't cross Jumin River yet, so you need to travel all the way back
