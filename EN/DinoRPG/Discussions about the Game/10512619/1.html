Gert,
Let me share with you the information I wish someone had shared with me when I had 1 dino:

1. U-skill dinos are 100% necessary, and the sooner you get them the better. (U-skills give you 3 more dinos, +5%, 10% discount at shops, more Inventory space, +10% on healing items, +1 equip items, and +1 when resting)

2. Collectors are necessary to complete Quests, unless you're going to put a lot of real money into the game. Also selling collected items gets you Notes or gold.

3. Only start working on Fighter Dinos after you've got a solid base of the above (say 12 dinos). You'll find your fighters level up MUCH faster! And by the time you have a dozen dinos you'll have a lot of experience so builds will come easier.

So IMHO, on your Moueffe I'd go for Acrobat or Vines and make him into a Collector.
