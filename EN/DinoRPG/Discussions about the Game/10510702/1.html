The way it's been known to work is:

Groups = + more money, + higher survivability, - less exp
Solo = + more exp, - less survivability

Ardaluc
It really depends on the level, the group size, and the location ...
For level 1 for example you get 25 alone, in groups of 2 or 3 you will get less.
At level 3 or 4 I normally started grouping, but as a group of 2 would face the same number of enemys then a solo, you get less in the group. A group of 3 on the other hand faces one more enemy, giving them more per dino then solo.

It probably changes often with the level, I'd imagine at level 6 the group of 2 would face the same enemys then a group of 3, giving them the best amount of .
For example I remember when doing the amnesic rice mission, were one level up changed the number of enemys from 6 piranoz to 9. bevore that level, a group of 2 would probably get 6 enemys, too, giving them more , after the level the group of 3 would get more.
But when you're not doing any missions, later, the number of enemys is kinda random, making it hard to tell what group would be best.

As you can't always have the best group size (you would have to move 6 dinoz together to be able to change between 2*3 or 3*2 everytime you think its best), so most of the time I use groups of 3, because of the saved I think its always better then solo, even if you get less from time to time.
