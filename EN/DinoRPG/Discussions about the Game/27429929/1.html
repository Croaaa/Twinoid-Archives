This is a dojo champion who needs no introduction. A permanent fixture in the latter stages of the IDT, and a master nobody wants to see their name next to in the first round... Rather than telling you all about how powerful a master Druid2k is, we'll just hand you straight over to the champion of IDT XXX!

"I love this format. 4 or more dinoz are my favorite. More room for tactics, and anybody knows my teams from the wars, they know that I always prefer to fight 5 vs 5. So that's may strength, I build my dinoz to be good in big teams, and I try to kill my opponent. Other strategy would be to live long for a black hole, or to outlive your opponent, but my way is to eliminate them.
Ok, I know that the different races format also favored me because I have high level dinoz in different races. Its a bit unfair to single race lovers, but well, maybe its improving diversity!

It was the scariest final I've ever been in. All big names, previous multi-times champions, all great and dangerous players. and at least 3 teams that was stronger than mine in many ways.
It was epic in all the way, first I have to mention this: finally I've met with Mohnkahn in the dojo!! We were on the same group always, and he was in the final about the same times I've been. Then LuxR, whom I fought with for the title of champion before, then the 2 times dojo champion Venar, with the biggest monkey ever! And in the final another 2 times dojo champion Mengo9999, with some of the strongest dinoz of the server!

All that I said in the post of the XXIV. dojo still stands. I won this dojo with lots of luck. In every fight all my dinoz did what was needed. Every time they spawned the skills that was best against the opponent. I risked giving Gamma Radiation to my kabuki (right before the battle with Venar), and it seems it worked. And this final was the closest match I've ever fought.

So thank you all for this epic dojo, it was the best so far! Maybe next time we will see a 6 vs 6 dojo? "

Thank you Druid2k, and CONGRATULATIONS!
