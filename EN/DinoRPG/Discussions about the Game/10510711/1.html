If you are reading this post hoping to find wonderful fantasy adventures like in some great journals I've seen you can stop reading now

This is only a cold report of a (bad) plan (and a lot of mistakes) from a master's point of view.
I'm writing it mainly as a reminder to myself, but hoping it could help other noobs like me to avoid the pitfalls I blindly jumped in. For sure it will rise some compassionated smiles in experienced masters

DinoRPG is a game of patience.

Since I started my XP/Group Research I began to think it was time to get the Fountain Pearl, so I planned to reach lvl9 with my first Dino (I know... Glidwings aren't the best choice to go against water) and then try to get the buoy.
I reached the University and Groo leveled up, let him rest for a turn (50 ), being a parsimonious (greed) master I didn't buy any food, sent him fight the elemental and...

Elemental attacks: 14 (OUCH !)
Groo: plain attack > 33 (hmmmmmm)
Elemental: 10 (uh oh...)
Groo: plain attack > 7 (he's gonna die )

and effectively... he died before he could use again

I didnt count exactly the damages Groo did, but I was pretty sure they where near 50 (why didnt I put a battle ration or 2 on him ? )
OK... no problems (he had 0 )... revived without the Angel Potion and did let him rest to full 50% again.
This time I decided I had to invest . Got Pie and Burgers to bring him 100 and 2 battle rations to equip.
Moved to University, lost 0 , equipped the Rations and Groo met the elemental again...

Groo: focus > 44 (WOW)
Elemental attacks: 14
Groo: focus > 18
Elemental dissolved

ARGHHHHH... 5K I could spare oh well...
Grouped Groo to go to fountain loosing as less as possible.

Two days later, When he reached Fountain (more ore less 19:00 server time) he still had 86 , but the experience with the Water Elemental did show me it will be REALLY hard, if not impossible, for Groo to get the pearl.
Suddenly I realized it was Wednesday and a brilliant (you'll see how brilliant it was...) new plan arose in my mind.
I could get the pearl avoiding 4 fights when going through Sticky Swamps... "pure genius", I thought (LOL)
I considered I had (little more than 14K), counted the number of Irmas I'd needed, considered I still had 2 Battle Rations and the original Angel Potion, and decided I could do it !

Did I already mention DinoRPG is a game of patience ?

Adventure starts !!!
Irma > Frisk Port > 2 Wolves > -2
Irma > Waikiki Island > 3 Piranhoz > -8 ("oh well, Islands are less hard than I thought...")
Irma > Sticky Swamps
Irma > Mutant Fall > 3 Piranhoz + 1 Kazkadine > -25 ("ACK")
Talk to Master Hydragol
Irma > Sticky Swamps
Irma > Waikiki Island > 3 Piranhoz > -18 ("Did I read they 'occasionally' fly ???")
Irma > Frisk Port > Smashroom and wolf > -1
Irma > Waikiki Island > 3 Piranhoz > -6
Irma > Sticky Swamps

Groo had 26 , I considered to equip battle rations, then, realizing he only got 1 hits 'till now, I had a premonition on what would probably happen:
one of them hits: -1
Groo: Battle Ration (ARGHHHH) > attack
they hit: -3
Groo: Battle Ration (Double ARGHHHH) > attack
and then he goes pinged to death anyway...
"NoNoNo... No Way! Better (LOL) plan, I wont equip the rations, move, and if he dies I'll use the Angel Potion, after rest I'll talk to the Master... Pearl is MINE !!!! NOTHING can stop me now !"

Irma > Mutant Fall > 3 Piranhoz + 1 Kazkadine > -26 > DEAD ("if only that last Piranhoz used fly one less time...")
Angel Potion

It was 19:40 and, since I had all my other dinos at Fountain, I thought I could stop rest at 23:40 with 13 (Groo has Regeneration), talk to the Master, get the pearl, and then use some Irmas and food to leave this deadly Islands (while Swamps was still no fight) !
"45 are well worth an Irma and a couple of Burgers !!!"

* Obviously my judgement was a bit (JUST a bit) obfuscated by greed *

Did I already mention DinoRPG is a game of patience ?

23:40 I'm ready !
Stop Rest > 13
"Now let's talk to the master and get the Pearl (finally !)"
"huh ? why there isn't the button for the master ? Curious... I'm pretty sure I did all I had to do, and have all I'm supposed to have... neither there is the button for the guard... curiouser and curiouser..."
Double checked all the steps, checked (3 or 4 times) I had the Lilypad, re-checked walkthrou... and suddenly...
"Of course... silly me... I don't see the buttons 'cause I already had my turn."
Used an Irma ("I'll need it anyway to go back to Waikiki").

* I was unaware that, 5 minutes ago, my brain embraced my judgement, and they went somewhere else, to observe my progresses in front of a good drink *

"HUH ? Buttons still aren't there... but how ?!? how ?!? ..."
"Oh, @#%#, I'm not in Mutant Falls... I'm at Sticky Swamps !!!"

Never died while moving, so never realized you fight BEFORE you move.

"Now, that's a BIG problem, in less than 10 mins I wont be able to move from the Swamps, so it will take 2 full days before I can get the Pearl. On top of that I have even wasted an Irma ? No Way !"
So got 2 Burgers... "huh... that will only bring Groo to 33 ... maybe he wont even be able to reach Mutant Falls..."
Got another Burger (I even had this strange buzzing sound in my ears, you know, like when someoneone is saying bad things about you...)
My basic math skill kicked in, and I marginally realized I just threw away 100
Less than 7 mins.... run run !
Groo with 43 > Mutant Falls > 2 Kazkadine > -7 ("phewww")
Talk to Master !!!
YES !!!! The Pearl !!! with a good 4 mins margin, too...
Now let's go back. Go Go Go !
Irma > Sticky Swamps
Irma > Waikiki Island > 3 Piranhoz > -10 ("Plan accomplished !")

"Hmmmm... 26 ... 2 mins left... why not bring Groo to Fountain so he will already be there to help Arcadio with his quest (Stamps), and enjoy the bonus of his efforts ?" (in an 'inebriated by success' voice)

Did I already mention DinoRPG is a game of patience ?

Got 2 Irmas ('till now I got them 1 by 1)
* Buzzing sound in my ears was growing louder and louder *
1054 left
Frisk Port > 1 Gluon + 2 Smashroom ("uh oh...")
Battle sequence doesn't matter, what it DOES matter is at a point Groo, with less than 10 , was in front of the nearly unscratched Gluon (a couple of absorption)

"Groo's as good as dead... "

My observation skill chose this moment to kick in, and informed me that:
a) Groo had more than 100 on him last time he checked
b) I already used the Angel Potion I had
c) I hadn't enough to buy another one !

I was there, looking in desperation at a flashing red bar and a nearly invisible yellow line, and suddenly luck turned: Gluon didnt absorb for some turns, Groo got focus twice on strong attacks and the Gluon melted.

Groo had 2 ... I used the second irma I got and let him rest... he deserved it.

The Pearl did cost me 20K (I'm not counting the Battle Rations, since they are still in my inventory) and an Angel Potion, delayed my general development by at least a week, caused the lost of some months of my life during the final 30 secs of total terror and desperation.

Was worth it ?

Sure, it's a great object, and I'm happy I got it (already enjoying it). As an extra bonus it even allowed me to enter a clan ( yes, I'm now a proud member of "The Ducks" ! ).

Was worth to wait some days to get it, wasting less money and taking nearly no risks ?

NO doubts !!
