There are 2 Lilly Pads, one lets you go to the Forrest and the other is a part of the Quest to get the Fountain Pearl. So you need to look for information on the Epic Item the Fountain Pearl that lets your Dinoz heal 5 if they are at the Fountain of Youth at 00:00 Server Time. I would get the Fountain Pearl if I was you (uses up a Lilly Pad), and then talk to Master Hydargol to get the 2nd Lilly Pad.
