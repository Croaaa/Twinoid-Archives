[765481]

Grinding in nimbao til lvl 49 wont make you lose any xp. You can do the venerable on your way to caushemesh then.
By the time you finished cassandras quest (best way is let all dinoz do the first 2 quest, do the other quests with a single dino), you wont hit lvl 50 (at least i didnt this way).
You can then start grinding in caushmesh, getting an easy lvl 50 up, unlock lvl50+ and do however you want to:
- grinding further
or
- defeating the collosi. The one roaming caushmesh should be no problem, you will kill him while grinding.
Steppes are pretty empty on most accounts these days since most people skip them, or just do 2 or 3 levels after the dark world and before heading to nimbao. However, i dont know if thats the case for you.
The most annoying colossus is the one roaming the forges, since he will kill your low level teams over and over and over and...
So this is a financial decision to take: Grind right away to get a good team early on (maybe you got plans for the upcoming manawar or dojo), or killing the collossi right away to avoid annoyance. Be aware that it sometimes takes like 100 fights until each of the colossi finally appears for a 3rd time so that you can kill him.
If you hunt the collosi, do as i suggested earlier, get to level 50 and unlock 50+ so you will make at least 1 additional level due to the colossi-xp.

Thats the way i did it. That doesnt mean that you should not head to caushemesh right away, but with the method i proposed, you make sure to not waste any xp and i even guess its the fastest, since nimbao xp til 49 seems to be slightly better in terms of losing hp/gaining xp ratio, AND you have the venerable on your way. In the end, it still depends on when you can open the gates (in case you havent). So i cant say "thats the way to go", but its a method which worked for me and several others.
Once you are midway through the quest and defeated the colossi, its probably wiser to go to caushmesh before lvl 49, especially since you dont have to do 30+ turns with close to no xp.
