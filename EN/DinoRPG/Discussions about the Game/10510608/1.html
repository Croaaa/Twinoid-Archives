[939152]
Well, I only have low level dinoz, but my opinion is that you need a decent primary attack (being your main element), and then use anything else to try and get specific skills. It is nice to have a couple of attacks that do greater than 10 damage for the chaff you fight (Flamelets, Phiranoz, etc), but the big first attack is the key. As you develop your dinoz, then the skills will start playing a bigger part in the fights, with Vines, Burning Breath, Dodge, etc.

Collection and Unique skills are another matter altogether. I would love to be able to get the same element time after time for Engineer/Leader, Cooker/Storekeeper, Professor, and Merchant/Priest. Alas, that sort of luck is pretty rare.
