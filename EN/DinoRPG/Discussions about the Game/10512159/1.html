As it was said like a million times:
Patience is the most important thing in this game.

I play for about 15 months and I have some higher level Dinoz and 23 total Dinoz. It takes a lot of time till you get more Dinoz at first, but when you have about 18 Dinoz, you get about 4000 per day. You can get Jackpots, Complete missions with rewards and you will get about 10000 per day. I think it is worth it

If you wasted all the money, my suggestion would be to reset your account, buy a 16.000 worth Dinoz, use your 10 Irma's whenever you want and you will have about 7.000 at the first day.


About healing:
It isn't what it looks like. If you go for the Fountain Pearl, it will heal ALL of you Dinoz 5 at 00.00 (midnight) Server time. I rest my Dinoz only 1 day. It gives them 23 which is enough for about 5 days. When you will have 2 Dinoz and one of them will need to rest, the other will earn coins and when you have a lot of Dinoz you won't even notice the resting. 1 or 2 Dinoz rests and the other (depends on how many you have) fights, moves, does their missions etc.

Good luck,

Suggestion given by ITS4U on developing gatherers is good but it might not be the best strategy. The dinos having gatherer paths are often weaker than their counterparts. This leads to more loss each fight and though you can earn some from selling ingredients, it's nowhere sufficient to fund your irmas/healing on a continuous basis.
In fact, the more frequent need to rest these dinos will more than offset the gain due to selling ingredients.

In my opinion, once you have 9-12 'regular' dinos then you can buy 3 dinos for ingredients collection, group them to avoid large loss per turn and benefit from ingredients collection. (This is one thing you'll quickly learn that dinos fighting in a group loose less and sometimes earn more and too on an average)

Also, I feel that with your first 3-4 dinos (give or take 1-2) you should not be afraid of experimenting and learning what strategy works best for you in this game.

Personal experience: the game became a lot interesting too me an year after I started. The reason was a great, fun loving clan I had joined. I think you'll enjoy clan based events like wars, championships more than growing your dinos. And ofcourse these 2 are inter-linked, but you knew that already.
