Hmmm should have seen this topic earlier but I tend to not read forums much these days... Anyway, dev or no dev, here's my 2 cents:

Blitz CotC? I hated it (but I respect the majority, who seems to have enjoyed it). I saw strategy belittled, and timetables that, added to the duration of each battle, prevented a lot of players from participating in most of the event and also meant that from those that were present at the right time, only a few could in fact take part on it due to the limits imposed.

As for the byes and pairing system, I keep feeling incredulous. If only there were widespread competitions all over the world, that had a fair formula proven to work, but in a really professional sport like, I don't know... football (soccer for our US friends)?

If there is no way to recode it, just say so. If there is, I find it hard to believe people in general would opt for the currently used type of pairing when a better solution is out there and is the most used worldwide.

So, to sum it up, I'd rather have an event where more players can take part on, and a group stage where everyone plays against everyone else one, even if it means creating more groups with less participants each. Like I said, my 2 (bitter) cents.
