There is one important exception where the excess kills will carry over to the other dinoz in the group doing the kill. The exception is this: If more than one dinoz has to kill 0/x enemies where x is 1,2,3,... and if none of the dinoz has killed any enemies and x + y enemies are killed, the excess, y, kills will carry over to the other dinoz. If y is > x, then (y - x) kills will be carried over to the next dinoz in the group and so on. For example say a group of 4 dinoz has the mission of kill 1 Gluon and each of the four have 0/1 kills and the group of 4 kills 3 Gluons. Three of the dinoz will be credited with 1/1 kills of Gluons and one dinoz will still have 0/1 Gluons to kill. Similarly if 4 dinoz have 0/4 Flamlets to kill and kill 9 Flamlets in one fight; 2 Dinoz will be credited with 4/4 Flamelets to kill, 1 Dinoz will have 1/4 Flamlets to kill, and the other dinoz will have 0/4 Flanlets to kill. Then if the same group of 4 again kill 9 Flamlets, the first 2 dinoz already have their kills, the third dinoz will have 4/4 Flamlets to kill, and the last dinoz will have 0/4 Flamlets to kill (kills do not carry over because 1st dinoz credited with kills is not starting with 0/4 kills). However, if you regroup the dinoz before the fight so that the leader had 0/4 Flamlets to kill and a follower had 1/4 Flamlets to kill, now I believe that you would get credit for the carry over and two dinoz would again get credit for 4/4 Flamlets to kill. Not sure about this last statement as have not actually carried it out. But have experienced all the rest in my many fights. As to who gets credit for the kills in the group of 4 dinoz, I have not really paid that much attention and do not know except that leader always gets credit for kills first.
