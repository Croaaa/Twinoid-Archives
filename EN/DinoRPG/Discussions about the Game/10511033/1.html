Here is a transcript from our Clan forum - one of our more trusted members posted them for the rest...


Wood Plates
Duration: 6 hours
Diminishes the damages done by the Castle assailants.


Pond
Duration: 10 hours
Increases the recovery time of the Dinoz attacking the castle.


Smashroom Cage
Duration: 5 hours
A Smashroom helps you to defend the castle.


Fortification
Duration: 24 hours
Increases the castle resistance by 50 points.


Apprentice Repairer
Duration: 5 hours
An help-repairer is going to repair your castle on time.
