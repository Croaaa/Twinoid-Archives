Rock costs 18,000 gold, the top clans have all the information you could ever want up on thier public pages, just go to ranking / clan points and have a look to see which ones are best, book mark them and read them as and when you need too.

Obviously the dino with the highest element of what you need will be best for gathering, you dont need to take more than 1 element of gathering skills on a dino. As every turn you may only gather once,

As for whats the best ingredient to gather it really depends, they all have their purposes. Air gathering is the most desired by clans. Lightning gathering for sphere's is the most usual for yourself, as well as gathering for Kubiki quest but personally it's easier to pay 30 notes for a "kubiki pack" lol

Good luck with your plans

Your current gorilla could get the U skill, I recommend just pushing all future levels into gaining it (leader that is) very useful.

The thing with getting U skills is the first 10 levels come really quickly, but going from 11-20 will take much longer, as such it's best to aim to get a U skill asap, you dont want to have a useless dino lingering around longer than needed.
