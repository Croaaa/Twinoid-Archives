Correct? yes as I understand the system.

how do you raise your defense skill?There are two ways to increase defence.

1. This way is not within your control. When you increase in level, the element that gets chosen as well as gets a +1 increase. The two elements after the chosen one gets >+1 increase, and the two elements before gets <+1 increase. Because of fractions and roundings, you sometimes see the two elements after getting +2, and the two elements before getting +0.

2. This way is within your control. Learning certain skills can increase the defence in particular elements.
