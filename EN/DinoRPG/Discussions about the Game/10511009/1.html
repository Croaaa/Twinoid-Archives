Cocoon, tier 3 wood skill. Add's +2 hp per hour to rest.

Regenration, tier 2 lighting skill. Add's +2 hp per hour to rest.

Priest, tier 5 lighting U skill. Add's +1 hp per hour to rest to all dinoz you own.

Only slightly related but the skill.
Accupuncture, tier 3 water. Gives you the status healing during the fight, +1 hp each turn of the combat and if an enemy hits you they take 1 hp damage, a nice little skill to keep you from having to rest as much. Their are a few other likewise skills.

Not to mention items can restore even more. If you get the Fountain Pearl you can regain 5hp every day at midnight (server time) at the fountain of youth. This applies to all dinos as the pearl is linked to the account and not the dino.

Other items that in my opinion are even better is the Tik Bracelet that you get after doing the quests from the Prowler at the Forges of the Big-all-hot. Its a bit annoying quest chain and will take some time unless you are willing to spend gold on potions but well worth it. It gives 10hp a day regen to the dino who has it equipped.

But combining these things are of course the best way. Standing at the fountain with skills present and pearl aquired would effectively give 24 (total regen for a day) + 10 (bracelet) + 5 (pearl) = 39hp a day which even fills up the hp bar of a dino with sumo ability. Skills on top of this and you can effectively use your dino on a daily basis as long as he can be given the most ideal conditions to restore his hp.
