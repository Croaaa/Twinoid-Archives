You can't delete or replace skills.

The only way to get rid of wrong skills is to learn Reincarnate skill (Lightning Path). This skill will return your Dinoz to level 1 (deleting all status items and skills) after dying at level 40. Also, it will give random element points to your Dinoz so it will be like a level 6 Dinoz instead of 1.

What Afreet said is a good option too if your Dinoz has few percent of Lightning and no chance on getting Reincarnation.
