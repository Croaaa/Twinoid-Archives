When you defeat a clan that previously defeated you, you still suffer the reputation penalty.

When a clan that beat you looses reputation, that doesn't mean that you loose reputation. Even if you do it's really small to notice. To be sure about that don't do wars for a few days and see how your reputation stays the same while Cloak and Dagger's falls down.

The formula as was said before calculates the reputations at the time when the war was declared (maybe when it was won), so even if BBC wins you it won't calculate it as 350-400 rep but the 100 they had cause we were the first victim And as you noticed well, after some time when you've done a lot of wars your reputations gets "stuck" at some level where it doesn't change much.
