And what a show!

Master RafaelSS has been crowned champion of the Inter-Dojo Tournament after a breathtaking battle against Mengo9999! You can see a blow by blow replay of this clash of the titans by clicking here.


Our congratulations to all the Dino Masters who managed to secure a place in the latest Inter-Dojo Tournament!

15th of October for the next IDT

Bring your best team of fighting Dinoz for the qualification rounds. The confrontations for the Zen Medal will be super intense and the prizes will match the intensity of the battles. Toufufu eggs, Napodinoz and stacks of Treasury Notes and Demon Tickets are on offer!

The Rules will be:

As always, during qualification there will be no equipped items!
You will need two Dinoz of two different races!
During the finals, magic items and consumables will NOT be allowed!

Stay tuned for more information!
