Well, there's a difference between "lucky" and "get Cp with a Feross". It is really hard to build a Feross, as it usually gets flooded with wood and some air (but not enough for bubble or vendetta). Building a Quetzu already has its tough choices and a well-built Feross is really similar tot that, except it has an even greater risk of getting overwhelming wood level-ups. I wouldn't even know what an "optimal" or "best" Feross build looks like...

I would probably call a race weak if even the best what they can do or get is way worse than another race's optimal (but not exceptional) build.
For example if you want a Pteroz, you can try as hard as you want, but you'll end up with a half-witted Softpig. It's also getting just like that with Kabukis, as try as hard as you wish, you'll either end up with a bubblewinks with more air and less water (which is bad), a cloudoz without Fujin (which is bad) or an Etherwasp without Hazard and a strong wood assault (which is not bad, but less powerful).

I would have been so happy, if demon Moueffes got an additional 5% counterattack chance, demonkabukis got an additional 5% chance to dodge, but no...

I can imagine a Gorilloz to be powerful, but it would need Sumo, Self-Control, CP and probably 0 air (or ET, but that's really stretching it) and that is a really hard to get build, even with a cube.

Castis became great with WW, Wanwans are great, Rockies are great, Winks' are the most durable and ancient choice (of mine ), Cloudoz are now strong and Glids are too one-sided. Tofufus focus on too many things with little HP and Mahamutis are awesome but lack immunity to poison, are expensive to get and can be overwhelmed with wood-rolls. Finally, even the best Santaz possible is not nearly as strong as an Etherwasp can be on average.
