It seems 200 is about normal, and I haven't seen any real per-fight increase (I'm still not far in, though). Actually, most fights have been above 200 lately, but I can't imagine that the normal prize ever reaches 900.

Sometimes you'll get a big prize, but you can't depend on it.

Don't spend the money on turns (Irma's Potions), because you'll want it for healing (food items). Rarely, you'll need to get to a location faster (an auction, the Merchant, etc.).

You'll definitely get more with more Dinoz, and some missions have good prizes. Each Irma's only works on one Dinoz, though, so there isn't a point where you get enough to keep going indefinitely -- it'll always cost 900 per Dinoz per extra turn.
