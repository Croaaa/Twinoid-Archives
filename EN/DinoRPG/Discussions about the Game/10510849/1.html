spaceguy66, I see you have the climbing gear and are Level 7. So, I would recommend that you fight the Water Elemental and get the Bouy which allows you to swim to the Atlantied Islands from the Frish Port. Then you should work on the quest (not mission) of getting the Pearl (an Epic Item) and doing missions from Bao Bob until you get to Level 12. Then go to the All-Hot and do missions from the Soft Shaman.

12agnar0kian's 3rd post in the Earth elemental thread in Discussions about the Game also outlines some of the early things that you should also consider doing.

Also, look at the post in Questions / Answers to answer some of your questions.

And my last and most important suggestion is to save your and only use it to buy more Dinoz. [If you spend real money on the game, also use it to buy more Dinoz first.] After you have 18 to 21 Dinoz you can think about spending on other things. Also, as you get more Dinoz, start grouping them in groups of three as soon as they reach Level 2. In general, you get more , lose less , and have the potential to get more with groups of 3. Try as much as possible to keep the Levels of the Dinoz in the groups of three at the same Level. Also, consider joining a Clan to open up that avenue of the game too.

That's my suggestion. Use any, all, or none of it as it suits you, but most important of all is Enjoy the Game!
