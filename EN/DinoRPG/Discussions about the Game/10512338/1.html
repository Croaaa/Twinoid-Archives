Clan Wars:
You can use it as a Defender together with the other assigned defenders (so grouped).

However, you attack castles all alone.

Championships:
You can fight along side with other Dinoz.

Dojo:
You can use it in the Dojo together with the other 2 Dinoz you assigned (so grouped).

Superdome:
You can't use the Superdome with Brave Dinoz, because you need a group of at least 2 Dinoz.
