What if the devs would listen to our feedback for once. Well this is a topic for suggestions for next (or future) CotC only.

As you may not know, FR has been reduced to 3 leagues, merging Elite and Pro league into a same one. I don't know at this point if our next one is a copy/paste of FR or not.

So right now it's looking (approximatively) like that:
Pro (FR): 40-70 (previously on EN: 45-70),
Amateur (FR): 25-40 (EN: 25-50),
Starter (FR): 10-25 (EN: 10-30).

This is a measure to counter tweaked dinoz, which would have been stopped at a certain level to ensure it will rule the next edition. So do you agree or disagree?

And what if you got a better idea? Feel free to post here.
As a matter of fact, values that can be changed are level caps, max power on map, AP tick, AP maximum stack, battle duration, resting time, battle locations, number of clans in one group. And also anything out-of-proportion has no success to be worked on.
