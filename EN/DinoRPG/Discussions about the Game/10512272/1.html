Defaitist
Well, your first dinoz will most likely be a bit messed up if you compare them to planned dinoz some veteran tamers have leveled following a master plan. But it helps to try out various strategies.

For beginners, putting 3 dinoz together in a group is always a good idea, since they share exps and can defeat their enemies quickly and take much less damage each combat than fighting one by one over various combat rounds.

You should read about U skills and what they'll do on the various excellent information pages of clans or in the forum or the inofficial wiki. But most likely if you want to buy a dinoz just to get a certain U skill they will stray from path and level up other elements. And then you should always have an idea for a plan B and C, since they may still become formidable fighters if you combine other element skills to them or from a certain point abandon the plan to make them an U skill dino.

There is not right or best way to play the game, there are various approaches to fights. Some elements are stronger than others, some of them in the early chapters of the long path of the dino level up career, some reach their peak of combat prowess very late in the high end levels. And their of course double skills.

The game admins try to implement a certain balance of the elements. So do not take the best skill set ups for being best forever.

I hate to rain on your parade, but it's only good if you pick good skills, which I'm sorry to say, you haven't
Perception is not good for a dinoz that doesn't roll much , it just won't do that much for you
the best option would have been to go for Mutation, so you could eventually learn Sumo
Mistral also wasn't a good idea, as it is weak enough on a dinoz that does have as its main element, but it is detrimental when isn't the dinoz's main element, like your Moueffe
Strategy was a good choice, as it will eventually allow you to learn the double skill Vendetta

as a general rule, there are a generally agreed set of preferred secondary skills to choose (secondary skills being those in an element that a dinoz has a small chance to roll and won't be very powerful in that element)
Wrath - unlock - Warm Blood - unlock - Burning Heart + Nap - unlock - Self Control
Carapace - unlock - Vines - unlock - Primal State
Mutation - unlock - Ventral Pouch - unlock - Sumo
Celerity - unlock - Double Hit - unlock - Career Plan
Agility - unlock - Dodge

these skills are chosen because they benefit all dinoz, and not just ones that get lots of level ups in the same element
with your Moueffe though, you don't want to be choosing only these skills when it levels up in as it has a good chance to get lots of (you still want to be choosing these ones, but many others as well)

it would probably be a good idea for you to have a look at some clan pages that show all the different skills for each of the elements
EC have some pages that show this... this is the skills, and they've got all the other elements there too (listed in the text just below their banner)

don't feel disheartened if you've messed up your first dinoz though, I don't know a single person on here that didn't make many mistakes with their first
