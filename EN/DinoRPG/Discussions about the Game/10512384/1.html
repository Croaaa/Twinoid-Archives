al-Aqsa
You have 3 options:

1) Sacrifice your dinos at Cemetary. This option is only available after finishing a rather long dungeon.

2) Freeze your dinos at Deep Gorges. For this you need a Lantern from Haunted Hills at Grumhel Forest.

3) Sell them at the Marketplace. Finding a buyer for "scwered up" dinos will be an issue though.

Looking at your account I don't think you should really be worrying about proper builds yet. Just keep on having fun with Demon Scroll (for sacrifices) and U-Skills in mind.
