To join the Chat without a Mibbit Account

Visit: http://chat.mibbit.com/#dinorpg

Type your DinorRPG Nick and click GO

Everyone is Welcome to participate

For Mirc users:
Server irc.mibbit.com
Channel #DinoRPG
For Chatzilla module on Firefox:- In Global settings: add your nickname on the nickname box.
- Then on the main window, write /attach irc.mibbit.com
- And on the server tab, write /join #DinoRPG.
You can right-click on each tabs to "Open this server/channel at startup.
To Set Up A Mibbit Account

Visit: http://mibbit.com/chat/#

(1) Click, Create an account
(1b) Choose a username/password, click [REGISTER]
(1c) Complete the registration process...

Once logged in...

(2) Register your nickname by clicking on Account
(2b) Input the following:
Server: irc.mibbit.com
Nick: (your DinoRPG name)
Pass: choose a password
Auth method: nickserv
Charset: UTF-8
(2c) Click [Add]
(2d) Click [Save and close]

(3) Register auto-login by clicking on Channels
(3b) Input the following:
Server: irc.mibbit.com
Channels: #DinoRPG
Auto-Connect: Yes
(3c) Click [Add]
(3d) Click [Save and close]

Now, when you login to the program, it will automatically take you to the DinoRPG chat channel!
