Piemaster
Those are the only 3 ways. And the third option seems to be giving you some confusion. You need to complete the Katatombs (at the cemetary) to get an item called the Demon Scroll. Once you have that item you can sacrifice any dino at the cemetary to get demon tickets. These demon tickets can be spent on demon dinos when you get enough of them (which will take a while).

As for which way s the 'best' that depends on a lot of things. If it's a dino with a U skill you need, or if it's a dino you might want to get back easily in the future then freezing is the best option. Otherwise sacrificing for demon tickets will mean you get something useful for your unwanted dino and you can get them back later providing you haven't spent the demon tickets. Selling at the market *could* get you some useful gold, but in reality they rarely sell unless they are very high level or have a great/unusual set of level-ups.
