HugoMVP

18 is the limit, but if you get the u-skill Leader on one of your dinoz you can have 21.

To remove some dinoz, you can freeze them (you need to get the lantern on the dinoz and then go to deep gorges with him), so they won't count for your limit, or you can sacrifice them by getting the demon scroll from the katacombs.
