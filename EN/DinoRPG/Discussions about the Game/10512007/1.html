On january 16, 2011
[939305]
Battles give reputation, not points. 2 if you win, 2 extra if you win and complete the challenge given.
You see the challenge before the fight, and must select the best pair of your-opponent dino to complete it. So going "ok, let's see what I can win and let my strong dino clean up what I couldn't beat otherwise" is not always the best solution.

Refusing a challenge makes you lose 3 reputation.

Losing a fight makes you lose no reputation.

Merit is the % of fights you won, from my experience keeping it over 60% is rather simple, taking it over 70% requires strong or dojo optimized dinoz. But as said, that is my case

Merit and reputation are multiplied, and you have your points.
