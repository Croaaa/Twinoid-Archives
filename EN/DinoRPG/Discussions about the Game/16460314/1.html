epoq

Following our ever so mysterious (not to mention poetic) news article, we thought we should give you loyal dinoz masters a little more info about this new world that's coming your way...

At one point in the scenario, a character will offer to make level 60 available to your dinoz. If you answer "Yes", the character will then ask you to make a choice:

1- that your dinoz leaves the original skill tree and starts on the new one.

2- that your dinoz continues to level-up on the original skill tree until a maximum of level 60. At any point during their evolution from level 50 to 60, it will be possible to speak to the same character again and leave the original skill tree and move to the new one. This action is irreversible.

(for example, you could choose to move from one tree to the other at level 52, but remember, this action will be irreversible and must be done for each dinoz)

The choice is yours!
