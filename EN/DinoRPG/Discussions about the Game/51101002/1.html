From December 15th (15:00 server time) to December 28th (23h59 server time)!

Take part in the christmas event and win a truckload of gold coins, rare Christmas Feross eggs, and more ingredients than Fanny Craddock !

  • You will get one Xmas Ticket for every 10.000 you buy from the Dinoland Bank, regardless of your payment method (also works if you purchase Treasury notes)
  • Move one of your Dinoz to Dinotown to use your Ticket and play the Snowplough game
  • Every ticket is a Winner! Did I mention RARE SANTAZ?
  • Each SMS code give one Xmas Ticket too, so get in the game!
  • There has never been a better time to jump-start your campaign in the world of DinoRPG!
  • Top Prizes : Unique Xmas Feross egg and and special Xmas egg which can turn into an Tricerithumps or the famous Santaz !
