I've heard several times the same complaints coming back at each previous wars we got on EN server, and went to ask (our new admin in place) skool if the rewards on our server could be changed to be somehow more attractive to you all (and tailored to our clan population).

  • So here's the changes:
    • 1st clan: Winner epic reward + 4 Napodinos + 150
    • 2nd to 5th clan: Epic reward + 3 Napodinos + 150

    • 21st to 30th clan: Epic reward + 1 Napodino + 50
    • 31st to 40th clan: Epic reward + 1 Napodino
Maybe there will be a further need for changes, but with the devs being very busy at the moment and the short time left, there's nothing more that could be done on the rules.
