I'm inclined to think Strategy replaces your dino's standard strat (use strongest attack first, second strongest next, ect... Special skills whenever they trigger) and instead tries to match its attacks with the enemy's weakest defenses (at least that's how I assume it works without testing it much). This can obviously be a bad thing if your Dino has a skill he isn't good at (Water cannon isn't likely to help a Cloud much... Unless you got a number of water stat ups) and the enemy's weakness encourages him to use it alot.

The 1 hp left thing however Redscorp is not Strategy's fault. That's not what strategy does, there is another skill "Without Mercy" in the Water skills that makes your dino prioritize lower HP enemies.

Overall I'm not much of a fan of Strategy from what I understand of it so unless you're going for a skill later in its tree I'd say avoid it if possible.
