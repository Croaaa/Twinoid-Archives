If your Dinoz has ales than 50% of his Health points, he can u e "Rest". That enables him to recover automatically healt points on time. In compensation, he can't do any action when he rests.

At the beginning, your Dinoz recovers 1 per hour, but it will be possible to improve it with some skills.

You can stop your Dinoz rest at any moment but your Dinoz can't recover more than half of his health points by resting.

Also the "Pearl", enable all your dinos at the "Fount of youth", to recover 5 HP at 00:00, server time. Ask the old man( he will ask you for a favor) in the "Mutant falls", at the islands, for it.

Good luck.

On february 26, 2009
Husaurus
Starting to Rest takes up a turn. However, if you stop resting on a new day, the new day's turn will not be used up by the resting.

So, if a Dinoz uses a turn to start resting at 00:30 on day 1 and stops resting at 23:31 on day 2, then the Dinoz will have a turn on day 2 and have rested 47 hours getting 47 back (unless it reached 50% before then).
