Dinoz masters!

Due to popular demand, it's coming back! Registration for the Championship of the Clans will begin on the 16th of January 2014. The event itself is scheduled to kick off on the 30th of January at 15h00 server time, so you'll have 2 weeks to decide which league is right for you and your clan and to get your game face on.

Full details and format for the leagues will be posted when registrations open, so you can start thinking about your clan and recruiting a big hitter or master strategist for the first clan event of 2014! As the great strategist Sun Tzu once stated, "Great victories can be achieved with small forces."

We've seen this before in the Championship, large clans being defeated by smaller, more determined, well-prepared clans. Will we see a Cinderella story this year? We certainly hope to see some epic combats in any case!

*Stay tuned for more information!
