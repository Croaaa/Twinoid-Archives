As some of you have already seen, some changes were made during the last week update (just before the war).

Here, I give some explanations about them.

Magic Shop Items are back to their previous prices, I will change them later (after the end of this war) and see for balancing properly the price according to their value (of course if there is big changes, I'll refund).

Somes changes about two monsters of Grumhel Forest were made in order to balance a bit more this region before the release of the Monster Island content update.
This update should be released after the Magnetic steppes, coming out next week.

Tawny-Bats are now stronger and got less health points: +10 element points, - 10.

Arboreal Guardians have less but are more resistant, the damage of their "Comet" attack have been fixed.
Dinoz will encounter them from the level 22 instead of 23.
Xp gain has been reduced but still higher than other monsters of DinoRPG.

Changes on mission system.
Now when you cancel a mission it takes a few days before you can do it again.

This was made to block exploiting the system, as some players were taking some missions in order to force the spawn of monsters, then cancelling once done, to do it again.
As this was not fair regarding the other players, this system was changed.

Please, if there is another change I have missed to explain, tell me and I'll update this post.
