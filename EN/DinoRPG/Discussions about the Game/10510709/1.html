To unlock Rockys, Hippoclamps and Pteroz you don't need to do a quest. You just have to go defeat the wild versions of each of these dinoz.

The Rocky is at Brutforce and you can't fight him until level 13. The Pteroz is at the Basalt Slopes and you can't fight him until level 10(I think). And the Hippo is at Waikiki Island you have to be level 8 to fight him.

Once you beat them they are available in the shop. Other dinoz like Kabuki, Santaz and Feross are collected in different ways.

The hippo is the easiest whereas the other 2 wild dinoz are not. Once you beat them, they'll be in the shop.

The kabuki is the only creature that doesn't require spending "real" money. Once you complete the quest, you go to totem island to buy one. However, the seller won't always have one with him and I hear he charges about 50K for one.

Unless, you want to spend real money, then by all means have a try at getting the other 2 special dinoz. The santaz is coming soon.
