the old system was 24 hours. period. a new turn started at 0:00 on the server clock (right side of the screen). you could take your turn any time during that 24 hour period and it did not affect the timing of your next turn.

this meant you could take one turn at 23:55 and your next turn five minutes later at 0:00.

if you started resting your dinoz at 0:00 day 1 and took your next turn at 23:59 day 2 you could gain up to 49 while only burning one action.
