AndySQdancer
Suio, from our Clan's description of the Skills I would say not.

Medicine -- Grants ability to recover up to 3 health at the end of the fight

So the Dinoz having this Skill can recover up to 3 that has been lost in the fight.

Hospital Porter -- Enables one other Dinoz in the group to recover up to 5 HP at the end of the fight

So a Dinoz in the group, other than the Dinoz having the Skill, can recover up to 5 . Since you do not have a group of 2 or more Dinoz there is no other Dinoz to recover up to 5 . It's just like the Wood Skill Precocious Springs, it heals other Dinoz in the group, not the Dinoz having the skill.

However, First Aid and Medicine do stack if the Dinoz having these Skills loses 2 or more .
