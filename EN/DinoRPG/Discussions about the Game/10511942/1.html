Assault (from dino A) - Defence (from dino B) = Damage
Constant

I've just done some calculations with this formula and I've noticed something. Have a look at my results. Mark my words, they're not fabricated!

Apparatus: dojo system

Method:
Go to "test your dinoz" in your dojo
Pitch your attacking dino (it can be any dino) against your weakest dino.
Record the attacking dino's best assault damage
Record the weakest dino's defence to that element (so if the attacking dino's stronget assault is , use )
Disable all skills that may alter your result (e.g. wrath, focus and all A skills. You want your strongest dino to make a clean attack without any skills to enhance it or replace it with a stronger/weaker one)
Watch the fight. Record the attacking dino's damage.
Use formula to calculate it.
