Hey there Dino Masters,

As promised (and stereotypically late) here's the update on what's happening with a bunch of stuff here at MT headquarters.

We had a meeting this morning and set aside time to address a bunch of issues with DinoRpG. We've got one or two things to finish off with Hordes and some mobile technology before we can get there, but in a little under a month kiroukou and skool will be spending a few weeks working on DinoRPG

The Smog quest and the translation tools:
  • The EN & ES servers will both be getting the new quest! Finally. We were hoping that epoq would be able to do some contract work and translate the new quest for us, but he's busy with his new career, so we've had to figure something else out. The long and short is that I will personally be taking the time to translate the quest into English which will then be translated professionally into Spanish.
  • While this is happening we'll be exploring the viability of implementing tools that would allow you guys to help out with the translation, not only of Dino, but of all of our games. As some of you know this is made difficult by the fact that Dino is in an older version of haxe (insert long winded dev-whinge here) and that's why we've been dragging our feet. In anycase we know that we need this tool and that we'll have to develop it eventually, so skool is going to take a closer look.
Level 80
  • We will be opening level 80 to all dinoz in the game. This won't exactly be at the same time, but it will be soon after the release of the new(ish) quest.
Other stuff
  • We're also working on some other changes that I'm deliberately talking about but not giving out any details. Some of you I imagine might react like this , but we think that you'll like what we've got in store. A lot of consultation has been done with the french community and we're trying to address some of the major points that keep coming up.

So, a little longer and all will be revealed. I'll keep you updated on the progress of the new features etc as soon as we actually start working on them.

Thanks,

buzzard
