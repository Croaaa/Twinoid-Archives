The turn system is different from all other servers.

Instead of 1 turn every day at 00.00 hours server time, you get a "new turn" button.

You get this button "X" amount of hours after clicking the last "New Turn" button.

Eventually it will be 23 hours (this is the maximum wait time)

But when you are new you start off with only 1 hour before your new turn.
I believe The Time Increment Increase is 30 mins per "New Turn" per Dino.

So you are currently at 1hr 30mins, with 1 dino, clicking "New Turn" will mean your next turn is in 2 hours.
After this if you brought some gold and then had 3 dino's. 2 hrs would lead to 3 hours and 30 minutes for your next turn.
