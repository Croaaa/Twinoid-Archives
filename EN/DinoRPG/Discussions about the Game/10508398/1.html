Yes, it's not so much an evolution, it's more like a growth process. As you can see on the gifs, the dinoz only get bigger and some features change when they reach lvl 10, and after that level, only some skills that grants extra would make them bigger. All the dinoz have different features.

Some examples are:

The winks' eyes get smaller, giving them a more serious look.
Ptreozes can change their color skin.
Softpigs can grow horns, if you buy them with the two little bumps on their forehead.
