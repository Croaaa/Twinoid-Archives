To group your dinoz bring them to the same place and use button follow which become availadle when your dinoz are at he same location. But I should mention that your second dinoz can do the same quests as first. So you will gain more if you do all the quest for your second dinoz and only later group them.

To heal you dinoz without resting you need a pearl - an item availible after you finish quest of monk from Atlanteid Islands. You will need to be at Fountain in 00.00 to heal 5

Other way of healing is by skills Precotious Spring or Leafy Dawn which accasionally restore in battle (this skills are awersome when your dinoz has high lvl). Or by skill Hospital Porter (heals 5 at the end of fight)
