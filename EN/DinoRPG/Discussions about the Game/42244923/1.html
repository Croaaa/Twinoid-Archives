The hunt will take place between the 3rd and the 10th of November 2014.

The monsters have been contained to Grumhel Forest, The Magic Steppes and the Dark World.

The best hunters will take away the spoils as follows:

1st place: 1 Etherwasp Egg + Epic Reward + 100 Treasury Notes
2nd - 5th: 1 Feross Egg + 60 Treasury Notes
6th - 10th: 1 Kabuki egg + 30 Treasury Notes
11th - 20th: 5 Tac Tickets + 10 Treasury Notes
21st - 50th: 50 Treasury Notes
51st - 100th: 25 Treasury Notes

And that's not all!

Each time you pass a new level in your personal rankings you'll unblock:

500 points: 5 Madam Irma's Potions
1000 points: 2 Authentic Warm Bread
1500 points: 2 Authentic Warm Bread + 3 Angel Potions
2000 points: 1 Pack of Nimbao island objects
3000 points: 2 Tac Tickets
4000 points: 200 Demon Tickets
5000 points: 100 Treasury Notes
6000 points: 1 Gold Napodino
