What I would do with a fighting glidwing:

:

go for vines, always a usefull skill. If you get more level ups, I would take primal state. That skill is meant for PvP but helps with flyers too, and when you encounter monsters that will poison you.

:

Wrath -> Warm Blood -> Burning Heart

A Glidwing is fast, having good initiative helps him so warm blood is a good skill. Burning Heart will help with low HP and add some punch to fire assaults.

:

go to sumo if you can get it

:

there are many options. One that is pretty straight forward is:

agility -> dodge -> elasticity, vacuum disc -> black hole

with elasticity and adrenalin, you can get the doubleskill overload, more doublehits. Don't get blackhole if you don't plan on joining a clan or fighting PvP.

:

Fighters should never get c-skills, unless they unlock an important fighting skill. A possible build that you could use is the following. It leaves out some skills, but this way you get a good fighting dino earlier. If you hit pretty often when leveling up, you might want to take the blazing twilight path as well.

For PvP purposes, saving puree is an option as well.
