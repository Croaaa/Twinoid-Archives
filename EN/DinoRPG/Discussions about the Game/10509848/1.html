Strategy guide can be used to turn on and of some skills.

Go the Volcanic mountain and talk to the Soft Shaman and do the 2 first quests.

1 The Testing Ordeal
-> Go to Ashpouk Ruins
-> Find the Dictionary (University)
-> Go to Ashpouk Ruins
-> Go to Lavapit and Validate the Mission

Reward: 20 XP

2 The Archelionscarer
-> Go to Venerable's Lair
-> Go to the Forges Of The Big All-Hot
-> Speak with the Forges Guard
-> Kill 6 random monsters (stay at the Forge!)
-> Speak with the Forges Guard
-> Kill 6 random monsters (stay at the Forge!)
-> Speak with the Forges Guard
-> Go to Lavapit and Validate the Mission

Reward: 60 XP and the Strategy guide

Good luck!
