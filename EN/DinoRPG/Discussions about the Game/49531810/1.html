On october 24, 2015

To freeze a dino you will have to collect several status items with your dino to reach the Deep Gorges location on the Big-All-Hot map. There you can talk to the Popotholeler to freeze your dino. How and where to get all those items can be found on clan pages of several clans or for example on Rianon's page.
