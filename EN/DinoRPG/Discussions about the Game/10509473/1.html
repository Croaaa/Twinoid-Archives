Quick advice: Don't get attack skills that aren't in your Dinoz' main element(s), unless you plan to get a specific skill in that skill line.

As for a Wanwan, the Agility path is a good choice since it unlocks Dodge (10% evasion), Jump (ability to hit flying enemies), and Elasticity, which combines with Adrenaline for the Double Skill Overload (+15% to multi-attack chance).
