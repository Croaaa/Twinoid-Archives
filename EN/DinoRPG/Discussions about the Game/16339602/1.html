As a level 49 pteroz owner myself, I'm not sure whether I can answer your question correctly. Why? Because it decided to specialise in rather than . And so I would say that they are alright because of their sudden burst damage (not effective against bubble dinoz) and high initiative.

But usually pteroz lack initiative (they don't always get that much like my one) and tend to lack lots of , unless it ends up with all skills, giving it 70 (+30 from mutation, so +100). Their common advantages are high chance of counter attack (up to 35% if you get the double skill), high speed, assuming it has celerity, brave, specialist (4 hits in a cycle rather than 5 so you can hit with the 2 strongest elements 50% of the time. This also strengthens counter a bit) and live wind, immunity to negative statuses (breath of life or self control) and the pteroz invocation which is pretty good against and dinoz, but not effective against bubble dinoz, and dinoz.

In conclusion, it's fair to say that they aren't really significant dinoz. Most of their abilities are easily countered. Although, I would like to see a combo of multiple pteroz equipped with TM, beer, and potions in practice. So Drp1ng, you could try this combo.
