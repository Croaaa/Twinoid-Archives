I agree with pretty much everything non-sober Ndeline said!

Some things I'd like to mention:

I see your builds are still for level 70. As Ndeline pointed out, there will likely not be new skills with level 80. So unless you want to power level to 70 right away, its probably better to plan your dinoz for level 80 if you play more long-term based. (plan them for level ~75 so you have a couple of levelups that can fail, allowing you more air to breathe (depending on if you have CP and easy elements to roll)).

You mentioned Frear Factor twice, for a Sirain and a Quetzu, I think for Fear Factor your brave dinoz need to have the same starter element as the dinoz it wants to follow. You also need TWO Fear Factors for two brave dinoz. AF clan link, my source and more info on FF (<- thanks to Sveis for finding that:p)

Personally I dont really like Double Face (air-ether). Its a silly skill, you cant turn it off. Twinoid 500mg is another option you could consider (in stead of qi gong) if you want just 7 air (roll away from air with CP). It requires the -15% endurance recovery from Whips, but it might be worth it if you dont like Double Face and can get other skills in other elements.

I agree with Ndeline on clepto, it weakens your own dino ALOT. And what does it accomplish? Maybe hoping that a poisonite is blocked and you can poison an enemy? Whereas you expect your strong opponents to have SC or at least LB anyway? Don't ruin your dino with -10 init and -20% speed.

Genral rough rules I use for ether: Air ether skills are pretty bad. Water, only MV is ok-ish (I say ok-ish since from 9 levels only MV is usefull and god banned can also be pretty nice). In lightning is not that much, however I personally like Love Hertz (which Ndeline also mentioned), +20% dodge could be the answer to the high amount of multihits and whistle users, and lighting has Cardiac massage aswell. Fire ether: relatively decent, Burning Fever being a key skill. Wood ether: Obviously very good. Even dinoz with low wood-chance will want to try and get Life Source.
