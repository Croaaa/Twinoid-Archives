XP is not split between the Dinoz.
The XP is reported as the sum of the total XP earned by all Dinoz in the group.

If a group of 4 earns 8 , it is not necessarily 2 each - but it could be. Alternatively, one might have earned 5 with the other three getting 1 each.

As has been said however, you will meet more monsters but each Dinoz will earn less than it would if it defeated the monster on its own. The big advantage is losing less health per Dinoz and therefore fighting monsters more often for more

If you keep all the dinoz at the same level within a group then they will all get the same amount of XP unless they have any XP modifiers liek "Intelligence"

Xp is always calculated seperatly for each dino, whether there in a group or not. And it is calculated on number of enemies, what an enemy gives in XP depends on the type of enemy monster and what level your dino is.

Now after the XP is calculated a group modifier is imposed, for the following group numbers XP is reduced as described.

2 Dinoz - 75%
3 Dinoz - 75%
4 Dinoz - 62.5%
5 Dinoz - 50%

But generally speaking if your dinoz is grouped it will earn more XP as there will be say 2 wolfs every fight instead of just 1.
2 * 75% is still more than 1.

Now as for what number of dinoz per group is best, that is easy, it is 3. At 3 dinoz you will always hit maximum number of enemies possible, starting from level 2. At level 1 being solo is best. A group of 2 however won't be as efficient as fighting solo at level 2.
However eventually a solo dino will maximise enemies in an area. What level depends on the area, I'm sure you can work it out yourself, In the forest for example, when your level 30 ish, being solo is better than being grouped.

This all based on XP maximisation. Which at the end of the day makes all your dinoz the best they can be as quick as they can get there. However there are other things to consider, HP loss and Gold Gained, both of these are better in a group, the bigger the group the better it is. Though Gold Gain varies very little from 2 man groups to 5 man groups it will make some difference. In a 5 dinoz group HP is hardly ever lost, as your dinoz first moves usually irradicate all enemies before they get a turn. However XP is hurt by 25%.

Thus the best group number is 3, it gives best XP, a good reduction in HP loss, and in Monetary Gain.
