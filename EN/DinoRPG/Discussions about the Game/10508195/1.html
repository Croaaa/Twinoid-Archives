There are features I look for in each type of Dinoz. In the Pteroz, I like the five eyes and the long nose. In the Rocky, I like the gold nugget in the hand. In the Winks, it has to have claws, and I also like the spiked back. In the Softpig, I like the long tail with the tuft at the end, but the horns and the head-fins together are very cool. With the Moueffe, I like the outstretched fingers with the claws, and the spiked knees.

Just browse through everyone's Dinoz in the Ranking pages... and if that isn't enough check out the Classement pages in the French version.

The looks of the Dinoz are one of the best parts of collecting the Dinoz.
