There are two ways of moving. In your account options (on the right menu, my account -> modify my information -> speed up moving on the map radio button) you can choose between them:
-When you click on the dinoz, there will be 'new turn', 'move', 'fight', 'follow', 'rest' and other options, according to the palce and quest you're in. If you choose the move, then click on the place on the map you want to travel and voila.
-If you have the radio button clicked, then if the dinoz is active (it has turn), you just have to click on the destionation on the map.

You can only fight and move if your dinoz have a turn. Dinoz lower than lvl20 gets a new turn every x hours, where x is their level. Dinoz lvl20+ get a new turn every midnight. You can also have new turns by using madam irma's potions from the shop.
