Every 5th of each month, I will (try) launch the DinoRPG EN Lottery, and it will end on the 25th. Rules are as follows:
Only one participation will be accepted*.
To participate, you need to write 5 numbers understood between 1 to 20, and one additional lucky number between 1 to 10. You can also use the dice commands (in RP tags) to decide more randomly*.

Two numbers cannot be the same, except of the lucky one with a normal one.
* In case you get two same normal numbers through dice commands, you can repost until you get the correct ones, previous posts will be erased.

On the 25th, I will use the dice tags to launch the results and you will have to check if any numbers correspond with yours. Please keep track of your numbers by copy/pasting the "share" link (right bottom corner of your post) on your Twinoid notepad.

Rewards will be as follows:
Jackpot with 6 numbersA special unique Epic Reward + a Mahamuti Egg + a napodinoz.2nd prize with 5 normal numbers or 4 + the lucky number150 demon tickets to share.3rd prize with 4 normal numbers or 3 + the lucky number100.000 to share.

If any of you won, please send a PM to me. Rewards will be distributed some times after the event finish but before the next lottery starts.

FR Gazette Questions to devs - March 2015

1. When will be the next new map? what will be its contents, new quest, missions, items?
The new map is still in pre-production, there will surely be new missions, but we cannot tell more about it.

2. When will be the lv80? And will there be new skills on current or new skill tree?
The new skill tree isn’t scheduled yet to come. There will be new skills on current tree for the lv80 (nb: Kiroukou didn’t know we discovered all the skills when he wrote that, as he thought undiscovered being new...). There won’t be a new skill tree.

3. Will there be two CotC per year?
Perhaps, but the calendar is already filled out.

4. Will the AP system for lower levels be upgraded?
We already thought about it, but we don’t know in which way right now.

5. If a dinoz with Sharingan steal one skill he has already deactivated, what will happen?
Better test it yourself in dojo, and find it all along.

Short news but still news:

  • Multi-hits: We will put a maximum on consecutive hits, something around 8-10. If it's a too big nerf, we will rollback on that decision.
  • Accepted Amphitheater suggestion on Whistle: Whistle holder cannot follow other whistle holders.
