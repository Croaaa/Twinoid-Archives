To elaborate - Basically you fight whichever enemies are in the zone you are moving to, not from.

For example, moving from the precipitous hills to the basalt slopes, you get fire monsters, because you are moving into the 'fire' zone.

So yeah, because the frish port is in dinoland, you fight dinoland monsters.

Although I always think in the transition places, you should get a random mix of the monsters from both zones
