Well, you might be 200 demon notes short for both wanwan and winks demons. The Winks combination of Water and Lightning can be quite powerful.

You could swing, Normal Wanwan (leader), 2x Demon Winks, a Rocky and 1x Kabuki... with your Demon Notes and Kabuki Eggs... and then also raise the second Kabuki, and level them as two teams at the start.
Wanwan (leader), 2x Demon Winks, Rocky, 2x Kabuki (not using one of the Kabuki in the final team).
The final team of 5 would probably contain the better team Kabuki of course... or skip the second Demon Winks and save for a Demon Wanwan, and raise a second Rocky in its place.
Wanwan (leader), Demon Winks, 2x Rocky, 2x Kabuki (not using one of the Kabuki in the final team).

The most important thing would be to use good builds, because poorly leveling Demons or Kabuki would be a huge waste.
