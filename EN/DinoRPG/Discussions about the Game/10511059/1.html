Maybe before suggesting to get gatherers, it must be important to explain what's a gatherer... And an attacker, and a defender.

Basically, for each dino you have you should have a plan of the skills you want that dino to get, with attention to what are the most probable elements that dino will get. You can have one or two out of three objectives in your choice of skills, in what will be the usefulness of your dino: a gathering dino, an attack dino, or a defender dino.
