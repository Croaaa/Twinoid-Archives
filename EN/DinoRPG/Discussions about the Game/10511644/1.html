Well, it's a curse caused by the cursed stick.
If you want to earn experience again, you need to go to the cursed shop, at the Ashpouk Ruins. This shop can only be seen by a cursed dino. There, you can buy a salve (or ointment, I don't know its name in English). It will cost you 6000 /salve. The salve will stop the effect of the curse.
