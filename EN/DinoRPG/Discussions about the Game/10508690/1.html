looks like you never been there before, so you will need to come from grumhel forest. Check the game maps, go frish port to islands area, mutant falls to grumhel forest. It will be a long way, since you need to do certain things to get access to grumhel forest. -RangerJoe

A long journey, indeed.
First, you'll need the zor's gloves. To get'em, you need to dig at the Fountain of Youth, Basalt Slopes, and the Sticky Swamp to get three things. Then go to Underwater Dome and talk with the old man who ask you for the three things, then you will fight the Earth Elemental. Win the batle and you will obtain the gloves. Then go to Mutant Falls and speak with Master Hydargol and he will give you a waterlily pad, which let you enter the Grumhel Forest. Then go to the Haunted Hills and talk with a strage man, then you will fight with a korgon (very easy), after defeating it, the man will give you the lantern. -w3ko
