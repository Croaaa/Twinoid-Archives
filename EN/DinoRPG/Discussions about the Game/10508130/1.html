If you did spend all your gold already, I suggest restarting your account now. That way you'll be able to:

1) have 20,000
2) use only 16,000 to buy a Dino (as opposed to wasting all 20,000 )
3) save the leftover gold to work on your second Dino
4) use your free Madam Irma potions to complete the missions in the most efficient manner

Don't buy Madam Irma Potions: For 900 you get an action that will net you ~200 . You lose ~700 .

There's a thread elsewhere with a walkthrough for Grandpa Joe's missions, as well as a few other threads strewn around for other missions. They seem to give only paltry experience but a few of them give good chunks of gold (500/2000/8000 ).

Those are some general strategies. Again, if you have no gold now, you'd be in a better position if you restart the game and follow those strategies. Even 6000 (which you will have if you restart the game and follow those strategies) is equivalent to 30 days of ~200 fights. You give up 7 days for 30 days.

Good luck.
