Most Loyal Dinoz Masters!

After a huge delay, we are proud to officially announce the winner of the missing Inter-Dojo Tournament XXIII: Venar!

Venar has accomplished this impressive feat once before and he has now done it again! He's been known as discoverer of frontiers, extender of boundaries, explorer of the unknown! He has more diversity in his company than most others out there and he's not afraid to venture into unknown lands! Hoth, his unstoppable canine armed with the new skills, Rock Thrower and Life Source as well as the almighty invocation, Werewolf, was this time accompanied by a silver jewel of a winks, Bling and Venar's legacy from old times, a living fossil, Blue Milk the Hippoclamp! The equally awesome opponent was Bengeldorn, with the gargantuan Baby Longtooth, the mighty Hairy Copper and the massive Rune Walker. Bengeldorn's trail of destruction featured Koakuma, Raynor and Larisek, while the ones left in ruins by Venar were Loppermann, Aleblacksito and Meischner...

In the finals, there were burning rocks of fiery destruction falling, the earth trembled numerous times and even the god of red eyes and white fangs descended! However, when the smoke cleared, one team was still standing, thus we once again are proud to present Venar as the winner of the XXIII. International Dojo Tournament! Congratulations!
