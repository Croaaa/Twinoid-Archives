DarkGryphon, the math isnt quite that simple. The odds for each square you pick depend on how many items and squares remain on the grid.
For example, say you bought 5 tickets and there are 5 santaz in a grid of 100 squares:
-For your first square you have a 5/100 (5%) chance of getting a Santaz.
-For your second square, your odds depend on whether you got a santaz in your first square. If you did not get a santaz then you have a 5/99 (5.05%) chance of getting a santaz with your second square. If you got a santaz in you first square, your odds of also getting a santaz on the second square drop to 4/99 (4.04%).
I know there is a formula for calculating your odds with this type of situation but I cannot remember it off the top of my head.
