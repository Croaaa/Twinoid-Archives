First, you must focus on buying 18 dinoz. The more dinoz you have, greater is your income.
Do not spend money in other way than buying dinoz as long as you got less than 18 dinoz.

If you want to grow the quicker way, you can block your first dinoz level 11 and fight and collect twice per day.
Your 9 first dinoz must be this way.

You need to go to Papy Joe house to do his mission.

Better dinoz to collect item to sell are :
Lightning dinoz : Glidwing.
You need to rush the collect-skills to improve your income:
When your dinoz level up is Lightning choose :
Intelligence then unlock, then chose Lightning Conductor, then unlock, then Elemental Fission.
Don't forget to block your dinoz level 11, a level 11 dinoz with no collect skill is better than a level 13 dinoz with all the collect skill.
If you got extra lightning up, try to unlock one more time, then chose Merchant. You need only 1 "Merchant" dinoz per account.

You can group up to 3 dinoz. They will lose less health point if you do that.
When you buy your 9th dinoz come see us again so we can tell you what to do next.

Rianon's is a helpfull guide to help you but i can't find it on the net.

Have a good start ^^ and see you.
