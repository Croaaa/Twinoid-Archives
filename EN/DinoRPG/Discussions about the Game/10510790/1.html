Immaterial is a status which your dinoz can acquire in one of two ways: the Vaporous Form skill and the Etheral Form skill.

If your dinoz is immaterial, he will take no damage from attacks whatsoever. However there are quite a few ways to drop immaterial status (Primal State, an assault from a dinoz with Perception, or any air assault, whether the attacking dinoz has Perception or not). I believe it is also dropped by sufficient accumulated damage, as it will not last long against the Venerable's Meteors attack and eventually wears out against other monster opponents as well.

Etheral Form (a level 6 air skill) means that your dinoz will start each fight with the Immaterial status.

Vaporous Form (a level 3 air skill) means that there is a slight chance of your dinoz going immaterial before any enemy attack (which means it will not take damage from that attack and the status stays until it is dropped). It is not an E skill; it activates only when your dinoz is defending, not when it is attacking.

Given that there are so many ways to defeat it, I don't think Etheral Form is worth the massive speed cost. However, it's nice to have Vaporous Form kick in every once in a while.
