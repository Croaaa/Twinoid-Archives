Maximum number of active dinoz is 21 (If you have the woodskill called LEADER). If you buy more then that you must freeze an equal amount of dinoz that you buy.

Example: You have 21 dinoz and buy 2 more, then you have to frezze 2 of the 21 active dinoz to be able to play with the 2 new dinos you just bought!
