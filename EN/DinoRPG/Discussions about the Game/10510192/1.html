Oh, thanks for clarifying Julien.

You want Air gatherers, specifically the Sharp-Eye skill for gathering plants. Although building, rebuilding and upgrading castles use other ingredients, they are basically never in short supply. Plants howerver are the only thing used for declaring war, and in 2 months every single clan runs out of their supply.

If you want to be attractive to a clan, only Air gatherers with Sharp-Eye will interest them.

With getting "Sharp-Eye" in mind, the best dinoz for that are Cloudoz, Pteroz, Santaz, and Kabuki. Kabuki you're limited to 3 of them and they will run you about 50k each assuming you buy the ingredients. Santaz are closer to 200k each, so those are out as well.

That leaves Cloudoz vs Pteroz. They each have the same chance of getting air levelups, but pteroz cost 6k more each, so I go with cloudoz.

Bottom line, get 3 cloudoz, and rush them for Sharp-Eye skill. By lvl 18 or so all 3 should have it.
