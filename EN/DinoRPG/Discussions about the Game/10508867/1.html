What you do is you click rest with dino 1 and then move with dino 2. When your timer is up, click new turn with dino 2. This gives ALL your dinos a new turn. Move with dino 2 and don't do anything with dino 1. When your counter is up again, go to dino 1 and stop resting. You will still have a turn you can use because you clicked new turn the day before. Use that turn with dino 1 and then the new turn button will be there immediately because your timer has already run out. You get 1 "move" on day 2 and 3 "moves" on day 3 so you haven't really lost the move you didn't use day 2 on the resting dino and you get 2 days of healing.

I hope that makes sense, I keep trying to find a simpler way to say it and I can't.
