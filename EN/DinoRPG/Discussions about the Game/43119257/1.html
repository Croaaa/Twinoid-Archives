Let's gather info about who cancel and who doesn't cancel immaterial status, coming from etheral form skill. In pvp it is all clear dinoz with perception remove immaterial status with any assault and dinoz without perception and with at least one level up in air remove immaterial status with air assault. Also all air based A- E- skills remove immaterial status.

- Bambooz friend can not remove immaterial
- Korgon Reinforcements of players dinoz have an element cycle of Wo>F=Wa=L=A (meanig wood has highest element value and comes first, while all other are at same element value and come in random order). So the Reinforcements can cancel immaterial status, while the PvE monsters can't.

sjefkeKorgons can cancel immaterial status.

Yes, and no.
Korgon Reinforcements of players dinoz have an element cycle of Wo>F=Wa=L=A (meanig wood has highest element value and comes first, while all other are at same element value and come in random order)

Facetious Korgons from the Grumhel Forest on the other hand have a cycle of Wo>F and then repeat this after only 2 attacks.

So the Reinforcements can cancel immaterial status, while the PvE monsters can't.

All Korgon Avengers (Dinotown Kingdom & Islands) have Wo>F, too.

Also wanting to add that - like Giant Chicken - Gluons also have a cycle of Wo=F=Wa=L=A, so they can remove imma. If you are lucky the air attack can be overtriggered by absorbtion though, which counts as a water a-skill.

Not sure about Don Fuego having perception or not, but he has Void attacks only, so no air element assault.

Trip The Wimp has Wo=F=Wa=L=A so he can cancel imma status.
