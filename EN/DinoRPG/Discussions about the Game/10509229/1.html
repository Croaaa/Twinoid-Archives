You lose one turn, but you can rest 46 hours without loosing another one, if you have another dinoz.

-> Start resting with one dinoz and make your turn with other dinoz as normal.
->After the 23 hours timer has run off, klick new turn at a dinoz which is not resting. This enables a new turn for all dinoz (even the resting ones) and immediately resets the timer. So, your resting dinoz has a turn enabled, when he stops resting, but don't stop jet!
->Let the timer run off again. Now, before you klick on new turn again, stop the resting of the dinoz and do his move first. If you would klick new turn before that, the other turn you saved 23 hours ago would be lost, because turns are not stacked.

This way, you rested 46 hours with one turn, and can do two turns in a row with this dinoz after that.

You can do the fountain thing with or without resting.

(These examples are without using irmas)

With resting:
->you go to the fountain
->you wait 23 hours and gain 5
->you klick "new turn" and start resting
->you wait 23 hours and gain 28 (23+5)
->you klick "new turn" with another dinoz
->you wait 23 hours and gain 28
->you stop resting and use your turn
->you klick new turn and use the next turn.

But you can use this tactic without resting, when you are just com past the fountain on your journey.
->you go to the fountain
->you wait 23 hours and gain 5
->you klick "new turn"
->you wait 23 hours and gain 5
->you move on
->you klick "new turn" and move again.

So, you stayed 2 days at the fountain, without losing the turn of the first day.

(You could also fight the first day and stay at the fountain, but that wouldn't be as effective, because when you move, you fight automatically )

The difference that a lot of people miss is how the new turn button works.

I think a lot of people expect new turn to mean they need to use that turn right now. Instead, new turn just enables you to use a turn, and starts counting till the next time you can click it.

So if you wanted to use two turns right next to each other, for example, you can click new turn but then not use your turn until 23.9 hours later. 0.1 hr from then, the waiting timer ends and you can click new turn again, only this time you use it right away. This way you've got two turns within 10 minutes, even though your turn counter is 24 hrs and you havent used any irma potions.

Over time it evens out to 1 per 24 hrs, but this is one way to do things in groups, like crossing the sticky swamp to avoid a fight, or passing through the fountain of youth at 00:00 for your free hp.
