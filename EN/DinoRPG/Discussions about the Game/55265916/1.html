Let us collect all known info on subject:
1. Rebirth is possible on lvl 80, Dinoz get 10 additional elements and becomes a lvl 1 dinoz. Dinoz keeps one (any: usual (also double skills included) or invocation or ether or spherical) skill from those, that it had at lvl 80.
2. Rebirth costs nothing, but dinoz needs to die.
3. Your dino starts at lvl 1 with enough xp for 9 level ups (so you hit lvl 10).
4. Rebirth is not stackable with reincarnation. Your dinoz gets only 10 additional element loosing those 5 from reincarnation.
5. You get both reincarnation icon and rebirth icon after rebirth. And you can't reincarnate reborn dinoz. You also can't rebirth your dinoz second time.
6. You lose all spheres upon rebirth but you can chose spherical skill as the start skill for reborn dinoz.
7. Skill that was kept upon rebirth doesn't count to element numbers of dinoz.
8. It is not possible to unlock skills behind or adjacent to the skill you took upon rebirth unless you take all skills leading to that skill after rebirth again.
Example: If you take Ventral Pouch as the rebirth skill you will need Mutation - Unlock - Unlock - Sumo/WM so 4/5 water rolls to hit Sumo/Wm source

You keep any skill you choose, and start leveling all over again. You will not need to unlock Ether to rebirth your skill.
