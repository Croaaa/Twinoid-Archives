The nights are drawing in... the cold, winter nights... and as dinoz masters retreat to the warmth of their homes and dojos, there are fewer enforcers around to keep the peace, and that's when they come. The evil beasts from the dark world... they come looking to feast on the ill-prepared and the timid alike. However, sometimes the only thing that cuts through the winter chill is the heat of battle and sometimes the only language these dark world monsters understand is that of violence.

To arms, masters! As has been previously shown, the King's generosity is legendary. Especially when it comes to keeping these lands safe from invaders...

The Halloween Hunt begins NOW and will last until the last of these wretched creatures is slain or turns tail and runs back to whence it came. We are not expecting an invasion of epic proportions, but there will be enough monsters to keep you all busy for a few days, of that we're sure. Unless information is released to the contrary, or you come across monsters the likes of which you have never seen before, the hunt should be over at 00h00 on Sunday the 4th of November.

Rewards will be announced in due course... right now!

The top hunters will win:

1st place: 1 Etherwasp Egg + Epic hunting Reward + 15 Treasury Notes
2nd - 5th place: 1 Feross Egg + 10 Treasury Notes
6th - 10th place: 1 Kabuki Egg + 5 Treasury Notes
11th - 20th place: 5 tac tickets.
21st to 50th place: 50 Treasury Notes.
51st to 100th place: 25 Treasury Notes.

That's not all!

Every time you take a step up your personal points ladder, there's more to be won:

500 points: 5 Irma's Potions
1000 points: 2 Warm Breads
1500 points: 2 Warm Breads + 3 Angel Potions
2000 points: 1 Pack of Nimbao objects
3000 points: 2 Tac Tickets
4000 points: 200 Demon Tickets
5000 points: 1 Napodino
