As you may not know, we can ask some few questions to the DinoRPG developers team on each FR Gazette edition.

Posts have to be formatted this way:

QX (number X): Short and concise question in bold

If argumentation, in italics

You cannot suggest though, any kind of suggestion disguised will be shot down. If you find any questions you like or dislike, you can post a +1 or a constructive -1.

The most likened (or +1) questions will go through and selected by the FR moderation team and myself. Then it will be answered on this month Gazette edition.

French players are forbidden to post on this topic, since they got their own on FR forums.

Kayin

For FF:
- If FF holder is the leader, any dinoz can follow it (up to 3 and plus follower skill, Sympathetic, Charisma and Aphrodisia).
- If FF holder is following a dinoz it needs at least one same starting elements.
i.e:
- A Bbrave Softpig could only follow a dinoz with a starting element in fire: Quetzu, Moueffe, Santaz, Feross etc... But not a Sirain.
- A Brave Sirain could follow only starting water.
- Any Brave can follow an Hippo.
