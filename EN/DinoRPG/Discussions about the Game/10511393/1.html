Sendos, magic resistance is a lot more spammable, while primal state has a good activation chance, compared to really low activation chances from argueably more powerful skills such as Tornado for instance thus why I refer to it as spammable because you can see a few activations in a row where as the chances of seeing say 3 tornados in a row or 3 black holes in a row would be much much smaller.

Magic resistance has a higher activation chance than Primal state, so if you have no other useful E skills, ( such as primal state / vines / korgon ) magic resistance is actually pretty useful, but the general fact is you would never choose magic resistance given a choice compared to much more useful paths so you normally never come across it, unless forced to take it on a high leveled gorilla. It would be hard for me to estimate a % activation on Magic resistance as I have no experience using and about as little seeing it in combats. But I do know for sure that it is higher than that of the primal state, so if you want Magic resistance constantly removing status affects its a good enough skill.
