With more dinoz, you will get more fun (because every turn you can make one move with each dinoz), more money (because you will battle twice as much or at least with more enemies if you group the dinoz together) and chance to explore other species and other skills.

"And when i do a move with one of my dino, can i make another move with other dino?"

Yes, you can set dinoz to Follow other ones. Up to three dinoz can be in a group normally, and there are skills to increase that limit up to five.

Questing in groups: The only slower part about questing in groups is that they don't get simultaneous credit for kills. (i.e., If you need to kill 6 Smashrooms for a quest, a group of two dinoz will need to kill 12.) Anything else, all the dinoz can get credit for on the same turn.
