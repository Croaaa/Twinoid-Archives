This game is somewhat different from Dinoparc. In Dinoparc, you can fight, then move to a new location, or move to a new location, and then find some other Dino to fight.

Here, you can choose to either stay in one place and fight another monster,(That's the fastest way to complete Joe's smashroom/wolf quest!) move to a new location and fight whatever is there, or rest, and allow your Dino to heal at the rate of 1 HP/hour.(up to a maximum of 1/2 of your dino's Maximum HP.)

While you are resting, you probably will *NOT* be attacked... I'm not certain about that. I have not explored all of this world and I don't know about all of the skills that are available for our dinos to learn in this game.

The Game help says that there are skills available that will allow your resting dino to heal more rapidly, but I don't know what they are... and there might be skills that allow High-Level Dinos to hunt down the resting Dinos.

Just doing my best to help!

DuncanOShea.
