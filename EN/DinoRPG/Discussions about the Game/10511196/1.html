Keep your 50k gold on hand (or be ready to buy gold at any time) and talk to yakuzi at the island every hour that you have acess to dino rpg each day. Eventually one of the hours you picked will be the one that was randomly choosen, unless your checking 18 hours a day, and even if you are, it can still take a while to guess correctly. As their is no "set" hour, i believe its completely random, theirs not 1 a day or 1 a week. I think it just does a random check each hour to see if yakuzi will sell an egg or not.
