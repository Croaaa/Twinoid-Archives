Now the element cycle only is used for normal attacks. If ur dino decides to use a skill like water canon or mistreal, this will replace the original attack and the damage ist calculated by a multiplicator of the element of the attack (i think water canon=7*, mistreal=3*:air. He will the go on to the next element with his normal attacks, so mistral is a bad choice for dinoz with low air element, becaus it can replace the much stronger normal attack of ur main element.
But u can get an item later that can turn of skills like mistreal.

Hope this cleares our confusion.
