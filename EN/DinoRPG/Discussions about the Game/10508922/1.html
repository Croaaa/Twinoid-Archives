I believe its completely random. The money that you earn is whatever the game sets up at the place that you are fighting on. If you have 2 dinoz they will just multiply the money. for example $153*2= $306 per fight. So if your lucky youll land on a huge number like 700 and get $1400 per fight (i did this yesterday). Also, it is better to group the dinoz together because it will save you from rest/healing time. The dinoz will take little to no damage in most fights. So this will save you on money if you buy health and time for resting. It really depends on you.. But for me i would much rather lose 1-2 hp per fight than lose 9-11 hp alone and hve to rest every 3-4 days
