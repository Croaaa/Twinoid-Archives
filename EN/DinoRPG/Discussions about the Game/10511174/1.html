You will find it takes about a month, taking all your turns when they are due, to get the second Dinoz. Without the starting Irma's, shorter turns, etc, it takes a month to get the third from the income from the first two (stick to the 16K Dinoz for the first few). It gets quicker and quicker to get each new Dinoz, until about 15 Dinoz when it takes less than a week.

Yes it is slow at the start, but you can take your time to plan and learn the game. If you get impatient and buy food or Irmas it will take longer to get more Dinoz.
