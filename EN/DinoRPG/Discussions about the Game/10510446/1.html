There are three independent actions during a dinoz's "turn".

Item use
'E' action
'A' action

You can have all 3 of, or 2, or none.

The 'A' Action replaces the standard attack, but the Item use and 'E' Action don't.

If you rest, you waste two turns worth of hunting (that's 6 sqaures of searching if you get all the skills)!

Resting uses one action.

And in fact cost nothing in term of gathering.

Do the following (assuming you play at 0:00) :

J0[00:01] Take new Turn
J0[00:01] Gather
J0[00:01] Rest
J1[00:01] Take new Turn
J1[23:59] Finish to Rest
J1[23:59] Gather
J1[23:59] Act
J2[00:01] Take new Turn
J2[00:01] Gather
J2[00:01] Act

In these 3 days, your dinoz has performed :
3 Gathering
2 Actions (like fighting)
47 hours of rest (meaning +94 hp if you have the priest skill)
