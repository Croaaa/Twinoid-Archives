Sanjar
There are many ways to work around the castle-defending problem and none are guaranteed to be successful. The smaller the clan and the more limited the available resources, the harder it is to risk taking that gamble. You could defend against an equal-sized clan for days successfully and have that entire defense wiped out when a larger clan does its rounds. When you're working with limited resources, this really is not an option.

Arilds, I agree on the need for a ranked clan war system. It would provide everyone with an enjoyable level of competition and allow them the freedom to choose their degree of investment and appropriate playstyle. Things like leaving a castle undefended are silly and counterintuitive. Although the current structure encourages innovative strategy-making and planning (and a good bit of gambling), it diminishes the spirit of competition in the long run. It's challenging, and that's always enjoyable, but perhaps it's challenging in all the wrong ways.

If I were in a top clan, I'd rather fight well-stocked defenses than burn through castle after castle with empty walls. Of course, it's a money-sink and smaller clans have to go through the same deal because that's the way this war is run. But perhaps toning down the senseless and unnecessary spending will increase the actual and more thoughtful spending in the long run.
