A lot of issues that seem important at low levels is probably not as important at high levels.

- 1 turn per 23 hours. But if you have 20 dinoz, you effectively have 20 turns.

- hard to get, bank idea. If you have 20 dinoz, and (conservative estimate) ten are resting and ten fighting, then you will get around 2500 per day.

- higher healed during rest. At 23 hours turn timer, you can already get 45 healed at the loss of one turn. With additional skills, I believe you can triple that to 135 for the loss of only one turn. That should be sufficient for any dinoz, because you cannot lose only 1/2 a turn.

I do think that when you increase your by a skill, the increase should be healed .

- Random items dropped in combat. If you do that, and have ten fights a day, your inventory will fill up very quickly. It will also devalue the items because it makes them easier to get.
