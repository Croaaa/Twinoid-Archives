Grandpa Joe's Mission tree:

The Lost Dog: Angel Potion, no missions unlocked

Fresh Fish: Unlocks The Hill's Smashrooms and The Rose Bush in Danger

The Hill's Smashrooms: 500, Unlocks Wolf Hunting
Wolf Hunting: 2000, Unlocks A Strange Monster
A Strange Monster: 500, end of branch

The Rose Bush in Danger: Unlocks The Recipe Book
The Recipe Book: Unlocks The Stamps
The Stamps: Unlocks The Confidential Letter
The Confidential Letter: end of branch

The Giants: Higher level needed
The Month Exploit: Higher level needed
