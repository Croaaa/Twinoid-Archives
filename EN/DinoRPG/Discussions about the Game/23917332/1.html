[ http://twinoid.com/en/article/4201/anzac-day-hunt ]

As some of you may be aware, the 25th of April marks the anniversary of the first military action involving both Australian and New Zealand troops. On ANZAC Day, these countries commemorate those lost in service as well as service people who returned home.

Your DinoRPG clanmates are ready to make the ultimate sacrifice for you, as service people the world over do for their brothers in arms. In commemoration of this, the King has commissioned a hunt to rid the lands of invaders once again. The King has gone further than ever to reward the bravest warriors.

The hunt begins now, and will continue until the 2nd of May 2013, at 12:00 server time (CEST). The prizes for the top hunters will be as follow:

1st Place: 1 Etherwasp Egg + Epic Reward + 30 Treasury Notes*
2nd to 5th: 1 Kabuki Egg + 20 Treasury Notes*
6th to 10th: 1 Feross Egg + 15 Treasury Notes*
11th to 20th: 10 Tac Tickets*
21st to 50th: 50 Treasury Notes
51st to 100th: 25 Treasury Notes

Individual progress prizes are bigger than ever...

Happy ANZAC Day, and happy hunting!
