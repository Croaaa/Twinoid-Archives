If you've only one Dinoz, there is nothing that will boost the you get in a fight.

You should do some missions for more , though.

The Hills' Smashrooms gives you 500.
Wolf Hunting gives you 2000.

You should have 16,000 by the time you do both of those and The Lost Dog (which awards no money, but gives an Angel Potion), but if not, rest and try A Strange Monster for another 500.

A new player can, if they play every turn as soon as it comes up, get a second Dinoz in just about a week. (I won't post the numbers unless someone really wants to see!)
