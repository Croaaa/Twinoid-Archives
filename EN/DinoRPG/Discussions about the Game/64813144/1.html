Berserk doubles your attacks... if you have skill froger or whatever it's called, that should still be able to bypass that so that's probably your best offensive choice.

Mud wall is a decent defensive choice since it triggers on enemies attacks if I remember correctly.

AR is good to disable water spheres, so you don't get ini dumped, but that's not really effective.

I don't really know how good energy sink realistically is, but I think you need a high number of procs for it to outweigh the streght of the first 2 skills.

So it depends on how fast your dinoz is and how much ini it has.... or what purpouse it has, but berserk should be the best option probably, mud wall the safe option if you're not sure.

Just to make sure you know what berserk is... once your dinoz uses it, it cannot continue to use A, E and I skills, it can use P skills and skill forger can make it relearn skills again, even multiple times, so you can proc life source multiple times on one attack recieved.... I think .

Mud wall is just a passive shield that blocks incoming damage up to 40 I think.
