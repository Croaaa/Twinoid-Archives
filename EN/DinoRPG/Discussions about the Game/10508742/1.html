You should start with 10 (unless they changed it??)

If you are not going to spend any real money, you could use one or two a day, starting on day two or so, so that you can get a second Dinoz sooner.

Also if you want to get to the second Dinoz faster, you should use the food you are given to heal when you need to, rather than resting early in the game.

Don't spend any money at all, on potions or food, for as long as you can stand it. If you do the Missions Fresh Fish, The Hills' Smashrooms, and Wolf Hunting, you get extra gold for the second two.

Then if you do Lost Dog, you should be close to a Dinoz (or beyond it) by the time you finish that Mission.

That gives you more Dinoz, so more turns (or more money per turn if you group them).

By the time you get near a third Dinoz, you probably won't want to buy potions any more, having gotten used to the pace of the game.

Then you can buy and use potions when you want to get a dinoz to a certain place on a certain turn, so they can team up, or buy things at market. All that comes later.

Irma potions do not affect your turn timer. All it does is give your dinoz an extra turn straight away.

ie, if your turn timer is five hours, and you wait two hours before using your potion, your next turn is still three hours away.
