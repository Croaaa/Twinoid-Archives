Could it be a good strategy to sell of all items from start?
There are three ways to use ingredients:

1. Go to the Tamer Market and buy notes with them, to get things (trained dinoz) there.

2. Donate them to your clan, where they contribute to upkeep.

3. Sell them for gold.

If selling them, watch for where the merchant is and what he wants to buy.
