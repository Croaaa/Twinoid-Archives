In succinct, treasury notes are the only currency officially recognized in the marketplace.

In the marketplace, you cannot bid with actual gold; instead, you need to purchase treasury notes to make an offer. Each treasury note is worth 1,000 gold. You can redeem your treasury notes for the aforementioned amount at any time, but this cannot be undone. Thus, for you to obtain another treasury note, you will need to purchase one at the dealer for 1,196 gold.
