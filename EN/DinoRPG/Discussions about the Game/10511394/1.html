Nap: yes, it is an A skill. So when it activates during the fight, it does this replacig an attack (assault). So instead of hitting the enemy, your dinoz recovers up to 20 , then falls asleep (do nothing until until it gets some damage or simply a few turns (1-3) pass)

Sleeping is a negative status, so if you have self control then that part is simply ignored, only the recovery will happen (replacing an attack)

AndySQdancer
When your Dinoz uses the (A) Skill Nap, it/she/he immediately falls asleep in the fight and unless it/she/he has Self Control, it/she/he sleeps until one of the enemies attacks it/her/him. Also I believe it/she/he only sleeps for so many rounds, then wakes up. There are other things that can happen too I believe that can wake it/her/him. It/She/He gains up to 20 (22 if yoou have Cooker) that it/she/he has lost so far in the fight. Nap is much better with Self Control, since your Dinoz does not sleep the fight away.
