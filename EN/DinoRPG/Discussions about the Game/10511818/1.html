After some battles and frustrations in the Little League I have 3 aspects which in my eyes badly imbalance the Little League at least badly:

1. Poison (mostly poisonous claws)

Deals out humongous amounts of damage (usally about 60 pts) in 5 rounds and thus is by far more any dinoz up to lvl 20 could do with other skills. Only a few skills protect against it (usally not being able to get up to lvl 20) or equipment (which again is unavailble to us wee Little Leaguers usally).

So please put the damage dealt by poison in line with other elemental damage. This still deals a good amount of damage but not outright decides the battle...

2. Equipment

It is hilarious high level players with plenty of resources use Scale, Relaxitive Pills and the like in Little League, but it really screws the balance when they can re-equip in between each fight. So the resourceful players gain a huge advantage. Dinoz should just be equipable before they enter the battlefield, but not while they are on there IMHO (like it is with warm bread right now).

3. Attacks from behind barriers

How can it be that a dinoz being seperated from the combat join in, when a group is build up, but it could not attack the enemy dinoz by itself???

To draw a minimalistic map:
....
A*C.
*BD.
....

So A cannot attack B, but when C and D attack B from behind, A joins in as well. Makes no sense to me...

If anyone can counter my critics, feel free. But IMHO it totally thwarts Little League at the moment when very resourceful players who would do very well in Intermediate League dominate Little league and just create utter frustration there by using equipment their >lvl 20 dinoz gather and the Poison skill is just the by far most powerful skill in the battles and decides most of them...
