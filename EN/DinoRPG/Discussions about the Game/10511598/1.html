I see you only begun the game. When you begin, you start with something like 20.000, 10 Irma's potions, and some healing items.

You got normally only one turn, until your timer reach zero and you will got a new turn (at the beginning you got to wait a number of minutes, until you reach 23 hours).

But with Irma's you can have additional turns on one dinoz. Those potions can be bought at the "Shop" (the button below "Buy Coins"). They will cost you 900.

Stefkoss,
You will get a single turn in which you can either move or fight every day (more or less). You will earn gold for those actions and the more Dinoz you have, the more actions you can have and therefore the more gold you will earn.

You cannot accelerate by just playing the game.
You have to be very patient and play a little every day.
You should save your money... do not buy Irma's, do not buy food... save up and buy Dinoz as you have already been told.


If you want to accelerate, the best way at your level in the game is to BUY COINS... buy a load of gold and buy several Dinoz with that gold...

If you want to keep moving fast, you have to keep buying gold.

If you don't want to spend money then you have to be patient and enjoy a free game.
