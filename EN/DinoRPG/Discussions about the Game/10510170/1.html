Similar, but related...

If you have the Pearl you get 5 hp at midnight (server time) if you are at the Fountain.

So, move to the Fountain and at midnight you get your 5 hp.

Take your next turn with the other Dinoz, but wait until midnight and get another 5 hp for the Dinoz at the Fountain.

Before you press new turn, take the turn of the Fountain Dinoz. Double Pearl bonus!

Here's a little step-by-step guide I think may help everyone, regardless of English language skills.

00:00 hours is your turn time. Dino1 is down to 5.
Use an Irma potion. Rest.
At 00:00 the next day, use Dino2 for a New Turn.
Do not stop resting Dino1, who should have 28 now.
At 23:00+ hours - when your Dino1 hits 50 - stop resting.
You can now take your turn with Dino1.
Now you can take a New Turn, Dino1 gained 45 for only 900 and didn't miss any turns.

Clear enough for everyone?
