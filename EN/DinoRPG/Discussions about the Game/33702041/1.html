Dinoz Masters!

The 3rd great Mana War has finally come to an end. The dust is settling and silence looms over the battlefields and fallen castles...

Not in the Last order of Translylania HQ though, there'll be dancing in the streets of LooTsville tonight, and celebrations will go on for the foreseeable future!

After many momentous battles, fallen dinoz and troubles with the taxman, one clan showed the most enduring spirit, the desire to go one step further, to go one rung higher, and never say die.

Congratulations to the Last Order of Transylvania, who managed to keep the ever-dangerous Magyar Királyság at bay for just long enough to retain the top spot and the rewards that go with their impressive title!

Luso-Brasilian Team, German Connection, and Rākshasa round out your top 5. If you want to check out the rest of the rankings, you know what to do.

The rewards have been distributed, and we hope you enjoyed the 3rd Mana War. With your help, we'll make the 4th even better!

Until the next time...
