Softpig is the "best" one-hit dinoz for a while because of his built-in skill that gives a bonus on the first hit. Any other dinoz that has a single element focus (gorilloz, sirain, etc.) will be close behind.

After the first few levels, though, when dinoz start meeting multiple creatures, you'll find that almost any dinoz can kill a single one in the first hit if it gets reasonable skill ups, and other things will be more important... like the second hit (on the second creature) and skills that help in a longer fight.

You'll regret picking all your dinoz with early game one-hit capability in mind.
