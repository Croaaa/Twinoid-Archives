Wrath > Fury > Vengeance
Fury > Incandascant Aura > Meteors
Wrath > Warm Blood

Strength > Martial Arts > Waïkikidô

You should be able to get some more though, those are the ones you really want to get. You can get other skills like Nap and Self-Control if you want. Burning Claws might be a good idea too.

Generally stay away from Collect skills.

So:
Skill Level 1
Wrath and Strength

Skill Level 2
Fury, Warm Blood, and Martial Arts

Skill Level 3
Waïkikidô, Vengeance, and Incandascant Aura

Skill Level 4
Meteors

Skill Level 5
Brave if you want, but you'll need to get Nap and Self Control.
