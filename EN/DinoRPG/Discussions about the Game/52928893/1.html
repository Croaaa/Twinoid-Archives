There are various options for a wasp considering their wide spread chance on element rolls. First you will have to decide if you want your wasp to be a 6man leader (Sympathetic, Charisma, ether-Aphrodisia), which is mainly usefull for full Force Control - Friendly Whistle teams. So probably a lesser priority if you dont have at least a couple FC dinoz.

In my opinion you should focuss on the following skills. Skills in bold should be top priority. For the others you will have to see how many and which you want to fit in your build. Good luck.

Pre-ether: ET, Hive Queen, Vines, Primal State, Acrobat. Bubble, Sumo, Perception, Without Mercy, Electrolysis?. Career Plan, Leafy Dawn?, Overload?. Life Breath, + mentioned double skills.

Ether: Force Control, River of Life. MV?. Cardiac Massage. Qi Gong.
