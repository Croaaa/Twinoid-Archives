My lowest level frozen is level 16 so far, but I suppose you could get it done at a lower level if you tried harder .

This is the quickest way of doing a "freeze" but you may want to be doing missions whilst doing these. Best to earn as much gold as possible before freezing, you also will want U skills on useless frozen dinos, and even get a sphere with them, in which case their is no such thing as a "quick method" but if all you want to do is freeze some bad choices so you can get better new choices brought this is how...

level 1, go to grandpa joe, start doing his missions,

***The only grandapa joes you really want to do if your rushing to freeze is as follow's

1. Fresh Fish
2. The Hills Smashrooms
3. Wolf Hunting
4. A Strange Monster
& if you want to get good gold rewards when your at the right level... (if your available and not frozen yet)
5. (lvl 11) The Giants
6. (lvl 1 The Month Exploit

You really don't "need" to do any other missions. But it's really a matter of how quickly you want to freeze and what you need. I.e if you are low in Angel Potions, the lost dog from joe is good.***

level 2, you will hit level 2 whilst still on joes first mission "fresh fish"
At this point, to gain the best XP, loose the least HP, A group of 3 level 2 Dinoz is best. However when doing missions to kill lots of enemies before moving on, usually un-grouping to perform solo fights gives the quickest result. But in general a group of 3 from here on will get you frozen the quickest.

continue.

A strange monster when you finish this mission you will have no other missions you need to do, and you will be at a level high enough to gain the bouy and climbing gear, so.

Move to university, make sure you have rested to half hp, and do one of the fights, rest and then do the other fight.

Now you have the bouy and climbing gear.

Move to frish port, and into the islands, go to coral rief, get the shovel, move to and dig at sticky swamp, move to mutant falls, talk with the guy their about the scorbascooba (bad spelling) he tells you to go to frish port *you will go thier eventually*, go back to coral rief, fix the shovel, move to dino land, move to fountain of youth, dig at fountain, move to frish port.

Rest up, scorba fight is pretty hard, kill scorba and gain scorba caller, move to islands, move to coral reef, fix shovel, now you must gather the last ingredient for zors glove, move to basalt slopes, dig, move to coral reef, fix shovel (** this last part can be skipped if you never plan on digging for the totem to fight in the brute force tournament **)

move to mutant falls, with the caller, you can now move to the underwater dome, rest up, fight the earth elemental, this gets you the zors glove.

move to mutant falls, talk to the crazy man on the lilly pad, he will give you a lilly pad for some silly promise lol! what a mug.

now you can move to the forest, move thier (** bat's will be hard to kill, as are plants, a group of 3 works best here, or just rest before each movement and pray for the best lol**), move to haunted hills, theirs a crazy man with a lantern scared of a korgon, kill the korgon then pick up the lantern, he runs off and leaves it.

Now you can access the deep gorges cave, however its quite a treck, go back the way you came, through the islands, through dinoland, through the mountains and there's the cave.

Congrats you have frozen a dino.

Not including resting, from the point where you first get the shovel, assuming no side missions or detors other than specified, it will take, (quickly does the math in head), 45 turns (not including resting or optional stuff)
