i'm not sure how to clarify it anymore than i already have, hahahaha.

let's say you just entered level 4, if you move to the left (turn left and move forward) until you come to an opening or stairwell, then turn left and move forward again until you come to the next opening or stairwell, and keep doing this...using each opening or stairwell, you will eventually clear the entire catacombs.

you are only turning left at each opening, going up or down as the stairwells allow, and if you hit a deadend, or a wall, the you turn right and follow the wall to the next opening or stairwell....guess what you should do.....turn left or go up or down as the stairwell allows.

and really, if you don't know where you are in the maze, then no one can really tell you where to go. it's just that in a cavern, you always turn the same direction, or keep a surface on the same side....that way, since it is a closed-end system, you will eventually end up right back where you started. at least that is the theory that i remember.
