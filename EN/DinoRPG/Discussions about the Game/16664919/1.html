The "goal of this game" is to have fun in the long run. The timer with only a few hours is misleading. This is in fact a game of "log in once a day for a short time and make your move". Games with constant play or hourly turns usually reach the "seen all, done all, move on to the next game" state after 2-5 weeks.
The other 3 language servers of DinoRPG have only daily turns, while this one has a timer. The short periods at the start of the game are a bonus, not the standard. This may mislead people to believe its a "fast" game. It isn't. It's a daily turn game, and it takes time to develop you account. If you are not a paying player it may take years.
By buying more dinos you get more things to do per day, and later in so called clan events there are times when you might invest a lot of time per day in playing DinoRPG. But you have to stick around for quite some time to reach that state.
So if you are looking for a fast game with constant or hourly play you came to the wrong place. If you are looking for a game that you can play and enjoy for years (and that usually leaves you some hours per day to lead a real life) you came to the right place. You just have to learn and adept to it.
