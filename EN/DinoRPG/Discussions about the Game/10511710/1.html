LeadingEdge, I'm afraid that you are all wet. Many fights that people think have to be solo, can really be done as a group. Before clicking on the button that causes your Dinoz to start the Solo Fight, make sure that your Dinoz also has the Fight/Move action available too. If he does not, use an Irma, wait till your next free daily turn, and/or regroup your Dinoz so that you have the Fight/Move action available too--then before selecting the Fight/Move action, select the option that used to cause the Dinoz to fight solo and walla the whole group does the fight.
