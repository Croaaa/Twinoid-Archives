Question 1: was it worth it doing the kabuki quest?
Yes, definitely. The quest itself is quite fun, not too much hastle and explores different parts of the game and the reward is excellent. One of the best parts of the game IMO.

Question 2: was it worth it waiting for, and paying for, the extra kabuki egg(s)?
I guess this comes down to how much you want extra Kabukis. But there are several opportunities in the game to 'grind' at totem island with no real loss, so I would say yes.

Question 3: was it worth it doing the strange alien quest?
Hmm, it's borderline this one. It's a lot of running around and the pen is pretty pointless. The napo is good, but of course 1 is useless, so it's only worth anything if you are active in clan wars in a clan that can make T20.

Question 4: was it worth it doing the katacombs?
Haven't done them yet, but it's next on my list and I'm looking forward to it. Something a bit different at least and the rewards are good.

Question 4: was it worth it getting a demon dinoz?
I think it's difficult to quantify. As someone previously said, it's something to do when you feel you have done everything else. An 'endgame' if you will. It also gives a use to those dinoz you really have nothing better to do with. For 99% of players though it is probably never worth it.
