Zephides
After Grandpa Joe's, I get the Buoy and Climbing Gear, hop to the Islands and do Bao Bob's. Whilst I'm doing Bao's I'll Dig up the Swamp Mud, Pure Water and Basalt Shard.

You can do this best under Bao Bob's since his mission goes to all these places. ^^

Then get the Zors Gloves at the Underwater Dome, get to Mutant Falls and get Waterlilly Pads.

After this you can go to Grumhell Forrest and do some Nicolas Mulots Missions (Should be around lvl 14/15 now). You want to hit early to the Forrest cuz at lvl 22/23 Arboreal Guardians will come and bite you!

Whilst doing Nicolas Mulot's, get the Lantern at the Haunted Hills.

Now when you're done with all these, you can finish up Bao Bob's, finish the rest of Grandpa Joe's and then do Soft Shaman's Missions.

You can also consider doing Strange Prowler's Mission first to get the Tik Bracelet if you don't have any yet.

I kinda always take this order, and it works fine with me
