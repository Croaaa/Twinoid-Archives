Hmm... Two unlocks at L6 is probably not the best choice for training, but maybe you can salvage it. Since you've already got the level 3 skills in the Hunter path unlocked, I'd go for Lava Flow. That attack won't trigger often, but it should be a fight ender when it does. You'll probably want to go back and learn the other level 1 skills, so you'll have access to the potent high level skills in those paths.

You might also consider starting over. I wouldn't suggest this lightly, but you're still early in the game, you don't have a second dinoz yet, and it doesn't look like you've invested real money, so it shouldn't be a big loss. To me, it's kind of a toss-up... you've sacrificed a broader knowledge (for your dinoz) to get a quick route to power. I wouldn't level up my own Softpig that way, but maybe you can make it work if you stick with your choice. Good luck!
