[ http://twinoid.com/en/article/4932/get-ready-for-the-v-mana-war ]

Hostile action will begin the 10th of September 2014 at 3PM CEST !

Train your dinoz, sharpen their claws and hairy paws, reunite the members of your clan, hold your war councils and recruit the most famous dino masters...

The King of Dinoland would like to remind you of the most important rules which were already in place for the previous war:

  • Experience gain is boosted during the battles.
  • The gain in mana will be distributed to attacking clans according to their contribution to the destruction of the castle.
  • Clans will earn a fixed amount of mana for every hour their castle stands.
  • It will not be possible to change clan during the war.

The spoils of war will be:
1st clan: Epic reward of the vanquisher + 3 Napodinos + 150 Treasury Notes
2nd to 5th clan : Epic reward + 3 Napodinos + 100 Treasury Notes
6th to 10th clan : Epic reward + 2 Napodinos + 150 Treasury Notes
11th to 20th clan : Epic reward + 2 Napodinos + 100 Treasury Notes
21st to 30th clan: Epic reward + 1 Napodino + 150 Treasury Notes
