You are allowed one turn per Dinoz; you also get to do other things like talk and such. When all your Dinoz have had their turns, that's it until your next set of turns. The English server is unique (new turns on other servers start at 24:00 server time) as new turns are timed, beginning when you press the new turn button. When you have one Dinoz, there is not much to do. When you have lots of Dinoz there is lots to do, so at the start the turns start at 30 mins and get longer each time by 30 mins, until it reaches 23 hours.

Don't think it will change - it won't.
