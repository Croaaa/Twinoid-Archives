No, you take as many skills as you like. But in order to reach level two skills, you have to learn the level one skills. Each level one skill leads to two level two skills. When you get a second level up in the same element, you will see that you have the option of learning another level one skill or unlocking the level two skills for the move you already know. So if you want to specialize your dino, you can unlock higher levels faster to get to the moves you really want.
