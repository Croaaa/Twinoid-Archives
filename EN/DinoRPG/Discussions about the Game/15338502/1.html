Bengeldorn

Sometimes I wonder, how people go into in an event like this?

First of all, I had a group of two Ferros in Dinoland Kingdom, and I didn't encounter any Monsters from the Dark World till they got Level 11.
So if you have low leveled Dinoz, and don't want to take part with the event, try to get them to the Dinoland, you should do Grandpa Joe's missions anyways at that level.

If you want to got expelictly for trophies, don't use a group greater than 2.
I used a single level 43 Dinoz in the Atlantean Islands and I had about 11 points per Action there. The Big-All Hot had somthing about 10,5 Pts/Act, but I got a higher rate when my pair of Ferros did the Soft Shaman missions (Level 12-18 ). Magnetics Steppes and Nimbao had the worst drop rate for me, followed by Dinoland Kingdom (which might be, because the higher levels of most of my dinoz).

Grumhel Forrest has allways been a dangerous place for low to mid leveled dinoz. During the Monster Hunt doesn't change a lot, because of the Mega Mushrooms. If your dinoz isn't immun to poison, you can loose a lot of . As soon as the event is announced, try to get your dinoz somewhere safe, like Atlantean Islands or the Big-All Hot (from my experience the drop rate there is much better anyways).

In general it is a good idea to use a dinoz, with high initiative, speed and the capability to poison the monsters.

If you're stuck at Grumhel Forrest, you could revive your dinoz at Dinotown. Yes you might loose , but you should compare how much reviving your dinoz over and over costs to the costs it'll take you to get these back.

So these are my tips. They are based on my experience with the monster hunt events. Other might have different experiences, but these are mine.

Good luck.
