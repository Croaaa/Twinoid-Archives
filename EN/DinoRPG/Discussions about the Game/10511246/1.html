think of demons as a goal. Its something you want (be that because they are cool or because they are strong doesn't matter) but it is also something you have to spend time to get.

(unless you are paying to play and don't mind paying alot to simply farm the market for cheap dinoz to sacrifice)

So if you are in the game for the long run, you will eventually find that you want to get some of them. But regardless you will have to wait to get them because of their price.

Personally i intend to simply raise dinoz to level 10(ish) and sell them then. Supposedly thats around the level that one can get the most amount of notes for the smallest effort.

Although lvling dinoz to 10 will take a bit of time, but consider this: each time you level a dino you make money. You can just rest them to keep them in play (so you don't need to spend money to max up their health) and by taking the time to get it to lvl 10 you don't have to buy irmas so you get a lot of money from quest, killing creeps and you get a freaking demon on your team at the end which you can choose if you want to spend all that money on to get it faster up to lvl.

All in all, those who wait, don't wait in vain
