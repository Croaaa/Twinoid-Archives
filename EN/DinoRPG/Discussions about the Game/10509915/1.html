My theory is that the more A skills you have, the higher the chances of an A skill activating.

However, the chances of each A skill should remain the same but not equal when you have more than one A skill.

But remember these rules:
The stronger the A skill, the less likely it is to activate. Possible exceptions to this rule are skills where you have to sacrifice something in return, such as (kamakazie) or initiative (blowtorch palm)

The weaker the A skill, the more likely it will activate. Flight (weak skill in terms of no additional damage) is a perfect example of this. I hear it is spammed randomly a lot. I just used burning breath twice in a battle so that's another example.

My recommendation is that you have more weak A skills than strong ones. If you just have strong skills, they will only be activated about once every 5-10 battles. Or you could get weak A skills that activate a lot.
