Zephides
You need to have 10 000 to start the kabuki's quest. Go find the Punk in the frish port. He wants to make a bet with you.

The first time, he will cheat and you will be forced to loose the bet and the 10 000.
The second one, you bet 100 000, and you win. But the Punk runs away and you have to chase him.

You find him at the forges. Once you ask your money back, a fight with him will start. (should be lvl 20 for a sure win)

Chase him again and find him at the deep gorges (?)To get there, you need the lantern. A new fight starts and this time a kabuki comes to help the Punk, who his in fact Yakusi. When running away again, Yakusi will drop a parchment.

Get to the University and give it to Igor. Wait for him to translate it and speak to him once again. He will ask you some ingredients to build the kabuki aura. You will need :

- 5 Lujidanee Groupers
- 5 Collecting Pelinae Leaves
- 5 Fur Tufts
- 2 flint
- 1 air energy
- 1 water energy

Igor will need 72 hours to build it. When he hands it to you, you realise he gave you an empty box and that he ran away. Find him at the
coral mining and fight him. You are forced to lose against him. He runs away once again, this time to Totem Island, only accessible with the aura. Yakusi comes to help you and with his aura, you are able to reach the island.

The final fight against Igor, Yakusi to your side, is starting. If you win, Yakusi will give you :
- An egg containing a Kabuki
- The aura
