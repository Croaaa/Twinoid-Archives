DinoRPG is free to play, you are not forced to buy coins. This is the model of EVERY free to play game whether it is this, something by Artix Entertainment or your traditional Asian MMOs like say Maplestory or Silkroad.

In the end these companies that make these games are a business and need to collect some kind of revenue, in DinoRPG it is to buy gold, for Silkroad, to buy special items only available for purchase with real money, ect.

This model is completely different from that of MMOs such as Everquest, WoW, DAoC, ect. This is why there are limitations, in the case of DinoRPG it is the turn system.

You can play this game without having to spend real world money, it only requires patience. This is a CASUAL game with a turn based game system at its core. To compare it to something like Everquest is faulty as they are two completely game models from pay to play to the dearth of content.
