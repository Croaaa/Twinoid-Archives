We answered the LD question. It's because of luck. Because there are damage ranges as I said. For example, my level 48 Wanwan can hit between 1-7 damage with LD on other level 49 dinoz. It all depends on luck. Sometimes it'll hit a lot of 5-7, sometime it'll hit a lot of 1-3.

If the first hit is abnormally high, probably because of Focus, Wrath, or Good Whack.
