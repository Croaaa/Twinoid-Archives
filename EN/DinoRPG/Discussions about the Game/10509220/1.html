It's the other way around: Initiative determines the wait-timer you start with in a fight, and speed determines the count that is added to the timer after an attack: The higher your speed, the lower the added waiting time. A longer description can be found here:

Lookie lookie

So, a dinoz with collossus would have a speed of 30%, so, according to the formula, the wait-timer would be increased by 10/0,3=33. A Dinoz with 100% (=normal) Speed will have the timer increased by 10 after an attack. So, your colossus will be attacked 3 and sometimes even 4 times, before it can attack again himself.
