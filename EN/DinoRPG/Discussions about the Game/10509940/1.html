It's a slow game. Especially at the beginning.

Honestly, saving up money and buying more dinoz is the best way to play. Once you have a dozen or more dinoz, you're earning several thousand gold each day, and you can buy healing and Irma potions easily. Plus, more dinoz = more actions you can take each day = less "boring".

But, still, in the end, it's a "one turn a day" game. Slow. If that's not your thing.... /shrug

As people mentioned, it might be a good idea to reset your account and try a new beginning. Since you're really just at the start, it won't really set you back.

-------
And for the other people's question - the max dinoz is 18 (or 21 with a special skill). You can also eventually "freeze" dinoz which frees up slots for you to try new ones.
