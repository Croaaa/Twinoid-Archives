Actually, you can regenerate at least 46 with only one Potion, if you do not reach 50% bevor that number, using the turn system to your advantage.
It's simple, after your turn, use the Irma and rest.
Next day, don't cancel the rest, start the new turn with other Dinoz and keep resting.
Next day, befor Starting the new turn, cancel the rest. Your Dino should be able to move/fight now. Then, if you start the Next turn, the Dino can move/fight again, you didn't lose a turn and had more then 46h resting.
This is of course the cheapest way to heal dinoz below 50%. If you need more healing, you should use pies, as they give more / then burger. Battle rations are a bit tricky, they are only usefull if you know there is afight were your Dino probably will lose more then 20, in other fights they are wasted as they only heal lost during that battle.

You can use the rest of the to level some of your Dinoz with Irmas potions, but if you want to get the most from your you could have most of it for until you got the Merchant (U) skill to get items cheaper.
