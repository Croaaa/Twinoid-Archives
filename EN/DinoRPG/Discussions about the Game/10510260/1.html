Deactivating water canon will not make your dino use fireball more often, but as water cannon can replace your normal fire attack ist probably still better to deactivate it.

Another note on your dino: for the long run, it is not a good idea for a dino with so high chances in one element to only use one skill tree, as you will waste a lot of level ups on unlocking. So when your dino reaches level 30 for example, an other dino leveled "smarter" can have all the skills your dino has, and some other skills were you wasted level ups.
