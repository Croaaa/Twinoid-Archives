You would never be able to compile a perfect guide as it would take forever and the fact that mob composition in random each fight there is always that element of randomness that could not be accounted for.
However, I think we have come to some conclusions thats we can all agree on. The larger and more difficult the mob, the more xp you will get and for a constant mob the having fewer dinos and lower lvls makes for more xp. That being said when you start to decrease the number of dinos in your group you tend to face smaller mobs and likewise when you decrease the lvl of the dinos in your group.
Therefore, it looks like its not a science and in the end it comes down to finding a balance to try and maximize xp and minimize hp loss.
