Battle Rations are randomly used by your dinoz. If you're really low on health but don't want to rest for a turn, then buy the other food items to recover.

Even if you decided to equip the rations and you dinoz are using them, it doesn't mean that you'll recover to the full 20. Rations recover the damages taken during the active battle.

For example, your dino goes into battle with 20/100, first hit and your dino takes 3 damage, then your dino uses a ration. That ration will only recover 3, no more, no less.

Rations to me are pretty much useless during the beginning, if you must you're better off equipping cloud burgers. But as you level and go into other maps, you'll face enemies hitting more than 20 damage. Rations then become very useful during those battles.

Of course, the most common advise on this forum is to save your coins for buying a new dino, and simply rest a turn if you're low on health.
