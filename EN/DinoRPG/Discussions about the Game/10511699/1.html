When you get the Demon Scroll (Complete the Katatombs in the Sunset Cementary) You gain access to the Demon shop.

You can sacrifice your Dinoz and it Cost only your Dinoz Every level increases the Demon notes when sacrificed.

To freeze your Dinoz you have to have the lantern and go to the Deep Gorges. There you can freeze the Dinoz and gain a new spot for an active Dinoz There can only be 18 Active Dinoz (21 with Leader U skill)
