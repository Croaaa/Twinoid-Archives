Great guide, Veren. You always contribute so much with your posts.

Just a few things I'll add from my experience:

For a quick trip back after you've gotten the lantern, plan it so you level up around then. Then allow your Dinoz to die and use the resurrect option which will revive your Dinoz back at Dinotown. From there you can head up the mountains to freeze your Dinoz. You'll be able to save some time and skip the Islands and Eeloz this way.

Freezing isn't too hard, just time-consuming. Any of your Dinoz should be able to start the process, as long as they can take on the Water Elemental. Level 8-9 would be relatively safe, depending on Dinoz race (Rockys usually find it difficult before level 10).

You can hop around the Islands for the Zors Glove quest from 9-11 and avoid the Eeloz (they show up at level 12). The enemies you face will be Piranhoz and Kazkadines, neither of which have strong attacks but can be difficult to take down.

Traveling from the Coral Mines to all the dig spots will be the most time-consuming part.

Once you have all three ingredients required for the Zors Glove, taking on the Earth Elemental at the Underwater Dome will be difficult. Your Dinoz should be at least level 11 or 12 for this. Plan to lose a lot of health.

Here's more info on the enemies you'll encounter: http://en.dinorpg.com/clan/125?page=1143
The difficult ones are the Scorscuba, Earth Elemental and Eeloz.

It's a way to 'deactivate' dinoz in you team, freeing up a roster spot for a new one you plan to get, or just getting rid of a weaker one without selling or sacrificing it. It keeps the levels of that dinoz for the purpose of determining your ranking, and if that dinoz has any U skills, your active ones still get the benefit of them.
