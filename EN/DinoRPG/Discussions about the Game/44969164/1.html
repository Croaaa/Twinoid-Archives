Dear Fellow DinoRPG Players!

This day has finally come, I have to say goodbye to the DinoRPG community.

Instead of tons of boring words, here is some interesting stuff about me and the game:

ENG server started at 2009.02.16.
I started playing at 2009.06.17. (joined Magyar Királyság at 2009.07.24.)

I have participated in
- 5 Clan Wars
- 5 Mana Wars
- 5 Championships (the 1st one was legendary! and MK didn't participate in the 2nd one)

I have won 7 dojos, first time I won the 4th one.
- the 2nd win was 1 days before my marriage (on which 4 MK members were present)
- the 5th one a few days before my son was born
- I won 1.975 notes and 1.400 demon tickets from the dojo along with 31 napodinos

I have gathered 79 napodinos
- 31 from dojo wins
- 10 from monster hunts
- 36 from war and championship events

I have 12 magic items
- including bamboo friend (I had the first one on the server at 2010.06.21. which was the day of my 33rd birthday)

I have dug up 105 spheres
- 51% of them were wood + fire
- I have 127 spheres on my dinoz

I have harvested 1.489 devourer seeds
- during my best month 60 seeds were harvested (farmed at all 3 spots)
- sold 833 to merchant
- sold 648 to other players
- my total income from selling seeds was 28.157.000 gold (worth about 4.000 euros of payment)
- yes, finally the northern one is vacant..

I have made a youtube channel under the name of "druid4k" lol, 2k was already taken, or what...
(I dont post videos there for a long time, but has some 360 videos)

I was the leader of MK for some long time, I pulled some of my friends in the game, they are still playing, met with other MK members in real life. And I exchanged words of wisdom, fun, and game-based-knowledge with lots of great players during these 2100+ days. This is what I will miss mostly.

This game, and YOU, its players was part of my life for 6 years.

Thank you all for everything.

Cheers,

-d2k-
