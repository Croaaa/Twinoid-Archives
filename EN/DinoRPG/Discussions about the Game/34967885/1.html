Dinoz masters and clan members!

For those of you who are raring to go, or just want more information on the settings for the forthcoming Championship of the Clans, here is what we have provisionally planned. Please leave any constructive comments below if you have suggestions on how to improve the event.

Pro League:
Dinoz level limit: 50-70
Power limit: 2700
Battles on Dinoland, Dinoland West, Big All Hot only
Clans must have at least 10 members and 45 dinoz of level 50 or higher.

Amateur League
Dinoz level limit: 30-50
Power limit: 1800
Battles on Dinoland and Dinoland West only
Each clan must have at least 10 members and 45 dinoz of level 30 or higher.

Beginners League
Dinoz level limit: 10-30
Power limit: 900
Battles on Dinoland only
Clans must have at least 5 members

Duration of battles: 72 hours
Break between battles: 44 hours
AP: every 60 minutes
Max AP Stock: 15

Let us know what you think!

There must be some limit though as not getting a nice prize just for registering in pro and not doing anything.

Not giving half the league a prize only works on the FR server in my eyes, because the leagues have enough clans in them to create the illusion that almost everyone may make it to the top half with just enough activity and strategic ability. On this server some clans will just KNOW that they will end up in the bottom half. Because of that I suggest a prize structur like last Cotc. Sure there will be cases of clans not putting up a lot of resistance in some battles, either because they don't want to, or simply just can't. But a battle needs winners and losers, and playing the punching bag trying to at least provide your opponents a good battle should not be punished by getting nothing.
