It makes more sense, to have them all collect the same stuff, or at least 2 ingredients that are adjacent on the map where you want to collect (Energies in Bruteforce, Fire Ingredients at Papy Joes). Once those dinos are finished, you want them to collect every day.

Personally I like Cloudoz. Air is always usefull and gives a nice amount of money from the itinerant merchant. Energies, especially of the Air Type, can be sold at the market to other players, and are used for some ingame stuff as well. For Water you could go with fishing on a cloudoz, or just get some battle skills, so it's easier for them tu survive.


If your only aim is to make money, I would get 3 Glidwings, and collect Air Energies in BF non stop and sell those at the market. An alternative would be to get 3 Air Collectors like Cloudoz, Pteroz or Castis and sell everything you find to the merchant. That is a more steady income, but you have to find the merchant at the right day of the week.

Since a dinoz can gather before or after it moves, but not both... so dual gatherers are unnecessary for exploiting the daily healing at the fountain.

I have a team of Rocky Collectors collecting Air Energies at BrutForce for the Sphere Missions. These Rockies gather, move to the Fountain, then free heal, then move to BrutForce, then gather again... then repeat.
