This server turns 8 years old in February. What kept it going was new content and a strong community spirit often guided by an admin on the server. There were some Q&A sessions every year which gave the game a sense of having a future. The pvp prizes were diversified and admin used polls to determine particular details within COTC and Mana Wars.

All those features are gone now. Level 80 is the end point, with rebirth being a false sense of continuity by creating something more powerful over again. The last admin we had left us about 2 years ago. The prizes are always the same. Napos are inflated these days and there is limited activity in the tamermarket, making not worth much beyond converting them into .

Before level 50 we had a "bubble" meta. Anyone who didn't have bubble dinoz would get wrecked quite easily by spell damage from a range of , and elements. Back then, was by far the weakest element and was merely a support element to get primal state and gorilloz spirits. Now it's a "Force Control" meta, where if you don't have sufficient starting initiative, disable and Force Control, you will get trampled well before you make your first move. There is no intention by MT to rebalance all the skills, so the game becomes a stale race of who gets the most and best dinoz to level 80 rebirthed.

We're also stuck with a few annoying features in Mana Wars and COTC. Mana Wars take 20 days to really kick off until things get interesting. Barely anyone attacks each other until 200 honour is reached. COTC events become annoying in a similar way, where there is really no point in participating in the qualifying stages, except when fighting against a clan of equal strength, until the finals occur and even then, some clans still give up because getting a prize is so stinking easy. The COTC awards were promising in that they introduced a new magic item that could only be acquired by getting your clan into qualifying stages, but they don't introduce a new magic item anymore and many of us have like 5 lifestealers now.

There is still some community spirit left, but it is petering out slowly. I think it's been a year since my clan had full membership.
