I suggest you go with a sirain or a gorilloz for your first dinoz. They are both cheap and both will be able to one-hit a single mob after the first few level-ups if they are good ones.

I recommend you buy one, take it to grandpa joe's and do the fresh fish and hills smashrooms missions using the irma's potions included with the account. You should get two level-ups during that time. If both of them aren't in water (for sirian) or wood (for gorilloz), reset the account and try again. Having a good start with a cheap dinoz will really speed up your game.

Then, follow advice given over and over: never spend on anything but buying new dinoz.
