There is not "PVP damage reduction" It is a defense reduction based on fighting high level Dinoz. The monsters are all very low level - most under 10. When fighting PvP you're dealing with Dinoz with massive level-ups in most elements - but the key here is their level up in the element. The other Dinoz' element level is your so-called "PVP damage" reduction. It isn't just an arbitrary divider based on the fact that you're battling PVP. There's another post somewhere in here that explains exactly how the damage is calculated. PVP, PvMonster... its all the same rules...
