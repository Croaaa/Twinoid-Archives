A long journey, indeed.
First, you'll need the zor's glove. To get it, you need to dig at the Fountain of Youth, Basalt Slopes, and the Sticky Swamp to get three things. Then go to Underwater Dome and talk with the old man who ask you for the three things, then you will fight the Earth Elemental. Win the batle and you will obtain the glove. Then go to Mutant Falls and talk with Master Hydargol and he will give you a waterlily pad which you need to enter the Grumhel Forest. Then go to the Haunted Hills and talk with a strage man, then you will fight with a korgon (very easy), after defeating it, the man will give you the lantern.
