well, depending on the dinos levels, the region, and some randomness, groups of 3 can face the same number of enemys as groups of two, also groups of 2 sometimes face the same number then one dino alone. In any case you will probably have less enemys then when each dino is fighting alone. But even if you are fighting the same number of enemys, in a group you will probably take less damage. Best example are the skills that hit multiple enemys like burning breath, cold shower, etc. They are much betterin groups as there are more enemys to hit, and if you face a bunch of piranoz or flamlets it does not matter how many there are, if one dino of your group triggers one of those skills, they are all dead.
I wouldnever send Dinoz outside of the first map wothout a group, if i can avoid it.
