If you stand to gain 30 in a battle with 3 Dinoz, ceretus paribus (levels, modifying skills), each Dinoz gets 10.

If one of those Dinoz is 5 away from leveling up, then you will only get 25 -- 10 for each of your other Dinoz, and 5 for the other.

You see, gained isn't a total number that is distributed among your Dinoz. It is the sum of the ACTUAL that each of your Dinoz earned from the fight.

Therefore, is really gained on an individual basis. The factors that affect this are (in the following order):

1) The monster(s) your Dinoz defeats.
2) Your level (higher level has % penalty)
3) The number of Dinoz you fought with (the more Dinoz you are grouped with has a % penalty).
4) Your skill modifiers (i.e. Intelligence).

What comes out is a raw number calculated for EACH individual Dinoz in the battle - and then the number you see at the end of the battle is nothing more than the total of these individual calculations.

The moral of the story? Group a Level 1 Dinoz with 4 level 30 Dinoz (make sure you have Hospital Porter on at least one of those Dinoz and one or two Rockys with the Rock skill as well) and watch that Level 1 Dinoz gain CRAPLOADS of per battle without actually losing any (because it will be fighting several Hardcocks and Green Giants all at once as a Level 1 Dinoz!). In fact, you can level it up to level 2 in one battle if you're lucky.
