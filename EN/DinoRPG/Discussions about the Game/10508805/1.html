It's always a good idea to choose a second dino with an element two spaces on the wheel away from your primary dino's.

For example, my first dino was a Sirain [], and for my second dino I chose a Mouefette []. This way, if I put them in a group, and one is weak against the foe, the other will most likely be stronger. Since I majorly lacked in the Air and Lightning department, I chose a Cloudoz [,] for my third dino.

Since you chose a Gorilloz, I would suggest a Glidwings [] or a Cloudoz [,]

Hope that helps.
