It would be nice if the xp transferred to the dino that has yet to lvl but thats not how it works. When in groups the xp is calculated and given out on an individual basis. The number you see at the end of the fight is just the sum of the xp received by the entire group. Once a dino has reached its max xp for a given lvl it cannot get any more xp unless it lvls up (or if the xp came from a mission validation, in which case it can carry over). What you are doing is wasting xp.

Boy we got a lot of all wet guys talking about something they know nothing about. Listen to perrbear2! Experience is given out after a fight to each individual dinoz and truncated if the dinoz Levels Up at just the amount to Level Up. Then the experience is summed for each individual dinoz to arrive at the total experience obtained by the group. There is NO, repeat NO, experience carried over from one dinoz to another from a fight. The excess experience is just wasted.
