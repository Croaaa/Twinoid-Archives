From Experience, one of the first thing you want to do is:

1-Fight the 2 elementals and Joverboze while the XP is good

2-Start with the first 2 Shaman mission to get the Strategy Book. Since your Demon is already level 10, he may carry some skills that annoy you, like Mistral on your Winks, or a weak Water Canon on your Softpig. Turning those off is really your priority here.
