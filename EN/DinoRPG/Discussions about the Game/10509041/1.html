They normally don't have crushing attacks since it's hard to focus in 1 or 2 elements, but they tend to have consistent attacks. Example: Instead of hitting 40+ at first then 5-10 for the next attacks, they will usually hit 20-25 in a row of attacks. (at least that's how my 2 hippoclamps fight). They won't finish an enemy as fast as a Gorilloz (Gorilloz have powerful punches at their first turn because of the high ), but they won't struggle past the first attack. So it's a good dino against gluons and other enemies imo.
Against other dinoz I can't tell surely since I'd never tried PvP.

To summarize, Hippoclamps got consistency as a pro. This could be great at high lvls.
But you never know, you could get a hippoclamp with a concentrated element, that's randomness to ya.
