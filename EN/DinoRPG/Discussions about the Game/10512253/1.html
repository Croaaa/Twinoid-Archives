You need to Korgon Fins - to get them, you need to go to the Deep Gorges (from the Tunnel Under the Branch) to get to Korgon's Camp and take Dian Korgsey's first mission. After you complete that mission, you can go from the Korgon's Camp to the Jumin River, and from there to the Wild Jungle.
