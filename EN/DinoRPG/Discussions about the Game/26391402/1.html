Bengeldorn

Go to Bao Bob talk to him that you want to meditate to enter the Dark World.

You need 7 Dinoz at a time to get your dinoz to the Dark World, but you might consider to join a clan, so these 7 dinoz can be of different masters of the clan.

After you entered the Dark World and defeated the Guardian at the tower, you can continue with missions in the Magnetic Steps, accessable @ Black Silva Door with the Blacksilva Key, which you get after beating the Guardian.

Edit: As Hailstone pointed out, doing the Nimbao Quest might also be an option.
