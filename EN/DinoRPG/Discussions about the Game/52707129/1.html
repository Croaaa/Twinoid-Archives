As it was announced last year, things have changed inside MT, now as Dev is directly managing DinoRPG. Then, he told us he would welcome a group of suggestions on one whole topic. So this begin now, right here (in French).

Now I'm gonna explain how that topic is working:
1st week: One subject will be created to receive your topic (limited to one post per player).

2nd week: Define which subject is the most important (using either +1 or poll).

3rd week: Topic is refined by Amphitheatre group.

4th week and following: Kiroukou will tell us if the game allow such modifications and will act upon them.

Last week of two months: Summary of what happened.

Topic rules:

One player can only post one subject (suggestions, questions [bugs are not understood under this. Since they have to be fixed anyway]).
Subject has to be explicit, commented properly with your arguments.
Subject has to be numbered in that way: [S01], [S02] etc.
Any players can still argue, comment or pintpoint out what's good or what's wrong about any subject (posting as such, is unlimited at the moment).
When voting starts, you cannot post any subject anymore, but you will still be able to comment, argue.
Once voting finish, the monthly topic gets locked.

Now, no subject can be posted in anything else than French. As such, you will need to find willing people to translate for you and you go post by yourself on the topic (since it's limited as one subject by player, translators cannot post for you).

At last, you need to think about a general subject, nothing about narrowing down to EN server only. So nothing about, in example: a new quest translation.

Who wants to help translate or be helped. You can find me on mibbit #DinoRPG IRC channel, generally on the afternoon up to 10PM CET.

S07. Revising invocations (translation summarized from original text in French):

I. Invocation cumulative effect: it was discussed if the game should disable invocation cumulative effect (ie: Fujin), then voted. Since community choose for the effect to remain, this topic hasn't been discussed further.


II. Invocation upgrades:

Defensive invocations ( Golem, Yggdrasil, Sacred Whale, Buddha and Ifrit Armor) became even more far beyond useless since Force-Control have been learnt. Buddha has also very incapacitating skills as prerequisites.

Amphitheatre gave three suggestions for modifications :

Proposition 1A: Trigger the invocation defensively (like S-skill Vaporous Form), as generally defensive dinoz are slow..
Proposition 1B: Change the defense boost into damage reduction. Ie: Ifrit Armor could reducte up to 5 damage points of , FC damage included (minimum 1 damage dealt). Same goes for others in their respective elements, except Buddha could reduce 3 damage points in every element.
Proposition 1C: Create a racial skill for Hippoclamps (maybe a demon one) which could have all invocations.


Offensive invocations don't need modifications outside of Kabuki ones.
Proposition 2: Raises its power to 40 and/or make it AoE.

Support invocations don't need modifications except Hades :
Proposition 3A: Make invocation poison bypass protection.
Proposition 3B: Raise slow effect % and make it AoE.

"Questions to devs" were something done with something as 5 questions per month passed between each Gazette edition (with a max of 10 of them), since EN community may or may not have same concerns about the game, some part of it were given to you through me.
