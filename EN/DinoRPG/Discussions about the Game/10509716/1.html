There is a cap on the amount of you can recover while resting--pearl or no perl--and that is 50% of life. If you are resting and have reached 50% , you then have to stop resting to get 5 from the pearl at 0:00 server time. If you continue to rest at 0:00 server time with 50% , you do not get 5 from the pearl. Also, there is a cap on the you can recieve form the pearl which of course is 100% .
