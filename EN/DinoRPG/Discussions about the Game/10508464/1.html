I thought if i took the quest with the leader, all 3 would have the quest... turns out you have to go talk to the old guy with each dino seperately (doesnt matter that they're in the group).

Even though all the NPCs talk as if they are talking to you, atleast the mission guy is realy talking to the dino. He treats each dino as a completely seperate entity, even if they're grouped.

So for the fish mission for example, if you have a group of 3, you have to:
1 get group to old guy
2 click on each dino, and then on the "talk to old guy" button in each dino's screen. (so you gotta do it 3 times for 3 dinos...)
3 walk the group to the port
4 click the "get a fish" thing on each dino screen.....
and so on.
