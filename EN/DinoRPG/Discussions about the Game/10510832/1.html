General Assumptions/Conclusions thus far, credit to Chackal.

.....

1) All calculations are done using floating point math, and are finally 'rounded' to assign actual (that explains some little glitches in figures)

2) is given on a 'per dinoz' base (each dinoz is calculated and THEN they are added to show group ).
This did let me astonished, since I assumed that , as in all the MUDs I played, is splitted between group's components, according to respective lvls.
This also means that there is no 'overflow' for lost when leveling

3) When grouped, dinoz gets 75% of the it'd get fighting same enemies solo.
Group size doesn't count (at least there is no difference between groups of 2 and groups of 3 dinos. I can't create a group of 4 or 5 yet, but I suspect it wont change a bit).
75% is a bit 'arbitrary'... it could be in the range 73% to 77%, but I'm assuming a human (the ideator, engineer, planner or programmer) did choose 75% instead of 74.265738%.

.....

New information, I just got the ability for the first time to have a 5 team group,

AndySQdancer
If the Dinoz in the group are the same level they get the same amount of experience (experienced truncated if Dinoz levels up). If the Dinoz are of different level, most likely they will get different amounts of experience and the greater the level differnece, the greater the difference between the experience. That's about all that can be said for sure. Cannot say for sure whether the higher level Dinoz will get the most experience--I've seen it go both ways.
