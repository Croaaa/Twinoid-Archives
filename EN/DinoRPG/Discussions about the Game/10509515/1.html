The main problem (if you could call it a problem) is that grouping dinoz for some mission can be a pain in the ass.

All missions that include killing enemys takes twice, three, four or five times longer (sometimes).

Exmaple: You group 2 dinoz to do the "kill smashrooms mission" The mission states that you must kill 6 wolfs or smashrooms. This does not mean that when you are in a group you only need to kill 6. No you must kill 12. First the 6 for dino number 1 and then 6 more for dino numer 2 and so on.

I think this system is a bit weird couse it in some ways negates the purpose of grouping on some missions.
