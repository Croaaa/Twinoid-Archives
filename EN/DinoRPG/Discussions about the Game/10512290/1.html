I haven't read everything, but I wanted to say that these skills are not random as somone said earlier, they choose the enemy regarding its defenses (which are different from the armor and the special skills).
So, for instance, if your dinoz is about to make a fire attack, and it has the strategy skill, it will choose the enemy with the poorest defense in fire. If your dinoz has the analysis skill, it will choose the enemy with the fewest life points.

I've been playing on the French server for 3 years, and I know some very good players who did statistics on this kind of skill, and that's what they told me when I asked them about strategy. I'm not so sure for analysis, but that's what I remember.
