Normally, Hippoclamps are neglected as fighters, but I believe I've come up with a solid way to level them as fighters.

For , rush career plan (5). That is all. Use career plan to make sure you get a lot of air level ups. Any additional level ups that are unavoidable, even with career plan, should be used to get focus>regeneration>hermetic aura.

For , get Achilles heel+vaporous form+vivacious wind>breath of life (11). Ideally, you want to use that final unlock to unlock bubble and vendetta as well, but if you can't, there's enough room in and to get them. For any extra level ups, get specialist and agility.

For , get sumo+sapper+bubble (9-10). If you aren't getting enough air, but you're getting plenty of water, you may want to use a water level up to unlock bubble. For any extra level ups, get accupuncture and underwater karate.

For , get Waikikido+vengeance+vendetta (9-10). If you aren't getting enough air, but you're getting plenty of fire, you may want to use a fire level up to unlock vendetta. For any extra level ups, get incandescent aura and vigilance.

For , go straight for colossus. Since this path includes cocoon, you can use either your 3rd or 4th unlock to unlock and get basalt armor (9). For any extra level ups, get wide jaw and carapace.

The only one that goes over 10 is air, which goes to 11. But I think, with rushing career plan, there's a good chance for 11.

This leaves the hippo with 38% counter attack and bubble, which makes up for it's lack of speed (also made up for with vivacious wind slightly). With colossus' power benefits and the nature of a hippoclamp, all returned counter attacks will be strong. Incvincible against spell attacks, incredible defense against melee. Plus with 350, yeah.
