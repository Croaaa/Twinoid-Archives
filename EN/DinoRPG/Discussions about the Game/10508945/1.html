Hold up, friend. You don't actually get 70k from the market by itself - that is how much the gae determined your dino is worth. From here on, somebody will actually have to bid on it, for you to get money.

Before you invest more money into this account, ask yourself is your current winks good enough?

Seeing that you are a new player, I assume that you most likely didn't level it up the most efficient way. You might have made some rookie mistakes, like picking wrong skills, or unlocking skills in dominant trees one at a time, which makes the dino less attractive to others.

Unfortunately this game doesn't allow you to sell dinos for less than what market thinks its worth. They say that it is to prevent market exploits, but I see those all the time. I.E. I'd see one person buying dinos excusively from another person (which is most likely the same guys with multiple accounts) for considerably higher price than those ar worth. Look up sales history and you will find someone buying 3 lvl 1-2 wanwans for 26-30k each from one guy.

So while people are still exploiting the market, we, the fair folks are stuck with poorly developed higher level dinos that won't be bought by anyopne because due to poor skill choice or bad luck with element rolls they are worth less than what market values them, yet we can't lower the price.

So my advice to you, my dear friend, think wether your dino is attractive enough for potential buyers before you invest more money into the account. It might be better starting fresh.
