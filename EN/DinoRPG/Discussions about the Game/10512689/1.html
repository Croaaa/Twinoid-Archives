There are lots of newbie guides out there, even one posted at the top as a sticky.

The best place to start isn't going to sound fun:

You need to build dinoz that you will never use in the future. You need the Unique skills for your account, which means making a Gorilloz that has Leader and Engineer.

You need a glidewings that you can give Priest and Merchant.

You need a cloudoz (probably best for now) to make Professor.

And you need a sirain to give you Cooker and Storekeeper.

The most important ones to work on now are the Gorilloz/Glidewings, in my opinion. Those skills are essential and take a while to get to.

After days of fighting and bloodshed, you finally gathered enough money to buy a second Dinoz. But what to pick? Well, you can basically pick any Dinoz you like, but I’d still choose one of the cheaper ones. If your first Dinoz was a Sirain, maybe a Dinoz with different elements would be the next best thing. There’s also another way to choose your Dinoz.
Mainly, there are 3 things where you could pick your Dinoz for:

Fighter – This one will do missions and advance in levels and perhaps fight in wars or any other Person vs Person event.
Collector – This one will collect ingredients. You will need special skills for that [marked with C] and you will need to travel to certain places before you can collect ingredients.
U–Skiller – This one will try to get Unique [marked with U] skills. U skills will count for all Dinoz, but can’t be stacked.

ZexyZap
If you ask me everyone puts too much emphasis on u-skills. There is no need to get them right at the start of the game. True, it will save you having to get them later, but it doesn't make too much difference.

It depends on what play style the player will use. If its a casual gaming style and just loging in and doing the daily turns and grow at a slow pace then U-skills may not be number one priority.

But if a player want to play at a competitive level then U-skills like Professor, Merchant, Leader, Engineer and Cooker is very important since you get more daily gold with more dinoz, more with Professor, cheaper Irma potions with Merchant, more equipment slots for each dinoz with Engineer and better healing when you use items if you have Cooker.
