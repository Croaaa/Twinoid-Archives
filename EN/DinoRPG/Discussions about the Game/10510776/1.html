AndySQdancer
I have Office on my computer and use it to keep all my notes, documentation, plans, and other stuff. Just devote a Directory of files to DinoRPG. It's also easy to cut and paste between the browser and Office. Thus, I have everything together, except when I go on vacation and take my laptop. Then, I no longer have my notes and have to rely on my memory. I also find it useful to paste the Skill trees into office and attach note boxes on the skills with a number indicating the order I will take the skills in a paticular element skill tree, including unlocks. With the dinoz probability of getting a particular element, you can gestimate how many boxes to put on a given element skill tree. Then, as I get the skills, I color the number of the skill obtained. Also, for the low probability of getting a particular skill, I flag it with a yellow color. This way I have a record of the skills obtained and a plan for the future of the dinoz. Also, if my luck is different for a particular dinoz, I can adjust his skills according to his luck. Also, I can modify the plan based on double-skills and also now with the spheres. It helps to prepare--to have a roadmap and a planned route.
