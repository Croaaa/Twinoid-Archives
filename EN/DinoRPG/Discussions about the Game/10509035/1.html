Well, a fire dino would complement your sirain more for two reasons. First he is two elements apart from him, while lightning is only one, and second because than you will have a dino who will have a much easier time on atlantic islands. With firebreath you will have very little trouble fighting piranhas, though I suppose a sirain with cold shower will have easy time there as well. Also, I'd buy a piggy, and not a moufette.

A glidewings would be fine too, but he wouldn't be joining your other dino on the islands for a while, since water ele annihilates those untill ther are level 7-8. Get one if you don't mind your sirain soloing the islands.

Oh, and the reason you wanna go to the islands asap is to get the pearl, which is a must once you have over 3 dinos.
