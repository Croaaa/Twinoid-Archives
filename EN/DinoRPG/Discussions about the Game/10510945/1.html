Also, Rokkyz are fast Dinoz that are pretty much one of the most dominant species in DinoRPG, because of the tree - Leafy Dawn, Lightning Dance, Lightning, Regeneration, Priest, Merchant, Gathering skills and Blazing Twilight are really AMAZING skills to have.
