We've come to the end of another Inter-Dojo Tournament, with some truly incredible fights! In the end, however, there can only be one winner... and that winner is no stranger to victory: Rjun74!

The term "group of death" is often overused in tournaments of all kinds, and here is no exception. However, to see Rjun74, LuigieMMA05, Raynor and ROHT in the same group was incredible. Pilaf, our runner up, is also highly experienced in the IDT and had a tough road to the final beating insanedar, Soagafihn, and john0452 (who had previously bested PerrBear AND Anskar!)Rjun was too much for An0nymous this time, but we feel an IDT victory is close!

Having already won the IDT and rarely been out of the last survivors, we thought we'd better hand this over to him...

"For this format Ive choosen a selection of dinoz which were all round solid build and most had decent HP
The dwans are reincarnated and have good assault power and A-skills fro dmg and they also have the powers to heal, leafy dawm/precocious spring/primal state. They have good initiative and speed and could benefit from the spheres skills and mass frenzy and other e-skills like vines. The dwink has no healing powers but could absorb dmg and summon clones and has an overall decent dmg output.
Though this team won, it still had its weakness as none were poison proof and I used 1 dwan with only 180 HP. I choose to game on the attack and healing abilities. Guess it worked out this time.

I've basically used the same dinoz every IDT, maybe 1 time used 1 different dinoz.
IMO any team could win an IDT, its really a matter of lucky rolls in the combats and a good group draw and final phase position.
Ive been lucky with the group draw and match ups and able to complete the route to the final and win.
My match against clanmate Lui was one of the closest fights for me in this edition.
But each IDT is always a pleasure to watch and so much monster battles going on.

Big up & Respect each and every dinoz master who keep the game and IDT an interesting challenge and nuff greetings to all my friends in the game and especially to all my people in Rakshasa for support!!!"

We're shaking things up for the next IDT finals, making it a 5 vs 5 brawl where everything goes. All magic and temporary items are allowed. It's going to be MAYHEM - a beat-down, drag-out WAR! Get ready!
