Sometimes, you look at the competition in the IDT and your heart sinks. The strength and depth of some teams is genuinely frightening. Fortunately for some masters it's what they do best. Even better, is that our IDT champions are always willing to share some of their winning tips with everyone else, thus making your climb to the top easier and their job much harder. Don't miss your opportunity! So without further ado, we pass you over to our reigning IDT Champion, Druid2K!

The 36th dojo had the format I like the most. 5 dinoz, and no items at all. Finally we will see some E skills in action!

But I dont want to write a report about the dojo this time. I had 4 feature fights, 2 or 3 of them was legendary in many ways. Mengo9999 with a team that seemed to be unbeatable, Rjun with a berserker gigacat, Loppermann whom I fight regularly in the final, and finally Muecke whom I have to congratulate to get to the final with only 3 dinoz (but those were tier 1 builds!).. I can write pages about the fights, but all who cared watched those, and they know these were epic fights.

What I wanted to say is that if you dont have the best builds of the server, or if your dinoz are not perfect, not flawless: all those doesnt matter! 3 out of my 5 dinoz was at most average. My cloud is a bad joke (that one). This game has a huge luck factor, but also its has space for tactics. Know the skills of the game. Knowledge is power. And use them. Use them by selecting dinoz with certain skills in your team. And manage those skills, because of every fight is a different one. Thats my advice.

6 IDT victories. lol. Quite unbelievable to me. My good old rabbit's foot is working. Or my secret fun club do some fantastic things: I have to thank all of those, who are writing to me before the fights, supporting me and giving trust.

THANK YOU ALL.

Nice words there from a prolific IDT Champion and an inspiration to all who dream of making it to the top! 6 IDT Wins already and showing no signs of slowing down, ladies and gentlemen, please make some noise for your champion, Druid2K
