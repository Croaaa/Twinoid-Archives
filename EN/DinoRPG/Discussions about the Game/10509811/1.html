Once you have two dinoz, it's a whole new ballgame. Here, there is a trick about resting. When you get low on , when your next turn comes, you put one dinoz to rest (don't use an Irma pot to put him to rest early unless it is one of the free ones and the time between turns is near 23 hours). Then, when your next turn comes, use the other dinoz to start the turn clock again and leave the first dinoz still resting. Now here is the good part--especially when the time between turns is 23 hours. Before taking your turn, wake up your resting dinoz. He will have a turn waiting for him. Giving you two moves in a row with that dinoz efectively giving you 23 without costing you a turn. This game can be played with any number of dinoz. The trick is to leave one dinoz not resting so he can start the turn clock.

Good luck getting the pearl, it will save you lots of early in a new dinoz life. Trick is to get it before the dinoz getting it reaches level 12 when the eeloz come out in the islands and can do lots of damage to your dinoz in the islands.
