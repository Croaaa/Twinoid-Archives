koukakaijin- When a dino harvest an ingredient, does he gain any exp?

No. Collecting ingredients does not give you XP. However, the ingedient collection skills work as an additional ability for the dino (when it is in the correct location where it can harvest an ingredient (Rianon ingredient maps)). This works seperate from the Actions your dinoz gets with which it can Move/Fight and gain XP.

- If he doesnot gain exp, i understand that it is better to harvest with the a dino of the lower level possible (to have more actions). Should some one "discard" a "harvester" dino if he rolls other element?
Yes, if you manage to keep your dino at level 11 with all the highest-level harvest skills you can claim an action (+additional harvest button) twice a day.
I think it is generally believed that Hunters Fire-element harvesters yield the most gold from their found ingredients. Some people use Wood harvesters aswell, but the items are more expensive and thus more rare. I dont have any personal experience with Wood harvesters.

So basically you can manage your account to have a couple of main-fighter dinoz and some Harvest dinoz on the side to yield a gold income that you can use on the fighter dinoz.
I see that you just started, also take into account that you want all U-skills, so its probably best to start with those right away.
