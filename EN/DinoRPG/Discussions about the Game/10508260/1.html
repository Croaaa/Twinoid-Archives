The treasure is composed of ingredients which are used by the clan to rebuild the castle or attack another clan during war.
You can't steal the treasure of another clan.

You destroy its castle, and your clan earns a victory. The more victory, the more points your clan gets.
By the end of the war, the members of the 30 first clans win a funny artefact that appears on your account page. In the french RPG, they also earn Napodinos, which enable them to buy things in the magical shop. I don't know when we will get them here, though.

In a word, the interest in war for your personal account lies in belonging to a Top 30 clan.
