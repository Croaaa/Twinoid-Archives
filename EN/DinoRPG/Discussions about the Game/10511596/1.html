If you turn off a skill, it disables ALL effects of the skill - you cannot expect it to only turn off some of the skill.

As an example, if you turn off Torch:
Dinoz is not on fire but still loses health each turn?
Dinoz is still on fire but no longer loses health?

It makes no sense to try and deal with any one effect of a multi-effect skill separately (and even less to try and discuss a skill that cannot be turned off anyway)
