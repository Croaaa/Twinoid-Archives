calorifere
The 'optimal' strategy for getting a lot of dinoz fast is to only buy 16k dinoz. It's optimal, but it's not necessarily fun, or best for getting strong attack dinoz.

If I had a new account, given what I know now, I would probably go for gorilloz, glidwings, moueffe, sirain as my first 4 because they only cost 16k and are good solo because they have 1 strong element.
Then, I would get softpig and winks because they also can solo, and only cost a little more.
Finally, I buy the other dinoz I want in pairs to protect those who don't have an element as dominant as the previous 6.
That way, you have a good balance between getting more fast early by buying inexpensive dinoz who can solo, and protecting the dinoz later by buying in groups when you have more .
