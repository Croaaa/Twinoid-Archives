For making it a strong fighting dino, the only good thing you have is high health allowing you to take a good amount of damage when at full health. Problem is, you don't really have anything making it a stronger attacker other than small increases: Strength, Martial Arts & Underwater Karate.

Focus and Mistral are both junk, considering its so low in those elements.

If you don't have cooker yet, now would be a good time to go ahead and try to get it. Then you can get Aqueous Clone if you want.
keep going for cocoon to get your 'basalt armor' if you want or take advantage of having search and get the other gathering skills (or try to do both?).
keep going to waikikido for double skill. If you get more, grab wrath and/or charge.
I can only say... go for regeneration if you decide to be a gathering dino, you can regain health faster.

Only other thing I can think of is making it a dino to support a really strong crew by getting Vines, Primal State and Precocious Spring.
