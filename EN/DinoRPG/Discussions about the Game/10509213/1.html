I was reading on these boards about the whole Rocky fight and I thought I would write a post on this for the future if anyone new needs it. There wasn't really a definitive answer for what I was getting myself into when doing this fight and I'm probably not the only person that thinks Rocky looks cool (and wants one... or five for myself).

I was all hyped and expecting the fight to be super hard because I just hit level 13 with my Sirain and from the boards I pretty much though this would be one hell of a fight at my level since everyone was suggesting level 15 so I bought 2 cakes and loaded up my Sirain to 107HP and threw a battle ration onto him just for good measure and I was utterly disappointed in wasting all of that money.

In the end my level 13 Sirrain only sustained 33HP points worth of damage and the first initial attack was only 16 damage followed by a bunch of 1s. Probably the biggest factor here is that electricity attack so if anyone is concerened about it:

Probably every Dinoz except maybe Cloudoz and Pteroz have anything to worry about in this fight. Sirain should have have a really easy time and at 18 Defense you're only looking at 16 lightning damage which is the biggest thing to look at. Unless your Dinoz is weak towards the Lightning element, you shouldn't worry too much about this fight. Having at least 50HP is a good idea if you have solid defenses.
