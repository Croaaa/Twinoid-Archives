Castivorous is really not the best choice for a first dino, so I think. In another DinoRPG server I have a Castivorous level 10, and he has to rest every two turns, at least, when going by himself. (He can kill a Gluon and a wolf or 2 Gluons, but will loose around 40 in a single combat).

You can skip skills when you unlock a number of skills, if you unlock after getting the 3 level 1 skills you will get 6 new skill choices next time.
If you get one level 1 skill then unlock, you get only 2 more skill choices next time, plus the other 2 level 1 skills you had not chosen before.

Given that, is is not always the best to unlock all level 1 skills first, this depends on the percentage of every element that your dino has. And the greater level skills that you want to get in the future.

You have to plan for the training path of your dinos given what you want from them, for example attack, defense, or gathering energies or other ingredients.

My suggestion to you is to restart your account and choose another dino that has better attack abilities, but that is just my opinion. You could find better for you to try to get a strong dino out of your current one.
