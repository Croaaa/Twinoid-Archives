the attack goes with the element points not the assault points. The assault points are for damages calculation.

on your 1st dino, the fire and thunder element is equal at 3 points, so the attack can be either on of those elements.

on your 2nd dino, the wood definitely the 1st attack since it has 3 points compare to water of only 1. however the water will do good damage (9 assault points because of perception).
