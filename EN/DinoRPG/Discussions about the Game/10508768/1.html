You start at level 1...

15 levels would get you all level 1, 5 more would then unlock all of level 2. Another 30 would be needed to get all level 2 skills.

That would be 50 levels, so level 51.

You can only get to 50, so there can never be a dinoz with all of the level 2 skills.
