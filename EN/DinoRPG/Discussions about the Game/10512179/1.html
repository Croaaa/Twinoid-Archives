Napodinos are special in that they have to be earned and cannot be bought. That is a central feature of dinorpg and it shouldn't change. That is also a major source of napodino's value in dinorpg.

Players can just throw money into dinorpg and go far... but without investing time and effort, players won't get the napodinos and magic items to help optimize their dinoz.

Hence the "market value" of napodinos is really moot... they are a part of a second economy within dinorpg.
