As you may find in many discussions, as you only have one Dinoz you should be careful about this. DO NOT spend money on healing, save it all to buy your second Dinoz. It is your choice about using your initial Irmas to take another turn, but DO NOT buy any more.

Resting will cost you a turn, and you gain 1 HP per hour. When you start resting, the timer tells you how long it is till the next heal, not the next turn. If you stop resting before the turn is due you will have to wait, and cannot return to resting (if your turn time is now 12 hours, and you rest for 10 hours, you still have to wait for the 12 to be up before the next turn). It does not really matter if you wait longer than the turn time, except for delaying everything.

Check how much damage you are taking in each fight. That is about the level you need to consider resting, but be aware that monsters get bigger as you increase levels (more damage!).

Search for resting again when you get your next Dinoz, as you have better options.
