In this game you can pretty much use them whenever you like.
You could save them for when the turns are 10+ hours apart, just so you can do things more often.
By the time you start to need them (to get to a certain place in time to buy or sell certain things) you should be able to get money to buy them easily enough.

This game isn't like DinoParc in that regard. There, you get gold every day just for logging in, so it is better to save them so you can get to, say, the bath at the beach, when you get injured. Using them up there means you take damage and run out of money.

I see them as being strategically advantageous to hold on to. Resting is the cheapest option for healing, especially when you have a long wait between turns or ar going to be a sleep for your next turn or two. Youd can either combine an Irma's potion with a long-wait move to both move and than spend the rest of the time healing or save them for unexpected healing. If you get in to a fight that deals more damage than expected, seeing as people often walk around with only between thirty and twenty HP unless planning a big battle, you may went to keep them for times you suddenlt find yourself low on HP.

Also, starting on them early may make you impatient too keep playing at the increased pace, even once the potions are gone. I've seen posts by people who spedn all their money on the potions, usually advising people to stay away from them in the beginning.
