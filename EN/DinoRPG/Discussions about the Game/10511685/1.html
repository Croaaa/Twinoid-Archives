Are you the next Steven Spielberg? Cameron Crowe? Alan Smithee? Coming to a screen near you on Monday, August 2nd, the new Cinema Paradino complex will be opening at Dinoplaza, and to celebrate this occasion we are calling all DinoRPG directors and filmmakers to participate in this historic event.

We challenge you to create a Cinema Paradino version of your favourite movie, starring your own Dinoz! The rules are simple:

* The film must be under 5 minutes in length
* Your version of the film must be suitable for all ages
* The finished film must be submitted by Friday the 20th of August at 5.00pm (CEST)

Winners will be decided by the votes cast by your fellow Dinoz masters, and then by a panel of judges, so make sure to invite your friends to check out and vote for your masterpiece! There will be prizes for the top movies, and also the honour of winning the first ever Dinoz d'Or award for Best Film.

Cameras at the ready...

Epoq

epoq
There will be no films showing at Cinema Paradino when the contest starts, but it is in your interests to get started asap, because as soon as your film is published (finished), other players will be able to vote on it... As other budding directors have stated, there is no advantage to using Irma's potions to race there before the contest starts.

In other words, The contest AND voting start on the 2nd of August.

The contest will be judged on many factors, which will necessitate more than one prize. At the moment I would envisage prizes for "Best Film", "Best Adaptation" and "Best Comedy". This will all be confirmed before the contest starts, but is just to give you an idea of how I'm thinking!

To answer other questions,

You can produce as many films as you like. However, if you create a trilogy, each film is voted on individually and will be judged as such.

The Dinoz d'Or is exactly that - the DinoRPG equivalent of the Palme d'Or, presented at the Dinoland Academy Film and Television Awards (DAFTAs).

As always, feel free to give me any of your ideas...

Epoq
