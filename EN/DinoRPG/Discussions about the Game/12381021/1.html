Once again the portals to the Dark World have been breached!

Dinoland is in danger. The earth has been shaking with the massive troop movements ahead of the forthcoming Championship of the Clans which has led to a portal being weakened.

From midnight tonight until Tuesday the 6th of June at 12h00 server time

We call on you, brave master, along with your friends and clanmates to save us from the impending threat of vicious monsters including the ferocious Amenpenne!
As always, killing monsters earns objects. The more monsters you kill, the more points you earn.

The King has promised to give the following prizes to the most fearless hunters:

  • 1st place: 1 Baby Mahamuti + Epic hunting Reward + 15 Treasury Notes.
  • 2nd - 5th place: 1 Feross Egg + 10 Treasury Notes.
  • 6th - 10th place: 1 Kabuki Egg + 5 Treasury Notes.
  • 11th - 20th place: 5 tac tickets.
  • 21st to 50th place: 50 Treasury Notes.
  • 51st to 100th place: 25 Treasury Notes.

Wait! That's not all!

Why should you join the hunt?
In addition to the prizes above, there are personal prizes for everyone who performs well in the hunt, regardless of final position.


* 500 points: 5 Irma's Potions
* 1000 points: 2 Warm Breads
* 1500 points: 2 Warm Breads + 3 Angel Potions
* 2000 points: 1 Pack of Nimbao objects
* 3000 points: 1 Tac Ticket + 1 Xmas Ticket
* 4000 points: 200 Demon Tickets

5000 points: 1 Napodinos

Strap on your hunting gear and show the Dark World invaders no mercy!
This is a great way to grab some extra loot before the Championship of the Clans starts!.
