So my question is if I lvl 2 times and put a point into burning claws followed by a point into wrath on my 3rd lvl will it allow me to unlock 4 skills?Although you may realize this already, it's important to note that such an unlock (which I call a "partial" unlock) only unlocks those four skills. It will not unlock the two second tier skills in the Strength path, because you had not learned Strength when you did the unlock. This means that if you later decided you wanted Charge, for instance, you would have to use one level-up for Strength, one to unlock the two next skills, and one to get Charge. That extra unlock represents a drain on a finite resource (level-ups), and is something you generally want to avoid. Usually, you will want to get all the low level skills desired before unlocking the next tier, to avoid wasting level-ups on extra unlocks.
