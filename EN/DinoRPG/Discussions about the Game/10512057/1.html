Its only been seven days of war, but you (I) can already see the trend and the effects of the new rules.

First I have to say that Epoq have been great and he has done the best he can. But the new clan war rules are not helping, not making it more fun or making it more tactical.

Here are my thoughts on the new rules and the other additions such as Mana, Spells and Morale.

* The 1000+ point rule - Clans over 1000 points may not attack with groups of dinoz whose power is greater than 90. This rule is silly and make the clan war a bit boring for the clans that are not super strong. The clans in the mid section that dont have lots of dinoz over lvl 40+ are hindered by this rule.

The top clans have no problem attacking any clan that are below 1000 or clans that reach 1000 points, but the clans in the mid section have to wait until weaker clans reach the 1000 point mark or clans around their own strength since they cant attack any of the stronger clans. So this rule only favor the really strong clans.

I can have sympathy for weak clans that to some extent dont have to worry that much about getting hammered over and over by stronger clans.

* Mana - Could have been a nice addition to the game, but yet again it favors those who have a huge stack of rare ingredients. No broblem for us, but I can see how it will be a problem for lower lvl clans to scrap together lots or rare ingredients from time to time.

* Spells - Cool idea, but the execution of the spells is not that good. Most spells are rather useless while some have a bigger impact. Need to be a better balance between useful spells and worthless spells.

* Morale/Heroic actions - I like this idea and I have seen some nice actions being triggered. Some better then others and some that are rather annoying like Air Extraction.

The big talk before the 5th clan war was launched, was how the rankings would change drastically and what amazing fights and strategy that was going to be implemented with the new rules. So far I can see no changes what so ever, except that the weak clans dont get their asses kicked all the time. But sooner or later they will pass 1000 points and get hammered. So its all about time.

There are some good ideas in the new applications, but they need to more adapted. Right now I have to say that so far this clan war is the most boring one ever.
