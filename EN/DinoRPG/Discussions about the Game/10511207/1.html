it's only one Dino at a time. The leader will get the kills first. then the second, then the third. if you fight 3 enemies at a time it'll be:

1st 6 monster kills go to the 1st Dino.

2nd 6 monster kills go to the 2nd Dino.

3rd 6 monster kills go to the 3rd Dino.

so you'll need to kill 18 enemies. 6 per Dino

Toadswart, one other quirk of the code is iffen you fight more monsters than the leader needs and the Leader has 0/? to kill. In this case the excess kills goes to the next Dinoz, and the next (assuming he had 0/? to kill also and got more than he needed). For example say you are killing Gluons, with a team of 3 and they all need to kill 0/1 Gluons. Iffen you kill 2 Gluons, the Leader and his first follower will get credit for kills and the 3rd Dinoz will still have 0/1 Gluons to kill. Sometimes this can work to your advantage with a group of 3 and killing more monsters than are required per dinoz for each fight. After the first fight, regroup and make the Leader the one needing 0/?, and the first follower the one still needing x/? to kill. This way the following Dinoz will get the excess kills where iffen you had not regrouped only 1 Dinoz would get credit for kills.
