Sanjar
What qualifies as the 'best' skill, the higher level ones?
Yes, the higher tier ones.

It has been posted before that the game rolls for the highest tier skills first, such as Meteors, before rolling for the lower tier ones. So essentially, your Dinoz will perform a roll for all its attack skills each round (starting from the highest tier ones) until it reaches one it succeeds on. Same for event skills. If your Dinoz ends up doing its regular attack, that means it has failed every single one of those rolls.

So no, it's generally not disadvantageous to get more 'A' skills, since your highest tier skills will get a check before your lower tier ones. Getting Lava Flow (tier 3) doesn't limit your Meteors use.
