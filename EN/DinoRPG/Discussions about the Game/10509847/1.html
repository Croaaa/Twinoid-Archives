Unlocks done too early was the reason for my rerol, though I has already had 2 dinoz at that time. When you are leveling dinoz you need to be patient and pick all the skills of current lvl before making unlock to make lvling more effective (especially for dinoz with one primary ellement).

Water cannon is good, but you should mind that it will replace your attack only accasionally, not always, so for stable damage Perception is also very good. My advise is: Water Cannon -> Perception -> Mutation (higher wont help to deal damage).
