After a bug on Day 1 of the IDT, a quick fix and a restart allowed us to get down to brass tacks the following day. We were not to be disappointed...

From the start, we could see that one master was devastating his opponents with his team of 3 Cloudoz...that master never faltered, and ultimately, never lost. That master is Mengo9999. The new Inter-Dojo Tournament Champion! Without further ado, we'll hand you over to him...

"Since my 3 clouds took me all the way to second place on my last IDT, I decided to stick with them. It was a no-itens IDT, so i seemed important that all of them were poison proof. Also, Fujin has put the cloudoz on a complete diferent level from before, though the recent update changed it a little bit, as you could see in some of the fights. I was lucky, being the right guy with the right dinoz on the right time. The fact that I levelled all 3 of my clouds to lvl60 before the IDT started made all the diference when I fought my clan mate RafaelSS on the second round. He was the only one that maneged to kill one of my clouds. Still, he was no match for me on this IDT. No one was!

After all this time and after 5 finalists in the last 6 IDT's, finally justice was made and a LBT has won this tournament!!! I really love this clan and could not ask for better partners on this game!! A special thanks to my clan leaders HugoMVP, RafaelSS, Pilaf, Pulgaman, Porradino and Pqpaew, who helped me a lot, not only when I was a begginer, but also on keep evolving and creating new and better builds. Thanks also to all my clan members/friends and to all that cheered for me on this IDT. A new champion is always welcome I think. "

How right you are Mengo9999! We love a new IDT Champion, and a new clan holding the title too. Congratulations once more to Mengo9999 and Luso-Brasilian Team!
