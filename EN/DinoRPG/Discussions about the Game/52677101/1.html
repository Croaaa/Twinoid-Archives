Shock adds the amount of wood element to the power of your lightning assaults, and vice versa. So on my level 70 wanwan (16/19/8/26/5) that is 19 (wood element) + 155 (base lightning assault power) = 174 lightning assault power. In pvp that translates to a few HP extra damage, so not really worth it. Just an OKish addition (on wanwan or tofu only) if you get too many wood/light and have Shock unlocked but cant proceed to ether yet because you still need levelups in other elements (as Afreet put it: Shock should not be the aim).

Overload increases your chance to multihit from 20% (assuming you have Double hit) to 38%, which means a lot of extra damage, especially with Force Control.

I dont quite follow what you mean with RoL and initiative. But go for Overload:p Don't forget about Earth Tremour.
