When I started this game, I chose glid, winks, casti and pteroz as team fighters. At that time, I had a kind of collector mentality: getting one dino per element and then getting other dinoz. This was a time when bubble wasn't known, when it was OP and invocations weren't in game. I mainly regret failing to get bubble on winks and having a rather wonky pteroz that didn't quite end up like muecke's.

Looking back, if I could reset account and get all my progress back (something like 5 years development translated into ), I would pick a casti, winks and cloudoz. The casti would help transform team of 3 fighters into team of 5.
