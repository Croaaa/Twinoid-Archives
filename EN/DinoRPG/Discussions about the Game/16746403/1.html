BhimaI hope this is obvious, but I meant the new 24 hour timer.

It is not a 24 hour timer... it is a Next Reset Timer. You won't miss any turns as long as you press it anytime between one reset and the next rest. Press it 5 minutes before reset and it will only count down 5 minutes to your next next turn... and you don't even have to make all your moves before it finishes counting down again - you just have to make your moves before you press the green Hourglass again.
