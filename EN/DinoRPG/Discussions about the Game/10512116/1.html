Loxagon
I think you still confuse the qualification phase with the tournament. The qualification phase or elimination phase is where you fight 1 on 1. If you end within rank 256 you are qualified for the tournament, if you end within rank 50 you get a Tofufu.
After that in the tournament you fight 3 on 3. There are 2 rounds. The group round (called first round in the help) with 16 groups, and the final round with the winners of the group round.
You don't get a Tofufu for your placement in the tournament, but for your ranking while trying to qualify for the tournament.
