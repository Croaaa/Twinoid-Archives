Imho, volcano would be better. The reasons:
- Your cloudoz is very weak against water, even if you defeat the water elemental (it's gonna be a tough fight btw, if you go with 65hp you're gonna get slaughtered; preferably full hp + at least 1 battle ration), you're still gonna have to face the water enemies from the islands, and you'll lose too many hp from kazkadines and pirhanoz and also struggle if you don't finish them off quickly, because of their annoying skills (immaterial and flight, respectively).
- Since you cloudoz is lvl 10 already, soon it's gonna be lvl 12 and Eeloz will start popping up, and believe me, you don't want to face Eeloz at lvl 12, PLUS with that weak water defense.
- It's better that you wait to get the Fountain Pearl with a 2nd dinoz, preferably a fire or wood dinoz (Moueffe, Softpig, Gorilloz are good options for your second dinoz). And try to leave the islands before you reach lvl 12. (I would say come back around lvl 15 only, so you can have an "easier" fight vs. Eeloz).

Why go to Volcano:
- Your fight against the fire elemental will be a lot easier.
- Go grab that strategy book.
- Since you have a decent fire defense, you're gonna have better chances than going to the islands, where the enemies are supposedly easier, but in fact they are tougher for your dinoz.
