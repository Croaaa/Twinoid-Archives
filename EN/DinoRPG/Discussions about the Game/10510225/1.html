You have to beat the Elementals at the University. Beating the Water Elemental will give you the Swimming Buoy which will let you get to the Island from Frish Port. Beating the Fire Elemental will give you the Climbing Rope which will let you get to the Mountains from the Hills.

You can challenge the Elementals as early as level 5, but I've seen people recommend you be level 8-9 to beat them. (I haven't managed it myself yet.)
