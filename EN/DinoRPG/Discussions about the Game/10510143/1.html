Whatever floats your boat. I didn't say they were bad or anything of the sort, just that: from experience and observation, they tend to take a little longer to get strong and functional. But, yes, like I said before, unless you're lucky with level ups and it somehow gets a very dedicated path of Lightning early on, which is a 40% chance at each level up.

My point is just that you might have an easier time starting off with a Dino that gets about a 55% or more chance of getting one element on level up. Just advice.

There are more than enough Wanwans scurrying about another day if you choose to change your mind on your first Dino.
