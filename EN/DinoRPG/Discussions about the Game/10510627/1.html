You can give up on missions at any time before you validate them and, no matter what level you are, the only thing that happens is that you have to start the mission again (should you want to)
You do not lose gold or XP or anything else if you give up the mission.

The only issue you might have is a problem in redoing the mission if it requires you to kill a particular type of monster randomly. Monsters are attracted to Dinoz of a particular level (e.g. Eeloz start being interested when your Dinoz is level 12) and they stop if your Dinoz is too powerful for them.
So, if you quit a mission at level 8 and then retry it at level 28 then you may find that you can never complete it (unless you can group with other Dinoz with other missions to get around the issue)
