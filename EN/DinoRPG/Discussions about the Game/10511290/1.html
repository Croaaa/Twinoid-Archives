Your choice really, there isn't a "wrong" dino to choose. At the early stages of the game, I'd go with something that's good at gathering so you can sell your ingredients to the merchant and get more money so you can get more dinoz quicker. Perhaps a Castivorous, Moueffe or another Cloudoz as they can all get gathering skills in multiple elements and are cheap.

Make sure you look at some of the clan's levelling guides so you choose the right skills to make your dinoz gatherers, then you can buy a more attacking dinoz (my personal choice is Gorilloz, but they're not so good in PvP... maybe go with a Glidwings, Winks, Wanwan or Softpig) at around your 5th or 6th dinoz.

Sorry for the long post, but I hope it's useful!
