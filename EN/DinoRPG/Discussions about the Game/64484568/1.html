In my opinion only Pigs or Wanwans make good demons.

Pigs decent torch defenders, and if lucky with skills can be a good fighter.
Probably arguable but some will say only ROL pigs are worth keeping, particularly if they have high counter attack percentage.

Wanwans can become some of the best attackers, especially after Rebirth, but are VERY hard to get all the necessary skills. Fast hi-initiative wans can run into the "first to die" category when facing passive damage. Sumo, steel skin, burning fever must haves. Overload a must for full multi hit. Double skills to enhance. Leafy to heal. Love hurtz to evade. Pain in the butt to get them all but its possible.

Gorillos have a soft place in my heart, but probably harder than wanwans to get just right. They deserve an honorable mention.

Im not gonna go into any detail about the rest but I think most would agree with what I wrote.

Cornduck summed it up pretty well.
What I want to add, are some notes for the bonus skills (I'll call it demonic skill) a demon race gets, which the base races don't have.

The demonic skill of Winks is pretty much useless, because armor plays allmost no factor in PvP.

The demonic skill from Kabukis a Moueffes are region based skills, meaning you only get the Bonus when you are in Caushemesh (Kabuki) or the Dark World (Moueffe).

I'd say Glidwings have the best demonic skill, but unless you can manage a total freak build, the race itself will be pretty much useless in PvP.

It's arguable if the demonic skill of Wanwans is that much of a bonus - I believe some player's even turn it off to have a better Chance at triggering better E-Skills.
Personally, I usually keep it activated though.

The demon skill of Softpigs, is decent. It ignores Bubble, and if I remember correctly, you also don't get passive Damage when attacking. But usually you'll go with high Multi-Attacks-Builds and want to have these instead of Softpig Charge.

The Gorilloz's demon skill adds 5 strength to all assualts. In early levels this is OK, but in higher levels it doesn't make too much of a difference.

Did I miss a race?
