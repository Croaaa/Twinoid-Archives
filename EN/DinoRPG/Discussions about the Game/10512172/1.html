since you cant affect what elements you roll, i guess u meant "what skills" u should choose

first of all, as written in other threads, think about what you want to do with your dinoz .

starting with your moueffe : since there are no u-skills in the fire tree, i advice you to take every single tier 1 skill, unlock, then do as u wish, but dont ignore smashroomhunter . You will get some ideas of how gathering works, can increase your income, and unlocking lava flow isnt that bad to , so just grab everything in fire and experiement with it. I dont think that you should focus on getting the perfect moueffe ( that is : defensive moueffe ) yet, its too early imho.
air : take strategy for the vendetta double skills
wood : continue down the carapace path to get vines and primal state , u might also choose endurance before unlocking to get basalt armor double skill eventually
water : perception is something you wouldnt have choosen if you are a more advanced player, but its not that bad either right now. At least you havent choosen watercanon . So just enjoy your mouffes ability to hit intangible enemys and follow down the mutation path from now on. If you continue to roll lots of water, you might even go for clones ( taking ventral pouch AND underwater karate next time before unlocking again to get sumo )

even though you´ve choosen perception, your moueffe is still able to reach everything you want him to reach.

sirain :
carapace was a good choice again ( it always is, to get vines , wich is one of the must have skills )
strenght is a bad skill for non fire dinoz, if you dont aim for the double skills basalt armor or sprint. Wouldnt recommend any of those beeing worth to get on a sirain . So next time : choose wrath / unlock / warm blood to be on the safe side, or think about other possibilitys beforehand. Strength sounds nice to a beginner ( " hey , +1 to every attack means +5 in total ! ") but it totally sucks, so its only worth getting if you want something else in that tree, or are swimming in fire ups due to your elemental chances
water is also pretty messed up. Sirains should get all 3 water tier 1 skills at the beginning. But that doesnt matter now, just take mutation the next time, and then your best of taking apprentice fisher to increase your income even more, and cold shower to prepare your dino getting the shopkeeper U-skill. U should try getting cooker too, while trying out some of the water skills, and freeze him eventually later on. U should get the PDA from skully, and the fountain pearl, with him before freezing . ( Fountain pearl : asap ^^ )

if you have no clue of what iam talking about : take a look at some skill pages : http://en.dinorpg.com/clan/125 for example. If you search around the forum, you should easily find enough advise on how to start and what to do
