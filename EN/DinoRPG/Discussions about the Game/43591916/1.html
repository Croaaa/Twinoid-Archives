As you may not know, we can ask some few questions to the DinoRPG developers team on each FR Gazette edition.

Posts have to be formatted this way:

QX (number X): Short and concise question in bold

If argumentation, in italics

The most likened (or +1) questions will go through and selected by the FR moderation team and myself. Then it will be answered on this month Gazette edition.

French players are forbidden to post on this topic, since they got their own on FR forums.

Answers from last time:
 September 2014

1. Will it be possible to reduce the amount of devourer seeds required for Jasper K. Quest? Or reduce their selling value, either a limit per account without validating the quest?
“We’re aware of the abuses around the Devourers. We will think about several things to avoid those.”

2. Could we have more informations about Lv80?
“We didn’t think about lv80 yet, but it could be coming in the 1st part of next year, but we cannot guarantee it.”

3. Could we get every races into demons?
“They will be coming little by little. It will never be at once.”

4. Could it be possible to add more Brutforce tower levels?
“We have added that on our to-do list.”

5. Could we have a descending/ascending order for prices on the market?
“We will think about it.”

6. For the future skills updates (through the amphitheater), what can be modified, in termets of values and/or skills?
“I cannot make a list for you, but as I said before we can’t change one skill effect. i.e: Mud Wall can protect up to 30 damages dealt, that value can be downgraded to 20 or upgraded to 70, depending on the community wishes.”

7. Will there be a new race this year?
“No there won’t be any, but we could do a new rare dinoz for the end of the year grid.”

8. What is the exact effect of the Temportal Reduction?“This magic item reduces the effect of any A, E, P skills on initiative within one fight.”

9. What skills and magic items affect clones?
“This answer would need a lot of research to be done. It would be better to give us a list of what you think may affect them, and we could answer you on each one of those.”

Well to be more precise on Q1, Lv80 is coming only after new map. Since nothing is hinting about any new map soon, you may want to ask something else.

For Q8-2nd: Ok, amphitheatre. This explains the so called promise.

The Amphitheatre is a Twinoid group made by players, with the nod of our devs (so official in its own way), to discuss about suggestions. Those players (called senators) have to postulate for a 6-months mandate and then chosen by moderators, "builders" (players who direct debates) and myself.

How it's functioning? well there's one topic on FR named Wishlist, people who want their suggestion to be discussed post there. Other people grade it with a +1 and suggestion is sent to the group if it reach ten +1 (right now 10, may be revised), regardless of the -1.

Then the group discuss on it until a consensus is being made. Then they summarized the texts and sent it back through Gazette to be answered by Gyhyom and published. They have no regards on what devs will do about their suggestions.

You can see the first round of suggestions/answers on this article.
Short and fast translation:

  • 1. About Travelling
    Answer: Affirmative, on their to-do list.
  • 2. On Lories
    Answer: "We will see". Already updated, see here.
  • 3. Tea/Stabilizer combo.
    Answer: "We will see". Already updated, see here.
  • 4. Superdome.
    Answer: "We won't update it".
  • 5. New ingredients exchange with the Shady Man.
    Answer: Refused.
  • 6. Skill balance.
    Answer: check Question to dev from Sept 2014 answer to Q2 given on 1st page. Amphitheatre group can't discuss without no knowledge on Lv80. Lv80 could be one answer to skill balance (or a total game killing).
  • 7. Multi hit threshold.
    Answer: Updated or so...They updated it according to their own idea and not from the Amphitheatre ideas...

So no promise. Totally not.
