With Motion-Twin's declaration on Twinoid and its (somehow) ending, a group of players have been reunited on Discord, to recreate Twinoid and their games (even the old ones), from DinoParc, through DinoCard, to DinoRPG. We're already around 400 players from any communities (and there's even deepnight on the list).
Due to forum rules, I cannot put the discord link here, you need to check on my account's nexus (wall).

Right now, we need to wait for some green light from MT part, in case they give us some assets directly or we're going to make everything from scratch. The group also did some legal work prior to this announcement, etc...

On april 23, 2020
Kayin

As Skool gave us MT's greenlight to continue their works on the web, we also have the rights to post the Discord link (so here you go). If it's expired, you can ask for it again or ask someone who's already there to invite you.

While this greenlight includes all their web games, starting from first Monlapin (don't worry if you don't know about this obscure one ) up to their latest Monster Hotel, it doesn't mean we will end up with all their game scripts revealed. But all the graphic assets, be it their .swf or anything else are free to use.

The Eternal Twin project goals are recreating the games as their current exact same states as they are right now (except it will be free of bugs, exploits etc...) and will try to follow MT policies of free spirit.

Since the main contributors are FR players, they speak mostly in French, but several of us are (somehow) good English writers. So don't hesitate to speak up on the DinoRPG channel.
