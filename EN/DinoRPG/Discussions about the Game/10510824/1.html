To get to Katatombs you need your dinoz to be with HP maximum 10 at Frish Port. If you have 10HP or below, the next turn on the map will appear Twilight Cemetery as well. If you manage to get to the Cemetery without dying (meaning that you won't encounter piranhoz) then an Enter button will appear, so you can find your way through the Katatombs.
Be careful, in the katatombs you need strong dinoz, high level ones and team them up!!!
There are 4 maps you need to finish there in order to get Demon Scrolls epics.
