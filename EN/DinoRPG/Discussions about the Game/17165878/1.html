Dinoz Masters!

After a significant delay, we are proud to officially announce the winner of Inter-Dojo Tournament XXI: Wooloomooloo! Woo has wone the IDT before, but that doesn't mean that it means any less. It is a tough title to claim as any master will tell you, and an extremely tough feat to repeat. We caught up with Wooloomooloo to see what tips we could pass along to the community.

"I dont really have anything special to say...but Ive done it again!! I won for the 2nd time!! I know it sounds cocky but Im proud and happy of my achievement!
I just used my 3 best dinoz, i.e 2 d-winkses and pink cloudoz. Pink being very important here...not the fujin, which causes lots of talks lately. Must have items in IDT were beer, mono and TM. Good bye rocks and wans, long live winkses and cloudz. As far as the strategy goes, I rely on pills and injections as well as on potions to slow down enemies or hurt big fire dinoz. Thanks to everybody for great fights and to LOoT for their support!"

Congratulations Wooloomooloo - not only on becoming 2-time IDT Champion, but also for having the toughest name to fit in the pictures!

A false start to IDT XXII means a slightly longer wait until the next champion is crowned... but who will it be?
