You need to take into consideration that skills have a chance of activating. Skills like water cannon have about a 25% chance of activating, because sometimes you can activate it about 3 times in a row.

However, powerful skills like leafy Dawn, meteors and blazing twilight tend to have a much smaller chance of being activated but since they are level 4 skills, they activate before the smaller level skills, for example:

Order of A skills:
1. level 4 skill(s) - if it doesn't activate then it moves down to the level 3 skill(s).
2. Level 3 skill(s) - " " to level 2 skill(s).
3. Etc etc...down to level 1 skill(s). If it doesn't activate, then your dinoz uses an assault.

E skills are similar instead if your level 1 skill doesn't activate, it moves on the A skills, if you have any.

However, all skills regardless of level have different chances of activating. One example would be magic resistance which is a level 3 skill which activates quite frequently (but is a rather pointless skill in my opinion if you have primal state or some poisonites). Another example is tornado (a level 2 skill), which can barely activate, like meteors.

NB: If you have 2 skills in the same level group, their chances of activating are decreased in order to prevent a case where you could have a 100% chance of activating at least 1 skill on a particular level.
