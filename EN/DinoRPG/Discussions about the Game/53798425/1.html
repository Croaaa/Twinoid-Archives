How about separating it to three reward category?

1, According to the rank of your clan at the end of the war and the number of participants (a clan considered as participating with at least 1 won war and as much mana as would get by have a standing castle for 1/4th of the event), you get the following rewards:
first place: 4 napodinoz, 1 x egg, 1 y egg (where x is the most wanted and y is the second most wanted race)
upper quarter: 3 napodinoz, 1 y egg (by upper quarter I mean places 2nd-5th in case of 20 participating clans, 2nd-6th with more clans)
middle section: 2 napodinoz (6th-15th in case of 20 clans, 7th-x with more)
lower quarter: 1 napodinoz (last 5 with 20 participants, 6 with more)

Notice that all clans get napodinoz which were at least try to participate.

2, In addition to the napoz and eggs, your clan is awarded by treasury notes. This is based on a different ranking, according to the number and quality of your won wars. The system would be the same as with the hidden point rankings in the CotC, so if you won 3 wars against the clan which had the third highest mana at the end of the event, and 20 clans would participate, that would worth you 3*(20-3) = 51 points in the end.

Your clan (!) gets 10 times the gathered point amount in treasury notes, which is distributed amongs the members by one of this rules:
- evenly
- distributed by the clan leader
- according to the attacks/defenses/damage done/received in the event (a.k.a. player statistics)

So if you achieve more with less members you get more rewards per head, which would encourage clans with fewer members, not all the power would gather into 5-6 clans.

3, Finally, additionally to the previous categories, all players would receive gold or irma potions according to the number of attacks/defenses they made. Not a big thing, like 1 irma per attack. This can't easily be exploited, since you effectively need 1-6 irma to attack anyway, and in defense you lose actions anyway. The purpose of this reward category would be compensate for the standby status in the war, namely that you need to unfreeze your fighters, decrease the number of gatherers for that time and use them as scouts, effectively crippling your ability to make gold during the war unless you leave the work to the others.


This is only a draft, but I'm interested if everyone would find this acceptable/improvement.

Cheers

Ps: other rewards can be spheres, pvp items etc, with them the rewards can be scaled more easily and diversely.
