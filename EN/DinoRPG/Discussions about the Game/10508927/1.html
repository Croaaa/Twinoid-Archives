Hiya. I have read and read and read over and over and over all the whining and complaints about the "turn-based" system that was implemented, and those complaining about how this should be done or that should be done...

Look folks...The bottom line is, the best things in life are not free. If you wanna' play, you gotta' pay.

The devs have developed this game, and many like it, for one purpose... to make money.

Yes, you read that correctly. To make MONEY.

Why is that so difficult to understand? It is a classic form of the shareware concept in software development. You give users a taste, and if they want more, they pay. In the case of DinoRPG, you can play more if you BUY MORE GOLD.

I am sure many people have done just that...They tried the game, liked it, but do not wish to wait X-amount of hours for a new turn, and so purchase coins with RL monies to be able to take many turns per day.

It is a business model. Video games are a BUSINESS. They are not created for YOUR benefit, but for the developers' benefit. They want to make money, and the turn-based system allows them to maximize the profitability of their efforts.

Once you understand that, you won't have such a hard time coming to grips with your "I wanna play the whole thing RIGHT NOW FOR FREE" idiom.

End of Line.
