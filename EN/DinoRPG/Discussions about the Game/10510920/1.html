Korgon Reinforcements give a little helper. Quite Useful.

Vines takes away enemies Initiative, very nice.

Primal State takes away negative status's like poison and takes away enemy positive status's like Accelerated when activated in battle. Very Useful.

Precious Spring heals all friendly dinoz according to your wood element.

Wild Instinct Increases your wood element (makes every thing else including normal attacks better.

Acrobat gives your dinoz a 15% speed boost.

Growth Gives a bonus to HP. You will survive longer

Sympathetic And Charisma make your dinoz be able to lead an additional dinoz. Great for making large PVP groups.

Lot's of useful stuff in the wood element, not designed to be particularily damaging I believe the wood element has 0 attack skills, the wood spheres have a acron throw and a +100% damage (like wrath/focus but better) skill, but those will take time to get
The wood element is good for support or to make a attacking dino slightly better by being able to support as well as strike heavy hits.
