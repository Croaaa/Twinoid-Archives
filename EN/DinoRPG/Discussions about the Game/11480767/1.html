On may 7, 2012
Kayin

Strangely the rules are only readable at Dinoplaza, so here's a copy/paste:

The Dinoz Beauty Contest is made up of two stages:

Stage 1 - Qualifying Rounds

During the 10 first days of the contest, all Dinoz can register for the contest. Voting is open to all users in order to select the 9 finalists for the 2nd phase of the competition.

To find out more about winning votes, first register your Dinoz for the competition and then read the rules..

Stage 2 - The Final

The 9 finalists are the Dinoz who have won the most votes by other players.

The final will last 4 days and will determine the three winners of the competition.

During the final, only players registered on DinoRPG and who took part in the Beauty Contest can vote for the finalists. Votes which are submitted using the page used for the preliminary stages will not be counted.

The three winners are determined at the end of the final.

Rewards

The winner will win 50.000 and a Gold Medal
The 2nd placed finalist will win 30.000 and a Silver Medal
The 3rd placed finalist will win 10.000 and a Broze Medal
The 6 other finalists will each win 3.000

Please Note: A Dinoz who has already won a medal cannot take place in future contests.
