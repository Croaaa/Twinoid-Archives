It's called the "Law of Large Numbers".

This Wiki definition is as good as any http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Law_of_larg e_numbers

Basically I'm saying that 20 or 30 players can do the Mahatmuti Quest and not get an egg, since each of them have a 1/6 chance. And the fact that the first person does NOT get an egg has no bearing on the 2nd, 3rd, or 30th. But if you do 1000 trials it should work out to about 1/6.
