I believe the Actual 1.3 Rule was take the Points of the Attackers(S) [= Sum of Level + 1/2 the # of Spheres}. The Rule was intended to allow Weaker Clans to successfuly attack stronger Clans that had Strong Defenders and not get wiped out by 5 Strong Defenders.
