Since you have a gorilloz not too far behind, I would go for Korgon reinforcement and leave the leader skill to the gorilloz.
But for the detailed analysis:

Tenacity
Advantages:
- more damage is always good
- leads to Charisma, which is important if you want to use your castivorous as a group leader
- leads to Leader which one of your dinoz should certainly get at some point (though, your gorilloz would be better for that)
Disadvantages:
- the +1 damage of Tenacity really isn't that much help in the short term

Korgon Reinforcement
Advantages:
- brings a friend who can deal damage
- brings a friend who will soak up damage (decoy)
- leads to Wild Instinct, which will increase your wood damage, as well as wood, water and lightning defense
- leads to Acrobat which will increase your speed
Disadvantages:
- Korgon Reinforcement will not be activated every fight, so it doesn't always help

In the short term, Korgon reinforcement will probably be more useful. In the long term, you will probably end up with both, especially if you use your castivorous as a group leader.
