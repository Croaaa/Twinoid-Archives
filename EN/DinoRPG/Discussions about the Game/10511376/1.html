Sorry for double post, but with the no skills thing, how do you know you won't get more fire level ups than wood?

That said, you don't actually lose any skills gained when you get specialist, you just won't do any fire assaults if it's your lowest

For example, a cloudoz with one up and specialist, has burning claws, in this case the skill is useless as it only works for fire assaults.

But if you had the same cloudoz but with wrath for their fire skill, your cloudoz could still use wrath (because you don't directly need to be doing a assault to use it).

Thats why I've planned out taking wrath, fury and vengeance, as there is a very small chance I'll get those level ups, and I'll then be able to get the double skill vendetta

You should always plan for the eventuality you get some really weird level ups

calorifere
I've seen people discuss cloudoz with no A skills before (mostly intended for killing bubble dinoz), and they tend to have essentially the same builds as what you have suggested.
Given that your build only has 28 ups, you still have some room for extra skills.

Possible add-ons:
- add Lightning attack and Hazard in
- add Jump and Plunging Attack in (I know, I know, Plunging Attack is technically an A skill, but it really behaves more like an E skill)
- add Lightning (disable) in and Marsh in and go for the double skill Electrolysis

Also, you should probably plan to get Wrath and Warm blood in and Carapace and Vines in should you hit them before you have Career plan

However, note that this kind of build, while strong against bubble dinoz, would not be as good to deal damage to everything else (and outside clan wars, you won't meet bubble dinoz that frequently). A more standard cloudoz build with Kaar-Sher Beam and Tornado activated would be much better damage dealers to anything without bubble.
Note also that, when it comes to killing bubble dinoz, a well built Castivorous with the double skill is probably better.

For your No A skills build, I would however suggest two things you should take into consideration,

Breath Of Life,

Leafy Dawn,

Though technically it is an A skill, so activating it will loose you an attack, gaining HP half way through the fight can be a tide turner, youll live longer to do more attacks
