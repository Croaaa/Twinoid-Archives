Theres already a whole thread about this quest:
http://en.dinorpg.com/forum/thread/70072

But to answer your questions:
This quest can only be done once, but all your dinoz can participate.
The megawolf will only appear if you press fight at Dinotown, not if you move to or from Dinotown, as far as i know.
The megawolf does the same demege as a normal wolf, but ist has much more (i think 70) and defense. So a level 1 dino wont be a good idea. I had a group of 4 level 11-12 dinoz attack him and i think he survived the main element attack of all of them because of his defense, i think the off elements did so vew damage that only the next main element attack of one of my dinoz killed him. He did only do 5 damage in that time, but it would probably be a bad idea to attack him with a low level dino alone.

There is no point in rushing to kill the Megawolf. Any of your Dinoz can progress the quest so you might as well just wait and kill it when you happen to be there anyway.
I would certainly not waste three turns moving to Dinotown, fighting and moving back to FOY)

Another point is that it appears that you need the Climbing Gear (which you do not yet have) for the next part of the quest so even if you kill the Megawolf you will see no benefit for a while.

Also, if you wait until one of your Dinoz is doing the Wolf Hunting quest then the Megawolf will apparently be much easier to kill (due to confusion between Wolf and Megawolf quests when both are active)
