Assassins Clan got this helpful guide: http://en.dinorpg.com/clan/2?page=213

When you come to a place where you can collect something, an action box gonna appear where the other actions are (move, fight etc) related to the collectible skill the dinoz got.
You can collect with any skill lvl (i.e. you have giant hunter, you can hunt at any hunting grid available, same with smashroom hunter (low lvl) and dragon hunter (highest lvl)).

Also, you don't lose your turn by clicking on the collecting action (hunt, fish, energize, excavate etc).
