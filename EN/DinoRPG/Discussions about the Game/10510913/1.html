Veren
no time limit for the upgrades, they work as long as your castel stand up
but once your castel is destroyed, all upgrades are lost and you have to rebuild them if you want them again


the time countdown is the time it require to build the upgrade

so for exemple, if you want to build the iron plates you have to

1) launch the wood plate upgrade, wait 6 hours for the upgrade to finish

2) launch the copper plates upgrade, wait 12 hours for the upgrade to finish

3) launch the iron plates upgrade, wait 100 hour for the upgrade to finish (4 days and 4 hours)
of course you only benefit from the upgrade when it is finished so hope you'll not be attacked while upgrading, or make sure your castel and defenders hold on
