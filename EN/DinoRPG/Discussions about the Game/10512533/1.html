Poisoned: if it is from Poisoned Claws, it always hurts 14. If it is from Fetid Breath or Toxic Cloud it is equal to the element (like Pirvan3 said). And finally if it is from Smelly Aura it always hurts 10.
Duration: five cycles.

Slowed: the Dinoz adds the double of "velocity points" after each assault (in other words, the Dinoz attacks once when he should attack twice).

Accelerated: contrary to slowed. The Dinoz adds half of "velocity points" so he attacks twice when he should attack once. The duration is the same.

Petrified: the petrified Dinoz can't attack for an undetermined number of cycles.

Sleeping: the Dinoz can't attack (like petrified) but he wake up in a little number of cycles - or when he is attacked.

Shield: +6 points to all defenses. I think it is permanent but I'm not sure...

Blessed: +3 points to all attacks. I also think it is permanent.

Intangible: the Dinoz doesn't take damage. It is removed by assaults and skills. Duration of five cycles.

Wobby
Nap lasts one turn. Obviously the Sleep status for the Potion lasts for 3 turns.

Poison lasts for 5 turns, although this is for Poisoned Claws, and I cannot confirm the duration for Fetid Breath, Toxic Cloud, or Smelly Aura (actually I can confirm it, I'm just too lazy to find clanmates with those skills)

Accelerated status is x2.0 speed, and Vivacious Wind and Mass Frenzy do not stack, as they are the exact same status.

Blessed is +2 assault value to all assaults, or some minuscule amount that makes absolutely no difference in PvP. Either way, it's useless.

Shield is believed to be +6 to all defenses, as stated above. Doesn't make much of a difference in PvP either.

Slowed as stated earlier, is x0.5 speed.

Intangible is not only removed by assaults and attacks. attacks will also remove the status. attacks are Wipeout, Softpig Charge, and Earth Tremor... Add any that I forgot.

Petrified either lasts for 2 turns, or for 4 turns. There are two possible mechanics to it, and it's either that the Petrified dinoz no longer affects the amount of moves needed in a turn (for example, 2 vs 2, one dinoz Petrified, now 3 moves per turn), resulting in a period of 2 turn Petrification. If not that, then it lasts for 4 turns (including the turn and move that it took to use the Petrification skill).

Another interesting thing to note about statuses, is that when reinforcements are called, like Korgons or Clones, the reinforcements actually prolong each turn. Let's say a fight is 2 vs 2, so there needs to be a total of 4 moves for one turn to elapse. If later in the match, there are 5 Korgons on the field, then a total of 9 moves are needed for one turn to elapse. This is pretty interesting, because if you pull off a Detonation chain with a ton of Korgons on the field with an Accelerated status, it will take much longer for the status to wear off. I'm actually not 100% sure if this applies to Korgons and Gorriloz Spirits, but I know that it applies to Clones during the short amount of time that they are alive.

[895738]
I have to admit, there's a lot of misleading information here. I'll add in my knowledge:

Poison:
Poison claws - deals 14 damage and lasts 4 turns. I know this because as soon as the poison is gone, you could get poisoned again just 2 battle turns after the poison effects wear off.
Toxic Cloud - same damage as element. Lasts about 4 turns, too. Area of effect.
- Mantooze toxic cloud - deals 3 damage, and is like toxic cloud.
Fetid Breath - same damage as element, however, it is PERMANENT! I've seen this in the dojo and it is annoying indeed. Only a skill like primal state can remove it (and of course, poisonites and antidote).
- Scorpwinks poison is also permanent and deals 5 damage.
Smelly Aura - 10 damage, lasts 4 turns. Triggers very often.
- Bigshroom contamination - lasts 2-3 turns and deals 3 damage. Activates when hit, like smelly aura.

Benediction - +3 damage, lasts 3 turns. Note: useful for those who have the capture glove, because this skill increases monsters' void damage by 3. I have seen a blessed smashroom do 5 damage. However, for dinoz, it is useless. It should have a tenacity effect: increase minimum damage by 3.

Hermetic aura - +6 defence, permanent.

speed - lasts 3 turns. Is most effective with passive speed buffs, as well relaxative pill. Not very effective against fast monsters, since I've seen slowed chickens and demyoms still do 2 attacks at once.

Slow - lasts 3 turns. I really don't know why players don't turn off gel for the very same dinoz as marsh.

Intangible:
Vaporous Form - 2 turns
Ethereal form - forever. Really good in pve, but do not get this skill for pvp!
Gorilloz spirit intangible - permanent until removed
moukitz intangible - about 3-4 turns
Kazkadine intangible - 2 turns
Other things
- Wipeout does NOT remove intangible, but maybe that's because my mahamuti does not have .
- Earth Tremor does remove intangible because it is a skill.

Petrification: - lasts 3 turns. It does remove flying and immaterial. Affected dinoz can't dodge or counter-attack, let alone attack, compared to a vined dino, which can still dodge and counter-attack.

Flying: - lasts 1 turn, but is the only status that can be reset if the dino activates flight multiple times in a row. Also makes dino immune to earth tremor wipeout as well as non-jumping assaults.
- Frukoptere flight is permanent, is an E skill and it enables non-flying dinoz to fly, even petrified ones! Never ever send dinoz without A skills and/or primal state to monster island.

NB: All statuses do not stack and their timers are not reset. For example, if your dinoz activates benediction again 2 turns after it initially did, the positive status will actually disappear after the third turn. With poison, if your dinoz has been poisoned by poison claws and then smelly aura, only the initial poison damage prevails. That means the dinoz will still suffer 14 damage each time.

Hope this information helps.

General information:
If Dinoz has a status and suffers from action that should give him the same status, then it doesn't get it and the time counter of duration continue working. Statuses are not stackable if they come from different skills (like different poison or acceleration or slowing)


Using of Primal State or Saving Puree by one of your Dinoz remove all statuses.

Duration will be described in terms of cycles:
According to the French admins:
A cycle cost 6 (60 with new motor changes) initiative points and represents the duration between the application of the status effects (poison, torch, accupuncture, etc).

Poisoned:
Damage from
Poison Claws: 14
Fetid Breath or Toxic Cloud: air element
Smelly Aura: 10
Duration: 5 cycles (duration from poison from fetid breath is permanent now but it is a bug. Source)

Damage from
Mantooze toxic cloud: 3
Duration: like toxic cloud
Scorpwinks poison: 5 damage
Duration: permanent?
Bigshroom contamination - 3
Duration: 2-3 turns?
Notes
Poisonite prevents Dinoz from getting status poisoned
Magic item Antidote prevents Dinoz from getting status poisoned


Flying:
Dinoz is immune to earth tremor, wipeout, vines, as well as non-jumping assaults.
Duration: lasts 1 turn, but is the only status that can be reset if the dino activates flight multiple times in a row.
Notes:
Frukoptere flight is permanent, is an E skill and it enables non-flying dinoz to fly, even petrified ones! Never ever send dinoz without A skills and/or primal state to monster island.
One Master Levitator in group make all assaults "jumping assaults"
Using of "mobile love" make all assaults of a dinoz "jumping assaults"

Slowed:
The Dinoz adds the double of "velocity points" after each assault.
Duration: 5 cycles

Accelerated:
Contrary to slowed. The Dinoz adds half of "velocity points" so he attacks twice when he should attack once.
Duration: 5 cycles

Petrified:
The petrified Dinoz can't attack, but can counter-attack, evade and become intangible.
Duration: 5 cycles
Notes:
Petrification cancels intangible status and flying.


Sleeping:
The Dinoz can't attack (like petrified), but can dodge.
Duration: few cycles or if dinoz will be attacked

Shield:
Dinoz gets +6 points to all defences.
Duration: 5 cycles

Blessed:
Dinoz gets +3 points to all attacks.
Duration: 5 cycles
Notes: Useful for those who have the capture glove, because this skill increases monsters' void damage by 3.

Intangible:
The Dinoz doesn't take damage.
Intangible status from:
Vaporous Form: Duration: 5 cycles?
Immaterial: Duration: permanent, till cancelled
Gorilloz spirit intangible - the same with immaterial
Moukitz intangible - about 3-4 turns
Kazkadine intangible - 2 turns?
Notes:
Dinoz with Perception always remove this status after assault, regardless of whether it hits the immaterial dinoz or the dinoz dodges the assault.
Using of air assault (but you must have at least one air element) or A-skills (Mistral, Tornado) or skill Earth Tremor remove the intangible status.
Softpig Charge remove the intangible status.
Wipeout doesn't remove intangible status.
Some of Monsters, that have elemental and not only void attacks can remove the intangible status.
Petrification remove this status.
When your dinoz lose intangible status he lose only 1 HP it doesn't matter how strong was attack or assault, that removed that status.


Another interesting info on subject:

Another interesting thing to note about statuses, is that when reinforcements are called, like Korgons or Clones, the reinforcements actually prolong each turn. Let's say a fight is 2 vs 2, so there needs to be a total of 4 moves for one turn to elapse. If later in the match, there are 5 Korgons on the field, then a total of 9 moves are needed for one turn to elapse. This is pretty interesting, because if you pull off a Detonation chain with a ton of Korgons on the field with an Accelerated status, it will take much longer for the status to wear off. I'm actually not 100% sure if this applies to Korgons and Gorriloz Spirits, but I know that it applies to Clones during the short amount of time that they are alive.

General information:
If Dinoz has a status and suffers from action that should give him the same status, then it doesn't get it and the time counter of duration continue working. Statuses are not stackable if they come from different skills (like different poison or acceleration or slowing)

Saving Puree: Using of Saving Puree remove all negative statuses (slowed, poisoned, petrified, burned (from Infernal Talons of Quetzu)) on your group, it activates only if one ore more dinoz in your group have negative status.

Primal State: Triggering of Primal State remove all negative statuses from your group and all positive statuses from your enemies. Torch on enemy dinoz will disappear. Healing from Acupuncture will disappear, but your dinoz will still get 1 HP damage when attack the enemy dinoz with Acupuncture. Your dinoz with Torch will still become 1 HP damage.
