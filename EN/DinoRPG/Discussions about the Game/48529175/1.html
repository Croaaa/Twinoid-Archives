You will also need some elemental fission gatherers to ensure that you have sufficient energies when doing EM missions. Once you work on Gulom's missions, you can shift them to collect energies.

As for ratio of fighters : gatherers, everyone has their own preference. I'd personally recommend a bit more fighters than gatherers because, as you progress, you can maximize your choices in mixing and matching to create your dream team. There are chances when you may get rather dud fighters and then you might end up concluding that your fighting crew isn't up to scratch. You also need diversity to respond to certain setups that your opponent(s) might rely on either in dojo (IDT) or events.

Having more gatherers than fighters is best if you know exactly what you're aiming for in your dream team, which is also backed up by advice from pro players. Then it's a matter of financing your dinoz with hunters/gatherers. So far, hunting is the preferred approach. With hunters, the common practice also seems to be to recycle them once they reach level 20 or something. It works particularly well for hunters because softpigs have a very good chance of getting and becoming gatherers by level 3. However, it does require some activity which may not be available if you're busy during the week.

I haven't read the whole thread, but looking at your account I see some reasons while your income become smaller:

Low level dinoz generally get more gold:
- more actions a day
- the xp and the gold you get om from fights correlates

Since your dinoz are around level 20, the initial locations (dinoland, islands, vulcano, grumhel forest) aren't giwing you good xp and income. You should move to the higher level locations to get more gold with them. The best choice in your case is the magnetic steppes, between 20 and 30 you get tremendous amount of gold there, and the xp is very good too (though its better in the Dark World).

Other thing is that you have to restructure your accounts. Every slot has its purpose: the U skillers should be frozen, and you should divide the 18-21-24 active slots between different diinoz, like 9 gatherers and 9 fighters. There's no state in the game where you can effectively level every one of your dinoz, you should focus your income on a few of them.
