Hello! In name of all the LBT clans members, I would like to introduce to you the first ever Portuguese forum of DinoRPG:

We created this forum[/lien] to unite all the Portuguese speakers under one forum, speaking our mother language: the Portuguese. Most of us can easily speak English, but nothing compares to the joy of speaking about the game in our original language. When we decided that our clan forum wasn't good enough to do it, because we couldn't have contact with beginners (because of our high requirements). So, we decided to create a whole new forum (since in this one English is compulsive), to which we want to invite all Portuguese speakers to join in discussing the game, but this time in Portuguese!

We want ALL Portuguese speaking players to register on it, from the ones that just begun, to the top players. We want to create in this forum an active and friendly community, and for that we need everyone!

Our goals with this are:
- for Portuguese-speaking players to get to know each other better, and have fun together;
- to discuss the game;
- to give advice and tips, not only to beginners, but to more advanced players too - an exchange of information to help each other grow;
- and of course, to show everybody how fun it is to be part of LBT, because the forum will also be a place of recruiting! If you are a beginner, you should join this forum if you expect to join LBT later on! This forum was made thinking of helping beginners, so that they are stronger when they join LBT!

We hope this forum will work as we expect it to be, and to get good results in the future (we are patient, and understand it can take some time to reach success). We also hope that MotionTwin and DinoRPG's team can see we are doing a service to the game and the community in introducing this forum, as we hope it will bring more interest and participation in the game to Portuguese-speaking players, who otherwise would not participate, or at least not as often, in the game forum, where we are obliged to speak English. Like I already said, it's not that we can't speak English, it's just that we prefer Portuguese!
