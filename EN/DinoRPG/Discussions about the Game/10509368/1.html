That is not true, you can get beginning gathering skills as low as level 3. (5 adv, 7 expert). Of course that is with a whole lot of luck. But still, I have quite a few dinoz that had at least adv resource finding profession by lvl 6-7.

Obviously, you'd want to put secondary ups in skills that boost your combat capacity, since you will be gimped by getting harvesting skills in your main tree. So for example if you have a goriloz rushing for last tier of archeologist, you should pick up stuff like burning claws and wrath in fire tree, agility in air, etc.

Hunter: First get burning claws, unlock next skills, then pick smashroom hunter.

search: first get endurance, unlock next skills, then pick search.

gathering: First get strategy, unlock next skills, then pick gathering

elements: first get intelligence, unlock next skills, then pick lightning conductor

fisher: first get perception, unlock next skills, then pick apprentice fisher.

If you level up again at an element, it is possible to unlock the better version of an gather skill. Lets say you unlocked gathering, and you level up at air again. unlock next skills, and then pick sharp eye. that will give you the ability to get more valuable items.
