Wobby
Since you're starting off with a Gorriloz, you shouldn't have to worry about Leader just yet. You won't really need it until later. But if you want it later in the game, then take the Savagery path for .
Since Gorriloz get plenty of levels ups, you can make it into a collecter/gatherer if you want. Take all 3 paths, just be sure to get Search, Detective, Archaelogist, and Planner. See here for Wood paths.

And this game really isn't like Pokemon. Balance between elements does not really mean a better team.

And some tips: Don't buy Iramas, don't buy food (use Resting instead), do the Grandpa Joe quests until you're about level 8-9, fight Water Elemental or Fire Elemental (water is easier)... Just about it.

For your second Dinoz... It's really up to you. Glidwings, Sirans, Cloudoz, Wanwan, or Mouffe, it's really up to you. Just don't get Castis. They're sort of useless. For your 3rd, just get whatever you want.

Average gorilloz can get both Engineer and Leader at lvl 16. He could also get one of those at lvl 11. Whether you go for 1 or both really depends on how lucky you are and your personal preference, but if you go for each separately (Engineer is nearly useless unless you're highly enough leveled or in a clan for the champs/war) on one gorilloz you have a much better chance to reach it before you get 18 dinoz without forking over cash for irmas. But at the same time you'll end up freezing at least one more dinoz. Life is full of trade-offs.

Oh, and gathering really isn't that good. there's only 3 flints in a grid of 100, and the artifacts are rarer than rare

But whatever you end up doing, good luck.
