The plural of Energy is, in fact, Energies
Online reference

If you were referring to different energy types (e.g. Fire and Lightning at Basalt Slopes) then Energies would definitely be correct.

If you are referring to only energy then it is more of a grey area. As Energy is a quantity, twice as much of the same thing is just a larger quantity rather than a plural - but in the context of gathering, it is not a quantity but rather a discrete item and therefore using the plural is appropriate.
