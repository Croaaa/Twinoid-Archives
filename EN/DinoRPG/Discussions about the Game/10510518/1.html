We all know that the item Scale isn't very balanced, and can still be used to make "cheap" kills..

With all the restrictions, the increase in price, etc..
I think its not the right step to balance it.
Our problem is having low level dinoz with low killing strong defenders with the efect of Scale.

In my opinion, the item should deal damage instead of an instant kill.
The damage would be based on the dino at the start of the fight.
For example, if your dino had 126 and died, he would deal those 126 back to the enemy.
However, a low level dino with 100 killing a high level dino with also 100 would not be balanced..
..so i thought the damage could also suffer some % variations depending on the level or element between dinoz.

Imagine a dino equiped with Scale, 100, and with 10.
The enemy dino, being more powerfull and with 15, kills the other dino. Scale triggers.
The dead dino has 2/3 of the enemy dino, so we have a damage reduction.
Due to the diference in levels, the damage from Scale would suffer a penalty of 33%, and it would deal 66 damage back to the enemy instead of the full 100.

This way, the item would be much more balanced, and still powerfull!

What do you think?
(of course the numbers could be tweaked a bit to improve it in terms of balance, but the main idea is here)
