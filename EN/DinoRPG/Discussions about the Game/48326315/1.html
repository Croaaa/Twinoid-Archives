I agree with Afreet. Reinced dinoz are nice for a little extra damage, but it is far more important that you level the good skills.
Also note that the higher and higher level dinoz are going to be, the lesser impact the added elements of reincarnation will have.

5x levelup in a 5% chance element is a pretty good score for a dino that doesnt get CP very soon. On default you will get 50 * 0.05 = 2.5 air levelups without CP (with a maha you probably want to unlock ether skills rather soon because of all the wood ether goodies).
Lets assume you get CP at level 25, (which is actually a low chance because 25 * 0.1 = 2.5 (not 5 levels required for CP));
(25 * 0.05) + (25 * 0.0975) = 3,6875 air levelups on default chance (if you favour air as your first priority with CP).

So what Im trying to say is that it requires some luck to get Bubble (and invoc) on a maha, you've had that luck now, so treasure it.
Try to get Life Breath in the remaining levels to 50 and you have a true winner
