When beginning to play DinoRPG I used Death's Children's pages a lot, and many other clans in the top 20 of the treasury ranking have similar pages to offer. Also some clans like GC and LBT offer pages in different languages (like skill or mission descriptions).

External sources could include Twinpedia, DinoArchivos and Dinocenter (depending on your language skills), but I found Rianon to be more structured/tidy usually.

Personally I use Axie's pages nowadays first (in case Voodoo has no equivalent page) and would recommend them, because BlackandLoki makes a big effort to present only confirmed information, not blindly accepting every hearsay from the players and forums, like some people did in the old days of DinoRPG.
