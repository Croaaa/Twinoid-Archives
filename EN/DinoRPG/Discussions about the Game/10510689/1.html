Say you have two dinos in a group. One of those dinos already has the max exp points for their current level. Will this dino still increase the number of experience points earned in a fight even though that dino will get any exp points for the fight?
On december 7, 2009
[None]
It usually works that a Dinoz gets the same xp per monster as when fighting alone. The difference when grouped is that the group attracts bigger / more monsters than when alone. Whether a Dinoz is on max xp or not will not affect the other Dinoz, as the division will happen the same. When the scores are shown, the max xp Dinoz will lose their xp; the other Dinoz will not.
On december 7, 2009
AndySQdancer
If a dinoz is at his Max Xp in a group and fights, that dinoz will not get any Xp and the Xp he/she would have gotten will be lost. Excess experience for fights is not shared, it is lost. So best level your dinoz up before fighting so that he gets his share of experience.
