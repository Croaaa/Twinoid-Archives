The 23 hour wait is better then the alternative, which is once a day, based on server clock. The answer to your other question is good though. Once you move your first dino, the timer starts counting again. Then when you move your second one, it doesnt impact the timer, and both your dinos use the same timer (the one that restarted when you moved the first guy).

So if you move 1 guy now, then wait 11 hours, move the second one then, wait the remainder of your 12 hour timer and be able to move both your dinoz.
