AndySQdancer
Career Plan works this way. When your Dinoz Levels Up, you get an extra option of Career Plan along with the Skills or Unlock options. If you choose one of the Skills or Unlock options you get that option. If you choose the Career Plan option, you get another spin of the wheel and may get skills of a different element. You then have to choose one of the second options. You do not get another spin or a chance to go back to the first set of options.
