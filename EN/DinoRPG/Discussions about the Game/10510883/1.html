In terms of Clan Size
Larger Clan Pros
Better rewards at end of war
More action (Participation questionable)
More people with more information

Larger Clan Cons
Higher Demands (likely to kick you out if inactive for any amount of time)
Might not get to fight and get stuck on ingredient gathering

Smaller Clan Pros
Better chance to get to fight
Get to know all clan members
Smaller gathering demands

Smaller Clan Cons
Less fights
Less information
Less chance at rewards
