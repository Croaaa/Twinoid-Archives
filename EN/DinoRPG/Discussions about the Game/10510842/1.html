Get A shovel from Coral Mines
Dig In Sticky Swamp
Talk to Atlantean Gaurd in Mutant Falls
* Talk about Scorbascuba (might be spelled wrong :p)Caller*
Repair Shovel at Coral Mines
Go To Basalt Slopes, Dig Basalt Shard
Repair Shovel at Coral Mines
Go To Fountain Of Youth, Dig Pure Water
Rest Here, you need good HP to fight scorbascuba
Go To Firsh Port, Talk to the guy there. He takes you out on a boat to fight the big fish
Go To Mutant Falls
(You Can stop by Coral Mines to fix your shovel again you will need to dig at ashpock ruins at some point to get ashpock totem for bruteforce, you can do this on your way back to dinoland after getting the latern from the forest after getting access via zors glove)
Use Scubascorba Caller to move to Underwater Dome
Talk to Arch - something or other - and fight the earth element to obtain Zors Glove.
Go back to mutant falls, talk to Master hydragol, he gives you water lilly pads, you can now access the Forest, A quick trip in here to the haunted hills will get you the latern which allows you access to the deep zero cave and the other side of the forest and eventually the fins to cross the river in the forest.
