@All:

Thanks to all of you who have proposed suggestions for improvements to the current system of Clan Wars.

However, as I have previously stated, we are in the process of re-evaluating the rules regarding joining and leaving clans, so for anyone who is just sitting sniping, saying "FIX THIS" or similar is not being constructive.

What I did was to try to clarify the current rules for joining and leaving clans. The purpose of this was to inform everyone and to make sure everyone could contribute to conversations on the topic, as this has been a contentious issue recently.

There will be no further clan wars on ANY server until there has been a meeting on this of all the server admins and the game designers. I have been noting your suggestions from forums and PMs to make sure all opinions are considered.

Once the rules have been finalised, they will be circulated on the forums to ensure they are crystal clear before the next war starts.

I look forward to reading more of your suggestions,
