As Eites says you will be able to afford new dinos faster and faster as you go on. The 2nd one takes forever to buy, but the 3rd one is a lot quicker, the 4th one even quicker and so on. Eventually it gets to the point where you just save up and buy 3 at once to make a group.

Additionally it takes longer and longer to get each level which means that ultimately your dinos 'squash together' in levels. So your Cloudoz is levle 9 when you get your second dino, but by the time those dinos are in their 20s, they will probably only be 1-2 levels apart and fine for grouping.
