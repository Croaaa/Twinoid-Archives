The elements and the skills chosen from those elements are chosen by the server. It may or may not be random but they are always preselected and displayed with the Demon picture before you buy.

You, as the purchaser, have the choice:
1 • do not buy
2 • buy a demon that looks nice but has awful skills
3 • buy a demon with good skills that looks awful
4 • wait ages to buy one that both has good skills and looks nice

Most serious people will go for option 4 but may well get bored waiting and take option 3
