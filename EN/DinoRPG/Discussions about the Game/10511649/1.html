He appears randomly at Frish Port.
If you talk to him you can bet 10,000 and you will lose.
If you do not pay him immediately then he will randomly appear at Frish Port until you do.
Once you paid him, he will randomly appear at Frish Port and you can make a second bet for 100,000

Once you have lost, he will run off and his appearances are no longer random.
The next random bit is when he randomly offers to sell you Kabuki eggs at Totem Island - he is always there though... taunting you and asking for a punch in the face.
