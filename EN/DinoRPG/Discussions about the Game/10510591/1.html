Piemaster
So it is best advised to do the larva pit first?
There's a 'window' to do the Islands between levels 8(ish) and 12. If you miss that window then yes, it's better to do the Mountian (where the lava pit is)

How do I access this location?
Fight the fire elemental at the university and get the climbing gear (no problem at your level). Then go to the Precipitous Hills and choose 'Move' and you will have the option to climb the mountain.

I assume you just go there and there should be a guide to with a set of missions, Right?
Actually there are several missions. If you take the Madam X mission called 'The Ashpouk Conspiracy' that will show you around the mountian nicely. After that the Soft Shaman can be found at the Lavapit.

What level is best to defeat the Eeloz?
Solo you are better off waiting until about level 16-18 depending on your dino. You can kill them before but they'll take a lot of health. In a group you can fight them a lot sooner.

IS the Eeloz like a water element where you fight him at one stage or does he appear randomly like glou?
Randomly like a Gluon. But only once you hit level 12, hence why you can do the Islands between levels 8-12 no problem.
