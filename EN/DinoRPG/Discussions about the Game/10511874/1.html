well... the standard "secondary element" (ie, element that you have a low chance of rolling) builds are:
-> Sumo
-> Career Plan
-> Black Hole
-> Burning Heart/Self-Control
-> Primal State (although you often see Growth and Korgons taken too)

so basically you want to go with those skills for your Softpig and Moueffe, except for as they have a good enough chance to get a lot of level ups, meaning you can go for more good skills
I'd say go for Waikikido, Lava Flow, Fire Ball, Meteors, Brave and Burning Heart
You could also go for Cocoon instead of Primal State for and then get the double skill Basalt Armour

something I forgot to mention for your Pteroz, you could go for the double skill Vendetta, although you have to get two pretty useless skills in order to get the required skill on the path for a skill that isn't really that great, but that's my preference, others might think differently
