Dig for the three ingredients: Mud at swamp, water at FOY and basalt at Basalt slopes with shovel.
Acquire scorpsuba caller and go to underwater dome.
Fight earth elemental. Gear up for it as his damage from his main element is huge.
Win the fight and go to mutant falls. Talk to master hydargol. He gives you the lilypad after you acquired the zors glove.
Get lantern and Korgon fins in order to go anywhere in the Grumhel Forest.

And please use search. There's a lot of info around. Read it before posting.

Repair your shovel at coral mines, dig at sticky swamp.
Go to mutant falls and talk to atlanteid guard, repair you shovel again.
Fight the scorpscuba at frish port to receive the scorpscuba caller.
Dig at fountain of youth, repair you shovel again at coral mines, after that dig at the basalt slopes.

Go to the underwater dome, fight earth elemental, go to mutant falls and talk to master hydargol to get the waterlily pads.

Move from there past the falls to the edge of the forest.

I'm afraid it will take a lot of time for you to get there.
