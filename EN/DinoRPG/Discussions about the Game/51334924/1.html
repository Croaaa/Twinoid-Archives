Gather your Dinos for the latest clan championship!

All the dino masters are ready, the hostilities will begin without delay. Tenacious fighters will battle it out in epic confrontations... May the best Master win!

This edition is a UNIQUE occurrence in Dinoland, more dense, more stressful, only the most organised clans will be able to master this new form of the challenge.

Exclusive

Just to drive home the unique nature of the event, a unique reward awaits the victors:
Check out the prizes...

The Championship will begin January 2nd at 18:00 (server time).
Clan leaders will be able to choose between three leagues from right now. Here's how it will be organised:

  • Location: Dinoland + Grand Tout Chaud
  • Battle length: 8hrs
  • Rest time: 72hrs
  • Max action points: 10
  • Action point regeneration speed: 10 mins
  • Score update : 20 mins
  • Action points at the beginning of the grid: 3
Elite League:

The elite league of DinoRPG. Only the best trained and most courageous clans should enter here.
From level 50 to 80.
Enrolment cost: 50 000
Power on the battlefield: 2300

Pro League:

This league is for clans that have already proved their worth in the War and have an understanding of strategy.
From level 30 to 50.
Enrollment cost: 25 000
Power on the battlefield: 1800

Starter League

The league for first timers and those seeking to understand exactly how the championship works. ow level dinos accepted.
From level 10 to 30
Enrollment cost: 10 000
Power on the battlefield: 1300

The pro and elite leagues could change based on the number of enrolments.
