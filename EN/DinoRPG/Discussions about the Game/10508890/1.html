Hey there. I've been thinking.. would it be good if Madam Irma's potions also make your next turn come a little quicker (reduce turn counters).

Let's say for example, if you have to wait for 4 hours to gain a new turn and you use Madam Irma's potion, after you use your newly gained turn next one will be available in 3 or 3:30 hours (whichever sounds more reasonable). If you use the potion again, the next free turn will come even quicker (2hrs or 3 if it only drops by 30 minutes).

This might add a new strategy, where, rather than using newly bought potions instantly, you could gather up more of them and use them up so that your future turns come much faster on that day. Ah and of course you wouldn't be able to drop the timer under 1 hour by using these.

The only bad thing that comes to my mind is that this could make those potions overpowered. What do you think? Would it be any useful? Or would this indeed make those potions too overpowered this way?
