A couple ideas come to mind for the Winks, depending on what you want to aim for:

Offensive
- Self-Control (0 *)
- Acrobat (5 )
- Sumo, Acid Blood, Clone, Acup., KSB, Marsh, Life Guard, Without Mercy (25 )
- CP, Lightning, , Adrenaline, Hazard (11 )
- Elasticity (5 )
Doubles - Electrolysis, Overload (2 )

That's exactly 49 , counting the unlock for Overload, either in or .

+50
Protein (1 )
Force Control (5 )
Vitality (9 )
Massage (3 )
Twinoid (7 )

25 , which means you have 5 more to try and get FC, and maybe even Sylphs in . Twinoid is rather relevant, since the point is to attack often. You don't have much HP (340), but Force Control gives you an edge against other winks. Another option is adding Lightning Dance, which AFAIK works nicely with FC. Also, you could just get crazy and try getting Burning Fever, but that's... well, crazy.
___________________________________________

Defensive
- Self-Control (0 *)
- Growth (3 )
- Sumo, Acid Blood, Clone, Sapper, Acup., KSB, Marsh, Life Guard, (25 )
- CP, Lightning, Leafy Dawn, Hazard (12 )
- Vaporous Form, Vivacious Wind (6 )
Doubles - Bubble, Electrolysis (2 )

48 , which means you get 1 more for a filler skill, like Without Mercy or Vines (if it's very -heavy)

+50
Protein (1 )
Life Source (3 )
Vitality, Group Therapy (13 )
Massage (3 )
Twinoid (7 )

27 , probably more than enough to get everything. Sylphs is reachable, but not easy. If it gets a lot of in +50, I'd try Backup Battery.

The point with this build is to maximize HP and to take as many hits as possible while retaliating with AB, LS and Acupuncture. You have 390 HP (440 if you get Battery) and at least 43 points in . You are slow as hell, but that's kind of the point.
