The general ranking table is constantly updated.

The reason the number on your account and the number in the table is different is because your points are so low you are tied with hundreds of people for the same rank. The actual raw rank # is calculated differently to display on your account and to display on the ranking table. Therefore, there is a RANGE of ranks that you could be listed at - the actual number within that range doesn't really matter. For practical purposes, you can consider your number in that range "random" really.

On the ranking table, you're lower down at 2069. Again, this actual number doesn't really matter though because you're tied with hundreds of people, so it could vary quite a bit without your REAL position in the game changing.

This would happen to me from time to time as well. Like I said, a different metric is used to calculate your position within the TIED positions. One metric is used for the actual table. Another is used for the # that appears on your "My Account" page. I don't know exactly what factors go into this metric, but when you first start playing, those factors aren't really having any effect because basically, everyone's account early on is identical. Eventually, your total # of points may be the same, but something about your account is different from people with the same # of points, and this is when the two different metrics for calculating your "rank" come into play, and why you get two different numbers.

Does this really matter of affect game play? No.
