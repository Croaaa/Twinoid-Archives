It makes your Dinoz follow each other, as the button says.

It makes your two Dinoz function as one, as they will use up both of their turns with every move the group makes, but it's worth it as HP lost is more spread out in fights, so you can go longer without healing, and the gold you get as rewards from fights is multiplied.

If your Dinoz are around the same levels, you should do it. Otherwise, don't.

It makes your two Dinoz function as one
For moving and fighting. When a mission requires your dino to speak to someone at a location, you need to do that with every dino in the group doing that mission. Also, you need to use gathering skills for each dino. And some specific missions have to be completed with each dino as well, for example killing the elementals at the university to be able to learn to climb and swim. Your dinoz will not fight as a group then, keep that in mind and make sure each dino can survive on their own.
