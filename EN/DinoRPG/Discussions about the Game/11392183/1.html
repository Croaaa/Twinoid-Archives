bace135

Once you complete a hunting grid, you got 1000 coins. You can donate the fur tuft ingredient (and any other ingredients you might find from other gathering skills) to your clan if you have one. Other options are trading them to the shady man at the marketplace for treasury notes which you can use to buy stuff at the market. Additionally you can trade them for coins to the itinerant merchant who moves to a new location every week. There is a pinned thread that says his latest location.
