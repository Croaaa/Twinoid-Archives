I'd say there is no dino best for gethering, it depends on what you want to gather. Many people mentioned Hippoclamp for gathering, because you could get every elemental gathering skill. But he will get the higher levels of the gathering skills very late. If u take a dino with one high element, it could get that skill much faster, but it cas only gather one type of ingredients.
One other important thing about gathering are the skills searches expert and planner, because they allow you to search additional fields when gathering.
This brings us to the questin on how gathering actual works. On every place u can gather ingredients with one gathering skill. If u are at this place (does not matter if u are there before or after moving) u will be able to use the gather option one time per turn (i dont have any dino with two gathering skills, so i dont know if it would be possible to gather two diffrent things in one turn, one before movind and one after, but i dont think so).
U will then get to pick one square in a grid (with the skills even 2 or 3 squares), and if u are lucky u will find ingredients. Gathering does not cost you anything, but u will get some bonus money when you completely cleared a grid and of cause u can sell the ingredients yue find.
