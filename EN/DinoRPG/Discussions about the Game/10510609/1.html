The numbers represent the order you should take the skills, start with nr. 1, then take skill nr. 2 and so on. Although, it doesn't really matter if you mix up the order a bit, for example if you want to take Apprentice Fisher at earlier than the 14th lvl up, that won't ruin your lvl up, it'll just mean you get the other lvl ups one lvl later.

The dark blue color means that those skills are - Double Skills - .

My guess for the green color is that it is optional if you want to make your Dino a gatherer or not, if not, then skip the green skills.

The ones marked 4, 9, 15 and 23 means that you should use that lvl up to unlock new skills. If you decide to change the way you lvl up a bit, just take into account that skills that are in line with eachother, have to be taken in that order.

For example: You need to take Perception before you can take Tricky Hits, even if you unlock new skills. And you need to take Tricky Hits before you take Fatal Hits. Sorry if you already knew this, I just felt I should mention it since I was already writing the post
