I would start at the Islands. Start with Bao Bob's, gather the Swamp Mud, Pure Water and Basalt Shard whilst doing his missions.

When you have those 3, go the Underwater Dome (also get Scrobscubacaller whilst doing Bao's missions) and get the Zors Gloves.

Then go to Mutant Falls, talk to Master Hydragol, get the Waterlilly Pads.

Then start out with Nicolas Mulots Missions to get the Backpack. Also get the Lantern whilst doing Mulots Missions.

Make sure you get out of the Forest before lvl 22!
