To get to level 20 it could take 6 months to a year . It does depend on what you do. If you use coins wisely, buy Dinoz as soon as you can until you have 18, then spend the coins on Irma's potions, you can get extra moves for the Dinoz and so get more XP and rise up quicker.

The turn system is a different question, as the turn time increases by 1/2 hour every turn until it reaches 23 hours - then it is always 23 hours.

Of course you can spend real money to buy coins to spend on Irmas. You can also play it your way, but you will still take quite a while.

Be patient.
