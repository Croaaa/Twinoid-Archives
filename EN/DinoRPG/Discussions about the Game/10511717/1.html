On september 25, 2010
Kayin
To take less space...
If your avatar were like 90*90px, it would take too much space, and people would be scrolling more for your avatar spaces than your text lines.
If it was 30*30px, nobody would be able to do more than a smiley.

Well if you cannot create something fitting that space it's your choice.

The limit weight of your image have to be lesser than 1mo (or 8mb). So your image could be whatever length * width pixels you want without reaching that weight limit (if static). For an animated one it depends greatly on how many frames it possess.

All (soon-to-be-) uploaded images (banners, images in clanpages, avatars) have that weight limit.
