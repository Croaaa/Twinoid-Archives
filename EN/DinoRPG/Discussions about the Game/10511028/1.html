AndySQdancer
sendos, what makes you think that the probabilities do not overlap? Because I think that they do overlap. Highler level A/E skills overlap the lower level A/E skills. The methodology is to go from higher level skills to lower level skills to see if they activate, not to check everythiong at once. Suppose that you had a Level 1, 2, and 3 E skill. The process is as follows:

1) Check to see if Level 3 E skill activates, if it does--do the skill, if it does not then check the next lower skill. Suppose it did not activate, then

2) Check to see if Level 2 E skill activates, if it does--do the skill, if it does not then check the next lower skill. Suppose it does activate, then

3) Do Level 2 E skill--done.

In this example the Level 1 E skill never got checked and thus did not get a chance to activate and was overlaped by the Level 2 E skill.

Now suppose in the same example, that the Level 2 skill was turned off. Thus it would not get checked and in step 2) the Level 1 skill would be checked in step 2) and it may or may not activate depending on its die roll. So the Level 2 skill may or may not overlap the Level 1 skill.

When its your dino's turn in combat it checks 2 things.

Firstly it checks E skills in Decreasing Priority (highest skills first) till it uses one or it doesnt.
If It doesnt use an E skill it will then check to see if any Combat Gear is triggered. If it doesn't then nothing Happens.
