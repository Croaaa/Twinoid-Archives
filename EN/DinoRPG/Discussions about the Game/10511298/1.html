calorifere
What you've described is really not unusual in the islands.

It's generally recommended to avoid the islands like the plague when your dinoz are between levels 12 to 16ish (more depending on the elements of your dinoz, fire and wood dinoz will have less trouble, lightning and air dinoz may have trouble there until level 20).

Also, I wouldn't recommend going to the islands if you don't have the strategy guide. Certain skills, like lightning dance, should ALWAYS be turned off if you encounter Eeloz frequently (and ALL A-skills should be turned off once you start getting Borgs).
