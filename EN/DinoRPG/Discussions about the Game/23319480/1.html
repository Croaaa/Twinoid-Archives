God Banned: Its a regular E skill, works just like that. TM blocks it. Chronic Cramp: Regular E skill too. When it activates, the caster dinoz got a "down" state. No other visible effect. After about 10 "down" states the caster's endurance regeneration will be as low that it will skip 2 actions out of 3.
