There are (c)ollector skills in this game, like elemental fission.

You would need a lightning heavy dino and give him the following skills:

intelligence -> unlock -> lightning conductor -> unlock -> elemental fission


you can of course take other skills before unlocking, depending an what you want your dino to look like. Many players went and raised dinos only to make them collectors to increase their income.

With the skills lightning conductor and elemental fission you can gather ingredients at certain places, bruteforce for example. The first skill, lc, will only give you tier1 ingredients, the second skill will let you find higher tier stuff like air energy.


You can also sell any kind of Tier 1 ingredient to the shady man in exchange for treasury notes. With those you can place a bid on the market.

If you really want to spend just gold to get the air and don't have an energy collector then this is what you need to do since you only have 14 dinoz.

1. Buy a Rocky
2. Buy Irmas to level up the rocky only unlocking the skills needed to get elemental fission. Intelligence - Unlock - Lightning Conductor - Unlock - Elemental Fission

3. Once you have Elemental Fission go to Brute Force, and use irmas and keep selecting Energrize. (There are 3 air per grid of 36) Once you get your desired amount of air energies you are done.


If you don't want to rush in this way, still by a rocky and follow this same plan but just quit the current mission for now. If you want this done right away then follow the plan above buying irmas.
