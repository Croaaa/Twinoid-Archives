You have to talk to the alien every time. The star should appear after you complete each step (the death's children guide is very good, doing it myself right now, i am at star 6) He will spout some gibberish (this much he admits in his own odd way) and you will have unlocked the next "star challenge" Its all pretty straight forward actually, but i recommend doing the final star challenge with a dino that has a bare minimum of xp. You will have to give up xp to gain this star, just that this way you won't have to blow of a whole lvlup on it.
