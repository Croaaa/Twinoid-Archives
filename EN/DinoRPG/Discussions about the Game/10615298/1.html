The U does stand for Unique, but that's not because you can only choose one of those skills on each dinoz or because you can't have more than one dinoz with that skill. It's Unique because its effect works only the first time it's chosen. Multiple dinoz with the same U skill don't make the abilities stack. For example, you can have one or five dinoz with leader, but in both cases you can only have a maximum of 21 dinoz.
