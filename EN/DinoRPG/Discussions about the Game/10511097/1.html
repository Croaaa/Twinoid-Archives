just the fontain, so you will not lose a turn resting

some way to increase chances to meet a gluon :

1) group your dinoz with low level dinoz (lvl 4-6 ) you may encounter gluons
your low level dinoz may find it hard to kill giant chicken but it will give them good and giant chickens don't hit hard

2) group with high level dinoz (lvl 25-30) at this level giant chickens fight with other monsters (wolfs, gluons, green giants...) so more chances to meet gluons

3) fight some monsters in the katacombs, lot of gluons there (but not sure it count for the quest as you are not exactly in dinoland...)

one sure way to meet a gluon

make you dinoz folow an other dinoe doing the "briefcase" mission from Mrs X
make sur that both dinoz have theyr turn when the leader open the briefcase (end of the quest) then both dinoz will fight the gluon hidden in it and your second dinoz can then validate his quest
