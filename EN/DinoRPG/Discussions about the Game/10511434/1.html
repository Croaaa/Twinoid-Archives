On may 27, 2010
Ardaluc
The letters on the stairs are not the order you have to use them or anything, they are only there so you can see were you end up using them. For example if you take the stairs down marked with A on the first floor, you will be at the stairs up marked with A on the 2nd floor.
You only have to follow the red line, if you want to walk the shortest way (you might have to clickon the maps to enlarge them to really see that line).
I don't see any problem on the maps beeing french, because you can understand nearly everything because of the symbols. Most of the text you probably don't understand are only the names of the keys.

And I think your team should be fine, but if you think you're loosing to much you can always leave and come back later, the monsters you killed will stay dead.
