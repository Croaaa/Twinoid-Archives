Of course, if you want to play on the German server just go there and create an account. You can even use the same name as on this server, or a different name; but they are two absolutely separate accounts and you cannot transfer anything between servers. You can keep your friendships between servers if your friends know you have an account on the other servers. You also transfer your knowledge, as they are the same game, but not quite as far advanced in available play.

If you are not worried about the language difficulties, you can have one account on each server.
