After a lengthy pause, we are proud to officially announce the winner of Inter-Dojo Tournament XXVII: Bengeldorn! Bengeldorn has definitely proven his worth by taking down non other then Mengo9999 in the finals, who has become the resident final boss of the IDT!
He has also levelled the teams of LuxR, HugoMVP and SUMMR, while Mengo9999 kept up his legacy over the charred remains of Anskar, Venar and B1oniC!

Then, in the finals, Chen really made a rake-in as Elemental Potions ruled the plains, Ember, St. Elma and Uvavu striking repeatedly, denying, damaging and detrimenting the fighters. The battlefield was seen burning, howling with winds and enduring massive electrical storms, trembling under massive Softpigs, repeatedly striking, surrounded by a fiery inferno...

Woe to you, Mengo9999 as an early Fujin and a destructive Raijin descended and your feline succumbed, followed by the medusalike stone's untimely passing. There were casualties on both sides, comrades falling after one-another but finally, after a ferocious multi-attack from the chiropteran marauder Gizmo, only the aforementioned winged beast and the notoriously rage-driven Baby Longtooth remained, if somewhat scathed, standing.

Once again, congratulations to the winner of the XXVII International Dojo Tournament, Bengeldorn!
