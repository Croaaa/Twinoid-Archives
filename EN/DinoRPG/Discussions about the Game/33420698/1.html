You seriously only ask NOW, after year and half of playing?

When your dinoz would level, look at how many fields for which element it have. If it have many fields for that element, it will be selected often and you will probably want most or all skills. If it have only few of them, you will only get few skills anyway, so it is more important to choose which ones. High-level skills are usually more powerful, so they can easily be worth the "wasted" level.

And no, it's not easy to get 29-35 of one element, unless the element is wood and the dinoz is gorilloz.
