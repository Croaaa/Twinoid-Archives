I picked up the perception ability on my Sirain. Now that I have fought a creature that has the ability to become immaterial, though, I am confused as to what it actually does.

When I hit a creature that has used immaterial, it only damages it for 1hp. If I use a special ability (such as water cannon), it doesn't do any damage at all. What's the point of perception if I still can't damage creatures that are immaterial?

Is this working as intended?

[None]
Without perception you would not be able to hit the imaterial animal at all.

With perception it allows you to hit the animal doing 1hp of damge and takeing the animal out of the imaterail state. This means that the next attack (unless it has used imaterial again) will do damage as normal. "A" skills such as watercannon are not affected by perception.

It is working as intended.

Without perception the attack will deal NO damage, the dinoz can only attack after the enemy's intangible form is over, that usually take up a few turns. With perception, the dinoz will hit a 1 and cancel said effect from "immaterial" saving up for the waiting of several turns to succeed with the attacks.
