Advantages:
Less lost, thus less time used on resting.
Higher chance of winning
When you get a large payout, it'll be amplified, say, from 2700 to 6000.

Disadvantages:
less obtained
Less obtained. I grouped my sirain and glidwings and they only made 320, whereas I separated them and they each made 200.

My recommendation to grouping is to let your early level dinoz gain and when going to other areas like the volcano or islands, group them. If you group them too early, your dinoz would take about 7 turns to level up rather than 5 if you just didn't let them group up.
