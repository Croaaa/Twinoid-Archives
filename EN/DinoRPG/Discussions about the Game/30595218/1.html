Actually I think 32 is to early to equip spheres. Every levelup is totally random. In most cases you will be able to see if a Dino has the chance to become useful at level 25. For example if you already got 4 air on a sirain or a winks or 3 or more water on a Pteroz or Wanwan. Of course a Dino that rolled perfect till level 30 can become totally useless in the next 20 levels and the other way around.
I for myself decide if a dino is worth the try after he finished the elemental master missions. No chance to get one of the op skills and i sacrifice him at level 25.
That's just my opinion. Soon you will see posts by other players that tell you the one and only way you have to do it

I think there is a lot of 'it depends' here. The problem is if you follow the advice on this forum you will never ever use your spheres, because by the time you get to the point where you would use them, he goalposts move again.

If you are a paying player, or one who is definitely in it for the long haul and doesn't mind waiting (literally) years, then sure wait until level 50+ to use your spheres. But if you are a newer player, not in a top clan, and don't see level 50 anywhere on the horizon, then you may as well use them earlier than spend the whole game chasing rainbows.

As Lopperman succinctly put it, "use them when they're ready to fight", whenever you consider that to be.
