You can check the xp awarded for a mission in a lot of the top clan information pages.

At low level, when your Dinoz finishes a mission, but before validating it, check how many xp you will be short if you did take the mission validation xp. If it is within 20 or so, take another fight or 3 instead of validation. You might save a few xp per level.

Then again, you might not think it is worth it. It is less useful at higher levels.
