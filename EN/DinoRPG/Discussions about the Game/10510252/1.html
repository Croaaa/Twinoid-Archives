As has been said many times before...

A group of Dinoz can do the same missions together... EACH ONE must do the talking, collecting or whatever. If you must fight, they can fight as a group, but the GROUP must defeat the sum of monsters required by all members. If you time it right, the group will attract more monsters and save time.

e.g. for wolf hunting, you need to beat two wolves at each location. Low level, solo Dinoz will get 1 per turn. A pair of Dinoz might get two wolves, each Dinoz may take down a Wolf with the first hit, or between them on the second. It still takes two turns before you move on, but you should get less damage.
