Well, we all can't be 100% sure but I think the XP gain is determined by the following factors:
-Level difference between group members.
-Levels of group members.
(For above 2, a group of a level 5, 4 and 2 will get more exp then a group of a level 7, 6 and 4, so it seems..)
-Level of the leader, higher level possibly attracts more/better monsters.

And of course, XP gain will be less when one dinoz in your group gains a level. (F.e. a group would gain 60 xp and your first dino needs 2 xp for level up, you won't see 60xp in the fight screen but maybe 35..)
