The roles as I see them are: Attacker, Defender, Gatherer, Scout

Attacker: the aim is to deal as much damage as possible in a short amount of time. Strong assaults, multihit and A-Skills with a high damage come to mind.

good Attackers: Rocky, Wanwan, Softpig and many more

Defender: Stand your ground, no matter what. If you kill the attackers, that's nice, but it is not what you aim for in the first place. You want a durable Dino that will survive attacks and prevents the enemy from hitting your castle.

You'll want big , healing abilities so you can defend even longer and possibly skills that will slow down the attackers(that includes summons).

good Defenders: Sirain, Sumo-Rockys etc.

Gatherer: well, you want to gather plants and some other stuff. Get a dino with a high enough level in the respective element and place it where it is needed.

Scouts: Dinos, maybe lowlevel, that are scattered around Dinoland. Make sure that you don't spend their turn before your clan has made its attacks. Really usefull to have those, so you and your m8s won't spend Irmas to find the enemys castle.

My basic structure is attackers, defenders and gatherers. I usually have my lower level dinoz spread around to find enemy castles, and if there's no defense I'll attack with them as well.

I haven't really gone out of my way to make specific attacking or defending dinoz, but I usually have my cloudoz on defense simply for his flight ability; my rocky attacking for his super speed and skills like blazing twilight, lightning and lightning dance; and my kabuki because of sumo, mutation, elusive, dodge and soon to be vaporous form.
