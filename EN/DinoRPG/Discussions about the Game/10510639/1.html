Yeah, do grandpa's missions from fresh fish to a strange monster. Only do the stamps mission once.

Then move on to Madam X and do her first two missions.

Then, depending on your dinoz, choose where you want to go next. If you have a softpig or a gorilloz, send them to the islands and acquire a shovel at coral mines, then do Bao Bob's 2nd mission. Use the death's children homepage to guide your way through since the places are not offered to you, you have to find the locations instead.

Then dig at the required places (sticky swamp, Fountain of Youth and Basalt slopes (do that location later)), and return to Dinoland to do the giants mission. You should be at level 11 by the time you finished with Bao Bob's mission and defeated the scorpsuba.

That's my plan usually for , and dinoz. Good luck!
