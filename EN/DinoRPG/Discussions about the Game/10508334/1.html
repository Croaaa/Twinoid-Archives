A guide would be a good idea.

As for the questions you asked, try reading the following threads:
1 - General questions about the game
2 - Best starting Dinoz
3 - Explanation on attack skills
4 - Explanation on defense
5 - Grandpa Joe's Missions

And try these sites:
1 - Helpful information including skills/races/maps
2 - Quick reference for skills

Three general tips:
1) buy a 16,000 Dino
2) save the leftover gold to work on your second Dino
3) use your free potions/food to help complete missions

Don't:
- buy Irma potions
- buy food (use Rest instead)

You receive on average 200 per fight at the early levels. Buy the second Dinoz as soon as possible and you'll have a second steady income source. You may occasionally get lucky and receive 800-1000 for a single fight. These will help greatly in hitting 16,000 for the second Dinoz. Of the Grandpa Joe missions, Smashrooms in the Hills (500 ) and Wolf Hunting (2,000 ) will grant gold as rewards and can be completed by each new Dinoz.

Good luck.

I have a few things to add.

1, you cannot do the wolf quest until you have completed "The Hills' Smashrooms".
You have the option to do either "Fresh Fish" or "The Lost Puppy" mission to start. Then you may do the Smashrooms, THEN, you can do the "Wolf Hunting" quest.

Wolf Hunting pays 30 and 2,000

The only other missions I see on the guide that pay are:

The Hills' Smashrooms: 30 and 500
A Strange Monster (level 7 - 11): 30 and 500
The Giants (level 11): 100 and 5,000
The Month Exploit (level 18 ): 200 and 8,000

You can see a guide similar to what you are all talking about, as well as all the missions and explanations here.
