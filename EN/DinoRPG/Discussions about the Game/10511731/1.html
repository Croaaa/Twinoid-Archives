TheFitz
Ok lets look at the two abilities then seeing this has turned into a discussion whether celerity or intelligence is better.

Celerity increases your speed by 15%. This means that you can kill monsters faster which means that you lose less hit points. Losing less hit points means you need to heal less. Needing to heal less means you have to rest or buy less food. Having to rest or buy less food means you can level quicker.

Intelligence gives you a 5% boost. By itself it won't give any extra exp until at least 15 exp. Then is may give 1 pt. With proffesor which you need to earn on a dino before it is helpful means 10 exp. Thus you need to earn a certain amount of exp for this ability to have any effect. But because this dino is slower there is a greater chance of taking hp loss thus increasing the chance of dieing thus increasing the chance of not getting exp.

Celerity helps in every battle to decrease the cost of gaining levels by killing monsters quickly while intelligence only helps if you gain exp in a fight.
