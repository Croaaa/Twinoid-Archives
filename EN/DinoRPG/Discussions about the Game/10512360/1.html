Hello,

There are new tags for DinoRPG forums! If any clan leaders wish to start a thread solely for other leaders to comment on, please include this in the heading using either [CLO] or [CL ONLY].
Moderators will delete any posts not made by the clan leader (or clan representative) in these tagged topics.
These tags will only be used in threads that pertain to clan issues.

It will not, for example, be used on a thread talking about if bubble should be changed (since this issue applies to people not in clans, and clans shouldn't have to come to a consensus on such issues).

If your clan is unable to come to an agreement on an issue (i.e.: war start date), then you may post so ("some of us believe it should start on the 8th while the rest of us agree that it should be the 18th").

Please also post the name of the clan you represent in your post.

To get your representative added to the list, have your clan leader mail a moderator. Otherwise, you will have your post deleted. One representative per clan. Clans will only be counted if they have 10 (or more) members. Clans who make up this 10 member minimum with obvious multiple accounts will not be included. In the event a representative is unable to continue their duties, then the representative can be changed (mail a moderator). However, keep in mind that if the old representative has already spoken on a topic, the new one will not get to add a second opinion.
