I would like to point something out. For example, you have a group of 2 dinoz doing a mission where you gotta kill 3 monsters. Let's say the first turn you get 2 monsters to fight then the first dinoz will get 2/3 count of monsters killed, and the second dinoz's gonna stay at 0/3. THE POINT IS, if you get another 2 monsters the next turn, your first dinoz will then get 3/3, thus completing the mission, BUT your second dinoz's gonna stay at 0/3 (and not 1/3). This is pretty unfair, but that's how it works.
