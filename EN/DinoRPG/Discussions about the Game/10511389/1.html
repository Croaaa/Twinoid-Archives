You buy a demon dino from the demon shop using sacrficial demon notes. They're are only two differences between them and normal dinos.

1. They look a lot cooler,
2. They have a unique skill, such as the Demon Wanwan has "Accelerate", gives the whole group the status accelerated, (the one from vivicacious wind) (+50% speed - I think)

As for reincarnation, it is a skill on the lightning path comes after career plan. If you have this skill and die after you are level 40 or over, there are 3 options. Use angel potion or go back to dinotown (resurect) but the third now exists, reincarnate. Which takes your dino back to level 1 so it can do everything all over again. It starts off with 5 extra elements randomly assigned.
