It is said that when one gazes long enough into the abyss, the abyss gazes back into the reckless one. Well, B1oniC was definitely not afraid of doing so as he dared to repeatedly call upon the powers of the deepest sea in his trials and triumphed above everyone else doing so! Thus we proudly announce the winner of IDT XXIX: B1oniC! He put his trust into Proffie, a demon emerging from Challenger Deep, Eurynome, the daughter of the Ocean and the solid power of the sacred wind, Kamikaze! They fought through the immensely powerful ranks of their enemies, levelling RafaelSS, Shakazul and Bowdy, culminating their struggle in a fight against Gaudlock! The opponent's team, consisting of one of his dreaded orange Sirains, Branna, Grandullon, the healer and Robert, the sturdy one, reached the final's battlegrounds by laying waste to Bengeldorn, MTP85 and Sjefke, all titans of our time! The finalists fought, unrestrained by any shackles or items and what a fight it was...

So they fought for three days and nights, but oh, those not well-protected from the raging attacks began to wither and so the first to fall was the feline companion of Gaudlock. Following not much later was Kamikaze, but before his clock hit midnight, he gathered all his remaining strength and managed to land some good whacks on his opponents, bereaving them of their chance to prevail. The single immensely durable tangerine dinoz knew he had to endure but the children of the deepest depths were not to be trifled with. Without further hesitation they inflicted mortal wounds on the single remaining foe. Thus standing victorious, they proved their master's undebatable wisdom and made him proud! Once again, congratulations to the winner of IDT XXIX: B1oniC!
