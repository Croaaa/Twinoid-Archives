I was thinking the same thing. I've seen others say the 2nd dinoz is the slowest and then the game starts speeding up but that probably doesn't happen till the 4th. When you start the game you have 4000 (if you buy a cheap dinoz, unlike me the idiot who spent 20k on a softpig). Then you've got 2 turns right away and 10 Irmas. After you've used those you're probably about 7000. So you need 9000 more. You've also got shorter turns, so you can play maybe 4 times a day on average which means it'll take you 9 days to get to 16k

Then you have 0 but 2 dinoz. At this point you get maybe 2 turns a day if you're lucky but you have 2 dinoz so that is like 4 turns. So at 4 turns a day it would take you around 16 days to get a new dinoz.

Then you have 0 but 3 dinoz. By now you're just getting 1 turn a day. Even if your timer is 18 or 20 hours you still have to sleep and stuff so you can't play exactly every 20 hours. So you get 3 turns a day, which is actually less than when you had 2 dinoz. But I hope by then you can fight some bigger things that get more gold (I don't know, I'm not that far and just guessing) so with better fights maybe it still takes just 16 days instead of the 21 it would take if you were still getting 250 per fight/move.


So it'll take a while but it's something to look forward to.
