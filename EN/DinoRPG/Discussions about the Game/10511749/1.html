The 47 hour trick (or 1.99 trick) is where you rest in, say, turn A. You keep resting into turn B and you stop resting before you reach turn C. If you bypass turn C, you will miss a turn for that dino resting. This will give you 2 quick turns for your dinoz.

I never knew this trick had a name, but I'll try to explain it:

You want a dino (Ted) to rest. So on turn A, you let him rest. He recovers even as you take turn B. On this turn you let him keep resting, but he still has an action he could take if he stopped. Just before turn C, you stop him from resting and use the action you got from turn B. Then you take turn C and he has another action, plus he got to rest a lot. Yay!
