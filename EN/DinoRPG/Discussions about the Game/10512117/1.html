Below the picture of your Dojo there are 6 buttons. The second one says "Test your Dinoz. Use this to have FREE fights (no coins, no inventory use, no loss of HP/XP). It is a good way of checking if that super-powerful Dinoz of yours is really as good as you think.

For the tournament fights, use the first button marked "View Challenges". You will need to choose 10 Dinoz (you can choose less, but don't) to fight the challenges. You don't need to choose your top level Dinoz, or level 50 ones, as the challenges are based on the level of Dinoz in your team. You get to see 5 challenges at a time. Choose a challenge and choose one of your Dinoz that you think will win both parts of the challenge. When you have finished those 5, or when the server reaches 0:00, you get another 5.

Your points from those challenges affect your ranking, and the top 256 go forward to the final round. The final round is not seeded, so you may meet a level 50 Dinoz there (if there were one about). Check the prizes and see how likely you are to get a prize, then how much it might cost.

Either use the "Test" option (free) or go for a prize or just do the challenges for fun. Its up to you.
