As luiiard say if you dont see the background clear your cache

On Internet Explorer :
Select "Tools" then "Internet options"
Select "Delete Files..."
Apply, close Internet Explorer, and refresh the page

On Firefox :
From the menu "Tools" choose "Options"
Click the Privacy icon
Select "Cache" and then click on "Clear Private Data Now".
Re-load the site by pressing F5
