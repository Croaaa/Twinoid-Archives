Prices remain fixed. The more you can generate, the more dinoz you will get in a smaller amount of time.

Buy the cheaper dinoz first. Softpigs are expensive because of their charge (first attack is increased by 20%), wanwans are expensive because of their element choices (a bit ambiguous but when you buy one, over time they will be powerful) and winks are expensive because of their additional armor (reduces all damage received by 1).

Each dinoz you have should generate about 200 each. In some cases you might receive less than 200 or you might receive up to 2500+ in just one fight. Don't rely on those bonuses though. They have about a 1-2% chance of occuring.

When you get more dinoz, begin grouping them. You shouldn't group dinoz with a wide level difference (e.g. a level 9 dinoz with a level 2 dinoz for imbalance reasons).
