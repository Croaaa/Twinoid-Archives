YoruOokami
Another limitation with the xp boosts is that the experience is calculated one enemy at a time, not with the whole group of enemies. That is, if you are fighting five small enemies, say flamelets, and you get 2 xp per flamelet, you don't get 2 * 5 = 10 * .15 = 11.5 = 11 rounded down, you get 2 * .15 = 2.3 = 2(rounded) * 5 = 10. That all being based on the encyclopedia's bonus, as an example.

So when you're getting small xp amounts from the individual mobs you are fighting, the xp bonus is rounded out completely. A sad reality, but good to know when planning these skills out.
