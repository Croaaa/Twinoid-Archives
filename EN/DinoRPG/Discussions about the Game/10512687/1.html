The skill Apprentice Fisherman is a gathering skill.With it,you can catch small fish in certain locations,such as Frish Port,Coral mines,etc.
Those fish you catch(Lujidanee Grouper),can be sold on tamer market,kept,sold at shady man or itinerant merchant or donated to the clan.
Once you get more advanced fisherman skills,you can catch even more worthy ingredients!
