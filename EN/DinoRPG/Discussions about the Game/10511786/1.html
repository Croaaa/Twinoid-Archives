Things to get:
1) Buoy and Climbing Gear at the University
- Talk to Professor Eugene

2) Shovel from the Coral Miner at the Coral Mines
- After each use you need to repair your shovel by the Coral Miner again

3) Dig up 3 items
- Swamp Mud at Sticky Swamp
- Pure Water at Fountain of Youth
- Basalt Shard at the Basalt Slopes

4) Get the Scorpscuba Caller
- Talk to Atlantean Guard at the Mutant Falls
- Meet Joveboze at Frish Port
- Fight a Scorpscuba Fish

5) Get the Zors Gloves
- Go to the Underwater Dome and show the 3 dug up items to the Archsage.
- Fight the Earth Elemental

6) Get the Waterlily Pads
- Show the Zors Gloves to Master Hydragol at Mutant Falls

7) Get the Lantern
- Go to the Haunted Hills and talk to the Strange Man
- You will fight a Korgon

With the Lantern you are able to enter the Deep Gorges. Since you can't cross the Jumin River yet, you need to walk all the way back to Mount Big All-Hot.
