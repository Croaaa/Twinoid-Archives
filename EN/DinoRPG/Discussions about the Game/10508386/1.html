They all work on the same timer, but you get a turn for each of them when that timer counts down.

It will make your turns slow down faster, though.

You move Dinoz #1, you need to wait 12 hours to move it again.
If you also move Dinoz #2, the timer will change to 12.5 hours before they can move again.

That stops when the wait gets to 23 hours, though. It never takes longer than that.
