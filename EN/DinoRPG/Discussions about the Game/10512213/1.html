shadow, there are only 2 ways to get a demon; 1) buy 1 off of the market, or 2) sacrifice a bunch of dinoz at the demon shops to get demon notes/tickets. and the only way to be able to sacrifice is to go through the katakombs at the cemetery and get the Demon Scroll. those are the only 2 ways of doing it.

reincarnation is just that, Reincarnation. your dinoz learns the skill, reaches level 40, dies, then and only then will you see the option to reincarnate. it does not make it a demon, it only makes it a reincarnated dinoz. you start over at level 1 with 5 - single, random, +1 rolls added to your elements strengths. in effect making your level 1 a level 6 dinoz...in strength only. you lose all skills and have to start the dinoz over.
