Piemaster's right. As soon as most of your dinoz explore other areas of Dinoworld (imo Dinoland refers to the first area consisting of dinotown and university, etc). As Piemaster said, you'd most likely be doing missions in the forest or at the volcano doing shaman's missions to do the elemental master's missions.

Another good recommendation already mentioned is to join a clan. It's pretty much the second half of this game, because once you have completed all the missions (which I doubt that you'd do for a long time, same with me) the only thing you could do would be to join a clan, or at least have a go at taking control of a devourer.

Mark our words, this game will get interesting after the boring phase of doing just grandpa Joe missions, as long as you explore other areas and adapt to the enemies in those areas. The pvp part will be the highlight of this game.

I've been playing for 1.5 years now and my advice to those who feel a sense of boredom in the game is to make sure that you keep saving up for new dinoz or a bulk of irmas (preferably after you get merchant U skill).
