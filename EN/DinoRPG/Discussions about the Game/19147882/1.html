Kayin

War hasn't begun yet, it will be reset wednesday.

Taxman will announce at midnight when he will come by from X:XX to Y:YY, something 2h30 in-between. That window will be from 0:00 to 24:00 in a day. He comes by hourly (meaning 1:00, 2:00, 3:00 etc) and stay only if your castle is entirely built. He's staying for 30 minutes.

If you pay him, he will ask specific ingredients, how much depends on your mana pool (big mana pool, lots of ingredients, small mana pool, only one or several cheap ingredients). Then he will grant you 10 honour points.

If you choose to not pay him or miss him, he will come back at midnight and take X% from your own mana pool and no honour is granted. X is the number of successive days you didn't pay him.
