It has been a very long time since the last Art Contest have seen the day on DinoRPG EN server, so here's one prepared especially for you, and sponsored by the AnimAction Team.

You need to depict a two-sides winter postcard, one face will be a drawing on DinoRPG theme in Winter and other face will be the text with the stamp. Any kind of support is accepted: digital, paper, 3d, paint etc.
Winter themes could be Christmas, Snow sports etc.
Senders and receivers could be anyone (DinoRPG NPCs, your clanmates etc). Be creative .
Your picture(s) can't be wider than 640px. You got until the 15th of March to post your creation(s) here on this topic. After that point, your creations will be judged by me and four other people. Those who want to be a judge please contact me. For convenient purposes, judges won't be from same clan. They won't be able to participate but can still post for the fun. The first three ranked will receive Creative Medal given by the AnimAction Team.
