It would be nice if there was a toggle so that your Dinoz would automatically consume an Irma's potion (if you have it in your inventory) as soon as they "needed" one.

Ease up on server usage/db refreshes by lowering # of clicks.
Negatives:
Available item find actions might go to waste after a player moves their Dinoz. This could be mitigated by making it automatic ONLY if there are no other auxiliary commands available (Harvest, Fish, Energize, etc.).
What are your thoughts guys?
