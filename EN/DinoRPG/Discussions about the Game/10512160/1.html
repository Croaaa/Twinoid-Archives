As mentioned before, it's probably a good idea to get Blazing Twilight regardless, because it's an awesome skill.

As for which has priority, my best guess would be to look at the list of skill in the "Details" tab of your dinoz. In my experience, the lower the skill in the list (within a given element), the higher its priority. For example, on a water dinoz, Aqueous Clone is below Marsh in the list, so I would expect Aqueous Clone to have a higher priority than Marsh.

There's nothing to worry about here. Skills on different tiers should not affect their chances of triggering. The attacks of a dino work as follows:

1) (any) Tier 4 A skill
2) If (any) Tier 4 A skill does not activate, Tier 3 A skill
3) If (any) Tier 3 A skill does not activate, Tier 2 A skill
...
4) Assault occurs if Tier 1 A skill doesn't trigger

P.S. Whatever you do, don't take such a dinoz with those skills activated to Monster Island or you'll get owned big time by the vegetox guards.
