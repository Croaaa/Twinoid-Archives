Loxagon

I don't think you need to register like in a Championship. Usually during war time an additional tab will appear for your clan where you can enter the war by building your castle for the first time (only clan leader can do that).
For building the castle (and in fact a lot of later actions like paying tax, declaring wars or repairing your castle) you will need ingredients from your clan treasury. To get this treasury build up you need your members to donate the ingredients and to be able to do that you need dinos that have collect skills.

That is the technical side of things.

Looking at your account with 6 dinos only and you being the only member of your clan I would say it is far to early to think about doing the PvP stuff. First you should try to build up your account by using the gold to buy more dinos and concentrate more on the PvM aspect of the game. Having more dinos will generate you more income per day and when having 18 it might be time to maybe join a clan doing mana wars to learn about how it works (and how expensive it is). Doing it with you own clan is still a long way away as you would only end up as a punching bag for the others in the current state.
