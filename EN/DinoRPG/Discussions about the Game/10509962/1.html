I would say a power house like a Softpig or Sirain both of which are good offensive dinos.

Sirains are good because they can get water cannon or such ranged attaks that can hit flying and such and it'll not be waste because of the high they get. My Winks/Sirain team owns flying theings and most stuff..Eeloz are not a problem really as my Winks hits them with vine and Water Cannon and my Sirain hits them with Water Cannon and Gel.

Softpigs and more straight forward. Pump them up in hard hitting stuff and the 20% horned charge first attack is great and powerful. esp paired with the other charge abilities.

Can't go wrong with either i think.

But it really is a personal taste sort of thing. Pick what you like best and work it up a few lvls and group it with others. They can be a few lvl's apart. Just not like 5+.
