Dinoz masters!

IDT XXIV was every bit as hotly contested as we expected, as soon as the draw was made for the finals, we knew some epic clashes were to follow! In the end, there can only be one winner, and he is no stranger to the IDT, or to victory. It is with the utmost respect and sincere congratulations that we hand you over to your IDT Champion, once more, Druid2k!

"Well, thank you for all the support, many players, whom I respect sent me PMs like, 'yeah, you can do it, go for it!' They belived in me more than I did, and I had enough luck too!

I have to highlight a few things:

1) It seems that IDT is cursed: will there never be a chance to face my clanmate Mohnkahn?
2) In the final I beat Pyxel!! It was a real payback, last dojo he beat me at the same position (fight for the final). I put myself about 40-60 in that battle. Thank you for the cheers - again! That was the hardest fight for me: only 2 of my dinoz survived, and with a sum of about 30 life..
3) I was afraid as hell of those bubble-self control sirains! Well, I hope they were at least as afraid of me as I was! Both Pyxel and in the final Gaudlock had 2 of those. Brrr. 40+ water, full of spheres: they are killer bunnies!

Tactics: well, you need Temporal Manipulator, Beer, Bubble, Fujin with big damage output, passive damage, spheres, and energy. Simple that. And lots of luck - because of almost everybody will have the same. And, well, use the strategy Guide! I've never ever turned off that many skills as in these final 2 battles!

It was a great tournament, I fought great battles, interesting and hard ones! And players become stronger and stronger. You never know who will beat you next time. Thank you all for the fun, and I have to say I cant wait to participate in the all different 5 vs 5 tournament next time! That will be awesome!!! "

Although we can't promise that the next IDT will be 5 vs 5, we will be announcing this over the next day or two. Stay tuned to find out what's coming next in the Inter-Dojo Tournament!

Congratulations once more go to Druid2K on another impressive win, and we wish you all the best for the coming event!
