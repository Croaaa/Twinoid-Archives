Your Dino will can swim or climb whe he kill the water and fire elementals in the university.
Then you will have a status, with this status your Dino will have the skill to swim or climb.

To collect ingredients your Dino has to get an extra level, then will appear the level list and yu can choose one. Read carefully the skill description or may be you habe to unluck another ones when your dino get that extra level.
