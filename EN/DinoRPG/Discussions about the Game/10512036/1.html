epoq
I have posted this elsewhere, but just so that everyone is up to speed: Permanent spells will last for the duration of CW5. However, if your castle is destroyed, you lose everything (upgrades, spells, paint-jobs etc.)

This is taken from the support site:

When your castle is destroyed, the following things will happen by default:

* All constructions / upgrades will be lost
* All spells will be lost (Including permanent spells)
* The castle's hit points will be reset (as any upgrades / reinforcing constructions will be lost)
* 50% of morale points will be lost (of the current total when the castle is destroyed)

Only mana which has been collected will be retained!
