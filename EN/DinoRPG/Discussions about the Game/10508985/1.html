Hi. Sometimes I have a situation where I know what I want to do next but I know I won't be able to play the game when the next turn arrives. I think a nice feature to alleviate this would be allowing people to queue up a single move. That is to say when their next turn is ready it will automatically happen. For instance, if I just had a fight and I got hurt real bad, now I know I want to rest, I should be able to set rest as my next move while the counter ticks, and it will make me rest when the time expires.

Anyone else agree?
