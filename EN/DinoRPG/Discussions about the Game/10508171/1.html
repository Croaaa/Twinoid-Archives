To achieve this you need to access from the Fish Porte, to Islands Atlantides (first need to have unlocked the ability of lifesaver able to navigate)

Speak with the Master Hydargolin the Mutants Falls, this sends you back to Fish Port and talk to
Apprentice Padamonje, which gives us a water lily leaf.

After returning to the water lily which Hidargol Master and give the sheet, this gives us the pearl of youth.


Pearl enables all dinos to take the Fountain of Youth regain 5 points of life when the server is updated at 00:00 (English-hours)
