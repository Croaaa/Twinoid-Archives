Welcome to the DinoRPG English Server forums!

By registering your Twinoid account, you agreed on Motion Twin's Terms & Conditions (If you did not read them yet, you are invited to do so).

We reserve the right to evaluate each incident on a case by case basis. The action we take may be more lenient or more severe depending on your moderation history.

English is the only written language on these forums.
Abusive provocation could be sanctioned.
Not respecting Netiquette could be sanctioned.
Advertisements aren't allowed and any websites which needs registration are forbidden.
New missions and quests can only be discussed under spoiler tags one week after their update release. Full walkthroughs are never allowed.

This policy is not language-restrictive. Language that falls under this policy will always be subject to the repercussions listed, whether it is inappropriate in English or any other language.

If you need to contact Motion-Twin or us, here's a list:
Support (payment & administrative problems).

For Moderators (forum problems):

In these forums you will encounter many other players who share different experiences and come from different backgrounds. While certain language and images may not be offensive to you, consider the fact that that the same language and images may have a completely different effect on someone else.

As a reminder, your access to these forums is a "privilege" and not a "right". Motion Twin reserves the right to suspend your access to these forums at any time for reasons that include, but are not necessarily limited to, failure to follow these rules.
