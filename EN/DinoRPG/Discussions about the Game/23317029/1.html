This brings me to some format feedback.


1- Forced different races does bring us more fun fights. Frustrating for some masters, but down the line a good decision.

2- Once again, it is obvious that fights are more fun without Beer and TM. More powers are used, more variety. Come on MT, wake up and fix those 2 metagame destroying items.

3- Life Source (and all passive) should be re-evaluated. Either increase attack damage, or put a cap on passive (suggestion: a passive source cannot exceed recieved damage)

4- Multi-hits... makes for uncanny and surprising moments for sure, but i feel like it really just increase the random luck factor. A lot of this game's strategies is based on statistics, and multi-hit just throws everything out the window.

My 2 cents, good luck for next dojo!
