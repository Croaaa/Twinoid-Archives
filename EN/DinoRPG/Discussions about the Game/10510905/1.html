mishoka333, attackers can be a group of 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5 Dinoz all from the same master and the Castle can be defended in a single attack by 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5 Dinoz who can be from up to 5 masters (15 different masters if you count the 10 waiting defenders too). Thus, any attack starts out as a max of 5 vs 5, but now with spheres who knows what it will end up as. There is a timer for how long any single attack last. If the timer runs out before the attacker group kills all the 5 defenders, the castle walls are saved and no damage is taken by the walls. After a master attacks, she/he has to wait 10 minutes before another attack can be made--by that attacking group. Of course if the master has a different group of dinoz there, this group can also attack and then will have to wait 10 minutes. If any or all of the 5 defenders dies in an attack, they are replaced by the next dinoz in the group of 15 defending dinoz, if there are any left. For the defenders, only the Master of the dinoz can heal, revive, etc. their own dinoz. Only the master of a dinoz can put there dinoz in the list of 15 dinoz to defend a castle (it's a special move once you dinoz arrives at the castle location just like attacking is a special move).

There are going to be a lot of dinoz die during the next war!
