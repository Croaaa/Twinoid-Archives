I will do it like this:

1. At lv5, either you're hunting the wolves or finished it go to the University and learn buoy.
2. Go get the shovel at Coral Mines.
3. Dig at the Swamp, then go to Mutant Falls to talk with the Atlantean Guard. Go repair at the Mines.
4. Dig at the fountain. Go back repairing.
5. Along the way up to Basalt Slopes, at the University learn the Climb Gear. Dig at the Slopes.
6. Go to Frish Port, talk with Joveboze to get the Scorpscuba Caller (after a fight).
7. Go to Underwater Dome, talk with the Archsage, get the Zors Glove (after a fight).
8. Go to Mutant Falls, talk with Master Hydargol, get the Waterlily Pads.
9. Go in Grumhel Forest, at the Haunted Hills when you talk with the NPC there, ignore him first, (after a fight) you get the Lantern.
10. Go way back to the Deep Gorges and talk with the Popotheleler.
