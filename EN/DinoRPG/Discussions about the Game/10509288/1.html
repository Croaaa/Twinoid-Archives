As I remember the attack value for a given element is:
[Element level]*5 + bonus (like the one given for savagery, or other skills).

For normal attacks (the 'tackle' ones) this value is used. But for the special attacks (like water cannon)I think is the (attack value) + (the number given in the description). So, in the case of water cannon, with a Water element of 3, and the Perception Skill would be:
3*5 + 4 (from Perception) + 6 (the bonus from Water Cannon) = 15+4+6 = 25

I think that the damage was the attack value minus the Element Defense of the target.
