It's very hard to do this sort of thing because it means that you have to be available almost every hour every day to do your turn at just exactly the right time. If you have a personal life and do anything else, invariably there will be some time during the 24 turns that you cannot take you turn at the correct time. And the bad part is that every hour you miss making your turn just puts you behind that many days in your schedule. So very hard to do for anyone. I try as much as possible since I am retired, single, can be up at almost any time. But there are days during the week that I have other things scheduled and therefore cannot take the turn at the correct time and have to wait several hours before I can take the turn. So it's nice in theory, but impossible in real life. But, I would not trade it for the way it is on the other servers--just too many good things about it otherwise.
