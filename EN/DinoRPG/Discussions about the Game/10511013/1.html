As Chison said, you just got unlucky, however, I strongly disagree about resetting the account. While your Sirian may not be 'optimal' in terms of level-ups it is still capable of making you money. By now you should be close to having enough money to get a second dino and once you do the game becomes a lot more involved as you will make money twice as fast.

Your Sirian on the other hand may recover and get better level-ups from now on or alternatively he might end up a lost cause and you will end up sacrificing or freezing him when you get to the max dino limit. Either way he is valuable to you now and it would be a mistake to start from scratch just because you didn't get the 'perfect start'.
