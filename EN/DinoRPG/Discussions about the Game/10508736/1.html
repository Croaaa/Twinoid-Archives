On may 6, 2009
POTAATO
You can only access the Forest from the Islands at first, and to do that you'll need Zors Glove and Waterlily Pads. In order to get Zors Glove, you'll need the Scorpscuba Caller. It's quite a run around in order to get all the needed items. Your Dinoz will probably level quite a bit, but you are getting the Fountain Pearl at the same time.


Assuming your dino already have the Buoy ...

Get the Shovel at Coral Mines

Might as well go to Mutant Falls and talk to the old Monk and the Guard there

On your way, you can dig at Sticky Swamp

You'll also need to dig at Basalt Slopes and at The Fountain of Youth
(... Remember: Repair that [un]Lucky Shovel at Coral Mines after every dig ...)

On your first time back at Frish Port, get the Waterlily Pads

Depending on your dino, you might be able to fight the Scorpscuba and get the Caller
(... level 10 should be enough. Plenty of chances as you go back and forth for the digs ...)

Back to Mutant Falls. You can talk with the old monk now and get the Pearl.

Go to Underwater Dome and have a chat with the old man there.

After you have the Glove, back to the Falls you go and get the Pads back from the monk

Now you'll have the Pearl, the Glove and the Pads. Your dino can travel to the south of the Forest, and your other dinoz can rest at the Fountain for the 5/day regeneration.

Since none of the above are part of the mission system, you may accept other missions and
