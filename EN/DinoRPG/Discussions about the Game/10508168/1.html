it seems like the amount of is randomly chosen from a range of numbers. for example, you might earn 50-5000 for a single dino. when you add dinos to the group, the range increases. two dinos might make the range 100-10000 and three dinos might make it 150-15000 . those numbers are just guesses of course, but it seems to match the possibilities fairly well. on average you will earn more per fight with more dinos in a group. the increase will not often be dramatic, but once or twice is enough to make you appreciate it.

as far as , my level 1 dinos lost around 6 when they fought alone. when fighting in a group of 3 the group lost an average of 2 per fight. that means one or two dinos were not hurt at all. those numbers are significant if you have no means of earning free (such as the Tik Bracelet or Fountain Pearl). you can fight more often without resting thus earning more and more .

groups also help your individual dinos get through the missions faster and without losing as much . make sure the group leader has accepted the mission if it involves fighting. if you choose missions wisely, the "following" dinos can take non-fighting missions like lost dog, rose bush, and recipe book and complete them while helping the leader with a hunting mission.
