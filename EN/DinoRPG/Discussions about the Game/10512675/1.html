The problem is with kabuki, that if you want it to be good, then 49 levelups, with no errors and with exactly the wanted number of elements is just enough..

preferred builds are:
warm blood( 3 )
hazard + career plan ( 7-8 )
vines or wild instinct+acrobat ( 3 or 5-6 )

you can see from this that it is based on LUCK that from 3 equal chance elements (fire/wood/lightning, all have 2 chance) you vant at least 5 (CP) from one, and about the same from the two others!!

but if you manage to have CP early, and not too much from the others, then you are lucky, and will have a good build ..

[and you used 11-17 levelups from the 49, so 32-38 remains]

next, you want bubble, sumo, petrification , lifebreath and black hole for sure. These are the must for the kabuki. without any of this your dinoz will be not good enough..
take 10 from water and 13 from air, so 9-15 levelups remain for anything from these:

kaar-sher ray , underwater karate, aqua clone, acupuncture, perception

elasticity, plunging attack, earth tremor, specialist, fetid breath..

as you can see, its quite easy to spend about 5-7 more water and 2-8 air levelups

example1: earth tremor+acrobat costs 6 more levelups
example2: vendetta costs 6 levelups too

so the key is: you have to get CP quick (with luck) and as few fire as you can

and everybody can dream of a perfect kabuki, with maxed speed and initiative, with earth tremor and kaar-sher ray, acid blood plus vendetta and specialist (which would be level 57)
