Well with lvl 12 you will be able to beat both water and fire elementals easy probably without losing much HP at all. Dont bother spending money on health, 50hp is enough. my wanwan did it and it cost 10hp for both of them at lvl 8.

Anyway my wanwan was lvl 12 and had:
Strength P

I left because of eeloz, but I could kill them reliably. Only problem was the cost 22-27hp per battle so I had to rest each day. Other than that eeloz are difficult but predictable to fight, and if you are ok with going slow then you can just rest every other day and you will be fine. If you go to the islands before lvl 12 you should have either perception or jump.
