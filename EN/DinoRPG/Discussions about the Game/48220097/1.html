At first I thought that the next set-up for the dojo wasn't so bad. After all, I have a high leveled Feross and two mid leveled Santaz, which is better than most players considering the scarceness of the five specified races. The problem is though, that you have to have at least four of these rare races, which is at best ridicculous. And at worst the most cynical way possible to try to force people into spending a lot of money on the game, because very few people have at least four of these races.

The set-up by itself isn't that horrible, it's ok that you can only chose between these rare races and that it's 5v5. But as long as it's four different races, more than half of the participants will not even be able to pick a team.

I have a few suggestions (not necessarily all of them work together) on how to make the set-up slightly better without changing it entirely so that people are actually able to participate:

1. In stead of demanding that people must use four different races, it could be a maximum of two dinoz within each of the specified races.
2. In stead of a minimum of four races, it could be a minimum of three races.
3. Add one or two more races. It would be ok if these races also were quite rare to maintain the spirit of this dojo set-up.
4. Make it possible to set a team consisting of fewer than five dinoz if you don't have at least four of the specified races.
