I think you are being too tough on yourself... you have two Dinoz. Just keep moving around, doing missions and exploring. Check the Clan pages for guides on the mission providers and items you can get and places and skills and....

Your first choice will be to decide to have patience, then to think what you want to get out of Dino RPG! The speed of play means that your plans develop slowly, but this means you have plenty of time to check all those guides and make plans. Above all, be realistic about what you can do.

Start by checking the skill guides to decide how to train each Dinoz; chose a fighter or gatherer or perhaps both. Use the XP gained each fight to judge how long each level will take. You need certain levels to gain access to different part of Dinoland. Consider how you might group Dinoz to make effective teams.

Think about what you can do in the next month - levels, new Dinoz, which missions. Don't worry about much after that time. As you get further, change the plan to match what has happened.

I repeat - have patience and don't worry (and I don't think I would reset).
