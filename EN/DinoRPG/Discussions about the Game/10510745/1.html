Zephides
There are 40 stages with different opponents. When you've finished a stage, you move on to the next one, and you can do that at any time. So when you did stage 1 at lvl 12, you can do stage 2 at lvl 23 for example. You can only go once through all 40 stages per Dino.

Click here to see the opponents!

Usually they are poorly lvld, but they still can be a challenge sometimes.

To enter the BFT, you need the Ashpouk Totem (dig old stone at Ashpouk Ruins -> show old stone to prof. Eugene). Show it to the Tournament Organiser and off you go
