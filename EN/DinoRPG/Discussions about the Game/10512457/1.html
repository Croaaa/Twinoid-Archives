Just to further clarify, this is a bug that occurs when a player with only one dino enters the Katatombs (or the Monster Island maze I assume). It is generally assumed that by the time players get around to doing that, they will have more than one dino, which can press the 'New Turn' button for your one exploring. But occasionally someone (like you) doesn't and they get stuck.

As Loamoats says, your options are to mail support or wait a week to get a free Irma. But in reality you really should look at saving up and buying another dino as soon as you can afford it. You will then get twice the number of turns per day and progress much faster.
