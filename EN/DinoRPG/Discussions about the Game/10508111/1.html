I'll help him out a bit...

For your first dino buy a 16k cost one this will save you 4k. Now you will start off with 1 turn and 10 irma potions essentially giving you 11 turns. With each turn fight you can get 160-250 with 200 being the more common amount won. If you win 160 per fight for your first 11 fights you will get 1760 on top of your 4000 you already have which is 5760 in total... If you win 200 per fight you will get 2200 which will total you off at 6200.

At most it will take you 64 days to get another 16k dino at which point your income will double. On average (200 per turn) you will take about 49 days to get another 16k dino.
