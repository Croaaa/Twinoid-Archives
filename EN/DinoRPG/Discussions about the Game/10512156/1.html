Fresh Players have advantage. The time between turns will be higher until it stops at 23h. On the other hand, there are skills which make dinoz regenerate more per hour - I have few capable of regenerating 6 per hour.

The 50% limit should be met at 50 for low-level dinoz and there are skills which raise the limit and therefore also the 50% rest limit. If it really stopped sooner it's a bug.

Using Angel Potions is wasting on new level dinoz. Select the other option - you will lose some but not so much because you have low level. Also, you need to get money for second dinoz as soon as possible. Try to not spend on anything else that dinoz until you have 18 of them.
