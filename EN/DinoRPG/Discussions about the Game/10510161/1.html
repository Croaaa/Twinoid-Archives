Look at the questions/answers forum section (above general discussion) for a description of how to get the pearl of youth. It is probably the most important Item I have gotten for my dinoz. Essential.

With your second dinoz it is up to you if you want to group or not. As others have saidd, grouping saves HP but you level more slowly. Also your lvl 10 dino will be fighting harder monsters, and they may be too difficult for your new lvl 1 dino.

If you get a dinoz that is all 1 element like gorilloz or rocky or sirain then it is more important to group them, because they dont fight well alone. However multi-element dinoz like wanwan, winks, or cloudoz will be ok for fighting alone.

Probably a softpig is also ok for fighting alone, because their first hit usually kills a single enemy. Only softpig is like that.
