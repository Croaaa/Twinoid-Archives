Trova
5 Lujidanee Groupers: You need skill Apprentice Fisherman. You can fish at Frish Port, Mutant Falls, Waikiki Island, and Jumin River.

5 Pelinae Leaves: You need skill Gathering. You can harvest at F.O.Y., University, Sticky Swamp, Sinister Path, Edge of the Forest, and Forges of the Big All Hot.

5 Fur Tufts: You need skill Smashroom Hunter. You can hunt at Precipitous Hills, Grandpa Joe's, Bao Bob's, Korgon Camp, and Wild Jungle.

2 Sharpened Flints: You need skill Search. You can excavate at Dinotown, Coral Mines, Haunted Hills, and Ashkapouk Ruins.

1 Air Energy: You need (lvl 3 skill) skill Elemental Fission. You can energize for air energy at Brutforce.

1 Water Energy: You need (lvl 3 skill) skill Elemental Fission. You can energize for water energy at Underwater Dome.
