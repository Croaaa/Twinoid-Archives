When talking about the evolving of the look of dinoz you have to differ between the look on your account page and the appearence of the dino in the fight.

On the account page, dinoz grow from level 1 to level 10, they become more mature in appearence and somtimes for example sofpigs grow horns.
Also you can see wounds and stuff, if your dino is beow 50%, and even more if he is below 10% (or 10, i dont really know as its the same in the beginning)

The appearence in the fight on the other hand is mostly based on the maximum your dino can have. The more he can have, the bigger he will be. Also i think you can notice big diffrences between level 1 and 10 here also, like the new horns for softpigs.
