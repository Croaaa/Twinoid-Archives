Since you don't have the Pearl yet I would suggest at least swim across to the island to get at least that, plus the (un)lucky shovel.

Madam X missions' payoff isn't that great, and watch out for the last two of her missions, the mandatory wait time can be longer than your current turn wait time.

Try the Shaman or the Skully missions next, you can get the Strategy Guide from the Shaman allowing you to make limited changes to your skills, and although it's quite hard to get to Skully, I find her missions more fun and better payoff than Madam X.
