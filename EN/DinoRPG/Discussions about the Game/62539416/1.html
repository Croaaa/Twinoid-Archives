1. Petitions and open letters are strictly forbidden on Twinoid forums.
Why? Motion-Twin is a "working cooperative", they are at the same time employees and shareholders, and don't have a hierarchy. They don't have to obey to someone because they have no one above themselves and no one under themselves.
So, because you lot will try something for nothing, the community will start some hate campaign etc... and still nothing for all your efforts.

2. The news bug have already been reported for all three servers, but the fix was non-existent. It seems it got repaired by itself on EN and FR, but not on ES.
Last thing which got fixed was Christmas event grid, back in december 2017. All other further bug reports have never been handled.

3. You can find on the web, several interviews, and in one of them, MT declared they won't continue on their mobile nor web adventure.
As of why? The digital market has more potential than Twinoid alone. That's what Buzzard told me face-to-face, back in october 2016.


Now, the solution is technically buying the intellectual rights, re-dev the game from its roots so you got something smooth without flash dependencies, develop one same UI for all languages, on only one server which will be crossplatform (available as a client, on a web browser, as a mobile app etc...).

It's a sacrifice, because you need to own a company, you could have just one super guy do everything from developping, adding graphic stuff, marketing, network admin, community manager, and moderator. It could be yourself too... but you need to win the lottery and be enough selfless to buy the game, maintain it and in the end, not play it.
