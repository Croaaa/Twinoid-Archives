Yo guys, I've let my nerdy side take over a bit and made a spreadsheet + chart of the popularity of races ever since the introduction of the dojo (january 2011)... Basicly it's a count of all the races in teams of finalists in every IDT, not sure who had that idea and kept a count of them, but I completed it up till the first dojo and also added some milestones like the introduction of certain (magic) items or new skills, which might be interesting to see what kinda effect it had on the popularity of certain races
Only a bit of a shame that there's not really a way to know the popularity of races before the introduction of the dojo, I do remember Kabuki was considered one of the strongest dino's back then and Winks was considered a "poor man's Kabuki"... Also since the introduction of Demons I remember Demon Wanwans instantly became popular because of their special skill. I just can't remember what the influence was of the introduction of poisonites, were Santaz popular before that or have they never been very popular (maybe someone can remember)?
Anyway, here's the graph and the statistics:

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/...

I could've done some small miscounts here and there, but overall it should give a pretty good idea of the progression of the metagame

Some (obvious) conclusions that can be made out of this:
- Since the introduction of lvl 60 and endurance Rocky's popularity has finally gone down after reigning for a long time... Surprisingly Beer has had hardly any influence on their popularity.
- Shortly after the introduction of invocations Cloudoz have finally become popular, after previously mostly being seen as a "good allround gatherer". Their popularity is currently on the way down again though, probably because of the rising popularity of Force Control.
- Kabuki's have always been quite popular but only recently they are on decline most probably because of the introduction of Boxer Shorts. Sirain have now become more popular than Kabuki.
- After previously reigning for a long time and then going downhill, Wanwans are finally increasing in popularity again.
- Winkses have always been a quite popular race.
- Since the introduction of lvl 60 Casti's and Quetzu's are becoming more popular. Also Mahamuti popularity is now slowly on the rise for the first time as well.
- Despite some very good results in recent IDT's Softpigs still haven't gotten any more popular than decently popular. This may be due to Brave, although Quetzu should suffer from that as well.

This thread can be used to both comment on these statistics as discussing the metagame itself Just keep it a civil, interesting discussion
