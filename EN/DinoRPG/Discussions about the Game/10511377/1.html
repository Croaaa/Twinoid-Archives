http://en.dinorpg.com/clan/41?page=1658 does this help at all? you should have already done this:
You need to have 10.000 coins to start the Kabuki's Quest. Go find the Punk in the Frish Port. He wants to make a bet with you. His appearence is random, changing every and each hour.
The first time, he will cheat and you will be forced to lose the bet and the 10.000 coins.
The second time you find him (once again, randomly), you bet 100.000 coins (you don't have to own 100.000 coins for the bet), and you win. But the Punk runs away and you have to chase him.
You can find him at the Forges. Once you ask for your money back, a fight with him will start. (should be lvl 20 for a sure win)
