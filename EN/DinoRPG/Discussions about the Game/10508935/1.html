To add on to what RangerJoe said, your dino lacks offensive ability. The skill path you have gone down is not an ideal one and you have in a sense wasted some skill levels.

You have had a total of 3 fire level ups and 3 wood level ups in essence. What you should have done, was take ALL of the tier 1 skills for those elements, your dino would have been much stronger with those skills which would have given you:

+1 to all of your assaults
chance to have your next attack increased by 25%
increased armor by 1
+5 to Wood assault

It will be difficult to rectify this as each skill level that is wasted you cannot get back. You are still low level, so in the long run of things, it shouldn't be too bad, but in the early game where you are, you might have some setbacks.
