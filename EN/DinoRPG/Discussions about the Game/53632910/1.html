Greetings Dino Masters!

For a while now you've been asking for a more complete calendar of events for DinoRPG. Today we're proud to present to you the very first Events Calendar for DinoRPG 2016 (and even a bit more than that)!

You can access the calendar through this link: http://en.dinorpg.com/calendar

It's a little plain for the moment, but it shows everything we've got planned for the next while.

Please Note:

  • The IDTs have been configured based on the French community group 'The Ampitheatre'.
  • An announcement warning you of the upcoming events will still appear 1 week before each event, with of course another to mark the official beginning of the event.

In other news there is also an new link in the menu at the top right of the page wich leads you to the new FAQ section. The new page should allow you to quickly find relevant information about the game. Don't hesitate to let the moderators know if there's some things that you think should be included on the page.

While you wait for more exciting news, happy planning!
