If you want better attack on groups of enemies, as well as the ability to attack flying enemies, then Burning Breath is definitely the one to get.

But if you want to get more coins, then Smashroom Hunter is the one to get. Whenever you're at a hunt-able location, you'll be able to use the hunt skill, without wasting a turn. The items you get from hunting you can convert them into Treasury Notes, which are needed for the Market Place, as well as one of Skully's missions. Or for even better pricing, you can sell them to the Itinerant Merchant.
