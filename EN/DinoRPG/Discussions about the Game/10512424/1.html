Cursed dinoz don't gain XP.

It can be cured with Devil Ointment that can be bought at Cursed Shop in Ashpouk Ruins or at the Tamer's Market. Only cursed dinoz can see Cursed Shop. I recommend to buy maximum Devil Ointments to avoid losing too much XP in the future.

- It gets a black teardrop status (curse)
- Cannot gain while cursed
- Have to go to the Cursed Shop at Ashpouk Ruins (only cursed dinoz can find it), and buy Devil's Ointment there to remove the cursed status
- Alternatively you can buy Devil's Ointment from other players at Tamer's Market..
