RavenMorgan, it's been said before, you need at least 2 Dinoz to go Exploring in Dungeons. Best Bet is to Buy an Irma Potion to get a New Turn (you do not have a Dinoz to Press New Turn if you are Sleeping in the Dungeon. Use the New Turn to exit the Dungeon by BackTracking where you have already been to get to the Steps at the Entrance so you can Exit. Another way would be to Die and Resurect in Dinoland.
