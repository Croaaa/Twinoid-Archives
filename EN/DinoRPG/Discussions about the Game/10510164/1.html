Frozen dinoz cant do anything untill they are unfrozen.

U skills are unique skills that count for all your Dinoz, but do not stack if you had it more then once. U skills from Frozen dinoz are still active, so many players make Dinoz for the sole propose of getting a U skill and then get frozen for the rest of their lives, because the U skills are wasted on fighting dinoz

Sacrificing a dino will give you Demon paper, they are needed to buy demon Dinoz, but those are very expensive. The Cemetery is next to fresh port, but only dinoz with below 10 can enter, if they did not finish some missions.
