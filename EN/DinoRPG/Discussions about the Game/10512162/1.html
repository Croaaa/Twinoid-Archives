Some of the large guilds have levelling guides for various dinos, but I would treat them with caution for a few reasons:

1. They tend to be designed with the view of a dino getting to level 50. 99.99(or something)% of dinos will probably never get to level 50.

2. MT are now talking about increasing the limit to 60 and likely adding more skills, so the build will be redundant by the time you get to complete it.

3. They assume you get the 'right' rolls and so if you get a strange distribution of elements you will find the guide doesn't work.

4. Some are old and designed without recent additions (such as Poisonites) in mind.


I would advise you, instead of learning a specific build, learn what the best skills are in each element (both for a dino with a primary in that element and for a splash). Then, when you buy a new dino, think for yourself about each element and what you would like to get in it. If you are building a Sirain you can take every good skill as you will roll so much . But you will probably only be able to get a couple of the good level 3-4 skills by level 40 or so. Other elements you won't get much of at all so really need to pick one level 3 (or even level 2) skill to aim for and hope you get there.

The Skills thread (sticky at the top of the forum) is deemed by the mods as the only place we're allowed to discuss this type of thing, so feel free to post in there for further help. Alternatively drop me or another experienced player a PM, most will be happy to help.
