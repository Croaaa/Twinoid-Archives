The point of buying a Dojo, even if you are not in a Clan, is that you can test your Dinoz. You can fight for free against your own Dinoz and check how well it has levelled. If you are in a Clan, these free fights can be against Dinoz from other members of your Clan.

These fights do not cost money, or a turn, nor do you lose health, use items or have to move to get there. If you like, you fight in your dreams

So, apart from the competition bit, a Dojo can help to weed out those Dinoz that you thought were good, but are not really, and change your plans to freeze or sacrifice it (it probably won't sell on the market. You have to decide if this is worth the initial cost.
