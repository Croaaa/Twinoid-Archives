ImScared

It is part of the game. Basically if the quest specifies that each dinoz needs to kill 2 smashrooms, that makes 4 smashrooms in total. It makes sense that you need 2 fights instead of one.

You can always ungroup, then wait for an action to be available on both dinoz, then regroup with the other as a leader, stay in the same location and fight again to complete the quest.

Hope this helped!
