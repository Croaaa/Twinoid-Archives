I suggest collect known info on subject and discuss if the system should be changed and how. The results are awaited by Epoq, please bear in mind it must be mostly setting adjustments cause devs has no time to implement something big or completely new.

For now:
1. Alliance can be created for duration: ??
2. If you are allied you lose 5 honour declaring the war on the same clan that is under attack of another allied clan.
3. Allied clan get 50% of mana won by another allied clan disregard the fact if the allied clan declared the war on the same clan or was up at all.

Problem: the stronger clans that are sure they will not fall out of the particular price range and can not get up higher stop care about mana and can pump insane amounts of mana in any allied clan killing the clan that couldn't be harmed by smaller clans at all. Even friendly killing gives not so much, cause it can't be repeated too often because of honour malus, but the pumping mana through alliance feature doesn't have any honour drawbacks for the clan that get mana for free.
It spoils the fun for some participators there fore it would be good to make some changes on system.

Possibilities:
1. Remove the alliance feature completely
2. Force the allied clan to be up to gain mana earned by their ally. Otherwise mana goes to the clan who did killing completely.
3. Reduce the mana gain by ally (to 10% 20% 30%?) if the ally didn't declare the war or simple make the dividing of mana due to damage done as it is for not allied clans. In this case allies are still useful for killing bigger clans but it will prevent the pumping of mana in weak clans, that can't damage the walls.
3. Even better would be to force allied clan to declare war on the clan under attack in order to get any mana, but remove the honour malus f.e. It will make possible of killing stronger clans by 2 smaller or attack of the clan of the same level with an ally. Maybe there will be some possibilities for abusing it, so feel free to improve this possibility.
