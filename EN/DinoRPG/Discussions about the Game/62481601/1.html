On january 16, 2019

Save your coins to buy new Dino's. Don't buy Irma potions you need more Dino's first. More Dino's = more gold earned per day.
Don't buy healing items, use your rest option when you need it.
Be patient and don't get discouraged it is slow going at first. We all started fresh one day.
