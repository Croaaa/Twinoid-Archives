When you have many Dinoz, you can make them move and fight together. For that, move two Dinoz to the same place and use the Follow action on one of them.
But you definitely do not want to group your level 8 dinoz with your level 1 dinoz. They will then fight high level enemies that can kill your level 1 dinoz very quickly. Most people suggest that grouped dinoz do not differ by more than 2 levels.
