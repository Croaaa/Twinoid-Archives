It is your choice... "In islands, the dinoz can fly, and be immaterial, do you have Jump or Perception ?" - check the Island Monsters.

In my view you should get the Pearl as soon as you feel able. Your Dinoz might take a lot of damage, so have to rest a lot. Resting is better when you have 2 Dinoz, because the non-resting Dinoz can start the turn timer (there are threads that explain this). Once you have the Pearl, the healing will let your Dinoz go on a lot longer before having to rest.

So, my advice, make sure you have done the Hills Smashrooms and Wolf Hunting missions for the coins, buy a second Dinoz, then get the Pearl.
