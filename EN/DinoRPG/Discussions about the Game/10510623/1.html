Piemaster
I don't think there is a 'best collector' per se. Some like the Hippoclamp because it can collect on all 5 elements given enough time. The downside is it takes the longest to get going, because you need 3 level-ups in the same element to start collecting.

The most sought after ingredients for high-level clans are the higher level ingredients, so you might want to go down that road if you are looking to join a warring clan. Unfortunately there are no easy-to-get dinos with high so you would have to make do with a Cloudoz, or somehow get your mits on a Kabuki or Santaz (when available).

Another school of thought would be to get a based dino as your first collector, i.e. a Rocky or Guildwings, because is the only element where you need the level 3 collecting skill for the Kabuki quest.

So in answer to your question, 'it depends'. Personally I have a Rocky and a Casti for collecting who hopefully will be able to collect between them eventually. I will grab a Sirian when I get the chance, to complete the set.
