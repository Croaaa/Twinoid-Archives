you can have a max of 50 lvls
this means a max of 49 lvl ups (you start at lvl 1)
The chances on a water are 1/20, or 5%, or 0.05 to 1
Each lvl up, the chances on getting an element are independent of previous lvl ups.


If the dino exactly levels as he's supposed to according to chances, you will have: 0.05 * 49 = 2,45 water lvl ups


So you are at lvl 13, and the dino already has had 3 water level ups. (He thus already had 12 lvl ups)
This means he still has 37 lvl ups to go...

In those 37 lvl ups, if he levels according to probability, he will have another: 0.05 * 37 = 1.85 lvl ups in water.

He already has 3 water lvl ups, and he will (according to probability) have another 1.85, which is a total of 4.85 water lvl ups.

If you have 100 dinos, 85 of those will get 5 lvl ups, 15 will get 4 lvl ups in water.

You should be able to get sumo, although there still is a (non negligible) chance of 15% that you will not get sumo.

The sooner you get another water lvl up, the greater the chances on getting sumo.

I mean, supposing that the probability is completely random and it should approximate 3/2.5 points of water at level 50, how could I calculate the chance of getting water again, taking in count that my Dinoz has already 3 points of water at level 13? The projected chance can't be 5% (for the law of big numbers?) but it can't be either 0%.

Probability cannot be random. The method of determining the element obtained is a weighted random roll and therefore you can calculate the probability using the simple probability tree.

If you only want to determine whether you will get water again, and don't care how many more water you may or may not get then it is pretty easy.

At your next level, the probability of getting water is simply 0.05.
The probability of getting water the level after that is 0.95 * 0.05 = 0.0475 (you didn't get water at first but then you did and you stop looking at that branch of the tree)
If you have not got more water then you go to the next level which has a probability of 0.95 * 0.95 * 0.05 = 0.045125 of you getting the water you need.


Continue working out the branches until all possibilities are exhausted. If you have career plan, then the chance at each level is doubled.
To determine the probability of getting water, you simply add up the probabilities across the branches (e.g. 0.05 + 0.0475 + 0.045125)

You will see that the possibility of getting one more water across many levels is actually a lot higher than 5%, especially if you have career plan.
