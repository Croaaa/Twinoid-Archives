Folks, this is *not* a game where you can win it in one day, this is a game where you *need* to have patience! You will not be able to win this game in a day, a week, a month, or even a year... This is a long term game where you will need to play for several years in order to fulfill all of the quests.

When you first start out, you will have to work to build up your stable of Dinoz, and wait for the next game day to train them. You won't get many game coins per day when you start, but when you buy more Dinoz, your income will increase.

Play for a few minutes, every day, and after a while, you will find that you are playing for longer and longer periods of time.

"Patience, Young Dinomaster!"
