- 7 dino with korgon fins (dian korgsley's 1st mission) are required
- they have to focus at the same time at Mr Bao Bob's house (to do this, talk to bao bob)
- they can be from the same clan, not only from the same master
- all who participated will get an "enter the portal" option
- the focus can be stopped anytime until the 7th joins
- if you pass the portal , you can only leave if you defeat the guardian or if you die and resurrect without angel poiton
- in the dark world you will find skeleton piranoz

- Dark World is a very small map: only 4 places (2 steps to reach the Dark Tower and one more to enter)
- After defeating the guardian, your whole team will be back to the Sticky Swamps
- They all will have a new status item "blacksylva key"
- With the key you can access Magnetic Steppes from Blacksylva Door (it is in the forest, at the NW corner) (like you can go to the island from frish port with the buoy..)
