For some time the debate has raged over who has the best-looking Dinoz in Dinoland... Well the time has come to put your favourite companion to the test!

This Monday, the 7th of May, will herald the start of the DinoRPG Beauty Contest. The qualifying phase will last for 10 days, after which the finals will start and will last for another 4 days. After 2 weeks, we will know for sure which Dinoz master has the hottest, coolest, or just plain awesome Dinoz around!

The contest takes place at Dinoplaza will run constantly from now on, and every fortnight a different race of Dinoz will be randomly selected. Don't worry - that Glidwings that looks like the Bat-symbol will have its day!

Good luck one and all!
