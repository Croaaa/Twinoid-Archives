- Small strategy book icon means that you should switch this skill off.
- Underlined font is used for very important skills.
- Normal font is used for important skills.
- Bold Italic is used for less important skills. Sometimes this skill could be taken before the previous Unlock, they can be used for balancing in order to get Double Skill. When you move to fast in one path and to slow in other path simple push that skills to the top and take them before previous Unlock. such skills oft should be taken only if you have enough rolls in this element.
- Italic is used for skills that should be taken only, if you hit a lot of rolls (more then average) in this element early enough.

AvaquenI've been interested in the Etherwasp and i'd like to hear some opinions from people who have actually trained one, or are training one for PvP. I made a build for level 70. Any opinions? I know it is kind of low on health, but I think earth tremor and bubble will help that a lot.

Etherwasp have rather low damage output ET is the only good aoe skill they have. It can be very good in dojo, casue of the fact that it can hold 2 magic iteams, but you need to have more then 3 item to get profit from it's racial skill. It can be an average castle defender as any bubble dinoz with BH, but good kabuki or cloudoz is actually better imho It can be also a part of attacking WM (tm+beer) team of 5-6 dinoz but there are also races that suits better for this . But if you decided to build it than let me make some minor remarks.

You do partly CP rush it is good, but I am not sure the lightning dance idea is ok. See you have power 60 (+20 with Strength of Zeus (we will fix it in tool soon so that it will be also calculated)). So 80 in best case at lvl 70, but check the dinoz of level 70 using tool almost all of them will have 80 or even more in lightning defence, so ldance will do only 1 point of damage on every hit and you will pay a high amount of energy for this and maybe even get some passive damage. As general rule, you should better plan A-skills in your build if they have at least strength 100 at level 50, unless those skills are needed for skills behind them.

I am also not sure if invocation is worth of taking (especially if it drains not percentage, but fixed amount of energy points), without it you could cut some wood before lvl 51 and get a little bit more water taking petrification, it is a very good skill with low energy costs and that doesn't depend on your water element. It also improves your bubble absorption rate Precocious spring is not good in pvp casue blocked by beer and TM. But after 50 you could switch to wood skills in hope to get Force control (5 rolls out of 20 with 20% probability and CP is not very easy but doable) if you will be lucky and hit more wood you can even get such great skill like Life source with only 2-3 additional rolls in wood. Force control will increase your damage output a lot and it is easier to get then Strength of Zeus, more over it will increase your ET damage output (the only good aoe on etherwasp) you can also drop specialist in that case unless you are going to make it pure dojo dinoz


Check points:
Wood: Carapace and vines can be dropped especially if it is about TM wasp.
Water: Petri must have, rest can be dropped
Lightning: if you forced in lightning you can go for Intellifgence if you forced very early you can go for hazard instead of intelligence but you'll get wasted unlock. And because probability for lighting only 20% I would recommend a direct and not partly CP rush (yes it will most probably will cost you hazard. But ini is not so important for you cause you will not have a lot of ini reducing skills that you need to trigger early.)
Air: BH should be dropped if it is pure dojo dinoz and specialist in that case must have (to resist cursed locker), you can drop flight and some water skills in that case.
For attacker/Defender take Bh and Specialist path can be dropped if oyu see you don'T get enough air. (but in that case Force Control becmoes even more important)

After 50:
Air: not clear cause high level skills are not known. Possible option for now go directly to Uvavu.
Water: If you will be forced in water go for Gamma Radiation
Lightning: if you will be forced in lightning go for Cardiac massage.
Wood: you need here all you can get with CP. Check point before taking unlock for force control you can take Muscular oxigenation instead in order not to waste unlock for Life source.
