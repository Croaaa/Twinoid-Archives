Clarification on 2:
Rest uses an action, but only when you start it.

The "New Turn" button give all your dinos an action, and starts its countdown to next "New Turn" when it is clicked, Not when the dinos take those actions.

So if you start rest with one dino, then later do a "New Turn" with another, you can stop rest on the resting one just before the NEXT time you click "New Turn" and get an action with them..
