Assuming no Uskills these are the health per cost ratios for healing items at the flying dinoland shop.

For 30 health you would need 3 cloud burgers at 2100 gold.

For 30 health you would need 1 meat pie at 2000 gold.

For 30 health you would need 30% of one warmbread at 1800 gold.

For 30 health you would need 1 and 1/2 battle ration at 1500 gold.

You can't of course use battle rations for anything other then a fight and you can't buy half of an item. So, it also depends on how much your healing.
