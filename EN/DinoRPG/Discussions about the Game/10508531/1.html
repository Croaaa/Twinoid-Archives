Technically you are better off with more hp when you take into account that to rest takes a turn to start.

So if you decide to heal at 10 hps and your 50% mark is 50 you have to wait a turn(up to 23 hours) plus 40 hours. Lets say you're lucky and are at 10 hours for your next turn. That makes 50 hours to heal 40 hp. If you do the math that makes 1.25 hours per hp or 0.8 hps per hour however you want to look at it.

Now say you have mutation so your 50% is at 65 hp. Again you're resting at 10 hps and have 10 hour wait for next turn. This means you have 55 hours to wait of actual rest time and 10 hours to wait for the turn to rest for a total of 65 hours. In this case you actually get 0.85 hps per hour or only 1.18 hours per hp.

That aside, with mutation you are at a higher hp level without having to spend coins on food items. This can be an advantage when fighting higher level monsters.

If you wait 4hrs for a turn, start resting, and stop 4hrs later, you'll still have 30min left to wait (the wait would have been 4:30).

If instead you stop 48hrs later, you can take a turn right away, and then wait 5hrs for the next turn. You only lose the turn you used to start resting. You get hp for every one of those hours, up to 50%.

Once you've got two dinoz, that changes a bit, because the second one can take turns while the other is resting. The resting dinoz will skip any turns it waits all the way through, but can get in on the last turn without an extra wait.
