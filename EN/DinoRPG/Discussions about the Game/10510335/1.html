Pulchria Cadeta is dedicated to a girl (Fallen beauty, actually Pulchritas, though that was too long). Oxys comes from Oxygen, due to its bubble and neutral white color. Chorrys follows his name, but with Corrosion due to its rusty color and Ch because it sounds nice.
Aquarius speaks for itself, due to its watery clor (ironicly he had mostly fire level ups though). Colucci is from a song I really like. Legion is based on the demon, and the actual purpose of leading a group, though not certain of that anymore. Silverback, based on Alpha male Gorilla. And Dohmin is twisted from Dominate.

I like good, mystic names. I always think it's bad to see someone naming his Dinoz with numbers.
