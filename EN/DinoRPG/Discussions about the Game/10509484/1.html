calorifere
About groups:
Every dinoz in the group can have a mission (same mission, or different mission, whatever you want), however, for those missions that require to kill monsters, only the leader is attributed kills. So if many of your dinoz in the group are doing a mission that requires to kill monsters, it may be a good idea to either break up the group for that mission, or at least change the leader of the group when the leader has completed all his kills.

And as said above, each dinoz has to complete all the steps of his mission. If many dinoz in the group are doing the same mission, if one of the dinoz completes a step of the mission, it is not automatically completed for the others.
