Well, I found a polynomial formula which gives the number of tickets for levels 8 through 50. But they might use a quite different formula.

int(0.0000037322 * lvl ** 5 + -0.00034422 * lvl ** 4 + 0.0173999 * lvl ** 3 + -0.32288 * lvl ** 2 + 4.832 * lvl + -15.52)
(lvl ** 5 = lvl raised to the 5-th power; etc.)

The above formula gives these values for levels 51-60:

658
711
768
830
896
968
1045
1127
1216
1311
