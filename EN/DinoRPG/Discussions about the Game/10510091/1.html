Casti is definitely a good dinoz. It is often overlooked as a group leader - of all things, because many think it's ugly - but contrary to gorilloz and wanwan, casti is has a very very good chance to have Sumo by level 30 making it the only good group leader of 5 that doesn't have to sacrifice speed to get high .
Glidwings is also pretty good. Most people tend to prefer the rocky as a dinoz because it will have a stronger Blazing Twilight, but the glidwings can get strong attack skills instead. If nothing else, you can use your glidwings to rush towards Priest and Merchant, and freeze it later.
