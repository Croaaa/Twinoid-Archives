Yes, I used the search function, the last Dino Fight official topic had been locked.

Anyway, I've been playing Dino Fight alot recently, and there's something about armless bunnies beating monsters up with their feet that I find adorable, which may be why I still care.

...Okay, Dino Fight isn't much of a game, but it's still a nice time-waster regardless of your dino. Me, I've been choosing moves carefully for my Sirain: Beamer. Beamer can be quite deadly with both Water Cannon and Gel at his disposal. I avoid teaching him moves that might hinder him like Burning Claws or anything not in his type specialty. ...Beamer's no contender, but he's still a formidable opponent with a decent record of over 70% wins.

...But right now, I have just decided I don't like the move Vines very much. Vines is a move that restrains the target and stops it from attacking. Sure, it doesn't do damage, but usually the opponent spams the move and keeps the target from EVER attacking. Losing? I can take a loss. In fact I kind of like seeing other dinos beat Beamer because that usually prompts me to add them as a friend and view their records. This is good cause I enjoy learning about other Dinos in Dinofight and comparing records between that dino and others of its species. ...But when your Dino loses cause the opponent spams the Vines move, that gets pretty upsetting real quick.
