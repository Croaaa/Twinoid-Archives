Finally worked out what the problem was.

The guides I was referencing weren't specific about how to talk to the Venerable. I wrongly assumed there would be a new dialogue box like there is on all the missions and many of the quest steps.

I talked to him in the standard dialogue box, choose the 'hide' option, and listened to him until the option of 'approach carefully' was available. Clicked on that and got the conversation started.

Toasted tourist has now split the scene, I've finished the quest, and picked up a nice little Pickye along the way.
