U-skills are certain level 4 skills a single dino learns, but affect your whole account. For example, the U-skill Leader will push your dino limit of 18 to 21, and Professor will increase the amount of experience all your dinos get from fights by 5%. However, U-skills don't stack so there is no point in getting a U-skill twice (unless you plan to sell or sacrifice a dino with a U-skill).

Here they are in all their glory *dum da da duuuum*:
Professor
Leader
Engineer - gives an extra equipment slot for each dino
Merchant - 10% discount in the Flying Shop
Priest - All dinos heal 1 extra each hour when resting
Cook (Chef would be a better name..) - Gives 10% more healing for any of your dinos that eats health-recovering items (including battle rations).
Shopkeeper - Increases the maximum inventory (but not ingrediant inventory) by 50%

I think that's all of them.
