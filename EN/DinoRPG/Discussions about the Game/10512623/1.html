3 dinoz of different races is a good option, imo.

A team of 3 winks (sirains, kabukis) can face hard times in steppes because there is a kind of monster that get healed with water attacks

If I were you, I would keep your milion of for later (steppes and nimbao). Otherwise, you will spend it very quickly without getting high levels.

The bonus of +5 at Fountain of youth and the bonus of +10 given by the bracelet can be a good help for the little dinoz, especially for the rockies because their racial skill (Rock) make them a favourite target for monsters (until you get the strategy book and turn it OFF).

So, my advice is: do all the missions you can (except the ones that reward with 100 or more - save them for later) with daily turns. As soon your best dinoz get level 32-33, go to the steppes (there spend but you will get a nice amount of ; so it will be a good investment); when you finish the steppes, do the missions with good reward (monster exploit, dinoland tour, last mission of Diana, strange prowler, ...). After this, go to the celeste island and get nice amounts of buying irma potions with your and fighting. Avoid buying breads /pies for your dinoz, cause they are quite expensive.

Priest (U skill) is a great help to heal dinoz
The start will be slow, but later you will get killer dinoz very quickly

Get extra with the dinoz you already own and use the extra to train your new teams till they are ready to go to the steppes.

I think that 1 milion is not enough to get 3 teams to high levels in few days/weeks.
