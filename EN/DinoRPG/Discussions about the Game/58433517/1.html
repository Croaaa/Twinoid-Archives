As stated above, go wide and deep in elements that your dino specializes in, while going specifically narrow in other elements. Consider reading the guides, especially rianon's guide, and look into the skills of each tree. Each new dino requires a lot of planning. Without it, you won't enjoy the game as much because your dinoz won't develop well enough to fight.
