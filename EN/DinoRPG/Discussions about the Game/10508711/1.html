When you die, you have 2 choices:


1: You get a free resurrection and get revived at DinoTown, and lose half of your gained (so if you had 50, you would lose 25.), and you would automatically be resting, you can stop resting at any time.


2: You use and Angel Potion and get revived at the same place where you died, and you lose no , and just like the 1st choice, you automatically rest.
