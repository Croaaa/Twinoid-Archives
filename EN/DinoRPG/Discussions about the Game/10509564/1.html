I deducted the following from past sales histories. Should be fairy accurate.

Level 1-10 follows the formula of:
base_price+(2000*level)=sell_price

level 11-15 follows the formula of:
price_at_level_10+(15000*level_over_10)= sell_price

level 16-20 follows the formula of:
price_at_level_15+(20000*level_over_15)= sell_price
