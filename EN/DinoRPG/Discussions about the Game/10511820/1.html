a) Every level you have 85% chance NOT to get (0.85)
b) in 28 leveling (lvl 29 means you only level 28 times...), if you fall in that .85 for 21 times you will NOT get 8

d) so, 100%-3.2% = 96.8% to get 8 (exactly 8.... to know 'at least 8' is a bit more complex) to get 8 by level 29
