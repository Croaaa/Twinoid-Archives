First of all you need to talk with the Atlantean Guard at the Mutant Falls, who'll tell you need a Scorpscuba flute. Then go to Frish Port and talk to Joveboze and he'll take you for a fishing trip - i.e. you'll fight the Scorpscuba. It's a water-based monsters, his first assault hits hard and he has 60 so if your strongest assault is good enough, you'll be able to take it down in 1 hit ;D
