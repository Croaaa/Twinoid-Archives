Loppermann
all is not lost, but you probably should have gone for carapace on your winks and wrath on your casti, as these lead to better skills

as a general rule, the best (or should I say most commonly chosen) secondary paths chosen are:
Wrath -> Warm Blood -> Nap/Burning Heart
Carapace -> Vines -> Primal State
Mutation -> Ventral Pouch -> Sumo
Celerity -> Double Hit -> Career Plan
Agility -> Dodge -> Vacuum Disk/Elasticity

there are of course exceptions and variations (like going for Specialist in , and of course double skills can alter which way you go), but these paths are generally the best to take if you only have 1-3 chances of rolling a certain element
