On september 17, 2012
Kayin

Problem is, right now I can tell you what was changed, but the Mana War we could have, could be an upgraded version. So I'm putting it under spoilers:

Alliances
An alliance contract can be made between two clans for 48h.
A clan cannot have more than 3 contracts at a same time.
A contract can be accepted/nullified if the clans aren't in war.
An alliance contract permits to win/lose half honour points when the ally attack same target as your clan.

Tax
You have to pay a tax daily, which is paid in ingredients.
By paying it you regain honour right away.
To pay the tax, you need your castle built.
If the clan didn't pay the tax, you lose X% mana points depending on how many Xday(s) you didn't pay him.

To pay the tax, a guy will appear on the castle tab and is here for 30 minutes any time on the day (by hour).

Honour
Honour loss at declaration.
Honour gain at winning.

Mana
A castle without defenders will give less mana.
