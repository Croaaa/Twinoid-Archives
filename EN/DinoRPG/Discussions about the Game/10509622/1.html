calorifere
I'm no cloudoz expert, but here's how I intend to train mine.

and : As soon as I get Career Plan, reroll those. If you can't reroll, stick with level 1 skills. I would give priority to Wrath in and Carapace in . Going for Burning Claws or Savagery feels a bit of a waste since one of those elements will be removed as soon as you get Specialist ( skill)

: In the Mutation path: get Sumo and Aqueous clone.
I would choose only ONE of the following two paths for your other points:
- If you want an offensive dinoz, go for the Water Cannon path and go all the way to Sher-Kaar ray. Petrification and cold shower and both very interesting if you have the extra skill points
- If you want a gatherer, go for the Perception path. You'll want all the fisher skills, then go all the way to Master Swimmer for extra hit points.

: I would probably ignore the Focus path completely, except maybe for Focus itself.
Career Path is very interesting to reroll weak elements like and , and Celerity and Double Hit you get on the way are necessities on pretty much any dinoz. Of course, if you want a gatherer, get Lightning Conductor and Elemental Fission. Then I would get Lightning and Lightning attack. Adrenaline and Hazard are very nice skills if you have extra points.

: I would get every skill in the Strategy path, maybe except Achilles' Heel, which looks a little weaker than others. Specialist in particular will make your cloudoz a LOT stronger. Then, I'd go for Mistral, Tornado, Vivacious Wind and Vaporous Form. All the other skills in the Mistral path will slow down you dinoz, so I would avoid them. Take a few skills in the Agility branch if you have extra points.

I went agility path and got elasticity and black hole. It is worth it because with elasicity and adrenaline you get the best double skill which gives an extra 15% chance to multihit
Dodge, Elasticity, Overload (the double skill), Vacuum Disk and Black Hole are all excellent skills, especially in pvp or castle attack, so if you want to use your Cloudoz that way, then it may not be a bad idea.
However, if you go that way, and if you decided to take the Perception path, then your cloudoz won't have any A skill that targets all enemies other than Toxic Cloud. In addition, my understanding is that Vacuum Disk and Black Hole really aren't used that often (this is admittedly where my experience is lacking, I don't have a dinoz with those skills - could you confirm this?). Plus, Black Hole doesn't seem that great against monsters: it's great if it rids you of a Gate Guardian, but rather underwhelming if it only kills a Korgon, Flamelet or a Pirhanoz.

In short, if you want to pvp or attack castles with your cloudoz, Black Hole isn't bad, but for anything else, I think I would rather have Mistral and Tornado. And I would definitely get Specialist before Black Hole.
