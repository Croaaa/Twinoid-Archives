I'm not a huge fan of rushing to CP. An additional problem not yet mentioned is that the earlier you get CP, the less likely you are to know how best to use it. For example, you might rush a Glid to CP, then aggressively reroll to get for Sumo, but then when you get to higher levels you have too much water, but can't seem to find that additional for Black Hole or whatever, when you rerolled a load previously. Or you'll reroll a lot of seemingly useless side-elements to get more for it's uber level 3-4 skills, only to find yourself later flooded by and really wishing you would roll more side elements.
