On may 28, 2009
POTAATO
You get a total of 18 Grapebooms when doing the Forest Warden missions, and upon completion, you'll get a Flourishing Branch of the Warden, which gives your dino access to the Fruity Shop.

The Stone of Grapeboom is what's left after using a Grapeboom. The Grapeboom itself heals your dino 15, and the Stone is a battle gear, doing a little poison damage to your enemies.
