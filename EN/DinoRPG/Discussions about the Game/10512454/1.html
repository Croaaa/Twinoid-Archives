HugoMVP
Well, first, there are the war events. Those events, when you move your dinos a lot, even when not fighting other dinos, in the long term, can mean an extra level at the end of a couple of wars, because this movements at the end are a irma burning. Then, if you find dinos on defense, there is even more xp to be earned.

Then, there is the process of burning irmas at the beginning. Like, go till lv15 without doing any mission. Costly, yeah, but it would cost much more to do it later. And you can do this more times: around level 20 fighting chickens, around level 30 in forest, around 35 in steppes, etc. I don't know the best timings exactly, because I never could fully do this myself. I'm not a heavy spender and I need some good dinos, but after you already own a nice team, you can start doing this with new ones, because in the end, it's a slower but cheaper process to get high level dinos.

Then, there were the Piglous last Christmas. Many people reported to have been able to level up a LOT in higher levels, thanks to them.

And then, there is the simples Irma burning. If you can afford, just spend the infinite number of dirmas it takes to level up on those levels.

Skaare
High leveling dino guide in short:

- spawn and do granpa missions and elementals for starters = level ~10
- soft shaman missions, ele master missions for sphere = level ~22
- first diana mission, portal quest, travel to steppes = level ~23
- grind hard. NO missions. steppe monsters are piss easy for well leveled dinoz not strong in water. you will get awesome AND good till level ~33, then it starts declining
- fortunately, at lvl 35 kaktuz start appearing and you will once again be getting great and . once again, grind hard, no missions.
- around level 45, you allready grinded hard enough, so do the remaining missions: steppes, warden, nicholas, diana, mr. bao bob, strange prowler (madam x and skully mission rewards sux, ignore them). then complete brutforce tourney and have a private talk with the venerable. you should be around lvl 47-48 by now, having some reserve for the content that was not added to this server yet.

good luck.
