This has already been mentioned in other servers, so I figured we should probably start a discussion on this here as well.

With the new turn systems, it is now possible to have low level gatherers that would be able to collect whatever it is they are gathering much more frequently.

Example: a lucky rocky or glidwings (possibly using the cube) can get Elemental Fission at level 6. If one decides to not level it further, this rocky or glidwings can gather every 6 hours.

Pushing this to the absurd: one could make a team of 3 dinoz (or possibly, many teams) that they intentionally keep at level 1 to serve as a "gold gathering dinoz", using their battle every hour to get more gold that they would otherwise get if they leveled their dinoz.

Is this an intended feature of the new turn system, of is it something that was overlooked by the devs and should be removed?

My personal opinion is that the extreme exploits of this system were overlooked, and that, if the devs really want to have a varying time between turns depending on the "age" of a dinoz, then the time between turns should vary depending on the number of turns the dinoz has played so far, instead of depending on its level.
