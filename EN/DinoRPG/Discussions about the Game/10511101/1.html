The answer to the guys question is simple.

It all depends on the build of your dino.
The worth of any skill choice you make will depend entirely on other skill choices you make, even those that are to come, so their are no right or wrong individual choices only a good or bad "build" made of many single choices. So think it through, what do you want out of the dino, use skill guides and whatever else is lurking about to make your choices, figure out the probabilities of getting certain elements over your dino's life time, include adjustments for random interference, write down your then made plans and stick to it.
