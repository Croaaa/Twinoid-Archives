There is no single best set of Dinoz for an account. There are 14 Dinoz types, and some masters would want at least one of each. Some other masters prefer just a few types of Dinoz... My current favorite is the Sirain, because it's Water element is so strong... but any can be good.

The limit is 18 Active Dinoz. Active meaning eventually you will be able to freeze Dinoz and make them Inactive. You will find out how to freeze Dinoz before you can finish the Kabuki quest.

The limit can be raised to 21 with a forth level Wood skill presumably called Leader. Guessing the skill names, it is above Savagery => Tenacity => Charisma or Magic Resistance => Leader

If you have a Gorilloz getting the Leader skill is not too hard... with a minimum of 7 choice levelings in Wood .
