There are 20 days till end of the war. A clan without castle loses 1 reputation point the first day, 2 points the second, 3 the third etc. So if some clan lost castle now and would not rebuild it till end of the war, they would loose 210 reputation points. Both Genesis and Twin Demon lack castle for a while now allready, so their reputation decline will be even faster, and both will have reputation 0 before end of the war, falling to the end of the rankings. Clan points are calculated = (sum of defeated clans reputation)*reputation/100, therefore clan with 0 reputation will have 0 clan points as well. All clans ranked lower than them will gain +2 rank as they fall, unless they rebuild castle.

I have found the following formula about clan reputation on french server:

Reputation is calculated from the formula:

R = ((500+PWIN) / (500+PLOST)) ^ 0.8 x 100

PWIN being the point-of-war won
PLOST being the point-of-war lost

Points wars are calculated from the balance of power between the clans at the launch of the war.
