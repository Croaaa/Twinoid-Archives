The Goblin Merguez are free and unlimited. Every Dinoz should always have as many as it can carry, whenever something else is not in the slot.

You really should just rest the Dinoz before the fight, rather than risk it dying and then having to rest even longer. In fact, that is one of the fights you should spend more on, to heal the Dinoz up as close to 100% as you can get. Remember that flying Dinoz can fly as often as every turn.

If you're worried about money, then stay in place fighting until you have the extra gold you need. The game requires patience, so practice it.
