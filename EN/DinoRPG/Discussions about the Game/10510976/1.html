1rst, there's no tier or any division in clanwars, it's a WAR : every clan can attack any clan (and manyclans can attack the same clan at the same moment )

and they may NEED it to win enough points for the ranking
but they'll not atack all small clans as such clan may finish with 0 reputation, and don't provide the big clan the points he's looking for, so attacking too weak ennemy is a waste of ressources (mainly ingredients)

where is the good level for the war, i don't know, it really depend the target of your clan and other members of your clan

there 4 way to paricipate to a war :

attacking : you need a team of 4-5 strong dinoz (when i said strong, it mean as strong as the strongest dinoz of the clan you want to attack so you have to choose your target wisely and don't attack a too strong ennemy
earn if you manage to kill ennemy defenders, but to succed, you may need to heal your dinoz and use irma potions to atack multiple times

defending ; need as many strong dinoz as you want (a master with only 1 strong dinoz can not attack, but he can easily defend as other membres help him)
no ideal level but it has to be the strongest dinoz of the clan
earn if your dinoz manage to survive an attack (and more if you kill attackers)
cost : need fast heal (so buy bread, pie ..., use rations), and need to connect often to chek your dinoz

breaking wall : as many dinoz as you want, no level needed also the higher the best
once your stronger members have kill the defenders, you can atack the now undeffended walls (your dinoz can only attack once every 10 minutes, so, if you want to break a castel faster, you need lot of dinoz, members that don't have a suffisant level to kill defenders can attack walls and speed up the victory, it also save some irma potions from your strong players so they are not the only one spending in your clan )

gathering : clan wars are free at start, but rapidly you will need ingredients to declare new wars and to repear your broken castel, so you need some dinoz with gathering skills that provide you with the necessary items (basic ingredients of all type and, if your clan win many wars and rank well, you will need all rare plants too (see clan pages for what ingredients i'm talking about and where to find them )

Zephides
-How can i find other castles?
Once you have declared a war against another castle, you search around Dinotown Map (not Cementary!). You will find an option to Attack the enemies castle once you found the correct place.

-if i found one, can i atack it alone?
Yes, but it's more fun when you bring some friends along

-if your clan is atacked...where can i see that so i can help?
You can look at the special War tab on your clan's presentation page. Also, you can look at your clan's History tab to see the fights that have occured. (Aswell as Attacking another clan or Defending your own).

-does one clan declare a war to another like about 1day earlier?
Once a war is declared, they can attack right away. Between each attack that has been done, they would have to wait (a minimum of) 10 minutes to be able to attack you again.

-my stronger dino is 16 lvl, can this dino attack others?
Ofcourse he can
