Yeah, I know what you mean... MotionTwin has attempted to design this game to get people to pay out as much as possible. It makes sense; MT's a business.

BUT this ranking system is such an incredibly transparent attempt to boost revenues that most players will recognize it for what it is and immediately disregard it. (If the rankings aren't measuring the strength of my dinos relative to those of other players, it's pretty much useless to me and provides very little incentive to pay out cash.)

That being said, if MT could provide a relatively accurate way to compare my dinoz against other players, there would be a more compelling reason to improve my dino's ranking... and, by extension, a more compelling reason to pay out real cash.

If MT doesn't want to abandon the current ranking system, they can always add one or more ranking systems to run in parallel. Doing so would give players multiple ways to work their way into the rankings (and multiple reasons to pay MT money).

Arcade games used this same model to ensure players continued to pump quarters into the machines. It can work the same way for this game.
