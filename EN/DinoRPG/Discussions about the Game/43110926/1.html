[ http://twinoid.com/en/article/5022/clan-championships ]

Ready yourselves and your dinoz for the latest installment of the Clan Championships!

The tournament will commence on the 8th of January 2015 at 19:00 server time.

The Clan Chiefs will be able to choose their league as early as the 23rd of December.

Here's the setup for the Championship:
  • The battles will last for 100 hours.
  • There will be 44 hours of rest between battles.
Pro League

Reserved for the elite fighters of Dinoland, only the best of the best can challenge here:
From level 40 to level 70, the battles will be held in Dinotown.

Winner: Epic Reward of the Vanquisher + 4 Napoz + 150 notes.
Runner-Up: Epic Reward + 3 Napoz + 150 notes.
Semi-Final: Epic Reward + 3 Napoz + 100 notes.
Finals: Epic Reward + 2 Napoz + 50 notes.

Amateur League

This is for the up and coming talent of Dinoland, come and test your mettle!
From level 20 to level 40, the battles will be held in Dinotown.

Winner: Epic Reward of the Vanquisher + 2 Napoz + 100 notes.
Runner-Up: Epic Reward + 1 Napo + 100 notes.
Semi-Final: Epic Reward + 80 notes.
Finals: Epic Reward + 20 notes.

Starter League

For the tough talking debutants! Come and put your money where your mouth is...
From level 10 to level 25, the battles will be held in Dinotown.

Winner: Epic Reward of the Vanquisher + 100 notes.
Runner-Up: Epic Reward + 80 notes.
Semi-Final: Epic Reward + 50 notes.
Finals: Epic Reward.

The leagues could be subject to change according to the number of clans who sign up.

Courage to all and may the best Master win...
