Yep, and each of them will need to accept and validate the missions individually. Each one will have to talk to granpa joe, each one will have to pick up a load of fish, each one will have to talk to Madam Squyd, then will have to talk to Granpa Joe to get their rewards.

Also, remember to check each of your Dinoz after every move! One might have a special ability that the others don't... So, if you forget to check your Dino with the fishing skill while you are at the Frish port, you'll miss a valuable opportunity to collect Ingredients!

On the Plus side, however, by grouping your Dinoz, they tend to lose a lot fewer in each battle. I have a trio that hasn't had to use the Rest action or any healing foods for almost two weeks... and I'm on here daily! They also tend to get a lot more per fight! Today, my trio of level 8 Dinoz won 2450 in a single move!

The best way that I have found to build up a good Trio is to save your coins until you can buy three Dinoz at once and then keep them as a team from Level 1.

I hope this helps.
