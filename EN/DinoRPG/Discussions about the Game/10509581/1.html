That's it exactly.

You get , and unlock some skills (2, 4, 6, whatever). You don't get them then, but next time you get , those skills will be available.

If you get the other skills before unlocking the ones for the next level, you can unlock all of the skills for the level at one time, so fewer levels are spent unlocking.

Of course, you might want to get to a certain skill faster, or not get certain skills at all. That's why you can unlock just the two.

If you have one skill already, you can then unlock the two skills behind it when you level up.

If you have two skills though, you will be able to unlock four skills with just one level up.

And if you have as much as three skills already, you can unlock six new skills with just one level up.

So unless you know exactly what skills you want (and don't want), I recommend you take all the skills you can take before you unlock anything.
