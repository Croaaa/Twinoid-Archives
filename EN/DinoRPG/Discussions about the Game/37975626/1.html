The 25th of April is ANZAC Day, a day of huge historical importance for our friends in Australia and New Zealand. On this day, these countries commemorate those lost in service as well as service people who returned home.

You stand shoulder to shoulder with your clanmates in every Mana War and Championship of the Clans, as service people the world over do for their brothers in arms. On this, the 99th anniversary of ANZAC day, the King has commissioned a hunt to rid the lands of invaders once again. The King has gone further than ever to reward the bravest warriors.

The hunt is already underway, and will continue until the 30th of April 2014, at 15:30 server time (CEST). The prizes for the top hunters will be as follow:

1st Place: 1 Etherwasp Egg + Epic Reward + 30 Treasury Notes*
2nd to 5th: 1 Kabuki Egg + 20 Treasury Notes*
6th to 10th: 1 Feross Egg + 15 Treasury Notes*
11th to 20th: 10 Tac Tickets*
21st to 50th: 50 Treasury Notes
51st to 100th: 25 Treasury Notes

Individual progress prizes even better than last time...

Happy ANZAC Day, and happy hunting!

boutYou have to hunt using Smashroom Hunter, Giant Hunter or Dragon Hunter, right?

No. It has nothing to do with collecting skills.

In this event you may encounter monsters (usually those that are encountered in the Dark World). If you defeat the monsters, you may get throphies.

These trophies have point values.

For each point mark you reach, you'll get a individual progress prize (for example 500 points -> 1 Warm Bread* or 5000 -> 1 Golden Napodino*).
In addition, the points estimate you rank for this event, where you get the listed prize.


*These are just made up, to provid examples.
