Every monster has a minimum level on which it will comes against your dinoz. You can find the list here: http://en.dinorpg.com/clan/125?page=1143
You get the most experience for a monster on this level. If your dino is under or over this level the experience gain will decrease. If you have a high level dino and a much lower level dino in one group, then only one of them will get good experience. Try to keep your groups in the same monster level category.
