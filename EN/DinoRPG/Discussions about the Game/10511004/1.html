Wrath/Fury/Vengeance = +25% next attack, +3 to all assaults, -2 to all defenses, & +5% chance to counter attack

Strength/Martial Arts/Waikikido = +3 to all assaults, +20 hp, +10% chance to counter attack, -4 initiative

I'd opt for your first choice of skills, so: Wrath -> Warm Blood -> Burning Heart.
All three are beneficial, without any negative impact. The most useful skill out of those is imho Warm Blood.
Other paths are hampering your defence (two points might not seem much, but sometimes are crucial) or greatly lowering initiative.
