Loppermann
those are some epic names!

the you spent on trying to get those air energies isn't a waste, as every little bit you spend brings you closer to getting them
in all honesty, you are quite a long way away from getting them, but it is vital to have a steady flow of energies if you want to do well in this game

my advice to you would be to:
1 Freeze your dinoz that is currently doing the air mission
2 Buy a glidwings and rush to elemental fission
3 Go to the university with all your dinoz and defeat the fire and water elementals
4 Group your dinoz of similar levels in groups of 3 (if you haven't already) and start getting the lantern with all of them

by this stage, it is likely your glidwings will have elemental fission, so park him at brutforce and gather some energies. Once you get the lantern with other dinoz, freeze some of them (the ones that aren't turning out like you'd hoped) and buy more glidwings and rush them to elemental fission
once you've done this, you'll be able to do the elemental master's missions, sell or use the spheres he gives you and also sell the surplus energies you get and hey presto, you'll be a force in this game!

it might also be a good idea to join an experienced clan, as they can offer lots of good advice and have guides in how to advance in the game
