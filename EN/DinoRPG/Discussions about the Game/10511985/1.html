Piemaster
The problem withthe training centre at the moment is that the reward is just not worth the cost. The cost is a turn + 500g and the reward is a bit of XP and gold. The dino could get the same benefit at the cost of just a turn, simply by moving or fighting.

So to 'fix' the TC you would have to either increase the reward or decrease the cost. Unfortunately this idea is inherently broken (a borrow a phrase from M:TG). It is very difficult to set the cost and reward at a level that is perfectly balanced. If the reward is too high compared to the cost, everyone will just sit at the training centre all the time and not experience all the cool zones, quests and monsters out there. On the other hand if it is too low then... well, we'll have the situation we're in now.

I think the best way of actually making the TC useful without being broken is to take it out of the bog-standard gold>XP model by making the reward something different that players (or at least some players) will actually want, but at the same time won't want to pursue exclusively at the expense of everything else. I guess the lazy thing to suggest at this point would be some kind of dino egg (a small chance of winning obviously), but something new and interesting would work well too.
