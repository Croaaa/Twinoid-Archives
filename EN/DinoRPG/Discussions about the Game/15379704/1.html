1st Your dinoz is missing the Pure water sample, go to fountain of youth and dig for it.

2nd Go to the Underwater Dome and talk to Archsage and he will receive the 3 items, basalt, swamp mud and water sample and in return you must battle the Earth Elemental. If you win the Archsage will give you Gor's Glove.

3rd Go to Mutant Falls and talk to Master Hydragol, make sure you make the right conversation, and he will give you the Waterlilly Pad.

Hey now you can access a small part of the forest. Want to access more parts, continue as follows.

4th Enter the forest by way of the falls and go to the Haunted Hills. Make the right converstion with the strange man and you will fight against a single weak Korgon. After you win the fight the strange man will flee leaving his lantern there, which becomes your property.

This lantern is needed to access the forest from the Tunnel under the Branch/Deep Gorges. Now to obtain full access of the forest you need to do Diane Korgsey 1st mission. Once completed she will award you fins to cross the river.

Good luck.
