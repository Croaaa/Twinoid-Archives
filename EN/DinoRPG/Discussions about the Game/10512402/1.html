reputation drop when castle is broken looks like this:
1 day - no rep penalty
2 day -1 penalty
3 day -2
4 day -3
etc

so if one clan XXXX has reputation 30 on the next day after its castle was broken then:
1 day rep 30
2 day rep 29=30-1
3 day rep 27=29-2
4 day rep 24=27-3
etc

Also, Clan Rep is only Calculated once a Day at Server Midnight during Server Reset at 00:00 Server Time and therefore does not change during the Day no matter how many Wins or Losses a Clan has and then is recalculated again after Server Reset. However, the Clan Points are Recalculated after each Win, Loss, or change in Rep of the Clan. Also, for the War, there is a final Clan Rep Recalculation at the Wars End.
