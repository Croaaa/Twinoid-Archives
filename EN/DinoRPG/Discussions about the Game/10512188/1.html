Piemaster
Got to be the islands for 6 reasons:

1. I'm not sure you can even fight the fire elemental until level 7
2. If you could, the mountains would be a major PITA at that level. Goblins would eat you alive.
3. You need the pearl ASAP
4. Once you get to level 12 the islands will be basically inaccessible for a while, so do it while you can.
5. As you have already identified, Gorilloz are much better in the islands than the mountains.
6. There isn't really anything in the mountains that you need at this stage. The Strategy Guide is handy eventually, but not really needed on a low-level Gorilloz.
