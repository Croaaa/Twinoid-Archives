To clarify:

- If you already have max dinos, you can't bid at all.

- If you have less than max dinos, you can bid.
After the bid, you might buy more dinos from the shop before the auction ended, meaning you now have max dinos + an auction bid.
If you win the auction, you can't get the new dino until you freeze another one of your dinos.
