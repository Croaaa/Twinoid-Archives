@Lorkan26: If you want a strong dinoz with a special skill on start, you should buy one of them. But they are more expensive than the "normal" ones. So, if you want to save money for your third dinoz, you should buy a cheaper one. Personally, I will buy a gorilloz next time (forth dinoz) and maybe after that a softpig (first "expensive" dinoz for me), when I earn money fast enough.

The special skills cannot be learned by other dinoz, but there are similar ones. Softpig's horned charge (+25% strength on first attack) has a similar effect as charge (+5 strength on first attack). But a softpig can have both, of course
