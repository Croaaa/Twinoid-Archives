Requiring the Membership Card:
Talk to the Venerable and follow this interaction path:
-> 'Uhh... It's me'
-> 'I was running after a Korgon and...'
-> 'He has escaped'
-> 'That's efficient...'
-> 'I would like my Dinoz to be stronger!'
Go to Dinotown and give the Injured Soldier a Warm Bread
