Yes, Primary school math may tell you that the chance of getting 6 hits is 0.032%, but that isn't the math being used.

Every time multi-hit comes up, you get 2 hits.
Each hit is an instance in itself, and has a 20% chance of being a multi-hit, in the same way that any coin flip has a 50% chance of coming up heads, regardless of what the previous flips may have been.
