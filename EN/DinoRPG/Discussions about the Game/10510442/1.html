Piemaster
Generally speaking, for fighting dinos, you want to take every skill at a level that you intend to take before unlocking the next level as this saves the number of level ups you need.

Obviously it's difficult to determine what skills you will eventually want when you first start, so it might take some forward planning or logical thinking. For example Gorilloz get so many wood level-ups, that they will likely take every wood skill if you play them long enough, whereas a Casti will get far less so you may want to ignore some of the paths.

Depends on the Dino and the element of the level up ...

For exampel, Any dino specialized in one element should get all level 1 skills, unlock, many level 2 skills (all you want), some level 3 skills, etc. for his main element. For other elements, were the dino might only get 5 level ups untill level 50, its often better to go only one path. Is a good exampel, were many Dinoz only go for MutationVentral PouchSumo, because those are the best skills for Dinoz with low level (A and E skills like Water Cannon are useless for those Dinoz in that element).
For balanced Dinoz you might want to take 2 level 1 skills in the main elements and go those paths.

So, the best thing woud be to make a Plan for your dino on what skills you want to have for level 45 for exampel based on the level ups your Dino is supposed to get. But depending on the level ups your dino really gets you might want to change that plan during the way to level 45.

A good exampel how a Plan for your dino can look like is the Skillz Guide by BishopX. There are some good Guides for nearly all Dinoz, but you might have to differ them depending on what role you want your dinoz to play. (Gatherer, PVE fighter, castle attacker, castle defender, U-Skill-Dinoz)
Aslo Flippy postet some good guides for Wanwan and Hippoclamp (just search the forum).
