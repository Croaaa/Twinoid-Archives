Every person can only write three words and together we make a story.
example:
person1 "The cow slowly"
person2 "ate a blade"
person3 "of crunchy grass"
person4 ". But then suddenly"
etc

few simple rules:
1. your three words should be the first three words in your post and in bold.
2. stay on topic. story can be random and silly but it should make sense, and have correct(ish) grammar. for example: "the cow ate" "he was afraid" doesn't make sense.
3. don't post a reply with out continuing the story. If you want to say something else say it under your addition to the story.
4. less of a rule just a mention: There should/can be punctuation in the story. And anyone can do it.
P1 "he hates corn." and
P1 "he hates corn"
P2 ", pizza, and fish" are both correct

I hope that makes since and wasn't to long an explanation.
