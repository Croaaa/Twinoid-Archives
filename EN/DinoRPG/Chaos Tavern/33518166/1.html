As a reminder you can read the DinoRPG Forum Rules

To create games, you have to follow these rules:

The game must be understandable and the rules exhaustive in the first post of the topic, otherwise it will be locked.
The game has to not be too similar to the other ones.
The game has to respect the forum rules.
The topic's title has to indicate the purpose of the game.

The moderators, according to the rules of the forum, reserve themselves the right to close a topic considered non-corresponding without warning.
