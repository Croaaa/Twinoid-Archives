One day, a mischevious scorpion had walked into a bar, and was about to place his order. The bartender asks him what he wants. The scorpion replied "Got any grapes?" The bartender explains that his bar didn't sell grapes. Discouraged, the scorpion left.

The next day, he returned to the same bar. Before the bartender could ask what he wanted, the scorpion said "Got any grapes?" The bartender tells the scorpion once again that he didn't have any grapes. The scorpion leaves.

Once again, the scorpion returned to the bar the next day. He asks "Got any grapes?" The bartender, irritated, tells the scorpion that if he asks for grapes again, he'll nail his tail to the wall. The scorpion leaves again.

One last time, the scorpion has returned to the bar. The bartender, still annoyed, gruffly asks the scorpion what he wants. The scorpion replied "Got any nails?" The bartender says no to this. The scorpion then says "Got any grapes?"
