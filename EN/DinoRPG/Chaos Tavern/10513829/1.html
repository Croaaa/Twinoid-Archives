The Story Of Welceon
By Killer60-The Storyteller
GHKing1-Co Writer

Chapter 1

Welceon was a boy sirain. He lived in a village at an area close to the market place. One day when he went hunting at night, his village was attacked by a horde of green giants. He was just returning when he saw his mother and father surrounded by 11 of the toughest green giants. They fought bravely but it was unavoidable that they fell, dead. After the tradegy, Welceon was horrified at what the village had become. The village was more like 3 jumbo ships smashed together on land. Wolves were fighting eachother for the leftover scraps of food. All he had now was his amulet that his mother gave him. There was nothing for him now that his village was destroyed, so he left traveling to the fountain of youth.

Chapter 2


When Welceon got to the fountain of youth, he was exhausted. So he rested on a branch of a enormous tree right above the fountain of youth where he was safe, or at least he thought he was. He quickly fell asleep and he woke in the morning. Welceon woke up to something picking at his foot. When he looked up he saw it was just a bird. He slid down the tree's trunk and landed on the ground with a thump. The bird was circling him from above. Welceon was hungry, so he went hunting. He came back with nothing. Welceon thought to himself that hunting on land is harder than at sea. He was thinking he could smell cooked fished and he could! He followed the smell to a fire at Frish Port. A guy there saw him and gave him some fish because he saw that the little sirain was starving. Welceon ate the fish and came to be friends with the guy. He listened to the guy explain what he was doing here. The guy said that he was waiting for somebody to pay him 10,000 gold coins. But he never said anything about his name. All he said next were about his 2 weird dinoz. He told Welceon that they were called Kabuki.....

Chapter 3

Next day, the guy tought Welceon the skill Water Cannon to help him hunt and fight on land. After that, the guy gave him a bag full of fish for his journey to avenge his village. Welceon said his thanks and goodbyes and left. Welceon traveled back to the fountain of youth and went back up in the tree. He was looking up in the sky when he saw the bird again. It had followed him all thhis time he thought. Then Welceon doze off to sleep.

Chapter 4 - In Association With GHKing1 - SPECIAL CHAPTER

When Welceon woke up, the bird was squaking on the branch next to him. For a moment he thought the bird said "Be Ca-a-arful" But, he thought it was just his imagination. He slid down the tree and went fishing at the river that flowed around the Fountain of Youth. While on his way to the river he saw a man walking to the fountain of youth, next to him was a purple Gorilloz. Welceon dove in the bushes to hide. He saw from his hiding spot that the man filled up a bottle with some kind of water oozing out from deep inside the fountain. He realized it was Pure Tear Water. He remembered from one of Golzok's stories. Golzok was an old sirain adventurer at his village, he often told stories about his adventures. He remembered that Pure Tear Water was made from the salt of the pained tears of the heart-broken lovers. It is said that an once epic romance between two people were broken up at the fountain of youth and both of then cried pain tears of sorrow. So many tears fell from their eyes that it created 7/8ths of the fountain's Pure Tear Water. The salt of the tears mixed with pure water creates a devasting grey liquid. He remember the risks and benefits of drinking it: There were multiple risks, but the most common and devastating is that your dino's love and compassion will be eaten by the liquid leaving a dark soul in it's place full of hatred and destruction. This usually turns your dino into a Demon Dino and the Demon Dino goes on a horrible rampage before settling down. There were two benefits, if your dino turns into a demon dino, it will have great reflexes and a special ability. Another benefit was that your dino would gain the speed of a SUPERCHARGED Wan-wan and have the RAW power of the Venerable for 20 days and there was a side affect with that much power, if your dino is overwhelmed by that power that dino will begin to morph into a TERRRIFYING beast that only cares for one thing: MORE POWER. The chance of that super benefit which makes your dino into a powerhouse is 4%. Only 2 dinoz have ever gotten that far, 1 was too weak and morphed and the other died when his master tried giving him another dose of Pure Tear Water. Welceon saw that the guy fed it to his Gorilloz. What happened next UNBELIEVABLE... The guy made the Gorilloz drink it! For a minute nothing happened, then a shockwave that knocked Welceon and the guy over exploded. The guy was unconscious and Welceon felt pain erupting from his back. When he recovered from the pain, he saw what happened. The purple Gorilloz was no longer purple, infact the gorilloz turned to the color of hatred: Red.

Chapter 5

BAMMM!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! Welceon got hit in the left leg, went flying, and hit a tree. Welceon tried using water cannon to defend himself but the gorilloz was too fast. But not as fast as a supercharged wan-wan because it turned into a demon dino. Another attack hit him in the chest knocking Welceon out cold. The gorilloz was gonna finish him off when it got struck in the shoulder by a Rocky?! Standing in the water was Perrbear2 with Tank by his side.Tank used vines against the gorilloz and used lightning dance. Then used lightning to knock the gorilloz out. Perrbear2 then got medical help...

Chapter 6

Welceon was rushed into the DinoLand Emergency Room. They reported that he has 12 fractured bones and has a concussion. Welceon was finally carried out of the emergency room 14 hours later, needing to stay in a hospital room for 2 months. Welceon woke up next morning and noticed he was in a hospital. He tried to get up when Perrbear2 told him to relax. Perrbear2 was going to stay with Welceon for a week before leaving. Welceon couldn't feel his legs. He looked around for his special amulet and was glad it was still on his neck. A week later.... Perrbear2 had to leave so he said his farewells and left. Welceon still wondered why the bird he saw at the fountain of youth was following him. At the time it was at the window starring at him. It was a weird bird but somehow he felt like he knows what kind of bird it is but can't remember the name...

Chapter 7

Welceon woke up 2 months later. he felt great except that he had back pains when he bends to far. He dicided to go to a place call the university. His parents told him that the university was full of humans and millions if not, billions of books. he always did want to see what it looked like for himself. But he dicided to stay in dinotown for awhile longer or atleast until he didn't ahve those pains and ready to fight again. He searched for a place to stay and he spotted a sirain villlage at the outskirts of town. The sirains there were all green. They welcomed Welceon for the night...

Chapter 8 - GHKing1


The next day Welceon made his way to the University, he encountered a few smashrroms but they were no problem. The bird that followed Welceon was still circling Welceon. He decided to name the bird Flash for it's speed. When he got to the University professors were running around and a group of sirains, winks, and hippoclamps were racing to an exit. Welceon heard a guy say that there was a forest fire at Precipitous Hills. Suddenly a Sirain from the group spotted Welceon and rushed toward him. Fortunately, he spoke sirain language and told Welceon that he should follow them to Precipitous Hills because they need all the help they can get to put of the fire. When Welceon got up there, he could see a blazing forest. Suddenly, Welceon had a flashback to the destruction of the village. He standed there shocked, while winks, sirains, and hippoclamps rushed past him...

Chapter 9

When Welceon woke up from the flashback he realized that he had been dozing off for a few minutes and a burning tree was next to him. He used water cannon to put it out and rushed down to aid the other dinoz. An explosion came from behind a rock. A tribe of Moueffe jumped out behind the rock. One of them did a war cry and used fireball which struck a Sirain down. Welceon in anger, knocked out a Moueffe with water cannon. Suddenly, a fight began infront of a blazing forest. Welceon didn't know who he hit or what hit him. He saw more and more explosions scattered everywhere. A dino used cold shower and it was raining. But, not enough rain to put out the fires. A cloudoz used toxic cloud and the last thing Welceon saw was that every dino in range of the toxic cloud fell down. Welceon went dreaming and saw a drop of water in darkness...

Chapter 10

The drop of water grew and took the shape of a sirain. Then it exploded into millions of sparkles. Welceon woke up, and saw he was still on the field next to the forest and a few other dinoz were starting to wake up. The tribe of Moueffe must have escaped thought Welceon. The forest fire had somehow been put out. Professors where scattered everywher. It was dark out and Welceon looked up. He saw a meteor shower, Welceon never say such a beautiful sight. Suddenly a small meteor crashed a few feet away from Welceon and caused Welceon to fall forward. He quickly looked back, trying to resist the pain in his legs. He saw that the meteor brock apart and inside it was a diamond-shaped green crystal. He was about to touch it when he remembered that it might be hot so he used water cannon to cool it down...

Chapter 11

Welceon touched the crystal and a vision of himself surrounded by twisting sand. Welceon fell backward. He stood back up and saw that other dinoz were coming toward him. A professor rushed to the crystal, armed with a twizer. He took a peice of the crystal with the twizer and put it in a bag. Welceon stared at him. He stared back. The professor turned and left muttering something under his breath. Welceon grabbed the rest of the book sized crystal and left. He could see a castle burning in the distance. Suddenly the one of the castle's wall collapsed...

Chapter 12

Welceon dicided to venture into the castle. He saw a scared korgan lying in a corner and the roof above it was about to collaspe. Welceon ran as fast as he could and saved the korgan. He rushed out of the castle. Welceon saw that the korgan was unconscious and put the little korgan on his shoulder. It was night and Welceon camped under the roots of a large tree. He made a fire by biting a stick and rubbing the end of the stick against a piece of flint. He soon fell asleep, not noticing that the green crystal had turned pink. He had a dream of a magical land where rocks floated magnetically and a group of sirains were playing on a rock. Welceon began weeping in his sleep...

Chapter 13

He woke up to the smell of a rainy day. He was laying over something and saw it was the bag that guy gave to him when he first started the journey about a week ago. The korgan was still sleeping on his shoulder. He saw something move in the bushes. He didn't want to wake the korgan so he sat as still as he could. The sped out of the bushes and infront of Welceon's resting place under a tree. Welceon was scared, the animal was a green giant. Welceon remembered that green giants didn't have good eyesight and they depend on smell. Welceon thought to himself that good thing it was raining. The green giant didn't seem to notice him and lept back into the bushes... Only for a fight begin in the bushes. Welceon could hear the sounds of screeches and cracking of branches. The fighting went on for about 6 minutes before a figure leaped out from the bushes, victorious...

Chapter 15

Leafblaze showed Welceon all his things from fur tufts to a peice of unmeltable ice. Leafblaze heard Welceon's stomach rumble and guided him to his dining room. The room was just any other room except that there was a little water fall, a wooden table, some smooth stone chairs, and box made out of unmeltable ice. Out of the box, Leafblaze pulled out some lemons but Welceon didn't know what they were so he decided to taste one. Leafblaze went to a pile of stuff and took out a flint and cut the lemon and the skin. He then gave Welceon a peice. Welceon popped the whole slice in his mouth. For a moment he stood there and then he fizzled out of control. He hopped around saying "WATER WATER I NEED WATER WATER!" Leafblaze told him "Get to the little waterfall" Welceon jumped into the waterfall....
