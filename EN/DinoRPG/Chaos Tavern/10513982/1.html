Just for fun, while I'm gone, I want to see who would you choose to be the favorite Under-Dome Series character of the year. Below are the names of 9 major UDS characters, and it is your job to vote for your favorite one. This game follows the same principles as the Might Pen Holder game (I'm sure many of you are familiar with it). If you don't know how to play, then here are the rules:

1) To vote, you add one point to a character while taking away one point from two other characters. Ex, if you vote for Thrust, and take away a point from Throttle and Azelle, then the chart would look like this:
Thrust 15 + 1 = 16
Azelle 15 - 1 = 14
Throttle 15 - 1 = 14

2) You can only vote once every 24 hours. If you skip one day, that does not mean that you can vote twice the next day.

3) The character who has no points left is officially voted out and his/her name is eliminated from the chart. The remaining character is declared Favorite Character of 2010.

4) Unlike the original game, there is no Monday Massacre. That's to keep the game fair for every candidate.

I'll see who you have voted to be your favorite when I come back. The following candidates are listed in alphabetical order. Let the elections begin!
