Oilstate:

It actually has to do with the fact that American football, soccer, and rugby all basically evolved from the same sport. If you look really closely at the rules, you'll see some similarities that are leftovers from the time when they were the same sport. For instance, soccer and American football both have 11 people per team on the field.

So, back when "football" first evolved away from soccer in the United States, it involved the foot much more than it does today. While the game changed so that kicking became much less important, the name "football" stuck around.
