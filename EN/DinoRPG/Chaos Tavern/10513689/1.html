so here's how it works:
Round 1: We will be guessing what the person looks like
We will call the person who we'll be guessing as the Star. So the star will give us 2 clues on what would he/she look like.

you could answer these question to help you out on giving clues.
*gender
*ethnicity
*age group (kids, teens,20's ,30's...and so on)
*hobbies
*where you are usually found each day
(note: You'll be surprised how people would picture you out based on your hobbies and hangouts)

Everyone else would then be describing how he/she thinks you look like. Only 1 guess per post. The players are given 5 chances to guess (collectively, not for each person guessing). The star should point out which of these guesses are correct.

by the end of the round, the star will post his/her reference picture and the person who got most of the guess correct or the closest wins the game and will be the next star of the game.

oh and on additional note: to avoid advertisement problems, use an online photo album to upload your image. post only the direct link to image.
