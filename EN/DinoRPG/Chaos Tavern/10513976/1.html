Well, I'll try to be understandable... What I'm proposing here is a kind of RP story everyone can join. But there are rules. It consists in a tavern, where you can play a character of your choice (even the owner or the waiter). It's a concept that already exists on the French server, and I really love it, since everyone respects the rules and nobody's flooding. Here's the presentation:

While you're walking in the Grumhel woods, you spot a building that seems to be a bar. You move closer and read this placard on the door :

The hidden tavern is a discussion space in which RP-style is required.

If you encounter a problem, please feel free to contact me by :, and I'll see what I can do to help you.

Interested, you decide to push the door, and enter the Tavern...
Suddenly, you spot 2 guys that give you a warm welcome : the owner and a waiter.


Here's the Menu :

Gluon Juice : 10
Giant Chicken Leg : 30
Smashrooms of Dinotown : 20
Green Giant Steak : 30

Of course, there are rules :

Write at least some lines of RP writing in each post !
Well, in short, rules are the same as in the forum :
we remind our deer customers of the prohibition on flood, insults, sms language, abuse of smileys, fraudulent links, and so on.

Concerning the role-playing, basic discussions can occur, but don't forget we are in a tavern.
And of course, you can't turn up suddenly thanks to a mega teleportation created by a magic spatiotemporal hole, right ?

Have fun with this Tavern

Legend :
Italics = action, narration
Bold = thoughts of the characters
Normal = dialogs
Underlined = out of place message.

I hope rules will be respected end this RP will be accepted by the moderation.
