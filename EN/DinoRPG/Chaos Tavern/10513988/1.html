No, I'm not advertising an MMO game that doesn't belong to Motion Twin. I know the title sounds like it, but no.

I came up with this game in inspiration from Pennylou's Tavern of Grumhel game. My game follows the same basic rules of Pennylou's game, but there are several features that I've changed.

Dungeon Raid RPG takes place in a tavern at Dinland called the Chaos Tavern (i.e. this tavern), where a group of raiders are plotting to infiltrate the Katatombs at Twilight Cemetary. There are several scenes in this game in which the players have to act out in their role before the following scene. The story progresses as the scenes go along.

Here are the rules:

1) You have to act as one of your dinoz; not anybody else's dinoz, or an NPC, or anybody/anything that you made up, etc.
(This is only for the first scene)

2) You can only have one character present at a time, so you can't act for two dinoz at once

3) You can play any role that you like, it's completely up to you

4) Try not to multi-post; better still is try not to multi-post at all

5) Bold printing is used to express thoughts; Italic printing is used to narate for your character; Underlined printing is used for off-hand comments; and regular printing is used when a character talks.

If you any questions, just tell me.

Thrust the Wanwan finally made it to the Chaos Tavern. He was told that this was a place where only the most daring and adventurous dinoz hanged out.

In need of a raiding party! he spoke out clearly into the crowds of the tavern. Katatomb raiding party! Who's interested? Consult me to join!
