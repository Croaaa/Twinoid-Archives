Kayin
How to advise you all... errr...

First of all, I know how to draw with my own hands. Pencil or an image editor are different tools but quite the same if we go for basics. All of these come from experience, practice, observation.
Two years ago, I would have use photoshop only for doing textures or colouring, I didn't touch clanpages until I've gone into active clans. So prior at that two years I was a photoshop noob, that only do layers work.

So I got different methods, drawing and scanning, already existing image editing, drawing with the mouse too with the standard first brush.

Now an example of how it's being done. Simple is fair enough, you don't have to do complicate things to have a good results, we're not doing this to be shown on some Art gallery.


Drawing, Scanning, Colouring:

Normally I'm scanning in 200dpi, but as DinoRPG is limiting the weight, I've reduced my own pics to 72dpi.
The scan, the lines, the base colours, the shadows & lights and the layer effects

Image editing & Animation:

I tried to show you how a cloaked kabuki is done, but I got no files with the process, the layers are all merged. And it would be too much hassle to only do a new one for an example. Truth to tell, I never have drawn the cloak or the hood, it's already an existing picture that I've resized, edited and coloured a bit to fit any kabuki.

So... for animation, here's Over2's avatar that he requested.
First you need the HQ picture of the dinoz, to let look alike a demon, you only need to redden his eyes, nothing really difficult there. Though to do the juggling thing you need to cut out his hands and recolour back what it's needed to.
Original Dinoz, Eyes and Hands, Result.

For the juggling, it's merely layer working, there's no magic. Here's the details: 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5. It's only 21 layers. You got only to keep in mind how it will look like after, it's like you have already your animation done inside your head.


Mouse Drawing (& Animation 2):

Best example is Coat & Dynamite's banner. 99% made with the mouse, using the standard first brush, only the dynamite rope was a real rope picture. Well probably because I've began that banner at 10am and it was 10pm, I was a bit tired, so did a shortcut. I got approximatively more than 200 layers, I've done fast colouring, and also some layer effects.

Though for that banner I got a storyboard, but with the uploading limit, I decided to shorten it by 2/3rd from the storyboard.


If you want specific tips, I can give you some.
