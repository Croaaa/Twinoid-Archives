- One player states three things about himself, two of which are true and one of which is a lie.

The other players have to guess which is the lie.

The player who guesses correctly first takes the next turn.


For example ,
me - I'am a boy
me - I'm a Girl
me - I'm Noob

then :

other player1 - "I'm a boy"
me : nope

other player2 - "I'm noob"
me: nope

other player 3 - "I'm a Girl"
me: Yes! now its your turn
.
.
Other player3 :
- I'm rich
- I'm poor
- I'm teenager

.....and so on

I hope you will join!!!
Remember : One player at a time , wait for another player to comment before you again
Enjoy !
