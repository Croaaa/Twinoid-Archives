So What's EDB?
EDB is a tool made in VisualBasic to plan and save your dinoz's element builds, stats calculator, a quick peek to Skills Trees and hopefully much more in the near future :p
You can store up to 30 dinoz on a single save file.
The application is a single .exe file and there's no internet connection required.
You have to enter you Existing dinoz's information manually (tho i'm planning to add a copy/paste skills import to make the process faster and easier. Thx ZONY for the suggestion)
