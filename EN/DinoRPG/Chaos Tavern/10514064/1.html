The storyline of dino-rpg set a couple hundred years (I assume) after the period featured in the original game, dinoparc. In the gap between the two games, Jazz Island falls to the ground, which creates a tidal wave over dinoland. This is called The Great cataclysm. Jazz Island splits into the atlantean islands, where the only similarity is the sticky swamp in the middle. As for kabukis, they all died out in the great cataclysm, except for the few yakuzis ancestor rescued. If you have done the kabuki quest, you will know that yakuzis ancestor also created the totem on totem island so that no one else could find them without the kabuki charm. The original kabuki charm was passed though yakuzis family until we all managed to make our own. During the gap, the breeds of dino changed slighty, The wanwan returned to the wild, the pigmon turned into the softpig and a few others were wiped out. Thats the general storyline, although I did skip a few things. It would make a good book really.
