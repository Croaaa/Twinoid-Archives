Hi, guys! Before you read Thrust and Welceon's Christmas Special, I'd like to tell you a story of the 12 Days of Christmas...Thrust style.
Thrust doesn't know anything about Christmas and Azelle is trying to pursuade him to learn and believe in it:

THRUST: “Alright, alright! Since you’re so pushy about Christmas, I’ll listen.”
AZELLE: “Great! I promise — no, I cross my heart — that you’ll be convinced!”
THRUST (silently): “Good luck.”
Azelle turned to the door and shouted, “Okay, guys!”
Boom! A whole choir of korgons and one smashroom flooded in through the door! And those included Thrust’s three old pals.
AMIGO: “Yep, who couldn’t resist the sweet carols of the Korgon Carolers?”
AMIGOS: “Si, true.”
BOB: “Jingle bells!”
THRUST: “You brought the whole choir with you?”
AZELLE: “They’re called Christmas carolers, and they’re gonna sing you a carol.”
THRUST: “Please don’t…”
AZELLE (to the korgons): “Er…what’s with the smashroom?”
A KORGON: “Don’t know. We just picked him up.”
SMASHROOM: “Bah, humbug! ”
AZELLE: “And where’s the guitarist?”
A korgon was trailing right behind the carolers who were in the building. He was dragging a heavy guitar (because of his petite size) in its case and then finally made it, falling face-down to the ground when he stopped.
GUITARIST KORGON (exhausted): “Ready to go…”
LEAD VOCALIST: “Okay, everybody! A one, a two, a one, two, three…”

SING ALONG, EVERYBODY!
