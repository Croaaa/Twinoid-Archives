You're being pretty hard with "hordes", lodis!

Even if you doesn't pay to have a "hero account", you can still play as any other player, enjoy the game and make a legendary city with the other inhabitants of the city.

Nowadays, Hordes and DinoRPG are my most time-consumer games.

The concept of hordes is very simple (and very addictive I think!)

You begin a city with 39 other citizens.
All you have at first is a well with a randomly amount of water.

Each night, zombies attack your city so during the day, you have to go outside to search for equipments and ressources to build some defense structures in your city, hoping to survive the midnight madness!

Each day, the number of zombies increase. With some buildings, you can predict how many there will be to better prepare the city.

When you die, all yous deeds will be added to your "soul".
Some of them will allow you to use some special rankings (like "builder", "thief", etc...)


Your goal is to stay alive as long as possible in a city.

The longest cities ar known as "legendary city"
