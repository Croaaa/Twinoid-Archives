I'd just like to use this thread to address my pet hate lol. The misuse of the words; your, you're; and there, they're, and their. People are forever using the wrong ones so hopefully this will help clear it up a bit.

Your implies ownership. For example: "Your dinoz levelled up."

You're is a contraction of you are. "You're sitting on a chair."

There refers to a place or location. "Go sit over there."

They're is a contraction of they are. "They're going shopping later.

Their implies ownership. "I stole their money"

Ok now I've got that off my chest

Some time prespositions/expressions to know...

These really aren't important for communicating on DinoRPG... but they might be useful to know in other scenarios (and that's why I wanted to create this thread)

IN
in the morning
in the afternoon
in the evening

AT night

but: 'In the middle of the night"

IN cont...
In June, July, August (months of the year)
In Spring (seasons)

ON
On Monday (Tuesday... days of the week)

AT
At 12am, at 2 O'clock, AT a specific time! So, a wedding can be at 11am, a clan war at 14.00, and a funeral at 3.

There's more but i won't bother you right now...

At the weekend...

These time expressions are some of the hardest things to learn in English, and they really don't matter that much as far as real communication goes...
