Welcome to my journey with my Mini Kyuubi, Lust!


The morning light overwhelmed the land. The crisp morning air filled my lungs as I took a deep sigh of relief. The sun's warmth embraced my flesh seemingly pushing me onward in my journey. As i opened my eyes the light burst through my lashes devouring my sight. Quickly succumbing to it's persistence i pulled myself up from the moss covered tree i had made into a bed, and started to push onward through the thick forest.

As i moved through the thick wilderness i noticed that the leaves around me started to tremble. I shamefully started to panic. I pushed my back against a nearby boulder clinging to a nearby stick as i trembled at the thought of a ravenous pack of wolves. Oh why did i have to be so clumsy as to get lost in the woods on my way to Dinotown!

An unusually large Smashroom launched itself from the bushes and slammed into my chest. The air was immediately escaped my lungs as i tried to understand what was happening. I was slammed into the boulder in my back and i fell face forward into the rocky dirt. I tried gasping for air as i coughed. I looked up dizzily at my attacker, he stood arrogantly, as if victorious in a difficult battle. I tried lifting my arm to smack the smug mushroom but to no avail. The smashroom quickly jumped upon my hand trying to crush my grip on the stick.

How pathetic, I was going to die to a mushroom, how proud my parents would be!

As my vision started to fade in pain a large roar filled the air. It seems I would quickly become food for whatever it was headed this way.

The Smashroom on top of me stopped jumping. He stood motionless on my arm, then he started to tremble. Quickly he tried to dart into the bush trying to flee. But as it leaped into the air, a red blur flung from the wilderness and slammed into it mid air. The Smashroom launched into a tree and fell to the dirt heavily. I looked up to see the new threat. There stood what looked at first to be some sort of cat. She stood proudly, her eyes flowed red and gold, as if they were made of flame. Her fur was a brilliant dark red, with flame kissed orange fur covered her leggs. Three tails flowed gracefully around her, her mere visage seemed as if it made everything in the forest awestruck.

My stare was quickly broken as another smashroom slammed into her back from atop a nearby tree. It seems the smashroom brought a friend. The cat quickly got up and lashed against the smashroom. It was obvious how overwhelmed the smashroom was, it tried to flee but the cat didn't give it the slightest chance to escape her grasp. The cat would soon be victorious.

I had no time to waste, my strength had partially came back to me. Now was my time to flee while the cat was occupied with the mushrooms, or else i would surely be her next meal. As i pushed myself up i saw the mushroom that was slammed into the tree quickly rushing towards the cat, no doubt to save his friend. The cat was completely unaware for this new threat, totally occupied with the smashroom under her claws.

Without thinking I clenched the stick and rushed towards the cat, and as the smashroom leaped into the fray, I stoped and swung as hard as i could into the smashroom. He flew back through the air slamming against rocks, I was victorious.

I just only hope it hurt as much as possible.

A large growl came from my back. My heart sank. I had not realized i was now a foot away from an even greater danger. I slowly turned, the smashroom was turned into ribbons on the ground. There stood the red cat leering at me with her eyes of flame. I must have forgotten to breathe as i gasped for air and fell backward. The cat slowly walked towards me almost walking on top of me. It then stoped, turned around, moved towards where she had pounced in. She looked back, let out a small growl and nodded her head forward.

Did she want me to follow?

I stood up and walked towards the cat. which now upon better examination looked much like a wanwan, but there was undoubtably something very unique about this wanwan. As i came closer the wanwan started to walk forward, leading me through the woods.

This may just be the start of a rather interesting new journey.

Day II

After a rather interesting hike, the red Wanwan has seemed to have led me through this god forsaken forest. Finally I can see the rolling grass hills in front of me. With radiant wild flowers embracing each step I took, I made a rather abrupt shout of victory. This must have startled the Wanwan as she turned around and roared at me, I wonder if she thought we were under attack. As I stood a bit embarrassed with my arms stretched about, the Wanwan once again leered at me with her head tilted to one side, as if to say, "What are you doing?!". Overwhelmed with embarrassment I put my arms down and kept my face to the ground.

As I couldn't tell if I was supposed to move or not i brought my head up slightly to see if the Wanwan had moved forward. But she had disappeared! In shock I looked about, and then a slight rumbling came from behind me. She was now sitting behind me. I stood curiously at her.

Was she no longer going to lead me? Was I going to lose my likely much needed protection?

These thoughts were dashed as the Wanwan charged my heels and started to nibble and claw at them. I jumped forward, the Wanwan following. I started to run through the hills, with the fiery Wanwan literally at my heels.

I am not sure how long I could run before the Wanwan is victorious. But this definitely seems like the begining of a rather interesting journey, though likely filled with alot more pain that i had previously assumed...
