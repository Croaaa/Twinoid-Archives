Well here we got these tools R E T. By drawing 4 letters and 2 consonants and 3 vowels, you have to compose a word.

One word cannot be given more than once, if more than one people got the exact same number of letter but a different word, they do a Scissors party to determine the winner.

The one who get the longest possible word roll a 4 and get that many points.

Once you get points you can decide to bet some of them and double them with Tails. Triple if you guess the symbol of a 8 of Clubs, quadruple if you guess the number of King of Diamonds. At a maximum of 5 points.

If you reach 100 points, you will be titled, God of Words. You cannot win by betting (even if you maxxed out).
