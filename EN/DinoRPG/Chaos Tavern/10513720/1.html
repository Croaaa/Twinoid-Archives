So, uhmm, this is a pun thread, it's different from a joke:

joke:
joke [jōk]
noun (plural jokes)
1. funny story: a story, anecdote, or wordplay that is intended to amuse
2. cause of amusement: anything said or done to make people laugh
dressed up the dog in a hat and sunglasses as a joke

3. something inadequate: somebody or something that is laughably inadequate or absurd (slang)
The surroundings were pleasant enough but the food was a joke.


Pun
pun [pun]
noun (plural puns)
play on words: a humorous use of words that involves a word or phrase that has more than one possible meaning


intransitive verb (past and past participle punned, present participle pun·ning, 3rd person present singular puns)
use play on words: to make a pun or use puns
