Had this idea for a while and saw in the suggestions topic a while ago that people were interested in this as well, so imma kick it off:

Let's do some gambling on our dino's!

The trick is simple, someone posts a screenshot of the beginning of a dojo-battle (the 3vs3 that's in everyone's account) and people can bet on which side will win. Then after a day the result will be posted, either through another screenshot of the end of the battle, or a video of the battle.

Every new player will get 10 points. For every right bet you will get +1 point, for every wrong bet you will get -2. Once you reach 0 points you can get yourself back into the game by posting a battle yourself, which will get you back to 5 points. (This only applies to people with 0 or less points, so you don't get +5 points for posting a battle)
Off course you're always free to post a battle when you want to.

Some rules on the battles:
- Only 1 battle may be posted every day, the result will be posted ~24 hours later in the form of another screenshot or video
- The battles have to be from people's dojo's, the one that's inside their account ("Go to Dojo")
- Besides the screenshot, you also have to post who's account the dino's are from and what levels they are
- If you want to post a battle you may reserve a place on the list (if any). Other people may not post a battle before you then, unless they're higher on the list
- Obviously you're not allowed to place a bet on your own posted battle
- Battles may be taken from anyone's account, it doesn't have to be your own account
- Important: The battle you post must not be predictable! This means only 1 dino will survive the battle, and battles will almost always come down to a 1on1 fight
- The outcome of the battle may not be shared to anyone before the final result one day later, also don't upload it yet before that time
- When giving the results you will also give an update of the participant's scores (make sure to not make any mistakes with that)

About the bets:
- Once you made your bet you're not allowed to change it anymore
- "Research" in the form of looking into people's dojo's to check the dino's skills is allowed

*Rules may be slightly changed or added if needed*
