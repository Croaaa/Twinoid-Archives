Suddenly, she heard a loud scream as a bolt of lightning flashed by. Feeling something was amiss, she went to her parent's room and found that both of them were dead.

Like a scythe, the Eeloz's claws tried to grab Evarinya, but she was quicker than it. She gave a hard kick at the Eeloz, stunning the creature and leaving it unconscious. She then went to her brother's room, carrying him with her mouth, and jumped of the window.

The Eeloz cursed, upon discovering that it's last two victims escaped. Searing an oath upon his honor that he would return to kill his survivors, or die trying.

________________________________________ ______________________


After twenty years, Istalri was an aspiring battle master of his clan. Strategic and cunning, he won several wars for his clan, leading an army of 100 against thousands.

One fateful day, a siege on the enemy's castle left Istalri mortally wounded. His army was reduced to nothing. He wished that there was something he could've done to save his army. Suddenly, an orb of light touched Istalri and turned the color of his skin from dark grey to blue, and his eyes from fearsome amber to heroic emerald. A flash emanated from him as his transformation came to an end.

It was a magnificent battle, Istalri's cunning was a big advantage. Blow after blow, skill after skill, and combo after combo the battle finally came to an end, leaving the Eeloz defeated.
