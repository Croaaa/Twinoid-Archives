Chapter One: Beginnings


The sun set at the horizon at Frish Port as the gentle waves sprayed ocean water at the shore. The peaceful, serene landscape was enjoyed by everyone, including Fangstriker and his sirain, Fricaya, who were playfully basking in the sand, chasing each other. But the enjoyment would be broken by a call.

"Fang, we need you and Fricaya at our house quickly." said a raspy, old voice. It was Grandpa Joe.

"But Grampa, we're still enjoying the day off"

"No buts, and besides the day is over. it's officially night time."

"I hate it when your right "

"Whatever, just come here pronto."

Fang and Fricaya sprinted towards the direction of Grandpa Joe's house, passing street after street of the peaceful Dinotown. They sprinted past Michael, who was guiding a little kid who got lost. They passed the University, the very center of education in Dinoland. Soon, they reached Grampa's House.

"So what's the dilly, Gramps?" said Fang.

"Cut with the slang and listen, boy. Tomorrow marks the day of your Coming of Age. And as customs state, the Coming of Age ceremony is the day a Master will be under the orders of King Godwin XIII. You and Fricaya must be present in time for the ceremony, which is at 7:00 sharp."

"So will I be the only one who will be marked?"

"No, there will be Roan and Ellis, your childhood friends."

"And their Dinoz?"

"Blackshot the Wanwan and Bliss the Cloudoz, respectively."

"Ok Gramps, got everything clear. Wake up, get dressed before 7:00."

"Wait........"

But Fangstriker already left.

___________________________________



The next morning, Fang woke up late.

"Oh crud! Gramps will scold me for this."

"I told you to sleep early."

"To be honest Fricaya, sometimes, your as annoying as gramps. "

"Whatever, just hurry up and get dressed."

They ran all the way to Dino Plaza, where the ceremony is being held. They arrived just in time as King Godwin XIII announced Fang's name.

'Grampa will scold me for almost being late.' thought Fangstriker.

"Fangstriker, and his partner, Fricaya!." a megaphone announced.


They marched down the aisle, up to the stage and had been given a vest that bear the Dinoland Seal, which symbolizes that the Master is from Dinoland and is loyal to it, and the Dinopedia, a Master's best friend.

After a lengthy speech by the King, Fangstriker, Roan and Ellis was summoned to the king's throne room to be assigned their first mission. Surveillance at the Precipitous Hills, where a group of smashrooms and wolves have been seen terrorizing a small village.

The night has fallen and the trio prepare for their mission. It was quite easy, yet dangerous if they were discovered by the pack of smashrooms and wolves.

Chapter Two: The Veterans

The dawn's early light shone upon the waters of the Fountain of Youth, the meeting place of the trio. The first one there was Ellis, the only female in the group, followed by Fang and Roan, who reached the fountain at the same time. They began to hike through the forest, and soon reached the hills.

"Guys, up at there," whispered Ellis, pointing to a tree, "That's a good place to observe the pack."

"Aye, let's start climbing." Roan whispered back.

The tree was quite high. It was around 20 feet in height and covered in thick leaves. Perfect for hiding in a surveillance mission.

During the climb, the group and their dinoz were halfway up the tree until the branch Roan lost his grip and strated to fall. Fortunately, Blackshot, Roan's wanwan, used the vine skill and caught his master. Unfortunately, the pack was alerted of our presence.

The "small" pack was not so small after all. It consists of 10 smashrooms and 5 wolves. Quite an overwhelming number for 3 Masters and their dinoz.

The pack was closing in on the tree, but Fang had an idea.

"Ellis, command Bliss to Mistral the ground."

"Great idea! A smokescreen would make a good diversion."

Ellis jumped on top of Bliss' back and started to descend, while shooting a Mistral straight at the ground. The Mistral's strength was enough to cover the entire clearing in the forest where the pack was camping on.As soon as the dust cleared, Fang and Roan made their move.

"Fricaya, Cold Shower now!" commanded Fang.

"Follow up with Lightning, Blackshot"

And as science states, Water and Lightning do not mix well.But, the pack was still standing despite that combo attack and the team was in trouble. They fought and they fought, but still the pack remains standing and began charging at the trio.

"Bliss, Bubble quickly!" screamed Ellis, completely terrified.

The bubble shielded them, until Bliss couldn't handle it anymore and collapsed. The team was now in trouble. Until.......

"Hurricane, Toxic Cloud!" said a strong, masculine voice.

"Grimoire, Leafy Dawn!" said another voice, much younger than the first.

Bliss started to regain consciousness, and Fricaya and Blackshot's fatigue started to wear off.

"Bliss, Bubble, shield us from the cloud."

As soon as the bubble completed, Hurricane's master told them to hide behind the tree.

Fang, Roan and Ellis watched as Hurricane and Grimoire took care of the pack until they emerged victorious.

"Kids, you can come out now." said the older voice.

"Who are you?" inquired Roan.

"We're Masters. I'm Dash, and he's my partner Norman." said the younger one, "we were patrolling, till Hurricane, Norman's kabuki, sensed danger."

"Oh, I see." said Fang,"We're sorry we caused you trouble, but we're novices assigned a surveillance mission, but went wrong when we were discovered."

"It's all right lad, it's your first mission, no problem." said Norman. "Anyway, it's getting a little late. Dash let's set camp here. Kids, you should join us. It's dangerous to be at the forest at night. Especially since the Smashrooms and Wolves populations have increased quite alot. We should rest."

At that time, somewhere at Mt. All-hot, something more sinister was being planned.....

"Mary Anne?" asked Fang.

"That be the name of me boat. She had seen better days, but still, she be shipshape to take ye to yer destinations."

"So where's your crew?" inquire Roan.

"I be me own crew laddies. Ye kin help me if ya want."

"I'll take the sails.", volunteered Fang.

Fang untied the rope once he got up the mast. The sail unfurled and immediately caught the wind.

Nothing has gone wrong into the first two hours of their journey, until suddenly, a massive creature, twice the size of the whole boat, suddenly surfaced. It was a Scorpscuba!

"Thar she blows! Tis be the Scorpscuba that had been spotted a week ago."

"Uh, Cap'n, I hate to set a panic, but I think it's headed for us." said Fang. But it was too late. The Scorpscuba crashed itself in to the port side of the ship.

"Ye blasted creature! Look what ye did to me boat! My beautiful Mary Anne!"

"There's no time to feel sad, Cap'n." Roan said, then suddenly shifted to Blackshot, "Lightning, now."

Blackshot concentrated, his fur gave of sparks. Suddenly, he sent a bolt of lightning into the sky, then it dropped to the oblivious Scorpscuba, who literally got the shock of his life.

The Scorpscuba dove into the water, once again and never returned.

"Everybody, hang on to Fricaya!" Fang shouted, "Swim us outta here Fricaya. Now!"

"As you wish." replied Fricaya.
