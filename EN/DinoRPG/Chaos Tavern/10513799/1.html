Since it's been pretty populair in the past, I reincarnated this game, offically made by RJ ^^

RULES:

- Anyone can play.

- All King of Chaos Tavern nominees will start with 10 points. You vote by substrating 1 point each from 2 nominees and adding 1 point to 1 nominee. If the point reach 0, the nominee is eliminated. The last nominee will be crowned as the King of Chaos Tavern.

- You can only vote 1 in every 24 hours and you can't vote yourself if you are a nominee.
If you break this rule, you will be banned on voting for 24 hour and the list will corrected accordingly.

- if the list comes down to 2, it will be just subtract 1 and add 1. if the choice is yourself as nominee, you simply do nothing.

- MONDAY Massacre.
On Monday, the point for subtraction is 2 (two) instead of 1 (one). The addition is still the same 1 (one) point.
If the nominee only has 1 point left on Monday, he/she can't be eliminated (no 2 points substration to minus point). It has to wait till Tuesday.

- The date and time is DinoRPG's date and time. Check the time just below the Game Help. Remember, you can only vote once in every 24 hours. If it is not so clear, I repeat you can NOT post/vote again until at least 24 hrs.
