Bengeldorn
You can see it by taking a look in the source code.

If you are on a page of one master, his/her dinoz are listed in the block of:
<ul class="tid_dinList">

Each dino has on this entry
<div id="tid_swf_dino_XX" class="tid_swf">

The XX is the ID of that dinoz.

Therefore I can see that your Craby is has the ID 805550, making it the 805550th dinoz being created on this server.

This link for example will lead you, and only you to the page your dino.
http://en.dinorpg.com/dino/805550
