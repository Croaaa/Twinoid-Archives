The first number is the Bomb countdown. Each new entry is counting down by ONE.
Rules:
- Only 1 post at a time per player.
- The only exception is if you detonate the bomb, you can then post again to depict how it explodes (words, smilies, ect...) In the SAME POST you then pick a number to start from again in any method you desire.
- The new bomb cannot be higher than 999.

Example:
Oh look I got the bomb! Look out

Lets start again with 54 + 15 * 3

There are 2 ways to detonate the bomb:
1-If the bomb sequence reaches ZERO, the bomb detonates.
2-The dice are trying to match the bomb sequence. If they do, you have detonated the bomb.
The dice can be any number, or Vowels.
The vowel O=0, I=1. Vowels are optional, not required.

Additional (OPTIONAL) game play:
- If your dice are matching 1 1 1, 2 2 2, 1 11, 99 9 in any combination including the vowels, you win a complimentary drink.
- Another player must grant you this drink, you cannot just post to take it yourself.

The card game is optional as well and has nothing to do with the bomb.
Rules:
- 5 cards only.
If you get any of the following hands, you win the snack/meal listed.
- Another player must offer you your prize in the next post.
Prizes:
- 1 pair Jacks or better scores a snack of choice (like donut, apple etc.)
- 3 of a kind includes a slice of Pizza.
- Straight includes additional breadsticks.
- Flush extra cheese and meat for the pizza.
- Full House half the pizza with all trimmings.
- 4 of a kind the whole pizza with all trimmings.
- Any straight flush including Royal gives 2 pitchers of beer and the whole pizza with all trimmings.

Heads or Tails can be used only when giving another player prizes for matching dice or the card game. You can only give small extras like making the drink something special, or add a topping to a treat.
Please do not give the entire pizza if the player did not earn it.
Example:
Heads = Alcohol for your drink. OR Tails = Cheeze on your pizza/jelly filled for your donut ect...
