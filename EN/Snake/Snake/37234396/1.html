Anglophone

Fruits contain vitamins. When you eat them, they will increase your fru(i)tibar, visible at the top of your screen. As you increase your frutibar, better fruit will appear. The frutibar will decrease when a fruit disappears and you have no shields remaining.

Increasing and protecting your frutibar should be your #1 concern when you're playing, in most cardsets.

Anglophone

I've already picked up rainbow (sold one, in fact), but I still use the 300k mark as my goal.


Rainbow allows you to enter the Rainbow Ranking, which is a daily competition. The five highest scores obtained with the rainbow card will win a ticket into the day's lottery draw. It sounds like a really great card, but the top five spaces are always occupied by hackers and cheaters. I use it in my set because it minimizes the risk of having a game ruined by a poorly timed card item appearing. Engrais (after 3 minutes), Eggshell and Rainbow are all safe to be flipped by the card item, giving me a 50% chance of salvaging the game.
