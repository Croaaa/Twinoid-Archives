I don't think the problem is with the controls.....

Basically if a card can be activated on demand a letter will appear in the bottom right corner of said card. To use that cards action you press the corresponding key.

For example say you are using scissors as your first card and you decide to activate it and the letter in the bottom right corner is "a" you press "a".

Some cards the action is repeatable, some are one and done. If you don't see a letter over where the card rarity usually is then it activates automatically.

If you pay attention to what is happening it should be common sense from that point on.
