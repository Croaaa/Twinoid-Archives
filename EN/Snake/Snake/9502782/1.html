Are you READY?

I am hosting another Snake Tournament of the forum! This time I hope to have more participants. I was also able to think of semi-interesting prizes.

To see the previous tournament, click here. (Norby89 was the winner.)


How to register:

Post in this thread that you will participate. Specify which cards you will play with on all of the days.
Wait until November 26, 2011. This is when registration ends.
You will receive a private message from me reminding you of the rules and telling you when to play and where to put the link.





How this works
Once the tournament starts, you play the game with the cards you chose upon registration. Leave your link of the game in the thread that will be created for this. (An additional thread will be created to host the links.)

-If the game you submit's cards do not match the ones that you indicated upon registration, your game will not be counted.
-You MUST provide a link to your game in the thread or your game cannot be viewed. Make sure to check that your link works and summons the game you submitted. Because the game needs to be viewable, French and Spanish players may not participate in the tournament. (Their snake.es.muxxu and snake.muxxu.com links cannot be viewed by the English players.)
-Of your daily Snake plays, only one game, the one which involves playing with the cards you choose upon registration is the one that is for the tournament.
-If you fail to post your fight, you will be disqualified.


Ranking

Each day, the lowest ranked player will be eliminated. The last player left standing wins the tournament.

Awards

For the first place winner:

The choice of 1 rare kube, 2 uncommon kubes, or 5 common kubes
And 1 guest a day for 7 days in MonsterMotel



Dates
Registration ends November 16. The tournament starts on December 3rd and ends depending on how many people register.

And now?
I declare the registration OPEN! Feel free to post any questions you have here. To register simply post the cards you will use.
