Hoi Machteld!

If you ever played the classic snake game, you'll know how to play Snake. Just eat the fruits by avoiding hitting the walls and yourself. There are hundreds of fruits and they're all stored in an encyclopedia called the Encyclopefruit where you can see everything you've eaten so far.

The thing that probably confuses you are the cards. Every card adds a bonus or an effect to your snake and you need them in order to play a round. Under each card there's a number called a Mojo. To play a round you need to choose cards that add up to 6 Mojos. It's up to you which combination of cards you choose, for example you can pick 2 cards worth 3 Mojos (3+3) or even 6 cards worth 1 Mojo (1+1+1+1+1+1) as long as they add up to 6. After the round is finished they are removed from your deck and you can choose other ones until you run out of cards.

You can get cards by completing quests, buying them in the shop or by participating in tournaments (which requires Muxxu Tokens). If anyone knows of other ways to acquire cards, let us know please.

And that's about it, you have a special card that lets you train for free anytime. You should try that before you use any of your other cards, you can also choose the controls there (Mouse, Keyboard A or Keyboard B), I prefer Keyboard B however it's up to you which do you like best.
