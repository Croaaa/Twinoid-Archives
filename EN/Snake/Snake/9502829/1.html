All cards can be used more than once a day as long as you maintain 6 mojo per game. Some cards, like Salt, have a lightning icon at the corner to indicate that they can be used more than once on the same game.

If you don't want doubles, once you have 20+ cards, you have a chance to sell a double for . However, the buyer (a computer) determines what card he wants to buy, so you can't force him to take a card from you until he offers something for that card. Also note, he usually offers you a price far below what it takes to buy a card from the Shop.
