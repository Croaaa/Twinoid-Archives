I'm on vacation and as I don't have nothing else to do now, I decide to post some errors or observations on cards descriptions:

Card descriptions:

- Speaker: Fruits are worth double during the first 30 sec of the game. <-- Wrong information (90 sec).
- Crystal: Shows you where the fruits will appear next <-- Dot missing.
- Bonuses: Makes 3 fruity bonuses appear. Reduces the maximum number of cards to 3.. <-- 2 dots.
- Yellow Potion: The snake automatically avoids a collision with a wall. <-- It not avoid the collision. If the snake collide to a wall, it will start again from where it collided.
- Horn: Cause your tail to shrink. <-- This description is all wrong. The snake moves towards the borders of the game board, so you can redo your route.
- Battery: Your snake will slow down when approaching the edge of the gameboard. <-- Missing a space (game board).
- Ball: Each fruit the ball hits will be swallowed, but your snake won't grow. <-- Missing information (When used: Launches a ball.).
- Sword: When used: Reduces the size of your snake and frutibar by 50%. <-- Misspelling (fruitibar).
- R.Vitamin: Every time you touch a rune, you frutibar increases by 3%. <-- Misspelling (your fruitibar).
- Plunger: Your snake will swallow two fruits at a time. <-- This card don't do this. It just drag the fruits to make them closer to each other.
- Bells: There are always at least 2 fruits on the gameboard. <-- Missing a space (game board).
- Herbicide: Bad fruits will disappear quickly and won't harm your snake. <-- It will be better if it says Fruits with negative values.
- Barrow: Increases the size of the frutibar by 20%. <-- Misspelling (fruitibar).
- Thorn: When you collect a flower, your tail will shrink by 50cm. Upon use: Creates a flower. <-- Lack of standardization (other cards use the expression When used).
- Timer: If the game lasts less than 90 sec you will win 100,000 pts. <-- Wrong information. If the game lasts less than 90 sec your score is doubled.
- P.Potion: All the fruits on the gameboard will jump around. <-- Missing a space (game board).
- Pick Axe: Increases the size of the gameboard, but reduces your max card limit to 3. <-- Missing a space (game board).
- Spring: Allows your snake to bounce off walls <-- Dot missing.
- Kazoo: Creates a row of fruits in front of the snake <-- Dot missing.
- Bomb: When used: Places a bomb on the gameboard which destroys everything. <-- Missing a space (game board).
- Fruitwin: Doubles the number of fruits on the gameboard. <-- This card actually duplicates the fruits on the game board.
- Noah: Similar fruits are attracted 2 by 2 and form a superior fruit (12 levels). Upon use: Cuts 50cm off your tail. <-- Lack of standardization (other cards use the expression When used).
- Dump: 60% of the fruits' calories will be dumped on the gameboard after being eaten. <-- Missing a space (game board).
- Dolphin: Blue fruits will not increase the size of your snake. A buoy will appear once used. <-- Lack of standardization (When used: Creates a buoy).
- Squirrel: Nuts are worth 5x more. <-- Actually its 10x more. And is missing the information of what happens when you use this card (I don't know what is...).
- Soup: All fruits disappear. You earn 1% fruitipower per fruit. <-- I never see the word fruitipower anywhere else in this game. Maybe its best to write: Your fruitibar is increased by 1% per fruit.
- Funnel: If 12 fruits are on the screen, they will gravitate towards your snake <-- Dot missing. And is missing the information that all these fruits will be eaten at the same time (creating a giant belly ).
- Globe: The speed of the timer is modified by turning your snake <-- Dot missing.
- M. Glass: Each fruit swallowed is worth double. <-- This description may cause a wrong interpretation. A better description may be: Each fruit swallowed is counted twice in the Encyclopefruit.
- Sponge: When used: Destroys all fruits on the gameboard. <-- Missing a space (game board).
- Lighter: You will not lose any points when leafy fruits disappear. Dynamite and matches do twice as much damage <-- Dot missing.
- Watercan: Increases the frutibar by 8%. <-- Misspelling (fruitibar).
- Cheese: Win 5,000 pts at the end of the game. <-- You actually win 5,000 pts when you die. I'll explain: if you have the Charm card, you'll win 5,000 pts the first time you die, not in the end of the game (and the Cheese card can be flipped during the second life play with the flip card bonus).
- Broom: Blood on the gameboard is cleaned up as the game progresses. <-- Missing a space (game board).
- Shroom: When a fruit disappears, it's value will be deducted from your score. <-- Wrong use of apostrophe (its).
- Bucket: Dynamite and matches will disappear more quickly <-- Dot missing.
- Fog: A thick fog covers the playing area. <-- Lack of standardization. It's game board or playing area?
- Pin: The frutibar will decrease automatically. <-- Misspelling (fruitibar).
