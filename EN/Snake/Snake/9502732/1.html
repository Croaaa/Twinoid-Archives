Belt: Your snake doesn't digests two fruits at a time. This means that if you are digesting one fruit, others fruits eaten doesn't grow your snake. Good to use with Magnet or Lonestar.

Wool: All unsweetened fruits will reduce the size of your snake by 10cm. All the fruits without the keyword sweet shrink your snake. But after the reduction, the fruit is digested and you grow a little bit again. Used together with cards that prevents your snake to grow, will keep your snake small. Good to use with Belt, Leaf, Worm or Syringe.

AnglophoneIt seems that the only way to accumulate them is through placing in the top 5 of this rainbow tournament. How do I get the card to enter this tournament?

Look under Quests. However, even if you do get the Rainbow card, it's very difficult to get in the top 5 using only the cards you get for free. Even if by some miracle you do get into the top 5, the chances of you winning a card by the lottery is very small.


AnglophoneAlso: I notice some fruits deduct points from your score. Should I just learn what they are and avoid them, or should I grab them to "unblock" them?

It depends. If you want to collect fruits and be fully rewarded, you need to get 500 of each type of fruit. Once you do that, or if you don't care to do that, then you just will have to learn which ones are worth negative and avoid them. Be careful though, avoiding too many fruits too soon may cause your frutibar to shrink, meaning you will have to work extra to getting rarer and higher-valued fruit.
