norby89
For those who have that card it's certainly an easy way. For the rest of us I can show you the way I did it and try to give you a few useful tips. You can watch my replay here.

Feather + Chainsaw seems the perfect combo for this quest, Chainsaw helps you keep your snake small as you maneuver through unwanted fruits and Feather slows your snake down giving you more time waiting for that right fruit.

get your score up to ~27k-29k
stop taking any fruit until your bar goes down completely
try to eat fruits like (7 pts) until your score is divisible by 10, I even made a list of such fruits for myself
after that it's just a matter of getting your score up to 30k by eating the right fruits, check what each fruit is worth before eating (Twinpedia), when you're very close to your goal eat only (10 pts) or other fruits worth 20-30 points

The last one is probably the most important step and where I failed most since I always rushed it ending up over 30k. Big thanks goes out to npfan for the tips, I hope it helps.
