Hello, Muxxiens!

Overview
In a response to this project of the Spanish forum, I have decided to let us have a tournament for Snake. I can't wait!

Categories
In order to separate the good players from the amateurs, there will be two categories of the tournament:

Beginner:You may only use the cards you come with, and the cards you can win in quests. You may not use cards that you bought, so you may only use the Magnet, Chainsaw, Feather, B Potion, Crystal, Windmill, Salt, Scissors, Hourglass, Tongue, Speaker, Rainbow, or the Kazoo.

Professional: You may use any card you wish to use.

Each player must choose one category.

How to partcipate
Once the tournament starts, you play the game with the cards you chose upon registration. Leave your link of the game in the thread that will be created for this.

-If the game you submit's cards do not match the ones that you indicated upon registration, your game will not be counted.
-You MUST provide a link to your game in the application, or your game cannot be viewed.
-Of your daily Snake plays, only one game, the one which involves playing with the cards you choose upon registration is counted for the tournament.

Ranking
Each day, the lowest ranking player will be eliminated. The last left standing wins the tournament.

Awards
No awards will be offered in this tournament. In the 2nd Snake Tournament, an MTPass willbe awarded to the 1st place winner, however.

Dates
Registration ends August 8th. Tournament starts August 9th and lasts depending on how many people register. This will be determined on August 8th.

And now?
I declare the registration OPEN! Feel freeto post any questions you have here. To register simply post the category you will register in and the cards you will use.

Lann
