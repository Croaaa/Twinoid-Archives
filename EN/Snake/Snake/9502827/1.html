You get a ticket, and enter it in the lottery whenever you want.

Then, in that lottery, if your ticket is the randomly chosen ticket, you win the card of the day.

That's it. It's exactly the same as any other standard lottery, the more tickets the lower your chance. You don't get to keep tickets if you lose.
