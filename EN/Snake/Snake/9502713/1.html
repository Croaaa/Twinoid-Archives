There needs the be a way for free players to earn cards as well.
Not many cards, but having a method of a player earning a random card or two every so often would actually probably greatly increase the amount of coins spent on this game.
How do I figure?

Easy.
People can't get addicted to collecting cards if they aren't allowed to collect a few slowly to begin with. The preset cards are not the same as having the players able to earn a random free card every few days so they can go "Hey awesome I love getting free cards, if only it happened more often... wait it can!"

A few ways cards could be earned:
-A total point counter that when reached gives a random card and then resets. Maybe 150,000 points per card? That would take the average player around 3 days, possibly more.
-Random appearing power up. Not reccomended since it would need to be so crazy rare but it's an idea.
-A free version of the Tombola, everyone can enter it if they get enough points. More points (best game of the day) means a higher chance of winning.

Just to make my point clearer allow me to present an example: Any Free to Play MMORPG.
They all allow your character to progress for no charge, and most (good ones) even allow you to compete with paying characters. What you can buy are either vanity items or, and this is more relevant, EXP boosts. Meaning you can pay money to progress faster.
The main draw of many games is progress, Snake is very VERY close to reaching that draw, but picture a Free to Play MMORPG where you need to pay to gain levels. No one would play it, because no one would have a chance to form a goal or have a character they want to improve.

Even the infamous World of Warcraft recently went Free to Play up to level 20, giving people a chance to get addicted to progress.

I will guarantee you that allowing free players to slowly earn cards would cause an increase in Muxxu coins spent on Snake.
