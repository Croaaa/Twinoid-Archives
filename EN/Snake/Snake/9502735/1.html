I know I'll never get most of the cards that exist here, since I'll likely never buy tokens, but I'm still curious as to what the cards are and what they do.
So I'd like it if people posted what the cards they have are called and what they do. No doubles, please.

Magnet: Your snake will automatically eat all fruits nearby.
Chainsaw: Reduces the size of your snake by between 60 and 120cm. (Has a rather long cooldown.)
Feather: Permanently reduces the speed of your snake by 30%.
Tongue:Use your snake's tongue to collect fruit. Fruits collected with this card are worth double. (Has a short cooldown.)
B potion: Your snake will not die if it bumps into itself for 10 seconds.
Crystal: Shows you where the fruits will appear next.
Windmill: Slightly increases your chance of bonuses appearing.
Salt: Increase the vale of all fruits collected by 100 points.
Scissors: Reduses the size of your snake by 10%. (One time use in game.)
Hourglass: All fruits will stay in the game longer.
