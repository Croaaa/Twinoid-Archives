I have.

Here's my method:
Don't worry about the score until you get over 29000. Afterwards, don't pick up any fruit except for (wolfberries - 10 points) and (chokeberries - 7 points). Pick up chokeberries until your score ends with a 0. Then, pick up enough wolfberries until you hit 30000. Afterwards, die. You can better control your score if you have the Scanner card, but that is something you have to be lucky enough to have purchased.
Hope that helps.
