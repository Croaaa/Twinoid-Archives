First, you should know that some monsters can take away more than 1 heart at a time. For example, the chicken-like takes away 2 instead of 1.


The first game is simply, as you say, to collect all the yellow blobs. The number above your ship tells you the percentage (i.e. you need to get to 100). The second game requires you to sneak away to a manhole. Movement is done by moving your mouse.
