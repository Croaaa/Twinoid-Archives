The objective is to make the frog jump off the cliff, using the fly as a bait. However, you lose if the frog manages to actually catch the fly, so what I recommend on doing is to "wiggle" the pole so that the fly is sort of rotating counter-clockwise. Then start to slowly close the distance between frog and fly. Eventually, the frog will jump and - if you are lucky and keep wiggling - miss the fly.
Alternatively, I guess you could try clicking when the frog jumps. Apparently that pulls back the fishing pole or something...? I don't know. The description you posted mentions clicking, but I have yet to try this out personally. So far I always used the wiggling strategy... with not a lot of success, honestly. But sometimes it works.
