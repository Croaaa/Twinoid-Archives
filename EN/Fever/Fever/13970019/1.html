Move mouse (I usually move it in circles) to move forward.
Stop moving mouse to stop and hide (spot lights can touch you in this form).

Goal is to avoid being spotted by the spot lights and make it to the right side where a sewer awaits.

Fairly difficult game in higher heat ratings... Almost impossible to dodge the lights and get to the end. Persevere it is possible.
