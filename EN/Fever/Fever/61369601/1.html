Thx Emvy!

Most useful items are as follows:
Notebook - you can ban one of the most unloved or difficult game for you. Very useful together with glasses
Glasses - You can see list of games at nearest monster.
Ice-cream - Gives an extra cube per day
Umbrella - Gives an extra rainbow per day
Dice - Takes 1 rainbow for a chance to get 1 ice cube. Useful when you need to kill monster but out of cubes and rainbows remained.
Prism - Turning ice cube into three rainbows. Useful when you want to travel a lot of islands.
Fork - 10% chance to break 2 hearts of monster in one attack.
And of cause mirror -
Emvyprevents petrification
