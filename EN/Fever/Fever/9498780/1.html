There is a game where meteors are coming down and you can shoot missiles or bullets up at them... but how do you actually stop the meteors? I've had my bullets pass right through them, sometimes they explode if I've fired four at once, but then I have no shots left to hit the other meteors.

When you click on the screen, you fire a missile that will explode at the point you clicked on, destroying meteors within a certain radius.

You have a limited supply of ammo, so you want to maintain at least 1:1 destroyed-meteor-to-missile ratio.
