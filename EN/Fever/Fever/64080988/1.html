This is one of the more difficult games. Your goal is to move the cursor left and right such that you avoid contact between the red and green balls. You just have to keep in mind that when you move the mouse, one set of balls moves in the mouse's direction and the other set moves in the opposite direction. Given that, you can prepare to some extent which way to move and how much to move and then act at the right time.
