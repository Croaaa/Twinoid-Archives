Translated from the Official French Muxxu Help website thanks to Reverso (and mild proof-reading):

On Tubulo:

You have to change all the tubes to green. Click anywhere on the board to turn a cross formation of tubes into their next colour. From orange, to blue, to green before the order repeats itself. Higher difficulties involve a bigger board (You'll get a 5*5 soon enough) and less starting green tubes.
