1st The Hammer game- this game starts off with this hammer on top of this piece of nail. To win the game, you click on the mouse and smash the nail down. After the nail reaches the bottom, you've won the game!!!!!
2nd The Spacebird game- this game starts off with this planet with space stations on it. There should be this flying chicken circling around the planet. To win this game, you click a space station which releases a rocket, at the same time the flying chicken goes above the rocket. When, the rocket hits the flying chicken, you've won the game!!!!
3rd The 3 Leaves game- this game starts off with 3 leaves and this yellow animal. To win this game, you have to create a bridge whenever the yellow animal goes up or down the leaf. To create a bridge, you put the mouse in the empty space in the middle part of the leaf, and a bridge should appear. If the yellow animal does not die by the time the mini-game finishes, then you've won the game!!!!!
4th The Windmill game- this game starts off with this bug which is trying to drop safely on this lilypad. To win this game, make sure the bug is falling in the right direction so that it falls on the lily. If it doesn't fall in the right direction, then use this windmill to make it go in the right direction. When the bug falls safely on the lilypad, you've won the game!!!!!
5th The Rectangular Puzzle game- this game starts off with two differently coloured pieces with a rectangular in the background. To win the game, place the two pieces within the space of the rectangle, making sure the 2 pieces fit into each other. Once these two pieces fill the rectangle you've won the game!!!!
6th The Falling Apples game- this game starts off with a basket at the bottom of the screen with apples falling down. What you are trying to do is to collect as many apples as possible. Once the basket is full of apples, you've won the game!!!!
7th Photoshoot game- this game starts off with this pink animal in this random place on the screen. To win this game you have to pinpoint the location of the pink animal. To do this, when the horizontal lines move vertically make sure it is in front of the animal, and when the vertical lines move horizontally, make sure it is in front of the animal. If you have pinpointed the animal properly, a gun should be able to shoot it. Once its shot, you've won the game.
8th The Mixing cups game- this game starts off with 4 cups. On the screen, there is a cup which contains a green ball. Concentrate on that cup as the cups get moved around. When the cups stop moving, select the cup which contains the green ball. If you select it correctly, you've won the game!!!!!!!

Some remarks:
1st The Hammer game- this game starts off with this hammer on top of this piece of nail. To win the game, you click on the mouse and smash the nail down. After the nail reaches the bottom, you've won the game!!!!!
You don't have to click on the mouse. Just position your mouse near the top of the screen and move down as fast as possible.
5th The Rectangular Puzzle game- this game starts off with two differently coloured pieces with a rectangular in the background. To win the game, place the two pieces within the space of the rectangle, making sure the 2 pieces fit into each other. Once these two pieces fill the rectangle you've won the game!!!!
It's not always 2 pieces. Depending on the difficulty (which depends on which monster you face), you could have more pieces.
8th The Mixing cups game- this game starts off with 4 cups. On the screen, there is a cup which contains a green ball. Concentrate on that cup as the cups get moved around. When the cups stop moving, select the cup which contains the green ball. If you select it correctly, you've won the game!!!!!!!
Similar as the last remark. You could have more than 4 cups.

Doram
The asteroids game: You are a little triangle in the middle of the screen, and there are two asteroids floating around. Click to shoot and destroy the two asteroids to win.

The kung-fu game: You are a martial arts master in white, standing at the foot of some stairs. Click to punch, and time your punches so that you kill all of the black wearing baddies who approach from the left. Survive until the timer runs out to win.

The target and pencils game: You are a small target in a field filled with floating pencils. Every time a pencil touches you, it sticks. Get hit by 3 pencils and you lose. Survive until the timer runs out to win.

The 9 balls game: There are 9 balls in a square in the middle of the field. As your mouse moves over them, the balls light up. Crossing a lit ball turns it back off. Light all the balls to win.

If I remember more, I will add them.
