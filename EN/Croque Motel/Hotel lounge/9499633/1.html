Yes, you need neighbor guest who is doing humidity
This is what is the game about, solving puzzle with guests placing into rooms - to have them all what they need and not ruin it with something they dislike...
In case you won't get such guest you need, there are two options:
install an equipment providing thing you need (affects only that one room)
ask a friend for guest you want (you can specify also his likes/dislikes)
