I'm also having some issues with the way the time works. :P
There are 2 clocks, one on the right shows the current time and the one on the left shows the time of your next guest's arrival. To make them arrive sooner you need to place one or more employees at the welcome desk. For example your next guest arrives at 17:00 and the current time is 13:30, moving an employee to the desk will reduce the time to 14:00. Placing another one reduces the time even more let's say to 13:00 and since that time is in the past your guest will arrive instantly.

Furthermore each day at midnight and at 10:00 your guests pay their rent. Treat them well and they will earn you lots of .
