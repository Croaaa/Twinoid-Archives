If a level 5 hotel is already enough for bequeathing, there is certainly no need to level up any further. If you're also aiming for fame points, you might think about leveling up, since it's not too expensive at low levels.

But one important thing about bequeathing is that you simply cannot have enough money if you're starting a new hotel. In my opinion, keeping your hotel at the actual size and just making money for two or three weeks is definately worth it - although you may be forced to pay the taxes a couple of times. I did this with my 2-star hotel and banked up more than 50000 during the last five weeks. I want to end the collecting period and bequeath the next sunday, because the next tax bill (level 11) will be 10200.

I don't think collecting THAT much is the fastest way to get a lot of stars, but I prefer to manage a big hotel right from the beginning. I think it's much more fun!
