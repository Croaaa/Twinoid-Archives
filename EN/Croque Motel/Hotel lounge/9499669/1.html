No clue if anyone pays attention to bug reports, but I finally figured out why at least one weird thing happens.

If a floor has been destroyed by a UFO or fighter jet crash, any future rooms you build on the floor start out as damaged. What must be happening is that the special event affects not only rooms you've already built on that floor, but also the hidden possible rooms that you could build in the future.
