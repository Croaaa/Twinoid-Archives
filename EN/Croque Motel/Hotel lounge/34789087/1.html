Guests do not get extra satisfacion from special rooms, but they will pay you a little money when they use it.

Guests who like humidity will use extra wet rooms, guests who like noise will use discos etc. They always go by themselves. After 2 hours they will go back to their own room and pay you 5 , leaving the special room vacant for the next guest.

Guests with 2 dislikes will never visit a special room.

Specifically, guests who like the effect of the room will go there randomly, and stay for 2 hours, then pay 5 and then not have a chance to return for 6 hours, but then after 6 hours, they could return normally. All guests with that like can go, even suspicious packages, who do not normally pay (but they will pay at an effect room). As a practical matter, if you have at least 3 guests with each like (which 15-20 guests should meet, on average), one set of effect rooms will normally be in use all the time. Guests that check out before the 2 hours in the room are up will not pay the 5 , so its possible for the room to be full, and not get paid close to checkout. Also, after checkout, there may not be enough remaining guests with the like to keep the room in use, until you place the new day's guests.


You will want to have one of each effect room type, before duplicating, because the odds are you will not have too many guests with each 'like'


Normally, a fully upgraded room with a happy guest is much more profitable than an effect room, so you don't really get extra benefit from these rooms, unless you have extra space after placing every guest you normally get in a room
