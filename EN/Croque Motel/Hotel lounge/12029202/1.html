Not at the same time. They will head to the room if it is empty only.


As a beginning (0 Star) hotel, if you are going to try to bequeath as soon as you can, you probably won't see a return on your investment for the 'money maker' rooms. A regular hotel room will bring in far more revenue as long as you get enough guests to fill the room 4 or 5 times.
