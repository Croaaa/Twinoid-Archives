For real that's not true - 10 is the highest satisfaction shown as 0 is the lowest satisfaction shown.
But if you calculate the real satisfaction score it can be -xy up to +yz.

For example you have base satisfaction from 5
4 times neighbors bonus = 4 x 2 = 8
2 times heat bonus = 2 x 2 = 4 (client likes heat)
3 times client jobs = 3 x 1 = 3

Real satisfaction in this case would be 20. A higher satisfaction than 10 helps to cope with negative events.
