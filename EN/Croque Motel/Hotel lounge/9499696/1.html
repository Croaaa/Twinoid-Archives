Par for the course! You'll get teddy bears in randomly equal proportion to all other equipment drops, but except for the opening 'teach you the game' quests and upon each bequeath no guests like neighbors.

There are a couple fringe uses for teddy bears, but no where near enough to keep the supply in use:
Equipping rooms with suspicious packages who like neighbors;
Equipping rooms to satisfy quests which have the 'assign X amount of equipment' conditions;

But frankly, suspicious packages are usually at a 10 satisfaction if your friend has given the placement any thought at all (they should ALL like neighbors, for example), and assigning equipment is usually better done with actual satisfaction boosting equipment.

And so I have 89 teddy bears...
