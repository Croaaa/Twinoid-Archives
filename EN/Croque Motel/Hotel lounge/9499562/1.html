Luck is luck. Most research items are a "increase the chance" (reduce the chance, same difference) effect, but they do not provide the important details needed to analyze the cost/benefit. You just have to buy them and try to work it out yourself! A very few have a fixed effect, such as Nomadic Pleasure reducing 100% of the time a fixed -2 to a fixed -1 satisfaction.

Explore, and learn. Share your observations. I've bought in this Bequeath round things such as Cheap Love (a fixed reduction in price from 10 to 8) and Doctor Feelgood (chance based increase in the awarding of a token of appreciation) that I did not buy in my zero star original hotel. I found them both to be reasonable Lab purchases, even with only 1/3 in each. Play around, see what works for you.
