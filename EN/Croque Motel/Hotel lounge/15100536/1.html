A hypno-cat with two dislikes can only have their special effect triggered if they are placed next to a zombie and they don't get any of their dislikes. I found this one out by accident. Very useful, if you have a zombie checked in.



Items that are destroyed at checkout (Isolation Chamber, Diamond Bed) are only destroyed if someone actually stays their last night in the room, then checks out. You can use and abuse this for MASSIVE profit, when you have two and three day guests and some extra rooms. This can also protect normal items from random breakage, even better than the Toughened Equipment tech. This can ALSO be used to milk Pyros for fast cash in a deluxe room at first, then giving them a normal one to trash on the last day


For Example: Level 3 room with Matelalala and Diamond Bed installed. Get a 3-day high-paying guest (bomb/plant/fish with upgrade) or blob. Put them in the room for two days, then move them to a normal room the last day. Done right, you will never use up the diamond bed or the Matelalala, even though the Diamond bed is supposed to be only a one-use item !!! And you will get the +25% cash bonus of the bed, on top of the +60% level 3 room bonus, the high-paying guest bonus, AND the Matelalala satisfaction bonus. This can make almost 200 CroquEuros from a single guest, EACH night !!!


The best part of this scam is when you finally move them on out to some normal room for the last day, they will STILL be +1 satisfaction over if they had never been moved at all (if you have the Nomadic Pleasure tech), because of the two days of free Matelalala bonuses !!


I learned this little trick checking out other higher level player's hotels, i was confused at all the rooms they had with this setup pre-installed (since these items are usually not put in until needed), until i saw it in action

Guests lock in their rooms on midnight game time on their last day, 10 hours until they check out. There are various ways to use this game mechanic. A Three-day guest can be moved around for the first two and a half days of their stay.


My preferred style is to re-arrange my non-checkout guests after the 10 AM checkout, because at that time, I can easily see who needs what effect, now that some guests that were providing effects have left.

You can visually see when a three day guest is on their third day, because they usually have 3 or more tokens of appreciation, just from room service requests and the matelalala.


I also like to trigger Hypno-cat's special ability as soon as i can. I will even put them into rooms right at 6 AM before 10 AM checkout, if i have the right guests to trigger the effect. Fame Farming


The little stars you get when you build certain things, and have level 10 satisfied guests check out. They have no real use but bragging rights, but if you want them, there are some tricks to getting them faster.


Token pumping :


You will first want to get as many guests as possible for this (see the help thread) If you have all three techs related to tokens of appreciation (Cheap Love,Job's a goon 'un, and Doctor Feelgood), you can pump these tokens into all your guests, quickly, with good odds and reasonable cost, guarantying that you get a star or 30 from every guest at checkout. If you have 4 employees, you can actually take your employees off the desk right after a guest arrives (every 2 hours), give tokens to 4 guests, then get back to the desk in time to lure the next guest, because you have 3 hours to get back to the desk to get the next guest at the usual time of arrival, and it only takes two hours to give a non-dumb fish token of appreciation with the tech upgrades. You just have to remember to put them back at the desk in two to three hours after you put them in rooms. This can easily put a token or two on every guest, even if you have a ton of guests, if you have the time to move the employees around during the day. If you have more than 4 employees, this will not work, but you still have 3-4 chances to give tokens at the time your last guest arrives, if you will be online during the in-game early morning hours when no new guests can arrive.


VIP's :


Good for 30x the normal stars at 10 satisfaction. The easiest way to get them is to buy a VIP campaign every day (every hotel campaign seems to have one VIP campaign, at least) , but you can also upgrade all your rooms to level 3. This insures that any guest that randomly becomes a VIP will not start pissed, or be locked into a basic room if they pop to VIP right before checkout. Also, when you bequeath, if you see that you got more than 2 VIP campaigns, or one that pulls lots of VIP's, you might consider switching to fame farming for that hotel, as having more VIP campaigns is pure luck, so you might as well take advantage of the opportunity, as you may not get the chance again if your next set of ad campaigns is meh. You can also get the stars from a bomb type VIP guest, if you pump them from 9 to 10 satisfaction withing 3 hours of checkout (so they don't explode in your hotel). This can be done by waiting on a room service request (if they make one at the right time), using an effect item they like, or placing a new 6 AM guest next to them with a effect they like (you could use a campaign to get extra early new guests, or a friend gifted guest for this), or use a new zombie with an effect they don't hate (if they have 2 hates, just like activating the Hypno-cats with 2 hates).
