As Voovits said, use more thought when placing your guests. Don't just throw them in any available room, look at their likes and dislikes and try to make sure that you don't put a guest who (like your current guest in Room 202) doesn't like bad smells and heat next to two sources of bad smells and one source of heat.

And don't bother sending the dogsbodies to try to give them a token of appreciation, either. You should recall them now. Do the math: (3-9) = -6. Adding 1 from a successful token of appreciation still leaves you at -5, and this guest will not pay. So don't invest in it further. The same applies to the dogsbody in Room 203, he'll never have time to get this guest above 0 satisfaction, so don't waste your time and moving the negative number slightly towards positive.

Sometimes it's fine to use a guest solely to add bonuses to other guests, even if they themselves won't be paying. You could have swapped the positions of the guests in Rooms 203 & 204 and had a generally higher payout come morning checkout.

Relax, regroup tomorrow, and start the day fresh with your new knowledge!
