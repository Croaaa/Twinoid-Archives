Was thinking of something that could come after reaching level 10 or 13 and I think I came up with a fun one.

Basically after reaching level 12 or so you'll start to see guest coming in pairs let it be a Hysterical with her Abomination husband or Capitalist ( piggy ) with his business partner Boring Witch. The way this will work as they will function as a single client but instead have 3 traits to them E.G Likes Water, Likes Heat, Hates Noise, Emits intense Heat, the Heat will function normally but it will either add or subtract an extra satisfaction point due to the intensity of the nature. Why not just have 2 emit traits like Monstro Plant? Well, for the sake of keeping him unique as a single client ( and probably less frustrations for players )

Paired Guest do require a level 2 or higher room and their base satisfaction is 6. The maximum satisfaction for paired guest is 15 with 0 ~ 6 being unsatisfactory, 7 ~ 11 normal satisfactory, 12 ~ 15 being pleasant satisfactory. CroquEruos will be the Sum of both clients normal individual payments + diamond values E.G Emotional Time Bomb paired with Capitalist in a Level 2 room will both pay 60 CroqEuros + 25 % = 70 CroqEuros each = 140 CroqEruos upon departure.

Paired guest also have twice the probability of leaving items, destroying rooms, the chance of destroying equipment remains the same.

Paired guest will share the same room service request, however it will take up to 3x the amount of time to complete set task with a satisfactory + of 2.

Putting monsters in the institute of happiness also functions differently, when you put a monster in a paired group in the institute of happiness, this does not effect the remaining monster as only 1 of the 2 will be escorted, the remaining guest simply returns to a normal guest ( as if they arrived alone ) the choice of which monster however, is random.

Celebrity paired guest function as normal celebrities, though they require a level 4 room ( platinum ) instead of a level 3 room ( gold ) Their base satisfaction is 5 and will only reward fame if their satisfaction is above 12 at + 50 fame +100 if their fame is 15.
