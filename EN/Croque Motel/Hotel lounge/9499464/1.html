Bequeathing your hotel allows you to:

Earn a new star next to your hotel's name,
Earn + 150 Pr ,
Increase the maximum number of research points which can be used in the Lab.

Start again with a new establishment (at level 2),

That's exactly what it does. Nothing more, nothing less. Also, I think you can get at least 6 stars.
