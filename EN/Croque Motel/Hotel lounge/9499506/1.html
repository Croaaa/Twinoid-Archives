As far as I know, decorations just make your hotel look nice and don't serve any other purpose, so don't worry if you don't have anywhere to put them right now. You'll have plenty of space for them as your hotel expands.

But if you do need to place some decorations now, in order to complete a quest perhaps, it's worth knowing that they can overlap. For example, you can put a desk in front of a painting, a light above the painting, and a vase of flowers on the desk. And if you don't like how that looks, you can always rearrange them later on.
