TradeFederation

Dont worry about the tax, you actually don't have to pay it, in fact, you don't even want to pay it and level up until you are ready.


The first thing you want to do is to get more employees and put them at the desk between the hours of 6:00 to 22:00 (assuming you have 4 or less). This brings in more paying guests. Once you have 4 employees at the desk you will bring in 9 paying guests a day. The strategy of the game is to put these guest near the effects they like, the rooms to the left and right, and above and below, and away from their dislikes.


Guests that are not placed by 23:59 will leave, so you need to log on before then and place them in rooms.


The friend guests don't pay, as you know, but they can still make you money, because you can get ones that create the effects your paying guests like, that another paying guest can't provide. Say you have 3 paying guests that like smells, but no smelly guests, so you get a friend to send you a guest that makes smells and likes neighbors, and the effects your other guests have. Thus you can get all the smell likers to 7 satisfaction by placing them around the smelly friend guest (if you meet their other need as well), and can make the base rate on each guest. If you can get a paying guest near more than one source of what the like, you can earn much more than the base rate. They pay twice, once at in-game midnight, and again at 10 am checkout, so getting a good flow of paying guests will fix your budget problems.


Leveling up gets you new rooms and new guest types, but its more important at the start to get the four employees you can buy with , and to expand the desk so they can all sit there and bring in more paying guests. Once you have some daily income coming in, then you can worry about new rooms and completing your starter missions.


A lot of the stuff in my making money thread does not apply at the very start, because you don't have the funds to build the room service rooms or upgraded rooms, but getting those initial paying guests in rooms and happy enough to get the money flowing is the first step.
