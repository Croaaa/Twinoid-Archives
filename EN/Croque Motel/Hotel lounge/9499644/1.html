Pretty much any upgrade in this game is expensive. Plus, as you expand everything gets more expensive. Ex: a new room could cost you 1000+ to expand, 100 to furnish and 250+600 for lv 3 = about 2000 which takes a lot of nights to earn back. Entertainment rooms: 200 + 100 more for each next one.
