There is another thread where many people have posted their ad campaigns. That thread, plus my own 5 different sets of ad campaigns, suggests to me that the ad campaigns are:
1) Not modified in any way by bequeath 'level';
b) Randomly assigned;
) Not important enough to warrant delaying a bequeath, unless your own goals in the game (Fame) make a set of plentiful and/or cheap celebrity ad campaigns attractive to retain.

Players dropping from this game seem to have a common complaint: Boredom. While there isn't any new content above 7th level, which is pretty easy to accomplish in 7 weeks, a few of the players who have dropped have never bequeathed. Which seems to me to be a self-limiting factor. If you never bequeath, you never have another goal to aspire to reach. So, boredom sets in. If you aspire to bequeath, you always have a new goal to try to attain. The game certainly slows down after the first or second bequeath, no argument here. But then I don't mind a challenge that takes me a long time to accomplish. It's not all about instant gratification, after all.
