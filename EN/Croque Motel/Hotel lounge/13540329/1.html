A zero-star hotel (means: a hotel you haven't bequeathed yet) has one major advantage: You can get a lot decoration items for free. All you have to do is completing the unique starting missions. Getting decoration items later on is difficult if you don't want to pay Muxxu tokens, as they are extremely rare. The missions you get AFTER you completed the starting missions will only give 5 fame points.

If I were you, I'd collect all those deco-items and bequeath as soon as all the starting missions are completed. Remember that you lose all your deco-items that are installed when you bequeath, so if you want to take them with you, DO NOT click on any "random decoration" or "paint" items in your "decoration" tab or you won't have them in your new hotel.

If you don't care about what your hotel looks like, you can ignore what I just wrote
