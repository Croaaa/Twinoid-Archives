If you can afford 4 dogsbodies, definitely get them. Otherwise, definitely get 3
Since Life, Business and Monster Hotel cannot be reduced to mathematics, the following are estimations and depend on too many other things, so don't take them too seriously...

IF you can get an average satisfaction of 7, with non-upgraded rooms and most monsters you would get 27 coins per night plus 27 on leaving. This (very optimistically?) gives 50 per guest per day (for simplified arithmatic).

A third dogsbody, costing 500, requires a welcome desk upgrade, costing 250. It gives 1 extra guest per night. 750 cost, 50 income gives a payback of 15 nights. This could be within the life of nearly all Hotels, depending on how much you are earning and when you plan to bequeath

A fourth dogsbody, costing 1000, requires another welcome desk upgrade, costing 500. It gives 3 extra guests per night. 1500 cost, 150 income gives a payback of 10 nights. This could also be within the life of most Hotels, and again depends on how much you are earning and when you plan to bequeath. It could easily be that saving the 1500 cost allows you to get the extra 1500 to bequeath in less than 10 days

If you have the cash, doing both costs 2250, gives 4 extra guests (200 per night), and gives a payback of 12 days

Before making the first bequeath, I am not convinced about the fourth dogsbody, as cash is in short supply
