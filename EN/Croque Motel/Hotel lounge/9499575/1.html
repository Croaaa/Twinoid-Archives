The welcome desk is where your guests arrive. You get 1 guest every 8 hours with just the desk (3 per day)

If you recruit employees (Supermarket) you can use them to repair rooms and provide services to your guests, but they can also work at the welcome desk; when they do you attract guests more quickly. Your initial desk can support 2 employees, giving you 1 guest every 4 hours (5 per day), but you need to upgrade the desk if you want more employees at the desk to speed up guest arrival
