You can't put bedrooms there at any point, you can build institutes of happiness, a laundromatrix... not bedrooms.The issue is one of definitions. The Institute, laundromatrix, etc. are not bedrooms, as in they cannot house a guest. But they count as rooms all the same. Go to your office and look at the required rooms for bequeathing, and you'll see your current room count. Add up your bedrooms and you'll find that the number falls short. Add the Chowrightnow, "money makers" etc. to your bedroom count and you'll find that it matches the room total cited in the Office.

Could they make it more clear? Sure. Is it an error in the FAQ? No, not by their definition of what counts as a room.
