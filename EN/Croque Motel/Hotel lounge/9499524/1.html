Erem
Above your employee and to the left of the three buttons you mention, there are (or should be) two more square buttons. The first has the image of a picture and the second has the image of arrows pointing in the four cardinal directions. They say "Change the furnishings" and "Expand my hotel" respectively when you hover over them.

Incidentally, the Expand button doesn't actually make the new room. Once you've expanded your hotel, you need to click on the blank wall you've just created and furnish a bedroom (or whatever type of room it is you want - what's available depends upon the level of your hotel).
