On march 1, 2012
Kompera
Yes, just as soon as you finish a quest you are issued another. Knowing that, it can pay off to be careful about the order in which you take some actions when you sit down to play a bit at your hotel. If you only need to collect a few more items guests left behind, for example, then you might not want to start by taking care of service requests. Once you collect the left behind items and get a new quest, it might have satisfying a few service requests as a condition. That means that you may be able to complete one quest and make progress on the next if you're careful about the order you do things.
