You are somewhat limited in the shapes you can build because of the necessity for each floor to be continuous. For example, my original intention had been to build a central block flanked by a couple of towers to house those guests who don't like neighbours. That idea went out the window when I discovered that I couldn't build separate towers, and my hotel ended up being first triangular and now a rather uninteresting block. Another limitation is that the hotels can only be seven rooms wide.

I've seen some hotels that are just a single tower or an inverted pyramid, but I don't think you will see any much more interesting than that due to the constraints on where you can build. Although, if anyone has managed to build something funny/creative, I look forward to seeing it.
