I think we should be able to rid clients without having to sacrifice them to the happy place. Sometimes you can accidentally fill up your entire hotel, get caught in a hysterical scream or scream chain and be left with no other option but to risk lowering the other clients satisfaction points; You may also end up having more clients than you have rooms or have a client you can no longer use.

The eviction option will work in the same way a account deletion would work, you will give a client an eviction notice which says he/she has 2 hours to leave the hotel and once set time is reached, the monster leaves. Of course they'll be a drawback to this feature in the form of a 70/30 chance of them destroying your room on departure, or maybe you having a chance to lose 10 when they leave ( either or )

Monster Motel could use a added feature, this game hasn't been updated in forever.
