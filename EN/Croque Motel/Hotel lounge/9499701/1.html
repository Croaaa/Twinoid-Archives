Bequeathing is stupid.Bequeathing is the only way to access more Lab options. These Lab options provide a great deal of value to your hotel. You could make the argument that bequeathing past 4 is not very valuable, because at 4 you have can have all Lab options save one, but not against bequeathing in general.
