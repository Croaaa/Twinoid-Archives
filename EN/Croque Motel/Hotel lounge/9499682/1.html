sebis7
The Emotional Bomb does not explode right away if the satisfaction level is at 0 or 10, instead it will become unstable. If you manage to change the satisfaction level within these 3 hours, everything goes back to normal. If you miss that time window, the bomb will be gone and the room will have 5 points of damage.

Therefore getting 30 fame points from a Bomb-VIP is possible, but this needs good timing..
