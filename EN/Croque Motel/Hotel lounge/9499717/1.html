Things you keep when bequeathing:
- Items in the green "Store" tab (All-you-can-eat-Buffet, Mummy in the Closet, ...)
- Items in the blue "Special" tab (Welcome Gift, Labyrinthian Wardrobe, ...)
- Items in the dark blue "Decoration" tab (Random Decoration) <= this is what I call "unwrapped decoration item"

Things you lose when bequeathing:
- everything else
- including decoration items you placed in you hotel. Decoration items that are right-clicked on in placement mode and this way are put back in the stash (orange button top left) count as placed decoration, too! <= this is what is meant by "whether you installed it or not" in the offices bequeathing description
