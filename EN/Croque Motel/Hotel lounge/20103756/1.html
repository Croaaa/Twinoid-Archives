The answer is different monster types have different payout amounts. The monster in the diamond bed level 3 room is a Hysterical type. They pay the normal rate. The other monster in the once upgraded room is a Potless Blob. They only pay 50% of the normal rate, unless they are at 10 satisfaction, then they pay 170% of the normal rate, and Since it is at 10 satisfaction, its actual payout will be higher than even the diamond bed and level 3 room can make on a normal guest.


Even so, the payment amounts seem off, I think you should check the actual checkout reports in the box on the right side. I believe the hysterical will pay 100, and the blob will pay 102. In the future, you might try to put your diamond bed with your highest paying guests. With the tech upgrades, Fish, Plants, and Bombs pay the best, but bombs usually have to stay below 10 satisfaction, so the first two actually pay out the highest.
