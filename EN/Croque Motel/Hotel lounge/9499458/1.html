Another option would be to allow a new account a one time option to set their time zone. This would prevent changing the account time zone as an exploit. As would allowing any number of account time zone changes, each accompanied by a 24 hour account lockout.

Yes, there might be USA players who moved from east to west coasts and had a time zone change of 3 hours. Yes, there might be French players who moved to Hungary, or vice versa. But the one time change (or any number of change along with the lockout) would allow players to set the game to play according to their schedule, while currently there appears to be significant usability constraints for players who are outside of the server timezone by more than a couple of hours, or by players who work non-traditional schedules such as swing or night shifts.
