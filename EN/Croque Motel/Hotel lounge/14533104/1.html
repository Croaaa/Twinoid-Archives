You build a room that is designed to satisfy guests with a particular like for something. Once you build it, a random guest with that like will use the room at a random time and use it for two hours. After two hours, you get 5 . The guest can use the same room again, but not until at least six hours have past since it entered the room the last time. In the meantime, another guest can use the room.
