The only issue I see with room fires is that you have limited options.

You either:
- Leave the guest in the room and eat the -3 room condition satisfaction penalty, and the resulting hit to the customer's room fee;
- Move the customer (-1 or -2 satisfaction per move, depending on your Lab picks), repair the room, and then either move them back or eat the hit to their and any surrounding guests satisfaction due to favorable likes and effect generations;
- Use a repair kit

The repair kit is the best option, but it either relies upon the vagaries of chance for guests leaving them behind, or you must purchase them with tokens which cost real money.

It's yet another reason to leave assigning guests to rooms until server midnight approaches. If the guest isn't in the room, a dogsbody can repair it just fine.
