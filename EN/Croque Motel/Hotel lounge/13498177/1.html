A few months ago, I posted about a bug in which my advertising campaign list was coming up completely blank. I know there were a couple other people who said they were dealing with the exact same problem.


Well, good news - I seem to have found a workaround.


If you are using Firefox with the AdBlocker Plus addon, one of the global filters may be blocking your advertising campaign list. Go to the red ABP stop sign icon that will appear in your browser Add-on Bar. Click on it and choose the "Open blockable items" option. You should open a list of page elements, one of which will be "sponsors" and will be in red.


Simply click on the red X associated with that entry, reload the Office page, and you should get your advertising campaign list back.


It seems AdBlock uses a generated list of common names for filtering advertising elements on webpages, and "sponsors" is one of the terms that gets universally filtered.


Hope this is useful for you, if your advertising campaigns had disappeared like mine had!
