Have you extended your hotel sideways so much that you can't see all the rooms simultaneously and have to keep dragging the display area to check for laundry baskets?

If you increase the magnification of the web page from 100% to 125%, the display window will increase in size but the contents will remain at the same scale, meaning that you'll be able see more of your hotel on screen at once.

I forgot to add that you may not be able to see your ground floor after increasing the magnification. If that happens, refreshing the page should solve the problem.
