It works for me. Since I'm in the USA buying an ad campaign is the only way for me to use the normal trickle of guests who arrive throughout the day, since I am at work and cannot access the game. It doesn't work on my mobile, I've tried.

But if I buy an ad campaign they arrive at my midnight, which allows me to place them about the hotel. When I get up in the morning there are a few others who have arrived, and I can try to work them in or chop one up. Then it's off to work.

But even for folks on a server schedule, it's worth it if your hotel is large enough. If you have 8 or more rooms and buy a campaign with 5 guests, and then wait for a few more to arrive before placing any in rooms. Since they don't make you any money during the day, there is no rush to place them and they can't catch the sink on fire or get eaten by a grue and lose satisfaction. Once you have a nice crop you can select the guests whose likes, dislikes, and effect generations work best together, and you should have a crop of 7+ guests, with several 10s. This should easily double your investment in the ad campaign, and give you a few monsters to chop up for research as well.
