There is a way to do this, but it won't help you save more money for a bequeath. You have to be hotel empty, then be offline more than 2 days. Since paying the tax is such a big deal later in this game, I've decided to not test what the exact limits are on this.


The best move is to just make more than the tax over 7 days and accumulate the difference, as long as the tax is less than your take. Once the tax is more, you can just bequeath the day before the tax is due.


Since you don't have many guests, and are playing without tokens, you might want to see if you can profit on your normal guest campaigns, you will need a fairly full hotel to profit over the tax.

All you need to do is spend yourself below the amount it takes to pay the taxes.


The best way to avoid leveling up is to spend your money on things that will help your hotel grow. Expand the hotel costs a lot, so I use that primarily. But you can also buy ad campaigns, which usually lets you spend a few thousand.


Just be aware that you earn the midnight income before the tax man tries to collect, so spend yourself under by at least the amount you anticipate making.

Then, if you're looking to have a nice nest egg for bequeathing, you need to make as much money as possible in the coming week, and bequeath before the tax man comes to collect again. This also gets you an instant L2 hotel at your new star rating.

The ad campaigns you bought will help stuff your rooms for the first couple of days. Use Diamond Beds on 3 day stay guests, and try to maximize satisfaction for the highest payouts, especially the guests who pay more.
