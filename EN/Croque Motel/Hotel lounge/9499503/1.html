Different timezones
- the basic idea of the game revolves around the player managing a hotel where guests check in before midnight and stay 1 or more days
- the game was obviously designed with the French timezone in mind and while players in Europe have no trouble with that (since the difference is 1-2 hours), for people living on the other side of the world (America, Asia etc) the game can become inconvenient to play
- possible solutions include a game option that allows the player to choose their timezone at the beginning of the game so that the clock would match

Errors in the Help pages


A quick run-down of the game:

Each of your guests has their particular needs and wants and their location in your hotel will have an impact on existing residents. (place a guest who likes evil smells close to one who stinks to high heaven).

Place should be capitalised as it begins a sentence, and the full stop at the end should be inside the brackets.


Bequeath your hotel:
In addition, you will be irresistable to the opposite sex, people will scatter rose petals wherever you walk and choirs of children will serenade you on your way.

This is politically incorrect as not all players would ever want to appeal to "the opposite sex", whatever it may be. There are also intersex people with characteristics of both sexes. Also, some people don't identify with any sex or gender. I propose this wording: you will be irresistibly attractive. Also note that "irresistable" is a spelling error.
