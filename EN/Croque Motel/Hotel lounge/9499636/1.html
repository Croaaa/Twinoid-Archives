The guests were tired, the employees were minding their business, noone was suspecting anything out of the ordinary. But then, all of a sudden, it happened.

19:26 Incredible! An employee discovered that the guest in Room 302 was in fact a media personality! From now on, this client is a prestigious client.

The hotel bearer was dumbstruck, when he heard of this. It was terrific news to him! A was in his hotel, using the very environment of the hotel, interacting with common folk. Little did he know, what this news really meant.

Her name was Rancodork and she was a dutch horror superstar. A spoled little brat. Hysterical too. She liked her room until now. But suddenly, it wasn't good enough for her anymore. She needed a deluxe room. Even the room she is in, the interiour of which was already improved once, is not enough for her. So she started screaming. As loud as in any dutch horror movie. That wasn't good at all for other guests. But what should the hotel bearer do?

The tax was coming and if he improved a room a second time, he wouldn't have enough money to pay it. He could try to satisfy her, but it is unfortunate, that she only likes heat and there are no hot guests at the moment. Even the overpowered radiator isn't enough heat for the spoiled little girl. Midnight is coming and there is not enough time to do enough room services to at least make her stop screaming. The other guests would be very disappointed.

The hotel bearer thought about the situation. There were no options. He was helpless. But then he found the only logical solution: Sacrifice the dutch little girl to be able to survive another week.

And the moral of the story is this: Discipline your children before they're killed!
