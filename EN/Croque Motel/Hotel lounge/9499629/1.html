The short answer is: you can't.

The longer answer is that it's broken at the moment because of the introduction of Twinoid and the new contacts system. In theory, you would add the other person to your contacts, and they would add you to theirs. You'll then be classed as friends and be able to see their hotel on the My Friends page. When you click on the name of your friend's hotel to visit it, you will see options on the right-hand side for specifying the guest's preferences (two likes or dislikes) and what they generate, and a Send button. (Actually, it's an Envoyer button but I'm hoping that one day the developers will get around to translating it from French.)

As I say, it's currently broken, so even if you add each other to your contact lists, your hotels won't appear on each others My Friends page and you won't be able to send them a guest.
