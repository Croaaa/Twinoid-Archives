Firstly, your hotel needs to be at least level 2. (You reach level 2 when you pay your Consortium Tax for the first time.)
Next, you need to click on a (preferably blank) wall where you want the Institute to be and it will be one of the options in the menu that appears. (If the wall you click isn't blank, the Institute will replace the room that was there previously, so be careful.)
