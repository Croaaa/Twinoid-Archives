Its random as hell, what you get for guests each day, that being said, certain things you get later really help your hotel. More rooms prevent guests from being stuck next to others they hate. More employees will get you much more guests per day, which increases your money fast. Paying your taxes gets you new guest types that are mostly good, and gets you a lot less pyros, which are one of the worst guest types. tax payment also gets you 2 and 3 day guests, which you will have more time to give room service to and get higher satisfaction out of, and let you fill up more rooms in your hotel at once. And science can lower your fixed costs like repair, and boost revenue for certain guest types.
