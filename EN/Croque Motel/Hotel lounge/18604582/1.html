On your first day playing, you receive neighbor loving guests.


When you bequeath your hotel and start over, you will immediately get a few neighbor loving guests on that first day.


You can only bequeath 7 times at most, so that's only eight total days that you theoretically could receive neighbor-loving guests 'naturally'


If you have a friend send you a guest, they can make their guest like neighbors, and you should ask for it if you get a friend guest.
