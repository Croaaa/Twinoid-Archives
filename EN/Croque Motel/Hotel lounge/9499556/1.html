Kompera
Once your hotel is level 4+, VIP guests need level 3 rooms to be satisfied.Just in case anyone reads this and thinks you cannot satisfy a VIP w/o the level 3+ room...
Clarification: They need a level 3 room to add a +2 satisfaction bonus to their normal low satisfaction level of 1. (It might be the other way around, they might suffer a -2 satisfaction penalty to the standard base of 3 if not housed in a level 3 or higher room, the result is the same.)

All normal bonuses (and penalties) apply, so you can park the VIP in a level 1 room and stack on satisfaction in other ways to get to 10+. The spiffy room just makes it much easier. As does having a VIP with "likes" as opposed to "dislikes".
