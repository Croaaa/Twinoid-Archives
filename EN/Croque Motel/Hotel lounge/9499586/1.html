How to do this and the advantages and disadvantages of doing so is a frequently asked question. There are dribs and drabs of information on the subject in the Ask Questions Here sticky topic, but it isn't all all in one place nor is all the information available presented. So here is all of the officially available information, all in one place.

This information is located in the Office, and is only peripherally referenced in the FAQ as an unrelated "extra" bit of information which has nothing to do with the FAQuestion at all.
Bequeath your hotel

You can choose to bequeath your hotel to an employee so that they can run it and take over its development. You will start again with a new establishment (at level 2), but you will retain the following possessions:

Your money,
1 employee,
All un-used objects (except decorative objects, whether installed or not).

Bequeathing your hotel allows you to:

Earn a new star next to your hotel's name,
Earn + 150 Pr ,
Increase the maximum number of research points which can be used in the Lab.

To bequeath your hotel, you must:

Have reached level 4 or higher,
Have at least 3000 C€ in reserve (this will not be lost),
Have built at least 12 rooms in your hotel (you have <x> at the moment).
FAQ reference:
How do I reach the next level?

You advance to the next level by paying the Consortium tax. The amount payable and the due date are indicated below your hotel and will automatically be collected if you have enough money at the time. When you bequeath, any quest you are in the midst of completing will be lost.
You will be issued a new quest.
You will receive several guests, not dependent on the standard clock.
Your guests will once again have a chance to like neighbors.
Your advertising campaigns will be reset.
Any unused lab points will be lost.
You will gain a number of lab points to allocate equal to 3 * (bequeath count). This is true at least for bequeaths one and two.

Personal experience:
Bequeathing for the first time:
I received 5 guests immediately.
Of those 5 guests, two liked neighbors.
One was a celebrity.
I got 3 lab points to allocate immediately.

Bequeathing for the second time:
I received 5 guests immediately.
Of those 5 guests, two liked neighbors (and each had an effect that the other liked!).
None were celebrities.
I got 6 lab points to allocate immediately.
