Imagine the employees out in front of the hotel holding a sign or advertising your hotel. When you put them at the front desk, they cause new guests to appear faster. The counter outside the hotel shows when the next guest will arrive, and it goes down as you add employees to your front desk.


You have to manually place the guests in your hotel after they arrive, that's the part of the game where you play it, instead of it playing itself.


You impress the guests by giving them, or getting them near the effect they like, and keeping them away from the ones they do not like. You can also have your employees give them tokens of appreciation, and meeting their room service requests.


Certain items can also be put in the rooms to make guests happy, you can get them if they are left behind when your guests check out
