Hit 1000 on 4/6/14. First completed MT game (and I can't see completing any others anytime soon) I like the game a lot, but I'll probably not be playing it too much anymore.


Biggest issues :


1. Midnight = 5 AP CST. Usually this doesn't matter in other MT games, but in this one, every guest not in a room at 'midnight' is lost, and my prime gaming time is right after 5 PM, when I get off work, so I am forced to log in, AT WORK !!, just to save my later guests each day, and this is stupid. Then, a few hours later, I am home and online and ready to play, but have very little to do besides hand out tokens and do room service requests.


Related - 10 AM in game checkout is 3 AM CST, this forces me to place all my guests in the morning, before i go to work. This has caused me to do most of my Hotel playing at an awkward time, when I would rather not be gaming. With 6 in-game Employees, placing a day's guests and campaign guests could take an hour or more each morning, and that is just a bad time for me to be playing so much.



2. Weekly taxes. Because the amount of the tax escalates, I feel I have to avoid paying it, and this requires me to save up for hotel extensions, buy them, then spend down all the surplus funds every Sunday. Since the later extensions cost a huge amount, 20,000 +, this means I have to play a week solid, every single day, to get an extension, and sabotage my earnings one day a week, to avoid leveling up. This means I HAVE to play each day, or I will not earn enough for an extension, and have to waste a lot of in-game money on rebuilding rooms, just to get rid of my in-game funds, so I do not level up. The net effect is I feel I have to work on this game continuously, every day, even if I am not in the mood for it, which is unreasonable for a game to require from me.



3. The 'Attract a Guest' link on main page. Now I know this is a Free2Play game, and they need to make money, but this is just annoying, and I actually willingly paid real cash to play the game, which I almost never do for F2P games ! Not only is this a poor use of your tokens in the game, but it has no 'are you sure' pop up, so I have more than once blown my coins on a worthless guest I had no intention of buying. With all the other paid items in a separate menu, I have never accidentally bought anything else that I didn't want to buy, except from this main page link. -- I WANT to support the game, and buy in-game stuff for my hotel with real money, but I DO NOT want to blow my coins on mis-clicks, which makes me feel like I got nothing for my coins
