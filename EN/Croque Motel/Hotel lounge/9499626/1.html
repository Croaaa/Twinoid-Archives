Kompera
The issue here is that everyone is looking at the system from their own, quite limited, perspective. The sample size is too small, and the actual data is highly suspect and subject to personal interpretation. If people would post their actual statistics, as in "I was at one star and received this many VIP guests", then we could start building a database of information which would have some real value outside of personal observations. Remember, the 1% chance event is likely to be reported, while the typical behavior is not likely to be reported since it is passé. This increases the perception that the 1% event is more normal or likely, when that is far from the actual case...
