If you work on building up your rooms and your runway of clients, you'll be amazed how quickly you can advance actually. The game is a huge time sink. So if your willing to put the time in, then you can advance more quickly. Notice when you use a turbo battery, it recharges in X time. you'll want to come back to the game after that time for another round. Before that time though, you can keep giving toilet paper and soap to guests to get them to full satisfaction (notice soda is limited to the cash they have to buy soda .. there are monster types / effects that can add cash to clients though). Also, you can use room effects if needed (safe for VIP and inspectors mainly .. but I use them on chickens as well to get gems).

Having more friends refills your hearts which allows you to keep satisfaction levels up. So having enough friends in the game is crucial as well. I think 5-10 is probably more than enough at least to start with.
