Well, it's official: MH is dead.

buzzardWhile we waited for a response from the CNC we started work on Monster Hotel, which was to be a short game finished in 2 months and released on the web and mobile.

In October of 2015 ... Apple and Google decided that they quite liked Monster Hotel and wanted to feature it on their app stores, on the condition that we added features XY and Z...

Fast forward to the end of December 2015 and Monster Hotel has been launched with a certain amount of success. The catch being, deepnight and Carduus have had enough of the game and were considering a murder suicide pact if they had to spend one more minute on it… They wanted to be working on Dead Cells. So we halted official development of MH and the guys dug up the DC files.

So this means that the beginning of 2016 has been entirely devoted to Dead Cells.
