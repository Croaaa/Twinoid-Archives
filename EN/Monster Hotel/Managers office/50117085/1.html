It is easy. Just make three clients happy (or happier) at a time by placing one monster in your hotel.

You will know that they get happier as their room will glow green for a moment. Similarly, their room will glow red if you anger them...

For instance, suposeyou have two "muncher" monsters:
- one that likes heat, and generates stink.
- one that likes stink, and generates heat.
If you place both of these monsters close to each other, you will get a 2-monster happiness combo. Hope this helps.
