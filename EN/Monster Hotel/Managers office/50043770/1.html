These are the official names and descriptions. SPOILERS!

Poring : He is easy to satisfy but he doesn't pay that much.
Nice neighbour : He gives +x happiness to all its neighbours.
Angry pear : If she has something she dislikes near her, she EXPLODES!
Bomber : He EXPLODES if its happiness is 0 or less, or above y. He drops extra money if he checkouts with a happiness of EXACTLY y.
Mama Blob : Mama blob has a VERY short stay duration. Another client (a Blobling), immediatly arrives in the queue if her happiness reaches x or more.
Blobling : He was generated by a Mama Blob. He has a very short stay duration and drops a gift if its happiness reaches x or more!
Floor ghost : +x happiness to every clients on the same floor.
Column ghost : +x happiness to every clients in the same column.
Schrodingcat : Gives you a present if its happiness reach x or more!
Leek : The effect generated by this client on nearby rooms is TWICE as powerful.
Repair : He repairs all of the rooms in your hotel as soon as he checks in. He gets +1 happiness for each room repaired this way.
Vampire : He hates sunlight, so avoid bedrooms with windows. He will drop an item if he checkouts with a happiness of x or more!
Gem chicken : Gives 1 gem if its happiness reach x or more!
Monop'guy : He drops a lot of money if its happiness reach x or more!
Inspector : He gives your hotel a new STAR and unlocks new content if its happiness reaches x!
Cuddle Bomb : He gives +1 happiness to every other clients if its Happiness reaches x or more!
Dragon : He always have lots of SAVINGS.
Diffuser : He will generate something around ONLY if you use an item on him.
Magic patron : He will distribute x SAVINGS to other clients when installed in a bedroom.

Here are all of the possible modifiers currently too.

SERIAL KILLER - Will eat a neighbour from time to time...
SQUATTER - Will stay in your hotel for quite a long time!
FAST - He will stay for a very short time so you'll have to be quick!
VIP - Drops cool stuff if totally satisfied!
ANNOYING - Everyone in the hotel gets x happiness!
GENEROUS - Everyone in the hotel gets +1 SAVING when this client is installed!
PYROMANIAC - Will damage some bedrooms when installed.
EXPLOSIVE - Will damage all bedrooms on the same floor.
NUCLEAR WARHEAD - Will damage every bedrooms in your hotel, except his own room.
BEER MASTER - Will boost a beer storage when installed.
MISTER POOP - Will boost a Toilet Paper storage when installed.
LAUNDRY NINJA - Will boost a laundry when installed.
SOCIABLE - Gets +x happiness when another client is installed.
STOCK THIEF - Empty all the hotel stocks when installed.
ALCOHOLIC - Drinks all the Beer storage when installed (and gets very happy for that).
ELECTRICIAN - Refills all your Turbo boosters.
DIFFICULT DIGESTION - Empty all your Toilet Paper stocks and damages your paper storage.
DIRTY - He will get a happiness bonus if its room is dirty, or a huge penalty otherwise.
THIEF - Steals savings from all other clients.
ROBIN HOOD - Converts ALL other client savings into money for you.
DEPRESSIVE - Has a very low happiness.
RICH UNCLE - He pays twice if he is totally satisfied!
ARCHITECT FAN - He drops money in all your hotel bedrooms.
AESTHETE - He gets much more happiness from a decorated bedroom.
DECORATION HATER - He gets a penalty for each decoration element in its room.
SUPER CHARGE - He will boost every rooms when installed. Yes. Every. Single. Rooms.
MOLE - He will get a happiness bonus if its room is underground, or a huge penalty otherwise.
ANTISOCIAL - He gets a penalty each time you install another client in your hotel!
TO THE TOP - He will get a happiness bonus if its room is the highest one in your hotel, or a huge penalty otherwise.
DECORATOR - He will drop 2 decoration items if satisfied!
