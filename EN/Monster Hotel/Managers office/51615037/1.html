norby89

I don't know, he's pretty easy to satisfy unless you don't have enough utility rooms or you're doing it wrong.

Assuming you have 3 Bloodcurdling Bars and he comes with 2 cash, leave a magician in your queue and give him beer, I mean soda, to raise his happiness by 30 right off the bat. Decorations and love provide another ~10 points. Place 4 guests around him for 16 minimum happiness, with the Monster Masher and Trash Bins you can get rid of up to 5 guests for another 20 minimum. This is of course if you don't get any leeks, friendly neighbors or column/floor creatures that would also give bonus happiness. For the remainder, use items if needed.
