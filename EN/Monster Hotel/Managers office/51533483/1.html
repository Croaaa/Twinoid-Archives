It depends if you play the game for new content or just farm to have a big hotel.

Dont remember well all the platinum stars but here you have some info ^^


Platinum I: New monster a magician, gives 3 random cash to customers who are inside of the hotel.

Platinum II: New monster a broom, cleans the dirty rooms and gains a little satisfaction for each room cleaned.

Platinum III: New monster vampire, gives a if its satisfaction is 35 or more.

Platinum IV: Mama Blob, it if reaches 35 satisfaction or more she will call her son and if you please him with 35 or more satisfaction it will give you 1000 gold coins.

Platinum V: Turbo Booster 1000 unlocked it costs 1.000.000 gold coins plus the maximum happiness increases to 45.

With Platinum V star you unlock the new Black stars.
