MotionTwit

Tomorrow, for the first time in 7 years, I won't be actively playing an MT game. Between April 2009 and April 2016, unless I'm on vacation without a computer, I've played at least one MT game on a daily basis.

But now, especially after last year's reorganization, MT is no longer what it once was. With less than half their former headcount, fewer games are being released than ever before, and the ones that do get published are abandoned faster than ever before.

In the meantime I've caught up, and grinded through some of the most tedious objectives in any game. I've done everything that I've cared to in the games that interest me, including $200+ in earnings from KadoKado and Arkadeo. All without spending a single cent.

That's not to say I won't ever come back, in the unlikely event that Monster Hotel gets another update, or MT releases another game that interests me (Dead Cells doesn't). But for now, good bye, and good luck to everyone still playing. Thanks to MT for 7 years of entertainment!
