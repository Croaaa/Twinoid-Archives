Someone told me about this. What happen when you buy this upgrade for 85 gems is that you put this " VIP room" somewhere. And you click on it, and a VIP show up. And like you do with all VIP, you try to get him to max happiness so he leave a gift, which might or might not be decoration. (it could be money). And this "VIP room" has an one hour timer before you can use it again.

So basically, instead of waiting for a random VIP to show up, this is a "guarantee method" to get a VIP to show up once an hour (you have to manually activate it. You can't leave for the day and come back 24 hours later and see 24 VIP waiting in your lobby).

Also, if you're struggling to make VIP happy when they randomly show up, then this upgrade probably isn't for you.
