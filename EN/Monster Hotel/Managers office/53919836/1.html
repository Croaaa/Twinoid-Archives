Lucario

norby89 is exactly right.
VIP Marketing Service is better than the bank because you can get a VIP every hour (If you are playing to activate it). By satisfying them, you get gold for a MAX happiness mosster, plus an additional 3,000 for the VIP tip. Also, you get a gift, which 50% of the time is 5,000 more gold. All of these gold bonuses make you get more than the bank, even if you activate it once per day. Plus, the bank requires one of your boosters to be useful anyway.

Just a word of advice. Save the Decoration Recycler for last. It is terrible. You pay 5 decorations, and get one gift. That gift might just be one of the decorations you used to get the gift in the first place.

I got the Booster first, then the VIP Service, then the bank, then the Recycler, but the VIP Service is the best.
