best way to make sure your guests are happy. so stacking up a like of 1 guest from different sides is key. also by doing that .. it diagonally gives you a double room effect as well to take advantage of. If your able to sit around every 1-2 minutes and select toilet paper or soap for quests then satisfaction will go up nicely.

As you advance through the game you'll be able to buy an upgrade to make toilet paper and soap. Don't forget to make sure all soda is given to guests as well.

Build up your funds and make more rooms. Then when you have enough premium crystals, keep expanding your runway for guests so you have plenty to work with. this allows stacking of likes to be much easier.

Much later on, its advised to get the upgrade for VIP clients. VIP clients give BIG cash bonuses if you completely satisfy them (5000 gold) or a room upgrade. They also come with a boost to one of your resource generating upgrades (soda, toilet paper, soap, etc).

I have 17 rooms myself right now and I like my hotel design since it allows a lot of rooms to have sunlight. Depending on how far you are, later on some monsters have a like of sunlight (access to a room with a window).

Also, as you progress through the game you will get more types of monsters that have unique abilities. One monster type will give +2 to every adjacent monster on the same row of rooms (limit effect of 4). One monster type will give +2 to every adjacent monster on the same column of rooms (limit effect of 4). One monster type will give +3 to all adjacent rooms around it (above, below, to the left of, to the right of). One monster hates sunlight and needs to always be in a room that has no windows (ironically he gives a bonus item of sunlight if he is completely satisfied). There are more types but it would take a long time to go through them and whats the fun in all the spoilers, right?

As you progress also, sanctification max increases (i'm currently at 45 for my max currently). Not sure how high it goes .. but I suspect 45/50 is probably as high as it goes.
