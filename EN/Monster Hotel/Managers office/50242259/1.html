Post some tips you have here.

* If you have a Trash room, you can send guests that are already placed to the trash by clicking on them, clicking Advanced and then selecting "Send this client to the trash" sometimes you might place guests who want to be below ground, or ones which hate elements that you place by mistake and they get angry.
* Click the gear at the top right. Then click settings. You can select options here that would be useful, such as requiring confirmation before spending gems.
* Saving up to buy the "Love magnet 1" gem item is a very good buy. It gives you 5 extra hearts per person you visit!
* Upgrading your lobby queue is not necessarily a good idea!
* The Monster Masher takes 11 hours before you can use it again. You get more elements faster from the cats, maybe better to never get it. Instead save up for another Bloodcurdling bar. Also, Monster Masher (AFAIK at least early on) can never generate the moon gift, which is the only one that would really make it worth it as the cats don't generate moons either.
* Have at least 1 room that is completely surrounded by other rooms on each of its sides. Have at least room that is completely away from other rooms by a support. Decorate these two rooms with your best items.
* Guests that want the underground are much more rare than guests that want windows. Don't bother building rooms underground early on. Consider building more than one room surrounded by no other rooms held by a support.
* The bird guests will drop gems. If you are close to getting them up to enough happiness it can be worth it to buy / spend gifts to get them up high enough as 3x gifts = 1x gem.
* Always put guests in the trash when you have room to. Easy money. Guests give more money based on type, not on how much cash they have on them. The poring slime guest gives the least amount of cash.
