Currently there's a bug that affects how the daily reward bonus is awarded or to be more precise, when it is awarded. The purpose of this thread is to answer some common questions that have popped up in the past few days. If you experienced this glitch, please do not create a new thread, the developers already know about it and they'll probably gonna fix it in an upcoming update.

What is the Daily Bonus glitch?
The glitch happens when the clock changes to midnight local time. The game thinks that it's time to award you your daily bonuses however that is not instantly reflected server wise. In other words you get "ghost" bonuses that only appear on your screen which means you cannot actually use them.

When do I get my daily bonuses then?
Good question. The answer is midnight server time (12:00 AM GMT+1). If you're still confused, here's a link that should help you compare timezones: http://www.worldtimeserver.com/curren...

If your clock is 2 hours ahead, then you need to wait 2 hours after midnight to get your bonuses. If it's 10 hours behind then it will fix itself 10 hours before midnight, your time.

I used Hearts/Gems, the game refreshed and now I lost them..
You used your "ghost" Hearts/Gems, nothing is lost. Wait until your clock reaches midnight server time to actually receive your bonuses, after that you may use them as you please.

The daily bonus window keeps popping up, what can I do about it?
Nothing, just play normally and do not spend any "ghost" bonuses until they're in your account. For example if you're meant to get 5 bonus Love points, then do not let your hearts go below 5 or the game will glitch and revert back to its previous state. Likewise, if you're 2 Gem short on buying an upgrade and the daily bonus said you received 2 Gems, you will not be able to buy that upgrade despite you having enough Gems for it.

You people are crazy, I'm not experiencing any glitches, the game works perfectly!
Lucky you. It means you're either from France or live in the same timezone. Meanwhile, the rest of us need to wait for the devs to fix it.
