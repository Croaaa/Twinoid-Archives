If you want to live... Follow me!

Assume part of your hotel looks like a big tall "L" building,
normally you would populate those rows with plant monster
and similar monsters who needs daylight.

Now assume that you have 2 Alien monsters in a "halogen light bulb"
in the queue and have 3+ "Creepy Crescent" gifts in the bank.

You put the aliens with windows on both sides next to each other.

Once one of them reaches about 35 ish, you light him up
with "Creepy Crescent" gift bonus,
then light up the next alien next to him,
who might be a bit below 35,
both are now fully satisfied at 45.

They get bonus from the gift, the window and "each other" generated light.

Very efficient way to get fully satisfied alien monster in no time.

The fact that light up they actually look like a halogen lamp
should give you a clue about how those gift items should be used...

On average, 29 base + crescent = 42 with two windows.
