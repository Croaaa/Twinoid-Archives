AllisonI have not been playing very long and I was wondering how do I give my guests light without using all of my blue things?

Guests receive light from being in rooms with windows. Rooms with two windows are just rooms without anything on the right or left of them. Windows will give your clients +4 happiness each.

One of the clients you will eventually unlock gives you a creepy crescent item when he reaches a certain happiness, much like a "cat-in-the-box".

You can also give a crescent to a Monstrous Medium (a client), which will make him want and produce light. These are the only ways.

PS, the blue things are called gems.
