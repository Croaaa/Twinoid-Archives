you have to put the monster in the lobby somewhere for another batch to come. You can either do this by putting them in rooms or create a trash room (you can build this when you get a certain number of stars, and putting a monster in here will "kill" them for gold. takes about 15 min before you can do it again.) you can also get them to max happiness to get them to leave sooner. Lower star hotel require less happiness. (i think it's 20, then it'll jump to 25, then 30, then 35, etc.) The more stars you have, the higher their MAX happiness will be.

Just keep moving your monster somewhere so the lobby is "empty" and more will arrive. It'll get to the point where all your rooms are filled, which mean you'll need to "monitor" your monster, giving them soap or toilet paper to get them slowly to max happiness. (giving hearts helps). It's either this or wait til the timer expire, and then they'll leave on their own (click on them to see how long til they leave, if you turn on that feature in the option)
