If you already have the magicians (the ones who distribute money to other clients) you can use them like this:
Remove all other clients from the cue but the inspector and magicians
fill up your cue and repeat step one
repeat filling up your cue until you have 2 or three magicians in the cue together with the inspector
Wait until your hotel is completely empty and devilish dumpsters are free, beer storage must be full.
Place the inspector
place the first magician next to the inspector and send him to the thrash quickly
place the second inspector...the third inspector...
...

By this way your inspector will become very rich and you can feed him with lots of beer.
However, you have to be very quick!

This was my strategy to get the third blue star. Of course, i spent 3 or 4 gems to fill up the cue until i had enough magicians.
