15->16 (Monster) MAGNANIMOUS MAGICIAN - Distributes 3 cash to other clients when checked in.
16->17 (Monster) SWEEPER - Likes DIRTY ROOMS. CLEANS ALL ROOMS at check in. Gains +1 happiness for every 2 rooms cleaned.
17->18 (Monster) VAMPIRE - Often seen at night, this monster hates LIGHT. If he checks out with 30 happiness or more, he'll leave you an ITEM [Creepy Crescent].
18->19 (Monster) BIG MAMA BLOB - Has a VERY short stay duration. Another client, a BLOBLING, immediately arrives in the queue if her happiness reaches 30 or more.
19->20 (Room) TURBO BOOSTER - Makes other rooms work faster.
20->21 (Monster) SNUGGLE BOMB - Naturally bubbly, he will fill your hotel with happiness if he reaches 35 or more!
