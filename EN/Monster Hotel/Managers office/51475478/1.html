Dead Cells 24h/24

The 1.4.2 update is being deployed on Android and the Web, it should be available within 4 hours. The iOS version should be available in January (Apple holidays!).

IMPORTANT: if you want to play on iOS, please do not run the game from the web, as it will update your hotel to the 1.4.2 version, making it impossible to play on iOS.
Major changes:
  • We finally fixed the infamous "Daily Reward" bug. We deeply apologize for all the troubles!
  • The 2 Obsidian Stars for high level hotels have been unlocked!
  • Twinoid achievements are now distributed properly for customization items
  • new Twinoid achievements for hotel stars
  • The Beer has been replaced by Soda. Hey, everybody likes soda, right? Well, our game is also now rated 3+ instead of 18+ in many countries.
  • Fixed many minor bugs or very rare crashes
  • Game balance adjustments
