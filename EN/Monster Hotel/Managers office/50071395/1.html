I see the Daylight Savings Time bug from Monster Motel has now become a 'feature' trait for some guests !


In the original game, if you didn't place monsters in the hotel on the day of the year when the time changed for daylight savings, they would not leave the lobby at midnight as normal, but instead become 0, 1 or 2 day guests, depending on if they were originally 1,2, or 3 day. When you placed a 0 day guest, they instantly checked out and paid, but you could still get their special bonuses, but you had to 'prep' their room so they were fully satisfied the moment they were placed. Its the same with the 30 sec guest, but you have a small window to also use stuff on them, or 'double dip' guest bonuses


I was able to get him to 30 satisfaction by putting in guests with the effects the inspector liked around his soon-to-be room, then waited until those guests were less than 30 seconds to checkout, so I could get their boost, then immediately replace them with other guests that the inspector also liked, assuming they didn't wreck the room when they left. it worked no problem, but it does require some prep work and fast clicking !
