There's just so much more that could be done with this game:

1) Shops, restaurants, and other retail units. Give each monster a personal preference on the type of food, beverages, etc. it likes best, rather than a constant 3-6pts per .
2) Vertical transport system (elevators, escalators, stairs.) Arguably, one of the funnest parts of SimTower was engineering this.
3) Natural disasters (floods, earthquakes, fires, etc.), vermin infestations, terrorist attacks
4) Objectives other than satisfying inspectors. For example, Sim Tower unlocks s based on population, the presence of certain rooms, and the attainment of specific metrics. All inspectors can be beaten in a trivial manner with , and after 22 of them, there isn't much more gameplay variety.
5) Amenities such as swimming pools, gymnasiums, etc.
And so on.
