I'd say mostly, yeah. I have three laundry rooms right now, and two TP storage. Since I very rarely use a booster on the TP, getting the second TP storage was very useful for me.

Basically, I've observed that very shortly after being served beer, a monster will request TP. They won't want anything else (soap, laundry, whatever) until after that is given.

If you keep up with the monsters' requests for TP, they're much more likely to request laundry again. Basically, if you can get a second TP storage, and keep at least the more valuable clients/monsters satisfied with TP, then they'll put your laundry rooms to good use again. If you use a lot of TP at once, you'll be generating two new TP every five minutes then, too, which helps tons.

Note that the higher a monster's happiness, the more valable their laundry, up to 97 gold at 34 happiness, in my experience. Right now they check out of my hotel after that.
