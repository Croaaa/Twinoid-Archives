Not sure if it was luck or what, but I went back to see how 54 was and got it on the first try without using any lives. I even got the power cell icon + coal for my troubles ...

My strategy was to first prioritize matching any of the 4 pairs of pesky tiles in the centre first :

  • If it was possible to dig one tile (1/2 of a pair) with existing board in one move, I'd spend one turn there
  • If two tiles in an undug pair are the same color, I would spend up to ~2 turns to dig that pair OR match some unrelated combos above them so new tiles form that can be matched more easily with the pair
  • Otherwise, spend turns making big combos underneath undug pesky tiles and hope one of the first two cases happen
  • If vertical or above-tier bombs appear, spend up to ~3 moving those into place to unreachables. I wasn't actively trying to create those, however
  • Watch out for lava. It will shuffle your board when they go down, possibly screwing up some matches in the pesky tiles in the process
On august 3, 2015
NERON

Finished 44 in one go without losing any lives as well. http://i.imgur.com/2tsenKw.png

  • Create Vertical (6 combo) or Vertical+Horizontal (7 combo) bombs in the top section, move them into place, and detonate
  • If you can't create one in the given grid with a reasonable (~5) amount of turns, make some small combos to jostle the area but don't get carried away
  • Notice how the top has slightly more space than left, right or bottom. That's why you want to focus mainly on working there
  • Discard all other bombs and use | or + only. You don't want to waste time with horizontal bombs even if you happen to make one. Those require you to either make them in the cramped left or right sections, or make them at the top and waste turns moving them down, possibly detonating them prematurely in the process
