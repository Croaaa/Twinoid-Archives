I think the general rule is that if you need lots of a certain type and there don't seem to be enough, just blow up anything that will let you make bombs and big cascades. Make enough space and more blue crystals will come. And with any luck the white ones will not be a problem. Don't waste a lot of moves getting a few extra whites when the cascade strategy will make them easier too.

I tried it just now with my one life (just finished L150 at last!) and this strategy left me almost there (just 2 blue crystals needed at the end). So with a bit of luck it should work eventually.
