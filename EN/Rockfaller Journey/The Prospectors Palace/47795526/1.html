On a probability-weighted basis, it's more like 3 out of 15, so I still have a long way to go ... and this is so incredibly tedious that it's sucking every bit of fun from the game.

I know treasure-collecting is optional, but the game actively encourages it, and as a primary gameplay objective that accounts for 773 out of 1000 Twinoid points, this annoyance cannot be disregarded.

buzzard, here are some possible ways to make this collect-a-thon a bit more bearable:

1) Narrow the gap between common and rare treasures' odds of appearance
2) Narrow the range of levels that each set appears in (for example, instead of Levels 1-15 having 3 possible sets (=15 treasures to collect), spread them out across 1-5, 6-10, and 11-15 having 1 set each)
3) Reduce the probability of getting fruits (since they appear everywhere)
4) Increase their appearance rate so players can get more than 1-2 treasures per game
5) Identify new treasures (0x collected) as such on the game board, so players can choose not to waste turns collecting them
6) Allow players to keep treasures even if they don't win the level
7) Allow players to keep treasures collected during Sugar Crush (like Sugar Drops)
