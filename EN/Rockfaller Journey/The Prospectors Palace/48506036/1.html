We're going to keep on digging!

Unsatisfied with just travelling to the centre of the Earth Otto Grumple and his robotic assistant are planning to keep digging and pop out the other side (in China presumably)!

Stay tuned for new levels and new challenges in the coming week (or so).

There'll be:

  • New levels
  • New treasures
  • New mechanics (well at least one...)

Here's a sneak peek of what's to come...


Where are we with production?

We're currently working out whether or not we can technically release the game on each platform separately (web, then Android, then iOS) or whether we need to wait until Apple gives us the nod and release them all at the same time. In any case we're submitting the game to Apple as I write this, and it shouldn't take too long to get through their review process... We'll keep you posted.

In the mean time. The French server had a lot of fun with a little game that we organised so I thought I'd see if you were interested too. Here's the low down:

Creative writing competition:

Imagine that you're a journalist who was present for the launch of the Mole O'Matic. Write an article that reports on the beginning of the adventure.

  • The best (or only) 3 entries will receive 500 gold to spend in the game!
  • Here's the link to the french results (if you speak a bit of francais, or wanted to give Google Translate a whirl).
  • Post your entries in the comments of this news.

Best of luck and happy digging!
