I just beat it for the first time, after being stuck there for a little over two weeks. So I might not be the best person to give advice, but anyway: on the time that I managed to beat it, I made a point of trying to match 6+ as often as possible so as to get bombs that would break as many of the rocks as I could. I started on one side of the field and worked my way to the bottom, then worked across, so that it was easier to get in at the rocks than trying to come down from the top.
It DID work and I cleared it with several moves in hand... but I think I got a bit lucky with the tiles that I was dealt.

Good luck.
