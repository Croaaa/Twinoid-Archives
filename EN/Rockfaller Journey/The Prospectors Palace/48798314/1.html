I just had a go at level 50 and cleared it, with a mediocre score of 26525 and 3 moves remaining.
This is what I was trying to do while I was doing it:

1) Need to make space, so try to set off early matches where as many of the pieces are touching the rocks as possible. Avoid setting off anything in the middle of the column that won't actually damage a rock, if you can.

2) Once space has been cleared - about one layer of rock on each side, about half a screen high - then focus on maximum piece destruction per turn. Don't worry about combos (unless that clears more pieces per turn) or trying to match the "target" pieces. Just clear as many pieces as you can, each turn. The only way to reach the target is to spawn lots of new pieces of the colours that you need, and that's not going to happen unless you are clearing out a LOT of everything else as well.

3) As the turn counter gets near the end, you will have to focus on meeting your targets. If there are not a lot of pieces of the type that you want on screen then that means that there will be lots of the wrong types, so look at setting up 6+ matches so as to get the more useful bomb types and be able to clear space. Cleared space = useful pieces spawn.

4) Don't worry too much about the lava. If you're not trying to set up combos then it shouldn't really interfere too much with whatever you're doing. Most of my matching was done near the middle of the screen as that's where the space opened up for me.

....that's the approach that I took, and it worked for me. Maybe try playing with those things in mind and see how it works out for you?
Either way, good luck.
