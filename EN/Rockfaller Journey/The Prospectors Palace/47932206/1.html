I went back and did 112 again. The first two times, I was missing 2 and 1 blocks respectively. I succeeded on the third try http://i.imgur.com/PlZfuJ2.png

The key here is to ignore the fact that the level seems to be designed so your goal is to move those top blocks down somehow. Those blocks are more likely to end up nowhere near the bottom ones when they come down and trying to control them is a waste of turns

The strategy I would recommend for this level is to create 7 or 8 block combos for horizontal or vertical-horizontal bombs at the bottom. The top 6 blocks should come down on their own just fine and you can deal with them using bombs. The main focus are the 6 at the bottom. If you can get your three bombs created and lined up for them, you're good to go
