I just went back and tried it again - won with 15 moves left. Just make lots and lots of vertical bombs. Don't bother breaking the stone.

I think I also took out a couple with a small bomb that happened to fall into one of the bottom 'funnels', but basically the plan is to keep matching six and using the resulting bombs to destroy blue crystals.

With so much space and no annoyances, you can usually match six every turn or two, and hopefully half of them will be vertical.
