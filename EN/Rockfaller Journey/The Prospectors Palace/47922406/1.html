I went back and did this one. It ate two lives but I got it on the third

  • As with all score-goal levels, combos are king. Taking the time to figure out where blocks will land makes a real difference.
  • Since we are short of space, first you should pick one side of the blockage and focus on clearing it up to around half. Top, left, right, bottom. Just pick the one with the most available matches, work on that and ignore the other sides. In order to make combos, you need a reasonably big space, not two or more smaller ones with a blockage in between
  • Once you get about half cleared, focus on combos. These will get you extra points. Clearing the entire screen of the grey blocks is not necessary. Don't get carried away
  • Do not worry about bombs. They won't get you extra points and are mediocre at clearing blocks

Bombs are meh for mining out the stone blocks. If they happen to appear, I'll use them to my advantage but I'm not wasting any turns to make them on levels like this. Also, all except the highest tier bomb will do nothing for your score

That's not to say bombs are useless in general. In later levels with the gold blocks or ice mining in the last biome, they are invaluable
