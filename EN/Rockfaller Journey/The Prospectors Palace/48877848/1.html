New levels and a few little tweeks for the mobile versions!

As you can probably tell, the new levels are officially available for everyone! If you're on iOS then you should receive a notification to update the application in the next few hours. Have fun and please let me know if there is something that's not doing what it should.

What to expect in the new version of Rockfaller Journey:

- A new world to explore!
- 30 extra levels!
- New treasures and title to dig up and unlock!

For the android users out there don't forget that you can now log in to Google's Play Game Services and unlock your twinoid achievements on your google account too! The Apple equivalent (Game Center) will be available in the next update with another new world and even more levels.

Stay tuned for more information!
