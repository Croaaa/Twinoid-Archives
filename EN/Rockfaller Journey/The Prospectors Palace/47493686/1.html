I think Rockfaller Journey is a well made game and all, but this isn't why I became a Twinoid fan. In the past Motion Twin made interesting games which used simple mechanics to create an interesting game. Kingdom was a strategy game in which you had to manage your units and use them to expand and defend your kingdom. The core gameplay, however, was nothing else but a matchmaking system. It was so simple and yet so addicting.
Then there were games like alphabounce which was a simply breakout clone with upgrades. But what made THAT one interesting was that it offered an explorable map and a quest system. There was enough to do to keep people playing for ages.
And then hordes. Fantastic new concept of gameplay I haven't yet seen anywhere. Played it for years until it became boring.

And then, after a long hiatus, they release a new game...which is...nothing but another candy crush clone no one needs. I always praised and loved the company's creativity and then they dish out this simple clone. At first I was willing to give it a chance, despite it obviously looking like a mere clone.
I thought: "This is MT, they are bound to have added a creative and fun feature somewhere in here" but then I played, and played, and played...and came to the conclusion that it's just all there is. Candy crush. No explorable map, no quests, no depth at all. Only a line of levels you need to beat. wow.
Sure, you can collect "treasures" but I have yet to see what that's good for other than showing off your collection. A boring feature if you are like me who doesn't give a damn about that.

So this is what I have waited for all this time? To see MT become a mainstream game developer? What a shame.

I hope the game will be successful and all, but man am I disappointed. And not because I don't like match 3 games, I am actually pretty indifferent about them...no...I am disappointed because I expected more from MT. I know they could have done better.

Even Minitroopers felt more original than this.
