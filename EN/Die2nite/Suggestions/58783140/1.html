Hi all. I have kept this server alive for several years now and to my pleasure see that it is still used enthusiastically. That makes it so much harder to take the next step; stopping the server.

If there is anyone among you (perhaps Twinoid?) who wants to take over ownership of the server and the domain, I am willing to engage in a discussion on this. Sorry for the bad news. It is really great to see this product still used. I hope you understand my reason for taking this step.

Even if we find a benevolent soul that can continue this project, it might stumble on a new problem:

The FDTD database/code-thingie will have to go to a different server, and in order to be used, it will have to be accessed manually (links on the Directory are added by Admins, and we know how often they show up to do that kind of stuff).

Alternatively, I propose to those stubborn players like me to start using and getting familiar with a french version map toll called BigBroth'Hordes, which allows us to connect our die2nite towns/accounts with it.

http://bbh.fred26.fr/

All you have to do is:

  • Conn. autres version button
  • At Serverz -> Anglais
  • At Clé -> paste Your identifier for external sites hash code (found at your Soul -> Settings
  • At Nome -> your nick

and there you go. I believe it has all the map tolls you might need. Its the best alternative we have now, as MapViewer is also discontinued and outdated.
