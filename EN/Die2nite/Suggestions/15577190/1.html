kiwiluke

i think that since the introduction of survivalist 100% camping chances, camping has become boring, there is no longer the thrill of logging in to see if you survived or not, however, i also see the benefits of this in regards to BP camping, it does suck to die early on in a good town trying to camp for BPs and then have to wait 3 weeks plus for your friends to die off, so i have 2 proposals on how i think survivalists could be nerfed to bring back some camping excitement while still allowing for BP camping

1: survivalists can only camp twice at 100%, after that the get the same 90% cap as other classes

or

2: survivalists can camp at 100% up till day 15, after that they get the 90% cap,

these changes may also bring about a more mixed range of heroes in meta towns, rather than the survivalist heavy mix we generally see at the moment
