1) Jerry cans: Cars don't run on water... different fuel would be better... it's own item like "Gas Can", which would, itself be a rare regular drop like a cat or something.
Also It's an interesting idea but I don't think anyone in their right mind would end up building this if it ran on a jerry can. Think about it. Each jerry with filter/purifier can net like 5-9 Water. Assuming the loss of 9 rations that's 54 AP to go 18 km in your van.

Attack Points: I assume you mean it can kill x# of zombies before breaking down? you're numbers are kind of high, and it's kind of a weird mechanic... what happens? you enter the zone and all the zombies die? You select each zombie to kill?

Easier solution for this, car gives +10 CP or something like that.

Slots are also kind of unbalanced, with a belt and manbag this would potentially allow me to bring back what? 20 something items? The 2 Heavies is a bit more reasonable.

Also found only in buildings... how do I get it back to town to "Fix it up"? Do I carry it? Is it stationary until fixed? Carrying it is silly, and a stationary object that needs to be fixed up that is basically an enterable object is, AFAIK outside the realm of the game programming right now. We haven't seen any massive changes in game mechanics like that with the exception of ruins, and that in itself is more like a mini-game not a super rare item.

Vehicles have been brought up in the past, more likely vehicles I might add such as bicycles or skateboards. Personally I'd rather MT work on something to enhance the flavor of the game for everyone, rather than implement super rare/impractical things that won't ever get used
