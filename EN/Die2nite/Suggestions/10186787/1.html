Home Improvement
Sink
Required Items to Build: 6 AP, 1 x Water Cooler, 1 x Copper Pipe, 1 x Wrought Iron(?)
Requires: Atleast 1 water ration in chest to use
Useable once per day
What it does: It cleans your wounds to prevent you from getting an infection at night. Lasts until the attack.
Can't use if not wounded.

Description when used:
In a panic, you quickly turn on the sink and rub the soap (which you made out of various things) all over your wound, and rinse it with water. You then give a sigh of relief. (open to better descriptions)
I made a status image
Status description: You have suffered a ghastly wound. Luckily it has been cleaned and will delay the infection for a while longer.

The wrought Iron has a question mark on it because I was unsure whether to add it because I thought it might be a bit too much. Also the "I made a status image" is clickable.
