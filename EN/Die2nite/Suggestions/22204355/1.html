Speaking from a third perspective - it can look warm, toasty hot in front of the fire that is other people who are all in the same boat of happiness that is a meta.
But for others, it looks like being in a shared prison cell.
You can't play here, you have to play there. You mustn't do this. Make sure you do that. Don't even think about doing this, that or the other, because you'll be forever exiled by everyone that is the community of this game. We will never forget.
Some people don't want to join because they want to be free to join whatever town they like - not be restricted to a jump pattern.
Others may be afraid of rejection - walking into a group of people you don't know, just for them to decide that they don't like the way you speak, the way you think or the way you act.
Maybe they think you're stupid because you don't know some things about the game yet.

That's why some people probably don't want to join metas.

I tend to destroy groups by accident somehow as well.
I'm not good news in that respect.
