Actually you get 4 tokens per day on Arkadeo.

You get another daily token per day if you have purchased more tokens at least once.

To me, the fastest way to get Kadeos (points to exchange for MTpasses) is the Solo Mission Mode.

Each completed Mission gives around 100 kadeos. Most of the mission steps can easily be beaten. It gets tougher later on, but if you are average-ish on every game you won't have any problems.

There are Kadeos hidden inside the games and Capman (Level Up and League version) is not the best one to get those points.

The solo mission steps also have games with the League version, so you are also playing at the League and will get bonus points at the end of the Season based on the final position you got on the League.

So the real math here is:

1500ish kadeos from solo Mission (granted at the moment you complete a misson!)

1000ish hidden kadeos obtained inside the games

+ ?? Bonus kadeos from League position (depends which League you are. I'm getting 800ish on Platinum League now.

Around 2000 Kadeos per Season (21 days).
