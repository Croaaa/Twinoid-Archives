  • this is not the main game. The french version is.
  • there is still sufficient revenue from the current player base of veteran players.
  • you don't see the authors of this game advertising it outside the platform. Which means they are satisfied with how things are already.

Now when it comes to real players, there are some game mechanics that prevent inactive players to join a town.
