ItsNathan

One way to make this work is to make this theft announced on the Registry, along with who performed the action, what they took, and from who. In this way, the town can easily discuss if they approve of the action or not and make an appropriate response.

Otherwise, all you're doing is giving grievers a powerful tool to use against a town while trying to prevent griefing. Imagine a griefer with the power to take whatever he wants even if the person thinks its safe and locked up while they're in town?

However, even with my suggested improvement, it could still easily be used in combination with the Manipulator ability to grief.


What's most important, and a lot of people usually forget, is that to suggest a true anti-grief ability, you have to think "what can a griefer do with this ability?" Otherwise, a lot of the time you're just giving them a new powerful tool to use against a town.
