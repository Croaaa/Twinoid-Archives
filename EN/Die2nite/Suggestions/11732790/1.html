So, I thought about a new ability, that comes with a new point system...

Guardians have Escort Points (EP), which don't get replenished everyday.
Escort Points are used to carry the other resident around without using their AP.
You start with 6, and can only replenish them by taking steroids.
Everytime you take steroids, youchoose to replenish AP or EP.
You will get the drugged status no matter the choice you made.
You toggle the use of EP on and off.


So with steroid+vodka you can get on a 24 AP rescue/scavenge mission. Or you can get addicted and do more...

Not sure whether, the escorted should retain their CP while EPs are used.

I don't actually expect this to ever get implemented or even discussed, I'm just sharing some thoughts. I was thinking about how to make guardians better without straying much from the "I am a defender type" style.
I already love guardians the most, and don't think of them as underpowered or anything like that. There are just not well suited for the meta playstyle
This could as well be a saloon thread, but it's still a valid suggestion.
