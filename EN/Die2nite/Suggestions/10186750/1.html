Embrace the 40 person coalitions as such make it easier for the 40 persons to jump together.

Create a fifth town type (small/distant/HC/private/40 person) that functions the same rules as the normal towns.
-Require 40 people in a coalition to join.
-Give those towns a separate ranking board.
-Give them a top town/top 15 distinctions as per standard (Or make it unique... what ever!)
-If you REALLY want to ensure people don't JUST play those towns, Limit each person to... say 2 or 3 40 person towns per season [Based on how long the actual season is... *stares at the calendar*] (Just like a counter that gets reset at the start of each "season") --That will give us two good shots at this leaderboard as we do like to take breaks from the super serious towns from time to time, and those of us not "leaders" in our 40 person towns, do like to take leadership roles in random ones. This would get us our fix but also keep us mobile within the community.

Implement a 20-30 person cap on the normal towns to help dissuade the 40 persons from dominating that board. It would also give a chance to Medium sized Coalitions a way to form up before they hit the 40 person towns.

Heck many of us lobbied for a second leader board when the cap happened. Personally I do not want to see newer players discouraged from failing to "rank" because they're running against people who have been playing for... more than a year now (Almost a year and a half I think for some of us beta players).

Honestly I think MT needs to embrace the 40 person coalition. I think they also need to give us reasons to step outside our 40 person groups, but don't force us to.

Dangle that carrot in front of me for Pete's sake!
