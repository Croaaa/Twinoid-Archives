So I had an idea for the next clash of sorts.

Coalitions of 5-10 people, join a small town and see how far they can take it.

It might get some new players involved in the game who other wise wouldn't have been interested.

I sort of did this with a small town on a personal challenge from another meta member. I was challenged to take a small town as far as we could go. We managed to take it to 12 days, not too shabby.

There wouldn't be one big large launch date like CotT, but teams could launch throughout the week and see how long they could take their towns.

This isn't necessarily a fair judge of leadership skills as much of it would involve who you got lucky enough to join your town, but more so a game wide attempt to bring more/fresh blood into the community.

Thoughts?
