This ability can be used by selecting the guardian shield profession tool from the rucksack. Leapfrog

I'm actually just starting out as a hero myself (I'm on my third hero incarnation) and I love the Guardian class, but I already can see why people think they don't have enough. Sure, I love the idea of a potential 7 CP, and a potential +10 (or was it 15?) defence to a town, but I think compared to the things the other classes bring to the table the Guardian just doesn't quite do enough. It's nice to be able to move about a little more freely than everyone else (except the Scout, but they can't do much while camouflaged) but rescues, which is another appealing aspect of the class, are sporadic and often complicated, especially in distant towns.

I'm happy with the direction of the class, and the idea of its utility, I just can see why people think it's not as good as the others, and I think it either needs a bit more of what it has already, or some new functionality - like:

1) Being able to use their shield to kill or stun zombies,

2) Permanently/semi-permanently increase the base CP in a zone, in addition to their own control points.

or

3) Being able to modify their shield (with a car door, maybe, if the town can spare it?) to increase CP even further.
