Hello citizens of die2nite!

After seeing a suggested by R3DD3R to make a thread to keep track of all suggestions in the Discussion forum.
I Packersfan250 have decided that I would make one and keep it up to date.

I plan to update this list every Saturday with new info and new suggestions.

A big thanks again to R3DD3R, for the idea of making this thread as well as editing it and another thanks to Capital And Tintedwho helped with the editing.

Please refrain from posting in this thread, I would like to keep it clean so that the most recent list is always easily visible as the next post, I will re-post this OP every 11 post to keep it up at the top of each page.
.

Warning
This thread may contain some spoilers of content found in die2nite.
There were many many great suggestions i came across this month and I hope this list makes it easier to keep track of suggestions in the future.

And finally, Their is a score system on this, the score’s were determined by a number 1-10, one being the lowest possible score ( a bad idea ) and ten being the highest ( holy smokes great idea )
1-3= Bad suggestions
4-6=Alright suggestions
7-8 Good suggestions
9-10-The best suggestions ever?


, Score was determined by the feedback of players, and suggestions were altered from their original post based on community feedback in the thread. feel free to discuss any of the suggestions listed here in another thread.
