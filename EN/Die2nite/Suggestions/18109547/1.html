Zumbi

if the town defenses with watchmen is enough to hold the horde, then everyone has 95% chance of survival, as stated in the w-man page

to that 95% is added any bonuses or penalties you hold, for example, ghouls and guardians have +5 survival chance as showed next to the human icon on your shield/ghoul face when you join the watch

therefore guardians and ghouls can have up to 100% survival chance(if they have no other statuses) when the combined defense of w-man is enough to hold the horde, individual defense makes no difference in this equation

other status like hooked, wounded, terrorized, infected etc... makes your survival chances even lower

be specially careful with drunk status, for it shows a bonus to your survival chance but it turns into hangover before the watch, which means you will have a penalty and not a bonus by the time attack happens


the number of zeds shown attacking each watchman is always, the number of zeds that breached the defense divided by the total number of watchmen

so lets say, with 210 zed breach and 3 watchmen you will see a text saying watchmen xxxx were attacked by 70 zeds (in case he gets wounded or die)
if nothing happens no text is shown

as i said before, personal defense makes no difference, so a tamer holding washing machine and 5 torch, will have 210 defense but will still have only 95% (max) survival chance, but a guardian with empty hands will have only 40 defense, but still have a survival chance of 100%

in the example given before, you could get a message saying the tamer was killed by 70 zeds (even though his defense was 210) but the guardian would not die... in case he got wounded though u would get a msg saying he was wounded by 70 zeds too


when the town has enough defense to hold the horde chances of getting wounded/terrorized are 10% as stated in the help, if the town has not enough defense even with watchmen, the chances of wound/terror are supposedly higher
