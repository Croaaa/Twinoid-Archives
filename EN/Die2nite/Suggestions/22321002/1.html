Shaman is, well, useless in random towns. Souls aren't worth jack to random towns, and healing isn't rival to the benefits awarded by other classes. That's why I suggest a new passive Shaman buff.

Each day, the shaman has a 10% chance of detecting someone being a ghoul. This is per person. Example: 3 ghouls in town. Each one has a 10%(?) chance of detection. If it happens, a icon appears by their name in the citizen list for the rest of the town. This only shows to the shaman, and has no gameplay effect.

This would have little to no effect in meta towns.

I was just thinking, perhaps something like this:

The Shaman will have a 30 - 40% chance to know if there is a Ghoul in the vicinity - they won't know who it is though.
They then can, once a day, Search a Citizen's Soul... - either being in town with them, or being outside in the same zone. This might give a 50-50 chance of revealing if they are the Ghoul or not.

Failure, Shaman checking: You try to reach into the depths of the citizen's soul, but something stops you. Whatever this force is, it is not allowing you in - you'll just have to try again tomorrow, when their guard is down...
Failure, Citizen checked: You feel a strange pressure, almost as though someone is trying to see inside your head! You try not to think about it, and get on with what you were doing - hopefully it will leave you alone...

Success, Shaman sees it is not a Ghoul: As you reach down into the person's thoughts and memories, their core burns with a bright flame. This person has done no wrong in this life - this is not the evil you are searching for...
Success, Citizen searched: You suddenly feel the presence of another consciousness inside your mind! As it looks through your past and your future, you feel warm and safe for once, almost as though there's a power keeping you from harm...

Success, Shaman sees it is a Ghoul: As you plunge into the citizen's very essence, everything feels hostile, a pitch-black ember glowing at their centre. The whole soul gives off a painful, fear-inspiring aura of the undead - this person is no longer truly alive inside...
Success, Ghoul searched: The Shaman searches through your soul for any secrets they can find - you are certain they must know about what you are! There's only two options left now - defend yourself and your secret, or face the town as the true horror you really are...
