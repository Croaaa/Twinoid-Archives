its happened in 1 town i was previously in, and i feel it was about to happen again in the town i'm currently in. maybe its because i'm still playing ND towns with noobs who don't know what to do or how to deal with ghouls.

i think that maybe people decide to just shun the ghouls instead of sacrificing 5 AP to kill because its easier. complaining has been the answer to everything up until ghouls showed up and it takes no AP to do so.

at the same time, how often does a player go ghoul? in all the towns i've played so far in season 6 (let's say 10 towns) i think only 3-4 of them had someone go ghoul. those 3-4 towns were also the shortest lived. what i'm getting at is, there are a lot of players out there who still aren't clear how to deal with ghouls, and since its not every single town that gets ghouls, there isn't much chance for said players to learn how to deal with them.

like i said if there was a distinction, i think that would be a good enough motive to go out and handle a ghoul. not like a rare distinction, just a distinction. i mean, you get distinctions for killing zombies, and they just stand there and take the beating. you have to actually do some detective work trying to identify the ghoul, then track them down and have 5 AP left to kill them. that's a lot of work!

and by killing a ghoul, you prevent player deaths, and who's to say the ghoul's next victim would've been the hero calling the shots, or the helpful resident who goes out of their way to help out others? ghouls can seriously turn the tide by killing only a few key players in town. players can turn that tide back if they deal with a ghoul early on enough, i just think (from what i've seen) they need a little motivation.

last points, there's a cannibalism distinction! whether you turn ghoul or not, you get a distinction for eating human meat!

and finally, ghouls get hero points for eating people. that's one hell of a motivation for people to BECOME ghouls, and be good ones at that! "i get free hero days for screwing over my town? screw it, why not?" i feel there should be a little more balance. just a little. (at least i didn't suggest getting hero days for killing a ghoul!)
