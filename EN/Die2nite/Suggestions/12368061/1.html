GOAL: The Mayor should be a role that leads the town, and fights off trolls and griefers.

ABILITIES:
The Mayor's shuns counts twice, however is no longer anonymous.

The Mayor can see the last activity a player has done, even if it was wiped with the hero skill or smoke bombs were used or someone stole something without getting caught. This can be only used once per day, and is balanced because you have to know who to investigate.

The Mayor can send messages to everyone in the town.
