Heroic Reborn is way too overpowered. Resetting all Heroic Actions? I could perhaps agree with being able to change Professions after Day 14, but not resetting your Heroic Actions.

Extra Power could go up to 3 zeds instead of 4, but I don't find 4 to be grossly overpowered. Rescuer should only go out to 3km instead of 4, and I don't like the idea of blocking construction projects, whether anonymously or not.

For Luckier Find, do you mean that you can use Seeker twice during the town, or that you will find two of the same item when you use Seeker?

Escaping is also too overpowered as it is. Perhaps staggering the chances of injury when you flee for the first flee attempt only (2nd and subsequent flee attempts have 100% chance of injury). For example:
10% if zeds have 1 more control point than humans,
20% if zeds have 2 more CP
35% if zeds have 3 more CP
60% if zeds have 4 more CP
90% if zeds have 5 more CP
100% if zeds have 6 or more CP

I kind of like Mobile Breaker. There's already Resourcefulness, Second Breakfast, Preventive Medicine, and Architect, so why not have one more free item? It would mean that all 7 slots in your chest would be filled after you unlock Architect (if I've counted correctly), but there's room for it.
