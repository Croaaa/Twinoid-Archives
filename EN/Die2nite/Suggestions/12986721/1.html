Okay, it isn't really about super-strength... That was more of a marketing slogan to catch your eye. It worked, didn't it?

Anyway. My suggestion?

Guardians can carry two heavy objects.

It's very basic, very rudimentary, and doesn't seem like anything big or important. Its definitely not something you would give me a community distinction for

But it is very feasible. Think about it. Guardians are protectors. They are big and bulky and are obviously strong. They should be able to lug two pieces of furniture behind them.

Guardians need more. I realize that recently there seem to be countless suggestions for new Guardian perks. But this is simple, yet effectively helpful.

Comments are welcome.
