When seeing the main screen with the people in the desert and the noisy image, CPU usage is at 100% (even on multiple cores!). When switching to the forum, CPU usage ist 0%. The flash plugin seems to use up ALL available cpu power.

Many people just leave their brower window open for several hours, which results in hundreds of extra watts in power consumption and heat production per person for absolutely nothing. My CPU uses 40 more watt just for this. Most people probably are not even aware of it. If multiplied with the amount of players in the english and french community, this adds to a huge amount of energy that is wasted for nothing and which is not good from an environmental aspect.

It would be good, if the flash plugin is tweaked so that it does not use full CPU power. Optimizing it for less energy consumption should not be a big thing, it rather looks like dirty programming that causes it right now.
