On february 4, 2013
UncleNature

Ruins often get depleted on the first day of them being visited. This would be an extension of their uses; fortification. Every ruin except explorable ruins would have the option to convert to outpost. This would disable ability to search the building and the zone it is in but would allow it to be entered like the town. Once inside there would be a list of options like in the town, these would be Construction sites, bank, gates and home.

These would all work the same as in the main town but on a smaller scale

Home:
Only upgradable as far as house and no heroic upgrades.
Chest has 2 slots and is not effected by being a hero etc but can have boxes added.

Bank:
Identical.

Town gates:
Identical, other than the expeditions could be planned to start and end at the fortified ruins as well town. (this would change at the town as well)

Construction sites:
Wall
The wall is needed for the ruin to survive the hordes (Cost 10 Twisted planks 10, wrought Iron, 20AP)
-Barbed Wire
-Armour plating
-Armour plating V2
-Armour plating V3
Watchtower
>Upgrade Level 1
Every morning, all the zombies within 1 zones of the ruin are detected. + 10 defense (cost: 5 twisted planks 10AP)
>Upgrade Level 2
Every morning, all the zombies within 3 zones of the ruin are detected. + 10 defense (cost: 5 Twisted planks, 2 wrought Iron 10AP)
>Upgrade Level3
Every morning, all the zombies within 5 zones of the ruin are detected. + 10 defense (cost: 5 twisted planks 4 Wrought Iron 15AP)
Foundations
-False town
unlocked by uncovering the blueprint as normal but available at all fortified ruins INSTEAD of the town

All construction built here would give half of their defensive value to the town and the attack on the ruin would be around 10% of the town's attack.

Blueprints can only be used in the main town as is currently the case.

If the wall is not built before the attack then the ruin will return to its previous state and depleted (regardless of its condition before being fortified)

In chaos mode the ruin will have an attack worth half of that of the town.

The Idea would be that these building would be:
-Enables access to far corners of the world beyond.
-Gives extra defense to town.
-Gives a place for shunned citizens to go.
-Gives a place for ghouls to go, if discovered.
