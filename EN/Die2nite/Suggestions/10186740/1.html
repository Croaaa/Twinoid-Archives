Everyone's always so focused on the guardian's control points. In an organized meta, the guardian's CP is useless because one good weapon means tamers kill all zeds on the map. In random towns, a lone guardian's CP is almost useless because he's still all alone, and if the town is bad enough, then most zones will be red (and thus have 5+ zombies) and thus be beyond a lone guardian's control.

Where a guardian's CP becomes boss is in a 5-man coalition, where a guardian or two becomes a boon to his friends, and the guardian has friends to help him, too.

But what people constantly forget about the guardian is he provides +5 defense to the town and has +1 personal defense. With end game camping strategies, a guardian has the effect of +1 DO in his chest, leaving one more DO to be passed out to the rest of the town. In hardcore towns, that's also 5 less DO's that need to be in the bank to risk being destroyed.

Think of how many times you've had someone die in town because 5 - 10 zombies got in town? Wouldn't you have loved to have a couple guardians to boost that final defense instead of gambling with the attack spread?

Everyone's so worried about "nonessential classes" being survivalists to reduce water consumption, but when you think about it, how important is that water rationing to an organized meta? Rationing can be beaten, Tamer town in CotT stopped rationing by the end of the first week. Most other towns do have survivalists to help absorb the blow, and with regular rationing, it's still usually not a problem by the end of the town.

The benefit of swapping 2 survivalists with 2 guardians is a little extra protection in the early days of the town, when there are no weapons and the attacks are small, those +2 CP's and that +10 town defense goes a long way. They're also useful at end of town when you're trying to split DO's to everyone, that's two less DO's you need to boost everyone to max.

And in hardcore towns, 2 guardians means 10 less DO's are needed in the bank, and thus can be removed instead of left there to potentially be destroyed.

Yeah, guardian control points are pretty useless to an organized meta, but a little town defense can go a long way.
