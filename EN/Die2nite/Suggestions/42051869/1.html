Here's something that slightly bugs me. It doesn't come up very often, but when it does, it's just mildly annoying.



So it's Day 8 or 10 or 12, okay? And this isn't a meta town or anything like that. In fact, it's a town that's fairly crappy and only 10-14 are left alive.


So I start to build up my house, make it stronger.

I take heed to use my "second wind" power (for the six AP needed to build a hovel ) and my "lucky find" power (for the plank and iron needed for a shack/house).


And then someone starts a discussion forum topic about how I'm "wasting AP and resources".












....What?

Pardon me?



First of all, I got the six AP needed for my hovel from my SECOND WIND power. That's MY power that I get from being a hero and I'm free to do with the AP whatever I want to do.

I can see and understand someone getting mad if I'm wasting NORMAL AP (AKA, AP you get normally each day, or from drinking, or from eating)....but THIS AP, the "Second Wind" AP is AP that town should NEVER EXPECT TO HAVE (in my opinion). If I wasn't a hero, then I wouldn't be able to put that AP to better use either, because it wouldn't even exist then....don't tell me I'm wasting AP when that's my personal, perk of being a hero AP.

If it's a meta town or a MMJ town or a town that has promise of going far and doing well...then, yes, I'll gladly even put my bonus AP into other stuff..
..but when the town is already winding down and not going to get any better than it is and it's been like that for a few days... don't accuse me of wasting AP. I'm free to use that AP however I want.









And then we have the whole "wasting resources" accusation.
Uh, no, that was my hero power too. I USED MY POWER TO FIND THAT PLANK AND THAT IRON OUT OF THIN AIR....

...AND you even got ONE EXTRA OUT OF THE DEAL, since that package finds THREE things. So if anything, you should be thanking me.

Again, that is my hero perk power and it's stuff you'd never have even had otherwise if I wasn't one.
If I'm taking stuff out of the bank that others found, and using that stuff, then YES, I can be seen as wasting resources....but this is MY power. How is it wasting resources? I could have DIED WITHOUT USING IT...would it have been wasting resources then? I could have RAN OUT OF HERO DAYS BEFORE USING IT...it wasting resources then? I COULD NOT HAVE THAT HERO POWER UNLOCKED YET...is it wasting resources then? I COULD HAVE JUST NEVER USED IT AT ALL...since I don't ever use all of my hero powers in a town. It wasting resources then?
No?
Well then just pretend I NEVER USED IT.




Anyway, I just needed to vent. Maybe I'm the one in the wrong here. Maybe it IS and CAN still be taken as being selfish.

But just to be (more) clear, I'm not talking about doing any of this in the first 8 days (or so)...or doing it if there are more than 15 people still alive.
I'm talking about times like right now...when there's about 12 left alive and it's day 10.
When it's that far along and I build a precious hovel or shack...don't accuse me or say I've been wasting AP or resources when I've been TRYING NOT TO by using my own personal power reserves.


I don't care who you are, second wind power is NOT ANY AP THAT ANY TOWN SHOULD HAVE EXPECTED TO HAVE.
