Hello there,
I'm very new here. I've just started playing and met already a number of fresh people, who are learning their way around. It's possible that it's being suggested again and again - I've read the suggestion tracker didn't find anything like I want to suggest, so here's my idea is a "King of the hill" game time.
It's a small map, single player only. He gains no soul points from playing there (maybe single one for each day). He controls all forty citizens. If player has hero days, he can choose any number of citizens as regular residents or heroes with profession. Each hero would use hero days. No used hero days distinctions are learned while in this mode.

In the nutshell fresh players (maybe experienced ones too) would be able to test/learn some survival strategies. If anyone is willing to flush a huge number of hero days in there - just let them. If someone decides to have 10 heroes, let them pay up for this.

So everyone could check out and learn the simple and straightforward way how to survive. What happens if you are not exploring. How to explore properly. What to build. New players would be able to dive and and learn A LOT in short time.

That's the major points I've thought off for now. How often something like this is being proposed?
