I'm still in the dreaded small towns where, from what I've seen, about 8-12 people die usually outside on the first day and around 10-15 usually die on day 4 due to dehydration. I'd like to suggest the ability to take one or two of the inactive players and "escort" them (more like tie them up and drag them) to remote parts of the small maps. They could be tied to ruins and serve as distractions for the zombies, thus slowing the zombie progression toward the town.

Yes this would be considered a forced sacrifice, but it would only be done to players on day 2 or 3. And it would only be possible if the inactive player
- didn't use any AP on day 1 (housing upgrades don't count, I'm referring to construction and exploration AP)
- didn't communicate in the forums

And there would have to be around 5+ players who would vote that the inactive person be sacrificed.

This would make the inactive players useful for once, and it would give active players some satisfaction.

Please consider adding this.
