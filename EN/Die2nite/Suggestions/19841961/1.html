Scrimshaw

The empty water cooler, once filled with water rations, does not have distinct selection options to either use the water from the bottle, or to replenish your own AP, when the water cooler is not filled completely and a player has reduced AP.

As example, today I was holding the water cooler with 2 x water rations inside it, and had another water ration in my inventory and what I was trying to do was fill the bottle with the ration to put 3 x rations inside it. Instead, while at 4 AP, the action caused me to drink the water. There was only one action hyperlink, and I think this needs updated. There should always be two hyperlinks for a partially filled water cooler bottle while holding a water ration (or more) -- to either drink water, or to fill the water cooler with the ration.

Related, the Water Cooler when filled does not fill the squirt gun, which is also a stupid flaw. The water cooler should be considered a multiple water ration source capable of filling plastic bags, squirt guns, etc., not just a source to drink water from.

Highly suggest this gets updated in the next patch, and that other players support these changes.
