1. Riot Shield Group Protection

For every additional guardian over 1 in a zone, each guardian's CP increases by 1 CP.

E.g 2 guardians in a zone contributes a total of 10 CP.

2. Riot Shield Bash

Can be used up to 3x per day.

50% chance to kill a zombie base. Each additional guardian in the zone increases the chance to kill by 10%.
