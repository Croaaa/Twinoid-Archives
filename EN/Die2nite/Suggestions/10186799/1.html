Some important points :
The developers at Motion-Twin have stated several times that they do not like petitions. In the best case they ignore them.
The new forums offer many things that the old ones didn't have.

The old forums cannot be improved so easily because they are based on obsolete code.

Even though I don't like the layout of the new forums and I have posted some suggestions to improve them, I really like the new features.

Among the new features offered by the new forums:
The ability to edit your messages for 10 minutes after posting them, as long as nobody else has replied in the meantime.
A new part of the forum open to non-heroes.
A button to quickly quote a message, including all tags and name of the author.
The option to follow a topic and be notified when someone replies to one of your messages.
A powerful search function that allows you to search by author, keywords, etc.
The ability to access the forums even during the attack and other maintenance periods.
The new player tag (@playername) that includes a direct link to the player's soul or Twinoid profile.
The new "side note" tag to replace the distracting spoilers when you want to add some off-topic comments to your messages.
Bullet lists that have the proper indentation, unlike this one.

The things that are currently not working in the new forums but will hopefully be fixed soon:
The  XTBoris tag is temporarily unavailable.
The RolePlay ([rp]) tag looks ugly and should get a different style.
The direct links between the various sections (Discussions, Saloon, Help) are not visible when the forums are accessed from within the game, but are there when the forums are accessed from Twinoid (but with the default layout). This should be fixed soon.
The buttons are blue/gray instead of the usual red.
