It all depends on what the town needs, exception that the Tamer can bring back scavenged goods they can also bring back weapons after clearing zeds areas which is what they mainly do.

That way, the 3rd person can fix the weapon and then the 2nd person who goes out can go through your cleared areas and use the weapons you've brought back for example.

The pet the Tamers have also give a small watchmen defense bonus, nothing about extra survival though.

Guardians can virtually not die on the watchmen unless there is just too much to defend against, a Guardian with Healthy Body & The other +1CP can easily rescue players especially in early but also secure zones in mid to later game.
