Now what I'm saying is we bring in a peasant version of each class with dramatically cut down version of that hero's original powers for example...

Guard: When in town supplies 2 Defense to the Town instead of 5, house has no defense bonus like a guardian and instead gets 1+cp instead of 2+cp due to his non - lethal police baton. He/she can also not use heroic abilities as another Handicap.

Pioneer: A dumbed down version of a scout has ill prepared camouflage made of poorly made fabric and has a 50/50 chance of being discovered in zones and any action reveals there position unlike scouts who can eat, drink etc...

Forager: With his/her trusty damaged shovel his digging time is not reduced at all but he/she is still more likely to find items. In ruins they get +25 seconds of air instead of 50.

Laborer: With there poorly stocked tool belt they get 3 construction points instead of 6 and they can take 1 impression per game instead of 1 per day.

Animal Lover: Starts with a pathetic weaner dog that can carry 2 items back to town once a day. Still gets a name.

Weekend Warrior: Can get the optimal chance of survival and can find food and water at the cost of 1 AP and they can only do this once a day for one. Ether food or water.
