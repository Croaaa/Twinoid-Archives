A conversation in IRC gave me this idea, so I thought I'd throw it out there:

A small map leaderboard, the idea behind it is basically to motivate newer players to getting ranking towns relatively early in the game, and the leaderboards will only be 10 towns long.

It also has a few rules to stop it being abused like hell.

- Town must have less than 20 people with more than 100+ SP in to rank, much like the requirement for the Spiritual Guide
- Only people with less than 100SP at the time can earn a distinction for ranking, it will be a seperate distinction to the 'ranked towns' distinction currently on offer.
- The top ranking small map at the end of the season will reward everyone who had less than 100SP at the time could be something like a sample of hero time (a Week?)

Thoughts, suggestions and comments?
