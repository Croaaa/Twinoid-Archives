I don't know if people remember from way back in the early seasons (before the forced Twinoid merge and the loss of the old-style forum) there were a few differences with things like the Bank, Workshop, Construction Sites and Town Upgrades.

Namely, this part.

Before the merge, it used to show you the last replies in the specific Sticky thread from the forum in a drop-down style; the posts in the Construction Site's stickied thread would show up in the Construction Site's page, you wouldn't even have to go to the forums to check.

This was good. It meant people who didn't read the forum at all could still see the stickied thread and go, "oh, the last post talks about not building the Foundations, even though they're available! I'm NOT gonna build the foundations, then!"

Nowadays, with people's interest in the game waning, I feel like this change would help random non-meta towns a lot. Some people mess things up not out of malice or wanting to ruin the town, but out of ignorance, sometimes even laziness.

Can we have at least a MINIMAL attempt to bring this little change back? I know it might be difficult, but it can't be impossible! Please?
