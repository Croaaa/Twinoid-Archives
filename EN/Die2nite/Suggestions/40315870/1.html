The Twinoid forum system considers deleting a thread or post to be an elevated permission, so you can't do it unless you're considered a moderator of the forum you're looking at

Normal users can have mod powers in a private forum if they are the creator of the group (or were given mod powers by said creator)
D2N town forums are generated by the game itself, so no one is labeled as the creator


The only thing you can do is edit your post to remove the contents
But you can only do this within 10 minutes of your reply being posted, and only if nobody has made a reply after yours
If you're wanting to delete an entire thread, your original post that started the thread should stay editable
