Warning: this post is a 'bit' on the long side...

This is based off a suggestion that I once saw on the forums (long time ago). Since I really liked it back then I figured I might suggest something similar, but with a few twists of my own. Since the idea was posted long ago, the original suggestion is pretty much unfindable.

The idea is that when you reach 250 soul points and want to join a town, you can also choose for a 'theme town' (much like how you can choose for 'hardcore'). The 'theme' gets decided randomly when the town is created. You can see what the town theme (or surroundings) are when choosing a town.

Theme 1: World of ice
In this world it's extremly cold and penguins now walk around on the map. Penguins can't be attacked and you can't move them to another zone. During the attack they move between 1 and 5 zones.

Other changes:
Due to the extreme cold, the attack at night is 15% lower then normal, as most of the zombies are frozen solid.
All weapons now have 20% of freezing after each use. A frozen weapon needs to be 'repaired'. (This 20% is on top of the chances of a weapon 'breaking')
Because almost everything is frozen, finding anything is now 20% harder (this does not aply for the scavenger).
Since everything is covered in ice, water is no longer a problem. The well now has unlimited water and you can't dehydrate.
After going out 6 zones your status changes to 'almost freezing', after 12 zones it becomes 'frozen', if you don't get unfrozen before the attack...you die. When you're frozen you can't move. You can prevent freezing by drinking 'coffee'. If you happen to be in a zone that has a penguin on it, the penguin will automatically warm you up (and reset your status back to 'normal'). Returning to town also resets you status, but you can't go out again on the same day...
Every town starts with the Blueprint of the 'house of coffee'. It needs 10 planks and 4 battery and this building generates 5 coffee each day and can be upgrades to provide 4 extra coffee a day.
NEW HERO 'Warmth bringer': When going outside his status changes to 'almost freezing' after 10 zones. After 20 zones he gets 'frozen'. Once a day he can use his 'warm blanket' to unfreeze himself OR someone else. After the attack he sees the location of between 1 and 5 penguins (much like the shaman sees lost souls). This hero is only available in this 'themed' town.

Theme 2: Jungle
This world has lot's of trees, bears and many playces for zombies to hide.

Other changes:
A random number of bears wonder the map. At attack all bears move between 1 and 5 zones and new bears may appear somewhere. 1 bear has 6cp. When using a weapon, on a bear, chances of breaking it are 40% higher, then they would otherwise be. After killing the bear you have 50% chance of gaining 2-5 “Tasty-looking Steaks”. Note that you can't see the bears using the watchtower.
Finding stuff is a lot easier. So when scavenging you have 80% chance of finding any kind of item and it takes twice as long to deplete a zone.
Zombies might be slow by day, but at attack time, they suddenly know how to clime trees. This results in the fact that the attack needs a lot more defenses to cover the attack. After day 3, the attack is 33% higher then the attack in the towns we currently have.
Zones not visited in 3 days (or more) have 50% chance of turning into 'Overgrown zones'. These zones have so many trees on them that when you visit them, you can only go back from where you came (you can't even scavenge on these zones). It takes 6ap to make it possible to turn this zone back into a 'regural' zone. Note that zombies can still show up on these 'overgrown zones'. Zombies need to be killed, before you can spend ap to turn the zone back into a 'normal' zone. A zone that has been visited that day, but also touches a 'overgrown zone' has 20% chance of becoming 'overgrown' at attack time.
Any zones within 2ap will never have a bear and cannot turn into an 'overgrown zone'.
HERO changes (only on this 'themed' map):
Guardian: The guardian has a big shield to protect everyone, and it can also hide his weapons so that the bear has more trouble destroying those weapons. (So when a guardian uses a weapon (on the bear), chances of the weapon breaking is only 20% higher (instead of the 40%).
Scouts: now can also see if there is a bear on the next zone...
Shaman uses all kinds of magic to see between 2 and 6 bears on the map.
Scavengers have 95% chance of finding an item

So... After reading all of that... What do all of you think?
