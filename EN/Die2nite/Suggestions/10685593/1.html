Scavenger: Very useful for clearing the zones around town quickly and getting more resources to the town in small towns where there aren't as many scavengers because of newbies being nervous to go outside. Although they might seem useless, they can be helpful to any town on the first few days because the 5+ defence they give to the town is valuable. Also very useful to new towns for rescuing players who are trapped by zombies, which happens very often in small towns.

Survivalist:
Slightly lowers need for water in the town by costing 1 less water each day, as well as being the ones to get the blueprints so that a citizen doesn't die while attempting to camp on a building.

Tamer:
Useful for bringing back twice as many items, which is helpful to clear zones faster and obviously to bring back items which have been left outside.

Technician:
Very useful builders, which can mean constructions get finished earlier, which in turn means towns may survive for days longer than they originally would have if they didn't get that construction built on time.

Roughly locate a soul which seems to be extremely bugged right now STILL even after an update. They can add +5 water rations to the town water supply..
