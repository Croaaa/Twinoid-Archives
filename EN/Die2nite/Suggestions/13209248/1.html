Yes, another one.

It seems incredibly awkward to me that the catapult can only be used by one randomly-selected person. Is that part of the design, so it's not overpowered or whatever? In that case, you could make it a higher-tier BP or make it cost more resources or only allow it to be used a certain amount of times per day.

Better yet, make it a low-cost, temporary construction that we have to rebuild each time - it would make sense that a cruddy thrown-together catapult would need readying each time, or require some sort of "shell" to fire the item in.

It's very helpful to be able to send items into the world beyond, but with the AP cost I don't think it's exactly game-breaking.

My temporary construction idea also lowers the risk of griefing, if that's the concern. Even if it stays as is but more people can use it, It doesn't seem any more abusable to me than the regular griefing that goes on, where people can just steal items from the bank and wander out to die. Just put an anti-abuse feature on it like the bank and alert the town when it's used.

I'd just like to see this construction freed up a little, because it has great strategic potential.

I'd even settle on letting the town vote to nominate a daily user for it, maybe two with the upgrade.

There is a way for the town to decide who is the user. But it is done only once and the user is bound to the catapult till death.


It's done by manipulating the choice rules:

A citizen will be randomly chosen from a predetermined group :

in the first instance, a citizen will be chosen from the heroes which have been active today,
if none are found, a citizen will be chosen from the heroes in town,
if still none are found, a citizen will be chosen from the citizens which have been active today,
finally, in a worst-case scenario, a citizen will be chosen at random.

You leave the catapult at 1 away.
After the attack don't let anyone but your chosen hero login into the game.
Have them complete their daily tasks (Scan the Tower, Post on the Forums, upgrade house and vote for upgrade)
Then finnish the catapult, and go to make the "random" choice.
Since the chosen one is the only active hero today, they will be the one to use it.

Of course nobody logins before al that is done.
