I would like to propose the idea of having an Olympic style event in which the towns were divided by country. So I’m from the USA, so I’d register for the USA’s Die2nite team, if someone is from France then they would register for France’s Die2nite team, and so on. I think the way it could work is that Die2nite makes a town for each country that has enough (40 or more) players. These 40 players would have to have 500 soul points or above. It would be first come first serve. In the name of fairness, only one team is allowed per country. I’m not sure how those in charge of making these large events would do it, but it seems as though there could be a way to see if the people who have signed up are legitimately from the country.
