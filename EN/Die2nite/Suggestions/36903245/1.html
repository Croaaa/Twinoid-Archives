To better Organize our personal and our AP I suggest we work in groups of five. The Teams can be broken down this format://

2 Builder Teams
1 Rescue Team
5 Salvage Teams

The builders can organize and discuss projects separately come up with a plan for a build order and collaborate the idea with the other group. This way the plans can be better worked out from different perspectives and plans can be managed on what is available and what is possibly best for the town. They could then work on the buildings preferably close to the days end so most of the salvaged loot can make it back to town. During their off hours the teams can move one zone out of town and gather logs and scrap. The builders would also only drink water and then only drinking when thirst creeps it's ugly head.
The builders potential AP would be 40-100-40-100... in that pattern. This manages our water consumption and any extra AP from other groups can boost that number daily.

The rescue team will be to back up any group in need of control or in need of a special zone being cleared of zombies for safer camping. This force will keep their eyes and ears out for Ruins to explore and will make goes at mapping if possible and clearing these important locations.
The rescue team when not active can focus on helping builders if nothing comes up during the day and keeping an eye on any illicit activity that can harm our town as a whole.

The salvage teams can work completely separate from one another. While trying to cross paths as least often as possible the teams can for expeditions preferably to special zones or as far from the town center as possible. This way the most material can be gathered for our survival.
With five Citizens the base CP for each group would be ten this allows for protection in the farthest flung zones in the wasteland.

These are my thoughts if I missed something please point it out if you want to be apart of this project please PM me or post it on here. I will need help organizing the various groups so a team manager should be chosen and each day the manager should change so each team can be managed differently each day so each member can work together as best as possible.

This is a plan I have had for a while but have yet to find a group to try it.
