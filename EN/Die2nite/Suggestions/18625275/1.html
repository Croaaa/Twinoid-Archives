1.For Distant Regions:
*Survival for 7-8 days provides 1 ticket

*Survival for 17-18 days provides 7 ticket
*Survival for 19-20 days provides 8 ticket
*Survival for 21-22 days provides 9 ticket
*Survival for 23+ days provides 10 ticket

1. Lowering the number of less experienced players in hardcore towns.

3. Annoy the trolls so that trolling in hardcore become harder and harder.
