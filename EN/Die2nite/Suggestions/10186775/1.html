I have a basic rundown for new heroes, but I'm at work, so will have to wait until a couple hours before the attack for me to get home and post it.

For the most part:
If you enjoyed camping as a resident, I suggest survivalist for their ability to get 100% chances
If you like finding stuff, I suggest scavenger for their increased chance of finding /something/
If you bringing needed supplies home, I suggest tamer
If you want to better help people who have become trapped in the world beyond, I suggest guardian for their boosted control points
If you tend to get stuck a lot yourself, with no one to help you, I suggest scout for their Camo thus ability to move through zombie controlled zones unseen
If you don't have a lot of time to play or get back to town before the attack, I suggest technician for their construction points (now usable for workshopping, or so I've been told)
