Thestudmuffin94
As it is now the gallows always kills shunned (and soon to be shunned) citizens as soon as they are contructed. How about allowing shunned citizens and citizens shunned while outside of town when the gallows are contructed escape the instant death? If they ever actually returned to town with the gallows constructed they would then die, but while they were outside of the town it would give them a chance to survive a bit longer. This would mean they would have to fend for themselves outside without the hope of returning to town for shelter or water, they would simply have to find those on their own. If the gallows actually were constructed the shunned citizen outside of town would have no idea that they actually were until it was too late when they came back to town so they would always have to be on their toes. A shunned citizen can't cause too much trouble outside of town but if they really are a problem then other citizens could always try to attack them, maybe even allow them to use weapons on the shunned if the gallows are constructed? In an attempt to help the shunned who are forced to live outside of the town they could have an effect to where it is difficult to locate them on the map or allow them to hide in a zone and not be detected unless found by a normal citizen searching the zone (much like a citizen finding hidden items in a zone placed by the shunned).

Sorry for the massive paragraph but I am welcome to opinions to this idea, it's not completely all planned out but I would like to see what others think.
