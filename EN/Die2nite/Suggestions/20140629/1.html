When i first became a hero in season 1, I loved being a scavenger...finding stuff a half hour sooner was awesome!

Now...not so much. A town might have 1 or 2 scavs to run the ruin for mapping, but its not really necessary.

So, a couple suggestions to boost the scav class.

1. A 1% chance per ap from town to find something in a depleted zone. i.e. its day 15, everything within 10 ap is depleted. A scav heads out and at 1 ap from town, she has a 1% chance to find something as if it was a non-depleted zone. at 2 ap a 2% chance, etc etc.

2. A boosted chance to find leather bags in undepleted zones. Not a huge boost. If a normal person finds a leather bag 1% of the time he searches, a scav would find a leather bag 2%. Whatever could be the most increase without unbalancing.

3. Once a day, a 50% chance to find something in a depleted building. i.e. The old police station is depleted, but man, we really need a weapon. Send the scav out there to do a "deep search" with 50% chance that something will turn up. This could be using their shovel, like the survivalist uses their book.

I don't think these suggestions would make it so the town needs 10 scavs to set the world on fire, but they might make it so every town wants 2-3 to help last longer.

i think its that scavengers don't have much in the way of a specific role.
scouts are explorers and soul grabbers
guardians are watchmen and escorts
tamers are warriors and retrievers
survivalists are campers
techs are builders and key masters
shamans are the soul finding score machines
scavengers are those dudes who find things.. you know, like everyone else but a little better.


in a world without the guarantee of a search tower voted 5 times is a world in which scavengers have seen some diminished value.

they did see a boon with the extra time in ruins. not only a plus for mapping, but the layout typically means a scavenger might be the only one to be able to run the whole ruins without skipping rooms to get back out in time.

the above suggestions
1. A 1% chance per ap from town to find something in a depleted zone - balanced makes sense for class, however you have to ask if you want to send your scavenger on a long trip for 15 10% chances to find something? would that really put it over the top? true he'd bring back a handful of items, but they might be just as useless as the scrap and logs already available. i really like this idea, but i don't know if it will ultimately give someone pause to play this class over another class with more task specific features
2. A boosted chance to find leather bags in undepleted zones. - seems too specific. even if it was in place, you'd just have a bunch of pissed off scavengers wondering why the 30 other people scavenging found the blueprint and not them with their double chances (even though the 30 other people combined have much better odds)
3. Once a day, a 50% chance to find something in a depleted building. - why not just have this have the same possibilities as 1


none of the above really address the concept that scavengers don't really have a team role

so in thinking about a team role for scavengers i had 2 thoughts

1) spend AP to undeplete a zone, somewhere between 3-6 AP's and must be at least a certain distance from town. probably in relation to the AP Cost
2) presence bonus, scavengers give a bonus to all those scavenging in the same zone, multiple scavengers multiple bonuses including to each other. bonus to chances to find something and/or time reduction

1 a few scavengers with ap items could create a pocket of searchable area for the town
2 makes them invaluable to expedition groups and promotes some additional teamwork
