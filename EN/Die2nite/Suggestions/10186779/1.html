Description: the mine worker was once upon a time, a peaceful person, then when he came up for lunch, the world , had changed.

Heroic item : the pick axe:- the mine worker can dig under ground, and make passage ways and tunnels deep under the baking sands of the desert.

Passive skill:- mine workers are used to being in low oxygen environments, they get 100% more oxygen.

The tunnels can be accessed by all... but who will come out the other side? who know?

Tunnels will cost 2 AP + one twisted plank to build per square on the map. Tunnels can go on for as long as the player wishes, in a straight line that is. To make a turn, left or right, or make a junction, to allow players to travel a number of different ways will cost four AP two twisted planks, and a Beam.

It will cost 2 ap to make a exit, at the cost of rope ( new item) and two twisted planks.

Like in Ruins, players will have oxygen, and must make it out before their air supply runs out.If a player runs out, they will instantly pass out, and die. If a player dies they will become a zombie, and travel around in the tunnels, waiting for a victim.

Like in ruins zombies will lark down in the tunnels after each attack, and will re-spawn there after each attack.
The more exits you have, the more likely the undead will linger down below.

Players can not auto search while under ground.
Though miners may find something while carving out a new square.

In hard core towns, the tunnels will ware out over time as they are used, and can cause a cave in.

Griefers can also plant Semtex ,and light it with am match, to blow a tunnel up.
