The sniper...
A class armed with a bolt action sniper rifle able to take out zombies, and has scopes that can really make the sniper a formidable opponent.

Special Abilities
-Snipe (Attack 1-2 zombie(s) from long range from any zone, can be used once per day, even in town (99% chance success).
-Action Snipe (Attack 2 zombies in the current zone, with 80% chance of success, once per day)
-Scoping Vision (Use the snipers dual band scope to detect zombies in a straight line, for example the sniper if he uses the vision he can see zombies from zone (0,1),(0,2),(0,3) until like (0,24), however the sniper can only use this once per 4 days to rest his eyes after a long strain!

Disadvantages
-The sniper has a reduced chance to injure a zombie by using a direct weapon such as a chainsaw
-The sniper has a 6.5% chance to break his sniper rifle but can be fixed by using 1 repair kit, or 2 kwik fixes.

Critiques:
Snipers should actually be less effective the farther out they are trying to shoot.

Why should the sniper's ability with the firearm reduce their abilities in hand-to-hand? If anything, their abilities should be increased due to increased training and discipline.
If the sniper rifle is their professional tool, then it can't break. If it's not their professional tool, then it should still only take 1 Kwik-Fix or Repair Kit, since they each require 1AP to use.

Regardless, I think it's a neat idea.
