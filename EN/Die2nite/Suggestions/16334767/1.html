Sure, the Aqua-splash is fun. The Devastator is great. The chainsaw kills huge amounts of zeds per use.

But the Aqua-splash takes water. Both the Devastator and the Chainsaw use batteries and take a crapton of materials to construct.

The penknife lives up to it's 'pathetic' adjective, and the machete breaks quite often. Excellent looking weapons such as the Lawnmower and Exploding Grapefruit I have not had the pleasure of using.

My favorite weapon that I've used is the Hurling Stick. No usage cost in water or batteries. Nothing to construct. And it's highly durable. Most of my zed kills are courtesy of this resilient zed killing machine. It rarely breaks, meaning that I can usually get 5+ zed kills with it before it breaks. It uses no water or batteries, and a Kwick-fix later it's back in business. Or it can be repaired in town like any other broken item.

What's your favorite?
