I would assume so. In my mind, it'd go like this:

Scavenger finds it somewhere. They automatically drop it on the floor.
They attempt to pick it up, it goes into their inventory, where it then says: You discreetly test the weight of the pallet. It's definitely too heavy to pick up alone - you'll need some help to manage this... Get two options: Give up, or Wait for Assistance.
Second person arrives, and next to the person there is a button with the Pallet picture on. Clicking it gives the message: <player> is obviously struggling to shift the pallet - there's no way they'll get it back to town alone. Will you offer to help? (WARNING - This player will determine your route back to town! Make sure you have enough AP, and that you trust them not to deceive you!) Get two options: Leave them alone, or Offer Assistance.
Offering assistance effectively puts you into Escort Mode - however, neither player needs to be a Hero for this. The first player guides the pair back to town.
On entering the town, both players receive the message: Phew! You've dumped your prize in the bank, now it's time to take a well-earned rest... The pallet immediately lodges itself in the bank, and cannot be removed.

See opening and contents aforementioned.
Also, you'd become thirsty twice as quickly while shifting a pallet, so it would require extra co-ordination to avoid Thirsty or Dehydrated statuses.
