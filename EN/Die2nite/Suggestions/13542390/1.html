Take into consideration the amount of heroes needed to get these unlocked before saying overpowered.

Most are inspired by previous suggestion made by other players (if not the same exact suggestions.)


???: Bank lock activates after 7 items are taken from the bank, 13 items during chaos mode
Unlocked at 330 Days (between Pro Camper and Preventative Medicine)

Gluttony:
Can eat 2 food per day but can only gain 5 from the second meal
Unlocked at 400 Days (between Preventative Medicine and Mayor)

Experienced Traveler:
Thirst Level goes down every 13 steps instead of the usual 11.
Unlocked at 800 days (after Architect)




Heroic Actions (useable once per town):
Master Converter
can specifically choose what an item is converted to (e.g., mechanism -> nuts and bolts, Flatpacked -> Jarpen)
Unlocked at 450 Days (between Preventative Medicine and Mayor)

A temporary change of heart
can choose to play as another hero profession for one day only
Unlocked at 600 days (between Mayor and Architect)

Upgraded Lucky Find
in addition to Lucky Find items, can now also summon: Electric Component, Handful of Nuts and Bolts, Exploding Water Bomb, Broken Human Bone
Unlocked at 860 days (after Architect)

Body Swap
Switch positions with another player in the World Beyond. Person initiating can be inside or outside the town. Other person can only be outside. Person switching with has to be on escort mode and allow an additional permission box.
Unlocked at 950 days (after Architec)
