This will be a 30 minutes of bare-hand fighting. You can use 1 to launch a knock-out punch to your opponent every 10 minutes. [between 00:00:00 - 00:10:00, 00:10:01 - 00:20:00 and 00:20:01 - 00:30:00]You have a 40% chance of knocking out your opponent, 10% chance of wounding him and 5% chance of killing him.

The winner will be featured in the gazette and will be given a distinction. [Maybe he should win something like a Chest for a chance of getting a Bandage]

The unlucky one will be silenced for 1 day. He cannot write anything in the town forums and he cannot send private messages. If he's still alive.

A fight can only be held every other day.
