I've been thinking about suggesting a few things, primarly having to do with batteries. So I came up with this

OVERCHARGED BATTERIES


Overcharged Batteries

These Batteries are not like the normal ones. They appear to have an overcharge, it DEFINETLY has to be better than a regular battery, though probaly a bit dangerous...Where did I put my Battery Launcher Mk. II? I need to do a few tests.


Has the description states Overcharged Batteries have more power than regular batteries, thus if used with Items that use batteries, they will be more effective, but at a risk. What? You didn't think you would get a more powerful item and get off scot free did you?


Creating OCBs

To create OCBs, you will need 2 regular batteries, and a new item

Twisted Wire

This wire is in terrible condition,but it still has a couple of uses, it can still carry an electrical current...You might also be able to choke a zombie to death.

Once you have the 2 Batteries and Twisted Wire in your Rucksack/Home Chest, click the action: Overcharge Battery

The overcharge can result in 1 of 4 ways:

Sucess, No injury 20%

You attach the Twisted Wire to the two Batteries and begin the electricity flow, everything goes has planned and you manage to get an: Ovecharged Battery!

Sucess, Injury 25%

You attach the Twisted Wire to the two batteries, the electrical current flows smoothly and at the end everything seems okay, but has you remove the Twisted Wire a joly of electrictly flashes through you! You have obtained: Overcharged Battery but have injured your: *Insert name of injurable body part here*

Failure, No Injury 30%

You attach the Twisted Wire to the two batteries, the electrical current won't flow for some reason, you check to see whats wrong when you realize both batteries were empty the entire time. Better luck next time.

Failure, Injury 25%

You attach the Twisted Wire to the two batteries, The electrical current won't flow for reasons unknown. You begin to check the Batteries and Wire, when suddenly the electricity from the Batteries and jolts through you. You have injured your: *Insert name of injurable body part here*


Effects of OCBs on items

OCBs can be used in Items that require Batteries just like their normal counterparts, only the effect will be doubled!
Put that OCB in a Devastator? 4 zombies dead with 1 use!
Put it in a lamp? Twice the Decoration points and lasts twice has long!

Note: Using an OCB in an MKII Battery Launcher and managing to retrieve the battery will cause it to lose its Overcharge.

BUT! Using an OCB in a Battery Launcher will have consiquences


Use the OCB in ANY Battery Launcher will have the chance of the Battery Launcher backfiring!

Failure 40%
