I'm 99% sure that this has been suggested in one form or another, but I've got a slightly different take on it and would like to throw it out there:

It basically works like the building upgrade system we have at the moment works, it comes with the construction of Building Registry, but it's not part of the existing upgrade table we have at the moment, it creates a second table where people can vote and has 3 options:

- Raise the bank lock limit by one
- Keep the bank lock limit the same
