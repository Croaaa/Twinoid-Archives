*Idea inspired from "Protect The Shamans Thread" and Scrimshaw. *

Description: Behold, the medic, he is based on healing, and is an expert on it. He can heal people anywhere, regardless. He can even himself, amazing! He can also heal infections, and any negative status. However he can only heal people or himself up to 4 times a day. Also, yes, he can forcibly heal ghouls if he wants to.

Permanent Item: Bandage Roll

Abilities:

Heal Wound (2)
Heal Infection (5)
Heal Drugged (6)
Heal Ghoulisim (6)
Heal Hooked (7)
Heal Terrorized (6)
I might've missed other statuses, but the rest can be 6.

Also, the medic can produce x1 bandage or x1 parcetoid per 2 days.

-The medic, believing in free rights and freedoms, can heal the shunned.

-The medic, being a believer in being useless by being shunned, cannot heal when shunned.

-The medic has a 25% chance when using a bandage of finding only a small wound and only using a small fraction of the bandage, and rendering the bandage useful to others.

-The medic can heal people when their offline, and any class.

-Unlike the shaman, he has a 100% of healing others, or himself.

SUPER ABILLITY: The medic has a 50% chance of tolerating drugs and not getting drugged, staying with a clean status!
