Tornadoes
Each day,
5 sets for small town and 15 sets for distant towns,
Tornadoes would be randomly formed at different locations in the map !

Each set of tornado is a 1 area block and they are areas that are inaccessible during that particular day.

If you happen to camp in the world beyond, there is a chance that a tornado can spawn on you and suck you up, throwing you randomly at another square which will cause an injury in the world beyond !


Balancing
To balance it, no tornado can spawn within 5ap from town and directly on ruin.

As for being caught by a tornado spawning on you, having a plastic bag would prevent the injury. It will be recorded in the gazette .

With Plastic Bag
"No one knows how ?? did it, but he/she was seen parachuting to coordinates x.y !! Now that's what life in dessert is all about !

Without Plastic Bag
"No one knows what the he/she is doing, but ??? was seen skydiving to coordinates x.y !! A mushroom of death was seen shortly after the touchdown.... hope he/she is alright !

Also, areas that has a tornado the day before would become fully replenished.


Misc
Tornadoes within watchtower sight would be automatically shown on the town map, whereas those beyond would have a chance to be found at a 10% per person using the watch tower to calculate the daily attack.
