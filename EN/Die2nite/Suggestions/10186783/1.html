Basically this character is a caretaker/farmer, their special equipment is a potted plant, which every day can be harvested for a suspicious looking vegetable or an apple (75/25% chance) it must be watered every three days or it becomes Dried potted plant, and produces no food, it can be watered by any water e.g. even stagnant water cans.

Also using a "festering flesh" on the plant will cause 1-3 Intestine melons to grow the next day.

As well as this, as the Grower has an extensive knowledge of plants, and therefore the clearing of plants, every AP invested into excavating is doubles, making them twice as effective at clearing unsearchable zones.

Any criticisms are welcome
