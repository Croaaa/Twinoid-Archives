iDare
I`d like to suggest changing current town`s choice when joining to the new town.

Currently we have available:
1) 1 Hardcore town
2) 3 Distant towns
3) 2 Small map towns

Suggested new choice:
1) 2 Hardcore towns
2) 2 Distant Towns
3) 2 Small map towns

From my experience jumping to the new distant town (waiting for new one to appear) is a real pain recently. My team has jumped twice into distant town in last 30 days and it took 4 and 6 hours respectively (last night we have been waiting since 23:40 till 5:30 for an empty town to appear - most people live in similar to the game time time zone).

I know about 2 other teams jumping a day earlier and they had to wait for ~3 hours as well. I honestly don`t remember those situations back in S2 or S3 even, new towns were filling much faster and even if you missed one or two towns in the beginning, there was no problem to wait another half of hour for next empty town (only one similar case in my mind, when MMJ 2 or 3 took place, but it was related to meta cap, when last players to jump had to wait for couple hours as well to drag their assigned coalitions to the towns, because all available towns were filled over 35 players already).

Not sure if suggested choice is perfect, but since I think there`s a lot less players in die2nite than 1 year ago and also more poeple tend to choose Hardcore to play (but it also freezes at some point), Distant towns fill way to slow (assuming even distribution of random non-hero jumpers) to have 3 of them available all the time.

That`s why I`d like to start this discussion to maybe find the best towns distribution and send final suggestion to Epoq in future to improve it(??), if possible at all.

Of course I can understand that the issue is very much related to meta gaming only and not everyone is going to agree with this proposition, but it will also affect all the organised events in future most likely, if it`s not improved (one of the reasons that CoTT had to be organised in private towns probably).
