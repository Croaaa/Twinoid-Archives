This isn't new stuff; I've previously posted these in other Guardian threads but I want to give it its own.

I love the Guardian class but I think most of us agree it needs a little more to be as useful as it should. The 2 CP bonus is double the starting base CP but only 1.5x the CP of a veteran hero, and it's not very significant. The added town defense is nice, but also hardly significant without a lot of Guardians.

Maybe the defense could use a tweak, or maybe we could use completely new abilities, but I'm going to focus on CP.

Simply put, more CP would be nice. I don't know exactly how much, but let's be conservative and say 1 more than it is now.
Giving it to us straight out could work nicely, but I don't mind the idea of earning it somehow. I like the concept of modifying our shields, with resources the town won't really miss - maybe two scrap metal and an incomplete car door?

Or we could gain the ability to create control zones. By that I mean we have an ability that lets us add, say, 3-5 CP to a single zone on the map so people can come and go safely. There are a lot of variables for this one, but I favour it being useable once a day, persisting after we leave the zone but being destroyed overnight.

I think either of these options are a good way to increase the utility of Guardians without going overboard.

So what do you say, MT? In time for the Clash would be lovely.

Perhaps allow the Guardian's shield to be modified in the workshop in line with what Leapfrog suggested, however the resources used must be made by the Guardian (except scrap), and only one modification per day.

Eg:

Day 1 - Modify shield with scrap metal for one extra CP (cost 1-3AP)

Day 2 - Modify with scrapwrought iron for a further CP (cost 2-6AP)

Day 3 - Modify with scrapironmetal support for a further CP (cost 3-9AP).

This method offsets a few things, as it is not overly common to have both the hacksaw and the factory in the first 3 days, so the conversions would likely be at the higher AP cost, therefore affecting scavenging in the crucial early days. Often the workshop isn't completed until late on the 1st day (sometimes there is no scrap in the bank until day 2), so that would also have a bearing on day 1 scavenging and rescues.

If a Guardian chooses to wait with the conversions until the AP cost is lower (which it may never be), it will still affect scavenging because the extra CP is often needed further away from town by that stage, however the Guardian would have used crucial AP already and may need drugs (loses a CP if healthy body is unlocked) or alcohol (clogs up a ruck space or affects scavenging ability).

All these things can also hamper the guardian with their house upgrades.

Maybe tweak the guard tower so that it no longer needs a blueprint, the needed resources are lowered, the defence boosted, but only Guardian's know the secret to building an effective tower, so only they can invest AP in to the construction.
