So, I just got done posting this in my current town;

So, with the horde now descending on our town, we're left with a few things to consider.

Kilozombie, while I consider his actions to be rather terrible, is right in his reasoning that this town is quickly approaching it's demise, as all towns do.

We've had a good run, but I don't see why we can't squeeze out another day or two. As the mantra goes:

KEEP CALM
and
KEEP BUILDING

But, let me add a bit more to that. Building will eventually not be able to absorb the full force of the attack... so what do we do?

Well, we could go out in the most painfully awesome way ever... AS SPARTANS.

Everyone! Grab your weapons, link your shields, and fight to the last man! No longer are we simple 'watchmen'... We are SPARTANS! If the Estate of Psychos must fall, then, as it is our Sparta, we will die defending it, together.

United we fall!

Call to arms, Banners fly in the wind!
For the glory of Hellas!
Coat of Arms, reading 'freedom or death'!
Blood of King Leonidas!

Just like their ancestors, ages ago,
who fought in the face of defeat!
Those 300 men left a pride to uphold,
freedom of death in effect!

Then, and now again,
Blood of Heroes saving the land!

And it basically got me thinking, what if there was a distinction for this?

The distinction being, of course, Spartan. It would have the titles Phalanx Fighter, This is SPARTA!, and King Leonidas unlocked at 1x, 5x, and 10x respectively. It would be a rare distinction.

The conditions for the distinction would be the following:

1) There must be at least 20 Watchmen participating in the defense of the town during an attack.

2) The town must be wiped out (Completely) while these watchmen are defending. Everyone in town must die.

If these two conditions are met, all players who were alive in the town at the time of the attack gain the "Spartan" distinction.

What does everyone think of that?
