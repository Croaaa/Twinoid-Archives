Currently the Technician is a bit misnamed. This Hero type simply gets 6 extra points per day to put toward construction and workshop tasks.

Recommended changes or upgrades:

1. Permanently assign a "Hacksaw" like ability to reduce Workshop tasks by 1 AP.

2. Offer item upgrade/repair chances at reduced costs. Example: for a tiered AP cost, you could get either random (less cost) or specified (more cost) reduction in resource requirements. So - the 5 items required to repair the engine could get reduced downward at cost. A random reduction for 2 AP, specified for 4 or 6. (too expensive?)

All of this is a concept, feel free to critique. For now, the Technician is not yet a true McGyver in my opinion.
