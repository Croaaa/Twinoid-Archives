Since Dino RPG is apparently more popular than die2nite (it must be, recent update bugs and glitches were fixed within the same week of an update) adding a dinosaur class seems financially sound and let's face it, fun!

Dinosaur class players would keep their dinos alive by feeding it Tamer dogs and Guardians. Guardians would finally have a use! Once a day move 3 squares, ignoring all zombies as you are riding on your dinos back. And finally, as a bit of a nerf (because let's face it, dinosaurs are OP), every night each citizen has a 50/50 chance to be terrorised by the fact you have a dino in your tent. Also, if you flee while on a dino there is a chance it becomes zombified and would count for 6 zombies.

We could also incorporate cameos from other MT games like Nintendo does - how about being able to use the paddle from alpha bounce as a weapon?

What would your dinosaur do for you if you were to design Dino® Class!?

I wanted to suggest this as a joke, but this seems as good a thread as any to do it in. .

Monster Motel can produce 1 brochure a day. You can pick up that brochure and port it over to die2nite. When in a zone with zombies, you can give the brochure to a zombie and that zombie will leave the zone and when you go back to your Monster Motel, you will have a new Jovial Zombie in the queue at your front desk.

Brochures don't have to be used the day you pick it up, brochures can be stocked on your character, but it's a class tool that can never be dropped. Don't be greedy. When you die, you start a new town with zero brochures.

Zombies have a 10% chance to drop the brochure when they leave for your motel, allowing you to reuse that brochure again (your brochure counter does not go down in this instance).
