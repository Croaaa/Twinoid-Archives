On the french server, residents got a bad reputation on the world forum for two main reasons:
A long time ago (end of 2008 if I remember correctly), all players got some hero days for free as a compensation for a bug. Right now, Hero Mode on Die2Nite gives you the following Advantages:

1. 6 special professions
2. Heroic Actions
3. An extra spot in your chest
4. Loyalty bonuses (unlocking advantages the longer you're a hero)
5. Upgrade: customizing your house to add more features.
6. An extra spot in your rucksack.
7. The ability to pick the town you want to jump into.
8. World Forum: unlimited access to the world forum
9. Armour Plating: +2 defense added to your house

When you purchase hero days, you are entering into a contract with MT. You are paying for the advantages listed above. If one of the advantages listed above is removed... say, given to everyone for free and therefore no longer an advantage for a hero... then you should be given an equal or greater advantage in return. Technically, by removing an item that you've paid for without giving you the option to cancel your service (and receive a refund) MT would be breaking the contract by not providing the services paid for.

Issues have come up in the past where someone says "But I paid for that!" but the issue in question wasn't listed in the actual services you were paying for.

The advantage of unlimited access to the World Forums is something that Heroes pay for. And there is nothing on the help page that explains Hero Mode that says that MT reserves the right to change or remove any of the advantages without notice.

Therefore, if that advantage is removed, heroes should be compensated, either with a refund of remaining hero days, or else a new advantage of equal or greater value.
