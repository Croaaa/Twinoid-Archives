Friends, Residents, Citizens!

As you will all be aware, this year will see the Olympics hosted in London, England. More importantly, however, it will see the launch of the Apocalympics; featuring the first resident-only event on Die2nNite!

This event will see as many towns as possible battling it out for the right to participate in the finals of the Apocalympics. Depending on how many citizens we have in the event, we could end up with several towns qualifying for the main-event... These towns will be eligible to take part in a Clash of the Titans-style event with one type of each Hero town, plus the victorious resident towns. In addition to distinctions, MTPasses and widespread fame, there will be hero days for the winners at the end of the whole event. It's time for the Residents to take a stand! Anyone caught becoming a hero during the event will be shot in the head.

Registration for this event will open soon (on Oval Office - in the directory), with the event itself tentatively scheduled to begin on or around the 26th of June. More information coming soon... stay tuned!

Be sure to tell your friends about it. This is already going to be the biggest event we've ever hosted - let's make this event the best ever too!
