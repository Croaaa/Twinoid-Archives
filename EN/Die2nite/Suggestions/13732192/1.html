As the subject says, I don't see why Purification Tablets can't be used in the World Beyond. While it's true that the Purifier/Filter obviates them, they are an exceptionally useful tool for long-term camping (whether endgame or expedition).

Under current mechanics, they can't be used outside of town, which from both a RP standpoint and a practical standpoint makes no sense. In a RP context, you drop the tablets into the jerrycan and make sure that they dissolve thoroughly into it, similar to iodine tablets. From a practical position, it's a simple item combination that doesn't require the Workshop.

It would be nice if this received some attention from Epoq, and maybe made it into the S6 release.
