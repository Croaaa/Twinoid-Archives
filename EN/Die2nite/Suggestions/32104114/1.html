I've been experementing with this strategy where I volinteer to be shunned so I'm able to search the town dump daily and provide the items I find to the town. So far it has been pretty damn effective with just myself doing it. I've been able to provide a steady flow of food, water, and drugs for the town at the cost of not being able to use the town bank or work on the construction site. Scavangers are able to search the dump a total of 4 times (instead of the usual 3) and because of this the chances of finding valuable resources each day are increased. I suggest other people try this strategy if they don't mind the shunned distinction and see how effective they are with a group of 3-5 people doing this. The town I'm currently in has been pretty greatful and being able to search the town dump in later days is very useful because it never becomes depleted and you don't have to run the risk of traveling to dangerous zones just to get to an area that isn't depleted.

The usual items you will find in the dump are:
Fleshrooms
Sticky Pastry Ball
Thick Solution
Pound of Flesh
Stagnant Water Can

Lucky finds can be found with a 12.5% chance and they are:
Wire Mesh
Chest
Twisted Plank
Wrouth Iron
Kwik-Fix
Makeshift Barricade
Boxes

Each time you search the town dump there is a 100% chance you will find something. Being able to search the desert and the town dump has allowed me to bring in usually 9 items a day so I really think people should consider this as a long term survival strategy.
