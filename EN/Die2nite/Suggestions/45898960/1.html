shaman can see the souls under unvisited zones

once the shaman gets sellected on day 2 he/she will have daily 5 Soul Action Points and here is what the shaman can do with them:


  • Making it Rain to either kill zeds or if used at the towns Gates it has a % chance to add about 5 water to the well. and this action costs 3 Soul action points which means it can be done only once daily.
  • Brew a Shamanic Potion this requires 1 Soul Action Point and 1 water and it can be done up to 5 times daily. as explained before this potion must be drank as a replacment for daily water when a non-shaman needs to pick up and collect a red tortured Soul to avoid immediate death and become immune for the entire length of that town (not just 1 day). and a reminder if you drink this Mystic Potion you will regain 6 normal AP and refreshed status , BUT at the cost of no longer being able to drink water again to regain another 6 AP. so its a simple exact replacment for water except giving the bonus immunity against tortured Souls.
  • shaman can pick up turtured Souls without any risk for death or the need for drink a mystic Potion and they are the only person in each town that can carry several souls of any form at once in their rucksacks. a shaman carrying 2 souls at once in rucksack , as you can see one of the souls is tortured and the other one is broken lost soul.   • leaving one or several Red Turtured Souls outside as long as each one is in a diffrent zone WILL increase the size of the attack by X = 15% for each red tortured Soul left outside over night. it will start with an increased change of all attack estimations from 30% to 100% and increase both with the X factor of the size of the actual attack which we usualy have as if no souls were outside:
    this factor is confirmed to be X = 15% of the actual size of the attack.
    so to make it simple in an example: if you have an attack size of 100 zombies (best estimation here, not maximum estimation) and you leave one red tortured Soul outside on the ground over night then your attack size will automaticaly become 115 which is 100 + 15% of the 100.
    and if you leave 2 red souls outside the attack will increase from 100 to 130, if you leave 10 red souls outside the attack will increase to 150% to go from 100 to 250.What are the effects of Blue Lost Soul (Broken) INSIDE the town in someone´s rucksack, Chest or the bank over night?
