I know I am going to get slated for saying this, but I don't care. I believe this needs to be said and discussed (again, most likely)

Motion Twin are amazing, in my opinion. They have brought us some pretty sweet games over the last few years and one almighty amazing, possibly the most addictive game in the world, Die2nite. I have searched and searched for an zombie MMO that rivals d2n and I can't find anything that is as accessible, as well put together and as welcoming as this game. To use a favorite quote of an old friend "They have really hit the nail on the head with this one."

Now I have always thought that D2N would be amazing if was cloned onto a version of the game, rather than browser based or 2D. I always though, however, that maybe MT aren't talented in crating that kind of game, maybe they don't have the fanbase for it, maybe they simply don't want to, but the more and more I think about it the more and more I get confused.

Now that Akadeo has been released I know that MT could deliver the game I crave. Now that Lerajie has started work on a fangame which has been met with LOADS of enthusiasm and excitement I know that others crave similar things to me. Now that I have seen others ask for a kinda falout version of Die2nite and have asked for Die2nite to be a downloaded game I know people REALLY crave what I crave.

I HAVE seen other games similar to what I want, but the problem is that these games aren't MT. They have non of that MT charm that makes D2N so playable and they don't have the community. When I go on a forum I want to see Workshop kiwiluke Packersfan joanne Annette rumata and all you other people who haven't got the last post in a thread in the world forums as I'm writing this...
