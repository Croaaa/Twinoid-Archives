What is reputation?
Reputation is a point system given when you do certain actions. Reputation is good because if you have enough, you can fight off 1 complaint, and the person who complained against you will not be able to complain about you.
What if you have a NEGATIVE reputation?
Well, if you have a negative reputation people may want to give you more complaints. Your reputation decreases everytime you take an item from the bank, when you attack some etc
How do you gain and lose reputation?
Here are examples that can increase and decrease your reputation. (You start with 50 base reputation)
-Giving an item to the town: +5 reputation
-Taking an item from the town: -6 reputation
-Becoming a watchmen: +10 reputation
-Attacking someone: -25 reputation
-Saving someone: +25 repuation
-Poisoning someone in town: -100 reputation
-Being a ghoul and eating someone in town: -100 reputation
-Being a watchmen for 2 days straight: +15 reputation
-Being a watchmen for 3 days+ straight: +20 reputation
-Discovering a blueprint: +30 reputation
There may be more, but this is all for now.

What can you do with the points?
Remove 1 complaint: Costs 120 reputation.
+1 Defense to your home: Costs 150 reputation.
Free random weapon: Costs 100 reputation
Super Extra water ration: Costs 110 Reputation.
