Zombait

Not sure if this has been suggested already or not...But like the title says, I think it would be a great idea to have the ability to send a user an anonymous compliment for a job well done. Its purpose would be to balance out the frequent negativity seen in towns of all sizes.

Much like how registering a complaint works, a user selects a compliment from a list of compliments.

I've come up with a couple of compliment suggestions (they don't have to be word for word):

1. Friendly/Helpful Citizen
2. Heroic/Brave Citizen (From saving a trapped citizen)
3. Good Water/Food Rationing
4. Active Forum Poster
5. Dutiful Watchman
6. Ghoul/Zombie Exterminator
7. Adventurous Citizen (from exploring the map)
8. Great Scavenger (from bringing back blueprints/other important or rare items)

These compliments would appear in the user's home alongside their complaint section


PROS:
* Balances out the negativity
* Lets citizens know that they've done a good job/heading in the right direction
* Adds to a user's reputation
* Potential for the receiver of compliments to acquire distinctions

CONS
* acquiring 8 compliments won't gain anything
* potential for coalition members to compliment each other for nothing


Please let me know what you think!
