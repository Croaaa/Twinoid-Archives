I've always assumed that the purpose of a Small Map town is as a training ground for newer players. As is stands now, are they really effective? In order to fulfill that goal and to encourage new long-term players, I would like make the following suggestions:

1) Change the name of Small Map to Training Town.

2) Create a list of training goals (basic mechanics every player should know).

3) Recruit a team of Moderators (with a limited tool bag of Admin powers) to play each town as a hero (thereby promoting pay-to-play). Workshop could be the team leader of the moderators! LOL

4) The Moderators could be rewarded with hero days for time invested. They should also be allowed to play in normal towns (simultaneously).

5) At the successful completion of two Training Towns the residents would "graduate" to Distant Towns/Hardcore Towns.

Please feel free to "hitchhike" on my idea with your suggestions.

My suggestion is to have an unlimited amount of Small Maps available to join at any given time - The catch would be that each Town would be capped with a maximum of 5-10 players with 10 Soul Points or less.

I see this as giving the newbs a stronger chance to get to know the game a bit better.
