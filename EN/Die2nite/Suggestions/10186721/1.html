When new players join up, they're automatically put into a "practice town" with other players who have signed up for the first time. This fixes two problems:

- Older players getting frustrated with new players who know nothing about nothing
- Towns being plagued with good-for-nothing players who die of dehydration on day 4 like clockwork because they signed in, realized the game wasn't for them, and never came back to the site

If the new players were put in these practice towns, it would give them a chance to see if the game is their style AND get a chance to learn the ropes.

Thoughts?
