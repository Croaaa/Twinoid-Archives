Guardian:
this one is a small town is good but in a distant town is useless.. not just that but theres non-guardians with same amount of CP as a guardian <_<
my idea is that instead of just giving +2CP it should turn the hero's base CP into 2x.
in other words: if its a new hero with 2CP then they would be a 4CP guardian.. But if its a old hero with 4CP then they would be a 8CP guardian!


Technician vs Survivalist:
i placed this 2 together just to show how useless technician is, besides the part where every town needs 1 technician to open the doors, and theres no need for any more technicians since the survivalists completely overcome them!
Lets compare 2 builders of each class and they both do the 1AP away scavenging.
- Technician uses 6CP in building + 4AP in workshop/building.
- Survivalist uses 10AP in building/workshop.
its same amount! But, survivalist can place his points in workshop... and as if that wasnt enough, technician needs to drink every other day!

So i have 2 possible suggestions (1 of them would be enough to balance it):
- either make the technician recover his CP every time a AP item is consumed;
- or make the survivalists "find water/food" skill with a bigger cooldown! instead of being able to use every day, make every other day;
