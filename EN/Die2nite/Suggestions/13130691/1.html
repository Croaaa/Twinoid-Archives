Okay, so I know Kwik-Fix is an option with the upgraded Seeker ability, but I think it'd be nice to have an additional hero power that allows us, once per game, to fix any broken item. It'd add a little extra scope and variety to the "Which hero power should I use today?" question, without really being overpowered in any way. It'd give us some leeway to venture out, one day per town, without having to worry about taking anything to fix our weapons.

We'd be able to conserve Seeker, sure, but if we had to repair things twice we might have to use it for that anyway.

I don't know, it's not really a big deal either way but I think it'd be a nice addition. It could also be a nice early unlock, because it sort of feels like there's nothing particularly useful (Brothers in Arms aside) for the first 60 days.

"By some miracle, poking and prodding and banging things together has allowed you to repair (item)! Just don't break it again..."
