I would say focus on materials like wood, metal. Then, you can either get water items, food (food is important for maximum ap usage), weapons, and then any other stuff. If you find any hero building items (you are going to need to look these hero items up), then you should bring it back. Hero items are like old washing machine and like microwave I think. With those hero items, heros can upgrade their homes; e.g. making a kitchen that allows cooking certain food for ap boost items for the town.
