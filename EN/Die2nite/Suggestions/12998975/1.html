GamerGuy898

I'm unsure if this has been suggested already, I apologize if it has.

I think it would be nice if there were an option on hero's soul pages that allowed them to deactivate hero mode.
When Hero mode is deactivated, only the "Resident" profession will be available to choose.
Basically, it'll be as if you never even had any hero days.

Notes: Hero mode cannot be deactivated/activated when the player is currently in a town and, obviously, days within a town while having hero mode deactivated will not count toward unlocking hero abilities.
Also, Player's with hero mode deactivated will be unable to choose a specific town to join.

This is choice of deactivation is useful for people that have won Last Man Standing hero days, because usually the amount of hero days from LMS doesn't last an entire town's length.
Thus, it would be helpful to deactivate Hero mode and wait until you win LMS again, so that you could be a Hero for the full time within a town.

Another advantage of this is for players who have bought 3-6+ months of Hero time and wish to partake in a town as a resident. After all, Being a Hero is quite demanding when you have residents pestering you to save them and escort them from outside of town.

The only disadvantage of this that I can see, is that Heroes may abuse this in order to enter the resident's apocalympics.
