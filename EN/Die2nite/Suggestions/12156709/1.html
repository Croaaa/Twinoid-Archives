[ http://twinoid.com/en/article/3464/zombie-attack ]

As you are aware, our servers have been under unforeseen but sustained attack during the last 48 hours . This concerted attack has had the result the zombies had hoped for, blocking citizens from returning to town after a long day's scavenging, making it impossible to close the town gates at night leaving citizens forced to face the might of the horde with nothing but harsh language and a feather duster...

Given the extreme nature of the situation, we have declared a state of emergency (and stocked up on coffee, Red Bull and Pro-Plus):
Until further notice, towns which have not been devastated will have their gates shut automatically before the attack!

Once our defences have been rebuilt and our servers are once more beyond reach, we will find a way to immortalize these recent times of anguish which we have shared with you .

What we can do though, is reassure you that we have made the most of this difficult time to:

  • Improve the Brothers In Arms system of donating Hero days.
  • Correct the bug which meant that some souls donated to the bank were not counted in the town score (this was only possible for current towns).
  • Improve the soul collection system. From now on the attribution of points will be clearer and more simple.
  • An attempt to correct an issue which some of you experienced: an involuntary page refresh issue.

Braaaaainnns...
