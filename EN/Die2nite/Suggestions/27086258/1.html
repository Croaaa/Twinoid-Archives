ImoseyamaThen try to join meta towns

This may help HeadcrabPL, but it does not help the game as a whole.

1. By having most of the stronger and/or active players regularly playing in metas, it creates a chasm. The meta Town's get stronger and stronger, whilst the random Town's get weaker and weaker.

It also helps to create a sense of ownership, or elitism, amongst some of the full on serious players, which in turn leads to the alienation of casual/non-serious players.

2. It does not address the issue, and the issue presented in this Thread IMO is that die2nite is not an attractive game for newcomers.

18 of the Dehydration Deaths HeadcrabPL refers to are the result of people registering their account, but never logging in again - None of them have Validated their Death.

The reasons for this are probably varied, however IMO, the overwhelming factor is that this game is 'promoted' as a Zombie game, where the reality is that it's a strategy game with a Zombie genre backdrop.

So in further answer to your question, HeadcrabPL, both MT and the serious die2nite players would do this game a great service if they started to push it in the 'strategy' and 'psychology' gamer circles and fraternities.

Cheers,

G
