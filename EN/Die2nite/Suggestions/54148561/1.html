I was looking at the MMJ scoreboard... and it was showing wrong results - one of the towns had 40 points more than the others (counting 1 more day for them). And I was thinking - what if we actually have a way to get more points?

And my idea was [missions/quests] - there could be a daily mission that gives extra points if you do it (points could vary from mission to mission) or it could be an item you find by digging that triggers the mission. (For example - there could be a [map] item - after you do task 1 2 3 4 - you reveal a new building or just get extra points for season score). (Another example - "Build a reactor for Joanne" ) (Another example - all citizens should walk total 500km for the day) (Another example - camp zones 1 2 3 4 5 by 1 camper - give camping bonus to the whole town)

The idea is to change the standard meta play that hasn't changed much for the last... lots of seasons

I can write more and more but I'll wait to hear some feedback how bad is the idea
