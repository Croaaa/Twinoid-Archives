Leapfrog

Guardians could use their shield to kill zombies?
Personally, I'm just starting as a guardian but I really like the idea of the class. Bear in mind I don't really have a lot of experience, but it seems that with heroes gaining CP-increasing benefits - which the guardian also gains - the CP gap seems a little smaller than intended. What starts as the guardian having double the CP of everyone else ends up with them having a max of 7 versus a max of 5 under the same circumstances. From a certain perspective this (comparatively) smaller gap makes guardians less useful.

I mean... believe me, I'm not jealously crying out that I don't want other classes able to have what I have - you see enough of that in other class-based online games. But common consensus seems to be that the guardian is a little underpowered, and... I think it'd be nice to be able to gain a little more CP. I think that would be in line with the idea of the guardian without making them too overpowered.

My initial thought was that maybe you could earn an extra CP through playing as a guardian for a certain amount of days, but then you'd have to give other classes experience-based unlockable abilities to be fair...

So my idea is that maybe, once or twice per town, the guardian could have the ability to upgrade their shield with a couple of components?

Er, anyway, I don't want to derail the topic. I haven't played the Tamer but they sound absolutely fine to me. Being able to send back a rucksack's worth of stuff is a huge AP saver. But I guess it's a pain to carry anabolic steroids if you want your labradoodle to carry heavy stuff, so maybe they could have the ability to find some pet-only steroids out in the wastes (using the labradoodle's nose or something) or be able to use Seeker to do the same?
