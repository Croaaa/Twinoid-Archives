Soo.. for those who played Legacy style board games, such as Pandemic Legacy, I would love to see a version of it with Die2nite assets. I even had a dream of playing a version of it. It would have:

Cards for random events (think Crossroad cards from Dead of Winter) and improvements
Dice to represent how many survivors you control, and die results dictating actions you can do
An assortment of tokens or some mechanic for resources
Some RNG feature for nightly attacks.
Role templets

Once the town was destroyed, you would get soul points to spend on various things, sticker fashion, that you could apply to your player-soul card, such as:

Survivor count, which is more dice you roll and the pips let you do certain actions
Abilities and bonuses going further
Starting items

I love Die2nite, but it is past it's prime. Something like this that could scale to table top would fit lovely into my board game collection, and could be a nice send off~
