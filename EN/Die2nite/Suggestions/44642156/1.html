So once a town gets devastated and there are alot of people out camping the ruins it can get a bit boring. I mean other than finding Defensive Objects and hoping you stashed enough water not much to do.

I am sitting at a camp site looking at the piles and piles of stuff that has been amassed around me. I am wondering... Why the heck can't I build something? I have stuff. I have AP and as a technician CP just sitting here.

I think it would be a nice change if there was an added ability to build constructions at your ruin to improve the defense of the site.

The builds could be generic ie find the required items and just improve campsite. Or they could be more specific to the ruin type like the improvement to a cave is adding a door to make it harder for the zombies to get in.

These constructions would need a lower AP count but could require a decent number of items so that it requires some scavanging and running around the waste to collect what is needed. Ie back to that cave door. I have a beam, I have wood planks, I have iron, I have duct tape. I have all the basics but finding that wood beam is a rarer find that makes it hard to build. Then make it take 10-15AP so it could be a 2 day build job.

The builds would permanently upgrade the topology of the ruin and not be lost like normal camp site improvements. However, could add in that there is chance if the building is not camped each night that it gets damaged.

Its not going to be anything that would radically change the end game but it would add something to do making the long days camping at the end of a town more interesting.

Beyond just ability to improve the defense of your ruin could add in some little things like the below.

If you get an oven, a fridge, a table and a chair you can build a kitchen to change 6AP food to 7AP food in the waste.

If your camped at a hospital you could with enough pharmaceticals, alcohol and a few other items rebuild it enough to cure wounds.

If your at a police station or an armory with time and materials you could fix broken weapons from the spare parts after making a weapon repair station.
