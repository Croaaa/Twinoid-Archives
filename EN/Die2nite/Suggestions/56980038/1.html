Something like this has been proposed multiple times, eg. here http://www.die2nite.com/tid/forum#!vi...
but it's not the same suggestion.

In my idea, there's new type of game what is basically private "town" with multiple towns.
From 2 to even 6 towns on single map (size of map would be 26-31, depending on towns amount). Imagine yourself CoT event in that form.
Zombie spawns should be around 2-5x bigger, depending on towns.
All game mechanics almost the same, with adding some options to interacting with enemy's towns(sneak into the town and steal something from the bank as a scout?) or players.
Camping can be even more funny than normal. Who will first get BP's from ruins? (Someone who will first hide for the night?) And who will faster kill each other in time of the end-camping? (Chaos mode?)

Advantages of new game type:
- More players on one map! Up to 240 on biggest events like CoT!
- Mayors can create new game whenever they want, they should also have ability to check if that game will be visible for random players to join.
- Possibility of creating that "town" type only by Mayor, no default hosting(too many slots to fill with people, waiting time to start would be around 6 days or even more).
- Randomizing players through teams when joining (or selecting what town they want join, if allowed by town creating Mayor).
- More emotions! You wanted to take that N&B's from World Beyond, but someone from the other team took it? Sad
- Town participation, stealing from each other if possible, of performing other actions, like catapulting bombs(exploding grapefruits!) on other town buildings? (catapult can be more useful then!)
- Map size depending on towns amount.
- Same with zed spawning amount.
- Make zombies harder? Having 2 CP instead of 1?
- More explorable ruins on map, depending on towns amount. From 2 to 4?
- A bit lower zombie attacks, survival will be anyway harder than in the normal town. Zombie attacks around 25% lower?
- New rankings for that type of "town".
- New distinctions related to this mode.
- Separated forums for each town.
- More buildings than in normal sized map.

One question, how to limit attacks on other town's citizens? They all can die because of wounds in first 5 days, if we don't limit it.

Any other suggestions?
