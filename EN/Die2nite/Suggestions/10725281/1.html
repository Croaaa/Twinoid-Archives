This is a list I just put together for when I jump random towns in the future, but I figured it might do yall some good in the mean time. It is only a guideline and probably needs some tweaks but hey what doesn't in the MT world

Item Scavenging Priority List

This is a generalized list of what to bring back to town. There is some variability depending on the towns needs so always give town requests priority over this list.
All food should be left 6 ap out

Most important:
1. Jarpen Table, Flatpacks (until enough tables have been found)
2. Hacksaw, Dice, Cards, Cafetiere, Cyanide, Can opener or Screwdriver (until 1 of each has been found)
3. Ultra Rucksack, Utility Belt, Manbag, Shopping Trolley
4. Machete, Chainsaw, Guard Dog, MK 2, Devastator, Fat Cat, Mobile Phone, Exploding Water Bomb, Exploding Grapefruit, Water Bomb, Lawn Mower, Large Chest
5. All Blue Prints, Sealed Arcitect's Chest
6. Defensive Objects (mattress, trestle, sheet metal, solid wooden board, unshaped concrete blocks, old door, jarpen table, Wire Mesh, car door complete)
7. Boxes, Iron Chest
8. Bag of Cement, Sheet Metal, Crate Lid
9. Vodka, Wake the Dead, Winness
10.Jerry cans, Water, Nuts and Bolts, Electronic Component, Safe, Repair Kit, Qwik Fix, Festering Flesh, Groundsheet
11. Twinoid, Anabolic Steroids

Important:
1. Metal Support, Patchwork Beam, Chest, Bandage, Pharmaceutical Products, Tool Bag
2. Aqua Splash, Hurling Stick, Serrated Knife,
3. Broken Electronic Device, Mechanism, Copper Pipe
4. Duct Tape, Semtex, Battery
5. Compact Detonator, Belt
6. Wonky Shopping Trolley
7. Tool Box, Sheet of Plywood

Good:
1. Wrought Iron, Twisted Plank
2. Electric Whisk, Jerrycan Gun, Human Bone, Rusty Chain, Engine
3. Huge Snake, Chicken, Stinking Pig, Giant Rat
4. Water Cooler Bottle,
5. Pocket Vibrator (one need one)
6. Betapropine, Unlabeled Drug, Hydratone, Paraectoid, Vallium Shot, Water Purifying Tablets,
7. Ness Quick Weed Killer, Opened Pack of Cigarettes, Radius Mark 2, Radius Beacon
8. Box of Matches

Okay:
1. Scrap Metal, Rotting Log,
2.Plastic Bag and Semtex, Plastic Bag, Water Pistol, Battery Launcher 1-ITF, Taser, Can Opener, Screwdriver, Adjustable Spanner, Box Cutter, Burnt Out Torch,Pathetic Penknife, Staff, Swiss Army Knife, EMS
3. Beer Fridge, Carcinogenic Oven, Ektorp-Gluten Chair, Old Washing Machine, PC Base Unit
4. Thick Solution

Least important (i.e. only bring back if there is absolutely nothing else):
1.Quality Log, Radio Cassete Player
2.Machine Gun, Revolver
3.Bedside Lamp, Doormat, Mini HiFi, Persian Rug, Rocking Chair, Cash, Padlock and Chain
4.Hacksaw, Cards, Dice, Cyanide, Pocket Vibrator (after 1 of each has already been brought back)
5. Pine Fresh, Strong Spices, Crushed battery, Unrecognizable Remains, Corrosive Liquid, Poisoned water(should be left in ruin) (or food though there shouldn't be any)
