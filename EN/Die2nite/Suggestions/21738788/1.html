Well before starting the building, I would like to change the battery system a little bit!

the current battery system includes a battery and a crushed battery, however the new system would include: power battery, regular battery (same as current battery) and depleted battery. as in the next link (image) (the image contains old and new systems)

current scavenging would give you a battery only (of course you wouldn't find crushed useless one), however in the new system you would be able to scavenge all types of batteries to get 20% of the time powerful battery 40% of the time regular batteries and 20% depleted batteries when finding a battery (you might need to go to town to check the battery charge). Or else, you would get a battery box that would give you one battery with the same ratio as above.

Now for the building, Battery Charger:
It would be freely available under Foundation.
It would cost:
4 x powerful batteries
1 x engine
1 x trestle
1 x copper pipe
2 x rough iron

The building would do the following:
after attack time:
all depleted batteries would tern into regular batteries
all the regular batteries would tern into powerful batteries
all powerful batteries would not be changed
batteries must be in bank in order for status changes to be applied. batteries at homes or rucksack or out of town would not be charged!


This would help in killing more zombies, change the strategy of what to take outside and what to bring back to town, changes the time to build foundation.

any other suggestions and modifications to this suggestion are welcomed
