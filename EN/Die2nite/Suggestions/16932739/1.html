The Zombie King spawns at a random corner of the map in hardcore towns and moves diagonally 1/2 spaces every day (as in Bresenham's line algorithm) towards the town. The corner that he spawns in is not initially known, and must be discovered with scouting.

He starts off with 100-200 HP that must be worn away by attrition every day. If he reaches the town (standing directly outside), the town defense is halved for that night. This could continue until he is dead every night, or I guess he could just die after the attack.

If you are in the same zone as the Zombie King, we can say that he requires 10 control points or something along with the other zombies that I guess he could spawn every day in the same zone as him.

Maybe he could be called Zombie of Zombies like in Lollipop Chainsaw too?
