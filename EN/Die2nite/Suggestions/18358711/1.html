The defence value shown at the top of the towns main page shows the total defence value of the town. This is misleading seeing as how the watchmen's defence only comes into play if the horde breaks into the town via using only the town defence.

In fact, there is no value given for the town defence, only for the watchmen so you have to subtract that from the total defence to find the towns defence.

This causes confusion as to whether the town defences will hold or not. Especially to newer players. It also becomes tedious to subtract the watchmen's defence everytime the town defence needs to be checked.
