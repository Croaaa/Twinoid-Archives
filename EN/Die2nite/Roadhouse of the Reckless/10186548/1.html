Hi Liros, sorry to hear that!

My suggestion is to look for a group with people that you think would be fun to play with, even for just one town. Judge people from their posts on the WF or their behavior on IRC. Then ask some of those people personally when they plan to jump into their next town, either by twinoid message or (preferably) on IRC. Just tell them that you're interested in playing with them, that usually works!

Most metas are not nearly as cliquey as they appear, you just have to approach them. General shout-outs of "Can I join some meta?" are often far less effective.

DjangoAX

Honestly, if you feel like quitting is the only way to make you happy, then do it.

However, if you're trying to find a reason to play, the best way to go about it is to join a meta, or hope to run into one as you play.

I feel like this game could easily start the monopoly effect, though. Dunno what the proper term is, but it's where you bring friends to the game to try and have a good time, but the longer you play with them, the more you'll want to kill them all.

The only way I've been able to play this game and have fun is abandoning all notion that you will work well with everyone, and only work with one or two people you would take a bullet for.

My bro is the only one I trust to play this game, and we collaborate without a lot of conflicting thoughts because of it. When we had a large group of 5 in the past, things quickly got out of hand and was the main reason it didn't become fun anymore, because there were too many thoughts and not enough unified actions.

If there isn't one person you'd want to spend eternity on an island to play this game with, the next best option would be to only focus on yourself in game and say "TO HELL WITH THE TOWN, THIS IS MY WORLD NOW."

I wouldn't recommend playing that way, but sometimes making other people angry is all it takes to make a game like this feel fun.

It's up to you to find joy in this game, and if you can't, this game may just not be the one for you.
