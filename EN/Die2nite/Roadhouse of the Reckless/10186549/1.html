Die2Nite Self-Profile

By taking this profile you will learn your personality style and how to make it work for you.

This should help:

Identify your particular playing style

Gain a better understanding of yourself and others

Predict how you or others will respond in given situations

Improve your communication with others that have different playing styles from yours


Of course this is just a guideline.
Most people will flex between styles based on what is going on in their private lives.
(IF they have a life outside of Die2nite)

There are no right or wrong answers. Trust me.

Disclaimer: FOR FUN ONLY.

What it all means!

Your Affiliative score measures your needs and desires for being around other people. If you scored high (4 or above) you enjoy playing with other people. Your profession of choice would most likely be as a class that you can interact with others. If you scored low (3 or below) you would most likely select a class that prefers to work independently.

Your Directive score measures your needs and tendencies to direct and control situations. A high score (4 or above) indicates you are comfortable meeting new people and controlling situations. If you scored low (3 or below) you are more comfortable being supportive and may prefer to determine how others feel before proceeding.

The higher or lower you are on these scales, the more likely you are to have the tendencies indicated. For instance if you scored 6 on the affiliative scale you probably have a high need to work with other players. This would be important in choosing a class that you will be happy playing. You need the contact with others to make the game enjoyable for you.
On the other hand, if you scored a 1, you would prefer playing solo. And most likely would not be chatty in town. Or would make a statement and expect everyone to understand it and not want to have a dialogue.

If you scored near the middle of either scale (a 3 or a 4) you will tend to be more flexible and will find you have characteristics of more than one quadrant. For instance, if you scored a 4 on the directive score, you probably don’t mind running an expedition but you don’t feel compelled to do it. You would be just fine with participating and giving input when asked. Or if you scored a 3, you might prefer to work alone, but would still feel comfortable in a small group.

Important!!!!! Your scores indicate preference not ability or skills!

When organizing a town…. this information would be good to have. For choosing classes for people and having a more coordinated team.



Die2nite Profile Graph

Using your converted directive score and your converted affiliative score look at the graph. If you put a dot on the horizontal affiliative line indicating your first score and put a dot on the vertical directive line indicating your second score and connect the 2 dots you should have a triangle in one of the 4 quadrants. The larger the triangle the more you dominate in that quadrant.

To be a fully functioning and effective player…(in this game and in life) you must, and DO, pull from all 4 aspects of your personality. For some people, one aspect dominates and creates a very definitive play style. For others, their preferences are less defined and their styles may blend.


Of course, just because you scored high in one quadrant today doesn’t mean you would get the same score if you took the profile again in a month. You can flip-flop to one style or another depending on your mood, what kind of town it is, what is going on in your real life. These are just tendencies. But generally, you will favor one style, preferring it over the other 3.
