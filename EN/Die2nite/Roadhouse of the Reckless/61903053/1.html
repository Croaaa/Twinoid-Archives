Dear Mr. eed of the present,

I was also against using MH because there was no external tool like FDTD.

But have you tried MH recently? A german guy took over the Fata Morgana tool and did some updates based on what we complained about it.

It is much better now. You can customize it in many ways: you can see previous day maps, bank, and items; you can send this spy version address of the map to someone else and they can check how your town is going. My only complaints right now are that it doesn't have a cached version of the map for us to see while the game is down for the attack; It doesn't have a Scout Sense thingie too. Other than that I really must say the german guy did a great job. MH is playable for me and will be a lot more enjoyable as soon as this alpha drug goes away.

That said, I want to thank BerZerg from the bottom of my heart for all these years being our eyes in the World Beyond and I wish him luck in his real life incarnation.
