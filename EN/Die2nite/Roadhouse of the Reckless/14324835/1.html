So many meta players frown at random / derp towns and apparently think they're too good for them and ignore their town forums.

Silly! It's silly!

These towns can be a lot of fun. Open up your range of expectations, post on the town forums and don't worry about a thing. You can make it fun and you'll find that there is always 1, 2 or 10 interesting players in there. Of course you can help them enjoy the game, learn a few tricks but you can also be goofy or devious, whatever rocks your socks.

Why not post jokes or bad puns or random comments or forum games (what is this video game/movie? (and you post a screenshot) | Poker | Rock Paper Scissors | Anagrams / Longest word you can find from random letters | dice games | riddles | Role Play | five word stories | etc.) and see how it goes?

See why people are playing Die2Nite or how they heard about it. See where players are from....

And of course and most importantly, if you see a random player called Workshop make sure you send them all your best stuff! That's always a good thing.
