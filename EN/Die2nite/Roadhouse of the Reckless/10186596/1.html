Bored with same-old blue-ish standard uniform?
Do you have this feeling that everyone looks the same?


Time for a change!

I present to you, first Die2Nite Character Creator!


Zombie-Fashion - Die2Nite Character Creator



You can design your character and then share it with others by posting a link!

Application is still under development... Expect some changes in nearest future. I will add more items (like weapons and hats) and more options, like saving your character as a picture file.

Please do not flood this topic with multiple links.
When posting a link, you should also write few words about the character you've created, to avoid spam.

I hope you like it and have fun!
