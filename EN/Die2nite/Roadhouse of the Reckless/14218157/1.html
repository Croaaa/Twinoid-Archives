It's 2 a.m. here and I can't sleep, so forgive my grumpiness, it's not intended (unless you are a certain company, then it is).
This might get rather long, so feel free to ignore it.

I just want to say how disappointed I am in this game and in this company. I had very high hopes that we might get a good game with an awesome community here, in contrast to most other browser games.
We do have a great community, a very active and dedicated community that keeps the game alive and fun for those involved. But that's no thanks to MT, who did their best to prevent us from joining up and playing metas etc...
Right now, the active, organized community is in large parts disjointed from the random original game.
We sit in our ivory towers and play to win. True. But you know what? Randoms suck pretty much. 10 dead in the first 2 days is very common in 100+ towns. After 5 seasons, MT still hasn't figured out a way to teach new players how to survive, or, god beware, how to keep them playing. Honestly, that's embarassing. No wonder people are trying to organize themselves and forget about that random horror.

You have the best community I've seen in any game so far and yet, you continously show us only disrespect and never ever listen. It would be so easy to support the community, but all I've ever seen is disruptive to communal playing. External forums are not allowed, non-paying players can't use the forums, the forums themselves are pretty bad... No talk of anything non-D2N related... That list could go on. And this is nothing recent or unheard of, it has been mentioned time after time with no response whatsoever.
This game lives by its community.
Yet everything you do is like a giant "f*** you" in our direction.
Don't like the forums? Oh, we'll change them. No, we're not making them better. Instead, you can't moderate threads anymore. Oh, you also need ten thousand clicks more to access anything. We also hid them, you'll never be bothered by new players again.
Then, communication. If there is any, it's either through Twinoid or better yet, through facebook or twitter. If it concerns the game, put it in the game where we look.
It would also be nice to have someone to talk to for once! I don't mean Epoq replying very rarely, but to have a chat with deepknight or someone in charge, or at least know that someone is actually reading the prizing discussions or suggestions we have. Because evidently, nobody ever does.
That makes me sad, because they are good discussions. There are awesome suggestions out there.
But why? People are paying anyway. Now they have to pay more than ever.

Yes, there were good changes. I like explorable ruins, Shaman needs tweaking but is fun and the game flow is as it always was. But that doesn't change the fundamental problems, because those aren't in the gameplay. They are in the way we as a community, paying or not, get treated.
The problems are that there are too few new players even trying, because random towns have become extremly depressing.
I'm sure things could improve a whole lot there, a decently implemented chat would help new players so much.
Any tutorial teaching you the basics, a mentoring program...
Instead we get forced Twinoid, crappy forums and a price increase.

I paid for this game because I wanted to support the devs, I wanted the promised original content, I wanted a good game.
I don't think I got much for the money, because what makes this game great for me does not come from the people making this game but rather from those playing it.
