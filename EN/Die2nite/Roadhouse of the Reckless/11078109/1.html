Scavenger Hunt Game

You are a Supreme Hunter, Alpha Class Predator, Top of the Food Chain, Resident with a BRD and this list, etc.
You have 100 days to complete Tasks within die2nite and come out as the Top Predator.
You get 30 Hero Days for this, bragging rights, adulation of your peers, sand balls to the head!!

The Game and Points ARE NOT FAIR.

Its been set up so that Hunting people gets the most points. Its been set up so its difficult to complete all task. Its also set up so that some task make other task almost impossible to complete. You are a Hunter so suck it up and do your best or worse. Act Like a Supreme Predator, it is your job to Hunt people down. NO BEGGING or SPAMMING, especially to those on Hunted list and video makers. Some Hunted will help you out, some will make you cry, and we want to see you cry.
The Game and Points ARE NOT FAIR.

Scoring:
You have 100 days or Season End (whichever is Less) to complete tasks
You Must Survive in town at least 5+ days for it to Count
You can Get Bonus Points but they only count when you complete all of 10 tasks

Each task is worth 1 point. There are 10 tasks you need to complete in Season 5:
Bonus points can only be achieved if you complete your 10 tasks

But Mommy It was a Bonus Last Season, well sugar your a HUNTER now
Survive in 10+ days in 5 towns or more
You Must Survive 10+ days in 5 separate towns.

[ ] Collect Multiple Names off Hunted List.
Every 2 extra names 3 points
You must Hunt your initial 5 players and you have to survive 5+ days for towns to count. For every 2 additional names off the Hunted list you get 3 points. Survive in 2 separate towns lasting 5+ days with those from your Hunted List and they count as extras. It is almost impossible to get all 25 "Hunted" for 36 total points in 100 days.

[ ] Survive 5+ days in more Word Towns.
For Every 2 towns 1 point
You get 2 points for your 1st 2 towns then 1 point for every 2 towns afterwards

[ ] Survive 20+ days in town as a resident 2 points each
Yeah Residents get a bonus if you don’t die2nite. You can be BIA’d while in town.

[ ] Kill 700 Zeds. Every 2000 killed 1 point
Yeah 700+1300=2000 that’s 2 points

[ ] Male Video & Female Video 2 points. [ ] Anti videos 8 points *
Survive 1 towns for 5+ days where primary town video is posted by a Female and Survive 1 towns for 5+ days where primary town video is posted by a Male (2 points). THEN survive in separate towns where same Male and/or Same Female Don’t make a video (8 additional points). You have to be in at least 2 towns to accomplish this and videos have to continue for 5 days (not necessarily 5 you survive).

NO Begging Citizens to make a video if it happens it happens.
You will be Disqualified for not acting like a Supreme HUNTER.
Yes you can make a video, but if its crap and doesn’t promote d2n or your town, we will laugh at you on IRC, forums, Twinoid and it won’t count. Remember practice make perfect and you'll get better at making them after our laughter and ridicule dies down. Then your videos will count.

Sashimi is a military mind and if you declare yourself a Hunter you better be ready to prove it. Do your own work.

Why 1000 Messages?
We hope that our HUNTERS won't spam but will create, chat games, engage and encourage others to use forums, post videos and music selections, and have a bit of fun with this.
(plus MrL567 and b00t really love this change)

Some Task make others harder, why is this?
You will need to actively participate with your town mates to complete task. When you work with your town mates all task can be completed with ease. We didn't realise it at first, but it now seems as if the Scavenger Hunt makes better MMJ, meta and coalition citizens.
