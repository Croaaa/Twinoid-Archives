Someone in my current town mentioned that for him, the map updater extension for Chrome was taking a long time and that's why they were hesitant to use it.

I had noted for months that 1 of the 4 maps was "broken" - Atlas - so I emailed Simas Toleikis and about half an hour later he emailed me back this:

" Thanks for the heads up. I am not playing Die2Nite actively myself right now so I was unaware of this. I have updated the extension with removed Atlas and some other minor tweaks. It should auto-update to all users shortly."

BTW, there is way in the extension's options to deselect certain maps it updates to speed things up further.

I highly recommend playing this game using the Chrome browser and the map auto-updater extension. Nothing else has such ease of use - it's all done automatically. All you have to do is wait for the light to turn green in a couple seconds and you're good to move on from your zone.
