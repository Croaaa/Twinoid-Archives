So I'm not quite sure where interest stands on this, but I said I'd open registration on the 1st of August. The event is planned for August 20.

The idea is to emulate the conditions of the Clash of the Titans and to fill in the gap between them, but for the sake of fun and/or practice rather than competition.

So there'll be seven towns, each made up of 40 of one hero class, and as usual each town tries to survive for as long as possible.

If all goes to plan and there's sufficient interest, I'll organize a jump for the 20th of August.

Towns will be led and organised by town leaders, who will be volunteers.

I'd like to avoid spreadsheets for the moment. I think if anyone interested just gives their name here, we can avoid spreadsheet vandalism and also have a very transparent indication of the sort of numbers we can expect.

Entry requirements are up to the town leaders but are encouraged to be very minor. Obviously hero days are required.

If you're interested in participating, please copy the list of your chosen class and add your name to it if there are less than 40 entrants:
