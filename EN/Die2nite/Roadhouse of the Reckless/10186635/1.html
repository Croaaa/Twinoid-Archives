IMO, any decoration item that doesn't count as a DO, or is needed for a construction/home improvement or count as a quality weapon (usually just the fat cat there) should be left in the desert, since even a rotten log is better than a wad of cash.

Most of the time, I don't see hoarding deco items as a shunnable offense, but taking food/water/boosters out the bank to get them? That's a different matter and may well be worth shunning someone.
