Lerajie
Well it's finally here!

It's time for the community to finally start designing their own D2N themed T-Shirts.

What do I need to do?
Simple really! Just design your very own Die2Nite themed T-Shirt, upload the images, post them here and be in with the chance to win free hero days!

Do you have any examples?
I sure do, here is a shirt I not only designed but actually got printed! It's not perfect but was an ideal test.
Front Concept
Back Concept
Front Printed
Back Printed
Back Detailed


How detailed do the designs need to be?
That's totally up to you, but the more detailed they are, the better you have a chance at winning! You never know, others might actually want to print your design.

How do I design them?
Use D2N related artwork or your own original designs, please try to keep other copyrighted images to a minimum (for obvious reasons).
Basic image editing software can suffice if needed.
Certain online services can also help. I used vistaprint to actually print my design and help finish off the text, etc. Such services can even provide the preview and concepts for ease of use.

Any other rules?
The end date will be February 20th! So you have roughly 1 month.
The winner(s) will be chosen by myself and a couple of other judges (which won't be revealed)
You can include as many designs as you like, but you can only win 1 prize! A single person won't be able to win 1st, 2nd & 3rd prizes.
Residents are more then welcome to enter the contest. Simply have a hero you know (or in your current town) post on your behalf or failing that, add me on Oval Office and I'll post for you.

What are the prizes?
1st place prize: 30 hero days.
2nd place prize: 20 hero days.
3rd place prize: 10 hero days.
If anyone would like to help include some hero time to these prizes then your more then welcome.

I hope that covers it! Any other questions then ask away. Good luck all and happy designing!

Alright, these will probably be my last entries. So very time consuming. @_@

Map Shirt

I didn't "finish" the whole shirt because that would take forever, but I'm pretty sure you get the gist of it. It's essentially a plaid shirt designed to look like the world beyond map.

It has some room for variation. This particular design has a breast pocket which I used as a point of focus for the map by putting a town and explored zones on it. You could just as easily have a shirt with no pockets and simply put zones wherever you want. It could have several towns on it even.

As you can see, I put some maps beside it for reference. It's not at all inconceivable to have different versions of the shirt to match the various external map applications. I darkened the hues of the original to give an idea of what that might look like:

Map Shirt 2


Lastly, I made a really quick design for tamer pants!

Tamer Pants 1

Tamer Pants 2

Basically they're cargo pants that use the tamer dog head as pocket flaps. On the first one, I marked a seam to show where the flap would bend up. It essentially leaves the mouth of the dog as the flap (and you could have the lower jaw as part of the pocket so it's like an opening mouth). You could even make the nose a button! Using larger pockets, you can have your puppy carry heavy items!
