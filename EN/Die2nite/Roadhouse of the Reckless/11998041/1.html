WalkinCrow

When I tried my hand at it I did an outline of what I wanted to talk about for that day. Sort of like we had to do in High School English class before writing a paper. Given your accent I'm guessing that isn't applicable.
I wrote down the things I wanted to talk about, in the order I wanted to talk about them. Opened a window for each subject so when I moved to the next subject I could show people what I was talking about, with a single click.
Town Overview - Click to the window
Well -click to the window
Bank - Click to the window
Map....
Once I had my outline and all my windows lined up in order on my task bar I had a general idea of what I was going to talk about(even if my voice just sounded like rolling thunder). After that I just winged it.
A ten minute vidcast usually took me about 45-60 minutes between figuring out what was interesting to talk about and having to start over again because I was 1/2 through before just plain getting tongue tied on a subject.
I hope that helps.
