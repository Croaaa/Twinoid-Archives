More spoilers

There's a few items listed on the wiki that are only available in other versions. Most seem to be seasonal, but some are related to reducing the effect of another "feature" we don't have yet, which is a penalty for searching zones at night (7pm-7am game time). Because of the language of the other versions, the vast majority of their users are likely to be in one timezone, whereas d2n is played by many people from all over the world. I really don't think it would be a good thing to implement here as it's likely to screw over a big chunk of the userbase (e.g. USA) while not really having much effect on people who are probably going to be ASing in their sleep (e.g. Europe). So hopefully that's another thing that won't get ported.

But if we don't get either of those things, what will we get? Up until now, we've been able to look at the differences between versions and predict the new toys we'll get in each new season, but there's not much left to port. I might be wrong (hopefully) but I think MT are too busy integrating Twinoid into everything right now to be adding new (unique) stuff for us. So I think season 6 is going to be the more interesting one for updates.
