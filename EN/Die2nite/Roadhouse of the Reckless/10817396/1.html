It's Curtains for You!
Even less morals and more violence

Link to the rules and all that.

Town will start Friday 4th May / Saturday 5th May, around 23.45 game time

So, *GAME TIME* that's the night between Friday 4th May and Saturday 5th May. It's late on Friday night, at 23.45.

If you can't understand that, don't take part because you'll probably be just as lost in a town as mean and violent as it's going to be

This event will also be open to non-heroes but we'd much rather see heroes take part as they can build curtains and use their perks for their own good and to plot the demise of others!

CURTAINS FOR YOU

Day 1 is co-operative: resources are gathered and things are built so that the town has a reasonable start and will last more than a few days.

Day 2 - All heroes MUST build curtains - this is absolutely compulsory. Other than that, we continue to try and set up the town.

Please be "fair and reasonable" during days 1 and 2. The idea is to set up the town. If you find a really good item that you want to keep, so be it, but don't start building hovels or reinforcement and don't grab a collection of planks, boosters or DOs from the bank.

Day 3 (starting straight AFTER the attack) = game ON! Everything is permitted in order to win it.

Well, almost everything: there will remain ONLY ONE RULE: DO NOT TOUCH THE GATES AT ALL BETWEEN MIDNIGHT AND 10PM (Game Time).

This means that you're not allowed to simply lock the door while everyone's outside. It would just turn silly, in a bad way. Note that it also means that you ARE allowed to do whatever you want with the gates between 10pm and midnight

Everyone must agree to this before joining. If someone breaks the rule, they will be excluded from future events and everyone in town WILL be asked to make them their prime target.

So, if this sounds nasty enough for you, join us for an exciting round of "It's Curtains for You!"

HOW TO ENROL?

POST IN THIS THREAD, tell me that you understand and agree with the rules and that you will be available for the jump. Tell me if you can be a coalition coordinator as well for the purpose of the jump.

Non-heroes can message me and I will add them, no worries.
