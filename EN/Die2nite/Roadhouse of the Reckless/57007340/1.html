If you want to live... Follow me!

Survivalist is not very useful early on,
because you did not unlock pro-camper,
so you can camp 2-4 days max.

With the 3-4 days needs Defense Object (DO) improvement.

With Pro-camper you can camp for almost 8-9 days safely.

Use this camping calculator:
http://astrotools.free.fr/d2n/index.php

Always do either:

8 upgrades + 2 DO + TOMB
9 upgrades + DO + TOMB
10 upgrades + TOMB

If you upgrades 10 times last night (maximum of manual)
then you will get 7 upgrades for free tomorrow (10 - 3 = 7).

Make sure your camping chances are OPTIMAL before hiding
or GOOD + TOMB = OPTIMAL before tombing.

Keep in mind that in some cases the map shows 13km,
when you are really at 12.4 km,
so the camping chances are really 12km.

The more far you camp from town, the easier it is.

You should normally always have 1-2 camping buddies.
This means you are 2-3 survivalist camping on the same camp site.

If the site is buried or heavily infected,
then your camping buddies can help.

Normally, kill the spread all around the camp site as you can,
then use EWB (Explosive Water Bomb)
or Kitten to clear the camp site.
Make sure you have a backup bandage for Kitten
as it might fail.

Hope this helps.

For new heroes, I would suggest starting with Tamer,
lots of meta needs 3-5 tamers per town,
so you can be put to good use early on.

But you can also try doing EE (Expert Expedition) camping
with experienced survivalists
inside a coalition to get some survivalist training boot camp.

If you are looking for camping buddies or META,
feel free to contact me, we are always recruiting.
