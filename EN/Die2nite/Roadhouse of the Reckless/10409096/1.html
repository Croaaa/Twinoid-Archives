Ok, granted I've skimmed this thread, but for the most part, I think the update was awesome.

Yes, the forums are not the best currently, the soul is a bit of a mess currently, but they're not actually core to the game play, MT are no doubt working on them and they will be improved in the near future, the forums, although not great at the moment, are a step in the right direction, as they have more potential.

But, onto the game content, what's not to like?

- The Shaman stuff:

More hero classes? great! It will help to keep the game more interesting, for both Meta towns and the random towns, as you can use death to your advantage, so longer surviving randoms/more intense battles for the 'boards! That's a win.

- New leaderboards:

Proof MT is LISTENING to OUR suggestions! It means you can better compare yourself to the players of the game soul wise, and compete with the vets over a season, to see if you're up to scratch with them, another win!

- Class Evolutions/Nerfs

More Tweaks to the game, making ruins in randoms a bit more accessible (tamer in ruins) and making Techs more useful while nerfing OP survs adds to the meta game more, as there are now more strategies as opposed to the Surv heavy towns of last season, that's great too!

- Modifications to the Dump:

We'll have to see how this pans out, but on the face of it looks like it will mean end game is more interesting/will require more decision making.

- NDR towns:

new towns are no longer the place of distinction whoring and will survive longer as a result, people will also go in there to help nuture young talent of the game, with rewards for both, great!

- New Constrctions

More things to build? what's not to like?

- More Distinctions!

More distinctions for everyone! what's not to like 'bout that either?

- Revised Attack curve.

Improving the formula will surely help make the game a bit more interesting.

So, although there are bad things with the forums/soul, the new update, the actual content is now better, I can live with the forums while they improve them, but what would you guys rather have? Crappy forums or crappy content? I know which I'd pick (and they aren't even that bad!)
