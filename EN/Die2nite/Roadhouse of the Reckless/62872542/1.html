There's kinda a discord but it's dead. The game is pretty dead as well with good reason. The devs have left it and flash is being killed off in 2020 so the game will be unplayable to the vast majority of the player base in the near future.

Most towns take a few days to fill and are filled with a large amount of inactives and there's a few groups of multi accounter greifers running around in them as well.

The couple/few metas left are very close knit and either very hard to get into or contact.

If you like the solo random town experience you may still find some fun to be had. There are some players floating around in those towns that are worth playing with (whether that be selfish players to have fun encounters with or team players that like to work with others).

But my personal advice would be to just move on unless you find a old friend that's still playing. I'm only still here because of friends, if they weren't here I wouldn't be either at this point.
