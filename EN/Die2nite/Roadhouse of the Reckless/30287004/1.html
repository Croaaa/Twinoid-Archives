The Amazing Race

The course has five checkpoints: the four corners of the map and a finish line at town. Teams of two will compete for the grand prize of a special distinction◊. Also, I'll give 30 hero days to the winning team.

Up to 20 teams can compete. We can do a private distant town.

Additional rules:
No shunning
No sleeping in town until completing the race
No changing teams, trading players, or combining teams
Use of Screaming Alarm Clock is banned
All living team members must visit each checkpoint•°
No cross-team collusion*

Notes
◊The special distinction: You will be distinct and special.
•If one team member dies, the other can continue and win for the team
°Only one player needs to "check-in" via forum post
*It is okay to make deals with other teams and players, but metagaming is strictly forbidden. Don't join with 3 friends so that two of you can win.

Race start: November 4, 2013 (Eastern Standard Time)
To enter: Reply to this thread with team members' Twinoids

Many of you will join. All of you will die.

Signups are open for backup teams and individuals! If you don't have a partner, post in here and ask for one! Once the post is made with both Twinoids, I will consider it an confirmed team.
