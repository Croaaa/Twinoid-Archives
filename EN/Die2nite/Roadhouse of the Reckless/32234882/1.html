I'm all for people bringing in a deco item a day (until their chest is full), eating human flesh for the cannibalism distinction, and several other forms of selfish play so long as the selfish play doesn't interfere with reasonable town survival.

Random towns aren't going to survive nearly as long as meta towns (for several reasons), so I'm all for alternative goals and achievements. but when they're combining all the PP's (or any other items that don't need to be combined), eating all the resulting drugs, grabbing tons of weapons and going on watch when defense is well past met already, or any other form of blatantly wasting resources or blowing AP in constructions that aren't wanted/needed, such that the town is no longer capable of survival, then shunning may be required.

Personal defenses are of a questionable nature. They are part of meta end-game. In random towns it's difficult to say when/if they'll be of the same importance, but if they are, it's usually a LOT sooner than in meta towns. My rule of thumb for personal defenses is Tent on day 1, Hovel not before day 4, Shack not before day 7 and House not before day 10 unless it's known beforehand that defenses can not be met.

Also be careful how openly you eat human flesh, for if it becomes known there is a ghoul in the town, those who have been known to eat flesh may be the first to get punched.
