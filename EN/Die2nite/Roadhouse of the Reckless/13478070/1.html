There is nothing wrong with the Clash Of The Titans being for heroes only. I created the Clash Of The Titans and organised it (with the help of Epoq and the wonderful town founders) but I also organised numerous contests and events open to residents and to everyone who was interested. This was just one event amongst many and the concept of the event was to have towns full of heroes of one class. No room for residents. It's the way it is.

There is nothing wrong with the Clash Of The Titans II. The towns got filled with a variety of heroes with different levels of commitment, patience and experience, and even with different amounts of hero days I suppose for some but hey, that's just part of the game. It may not have been as smooth and it probably was quite intensive for the glorious town founders but it took off and reached full flight. It is a genuine competition encompassing most aspects of Die2Nite.

There is nothing wrong with Rulesy's excellent project of Wrath Of The Titans. It's a great idea and there is quite clearly a strong desire for it from a significant part of the community. It is sure to be thrilling and followed closely by most!

There are events for people who enjoy being sneaky and belligerent (Curtains For You, Camp2Nite), it's only fair that there should be events for people who like to be disciplined and team oriented.

There is nothing wrong with disagreeing and not wanting to take part in something.

But if you're not happy with an event, you don't need to go around bitching and whining about it. Go create your own event, the way you like it.

The more the merrier. Of course it's not that easy, you need the right ideas and you need to be realistic about it and it helps to be friendly and get players to help out

I'm looking forward to seeing COTT2 progress, WOTT take shape and to seeing Andrewmaniscool finally get one of his ideas take off!

Cheers all!
