Leapfrog

I think it's fantastic that you want to contribute, and that there are any number of things that could be done.

... I don't necessarily know what they are, but I'm sure they exist!

I think MT could do with some assistance when it comes to communicating with the players, but such a role has to be given... by all means ask, though, and you may find the answer favourable.

As for the wiki, I don't think coding is necessary. I haven't tried to edit it myself yet but most wikis have a pretty simple interface for it. There's a lot of information on there that needs updating, adding or fleshing out, but a lot of that will involve collecting data too.

Some players like to go into Local towns to aid and teach newer players, and this carries a distinction with it as of Season 5. It's quite helpful to inform the newer players about the ins and outs of the game and the community, at least with those willing to listen. Raising the general education level of those who may go on to join distant towns or become heroes - or indeed encouraging them to - is quite helpful to the community.

Of course, you should be prepared to suffer some absolute failure towns as some players refuse to listen and others drop dead of dehydration - but at least your presence there will make it fail less hard.

I'm sure others have suggestions too!

(Don't worry about sounding big-headed. There are always problems, there's always room for improvement. We need people like you to make that happen.)
