Posting this on behalf of Charbon.

http://tinyurl.com/breakbiden

This is a form to collect data on weapon breakage and biden(missing) for all weapons that don’t have a sure shot at hitting or a chance of breaking. Considering the stats we currently have are either unsourced (from the wiki) or have a small dataset, I thought I’d try to collect as much data as possible to make the numbers more coherent. At the end of the season, I will release an analysis of all the data I have received.

The rules of the form are relatively simple: use a weapon until it breaks and register the number of swings you made (either missing or hitting) in the proper location. For weapons that have a chance of missing, there are two boxes you must fill out. One for the swings and one for the misses. To register misses, type the number of misses over the number of swings (example: with a staff, I swang 3 time and missed twice. I will therefor put 3 in the swing box for the staff and 2 /3 in the biden box.)

NOTE: Only register the numbers for weapons that have broken. If you use a weapon and it does not break, do not register the number. The reason for this is to keep a statistical consistency.

I've compiled 3 tables of death probabilities versus the house defense and the number of attacking zombies. Tamle provided a simple formula for predicting the number of attacking zombies and I based the numbers on that. We call this the WoB strategy.

Just a note for interpreting the data, each percentage is the probability of one person in the set of people attacked dying. To calculate the number of losses based on this data, multiply the percentage by the number of people attacked.

Example: Let's consider 9 people getting attacked on day 17 with everyone having 28 house defense. The table says that they have a 13% chance of death. We can therefor expect that 9*13%= 1.17 We can therefor expect 1 person to die.

The probabilities are based on a binomial distribution calculated using Maple. I have provided the formula for any curious people out there.
