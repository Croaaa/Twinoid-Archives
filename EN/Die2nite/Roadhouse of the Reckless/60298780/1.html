If you want to live... Follow me!

I would say if you want it the nice way,
then delegate these tasks:

1- a few zoo planner / zoo keeper draw routes and opened to suggestions

2- let the tamer handle some of the tasks once general zoo planning is done

3- let the survivalist organised themselves by groups of 2 to 4

4- have someone to post construction plans

5- have someone to post scavenging plans

6- have someone to post the watchmen plans

7- have someone to post the end game camping plans

This way you won't get overwhelmed to micro-manage everything
and you can focus on making sure as a meta leader
to show the way while letting everyone contribute usefully
to the meta and learn about the game mechanics
at the same time.
