No, it actually said "This town is dead". The scavs apparently aren't big OO users... it seems to flip to dead every other day.

I'm pretty sure this is how it works:

It goes to "this town might be dead" on the first day that OO receives no updates on that town (essentially meaning that no one in that town has logged in to OO).

It changes to "this town is dead" on the second day that OO receives no updates.

Since the apps aren't allowed to ever pull data (has to be pushed from the user's side) from MT's servers, they have no way of verifying that a town is dead and they all they really have to go by is how long it's been since it last saw someone active in that town.

Map viewer also has a leaderboard and has a COTT tab to check the towns and how they're doing. Since that gets all the pushes from the plugins, it's usually a little more up to date, but you can't see information for previous days like you can in OO.
