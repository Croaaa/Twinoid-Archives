To clarify, the immediate goal on the start of the town wasn't to "AMASS ALL THE GOOD STUFF!!!!!!!!1111" We started out with just a twisted plank or two, and gradually shifted onto other objects as it became apparent people didn't really seem to care. For what good it'll do, this was done from an RP perspective. We RP'd a group of mentally unstable (and somewhat insanely overly religious) people fearing god's wrath for the abominations walking the earth. We spoke cryptically in forums at all times, gradually revealing what we were doing (but mostly speaking of the coming great flood) through little codes, poetry, disjointed typing, and so on. It actually amused a lot of the residents, some even going so far as to claim we're crazy and losing it.

All things considered, is this something we want to do in every town? Certainly not. It was just an idea we cooked up shortly before jumping into this town and we decided to run with it to see how it would go. Overall, it seemed more to amuse others in the town than anything else. What more could you ask for?
