Hacksaw

Or you can put the @ symbol before their name, like @ghost will make ghost, though in this case, it'll take the first ghost who registered.

To select a specific ghost (me, for example) you'd have to put their twinoid ID number after their name like this: @ghost:26928

Or you can cut out the middle man and just put in the numbers like this: @:26928

Both of those will get you Ghost which is me.
