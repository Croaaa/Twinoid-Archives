MMJ #1 Season 5

Woods of Fate

When I called the jump I had asked Fineous to hesitate a moment before hitting the join random town button. This was a bit of selfishness on my part. See we had two towns at the time, one with 7 randoms, one with 17. I didn't didn't want to jump into that town of 17 randoms.

Fineous didn't get my message though, and with my luck with the game, I of course got dropped into the town with 17 randoms. There were 15 residents and 2 heroes when I made the call. We filled up the town with 23 MM Jumpers.

Realizing the situation I was in, I decided to make all the random people in the town aware of just what had happened to them. I laid out what the MMJ was about, set up an IRC chat channel, posted generic messages about what to bring back etc. Much to my pleasant surprise we didn't lose a single person on day 0 and had most of the map within a 9ap range explored (Quite a feat really!)

My notions about random players were proven wrong time and time again. They rationed water without question (with a few exceptions that just needed a little PMing), they did not waste boosters to build things (again with the exception of one guy who came around with a little PMing). They built what they were asked... some of them even logged onto IRC and became active members of our community (I heard one of them was just recruited by RFM, and will shortly be buying hero time!)

I just want to say... to the 17 randoms in WoF (if you ever read this). You exceeded my wildest expectations. Thank you!

To all the MMJs that joined me.. and followed my "Leadership", thank you. Thank you for trusting me, thank you for being patient, thank you for the very minimal amount of Drama.... Just... Thank you.

If we're lucky, we're about to take 2nd place in the MMJ tonight. I hope you guys realize just how fantastic of a feat this is.

There were exceptional individuals in this town, but realistically we were all a team, and we all did this together. So... To each one of you:
dd790, Epoxy, llecha, Troo, Sharpg, TheRoostingWolf, TheGreatDeadman, Tsangerun, LeoPl, hellosaturn, Advena, Armlegfoot, Teia, sirnh1, Madarkon, MonkeyLuffy, babane, Feanor, Hawks, Jubhub, Nephirolis, Reesan, Zealon, jhdobbins, 1337irl, Arintintin, Daible, Flume, mathrouc, Arturius, KingCrizzo, NatanX, NoUseForAName, Xeeroman, snakekiller, 10011, Ironoslav, Thornes, Fineous

Thank you.
Thank you for making this jump and my "leadership" of the town worthwhile.
