Message from KlarkMorrigan (non hero):
I am not entirely convinced TheCrow needs pixel art. But here is what I have got, anyway:

40x40 Die2Nite forums standard
80x80 Twinoid forums standard
100x100 Twinoid profile standard (resized)
120x120 (details below)

Some TL;DR ahead.

I do not know what the development team has in mind for the future, and if TheCrow's account differs from any other in terms of avatar files, so I'm including the 120x120 version as well, based off 40x40 one (magnified three times).

The reason behind this is that (as far as I am able to tell) Twinoid scales files down to aforementioned standards, but does not scale anything up from lesser dimensions. A 120x120 magnified version of 40x40 scales down to multiples of 40 without any loss of quality, yet looks quite sharp and adequate pixel art-wise on Twinoid/Die2Nite profile cards (if you don't mind it being bigger of course). The Avatar Factory experience has proved 120x120 to be a practical compromise for preserving pixel details on the standards used the most.

The 100x100 version I provided had blurred up a little due to resizing (as I understand it is not used as a universal standard), but is still workable. As I tested it, it appears in 40x40 surprisingly sharp, while in 80x80 it doesn't look as lively.

To resume, I'm not sure which of my ways will be obsolete with the following updates and forum migration, and which I will have to re-purpose. So I am presenting what I have got worked out so far, in case TheCrow is more informed by Motion Twin.
