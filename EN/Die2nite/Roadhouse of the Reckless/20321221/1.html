Hey everyone!

I'm sure you remember the story about how Soradobi & I met on die2nite in Season 1 and how he proposed in November 2011. We've been planning our wedding for a while, and it's coming up soon... less than 3 months!

Because we want to incorporate D2N in our wedding somehow, without making it a zombie themed wedding, I thought about having die2nite-related artwork for the table numbers, place cards, etc. And this is where I need your help, since stick figures laugh at me when I try to draw them.

Rules:

1. Artwork must be in Black & White.

2. Artwork must be appropriate for a wedding - meaning no severed body parts, blood, etc.

3. Artwork must be original... nothing that will get me in trouble for copyright infringement.

4. Cute/Chibi styles work, as do more artistic.... so have fun! Just make sure that whatever you're drawing will print out well, since I'll be printing these onto card stock.

5. If you have to upload a resized/smaller image due to file size constraints on whatever image uploader program you're using, I will contact you for the full size if you are one of the winners.

5. Submissions should be posted by Feb 15, 2013.


1st Place will win 30 hero days.
2nd Place will win 10 hero days.
