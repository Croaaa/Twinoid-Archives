The difference is, Andrew, instead of two separate towns, there would instead be just one town, with both men and women inside it, evenly divided.

I LOVE this idea, personally. Whereas there was too much manly testosterone (and too much estrogen, to hear it from the ladies) in each respective town, this would not only serve to balance out that imbalance, but also introduce new elements into the gameplay -- A town bent on destroying itself while still preserving itself at the same time.

Typical town scores would no longer matter (which thankfully destroys the selfishness that is inherent in any town, since nobody in it for personal gain would join this town in the first place), and we all get to hang out at the same time. Plus, where there was an issue with finding enough men for the manly town, there'll be absolutely no problem at all filling up a 50/50 town.

If we have enough leftovers (doubtful) we could even run 2 50/50 towns at the same time, and just average the scores, providing more of the inter-town competition, but still creating the inner town struggles.

I would say sign me up for this event asap, but I've neglected my meta for far too long, and I'd say I owe it to them to spend some time with them now. However, provided this event happens in the nearish future (a few months from now), I say save me a spot fo' sho. I wanna hang out, flirt, and beat up some girls!
