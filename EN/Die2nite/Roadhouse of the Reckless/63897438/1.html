anjoanyone have a private town experience they'd like to share?

Very few people on die2nite can tell you about Private Towns experience because we had VERY FEW Private towns ever.

When the update that brought more ranking spots came it exposed how sad this was. If you go to your Soul > Season 13 tab > Scroll down to "Private Town Ranking" Button, you'll see that there are 2 Private Hardcore towns and 31 Distant Region Private towns.

Most of those are event towns as described here:
https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachment...

The original version of this game, Hordes, has frequent Private towns and events. But as you can see, we couldn't even fill the 35 slots of the leaderboard.

Why?

Well, for start Private Towns aren't attractive. You don't get all distinctions and soul points from them and, in order to start them, you need 40 people to actively enter the town via individual password. This needs to be done within 2-3 days or the town is reset and everyone that already joined it will be kicked from the town.

Before that, you need to be an experienced Hero with Mayor perk unlocked (541 accumulated hero days) in order to create a Private Town. We do have a bunch of Mayors around, but most of them know about the first thing I listed and prefer to do regular towns with planned coalition jumping (metas) rather than use Private towns.

Private towns didn't award Ranking distinctions so while the Seasons were still changing, people would rather focus their time and invest their resources on regular towns where you could earn those.

Even though Mayors can select the town type and adjust some options for the town, the complete selection of options for a customized town can only be done by admins and Crows. further reading

So TL; DR: We had very few Private Towns in Die2nite.
