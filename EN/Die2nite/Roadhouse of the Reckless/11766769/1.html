To cause a bit of excitement and paranoia in season 5, I'm proposing to have a game of Assassin. Here are the rules:

The goal: Kill your target, don't get killed, be the last man (or woman) standing.

The means: Hanging, Poison, closing the gates, meat lockering, attacking and causing infection, anything else that will cause your target to die. Just don't hurt any innocent bystanders.

How it works: Everyone that signs up will be given a "Target" to hunt, if you kill your target, you will then hunt their "Target". You must have some kind of proof that you caused your targets death, either directly or indirectly. The proof can be by advanced notification of your plans, screen shots or confirmation by target that they were killed because of you.

If there is "collateral damage", for example, closing the gates and killing your target plus 5 others, you will be eliminated and the target will stay in the game.

Creative killing is encouraged.

Anyone who joins a new town will have to post when they will be joining the new town at least 6 hours prior to joining. There may also be "Assassin" towns to join for added excitement.

Post here if you have any interest and/or questions or comments.

Regis

D2N Assassin Rules Revised

1. Join the game by Twinoid message, Oval Office message or IRC private chat to regisphilbin.

2. Game will start at a date at time to be determined.

3. Each player will receive 3 others to “hunt” and be hunted by 3 other unknown players.

4. The object is to “kill”, directly or indirectly, the 3 you are hunting and the 3 hunting you until all others are dead.

5. If you are killed by another player, you are out of the game.

6. If you kill an innocent bystander, you are out of the game. If you kill a player that is not either hunting or being hunted by you, you are out of the game.

7. If you kill a player you are hunting, you will receive a new player to hunt.

8. If you kill a player hunting you, another unknown hunter will be hunting you

9. How you find and kill other players is up to you.

10. “Proof” will be necessary that you were the cause of death and provided to the game referee.

11. The referee’s decisions will be final.

12. Posting in the forums about active players or speaking about them in IRC, “outing them” will cause your death and/or banishment from future games.

These are working rules, meaning that if you have a suggestion to make them better, it will be considered and possibly adopted.

If you would like to play, please send me a private message and I'll start a list. More sign-ups mean starting sooner.

Two arguments why, in my opinion, the "kill" should be a roleplay murder as you put it and not an actual in-game murder:

1. To have to dedicate your time in a town to the pursuit of killing one member could cause you to inadvertently ruin the town; whether you're griefing on purpose or not, that would be the overall effect. Granted, most of the ways you listed above wouldn't have too bad of an effect on the town (except closing the gates while they're outside), but in reality purposefully killing even one member of the town is hindering the town to some extent.

2. The real game of Assassins works perfectly well with the "kill" being some representative action you have to carry out, there's no reason it wouldn't work as well here. If you're concerned about it being too easy, make it a more difficult action to achieve.
