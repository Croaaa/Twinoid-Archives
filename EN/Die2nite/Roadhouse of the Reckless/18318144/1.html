You've gotta drink water to make water...

Now that the Survivalist Book has a failure rate, a good strategy for any organized town with 2 or more survivalists would be to set up what I call a water web.

In each of the 8 cardinal directions, survivalists should place a water ration 6 AP out of town: N, NE, E, SE, S, SW, W, and NW.

Survivalists can then plan the drinking portion of their trip to land on one of these sectors. If their book fails, they can use the water that's there and the next Survivalist out can replace it.

8 water rations seems like a lot, but with a 20 - 25% failure rate, it will save a lot of trips out to rescue them. It also means they won't have to carry redundant water, forcing them to leave more items out in the field.

Just a little food for thought.
