It's time for another MMJ! Welcome to the 7th Mega Meta Jump!

Jump planned for: Monday, April 23rd at 23:45 game time!

This may be Tuesday in some time zones. To see the time in your time zone, click here.

What is MMJ?

MMJ stands for "Mega Meta Jump" and is a mass jump, where many players jump together at the same time. The goal is to create towns with a large number of active citizens who are interested in working together to do the best they can. MMJ towns are typically a mix of experienced players as well as newer players who are eager to learn.

Why participate in MMJ?

MMJs are a great way to meet new players and make friends! If you are new to the game, MMJs are a nice place to connect with other active players and can be an introduction to a more competitive Die2nite experience. Now that there is no Meta Cap, many experienced players will be jumping with Metas throughout the season but MMJs will still continue and we hope that the experienced players will choose to start out the new season with an MMJ town in order to play with those outside their Metas as well as to guide newer players. For this 7th MMJ, we have heard supports from both metas that won the last season, and interests from several other teams that did equally well. Letâ€™s make this a fun and memorable experience, with your participation!

Will my MMJ town make the rankings?

There is no guarantee of making the rankings by participating in MMJ, but your town is what you and the others who land in your town make of it! Many MMJ towns do end up organizing toward the goal of ranking on the leaderboard, some with good success!

How do I participate, where can I sign up?

If you are available to jump: You do not need to sign up but you are encouraged to so we can later have a better idea of how many participants are in each town. If you are willing to drag off-line participants with you in a coalition, then please do sign up and include your email so we can contact you as a jumper.

Due to no Meta Cap, you are free to jump with your own coalition/friends!

If you are not available to jump: If you cannot be online to jump for yourself or have fewer than 100 soul points, then you will need to be in a coalition. If you have a friend who is participating, feel free to coalition up and have your friend jump for you. If you do not have someone who can jump for you, you will need to sign up so that we can assign you to a coalition. Please leave any coalition you are in right away so that you can receive an invitation.

Sign-ups: http://tinyurl.com/mmjsignupform

Contact information and more

Further announcements and listings of coalitions will be at the usual MMJ spreadsheet: http://tinyurl.com/megameta

If any problems, information on irc is at the above link where you can find us to ask questions.

We picked this date specifically to overlap with BOTS, really. BOTS only accommodated 80 people total and a lot of folks in the community have been requesting an MMJ. We decided not to announce it before BOTS got up and running because we didn't want to "steal" players from that group - especially since they had such a hard time getting at least one of the towns filled as it was.

The first MMJ of the season has been the "learning curve" jump, really. If there's still demand when this MMJ is over, we'll do another one. But that's going to completely depend on what community events are happening, how large they are, and what the community interest is in having another MMJ.

I feel like I should mention that when I say "we'll do another one" or anything of that sort, it means organizing the jump.
