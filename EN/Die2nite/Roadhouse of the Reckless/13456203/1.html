War of the Worlds!

Look! Another Event! Basically, myself and CaptainCanada are planning on starting up an inter-continent challenge, named "War of the Worlds", which will be a competition between players on each of the continents to find out which is the best continent!

It's going to be an event for heroes and residents alike, and will be a straight fight between 40 person towns on points, it's up to each individual town to co-ordinate hero classes and whatever else they wish to co-ordinate before and indeed while in the town for ultimate bragging rights!

For now we'd just like to gather some info on whether people are interested (indicated by if you fill the form in ) so we know what everyone wants!
