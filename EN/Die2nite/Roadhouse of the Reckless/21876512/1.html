I've asked it before when someone else posted the report from an attack and the short story is there are essentially three functions to the attack.

  • Town defense
  • Watchmen defense
  • Assault on the town proper

"Lazy" zeds only apply to the zombies included in the assault on the town proper and have nothing to do with how many may or may not have devoured any watchmen. The watchmen is new flavor text added to the usual blurb for the attack, but the old blurb (in this case the 395 that didn't attack anyone) doesn't take them into account because MT never modified the out text to do so.

Esentially it's like this:

  • the zombies attack the town's defense excluding watchmen.
  • If the gates are closed and there isn't enough defense to stop them, then any overflow attack the watchmen.
  • If the gates are open, then all attacking zombies plus any residents who died in the world beyond (a.k.a. returning residents) attack the watchmen.
  • Any zombies that are not stopped by the town's defense and watchmen combined enter town and the standard distribution of zombies amongst all citizens (including surviving watchmen) occurs.

Each function is a separate function and does not effect the next except in how much it reduces (or increases) the attacking zombie pool, if any.
