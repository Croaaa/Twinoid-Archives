Sense

Your call to action is for players who are already familiar with the World Forums. While you may gather temporary interest, I don't think it addresses the decided causes of the Saloon's death.

Many new players don't know this place even exists, and the flow of players through the game is what separates active forums from dead forums, just like the flow of blood through our veins separates us from the horde

I perceive two sources of the problem, which may or may not be accurate.

Firstly, it's really difficult to find the world forums. It used to be so easy. It also used to be more fun. There were more colors and options and it wasn't so generic, as Twinoid has changed things. Take a look at this screenshot comparison I made from two different Die2Nite towns:

Pre and Post Twinoid Integration

Do you even remember how cool the forums were back then? They were unlike any forum I had ever used. Features integrated with the game seamlessly. The emoticons were on the right and we could question and wonder what each one meant. The options are overwhelming now. Sure, different doesn't necessarily mean better or worse, but it used to be specialized and unique.

The second problem I perceive stems from the first. Since discovery is so low, most people using the Saloon are old players. Old players like us, with memories of the "good times" are running out of things to talk about. We've gone through the arguing and competing and we have the game mechanics pretty much figured out. It's just a matter of execution and there's no ambiguity as to what's best and what's not. The best memories I have of the world forums came from competition and debate over technique and strategy. I also really enjoyed some of the roleplay threads such as Memoirs of a Camper and deeper threads than shallow "add 5 words!" games.

Take the Residents forum for example. It looks pretty fun. Not as active as the saloon used to be, but still better than what it is now. I think this supports my theory. Maybe we should all just post there

But what are the real solutions? This thread is a desperate plea for activity. Look how lonely it is in the saloon We can move our discussion to the residents forum. We can campaign to raise awareness of the forums amongst the players we meet in our towns. We can transition to a focus on the everlasting .net where old threads aren't automatically purged. I'm really hoping there will be some further amendments to the twinoid integration, maybe coincidental with season 6 which I'd really like to hear more about....!
