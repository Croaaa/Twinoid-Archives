On may 15, 2012
Tetsab

The best way to do it if possible ('cause it hits 2 important things to do) is to agree that everyone will upgrade on their water rationing day.

At the start of the town try to get everyone to agree (and follow up with general prodding first in general on the Forums and later in PMs if some aren't) that pure builders will drink then ration drink then ration (DR) and that scavengers will drink (try to get about half doing their initial drink on odd days and the rest doing their 1st drink on evens and often it helps to have people say if they're doing odds or evens in their home message) then drink the next day too then ration (DDR) and that on those R days people will upgrade their homes until they end up in a House at a minimum (I also like to encourage people to have enough AP to Workshop their own planks/irons needed for upgrades on these R days).

Otherwise, yes, folks will go with half or 14/14/12 or 10/10/10/10 systems. It largely depends on how capable your town looks (you need at least middle range ability and if you're at a higher range other more situation-specific systems are possible).
