I was reluctant to post this alongside Rulesy's proposed Titan event, but given the complaints about notice for the current Clash, I had planned to do so today, fully two months before the planned start date. I have no wish to conflict with his event but think the fairest thing is to get an idea of interest.

I also want to state that this event is in no way intended to replace the Clash of the Titans nor to rectify any perceived imbalances or issues surrounding it. The still-ongoing Clash of the Titans is an important and unique community event in the tradition of inter-class competition and glory, and those all-important distinctions. The MiniClash, while inspired by the Clash of the Titans, is a casual, noncompetitive event geared towards fun.

I've learned a few things about organisation from this Clash and I intend to initiate a jump on 20th August into seven single-class towns. One town per hero class, like the Clash, but including Shamans. Residents are welcome to attend a town if the interest is there despite the Apocalympics.

We're going to be pretty Clashed out by July, but CotT #3 will seem a long way off. I figure after a month or so of rest we may wish for another go on the Single-Class Town ride.

If anybody has thoughts on the date, I will be glad to hear them. I will also happily change it if it interferes with any other events, including any hero portion of the upcoming Apocalympics or Rulesy's Wrath of the Titans.


This is not intended to be a competitive event. It is intended to be fun and casual, to provide for practice between Clashes, and to allow those who supported a particular class in the Clash to try a different class if they wish to.

Expressions of interest are welcome, but sign-ups are (tentatively) to begin on August 1. I will coordinate event-wide things such as the jumps but town leaders will be responsible for the recruitment and organization of their own towns.

Prior to sign-ups, town leaders will be accepted on a volunteer basis, with the preference of the community taken into consideration. I will gladly organize one town directly, but will stand down in the event that a more suitable candidate volunteers.

Anybody is welcome to attend, with the obvious prerequisite of sufficient hero days for all but the (possible) Resident town. There will also ideally be a nominal screening process at the discretion of the town leaders. The less experienced among us are welcome to join, but despite the more casual nature of this event participants should only sign up if they intend to play for the full length of the town.

Should there be interest, ItsNathan and I are still eager and willing to run a seperate, unofficial Shaman town as an extension of this current Clash; see the topic here. -> SHAMAN (un)OFFICIAL COTT2 TOWN <-
