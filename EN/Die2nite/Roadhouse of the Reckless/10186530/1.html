THE NEW DIE2NITE HERALD


So heres a quick breakdown of the new Herald:

HOVEL
Thats basically the news feed. But now instead of just releasing new Herald issues when i have material we will also have constant supply of current affairs news. Sometimes something interesting happens and it might not be necceserily a good idea to wait for a whole Issue to come out in order to tell the people about it.
So all kinds of small updates can be expected:
-When a new Herald Issue comes out.
-Updated Library in the RP section.
-An interesting thread.
-Event news and updates on competitions like Workshop`s caption contest.
-MMJ Town statuses- How are the towns faring and maybe even some tease msgs from towns to their riavls. (U cant settles scores on the World Forums but u can do some boasting on the Herald)
-Political updates (more on that later)

HERALD
So thats where you can read all published Herald issues. The herald issues will now be released less frequently concentrating on more serious topics, interviews with players and stuff like that. It will be like a big pile of info that can fill soem time between auto-searches of bored players who are just hanging out aruond the WF with nothing to do.

POLITICS
The FASI HAS ARRIVED thread has spawned some amazing RP and great fun rivalries. Its incredibly entertaining and it is probably the 2nd most succesfull thread on the WFs. (first being the Brag thread series). So after watcing that thread for awhile i have decided to host an ELECTION that will decide the TRUE MAYOR! The best liked, most popular and most cunning player in the game.
Id like to point out that this election is OFFICIALLY backed up by MT. Ive talked numerous times with Epoq about this idea and ill also provide some great rewards for the winner. But due to the nature of the event both me and Epoq have agreed to leave the start of the election for Season 4.
The Politics Page Is Almost ready hopefully ill put it up online after the Christmas hangover is done

STORIES FROM THE WASTELAND
This is where i will publish cool roleplayign texts. Any player who wants can have his RP story from the WF published there. Everybody will get his credit and will have his name shown on the menu and at the end of the story. So everybody who reads it will know who wrote the story. Im just providing the means. Also all entries in the Herald`s Writing Competition are automatically published.

COMMUNITY PROJECTS
This is where the Herald will promote its own community events and other players projects.
Other things like the map apps or meatlocker incident if the creators want a spot will need to reach me and we can talk about how things are going to be done.
There no immidiate events the Herald is running.

PROJECT X
You have to go there and see.....

GALLERY
Cool pictures related to d2n will be posted there. I have decided in this particular case not to always ask players if they are okay with me putting their screenshots online. But i will most of the time ask but if you see some of your stuff there dont be too surprised. (Quent was kind of enough to allow me to host most of his work on the Herald and we might even do something together in the future)

FORUM
I am hoping the Herald to become THE NUMBER ONE spot for d2n things and to manage to pull alot of new players to get more heavily involved with the game. Since the WF are restricted to heroes only maybe we can get them to join the an external forum. Im waiting for the .net issue to be resolved. Since its an already established community forum i think it would be much better if i can just open up a portal page that redirects people there.

STAFF
A list and short description of everybody who works on the Herald and what they do. I believe everybody who helps out in this new project deserves their credit given.

When i started the Herald i thought id do this thing on my own but as i got more ambitious i realized it would be much better to get other community workers on board and make this thing huge. And also even tho i can afford to spend alot of cash on the herald i dont have that much time. Since i need to work and work pays for the site...so im taking a more of a producer kind of position where im just using my resources to open up new opportunities for our community. Ill still do the heralds and the news and stuff but im hoping ill get a nice crew going on to really make the new Herald shine.

I hope you guys like it
