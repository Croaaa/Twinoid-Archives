Hey guys, if you're only submitting your failures, your data is not only useless but counterproductive.

When I go through the data and scrub out the entries where people only entered one day or, worse, only entered 4 failures out of a 15ish day town, it changes the numbers by quite a bit. (For example, the last bit is a 100% fail rate according to formulas when a person would know that's only a little over 26%. Spreadsheets don't know how to fill in the gaps in information like that... they can only deal with the information they're given.)

In order to come anywhere close to accurate numbers, we really need ALL of the data available. That means failures as well as successes.
