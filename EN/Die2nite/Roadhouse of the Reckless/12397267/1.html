Let me begin by saying that I'm not suggesting that this be some sort of feature, or that Die2Nite is in any way lacking for not incorporating it. I know that classes are designed and (theoretically) balanced around being able to do a few limited things.

But still, sometimes the "What if...?" in me considers just how well certain abilities could work together. Just for fun, if you could combine the abilities of two classes, what would they be and why?

For example, a Scavenger + Tamer combination (Scavenger/Tamer in D&D terms) could not only find more items, but also send them back to town. Plus they'd get both ruin exploration bonuses (50% more oxygen and an arrow pointing to the exit).

Again, this is just a hypothetical thing. I know the classes are designed to be separate and they'd be too powerful or no fun if they could do everything. But maybe I've just spent too much time coming up with class combinations in various RPGs.
