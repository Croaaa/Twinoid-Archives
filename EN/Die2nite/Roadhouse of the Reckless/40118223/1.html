Hello Survivors (and non-Survivors)!

Have you heard about ZombVival? It’s a zombie game based on Die2Nite that you can play for free via the Die2Nite directory. If this is the first time you’ve heard about ZombVival, I’d recommend checking it out.
But let’s cut the advertisment; This is a ZombVival Item Contest, where you can design your very own ZombVival Items and win some awesome prizes. Aside from the usual ZombVival-internal achievements, you can also get some Twinoid Emoticons as well as the highly coveted D2N Event Distinction. Of course, the best ideas will be integrated into ZombVival!

To submit your ideas, just post them as responses to this news article. Your submissions should contain your item’s name, description and any effect(s) it may have. Of course, you can also make suggestions on how or where to obtain your item. Submitting an icon for your item idea is not required, but it also can’t hurt.

This contest runs concurrently on Die2Nite and Die Verdammten. Please note that you can only take part in one of these contests. The winner will be determined via two public votes. In the first one, you’ll get to pick three finalists from each server (D2N and DV), who will then compete against each other in the final vote.
You have 3 weeks to submit your ideas (until August 7th.), after that we'll organize the public votes (which will run 1 week each).

The ultimate winner of this contest will receive a set of new Twinoid Emoticons, 3 Eventwinner-achievements on ZombVival and one Event Distinction on his or her server (Die2Nite or Die Verdammten). Also, the winner’s item is guaranteed to be implemented in ZombVival!
The other finalists will receive the Twinoid-Emoticons and 1 Eventwinner-achievement on ZombVival. If I like the idea, I’ll implement it and give out another Eventwinner-achievement.
All other participants will receive a Participation Achievement on ZombVival.

I hope you’ll like this event and I’m looking forward to a lot of awesome submissions. Have fun (and good luck)!

Brainbox
