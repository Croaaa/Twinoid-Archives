Handicapped Towns

Well, this idea has been floating around a bit, and I just remembered it...
This is only a brainstorming to gather ideas and to gauge general interest in a such a town.

The basic idea is that towns compete, but with individual handicaps that you have to work around. I think that is something the game lacks now, alternate ways of playing, but with the goal of town survival.
The second aspect I'd like to incorporate would be a draft system for town slots. Town leaders get chosen beforehand and then draft the available players. This serves to mix things up a bit and I guess the drafting itself might be quite fun

To give you an idea what I mean:

Town 1 is inhabited by the "League for Undead Rights", which means they are not allowed to kill zombies. Not at all. Zombies at the gates don't count because they kill themselves in our defenses, can't do anything about that.
All the town's inhabitants are also compulsive aesthetes, that means bringing in Decoration Objects has priority above everything!

Town 2 is inhabited by a very religious sect which is not allowed to consume any drugs or alcohol, nor coffee (the cinema is not allowed for the same reason...) They are also gluttons, if possible each inhabitant eats every day (see a food in the bank, grab it and use it!).

Obviously those are only example handicaps, I have quite some more ideas and you guys as well I hope.
I do realize that it will be pretty much impossible to really balance the handicaps out, though we can and will try.

Towns will have a major and a minor handicap, as well as a random task that needs to be fulfilled each day, perhaps for bonus points. The random task would be something like "Drop a reference to a Justin Bieber song in the forum", not something that interferes with the game.

The draft system needs some more thought, depending on interest the players would have to be split up, because no one wants to draft 300 people.

Some more ideas for handicaps:

Vegetarians
Can't consume any meat. Possibly Vegans, no eggs would make things harder still.

Raging Alcoholic
Booze is consumed as soon as possible, may it never stay in the bank.

Addicted
Take drugs as soon as possible. This could be a major handicap since anyone scavving up two drugs would become addicted.

The Simple Life
Life is to be endured, you are allowed no comfort. No house upgrades, not entertainment (no deco, no cards, dice, BRD, etc...)

Contagious
Everybody who has camped is not allowed to enter the town again.

Claustrophobia
Can't search buildings, can't enter ruins.

Any of those can be combined obviously, for example "Vegetarian" with "Raging Alcoholic" for added fun and games. I'm sure that's a fun combination

Thoughts/ideas/critique?
