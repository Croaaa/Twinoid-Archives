Dunno if I should address the, uh, "last meta of S5 link" in relation to the comments above or the original raffle idea but since this is the raffle thread lets go with that:

My understanding was that this was to help folks who'd like into a long-lived town get into a long-lived town rather that help train them up to be non-noobs. Yes, they can keep MMJing 'till they hit a really good one (or keep shopping themselves out to established metas, which I always wonder how that's going) but this should be a nice fast one-off.

Speaking to "turning everything into a event" more generally: I like the event aspect if it's supportable (lately there have been a number of events that seem to have struggled to reach critical mass) as it showcases an engaged community (when they don't hit critical mass that's what really makes me think things are dying). Many, many folks do the small town help out role (now with bonus distinction but did it anyway even w/o such a thing) but it's a fairly isolated activity (more folks need to do Nyx-style diary-of-a-small-town!) in contrast to things announced event-style that makes community building more visible and sometimes makes fostering creativity in play a key goal.
