Who will you trust when you're going to die2nite? In Townies vs Mafia, you're in a town with mostly innocent outstanding citizens.. but a few randomly selected mafia is out there to wreck your town.

The Mafia knows each other, and can plan together for maximum effectiveness. Their goal is to be the sole last survivors.

The townies are confused. Who is on your side, and who is out to secretly murder you?

HOW TO WIN
Mafias will win if at any day, only mafias are surviving. The earlier this day occurs, the more points they get! Will you protect your mates when the blame is shifted onto them, or will you act normal and not cause suspicion?

Townies will win if all Mafias are killed. Use shunning, omniscience, logic, deduction, and a little bit of gut feelings to win! Just make sure you don't shun the wrong person.. and that your "best friend" isn't trying to assault you!
