Story 3. Speaking of playing in airports I've been doing a fair bit of that as last week I was travelling for work... right at the start of CotT. I let chat know I'm off to try a hard reset on the router and when that doesn't work plan to drag an 8 year old laptop out of my closet (since it actually has ye olde phone port on it) and use it to connect to some dial-up I have access to (& had my car not blown up 2 weeks before I could have planned to drive to the closest library ).

Naturally I assume this is a cruel joke until my Champaign Ride PMs me and apologies for Failure To Save.
