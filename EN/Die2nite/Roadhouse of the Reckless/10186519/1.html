Please take a moment to read THIS THREAD and the forums rules.



Some etiquette


1. Pointing out spelling and grammar errors isn't necessary.
Although it's nice when people don't butcher the English language it is not necessary to point out when someone has misspelled something or used a dangling participle. Note that intentional use of txt-spk or chat-speak is not the same thing as making a typo, and is prohibited as mentioned in the forums rules.

2. Reputation, length of time on the forums, or any other means of measurement does not absolve you of responsibility.
If anything, we expect long standing members to set a higher standard for the newer folks to follow. They'll usually post in a manner which they see has been done in the past here so you guys need to keep up the good work and show them how polite, courteous, and helpful we are here at die2nite.

3. You are not a moderator.
There's no need to tell other members what they can and can't do. It's OK to nudge them away from the 'dark side' but don't get authoritarian on someone if they've broken a rule. If it's a major infraction the best thing to do is to report the post by clicking "report".

4. You do not have the right to insult/attack/etc someone for ANY reason.
Simple enough.

5. Don't stray off topic or hijack another member's thread.
Don't hijack someone's thread and completely change the direction it was meant for.
