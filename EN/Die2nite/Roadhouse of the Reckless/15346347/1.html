The World Forums are seriously struggling to pick up again from the disastrous transition to Season 5.

I remember at the end of Season 4, they were a hive of activity. Everyone was enthusiastic, high on the success of the first Clash and the community was vibrant with contests, prizes, stories, healthy competition and generally pumped about the game.

Then cam the crash, towns were lost, forums were chaotic and nothing much was happening. Then came Season 5 and the new look souls and forums and a massive bunch of players (myself included) gave up. It looked shithouse, everything was hard to find, a significant amount of things we treasured were broken. It felt like most of the efforts of the community had been slashed by a careless Motion Twin.

Slowly, bugs got fixed, communication improved slightly and souls and forums started to look a bit better. A few of us came back. Some of the ones who had stayed positive when all was bleak (r3dd3r and Quent) got quiet for a while.

I'm impatient for things to pick up again - but certainly not as motivated as I used to be. I've been meaning to and promising to send questions for my new edition of Workshopping the Community for weeks if not months now but can't be arsed just now.

So what do we need? A few sparks? Patience? A few exciting events (I think that's the answer)?

A horribly misleading and aggressive title to start new controversies and fights, so people get active again?
