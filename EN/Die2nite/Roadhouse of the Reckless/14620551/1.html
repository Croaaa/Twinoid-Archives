Wow, wow, wow. I guess I've been spoiled up until now. My coalition has been fairly good and fairly strong. We had some fun with CotT and have had even more fun in small and medium towns, meeting up with new people and shepherding some real newbies. However, for the most recent meta jumps, two of our coalition were invited while the rest weren't. Not that this has split us up, but it is harder when we're in two separate towns.

The other three of us decided to try a HC town of which only one was available. The first one for all three of us, though the other two have done a couple before. What a mistake! At least two coalitions of griefers.

Day 1: posts appeared on the forums about manually updated dusk til dawn and building the workshop asap. All pharmaceuticals and alcohol appearing in the bank was subsequently stolen. Nobody was posting when they got trapped. I helped clear out the zombies for one person (who never logged back in and died anyhow) then got stuck and asked for assistance so I could camp and bring back a bp. Someone came and was attacked by another person in front of me, severely injured. Then stole the campsite before I could get in. Subsequently died. I didn't. Vindication.

Day 2: Intentional sabotage builds to avoid the ability to build the workshop were made. More thefts. Someone upgraded straight to a house. A few of us went back and forth on the forums about how bogus this was and trying to get the town in gear to get things done. Supplies were brought in to be able to work on the workshop. Hacksaw found. Just before the attack, gates were opened on us. One in town died, 4 terrorized.

Day 3: All drugs and alcohol again taken. Hacksaw stolen and hidden. The last required parts needed for the workshop stolen and hidden. With seven people going out to try to bring some of this back in at 23 hours before the next attack, the gates closed again. One of our coalition is so pissed off he's suiciding tonight. The town's not even fun when some are actually trying and the goal for others seems to be to make it downright impossible to do the most basic town starting steps. We thought HC had to do with your ability to survive the additional limitations, not struggle with over half the town to even attempt to survive.

Is this standard HC fare? What a gyp! We'll see what tomorrow brings and whether or not the other two of us are willing to try to stick it out any longer.
