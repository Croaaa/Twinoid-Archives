It's usually better in distant towns, but even some of those can be dreadful.

Sometimes, in addition to what Borz said, people will like the game but forget to check in every day, not realizing they'll die after a few days of no water.

This game isn't too user-friendly for newbies, imo. It doesn't do nearly as much hand holding as other games do. People might like the game from what they see but just don't want to figure stuff out, so they give up. There is a tutorial UI from what I remember, but there's a lot of stuff for a new player to remember.

I suggest finding people in your town with similar play styles, forming a coalition, and going from town to town with those people. Doing so can make each town less painful.
