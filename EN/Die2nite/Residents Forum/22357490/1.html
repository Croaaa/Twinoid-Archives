This is a contest for 1-5 player coalitions. Or a Micro Meta of 10 players. It is a different spin on the Small Map Wars Contest.

The goal is to have at least 35 players alive until Day 10 +

You have the option to attempt this in a small map or random distant map.

Scoring:

100 pts for each day over 10 Days that the town survives. You must have 35 still alive on Day 10, after that deaths will not be counted against you.

10 pts for each player in town that posts on the World Forums

100 pts for a daily journal

100 pts for a town video

100 pts for a Wonder or Reactor built

Rules:
Only 2 coalitions are eligible per town. You must register your coalitions in this thread on Day 1 with the Town name and the members of your coalitions. You have the option to have anywhere from 1-10 players help you influence the other 30 players.

You can only attempt this once. Any coalitions that register a town have only 1 shot to make this happen. This is to avoid repeat suiciding.

NyxOwl Prize:

60 hero days which can either be broken up into 30 days for 2 players of the town or 15 days for 4 players of the town.

Good luck!
