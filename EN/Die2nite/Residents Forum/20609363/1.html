N&C Meta (Nicky and Company)

We started in S4 brought together by Nickyg in a random town. In S5 we decided it was time to come up with a name. We would talk hours on end about what to call us. Nicky would always be quiet in the corner not giving any suggestions. Every time we asked him what he thought our name should be, he would jokingly respond Nicky and Company. After many sessions not ever getting anywhere we finally agreed to just call ourselfs Nicky and Company since we wouldn't even be a group if it wasn't for him anyway.

Since then I've taken over most leadership responsibility including recruitment and in town leadership. Always with the help of my second in command Greenbeen. Who in-tern questions my every decision.

We are a half meta that has self capped itself at 20 members (although we currently stand at 17. 14 heros 3 residents). We do all recruitment in town.

We are a fun loving bunch that accommodates most playing styles, distinction hunting, living long, or training noobs.

I have to say the best part of playing half meta style is all the players you get to meet. I'm no just talking about randoms, we've run into players from 7sins, RFM, CoS, Clunky Bastards, and I'm sure there's been a few more.

Our current claim to fame is ranking a mostly random town in S4 and S5. We may try for a third this season, but with ghouls it will be much harder.

Think that's enough about us..
