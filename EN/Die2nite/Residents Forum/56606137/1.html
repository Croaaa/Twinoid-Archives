There`a an app called Twinoid, it allows for accessing twinoid messages way easier, also phone notifications are applied.

In terms of playing die2nite on phone (full game experience, including moving in the desert option), I`ve checked many possibilities and those are the best ones I`ve settled with (a subjective judgment):
Android: Mozilla Firefox or Dolphin browsers, imo the Firefox is much faster and more convinient in daily use.
IOS: Photon Flash Player & Browser, very nice browser for iphone (although Dolphin was also available if I remember, one of those required paying ~2.5 Euros after 2 weeks trial to keep using it).

Interface is user friendly on phones measuring 5 or more inches in my opinion. You need some room on screen to avoid clicking on wrong items. Also switching between cards in browser isn`t that pleasant on smaller phones, especially when navigating between the game and dusk till dawn map.
