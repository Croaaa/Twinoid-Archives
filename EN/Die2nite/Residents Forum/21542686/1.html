Yes, it`s possible to die immediately, but only under certain circumstances. Need to do one of the following:

Take a cyanide
Eat/drink poisoned food/water/steroid (possible to poison with vial of poison, water often found poisoned already inside abandoned ruins)
Allow a ghoul to eat you
Become a ghoul and get punched by someone
Walk to dehydration: Dehydration Guide - try to not follow it - your status changes every 11 steps, so possible to die after 11 when starting dehydrated or 22 steps when starting thirsty. You`d need to have some boosters though and avoid to drink water inbetween.

Note that getting killed in the middle of the day, while not hitting the camp button outside means you won`t be accounted for today (you`re currently on day 12, but you`ll be counted for day 11 in your soul only).
