Moderator

[ http://twinoid.com/en/article/3842/citizens-creative-contest-get-[...] ]

Survival requires creativity and heroism in equal measures. Take part in this contest which gives you the chance to show your inner artistic monster.

PRIZES

6 prizes of 15 days of Hero mode Die2Nite will be winging their way to the most impressive entries.


Rules

All images must be completely original and must contain a reference or mention of Die2Nite. Images "borrowed" from the interwebs or which have nothing to do with the game will not be considered.

If you make a video, please make it under 3 minutes long. In videos, you can include material not created by you, but the video itself and the underlying idea must be yours. Who knows, your video could be the next Youtube sensation!

The Jury will be made up of the team behind the game. If the picture or video gets a lot of likes, it just might attract the attention of the jury (if people are going to like it, there must be a good reason), but just like some other elections, having the most votes doesn't always mean you're going to win.
You already know the game, and how we roll here, surprise us!


How to take part

Publish your work on the Official Facebook Die2Nite page before midnight on Sunday the 11th of November.
Important: Don't forget to mention your nickname/pseudo so we know who to reward.

Prizewinners will be announced next week - so get going!
