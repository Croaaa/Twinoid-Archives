yes a site can be upgraded with as many DO's as you like, however a site has 10 upgrades max and a DO counts as 1.99 upgrades so installing more than 2 is inefficient, to get the most from a site, upgrade 8 times and then install 2 DOs and you manage to get 11.99 upgrades

however you will find most sites don't need this many upgrades unless there are lots of zeds or you have camped multiple days

you can use the camping calculator to help work out how many upgrades you need
