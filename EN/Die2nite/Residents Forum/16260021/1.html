As she wandered through the desert, she saw a small town of sorts. Her hand drifted up to just above her eyes, shading them from the sun.

Could it be?
Survivors?
When she neared the town, she saw a heavy set of wooden gates set into the hastily-built walls.
"Thank goodness..." she whispered to herself.
Zoe slowly approached the gates, pausing just outside, her eyes sweeping the area for any signs of life.
"Hello?" she said softly, not wanting to attract the attention of any undead, should there be any lurking out of sight, "Is anyone there?"
"Hello?" a voice said in reply, nearly making her leap in surprise at its sudden appearance, "Who are you?"
