Yes it`s normal or at least not a glitch in die2nite.

Dying on the watch is possible with even 1 zombie entering the town (when the attack before w-men start working is insufficient) for non-guardians/non-ghouls or people with certain statuses lowering the odds - so anyone with less than 100% chances of surviving on the watch.

kiwiluke

the number of zombies who get past the gates but are stopped by the watchmen does not matter, be there 1 zombie or 1000 zombies, all watchmen have 95% odds of survival before personal modifiers (for example, guardians and ghouls get 5% bonus, wounded get 20% penalty)

so a non guardian/ghoul with no negative statuses has 5% chance of dying on watch, the game then rolls a d20 for each watchmen, if it comes up 1-19, they live, if it comes up 20, they die,

the message in the gazette is just flavour text calculated by dividing how many zeds got into town by the total watchmen.

the game then removes any def contributed by dead watchmen, subtracts the number of zeds that got past gate from remaining watchmen def, if there are now more zeds than def, those zeds attack homes, if there is still more def than attacking zeds no zeds will attack homes.

in addition, there is also a chance for watchmen to become injured or terrorised, but these statuses appear to be added after the attack and do not influence how many zeds get stopped
