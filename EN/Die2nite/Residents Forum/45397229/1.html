here are some informations discovered so far about Souls and shaman in season 11:

How does the Shaman get selected?

  • in each new town the shaman get selected on Day 2. and each time the current shaman dies the next day a new shaman will be selected as the new shaman. this also means each town can only have one shaman at all times.
    its 100% random, we tried to find any kind of connection to who may get selected (twice after having the first shaman suicide) according to activity, first to log in on previous day or current day, lowest and highest activity, hero or resident, all of this had nothing to do with who got selected a new shaman.

What are The Forms a Soul can take:
each time a town has a death, a Blue Lost Soul gets generated outside in the Desert:

  • Blue Lost Souls: if someone dies Inside or Outside the town as a watchmen, dehydration, Infection, withdrawl, Poison, Eaten by zeds in town, camping outside or not camping at all, ALL those types of deaths will Generate a Blue Lost Soul in the desert next day. (Death by Poison or cyanide immediately generate a new lost souls in the same day of the death)
    It is completely safe for anyone of any class shaman or not to pick up this types of souls without any need for an immunity from Mystic potions. they are harmless when picked up.
  • Red Tortured Souls: this Form only appear in the game after a Blue Lost Soul transform over night into a Tortured Soul.
    If a Blue Lost Soul is left outside in the desert for at least one day then there is a chance that the lost Soul WILL transform into a Red Tortured Soul Tortured Souls look like this
    this Form of Souls will kill the citizen which will attempt to pick them up immediately as if it was a poison AS LONG THE SOULS ARE OUTSIDE!!!
    BUT there are 2 Ways to avoid this death by either
    • letting the shaman pick them up which will be immune to their effect because of the shamanic power, OR
    • letting any non-shaman citizen Drink a so called Mystic Potion or Shamanic Potion which can be made by the shaman in his home using 1 Soul Action and 1 water like this and the person drinking it will become immune for the entire length of that town (not just 1 day) and allow him/her to pick any tortured Soul safely from that point on forward. here is the message you get after Drinking the Mystic Potion and here is the message you get when you successfully pick up a tortured Soul while being immune here Picking up a red tortured Soul
      THEN once the red tortured Soul is collected and put in the bank Anyone in town can touch the Red Tortured Soul in town from bank safely WITHOUT the need for drinking a Mystic Potion. Red Tortured Souls are only dangerous to pick up outside!!!

Why does each town get a shaman and what can this Skill/class Do and why it is one of the most important Job in each town?

  • A Shaman can see all souls regardless of their current FORM at all times even after they have been picked up and dropped again . they will be hovering in a square of 4 zones, and the Soul will always be in the lower right zone of that square. the shaman can see the souls under unvisited zones so it is no longer needed to open the entire map by walking through all zones as it was the case in previous seasons.
    shaman can see the souls under unvisited zones

Once the shaman gets selected on day 2 he/she will have a daily 5 Soul Action Points and here is what the shaman can do with them:

  • Making it Rain : to either kill zeds or if used at the townÂ´s Gates it has a 50% chance (confirmed form a small data base for now) to add 5 water to the well. and this action costs 3 Soul Action Points which means it can be done only once daily.
  • Brew a Shamanic Potion this requires 1 Soul Action Point and 1 water and it can be done up to 5 times daily. as explained before this potion must be drank as a replacement for daily water when a non-shaman needs to pick up and collect a red tortured Soul to avoid immediate death and become immune for the entire length of that town (not just 1 day). and a reminder if you drink this Mystic Potion you will regain 6 normal AP and refreshed status , BUT at the cost of no longer being able to drink water again to regain another 6 AP. so its a simple exact replacement for water except giving the bonus of immunity against tortured Souls.
  • shaman can pick up tortured Souls without any risk for death or the need to drink a mystic Potion and they are the only person in each town that can carry several souls of any form at once in their rucksacks. a shaman carrying 2 souls at once in rucksack , as you can see one of the souls is tortured and the other one is lost soul. And here you can see that Non-shaman classes still as before can not carry 2 souls at once
  • Healing illness and other statuses , it can fail or succeed and sometimes the illness gets transformed to the shaman. not very interesting for testing since it has small chances to succeed to cure only (terror, infection and injuries) , it CAN NOT cure dehydration or being hooked.

What are the effects of NOT purifying and having/leaving a tortured soul outside in the desert over night or Inside in the bank, someoneÂ´s Chest or someoneÂ´s Rucksack?

  • leaving one or several Red Tortured Souls outside or inside in Bank, Chests , or Rucksacks WILL increase the size of the attack by X = 15% for each red tortured Soul left Not Purified over night. it will be visible by an increased change of all attack estimation numbers of the 30% to 100% and it will increase both with the X factor of the size of the actual attack which we usually have as if no souls were outside:
    this factor is confirmed to be X = 15% of the actual size of the attack.
    so to make it simple in an example: if you have an attack size of 100 zombies (best estimation here, not maximum estimation) and you leave one red tortured Soul Not Purified before the attack then your attack size will automatically become 115 which is 100 + 15% of the 100.
    and if you leave 2 red Tortured souls Not Purified the attack size will increase from 100 to 130, if you leave 10 red souls Not Purified the attack will increase to 150% to go from 100 to 250.
