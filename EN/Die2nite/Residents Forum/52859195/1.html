And my pro tip: If players tell you what is the best thing you should do and you don't understand why, don't feel bad to ask for a reason.

There are way too many players running around just repeating what other players said to them or what they think is a good thing to do without even knowing why that is supposed to be the case, and not all of them are right^^

That said, if you go out scavenging, to increase efficiency you should leave as soon after attack as possible and return one or two hours before attack and not earlier (both only if possible ofc, this is idealized and highly depends on your free time and time zone). That allows you to utilize as many autosearches (the 2 hours search timer) as possible. Also try to go to an as far away zone as possible (without watchtower votes usually 9 AP away from town with food and water, if there is no food available for it it's 6 AP away) and autosearch there over the day so the near-town zones get depleted slower.

Possible reasons NOT to go scavenging and build in town includes that the town would simply not survive the night(s) otherwise. So what you're supposed to do on any given day depends a lot on the general situation of the town - which means you should try to understand and discuss that situations in the forums if you really want to be able to do and understand what's best for the town.

Other than that, what isliceyobread and matsuu said is completely valid ofc.^^

shlominus

once you reach 100 soul point and can enter the larger towns things will get better. now and then you'll still get plenty of towns with bad or next no communication (which usually means they don't last long), but it's rarer than in beginner towns.

there are also a lot of players that play the game in a very structured and planned way, those towns usually do very well in the rankings and you can learn A LOT there, so sooner or later you might want to try and join such a town. there are a couple people who are very helpful to newbies and if you ask, i am sure you will get an invitation to one of those towns.

before you do that though, i would advise you to find out some stuff on your own. when in doubt, ask (but you already know that. ).

have fun and don't die2nite!
