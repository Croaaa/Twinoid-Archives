Hello. Welcome here.

If there is no active group of people who organize the town, the town is pretty much done. It can collapse as fast as in 5-6 days only. If some people are trying to do, you have a good start. Just follow what they say. Ask them and help them. If none, you need to try to do it yourself.

First thing is to ask people to close gate before attack. Assign a few gatekeepers. Reserve 1 AP to close gates.

"Automatic Piston Lock" (required a common blueprint) is good for random town with poor organization. It will close gate automatically at 23:30 (game time).

Post construction plan and ask people to work on the same construction. Don't spread effort (thus wasting APs). Use your best guess even though the plan is not good. It is still better than nothing. Try to focus on defense building like Armour Plating.

Ask people to sign up on watch if your town defence cannot meet the zombie attack. Bring as many weapons as possible. Drink any booze to increase your survival rate.

Use Twinoid or the town pm system. Some people just don't read town forum.

After this town, try to form a coalition if you find good people inside that town. You may also join some coalitions or metas so you will get better ideas how the game should play. Fewer inactives and griefers should give you a much more satisfactory gaming experience. There is a sticky thread above which you can post join request.

Feel free to contact me if you have anything you don't understand. I'm more than happy to help and answer your questions.
