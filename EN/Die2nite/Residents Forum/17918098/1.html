LeJabbit

Having read a post by WalkinCrow about not wanting to spend hero days on PiB towns (Pay it Back town: Enter a small town just to teach the new players how to play the game), and given that MT does not allow heros to play resident (thus not spending hero days). I think it would be really good for the game if heros could choose to be residents (only) on small towns.

Giving heros the chance to enter small towns (for free) in between jumps will give the new players a new perspective of the game and it might even make them want to buy hero days in the future since the hero may tell them the advantages. A meta cap, or some other measures, can also be implemented so the whole town does not get filled with a meta wanting to have fun and not teaching.

I met WalkinCrow on a PiB town some months ago, it was my first PIB town as a hero and it was a really good experience. But, as He said, I'm not really fond of spending hero days on a small town.

Thoughts?
