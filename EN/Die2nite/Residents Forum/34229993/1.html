Ah, what you'll want to do is form up with your friends into a Coalition. You can find the Coalition page under the Coalition Tab on your Soul Page. You can access your Soul Page by clicking the blue flame icon near the top of the screen, under the Citizens are you Read to Die2nite sign. It says "My Soul" on it. From the Coalition Tab you can make a coalition and then by typing in their usernames, you can invite them to your coalition (coalitions may have a maximum of five people in them). From there you can all join into the same town once all of you die. If you have questions about what I said or further questions, don't hesitate to ask.
