What is MHJ?

Mega Hardcore Jump (MHJ) is a mass jump, where many players jump together at the same time into Hardcore towns. The goal is to create towns with a large number of active citizens who are interested in working together to do the best they can in a adverse enviroment. This event is inspired on the MMJ (Mega Meta Jump) with the main and only difference of being hosted in Hardcore towns.

MHJ towns will have a mix of experienced players as well as newer players, solo players and meta players who are eager to try a different set of game mechanics or to teach those mechanics to players not familiar with them. Its a great oportunity to meet people and have fun on decent towns.

Who can participate?

MHJ is open to all players who are interested. The jump is just as successful as the participants make it! Theres only one Hardcore town filling at a time (asuming they are still there on the new Season...) and both Heroes and Residents can join it.

The only problem is if you have less than 100 Soul Points. You can only join if someone else drags you there in a coalition. (see "How do I participate" below)

Why participate in MHJ?

Some players dont like Hardcore because of many things: Manual map update, Construction Repairs, low Well, Higher attack curve. MHJ is a excuse for those players to try it. You are going to meet lots of decent players and probably have a decent town. Depending on the Season lenght you can even rank it, as many metas leave their Hardcore jumps for late in the Season.

You can find a build a Wonder to get the distinction. You can find a group of players which you sympathize and join in future towns with them. Theres only victory if you join this event.

How do I participate?

The MHJ will happen one day after the Season 8 start. 9th August, a Friday, after the attack. Here is a countdown.

As soon as that day attack ends all Jumpers hit the "Let me in I´m Hardcore!" button (or click on the Hardcore town if you are a Heroe).

If you are available to jump in that day You do not need to sign up but you are encouraged to so we can later have a better idea of how many participants we will have.

You can enlist in this Form: Form link

If you need invite choose that option in the form. Make a post here on this thread as well. It can help you get an invitation fast.

You are also free to jump with your own coalition/friends!

If you are not available to jump: If you cannot be online to jump for yourself or have fewer than 100 soul points, then you will need to be in a coalition.
