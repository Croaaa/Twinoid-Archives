Igloo has an Attack Calculator, it's pretty useful.

It collects data from the tower as people update it. This means that people don't have to record it in the forums (though it might not be a bad idea anyway).

Yesterday Neuroguy found a bug that I had seen before:

Neuroguy's attack history

When I went to view it, I saw something different:

My attack history

Neuroguy found that the history could be cleared by searching through and deleting "http_igloo.omameier.net_0.localstorage"

Jedermann has since provided a better solution, though the situation could use a bit more research.

To clear this in Chrome:
1) Go to Igloo
2) Press f12
3) Go to "Resources" and "Local Storage"
http://i.imgur.com/0KvXvut.png
4) Note the "estimations" and "estimationsLC". Delete both.

To clear this in Firefox:
1) Go to Igloo
2) Press f12
3) find the '>' and type:

localStorage.removeItem('estimations')
localStorage.removeItem('estimationsLC')

There's a Storage manager for Firefox also, if you press f12, and enable it. I couldn't get it to work though.
