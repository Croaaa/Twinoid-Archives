Welcome! Understand that the mistake you just made is a common one, especially in small towns, and unfortunately results in death if someone doesn't help you. If you weren't in a small town and you posted in the town forum, someone would eventually come to save you unless it was close to the attack time.

As a general rule, only move half your total AP out into the desert (usually 9 if you use food+water, which I recommend) then stay there for as long as you can to maximise the auto-search, then return using a different route. Essentially, walk in a loop, and try not to go where you've already been in the same day. (You can't search the same zone twice in one day.)

Also, use dusk till dawn (in the little book menu in the top left corner). Whilst nobody in small towns will use it, when you get to distant regions, everyone will be grateful if you make use of it in the desert. (It keeps track of where items are in the desert, and what state the zones are in.)

Finally, I hope you're able to put up with the nonsense and "dehydraters" (wait till the end of the third day and you will see what I mean) until you get 100 soul points and are able to join distant regions and the game will be a lot more enjoyable.
