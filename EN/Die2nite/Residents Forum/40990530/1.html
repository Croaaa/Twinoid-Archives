The original wiki is http://die2nitewiki.com/ . It is still being actively edited.

You may use Google to search to go to the site, e.g. "Box cutter die2nite wiki". You can find the info you want quickly in this way.

====

We have two maps but nearly all people use From Dusk Till Dawn. You can access the link through the menu (at the top left, which is next to the "Are you ready to Die2Nite" board).

There is a very useful plugin which can auto-update the map plus a few features.

If you use Firefox:
Install Greasemonkey and restart
Then click to install this script.

If you use Google Chrome:
Install Tampermonkey
Then click to install this script.

Tips
It will also add a button (next to "objects" column) in the game where you can click the button to update the maps. I always click the button before I leave a zone, just to make sure the update is done properly. It is a good practice.

A bit of a belated reply to this:

We discourage the use of foreign languages on the World Forum. Even when put behind a spoiler tag, it's generally less useful to the population of Die2Nite than the broken English most players can come up with on their own. Posts made entirely in foreign languages will be deleted/warned as appropriate.

We will not be punishing/warning posts in broken English unless it's very obviously a general refusal to even try to participate and everything is run through Google Translate.

We do recognize that we have a very large international user base here and that not everyone is fluent in English. Give it your best shot, that's all we ask.

To those of you that have been active already, your posts are certainly understandable. Welcome to the forums and it's good to have you here!
