So I'm a captain of my school's cross country team, and we were thinking of fun and different fundraisers we could possibly do this year, and we thought we would host our own Zombie Apocalypse 5K Race. I'll admit that die2nite didn't even cross my mind until tonight... but I realized that this would be a great source of zombie creativity. Seeing Joanne's recent post, I thought I could sort of turn this into a contest.

What I'm looking for is a poster/flyer that we could use to advertise our event. We don't have a specific date yet, and a lot of details still need to be firmed up, but I figured it would be great to get this out now so that we have time to let the contest run, and the blanks could be filled in later. Give it a cool name, add whatever you think makes it look cool, just please make it appropriate for children as well.

If you would like to include some of the event information in the poster now, here are some important points:

There will be two races, an open 5K and a 1 mile fun run for kids (a team relay is under discussion as well, but is extremely iffy at this point).
The fun run will feature a costume contest- kids dress up as whatever they want, zombie or not.
There will be prizes- one for the winner of each race, and a prize for the best costume.
There will be a cookout as well, featuring guts and brains (burgers and hot dogs).

If anything changes I'll be sure to let you know. Even if there are no details whatsoever, the design is what I'm really interested in.

The contest will close on Wednesday, October 9th at attack time (that's 6 attacks from the time of this post). I don't have any hero days to offer, but I'll pick the winner that night and we'll use the poster to advertise our fundraiser!
