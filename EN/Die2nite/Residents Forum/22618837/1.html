I've got 1 entry, but there are many different variations. So I will just choose the 2 variations, which I like the most.

Die2Nite Easter Eggs(with me!)

#1

#2

Whole album (#3, #4, #5, ...)

No photoshop was used in these pictures. Only a good camera and creative skills.

TUTS for D2N egg-making
First, you take some eggs. Then blow out the slimy gooey egg milk out of the egg, which leaves you an empty egg shell. Google the procedure of blowing an egg, since this is about making the eggs, you see in the pictures.

After you cleaned the insides of the shell, so it doesn't smell, you can start painting them. Maybe wait after the eggs are dry, if there is water on the eggs.

You can either use actual paint or ink. If you want your eggs to have a specific colour first, then use ink. The reason so, is because paint dries quicker than ink. Paint is quicker to use up too. "Why don't you just mix the paint with water then?" I wouldn't suggest doing that, because the liquid wouldn't stick on the smooth shell. It would just drip.

In my case I went with black ink. Ink is also liquid, however it does stick to the shell.

After I made the all the eggs black, I went to sleep, since it would take time to dry.

The next day, the eggs were smoothly black. Now, you can use paint or continue with ink. I used paint though, since I hadn't any other colour of ink than black. I advise you to take a pencil first and draw a draft of what you want on a paper.

Make an outline and cut the insides of it. For example, for letter D, you would have a piece of paper with a hole in it, in the shape of D. Put it over the egg and start colouring the hole. If you take out the paper, you should have a clean letter D on the paper. - I didn't do this part, cuz I had no time.

Well. Continue doing so on the other eggs and in the end you should have the result, that I had or maybe even bette. Enjoy.

BECAUSE PRESENTATION MATTERS! Also, no eggs were crushed in making.

The hand is painted with red paint.
