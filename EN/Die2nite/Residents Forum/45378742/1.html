Take a leap of faith and make some new friends!

Mega Meta Jump (MMJ) is a mass jump event, where many players enter together at the same time into Distant Region towns. The goal is to create towns with a large number of active citizens who are interested in working together to do the best they can.

MMJ towns will have a mix of experienced players as well as newer players, solo players and meta players. Its a great opportunity to meet people and have fun.

This form was designed to help people find a place for this event. If you can´t be online at the event jump time you will need a coalition to drag you in. If you can be online at that time you can also help those people that can´t by forming a coalition and acting as a Jumper for them.
