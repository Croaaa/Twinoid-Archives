Oh, forgot to explain the warriors abilities for our newbies.

Now every warrior got some fury, look at the spreadsheet. Now you can use it to make special abilities. Also you can see at the "abilities" page in spreadsheet how to earn more fury.

Abilities will add power to your attacks or protections. If you use "Powerful strike" with steel sword you will deal 4+1=5 damage, but spend 1 fury for it (though if this attack will be "succesful", you will earn fury back ). Deadly strike -> 4+2=6 attack, and so on.
"Piercing strike" will allow your attack to ignore 2 points of protection (not armor!) in the cost of 1 fury, so it's pretty good. Though if your target will not be protected this action will not have extra-effect and 1 fury will be wasted.
But the most powerful are "group actions", that require participation of 2-3 warriors. "Double attack" will allow you to deal 1,5 damage in the cost of 1 fury, triple attack 1,66 damage, so it's way better than "powerful strike".
