Horse:

Can be used and act like a citizen's dehydration stats. Gains 1-2 CP for the zone.
Must be given a water ration once or once a two days to prevent dehydrated death.

Can be found in the following buildings:
Citizen's Tent
Mac's Atomic Cafe
Old Police Station

4 KM of use: Thirsty

12 KM of use: Dehydrated

15-18 KM of use: Death of the horse.

Rusted Car

To build the Rusted Car, you must first construct the basic car in the construction site for around 15-30. Once built, allows for an extra person in shotgun, and +4 inventory slots. When there is no extra person on board, you get +5!

Must be refilled after the Gas Can runs out of fuel.

One Gas Can can hold for around 14-16 KM

Can be modified in your house to have Sheet Metal added which gives an x amount of CP when outside. Base CP is 4 and 6 with Guardian.

Requires:
Engine.
x6 Twisted Planks
x6 Wrought Iron
x1 Belt
x1 gas can(new item!)

Item: Gas Can
Found in:
Wrecked Cars
Home Depot
Construction Site Shelter
Motorway Services


Rusty Bike:

Being rusty and old after all this time, it'll only go so far. Use it while you can to get where you need to go. It has no extra inventory slots.

Can ride for 5-8 KM and then turns BROKEN, can be fixed with a repair kit or kwik-fix.

Found in:
Looted Supermarket
Burnt School
Old Bicycle Hire Shop
Town Library
Home Depot
Wrecked Transporter
