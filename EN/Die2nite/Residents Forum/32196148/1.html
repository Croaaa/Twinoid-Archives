Man, I miss having SaucyEmmettC in the coalition...That was the day... Back to actually answering the post. Essentially this game is centered around forum, resource management, and community. Some people can't find the forums (and I mean the town forums, as the world forums are even worse). This isn't great. Small towns are often hard to get along in, but don't be afraid to point out others mistakes. Loudly, and in a nagging tone, and constantly. I'm getting off-topic again. Small towns kind of suck, use them as a learning experience. Once you get 100 Soul Points, you enter the Distant Regions. They're much better (cynical meta opinions aside ). There, people actually respond when you say, "hey, we need that weapon for constructions/ Watchmen". Most of the time at least. My advice, don't jump into a meta too fast. A meta by the way, is a shortening of metagame organization/association. Essentially beyond the "coalition" level, it is more people going into the same town and working together. While it has it's advantages, it lacks the breadth of random play, in some ways. A warning: certain sections of the Help Section are wrong. I don't mean imprecise, or inexact, I mean straight-up wrong. If you have any questions about the game or areas where the wiki is inexact, please feel free to Private Message me.
