Yes, eating cyanide on day 2 (or dying in any other way on day 2 besides in town by zombies attacking - for instance dehydrating, dying outside by not returning to town nor camping, withdrawal, etc) will cause gaining all the distinctions from day 1, none (with some exceptions mentioned below) from day 2 and displaying your death as happening on day 1 in your soul in this town.


All distinctions are counted if you survive the current day, so suiciding at any time of the following day is enough to avoid losing any distinctions from past days (immediately after the attack is 23:16 server time this season, you don`t have to wait till 0:00).
Starting since day 5 you gain all the distinctions immediately, so for instance on the beginning of day 5 if you pillage 30 items in case of chaos mode being triggered and then suicide straight afterwards, you will have those granted as well.

Small map towns are the only exception, where living till day 8 rule still exists to gain some of the previous days distinctions.

Roleplayer distinctions are granted immediately since several seasons, they can`t be lost anymore, doesn`t matter what day or map you`re playing. It`s similar to some other distinctions, like Soul Collector.
