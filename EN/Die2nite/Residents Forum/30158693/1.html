On september 17, 2013
Hacksaw

Workshop upgrades don't effect conversion costs. Only building the factory and/or holding a hacksaw effects conversion costs.

My thoughts are workshop upgrades are considered useless for two main reasons:

  • Firstly, the AP reduction it gives to projects is too small to really be considered worthwhile, unless the project initially requires a huge pool of AP (such as the false town or labyrinth (which are both resource intensive) or a wonder (which are useless constructions that only grant distinctions))
  • Secondly, even if upgrades did provide a worthwhile reduction in AP cost, if your town needs that reduction then it probably doesn't have enough active players (and therefore scavengers) to have the resources needed to work on projects, rendering any AP reduction moot since zero AP can be spent on them anyways.

In my opinion, you're much better off building and upgrading the great pit before you upgrade the workshop, if only slightly better off.
