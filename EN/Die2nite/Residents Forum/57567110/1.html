The point of this very stupid forum game is to see how long you survive after building the battlements on day 1 (yeah, just because), the amount of zombies will be decided randomly, starting with two dice rolls, then 100 + 2 rolls, 200+ 2 rolls and so on.

This is how you waste your time in this crappy game if you are bored to death:

The town starts with 10 defense because that's just what your gate (if you can call that a gate) can hold.

You don't have to open or close the gate because he does it by himself and doesn't need you.

You can go outside and do up to three searches a day.
Roll the virtual cheated 10 face dice if you want to know what the heck happened to your poor, non-hero soul.
_
... -> If a roll equals 1 to 3, you win 1 Resource Point (remember that you can roll three times a day, son)
... -> If you get 4 or 5 you find a wepon [sick] (a guard wepon, not a wepon wepon)(see: wepons and odds below to find out what you just got)
... -> If you get 6 or 7 you find some sort of food, most of them will give you three more scavenge rolls but there are some kinds that will give you extra effects (see: foods and odds below to see what you just found) (NOTE:If you find some sort of food, any food, know that you'll eat it immediatly, there's no limit for food, you can eat as much as you find and also you don't care if the food is rotten or poisoned, you are hungry as hell anyway).
... -> An 8 roll'd up will give you one water ration, it can either give you three more scavenge rolls or refill a water gun, you choose.
... -> 9 is your lucky number, as you uncover a box full of materials, 5 Resouce Points for you, boy!
... -> 10 is a very important number, because you found a rare, Ophiophagus trouser! But don't be silly, the serpent will eat you alive and you die from it, yeah.
_
Every citizen is a W-man, you have 10 points from your original meat shield + the amount of points from their wepons(heroes don't benefit from their professions here, dud)

As a W-man, remember that you have 10% of chance to receive a glorious wound, or 5% to receive a painful death (wounded citizens can't scavenge while they are injured, a wound does NOT develop into a infection, we don't have infections here. But you will magically treat yourself the next day).

WEPONS AND ODDS:
If you find a wepon, roll a 100 sided dice and read you destiny:

1-10: You got a beer fridge: 19 guard points (heavy)
11-15: A rare exploding water bomb: 20 guard points
16-25: More common water bomb: 8 guard points
26-40: Ektop gluten chair: 8 guard points (heavy)
41-50: Fat cat: 8 guard points
51-60: Lawnmower: 15 guard points (heavy)
61-73: Water pistol: (empty): 8 guard points (if charged)
74-80: Aqua-splash: (empty): 8 guard points (if charged)
81-98: Burn out torch: 4 guard points
99-100: Hastily built desk: 23 guard points (heavy)

You can only carry 2 heavy items at a time, if you find three or more, drop one and take another. Other items are unrestricted.

FOODS AND ODDS:
Roll a 10 side dice, and see if you don't pick rice:

1-2: Half eaten chicken wings: 3 scav rolls, no special bonus
3: Tasty steak: 4 scav rolls instead of 3, also gives you 10 guard points for one day.
4: Packet of soft crisps: 3 scav rolls, no special bonus
5: Unspecified meat: you feel the stomachache, makes you lose all your items
6: Spicy chinese noodles: make you stronger, you can now carry 1 more heavy items (stacks with other spicy noodles effects)
7-9: Dried chewing gum: 3 scav rolls, no special effects
10: Poisoned rice: you die.

BUILDINGS:
Spend your Resouce Points here to build stuff.

Wall upgrade v1: costs 20 RP - gives 30 defense.
-| Fences: costs 10 RP - gives 15 defense
-| Barbed Wire: costs 8 AP - gives 10 defense
-| Wall upgrade v2: costs 30 RP - gives 45 defense
-| Armor plating: costs 15 RP - gives 25 defense
-| Armor plating v2: costs 25 RP - gives 35 defense
Temporary buildings:
-| Rebuildable wall costs 5 RP - gives 12 defense
-| small cafe: costs 6 RP - gives everyone 1 more scav roll
-| Infirmary: lasts 2 days: costs 12 RP and prevents wounded status
-| Mega Water gun: costs 15 RP - gives 30 defense
-| Rocket Propelled Potatoes: costs 23 RP - gives 32 defense and also gives everyone 2 more scav rolls today.

DEATH
If you die, you should remain dead during 3 days, you can revive by typing Back from the dead if someone is still alive, or you can stop playing if you are extremely bored.
Example: You died2nite at day 1, don't come back till day 5.
You died before the attack at day 1, don't come back until day 4, got it?

YOU DON'T NEED TO EAT OR DRINK you just get bonuses from them.

Every night, if your defenses are not good enough, zombies will go thru and for each 10 zombies that get into your town, one guy die.

EX: 23 zombies got in, the game master rolls two dice, one number for each citizen, he gets numbers 4 and 10, Mr.Bob666 is number 4 and YOLO2 is number 10, they die.

REGISTRATION
Just type anything below and the GM will take note, you can't register when the town is at day 4 or later.
