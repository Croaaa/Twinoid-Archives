welcome to the world forum

i see you are on day 4 which means if you can finish battlements it will allow you to join watch

joining watch is an option you get once you finish building battlements and with it you can carry certain items like furniture or weapons or torches to defend the attack. on day 4 it should be less than 150 attack size so that is manageable with just one watchmen.

about the bodies in town you have 2 options:

  • either remove each body using 2 AP for each
  • water the corpses by simply taking a water from the bank or the well and going to the houses of the dead and click at the option "water the corpse"

    here remember if you do not remove or water the corpses there is a high chance one or several of them will turn into a zombie and kill you on your sleep after the next attack.

about being the shaman , yes you can step at the gates and make it rain using 3 of your Soul Action points which has 2 outcomes:

  • if it will succeed: you will add 5 water rations to the well which you can take 2 of them from the well once you go back inside and use them to water the corpses or drink or whatever you need water for.
  • if it will fail: nothing will happen

if you survive todays attack tomorrow one of you the last 7 survivors will get a Last Man Standing reward which include one free hero day.
so good luck
