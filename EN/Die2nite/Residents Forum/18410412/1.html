You can`t use "active" hero improvements (kitchen, lab, siesta) after hero days run out, it was always like this. They will start working instantly again after renewing your hero status in the same town, as they`re still built, only not usable when being resident.

It`s still possible to benefit from "passive" hero improvements as non-hero (fence, reinforcements, more storage, curtain, etc...) as long as you did any of those before turning into resident.
