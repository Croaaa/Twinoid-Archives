Superman32

To answer your question: nothing happens.

If you are standing out in the desert at attack time and there are less than 40 citizens in the town, the attack on your town will be held off.

Like the others have said, however, you need to take precautions.

If you are going to be online before the attack, you can always post a message on the forums and hopefully, one of your town mates will let you know if the town is full or not.

If you aren't going to be online near the attack, you need to do some form of camping, to offer some protection against a possible attack.
