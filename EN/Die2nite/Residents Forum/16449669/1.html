An honest to god account of a complex poisoning in my best town "The Retarded Centre." Enjoy ! Names have not been changed to protect the innocent.


Ryanboy wakes up in the morning. He lived. Stepping over a dead body, he heads out of his front door. Walking across the street, he spies a zombie snacking on a friend of his' face. He gives it a swift kick to the jaw, severing it's neck from it's spinal cord, and it drops. Nothing could go wrong with this day.

As he begins working, he gets thirsty. He heads out to the well, and grabs some water. As he drinks half of it, it begins to feel... plain. Granted, he's in no mood to complain during the zombie apocalypse, but still. Ryan heads out to the bank and grabs a can of food. eating it, he feels infinately better.

He gets back out to the construction site and climbs up to get back on task. He starts to work, and gets dizzy. Somethings wrong. He pukes, and manages to nail Altair in the face with it. Altair turns around, about to yell at him, when he sees Ryan fall and smash his head on a metal support.

As Altair is seized for the crime of killing Ryanboy, Meilow takes a closer look. Ryan was poisoned... but how? According to his searches, it was from a can of food. Good thing they didn't pull the cremato-cue on him yet.

Altair is released, but now there is a whole new line of suspects. Sew360, for holding the can of food. Hazez, for being the one to take it from Sew and put it in the bank. And Sharlej, for being the one Sew got the can of food from.

It is found that Hazez had left the town recently before taking the can of food and giving it to the town. Sew had all the materials to make the poison. The only one who is eliminated is Sharlej, as they had a clear record.

Soon, hazez comes and checks out what's going on. Thrown to the ground and waterboarded, he doesn't admit poisoning the water. He was simply being courteous to the town. And now they're 7 water rations down.

Sew was locked up, held above the Great Pit, and asked why he did it. He admitted to poisoning the water, but only to catch the person stealing his items.

None were at fault, yet somehow their combined actions caused the death of an unsuspecting citizen. And that, my friends, is why our town is named The Retarded Centre.
