This game has held quite special memories for many im sure. I still remember when Mods would occasionally beam into the game and serve as Moderators. I haven't really played since College, however i simply cannot forget D2N and all the Meta's, random towns and friends from around the world that were united by staying active and working toward common goals.

I havent likely posted in upwards of 5 years but hearing this game will be inaccessible due to Flash becoming obsolete shortly it just makes the memories all the more relevant. What a journey we could all describe through this rather simplistic browser game. I used to dedicate more time to D2N in college than any modern console games even though competing in those was always a joy too. Left 4 Dead particularly with its team focus.

I really think this game deserves a thread designated to talking about our past experiences, teams and friendships. Just going through my and seeing the towns and assorted Meta's is sincerely emotional. Sorry this is longwinded. Die2Nite may actually Die without flash.
