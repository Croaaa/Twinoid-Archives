biggest change this season was using 15-20 guardians as watchmen, this allows you to build non defense builds earlier such as water and food builds, which then allows you to have more AP later in game, these guardian watchmen also help delay the need for temp building until much later too

the more you ration, the more you fall behind

spacekadt
SaucyemmettCOf course they don't. Having a soul requires humanity, and they traded that in for flesh and power...

I can't tell if you're joking or not, so I'll give the serious answer.

ALL deaths that occur during the attack downtime produce a soul, regardless of the player's status. If the ghoul dies of attack in town or from failcamping, they will produce a soul just like anyone else.

If the ghoul is killed by another player's attack, that is not an attack time death and will not produce a soul for collection.
