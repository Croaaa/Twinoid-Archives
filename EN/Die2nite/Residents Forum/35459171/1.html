Hey all,

as some might have read already, I am programming an app to compare the participating towns during the UFC event initiated by EL_ConardO and organized at Die2Nite by iDare. At first, my intention was to make the whole app scroll horizontal due to the amount of towns that need to be compared in a single table (if possible). However, some seem not to be happy with that solution, so I also added a vertical scroll version. You can choose your preferred design by clicking the red "i" in the menu bar at http://do.not.die2nite.org

However, here's the catch. I am just a nerdy developer, not a designer. Though I am familiar with CSS and color schemes, I tend to make heavy weather of coming up with a nice design. I do know it has flaws but I can't name or even correct them.

So, since there are some designers out there (I am sure of that), I would like to ask you to come up with some cool (and readable) design for the app. If you are willing to spend some time on that and are able to send me a single CSS file, I will include your design in the app (giving credit to you, of course).

For questions or other feedback you can twin me or find me on mibbit (SinCC in #rfm) often.

Thanks in advance,
