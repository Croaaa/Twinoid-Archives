Hey all,

It's sad to see this piece of gaming history coming to an end, yet another reminder that everything eventually dies. At least we'll always have our memories, good or bad.

I figured we could share an anecdote from our time with this game, anything that makes you smile or maybe something that makes your blood pressure rise to an unhealthy level. Something uniquely Die2Nite.

I'll start but first I gotta give a huge shoutout to my first meta, The Oozing Inn. They took me in and showed me what an organized town can actually accomplish, all while not being a buncha jerks. I'll never forget you all. <3


Here one of my memories:

Ages ago there was a player by the name of Mayor Fasi, he'd run around a town spewing rhetoric about how "MAYOR FASI GETS IT DONE.", trying to gather more supporters for his campaign. Sometimes he would then proceed to die on day 1. If he didn't die, however, you got to look forward to hearing his constant political banter until he did.

I also gotta mention (one of?) his political rivals, Private Buttkiss. I first heard of Buttkiss through the World Forums on his campaign trail against the incumbent mayor and years later became very close friends with him. To be honest I don't think Buttkiss stood a chance against Fasi, he just had too much charisma, and a campaign jingle. Like I said, no chance.

Private Buttkiss has unfortunately passed away since from an accident.


Sorry to end mine on such a down note, I look forward to reading the good times and the bad.

I'll miss you guys.
