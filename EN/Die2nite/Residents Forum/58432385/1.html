Griffonn

I'd have to say that one of my best memories was meeting my long-time coalition buddy DrJamez!

You guys may remember him from around season 4 or so. Our story goes something like this...

So there I was, in a hardcore random town. I hadn't gotten too involved in the forums or community before this town, so I was just an average, under the radar player.

For one reason or another, I ended up pissing in some guy's soup in the town. Chaos had erupted in the town forums over whether or not I was to blame, (I maintain that I was innocent), and it was looking as though I was going to get shunned and hung with the gallows.

The guy who wanted me dead was in a coalition that he called "The Family". Yeah, definitely the wrong guys to piss off.

Through The Family's angry forum posts, came a single voice in my defense. It was none other than that of DrJamez. Unfortunately, what started as a heroic moment of justice for him ended with his assimilation in my convictions.

Like a pair of runaway lovers, we headed off into the world beyond to avoid attack from The Family. We camped together that night by a pair of silos, both having very low chances of survival. Mind you, totally platonic.

Anyways, whether we survived or not, we expected to be shunned and hung by the time we woke up.

Come morning, the crow caws from the top of the silo, and somehow we have both managed to neither be taken by rope or by zed. Unfortunately, the family had paid us a visit and wounded us the night before, leaving us both infected.

We return to town, and I realize that I have a note on my hovel doorstep. It came from a silent hero in our town, who had used his mystical abilities to steal the rusty chain from the bank and delete the log in the town registry, saving Jamez and I from being gallows fodder.

Ultimately, we succumbed to our wounds. After that town, though,
Jamez and I were an inseparable and unstoppable team! Eventually we both stopped playing D2N for a while. I have finally returned, but unfortunately he has not.
