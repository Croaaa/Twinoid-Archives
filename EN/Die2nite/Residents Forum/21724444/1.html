SheDevil

Rationing is a bad idea, in my opinion. You're almost always going to need people scavenging, and they're going to need to go as far away from town as possible to hit all of those undepleted zones. Even getting builders to ration can be crippling to the town, as you'll soon see that you're struggling for AP for defences, and once you fall behind in the defence race, you tend to stay behind.

On the other hand, sometimes it's necessary to ration. If you don't find the blueprints you need, for example. On the whole, though, I strongly believe that rationing should be avoided as much as possible, it's easy to keep on top of water reserves now.

To answer the question, rationing day 2 is counterproductive for sure.

I generally don't ration at all! I only ration when I'm busy and not able to go out that day and only if we need AP for building do I drink when not thirsty. But, if I'm around, I drink and scavenge! I haven't been shunned yet! For that anyway.

But, rationing is always in the back of my mind. A hold over from the beginning when we very strictly rationed. Two days drinking one day inside building. You would scavenge a far ways day 1, and often end thirsty, day 2 you would clear thirst by only going 16 AP, day 3 you didn't drink at all and staying inside to build or only walk 10 AP (we don't do the 10 AP scav anymore though as it's best to use that AP building and save food for long range scavs.). This isn't day 1, 2 and 3 just an example of how you would ration on a 3 day cycle.

I was in a town recently where water was an issue for a couple of days but there are always Pump upgrades, the Rig and other things to keep water in the well so we ended up just fine, with more than enough water by the time we died on day 16.
