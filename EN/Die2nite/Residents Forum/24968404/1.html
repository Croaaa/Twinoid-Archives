The number of days credited is the total number of days survived.

Deaths by Infection, Withdrawal, Dehydration, and forgetting to camp always occur before the attack, therefore you did not survive the day.

Dying while camping before Devastation comes before the attack, but if the town is devastated then it is counted as occurring during/after the attack.

Getting eaten in your bed and watching the Reactor blow up always occur during/after the attack.
