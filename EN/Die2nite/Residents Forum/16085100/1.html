Alright, I'm tired of waiting for people to reply to other messages so I'm going to ramble on about my dreams.
Most other people find it boring when people talk about their dreams but I'm just going to ignore that for a bit
I've had a lot of dreams that are somehow related to Die2nite... And I don't know if it's because I'm too obsessed or because... I'm to obsessed. (What other reason could there be?!)

So, these are mostly in chronological order.


1. About a year ago- I dreamed that I was the last person surviving in town on some large numbered day (was it 54?). The Bank took up the whole screen and was chock full of goodies (many that didn't exist irl, like happy faces and random flowers). I was really excited and just started taking random stuff. I was sad when I woke up, signed in to d2n, and realized that I died (Day 3) because of open gates. : /


2. June 22, 2012- I had a dream (a nightmare, really) where I came back from school (though it was already summer vacation) and remembering that it was the last day to register for the Apocalympics. My dad was watching a Chinese movie, so he didn't let me use the computer and made me play piano instead. I was really jumpy and kept on looking at the clock. After a while, I realized that the d2n update time already passed and as a result, the signup date already passed and I would miss the Apocalympics!
I woke up with a start, and realized that it was still the 22nd and I would still have a chance. That was the scariest dream I've ever had.
So I registered happily and checked the website at least 20 times to find the start date, only to find out a few days later that it was postponed to the 27th of July. I had a summer camp during that time and I was really, really depressed for a week or so.
When I came back from summer camp I found out that the start date has been postponed again, and I would've made it had my computer not turned weird and not let me on Die2nite. So, in the end, my nightmare kind of turned true. This was an interesting journey of highs (finding out about the Apocalympics) and lows (nightmare) and highs (realizing my nightmare wasn't real life) and lows (finding out the Apocalympics was starting in July, which meant that I would miss it) and highs (finding out it got postponed again) and one final low (my computer doesn't let me go on Die2nite. That made me bang my head against the wall and freak out in PMs to Muido.)
*sigh*


3. about a week ago- I dreamed that a lot of Die2nite players came together irl to make a town. I know for sure that Workshop was there, and so was Anti. We all had this starchy uniform and I kept on trying to abduct the Tamers' labradoodles. I killed a bunch of zombies with a sewing needle (they just kind of popped like a balloon). I remember a conversation with someone:

Someone: Did you know that if you take two Cyanides, you will get 20AP and then you could turn zombies into chocolate?
Me: Really?
Someone: Yup.
Me: Would one Cyanide work?
Someone: No.
Me: Aww darn it.

I also remembered that I turned into a zombie and tried to eat other people. I was thinking, Wait! They're your friends! And then thinking, Nah. They're too tasty.
Sadly, I woke up soon afterwards.


4. Last night- I dreamed that we were in a desert. I was with a few other people. Somehow in the middle of it I turned into a different person (that happens once in a while) named Meggi or Mega or something. <_<
She took out the Ace of Sheep (apparently everyone had a deck of cards and Sheep was a legitimate suit) and using Shaman powers, turned it into a real sheep. And then she wandered further into the World Beyond.
(Here I turned back to myself) I couldn't find Meggi (I think she was my best friend or something) so I called some friends over and went to look for her. And then I got attacked, apparently, and lost consciousness.
When I woke up the Crow was standing next to me.
I started complaining about my life in general, then decided to say something more useful.
I asked the Crow, "Where's Meggi?"
It told me that she was dead. I was kind of sad.
Then I asked him, "Wait... Am I dead?" The Crow answered, "Yes."
Then I suddenly remembered: "Isn't there a temple just full of dogs and a garden full of cats in the world for the dead?" The Crow nodded.
"Can I talk to you whenever I want?" I asked.
The answer was 'yes'.
"Whoa, that's awesome!" I smiled.
I then went to look for Meggi. I found her but she didn't recognize me. I realized that I was slowly losing my memories too. The Crow told me it was normal, and by the time I joined another life my old memories would be completely gone.
"How can you accumulate knowledge then?" I asked, startled. that was my exact wording.
"Write diaries," the Crow told me.
I was too lazy so I decided to just try to remember.
One of my friends tried to help by quizzing me on my memory.
"What did Lucius Malfoy's father do when he was little?" she asked.
I wracked my brain, but I couldn't remember. Perhaps because the answer doesn't exist?
I was really frustrated and decided to just go and play with the kitties.
I got woken up by my sister soon after.
There was a part where my husband (apparently I was married O_O) got mad at me for falling in love with his father. And I tried to defend myself. Strangely, my husband was the same age as his father. : /
I don't really remember this part that well, except that it was one of the things that I complained to the Crow about.

If you managed to read this whole post without getting too bored, kudos to you! I will wait for the influx of hate mail from people because I wasted their time. Oh well, this was really fun to type! especially since my sister and my friends aren't that interested in my dreams, which makes me sad.
This was fun!
