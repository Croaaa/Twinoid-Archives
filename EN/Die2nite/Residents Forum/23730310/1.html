The Die2Nite Adventure Game

I will start with parts 1, 2, and 3 as the introduction, each will end with a choice, which will instruct people to one of several sections, which will be numbered consecutively (go to 2, go to 3, go to 4...)

The next person writes up the next section Get up and hide in your room (go to 4) Based upon the choice that would have led someone there, they describe the new situation, and a new list of options at the end, again, with numbers increasing consecutively from those unallocated. Alternatively you can send the reader back to a section that's already been written. Or, if you decide that there's no hope for the reader who has reached your section (or that they may have somehow 'won') you can end your section with THE END.

Feel free to be as surreal as you like!

Lets see how far we can get without getting too confused. I hope I wrote the instructions clearly. If not just ask. ♥
