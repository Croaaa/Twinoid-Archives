BekeyAs well as Dehydration, Withdrawal and Infection. Those are all pre-attack time deaths, if you can call it that.

For purposes of soul collections, these are attack time deaths. Any death that happens after 23:00 and before 23:10 will produce a soul. (While the game is down)

Hanging, meat locker, walking to dehydration, cyanide and any other daytime death will not produce a soul.
