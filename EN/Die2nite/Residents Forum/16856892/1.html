Colt1911

It doesnt actually have to be a town since it's a forum game. We can play mafia here.

But a town event is very intriguing. Let's not call it mafia though. Let's style it into Die2Nite themes.

Here's a suggestion.

Mafias would be the Infected
The police would be the Zombie Hunters
And then there's regular townies

Online mafia is usually a roleplay, so why not a backstory? Our mafia are actually those who are infected, who are hiding and are spreading their disease against their will. They don't really want to die so they don't say anything, otherwise the people will hang them, but because they won't say they've contracted the virus, people die.

Meanwhile the Zombie Hunters are a group bent on finding the Infected. They have the powers of the Detective and can determine if a person is Infected or not. If there is more than one Zombie Hunter, they either dont know who the other Hunters are and act independently, or know who the others are and share the power of the Detective between them (they decide who they want to vote every night phase).

We also need an Arbiter/Narrator. Arbiter/Narrator - the one who set up the game, immune to shuns and complaints, the one who knows all the identities of the others. Will not participate in the game other than to be "physically" present, to oversee the entire event. Everybody knows who the Arbiter is, but the Arbiter does not interact with anybody, and nobody interacts with the Arbiter.

In the spirit of the Die2Nite theme, why don't we name the Arbiter Epoq? XD

Those are the basic mafia roles and tbh those are the only ones I'm familiar with, but with a game of 40 people, there should be more roles. Pulling up a wikipedia article, here's more to add:

The Witness + Siblings - Witnesses know everything about everyone, and know the things that happen in the night phase (who the Infected kill, the Zombie Hunters investigate, etc). This is because the Arbiter (Epoq) tells them this. Meanwhile, the Siblings are usually a pair of people who must win by being the last two people left in the game. One is usually Mafia (Infected) and the other is a townie. If one of the Siblings die, they both die.

The combination sounds like perfect Crows to me. They're a separate faction because they lie in wait for people to die so they can eat the dead's livers. If one of the Crows are hanged, the other commits suicide by going out in the World Beyond and not camping.

Lynching could be done by hanging, so a vote doesn't need a majority to win. It only needs to reach 8 votes. It only needs to reach 8 votes. Obviously, there will be no revival rules. Or lynching could be done by exile instead. The lynched person has to go outside to camp. But if they survive, they have to be lynched again.

There's got to be some system that lets the game end quickly. Chances are, we won't have the time to set it up so that the town lasts 10 towns (if the game is fully mafia oriented).

Which brings me to ask. How is this going to be mafia like? What are the mechanics?
