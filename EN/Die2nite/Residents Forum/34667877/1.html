iDare

Great reminder

I was using pen and paper in several first towns, simple notebook, then exchanged notes on chat with more and more friends while moving from town to town and recruiting willing to cooperate & like-minded players. It had many pages written down before Rulesy made great external map. Posting in forums was a very common habit back then too, most caring people would do that.

Actually on the first season when explorable ruins have been implemented (and no mapping option in any external site back then for sometime) we were drawing using pen and paper as well next sending photos of drawn pictures via mail, so the next person could continue mapping from there.

I`m so glad someone finally suggested using google docs for that too at some point.


This game used to take so much more time back then if I recall it correctly, just to go outside of town for instance. Now everyone knows what to do mostly and we basically post simple directives for the day, next hang out in chat talking about things not related to the game.


Edit after lordwok`s post: Yes! The ingame map was the only map we had back then. If you look carefully at it, you`ll notice quick glance of scary sentence appearing on it for a half of second. There`s few of them.
