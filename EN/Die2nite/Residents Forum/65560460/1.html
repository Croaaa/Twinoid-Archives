Hello everyone !

I am making this announcement regarding the end of alpha, and therefore the start of beta and the arrival of the Purge .

With the other devs we believe that MyHordes is close to leaving the alpha phase, and as we announced at the end of April, we are announcing a date: September 1, 2021 !

What will happen?

It's simple: we believe that the major flaws in the game have been corrected, and that most of the game's functions are implemented correctly (as best we can, because I remind you that we do not have the code for the game. 'origin so we redo everything from the data we gather).
Therefore, if all goes well on September 1 will take place the Purge, and therefore the end of the alpha phase and the betapropine. For info, all running towns during The Purge will be ended.

Are we going to lose everything then?

No ! The Purge will only keep "essential" data such as your login data, preferences, and imported soul data. We will also keep the heroic experience performed in MH, and the cities made during the alpha (but the alpha ranking will disappear normally, as well as all the pictograms associated with these cities). It is possible that the classification is kept, this remains to be defined but in any case it will not give rise to rewards.

What happens next?

The beta phase will allow to re-adjust what needs it, to optimize the game to make it fluid and easily playable on mobile, also it will allow to correct the remaining bugs, to implement the missing functions and to finalize the game. integration with EternalTwin.

If you ever have any questions, do not hesitate to here or contact me in PM
