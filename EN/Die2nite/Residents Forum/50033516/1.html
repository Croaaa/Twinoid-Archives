Welcome to the sign up for Season 12 Mega Meta Jump (MMJ)

the date for this Event is suggested to be December 9th 2015 which is 2 days after the start of season 12 to make sure no one get stuck in Quarantined towns or towns which wont rank in the new season Leaderboard ranking. (if there is any need to change the date feel free to mention why and we could change it if there is a good reason for that)


  • Introduction: What is MMJ?
    Mass Meta Jump (MMJ) is a mass jump Event, where many players enter together at the same time into Distant Region towns. The goal is to create towns with a large number of active citizens who are interested in working together to do the best they can for a successful long Town.

    MMJ towns will have a mix of experienced players as well as newer players, solo players and meta players. Its a great opportunity to meet people and have fun along with learning new ways to play the game.
  • All who wish to participate in this Event need to please fill out this
    Sign Up Form

    Also those who can be online at the jump date and wish to help drag in others with them who can´t be online need to fill out this form so the organizers can make sure you get 4 names to invite into your coalitions and act as a Jumper for them. and be aware we need everyone´s help with this.
    big thanks in advanced.

  • Official IRC Chat Channel to talk about anything related to this MMJ event and be there if need on the jump date.
