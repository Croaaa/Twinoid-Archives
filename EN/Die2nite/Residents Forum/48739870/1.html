2 x Wonder Towns Sign Up
Our group of about 45 people are planning 2 x wonder towns in about 7 to 9 days which will jump on the same day, the jump date for both towns will be between
17th and 19th Septemeber. (still not set)

we are searching for about 30 to 35 people interested in joining those 2 wonder towns to first get introduced to organized meta play plus get some Wonders/Atomic Death distinction out of it and second get to know new players and have some fun you cant get in random towns.

Anyone is welcome to join, Resident or Hero, Newbie or Experienced , below 100 Soul points or above , all are welcome to join as long you can stick to the following simple rules:

  • 1- Never forget to return home (set alarm in your phone at 22.10 server time) and avoid to go or stay outside if you may not get to an internet connection before 22.15!
  • 2- Always return home before 22.15 server time even if that will make you lose last search
  • 3 -Always turn full Escort on no matter when or where you outside. escort can save your life
  • 4- Always update the external map "from dust til dawn"
  • 5- Always read the forum.

we will divide the 30 to 35 sign ups into 2 groups to have both towns full of mix between meta and non-meta players.

if you are interested please copy and paste the Sign Up list below then post the new list after adding your name to it:
and feel free to post or comment anything you want as long its not Spam
