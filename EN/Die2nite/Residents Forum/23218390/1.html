ImbuedGreen

It is best to max the Watchtower first (unless Searchtower is built early on).

At that point, you will most likely either have a need for water, and thus vote Pump... Or will have found Blueprints for the Searchtower/Evolutive Wall/Water Turrets.

If none of the above are the case, then voting Great Pit wouldn't hurt, although voting Workshop may be better. AP saved is AP saved, and unless you are unlucky enough to have been forced to choose between Great Pit and Workshop over multiple days because you have nothing else, voting Great Pit once for +13 defense just isn't comparable to a 5% AP reduction of constructions.

In the big scheme of things, it depends on your circumstances and your town's strategy.

Searchtower replenishes depleted zones and buildings, which basically saves AP on its own. A town surrounded with depleted zones must spend extra AP to scavenge further from town, and has little hopes of replenishing their food stock, among other things. The faster you vote up the ST, the faster you'll replenish zones.
I's up to you to find the balance... But I'd advise voting Searchtower somewhere in your plans
