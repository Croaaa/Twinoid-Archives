hottest

There are several objectives and styles of play in this game. Mainly we can split it into two categories. The first is selfish play. The second is cooperative play.

The selfish play is simple. Try to be the last one who survives in the town, even though at the expense of the town and other citizens.

Then it comes to cooperative play. So everyone tries to work together and help the town to survive as long s possible. Teamwork is very important, and this game is deep in strategy when it comes to that. A town can last for anything from 5-6 days up to over 40 days. And there is a leaderboard for that.

Unfortunately there are conflicts between two kinds of players. People who want to work together will get pissed off by the selfish players who ruins their game. The concept of the game sounds nice (well there are always some lowlife you will encounter in your life), but you are not going to be happy when you spent days of hard work on it and get ruined by a lowlife in the end. They call them "griefers".

To counter this issue, Meta Play is born. Literally speaking, a meta is just a group of 40 people who join together and play in a town. So it can be even a group of 40 lowlife in a town too. But in reality, nearly always a meta is for cooperative play.

Meta is not officially supported. People just find a way to work around it. Anyone can create a coalition but you can have 5 people at max. So a meta is to find 8 coalitions, one of which has to be a hero (only hero can select which town to jump). The one who can jump is called jumper. 8 jumpers meet together using third-party tools, like IRC. Then they arrange a date and time to meet at the same time and jump together.

It hasn't to be a full meta. You can jump with 2 coalitions, 5 coalitions or whatever. It is up to you.
But it is the setup of the group who try to compete and rank at the top of the leaderboard.

To get an idea what a meta play and how they organize a jump, see this thread:
Fun Town - New Faces

If you are interested in cooperative play and want to find partners, ask for recruitment in this thread:
Official Recruitment Thread (resident)
