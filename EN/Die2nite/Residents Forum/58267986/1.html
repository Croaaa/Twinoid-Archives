Players willing to ask for help are worth more than most things in this game.

Don't build small cafe, or portal lock, they're basically a waste of AP in prettymuch every situation they appear in.

Broken staffs can be combined with an empty oil can and wire reel to make the guitar, which gives everyone in town one AP once a day. Having more than one doesn't give you more plays of it, but they're great watch weapons so it's still worth to make more.
