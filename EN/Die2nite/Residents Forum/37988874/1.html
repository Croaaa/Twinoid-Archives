Investadefense: Rare BP

"The town decides to causally build a defensive project without really trying. Everyday after it is built, the citizens work on the Investadefense whenever they please, improving its defenses."

Provides 1 defense when it is built. Every day after the Investadefense is built, it doubles its defense. The Investadefense will continue to double its defenses 11 times, ending with a grand total of 2048 defense. Everyday after the defense cap is reached, the Investadefense provides every citizen with a 20% chance to gain a home defense point, and a 5% chance to automatically receive a home upgrade. The home upgrade does not count as your daily upgrade. You can also receive a home upgrade past a house, but only through the Investadefense. If the town has Builders Merchant, the odds of receiving an automatic home upgrade, increases from 5% to 20%.

Building Materials:
1 Nuts & Bolts
4 Sheet Metal
4 Unshaped Concrete Blocks
10 Patchwork Beams
10 Metal Supports
120 AP

Notes: The construction must be repaired everyday in a hardcore town. This construction is probably most useful in a meta distant regions town.
