If you are hero and in town, you can use Hero Rescue action (go to your home page, you will see it there), BUT, the person must be in specific range near town.

See this pic, if person is within yellow lines you can teleport him in.

Also, you must not have used any other Hero action before that day. You can only use one Hero Action a day. Giving hero day Brother in Arms skill (if you have it unlocked) doesnt count towards hero actions.

You could also, if you gave AP to get to him and back, maybe drop him booster? Like food water booze drug if town has them.
