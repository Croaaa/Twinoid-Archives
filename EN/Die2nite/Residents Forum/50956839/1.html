Here's how it's gonna work:

1. Everyday between 9:00 and 11:00 i will publish a post breaking down each of the MMJ towns and report on what they have established, any drama, predictions, their attack results and etc. (NOTE: I will try to keep track of the Wonder Games as well i will explain more about it later)

PRO TIP: To be ranked first at the end of each day, you will need to keep the most players alive and keep up with the attack numbers. Other factors contributing to where you get placed could be:

- blueprints
- construction buildings
- Defence
- Ruins
- End game camping locations/distance
- current resources in bank

3. Starting from day 6 onwards i will release 2 reports a day. The first focusing on resources, possible planning, attack results and i guess the goals in general at the normal time.

The second report will focus only on the incoming attack and will be a shorter report. This report will probably get posted up to 2 hours before attack.

4. I know there is the Wonder Games going on as well so while there is the "Strongest town" ranking (aka my prediction ranking), i will also create a second ranking specifically for the Wonder Games. Using the system every day i will keep track of which town is winning the Wonder Games.

NOTE: i will need somebody from each town to tell me exactly when they achieve one of the goals from the Wonder Games (I will cover the Empty Corridors town you guys are lucky)

If you do want to feed me information about which direction your town is heading, future plans of your town and any report-worthy moments you think is a game-changing moment for your town. PM ME.
