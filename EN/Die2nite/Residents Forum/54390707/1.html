I'm defining "underrated" here as "Items that, in your experience, players don't seem to use that often, but that you think are very useful or like to use"


There are two items, specifically, that I think are usually vastly underrated (or seem to be, from my observations and experiences in towns) and those are: Flash Grenades and Radius Radar Beacons.

Both are especially useful in the early days (1-3) of a town, I find, although RRBs tend to go down once the watchtower is upgraded and/or if there are a lot of scouts.



Flash Grenades, however, I think might just be the MOST underrated item in the game. I never seem to see very many people use them and they seem to always build up in the bank....but I've been saved by a FG many, many times, so...they're not totally useless. :p If there are a lot of extra ones and I'm going out to unknown zones or to rescue someone who is trapped, I will usually take one with me.


This is just in my opinion, though...you probably have your own, so..what items do you think are underrated if any are?
