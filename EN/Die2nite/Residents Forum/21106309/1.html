Dear all,

due to various reasons and changes in 2013 that already happened and will definitely happen, I need to restructure my server environment. Ultimately, this will result in not offering the Oval Office anymore.

However, I am aware that at least some players are using my app for various reasons and I don't want to take it away from these who still like it. Therefore, I am giving notice well ahead of the shut down which will take place on August, 31st latest.

Second, I am willing to give away the project. If there is any interested and capable player, you may continue to use develop the app. It is based on PHP and mySQL. The main application in PHP isn't that large (no more than 10 MB), but the OO provides an XML archive which is about 1.1 GB in size currently. Additionally, the database currently uses 750 MB.
Transfer is rather low these days, during events though the daily transfer would reach up to 4 GB.

Have this information in mind if you would like to take over the Oval Office. Don't hurry, you'll have a couple of month to decide if you really want to do this. Maybe, two or three persons could team up and build some kind of a cloud because that's what I am doing with these apps. There are two web servers and two database servers to balance load and to avoid downtimes.

If you still want to do this, just send me an email to the address which is found on the external site information page when accessing the Oval Office through the site directory.

CC
