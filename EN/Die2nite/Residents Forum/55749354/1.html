No. There are infinite uses for that item:

Here is one example of a good thing you can do with it:

  • The construction All or Nothing will generate 1 for each Crushed battery in the bank.

Here is one example of a bad thing you can do with it:

  • You can discretely donate it to someone else's chest (assuming there are already items there and room for the crushed battery) and you can later make a post in the forum saying that the person is bringing useless items to town and should be shunned (increasing your chances on LMS).
