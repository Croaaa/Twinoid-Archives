First of all, welcome to the World Forums - Resident Edition!Advice #1: keep away from anyone from the Corvus family, they bite and peck your eyes and liver out if you slip a single bad word near them. And epoq will swallow if you do it near him.

Your Rucksack is "reset" when you die. Always, no exception. A Manbag is an item that's within the Rucksack, so it also is "lost" when you die. Your Chest contents are also reset to the Citizens Welcome Pack + Food combo you got in the beginning of your town.

What happens? There are 2 possible outcomes for your stuff:

  • If you die outside of the town, your Rucksack content will be on the ground the next day (along a Meaty Bone or Unrecognizable Remains (if you die of Poison or Cyanide)) and your Chest contents will also be available for Pillaging (basically recovering stuff from dead people) for your former townmates to loot in your house in the Citizens Directory;
  • If you die inside the town, both your Rucksack and Chest contents will become available for your townmates to Pillage (along a drag of needing to drag your corpse outside at the cost of 2AP or a Water Ration).

There's no set days for you when infected, there's 50% chances of death during the attack. You can cure it by swallowing a simple and rare Paracetoid. But, if you got Infected and is also Injured, wait for a Bandage, otherwise you'll be infected again the next day.

Any other questions?
