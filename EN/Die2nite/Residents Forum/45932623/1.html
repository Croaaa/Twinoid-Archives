If someone dies in your town, go at the gates and take a look on the map. You can see the soul (the soul flies over 4 zones, but it should be located the right down corner of this 4 zones). Post this location in your townforum and someone can go and grab the soul and purify it in town if your town built the hamman. You can do it by yourself, if the soul is not so far away.

Leave the town. Make it rain at 0/0 and if it works 5 rations water will be added to your well (my experience after 23 days beeing a shaman 3 of 4 times it works). Try this every day.

Brew a few mystic potions in your home and put it in the bank. It's for retrieving tortured souls, because only you or a citizien who drank a mystic potion can touch it without dying.
