kiwiluke
Love777So has anyone figured out a formula for reaching 95% survival (so as to give Guardians 100% chance of survival). My assumption was that you had to reach the maximum attack but I found this not true as in the last attack we were about 40 below and still had 95%

the french have worked out the following formula

Probabilty = 100 - 5.47 * Z / X
where Z equals the number of zombie overflow (max attack - town def (not including watchmen))
and X equals watchmen def,

they claim its been tested on over 100 towns with an error rate of less than 0.5%

i haven't had time since learning this to test its accuracy, but would expect the same formula to work here
