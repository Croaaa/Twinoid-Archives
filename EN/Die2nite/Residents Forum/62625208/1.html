Explorable ruins

for those who would like to learn, here is all you need to know and must do to map a ruin:


  • First of all you need a to record your ruin run: Do not even bother claiming you have a good memory or you can draw the path with pen a paper. it never works well and it always makes wrong maps which leads to people running out of Oxygen and getting injured and needing bandages which are very rare.

so i highly recommend to Download and install
http://screencast-o-matic.com/

Please never use the online version and start recording online. in so many cases the recording fails and your whole hard works gets wasted if it fails to record, but the installed version never fail (from personal experience)

also remember that updating the map without recording your run will most likely be messing up the work already done and if that happens the mapping must start over again from scratch.

  • Mapping in from dusk till down
  • first click at the zone of the explorable ruin in FDTD external map:
  • then click on "Map Ruin" , you can see it here
  • then you will see the mapping tool here which you can start updating while you are watching the recording of your run . each DOT means one step taken , Left/Right/Up/Down. its easy BUT be careful to double check your updates, one mistake and its a disaster.
  • Tips While inside the ruin:
  • First of all you need 1 AP to enter the ruin which means if you have just 1 AP and enter the ruin you will end up with zero AP at the Entrance and that means you have the status "Exhausted" this status does´t allow you to use certain weapons like hurling sticks, serrated knifes, mower, machete so if that is the case and only if it is the case of running out of AP after entering you should bring in next Food/water/drug you are planned to consume next to be able to gain full AP and use all weapons inside without any problems.
  • now you get 100% oxygen inside (scavengers get 50% bonus making it total 150% oxygen) so the best way to manage the O² you have when mapping is to search for the walls of the ruin which are either right or left from the entrance. each ruin map is exactly 13 steps wide and 13 steps deep so from the level of the line after the entrance you have exactly 12 steps till you hit the lower walls example. this helps a lot counting to know when the end of the corridor is going to be and helps knowing when to return towards the entrance.
    the best safe strategy is to invest 50% of your oxygen in going deep and 50% to stop exploring and return either the same way you walked or take a small risk and find another way to return to the entrance
    its all about who has fastest internet connection and good memory remembering the steps you took.
  • always make a screen shot of the map and place it next to your browser window so you can see exactly what your next step will be and be able to correct and find the correct path if you get lost by mistake. its very easy : minimize your browser window and open the saved map screen shot as a picture you open and minimize to fit in your screen like this.
  • Zeds:
    when you run into a zeds (there can be 1, 2 , 3, or 4) in one of the zones inside you will be stopped from moving and you have exactly 2 options:
  • kill them to be able to carry on moving
  • flee: if you choose this option you will lose about 5% to 7% from your oxygen but then still can carry on.
    NOW in an organized town you need to kill them all to make empty the ruin from zeds for next ruin runners to be able to make their runs without any further risks, so it is highly recommended you do not go inside without any weapons unless the ruin runner before you confirms that there are no more zeds inside and the ruin is fully mapped. SO kill even if it means you will lose a lot of time inside and be forced to cut your trip short, the next ruin runner after you will thank you for it.
  • Doors/Rooms
    each ruin must always have exactly 10 doors. the number of unlocked doors can be on average 4 and the number of locked doors is on average 6. but it is different in each map so you should always be prepared to have the worst case of 8 locked doors and only 2 unlocked.

    the only way to open those locked doors is to send a Technician who will simply click at the locked door to make an imprint for the key and mark the type of the key (we have 3 types: Bottle opener, Magnetic key, and Bump key)

    once the imprints are back in town anyone of any class can convert the imprints into keys using workshop/hacksaw.

    then anyone from any class can take all keys and go unlock all locked doors while carrying all the keys in rucksack. here a manbag or a trolley will be almost a must so all doors gets unlocked in one run to maximize the number of doors searched inside.
  • Notes:
  • zeds respawn/gets born after each new day inside the ruin, so if you kill all on day 4 there is still going to be a chance that new zeds will appear on day 5 and so on , so in the start of each new day first ruin runner must always take few weapons inside to kill all in the path which next runners will walk.
  • you should draw an Optimal path like this for all runners to walk after all doors have been located and unlocked so the first runner check for zeds and kill them and next runners can move freely and safely knowing they will not run into an unexpected zeds.
  • Warning : Poisoned Water:
    in each of the 3 possible Explorable ruins each map can have, all of them can drop water and most of this water rations look exactly like the normal clean water we take daily from the well BUT they are poisoned and they will kill you the second you will drink them.
    so one of my most important advice of all in this game: DO NOT EVER bring any water found inside the explorable ruin outside when you step out of the entrance by the end of your ruin run. ALWAYS drop any water rations you get from searching any door inside the ruin before stepping outside.
  • Heavy items found inside ruins:
    all ruins drop certain heavy items: concrete, Chairs or water cooler bottles Empty or full even complete chainsaw.
    so whatever you do never go inside the ruin with a trolley because that will prevent you from bringing a heavy item out of the ruin because the trolley is a heavy item as well.
