Lots of good memories but that first stupid death will always stick in my mind.

Beta ended end of November 2010, so alittle late on the 3 year birthday, but with how Motion-Twin handle updates around here, its a fittng homage to celebrate it late

Read about the game on NeoGaf so tried it out, 1st town didn't last long. last day and 3 left, I went outside and got stuck, who knew terror took your control points, embarrassed by my own fail, I became a hero so I could HR back and the rest is history. Eventually I went on to be the first to both the Scavanger and Survivalist 300 day titles.

2nd town somehow lasted 15 days and ran through xmas 2010, about 15-20 of us joined together to do a town in the new year. Half were a bunch of cool Polish guys who slowly stopped playing but I also met Dainz, Duckman and Alphabet who I would play many seasons with.

Also met Andaho in that town who was the original creator of die2nite.net and he set up a chat room for our expeditions, which eventually became open to all and so busy it was moved onto the quakenet irc and became what it is now. He didn't last long as he was farming infected distinctions but also because of him I would later meet Annette/Scout but I'm skipping ahead now

So we jumped after the new year and was followed by another half coalition led by a guy called Hoffenpepper, he had seen me arguing with an old school meta called The Crows, who had I think the longest beta town that you didn't *need* to upgrade Watchtower and could cope without it (this was back when maps were 15x15. Still, no WT upgrades was a bad idea ha) So in that town we didn't upgrade it and were the first town to reach 25 days, until Odd Park beat us, we also survived longer then the Crows who jumped at the same time, so that was pretty rewarding.

One of Hoffenpeppers crew was Virg who is here in the same town as me right now and I've played with him from Season 0-8. After that town we combined into a full meta and headed into season 1.

Originally I only wanted to point out that beta was 2010 and was on my way to sleep, but then I started to write and it turned into a eulogy of sorts. I don't really play much now and am an old man in D2N terms, not many towns left in me, but I'll continue this story tomorrow..
