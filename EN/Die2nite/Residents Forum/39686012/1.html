I just got inspired from being on watchmen duty... here are some suggestions!

Name: Watchmen Training
-Uncommon Blueprint
-Provides 0
-Requirements: 10 Wooden planks, 2 wrought iron, 1 twinoid. 30
Effect: All watchmen now have an additional survival rate of 3%, it is now impossible to get terrorized as a watchmen, and all watchmen provide an extra 3.
Description: Training wouldn't hurt, would it? Why not train our watchmen to be better defenders of the town?

Name: Watchmen Veteran Training
-Automatically buildable when Watchmen Training is built.
-Provides 0
-Requirements: 12 Wooden Planks, 5 wrought iron, 1 sheet metal, 1 trestle, Any weapon. 60
Effect: All watchmen now have an additional survival rate of 7% (Stacks with the 3%). For every guardian on watch, each watchmen has an increased survival rate of 1.5%. Each watchmen now provides an extra 12 (Stacks with the 3). Weapons now used by watchmen have an increased defense output of 10.
Description: Enough training is never enough!

Name: The Final Watch
-Rare Blueprint
-Provides 15.
-Temporary Building!, And cannot be built again.
-Requirements: 3 sheet metal, 2 trestle, 10 wrought iron, 8 wooden planks, 5 torches, 10 water rations, 1 valium shot, 2 Semtex, Bandage. #Of citizens*6, eg. 40 citizens*6 will require 240 to build this.
Effect: Each watchmen now provides 1000! All citizens are now watchmens! Survival chance is now strictly 25% (30% Survival chance for Guardians). The watchmen tower of the town is destroyed after the night of the "Final Watch"!
Description: You know what? If we don't act now, 10000 are going to try to kill us. Why not make everyone lay down their lives for a ridiculous defense bonus?!
