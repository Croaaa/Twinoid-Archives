Ode to Balohan

*Strums Guitar*
I'm just simple drug dealer
With Balohan, a quite neat mister
In the desert, cold and bitter
But I can't forget the zombie that it all better

So I heard old B needed some help
The old hydro pump is where he started to yelp
I came fast as I could, bringing whatever I can
I cleared the path, though he said he had a plan.

He said he was gonna stay here and camp
I was out of AP, so I thought I would be a tramp
And stay here with him, he still needed a friend
What's camping outside without a buddy at hand?

I'm just a simple drug dealer
With Balohan, a quite neat mister
In the desert, cold and bitter
But I can't forget the zombie that made it all better

A 50/50 chance of sleeping safe in our tent
And if we wake up alive there would be a blueprint
There was only one problem to this little trip
A hungry zombie lurching over without its lower lip

Balohan went to bed, but I couldn't sleep a wink
Because the zom was coming over and it was a dink
Ended up goofing off since it's the best I've done
But soon the zombie joined in on the fun.

I'm just a simple drug dealer
With Balohan, a quite neat mister
In the desert, cold and bitter
But I can't forget the zombie that made it all better

It was a jolly old just dancing around
Playing with sticks and building sand mounds
It was a blast until the midnight came
And soon all the undead had gone insane

I had a few moments with my new zombie friend
Before he saved my butt by burying me in the sand
The attack came and went while I was hiding in my cover
But soon found out Balohan had been chewed all over

I'm just a simple drug dealer
With Balohan, a quite dead mister
In the desert, cold and bitter
But I can't forget the zombes that made it all deader
