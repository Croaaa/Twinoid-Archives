What is this event?
☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠

This event is based on FIFA´s World Cup of footbal that will happen on Brazil in June, 2014.

It will be splitted in 2 stages. A raffle and a town event.

Participants will register for the event in hope to represent their country. A raffle will be made to see who will represent that country from all enlisted to it. The winners of the raffle will then compete against all other country representants in a Private town for Last Man Standing, which gives the bigger prize.

  The raffle winners will win 1 MTPass each. The town LMS winner will receive 10 MTPasses.☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠
Who can play?
☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠

Anyone. Hero or Resident, 0 Soul points or infinite soul points. Griefer, solo, meta... any kind of players.

All you need to do is to enlist for the first stage of the event (see below).

RULES FOR THE FIRST STAGE OF THE EVENT
(The enlistment/raffle part).

All participants must fill this form: LINK

The Form asks the participant name, the country he wishes to represent, a second choice of country (see why below) and if he has the Mayor ability unlocked (just for town set up purposes, see below).

The Form will be open for registry until 12 June´s attack time.

After that I will raffle (public raffle using RP 100 sided dice tags) one player to represent each country qualified on FIFA`s World Cup (LINK)

Everyone will be checked to see if they are living on said countries (by IP check on event chat and/or Arkadeo flag). Theres a priority for a native player on top of another player that choosed to play for that country. So even if the non-native wins the raffle, the next native will take the slot. Some countries may not have native players and theres a chance you get into the event by enlisting for them.

As you saw theres only 32 countries on the World Cup. We will also add more countries that the community will vote (until June 12).

That will take place on this nexus post: LINK <- forget that. Vote here on this forum thread.

All you need to do is make a single reply there asking for a country to be added on the event. Everyone can only vote once and theres no way to change your mind.

For example: "I want Canada to participate!" Canada will then get 1 vote. You can ask your friends to give more votes for Canada. People from countries that already are on FIFA´s cup can also vote for additional countries if they wish.

The 7 top countries of this poll will be added to the event. It will be 8 countries if one of the raffle winners is a Mayor (that can create the event town for us). If we dont get Mayors on the raffle winners then Corvus Cloud will be the 40th member of the town. (He can play the event if he wants).

I will be updating the runners-up countries here: LINK

Tiebrake
If two or more people get the same higher rolls to be a country representant then the first to enlist gets the spot.
If some extra countries tie for the 7th-8th spoth then the first to be suggested gets the spot.

You can check who enlisted and where he applied here: LINK
Attention: Players enlisted for countries NOT on FIFA´s cup will be managed on a sheet thats off-limits for everyone.

RULES FOR SECOND STAGE OF THE EVENT
(The town and LMS competition).

A Private town will be opened on June 15. Everyone will get the pass/invite to enter it and, as soon as its created, the second part of the event starts (we are not waiting for a new day by postponing the last person to join the town or something like that).

The town will be a Small Map Private town. I´m still thinking on other options for it. Feel free to suggest any.

Inside the town everything is valid: Shun, complaints, attacks, hangs, ghouls, poison, alliances, treason... Be creative. I can not guarantee that shun and hang distinctions will not be counted for the soul, so if you don´t like those: DO NOT play this event.

The only rule is that the Town Gates can only be closed within 1 hour BEFORE the attack.

Again: You can open the gates at any time, but you can only close it 1 hour before the attack or less.

If someone closes the gate not according to that time they will be disqualified.

IF by any chance no one gets the LMS award (all died outside, or town dies before day 5-8 or w/e ...not sure about this) then the winner of the event is the FIRST person that died on the town. Yeah, thats right.

F.A.Q.S
☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠☠

``I live on the Vatican City... I think im the only Die2niter here.. I want to play this event but I will never make Vatican be one of the extra countries...´´
Then enlist for those countries that already are on the World Cup and that are unlikely to have a native Die2nite player. Like Bosnia and Herzegovina for example. Keep checking the sheets and make a new Form submission changing your country choices to increase your chances.

``I choosed the wrong country, can I change it?´´
Yes, see the question above. Send me a Twinoid message to confirm that is really you that want to change it.

``I´m currently living on another country, but i want to represent my native country. How I proceed?´´
Send me a Twinoid message so we can discuss it.

`` My raffle number was good but theres 2 or 3 people with better rolls...its the end...``
Wait. Some people might miss the event because of a meta jump, inactivity or other reasons. Never lose hope.

``Hey nice, I won both my first choice and second choice countries. Will I get 2 MTPasses?´´
No. You will play on your first choice. The next avaliable on your second choice will get that slot (and the pass).

``Theres a yellow note next to my name on the raffle sheet saying "PC". What´s that?``
It´s "Pending Confirmation". I will contact you so we can check if your nationality is really where you are, or if you are trying to play for a country and its not native. If you status remain PC until the raffle happens you will be counted as a Non-native player.

``Theres a green note next to my name on the raffle sheet saying "LG". What´s that?``
It means you are a legit player from that country and that you were checked about it already.

``Theres a red note next to my name on the raffle sheet saying "NN". What´s that?``
It means you are Not Native from that country. You can still participate, but if some legit player show up you better enlist elsewhere. I will not warn you about this!

`` Ha! I can use proxy/haxor skills to lure you into thinking I´m from Botswana or something! ´´
True, but if I suspect you are trying to fool me you will not play. Good luck trying that.
