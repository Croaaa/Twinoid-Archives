TOShokking

Fine then. Maybe a flag system would work better.

In towns, people can 'flag' others who are doing things that are unpleasant or damaging to a group who are trying to play properly (excessive stealing, attacking, griefing, etc.)
In this case, though, you can't see what other people have put in complaints for - If in a specific town, three or four people all complain about the exact same category, then that player will get a Red Flag.
If you get more than five Red Flags, you are restricted to designated Red-Flagged towns - anyone can join, but people with repeated annoying or undesirable behaviour would be restricted to those.
Things like dehydrating due to inactivity could also automatically award a Red Flag - hell, give a reason to tidying the home, that would count as an activity.
If you survive for at least five days, and don't get a Red Flag for that town, you lose one from your total - play fairly, you can go back into normal towns.
Also, you could impose a 'commendation' scheme - if five people all commend that player (regardless of reason), then you would either destroy another red flag, or gain a Green Flag - this could be used as a measure of how fairly they play and how friendly they are.
A person's Flag Count would be displayed on their D2N profile, on the end-of-game register where everyone's last messages and points gained are listed, and on the Citizen page of the town.

BASICALLY PUT:

  • Town complains about your play
  • If you get three identical ones, you get a Red Flag
  • Red Flags are bad
  • 5+ Red Flags, you can only play in special Red-Flag towns
  • Play a town fairly, you lose a Red Flag
  • Help out others and stick together, they can help get rid of your flags for you by 'commending' you.
  • Three commendations equals losing a Red Flag or gaining a Green Flag.
  • Get Green Flags for being friendly and helpful normally, they are the opposite of Red Flags
  • Everyone can see your Flag Count to gauge your teamwork/self-centrance
  • People can only complain four times per town - complaining more than twice gets you a Red Flag yourself (anti-abuse feature).
  • 3+ Red Flags prohibits you from complaining and getting them on other people.
  • You cannot 'commend' (apply for a Green Flag) on your Coalition.
  • You can only 'commend' twice per town.

Also, there could be Green-Flag towns for people with at least 5 Green Flags too.
