A little update following the new season just went down.

We went over a few bugs that we'd found and retouched some of the calibration of certain mechanics.

In short:

  • Calibration of the difficulty of the watch in Hardcore towns. Now it increases with each watch that you go on as opposed to being immediate from day 1.
  • Reduced the damage imposed on construction sites in Hardcore towns.
  • Adjusted the resources needed to construct the Battlements.
  • Added new icons for the Shaman's actions.
  • Removal of the Shaman for the Non-distant towns (too hard for the newbies...)
  • Fixed the API for external applications.

    Fixes:
  • The addition of watches now works again.
  • Expeditions work as they should
  • The Shamanic Potion has been tweaked.
  • The drop rate for Tortured Souls has been fixed (can't appear on the first night now).
  • The transformation of lost souls now takes into account souls left in town and in peoples chest's. Don't forget to purify them!
  • The dead now generate a Lost Soul.
  • The election for the Shaman can no longer pick a non-existent/dead player.

Have fun!
