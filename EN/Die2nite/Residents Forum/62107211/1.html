The writer of the guide was just being dramatic. As in the creator of the COA would have to be evil to disband a group of friends like that.

It's also important to note that when the guide was written twin messaging wasn't a thing. So if you didn't have a way to contact a player outside of d2n there was pretty much no way of contacting another player outside of a COA (even the WF was heroes only at the time). So if everyone is happy in a COA and then it was dissolved you were unlikely to ever talk to those players again unless you randomly landed back in a town with them. Which was way harder to do since there were actually a decent amount of people still playing back then. I suppose you could have still invited them back to a COA but still, it was a pain.

In short no, there is no hell database.
