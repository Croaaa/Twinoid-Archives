Some further details about the Ultimate Fighting Championship event. Don`t hesitate to ask questions below, if having any doubts, I`ll be trying to respond to all as fast as possible from now on.

Please do not discuss the methods of picking the players in this thread, since it would likely be the most discussed topic spamming other questions and further informations, there will be another thread made especially for this one purpose.



Event start is unchanged, 5th and 6th of April (Saturday and Sunday). Details about how the jumping into the towns will look like are below in this post.


3 distant towns per server with similar game conditions (it`s exact quote of Motion Twin`s response, still unsure from this message if the towns are going to be exactly the same, adjusted or just normal random distant towns generated by game. This manner will be confirmed in close future).


Spanish server - Zombinoia - just might be allowed to participate in the end, they will try to fill their 3 towns as well and currently discussing disadvantages, for instance not having a single Architect (perk giving common blueprint at the town`s start) in their game. They may receive some common BPs straight to the bank if it`s accepted by Motion Twin, I don`t know any details if it`s possible though, it was suggested by spanish organizers only. There was an idea to lock the Architect perk for the event purpose, but Motion Twin devs rejected it instantly.


Rewards - distinctions for participants and winners - are being discussed as well, but at this time it`s not a priority in preparations and nothing set in stone really. Participation in this kind of event is considered to be a prize on it`s own.


I`m currently discussing making an external application tracker for all the towns with CountCount, for live town comparison purposes. The main issue for this kind of app according to him would be synchronizing all servers not only due to the timezones but also for data availability. Anyone able to help with this kind of application is welcome (PHP/Javascript programmer/coder), since french organisers claim they are too busy and won`t find anyone till the event start. They are leaning towards tracking all the towns in google docs updated several times per day by chosen participants - this option sounds like absolutely last resort in my opinion.


Each server chooses 120 participants + up to 15 backups in case of someone missing (since the event start is months later than the signings, odds for someone not being able to join in the end are rather decent, due to many factors, real life for instance).
Lists with all the names will be sent to Epoq and participants will receive codes making it possible to join a proper private town on April 5th. Each town will have a person joining at agreed time on April 6th, making it possible to open the gates and start playing. Currently hordes` players are pushing that those chosen people should join not before 10:00am server time on sunday so all the towns start at very similar time, making the first day rather short, but seems we`ll need to adjust (all 3 other servers have the same time zone which corresponds with our game clock) - no final decision on that matter yet though.


Again there were various suggestions, but currently it seems one player will be allowed to sign in & play on one chosen server only. Can`t participate in this event on more than one server even if someone is playing regularly on all of them.


No shamans, no souls, no hurling sticks, no Anzac badges nor Easter eggs. At the time being from the looks we will have Makeshift guitars available, so in general we will play on last update rules from hordes.fr basically, without their rule of day/night time affecting searches.
I promise I will pay extra attention and spam all the coders to make sure that at least the language in town is anything else than french...


Recruiting

We are free to decide how the recruiting is done, as long as the list with 120 players divided for 3 towns + backups is sent to Epoq by March 15th.

120 players is not little number, but certainly not everyone interested is going to participate, that`s why probably best decision will be to discuss all ideas in new thread and vote for best idea as a community.

Aside from discussion, a simple google docs form will be made later today to start signing, so we are ready to act immediately after the decision is made.


One big request is to avoid discussion about the method of picking those 120 players in this thread, please leave it free for discussion/questions concerning all other matters. Another thread will be started in Residents Forum (so all heroes and residents can post), if someone has suggestions and would like to discuss how the 3 teams and their backups should be picked.
