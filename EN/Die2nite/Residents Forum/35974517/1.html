The new season is about to start so we should start organizing ourselves for another Mega Meta Jump (MMJ)!

Whats MMJ?It´s an event where everyone band together and jump randomly into a type of town at the same time. This results in several mixed towns full of good and experieced people and also fresh players. This is a tradition on Die2nite since it was used to drible the Meta Cap eons ago.

We still don´t know when the new season will start (theres a poll for this here!), so we can´t set a date for this event.

On Season 8 we jumped on Hardcore towns, 2 of them are on the leaderboards and will probably rank this season (I´m so proud! )

We can jump on Distant tows or even Small maps too. Theres a couple things we need to considerate though:

Distant Town Jump -> For those that don´t like Hardcore style. We necessarily dont need Hero jumpers since we are doing a random jump. However if you are a 99 or lower Soul points player you will need someone to drag you!

Hardcore Town Jump - > Same as Distant. You need a 100+ SP jumper if you are a rookie player. This kind of town has some limitations and annoying penalties, but it has a better chance for ranking than a random Distant town as metas avoid jumping there through the season.

Small Town Jump -> Here you will need a Hero jumper or a 99 Soul points jumper. You cant jump randomly on Small map town, so this kind of jump is a bit tricky. Small maps are fun, but they doesnt count for the leaderboards.
