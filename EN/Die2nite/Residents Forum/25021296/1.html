Fineous
Kainy

Hello again! Does fellow citizens in the same area boost camping chances? I currently have optimal odds but I don't know if the person with me affects the %.

Also, when does zombies die of loneliness? I killed 7 out of 9 zombies in the area and the other areas have 1-2 zeds in them, will the other 2 despawn? I am always confused of the formula for this kind of thing.

No, fellow citizens do NOT boost camping chances. In fact, every other citizen lowers your chance. However, camping chances are set at the moment that you click 'camp'. Once you are camped at optimal you have a 100% chance of surviving the night. The next person to camp will have a slightly reduced base chance (I believe the second to camp is -5%, the third to camp -15%, etc) but that only matters once they have clicked the camp button. The moral of this story is to camp first. Search for a wiki with the same name as this website (posting outside links is not allowed) to get a complete breakdown on camping chances.

As for zombies dying of loneliness, the formula is very simple. At midnight, 1 zombie will die in the zone for every 2 that were killed in that zone. Loneliness ONLY affects that zone, and it doesn't take into account possible spread from adjacent zones, although it might kill off spread from other zones if you did enough. (For example, there are 8 zombies in our zone, you kill 6. That would mean that the last 2 would die when the attack is calculated. If 2 more spread in from adjacent zones, one of them may die. This particular mechanic has changed multiple times over the seasons, though, so it is better not to count on it.)

alwaysmetaraHow the heck have I never heard this before??!!
Then how is the damage calculated?

In short:

1) The total damage on constructions is half the zombies attacking that day. Formula is actually (Zombies - Overflow Zombies)/2
See this attack for example:
http://i.imgur.com/FndLXL3.jpg
(50-16)/2 = 17. A.Study and Workshop got 17 damage.

2) Most of the time the total damage will be less than expected due to the fact that Constructions can only take 70% of its Hit Points in damage on each attack.

In the first example neither A.Study nor Workshop received its maximum damage.

Now look at this one:
http://i.imgur.com/JsgR4pW.jpg
(108-27)/2 = 41. However the sum of all 4 constructions is 31
Here WS got its maximum damage (18 ). Actually it took 28 points of damage but it was capped to its 70% hit points.

3) The only random part on all this are:
The number of constructions that will be damaged everyday.
(but depends on the day and the pool of damageable constructions avaliable)1-3 on day 1
1-5 on day 2
2-7 on day 3
(...)Which constructions will be the ones to get damaged from the pool of damageable structures.RNG decided that 2 constructions will be damaged
There is 3 constructions that can be damaged
It raffles 2 of those 3 to get the damage.Once all constructions are raffled by RNG the ammount of damage each one will get from the total damage is random (respecting the 70%, but Im not 100% sure of this)

The print order of the constructions in the gazette is not random! They all follow a preset order. (Guard Tower on top -> Extrawall -> Plywood and so on...)

So.. if we add Portal Lock to the pool of damageable constructions we will increase the chance of avoiding damage on other constructions.
