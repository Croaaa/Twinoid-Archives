-Requires 300 Soul points to join.
-Rewards 4x Hero Days!
-Chaos mode is IMMEDIATELY in effect.
-The well starts with 20 water rations.
-The town starts with 5 defense.
-Watchmen provide 2 defense by default.

New status: Hunger (Only in Insane Towns)
After not eating for 3 days you seem famished.
After not eating for 7 days you have a 90% chance of dying everyday.

New status: Insanity (Only in Insane Towns)
After being terrorized for 2 days, insanity occurs. When having the insanity status, you have a 25% chance of dying everyday, and have a 80% chance of injuring yourself everyday, and have a 10% chance of injuring 1-4 people everyday. You also have a 12.5% chance of getting the insanity status when you consume unlabeled drug. Hugging a teddy bear has a 10% chance of curing insanity.

New status: Greed (Only in Insane towns)
After stealing once, greed starts to consume you! You MUST steal at least one item per day or you face the risk of going insane!!!

New status: Sadness (Only in insane towns)
After camping for two straight days... you feel sad... you feel.. that you're alone... that everything is falling to pieces... (Cured by valium shot or hugging a teddy bear.) When you are sad, you have a 50% of going insane tomorrow!

New status: Rage (Only in Insane towns)
After atleast 20 people have died, the whole town becomes angry that the zombies killed half of their people. The citizens are filled with rage. When rage occurs, watchmen provide 20 defense, citizens have 1 extra control point, however with each day that passes, every citizen has a 10% chance of going insane. (10% chance doubles every day citizens have the rage status. 10,20,40... etc.) There is no cure for rage!

When there are 5 people left, EVERYTHING IS DESTROYED, except the town gates.

By the very end of Insanity, towns... most likely every single person in town would be insane.

After the 15th day you survive in an insane town, and have atleast 20 people remaining, everyone receives one hero day for each day they survive after Day 15.
