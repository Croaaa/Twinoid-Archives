Some heroes will jump residents who are interested in joining a coop town and who do not want to be scattered across different towns into a certain town.

This is NO true Meta Jump. The whole town won't be organized as well as a Meta. The main idea is simply to help residents who want to play coop to gather and to jump together into a single town.

The whole "shuttle service" could become bigger and better organized if further heroes will volunteer to jump residents.


How it works:

  • Have a look at the latest postings to see if and when a "shuttle service" will be organized and how many free places are left.
  • Leave a message here
  • You will be invited by one of the jumpers to join a coalition
  • Once you've accepted the invitation, DO NOT JOIN a town on your own. Otherwise you will pull all other members of that coa with you into a random town. If you do so, you will not be invited again.
  • Heroes will jump you into the first empty town after the attack. This should be between 23:15 and 0:15
  • Once everyone is in the town, the job is done. The coalition made to jump you will be cancelled. Every resident is on his own again. Feel free to organize yourselves as you wish.
  • Town will be announced here in case others want to join
