TOShokking

What I usually check:

  • The gates coming in and out - people can't enter town without leaving at some point...
  • The Citizens page - Does it say they're outside, but the town register says they never left? That's odd...
  • The Bank log - people who supposedly never left town can't possibly be turning up lots of planks and items, while having citizens disappear at about the same time...
  • Food - If only one person's been taking Meaty Bones out of the bank and putting Broken Bones back in, then suddenly they stop withdrawing food and start venturing outside more often, it's looking suspicious...
  • Camping - Someone who isn't a Survivalist who camps outside for four days running and has people mysteriously disappear in their local vicinity, they may have something to hide...
  • Water - Some Ghouls know they can't drink their water, so they just don't withdraw any. Why has that Resident stopped drawing water on the exact same day that the people started going missing, I wonder...
  • Drugs - Ghouls inherit things like the Hooked! status - has the town druggie gone missing, and now someone else who never took drugs has started withdrawing one every day...

There are others I'm sure, but that's most of the stuff I look for.

VekengAFAIK ghouls canExactly the point, if someone came back inside and there's no register of them going out, you can be 99.9% sure that they are a derpy Ghoul.

Even though that is a great list on how to catch a Ghoul with their pants down, a good Ghoul still can easily dodge all of those without breaking a sweat. A little on how to avoid them:

  • Go out normally, eat someone far away and rapidly go back to 1AP away and stay ASing for Scraps. This breaks the logic of the "Outside but never left" and "Magically give items to the town";
  • Take a water ration and "Discretely donate it", people won't find you and it will seem like you drink water properly like a non-Ghoul. People will know there's a Ghoul sending water to chests, but they will have no means to track down who is it;
  • Don't stay outside camping and avoid getting yourself camping for BPs;
  • Check the person's home to see if they are Hooked or Infected and avoid eating them.

Easy, fast to do things that cover pretty well your Ghoulishness, and leave people wondering who you are.

And I think I mentioned it, but I can't see my own post while writing this one - even the best Ghoul who sneaks in and out can't turn invisible outside. Ultimately, I'd say the best way to catch one if you know there is one is to travel in pairs, or threes.

In pairs - if one person is eaten, the other is probably the ghoul, or the person who isn't killed will see it in the log when they log back in.

In threes - if one person is eaten because the ghoul is in the group, the other non-ghoul will see the message in the log.

This only falls apart if it's the Hero leading the group - even then, if someone is eaten and the other two move off hurriedly, it's probably the Hero leading.
