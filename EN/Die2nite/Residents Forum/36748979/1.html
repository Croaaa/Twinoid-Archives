Zombies aren't alive in the same sense as a human. They are basically a virus seizing control of a human body. Most organs die and start rotting, leaving only those that are required for the zombie's survival (digestive system, brain, etc). The goal of the zombie is spreading the virus to other potential hosts. They would eventually "die" of starvation as their exposed muscle and bone tissue deteriorates to the point they can't keep moving and feeding anymore. You can accelerate the process by destroying their brain or causing catastrophic damage to their nerve system.

Yep, that's my vision of a zombie. Magic zombies or nanobot zombies are a bit silly for my taste.
