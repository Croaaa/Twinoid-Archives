Why great pit sucks

-by fward(dwarf?)

Great Pit is a n00b build
why n00bs build it:
1) costs only planks and there are usually several in the bank
20 they can vote on it for more defense

Why great pit is for non-n00bs:
1) materials are every where outside, if we are short go out and get some scraps and logs and convert them.
2) 80 AP for 10 defense and uses up 8 planks, this has two drawbacks:
a- ) it is really expensive to repair for very little return (HC), so you are screwing us in the future..
-b) it is so inefficient on investment return, that building a hovel (no repair no resource) is the better choice for AP use.
3) "but you can vote on it...) - the big lie. in a real town, great pit is a vote of last resort. even if we are short 13 def you'd should rather build temp def than waste a vote on such dismal def, with the importance of watchtower 4-5 and the constant need for pump .. its paramount not to waste votes on the sham of a pit.

so you can build great pit and waste AP both current and future, burn up lots of planks, build something that is worse and get shunned for it... OR you can do something worthwhile with your APs.
