That depends on many factors. Is it a organized or a random town? How many skilled and trusty players you got in there? Are they heroes, what profession?

If you're in random towns there will always be residents camping nearby buildings but you still need rare BPs to reach farther days, so picking survivalist with at least 1 or 2 friends helps to explore further and hopefully camp a couple buildings and walk the map for chests.

As for LMS, I'm no expert but there are a few tricks to obtain it. Others can help you.

And zen camper, well. When town can't defend anymore you go camp or you can die on attack. That's all about luck and knowing how to camp effectively. Some wiki reading will help for sure.
