spacekadt

BiA itself does go away before the attack, but your camp chances are locked in at the time that you hit the camp button.

We've used that many times and you will retain your optimal status even though BiA wears off.

Guardian *will not* give the bonus at attack time since many defense never locks. Upgrades, gates, watchmen, deaths... they all get constantly updated as they happen and affect the town defense. BiA wearing off would be one of those things that happens before attack so it wouldn't even see the bonus.
