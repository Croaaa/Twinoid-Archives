MV doesn't get data from DTD... it gets it from Die2nite when people use the plugins, hit the "update zone" button on the site or through manual updates (just like DTD). A question mark in MV means the status of the zone (depleted or not) is unknown, which usually means the zone has never been updated. Though this can also be set manually, it's an odd thing to do. All the current plugins update both DTD and MV by default, so yea, if they're sitting on a question mark in MV it's very likely they are not updating DTD either. Bear in mind that a minority manually update only DTD, or have disabled MV in their plugin settings, but probably not enough to take into consideration.
