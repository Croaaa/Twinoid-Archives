Jedermann

It's not really Motion Twins lack of communication, it's my lack of communication. I wanted to write something about it in the forums but didn't get around to do so^^

I'm the author of both the german and english versions of the Igloo. I used the app to plan campings for Mixtown, the town that is current rank one (will soon be rank 2 because 6fo/rfm has beaten us by 10 points or so), and I'm pretty confident the results fit the real values quite good. Being off by up to 10 percent might happen from time to time though, especially for more rare camping conditions.

I also apologize for the app not being fully translated (the help section is not translated yet).

The main purpose of the igloo is a) Providing a camping calculator for people less familiar with the calculator spreadsheets and the camping mechanics and b) Providing a way to quickly and easily check and compare camping chances for different people/camping sites/etc.
