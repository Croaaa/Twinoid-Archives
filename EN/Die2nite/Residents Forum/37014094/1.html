spacekadt

Prior to 2007 (when the 2005 revision kicked in), we used to change our clocks on the first Sunday in April and the last Sunday in October. Now it's the second Sunday in March and the first Sunday in November.

European Summer Time begins on the last Sunday in March and ends on the last Sunday in October. It looks like this has been the case since 1996.

I'm 100% sure you're thinking of the one week difference in autumn since the time difference has always been us before them in spring for as long as d2n has been around.
