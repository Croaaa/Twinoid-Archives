1) Originally, the ability to post in the world forums was solely a paid perk. This forum was made to appease all the people who had an on-site outlet to talk about the game (General Twinoid), since it was ridiculous that standard-grade residents had to talk about D2N in a non-D2N forum

Before the Twinoid merge, there was an unofficial forum that anyone could post in, but it was easier to ignore(?)

The forums in the French and Spanish versions of the game are mostly open to all players (with a token forum that's heroes only). Our version and the German version didn't follow the pattern due to the decision of a past admin. Thankfully, it seems that the current guy will be willing to adjust permissions and have a forum setup that's more welcoming to new players. No promises on when that might happen though

2) Since the forums run on a unified system, some pieces of text cannot be customized for a specific game. While it would be nice to have something that says that the forum is heroes-only, the devs generally don't add functionality unless they feel that it would be beneficial for most players or themselves. We're a minority around here, so it's unlikely that something will be done for the benefit of non-French players
