spacekadt

Their points won't matter... just the person doing the jumping (in this case, that's you).

Random takes you into a random town that's appropriate for your SP level. So if you have 100 SP or more, that's always going to be a distant town. If you have less than 100 SP, that's always going to be a small town.

Hardcore is the only way to "choose" what town you end up in since there's only ever one HC town on the jump list at any given time and it can be joined by any player, regardless of SP.
