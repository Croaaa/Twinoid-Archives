Hey all. I'm a relatively unknown guy around here, but reading through SoltBegin's diary, I want to organize a contest about writing D2N related short stories for residents (and Heroes! ). The idea stems from Azmael's writing contest.

This thread isn't starting the actual contest, but I'd just like to see how many would be actually interested in participating, to see if it would generate enough entries regularly.

The basics that I've thought of so far, if it goes through, are (adopting a few ideas from the original contest):
It will run every two weeks (bi-weekly).
There will be a non-compulsory theme every 2 weeks
External free-hosted forums showcasing all entries (library), and provide space to discuss Write2Nite new themes and ideas.

Original ideas?:
This is either no reward or sponsored.
If it's no reward, it's just for fun.
If it's sponsored, I'll try to pull in Hero sponsors. Winners get Hero days depending on the sponsoring Hero(es).
Prizes! Other than possible Hero days, I can provide custom .png avatars for the winner's use.
Judging will be more objective.
One to three judges per round. Me as the organizer, and 2 free seats for judging (guest judges!). If we have a sponsor, they will occupy one seat if they want.

There can be other mechanics, but for now I think those are enough groundwork laid down.

Is there sufficient interest? Interest in submitting entires, in being judge, in sponsoring, or helping out in any way?

Write2Nite is a working name.
