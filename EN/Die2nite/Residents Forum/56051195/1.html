A common tactic used in die2nite is the one when citizens (specially builders) go out after the attack to a zone adjacent to town and spend all day searching for logs and scrap metal.


Why is this useful?

Every day that passes on die2nite the number of zombies attacking the town increase, so in the last days this number can become almost impossible to defend with the defence of buildings and watchmen. So, to cover the attack, we need to dump scrap that gives the town temporary defence, boosting our chances of survival.


What do you mean with temporary defence?

Temporary defence is a term used to describe a type of defence who only last for a day. For example, if the town have +250 temp. def. that means the town have +250 only for that day (besides all the permanent defence that the constructed building gives).


Which buildings needs the town to dump scraps?

The most crucial building to do this are:
- Garbage Dump. Allows items to be dumped for temp. def. and unlocks all the buildings described below.
- Wood Dump. Logs dumped gives +1 temp. def. on top of +1 given by Garbage Dump.
- Metal Dump. Scrap metal dumped gives +1 temp. def. on top of +1 given by Garbage Dump.
- Free Dump. If you dont have this building, every scrap or log that you dump is gonna cost you 1 AP each, but with Free Dump you can dump all that you want for no AP cost.


What do I need to do to collect scraps?

Collecting scrap is easy and dont requires a lot of work. You will only need 2 AP (or just 1 AP if your town has Watchtower level 4 or 5). After attack you need to go outside the town 1 AP away and click the search option and then activate the escort mode (with the option checked that says "Allow the objects in my rucksack to be viewed and used"). This way you can spend all day searching for scraps and build at the end of the day. Remember, more scraps means more defence and more defence means more days holding the zombies away.


Is anythig more that we can dump for defence?

Yeah, depending on how BPs your town got, you can construct other buildings to drop things and booster the town's defences. Those are:
- Dump Upgrade. Gives +1 extra temp. def. for every item dumped (for example, a log with wood dump and dump upgrade gives +3 temp. def.).
- Weapons Dump. Gives +5 (on top of the +1 for Garbage Dump) temp. defence. for every weapon dumped. Extremely usefull in combination with Faucet, since you can fill Plastic Bags with free water to create Water Bombs for dropping.
- Defence Dump. Gives +2 temp. def. for every DO dumped (on top of the +4 for Garbage Dump). A lot of defence but do it with care, DO's are precious things for a town.
- Food Dump. Gives +3 temp def. for every food item dumped (on top the +1 for Garbage Dump).
- Animal Dump. Gives +6 temp. def. for every animal dumped (on top the +1 for Garbage Dump).


How do I dump objects for defence?

When you build the Garbage Dump you will see a Dump icon when the workshop, proyects and so on are. If you click there you will access the dump, where you can dump things for temp. defence. We careful with this and plan it before hand with your town. Worse thing that you can do is not asking what and how much dump and wasting resources that can be used the next day.
