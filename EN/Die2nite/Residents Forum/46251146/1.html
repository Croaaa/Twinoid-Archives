Before players choose their class when they join the map, they will be able to choose which one of the towns they would like to be their "home" town (from map viewer or something); each town on the map acts entirely like it's own independent town once it is selected as a players home town, including forums, a player can only use the bank, well, construction site, ect in their own town (maybe there could be a construction that permits access for the players from 1 other town) but players can select to use another towns forums from an option that's present when you enter another town, perhaps posts in your home town's forum that are written by out of town players can show up in a different colour and out of town people can be shunned from your home town's forums by 8 people leaving complaints via an added button under user's comments in the forums. Only 40 people can ever stay in a town over an attack but maybe citizens from other towns can get voted in to a town, needing 10 votes (again maybe along side the added leave a complaint button in the forums or something) to effectively swap home towns (a swapped player's actions in a new town would maybe have to be nerfed a little for balance) as long as their is a "dead citizen's bed" they can take, also there would probably have to be a cap on how many citizens from other towns you can take into your town.
