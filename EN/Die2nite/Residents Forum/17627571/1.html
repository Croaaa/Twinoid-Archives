Colette

Let me see if I got that. Basically you're suggesting that all active towns should be interconnected via new maps called the Great Wastes, right? Hmm, no . First, it would require a complete revamp and rebalance of the game mechanics. It shouldn't be too hard to cross so it is technically impossible, or too easy that you can just cross and get into a new town. Second, towns are supposed to be isolated, and no outside interference should ever affect them. It creates the "we are the last survivors of humanity" feel, and if new citizens suddenly move in, this feel would be destroyed. And third, it has the potential to create strange paradoxes and screw up the gameplay. Imagine that I die in one town, reincarnate in another and join an expedition to these Great Wastes, eventually reaching my first town. There I find my own corpse and the universe collapses on itself .

Long story short, it would be too complex to implement something like that without screwing up the game mechanics and its feel, so I'd discard the idea.
