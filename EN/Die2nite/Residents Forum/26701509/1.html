MadDonut

Using MSPaint, I simply zoom in with the magnifying tool until the pencil tool on its smallest size only draws one pixel per click. From there, I slowly draw everything out. The biggest thing about pixel art, in my opinion, is figuring out the best shades of colors to be able to define a drawing in such a limited amount of space.
