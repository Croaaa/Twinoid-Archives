No.

They stopped developing Mush in 2015 when Blackmagic (the developer which came up with idea for Mush and wrote most code for the game) left Motion Twin.

Typix replaced him in Mush area but all he's supposed to do is make sure the game is running and fix eventual game-breaking bugs (none of those happened since 2015).

We get season changes but no new content - all the options of seasons appeared earlier anyway but at least we got option to vote. I may be wrong here but I wouldn't be surprised if Mush mods actually went with initiative and suggested this to MT.

Here's an update from them regarding changes in MT and some of devs (including Blackmagic) and graphical artists leaving. From that moment development in Mush was officially halted. I believe that's when they decided browser games are sinking ship and decided to focus mainly on Dead Cells.

On a side note - I think they made right decision, business wise. Browser games reached peak popularity probably some time ago, in a different era of online gaming.
