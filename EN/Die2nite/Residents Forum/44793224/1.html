Pocky

Soradobi & I are a little unusual... we had a D2N-influenced wedding reception and included the entire community by having art contests (with hero days as rewards) to come up with ideas for our table numbers, table names, etc.

Since we met through D2N, living on opposite sides of the USA, we wants to include the game somehow. There is even a RP text out there that is actual text taken from our wedding program, and D2N (as well as the contest winners) were mentioned in the wedding program as well. We sent pictures of everything to Motion Twin afterward too.

So every once in a while, someone will find the RP texts about us, or end up in a Pocky & Soradobi-named town. It always makes me happy because, even though I haven't had time to play lately, this game will always have a place in my heart.

It'll be our second wedding anniversary on April 5th... I can't believe it's been that long!
