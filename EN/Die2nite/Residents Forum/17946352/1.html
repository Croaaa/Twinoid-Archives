Now, don't write this off as another silly suggestion of something huge to add to die2nite. This is a simple, short, and important suggestion. Take it seriously.
You know what Die2Nite needs more of?
Death.
No, no, don't go clicking away now.
No, I don't plan on begging for super-spam zombies and whatnot. Or more poisons or so. Quite simply, Die2Nite has a lot of ways to die, but oddly enough, there isn't much for it. You die, and they dump your corpse into the well and whine for a bit. That's it. We need more death related items.
Like a will.
Quite simply, a will is just a peice of paper entrusting your belongings to someone in town, and can be written at any time, provided you have paper, and optionally a pen. Remember, you can just nick your finger and write with your blood, that's a pretty apocolypse- like thing to do. Also creepy.
So, say there's a ton of zombies bearing down, and you don't have the necassary stuff to survive. You could write a will entrusting all of your items, and by this i mean the stuff in your chest and with you, to someone else, who you hope will survive or something. Or even in the wastes, write a will if you can't get back to town and are gonna die. Instead of your items being pillaged when you die, they are entrusted to the player whom you have written your will too, and they are deposited in his chest. If he doesn't have enough room for them, they go to the bank. This ensures stuff can't be stolen by griefers.

I know this could be abused by griefers, but it narrows down the possibilty of it. After all, there is usually only one or two griefers in a town, and they hardly work in conjecture. So, no one would ever entrust stuff to them. If they took stuff from the bank and used the wills to make sure it got passed around to the wrong people...
Well then, that's your job to prevent that. See, shunning makes sure that they can't take stuff from the bank, so they can't steal the vodka and whatnot.
And even if they did, if there were so many griefers so that would happen, the town was dying anyway.

As is in all TV shows before Hollywood was brutually slaughtered after a ten- day war, there will be fights over wills. Expect people to get angry if they aren't included in a will, and for those people to break in and enter the house where the will is kept.
There, they can:
Burn the will (Needs box of Matches or Torch) This ensures the will is no longer in effect and it's a free for all pillage when the person dies. 50% chance of being seen with torch, 25% with matches.
Edit the will (Needs pen) There is a 50% chance of being seen while doing this. Editing the will changes it to be entrusted to whomever you want. It can be changed back by the owner, at least until he inevitably ends up as zombie food.
Poison the will (Needs poison or Toxin) Does what the name implies, touching, sneezing on, or wiping with the will results in death or infection. 25% chance of being seen.

Remember, people can and will kill with wills, perhaps by giving you posioned food or water entrusted in the will.

Every will with you in it will give you a distinction. Come up with the name for it.

Needed requirements: Any paper item such as an RP text, a letter, or just general paper ( General paper is a New item found in regular zones)

Enjoy!
