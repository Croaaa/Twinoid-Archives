Anyway, back to the thread: there isn't a better class, both specialize at different aspects of the game that can be of extreme benefit if you put the right class at the right situation, but that can't be compared between them.

  • Scouts
    Great for towns that you'll be out tracking for far away buildings, camping them without the hassle of needing to cut the zeds numbers like a Surv would need (although it isn't that much worth it due the 10% death chances).
    Are also excellent choices for gathering Souls in endgame as they'd need to use a Flash Grenade/Camera/Breakthrough only in the zone of the Soul, without needing to open their way there.
    Also terrific for whoring distinctions such as Expert Explorer in random towns.Not even mentioning they're also modestly great to find Arch Chests.
  • Guardians
    Great for towns that you don't want to go much outside or can log on only pretty late, perfect for last minute rescues and building.
    The WM bonus is excellent if you're aiming at surviving longer for SPs, the sake of town or if you're in a meta aiming to go up in the Leaderboards.
    Aside these, unfortunately, they're not much of use after WT3 in half-decent towns, as good players generally avoid getting stuck when going outside to AS, and group expos generally take lots of people with them, enough to hold over 10 zeds easily.
    Even though, this doesn't hold itself in random towns, where being a Guardian can be a big difference in expos and you have less worries about zeds.Not as less as Scouts but still.But, considering those points and assuming I'm forced to chose one, I'll go with the Scout, both because the very premises of the class are attractive to me and because I saw the entire aversion that Guards suffered till S6, and I haven't managed to wash that aversion completely yet.

Shamans are the better of the two classes. Although Shamans have to convert Souls to use their ability, when they do get souls to convert, they can do a lot of good for the town. Most notably is their ability to saved trapped citizens from afar.

Unlike other heroes, with their ability, Shamans can destroy a group of zombies without any risk of being caught by a hoard themselves through releasing a soul. Since it only costs 1 AP per initial soul conversion (meaning the souls can just be saved for later when its needed), a Shaman can save multiple people per day as long as they have enough souls in town.

Even when there aren't people to save, Shamans are still a blessing for the town, almost literally. When they do their rain dance of zombie death, instead of attacking zombies, they can choose to add precious liquid into the town's well in order to keep its inhabitants from all dehydrating. All in all, Shamans have a very powerful ability.
