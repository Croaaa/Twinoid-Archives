Grief is doing stuff with other players without self benefit, as stated in the first post of that thread

Griefing is a series of deliberate actions taken solely to harm the town with no benefit to oneself.

Which includes:
-punching and hiding at the gates for more punching later
-operating gates in harmful ways
-hijacking important objects and dumping them in the WB

Remembering that those will be done for no benefit for the perpetrator, just to ruin towns and create grief.

So if the person punches someone and hides at the gate hoping to increase LMS later in the game, it is selfish play and valid. Opening/Closing gates on people to increase LMS or other selfish purpose is valid. Sequestering objects to create a private hoard for later use in the town is selfish play and valid.

Unlabeled drugs are a item so they are meant to be used. Reckless use should not be framed into griefing. One can use unlabeled hoping to get AP to do grief things, but we cant frame the item just because of that.... If that's the case we would hve to frame all boosters, and this is dumb

It is very common that U.D. be used for Distinction Whore, as if you get AP from it you earn a Drug Test distinction on your soul. It can also lead for Terrorized status which can be healed with means, not to mention Withdrawn deaths (ye people hunt for those too).
