Requirements
HUNT for those on HUNTED list
Complete Tasks on GHOST SHEETS™

To compete in the Scavenger Hunt you must sign up on this particular season’s Scavenger Hunter Thread. You will also be required to fill out and complete tasks on the GHOST SHEETS™ and post it on this Game Thread at end of play. You will be given 5 specific players to Hunt down, from the 20 announced on the SCAVENGER HUNTED Thread, once you sign up here. Play begins at the beginning of Season 9 March 12 and ends June 30 or end of season (whichever is first).

The main point is to hunt down other players. Completing other tasks also brings you points. You collect players by being in town with them. HUNTERS must survive four (4) days for anything in a town to count. HUNTED do not have to survive; a Hunter cannot be penalized if a Hunted dies early. Remember: HUNTING PEOPLE GETS YOU THE MOST POINTS!

A few rules for the Hunters:
You may send a Twinoid PM to the Hunted to see if they will jump into a town with you. They are not required to respond or facilitate your request. Repeatedly asking the same Hunted to jump into a town will be considered harassment and could result in disqualification for Conduct Unbecoming a Supreme Hunter.
You may ask someone to make a video for your town, or you may make your own video. The video must be of decent quality and must promote d2n in some fashion to count for points. Videos of Ruin Runs do not count as videos for this contest. Begging for videos will not be tolerated and could result in disqualification for Conduct Unbecoming a Supreme Hunter.
Only distinctions awarded between March 12 and June 30 will count. A town must appear on your Soul Page for the current Season to count for the Hunt. Nothing earned in any town still active when Soul Page screenshots are taken after the Hunt ends will count, except videos and screenshots that have already been posted on the World Forums.
Please make a note after your town indicating what Hero profession you chose. This note will be visible on your Soul Page at the end of the Hunt and will serve as verification.
Acceptable words for town names (this list may not be all-inclusive):
DIE - Die, dying, dead, deadly, death, deathly, undead
2 - Two, to, too, twin
NITE - Night, nightly, midnight, overnight, knight, knights
BLOOD - Blood, bloody, bloodying, bloodless
SAND - Sand, sandy, sands, sandlot, sandless
LOST - Lost, lose, losing, loser, losers
Unacceptable words for town names (this list may not be all-inclusive):
Bleed, bleeds, bleeding, bled, Bloods & Crips, Twinoid, MotionTwin, Epoq
Ask jkudlick or Sashimi if there are any questions about whether a word should count.

A few rules for the Hunted:
You must be as active as possible. We understand taking a few days off because you’re out of town or just need a break, but please notify jkudlick or Sashimi if you cannot continue as a Hunted. If you do not believe you can be reasonably active throughout the Season (i.e. in a town at least 85-90 days during the next 110+ days), please step aside and lets someone else be a Hunted.
You are not required to assist any Hunters in their quest, but as stated above, you must remain reasonably active.

TIE-BREAK PROCEDURE
In the event of a tie for the win, the following tie-breakers will be used to determine a winner:
The player who hunted the most players.
The player who submitted their GHOST SHEETS™ first.

You are a Supreme Hunter, Alpha Class Predator, Top of the Food Chain, Resident with a BRD and this list, etc.
You have 110+ days to complete Tasks within die2nite and come out as the Top Predator.
You get 30 Hero Days for this, bragging rights, adulation of your peers, sand balls to the head!!

Season 9 Scavenger Hunt Ends when the zeds attack on June 30 or the beginning of Season 10, whichever is first.
