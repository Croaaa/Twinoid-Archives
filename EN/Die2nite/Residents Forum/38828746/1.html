NightmareShirlThe upgrades are permanent, but the tomb's medium bonus is only for that night. The building's topology degrades every time it is camped however. Someone correct me XD

The topology of the building doesn't decrease per se, it's just that a particular camper's odds decrease every night they camp, regardless of where.

For clarification, the so-called permanent upgrades aren't so permanent. They degrade at a rate of 3 upgrades per night until they're back to 0 again. This happens even if the building isn't camped.

Let's make it clearer.

The topology of a building can be improved through campsite upgrades. Every camper can benefit from them. And each night 3 upgrades (if any) will be taken away. It will never fall below the default topology.

For example if the default topology of a building is +7
You upgraded the site 5 times. The topology will become +12
After the first night, it will be reduced to +9. (-3)
After the second night, it will be reduced to +7. (-2, didn't fall below the default)

Tombing won't affect the topology of a building. The bonus only applies to you personally. It lasts for one night only. It will be gone tomorrow morning.

One upgrade will improve your survival chance by 5%.
Tombing, 8%.
Just hiding, 0%.

If you are NOT a survivalist, you will be capped at 90% (decent). Otherwise you can reach 100% (optimal).
