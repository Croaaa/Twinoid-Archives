My idea:

Sponsored towns

Have the ability for any town that still has 40 citizens to be sponsored, giving everyone hero mode until the town's death

How:

- Price sponsorship in hero days; (100?)
Allow heroes with at least 15 days spare to pledge up to 10 days each via an option in their house.
If the funding goal isn't met before the first citizen dies; hero days are returned.


Why:

- To further monetise your current paying userbase!
Camera owners will spend hero days reklessly!
Users will spend more on one-off purchases with the expectation of using hero days this way!

- To give non-paying users enough exposure to hero mode for a sense of loss when left without it.
A hero nag screen when users die from a sponsored town will have great success, especially if a discount is applied if x amount of residents sign up within 24hrs of eachother

- To make d2n more enjoyable
By making it it easier for partial metas and random towns to have the extended gameplay experience of a full meta/full hero town.

- To make promo towns easier
Once you have a fixed cost for a group deal, you can look at outside sponsorship!
You can look at promo towns!
you can look at coalitions recruiting new players or new heroes to fund a town

but most importantly, we might get enough people for another clash of titans!

####

Yes it is pay2win, but only insofar as hero days already are!

The fact that there's no clear path from noob to meta hero is killing the game! if the downward trend isn't fixed, we'll all have to learn french.
