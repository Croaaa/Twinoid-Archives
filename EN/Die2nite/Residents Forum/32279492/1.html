Sense

TEAM ONE RACE COURSE CHRONICLE DAY ONE

Before the start, we planned to go Southwest and proceed counterclockwise, being able to heroic return immediately after reaching the fourth corner. The map is 25x25 so we got a lot of walkin' to do! Our competition was mainly survivalist class, but we noticed two scouts. Very interesting...

Day 1

We waited about 3 hours to leave town. We were happy to see more people going Southeast than our direction. We were able to visit two teams on our way to the corner. We traded our Paracetoid for isaaclw's matches before he could protest. We figured matches can make a weapon with a rotting log which is probably worth two kills. Not bad. We continued to find another team. Unfortunately, WalkinCrow and PinkNocturne didn't have much for us to steal, if I recall correctly.


Base 6 + Food 6 + Steroid 6 + Water 6
Base 6 + Food 7 + Second Wind 6 + Water 6

We reached the Southeast corner without using any weapons and had five ap leftover.

We camped immediately to discourage people from joining us. If they did want to camp with us, they'd have to put in a lot of upgrades to make it worth it since we didn't spend any AP upgrading before camping. Joanne and zoned visited. We feared they would camp on us, but they must not have liked their odds because they went north to hang out with Ghost and ImbuedGreen. Once they left we did our upgrades and camped for real at "Good" or ~95%

We were shocked to see some people had gone Northwest, to one of the farthest corners. If I recall correctly, ace123456 and drakh camped 26AP away, putting them 4AP ahead of us already on day one. Shit! Well, maybe they'll die overnight
