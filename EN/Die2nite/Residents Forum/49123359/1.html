I sort of remember seeing some times when Scout Sense was 0, but there were zombies. Can someone else verify that the zombie count is ALWAYS +/- 2 regardless of sense?

scouts always have margin of error between [-2, -1, 0 , +1 . +2]

and here are the exceptions:

  • when scouts sense something which is at least 1 zed or more then it is guaranteed you will find at least 1 zed there , so sensing actual number means that there must be zeds there and the margin of error is only [0 , +1 . +2]
  • when scouts sense nothing as in no number at all: which means ZERO, then there is always a chance there could be still 1 or 2 zeds there , so sensing zero zeds means you can have either 0, 1 or 2. so the interval is [0 , +1 . +2]
  • when scouts sense 3 or more zeds then you have the 5 possibilities of finding: either, 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5 zeds , hint this interval [-2, -1, 0 , +1 . +2]

all tested with few screen shots i have to find later but take my word for it since you see it all when making zoos.
