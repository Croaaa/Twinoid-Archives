The problem is the exponential growth of zombies as the days pass. After a certain day, the town grows weaker and weaker as all defenses are built, less zones are available to scavenge, more citizens die and new blueprints run out, yet the zombie attacks keep increasing exponentially. Some may enjoy the feeling of powerlessness it gives, but I believe towns should be given a chance to defend themselves. For example, by Day 8 the average attack (according to Oval Office) is 664. Three days later it's 1164. Only one day later the average is 1428. There's no way a town can build so many defenses to keep up with the increasing attacks while still dealing with the attrition problems and lack of new projects. I, for one, would like to see the growth of zombies tweaked. Perhaps a diminished growth of zombies before day 15?
