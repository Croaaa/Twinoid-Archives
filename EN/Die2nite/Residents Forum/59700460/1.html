This Zombie Zoo is North-East:
https://postimg.org/image/b76baoohl/

This Zombie Zoo is South-East:

The rest of the map has no zombie whatsoever.

You need lots of organisation, lots of scavenging and speed.

Basically, the zoo must be made day 2-3-4-5-6
for the most part, then it should be small clean up tasks
and trimming due to exponential growth.

So, if a town decides to have a zoo late in the game
and start killing spread day 5+, it's game over.
Too late in the game to start killing and making a zoo.

Day 2 is mostly build pump and waste
as much water using WP3 and EWB,
then hurling sticks, chains, knives and similar until
you get a mower, mk2, dev, chainsaw or similar weapons.

Camp sites are cleared with kitten / bandage, EWB
or good old unbreakable Maytag items (washer, fridge, oven).
