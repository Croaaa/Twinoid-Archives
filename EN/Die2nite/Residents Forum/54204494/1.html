Zhejie

In the view of a Meta Town (just cause its easier )

1. Small Cemetary. The +10 Def/Citizen is not really worth it, especially if you're looking to get onto the leaderboards. By the time it provides lots of defense, there aren't really enough citizens for it to be worth it or the attacks will be massive compared to the defense it gives.

2. The most useful uncommon BP is probably Grabeboom. With it, you get a guarunteed 15 zombies killed per day (minimum) and a maximum of 45. From one BP. On top of that, you need to build Vegetable Plot so you get Intestine Melons as well. The most useless uncommon BP is probably the Ventilation System. Meta Towns probably aren't even going to build the Portal Lock, and it is pretty much negated after day 4 due to the teleport option from voting the watchtower up.

3. The best rare BP in my opinion is probably the Faucet. There are a lot of good rare BP's, but I think that the faucet is the most effective. A lot of towns tend to run into water problems (especially Hardcore) and this will cut down on the water usage (assuming they havn't had any other luck with buildings such as Eden Project/Divining Rockets). Later in the game, many towns rely on weapons such as MK2 and Aqua Splash/EWBs. The amount of water rations saved using Faucet can reach an insane amount, depending on how early this BP is found + built. The most useless rare BP is probably the Infirmary. Most Meta town's won't be getting people injured, and if they do, then they will have 2-3 bandages stored up in the bank. It is rare to run out of oxygen while running a ruin, and 99.99% of the time you won't ever have to run from zombies. In addition, the AP costs are massive, so you will spend all your AP just healing yourself. And in the event that someone gets and infection, pretty much all towns will have lots of paracetoids.

4. The (coolest) VR BP is probably builders merchant. The amount of times it is used to great effect is close to 0, since by the time everyone would be building palaces all the citizens would probably be camping out and about.
The most useful VRBP would probably be No Holes Barred. 150 rations of water is a lot. Even a town with all 40 citizens still alive would take 4 days to go through all of the water. It's a nice BP to have, and can help shift the focus of a town from water problems to keeping zombie numbers down.
I would have to say the most useless VR BP's would have to be all of the wonders. They serve no purpose (in my opinion) other than the distiction they give. There is no actual benefit from the wonders to the town (no defense, no nothing).

5. I would say that if there is a JT found day 1, then it should definitely be used to build the factory. In a Meta Town, the first 5 days are devoted to voting the watch tower anyways, so there wouldn't be any benefit to building Arch Study that early. In addition, hopefully (since you were lucky enough to get JT) you were also lucky enough to get the really good common BP's. Such as Searchtower and Veggie Plot.

6. The best item from an upgraded seeker would probably be BED. You can't really get anything bad from this item, and you also have the possibility of grabbing some really good items (N&B, EC, Compact Detonator).

7. The best Hero Skill is Hero Return. It's the most useful one, especially for me. If I ever become a hero, its through a BiA. And that BiA is most often for a Scout Mission. That extra 11AP that Hero Return saves you lets you scout even further away and even more zones. The amount of buildings found on a scout run without Hero Return could well go from 2-4 to 0.

8. The most useless Hero Perk is probably Mayor. The only thing that this grants you is the ability to create private towns. These towns aren't even able to be ranked. If there was to be an event (such as the apocalympics) where private towns are needed, then it is possible for the admins to create them.

9. I think that survivalists are the best hero class. They are the reason that towns are able to get so many rare BPs, and they also help a ton in the making of a successful zoo.
The most useless hero class, in my opinion, would have to be scavenger. All other classes are very important in the ongoing of a town, while a scavenger just strolls out to the desert and depletes zones faster than residents
