Hannelor

There are two things really, so let's start from the start. Bear with me.

I'm an LMS player. I like to think I'm a good one.
Most of my towns involve me plotting against, poisoning and outright murdering my town.

Every now and then, when there are only a few left, I realize that I don't want to kill them. Those few people who are enjoyable enough to be worth more alive than dead. It's comforting to know there are so many good people out there.

So I put the knives away, curl up on a stolen mattress and rest.
For my work there is done. Injustice has been served. And all is right with the world.
Another cruel world awaits tomorrow. And I'm going happy.

The second is a little less morbid.
Finding meaty bones. Each one has a story to tell.
Some are found in wastelands. Barren of items and completely depleted.
Was it bad luck? Poor mapwork? Both?
Perhaps they stayed outside to mitigate the dishonour of returning empty-handed.

Other graves are filled with glorious things to steal. So take what you want!
They aren't going to need it anymore. Not where they're going...

Sometimes you can even work out who it was...

Then another great moment occurs after reading through there posts.
The final decision of how delicious you think they would taste.

It's surprisingly enjoyable.
