Game objective, story:
Bad saran has escaped sota's police forces, and now tries to hide in a forest!
You have 10 days to find him and... catch him!
Meatlocker in town will be waiting for him... if it'll be built!
SotA offers big reward for killing him! Do it now and be the one to gain magical staff of the crow.

h - haunted forest,

a - almighty town square,
n - nice trap

0-9 - citizen's identificators. Other characters available as well, if not used ingame already. For example 'p'.


If location symbol is white (for example 'c' instead of 'c' ) that means citizens have vision on that location.

If citizens have vision, they know when something/someone has passed zone, or if anything stands in zone.

Everyone has 3 action points to do daily, and 2 move actions.
Citizens starts at random zone. They throw d4 dice to know where:
d4 1 = 0/0
d4 2 = 9/9
d4 3 = 2/5
d4 4 = 5/2

Moving into adjacent zones (not diagonal) costs 1 move point.

If zone is h citizens can't build anything, or see anything what happens here (eg. someone moves, hides).

To clear a zone citizen needs to use 1 ap.
You can write something like "*Cuts off forest at x/x*"
or "*Clears a zone at x/x*".
Zone becomes c then.

Escaper is caught if he is visible 2 turns in a row (on a white zone, and not in a forest).

After turn ends it's time for escaper to move. If he steps up on a trap or something built in previous turn, citizens will be notified. But they can't grab him here... because he isn't frozen yet! If after next turn and his next movement, he will be on a visible zone again, he is frozen (scared of you, people with battery guns) and safe to pickup. Like a PC Base Unit.

When escaper is caught, someone must escort him into town, and put in meatlocker. Citizen is able to grab escaper when is in the same zone. Next he needs to escort him.

Tower can be built in a town and c zone. Town's icon changes into t then.
Nice trap can be built in a h and c zone.

To be able to build anything in a zone, you must be in that zone.

Constructions List:
Nice Trap - 2 action points. (When something/someone passes through zone with a trap, citizens are noticed)

Seeking Tower - 5 action points. (Adds visibility into adjacent areas. Citizens are noticed, when someone passes through visible area)

Altar of Blóód - 5 action points. (Allows for converting three action points into two move points. Can be built only in town, but works outside, once built)

Meatlocker - 10 action points. (Can be built only in town. Allows for meatlocking citizens and GREAT ESCAPER SARAN, YOUR ULTIMATE ENEMY also serves good place for a rest. You can put escaper here to win the game... or another citizen. But that's a mystery)

You have to post/remember progress of building by yourselves.

New day starts after an attack.


Town location will be randomized at game start.
Same as saran's position.

You'll have to figure out how saran's movements works during game.


How to begin:
- Throw d4 to check where you spawn.
- Read instructions.
- Communicate with others.
- Profit.

I hope this will be fun
