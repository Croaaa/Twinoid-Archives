8 upgrades, 2 DOs, that's the correct order.

A DO has a defensive value of 1.8. You can't upgrade the site anymore after you have reached a total value of 10.

So 8 normal upgrades and 2 DOs provide a value of 11.6, which is the maximum.

9 upgrades + 1 DO -> 10.8

8 + DO + 1 -> 10.8
