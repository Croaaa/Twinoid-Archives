Santa zed is a special kind of zombie that delivers your Citizen welcome pack and Doggy bag when town starts, whether you believe in Him or not.

It is said He lives in a Sandcastle ruin located 1 zone north from the northernmost zone of your map.

/sarcasm

EE stands for Expert Expedition, which is the name of a rare distinction: <- This one.

To get it you need to successfully explore a ruin that is 18Km or more from town.

Each successful exploration (item found) equals 1x of those distinctions when you die.

Maps with relatively centered towns might get none of such ruins. What fdamned is whining in this thread is that, even in a cornered town, he couldn't find lots of the EE ruins he want so badly. Hence the "where is santa zed" carp.

The ovni building is what people call the Strange Circular Device ruin. It is a rare ruin that spawns further from the 18Km+ range, so it is always a EE kind of ruin.
