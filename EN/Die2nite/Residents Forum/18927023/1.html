Since this forum is now available to residents, I thought it would be a good idea to post a thread where people can discuss, and post issues about the plugin:

Download the latest Mozilla approved addon: https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/fire...

Occasionally I will release an update. These updates need to be approved by mozilla before they will automatically update to your client, or show up on this download page.

The current version is 0.4.5 and updates:
- Cartographer
- Map Viewer
- Dusk till Dawn

Note: Unfortunately development has effectively halted. However, I hate to see these things die so I will be maintaining.
