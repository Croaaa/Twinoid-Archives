Colette
ohnoesWhat do you do about people who keep dying because they don't drink water?

This one's tricky. There's no way of forcing other players to do anything, and all but the best towns have players that will eventually die of dehydration.

There are just two things that you can do to find less bad players. You can try looking for a better town (randomly if you don't have hero days, though). Newbie towns are usually filled with players that die early. Once you get 100 Soul Points you'll be able to jump to distant towns, which are usually slightly better, though they are far from perfect. If it really bothers you, your second and only choice is to join a good meta (a group of coalitions who jump and play together), since they're usually more organized, but unless you can find a meta with 39 other good players you might still see random people dehydrating in your town.
