This is an idea that has been around since S1 pretty much.

It works like this:

Each player gets assigned a character. Each character has their own motivations, ideas and behaviour and players must act according to their characters while still playing a normal game of D2N. This includes both actions and communication.
It would be pretty freeform, as in no stats-tracking or points for actions - this allows a wider cast of characters, including some pretty outlandish ones.

A few examples of characters:


The JunkieAlways on the lookout for the next high, the junkie has a serious habit and will try to get their hands on every drug possible. He prefers Valium and Twinoid, but won't say no to Steroids or even Paracetoid.
His urge is strong enough to drive him into the desert and into a pack of zombies if he doesn't get his fix.
If he has had his fix he's able to communicate and and cooperate pretty well, but if he's high up to the roof or in serious need of a fix don't expect complete sentences.

The Tough GuyHe constantly needs to prove his manhood - usually by bashing large numbers of zeds to a pulp, ideally with his bare fists. Bandages and antibiotics are for wimps, real men cauterize wounds with booze.
He has a dislike for wimps, scientists and anyone who can't punch zombies with their bare hands.

In addition to the characters the town will have a few "observers". Their role is to moderate if tempers run too high and to sum the day up for anyone interested.

This post is mostly to see if there's interest and to get organized.

What needs to be done:

*Basic rules - attacking other players ok?, bank access?, how seriously to take surviving....
*Moderator functions - when to intervene, how to handle OOC things...
*Practical aspects - what settings for a private town, how to handle selection....
*Writing characters, how to dostribute characters
*How and where to organize - IRC, the .net forums, the WF...
