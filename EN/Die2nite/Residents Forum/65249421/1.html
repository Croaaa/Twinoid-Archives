Eternal Twin (or MyHordes if you prefer) released an app specially adapted to keep playing on MT sites after Flash ends.

https://eternal-twin.net/docs/desktop

Concretely, it presents itself as a classic browser. It is not a plug-in to integrate.



Is this stuff reliable? Yes, it was developed by Eternal Twin based on Chromium and the application code is public:
https://gitlab.com/eternal-twin/etwin...

In addition, it only allows browsing on MT games sites. It cannot be used to run Flash files from unknown source (or you will have modified it for that, but that's your problem) .

It can run on Windows, Linux and Mac. Do not hesitate to report any bugs, and to share the link around you so that as many players as possible continue to play in the coming months.
