Fact #1: Around 90% long term heroes (not LMS/ghoul heroes) play in metas or in organized groups.

Fact #2: The only option to assemble a full town of players that want to play togheter easily (Private Towns) is not widely used because:
_______A) It doesn´t award all distinctions and Soul Points.
_______B) It doesn´t have a rank system in which it would estimulate competitive play.

Fact #3: With the lack of support and attratives this game is slowly losing players. The ones that remain find it difficult to play as a group as the towns fill slower than usual.

Conclusion #1: It´s the heroes and, therefore, the metas that support this game financially.

Conclusion #2: Playing in a meta that aims ranking is extremely hard as there´s nothing in this game to help them get in a town togheter.

Conclusion #3: Motion Twin doesn´t like meta play and will do nothing to assist metas.

So let´s help ourselves. We don´t need to rely on MT.

I propose something that will help all metas surpass the Jump delay problem. If you ever belonged to a meta you probably went through this situation:

Hypotetical situationIt´s the day of the jump and everything is ready! All coalitions are full, all jumpers are on the chat. All that´s left is to wait for that town to fill, a new one will appear, and you and your group will enter on it.

30 minutes pass and that town is not filling

more 30 minutes, little has changed...

Another 30 minutes... Some of the jumpers of your group can not be avaliable to be Jumpers anymore... Your group makes some coalition shenanigans so you can still try it today.

Another hour has passed and you still can´t jump. More jumpers have to leave and the meta leader decide to postpone the jump for tomorrow.

Several changes needs to be done for tomorrow jump as Bob and Lucy can´t be jumpers that day.

Tomorrow arrives but the filling town situation is worst than before. This time you postpone it early. However you still need to make preparations and changes for the next day.

The days pass and there´s still no jump...

When you finally enter a town there are some coalitions that missed it, the randoms you got are lame or griefers and you decide to reset the jump and try again.

You face more postponings...

In the end of the season you check your towns and see that only 1 or 2 were decent. All that time wasted waiting for the ideal conditions took away MONTHS from your game time.

So my proposal is that we, as the English server meta players, help Spanish, French and German metas to fill their towns. They will also do the same for us here.

Please check this group page for more details:

TOWN FILLERS GLOBAL ASSOCIATION GROUP
