So I was on the MMJ channel discussing the game with CETHEGUS_drp and the convo directed to the situation of Die Verdammten. If you don't know, DV is the german version of die2nite. There are 4 versions, by the way, Hordes (the original in french), Die2nite (us :> ), Zombinoia (Spanish version) and Die Verdammten (german).

If you think our towns are filling slowly, taking sometimes a full week to start, you surely have no idea how bad things are in DV in this aspect. Towns can stay opened for months if not YEARS, waiting 40 players to join it.

The situation there gets worse because, unlike us, they have 3 Distant Region maps opened at a time (we used to have this too).

At some point in our convo, Cethegus mentioned the absurd amount of items the extended Day 1 they have there generates. Surely you've been to a town here that took a few days to fill and enjoyed scavenging non-stop for days, getting lots of loot. But there, it is fairly common to find Day 1 towns with over 1000 rotting logs in the World Beyond.

Morgaine then asked how many items someone could find in a single zone, and that sparked a science flame that drove me to go test this out. Not only I was going to check how many empty searches i'd get, but also the ratio between logs and scraps that appear too.

So my test begun. I luckily joined the DR town with the fewest amount of citizens (Autorit채res St채dtchen, 8 citz. on the 10 of July 2019) and went to 0/3 to auto-scavenge.

I set up an alarm in my phone for 1 month thinking that this was a crazy idea and I'd prob. be dead long before that.

Well, a few days ago (August 10th) the alarm rang (i totally forgot about it), so I went there to see if I had a nice stash of items or a pretty german death page.

https://i.imgur.com/1ceZpDV.png

I was still alive. Zone was depleted. A sheep ton of scraps and logs was at my feet, and 13 citizens were on the town.

Well lets run down some numbers in a graphic then. So from the 371 searches (counting the depleted searches only) we have:

https://i.imgur.com/aIo3nGY.png

Other than that I noticed the items were evenly distributed along the searches. Some logs, then a scrap or two, some empty searches, etc... I had 8 missed searches in a row max

Here is a nice tip for you, to see the logs of a zone that had too many logs for a day, click on the day tab below the chat log so it will load all the messages the zone had for that day (someone must be in the zone recording this data, if someone leaves and another person joins it later there won't be any zone log).

I'll keep checking to see how big the list of items get. There are still 13 citizens in that town right now. If it only got 5 citizens in one month (me included), the town will be close to fill in about 5 months from now, I think.

no Dachshunds were harmed at this test... yet
