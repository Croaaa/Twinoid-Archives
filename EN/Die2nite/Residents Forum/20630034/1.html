TOShokking

I see this idea:

Water Car
This... thing, apparently based on a dependable bird of all things, lets people get around a bit easier. The hydraulics now occupy what would have been passenger space, but something's better than nothing, right?

Requires Garage building:
30AP, 2x Metal Support, 2x Bag of Cement, 1x Handful of nuts and bolts
Not much to look at, but it gives us space to put together one of the old essentials - transport.

Built in the Garage, costs:
1x Engine
4x Wrought Iron
1x Metal Support
3x Copper Pipe
1x Jerrycan Hose
4x Water Ration
3x Plastic Bag
1x Handful of nuts and bolts
2x Battery
[In a warning box: "You get the design ready. Wait, you realise that this will take <list of components> to build, don't you!" If you don't have the right parts, says "This design needs <missing parts> from the Bank, but we don't have enough."]

Benefits:

  • Gives you 2 extra Control Points.
  • Gives you 50% chance not to use an AP moving into a zone with no zombies.
  • Gives you 3 extra inventory slots (including the one it occupies).
  • Allows you to carry 3 Heavy items (including itself).
  • Gives you an extra 5 House Defense in town.

Negatives

  • Requires fuelling with a Full Jerrycan to use - gives you 50 movement units. It is used up by the following (Technicians use only half of this amount):
    • Each movement = -2 MU
    • For every 2 zombies in the zone you moved into = -1 MU
    • Moving in or out of a building = -2 MU
    • Every search or auto-search = -1 MU
  • Refuelling costs 2AP, to account for the time lapse.
  • You cannot move while 'holding' an unfuelled Water Car - dropping the Water Car also drops any other Heavy items in your inventory.

Other Info

  • If you camp or zombies get past the walls (whether the Watchmen stop them or not), there is a 5-10% chance of the Car being destroyed, placing one of the following in the owner's inventory: Full Jerrycan, Jerrycan Hose, Copper Pipe, Wrought Iron, Engine.
  • If the car is destroyed in town, it puts a listing in the Registry.
  • Ghouls cannot 'hold' cars on account of all the (slightly leaky) hydraulics.
  • If a camper is killed while holding the car, no item is dropped but instead a new option on the zone appears: "Dismantle Wrecked Car". Costs 1AP, places the following on the floor:
    • Engine (20% chance)
    • Copper Pipe (70% chance)
    • Wrought Iron (100% chance)
    • Full Jerrycan (20% chance)
    • Handful of nuts and bolts (30% chance)
    • Traveller's Corpse (100% chance)

Achievements

  • Vehicle Guru - Can they fix it? Yes they can! (Number of cars built)
  • Camping Can - It's like a sardine tin - it keeps the nasties on the outside. (Nights camped in a car successfully)
  • Tinned Survivor - Number of times your driving licence got revoked. Permanently. (Times killed in a car)
  • Grabbin' Fuel - Refuelling! (Times you've refuelled an empty car with a Full Jerrycan)

Just my (long list of an) idea.
