goob

Well there isn't much planning you *can* do when it's your first town, in terms of picking a town, assuming you don't know anyone else who plays the game. You can't pick a specific town unless you're a hero, and until you get 100 soul points (SP) you're stuck in the small towns unless you get someone with 100+ SP to pull you into their coa.

The larger distant towns are slightly chattier, but even in those there are a lot of people who don't want to communicate in the town forum.

I suggest going through the small towns and keeping an eye out for those who talk and are willing to work as a group. If they're also new to the game, send them a private message and ask if they want to form a coalition. It also helps if they have a sense of humor. The goal is to kill stiffs outside of town... not group with them.

Assuming you form/join a coa, y'all will likely die on different days. You can wait until the others die, or you can join another town without them to kill time until eveyrone's ready again.
