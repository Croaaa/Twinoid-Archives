ok this is a bit mixed and related to other scores.
first of all: i will talk about distant in a seperate case compared to HC.

first you need to know i intend to reduce the ranked distant town points from 60 to 50 points, and for HC ranked towns from 40 to 35 points. this is relevant so i had to mention it first

now i will start with distant towns:
if a person reaches a town that survived day 15 or day 20 or in rare cases day 25 , that does not automaticly mean that this town will rank in top 15 (also count that in S10 there is no UFC so 3 top metas will rank 3 top towns and make ranking harder), this also means trying to die early or go easy after reaching day 15 or 20 to go start a new town and do the same again to score more is a "losing and risky strategy" because that will risk all your towns to not get ranked in top 15 and with it you lose the scoring points from ranked towns which are significantely more than surviving more days per town without them ranking, so the higher days you reach the more points you gain in total as a bonus from "Ranked Towns"
this means bigger scores fro higher days will create a disadvantage to those who cant be part of top metas due to lack of spots, and with it give a huge boost of advantage to all who can get an invite to top meta towns (here i mean unnamed, 6FO, RFM and also Oozing Inn). this will be unfair for the rest who wont have spots left to join those 4 groups but still joins a meta trying to compete for top 4.

  • 5- so in my opinion and after providing this explaination i find your suggestion about increasing sore points for higher days survived imbalances the scoring system for distant maps.

now we come to HC maps:
for HC maps reaching day 15 is not a garantee to get your town ranked BUT reaching day 20 is almost a 100% garantee to rank your town and with it get not only the 20 days score points but also 35 points for a HC ranked town. of course since the diffrence is that reaching day 20 in HC is harder than reaching day 25 in distant balances the scoring system because then you have to be very good organized HC team to get day 20 in HC but still get 15 less points by ranking it compared to ranking a distant map which gives not only 15 more scoring points but also allow you to reach day 25 and with it gain 5 more points because reaching distant day 25 is as hard as reaching HC day 20.

in the end it means one must rank 3 HC 15 days towns in "50 days" gaining 3 x 20 points for reaching 15 days each + 3 x 35 points for ranking those 3 HC towns = 60 + 105 = 165 points total.

if one will do only 2 distant towns both reach 25 days in "50 days" gaining 2 x 30 points for reaching 25 days each + 2 x 50 points for ranking those 2 distant towns = 60 + 100 = 160 points total.

  • 6- so as you can see the system is well balanced after my reduction of the ranked town score system from60 to 50 distant ranked towns and from 40 to 35 HC ranked towns
    and in both types the longer you survive the bigger you score , so i fear we can not break this balance by increasing the reward fro surviving days because then it will break the balance between scoring HC towns and distant towns.
