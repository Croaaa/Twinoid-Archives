The Beginning - A Surprise
A dark room. Basically intro. We see teenager sitting and playing on a computer. Suddenly there appears death screen with gallows on it. Player's monoluge:
"What."
"They did it."
"Damn metaplayers!!"
"I'm going to... destroy this game. The reactor. Those kiddies won't be able to play!"
"Nobody can stop me, hahaha!"
Player buys a few things on DarkWeb and makes preparations.


Desperation
Bordeaux, France. The night. We start on the street, controlling our frustrated player Muido, wearing black ninja outfit. Now we need to get to the MT Headquarters. But what is i... Impossible! They have armed guards in the whole city, reporting all abnormal things to the central?!
Oh, wait. It is just a police. Neverthless, we need to avoid them, because ninja in the middle of the city would look suspicious. We can kill them using our sneaky powers, or avoid them using our mighty grappling hook on buildings. Finally we're at our destination.


Revenge
We see a great game studio. Now what? We have to enter it and seek our revenge, of course. But now there's a threat - REAL MT guards, cameras, sensors of movement and... what? Automated turrets..
After avoiding all of that, we enter the building, deal with more guards and finally we are in a CORE room, with all of the reactors, each powering its own game. Die2Nite, Mush, Twinoid platform... We can choose which one we want to disable. Or all of them. Suddenly, there's a hidden room behind one of the reactors.


Discovery
The player enters a dark room with tables, papers on them, and posters with sketches and notes on walls. It turns out that MT has some dark interests in here... that's why they have armed guard, I guess. But what's that? Weird chemical tables and schemes in the next rooms?
Hopefully we can get to know more things if we look at their posters and computers... in this chem lab.
Plans of apocalypse?! Zombies virus?! Estimated time of arrival in 3 minutes? WHA...


Fear
The bad thing is, all the guards that were killed by the player before, are now being reincarnated as fast and strong zombies. Plus they can walk on the ceilings. We see the last remaining guards defending themselves, but at the end they are killed by zombies, and reincarnated as well.
We need to get outta here as fast as possible and alert government or... I don't want to even think about what can happen.


The Fall
I lost my consciousness before... and it's time wake up now. But what is this place? Only sand... I can see deserted basement. Someone had to bring me here. Why?


Freedom
Imagine all the opportunities! Of course, the world is a wasteland, there are zombies and the last remaining humans are organized in anarchist societes... but we have a bunch of a new options! Imagine the goods that are left by everyone else who is a zombie now. We can grab'em all for us!
