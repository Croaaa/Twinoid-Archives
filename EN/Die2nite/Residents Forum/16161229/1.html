babooshka76

If you're a resident you can only join a random town, there are 3 open towns at one time and you get thrown into one of them.
If you're a hero you can choose which town in particular you want to join.
In both cases, you can look at the citizen lists to see who's in them.
If you're in a coalition with a hero, the hero can pull you into a specific town out of the 3 available (1 for hardcore towns).
Once a town is filled, a new one becomes open.
