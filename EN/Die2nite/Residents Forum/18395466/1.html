iDare

iPhone has no official Flash support, that`s why other phones, even older ones, can be way better to play Die2Nite.


I can confirm that reading/posting in forums & twinoid as well as basically all the actions in town (including closing the gates) are possible to perform from iPhone. They should be doable from any phone able to connect to internet really.

Moving in the world beyond is whole different thing, requires Flash Player and may be impossible to do on your phone.
On iPhone you need Frash + jailbreak or Photon Flash Player & Private Web Browser app (~3.50 Euro in app store), both mentioned in posts above. Photon works really well I must say.
On many other phones Flash Player is available to download and install for free or already installed. Even if some phones are not supported officially, there are workarounds possible to find after typing your issues in simple google.
