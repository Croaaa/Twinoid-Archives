GhostIf I remember correctly, citizens dead in their bed who's body is NOT disposed of before the attack have a chance of either destroying water in the well or killing a living citizen in town.

Yep, that is how it works. Also remember that your personal home defense doesn't count when you are attacked by corpses left inside town. Corpses falling into the well will destroy 20 water rations each.

Few seasons back I was in town where we didn't get rid off all the bodies. We had 35-40 rations in well (don't remember the exact amount). 2 zeds destroyed all the water rations in the well and some of the people were eaten.
