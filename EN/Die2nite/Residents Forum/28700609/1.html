Fewest I saw in my small history was 3, and the most was 15 when some idiot closed the gates hours early and nobody reopened.

What I've found so far is that posting a thread with basics such as
- don't flee
- if stuck in a zone, post in the forums with # of zeds and whether you drank or ate, whether you have water or food, # of remaining APs, etc.
- take water and food before leaving
- don't build the portal lock
- watch your action points

helps sometimes if there are new people who actually read the forums, but the problem is there are so many people, at least in the small towns, who don't.

I can't speak for the larger towns, but in the small towns it really is luck of the draw.
