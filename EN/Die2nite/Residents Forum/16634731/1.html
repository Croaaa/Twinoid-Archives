spacekadt
EmyFireActually (unless they have changed that) people in a coalition have the option to join the town another member of the coalition has joined alone (for example while being unavaible), if it has still room.

This is right.

When in a coalition, if one person is in a town and the others aren't, there is a link to "join" the person in the town. This will bring anyone (resident or hero) into the same town as that person.

However, each person that joins after the fact must do it manually. There's no way for the person that did the jumping to automatically bring people in after the fact.
