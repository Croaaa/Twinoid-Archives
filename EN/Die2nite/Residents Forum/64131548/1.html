Hello Twinoid!

It's been a long time since we announced anything to you, far too long.

Today we want to tell you important news about Twinoid and all of the Motion Twin web games.

For several years now, we have not updated our online games that some of you still like you as much. We are not going to go into detail here about the reasons that led to this situation, let's just say that it had become necessary for us, as a video game studio, to evolve. And now is the time to draw conclusions from this development.

We therefore officially announce to you today how all of our web games will be gradually stopped.

The first change you will see today is that it is no longer possible to pay for anything on any of our web games. Instead, we will offer many things completely free of charge. For example, if you want to take advantage of the Hero mode on Hordes, it will be accessible completely free of charge, to all players, in a few clicks.

All of the games will thus remain accessible to everyone until the end of 2020. Beyond that, most of our games are based on Flash technology whose final cessation in web browsers is planned for the end year, their access will unfortunately become very difficult. We will therefore have to close them gradually, as and when they drop in attendance.

Of course, for all players who have made purchases, you will be able to continue to benefit for several months from what you have already purchased. It is also an opportunity for us to thank you for your support, it is thanks to you that Motion Twin has been able to develop and bring to life, for more than fifteen years, so many games!

We also want to take this opportunity to warmly thank all of the players who have been involved in the communities during all these years, and in particular the moderators who have devoted a lot of time, voluntarily, to bring them to life.

We will now present to you, game by game, what will change (some changes have been made this morning, others will be implemented gradually in the hours and days to come):

Alphabounce : You can get capsules (games) free and unlimited through the bank.
Arkadeo : The number of free daily games is now unlimited.
Café Jeux : The evolution of Flash support in browsers has made the site almost inaccessible for a long time. Payments were stopped in early 2018.
Croquemonster : Monster Credits can only be obtained by playing (or using an MTPass).
Dinoparc and Dinocard : As these games are almost no longer used, payments had already been stopped in 2019.
DinoRPG : The actions performed with your Dinoz are now unlimited, without the need to use Irma potions. The benefits of the Elite pack will now be available to everyone. Certain payments for access to the Elite pack will be partially reimbursed (see below). Events (Mana War, CDC, Monster Hunt) will be relaunched for the next few months. Gold coins can only be obtained by playing (or using an MTPass).
Hammerfest : We will make the source code of the game available very soon, under the CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license .
Hordes : Hero mode is now available for free, via the old payment interface. Certain payments for access to Hero mode will be partially reimbursed (see below).
Hyperliner : The payments, which were no longer used at all, were stopped during 2018.
KadoKado : The number of free daily games is now unlimited. Amazon gift certificates can still be obtained with your Kado points, but it's now an auction system, which will remain in place until the end of September.
Monster Hotel : You can now get free and unlimited gems via the old payment interface. Gold coins can only be obtained by playing (or using an MTPass).
MTPass : It is no longer possible to buy MTPass, but they can still be used on all games.
Mush : Gold mode is now available for free, via the old payment interface. Certain payments for access to Gold mode will be partially reimbursed (see below).
Muxxu : You can now get tokens for free and unlimited, via the old payment interface.
Naturalchemistry 2 : You can now get pyramids (games) for free and unlimited via the old payment interface.
Popotamo : You can now get tokens for free and unlimited via the old payment interface. Quids are only available when playing.
Rockfaller : You can now get unlimited free gold coins via the old payment interface.
Street Writer : You can now get unlimited free gold coins via the old payment interface.
Skywar : The subscription is now available for free, via the old payment interface.
Teacher Story : You can now get unlimited free gold coins via the old payment interface.
For games offering systems similar to a subscription, these subscriptions are now free, we felt that this was particularly unfair for players who recently paid for their subscription.

This is why we will proceed to a partial refund of recent purchases of Hero days on Hordes, of the Elite pack on DinoRPG or of the Gold mode on Mush. For example, a player who bought 6 months of Hero days on Hordes 3 months ago will be reimbursed for half of his payment.

The number of days actually consumed will not be taken into account, the refund will be made in proportion to the number of days since the purchase compared to the number of days paid. This refund will be made automatically in the coming days. It will be limited to purchases made by bank card or Paypal and can only apply to the last purchase made by each player. If the bank card or Paypal account used at the time of payment is no longer valid, the refund will unfortunately not be made.

Since there are a bunch of people that come back to the forums once in a while, I just want to say that even though there are very few responses in this thread, most of the reactions happened on various d2n-related Discord servers (if you haven't already, check-out d2n & friends).

It might not look like it from the World Forums but this community is still alive and kicking. Much credit can be given to Joanne for organising the MMJ in August 2019 and bringing people back together for this final ride.

- - - - - - - - - - - - - -
WoT for those who are interested in community-led solutions to keeping our beloved game(s) alive:

There are various efforts, by various people, across the servers, and across the games to either keep the current games alive as-is, or to recreate similar games. Some of the Hordes/d2n-related ones were already mentioned in this thread. Others were brought up in the #ideas-to-keep-d2n-alive channel on the d2n & friends server I linked above. Most (all?) of these have been specific to the game the community belongs to.

Yesterday, following MT's announcement, a group of French players decided to get together and think about the future of MT's browser games as a whole. Discord server: Eternal Twin

For the moment, this initiative is tending towards conservation of the games in their current state, rather than making improved versions (although I believe that will likely follow if things work out, a good example seems to be EternalFest). If I understand correctly, the idea is also to continue having a common platform for these games.

Not everyone there agrees on the way forward, and I think things aren't set in stone yet. I don't know where I stand on things myself, I just feel like we need to do something.

I'm just sharing this here so new/returning players know that there are things happening. Maybe we can figure out a way for things not to end here
