Hello fellow Die2Niters!

I got some bad news for you - but I also got some good news!
Bad news is that FDTD is about to expire at the end of November.
The good news is that there's something we can do about it as a community!

Some of you might remember this being discussed last year? We all owe thanks to the anonymous benefactor who paid the hosting costs for 2018!

Now that time is nearly over.
This is where the community steps up, right?

Neuroguy has been in contact with BerZerg, the creator and host of FDTD, and there's an agreement of FDTD staying up for us all to use and enjoy, if we can gather the money for hosting it for another year.

- - -

If you have some extra coins to use for the greater good, this is how to do it:

Contact me through Twinoid PM. I'll give you the PayPal address of Neuroguy (who kindly has agreed to be a middleman here) and further instructions. Once we've enough, he will transfer money to BerZerg. The hosting cost for one year is 143 Euros.When sending funds via PayPal, mark it personal ("sending to a friend" ), or otherwise a fee gets deducted.

Please make contributions in Euros.

You can add your game name to Comments for the spreadsheet, unless you want to be an anonymous donator.

Here is the spreadsheet where the donated money will be marked down for everyone to see. If you want to be a anonymous donator, that's fine too.

- - -

Spread the word, people! Even if you can't donate, please share this on your town forum, group, wherever.

We can do this together!

This is a very impotant thread. I am posting here to endorse this effort and ask the whole community to help raising funds and to spread the message among your friends, retired players and anyone who might help!
