I know there are people who will find this naively optimistic and then some, hell, it probably is, but as Epoq did ask for constructive ideas I thought I'd throw this out here. The idea is to rank player's based on existing data, give them a constructive player ranking based on already tracked stats. Anyone familiar with ELO ranking systems may know where I'm going with this. Basically shuns are worth negative points and dehydration deaths are worth negative points. Right now things like day one attempts to attack other citizens or eat cyanide may not be tracked but they could be tracked either visibly or not. On the other hand things like building, zombie killing, scavenging, surviving the night, etc. could be considered constructive and be worth points.

Everyone would start at an arbitrary positive value (100 for instance) and towns would have an ELO score requirement. Each town would have a MINIMUM ELO requirement. If you grief a lot then you quickly drop from the 100ELO towns which any new player can get into to 50ELO towns where most people have been doing bad stuff. Keep it up and they end up in 25ELO towns. On the flip side of this, if you work with people to do good in a town you gain ELO so you quickly graduate to 125ELO towns, 150, etc. If you make the ELO points you gain or lose higher for the first few games and apply them only for events that are CLEARLY helpful or CLEARLY negative it should help discourage griefers and allow people to play 125+ELO towns to largely escape the griefers.

Biggest problem I see with this is that towns require many people in order to fill up and they can already be a bit sluggish to fill. This means you can't make TOO many tiers (i.e. no 500ELO tiers). You'd really want to make it three tiers, the 0-50ELO tier, the 50-150 tier, and the 150+ tier. You could even make it so that griefing penalties were more dramatic in the 150+ tier so that it's not efficient to try to build up a positive rating then grief tons of towns. Make it easy to move up (or down) from the middle tier, make it easy to move down from the top tier, and make it desirable to move up from the bottom tier but not particularly hard or easy to do so.

Ok, now tell me how impractical I am and that this is MT not LoL/DotA/HoN etc.
