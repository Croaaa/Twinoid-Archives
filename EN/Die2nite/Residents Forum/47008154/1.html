Chaos Colosseum Twilight blurred the outlines of the ancient town ruins. There were always twilight, no matter how much time will flow: probably because there was no sky. A huge purple dome a couple of miles in radius covered this town. It did not pass the light and sounds from outside, though who knows is there any outside existing at all, or the whole world includes nothing but this city?
Dozens of the repetitive two-storey rough stone buildings were located along the flat streets, forming square quarters. The only difference between them was the degree of destruction: one building had no roof, other was lack of the wall, some of them became just a pile of stone. The dome just cut off the part of this tidy ruined town, sometimes slicing through the building, sometimes – through the cobblestone road. Ruined buildings made of stone with no furniture, stone road, sand in the alleys and an impenetrable dome – that's all that was here. Though rarely you could meet human remains: just a couple of bones, so ancient, that will crumble into dust if you touch it.
Twelve souls appeared here at once in a different places, and before they managed to figure out anything the loud Emperor’s voice sounded:
- You idiots burned my lovely forest during the previous game, so I had to use my old world this time. Pretty rough and dusty, don’t you find? Spectators will not be pleased to see that. Hope you will paint this place in a beautiful color of blood!

Find your allies and kill enemies – this is all I want from you. Prepare to fight and call me when you will be ready to spend your money. Farewell!
