I wrote a little poem in our town forum today, thought it was amusing. Figured Id share it with everyone. It was all in good fun.

There once was a town full of deafening cries, and in this town everyone dies.... Be it by reactor, or by ghoul we should all follow the simple rules.... A random was eaten for his greifing, The druggie with his poor taste, what a waste...Bacon never comes out to play, maybe life got in the way. Brams got jokes like no other, Max is like the town father, full of insight and a moralist. Profanity is quite profane, Monkey knows how to make it rain. Ceth is always keeping score, Skyguys questions never bore. Joanne my love for you is true, greveur made us all so blue .

Senseijason is a great newb, teams up well with our goob. Always on point and extremely essential, Staminah to the rescue. Nutcase and Soradobi are massively crazy, make sure you behave well around their ladies. Rawr is full of life, pocky full of joy, make sure we dont forget toonboy. Species an adorable boss, Thanks for the learning. Soon we will all be screaming, and burning.

Foxfira and I go way back, Radiated always picks up the town slack. Kof scares me immensely, but his sorry melts me. Mricy is pretty cold. Dessi is so upstanding and bold. As for me im just a little Feisty and im quite rare. And so I dare to speak of my great friends, when its time, youll all be missed. So many more are lost in the desert, sadly just to rise as dead. Dont worry though just like I said, the deafening cries will all be silenced. In a matter of time this town explodes. All will be lost except our beautiful souls.
