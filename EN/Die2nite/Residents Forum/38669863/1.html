Q: What are Hordes and DV?
A: Hordes is the French version of Die2Nite.
DV is the German version.
You can choose to join Hordes event only, or both or whatever. It's up to you.

Q: I don't have an account yet. Does it mean I'm out?
A: Definitely not. Just visit Hordes site and click join.
It is just one button and a French account will be created instantly (using the same name and pass here).

Q: Do I need to be a hero there?
A: Both residents and heroes are welcome.

Q: Do I need to know how to speak French?
A: No need! We can communicate in English in chatroom and in Twinoid. No need to use the town forum.
We have players who can speak French too. They can help if you need translation too.

Q: I don't understand the texts in the game. Help!
A: Don't worry buddy. Use Chrome. It has a built-in translator.
If you use Firefox, just install this Google Translator addon.
