hottest

Building changes

  • Architect Study is upgradable
    • 1st upgrade: giving 4 commons after attack
    • 2nd upgrade: http://cl.ly/XjVC ... the surprise is Common BP?
    • 3rd upgrade: http://cl.ly/XjV3 ... the surprise is Very rare BP?
  • Defensive focus is improved. (changes in update paths, details unknown)



Item changes

  • Burning Laser Pointer needs N&Bs and cooper pipe to build now.
  • Earplugs will now reduce terror (how to use? Any details?)
  • Box cutter gives 15 watchman defense now.
  • Impersonal Exploding Doormat corrected (what is corrected?)
  • Staff AND Broken Staff improved (details unknown)



Hero changes

  • Veilleur Pro (Pro Watchman) (unlock after 1000 hero days or so): You increase your maximum chance of survival by 5% after watches. (Therefore the first night is a 100% increase).
    • Also -5% chance to be wounded on watch
  • Shaman is gone?! (unintended mistakes, but shaman will take a break in S10)
    • But someone reported finding a soul in the town.



Mechanism changes

  • Accumulated fatigue. You lose 10% chance of survival with every night spent on lookout. You recover 5% with every night you rest.
  • It is easier to get wounded during watch now.
  • You will see the chance of death and wound now.
  • Watchmen logs are improved (details unknown)
  • Terror is harder to cure (details unknown)
  • Immunised status corrected (what is corrected?)
  • Theft from the bank is possible now. (Note: Shunned citizens cannot steal)
  • Night time penalties and bonuses apply to English servers by mistake. Will be removed ASAP.
  • New button "Call for Help" available when you get trapped
  • Food now at risk? New flavor text which seems to indicate that.



Please feel free to share any new changes or items you find in S10 here. (Don't worry about possible duplicate reports. Sharing is loving. Just share. Don't worry.)
Thank you. Enjoy!

Corvus Epithumia

In an effort to maximize efficiency and minimize bugs due to differences between servers, the sturdy gallows are no more. The gallows will continue to behave as they currently do and be destroyed after every use.

To make up for this change, we are working with Motion Twin to expand our scope from the current policy regarding 0/0 punchers and apply these same policies to griefing in general. It is worth noting, however, that there is a distinct line between griefing and selfish play. Please allow us some time to figure out where that line is. In the meantime, you can report any griefer activity to Corvus Cloud, Corvus Epithumia and coro. It is preferred that you send your reports to all three of us so that we all have the same information to work from when discussing these cases.

Please feel free to leave any comments and questions in this thread and we will do what we can to get your questions answered.
