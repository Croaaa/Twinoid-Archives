Q1
It is NOT a characteristic of a "Zombie Zoo"
A) Zombies are put under control on the nearest corner of the map.
B) It makes the Soul Collecting stage easy.
C) It increases the ammount of zones to be depleted safely.
D) Takes 8 - 15 days to sucessfully build a Zoo on a large map.
E) Towns with most ruins nearby are the easiest to make a Zoon in.

A was meant to be the correct answer, (i picked C FYI since i saw all characteristic of a "Zombie Zoo" , so i considered the word "safely" as a trap where its not relevant in meta play since no one is allwed to take any risks )
however neither are correct when playing optimally. A is only true according to how i made the guide which is to concentrate on trimming and saving boosters and ammo while keeping the zoo controlled and avoid to scavenge in groups inside 11 AP range inside the zoo. BUT we have to always treat each map differently since many factors play a more important role in where the zoo should be other than how close or far it is from town..(buildings with good topology, the zed spread direction in first 3 days... and many other factors...)
BUT for the sake of the Exam being made based on the guide consider A as the closest to a correct answer.

Q2: B

Q3: C

Q4: the best way is to carry all you have on the ground and move to the small House at 1/2 , kill zeds with the concrete blocks and keep killing til all are dead or the concrete breaks.
then upgrade the site 1 permanent upgrade with last AP then use the twinoid and upgrade 6 more permanent upgrades, then install the sheet of playwood with 1 AP , then tomb with last AP. giving me around 80% to 90% chances to survive depending on how many the concrete killed.
(i answered wrong because didnt consoider killing the zeds with concrete to eliminate one or 2 or 3 zeds penalties.)

Q5: D

Q6:

  • 1- Water: i must keep the water to be able to get back to town.
  • 2- cement bag: to always bring a heavy item back to town or closer to it.
  • 3- Ness-quick weedkiller : campers will need this in the bank or at a 6 AP ranged zone on day 2 to unbury their campsite building for 2-3 free debris.
  • 4- Wrought Iron: Iron is a pit rarer to find than twisted Planks so Iron always gets priority over Planks. and iron get priority over the Giant Rat because the town must build watchtower and Workshop on day 1 and needs enough Iron in the bank.

Q7: first i will drop everything and build the shopping Trolley (incomplete trolley + Tape + Pipe + Iron)
that will now allow me to carry remaining 6 items but none of them is heavy
