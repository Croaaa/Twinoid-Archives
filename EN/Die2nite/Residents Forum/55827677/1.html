my short answer is: still 4 to 5 tamers in HC. your key is always 8 active survivalists and nothing less than 8 survivalists and very very early killing with tuns of water and hurling sticks and all batteries used in mk2/devastator if found plus every last Heroic Return and second wind your town has to offer.

the long answer is: there are several ways to pick the perfect set of heroes , but not to build a zoo only, its to run a town from day 1 til it dies. so some numbers are less optimal than others.
i am a big fan of having as many Guardians as possible which are considered Diamond with Guardtower and Watch and the only way to have more guardians is to be forced to limit the number of tamers. So its not really about the usefulness of the class tamer, its about the fact that each extra tamer other than the 4 to 5 obligatory ones means one less guardian. SO the main idea is : build a zoo with only 4 to 5 tamers and your advantage with the extra guardians you started with has been magnified especially because in HC watch penalties are higher and harsher.

i would´t say the towns which have more than 5 tamers are on the wrong track or they have strategy wise picked wrong set of heroes. not at all because in the end they will still build a zoo and will get 5 to 6 more items extra in bank per tamer daily compared to a town which has less tamers than them, BUT in my personal opinion and experience: tamers are NOT the key to build a zoo so their extra numbers is completely overrated compared to have a giant Guardian giving 20 extra def from guardtower and their sweet bonuses on watch. only 8 Survivalists and Early killing are the key in HC zooing imo.

the more detailed mechanic of zooing is that you can easily make killing missions end exactly where the next killing mission will start by dropping the weapons on the ground to be picked up by next mission with fresh new ammo, and all Guardians have exactly same heroic skills as tamers which are heroic return and second wind. and with that you have eliminated the entire usefulness of tamer class and made it completely unnecessary for zoo making purposes. you may think that killing with repairable weapons will require tamers sending Repair-Kits which you obviously should use with your 4 to 5 tamers daily and it is undeniably very useful, but there is absolutely no need to use those weapons more than 5 times daily because you always have water and battery weapons for other classes til you run out of boosters and heroic returns/second winds to kill zeds.
and there is the most important rule of all for making zoo that makes the number of guardians or tamers unimportant, which is that the zoo work must start day 2 and must be finished on day 5 , day 6 if you are low on ammo no matter if HC or distant, if half hero team or full hero town. so you would´t have that many zeds to kill with more than 4 to 5 tamers and daily 6 to 8 guardians with their Heroic return and second wind if you simply start killing early.

Just wanted to add:
As if we are going to use all those 500 clean zones and deplete them all... It doesn't happen on Distant, what to say about HC and their camp penalties?

In the last few #1 distant tries I played, we depleted about all the 11ap-range diamond (zones 11ap away from town, if you don't count the return route). We had systematic wind routes to make that happen so that at the end of every day everything would be gone. Additionally to that we made several drug tours a day to work on zones farther away than that.

Without a zoo, all that would either not be possible, or only be possible wasting:

a) A lot of additional AP and people to clear those zones every day

b) Collection potential - that means, slots that would be used to collect items from the world beyond will be used to bring e.g. damaged weapons back to town

c) Weapons that could be dumped later, but will be used up for the killing (batteries, ...)

About the tamer question: Dream is, in my personal opinion, totally correct in that you don't need 9 tamers to build a zoo, not even close to 9. The reason why we (as in HIVE) take that many is that we noticed that with our scavenging strategies, even though we already optimized them on collection, we still don't get many of the high-defense (temporary defense) items like weapons back into town (along with big amounts of low-defense items like scrap and co ofc). Items which are just lying around in the world beyond - already dug up, but not picked up and brought back to town. The additional tamers help with that.

To counteract the defense bonuses of a guardian, a tamer only has to bring about 15+10+(30-20)/3 => ca. 28 (Guard Tower + Organize defense + the 10 def difference on watch every 3 days) additional temporary defense to town each day compared to a guardian. And that only on the days where that extra guardian defense will actually be used - which is only in the last days of the town before endgame camping. On the other days the guardian brings - with a zoo built - only very little to no bonus to the town.

That means by just bringing in a tiny bit of extra dump defense per day (a weapon or plastic bag is e.g. worth 7 defense with Dump Upgrade built), in our opinion a tamer can be just as valuable as a guardian (the safety benefits on watch neglected - whether or not one should do that is to be discussed ofc).

That's why we go with a slightly increased amount of tamers compared to what Dream has usually choosen (or what he recommends in his guide).
