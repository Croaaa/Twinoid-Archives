A meta is nothing implemented in the game. Just enter some external chat and coordinate your efforts to jump in the same city.

Form as few coalitions as necessary for everyone (one hero in each of them which should be online when jumping) and then jump all at the same time in the same city - thats it.

EDIT: Of course you might also coordinate using twinoid PMs. That's just not as comfortable as what you're doing is time critical.

Animated Corpse

A meta is formed when a significant (I'd say 15+, maybe 35+) amount of players decide to stick together and jump together, grouped in a number of coalitions.

In a meta, coalitions should not matter and you should be prepared to move around coalitions in order to help the group jump together. Yes, you need a few heroes, ideally one for each coalition at least, who is available to co-ordinate the jump in the same town at a given time.
