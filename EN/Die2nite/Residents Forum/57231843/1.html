tl;dr: shreenshots and reports for the win, if that doesn't help, name and shame as needed.

ignore the "naming and shaming"-policy. it's useless. the intention might be reasonable (imo it's not), but the result is that it actually helps protecting the griefers.

name and shame if you really think it's needed. don't name and shame for trivial stuff. you will get a slap on the wrist from the admins, but more people will know about the griefer, which means that they will be able to shun/hang the player in question quicker and be more likely to report/screenshot the griefer's actions, which leads to faster banning.

naming and shaming is only a problem if the forum is full of threads complaining about giefing (which would mean that griefing is a widespread problem concerning many people which would in turn mean that there's a much worse problem than "naming and shaming".). naming and shaming is never the real problem. as it is, the forum is almost dead anyway. so n&s-threads might actually be an improvement.

the few real griefers should be banned asap, naming and shaming helps to make that happen. but even more important, before you complain on the forum, make screenshots and report the griefers and then wait to see what happens. the admins are actually quite quick if they have evidence.

screenshotting/reporting > naming and shaming
