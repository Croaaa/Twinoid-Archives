Griefing is an independent act.
(extension: when griefing occurs, the griefer can be identified)
(extension: griefing may be conscious or unconscious)
Griefing is detrimental in the long run.
(extension: it may be beneficial in the short run; these cover "smart griefers")
Griefing is relative to a goal.
(extension: you can be a griefer and a selfish player at the same time)

If it violates any of these natures, it is not griefing.
