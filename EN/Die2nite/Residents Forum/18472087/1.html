On november 17, 2012
jkudlick

Multiple hacksaws are good. That allows multiple people to use the Workshop at the same time, or if someone grabs the hacksaw then either loses internet access or goes offline without returning it then you don't have to shun them to get a hacksaw. My town currently has 2 hacksaws. Having 3 is almost unheard of.

I would definitely build the 2nd hacksaw, but only build the 3rd if you have a crapload of N&B.

BTW - hacksaws don't stack, so whether you are holding 1 or 3 hacksaws, you still only save 1AP per conversion.
