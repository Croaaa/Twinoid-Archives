The apocalypse is upon us! Zombies slowly encroach on your hiding spot. Breathing deeply with sweat pouring from your brow, you lower your head and imagine all the despicable, painful and horrendous things the undead will do to you. You hope it is over quickly.
Moans paired with shuffling footsteps approach. Their ungodly gurgling noises become louder, seemingly signifying an upcoming kill. You are surrounded.

How many zombies are in the Horde? Will they bit your Head first? Or your Tail?

Once a Day you can guess a number from 1-100 and also guess Heads or Tails.
Roll the corresponding dice commands (d100 ) and (ht)

If you guess both correct, you win 15 hero days ( In MT passes so you can gift them to others too)

Got it?

thanks MT for Hero days and Dream for game idea!
