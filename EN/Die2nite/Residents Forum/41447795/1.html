I did not know where to post this so I decided to make a seperate thread, this is my own Story and a response to Jean's Battle Of The Bands thread...

Enjoy!

It’s 2300. The night looks hazy and I’m uncertain what day it is 17? 20? 27? I’m getting delirious, the lack of sleep and bland food is taking It’s toll. I feel like I’m going Crazy In The Head. I make my way towards the wall, The whiteboard is full of dust and in the Sign-Up category It’s empty. It’s past 2300 and no one has signed up. I take out my flask with the initials FTW and take a sip of what I think is whiskey, It tastes mighty awful (even for whiskey standards) but, It does the job and minutes later I walk up to the whiteboard. I’m having thoughts about doing this…. Again. Last time it went smooth and I told myself I would Never Die but, now with the days passing and the zeds getting closer to our encampment I don’t know what to think. My thoughts are overpowering the alcohol so I take another sip, write my name down and Keep On Driving. Minutes pass and I’m having second thoughts, those thoughts are Runnin and I want to go back and wipe out my name but, in a perfect world you can get away with that. But, this isn’t the perfect world, If you wipe out your name even with water you leave a trail and I rather get mauled by zeds than be put in the spotlight by SGT. JasonBourne and possibly get hanged for being a Coward ( I do have some dignity, it’s in there hidden with all the dirt and tattered rags called clothes). I stumble on to the debriefing Watchmen area, I feel warm and brave, since food is short all I ate that day was a Mouldy Neapolitan with whiskey and to top it off more whiskey with a hint of fear on the top. Our commander Bram is barking orders but, at the moment all I hear is a Bitter Sweet Symphany I’m all smiles and ready to give it all out including my life for the sake of the town, the others I cannot speak for as I see anguish, fear and remorse in the many faces that will occupy the wall this night. The times get closer and you can feel it in the air, It might be the tension or the screams of the zeds closing by but, all I know is im singing a Ballad Of A Lonely Man my comrades are all around me but, I feel lonesome. I somehow feel like I’m going Towards Destiny since this is my 10th time doing the wall detail. Am I laughing at death? Mocking it? I do not know, all I know Is it’s called Survival but, regardless with or without me The Show Goes On and the town will keep on fighting. But as long as I’m alive (so I Tell myself I am)I will Keep Pounding. I Work Hard Play Hard. Today I feel different and I keep on reassuring myself that this is just a Nightmare and I will wake up soon...

The debriefing is almost at end and the sulking is heavy and brooding. The Grey Dawn Breaking and I’m having second thoughts. The pre-attack jitters are kicking in, they’re kicking in hard. My gut feels like It has an knot, it’s heavy like an anchor and it throbs like an angry hangover. Man this is some good whiskey. I’m making my way to the wall and take this time to reminiscent about the “good” times I had in this wasted depleted hell-hole I called “Home”. Talking about home my hovel, I will miss my hovel or as I call it the Haunted Cathouse. Ah the times spent breaking my back at the workshop, climbing up to the tower with only a notepad and a pencil to count the zeds, heh I’ll even miss the times I HotProwled a drunken citizen’s home just to get those chuck figurines, yes that was one of my many vices, you know the figurine collecting I mean there’s nothing wrong with breaking in to one’s home just to “borrow” some items while they lay there passed out with a bottle of 'Wake The Dead' at their fingertips. Poor deluded bastard I just borrowed your chuck figurines and took a candid picture and It’s going in the pile of shame. You are there and me here because I have The Ace Of Spades. The Kool-Aid smile fades away as I return back to reality I don’t even recall going to the bank to receive my weapons but, I have 2 hurling sticks strapped to my back and an Aqua-Splash at arms, man this is some good whiskey. I have seen this view numerous times and all those times it has tormented me but, this night it looks Beautiful.

It’s almost game time and the pre jitters have departed. It’s time to Rock This Town and For a moment I am Still. In Bloom but, deep down I know my number is up and I will enter the Valley Of Dreams once this attack is over. Me and my comrades the Watch-Men are on Dead Body Disposal. They are rushing us left and right, 10 of us versus dozens of them I would say more but, all I see is dozens, maybe there’s hundreds behind the dozens. It’s loud ass hell, the screams are muffled by the groans of the zeds. Flesh is being torn apart, people are swearing, people are freaking out! It’s over I know it. Even with all the Madness there is a smile taped to my face. In a matter of moments I will be joining her, she was taken away from me days ago and I will be joining HER in the Afterworld the True Romance is coming altogether and I can’t help it but, to come with terms… It’s over soldier but, you gave it your best and in the end that’s what matters the most… This Lost Soul is going home…
