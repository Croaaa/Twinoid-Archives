MT and our new Admin buzzard were kind enough to again sponsor this contest for season 10

this Contest´s Main Goal is to encourage everyone who isn´t part of a Top Meta or is part of a Meta which isn´t serious enough for ranking in Leaderboard to level up their organized play and rank higher and make the competiton for ranking in Top 15 harder, tighter and more exciting:

the prizes will be

  • 30 Hero days for the 1st place with highest Score
  • 15 Hero days for the 2nd place with second highest Score
  • 15 Hero days for the 3rd place with Third highest Score
  • 15 Hero days for the highest score by a full time Resident if all first 3 Places are won by Full Time Heroes
  • 15 Hero days for a randomly chosen Participant from the list of all Participants who manage to Rank at least one Distant Town (HC Ranked Towns wont count for this prize)

Big thanks to MT for sponsering all 5 Prizes for us
___________________

The Rules: (remember those Rules are made like this to make the contest as fair as possible for all no matter what class you play or if you are a hero or a resident. but of course being a Hero gives you a slight advantage in spite of this scoring system because in order to be able to rank towns you need more Heroes in them)

  • You are not allowed to participate in this Contest if you were part of the Top 2 Distant Towns in season 9 Ranking. (only Distant Top 2 are excluded, so Top 2 HC Towns are allowed to participate as long they wern´t part of any of the top 2 Distant Towns)
  • All Contest Points scored in Distant, HC, Small or Private Towns over the entire length of Season 10 will count.
  • You gain Points for these accomplishments in Season 10:
    • 1- for each Distant Ranked Town: 50 Points
    • 2- for each HardCore Ranked Town: 35 Points
    • 3- for each Town in Season 10 if you survive for:
      • 15 days: 20 Points
      • 20 days: 25 Points
      • 25 days 30 Points
      • 30 days 35 Points
      • 35+ days 40 Points
    • 4- for each Distant or HC town you manage to build a zombie zoo and provide a saved mapviewer link to your town
      • for each Zoo in HC maps: 40 Points
      • for each Zoo in Distant maps: 30 Points
      • one single exception: the points for building a ZOO will not count for anyone who manage to build one while being a guest or part of a serious rank attempts with Unnamed, RFM or 6FO meta Towns. this rule has been added to provide fairness since those 3 metas have an almost garantee to build a zoo in most of their towns.
    • 5- for making it to
      • the top 180 of highest Soul points gathered only in season 10 ranking (Page 6) 25 Points
      • the top 150 of highest Soul points gathered only in season 10 ranking (Page 5) 30 Points
      • the top 120 of highest Soul points gathered only in season 10 ranking (Page 4) 35 Points
      • the top 90 of highest Soul points gathered only in season 10 ranking (Page 3, 2 and 1) 40 Points
    • 6- for each Atomic death from blowing up a Reactor gained in season 10 (in all maps, small, HC , distant) 10 Points
      this should be reported by one person only in here with a link of mapviewer to verify

  • The highest 3 Scores in the End of the season will be the winners
  • In case of a Tie
    • the highest number of ranked towns will decide the winner,
    • if still a tie then the highest number of days survived in a town.
    • if still a tie then the highest Soul Points gathered in Season 10.

  • All Hero days from BiA, winning LMS or being a Ghoul (by eating others to gain hero days) will not count as Full Hero Status. It will be best for all Residents participating in this Contest to post in here when/if they get LMS Hero days to maintain their Resident Status is case they sign up as Full Time Resident
