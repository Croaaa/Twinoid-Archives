On november 16, 2012
jkudlick

It depends upon the upgrade. Each WT upgrade requires more water in the well than the previous upgrade. With no upgrades, the WT requires no additional water in the well. If there is insufficient water in the well for the current upgrade, WT will revert to the base defense rather than to the upgrade for which there is sufficient water.

At least, that's how it has been in the past. Only experimentation will tell us whether MT has changed that.
