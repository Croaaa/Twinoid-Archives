RANDOM HOT TIP #1



If you have a hacksaw, having can openers in the bank is a mistake. They belong in the world beyond.

They are entirely useless in town - the hacksaw can be used as a tin opener.

In the desert, you always need them to open chests.

Tin openers (and also screwdrivers) should not be used as weapons and should not come to town. They should be left lying around a little everywhere.
