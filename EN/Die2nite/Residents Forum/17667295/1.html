The story of my first meta town: the Disturbed Palisades
Prejump~
Tara: Yay! My first meta jump ever! We chat a lot on the chat.
I am the self declared == in command.

Jump~
I sit and watch the chat. The attack comes and the hardcore town fills up. Slowly.
There were 39 people.
Then, there were 39 people.
Still 39... 39. 39. And 39.
Finally, town filled up! Jump!
MageShooter jumped for me. Most of us made it in!
I checked my Twinoid mail... And realized that the zed race jump was also taking place today! My heart sank as I realized: if the zed race people joined our town we'd be kind of screwed. Not because they are bad people, as they were nice people, but because if they were there they would've hogged all the weapons and used water to get rid of people's bodies. (In the end, I needn't have worried since we got no good weapons anyway )
So, like the messenger that collapsed after sending the missive, I ran over to the chat room and told them that the present hardcore town was occupied by the WCH. They were jumping later in the day so I didn't have to freak out
Notable incident: In the chat, someone notes, "There's an empty town. I think that's our ride." Half a minute later: "Now there's 27 people in the town?! I could've sworn it was empty a moment ago..." Made me lol
Jump was successful.

Day 1~
Woody beat me to making a hello thread. Annoyed. I make another thread anyway.
Penix ended up in our town. In our meta's chat, we had a debate on whether Penix was a griefer, as he was on our griefer list. I said, no, he isn't, he's just a bit annoying at times
Our griefer list is strange.
Soon, we discover that there was a citizen's house one AP away from town. Waaay too close to camp
About three hours before attack I tried to go on to use my AP. But somehow I couldn't get on die2nite. The screen was blank and it was perpetually loading.
But I had to go to Chinese school, and no matter how many times I refresh the page, die2nite just wouldn't load.
Our car, driving away from home. Site still loading as wifi dies... Six AP wasted.
During break I tried to use the school's wifi but did not work. I soon gave up and started reading Foam's guide which I had loaded at home.

Day 2~
I went to the library. I used their computers to actually scavenge outside! Yay! This was when I found out that a meta strategy was to leave food outside, which made a lot of sense. I found a chest just lying on the floor and I lugged it back. Inside was a bottle of Wake the Dead. I chucked it in the bank.
On the chat, trynt told us that his noodles were stolen. He launched an all-out investigation. Even I wasn't spared, though I had put my own noodles in the bank on Day 1 so it really didn't make sense for me to steal the noodles ._.'

Day 3~
Today there were suspicions of a griefer. Malkev had a hovel and a beer fridge. We coordinated our shuns through chat. We were afraid that he would take everything from the bank the moment a complaint was made. In the end we realized we probably should've talk to him before shunning. But I was always a very shun-happy individual. I want to shun people for the smallest offenses :/
Miudo was camping with 75% chances and Sagittaeri (Sage) with 80%. I hoped for the best for both of them.

Day 4~
MIUDO DIED! We were heartbroken. We had lost our leader.
We found a Jarpen table so registry was quickly built, followed by the study.
The portal lock was also built, to our dismay. ._.'
I got scarred for life from what I saw on the chat. I took it out on trynt, who was one of the perpetrators. The result was ugly. He still owes me a RP text.

Day 5~
Factory was built. Not much happened.
Thomith sent me a DO. A sweet gesture.

Day 6~
First day (only day?) when the gazette reported no dead.
Penix committed suicide by cyanide to join his meta jump though. However we weren't going to be able to meet attack in the 300s with only 200 defense.
I built a hovel and offered my DO to 2tousent. He declined, suggesting I give it to iHappy. So I did.

Here's part 2.

Day 7~
A few people died, 2tousent included. There were 21 left. However the common BP from the study revealed a small cemetery. I was jubilant. In an estimate of 250-300, we had exactly 300 defense. Beautiful.
I was terrorized by last night's attack which delighted me. I just love masochism distinctions
Two hours before attack we discussed endgame strategies on the chat. Zombies would make it hard to camp and move around; we had no good weapons. We formulated plans. At one point I remarked, "When there's only ten people left there will be 300 defense from dead alone. Such is the power of the small cemetery." I did not know how ironic that would turn out to be.
Half an hour before attack I went to close the gates. I thoroughly checked citizens page to make sure no one was going to come in, and then I headed to the gates. The chat had been spazzy and error prone, so I finally closed that tab.
When I tried to close the gates the screen went blank and started perpetual loading, like on Day 1. After a while I just tried getting on chat to ask if gates were closed. But chat stopped working for me too so I tried going on quakenet. I spent five minutes trying to join the channel because I forgot the # before channel name
By the time I finally logged on it was almost attack. I kept on refreshing die2nite page but no luck. It resembled the error I had during the summer where the page was normal except sign in button was missing. I couldn't do anything. 5:00 arrived. So I resigned myself to talking on quakenet and hoping beyond hope that the gates were closed.

Day 8~
At 5:10, trynt informed me that
The gates were open.
Fifteen people died.
I died. Woody died. Mollz died. Sage died. A bunch of other people died.
Ten people were left.
Ten.
The most ironic thing of the century.
The town killed by open gates, because my phone stopped working. On a day when there was finally enough defense.
Leaving us with ten people alive. Ten.
It was all my phone's fault, you see.

Aftermath~
Thus is the story of my first meta town.
Because normal things never happen to me.
I'm allergic to normal.
Instead, I am a magnet that attracts weird things all the time. And this is no exception.
This would make an interesting story though, to tell on a cold night around a campfire, surrounded by my die2nite family....
The story of my first meta town.

The == in command,
Tara
Over and out!
