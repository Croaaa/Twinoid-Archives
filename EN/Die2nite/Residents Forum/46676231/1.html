2 months have passed after the previous game.. But finally it's here!

Chaos Colosseum is a forum game with a complex rules. Speaking in two words you just have to make two actions daily: attack and/or protect other players, the goal is to kill enemies and survive. Meanwhile you can secretly communicate with people, roleplay on forums and make bets on who will die this day to earn in-game gold. Gold will allow you to buy better equipment, that will increase your attack and defence power, spend it on potions to restore health and mana, gain Fury...

To take part in it you shall:
1. Sign up in this thread
2. Roll a d100. Highest roll will allow you to take part in the game if i will gather more players than needed.
3. Make sure that you are able to look at the google spreadsheets (mobile internet may be not good enough).
4. Prepare to log in daily in next two weeks

You can read rules here. Don't worry if something missing, i will make further explanations during the game. Also you can always ask me a questions.

This time it will be a team battle. 12 players are requested for the game, which will be randomly divided into 4 teams of 3 players each. Team, which will kill every other player and will still have at least one member or creature alive will be declared a winner. Every member of winning team will get 2 MT passes! (4 MT passes = 15 hero days in die2nite). But if there will be no survivor noone will get the prize.

Couple of huge changes are done for this game:
1) Day 0 is back. During a day 0 you will be able to pick your classes with teammates (warriors and/or mages) and buy starting items. Your team members will be a secret to another players.
2) According to your team setting you will randomly get one additional team action. It can be used only once per game and will reveal your team.
3) Summoned creatures are now tied to a summoner and will always absorb all the damage from the mage untill they die. Zombie is empowered a little bit.

Several advices about roleplay.

First of all create your character. Make a story about his past life, who it was: a great warrior, powerfull mage, someone who didn't know about his magical powers untill he got here? How old is he, what is his temperament and passions? In Chaos Colosseum all of you are a dead heroes from different worlds and eras, so you are not limited in your imagination. Maybe you will want to roleplay someone from Game of Thrones or Dota? Everything is accepted here.
But you don't have to write this whole story from the very beggining. Place it inside your roleplay, like "He saw the blood. It didn't surprise him - a habitual picture for a royal guardsman, that passed through a lot of wars..."
The main part of people's roleplay here is a reaction on a game situation, the description of actions from the own point of view. I am usually roleplaying myself the most important actions - who died, how did that happen, etc., while you can make a more wise story about things i missed, how did your character felt himself in this situation, etc.
The other part of roleplay is interaction and dialogues between players. You can act an introduction, the development of relations, your reaction on your old/new friend's death or suffering. Otherwise you can call The Emperor for dialogue, i am always pleased to take part in roleplay.

Check previous games, roleplay is a really cool part of the game, and remember that people with the best roleplays always won thanks to a bonus money i rewarded them

I thought of an idea for a Team Ability. Although I only thought of it recently, so it's not that well thought of. It may be too over-powered or not powerful enough.

For a Team with 2 Mages and 1 Warrior, using this power grants:

-One Mage receives the potential of infinite stamina, to have no cap in the amount of Health they can have. Basically, they can be healed to have more than 10 Health. So instead of always having x/10 Health, they have x/infinity Health. And it needs to be a Mage, because it can be as if it was a spell where they are able to exceed the stamina limit that a normal human can have. They have the potential to have the stamina of a god (or creature ) more so, if they are able to get enough Health. This player won't receive any health from this ability (so 10/10 will just be 10/infinity or 3/10 will just be 3/infinity), but they also won't be able to take any damage on the turn this ability is used (so they won't lose any Health either).

-The other Mage receives the potential to have infinite Mana. So x/10 Mana becomes x/infinity Mana. On the turn the ability is used, they can't lose any Health (just like the other Mage).

-The Warrior receives the potential to have infinite Fury, there is no longer a limit to how angry they can be. So x/3 Fury becomes x/infinity Fury. On the turn the ability is used they CAN lose Health, unlike the Mages. BUT they can't die on this turn either (they end with a minimum of 1 Health). Also, If the Warrior receives enough damage which would have otherwise killed them (like they end with 1 Health even though they received more damage than Health they had), the Warrior receives an additional Fury. So instead of getting 1 Fury from taking damage, they get 2 Fury.

The Team Ability is called "No Limits"- AP/Mana Cost: 6AP and 3 Mana (Mana can't be provided from Mage receiving infinite Mana cap)

If you're willing to add this Team Ability to the game, feel free to tweak it to make it more balanced and less over-powered/under-powered. I am well aware that the way I have it set up, is probably not balanced enough.Although I did try my best to balance it like all the other 16 Team Abilities.Also the name I gave it was the first thing I thought of, so feel free to change the name too.
