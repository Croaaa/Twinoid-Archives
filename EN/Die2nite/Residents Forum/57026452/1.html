you need

  • 1 x N&B to build battlements,
  • 2 x sheet metal to build hammam.
  • 1 x Pipe to build pump

Notes:

  • souls will not land inside 4 km ranges but outside it. so you would have to make damn sure NO ONE dies , and if someone dies you will be forced to go get their soul from outside 3 km ranges , or risk defending way less days due to defending 7.5% increase of attack size per each red tortured soul left outside for 48+ hours
  • you do not need to find all planks and iron to finish workshop from those zones in 3 km range since any hero can seek a construction kit which gives you 3 of those , so 3 to 4 heroes should get you to complete the missing planks and iron for workshop.

if and that is a huge big IF you find those listed above you can survive up to day 12+ maybe 13 with

  • building only the projects that only require wood and metal that can be converted in workshop,
  • then using loaded battery launchers, loaded water pistols and torches to defend the attacks on watch, here assuming you are limited on furnitures because you didnt move outside 3km range to find any like washers, fridges, ovens, guitars...
  • when everything to build runs out you start building emergency supplies and with it you can build up to 150 temporary defence daily only and mainly out of planks and iron and nothing more.
  • then when you run out of batteries, water and torches to use for watch , you start posting watchmen on the wall with just their basic 10 watchmen defence

everything else will be limited to you due to lack of BPs to open from all kinds and lack of N&B and concrete to increase your town defence to a level of defending higher days than day 12+

but i could guarantee day 12 without stepping outside 3km range IF you find that magic N&B , Pipe and 2 sheet metal (sheet metal parts)
