I would like to organise a new massive event in Die2Nite.

Or perhaps a big one and then a medium size one... Anyway!

Here are ideas I came up with or gathered from speaking with a few players.

They are numbered just as a reference, they're not in any particular order.

I am thinking that an event could either include all these ideas together, or some of them. This is NOT about doing another "Clash" or anything too similar.

What is your opinion? What would attract you?

Do you have any other good suggestions?

1- Potential Participants express interest and go in a pool. Town leaders/founders pick them 1 by 1, taking turns, in a Live Draft.

2- Competing towns have got the EXACT SAME MAP.
Yes it means you can spy on other towns to know what to expect, but that can be part of the fun and part of the dynamic of the contest and it may mean new strategies to use this as well as you can.

3- Open to everyone above 200 Soul Points.
It's not hard to get there and it doesn't have to take that long. Having complete newcomers take part in massive events can be a massive drain on everyone else.

4- Extra Points on top of the usual point system, with special criteria: * Wonder
* Nuclear reactor / nuclear death
* Number of cats / dogs / hacksaws / teddy bears in the bank on a specific day at a specific time.
* Drawing a specific picture on the in game map using players (little yellow dots) to draw the picture. It needs to be a specific pic (ghost? zombie? star?) and the model needs to be provided before the towns start.

5- One traitor per town.
Special unique distinction for the most successful traitor (judged on how poorly your town went in the end). Traitor do not get winning town distinction.
They try to make the town fail. It can be a brutal act of treachery or it can be various subtle acts and posts throughout the town. Traitor would probably need to report to the event organiser regularly. Would be selected by organiser and picked from volunteers to make sure the traitor is committed and not necessarily too obvious.
This means the organiser doesn't play the event and behaves as a MC.

6- Prizes. Prizes? > Unique distinction for participants of the winning town + one for the most successful traitor?
