Proof of Achievement
To get one achievement you sometimes need to screenshot something and post at the MMJ Scav. Hunt thread (this one!). Make sure your screenshot contains all the information required for it and that it shows the d2n clock showing your town name and current day of the town. Town related Achievements (TRA)
There are 28 Achievements in total, divided in 2 groups. The Town achievements are points generated by achievements completed by the MMJ town you are in right now. So lots of team work is required to get them. The TRA are:

  • {If you check the sheet you'll see that there are 7 hidden TR achievements. These will be revelead shortly after the jump. They are hidden because... spoiler :p} The others are:
  • Is Any Wonder? (Won)
    Equals the number of Wonder Constructions built by the town.
    (proof by screenie of Construction Page or Death Screen showing distinctions).
    Points = 888 per Wonder
  • Reactor Ending (Rea)
    Town finished by Reactor Explosion
    Points = 1932 + (100 * citz. dead by it)
  • Thematic Town (RPT)
    Have more than half the town involved on a RP themed-town based on cartoons, tv series, movies, or any other thing; playing forum games, wearing theme avatars, etc...
    Post 3 screenies or more showing diff aspects of this; preferably on different moments/days
    Points = 1000 (credited on town end)
  • Rare Stuff! (RaS)
    Take screenie of bank or World Beyond with one of the following items (once per item):
    -Car Door (assembled or not)
    -Swiss Army Knife
    -Guard Dog,
    -Hacksaw,
    -Cursed Cuddly Toy,
    -Radius mk2 (assembled or not)
    Points = 50 points per item
  • My Precious (MyP)
    A citizen will be randomly selected on each MMJ town. The longer the citizen lives, the more points the town earns.
    Points = 30 x days*Days will be = to the number of days the person lived according to their soul page, so type of death matters.
  • Cheerleader (Che)
    First citzen to die in the mmj town has to come to scav. hunt thread and cheer for their comrades every day until the town is no more. (by means of posting something that can be considered "cheering"
    Points = 500 points when town ends
  • Not a Democracy (NoD)
    Each day, between 22:20-22:40 game time, take a screenie of your town Daily Upgrades votes so far and post it at the Scav Hunt thread.
    For each day, the mmj town with the LEAST votes in total for that day will get 50 points.
    You can post the screenie in the next day but before the attack.
    Obviously the person taking the pic needs to show the number of votes so he/she would have to vote first on something to be able to see them.
    Points = 50 /dayDont forget: The d2n clock with the time and day as well as the town name need to appear at the screenie.

Individual related Achievements (IRA)
The IRAs are things only you can get, although you can get help from your fellow town members to receive them. Most of these achievements require a screenshot proof posted here in order to be counted. The IRAs are:

  • Unlucky Day (UnD)
    Post a screenie at the Scav. Hunt thread of you having 8+ "Find Nothing" during Auto-Searches in the same day at the same zone.
    Points 100 points each screenie
  • [resident] RP Party (RPP)
    Send a screenshot of a funny RP story you did using the [resident] tag in the chat zone of the World Beyond.
    You can use other tags too. Use short sentences to build a large text.
    Points = 100 (awarded just once)
  • Luck with Cards (LwC)
    Send a screenie of you getting 1AP from Cards.
    (Note: since this probably hard to screen with the town clock, try to show it with your computer/phone (drop-down menu) clock/date showing)
    Points = 30 per screenie
  • Unlucky with Cards (UwC)
    Send a screenie of you getting Terrorized from Cards
    (if using Book of Calm to prevent terror, you can send a pic too and you get the points as well)
    Points = 40 per screenie
  • 7 Seas and 4 Corners (4Co)
    Screenie of you in one of the corners of your town (up to 4 screenies if you are crazy enough).
    Points = 150 per screenie
  • Camp Well (Wel)
    Camp an Abandoned Well 20Km+ from your town
    Show a screenie of you hidden in the sector and of you alive in the day after.
    Points = 300 awarded only once
  • Woman/Man of Status (Sta)
    Send a screenie of your Status bar showing as much status changes as you can.
    (You can send another pic to overwrite a previous pic you had)
    Points = 15 * number of status
  • Local Tourist (LoT)
    Post a screenie of you and the Town Guide (no need if you are the Guide...) in the same zone
    AND
    Post a screenie of you at 3 different ruins of the World Beyond of your MMJ town.
    All 4 pics (unless you take a pic of guide and a ruin at the same time...) will give you 150 points.
    Points = 150
  • Master Chef (Mst)
    Send screenie for the Scav. Hunt thread with your chest and Rucksack filled with one of each type of food items
    There has to be 8+ types of food for it to count.
    (All need to be edible, so Raw Marshmallows and Doggy Bags don't count)
    Points = 200 points
  • E. Tuber (E.T)
    Post a youtube vid link of a ruin run you did at this mmj town on the Scav. Hunt thread.
    Points = 35 points for each different vid.
  • Meme2nite (Mem)
    Post a d2n meme at the d2n meme thread (link to that post at the Scav. Hunt thread)
    15 points, awarded just once.
  • Distinction Hoarder (DiH)
    Post a screenie of your death page showing all the distinctions you got on your mmj town.
    Each regular distinction is worth 1 point. Rare distinctions are worth 5. (Forum post distinctions are not counted)
    You have 1 day after the event is over (last person died) to post this screenie.
    Post more than one pic if there's too many distinctions and you can't fit them in one screenie
    Points = 1 per distinction (5 if Rare)
  • MMJ Champion (MMJ)
    Be the last to die in the MMJ event.
    Points = 1000 (can be awarded to more than one person if all die at the same time for last)
