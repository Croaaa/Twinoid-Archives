Some plum fool mentioned to a few other d2n players in a Facebook post that a d2n fan group might be a good idea. Chain went ahead and made the group. In 24 hours we went from a few people telling the d2n players they knew on facebook to spread out and reach over 80 people.
We are thinking that it would be a good place to gather people, plan jumps, maybe try to get last minute fillers and just discuss the game in general. Of course people are free to bring up anything a they think a group of d2n players might be interested in. Casual chat, banter, whatever. In other words; it will become what we make of it.
If you are reading this I'm guessing you are a fan of the game. Just go to https://www.facebook.com/groups/Cyani... and request to join the group. There are four admins. so you shouldn't have to wait long to be accepted.

Of course if you are on Facebook you should already know about the official d2n facebook page...https://www.facebook.com/die2nite If you didn't know about that you should check it out too.
