TL;DR
How to Play? 
 * {rps} + {d100}.
When Can I Replay?
 * 3 messages after my defeat.

Today, I'll be your host for the daily give away Joanne organized.

And the game will be a "King of the Hill".

Each player will use a Rock and a 47 in hope to take the place of the current king. I hope everybody know the rule. Paper beat Rock, Rock beat Scissor, Scissor beat Paper. In case of draw, if the d100's value is higher than the king, the player win. If the d100's value is equal or lower than the king d100, the player lose.
If a player win, he become the new king.

You are not allowed to play against yourselves. Of course. And you can play again right after you were defeated. You have to wait 3 messages after your last defeat. If you didn't beat the king, simply wait for 3 player then you can replay. If you become the king, you have to wait for someone to defeat you plus three other player.

The winner is the one with the longest streak. You start at streak 1 if you beat the current king and you get +1 for each people not winning against you. In case of equality, I'll sum the three worst d100. If it's still not enough, the last one who was the king will win.

If any part of the rule isn't clear, if you think it's too complex, that I my writing is the worst you have ever seen, if I missed anything, don't hesitate. Right here or send me a pm.

I'll be the first king o/
