XentiosWhat does it mean at 0/40 or 3/40.

It is the number of people in an 'available' Town who are there as part of a Coalition (there could be anywhere from 1 to 19 Coalitions in a Town) PLUS any Heroes who are there solo, but by choice.

As far as I know a Hero who jumps random and solo does not add to the coalesced number.
