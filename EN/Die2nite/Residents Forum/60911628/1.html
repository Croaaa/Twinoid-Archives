Well this is mostly a forum game. I'm not inside a town since October last year but i do check the forum at least once a day and post occasionally. That is kinda playing for me and i do have some bit of fun.

It is because of the forums, the community, all we achieved so far. The good thingies, the bad thingies...

- I guess it's no longer about the leaderboard at this point, eh?
Correct. Although you can still find some people playing just for that, even if they are no longer resetting the board.

And not only that, seasons should last a similar number of days so that everyone has even chances to figure on the top. In 27 of April, not long from now, it will be 2 years of Season 13. That's enough to just one top group to get most ranking positions there.

But you're right, no one should be caring about this anymore.

- Do you mainly play because you like the risk of camping?
Camping imo should be a late thing, unless you are into these zoo thingies and all. But for towns i used to play, where you always had a chance of dying from camping, i was always a fierce defender of end-game camping only (if not for BPs earlier).

- Is it about distinctions?
It is about having a good time, to remember what happened on each town. Distinctions can tell you that if you don't look for them. I used to be a good soul-reader, i could tell someone's play-style just by looking at their souls. Some are hard to read 'cuz they do force the earning of distinctions, but hey, at least it tells me: Alert! Distinction Whore up ahead.

- Do you play because you're hoping to find explorable ruins?
Last year my towns were just small map towns so no E.ruins. I used to be good at those, though. Lol i think my youtube channel is the one with more ruin mapping runs there is, although i never bothered to look for other recordings.

- Are you here mainly to see how long you and the town survive?
Kinda like that, but there are situations where premature death of town are even more remarkable than 30+ day towns. I honestly cant remember what happened on those towns i was alive for a month. Things can get pretty boring.

- Do you like the game primarily because each town involves 39 other people?
Yes! Jackpot!
And there's two sides of this coin. 39 totally unknown citizens or 39 same old faces. Some people like the former, i prefer a balanced mix.

- Are you here because this is the only game where you can be a ghoul and eat other people?
Ghoul is a french invention that kinda ruined things really. There were tons of stuff for evil/selfish play already, they should have favored other kind of features/fixes instead of this.

Also, being a ghoul today is kinda repetitive, everyone knows how to be an effective ghoul and how to catch one. No one dare trying special tricks or playing as a ghoul in a different way.

- Do you play only because you're secretly a lover of Flash games?
Nah, although if this was turned into an app of sorts i'd quit first thing.

What's your main interest in the game if you had to pick just one?
Kinda covered this one in my introduction.

sorry i like answering all thingies
