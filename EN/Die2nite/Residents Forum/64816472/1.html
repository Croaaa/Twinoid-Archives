And that's it.

Today (7 Oct. 2020) ends the MMJ event with Joanne and Superman32 dying for last in Yellow canyons

If you weren't tracking the event here's a summary of what the towns did:

  • Pale hills of dying flesh ended with 547 town points with the last player dying on day 22 (5x camping days) and built the Crow Statue and Wonder Wheel.
  • Wicked gang of corpses ended with 971 town points with the last player dying on day 30 (6x camping days) and built the Giant BRD (iDare even got 2 Aquas and 1x Chainsaw all by himself...).
  • Yellow canyons ended with 949 town points with the last player dying on day 32 (7x camping days
  • The tormented crevasse ended with 455 town points with the last player dying on day 23 (5x camping days).
  • Gloomy basement ended with 173 town points with the last player dying on day 9.

Congrats everyone!

So what's next?

We have 2 months until the death of Flash, but you can already go check the new Die2nite game called MyHordes. The game is still in alpha testing and many things are missing/were added, but it is playable and you can help improve it. There is a function to import your die2nite soul to the game too.

In MMJ you had a taste of how meta play worked. If you liked that you could try finding a group of meta players by posting in the World's Forum Recruitment Thread

You can even try gathering a bunch of people you meet at the event and create your own group to start playing together. Many metas of the old times emerged from MMJ events.

Keep hanging around the Discord Channel for updates on how MyHordes is going and anything related to Die2nite.
