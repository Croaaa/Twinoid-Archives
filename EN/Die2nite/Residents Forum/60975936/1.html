I can tell you that when you arrive in a zone with people you gain +1cp for each person, but that's not the case if they arrive in a zone after you, so for example you reach 2 stuck peeople and add to their cp +2cp bonus, then escort them into the next zone which means you entered it first and only then followed by any escortees, you lose the 2cp aka 1cp per escortee which you wouldn't lose if they are online and move ahead of you after you reached their zone with you following and arriving last
