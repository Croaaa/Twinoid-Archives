First of all you should consider: Is it good for the town if I kill the ghoul? Isn't it part of the town's planning? Has anyone claimed to be a "good" ghoul that will take care of griefers/selfish/inactive/corpses ?

Remember: Killing a ghoul won't give you any reward. You will just spend 3AP that day. Sometimes you might even get trapped chasing a ghoul and end up dying for nothing.

People go too much on "Awmigods!! a ghoul is afoot! Prepare the torches lets burn it to its core!!" that they forget to care about themselves. After all, if there is a real nasty ghoul around, its first and main target will be the person that will claim that will go hunt it.

Nasty ghouls go for clever people first, so here is your first hint: lay low. Don't make posts saying "oh a ghoul in town yadayada...", just do your detective thing and let others spray the salt on themselves.

Second hint is: The Gazette is your friend. Ghouls are often caught when they slip outside/inside undetected or do some actions that are recorded near the time of it's victim's death.

Take note of the time of the death and compare it with each of your neighbors activity on their houses. Discard people that wasn't online after the fact. For the others, check their actions: was he/she outside? what is he/she doing with their AP? is he/she updating external map? etc...

Sometimes it takes more victims in order to thin out the suspects. A really careful ghoul can, and possibly will, go unpunished. If you are down to a handful of suspects and the victims are still increasing, making you a next possible target, then its kinda OK to go and punch all of those, right?
