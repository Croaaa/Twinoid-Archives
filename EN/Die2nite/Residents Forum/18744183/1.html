spacekadt

Coro's right. A report about griefers generally doesn't get a response.

But also, be sure that you're reporting *griefing*, not just selfish play. If someone's taking all the drugs to go on an epic expedition, that's selfish. If they're opening the gate at the last second in hopes of getting LMS, that's selfish.

If they're taking crucial supplies out of the bank and dropping them in the wastelands just so the town can't build something, that's probably griefing.

The line seems to be: Does the player get something out of it? LMS, distinctions, ability to wander the desert? That's selfish. Is the player just as screwed by his actions as the rest of the town? That's probably griefing and worth sending the report.
