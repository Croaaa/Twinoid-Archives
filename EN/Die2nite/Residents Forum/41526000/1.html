Which day are you at?

The town needs to have survived for at least 5 full days first, or no one can get LMS. Also if the town is in devastated status, you couldn't earn LMS either.

As long as you stay and die in town during the zombie attack, it does not matter whether you die on watch or die on the bed. However the death has to happen during the zombie attack. That means you couldn't commit suicide, die from dehydration, die from withdrawal etc. Those deaths do not count as survival for a full day.

I don't believe there is a change in LMS mechanism.
