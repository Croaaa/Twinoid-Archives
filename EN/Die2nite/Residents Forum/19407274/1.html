Nights are long and they needed some distraction from zombies groans outside the town wall. Frozy found a gun and some bullets out scavenging today. He took good old six shooter, loaded it with a bullet and raised it on his forehead. I will win this, I will live. Little did he know, you just can't win this game. Bits of brains from last players on the wall confirmed it.

RULES:

1) Player will make a call out to the last winner
2) Caller shoots first
3) Shooting works like this:
Round starts with 6 cambers and counts down by one with every shot (ie: 6 chambers, 5 chambers, 4 chambers, ...).
4) Players takes turns shooting, one who dies, loses
5) You have to roll following to shoot:
6 chambers = 1 (roll 1 and your dead)
5 chambers = 3 (roll 1 or 2 and your dead)
4 chambers = 3 (roll 1 and your dead)
3 chambers = Paper (rock and your dead)
2 chambers = Tails (heads and your dead)
1 chambers = well, you don't have to roll, your dead
6) If you die you can not compete again (because your dead *duh*)
7) start from rule no 1
extra rule - If you don't play by the rules, you won't play at all!
