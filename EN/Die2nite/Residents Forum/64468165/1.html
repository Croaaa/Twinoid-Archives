Hello everyone

It was already mentioned here and there on the forum. As part of the Eternal Twin project, there is a game called "MyHordes" beeing developed by Brainbox and some other devs to replace hordes/die2nite/dieverdammten/zombinoia with mostly a direct copy after the shutdown of flash and the Twinoid-games at the end of 2020.

At the moment we are in alpha-testing stage, but the game is very playable already. Development is going fast with updates every couple of days.

We always need people for testing and reporting feedback, but also developers who wanna contribute are welcome.

Currently there are mostly the french and germans engaged with this project, so we kinda lack translations to english as well as a general representation of the die2nite folks in the community.

If you have interest in checking it out, regardless if you wanna just play around or contribute something, use this link to create an account: https://zombvival.de/myhordes

There are also already public forums there

Welcome everyone

Patat

Almost no substantive issue has been addressed.
The method used is to get your hands dirty directly so as not to lose motivation, and to code what most looks like a current hordes, without everyone agreeing on what it means, nor whether the doctrine of "coding a current hordes" is the most suitable.

The initiative, the volunteer efforts of the devs are commendable, I admit I was amazed by the progress of the project and there really seems to be voluntary energies.
But for me, it is obvious that it will go straight into the wall if MyHordes does not initiate a radical change in the way of thinking about the fundamental questions: which mode of play do we want to promote? is the forum the heart of a city? will rots be treated as on d2n? what difficulty for pandés? .... etc.

I answer him here because i think it can asnwer questions some players have too.

I can't answer everything, i'm not the spokesperson for the devs so I can't give "a dev review" without asking the others (each person has their own voice), and then among the devs , I am perhaps the one who has the least technical or logistical knowledge (see even expression, I have problems on that)

However, I will prepare a block (which some will not read) with the other devs that are available (because some are on vacation, or are unavailable because of ... well real life ).
We will try to answer as much as possible of what people have asked (in the other topics, in the other game variants and also on the Discord) but also to tell you how we intend to develop the project, why and how you think it can be improved. We may be devs but in my opinion yes it is necessary to say "well we plan to do like that, what do you think? It sucks?"

We cannot reproduce the current interface to the nearest pixel either, for example. Our version is intended to be multi-lingual and mobile compatible, "which is not Hordes & Co". Due to its age, differences are necessary.

In addition, MT itself was making interface changes. Who spotted the disappearance of the belt between the two pulleys for example?

Multilingual cities will be available (currently it is only accessible in VP). Perhaps an AI-powered machine translation system will be implemented (provided players don't use shorthand words like AP / etc). If this causes too much concern vis-à-vis that we will remove (just like the possibility of carrying two bags for example, we have removed that).

In addition, we had access to a list of modifications / rebalances which should have taken place in S14 and some did not and we intend to take them into account for the development. The existing bugs on the original game will also be fixed (the time change for example). To re-introduce them would be nonsense. So just for these points anyone can say "MyHordes is not the same as Hordes" .

By the way, the game is called "MyHordes", not because it's supposed to be a copy of Hordes specifically. By that I mean that the name can very well be changed later. It's just that because Brainbox named it like that because 'MyDieVerdammten' or the other names didn't sound good.
Well why not call the game "Hordes" or "Hordes 2.0".
This is confusing for you, because you may believe the game was made by MT or something like that, this is why Mush -> eMush, same for HammerFest -> EternalFest, Twinoid -> Etwin. And then when it comes to copyright, we prefer to avoid using the same name as the original game.

I can also refer you to what is listed on the MyHordes home page .
In other words, MyHordes is:

a fan project, made by people from the community
the source code is available to everyone on Gitlab. Anyone can make a copy of MyHordes and improve it however they want. He can even suggest changes to put in MyHordes. He can very well make his own "more conformal" copy if he wishes too.
the goal is to do it without Flash, and if possible responsive for mobile
we try to the maximum , so that it is identical to the original game. Not only "Hordes.fr" but also to the other variants of the game, so yes it is complicated, yes it will not be 100% feasible. But we try to do all of that in the same game.
when the original game closes, we will launch seasons like MT did before and we will do "new content".
apart from the people in the 'Programming' tab at the bottom right, and a few contributors on Gitlab not named for the moment, we may have a maximum of 10 people, we are not pros / experts in this or that field . And we don't work at 10 all the time on it.

On the other hand, I can say that from the devs until we have made the big thread;

Our current approach is to ask the community, see how they see these possible problems / solutions and in the end, implement what seems to have the best support from the community.
The alternative would be to just do "something", and hope everyone is okay with it.
Which in our opinion would be an even better way to smash the project into a wall ^^
The most reason of these issues are not covered in MyHordes yet is that we want to give the community as much time as possible to chat, so as long as there are other important things to do, we are waiting for the community
We are accused of having lied or whatever about the fact "MyHordes is a perfect copy of Hordes", but we have never promised this and have in fact stated our intentions to bring all the versions of Hordes together on multiple occasions.
