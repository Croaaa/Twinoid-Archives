Rain

If someone is taking things out into the desert to die with them specifically for the purpose of hurting the town with no gain to themselves, you can report that person for griefing. That sort of gameplay is against the rules, and you won't encounter as much of it once you get 100 soul points and get to distant region towns.

If, however, they are doing things like taking all the boosts or weapons and going out into the desert to do whatever they want, that is considered selfish play, and well within the viable ways for someone to choose to play.

I'd suggest you go here and sign up for the MMJ. It's a once a season community event where a bunch of players who want to play a town well without being in a meta town sign up and join towns at the same time, and a great way to see some different gameplay than you've seen thus far. Also, it'll help you get out of those small map towns much quicker if you stay alive in it.
