Ravenclaw

Getting people online and working at same time can be difficult especially across different time zones.

Why not make a automatic expedition system?

This will comprise of the following steps

Step 1:
Expedition has a Team Leader that declare he leading a expedition.

Step 2:
Team leader sets a minimum requirements for application, eg minimum AP, and if person needs food and or water.

Step 3:
Players Sign up to join the expedition leader and they become a volunteer, they need to meet the requirements to join, if leader want everyone to have water and you don't, then you can't sign up.

Step 4:
Leader picks who will come from the list on volunteers availble.

Step 5:
Expedition begins when leader leaves the gates...

During this stage the leader then responsible for everyones action, basically whatever the leader does all the volunteers mirror the same action, so if leader searches everyone searchs, when leader drinks everyone drinks.

For this reason volunteers need to be able to place there trust as bad leader might get them killed, however a good leader will allow a group safty in numbers, and it will not require everyone to be online at the same time.

Hacksaw

Making it so only heroes can escort people is MT's way of trying to encourage people to buy hero mode.

Problem is, escort is primarily only useful in meta towns, where most/all of the people being hero is already preferred. Even metas that like/employ residents know that all/mostly hero metas have distinct advantages.

Primary reason it isn't as helpful in random towns is it requires the random to know about the function, trust that his townmates will have his best interests in mind, and log back in again to make use of the escort function on the same or subsequent days. Many times (especially in newbie towns) this doesn't happen.

Even in distant towns where we would like to think people know to log back in again, many of them still don't come back, making escort still not so useful.
