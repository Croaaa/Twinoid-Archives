I've had people request me to make this topic in the Residents Forum before, although that was a few years ago. Here it is.

Many of you will already know about this (either because you were smart enough to find it on your own or because I once pointed it out to you in the (former) Saloon (about two years ago)....
...but I'm betting there may still be a few on here who don't yet know about this feature, and here it is:


Hover over your name in the top, right hand corner of this page (so it brings the motiontwin sidebar into view, the one that shows your messages).

Next, click "CONTACTS"..at the top, second row, second thing over.

In the box that pops up, click the tab "Show people who've added you".

Ta da!
How many people do you see/have there? :p

Unless you already knew about it or remember to check it often, you might have more than a couple.
