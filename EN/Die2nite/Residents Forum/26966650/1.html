You can lock individual cells, to avoid such troubles.
It isn't straightforward (at least wasn't when I fail-proofed my camp calculator), but can be done.

Basically:

  • share the spreadsheet as "view only"
  • for each range you wish to protect:
    • create a Named Range
    • mark it as protected
  • share the spreadsheet as "edit" for everyone
