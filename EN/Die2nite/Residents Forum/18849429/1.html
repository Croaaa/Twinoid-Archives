If I build it and then run out of hero days do I lose it's bonus'?
On november 29, 2012
ChooJeremy

Yes. Once you lose your hero days you lose all bonuses that require 'activation', for example, kitchen, siesta, drug lab.

You keep all bonuses that are passive, such as fence, extra storage.

Yes, only passive hero improvements to your house like reinforcements, fence, or more storage will retain their use when your hero days run out.

If you but more hero days later on in the same town you will be able to use Siesta Time and such again.
