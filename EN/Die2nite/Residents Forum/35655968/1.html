In preparation for the forthcoming season of misery and torment, we thought we'd give you all another chance to leave your mark on Die2Nite forever, and get your hands on some Hero Days in our Wilderness Wordsworth contest! All texts featured in the game will win a minimum of 2 weeks of Hero mode! One star prize winner will receive much more...

You can enter up to 3 texts using the Competition Entry Box on the support site, or on our Official Die2Nite Facebook Page which will all be considered before the next season begins. Closing date for entries is Feb 24th.

Texts should be under 100 words in length, and can be short stories, diary entries, poems, playing tips, riddles.. whatever really! Have fun with it, make it fun to find and you could be part of the game forever.

Citizens! To your antiquated writing machines! Good luck!
