Guys, let's get a few things clear.

Mods can *only* ban players. A ban means that a player cannot post to the forums. For anything 31 days and above, it's a global ban and affects every Twinoid site. (Well, we can assassinate players out of towns, but you've now seen why we don't do that. It just lets the player grief even more towns.)

Deleting an account is not a ban and can only be done by an actual MT Employee (either admin or dev).

The griefing rules are that a person is banned for 2 weeks along with a warning message that they've received their warning. Additional griefing beyond that will be escalated to MT for account deletion. There's only so much that we mods can do here. We just don't have this kind of power (and truthfully, I don't want it).
