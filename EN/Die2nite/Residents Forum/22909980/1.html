for this game and future games...
for those who dont or cant play much, if you want to scavange...

there usualy a hero or two in every random town...

at the start of the game link up with that hero form a coalition
then all ya gotta do is go outside town after the attack and put your self on escort mode - click the box to allow yourself to go away from town and have 1 food and 1 water in your pack (the food cannot be inside a doggy bag if you dont allow your escort to access your inventory) he will take you along on his scavange route and bring you right back inside town with himself

the advantages are
1- ghouls cant eat someone in a group outside without giving up their identity
2- more control points your are far less likely to be randomly stuck (a hero can escort multi player i have done it with up to four not sure if you can do more yet, doesnt seem to be a cap)
3- you auto deplete zones much faster and saves ap in the long run from not having to reenter those zones again
4- you dont have to use from dusk til dawn ever because your escort will do it for you
5- you dont have to log in until you want to or can (usualy only right after the attack period)
