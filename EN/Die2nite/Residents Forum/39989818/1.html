Bug again?

It is not intended to give LMS for people who can't complete the day.

According to the help:

to earn this distinction [LMS], three rules must be satisfied:

The town must have survived for at least 5 complete days,
The citizen must have been devoured during the Horde's attack which devastated the town,
The town can not have been devastated on a previous attack (a town is devastated if a night passes and there are no citizens within the town walls.).

a citizen who commits suicide, dies from dehydration on the last night or dies in the World Beyond, will not earn this distinction.

Similarly, a citizen who is camping in the World Beyond will not be given this distinction.

We did see some bugs in the past, like Chaos mode did not trigger even satisfying the requirements, watchmen items which should be broken disappeared sometimes (that bug re-occurred several times and MT claimed to fix it "several times" too). Both should have been fixed now.
