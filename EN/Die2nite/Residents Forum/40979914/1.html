First the IDs for poisoned and clean water rations are different. They are not the same.

Second the IDs are dynamic. They are randomized for different players and different connection sessions. Even for the same player, the IDs can be different in different sessions. Your IDs can be changed even in the same day if your session gets reset. If you keep the window open and the connection isn't interrupted, you should be fine.

There are two ways to detect poisoned water rations technically. First before you go inside the ruin, drop your water ration on your ground. Jot down the ID. Later when you find a water ration in the ruin, you may drop it on the ground and see the ID. If the ID match yours, that water ration is clean. Otherwise it is poisoned.

The second method is to simply drop the questionable water ration on the ground, and then update FDTD. FDTD will tell you whether the water ration is poisoned or not. Please do it one by one - 1 water ration at a time, or it won't work properly.
