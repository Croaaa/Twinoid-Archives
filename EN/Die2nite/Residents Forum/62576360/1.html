I have a dream...

....a dream of a town of ALL heroes!



I have mentioned this before, to a few of you.




Is it possible?


It would take a lot of work. It might take a few experienced (in a few areas) experts. I'd want only the best. It would take a good planner or two... It might take players who were good at EE.... I'd hope it would be players who were used to playing EVERY DAY, without fail.



And me.... what I'd provide...
...well, let's say this...

ANYONE WHO IS NOT ALREADY A HERO, I WOULD MAKE THEM ONE.

Yes, you read that right. I would provide MTPasses so that they could become a hero for 15 DAYS...
...and then, if the town was still going and their hero days ran out, but they were still alive... I'd send them four more codes to extend it.






But we'd need GOOD, RELIABLE players for this. Ones on every day. People who could work together.

Me, I'm not a leader and I don't like to plan...so it would need a planner who was good at it, maybe.


Any interest in this? An ALL-HERO town?

Sign up here if you think you'd want to participate. IF YOU ARE NOT A HERO, then I will make you one, simple as that.











HOWEVER, there are rules.


1. YOU MUST be active. You MUST be a player who visits and checks in and plays EVERY day.

2. You must use the codes I'd give you for this game, obviously.

3. PLAYERS MUST WRITE IN HERE THEMSELVES that they want to join. This is so I know they're active. I have added my own coa, just because I already know they're all active, so I'm allowed to do it.....
... I DON'T know that all of your coa is active, though, so they need to post in here signing up themselves, don't sign up for them.

3. You CANNOT be someone who is largely considered a troll, griefer, or anti-town*

*This is tricky, though... because it's all relative. Someone might think someone else (or evena whole group of players) is a troll, just because they disagree with the others style of play. There are many groups here, I've noticed, that seem to clash and go head to head in difference of opinion. Some are the more "let's play selfish and every man for themselves" way because the options are there. Is this the WRONG way to play? Are there any WRONG ways to play?


So, I can understand there being a debate over who might be a troll and for what reasons. Let's just say that I will take everything into account and I won't count anyone out, but...in this topic, if someone signs up who the majority of OTHERS in this topic say is a troll/griefer, I may say they can't sign up.


Also, I MAY be willing to do an ALL HERO town of just people who play in the "selfish" style somewhere down the line, just to see how interesting it would be.


Okay, here's the sign up sheet.
There's no spreadsheet. I hate those things. I like things being as simple as possible. Just sign up here if you want to be in it..... but realize that with how fast the game is, these days, it might be awhile before this starts.

I was thinking of setting up a date whenever there are enough people.

Might take awhile, though. By the time we get enough, most of the early signers on might not be playing anymore.

And heya, Dream! Nice to see you on these forums again..wish you'd come back and play regularly. :p There isn't any way I can entice ya, is there?


About your questions.... yes, I was planning to set up somewhere (maybe a server on Discord) to talk and stuff..

..but regarding spreadsheets and all that stuff.... I dunno, I'm more of a "have faith that others will do the right thing and be able to MOSTLY help the town"... in that, I just let the chips fall where they may.
I figure having a sign up is helpful, at the very least... it's just a list to see if enough are interested in this, though, it's not set in stone.

Truth be told, planning hardcore can only get you so far, and even a good plan and ten back up plans might fail.

I figured this would just be a "whoever can get in, gets in" type of town... like, a date will be agreed on and, on that day, whoever is ready and on can jump into it. Easy peasy. If we get randoms, oh well.. I don't mind. If we get people looking to troll or grief...well, it happens, I might hold another, then, just to make up for it.



But the whole signing up on a spreadsheet with exactly what you're gonna be and what powers you have and what your profile is and if you have this or if you have that and what your children are named and what your last five addresses were and the references of ten other players and your zip code................meh...it's just not needed. I never liked that sort of thing.
Simple sign up sheet works. I don't care what people pick when they pick a hero class. So many metas seem to say "okay, you can ONLY BE these things" and they don't like things like....scavengers. And there are rules like "one tech only" and "one scout only" and all that. I want to give people the freedom to pick any hero class they want... if everyone wants to be a scavenger.. I say go for it.

Me, I love being a scavenger... but here's the thing: Most metas see that class as totally useless. I tend to pick another one for meta towns, ones I know they like ... like tamer or survivalist or guardian. Those three seem to be the ones metas like the MOST...
..but see, I don't like playing as any of those classes... I like scavenger.


So, born from that......that whole "simple freedom" type of play... a simple sort of game where people mostly have freedom and are trusted to do the right thing.....
....came this idea.




Again, if I hold it and people mess it up... oh well. It's just a game. I can hold another. And another. And another.



I'm rich.
