When someone is on your contact list their name is blue; when they aren't their name is red.

It could be you are a person of interest to them because you post a lot in forums they frequent, so your name appearing as blue tells them you are the real joanne and you can be trusted.

Also, when looking for a town to join, more blue names means more people you've "liked" and are probably good people to be in town with.

Also, some games (Minster Mitel and Fever) only show the scores of people on your contact list (and you can only give guests in Minster Motel to oeople on your contact list), so more names = more scores to compare to.

Hacksaw

People on your list appear in blue.
People not on your list appear in red.
So if someone adds you, but you don't add them back, you'll appear blue to them and they'll appear red to you.
Note that you always appear blue to you (in case you haven't noticed ).

Edit: The opposite is also true. If you add them they'll appear blue to you, and if they don't add you back you'll still appear red to them.
