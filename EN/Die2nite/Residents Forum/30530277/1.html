I can't speak for sure on the counting down in a not full town, I imagine it's only when an attack happens, but I'm not confident enough to say with certainty.

As for bia'ing with 1 day left, no, you can't. Technically "1 day left" is your last day. It's odd phrasing, but that's how it is. Since you don't have any more days in the bank, you don't have anything to give away.
