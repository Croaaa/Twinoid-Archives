You can gain hero days by two ways:

  • getting LMS (Last Man Standing) in a Distant or Hardcore town. For this you have to be in the last group of people that die in town, and lucky enough to randomly get selected for LMS. Or be the only one inside before the town is devastated, then you'll definitely get it.
  • Become a ghoul and eat a couple fellow citizens. You get days for them too after you die in that town.

When you have hero days you have to use them right away. This means if you buy them while in a town, then you have to choose a job before you could continue playing; when you win them by one of the ways above then you'll have to start using them in your next town.

wood18

there are plenty of "jobs" in town even if you are only a resident.

-gatekeeper (just be consistently online before attack and make sure the gates get closed)

- short range or long range scavenging (with a little planning you can deplete some zones without getting stuck and then bring back essential goodies)

-town planner (take charge of the construction thread and make knowledgeable decisions on what should be built and in what order)

- detective (with some sleuthing using the gazette and looking at soul pages you can help the town weed out possible troublemakers and pesky ghouls)

- workshopaholic (since using the workshop doesn't give distinction titles, not too many people convert unless its desperately needed. you can make sure there are plenty of materials for all upcoming projects)

-zombie killer (with the right weapons and some risk management you can help keep the map clear for scavengers)

- on call rescue (whenever somebody posts a help thread be ready to affect a plan to help them out)

- the entertainer (keep those forums active with creative threads and witty posts)



disclaimer - towns with a high number of heroes and/or experienced players may limit the availability and usefulness of some of these jobs...
