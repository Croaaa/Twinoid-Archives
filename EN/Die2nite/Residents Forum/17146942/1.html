Being the first one in
Tamers since S5 are pretty much obligatory Ruin first runners. Maybe a Scav with a good space sense and memory, but Tamers are the ones meant for that.

Mapping
FDTD mapping feature or a good spreed. Anyway, if you plan not worrying about noting down the rooms, a screen capture soft is pretty much needed. I'd suggest Screencast O-Matic or aTube Catcher screen capture in-built feature.

The differences between the types of ruins (Bunker, Hotel, Hospital)
Is there any functional difference aside the preset BPs pool? I never noticed any, maybe rooms disposition?

Poisoned Items
Never take Water Rations or normal food inside the Ruin. Never take ANY outside too, the only safe ones are Doggy Bags and Water Coolers.

BP's
Don't need input, bring back every BP you find as long as the pool for that rarity within the presets is already filled.

Technicians
Go there only when the Ruin is fully mapped or the last locked room(s) need a trip there just for them.

Planning your Route
When possible, always plan it in a circle motion, go to left after the exit, get back there by the right, and vice-versa. If not, make the route nearest to a circle as possible.

Running into a zombies(s) "To flee or not to flee"
4-5, only kill if you have a Chainsaw or other multi-killing weapon. If you only got a Mk.II, run.
1-3, kill them.
Special high ping connection cases: dafuq you're doing in the Ruins???
