You probably already know but Season 12 starts on the 7th of December
To start off each new season the community organise a mega-metajump, where as many players as possible organise into coalitions and join distant towns at the same time; competing to see who lives longest, etc.
It is a lot of fun, and a great intro to organised play if you've only ever jumped randomly before.
D2N when you can get past week 2 is a different game alltogether, everyone should experience it at least once

Signup for this season is at the MMJ Thread In the resident's forum. click 'more forums' next to where it says 'YOUR TOWN NAME HERE' at the top-left of this page, then click Reident's forum to get there without using my url.
If you have any questions for the organisers or just want to hang out you can find the mmj chatroom https://client01.chat.mibbit.com/#MMJ or at #mmj on irc.mibbit.net

I hope to see you all there
