2 Christmas seasons ago.

I received 3 (at the same family get together) of my signature perfume.
Estée Lauders "beautiful" I was delighted. The gifts were purchased at macys and were wrapped identically. And they were all the same set. Large perfume bottle. Lotion. Shower gel and a small purse bottle. The givers were embarrassed. But I was delighted!
They all know me well.

When I was 6 I was fostered out for the holidays. I had a great Christmas. And when I was returned Home I had a carload of gifts. 2 other siblings were fostered out too. It was typically childless couples who wanted to treat poor kids to a fantastical Christmas.
They also wanted beautiful kids. Looking back I can see the motivations. But not then of course.
Anyway. I return home ready to show off my bounty to the other 9 siblings. Every one was excited to see my new toys. And I was doing the typical little mine, mine, mine thing.
My mom went into her room an when I opened the door I saw her crying. Which was rare enough to panic me. She hugged and told me she wished she could have spent Christmas with all her children and she was sad that she couldn't give the kids that weren't chosen for fostering a better Christmas too. So this lesson in humility. I gave my sibling most of thr toys.
