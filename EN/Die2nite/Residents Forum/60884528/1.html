While I wait to die I'm curious to read about your ghoul stories. Regardless of whether you were the ghoul, if something interesting happened do tell.

I was in a random town in several seasons ago where there were a few people who were signing in every day and getting water and doing nothing, and there were a few who were signing in every other day and getting water and barely contributing. Then we ended up with a ghoul. Somehow we found out who the ghoul was, and instead of adding a ghoul to our town's list of problems we figured out a way to not get eaten, let the active ghoul live, and get rid of the dead weight.

We had a discussion in the forum about what to do with the ghoul. Someone suggested the ghoul eat the inactives so we could make better use of our resources. Those of us who were participating in the forum thread agreed, including the ghoul, and each day we voted on someone for the ghoul to eat the following day. The ghoul gave plenty of time for the next day's victim to read the forum and say, "Hey, I'm active, don't eat me" but that never happened, and the ghoul was never punched. We got rid of probably at least 4 people that way.

SteveFrench

I remember when ghouls were first introduced and there was a ton of things you could eat to give you a chance at turning into a ghoul. Random towns used to be rampant with ghouls so me and a full coalition became ghoul hunters and would hunt and kill ghouls, role playing as such in the forums- who ya gonna call? The Ghoulhunters! I remember one slippery little ghoul in particular who got scared we'd figured him out and ran off into the desert, this was not enough. We gave chase and on the second day we caught and killed him, victoriously, we returned home and I have a very specific memory that there was no parade once we got there, despite subtle requests. It was a thankless job, but all the detective work, secret communication and the ultimate satisfaction of catching your perp and cleaning up your city, makes it a fond memory for me from die2nite... It's just a shame having such a huge number of ghouls all the time ruined the rest of the game
