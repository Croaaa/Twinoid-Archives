fdamned26
If you want to live... Follow me!

9AP diamond day 1 is very common.

It means using daily 6AP, eating 6AP food, walking 3AP,
then walking 3AP back to town, drinking 6AP water,
going back to town, which is really 9AP out, 9AP back.

Looks like this:
http://i.imgur.com/VR7ekg7.png

Bring items towards the town, not towards the desert.

Prioritize good items (there is a list)

Priority Items to bring back on Day 1, 2 & 3:

Incomplete shopping Trolley, Manbag, Ultra Rucksack
Semtex, Detonator, Duct Tape, plastic bags (for Exploding Water Bombs)
Radius Mark II, Raduis Radar Beacon
Kwik-fix, Broken electric Device
FatCat, Hurling stick, serrated knife, Box Cutter
Furious Kitten (do not use it outside at all, survivalists will need them for far away expeditions)
Bandages or Chests if you can´t open them in the desert
Box Cutter use it with Fat Serpant in the desert if possible to get a Furious Kitten
Flash Powder only 4 are needed for 4 x Flash Granade
Water Purifying Tablets + Jerrycans
All kinds of powerful weapons you can build: mower, machete, chainsaw, Puta mk2, devastator.
1 Broken Staff, 1 Wire Reel & 1 Empty Oil can only one of each nothing more is needed first 4 days.

Read the forum, ask for help, join the town IRC chat, if there is one.

Try to help, that's what this game is all about.

If you get trap, stay calm, put escort mode on
and ask for help in the forums with:

[HELP] [Day 1] Trapped at x/y with N zombies

I have N ap left, there is N zombies at x/y.
I have full escort on and XYZ items with me.
I have N cp, I need M cp more or M zombies killed to move out
or a flash grenade or camera flash


Activate plugins under "settings",
then update FDTD and MapViewer external maps, very important,
this way we know where the items are.

For more information, read this thread:
http://twd.io/e/JSFv0w/0

or ask more questions
and welcome to the game

Assuming you are in the 'vote Watchtower on Day 1' camp:

If your town can't even build both WT and WS on the first day, then it should be fine to only have WT, as long as the WS is the first thing completed on the next day. By then you will know what kind of town you're in and can decide whether to do 9AP scavenging or lead in building.

But on Day 1, I would say definitely go 9AP out, especially if you are one of the first citizens (higher chance of someone rescuing if you need help, and more AS otherwise). If you are late to join the town, you can save your AP till as late as you can be online to see if anyone needs help, and otherwise build.

There are more people that understand that WS and WT have to be built on Day 1 than people who understand the importance of 9AP scavenging.
