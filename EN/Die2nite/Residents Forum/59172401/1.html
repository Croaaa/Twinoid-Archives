And the town has ended. Points of the event can be found from post above ^

Little about the event, tasks and points:

teams were formed before town. Participating to tasks wasn't mandatory, as you can see from the amount of teams. Teams were good mixes of residents and heroes and they were, mostly, created over coalition borders.

Each team ready and set before the town started got 12 points and purely arbitrarily BigSis gave 1 extra points for those teams who were ready way before others.

Teams didn't know anything about the tasks beforehand, each task was revealed at the start of the day.

Day 1 task: Bring me horizon!

First day was naturally about mapping. Teams got points for finding buildings (almost purely based on luck).

Other tasks, which was ongoing during the whole town, was promoting D2N on any social media and posting about the event to World Forum. Both of the tasks were once only.

And also the super secret diary room was opened! Each person writing a diary entry got 1 point. This also was an ongoing task, so there was 4 easy points daily for each team, 1 point per post.

Day 2: Plank challenge

Bringing in planks. Extra points for posting a picture about yourself planking. This was easy points for fastest people and naturally Tamers ruled the day.

Day 3: What a nice morning to set up a tent!

Points for each tent built either on day 3 or previously. No extra points for posting a pic of morning tents.

Day 4: Make chat great again!

Points for being social and joining town chat

Day 5: Iron challenge

Similar to plank challenge plus extra points of posting picture of yourself ironing (some very good shots!)

Day 6: It's party time!

Points for bringing in drugs, alcohol and food. We had a good evening

Days 7 and 8: Break a leg!

Points for succesfull ruin runs and zombie killing.

Day 9: Day as a McGyver

Points for building items outside. Taking items from Bank for this purpose was forbidden and naturally no one was mixing Pharms in the desert.

Day 10: Saturday Night Fever

Teams presents 3 songs from 3 different categories:
-song I'd take to deserted island
-song which describes the town the best
-teams victory song

Other teams gave votes for the songs and the winning songs from each category got points

Day 11: Purely based on luck!

You all know the good old forum game about rolling d6? Thought so. Teams got points for killing others in the game during day 11.

Day 12: Love is in the air!

..or is it? High arts: poetry. Writing a poem to someone in town. Three best poems got points.

Day 13: Literacy feast

More highbrow cultural stuff. Some questionable books which managed to get through MiniTrue were raffled.
No points from this.

Day 14: No place like home

Building a house or having house built already.

Days 15, 16, 17 and 18: Taking the Black

Mainly about joining the Watch, some extra points for various little tasks, as well as surviving to the next day.

Day 19: The End

Last three citizens probably partied and pillaged the whole town. And 1 extra point for lucky LMS

- - - -

That's it, in short. It's been a pleasure for watching over you, dear citizens.
I also hope there was happy cheering to each team equally from the community

And congratulations to the winners!
