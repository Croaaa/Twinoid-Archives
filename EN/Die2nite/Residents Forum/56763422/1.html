Inspiration for the people

The number of lazy zeds who do not attack citizens is not related to the current day . Its only related to the number of zeds who pass through the towns defence and watchmen. If you want to increase the numbers of lazy zeds and with it have less zeds attacking citizens , then you need to not break the conditions required to activate the lazy zeds rule for positive results which are:

  • exactly 10 must be inside
  • the number of zombies who pass through the gates and watchmen needs to be as high as possible , why? Because it was noticed that you reach minimum number of deaths due to low number of attacking zeds when All zeds pass through the gates and none gets stopped. It is not confirmed 100% since there were also few positive results when those 2 conditions were broken, but if we look at statistics then having 10 inside , not 9 nor 11. Exactly 10 and an open gates give minimum number of deaths amoung those 10 assuming they have high home defence of course from Defensive objects and upgrade...
