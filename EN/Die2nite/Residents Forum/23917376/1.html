My story about how my town crumbled due to ANZAC badges.


Now, battles have been fought on water, in the air. Been fought about money, land. Been lost on account of a lack of food, water, toilet paper. War.... war changes. Its changed. It is now a historical term and a concept now rather than a reality.
One ironic bloodbath happened in The Foothill of Knives all due to the mysterious ANZAC Badge.

It all started that morning before the downfall of that fateful town. Zelgadis, a happy go lucky scavenger was being cautious around the outskirts of town. He spotted a chest in the distance in the sandstorm. He ran to the shimmer in hopes of finding rare loot. Instead, he tripped and gashed his ankle on an unforeseen pain in his side. Before the blood even hit the sand, Zeds stumbled after him. Picking up the proverbial thorn, he ran in the opposite direction upon spotting the 200 or so zombies heading his way.

He strolled into town, fiddling with strange pricked metal, feeling somewhat curious as to what it even was. His head tilted up, spying out of the corner of his eye Peterta trying to "play" the strange instrument. He looked to his left and Embracie was trying to cut soulsurvivor555's sandwiches with one of them. Then, in the town square, redbull97, Kovi and liderb were all trying to repair the constructions with the sharp things. Everyone who was still alive was trying to figure out what it was. Everyone had one.

The newly elected Mayor, Brendihni, was less than pleased about the lack of work and the climbing rate in cuts and bleeding. He organized a town meeting to discuss their purpose once and for all.
Some cried that they were religious headwear, worn by those who worshipped the old world religion known as "The Watchmen." Others thought it was a missing piece of a record player. One argued the idea of it being some sort of cutlery and was barked down heavily.

Talks ended when HereticSquirrel ate one, thinking it was a food capsule from the future.
Brendihni grew tired of the mess of a meeting that was held and decided to store them inside a shipping container outside the town, stating they would be used as defenses for the nights attack. Everyone moaned at the idea. Except HereticSquirrel who has slashed his vocal chords in the process of swallowing his yellow thing.

Zelgadis and fun883 kept one back to talk about on their turn as Watchmen. They completely shut the world out while they dissected the smiley faced oddity.
While they whittled away, the rest of the town sat awake, waiting for Brendihni to fall asleep.
Y'see, the town has had some legends walk in and out from time to time but in the last few days the towns entertainment had been diminished to hitting holes into Jarpen tables with sticks. So playing with the unidentifiable watcha-ma-call-its really pleased the people. The temptation to steal them back was felt by almost everyone.

When the mayoral snores travelled the airwaves after hours in wait, the residents snuck to the gates and burst through like kids breaking into a candy store. This candy store was filled with Zeds sadly. The screams and thuds of the newly found slaughter grabbed the Watchmen's attention.
They stood, mouths gaping at the massacre. People were using other peoples limbs as weapons. Already 6 were dead and being eaten. If they didn't act fast enough the Zeds would then run the town red. Zelgadis simply looked at fun883 and they both snuck to the sides of the entrance and shut the gates up again.

Mr_Technology, the last left alive on top of the container, sweating claret. Swore in vain, cursing the portal lock to all hell! He took a look all around, he was sure to be eaten soon enough. He slipped a few of the stabby items and clenched his fingers neatly around them like knuckle dusters. Leaping into the fray like wolverine, he clawed and killed about 5 of the flesh biters before biting the dust himself.

By this point, the 8 left in town watched them tear into Mr_Technology like ducks fighting over bread. In the chaos, Zelgadis looked at the back of the item. It said "ANZAC Badge" quite plainly in view. He pricked it onto his uniform and gazed into the bloodfest.
Brendihni noticed the fashion genius. "So its a badge?"
Zelgadis nodded and replied "An ANZAC badge actually." Brendihni paused before yelling with force "SO WHAT THE HELL DOES ANZAC MEAN!?"
fun883 dispersed the entire undead crowd with one easter egg, wearing three badges across his jacket. "Beats me."
