DarkTheRaven

It really depends on the player, i've had to drop multilple members as a coalition leader, the main things i do to soften the blow is give them 3 days just to tie loose ends with members, then either they leave during these 72 hours or i kick them, also, it's quite important to keep contact in my opinion send them a message stating the reason(s) why they were kicked and encourage them to ask any questions they have, my coalition also has a charter which has a section about inactivity it's good to let people know how long they can be inactive for, otherwise they tend to become either violent claiming you to be judgemental, or emotional begging for another chance in my experience obviously not everyone is like that. I hope these help and if you have any other questions you can contact me via twiniid or forums
