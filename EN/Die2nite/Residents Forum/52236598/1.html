Unfortunately this is only a 2 player game. Would be cool if people gave any ideas on how to let more players be included.

At the start of the game a card is picked, the suit of that card is the "trump suit" (say a four of spades is picked, the trump suit is spades) This suit can beat any other card of a different suit, but cannot beat a higher card of the same suit. (A four of spades can beat an ace of diamonds, but not a jack of spades) The person who goes first attacks with three cards, and the other player defends with six. The defending player can put unused trump cards in their bank. Example: Player A attacks with a 7 of diamonds, a 10 of clubs, and a 4 of hearts. Player B defends with a 3 of clubs, an 8 of diamonds, a jack of hearts, a 5 of spades, a king of clubs, and a 2 of hearts. Since Player B can beat Player A's cards without using his trump card, the 5 of spades goes in the player's bank, if Player B needs it to defend later on, he can use it. Also if a player cannot beat an attack that included a trump, the defeated player obtains the trump in their bank.

The goal of the game is to bring your opponent's life down to 0.
