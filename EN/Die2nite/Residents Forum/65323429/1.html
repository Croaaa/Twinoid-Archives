Inspiration for the people

Its the end of 2020 and with it the end of Die2nite Era in just few days.

It was a fun ride for about 9 or 10 years for me. about 12 years or more for the game and its veterans. and it was a blast.

The fun , excitement, competition, conflicts , struggle, strategies, chaos, panic, stress, relief, luck and best of all the nice community with the good and bad from all over the World who made this game experience an amazing unique experience i will never forget.

we might not know when the heart of this game will stop in 2021 but sadly we know its coming and its very soon with the death of flash and MT Pulling the Plug, so we might as well say our good byes while we still can.
Discord will still be there for the community, but this forum will no longer exist.

Big Thanks to All who i have met, played or competed with throughout all those years
especially those who put up with my nagging, controlling, micromanagement and sometimes aggressiveness.
I am proud and honored to call each and every one of you a friend and a teammate.

only the future will tell if we will meet in the future Version of this game MyHordes which is currently in the beta phase. and i encourage everyone to check it out immediately to mainly see the amazing hard work being done by the guys over there and all the volunteers helping them out and also to see that the end of d2n/hordes/DV/Zombinoia is the beginning of MyHordes.
Also as it was mentioned in another thread in WF to Transfer your Souls, Achievements and Hero Perks Unlocked in the original game before the Server gets shut down by MT so that if you end up trying out the new version you wont start all over from zero.

Fair Well Die2Nite!
May we meet again!
