That's right, die2nite was meant to be Random towns only. Pretty much what you experienced on seasons 1 and 2.

With the advent of metas, decent players were focused to a handful of towns or less per season, lowering the quality of Random towns to the point of turning them unpracticable.

And it has become a vicious cycle. Decent players looking for decent towns needed to join a meta.

The devs of this game even tried to stop this gaming behavior but failed. Maybe that's one of the reasons they abandoned us.

So yeah, it is true. Today if you want a good time, you play meta. However, by doing that, you are contributing to this game's premature death.

Do whatever your conscience can withstand.
