Scavenger Hunt Game 30 Hero Days to The WINNER

To Compete in the Scavenger Hunt you must sign up on that particular seasons Scavenger Hunter Thread. You will be required to take a screenshot of your soul page at the beginning of the season and another screenshot at the end of play. You will also be required to fill out and complete tasks on the GHOST SHEETS™ and post it on the Hunter Thread at end of play. Play begins at the beginning of season and ends at Date (June 30 2013) Noted or end of season (which ever is less).

The Main point is to Hunt Down other Players. You collect players by being in town with them. We don’t wont HUNTERS joining a town and dieing off. So YOU have to Survive 4 days, your soul page must show this, to get credit for being in a town with anyone on the HUNTED list. ONLY HUNTERS have to survive 4 days for HUNTED, word towns, hero towns, wonder/reactor towns, event towns, and video towns. Remember Hunting People Gets you the most Points

You are a Supreme Hunter, Alpha Class Predator, Top of the Food Chain, Resident with a BRD and this list, etc.
You have 110+ days to complete Tasks within die2nite and come out as the Top Predator.
You get 30 Hero Days for this, bragging rights, adulation of your peers, sand balls to the head!!

The Game and Points ARE NOT FAIR. Its been set up so that Hunting people gets the most points. Its been set up so its almost impossible to complete all task. Its also set up so that some task make other task almost impossible to complete. You are a Hunter so suck it up and do your best or worse. Act Like a Supreme Predator, it is your job to Hunt people down. NO BEGGING or SPAMMING, especially to those on Hunted list and video makers. Some Hunted will help you out, some will make you cry, and we want to see you cry. The Game and Points ARE NOT FAIR.

Scoring:
You have 110+ days or Season End (whichever is Less) to complete tasks
Some Task require you must survive in town at least 4+ days for it to Count
You can Get Bonus Points but they only count when you complete all 10 GHOST SHEETS™ tasks

Each task is worth 1 point. There are 10 tasks you need to complete every Season:

Bonus points can only be achieved if you complete your 10 tasks

[ ] Survive in 10+ days in 5 towns or more

[ ] Collect Multiple Names off Hunted List. Every 2 extra names 3 points
You must Hunt your initial 5 players and you have to survive 4+ days for towns to count. For every 2 additional names off the Hunted list you get 3 points. Survive in 2 separate towns lasting 5+ days with those from your Hunted List and they count as extras. It is almost impossible to get all 25 for 36 total points in 110 days.

[ ] Survive 4+ days in more Word Towns.
For Every 2 towns 1 point
You get 2 points for your 1st 2 towns then 1 point for every 2 towns afterwards
1st town 1 point, 2nd town 2 points, 4 town 3 points, 6th town 4 points, etc…
Blood – bloody etc, Sand Sandless etc, Lost lose etc , die death dead deaf, 2 to too twin, night knightly etc

[ ] Survive 20+ days in town as a resident 2 points each
Yeah Residents get a bonus if you don’t d2n. You can be BIA’d while in town.

[ ] Kill 700 Zeds. Every 1000 killed 1 point
Yeah 200+500= 700 that’s 2 points + 300 = 1000 that’s 3 points
kill 2000 that’s 4 points

[ ] Male Video & Female Video points 2
Survive towns for 4+ days where primary video is posted by a Female and Survive in towns for 4+ days where primary video is posted by a Male (2 points). NO Begging Citizens to make a video if it happens it happens.
You will be Disqualified for not acting like a Supreme Hunter.
Yes you can make a video but if its crap and doesn’t promote d2n or your town, we will laugh at you on IRC, forums, Twinoid and it won’t count. Remember practice make perfect and you'll get better at making them after our laughter and ridicule dies down.
