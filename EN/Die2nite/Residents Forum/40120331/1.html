The front page is still very much a work in progress, but the meat of the site is all transferred over already.

www.d2nwiki.com

Thanks to isaaclw's scripting and my already-existing hosting, the site is now up and stable and much more responsive than the old site.

And bonus - it's free of ads! I've already requested to have it added to the site directory.

Note to previous wiki admins: We are not trying to take over your hard work! All work was transferred including previous edits with the usernames attached so you still get credit for your work. Additionally, I'm more than happy to add rights for anyone that had them at the old site, please just drop me a line and let me know! (via twinoid will be faster than through the wiki)

Note to everyone: Your old accounts did not transfer over. You will need to recreate your account if you want to edit any pages. There is a captcha when you sign up, but the site does NOT require your email address. I wanted to make sure that everyone could preserve whatever level of privacy they wanted while still preventing spambot attacks. All you need to do in order to edit the wiki is sign up for an account - there is no need to confirm your email address or anything of the sort.

Feel free to let me know if you have any questions, concerns, suggestions, general comments.
