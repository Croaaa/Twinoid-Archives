Residents can never ever ever have more than 3 CP as Guide. If a Resident Guide and another player are in same zone they each get 1 Extra CP which means if both were residents they have 6 CP max. If the other player is a hero with 4 CP they can have up to 8 max.

It does also matters who escorts or moves into the new zone first.

  • if the guide escort others or moves first then the other player joins him then the guide feature does NOT give additional CP to the non-guide
  • if the other player moves first into a new zone then the guide joins him OR the other player does escort the guide into a new zone, then the guide gives exactly 1 extra CP for each player in zone.
  • if the Guide camp in a zone with a bonus CP, the next day the Guide will wake up without the Bonus CP from being a guide in the campsite zone. However the second the guide moves into a new zone , the bonus CP will come back.
