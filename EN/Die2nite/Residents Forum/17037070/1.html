The Secret of Reincarnation

Lately I've felt
A strange sense of
Deja vu
Like all this has happened before.

While I am scavenging
While I am toiling
Under the ruthless sun
I cannot stop thinking
About this.

Despite our work
We're gonna die2nite.
The bank is barren
The gates are open wide
When you know you're gonna die
You can party all night.

I want to scream
I want to say
Don't you have any hope?
Close the gates!
Or else we shall surely all die!

Someone laughs and responds
It's alright
It's more fair this way
And somehow that made sense.

A minute before midnight I am calm
Calm with the knowledge of towns before
Calm with the knowledge of towns beyond
When the zombies knock on my door
I am ready.

I rip apart the corner of my town uniform
Take out the small flask sewed inside
Pop out the stopper.

The zombies standing before me
Dead arms reaching out toward me
I look into their faces without a trace of fear.
I am ready.
I toss the contents of the flask
Down my throat and into my soul.

I am flying.
Spinning in midair
I am nothing
But the whisper of the wind
The endless waves of the far-away ocean
The particles of dust on the ground.

Then...
Darkness.
Nothing.

After a millennium and a half
I wake up
Look around.
Where am I?
In the middle of a wasteland
Zombies in the distance
A town in front of me.

Someone is waving from atop the gates
I feel as if this has happened before.

I must be mistaken.
