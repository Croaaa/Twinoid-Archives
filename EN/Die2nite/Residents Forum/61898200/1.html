New Chrome Version and they just removed Flash again.

So the only work-around left is the following:

Open F12 Developer console, paste this on the command line


jQuery version

jQuery( "#tid_bar" ).after( '<div><h1><a '+ ' href=https://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/ > FLASH <div> ' );

Press ENTER.

CLICK FLASH in the TOP-RIGHT corner

Accept "ALLOW FLASH" popup.

Press F5...

There you go.



Explanation:


Chrome accepts "allow flash" only if you click EXACTLY
on that get flash player link.

Twinoid have either no links or the wrong link to shockwave flash.

Therefore, you cannot authorize flash anymore in Chrome v71+
without this work-around hack.
