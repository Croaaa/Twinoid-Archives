Sounds interesting? You enjoy witchhunts, and thinking yourself into saboteurs? You enjoy finding out what is going on, identifying the bad guys and bringing them to justice? Read further

Maybe you also think you would be good at being bad, and just want a try for once? Come to the dark side, we have hash cookies…

What it’s about
We (Jedermann and Ivana) want to organize a D2N event with one or multiple town depending on the amount of participants. Each town will get 5 saboteurs picked by us randomly - but no one will know who they will be. Only the saboteurs will get to know the identities of each other so that they are able to work together.

The goal - for the good guys:

  • Survive as long as possible
  • Find the saboteurs - and hang them!
  • Reach some town goals specified by us - the other team will hinder you though…
  • Prevent the saboteurs from reaching their goals

The goal - for the bad guys:

  • Get the wrong people shunned, or on the gallows
  • Harm the towns survival
  • Prevent the town from reaching several goals
  • Do not get caught (self sacrifice for single players may be unavoidable at some point, but once everyone is caught, there’d be no more harming the town)

The rules

  • Shunning and hanging is allowed
  • Don’t tell anyone whether you’re saboteur or not
  • Private communication and teambuilding is allowed of course, as long as you don’t reveal whether you’re saboteur
  • Don’t hurt your own team
  • It’s fine for saboteurs to bash on other saboteurs for strategical reasons - but you should make it a team effort. Acting together is what makes you strong.
  • We as organizers of the event will take part in the town(s) as neutral spectators so that we can watch over things - we are not to be shunned, hanged, filed complaints against, etc.

More exact rules will follow a bit later. We’re still thinking about good victory conditions for the team. If we have enough ppl taking part, we might make multiple towns and have them compete (saboteurs with the ones from the other town, good ppl with the ones from the other town).

Also we’ll set tasks for the good and bad guys of the town - tasks that go well with helping/harming the town. Townspeople would then have to prevent the saboteurs task from being fulfilled and vice versa.

We’re aware that it’s easy to set the wrong goals there, to make things unfair, and to fuck things up. That’s why we still want some more time to discuss the specifics and have a few other people look over it before we publish them. Just trust our experience as #1 town planners for now

The date

  • 17th-20th July (exact jump date will be set in the following weeks)
