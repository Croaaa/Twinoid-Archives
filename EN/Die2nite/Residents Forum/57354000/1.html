End Game:

  • attack cant be covered now. so the second all see the attack estimation is off the roof , all start mixing pharmas and taking DOs from bank and planks and iron to upgrade their homes , then in a matter of few hours suddenly its a blood bath for DOs and all of them are gone and landed in people´s chest

  • next day only few are left and the rest all died , one guy is always on stand by 10 seconds after the attack is over to start pillaging and watering all the corpses before someone else beats him to it.
