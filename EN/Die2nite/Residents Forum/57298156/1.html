Day 14
I hear whispers in the dark! What is it... Demons of old Shaman, or the rebelliants?! Souls are whispering to me all the time. What's difference between reality and eternal world?! I'm losing my reality, my humanity. Souls are eating the whole me. Oh... I see... these were no eternal whispers! Who are you, HUMANS IN THE DARK, AROUND CENTER TOWN CAMPFIRE?! Shaman turns his head back, to the brick wall of nearby house.

Shaman grabs marker and starts to write words... "I'm not sure what people in town are planning. Their look at me is evil, upset. They aren't talking with me anymore. It's day 7 after plague came". "Souls are taking control over my body, I'm losing feeling of real world".
Shaman throws marker away. It lands on the ground with a few grass knots. - "Holy shit" He said second later. - "I just scribbled someone's house with grafitti and my own thoughts".
"- Fuck it, we will die soon anyway". Shaman goes to people within campfire. Takes knife slowly out of his pocket, ignoring the people's look. Starts to cut his hand in middle. Blood pours the fire, and Shaman is getting even more closer to it. Fire starts to burn his robe.

"- It looks our shaman is mad, what is he doing, the fuck?" "- Ha, he wants to sacrifice himself to please gods" - People are starting to scream, some of them approaches to the shaman, trying to get him out of the fire.
Others are kicking him even deeper into the fire, shouting "FOR DRY RAIN". Some are even pouring alcohol over him.

But... what is bad with that fire?! It burns only shaman's robe and clothes of the Shaman?! His body remains undamaged! What kind of magic is it?! Oh, people starts to cry, when they saw naked body of old shaman. Right, people don't cry. They are really scared. As they are starting to being disgusted, fire around shaman changes his colour to blue, and flies around shaman, when he says old, even ancient words: "- The Crow, I'm calling you. Leave your souls, leave all our mortal bodies. I am going to sacrifice people that you see around me, in order to create divine element on earth, once again. To get time back, before Plague. Are you going to accept my offer, mighty Crow?".

Pepple are throwing stones to shaman, but fire flying around him reflects stones, throwing it extactly at way back, hitting the cow in the face.
The shaman suddenly falls on the ground, that fire, no, THESE SOULS, penetrate the body of shaman. After a moment, blue explosion appears in the center of shaman's body, knocking all people around back. After a while, blue smoke covers all terrain, no one sees anything. It's..."- IT'S night!", people starts to scream, when they discovered that zombies are attacking main gates. "- To the gates!", "- Hold the door!", "HODOR" - they're shouting.

Few hours later, after attack in which many people sacrificied their lives, citizens are coming into the campfire. They have found burned and charred body of the ugly shaman. But there was still some edible flesh left, so they decided that at least once shaman will be useful for something.

Body looks like burned not by fire, but by souls, what's s small difference. What happened here?! Some citizens are rumouring about shaman, who sacrificied his life in order to create mystical smoke over the town, that killed hundreds of walking deads today. We live thanks to him?!

Some hours later they have discovered the new thing.
The new world is here. Zombies stopped to appear, after last night, why? A few citizens, who were near shaman in that memorable night, said he did this.

Something went wrong? Shaman is dead, we are alive. Two days later wanderer came to the town. Spokesman of the new credence, of the new gods. He proclaimed that on the west many groups are organizing themselves into societes, trying to live as they lived before Plague. But that's not possible, world has many new rules now. Nobody is going to trust anyone, powerful ones will eliminate the weaker. People are claiming several buildings, earlier known as factories or powerhouses. Sources of energy are rare and wanted now. But no one will forget about those, who sacrificied their lives staying alone on the watch. In order to defend children and womens.
Now, when old tales are still remembered by us, you can give honours to those who died defending towns, true heroes.

9 days later, day 23
We're in an abandoned factory, 25 of us. Only those who survived. Trying to build vegetable farm in these dry lands, and communicate with other societes. We hopefully can unite into one big group soon.
At least we have fun now, everyday at midnight we hear the only radio show in the world, named Pampas of the Haunted Radio Show, haha, those guys still don't know that apocalypse has ended. We would tell them, but our only radio is a bit broken, and can't broadcast. And that's too funny to end this.
