Hello everyone hope you're having a great MMj so far!!

I'm going to be giving away 30 FREE Hero days to the winner of this competition.

Are you guys ready to Save Slay some Chickens??


How do you win? Well, that's a great question. here are the rules:

How to play: The goal of this competition is this collect as many Chicken items as you can.
Each Chicken item is worth a set amount of points
A normal Chicken is worth 3 points
An Egg is worth 2 points
Half-eaten Chicken Wings are worth 1 point

These items MUST be in your Rucksack and or Chest during the time of the screenshot.(You can put them into the back after you take the screenshot if you want to)

If you would like to submit an entry you must, submit a screenshot of your rucksack/chest with all the items to this forum post.
You are only allowed to submit up to 2 screenshots. So make sure that when you submit a screenshot it has the most Chicken items as possible.
Also, please try to take a screenshot of your whole D2N screen so that way we can tell when the screenshot was taken and what town you are in. It will hopefully stop people from photoshopping as much.

Now remember, 30 Hero Days are on the line!
The winner will be the person that has the most points by the end of the last MMJ town.
You do not have to be in an MMJ town to be eligible to win the hero days, you just have to submit the photo/photos before the last MMJ town ends.


If you have any questions feel free to ask.
