D2N Short Stories part one


Day One:No more hereos.

.It was 9Am at Lilliput. An the town was very anxious to hear what the town meeting was about. The crowd was nervous waiting for the mayor Franklyn to address the purpose of the meeting with tears in his eyes he said to the town," Our hereos have stolen from us. The bank has been raided so many items and so many items lost because of our town hereos. An it seems the only way for this to stop is death!"

The towns people made a gasp of surprise for What the mayor has told them. The hereos tried to escape only for them to lose their arm from a shotgun blast.

A few hours later metal tombs were created for the special hereos. They had realised their true potential to be roasted for being unbelieveably annoying. The hereos were placed in these metal tombs and submerged in water with air holes in the top for the hereos to breathe. A fire was set and hereos were cooked alive and eaten their soft tender flesh tasted like chicken. They did scream for a while as the heat burned them in their metal tombs. Their skin felt the heat and pain witb the contact of the walls. At least their suffering was not in vein. It survived a purpose to give the town dinner that would last weeks before it dried up. These hereos are forgiven for their crimes.
