So here's my flow chart definition of a META:


1) Are you less than 6 organised people ?

____> YES: You are NOT a META

____> NO: You are AT LEAST a small META of 2 coalitions


2) Is a JUMP date planned with jumpers of at least 2 coalitions ?

____> YES: You are inside an ORGANISED META


3) Is there at least 2+ coalitions joining ad-hoc inside a random town?

____> YES: You are inside a META town

  • 3a) Is the town disorganised and mostly EE / Wonder only ?

___> YES: You are inside a random META jump between organised META jumps


  • 3b) Is the random following your lead instead of griefing ?

___> YES: You are inside an organised META town with random trained by veterans*


  • 3c) Is other coalition from other META joined your "ad-hoc random town" ?

___> YES: You are inside an ad-hoc META town (which will probably turn into ranking or wonder)


4) Are you around 20 organised people ?

____> YES: You are a half-META with randoms or invites. You are ALSO inside a semi-organised town.


5) Are you around 40 organised people ?

____> YES: You are in a FULL META


6) Is this a special town with many invites from different META organised groups ?

____> YES: You are inside an EVENT META town


7) Are you trying to build a ZOO, a half-zoo, a quarter ZOO, with at least 10+ heroes ?

____> YES: You are inside a ZOO META town


8 ) Is there a MAYOR, main PLANNER inside your META town?

____> YES: You are inside an ORGANISED META town

  • 8 a) Do you risk being kicked out if you do NOT follow plans to the exact letter (not be confused with griefing) ?

____> YES: You are inside a MILITARY STYLE META town

  • 8 b) Is the META town aborting before day 5 ish?

____> YES: You are inside a TOP RANKING MILITARY STYLE META town who will rejump soon to get a better centered map or less early deaths.
