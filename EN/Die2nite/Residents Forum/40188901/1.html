I find the previous Scavenger Hunts relied too much on looking for people and being a hero and working together and participating in community events and otherwise being a "social interaction hunt" than an honest to goodness "scavenger hunt."

I propose a Scavenger Hunt where you really do just hunt down a bunch of things and not a bunch of people or have to be a hero (either full time or BiA'ed) to score points.

I present to you a high stakes list:
Scavenger Hunt ChecklistSeven Four Wonders of Die2Nite
[ ] Build 1 Crow Statue [10 points]
[ ] Build 1 Giant BRD [10 points]
[ ] Build 1 Giant Sandcastle [10 points]
[ ] Build 1 Fearfulness wheel [10 points]
Bonus:
[ ] Build all four wonders [10 points]

What Luck!
[ ] Find 1 Large Chest [10 points]
(Luck distinction)
Weapons of Mass Destruction
[ ] Build 1 Battery Launcher [10 points]
[ ] Build 1 Water Gun [10 points]
[ ] Build 1 Chainsaw [10 points]
Bonus:
[ ] Build all three Weapons of Mass Destruction [10 points]

Ultimate Weapon of Mass Destruction
[ ] Build the Reactor and allow it to melt down [10 points]
(Atomic Death distinction)
I AM a Weapon of Mass Destruction
[ ] Successfully kill 1 zombie with your bare hands [10 points]
(Fights of Despair distinction)

Add it up, that's a whopping 120 points one can score for finding everything possible on the list! Some do promote the town work together to achieve while some may require you to be sneaky sneaky to get your points (or block someone else from getting theirs ). But that's not all! While these are some big scorers, what scavenger hunt could be complete without the bonus points!

Lightning points bonus round!

Feast or famine?
[ ] +1 point for each Cannibalism distinction earned
[ ] +1 point for each Alcohol distinction earned

Extreme Camper
[ ] +1 point for each Daredevil Camper distinction earned
[ ] +1 point for each Zen Camper distinction earned

Bring out yer dead!
[ ] +1 point for each Removal distinction earned
[ ] +1 point for each Sprinkler distinction earned
[ ] +1 point for each Extreme BBQ Chef distinction earned
[ ] +1 points for each Butcher distinction earned

Hitchhiker's Guide to the World Beyond
[ ] +1 point for each Distant Discoveries distinction earned
[ ] +1 points for each Extreme Expeditions distinction earned
[ ] +1 points for each Ruin Explorations distinction earned

There is no limit to the number of points one can earn from these distinctions. The only limit is in how often one is capable of performing each of these actions.

This look like a good list for a scavenger hunt?
What are your thoughts?
