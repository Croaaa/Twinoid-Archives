Small Maps (the only kind of town you can join unless you find a coalition with a member with more that 100 Soul Points) are far less likely to have heroes because they are rookie towns and so not as many players in them have decided to pay for hero days yet (and some people with greater than 100 soul points aren't that into joining Small Maps because they lose distinctions if they die before Day 8 ).
