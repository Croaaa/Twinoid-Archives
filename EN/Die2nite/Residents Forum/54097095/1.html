Inspiration for the people

i wrote in my town forum that it doesnt really matter who becomes the Guide. its very hot discussion and exciting to talk about for some BUT in reality the guide is more or less just personal gain for one citizen who gets nagged and screamed at and in organized towns maybe he gets to nag and scream at others . BUT it has very little significance to our server . and here is why:

this kind of feature is mainly made for hordes the french version because in there they almost never build any zoos and they even never bother killing zeds in short ranges like inside 10km range.
they do have the Night Penalty (they cant find shit during the dark from 19.00 til 7.00) and novelty torches to cancel out the night penalty when scavenging for successful searches. so they are FORCED to scavenge in groups no matter if there are zeds outside or no due to having to use charged novelty torch while scavenging and each one can be used per zone even if 40 people Autosearch there.

SOOOOO for hordes the maps get red very fast because majority are anti-clearing zeds because they consider it wasteful to kill any and at the same time they must scavenge in a group of 3 to 4 due to night penalty.

so a citizen of ANY CLASS who can increase the Control Points of the group scavenging will be very helpful.
so for hordes its a great evolutionary update.
 some may have gotten orgasms from it in france

for Die2Nite its more or less giving one citizen the ability to escort others when there is really no one in need of escorting.
in D2N either no one is organized enough in random or half meta non-zoo maps to run group scavenging routes under hundreds of zeds OR there is enough killing zeds to build a zoo, or half zoo or even zeds dead inside 10km ranges that group scavenging isnt that much needed and if needed it can easily be done with 2 guardians in 4 man group.

so in my opinion for D2N its not super important who gets to be the guide regarding the class. in the end there is no magic or rare items coming out of it.

its only important that the citizen who will be the guide must be Super active going scavenging everyday in max ranges possible from 23.15 til 22-15. even if that citizen has the goat class or king class , the important factor is activity...

and as Ivana mentioned a PERSONAL bonus comes for Giving residents a taste of what its like to be a hero to gain the experience of escorting others which comes for free.
