Most of the time the first järpen will be used for the factory.
Problem is that many towns don't find more than one järpen, so architect study might never get built and it is such a useful building.

Advantages Factory:
- Save 1 ap for each converted resource
(very good if you convert a lot of stuff)

Advantages Building Reg/Architect Study:
- more defense buildings to chose from
- every day chance to get bp for Vegetable Plot. i would say that once you get the plot, the ap gain from vegetables will outweight the savings from factory. the only drawback is that it isn't guaranteed that your town will get the plot.
- every day chance to get bp for Searchtower. with it you will be able to find more resources close to town. this also increases your chance of finding another järpen. (if you want to have Factory AND Study AND Searchtower this might be the only hope of getting them all)

Inspiration for the people

all depends on who you are playing with. if you see in first 2 days that more than 12 people are going outside daily to scavenge then you can be 75% to 90% sure more flatpacks are coming daily and one of them will eventually be the second Järpen Table.
and in that case factory is best to be built first

but if you see that no more than 10 people are outside scavenging on day 2 then your town is not gonna make it to build enough. and you wont get to the point of building many projects with help of factory and in that case building registry and study is the better option because there is always the option upgrading the study for more BPs of all kinds, common, uncommon, rare and even very rare.

so for active towns always go for factory, for inactive towns go for study.
