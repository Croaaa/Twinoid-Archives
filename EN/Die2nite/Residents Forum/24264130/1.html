How to Do most of the things the Heros do as a Resident

Scavenging Zed Clearing Ruin Exploration
Building Camping

1 Communicate with your town mates, be patient (I know)
Read the your town forums. Start a Forum thread in your town. Send messages from your home to your town mates. Send message to your townmates via Twinoid PM. Find and or start a town IRC chat. Be patient when your trying to get an answer, you may be running out of time but others may just be busy.

2 Make yourself available during busy time for die2nite activity
Your entire d2n experience is directed by the attack - clock time 2300. That means that 2100 - 0100 is the busy time for most players on d2n. Things need to get done right before attack, others need to happen right after the attack. If you play during 2100-0100 game time your most likely to be asked to do one of the jobs

3 Set yourself & townmates up to succeed
"I went 9ap to AS, Tamers are suppose to kill zeds". Horrible excuse and the laziest way to play the game. Yes you help the town but did you really help your townmates? How about setting food or a booster out for your town mates on your way to an AS spot. How aboutgoing with them on their killing run and bringing more weapons? The point is be available to do more than AS in a safe zone.

4 See a job, Get the job done and get back in town
Look for a construction list, look for a scavenge list, figure out or ask others what does the town need today. Day 2, heros need materials for labs/kitchens - go get it bring it back, sorry you dont get to AS today but you got the town moving. Less than 20 irons in bank, pick a path on from dusk til dawn that gets you to AS and pick up as many irons as possible. All the Washers and hurl sticks broke, fix theim. Sometimes you dont have to wait for others to tell you what to do. Sometimes no one is around to help you decide what to do. See a job, Get it done.
