While most want to stay inside, the ones who go outside are usually the same people each day, and the number of people who venture out declines each day. If you've done enough random towns I'm sure you've observed the same thing.

First day usually has most people going out with up to a dozen not returning or camping. Second day usually has 10 or so going outside, and of those usually 2-3 don't return or camp. Third day often has maybe 7 or 8 going out, and much of the time at least 1 won't return or camp.

Then the dehydration deaths occur. Maybe 5-10 of those.

Days 4 and on will usually have a slower decline of the number of people going outside each day, even 1 spot out. Whether the watchtower is built and upgraded doesn't seem to affect whether people are willing to go out 1 zone. (Don't get me wrong, builders are necessary, but searching one zone out is still helpful)

There are some exceptions, like the town I'm currently in which still has 24 people alive on day 5 and 10 people outside auto-searching.

I'd have to agree with Saran though, rarely are there more than 10 scavenging after day 1. First day sure, but often many of those don't make it back inside. The workshop seems far more advantageous in random towns in terms of AP.
