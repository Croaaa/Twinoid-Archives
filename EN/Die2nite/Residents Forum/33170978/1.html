ok let me try and explain this as much as i can to save confusion

first off, for those that don't have it, The Camping Calculator

firstly you have the base zone chances which is calculated on the topology of the zone and the distance from town, for a regular building like the fast food restaurant that is 8km from town that will be base chances of 85%,

next you can upgrade a zone till you reach 10 upgrades, you can get over 10 by use of DOs as they are worth approx 1.85 upgrades, so by using 8 normal upgrades and then 2 DOs you can get zone to 11.7 upgrades, each night during attack a zone loses 3 upgrades down to min of 0, if a zone was upgraded to 11.7 upgrades, the next day it will be at 8.7 meaning you can do 2 upgrades or 1 upgrade and 1 DO only

camp items count separate from zone upgrades and are only capped by how many you can carry

once the zone chances are fully worked out including upgrades and your camp items, then the penalties are removed for being 3rd or more camper in zone and for how many days you have camped. your penalties are personal and have no effect on zone base chances

lastly if you are not a surv the game caps your chances at 90% if you were over that, due to this survs should always camp FIRST, unless the non surv has had many more camp days, but if a zone is at exactly 100%, and you have 3 new campers, 2 survs and a scout, the 2 survs can camp first at 100% and then the scout with 3rd camper penalty gets 90% which is their max anyway, if the scout camped first they would get 90%, 1 surv get 100% and 2nd surv would get 90%

any questions i didn't cover?
