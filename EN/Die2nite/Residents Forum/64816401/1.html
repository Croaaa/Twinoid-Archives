I open this topic to talk (again) about MyHordes. With a block, again
This topic is there for questions you may have but also to allow us to give you the information we have regarding transfers. Screenshots provided in this topic will be in German but it will be translated into your lang into the public version
For those who don't know what MyHordes is, I invite you to read the spoiler below

MyHordes is a project integrated into the Eternal Twin project. The overall goal is to save all possible Motion Twin games and make them accessible without Flash (which at the end of 2020 will no longer be updated). These games which, as they go down in frequentation, may therefore close.
And so, MyHordes is the Flash-free version of Hordes (and its German / Spanish & English versions) because we assemble all the versions into one
The game is currently available here and are in Season 15 with some changes



Via the API of Twinoid, we can get for the moment:

Soul Points
All pictos
The list of cities in which you have participated
The titles and images unlocked



Thanks to these information, we can unlock:

The special bonuses given to those who have the Witness to Armageddon / Witness to the Great Contamination pictos (the system which gives these bonuses is already in place normally).

Hero experience approximate, based for the moment on the addition of all the heros pictograms. This does not represent the real amount but for the moment this is what we can do in a safe way, it can represent a loss of 5 to 20% of the heroic experience, but we are working on a calculation method which would allow to have only about 1% loss

For RP texts, we cannot have the list of texts that you have unlocked. However we can unlock a number of random writing corresponding to the number of pictograms RP among the list of texts or you will have the possibility to choose the texts that you will unlock (the number of texts unlocked will not change and will correspond to the number of picto RP)



Concerning, the operation of the transfer system it will be composed to summarize like this:

On MyHordes, a button will be accessible in your soul settings to make the transfer.

Once you have clicked on this button, you can transfer a soul from DV, D2N, Hordes and ZN. Soul transfers will not be blocked but limited (details below). A page on the Twinoid website will put a message like "Do you want to share your data with the MyHordes application?" with the choice "Yes / No", this therefore requires that you are logged into your Twinoid account. If you agree, your Twinoid account will be associated with your MyHordes account and cannot be dissociated after, if you refuse this cancels the import and no data is sent to MyHordes.

You will have a global view of the transferred data and if you confirm, the raw data will be saved in your MyHordes profile, so basically no information can be lost or altered after the transfer.

This will merge the past cities of all your souls if you transfer multiple versions of the game.

If you will transfer multiple versions of the game, you will have to choose the pictos & soul points of which version to keep, only one version of the game can be selected for the pictos & soul points, and will not be not editable after.

The advantage of this is that since we store the raw data given by MT via the API, all the data is always mirrored on MH so even if MT stops, nothing is lost from the time you made the transfer before.

If you import your soul again later, this will update these data. For example if after the transfer you have gained 100 soul points and pictograms in the original game, they will also be put on MyHordes only if you redo the import, the data stored by MyHordes cannot be updated automatically, this only done manually when you click the Import button.

Once the import is done, a banner will be displayed on your soul which will say "This player transferred his profile from the original game", to differentiate the people who have the transfer and those who have not fact.

Concerning the special pictograms like for example "Champion of the Clash of the heroes", "Clash of the heroes", "Festival of the Video Game 2010" : they will be well transferred during the import, but as we have not yet created these pictos only the name and the number will be kept, so the icon and the description will not appear until we have recreated these special pictograms.
After we have recreated these pictos, you will need to import your profile again. Because we store the last imported data on the MyHordes server, these pictos will be good again even if Twinoid or the original game have shut down. When you import pictos which is not created on MyHordes, a message will say it to you. Actually, here all pictos created on MyHordes.



For the current season rankings, we do not currently have a reliable trick to be able to award the pictograms "Legendary city!", "City classified" / "Participating city" as well as the alarm clock and the glorious tag. So for the moment we can't say anything.


I think I have done a bit of everything with regard to soul transfer, if you have any questions or suggestions don't hesitate

Hello,

Since version 0.5.0 alpha.1 we have included the soul import system in the MyHordes public server. And now since alpha 6 we can proudly announce that we have received Architectoire validation for our application, which means that anyone can now use the application to transfer their soul

However, keep in mind that not everything is perfect;

A difference to the Hero experience is still relevant. The difference can generally be 1-5% less, in some cases it can also overestimate your experience and in this case you will have a little more (around 1-5% more) . We can't really do better. And keep in mind that this imported experience will therefore be in addition to the one you had already done on MyHordes.
So if you've already played 15 days and import 500 days, your MyHordes soul total will be 515 days.

A difference in the statistics points may be present between imported soul and original soul. This is due to some pictograms which do not yet have points on MH, this will be corrected soon.

The names of citizens of imported cities cannot be retrieved, so they will appear "empty". Only the people who imported their soul and were in the same city as you will be put in those imported cities.
Also, the difference of imported cities will be lost, so all imported cities will be seen on MyHordes as REs (panded / private imported cities will not be differentiable). We cannot fix this unfortunately.

We cannot know which RP writings you have unblocked. Consequently, currently a number corresponding to the number of RP pictograms will be released randomly. We also do not have all the writings so you may have less than the number of pictograms.
In the future, we may do something to let you choose which writings are unlocked (the number will always correspond to the number of RP pictograms)

The titles and icons are well imported, however we have not made the system which allows to choose it. This will be added soon.

:horde _!!: Please send me a message in PM if you see any problem relating to the import system (incorrect data / problem / bug / etc)
