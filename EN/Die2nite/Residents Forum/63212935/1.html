There was once a man named Michael Finnegan Donelou, first of his name, the WorldEater, the Taint licker, the Sunburned and subsequent, Lampless acolyte of the 11th Moth Circle. What follows is how the latter title came to be... It is said that in his home lay a coveted and very valuable lamp, not the type of lamp you rub and get 3 wishes out of; they type from which you get a satisfying click - as it giveth and taketh away the Holy 8 watt LED light. But one day tragedy befell Don, when he returned to his ramshackle abode to find that the lamp had mysteriously vanished! With no clue as to who had taken it. Donelou lamented the loss of his precious lamp, and what would surely lead to the loss of his title of Lightmaster Plug (a name he had built a semi-successful rap career on); shortly afterward however; a note - from the thief! It read: Fuck you, my lamp now

But when he finally arrived at the thief's house, he found that he could not enter to retrieve his lamp, why you ask? Merely because of a padlock, a padlock that prevented him from opening the door, but a padlock he knew was there from looking through a window in the thief's house, a window that was somehow not coded into his plane of existence, and thus a window he could simply not use to circumvent the padlock and retrieve his precious lamp. So, with that, the lamp remains in the thief's house, never to be held by Don again, never again satisfying him with its resounding *click*. My lamp now.
