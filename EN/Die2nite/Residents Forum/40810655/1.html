BOTH MULTIS and ACCOUNT SITTING ARE FORBIDDEN ON DIE2NITE

Please consult the rules on sitting and account sharing HERE:

By "user" Motion Twin understands only one person.
A user can only have one account.

To Report a Multi or suspicion of account sitting, you can send a message to the "Die2Nite Crows" inbox on this page.


To report a post on the forum you can "Block" it and the mods can then look it over. This goes for World and Town forums. Hover over the bottom right of the post and click the red exclamation mark.


Players who go into a town just to wound other players and hide at 0/0 before dying on day 1 or 2, will receive a warning and a 2 week ban. If they choose to ignore the warning and continue, then their whole account will be deleted.

Please report such behaviour with screenshots to the "Die2Nite Crows" inbox on this page. The use of this inbox is preferable over sending a message to a specific Crow. Remember that this is for serious griefing only, please only report these cases.
