I prefer towns that have a 20km zone 20AP away (in other-words the axis is 20km in one direction). 19km is probably also fine.
That way I can get to the 18km range without spending more than 18AP.

Then I start going to the corner farthest from town, staying in this 18km range.

Hopefully by the time I find a building, I've found weapons. As a resident, or player without a Camera, you might want to wait until you have a flash grenade built, or a weapon that makes you feel confident to take on ~20 zombies.

My favorite time to jump is 3-5 hours before the attack, if I'm confident that the town is very empty and won't fill. This way I get extra searches in.
