Die2nite Casino Dice Game

Mandatory

1. Roll a d100, the result represents how many points you have.
Example: 86

Optional

2. Would you like to gamble 4 of your points to flip a coin? If you guess the flip correctly, 4 points are added to your score. If you guess incorrectly, 4 points are subtracted from your score.
Example: Heads Tails (Win with Heads)
Odds: 1 to 1

3. Would you like to gamble 4 of your points to play Rock-Paper-Scissors? If you win, 8 points are added to your score. If you lose or tie, 4 points are subtracted from you score.
Example: Scissors Paper (Win against Paper)
Odds: 2 to 1

4. Would you like to gamble 4 of your points to guess a card's suit? If you guess the suit correctly, 12 points are added to your score. If you guess incorrectly, 4 points are subtracted from your score.
Example: Hearts King of Diamonds (Win with Hearts suit)
Odds: 3 to 1

5. Would you like to gamble 4 of your points to guess a vowel? If you guess the vowel correctly, 16 points are added to your score. If you guess incorrectly, 4 points are subtracted from your score.
Example 1 (keeping it simple): A E (Win with A)
Example 2 (choose any word with your vowel): A
TRAP TREP (Win with A)
Odds: 4 to 1

6. Would you like to gamble 4 of your points to roll a six-sided dice? If you guess the roll correctly, 20 points are added to your score. If you guess incorrectly, 4 points are subtracted from your score.
Example: 5 6 (Win with 5)
Odds: 5 to 1

The person with the most points wins

1. Everything must be done in the same post. So you're basically gambling points that you don't even know you have (final score could be negative).
2. You can do any of the Optional steps that you want to do. But you can't do the same thing twice.
3. If you could care less about doing any of the Optional steps, feel free to do nothing more than to roll a d100.
4. Also feel free to do all of the Optional steps as well as rolling the d100.
5. Maximum score is 160.
6. Minimum score is -19.

I've been working on this RP game for a while and have been improving it each time I share it in a town. Hopefully this improved version of the game is good enough for the Hero Day Raffle.
