Season 9 Scavenger Hunted List

Thank you all for volunteering - the scav hunt will not happen with out you this is how the list played out. You will all be hunted Season 9.

You need to play in as many towns as possible during the 110+ days of the Scavenger Hunt.
Post here if you have to leave the game.

We generate your names into specific HUNTED using 8d20. You will all be on someone’s specific hunted list, even if we have to help the RNG.
Post here or Game Thread for questions.

NO ONE is allowed to abuse you or spam you. Contact Sashimi, jkudlick if this happens.
 
A few rules for the Hunted:
You must be as active as possible. We understand taking a few days off because you’re out of town or just need a break, but please notify jkudlick or Sashimi if you cannot continue as a Hunted. If you do not believe you can be reasonably active throughout the Season (i.e. in a town at least 85-90 days during the next 110+ days), please step aside and lets someone else be a Hunted.
You are not required to assist any Hunters in their quest, but as stated above, you must remain reasonably active.
