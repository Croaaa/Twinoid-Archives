CarmenK72lol being drunk actually changes your text that's awesome

congrats on being drunk on your first day.

Also, the kind of towns you are playing fill a lot faster than the Regular kind-of-Veteran towns.

There are always 3 Small Map towns opened at a time, but even with 120 slots, they fill fast. If you get an extended day 1 (not enough players to start the town), you will definitely not having that issue again on the 'next day'.

Anyhow, you will soon find out that your kind of towns have another issue, which is the death of half the town by inactivity on day 3-4. Those are the players that logged once, didn't like what they saw and will never come back again.

We can't blame them, but their corpses and the lack of AP ends up hindering most Rookie towns. When this happens constantly it also ends up hurting the morale of the other people that want to try this game further.

So be brave and persistent and you will soon find great joy with this game and community.
