A lot of the game seems to revolve around the strategy of working with others but in a way to then turn against them by the end in order to win. In that way it sounds like d2n, in that working together gets you farther but you can play selfishly. I'm thinking they have designed the game to more emphasize the need to become selfish at some point and to make a move against the other players.

The things you can work together on include setting traps, defenses, and setting up better vantage points to shoot the zombies. It also looks like you need to make decisions such as staying in the same place you have barricaded or to take the risk of moving to dangerous areas in order to procure better weapons. So there are definitely elements you can compare with d2n.
