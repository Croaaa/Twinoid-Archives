Okay... so..
...there exist metas in this game. Quite a few of them.

First of all, does anyone know how many are still active? How about how many have gone away or are currently defunct?
How many are there are TOTAL, does anyone really know?

Okay, let me try.

First of all, there is, in my mind....THE KING of metas: 6FO.

Now, 6FO has a glorious history. Originally it was stared by a player named Damoksta and it had the name DoBFoS. What that stood for, I never knew, but I was invited to it early on and was a member of it for about a year, or one full season (a little of season four and a little of season five), then I stopped playing.

When I came back, about a year and a half later... DoBFoS was now 6FO (which, I still don't know what it stands for...does anyone know), and is now mostly managed by great planner Dream. I was invited a few times again... and jumped with them 4-5 times, maybe.... but then I found a meta that was much more relaxed....

This was ZOA.... which stands for.. uh.. Zombies.. something something.
So, ZOA...a second meta I can name. This one was run by a fellow named Embacie with a little help from the grand UrsulaK. I was with ZOA for almost two years. It was great. To this day, that meta was one of the most accepting, understanding, greatest metas I have played with. It was kind of a "teaching" meta and accepted pretty much anyone, as long as they weren't a troll or greifer. A newbie willing to learn? Sure! Come join ZOA. We had a lot of good, strong, smart, and experienced players play regularly with us, though... and we went pretty far a few times.

ZOA eventually became less and less active, though...and soon, I had gotten an invite to jump and be a member of another meta, at the time: 7Sins.


Ahhhhh, 7Sins, what an awesome name for a meta. It stands for.. uh... well, like all the other meta initials, I couldn't tell ya. Maybe it just stood for what it says: 7 Sins (after the seven deadly sins).

Anyway, I jumped with 7Sins two or three times... and it was mostly run and lead by a charming woman named Sashimi. She was a very good planner, too...and very kind and friendly to all.
So that's three. 7Sins was another meta, but I think they faded in activity, over time, too.


Meanwhile, during this time...well, there had always existed a meta I HEARD about, but never knew any members from. It was like a ghost meta. I never could quite meet or talk to any of the people in it. It was named INGSOC, and if ANY meta rivialed 6FO over being the TOP meta, this one was it.
I had heard legendary tales of INGSOC, although, again, I had never talked to anyone from it or knew of anyone who was in it. it was like, the FREEMASONS or ILLUMINATI of metas.

However, since I had never had any experience with INGSOC, they just couldn't rise-in my mind-as being better than 6FO, who I knew members and leader of PERSONALLY.

So, IMO (back in those days) 6FO was the best, INGSOC was second, 7SINS was third, and ZOA was at the end, trailing behind. This was "MY" insight, from what I had gleaned and experienced with playing with these groups.

By the way, INGSOC? Don't know what it stands for. That should be no surprise by now. I don't know what ANY of these groups stand for. :p Anyone care to enlighten?

It was only until later (much later) that I finally had the honor of meeting and talking to the wonderful leader, Sinistra. Turns out she isn't some huge, secret, elite leader.... but a very kind, smart, well-read, well-spoken person. Oh, I'm sure she has a "general" type side to her, too, when organizing and operating towns...
...but from the PMs I've exchanged with her, she's very nice.
She also invited me to jump with them...so, the day I do, I will jump for joy at being a person who has jumped with all four of the "BIG" metas: INGSOC, 6FO, 7SINS, and ZOA














But wait... there's more. What about other metas? Do they exist? I'm betting they do...and, in fact, I know of at least three more. Three more that have seemingly started when I finally took a break again...this time FOR TWO YEARS. I could be wrong, though... they could have existed before them, but suffice to say, I had not heard of them before.

The first is SotA..which...HEY, I actually know what this one stands for, for once! It stands for "Sons of the Atom". Yep, you better capitalize that "A", lest Schroedinger get mad at you.
SotA is filled, again, with really great and intelligent members, I find. I jumped with them a few times, once in a Christmas town (there were about half of them in there) that went past day 20...and another two times after that, with various members of it. Very steadfast, strong group.


And then there's one called CoS, apparently... but no idea what that stands for (no surprise,.. me not knowing what a meta name stands for should be a drinking game by now), but I don't think I know any of its members and I've never jumped with them.

And finally, we have a meta I have only heard the NAME about. fdamned26 told me about a meta named HIVE. I think HIVE might just be the NEWEST meta around, and I certainly don't know who is in it or what it stands for (DRINK!).


Anyway, as I said, I took another break, this time for TWO FREAKING YEARS.

I recently came back around last November.

Since then, I've learned a few things:
6FO seems to be inactive, currently.
7Sins seems to have disappeared.
ZOA is still no more.

....and so, the new "TOP" meta (in my mind) becomes INGSOC. Current kings...going far almost every time.
Second to them is Sota....oops, sorry... I meant SotA
And behind them are CoS, in third place, and HIVE in fourth.





Are these all of the metas? I'm thinking not. I know I must have missed or forgotten about 2 or 3.. or possibly even 6 or 7.
And who knows how many "small" ones there are.. or ones that are currently being built and made? I know some that are nameless, right now.....so I can't very well list them. I know Too_Ton was trying to make another one...and I've got one I'm sorta with that is nameless and has members like Ice_ and Deuteronomy and DevilFighter and RagingRhonda...no idea who else is a part of their group, though.

Sometimes I wonder why all of the groups and metas don't just COMBINE into ONE, HUGE meta. Join forces and all that.... it would be pretty amazing... a huge force to be reckoned with.

I doubt that will ever happen, though....people tend to like to be independent and not all folks get along with one another. Difference in opinion in how the game is played and what to do can flare up, after all. Instead we have a ton of players who probably interact and jump with ALL metas from time to time. Seriously, how many metas have YOU been a part of, in your history? I'm betting- if you're a long time player who is also a hero- a LOT. Maybe you're like me and have been a member of ALL of these metas at one point or another.

So during this period of time I'd rank the metas as UNnamed>RFM>Oozing Inn>LS>7sins>WF

As this age calmed down and LS and WF broke up and other metas started popping up, this is around the time CoS, N&C, Ingsoc and WCH sprung up in full force. Which i shall never forgive her for ).

Also I wouldn't call Hive a meta, they were more like a group of players from a bunch of different metas coming together as the game died (but i guess that's just my opinion). I also don't think any of those players are still around for the most part.

As for CoS the remaining members play with INGSOC and there's only a few of them left (maybe one of them wants to speak for themselves though instead of me...).

INGSOC also has a good hand full of EX N&C members as well as members from pretty much every former meta. But now are all just called INGSOC members. Even some of the EX N&C members now in INGSOC were part of other metas before N&C. It's just the nature of the game, a meta falls apart and the members all split off to new metas.
