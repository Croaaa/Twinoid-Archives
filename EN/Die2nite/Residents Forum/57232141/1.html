SlimShaneyIs it safe to have a half zoo by day 3 in a small town?

depends where the buildings are:
if you have one building in one half and all the rest of buildings in the other half you will most likely have low number of zeds in the 1 building half and cause a GR.

the best way to do it safely is math. just calculate the

  • GR = (2 x map length x day) (for large maps use GR = 26 x day)
    and try to approximately predict the number of zeds present in the half you plan to leave as a zoo. and as long GR is lower than predicted zed numbers by the start of each new day including the new day spread , you will be fine.

as how to predict zed numbers: (optional, you dont have to read this unless you really want to learn how)
count each building outside 6 km range with about 11 starting zeds and 6 to 7 zeds in the zones surrounding it (with each farther away 1 km from 6 m add 2 new zeds to both numbers i mentioned). that makes total 18 zeds per building. and with each new day try to predict that each zombie infested zone has either grown either 1 zed in itself , or has spread one zed to one of its surrounding zones. that is a growth of about 8 to 12 new zeds daily (there must be min and max since sometimes zombies neither grow nor spread).

so building starts with about 11 (in 6 km range) and 6 to 7 around it = 18 ish.
add from 8 to 12 new zeds daily so min is 24 max is 30 per building.
AND the number of new zeds could decrease, since if all surrounding zones are zombie infested all zeds grow by just 1 new zed daily...

if 2 buildings are close to each other and their spread mixes the numbers change a little bit, its countable , but unwise to explain how here... needs screen shots and examples...
