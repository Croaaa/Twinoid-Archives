Q#1:

Since LMS is granted for a random citizen on the last day of the town, it is worthless upgrade your house now or its defense.

It is very important that you get rid of the corpses from last attack, though. Those can rise as zombies and eat you on your house regardless of the defense you have, and this removes you from the LMS eligibility.

If you answered G) Clean and tidy your home. | The only known effect of this is that it increase the chance to detect thefts made on your house. It has no effect on LMS though.

Correct answers here: E,F

Q#2:

This is a tricky question. Being a Miscellaneous item, the calibrator is NOT lost when the town shuns you. So you can either A) leave on rucksack, B) leave on chest or even doing D) and F) are acceptable. The other alternatives are wrong/impossible.

Q#3:

Cyanide = Even if it can only kill you, it doesn't mean it cant kill the others, either by mistake or just because its available.
Boxes = Increase your Home slots to add more defense definitely helps
Wire Mesh = Can be used to make Reinforcements if you are a hero.
Sheet Metal (parts) = Can be turned to defensive items and even used on home upgrades beyond House
Corrosive Liquid = One of the components to make poison
Chuck Figurine = It is a high-valued decoration item, people usually collect them instead of defense, so it helps you to beat them indirectly.
Rusty Chain = Many uses for LMS: helps build a Fence, a Lock, build/prevent Gallows, etc...
Crushed Battery = This one is the only alternative to go in this question
Earplugs = Component to make advanced locks with hi-fi

Q#4:
-Shunned people can't vote for upgrades, so it doesn't depend on you.
-You can't purify jerrycans outside
-Hydratones are nice, but I said you are also drugged, so if you pop a hydratone you will get addicted and this is bad for someone that is shunned.
- D, E, F are correct
- Stagnant water cans need a water tablet before becoming useful.

- Falsification of a registry and Curtains are Hero-only and people will easily spot you if you do those in this town.
