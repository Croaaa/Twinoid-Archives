Duly Noted, our heroes are the main characters in the series. The 40 citizen count is to follow by the games rules, give extras the chance to play citizens and zombies.
My favorite main character out of the 7 is the Shaman.
We have sprinkled the script with dark humor whenever we can .
The chuck Statue never came up in brain-storming . Adding a storyline with the chuck statue is awesome. (Il see if props can make it speak Chuck Norris Film Quotes ), it may be added in later episodes as the pilot has already been created.
Ghoul hunts are going to be great we already have a storyline with ghouls involved. Which i'm personally excited for because I work with liquid latex and iv'e never created a half-human-half-zombie hybrid. The only trouble i'm having is making him look infected without making it obvious to other citizens or the audience. Ghoul design is something that is open to ideas.

When the pilot was created we wanted early deaths . Just like every town I play! But we have multiple ways that citizens and heroes are able to kill, kill themselves or get killed. Which will come into play when tensions rise between the citizens.
Being loyal to the game, I had to cut out electricity from the camp (which pained me). We do have afew things that step out of the game's rules, I.E, Town Doctor, radio transmissions and the "Legends Of The Wasteland".
