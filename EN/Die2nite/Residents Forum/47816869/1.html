Hi, and welcome back.
Yeah, sadly as you noticed not many significant changes in ages, but a few things you should be aware about small maps:

-Now you just can't camp in small maps, so if you're left outside you're pretty much dead. Blueprints are automatically on ground on building zones, so you just have to pass by and collect.

-Souls outside can turn red once left out during an attack. That means you can't collect them or you'll die. Unless you drink a shaman potion before. And those red souls increase daily attack by 15% EACH (yeah, it's that lamely programmed).
So in short, small towns with tons of early deaths, no active shaman to point souls nor active and coordinated ppl to collect them means town is gonna be overrun in 5-6 days.

-You need to build hammam to purify the souls.

-You need to build battlements in order to use watchmen.

So yeah, in short: non-meta towns are pretty much screwed this season ^^
