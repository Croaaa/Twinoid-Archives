Unless there's been a glitch on the game you should be able to.

Your Shaman Points are directly underneath your AP points counter. (If you go right from the Help tab at the top of your screen, you will see it) they look like this you get 5 of them everyday.

You need a Water Ration AND a Shaman point to brew a potion. (Word of advice, don't bother brewing them unless people in your town are dead and their souls have been outside more than 48 hours)

Also, to "make it rain", you need to be AT THE TOWN GATES, thats (0,0), just click "Go outside" and then don't move and from there it cost 3 Shaman points to make it rain.
