Alright, someone had suggested insane towns earlier this week, which sounded fun but I didn't think it solved any problems that D2N has.
As a matter of fact i think it may cause more problems if instituted.
Die2nite needs more players.
It's a for profit game which is Free to play. And a Free to play model requires a constant in flux of players in order to be profitable.
So my suggestion is new type of town, complete with it's own leader board.
as discussed in the "final objective" thread it seems most people define success of D2N to rank. However it is IMPOSSIBLE to rank in a random town. I know a lot of folks on the world forums do not like Coalitions so this will appeal to them.

Strange towns are towns where no Coalized (sp?) teams are allowed in. The towns aren't even listed, you simply hit "join a Strange town" and you are thrown into a random, stranger filled town. Of course there is a chance you will see some familiar faces but coalitions will have no power.
The rules are the same as regular towns and the rewards are the same too. I would call more attention to the Forums button on the screen too.
The important part is the town get it's own leader board. Suddenly your random town that survives 10 days actually has a shot at recognition.
I feel this will help the community in a couple ways.
1st) experienced people who play this way will be forced to explain their direction to newer players so that they understand. They will no longer have voting majority and can't just say "do it this way, or else"
2nd) New people will see a leader board that seems obtainable. I don't anticipate a lot of us who are used to playing meta games spending a lot of time so maybe the top position is 14 days at the end of the season.
3rd) People will meet other people and start there own coalitions and get excited about organized play

No, this type of town doesn't have a lot of defense against greifers perhaps if these towns began with gallows that would help. but i'm not sure.
I think bringing in new blood is what twinoid should be most concerned with. As I am tired of seeing the same 7 avatars flood the world forums every day.
I await those 7 avatars comments on my idea.
thank you
