This event is based off of Sense's old Amazing Race Event.
I want to credit him for this awesome idea.

We WILL adjust the date if MMJ people are still alive.

The Rules:

38 people will form 19 groups of 2.

Each group will compete to be the first to touch all 4 corners of the map and be the first to make it back to town.

Both members of the group must touch the corner at the same time.

Screenshots will be required.

If your partner dies, you may carry on without them.

The other 2 people in town will be Guardian volunteers that will guarantee that the town doesn't fall into devastation before the Marathon is complete.

Anything goes. Literally anything. But only once the gates are open.

Any classes are welcome, including residents.

Please only sign up with your confirmed partner.

This will be a Private Town, and therefore have limited distinctions. The reason for this is that we can have one of the organizers join after the attack on September 20th. Only then can the Gates be opened. This will allow everyone to have an even playing field. Everyone else will be expected to join prior to September 20th.
