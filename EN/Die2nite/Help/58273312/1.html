The Duck Mafia

About
The duck Mafia is a gaming clan for die2nite. Our main purpose is to gain more points than meta towns combined together as a whole. An our ultimate goal is to rid the world of the zombie menace that plagues us all.

Our Motto
"We are the Duck Mafia. We are everywhere. There is no escape alive or dead. The Duck Mafia is eternal."

How to Join

1. Most have 30 soul points.
2. Most be 18yrs old or older.
3. Most have lived in at least five towns before death.
4. Most be active at least four times in a week.

Rules
1. No Racism is permitted.
2. No Sexual talk or harassment is allowed.
3. No bullying of other members is allowed.
4. No verbal abuse of other members.
