Neither.

This is a feature of the French version of the game called Hordes.

Some things work different for them if its night time during the game (mostly item dropping odds).

Here we see the change if its day, afternoon and night, but there is no such thing affecting the drop chances and other.

Although we had this night penalty feature accidentally after some seasons update, MT always removed it as it doesn't make sense to employ this feature for a "global" server.

It is true, though, that we keep finding items related to this feature, such as the Laser Diode (a weapon that needs the flashlight to be assembled, although the flashlight is non-existant here).

Also, most things at the Updates section are blunt translation of what Hordes has/had.
