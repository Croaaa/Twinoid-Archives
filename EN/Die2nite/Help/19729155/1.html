It's the apocalypse, ergo there are no more radio towers, ergo the only thing "playing" on the radio is static. Have you ever tried listening to static? It's annoying and distracting! Not to mention the sound attracts zeds, meaning you have to face more than you can handle, on top of the distraction means you're already annoyed and unable to concentrate on attacking, hence you can't kill as many as you might have been able to before. Oh, and being surrounded by so many zombies when you aren't fighting your best means you aren't likely to survive as well as you normally would be able to.

That's my 2 worth of flavor text for it.
