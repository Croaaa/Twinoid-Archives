qpa88

If you die in town eaten by zombies during the attack (not because of infection, dehydration, etc.) your day is counted as completed.

If you die any other way, the day is not counted as completed.

Its all in the Help section:
A day is considered "lived in full" if you survive the night or die from consequences arising from the zombie attack at 23:00. Death of any other kind will render the day invalid.
