Yes, thats right. Mystic points are awarded for every Weak Soul (blue) converted into Powerful Soul (red).

You can use the Powerful Souls as resources on some defensive Constructions under Emmegency Supplies tree. You can use the Weak Souls in your house to give the town 10 points for the leaderboards.

If you want Mystic Points i suggest to keep the blue souls you find, drop in the bank and convert them quick before someone uses the soul in their house.
