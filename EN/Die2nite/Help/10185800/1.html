Annoying eh

Ok if you used either of the solutions posted above to temporarily get onto D2N using Firefox, you should now undo the changes...

If you used my solution,

Type about:config into your address bar
In the search box, type general.useragent.override
Right-click on the line that appears, and click Reset

If you used j000's solution,

Type about:config into your address bar
In the search box, type network.http.accept-encoding
Right-click on the line that appears, and click Reset
