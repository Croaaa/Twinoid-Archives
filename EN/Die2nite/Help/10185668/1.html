Hacksaw
A general opinion:

Selfish play
Doing things that benefit yourself, whether or not those actions are destructive for the town.

Griefing
Doing things that are destructive to the town, even if those actions don't benefit you.

Where the line blurs is where the actions overlap.

Eating all the town's drugs does cause grief for the town, but could be beneficial to the one eating them, so long as he's going for drugs and drug tester distinctions. This action is most likely being selfish.

But if they're being eaten so the person can continuously pull items out of the bank and run them into the desert, that action doesn't help anyone, not even the person pulling out the items. This action is most likely griefing, and since the person is just as likely to suicide on day 1 by standing out in the desert, it turns all that drug eating into griefing since he's likely not going to keep those distinctions.
