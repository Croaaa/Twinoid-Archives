Heroes don't have better max energy. 6AP is normal max, premium food gives temporary 7AP (1 bonus), twinoid 8AP (2 bonus), dice/cards can give bonus AP up to total 7 (1 bonus), but there is no way to increase max energy.

You might still get stuck outside even if it could be upgraded if you don't check your AP when walking outside.

You get 6 AP daily
Food gives 6-7 AP depending on food (can eat once a day)
Water 6 AP (can use more then once but only gain AP for first use)
Booze 6 AP (can use once every other day)
Drugs 6-8 AP (depends on drug, can use more then one a day but you will get addicted if using more then 1 per day)
Dice chance for 1 AP every day
Cards chance for 1 AP once per day but can also get terrorized
Playing guitar 1 AP for everyone in town at that time

Hard to get
Alarm clock gives extra AP next day if you set it up, to get it you must be in top1 town in previous season

Hero only
Siesta time chance (% depends on siesta level) for 2 AP once per day
Second Wind hero power 6 AP once per town
