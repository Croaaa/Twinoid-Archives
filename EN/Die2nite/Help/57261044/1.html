Inspiration for the people

from best to last:

  • Automatic Sprinklers : 150 def for 20 rations
  • Mist Spray : 80 def for 15 rations.

then comes the 2 little ones :

  • Sluice: 60 def for 15 rations
  • Slip 'n' Slide: 60 def for 15 rations

then the one which really isnt worth ti unless you have plenty of water you dont need for drinking or killing or other constructions.

  • Decon Shower

However there are 3 projects which you dont wanna skip first if you have them unlocked and have enough water:

  • Apple Tree: daily 3 to 5 premium food: for 10 rations
  • Vegi Plot : daily food... both premium and normal 10 Rations
  • fertilizers: increase food gain from vegi plot: 10 rations
  • Grapeboom: give daily grapefruits for both watch or killing zeds.

the next 2 projects need water BUT you dont really make a good use of them unless you are in a very organized town with many active players: so its not recommended for random towns or young metas who struggle with getting enough water as it is:

  • Water turrets: 70 def and can be upgraded : for 40 rations
  • Shooting gallery: 50 def and bonus for water weapons on watch, for 30 rations.

and last is ONE of the most powerful project in the game which also only can be useful in organized and super active and experienced towns:

  • Dump Upgrade: gives extra +1 def for each item you dump for temporary dump. for 20 rations.

all that being said : water management to get what you need to be able to build some or most of those above require very early expeditions because you need to find as many Worn leather bags with common BPs in them for 4 of the above , and you need to camp almost all buildings outside 9 km range to get rare BPs, because rare BPs are the only type of BPs which can give you a great supply of water. very rare BPs can help too but they are about luck more than skill so they come when they come but you dont plan with them...
