Hacksaw
Upgrading the zone adds 1 point to zone improvements and gives ~5% bonus to your survival chances

Installing a DO or plywood adds ~1.8 points to the zone and gives ~10% bonus to your survival chances.

Camping items give ~5% bonus per item to your survival chances.

Unless you have optimal chances (survivalist only) there's always a chance, however small it is, that you will die anyways.

Non-survivalists always have a minimum 10% chance to die. Survivalists could have as little as ~1% chance to die and still die.
