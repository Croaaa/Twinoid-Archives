When built, you can heal one Injury and one Infection per day in your house, by spending all your AP.
If you are Wounded:
The "Heal your injury (5 )" option appears at your Home
The following text will appear: "Your wound has been healed, at least superficially..."
If you are Infected:
"Heal your infection (6 )" option appears at your Home
The following text will appear: "Your wretched infection has been cured!".
If you are both Wounded and Infected:
You can only choose the "Heal your infection (5 )" option to heal your infection, spending 5 .
Then you can choose to "Heal your infection (5 )" again to heal your wound, spending another 5 .

Thats from wiki.
