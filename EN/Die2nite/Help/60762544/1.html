Taken from the Guide itself

Basically, everything strictly needed to kill zombies on Day 2.

Plus, enough iron/planks to build the basic stuff:
- Workshop
- Watchtower
- Pump (to get extra water for water pistols)
- Wall Upgrade v1

Plus, any melee weapons to kill zombies on day 1:
- Washer
- Fridge
- Oven
- Animals
- Fat cat
- Knife
- Hurling sticks
- Water Pistol
- Machete
- etc.

_________________________________

  • Priority Items to bring back on Day 1, 2 & 3:
    this list is extremly important and all items listed below must not be left outside because the entire plan for day 2, 3 & 4 depends on these items.
    first do not under any circumstances use Alcohol, Twinoids, Steroids or 7 AP food , bring them back with you because you will need them all for at least 12 to 14 missions on day 2 so every single booster will count.
    Priority Items to bring back on Day 1 for Day 2 scout´s and Survivalist´s Expditions:
  • Incomplete shopping Trolley, Manbag, Ultra Rucksack
  • Semtex, Detonator, Duct Tape, plastic bags (for Exploding Water Bombs)
  • Radius Mark II, Raduis Radar Beacon
  • Kwik-fix, Broken electric Device
  • FatCat, Hurling stick, serrated knife, Box Cutter
  • Furious Kitten (do not use it outside at all, survivalists will need them for far away expeditions)
  • Bandages or Chests if you can´t open them in the desert
  • Box Cutter use it with Fat Serpant in the desert if possible to get a Furious Kitten
  • Flash Powder only 4 are needed for 4 x Flash Granade
  • Water Purifying Tablets + Jerrycans
  • All kinds of powerful weapons you can build: mower, machete, chainsaw, Puta mk2, devastator.
  • 1 Broken Staff, 1 Wire Reel & 1 Empty Oil can only one of each nothing more is needed first 4 days.

    of course this list will change around Day 4 to 5+
