isaaclw

If you're using it in firefox, it shouldn't be different, but I'm not sure.

Can you verify that it's installed? Ctrl+Shift+A pulls up all your addons.

Can you verify that the version is the lastest version? 0.4.7

There are two ways to use it:
1) right click and choose "update maps"
2) find the button, and click it.

In order to use the button, you may need to drag it to your button bar.
You can do this by right clicking near the bar (just below it...)
http://i.imgur.com/Gz3nV5C.png

And then choosing "Customize"
http://i.imgur.com/ofwBOPc.png

Actually, easier is to do:
View -> Toolbars -> Customize

Then scroll through and find the button. Drag it to where you want to click it.

This plugin isn't as cool as Rulsey's script, or the chrome extension, so you have to manually click to use it.
