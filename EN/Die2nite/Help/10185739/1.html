The attack always takes place at 23:00.

But it takes place at 23:00 server time, and server time is France time.

France had their daylight savings this morning, so their clocks got boosted an hour at 2am (making it 3am instantaneously), so unless your country also bumped the clock an hour then it'll appear to be an hour earlier.

(To my understanding, countries in the southern hemisphere end up with a total two-hour shift since northern hemisphere goes forward an hour where southern hemisphere goes backwards an hour.)
