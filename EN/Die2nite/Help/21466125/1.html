Yeah - every time I go on watch, I find that certain items disappear or are broken in my inventory.
As far as I can tell (from limited experience):

  • Furniture has a tendency to break if it's a Guard item
  • Water guns and Battery guns weapons sometimes deplete/empty, partially or fully
  • Water bombs and consumables vanish
  • Food vanishes, water doesn't
  • Unassembled items can build themselves
  • Alcohol and drugs vanish sometimes too, but don't cause statuses (not sure, but I swear I've lost my Vodka on watch more than once). *hic*
