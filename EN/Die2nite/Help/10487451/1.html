HC town camping penalty is rather large, up to 13-14 AP of improvements (it is counted as total AP penalty, having Lighthouse built or several camping items could decrease it a bit). So if you started with: You reckon your chances of surviving here are hee haw... Might as well take some cyanide now
it is possible that you haven`t managed to pass this penalty yet to come out of the lowest chances. After you reach next level after hee-haw -> poor - chances should start to increase every couple AP spent. 10 AP is max you can put into one zone still of course, so you can never ugrade the zone to the level as in normal towns.

Still you should be able to obtain decent chances on first night camping even in HC town, if doing it right. Are you upgrading building zone or normal zone in the world beyond? Are there any zombies with you? How many AP from town are you? It all matters.
