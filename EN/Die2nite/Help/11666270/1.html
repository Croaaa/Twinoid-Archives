Layla

I believe I read somewhere that distinctions other than spiritual guide don't get awarded at all in those towns?

A) Is that true or false?

False (x2). Joker started to cover it. You (not the town per say but you specifically) must make it to Day 8 to be assured that all distinctions other than Spiritual Guide will count.

The 2nd (potential) falsehood is that you will get Spiritual Guide. It's probably the case for you since you said you're in to help the new folks but just to be sure: you only get that distinction if at least half the folks in town have less than 100SP.

B) If true, I'm assuming I should *not* read any RP items, since they wouldn't be counted, but the text would still be in my list, thereby blocking it from giving me a distinction later.

Is that correct?

The text will still be in your list and won't be counted ever if you weren't able to get the distinction in that town. The only way to be sure you'll get the distinction is to make it to Day 8 (dead in bed or post-devastation camping death).

HOWEVER (and this is a huge however) it looks like in Small Towns for 100+SP players who die before Day 8 there is some chance some distinctions other than Spiritual Guide will be given. Specifically, that Distinctions are assigned kinda like the Clash Private Towns with only a percentage of them given out randomly. I'm basing this off the This early distinction-loss filled 100+SP Day 7 Small Town death.

They got Spiritual Guide of course but also (for some unclear reason since Help says you shouldn't get nothing other than S.G.) Thefts, Profession, Deco, and Dead in Bed.

I don't know what's going on here but it could be 1) an early bug since corrected; 2) a bug still in effect; 3) an error in Help; 4) a standard happening based on some things we haven't determined yet (like getting a Dead In Bed the day before everything would have counted gets you a random %).

TL;DR: if you don't want to risk losing the text don't read it 'till you're sure it'll count. Hang on to it or raffle it or figure out a contest for it to be a prize for newbie fun if you're so inclined.
