If you're in a random town, scouting distant buildings is much less useful.

It might be very helpful to go on a multi-day survivalist camping spree, if you don't have many survivalists in town. That way you're saving the risk of non-survivalists dying from camping.

In a good town, Scout is typically the best choice for the first few days, though.

If you like camping, go Survivalist.
If you like bringing lots of stuff home, go Tamer.
If you like finding lots of stuff in the first place, go Scavenger.
If you like building stuff, go Technician.
If you're always helping people who are stuck, go Guardian.
If you are always getting stuck with no help from your townmates, go Scout.

With only a few days, I'd not suggest Shaman, can't always guarantee you'll get to see the pretty pretty floating souls on the map, and less likely you'll be able to retrieve them while you can still use them.
