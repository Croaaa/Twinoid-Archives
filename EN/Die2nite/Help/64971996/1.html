Sadly towns are taking more time to fill these days. Even if you find means to kill yourself now you'll enter a new town and will probably have to wait a few days (or even months if you choose Hardcore towns) to get day 1 rolling.

Get used to do long scavenge day 1. Deplete one zone at a time and keep checking the town numbers so you can go back when before it fills.

Other than that you can join D2n discord channel and try finding a group of people that play together so you don't have to wait.
