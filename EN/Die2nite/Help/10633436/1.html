Go to our die2nitewiki (search google) and search "Broken" in that site. The page that shows up explains how to repair broken items.

Items occasionally break when they are used as weapons in the World Beyond. Broken items are clearly marked by a red dashed border surrounding the item and the Broken... marker in their description.

Broken items can be repaired with a Kwik-Fix or a Repair Kit which costs 1 AP.
A Repair Kit breaks when used to repair a weapon. This must be repaired in the workshop.
