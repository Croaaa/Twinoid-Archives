GODisZED

everyone gets 18 ap to do whatever they want w/ if food and water are consumed (you start everyday w/ 6ap).
what makes the tech such an asset to the town, is that on top of the 6 AP given every morning, he/she receives 6 CP (construction points) which can be used to construct buildings or to convert materials in the workshop.
but remember, you MUST HAVE AT LEAST 1 AP available in order to use the CP.
it is extremely frustrating to come back in from a hard day of scavenging thinking you're going to use your 6 CP on something, only to find that you can't use them because you're out of AP!

MonkeyFLuffy

Tech is a great class if you only is capable of being on at the attack or one time a day or you like to be a builder =D

If you are able to make it out and scavenge you should do so but always calculate your route so you have 1 ap left when you get back to town =D as you need that ap for using your Construction Points(CP) =D

if you are not able to make it out and scavenge you should skip the water when you are not thirsty and skip the food all days you stay in town =D because you will have 6ap at the start of the day as everyone else but you will also have 6 CP to use in the Workshop/Construction site =D

One great way to use your CP is to wait until the end of the day before you use them as mopst resources and Blueprints will have been found until then =D (but that might not be able depending on your RL and Time zone)

Tech is also able to make key prints of the locked doors in the ruin =D
If you enter a ruin you should always make sure you hit so many locked doors at possible so you get all the key prints =D

When you are a tech you should not be the first one to enter the ruin as the only bonus/power you have in there you need some locked doors =D it's better to wait and let a Scavenger or Tamer go in first as they have different bonuses in the ruin =D (Scavenger have 50% extra oxygen and Tamer have a arrow pointing at the exit)

One more tip or play style with tech(at least i'm doing it)..
You can be the Gate keeper(if the time zone is right) because if you wait until the end of the day to use the CP you will have 1 ap left =D and it only need 1 ap to close the gates =D that's why it a good thing to be a Gate keeper as tech if you are able to be on at the attack time =D
