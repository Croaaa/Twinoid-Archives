If the other person is online and you coordinate with them they can leave by themselves before the countdown clock ends.

Using flashgrenades, breaktrough ability and camera shots will also turn that zone into a temporary safe zone for everyone in there. If no more players are there the zone is not safe anymore though (in case some new person enters there or someone decide to come back).
