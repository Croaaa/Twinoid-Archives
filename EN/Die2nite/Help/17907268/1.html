Correct, While we can't link to the wiki it's a good place to find info on questions such as this. From the wiki:

-Anyone can be escorted, but only Heroes can escort.
-One person can escort up to four Citizens at once.
-Escortees still contribute Control Points.
-Escortees can be given Food and Water, but not drugs or alcohol
-Escorts cannot be made to use hero abilities, e.g. tamers' dogs, or survivalists' books
-Escortees can be prompted to Search any zone, but they cannot go into Ruins.
-By default the escortees cannot be taken further away from Town, only closer, unless marked otherwise.
-By default the escort can view the escortees' Inventory and pick up/drop items, but only if marked so by the escortee
