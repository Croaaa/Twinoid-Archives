@Maeve:

How long is an SMS with codes supposed to take?

For each one, would s/he pay the payment sum or just the SMS cost?

Sometimes the sending of an SMS is delayed. This can take several hours but the SMS will be sent be our operator "Rentabiliweb". A delayed SMS is however a rare exception.

If after 24 hours you have not received your code(s), please contact our Technical Support (there is a special section for SMS) and they will help you.

You have to tell them:

Your mobile phone number (with prefix(es))
The date and time you have sent an SMS / made a call

The Rentabiliweb Support will then check it and give you the right code or a new code (depending on if they have found something in their data base).

You need 6 SMS in order to get two weeks of hero mode. Don't blame us for the prices, we do not make them. Our service providers take more than 50% on each SMS.

Payment by SMS is not recommended, we still offer it, because some people do not have access to credit cards or PayPal (or don't want to pay with these). We are working on new payment solutions to give you the biggest flexibility possible, but this will still take sometime.

Dayan
