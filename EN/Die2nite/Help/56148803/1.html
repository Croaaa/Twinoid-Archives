http://d2nwiki.com/
There you find lots of useful info

Water you get from well, you usually need to drink only 1 water in a day, but building pump lets you take 2 rations from well. Some buildings (drilling rig, eden project...) adds more water to well.

Food you can find from desert, by butchering animals (need butcher) and some buildings (apple tree, vegetable plot...) adds food into bank when day starts.

Other way of getting AP are drugs, alcohol,coffee and some smaller AP givers are dice, cards and guitar, but careful when using these, others may not like that you use all drugs when they might need them for some important expeditions.
