On march 11, 2013
melonhead

Back to the original question, radios no longer get turned on when held by watchmen, and lamps never did.

Your 2nd question, yes water from the hospital can be poisoned and in my experience they are poisoned more often than not (those in partially filled coolers cannot be poisoned).

The best way i find to 'deal' with the water is to never go in with your good water, and go in with an empty pistol or aqua splash. If you find a ration try to fill the weapon, if it doesn't fill then water is poison and if it does fill then you get a free weapon.

Never bring water rations out of a ruin because they can be easily mixed up with the good rations. If it happens you need to get them back to the bank where a poisoned ration won't stack.

I read somewhere you can check the XML feed to find out if a ration is poisoned but I don't know how to do that.
