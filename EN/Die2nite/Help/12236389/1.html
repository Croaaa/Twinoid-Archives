Reesan

The numbers next to your wins are the amount of titles you have won.

The numbers ItsNathan is talking about is your Twinoid score, which shows above your top distinctions. That's your Twinoid score on Die2Nite. The maximum is 1000, meaning you're very good at that game.

Edit: The wins (titles) give a set amount of points which contribute to that total. So the more titles you have, the higher your Twinoid score will be. It's Twinoid's way of saying you're very good at the game, by the way.
