The attack time is always at 23:00 and maintenance time is always at 0:00 (or thereabout). Most of North America changed their clocks already which is what makes it seem like the attack time has changed. Europe changes their clocks a week from now. Southern Hemisphere is a whole different animal that jumps in the opposite direction on their respective dates.

When the time changes in Europe, yes, the time will change here as well. Your best bet is to watch the server clock in the upper right of every screen to make sure you're back in town in time for the attack, or play it safe and just don't go out that day.
