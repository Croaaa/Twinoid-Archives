iDare

You only obtain bonus points for using the souls in your house, not actually collecting them in the desert. The first person to touch a soul (grab it to the rucksack) receives distinction Soul Collector, but nothing prevents from donating the soul to the bank afterwards or holding it in chest for later, it`s not being used immediately.

Still no confirmation on leaderboards that using souls gives points as it should (no bonus points for any town displayed), there have been some issues with it in Hordes too and players from french version claim that it was fixed and although it doesn`t show any bonus points, the points are counted and added to total score of your town.

I suggest keeping the souls in bank/chest though, until it`s 100% confirmed.
