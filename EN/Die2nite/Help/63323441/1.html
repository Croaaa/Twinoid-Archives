If you use the Exact Estimate calculator
with +25 / +50 / +75 / +100 overflow
depending on day count
and yesterday overflow actual estimate
then you can use this formula to help you plan defense
without over covering:

=IF( B3,ROUND(((B5*B4)-(B3*B6))/(B5+B4-B3-B6),0),"-" )

Basically:

B3 = Low 30% = L30
B4 = High 30% = H30

B5 = Low 100% = L100
B6 = High 100% = H100


EE = ((B5*B4)-(B3*B6))/(B5+B4-B3-B6)

EE = ((L100*H30)-(L30*H100))/(L100+H30-L30-H100)

will give you a number to cover instead of MAX

then later on, you
add +25 for overflow
then +50 for overflow
then +75 for overflow
and +100 for overflow from day 20 to 40
