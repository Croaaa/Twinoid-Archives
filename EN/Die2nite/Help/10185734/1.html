On march 25, 2012
Hacksaw
If the only way you have to open a can is by spending AP in the workshop, then best to leave them closed until another (free to use) opener comes along, such as a a can opener, screwdriver, or hacksaw (I'm pretty sure there are a few other tools that'll open them, but don't remember them offhand). Usually doesn't take long for another opener to appear, so long as there's a decent number of people AS'ing through the day.

Doggy bags, on the other hand, are best left closed until you're ready to eat/cook them, so that escortees can be made to drop them.
