If you want to live... Follow me!

When you join a town,
please update From Dusk Till Dawn external map,
the lives and the quality of your town depends on it.

Click [My Soul]
Click [Settings]

Check: Authorise external applications [x]


In Site Settings of die2nite.com, allow:
[Allow] JavaScript
[Allow] Flash
[Allow] Images
[Allow] Pop-ups and Redirects
[Allow] Ads

Reload the page

Click on the book (game page top-left corner)
Click on From Dusk till Dawn (4th red icon in the overlay menu)
Click the red button: Proceed to From Dusk Till Dawn

if it does NOTHING, you have a popup blocker,
or the Chrome built-in popup blocker (see above).

The URL should be something like:

http://d2n.duskdawn.net/map?key={YOURKEY}

Bookmark this page.

Click [Update My Zone] for each square you visit.

Thank you
