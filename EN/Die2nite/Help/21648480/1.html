The shaman actions like curing infections and making it rain with a soul are not 100%. It is not uncommon for them to fail.

From the Shaman page in the Help Section:
Heal citizens in town. Relieve terror, infections or to even extract alcohol or drugs from the bodies of their comrades. Success is not guaranteed, however. Although, sometimes during the healing process, the Shaman takes the illness which plagues the victim into their own body.
