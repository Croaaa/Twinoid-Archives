It won't be a 'zone', it will be a corridor.

If you come across in a ruin you get the option to use something from your rucksack, or to flee.

The 7% part is not exactly correct, because much depends on the speed of your internet connection, and personal response time.

If you have a very slow connection, and slow response time, from the time you come across until you have fled in to the next corridor, you may have used up to 15% of your oxygen (10% for a ).

Beware that if the 's you fled will be blocking your path back to the entry/exit you will have to go through the same 'fleeing' process again.

The advice you have been given on water found in the ruins is not correct.

From my experience, well over 50% of the water found in ruins is poisoned. The exception is in an abandoned hospital where you can find a water cooler with X number of rations that will not be poisoned.

It's good practice to take empty water based weapons or an empty water cooler in to a ruin - DO NOT TAKE YOUR OWN WATER.

Test any water you find, and if it fills the weapon or the cooler then you get a free weapon or water, and if it doesn't fill then you know you have a poisoned ration, and you should drop it where you found it.

It will use up oxygen while you try to fill a weapon or a water cooler in a ruin - Again this depends on your net connection and personal response time.
