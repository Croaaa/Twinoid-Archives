Tips & Advice for New Players [Guide]

Hello and welcome to Die2nite World:
here are few Tips and Advice which may help you understand, survive and enjoy the game:

  • External Map FDTD
    this game is almost impossible to play without using the external map, all players use FDTD because the game´s map does not offer any informations like exact number of zombies or which items are on the ground. so if you avoid using the external map you will most likely get trapped by zombies and die almost every time you go outside. also it is considered team play if everyone uses FDTD so others can avoid zombies and we know which items to collect from each zone outside.
    the external map is called "From Dusk Til Dawn"
    you can find it in the Directory Book here
    you can set it up to work by following this step to Authorize External Application here
  • Stay alive:
    most important advice is to never take risks. its best to always count your AP exactly to return back to town and never camp outside unless its well planned camping a known building.
  • Daily Action Points
    we each get by start of each day 6 Starting Action Points (AP) and you can gain daily another 6 AP from drinking a water ration, and another 6 AP from eating food.
  • Drink Everyday!
    always drink water daily and never skip a day. those daily 6 AP are very valuable and they are lost from the town if you dont drink.
  • Food is For Scavenging:
    its best to save food for scavengers who go outside to look for items outside.
  • Don´t waste Drugs or Alcohol
    its best to not use drugs or alcohol unless its either an Emergency to help someone out of AP or a planned trip outside which require more than your daily 18 AP from water and food to reach a specific zone.
  • Never Mix Pharmaceutical Products
    wait and see if a hero is in town to build a Lab to mix them to gain good drugs for the town instead of getting bad or useless drugs from direct mixing
  • Drop everything in bank
    keeping items to yourself wont get you far since its a team player game and the bank is for everyone, only at the end of each town when the end is near , keeping Defensive objects or decorations becomes ok. but the point here is to no overreact when useless items are being kept by new players because they need to learn the mechanics first before you can judge them.
    registering complaints for people who keep normal not important items is´t wise or useful strategy. it only create tension without any reward. positive atmosphere is the best thing to have especially for new players.
  • Building Projects in Construction Site:
    try to read the forum especially the sticky thread called "Construction Site" to see what other players have suggested to build next to avoid randomly working on random projects not needed or suggested by others. the main idea behind Construction site is to build what the town needs at each day and stage. sometimes it needs water so you build (Pump) and water project as a high priority. sometimes it needs projects which save AP like Factory. most of the times Defense is needed , so we build defensive projects to cover daily attacks. and with time you will learn which projects are the most needed and with it learn how Blue Prints work.
  • Scavenging:
    there can be no work in construction site to build projects if we don´t bring home resources like Plank, Iron, Nuts&Bolts..etc. so the key for success is to Scavenge outside by depleting replenished zones. a replenished zone can give us a rare items from either first search or every 2 hours Autosearching in the same zone. once the zone depletes then it can no longer give us rare items and instead it can only give (Rotten Logs or Metal Scrap) so its essential to stay in each replenished zone til it depletes then move to next replenished zone which may require logging in 2 times a day to check and move.
    when you plan to go outside take your water and food from the Well and Bank and plan to have exactly enough AP to return back home. for example if you plan to go to 9 AP range then you need 9 AP to get there then 9 AP to return back home safely. so count your AP and open the FDTD map before you go outside to avoid zombies.
    if your destination is not uncovered yet by other players then take weapons from bank like loaded water pistols, battery launchers or any repairable weapons.
  • If you get Trapped, ask for Help in forum and set full Escort on:
    never punch zeds with your bare hands or flee zombies which will get you injured, instead ask for help and be patient, others need you alive so you can help them with your AP. so someone will come help you eventually. remember to set Full Escort on by allowing others to see your items and move you towards town to save you.
  • Return to town before 22.30
    set alarm in your phone to remind you everyday to get online to come back home. most new players die because they forget to return.
  • Do Not Play The Guitar early before 22.15
    the Guitar is an item which can be built from (Wire Reel, broken Staff and Empty Oil Can). the Guitar can be used on watch, so it must be in the bank at all times. however the guitar can be played once daily to give all players present inside town 1 bonus AP. but we can only get this 1 AP if we have less than 6 AP. so the best and only time to play the guitar is in the last 45 minutes before the attack. by then the majority would have used their AP and ca win the bonus 1 AP. therefore it guarantees the maximum gain of AP for the town.
    so please do not play the guitar before 22.15 server time and its best to even wait til all scavengers are back inside before the day ends.

this so far seem to cover most of the basics and more may become overwhelming. so if you have questions ask or read the Die2Nite Wiki which is 90% accurate

Inspiration for the people

one of the 2 most important things which random towns struggle with are

  • Blue Prints (the lack of knowledge to manage them by holding normal BPs til explorable ruin BPs get opened), with this problem comes the fear/avoiding to map and explore the explorable ruin which offers a huge boost of projects that can be unlocked which can be considered overpowered for random towns. so this issue can be solved by encouraging randoms to search for the explorable ruin which is inside 10km range (between 7 and 14 AP range ish) and once located encourage them to go map it and send techs to open locked doors and deplete it. and alongside that educate about which type of BPs to open and which not to open yet and why... the organized Play Guide in residents forum index has that.
    • follow up to this point: most common mistake is camping building inside 9 km ranges , those all give uncommon BPs this category of projects is not really game changing with one exception out of 28 (guardtower + lots and lots of guardian heroes). so if there is going to be any effort or boosters (drugs and alcohol and exploding water bombes) used to camp a building then the building should be in 10 km range or higher to gain a rare BP , rare BPs category unlocks 4 water projects, 3 food projects and the majority of all powerful effective defensive projects , so rare BPs are the main and highest priority goal to camp for.
      finding the 5 architect chests randomly placed on 5 zones which you have to look for will give 5 free rare BPs out of the total 22.
  • killing zombies in close ranges, random town players in general avoid going out after day 2 because of fear of running into zeds and getting trapped , also they fear of getting shunned or getting complaints if they use water for weapons to kill zeds. and thats a very common mistake. the best way to take full control of the map in a random town is to kill every single zed inside 9 AP range in the first 3 days , if those zeds spread to 10 or 11 or 12 AP ranges , then also kill them using drugs and alcohol. zed free scavenging ground for all is the key to finding key rare resources like N&B and EC for construction site and Guitar components..etc
    this point leads to effective scavenging and with that you find resources to build high defense projects.



souls are easy and trivial thing which randoms manage well most of the times, making the shaman make it rain at the gates for extra water is the most important part about shaman.



about hero class selection:
since in random towns freedom of choosing a hero class is the Norm it will be difficult to control what each person picks because they cant access forum to read before picking a class. so all random players have to rely on their favorite choice of class before they join or experience of what the town needs the most.



but in general for random towns or organized here are few tips:

  • one tech is enough to make Blanks of keys for the explorable ruin locked doors maybe 2 if one cant be relied on to volunteer. the common mistake that the 6 CAP are the most useful from this class is statistically false. most people who pick tech in random towns are those who rarely go outside and those do not really scavenge at all or if they go out, just for few searches, not all day long. so this is overrated class even in random towns because those who pick it like to build, but the key in this game is scavenging hard in big numbers.
  • guardians are the most helpful for long survival, for more town defense and safety on watch. with guadrtower it becomes an overpowered combo. so if a town can have 25 guardians its still not enough...
  • you need maybe 6 to 8 survivalists to both save some water to use it for water pistols to kill zeds with or use it for constructions, and to camp for rare BPs with 100% survival odds.
  • tamers are good for using light weapons and sending them home after use. but since its a random town and almost nothing will be killed outside 10 km ranges , its not really badly needed. you can manage a random town without any tamers.
  • scavenger: this is the most useless class in the game, but sadly the most fun to play. so good luck convincing people not to choose it. every zone inside 9 AP range eventually get depleted in first 5 days if enough people go scavenge, so it really doesnt matter if you deplete zones in 6 hours or 8 hours or 20 hours... the extra oxygen inside the explorable ruin is nice , but almost everyone can manage ruins without it. so no scavengers is best...
  • scouts, this is a unique class which ca´t be replaced with another , and if exploring the map outside 10 km range is a goal to find building to camp for rare BPs then you would need 2 to 3 scouts . collecting souls is also part of this class job. as long the one who picks it volunteers to collect souls.



sorry if its too much to read. i tend to write 10 lines and i finish with 100.
