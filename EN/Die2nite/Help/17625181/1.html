The answer for all the 3 above questions is yes.
Each ranked town in your history counts for the distinction.

For instance: If you have 1 ranked town from Season 3 in distant ranks, next you manage to place 1 town in distant rankings + 2 towns in HC ranking this season, you will have total of 4 ranked towns on your soul when the next season starts.
