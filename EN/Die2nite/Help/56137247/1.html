thewolf661. What is the chance you become wounded if someone hits you with a bare hands?

Depends of town type and where the attack happened (inside, outside).
As a rule of thumb, if you are outside or if you are playing a Hardcore town, your chances of getting wounded are higher.

thewolf662. Does equipping weapons incrase your chances to kill somebody?
2a. If it does, does it matter which weapon you choose. Example-knives are very useful, water weapons dont do anything because they work only against zeds.

As Catnoir said, you can't kill people by attacking them this way. Equipping weapons or do it "bare-handed" has no effect whatsoever on the wound chance, but this was not thoroughly tested


thewolf663. Can you insta-kill someone?

Yes, but not by attacking them. You are using the wrong tool there.

thewolf664. What happens with the town register, is the murder always recorded or it is 50/50 like the thefts?

All deaths are recorded in the registry.

Thefts are not always 50/50. There are some other factors playing here, such as if you cleaned your uniform or not and if the target cleaned and tidied his home or not.
