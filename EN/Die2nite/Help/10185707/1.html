Hacksaw
Animals can't be dumped as weapons or food, only as animals.

I'm fairly certain
logs and planks can only be dumped an the wood dump
iron and scrap metal can only be dumped in the metal dump
weapons can only be dumped in the weapon's dump (most of these appear in the weapon's section of the bank, but certain weapons, like the pistol and machine gun, can appear in other sections; also, I believe the torch counts as a DO, but the burnt out torch is a weapon; also also, weapons with charges (like water pistols) must have at least one shot remaining to be a weapon, empty water pistols and battery launchers don't count for some odd reason)
DO's can only be dumped in the defense dump
animals can only be dumped in the animal dump (note the fat cat appears in the decoration section and the guard dog appears in the DO section of the bank)
