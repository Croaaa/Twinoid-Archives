That`s difficult to answer as most likely was never tried in our version. You can`t use Cyanide on day 1, so dehydration death from walking would be best option to try it, still boosts allowing for it (drugs, coffee, booze) are hard to get often instantly after joining new town - and instant death is needed to test this.

In hordes some players abused game in similar way (all those who didn`t have been granted a good guy distinction), walking and dying on day 1 repeatedly, to gain some very rare distinctions (like rp texts) thanks to joining multiple towns on same day granting multiple searches when walking before dying, but there is far bigger activity in french version allowing the towns to fill faster. Also rules of granting distinctions were changed after this happening, to prevent it.


Trying to answer your question, it should be possible to join a new town after dying, as long as it`s not the very same town you just died in. With new rules there is a ban after 2 straight early dehydration deaths though, it could be some issue if walking dehydration is counted as being inactive. There are also ghoul related deaths possible to maybe allow for this (either be eaten by one or punched when being a ghoul).

Perhaps there`s someone who managed to die before their town has filled to answer if the same town is crossed out in available towns list.
