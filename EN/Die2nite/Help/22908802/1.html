spacekadt

You lose the hero day *after* that day has been used, whether by surviving and seeing the next day or by dying.

The only way I know of to confirm this (and has been confirmed many times in our meta towns) is that when your soul says you have 1 day left and you're in a town, you will lose hero status in the next attack.

There are a few caveats that aren't terribly important (like not losing more than one hero day per physical day), but for the sake of the original question, the system is working as intended.
