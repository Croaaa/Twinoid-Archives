After viewing source code on Hordes and then looking for the same bit of code in D2N's source I just copied the necessary text into the town link -

.die2nite.com/#city/enter? go=city/guard;sk=be07f <-- add that part onto end of town "Homepage" link (the link that appears after you click Overview of town) and that will open Watch page; the sk= part appears to be the town code so yours will be different than this one.

At the part of the whole link where it says - go=city/guard you have to add the word 'City' at the end, so it'll look like go=city/guardCity

The whole thing to get it to work should look like -

.die2nite.com/#city/enter?go=city/guardCity;sk=be07f - obviously your sk= will be different and you need to put www. first, but yeah, that overcomes the issue...

So that gets you on Watch without having to click the Watchmen button.

EDIT: To get off watch, replace go=city/guardCity with go=city/unguardCity
