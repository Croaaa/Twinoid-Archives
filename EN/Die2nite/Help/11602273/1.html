I think it is pretty clear that he means Soul Points.
And here is the answer: you get 1 point for staying alive on Day 1.
You get +2 for Day 2 (total of 3), etc.
Day 10 would mean 55 points.

A day only counts as lived if you die by the horde, or die camping after the town is devastated.
