Welcome to the Die2Nite Help Forum!

By registering your Twinoid account, you agreed to Motion Twin's Terms & Conditions (If you did not read them yet, you are encouraged to do so).

We reserve the right to evaluate each incident on a case by case basis. The action we take may be more lenient or more severe depending on your moderation history.

Forum Rules
English is the only written language on these forums.
Don't use excessive text-speak.
Don't hijack threads for your own purposes/try to stay on topic.
Don't engage in personal insults or attack other users, moderators or administrators. (This includes public accusations of improper behavior/"name and shame" posts.)
Keep all topics specific to Die2Nite or Die2Nite-related. Discussion of other MT games is not appropriate here.
Do not spam threads with excessive smilies, text, line breaks or other disruptive content. This includes repeated incorrect usage of cite/rp/other tags.
Don't encourage rule-breaking or visibly react to those that do so. Use the report functionality to block a post and alert the moderators.
Advertisements aren't allowed and any links to websites which require registration are forbidden.

Be aware that there is a zero tolerance policy for anyone attacking any past or present moderators for D2N or moderators for any Motion-Twin game. This includes Twinoid admins and developers.

This policy is not language-restrictive. Language that falls under this policy will always be subject to the repercussions listed, whether it is inappropriate in English or any other language.

If you need to contact Motion-Twin or us, here's a list:

In these forums you will encounter many other players who share different experiences and come from different backgrounds. While certain language and images may not be offensive to you, consider the fact that the same language and images may have a completely different effect on someone else.

As a reminder, your access to these forums is a privilege and not a right. Motion Twin reserves the right to suspend your access to these forums at any time for reasons that include, but are not necessarily limited to, failure to follow these rules.

The Help forum is for questions about the game. Feel free to ask your questions here and/or answer others' questions. If you do answer questions, please make sure you are giving correct information. If you have to preface your answer with "I think this is how it works, but..." then you are better off leaving the question for someone that is sure of the answer.

If there is a thread with multiple questions, please quote the question you're answering so that 1) it cuts down on confusion, 2) the question and the answer are easily visible in the same post.
