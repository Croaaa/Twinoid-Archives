Yes, but not because it is in the same spot, because it is a consecutive night of penalty.

Actually every new attempt of camping in the same town (if you camped 3 days from now for example) will have the multi-night penalty. It is not consecutive, it is cumulative

These penalties are also different depending on the Town Type (normal / hardcore) and are affected by the Pro-Camper perk (if you are a Hero.

Regular people without the perk:
-20/-20 2nd Night (Normal/HC)
-45/-30 3rd Night (Normal/HC)
-65/-40 4th Night (Normal/HC)
-80/-50 5th Night (Normal/HC)

Pro-Camper Heroes:
-10/-5 2nd Night Pro (Normal/HC)
-20/-10 3rd Night Pro (Normal/HC)
-40/-20 4th Night Pro (Normal/HC)
-50/-30 5th Night Pro (Normal/HC)
-65/-40 6th Night Pro (Normal/HC)
-80/-50 7th Night Pro (Normal/HC)
