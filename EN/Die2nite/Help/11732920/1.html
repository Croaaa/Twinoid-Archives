It's possible a poisoned ration and a non-poisoned ration will have a predetermined order in your rucksack, like everything else seems to have, so figure out that order and you can guess which one to drop. Then again, if the only ration in the zone is a poisoned ration, I believe Dusk Till Dawn will note it as poisoned.

But as ItsNathan said, when you go to use an item form your rucksack, the list randomizes, so you can never know which ration you are using.
