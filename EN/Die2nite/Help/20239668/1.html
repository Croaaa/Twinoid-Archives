You are correct (no matter even, if we assume a DO install gives 1.8 or 1.99 chances increase, depending of the source).

To max your chances make 8 normal upgrades + 2 installs (in this order - install DOs or Sheet of Plywood) for total of ~12 upgrades + another ~2 upgrades worth from tombing in the end.

You keep your max zone topology with 1 upgrade + 1 install + tombing on each of the next days.


Using Camping Calculator you can easily estimate your chances everyday and plan on which day you want to start improving using this method. It`s only really needed from the day your chances are dropping below 100% for survivalist or 90% for non-surv when using normal 10 upgrades camping method (multi camping penalty).
