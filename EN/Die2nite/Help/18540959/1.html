Hacksaw

I can get behind the granted hero days

  • if a hero is eaten, the hero day isn't totally wasted (though I'm not behind paying for someone else's her days against my will
    • edit: and if a hero is eaten near the end of the day, it's like extending that one hero day into two, increasing the value of the dollar used to buy it.
  • it's a good way for heroes to recoup some days if they land in a crappy town
  • it's a good way for heroes to stock up a few extra days for brother in arms
  • it's another way for residents to taste hero mode, which could lure them into becoming paying customers
  • it can tempt greedy people thus increasing their chances of getting caught and killed sooner (the double edge is it also tempts them into being more destructive than they need to be).



All in all the hero days earned are pretty minimal compared to the life of the town you'll most likely be spending them in, so it isn't a very good incentive to be eating people unless you can live a good long while in the town you are a ghoul or are already a hero to stack the very few days on top of.
