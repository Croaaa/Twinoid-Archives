Souls are visible in in-game map only (PICTURE) and only when spawned in revealed zones (doesn`t matter if they`re revealed thanks to being walked or by Watchtower, Radius Radar Beacon, finally by Hot Air Balloon which opens the whole map) and they are always located in the bottom-right corner of the 4 zones-square the soul flies by.

It is possible that there are 2 or more souls in one and the same zone and there will be only visible one soul in shaman`s vision.

Sometimes the soul appears to be flying beyond the map or it is flying over the unrevealed zones, but the bottom-right corner zone is always revealed.
For instance: If a soul was located in -14/-3 zone in THIS example, its route in shaman`s vision would look more or less like THIS.

Souls only appear from deaths at attack time, so cyanide/poison death, dehydration by walking, eaten by ghoul or ghoul punched won`t produce any soul till the end of the town.
