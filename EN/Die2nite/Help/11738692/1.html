To follow up, remember the Order of Attack, which is roughly:

1. At the end of the day, anyone caught outside who isn’t camping dies. Anyone who’s infected has a chance to die. Anyone addicted who hasn’t had a drug dies. Anyone who’s dehydrated dies.

2. There is usually a chance someone will die if they are camping. These people die now if they are not lucky.

3.The upgrade voted on by the town is now upgraded. Water Turrets consume their water and provide their defense.

4. Damage done to buildings in Hardcore mode is now applied.

5. Any corpses left inside have a chance to kill people and a chance to destroy rations in the well.

6.LASTLY, the attack occurs. Anyone eaten dies. Anyone attacked but not eaten becomes Terrorized.

Since LMS is awarded from being the last alive eaten in town, well, there you go, cause (6) is later than (1).

Maybe you'll get lucky.
