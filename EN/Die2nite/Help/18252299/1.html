Well, aside Ghouls and Watchmen (wtf with Scouts?) the other MAJOR changes are:

  • Survs book now has a sensible chance of failing.
    Actual chances are still being figured out here;
  • Shamans and Souls distinctions/title no longer count towards Twinoid Score;
  • Hardcore Towns now have 2 Explorable Ruins per map;
  • Preset BPs pools now have different icons from the vanilla BPs. Each Ruin BP pool now has a specific icon for it, no more confusing on what is preset and what isn't.
    Oh, and Guardians now might be slightly more useful than a Resident in meta towns, but are fearful opponents in the fore-coming Clashes, although, considering all the changed things this season, I'd bet higher on 1st Tamers, 2nd Guardians, 3rd Survs/Scouts.
