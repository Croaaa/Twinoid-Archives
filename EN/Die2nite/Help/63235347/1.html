Not really, in organized towns people used to combine the two roles (shaman + guide) to make the Shaman job of recovering souls easier (even if it is just +1 CP).

In semi organized towns or towns with less heroes the Guide can be a Resident whose job is to save AP and rescue people that failed to login later in the day, or someone that organize expeditions.

On random towns the Guide role doesn't always land for someone that will be able to use it, whether because the playstyle doesn't match the Guide benefits or whether the person is willing to help town that much.
