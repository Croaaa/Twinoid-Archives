And to pull from Help:Your Soul
+++++
Distinctions

Every time you die, you are awarded Distinctions for many things you have done during the game: number of zombies killed, rare objects found, etc...

Distinctions show what you have achieved during your lives and can be seen on your profile page by other players.

Warning: if you die, distinctions earned on the last day will not be awarded (a few distinctions are exceptions to this rule, for example: banishment distinctions, distinctions relating to death, etc.)

In any event, starting on day5, this rule will not apply any more: all distinctions you earn will be awarded.
+++++

So after Day 5 in a town that shouldn't be happening.

Also, I will add again, each time I died from the attack while in town, as per Death and Soul Points:
A)
+++++
When you die, you are awarded a certain number of points depending on the number of complete days survived during your last incarnation
A day is considered "lived in full" if you survive the night or die from consequences arising from the zombie attack at 23:00. Death of any other kind will render the day invalid.
+++++
B)
+++++
If you die in town during the zombie attack: your day is complete! For example, if you die during the zombie attack on the night following day 5, you will be awarded points for the full 5 days you survived (the 5th and final day is valid).
+++++
