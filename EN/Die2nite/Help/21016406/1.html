spacekadt

A wounded leg can cause you to not move and lose an AP. This is from when you originally got wounded, not a new wound from walking (which isn't possible).

From the wiki:

There are six different injuries; all of them reduce you to a maximum of 5 AP and may contribute other effects:

Eye - Chance to find an item while scavenging is reduced

Foot - No other penalty

Head - Your speech in the forums is altered, as if you were stuttering.

Arm - You cannot work at the construction sites (building or repairing building damage) or open/close the town gates. (You can transform items in the Workshop.)

Leg - As you walk around in the wastelands, there is a chance that you will fail to move from one screen to another, resulting in the loss of that AP used.

Hands - Certain objects become unusable.
