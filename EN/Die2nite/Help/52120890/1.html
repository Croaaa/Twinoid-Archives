you only count as a watchmen if the attack is one single zed or higer than the town defence.

so if you join watch and at the same time the townÂ´s defence without your watchmen defence is higher than the attack you will NOT count as a watchmen at all. neither will your items in your rucksack will be used and nor will you get any penalty.

about the penalty: you get 10% negative safety after each night you join watch. and you get rid of only 5% of that penalty every day you do not join watch and take a break.

so if you join watch day 4 for example you join with 95% as a resident for example.
on day 5 you will have 95% - 10% = 85% safety.
if you dont join watch on day 5 you will have 90% on day 6 .
if you also dont join watch on day 6 you will have 95% on day 7.

if you join 2 nights in a row your penalty will be 10% + 10% = 20% negative penalty.
