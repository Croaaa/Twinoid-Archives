Actually, if you give someone an item via private message, you cannot steal from that person for the rest of the day. But you CAN steal and then send the item back via private message (leave a note on their door and send gifts).

"Discreetly donating" an object counts as your break-in for the day, so you can no longer steal or pillage that day (unless in chaos mode, but that's a whole different kettle of fish).
