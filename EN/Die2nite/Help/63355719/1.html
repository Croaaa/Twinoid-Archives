Watchtower wins on small maps too in my opinion.

Not only it reveals pretty much all map on WT lv3 (so less people get trapped), but it can make you reach most zones with base + food + water on WT lv5 with the teleport function.

That alone saves more AP in the long run than all of Workshop upgrades combined. If you do the math. A 25% discount on AP for constructions isnt soooooo good. Most constructions are around 30-50 AP to build right? So with WS lvl 5 they'll be 22-37 AP.

Now a few things:

  • You should either go WT or WS. Not 1 upgrade of this then another upgrade of WS and so on.
  • The Daily Upgrade vote is not something the town should decide based on "whoever gets the most vote wins" it should be debated which one the town needs and all town (or just one people really) vote for it.

As a matter of fact and it applies to all Upgrades, no one should vote at all. Only the town planner (and usually at the end of the day) votes for whatever the town needs most and the town will get it. Early votes to the wrong thing can ruin a town easily.
