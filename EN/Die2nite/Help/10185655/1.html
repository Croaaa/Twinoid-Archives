Shadow
Scout- sneak past almost all zombies, can see the a building that's buried (perk for scouts)

Guardian- gives one cp when up against zombies, plus one defense for the town.

Tamer- has a dog that brings back things to town and either place them in your chest or the actual bank (your choice) but can't bring back heavy items unless you give him steroids.

Scavenger-cuts half an hour on searches, and also more oxygen when exploring ruins.

Technician- they have special kind of ap called Cp (standing for construction points) that allows them to help better in the construction of things but you need at least one ap to use these. they can make blank keys from the explorable ruins that you can bring back to the workshop to make the actual key.

Survivalist- they can get a 100% chance of living for camping. they also get free water everyday..

now...there isn't a "best" one, you can choose your favorite though, i would recommend the scout if you want to work alone or search for buildings all over the map..(and showing zombie locations)
