It happens, but don't bet on it. What you need to do is be proactive. If you are in a town with 5 or 6 people who are active, then ask them if they would want to join up with you in another town. Then in the next town reach out to more people, and so on. Build groups of coalitions, with enough heroes to lead the coalitions into the same town at the same time. Go through 5 or 6 towns collecting 1 or 2 people each time, and pretty soon you have a Meta coalition going.

That is the best way to get a decent town together.
