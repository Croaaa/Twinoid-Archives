Go to your twinoid profile. You can get there from hovering over your name in the top right corner and in that little side pop out menu clicking on the icon on the top left corner that has your profile picture and your name.

Once at your twinoid profile, towards the top is a link that says "account settings", click on it.

Once there you'll see a tab that says "login / Email", click on it.

You should be able to change it there. Although I've never done it myself this seems to be the correct place.
