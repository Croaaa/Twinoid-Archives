In my last town I bought a pre-war camera and found I had access to it instantly. This fact may or may not be relevant.

The first charge I used was a success. It allowed me to escape a zone unscathed, and so I survived for one more day.

The second charge, which I had to use on the following day, failed to work. This is to be expected; I understand the chance of success is smaller with each successive charge.

When I used the third charge, I was informed that it had been successful, and a 30-second timer appeared. The problem is, it didn't actually allow me to run. No movement arrows appeared in the window, even when I refreshed my browser (Chrome). It was as though I was still trapped. The 30 seconds quickly depleted and I was left with no other option but to flee and become injured.

I died that night. I don't know that I can blame the camera's failure directly, but the injury made returning to town much less practical (less AP) and there were no bandages there anyway, so I just pressed on to a doomed camping attempt. Either way, I think this is a pretty serious bug - if it is indeed a bug, but I don't see what else would have prevented me from leaving the zone during the 30 seconds after I used the camera.

(EDIT: Before anybody informs me that I should have waited for someone to rescue me - well, first of all that's not the point here, and secondly I was pretty far from town and I don't think anyone would have been able.)
