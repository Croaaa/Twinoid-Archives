Hacksaw

The numbers by the -like symbol are added together to determine the total number of zombies you can help prevent from breaking in town. (positive numbers are a bonus that's added and negative numbers are a penalty that's subtracted).

The numbers by the -like symbol are added together AND added to the base percent chance of survival as seen on the town overview page to determine your chances of surviving while on watch. (again, positive numbers are a bonus that's added and negative numbers are a penalty that's subtracted).

Nothing I'm aware of changes your chances of being injured or terrorized.
