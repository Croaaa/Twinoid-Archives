scavenge scavenge, the first 4 days minimum of every town, unless you log in at like >20:00 and see a key building not complete, there are a lot of people who like to sit and build, being one to scavenge makes the town run a lot smoother.

as far as what to build, whatever gives good defense. You always want battlements, the wiki can tell you a lot more about the buildings, sometimes even outright saying if their worth it or not.

in random towns i find resources to be the first limit, then total town AP, though a good one might have blueprints starve them out(though there are some really key ones you'd like to find).

Scavenge scavenge scavenge, then build every decent permanent defensive structure you can and see where you go from there.
