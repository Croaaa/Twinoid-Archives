Looks like you are starting over whit a new account and therefore have to play some small towns.

What you describe is quite typical for small towns. Usually there is about 15 - 20 people inactive. My guess is, those are people who play other twinoid games, decide to try out D2N, but don't like what they see, and immediately leave again. Nothing you can do about them really.

Then there is the usual 5 - 10 day 1 deaths. This is often a mixed group. Some people forget to watch their Ap and get stuck. Some try to explorer far from town and get stuck whit zombies, and some just forget to return in time.

So what you really is left whit, is about 10 guys who are actually playing.
If you can get those 10 guys to work together, then you can get the town running for 6 - 8 days.


But you have to do something yourself. If the forums is dead, well then what are you doing to change that ?

When joining a new town, then look for a welcome post and say hi to the other guys, if there is none, then start one yourself.
Look in the construction and town upgrade forums and see if anyone have suggested something to build and vote for.
Perhaps someone is asking for help, well help them out if you can.

My point is, beginner towns are, believe it or not, full of beginners. If no one says anything, the town will die after a few days. But if you make an effort yourself, then often you will see other people respond and start talking about what to do.



And the same apply to your overall question, whatever the community is dead or not. Some people will say it is, some people will say it's not.
New towns are stating daily. Some last a few days, some a week or two, some more then a month.
You can get to play the game in whatever flavour you prefer, if you make an effort yourself.
