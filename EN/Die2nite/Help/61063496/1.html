Look pal, there are no bugs in that feature. You are just misusing it somehow.

For someone to follow you into a town they must be available in Limbo (not in a town) and then go to Soul > Coalition and Join a new town.

It is not you that needs to click 'Join a town' to bring people there, it is them. Being a hero or not doesnt matter, all it takes is for them to be available (dead, in Limbo) and in the same coalition as you as well as having slots on the town you are.

To drag others to teh same town of you (the jump) you click to join a town in the town select page (so, you are dead before that, right?) and all available members will follow you automatically.

On the coalition page you have 2 red buttons:

Change my status
Quit the coalition

On top of those there's a text saying your current status.

If someone is dead and their coalition status is set to 'unavailable', that person will not follow you to a town if you enter it.

Sometimes people on coalition are automatic set to Unavailable if they don't log often (to prevent other players to drag a dead body inside a town).

I think your buddy was set to Unavailable and couldn't follow you there when you jumped.

Now why he failed to join you through the join button? I dunno, maybe he was already in that town (you cant rejoin a town you just died/left) or some other reason.

There is nothing about being Hero or not.
