I agree with Dream, that page has quite a few wrong values.

I do have lots of sample data gathered by the igloo, so let's take a look at it:

4568 hurling stick kills, 1144 hurling stick breakages, 3008 hurling stick misses => ~3.99 average kills per stick (without repairing).

1158 kills with the serrated knife, 386 knife breaks => 3.00 average kills per knife (without repairing).

As comparison:

740 kills with a fridge, 280 fridge breakages => ~2.64 average kills per fridge (without repairing).

I have a few more values, and I think these datasets are probably the biggest sets available atm.
