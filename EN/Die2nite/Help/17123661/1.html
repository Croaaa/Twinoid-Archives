Hi Baller and welcome to the World Forums =)

- While you don't play, hero days don't get used up.
So if you have 30 days left of hero days and you don't play the game for 20 days, you will still have 30 hero days when you come back.

- You cannot play as a resident if you have hero days. In other words, you don't have a choice about using or not using your hero days when you play.

- If you join a town and hero days run out during the game, you lose all your hero abilities, that is correct. You lose your profession and become a resident again overnight.
