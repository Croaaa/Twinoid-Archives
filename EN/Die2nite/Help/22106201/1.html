TOShokking

I'm pretty sure it's all to do with log-on times, what towns you're in, where your characters go, etc.

If they always log in next to each other, for five minutes each, follow the same route on the same town every day, it's a bit of a give-away that it's one person.
If you are (two separate people, obviously, and are) both in different towns, at different times of day, and do roughly your own thing, you should probably be fine.

That's usually how this sort of thing picks out multi's.
That, near identical usernames, and people generally either admitting it or having lots of complaints about them for it.
