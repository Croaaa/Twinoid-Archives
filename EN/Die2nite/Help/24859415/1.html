This is my own experience btw in short.

I would not recommend Scout. Its pretty advanced and if done wrong it can really hurt yourself in the end. They're indeed useful to explore zombie-infested areas. However if you're not in an organized group or meta and if your camo fails the chance will be pretty high that no one will come to save you.

If you seek something easy and less time consuming Guardian would be the best option, it can save people early in the day when they get stuck outside and there is no risk of dying if you have more defence combined with 2+ more watchmen.

As a Technician you're very useful too, an extra 6 construction points that can be spent on the construction sites, workshop, repairs etc. Also makes key blanks in expedition ruins.

A Shaman can heal certain status effects and also track down souls in the world beyond, as he has no penalty into carrying multiple back in town infact! Not really a starter class I would recommend.

Afraid dying alone? As a Tamer you can send back your dog to the town at any zone outside, it will take everything in your inventory (So double check before sending back your valuable water ration that you need to go back).

Survivalists are needed to camp ruins, so they can bring back blueprints for the town to make advanced construction sites! They also have a handy book to search for food or water outside town (Minimal range 4 KM away of the town). They're the only class that can get 100% survival rates. Repeat camping have penalties...

And as the last class: Scavenger can find more items in a shorter amount of time. Also the chance is greater that it will find something useful. Depleted zones are always giving poor quality materials though...

There is more info, in a wiki outside. But you'll have to find that yourself with google!

I started out as a scav and I think it is the most straight forward. If you like to go out and dig stuff up do that, or Tamer since you basically get to drop off another load of stuff.

If you want to camp, be a survivalist.

If you want to mostly stay inside or build, Technician or Gaurdian.

I'd say Scout and Shaman are both a little advanced for your first go.
