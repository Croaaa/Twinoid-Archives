Just to complement my answer in case someone is looking for this in the future:

On 22 September 2014, Season 10 update brought a "simplification" of non-distant regions (small maps) where, among other things, the Camping feature was removed.

It was removed not only here, but in all servers apparently.

There were a lot of complaints about this from multitudes of confused new players (and some veteran ones...).

Because of that, on 07 December 2015, Season 12, they brought back the Camping ability on Small Maps. CAMPING is back for the small maps! A lot of people have complained about not being able to show the new players a good deal of the game and we're running a test just on the EN server to see if this is a good thing or not.

Henceforward, Camping is still available for Die2nite small mapsalthough it STILL shows as unavailable on the Help Section

i wanted to answer your question, then i saw you saying you choose otep over opeth and i changed my mind JK

nothing happens when you dont camp, and usually you dont camp, you just stay in town for the most part of the game... although camping gives several advantages inside the game the power is pretty much limited by the game play of small towns (one reason being that buildings are too damn close to the town).

now does it help to camp? depends on your view, camping in each building gives you a blueprint (uncommon and rare depending on the distance between the individual building and the town), also when you want to do a long expedition perhaps a multi purpose one, sometimes it's a good idea to camp outside in a remote place, but it doesn't usually happen at least not for normal residents.

the only time i camped in a small town was when i tried to help another player with my control points, and told him not to leave the zone without checking with me first, and he didn't listen... guess what i was stuck and well... HAD TO CAMP!

it's really good that it's there to be honest, people can have some benefit from it, and it will save you at times... but the bonuses and everything else about it is not really the small town essential thing...

does it affect me somehow? yes it will give you a search bonus (or at least it says it does in smalls). but mostly if you feel lucky, when the day that your town's defense is not going to hold comes, grab some gear and go camping! you might survive a few nights while everyone in town dies... OR NOT! remember there's always a chance of dying while camping (unless for survivalists) it all depends on the distance of the building from the town, number of zombies, number of campers... and of course the amount of improvements done on the camp site.

have fun.
