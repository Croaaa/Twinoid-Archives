spacekadt
Simply put, Apple products don't support flash and can't use the site to its full potential. There are workarounds, but they're usually at least a few extra steps and are sketchy now that Twinoid is in the mix. Even if you are able to log in, you won't be able to move around outside, since that's flash based.

Android products will support flash, but make sure you get something with full flash support rather than flash lite. If you have full flash support, you'll be fine to use the site as normal... it'll just be SLOOOW if you're moving around outside.
