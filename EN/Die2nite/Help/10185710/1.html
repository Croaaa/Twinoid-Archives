You should have already figured the answer out by now, but let's put it here for others to see.

When the town is not full, the first day gets extended, meaning that you will not get and attack, or anything else that happens during attack time...
You won't:
Get an Attack.
Die outside, or from dehydration etc. (You can still die walking)
Refill AP and CP.
Get an increase in your thirst.
Lose druged status.
Became infected if already wounded
And so on...

The good thing is that if you are sure that will happen, you can extend your day 1 scavenging and find many more items, but most often you end up with a day doing nothing, because you didn't get your ap refilled!
