Hacksaw

Shunned citizens can upgrade their homes (including hero upgrades) same as any other citizen. They just can't get the materials to do such from the bank.

Dead shunned citizen is the same as any other dead citizen. Their house, along with any improvements and upgrades, become unusable by the town, and it also no longer applies any extra defense to the town (personal defense x 0.4 formula)

All you can do is pillage any items left inside. Could also recycle the house, but other than all the items in the chest, not sure what you get from recycling.
