Yes, if the Wonder is built on Day 3 and you survive that night's attack, then you will get the distinction.

A further caveat is that if you have more than 100SP and are in a Small Map, then the only distinctions you will receive if you die before Day 8 are the Spiritual Guide, shunning, and method of death distinctions. I'm not sure if Role Play Text or Hero Class (e.g. 5 days spent as a Guardian) distinctions are awarded.
