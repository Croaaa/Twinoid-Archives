1: it is reduced, depending on the town type (Hardcore/Normal) and whether you are a Pro Camper or not. You can camp even up to 8 days successfully in normal towns as a Pro Camper, if you find a good distant building.

Here are the numbers (in upgrades - 1 upgrade is about 5%):

-Normal town-
day non-PC, PC

2nd____4____2
3rd____ 9____4
4th___ 13____8

6th___ 26___13

8th____?____26


-Hardcore-
day non-PC, PC
1st____ 0____0
2nd____4____1
3rd____ 6____2
4th____ 8____4
5th___ 10____6
6th____?____ 8
7th____?____10

2: it only acts as a penalty, the upper limit is still 90% (100% for Survivalists).

3: the penalty applies to your whole lifespan in the town, camping don't have to be consecutive.
