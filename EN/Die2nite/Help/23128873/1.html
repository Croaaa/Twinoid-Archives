The information below has been here for over three years, since the game was launched.Neither Chrome nor Chromium browsers are supported 100% on Die2Nite. Please consult the game help for more information.
Nonetheless, Die2Nite works with Chrome. If the map doesn't work when you are at the town gates, you may have to refresh the page several times before the arrows for moving around appear. press F5 or click the refresh button on the page.
Also:
This problem happens often when using Chrome, which doesn't offer full Adobe Flash support. We recommend that you use Firefox (It's free!) :
http://www.getfirefox.com
