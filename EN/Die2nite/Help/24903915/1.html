I like being a scout but Im not the best but here is what I know
Always reach out as far as you can to step on Unvisited zones to find Arch Chests and buildings too Feel free to use boosters everyday ( mostly, i think) if there are unvisited zones, unless they shun you or something ( eek random towns). but you want to discover zones, that is your job.

always use from dusk till dawn! there is a spot under Options that tells you if a zone has been stepped on before, which is handy. Also You will see the zombies counts on the markers around you ( they can deviate from that "sensed" number by like 2. Example, you may sense 5 are there, but its 7 instead. Or maybe you sense 3, and its only 1 ) Always mark what you sense on FromDuskTillDawn too, even the zones beside you, anything you can sense

You can go through zombies of course, but dont search the area if there are more zombies than you have control points- you will lose camo and get stuck!
Always remember to carry a few flash grenades! and remember after you flash you have a few minutes so Put your scout camo back on- Manually!

Dont open a doggy bag while you are surrounded by zombies, it will make you lose camo
you can lose camo too in very high dense zombie zones. Flash Grenades are your best friend!
you will lose camo trying to pick up something in a zombie infested zone too remember. Dont pick up anything or drop! be sneaky!
Scouts like beacons too!Feel free to haul one around! If you sense a large number of zombies in a zone and you are scared, or something, you can set off a beacon and see what the building is yay!
Also when you go past Buried buildings you can peek ( and record on fromdusktilldawn) and see what type of building it is under there and help the town prioritize whether to dig it up or not

Is that what you wanted to know? Hope so haha
