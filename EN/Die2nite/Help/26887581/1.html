I answered this in IRC but in case anyone else is wondering...

when you let a parent building die in hardcore, it does not allow you to repair any child building underneath it.

in workshop's case, it's no big deal to let foundations die, but if you're in a serious town and need the dumps or something like that, you'd need to repair the foundations in order to keep the dumps in good repair status.
