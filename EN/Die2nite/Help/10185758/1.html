Here is a quick translation of the information given by Skool about today's maintenance:

This will be Twinoid 2.0 (just in time compared to what was announced; for 3.0 Twinoid we will give ourselves a bit more room)

The Muxxu portal and Muxxu games will be in complete maintenance:
at least during the time needed to import trophies, to update the Muxxu site and all Muxxu games, in all languages,
probably during the time to import the walls,
possibly during the time to import the forums (depending on how long it takes, we could re-open access the games while we import the forums in parallel)

The Twinoid site itself as well as other Twinoid games (Die2Nite, DinoRPG, ...) should remain available throughout the day or almost: there may be one (or more) periods of ten minutes blocking.

However, like any major update, there may be some unforeseen circumstances. It is hoped that the database server will withstand the shock.

Other Twinoid games (Die2Nite, DinoRPG, AlphaBounce, did I forget any?) will migrate their forums and their system of trophies to Twinoid quickly: this will depend on any technical problems found during the Muxxu migration that will have to be corrected for the following migrations, this will also depend on the team in charge of each game, the weather. If all goes well it should last a week or two, but ...

In summary, today's maintenance will focus mostly on Muxxu games. Die2Nite will be affected by short maintenance periods, but they should only have a minimal impact on the game.

The migration of Die2Nite to the Twinoid forums should take place sometime in the next two weeks.
