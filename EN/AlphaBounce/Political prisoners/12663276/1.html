Just to make sure, you need the landing pod from Salmeen.

After this, you have to click and hold to stop your fall. Be sure to approach the surface (as flat as possible) VERY slowly, or else you might crash. Once you are close to the land, your landing pods will deploy automatically and you will be done

Watch out for your gas tank (top right corner), when it is empty you can't use your engine anymore!

When done on the planet, you have to go all the way back up in the sky to complete the level.

Good luck

To have the Space Suit, you should have Salmeen and sacrified another in order to install the landing pods (remember Salmeen frees his when you bring him back to his planet ) . Once you have them, the way to activate the "Space Suit" mission is to go over 70 paces away from the origin (sum of absolute value of both coordinates). Then you'll have a mission to go and get it on Cilorile

Note to all players: this is valid only for those who belong to the ESCorp. If you are in the FURI, then another method will get you to the Space Suit

It is worth it (from my point of view) since some planets have MANY on their surface, and you can also find other transport missions on them, which are paid in and sometimes a few your choice
