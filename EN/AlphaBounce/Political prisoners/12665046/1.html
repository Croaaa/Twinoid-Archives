Actualy, ANS, in the FAQ I only specify two of the 4 extra lives. The Douglas one and the merchant one, because these are the two that are of immediate concern once your extra ones are taken away.

Anyway, there's actually FIVE extra lives, however, a mission requires that you give one up, so you end up with 4. They are as follows:
-1 from the Distress Signal mission
-1 from in-game merchants (look to the west of Lycans)
-1 by purchasing SHCs from the Interstellar Bank
-1 by completing the Invasion mission

You LOSE one when you complete Salmeen the Mechanic part 2, and you regain it once you return Salmeen back to Khorlan (which can be done once you've done his missions inside the Karbonis Belt).
