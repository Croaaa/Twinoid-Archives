If you pause the game you can use your capsules, they give you the effect of one powerup. So far I have seen lightning, and fire, which give the volt and flame powerups. G is glacial, when the ball hits a block, it freezes, and when it hits it a second time it breaks it. THis will make it take longer to break blocks that only take one hit, but it will make it take less time to break blocks that take more than 2, or are impossible to destroy.
