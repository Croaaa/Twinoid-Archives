I'd say that going all the way to the merchant and forth is only going to be a waste of time.
Use the [V] capsule, and try to pass the ball between the side and center minerals. It should prove fairly easy to break the first three lines of bricks, and then bounce the ball up and down until you hit the top of the level. By that time, you drone should have disabled quite a few sentinels, hopefully ones close to the bottom.
The ball should not be too fast to just divert through the bottom to the other side and repeat, but you should be able to move it slowly through the center to the other side, breaking things along the way.

As long as you use [V] properly and give your drone enough time, there really should be little problem.

I outline some specific hints you may want to take a look at in the FAQ as well, so you may want to check that out too.
