There are a few areas of "antiradars" in the game, where asteroids damage your radar (no matter if you pass the square or not). To repair it, you need visit any merchants, then buy an "emergency radar" item (or something like that, dont know exactly) for about 60 . Until then you can move only square-to-square on any areas you have not visited before.
See abmap.hq-gamehosting.de/global ... to find where antiradars are located - squares with brown rocks. (of course except the Karbonis Belt, Karbonis Belt is visible in normal game and is not antiradar. Antiradars are not visible in the game)
