Sorry for the second post.

Just as a side note, since I forgot to look at your profile.
You currently have 3 lives. So for the sake of humanity,
DO NOT CROSS any cells with a number 5 or higher.

Restrict yourself to [-4,-4] to [4,4] inclusive,
go to each of the location in there that have some minerals in it and get it.

You will be tempted to finish the first cleaning mission, DO NOT!
Wait until you have harvested all the easy minerals with your 3 lives, else you are looking for a painful beginning.

On the Path Planner, there is an option just for you called:

"Escorp Only - +3 Initial lifes (4x4)"

that simply eliminates position with the number 5 or higher.

j2k.sf.net/svg/escorp.php?x=0& ...

As you can see, all the cells which are not 0$, take
them, doing a spiral or S like tight pattern,
then take the missile and do the first harvest last.

{Press ctrl-A to see the spoilers}

You will lose all your extra lives, but you will have 380$
in bank. Going for debris 1, you get 432$ and ~489$ by
the time you collected all debris.


Go to lycans, obtain all the parts.


Make sure you win every level, when the guy with the spare enveloppe "leaves you", this will ensure that the amount of game required to get it formally is shortened.

After you get the Extra Missile 4 ( 5, 8 ), you should be around 618$, knowing that the 3rd extra life cost 470$ at (-13, 8 ). If instead you go for genemill, you should be around ~856$.


For lycans, only DO the "SUPER EASY" one,
which means that you need only 1 life.

Second possible path is "Easy", which needs 1 or 2 lifes,
but you may be more talented.

After that, you will get an extra life back
and you should be okay for a while...
