Numinumie, read about this in the Alphabounce wiki.

Ways to lose spare envelopes
After travelling about 5 sectors away from the origin, the ESCorp takes back three extra envelopes to give to another new prisoner (see "Emergency Envelopes Sent Back" ).
A spare envelope is used up when Salmeen converts it into Landing Pods for your ship in the mission "Salmeen the Mechanic: Step 2".

Also, other useful links if you're a new player and don't know about them would be The Abmap and The Minemap. They're both maps, but complementary.
