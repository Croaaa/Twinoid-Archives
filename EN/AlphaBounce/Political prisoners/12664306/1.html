1.
In Clans, you Attack (which is basically plating a game, then your score is sent over for the people of the clan you Attacked to Defend), which costs no extra gems, and you Defend, which is where you play the same game that the Attacker played, while trying to beat his previous score.

If an Attack goes unDefended for 12 Hours, up to 10 KlanPoints are taken from the Attacked clan and given to the Attacking Clan.

There are clan rankings, where KlanPoints are compared and the clans sorted to their respective levels (1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.).

At the end of a Clan Tournament (which I believe is every 2nd Period), Koins are distributed to each clan over 500th Place based on their level, and then those Koins are divided among the members of that Clan.



2.
To join Political Prisoners V2, you need to message me on KadoKado. From there, I will message Lavos telling him that you have confirmed your reservation, and once enough reservations are collected, the clan will be formed.
