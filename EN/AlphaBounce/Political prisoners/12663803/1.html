Molecules can be killed with:
-Soldier ball hit.
-Laser hit.
-Javelin hit.
-Direct missile hit (note: there is no blast).
-Blast from missile hitting a brick nearby.
-Sucked by Quasar.

The "orange/yellow regenerating brick" is a spring conglomerate.
It flips back on ("regenerates") when any ball flies higher than it and is more often usefull than not since it can act as a one-way barrier.

And yes both Purple & Spring can be removed with T.
