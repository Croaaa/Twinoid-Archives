You have Douglas so now you just need to move a few spaces and he'll give you a free life soon.

Looking at the minemap I would say take this route:


[4][-3] - not many bricks, take out all the ones on the left first when the ball is the slowest and it should be an easy level to beat

[4][-2] - try to hit the orange bricks, they blow up several bricks around them. Also it says there is an [N], [J], [C], and [D] in the level. Avoid the [D] but get all the others. If you get the [N] it's like a brand new life and [C] can help you hold the ball to use auto-javelin if you need to.

[5][-2] - this level has a medical brick, hit that and it gives you a free life (for that level only)

[5][-1] - this level should be easy to get your ball stuck up top so that it takes out a lot of bricks without requiring you to hit it very much

[5][0] - this is similar to the last one but has even fewer bricks


by the time you beat all of these levels you should have your extra life from Douglas
