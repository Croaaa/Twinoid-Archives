I guess you're talking about the AR-57 mission for the rock samples.

In the mission description, there are 2 coordinates of both rock samples (located on Lycans ans Spygnisos). On each of these 2 locations, there should be a [ ? ] box in the game, with an object (the rock sample) that falls from it when hit. Just use your envelope ( ) to catch it as it falls to get it. If there is no such box, then finishing the level should be enough.

Rock samples are visible on your map to help you

Also, be careful while visiting Spignysos, this planet is icy and your envelope will be very slow.

I suggest you first get hold of Salmeen on planet Tiboon (following MacLambert's instructions), then head for planet Moltear. On that planet you will find both the Ambro-X (radar enhancer, allows you to travel further in unknown space), and 3 pieces of the Zonkerian Medallion. This object will help you on Spygnisos as the "ice slowing" effect will be reduced. However, you might wish to buy sunglasses (cheap enough) somewhere, as many parasite molecules on planet Moltear could make you blind without them.

Here are the 3 pieces:
[-58,30], [-51,37], [-54,40]
(no mission needed to get them)

For the sample on Spygnisos, here is the easiest route you can follow (thanks to Aaron for the screenshot).
