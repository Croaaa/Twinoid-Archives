Perforation tool doubles the efficiency of your drones, but costs more than the 3rd drone alone.
Compare:
Drone n°1(65+n°2(1316) cost 1974 for a power of 1+1=2.
Drone n°1(65+Tool(2891) cost 3549 for a power of 1x2 = 2.


Plus the best price by far is on [66,155], on Nalikor's Surface.
That is not only a long way, you also need to have completed quite a few missions to be able to land.
One of which removes a spare envelope, not exactly a wise move before Balixt...


Thus it is recommended to get 2 drones before worrying about perforation tool.
