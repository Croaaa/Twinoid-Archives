No . If a merchant A sells a given drone cheaper than a merchant B, then all drones sold by A are cheaper than the corresponding drones sold by B.

If you look at the merchant at [17][4] in the Excel file for Shops & Items, then you'll see in all 10 columns for drones that it has the best prices. This means that if you go to another shop who sells drones, even if you don't know his price for the drone you need, this price will be higher anyway.
