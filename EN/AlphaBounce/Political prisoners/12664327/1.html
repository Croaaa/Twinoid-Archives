When you get the "R" (Regeneration) power-up you can do this:

Wait for the bar to fill up, click the screen and the new ball will appear.

Then, instead of waiting patiently for the bar to fill again, pause the game and go to another program (Word, MSN...), then go back and unpause.

When the bar fills, the ball will not get away from the envelope!

Then you can get as many balls as desired without losing any one of them!
