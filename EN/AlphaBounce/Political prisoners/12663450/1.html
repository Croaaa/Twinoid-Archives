Spingysos has a dual problem with it's sectors: Your paddle moves at half-speed due to the cold atmosphere, and there's flash molecules which blind you and change the direction of your ball. A Zonkerian Medallion from Moltear will fix your speed problem, and Sunglasses bought from a merchant will fix the flashes. If you want to tackle Spingysos, I'd recommend at least the glasses.

There's also other threads further down about Spingysos; check them for more info.
