More than 4 years, playing mostly daily with few small breaks...

I am already far over 5474 explorations (probably more since I redid many mineral rich locations few times over) and I did not finish,
nor reach, all the planets yet, only those near the Karbonis belt.

Furthermore, I spent quite some time finishing all the inside belt quests and getting all possible items within reach, before leaving the belt.

As you can see from my page, I have all the major equipment and enough drones and missiles to do enough damage when needed, except for the nasty gray metallic Grimorn levels

Currently, I am heading towards the mighty Holovan.

This game is not a sprint race, it is a very very long marathon...

Knowing that this game is that long, there is no point doing it blindly and not using maps and tutorials to optimize your way
to the finishing line.

Otherwise, you might take twice or three times as long without them. Moreover, you will have a much harder game play then needed, due to an unoptimize route, minerals, items and planning.

Example, going to the far planets without enough missiles, equipments or drones. Worst trying to get one piece of equipment by taking the wrong route, mostly for Spignysos and Moltear quests.

It is a bit like going through a mined field trip equipped with a map, a compass, a GPS and a planned route and doing it without one and getting lost in the cold without a radar...

Although, I agree that creating such maps in the first place was an exciting race in the beginning of the game when it started.

In the end that will be your choice

Have fun!
