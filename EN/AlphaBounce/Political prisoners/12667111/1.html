Actually, it does not double the power of your ball. It adds +1 to it.

For example, both RCD-20 and OX-Soldier have Power of 1. If you use a fire capsule on it, it will have Power of 2 (1+1=2).
But if you have OX-Delta which has basic Power of 2, and use a fire capsule on it, it will then have Power of 3 (2+1=3).

Also, the "Power" of a ball is taken into consideration if you are breaking ferrous (brown) bricks and on regular bricks, but only if you have taken a [B]oost power-up.

I hope that makes things clearer
