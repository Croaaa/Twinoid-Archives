Well...

These are the things I see you are missing. In which order you wish to do them is entirely up to you

1) That "powerful drilling ball" is called OX-Delta and is twice as good as the one you're using right now.

2) I take it you joined FURI (or at least defected from ESCorp). Why don't you do some Liberations? You'll get three new missions, at 5, 20 and 100 liberations. And the rewards.

If you haven't (although i can't see how's that possible), do Cleanups. I don't know at which numbers you get the missions, though

3) Karbonite tablets. Collect the rest of them (and start at the southernmost point). You'll get a bonus radar point if you turn them in on Nalikors.

4) Buy a Ki-Wi antenna so that you always have an access to the forums

5) Do transports. Collect pink and blue minerals on planet surfaces.

5.b) Buy better landing pods and surface engine. You'll need them for harder planets (except the last, most expensive ones... they're not unnecessery). And Perf. Tools (on Nalikors).

6) Buy the rest of the PID pieces to "finish" the game and return to Terre.

And tools to help youin your quest:
TheMap (free) and/or MineMap (registration needed)
One and only, the AB Wiki


Hope that all helped a bit.
