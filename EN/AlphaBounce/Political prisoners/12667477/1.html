I know it's kinda not within everyone's minds, but I wanted to share with you a future project of mine, that not only serves as a non-profit tribute to Breakout games in general, but also features an entire re-take on Alphabounce's formula and storyline. Given up on all hope yet? Well here's what I'm planning on with this game.

Yes it's meant to be a sequel to another game I'm going to work on prior to this one.

Anyways onto the list.

The game's main "campaign" mode takes place in the Alphabounce universe and will contain 4,000,000 levels on a 2000x2000 grid, all played in AlphaBounce's traditional format, but with more user-friendly benefits (the game is still difficult ). No level is randomly-generated, every one is hand-created from scratch with these block graphics with assistance from what will hopefully be many level authors.
The elimination of and . The game is free, so there's no point forcing payment or some form of limitation.
Bosses that roam completed planets, when defeated they yield bonuses, like many or upgrades, among other bonuses.
Post-game scenarios, such as acquiring upgrades for the not possible with the clan you choose to affiliate with, and also rebuild the planet Karbonis after it's destruction.
The presence of a detailed easy-to-use level editor that does not require completing a year-long mission to unlock, and allows much easier methods of sharing levels with a server. Also allows sets of levels.
A standard "Arcade" mode with four dimensions (travel through each with special crystals, not to be confused with :mineral with 150+ levels in each dimension to travel through, and a "VS" mode that contains gameplay types alluding to arcade titles Puchi Carat, Blocken, Twin Squash, and Cuby Bop.
50+ powerups.
Selective soundtracks and sound effects.

I'm sure many of you will hate this idea, but let me elaborate; I don't hate Alphabounce. It's just I want to attempt it's story and gameplay with my own ideas. Missions are still the same, as are the surfaces of the planets, but the later has new, redesigned surfaces in landing sequences, though they are all in the same color as Alphabounce.

So yea, leave your thoughts, if you want that is.
