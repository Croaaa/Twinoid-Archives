Once you find the alien, just leave Tiboon. You said you have the generator? Start heading towards Lycans to get the first rock needed for the AR-57 missile. You will be able to understand what the alien is saying just after a few more levels (not on Tiboon, anywhere).

No, you don't get new missions when you explore planets. Only a few ones that are totally optional (like Life on Tiboon). ESCorp will give you missions when you accomplish other missions they have given to you, and after set amounts of clean-up missions.

Once you only have clean-up missions to do in order to unlock other ESCorp missions, you can choose between ESCorp and FURI, search the forums for more information between the 2 factions.
