Drones turn sentinels into regular blocks.
Perforation tools make your drones work faster.
Reactor support makes your drones move faster.
Collector allows your drones to collect mineral blocks.
Converter makes the drones turn sentinels into green mineral blocks.

Since one drone launches each time the ball hits your pad, the later and more expensive drones are probably the most inefficient item in the game. I've gotten by fine with four, you might want six, but it's hard to imagine wanting more than that.

The perforation tools are very useful, and relatively cheap. Reactor support isn't terribly useful but is very cheap. Collector is nearly useless unless you really want to complete levels slightly faster. Converter is extremely expensive, and will take a prohibitively long time to return your investment.
