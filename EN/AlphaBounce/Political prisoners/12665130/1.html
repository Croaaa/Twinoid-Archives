470 ? that's a LOT


There's a location with 100$ and another with 302$ on Lycans and two other locations with 100$ each south of Lycans, so definitively feasible:

j2k.sf.net/svg/escorp.php?lang ...

So as soon as you have 68$, go to these locations and claim your price. Try to do the levels marked as "EASY".

You can also guess the difficulty by using this map preview:
abmap.hq-gamehosting.de/index. ...

You can also plan your trajectory and purchases with this interface:
abmap.hq-gamehosting.de/global ...

It gives the top 5 cheapest marchands for each item

BTW, 470$ is a lot of money near the center,
because the CQR are rare, very rare.

Easy levels == not much mineral
Hard levels == tons of mineral

As you get better, you will get more mineral by doing harder level.

Also, near the belt, without crossing it, when you have the space suit,
you can "double collect" while landing with drones. So you try to land near a big green crystal and while your drones are collecting you collect it also with your spacesuit at the same time.
Results: The same minerals are collected twice

The only problem you won't be doing this before 600 days or so, so in the mean time practice and you can concentrate on rich locations and small missions.

My map is okay but far from complete,
but there is also two other good maps:

abmap.hq-gamehosting.de/global ...
minemap.golbarg.fr

and the VERY old one
(not maintained and the price of CHS are now doubled or tripled):
abmap.info

There's a lot of rich minerals locations (CQR)
where Karbonis was and elsewhere, I can tell you this, it will be your "favorite spot" for the next year and half,
unless you buy some 350+ CHS package.

You can do a mission come back 200 square later and reharvest it again, do another mission, come back, reharvest it again, etc.

See those blue squares on abmap?
abmap.hq-gamehosting.de/index. ...
j2k.sf.net/svg/escorp.php?lang ...

By the way, CQR is the abbreviation in french for "rich locations":
Case Qui Rapportent

I say whatever is higher than 12$ is interesting, everything below I try to avoid.

Here's another one near where I am, North of Moltear:
abmap.hq-gamehosting.de/?x=-70 ...
j2k.sf.net/svg/escorp.php?lang ...

As far as I'm concerned, I would say that I recommend playing the game daily for at least 6 months get the basic missions out, reduce your error rate to about 1% and then it might be worth investing in some CHS to speed things up a bit.

If you have other questions let me know

Enjoy!

Well, I spoiled some elements of the game, but I say that with 3 / day, not using advice and map is kinda tricky. I prefer knowing the stuff out to use my daily those wisely and plan ahead.

It's not like a PS3 game where you can finish it in a day or a week, this game takes lots of time and practice.

Here's another advice, when you get to Spignysos to get the cold piece, go south and at the same time make sure you have enough money to buy some cheap equipment near by Spignysos, such that you can go straight-ahead to Moltear right after that.

That's the way I did it without Salmeen.

Another thing that some people did is to go on Tiboon south west and get Salmeen before getting the cold piece, so you have him on board when reaching Moltear to get some extra bonus at the same time.

I would suggest having 1 drone before going to Spignysos, one is enough before Balixt, so you can easily convert 1-3 sentinelles on your way slowly but surely. I would suggest 3-4 drones for Balixt, some people did it with 1 or 2, but it's much harder and you need to use capsules instead.

Don't worry Salmeen is pad friendly,
Salmeen won't take you an extra life like every other passengers
That is very important to know, since I didn't want to lose a life, so that's why I didn't take him on board in the first place.

After that you go south, get the teal star, go east under the anti-radar spot, get some anti-matter piece, get the missile map, head north, finish the anti-matter pieces to get the MAS-Z black missile, then go to Balixt or do Salmeen part 2 mission.

By the way, since Spignysos is VERY VERY tricky, to not say scary early in the game, much more feasible with lots of lives, tons of missiles and better equipment.

The easiest way is to go south around the planet clockwise,
then south-west, get the CQR cash, go to the easy spot on the planet south-west win that location, then get the cold piece and lose that game,
this way you head back directly to the previous level for free, then do a square outside the planet.

It's kind of cheating, but it's the easiest way
to get that cold piece without going totally nuts.
This way you only have to win 2 levels on Spignysos to get the cold piece.

This game is not like any other Arkanoïd, it's very challenging, fun with missions, a story and difficult levels in a smooth increasing fashion,
very addictive I would say.
