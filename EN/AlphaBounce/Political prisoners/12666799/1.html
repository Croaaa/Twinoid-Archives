Thanks to Lady Luck herself I've quickly made it to my 52nd cleanup mission. However, looking back over the last year or so I'm thinking I could have been here much sooner.

I did a few cleanups at the start and then started doing missions, and now have done all my in-belt missions and had about 15-20 more cleanups to do. Those specific cleanups have taken me to Moltear twice, across to Spiggynosis twice too which got me thinking. How would the game have panned out for me if I'd have made cleanups my primary goal done missions if a cleanup just happened to be in close to one of the cleanups.

I feel it would have got me to where I am now much sooner. The reason being I could have done all my Moltear missions (ambro-x, the medallion, 2nd ball, turquoise star and even 1 or 2 of the antimatters) when I traveled to the cleanup that was right next to Moltear killing two birds with one stone saving me a couple of weeks worth of 's.

This is why I plan to keep doing cleanups until I reach 73 (not 100, i don't feel the final extension is overly important) and if one of those cleanups just happens to take me close to Nalikors, Khorlan or Cilorile, then and only then will I clear the planet.

Is this a good strategy?

The only problems I can foresee with this plan are.

Deciding how close a cleanup is to a mission to go do that mission.

Not having good enough upgrades due to not doing missions (this may only be a major problem with the pre-52 cleanups) to complete harder levels / losing 's

Discuss.
