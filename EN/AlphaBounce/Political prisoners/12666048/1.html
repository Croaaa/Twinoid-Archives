If you go further than 150 in any direction, you won't be able to read the forum.
This "rule" can be easily avoided with these small steps :
1 - log out
2 - click on the "Forum" button just below the flash screen
3 - read the forum.

And you're done! The only bad side is that it doesn't keep track of what posts you have read and which you didn't.
