As cool as it is to buy and have capsules at your disposal (and I know at first I was using them frequently just cuz I could) ... If you can finish a sector without using a capsule, you'll thank yourself later for saving your ... considering an average sector has 8 to 12 minerals (if any at all ... some have a lot of but they're not that common), it takes a few / to travel around and save up enough for a new capsule.

When you get to the point you want to buy something expensive, you'll wish you still had the hundreds of minerals you'd spent on capsules that you bought and used when you didn't really need to use them.

Just be thrifty with your , is all I'm saying. Use them when you think it's really in your best interests.

A prime example - You know when you're in a sector with a row of steel 'unbreakable' blocks and it only gives you a 1 or 2 block gap to thread your ball through? Those are tough as hell. No better time to use the ice capsule.

I'm sure I'm probably preaching to teh choir here ... but ... well ... you know. I guess this post is more relevant to noobs.

Wish I'd not used so many earlier in the game. Trying to save you some grief, I guess.
