Level 2 radar is obtained by:
Circling the Karbonis asteroid belt, landing on specific asteroids and talking to aliens there to get the 12 Karbonite tablets. After you get the all the tablets you must travel to Nalikors and land at the location [70][151] and talk to the alien there.
For a list of the locations you can go to this page on the Alpha Bounce Wiki, or use "The Map"
