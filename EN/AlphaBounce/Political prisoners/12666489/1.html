A supporting drone hops out of your envelope when your ball hits it. The primary function of a drone is to dismantle a sentiel (spelling?). Sentiels look like a silver block with a coloured circle in the middle. When your drilling ball hits these, they release a ray of coloured light at your envelope, which effects it in some way. When a drone pops out of your envelope, if there is any sentiels in the level, it will fly to a random one and start dismantling it, into a normal block.
