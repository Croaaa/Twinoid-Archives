Until you have the collector upgrade for them, the only time you're gonna actually see a drone go to work is in the vicinity of Balixt. The collector will send your drones to work whenever you see minerals on your screen, which is kind of handy when it's a tough level that you might potentially die or accidentally pick up a Q or O power-up - they go right to work getting all the minerals off the screen for you. Nice when you're on a tough level that has pinks and blues hidden behind a row of those indestructible steel blocks at the top of the screen. You'll (in theory) never finish a level with minerals left behind.

And no, they won't tackle the parasite molecules, unfortunately.
