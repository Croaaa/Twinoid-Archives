The button you used was only for leaving the envelope, to start walking on the planet. That will show on all levels where you land.

In levels with missions, you need to walk around and find the house, enter, then talk to the alien inside. In some other levels you can also find minerals lying around. You need to walk thru them to collect them. Then return back to the envelope and liftoff.

jusuck, don't just go around picking everybody for a ride... each passenger you pick will occupy one of your emergency envelopes until you deliver them to their destination, causing you to play all the levels between pickup and drop-off with one less life. I recommend you do that when you're sure you can handle with less lives (I see you have 3 now).

I preferred to start transporting passengers when I got a few upgrades to make my life easier with one less life, but maybe you can handle fine with less. Just wanted to warn you about the temporary life lost during transport in case you didn't know
