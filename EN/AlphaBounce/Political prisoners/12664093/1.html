The signal drops first when you get a coordinate over 50. It drops further when you get a coordinate over 100, and you lose access altogether when a coordinate goes above 150. When you buy the Kiwi Antenna however, the signal will never drop. The weak signal only makes it harder to see the forums, but it doesn't have any other effect.
