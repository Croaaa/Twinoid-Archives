For starters, it's Pofiak. No r. (no I don't care if you don't care about the name, it's small and easy)

About the parasites, they are released when you hit some parasite bricks,and try to stick on your pad. If they do stick, they make it vibrate left and right; the effect is very small with only one, but if more stick, the pad will also be vibrating more. This makes it easier to miss the ball when it returns.

When you hit a parasite brick, several parasites are released at once, and they move towards your pad. They will stick if you don't move it away, and those that don't manage to stick start following it around, so you have to constantly move the pad to avoid the parasites, all the while also paying attention to the ball.

It may sound hard, but from my experience, it's just hard enough to make things interesting.

Pofiak was a fun planet to play. Everyone concentrates on the parasites but to me the most challenging part was the molecules.

On Pofiak there are several levels with brick creating molecules. Once you release them, they start flying around the level creating a trail of new bricks in their path. If you kill them quickly it's not a big deal but if they get out and running it can be a huge problem because they keep laying blocks and the ball keeps getting faster and faster.

Overall it was pretty easy, I think I only died on one of the 16 levels (and that was because one of those molecules got out of control and I couldn't kill it).
