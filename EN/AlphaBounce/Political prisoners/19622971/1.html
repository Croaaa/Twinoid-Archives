I agree with Raron - You completed the surface engine mission for Salmeen and lost your extra . There are two relatively easy ways to get a new / spare one:

The first is to buy some with real money via the bank (although you can use an MTpass for the same result, and it's possible to get those for free, primarily by playing a lot of carapass.).

The second is to find a shop that sells an extra , there's one near Lycans that sells the cheapest, I think. The current popular Merchant search is at http://thedarkworld.net/abshop.html

There's a third way, by completing the Balixt mission - but I think you'll want at least one emergency from the above choices before you try that. And probably a drone or two.
