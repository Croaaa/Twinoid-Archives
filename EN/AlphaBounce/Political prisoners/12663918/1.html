Allow me to clarify a few things:

Radars -
The radar covers a (2n+3) by (2n+3) square around every explored sector, where n is the number shown in the profile. The number is added every time you update, despite which one you do first.

Generators -
The generator allows you to go n+1 sectors away from your current location, where x+y cannot exceed n+1, where x and y are the number of sectors away from where you're at horizontally and vertically, respectively. The generator is limited by one more than the square your radar covers. This is probably the only game item that you can skip generators and different levels.

Landing pods -
Every extension adds 2 metres to your pods, despite what order you go in.

Surface engine -
Every upgrade adds power to your engine, despite what order you go in.
