You can only rearrange whatever bricks are in the level. The edit won't take effect until a mod approves it. For a mod to find an edited level, he/she would have to either stumble upon the level, or you can report the level you edited here.

If you're going to edit a level, make it creative and don't make the level much easier or much harder. Otherwise, a mod can easily reject any edit proposals.
