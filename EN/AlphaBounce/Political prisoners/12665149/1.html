Missiles destroy bricks. When you first get the missile, you only have one of them, and it only destroys a single brick, given that it's not a silver or otherwise indestructible brick. You fire them by pressing and holding the Spacebar (but take note, while you do this the ball can pass through the pad).

Once you use a missile, you can get it back by various means; the most easily accessible ones are missile refills bought at shops (which refill all your used-up missiles) and missile refill bricks in levels (which refill one used up missile each). Additionally, the P powerup refills all your used-up missiles.

Some levels have [?] bricks, which drop a missile; when you pick this up, when you are done with the level (winning or losing doesn't matter) you will be able to carry one more missile at a time, and from my experience, this also refills all your used-up missiles as well.

Missiles at first may seem useless, but there's a few upgrades for them as you progress through the game. The first upgrade, the AR-57, makes the missile destroy all bricks in a 3x3 area centered on the brick you hit. The second upgrade, the MAS-Z missile, allows your missiles to destroy any kind of brick, even silver ones. There's also another upgrade, which makes the missile destroy a 5x5 area around the brick hit, but that's very, very far in the game for you.
