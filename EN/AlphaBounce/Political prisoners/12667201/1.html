You're confusing anti-radar asteroids with the Karbonis asteroid belt.

Anti-radar asteroids cannot be seen during game play, they ar 100% invisable. When you enter a patch of them, you lose your radar and have to head back to a merchant to get it fixed.

If you can only move one space at a time in unexplored space, your radar is broken and you need to head to a merchant to get it fixed.

If you can move 2 spaces at a time in unexplored space, you radar is fine. This is the more likely than the situation I previously mentioned.

The Karbonis Belt (the asteroids you've crossed) is as far as you're told you're allowed to go by the ESCorp until if you do 52 cleanup missions. You will have had a warning that you are now allowed to cross the belt which will now be gone. If you leave the karbonis belt before you've done 52 cleanup missions you'll lose your envelope extension, you will never be able to get it back.

The good news though is that about half of the players in alphabounce do this on purpose, even though they lose the envelope extension, you'll eventually join the FURI and they offer some very cool upgrades which makes up for the shorter envelope.

What I would suggest you do now is What I suggest is heading back to 7,-10 and completing this level again on Tiboon, you will pick up an important passenger. Then either head to Moltear, then back to finish off Balixt
