I'm not YF23 but I recently joined FURI...

The main disadavantage of FURI is that your envelope gets shrinked to its original size again. I got used to it after maybe 15-20 levels (those were hard levels so it might be quicker for you) and now don't loose more often than before.

The advantages of FURI are:
1) You don't need to do 52 clean-up missions and stay in the belt for ages - well, depends on what you like if that's an advantage or not
2) You get the 4th ball which destroys a couple of bricks per shot
3) The Twinner gives you two balls when starting the level
4) The Accelerator gives you a free extra a day

The ESCorps of course have their own upgrades, but let them write it.

All in all, regarding difficulty I think MT did an excellent job of making both sides equally strong/easy when it comes to beating levels.

I chose FURI because I didn't want to spend weeks to do another 37 or so clean-ups. Also I wanted to get that 4th ball, not the 4th missile.

The envelope shrinkling scared me a little but even if you won't believe it at first you get used to it and don't loose more often than before. Also many levels contain [E] which extend your envelope anyway.
