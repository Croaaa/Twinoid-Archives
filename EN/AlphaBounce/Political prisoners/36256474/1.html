To jump back in the envelope you must click and hold the click while you're in the air. So basically almost like a double click, but then keep the mouse button pressed and you'll start flying around with your jetpack.

Some of the planet surface levels don't have anything special in them, but some have missiond or items or crystals or shops that can be visited. So as far as I can remember, you can find some of these items on a surface level:

- nothing

- regular green minerals (just touch them to collect them). they only count as collected if you manage to get back to your envelope. if you crash or something happens (like a browser refresh), if you land again you won't find those minerals there again. iirc they generate back after 200 turns

- special minerals (these are in houses), blue or pink, usually worth much more than regular ones (on Samosa for example there's one that's worth 2157 ). You only have one chance to collect them. If you don't collect them the first time you land in this level or you crash, they will never appear again.

- locked houses (maybe this was saved for future updates that will most likely never happen). nothing to do here

- houses with aliens that can either give you items (if you have some missions active), sell you items (land shop), want to travel with you (and they can either give you , or nothing) or just tell you some random bits of info (for example where some missiles are, etc).

- you can trigger missions specific to FURI or ESCORP

Your best guide on what can be found on some of the surface levels is moneymaker1's minemap.

That's about it as far as I can remember. If I missed out on something, feel free to correct me or add more info .

Knowing you don't have the space suit and you're on Soupaline right now:

To get back to your ship and fly back to orbit, you have to make your character jump over your envelope and fall on it. If you succeed, you will enter your envelope, and you can now hold the left mouse button to fly up again.
