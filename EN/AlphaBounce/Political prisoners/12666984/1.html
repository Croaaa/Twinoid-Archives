where are they? ive heard rumors of landings worth 8,000
The landing involves a transport mission worth that many minerals. Otherwise, no landing is that mineral-rich.

There are blue/pink minerals on almost every planet, some worth more than others, of course.

The richest landings not counting blue/pink minerals are on Douriv and Chagarina. I would recommend you visit Douriv, but you might want upgrade your surface engine before you land. The richest blue/pink minerals are on Balmanch and Samosa.
