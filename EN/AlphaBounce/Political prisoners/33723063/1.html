Those of you that intend to join FURI, make sure you have already completed salmeen the mechanic step 2 mission when you cross the belt to become an interstellar fugitive. I know it makes every sense in the world to keep all your extra lives as long as you can and give one away for the pods as late as possible, but if you become an interstellar fugitive without having completed the other mission first, thinking it's a good idea to give the extra life away when you're back from the belt, Salmeen will never talk about Gregune's location and if you go there without having actually unlocked the spacesuit mission, all you're gonna find down there is a locked house. So acquire the landing pods first and just go cross the belt with your remaining lives, won't work otherwise. Having Salmeen with you alone is not enough, you need the pods as well before you cross the belt.

Are you lost?
If you are already wondering why the mission doesn't show while you have completed both salmeen the mechanic step 2 and you are an interstellar fugitive, it's because you did it in the wrong order, but don't worry you just need to cross the belt again, then Salmeen will wake up and talk to you about Gregune. You may have crossed the belt once, but Salmeen was probably having a hangover and never knew you became a fugitive.

Those who maintain the alphabounce wiki, I think it's a good idea that you edit the note here telling players that they have to do it in the right order. Current note doesn't clarify whether there is an order or the 2 requirements can be completed however the player wants and it's obvious everyone would rather keep their extra lives as long as possible. The note could say: Make sure you have already acquired the landing pods and you have Salmeen when you are past the Karbonis Belt.
