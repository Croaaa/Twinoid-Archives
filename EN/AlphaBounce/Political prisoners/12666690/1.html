1 - get Salmeen.
2 - go to Moltear. There you will collect Ambro-X which will boost your radar by 1. If you have Stardust mission, you can even collect the nearby Star.
3 - do Salmeen the Mechanic Part 1 and 2 missions, and then get the Spacesuit.

Then, depending on your choice, stick to ESCorp or defect to FURI.
But before you leave the Belt, I would recommend that you collect more missiles and buy perforation tools. And at least 1 more drone, before leaving the Belt.
You could also buy Side Reactors, Cooling Liquid and maybe even the Collector, if you happen to have enough minerals

Of course, if you are sticking to ESCorp, when you get Ambro-X, you should make extra minerals by doing the necessary clean-ups.

I hope I answered your question.

I would recommend you something else:

1. Pick up Salmeen.
2. Complete Salmeen missions 1st & 2nd (or just 1st).
3. Pick up Ambro-X.
4. Pick up Turquise Star.

On your way to Moltear and back you will have a few missiles near. You should definately collect them. It would be very good if you save some money to buy another drone.

5. Head to Cilorile (if you didn't make Salmeen 2nd mission - do it while going on Cilorile).
6. When message will appear - go on Cilorile and pick-up your suit.
7. Go to Khorlan and drop Salmeen off.
8. Pick up passenger to Holovan.
9. Go through the wormhole to Holovan.
10. Pick up passenger to Nalikors.
11. Go to Nalikors, join FURI and buy there the cheapest Perforation Tools.
