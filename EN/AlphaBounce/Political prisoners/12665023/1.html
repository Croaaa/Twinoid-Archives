Hi,

if you leave the Belt too early, you lose all link to ESCorp and become a fugitive .

Then, you can't avoid getting the mission for joining the FURI, but you don't have to complete it either. In fact, to become a FURI member you have to ...
land on Nalikors on a square told in that mission

So you can remain an outlaw if you really want to, but there is absolutely no interest of doing this, since you'll be cut off from the missions of both sides (FURI and Escorp). In other words, there is no real "third faction"
