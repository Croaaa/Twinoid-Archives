There three types of sectors:
Unexplored - Black
Explored by radar - Deep purple (not the band )
Explored by the envelope (played the level with Mission_Ok result) - Bright purple

Currently, when you travel, your radar radius is +1

R R R
R R
R R R

Beyond that point, the area is unexplored. You can only travel one square beyond the radar. Later on, you will get a special upgrade that will increase your radar radius by 1. So, your radar radius will be +2.

It might sound as a disadvantage that you cannot explore further, but look at the situation in another perspective.

On the way back, you will travel much faster since the unexplored areas are now almost explored (only explored by the radar). Later on, you will discover that traveling in common places or routes will be ALOT faster.
