You died because you aren't doing it correctly.

When you get the [R] powerup, you click straight away spawning a ball on your . You then wait until the ball is automatically released (around 5-seconds) then as soon as the ball has automatically left your you click again spawning another ball. Then you repeat to process. This means you have a ball on your all the time.

While the ball is sat on the paddle you play with all the other balls that are in play. If there isn't any balls wait for the ball on the to be released automatically.
