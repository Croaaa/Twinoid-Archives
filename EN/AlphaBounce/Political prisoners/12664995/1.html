Normal Envelope - Just a normal envelope.

Blue Envelope - It's envelope with Zonkerian Medallion - "(..) makes your envelope more responsive to mouse movements in-game (or quicker when using arrow keys). The main benefit of obtaining this is when you're trying to navigate your way around Spignysos, as it helps to somewhat counteract the cooling effect the planet has on your envelope." - from ABWiki

With the blue you can move way better on Spignysos, but don't expect 'normal' movement!

The second thing you get is the effect of pad 'teleportation'. If you move the pad to the left and then leave the playing field with your mouse and go back into it on the right, your pad won't move there the normal way but will be there instantly. I hope this explanation is enough to understand the effect ... actually this will work with every position you move your mouse to.
