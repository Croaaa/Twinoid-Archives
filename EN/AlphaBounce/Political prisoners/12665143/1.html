That's right. Most of the planets don't give a reward for completion, but personal satisfaction can be considered reward enough.

As for those that DO have a reward, completing them will give you the reward either upon completion (in case you complete them after you get the mission) or upon unlocking the mission (if you have done them beforehand for whatever reason).

In the latter case, you may need to play another level or two to actually get the reward, or log out and back in again (perhaps reloading the missions page might also do the trick?). Also remember that clean-ups act in the exact same way.
