Actually, I think I've already played a couple of those levels shown in the demo. Most of them aren't too far from the origin.

However, not all levels can be played exactly as shown...

A new level in the demo always starts with a complete field (as it would in regular play), but quite often, the "demo player" will start the game with some sort of upgrade and/or multiple balls. These ugrades are just added to show off their function (and the accomagnying special effects) and are not part of the level itself.
