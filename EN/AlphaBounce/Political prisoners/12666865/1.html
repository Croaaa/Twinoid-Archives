Use this to show where all the missiles are on the game, look at the top left and click on 'show missiles'. 3 can be bought from merchants, however the 2nd and 3rd are quite expensive.

Levels with more minerals are further away. Some levels can have around 400 on them and some can be double that although they're rare. Try to use the map to play levels with more on them however you collect quite a lot of minerals just by traveling to and from missions and doing cleanups.
