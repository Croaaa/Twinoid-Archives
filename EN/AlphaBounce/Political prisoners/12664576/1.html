You get the "Red Lycans" mission at the same time as the "A New Colony" mission (the one where you're asked to explore all sectors on Soupaline)

Click here to see both mission briefings.


Until you collect the seven debris parts, they are shown on your map screen, flashing, in the northern part of Lycans. So, you ought to have seen them when you went for the Lyans rock sample. Also. if you have not completed this mission, it should still show on your Missions page (possibly pretty far down the list, so you might need to scroll down to see it)


When you've collected them all, you get the Red Lycans debriefing.

Click here to see the debriefing.


At the bottom of your Missions page there is a button labelled "History". Clicking this will take you to a log of all mission briefings and debriefings you've recieved in the course of your career.

Click here to find the History button.
