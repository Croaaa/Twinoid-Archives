The best you can do right now is to carefully take note of the ball's trajectory and quickly move the pad away at the last instant. Or you may want to start moving it beforehand.

After some practice it shouldn't be hard at all to do, but Guardian-heavy levels can still be tough.

A couple of notes though:
Trying to shoot a missile will immediatelly result in a Guardian firing at you. Since you are shooting a missile, you are wide open and WILL get hit.
Finally, if you had the Zonkerian medallion, you could pause the game, move the cursor away, and unpause to avoid a shot. This only requires a bit of timing with the pause key. But since you are already at Cilorile, you may not want ot go all the way back to Moltear for the ZM.
