Clean-ups will soon grow bigger in size (up to 6x6 clean-ups), and as already said, you'll get 10 minerals per square cleared. As such, a 5x5 clean-up will net you 250 minerals, for example.

Now, there's a crossroad you'll come across at some point. You'll have to choose between two factions, ESCorp and FURI. You can choose ESCorp by completing 52 clean-ups, and if you DO choose ESCorp, then there are various marks upon which clean-ups unlock new missions.

Basically, complete all missions that are within the Karbonis Belt, and if you don't know what FURI is about, look it up. Leave clea-ups for times when you are in dire need of minerals, or after all the other missions (well, the ones you have access to anyway).
