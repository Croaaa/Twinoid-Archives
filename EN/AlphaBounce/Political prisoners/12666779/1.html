The game is designed so that you either end up in ESCorp permanently or the FURI permanently.

By doing 52 cleanup missions you're showing your dedication to ESCorp, at that time you can leave the belt freely.

If you leave the belt befor 52 cleanup missions, you'll be kicked out of the ESCorp and become and interstellar fugitive. Being an interstellar fugitive allows you to have access to only one extra mission and that is a different spacesuit located on Soupaline if you haven't already got the one on Cilorile.

Then, you're only option being an interstellar fugitive is to eventually join the FURI.

There's no way you can get rewards from both organizations.
