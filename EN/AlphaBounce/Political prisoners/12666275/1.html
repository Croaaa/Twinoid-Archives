The landing pods are there, but they will only come out when your envelope is very close to the surface, meaning you need to approach the ground slowly over a level area, keeping your envelope perfectly level as you do.

Unfortunately, you picked an extremely difficult planet to land on -- I've tried twice with the same landing equipment you have (later on you'll get more missions that require you to land), and failed both times.

Try Soupaline or Cilorile -- the gravity there is much lighter, and there are plenty of good, flat surfaces to land on. Plus the air is breathable, meaning you and get out and walk around even without a spacesuit.
