Hi,

it seems you are mistaken by two different things.

1) The "trou noir" capsule is an item to be bought at shops. It can be used once in a game with effects similar to a [Q] powerup

2) "Trous noirs" (or in English, black holes or wormholes) are teleportation zones from one square to another. 5 pairs of wormholes are known ; since you speak French I refer you to the sticky "Aide générale" in the "Aide aux nouveaux détenus" channel or to various maps to find them

A shortcut to get the OX-Delta : take the wormhole from [5][-61]
to [-230][1]
