Well... I'm not sure what are you exactly asking about... But I'll try to answer anyway

You can go as far away as you want, if that's your idea of entertainment. It would be recommended to do missions though, there is low amount of missions that can be done at any moment in game storyline...

There is one border if you continue travelling in straight line - asteroid belt. When you get close to it a message with a warning will appear. After you cross it you will be kicked out of ES-Corp. I suggest searching forum for more information about this.

And yes, most of the space is empty. Some merchants, if you're lucky mayby a planet... But it's better to look at some galaxy maps for that kind of things. There are many, also search forum for info. If you do not want to use such thing just continue to do missions, places further away in the galaxy will reveal themselves.
