Short answer, it will take you about 12 to 18 months to become FURI using 3 CHL/day only (no payments) by optimizing your route with some of the maps.

Once you are FURI, you can make a bunch of missions then get the Syntrogenic Accelerator to get 4 CHL/day.

To give you an idea using a safe planned trajectory, I have been playing this game for 16 months only with CHL and I just finished all the inner belt missions, after recently buying a few sets of CHS to speed things up; otherwise, I would not have been able to finish all those missions before next year...

Some people try to optimize for the Syntrogenic Accelerator by bypassing all the inner belt missions to become FURI as soon as possible.

So, the short answer is keep playing...
