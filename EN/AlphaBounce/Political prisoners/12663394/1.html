So, basically, (liquid) only last for a day. If you don't use them in a day, they're gone, and you'll need to wait until the next day to get more. If you don't play for 10 days, when you come back, you'll only have the ones you earned on the 10th day because the other 9 days expired.

The (solid) last until you use them. If you buy 1000 then whether or not it takes 2 days or 2 years, they'll be there until you use them, no matter how many show up on the bar. Also, even if you have 1000 , you'll still get your daily .

At least, that's how I understand it to work.
