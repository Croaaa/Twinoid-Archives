If you cross the belt before you finish 52 cleanup missions, you are considered a fugitive from the law, and ESCorp destroys your extentions, making your paddle shorter.

But there are no other consequenses when you come back within the bounds of the belt. It's not like it makes the game harder; it's just that by leaving the belt early, you're choosing to become a member of FURI.
