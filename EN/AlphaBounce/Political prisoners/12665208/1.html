ANS1
A: Attraction - Magnet, click and hold to activate
B: Buffer - Adds 2 or 3 (can't remember) to the number of hits needed to break every brick
C: Capture - Sticky , click to launch ball
D: Diminution - Diminishes the size of your by one increment
E: Expansion - Expands the size of your by one increment
F: Flame, adds 1 to the power of the ball (a 2-hit brick will be broken with a regular ball)
G: Glacial - Ice, hit a brick one to freeze it, a second time to break it. Sentinels won't shoot when Frozen. No collected from frozen blocks.
H: Halo, ball can go through bricks while going upwards.
I: Indigestion, adds bricks around existing bricks
J: Javelin - Javelin, destroys a whole column of bricks, molecules, etc. when clicked.
K: Kamikaze, ball(s) head straight for bricks
L: Laser, click to fire
M: Multiball, duplicates every ball in the game (there is a maximum limit)
N: New ball - that's self-explanatory
O: Opening, All bricks separated from the center, 3 bricks to the outside (leaving a 6-brickwide gap in the middle)
P: Provision, fills up your missiles (e.g. you have 3/11, then take P, then you'll have 11/11)
Q: Quazar, makes the center bricks disappear in a lack hole, and others will be lost.
R: Regeneration, creates a new ball on click
S: Safety - Rescue, grants an extra
T: Transformation, click and hold to transform any brick to a regular one.
U: Still unknown
V: Volt, will destroy nearby bricks after some time with the ball nearby. Sentinels won't fire.
W: Whisky, ball(s) get drunk and can change direction when they want.
X: Xanax, slowing effect
Y: Yoyo, ball goes faster when on top of screen, slower when at bottom.
Z: Zeal, acceleration effect
