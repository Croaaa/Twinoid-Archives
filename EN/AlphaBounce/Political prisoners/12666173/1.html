the main thing that people seem to be referring to is the fact that as they move their mouse the cursor strays up or down out of the gameplay screen.

The reason for this isn't with the game itself, but is rather with your body and the way it's built, and how you actually play.

See, most of the people that are complaining about this use a standard mouse rather than a trackball mouse (which, admittedly I've not seen many of them in stores lately -.-) ... the people with the problem seem to have their wrist sitting on their mouse surface. When moving your mouse around, your wrist will bend but not your arm obviously. the way your wrist bends from side to side ends up being a slight arc.

Mouse sensitivity and mouse "speed increase" are also in play here. the faster you move your mouse, the more exagerated the movement is on screen, so if you're off to one side, and need to get to the other in a hurry, you arc off to that side, next time you may be slower, so that arc isn't as tall for the on-screen movement. this makes your cursor stray upwards of course.

Two things you can do to prevent / calm this down ... lift your wrist off the playing surface! keep your arm up too! ... have it where none of your mouse arm is touching anything other than the mouse, use your entire arm to move rather than just your wrist. Not only will this help out with any wrist pain you may be developing, but it will also keep the mouse cursor from going up/down.

The next thing, is to go into your mouse settings and turn off the mouse acceleration. you'll have to get used to it of course, but that can calm it down a bit more if you forget to play with your arm rather than your wrist ^^

(to practice, open up paint, choose the pencil thing, then somewhere in the middle, draw a line like you would normally try to move from one side of the AB screen to the other. If you leave your wrist down, you'll see this slight arc. Now lift your arm and try to do the same with just your arm... much straighter, if not perfectly straight. Play around with your mouse settings, and try again.))
