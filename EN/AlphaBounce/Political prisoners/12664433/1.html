I'm introducing 2 Excel files which I regularly keep up to date :

ABCazutil (, P powerups, antiradar squares, missiles, black holes, PID and location of planets & miscellaneous items)

I'm translating here only what concerns the "niveaux édités" (=edited levels) tab from the ABCuriosités spreadsheet, otherwise it'd be quite long ...

Levels locked in edition (=validated in the level editor with a shape identical to the original one, so these levels can no longer be edited) are shown in blue
Levels changed by a Guardian (=admin) but that can still be edited are shown in brown.
Levels changed by a Guardian but that can no longer be edited are shown in red.
This tab was recorded mainly from the official anomaly database.
