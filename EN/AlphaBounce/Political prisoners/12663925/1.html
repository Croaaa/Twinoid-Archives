I guess you are asking how is possible to clear a sector, not a map, it is just a minor detail so no big fuss

Spignysos is an "artic" planet, according to the planet description from other players (those who 100% explored Spignysos), it is a planet with -50 degrees below 0. Due to this fact, it is hard to move with your envelope.

I assume you are for the rock sample, right?. However, there are two important sector you must explore, the first one is a missile slot upgrade [-34,-7] and the rock sample (it should be visible on the map once you are close to Spignysos.

The trick is to play the level that you want, for instance, the free missile level, get the falling missile (after you hit the with ) and intensionally die, so you will return to the previous point, because traveling within Spignysos is hazardous without the Zarkonian medallion. (check players with high ranking and the forum to learn more about this upgrade). So, you must do your business in Spignysos ASAP and get out of there

I have been in Spignysos last week, I only explore three sectors:
[-34,-7] Got the missile and killed myself to return to the map
[-39,-7] Two sectors below the rock sample, I had to pass this level in order to move two sectors above my current position. There is a destroyer molecules in that level that will destroy in 3*3 radius
[-39,-9] Got the rock sample and returned to sector [-39,-7] and then off from Spignysos.

You should had read the forum more about Spignysos (use the search function) to be more prepared before going there.

There are two mistakes that you have done before exploring Spignysos:
You did buy the sunglasses - protect you from the effect of flash molecules in Spignysos (cost 200 at merchant [-30,-5])

You did not pick up Salmeen in Tiboon - a new mission will unlock when you explore Moltear (after completing Spignysos) - check the forum about Salmeen and his missions.

Just a word of advice from me: Read the forum to get familiar with the stuff that you have to know about the sectors you are visiting
Check this map - Alphabounce map to get familiar with the stuff you will find on the way to the sectors you are heading to.

Other than that, Good luck
