Only buy the converter if you plan on playing for a long time. If you do plan on playing for a long time, then get the cheapest converter as soon as you can. It's expensive, but it's meant as a long-term investment.

As for the PSM, it depends on how much you rely on missiles. I don't know when is the best time to buy it. I personally bought it when it was on my way and I already had plenty of minerals. However, I would suggest getting it as soon as you travel very far in space and/or plan to explore tough planets like Volcer and Chagarina.
