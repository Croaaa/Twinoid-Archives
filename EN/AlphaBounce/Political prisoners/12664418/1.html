Regardless of how fast your engine is, you can never fly further than one sectorbeyond the range of your Radar in a single move. Any sector that has been in your Radar range previously will remain marked "semi-explored" (note the lighter shade of the background). Inside this mapped area you can fly at top speed, but if you head beyond its bounds you're limited by your Radar range.
