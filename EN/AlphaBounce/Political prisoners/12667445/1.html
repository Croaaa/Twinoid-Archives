With regards to BOTH landing pods and surface engines, there are 4 different types of each. In both cases you revive the landing pods and surface engine, and then you have the 3 upgrades to buy at merchants.

So to answer your question, the surface engine you get with salmeen could be considered x0. Then you buy the x1, then the x2, then the x3.

If he were to buy the 2x now, he would receives the 1x for the price of the 2x. But then after that, if he bought the 1x he would receive the 2x. (Might actually be worth it if he's close to it and has the right now).

You will always get the next upgrade on both landing pods and surface engine. Unlike the generator you can't just skip a level.

So if the shop sells one of the items (landing pods/surface engine) you will always get the next one for the correct price. You can't do something wrong here.
