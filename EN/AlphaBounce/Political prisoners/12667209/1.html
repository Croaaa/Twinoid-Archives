You should have come in with a second drone. You could still do this level with one drone, but it will take some luck to complete this level.

Something you could try:
Whenever you feel the ball is going very fast, aim for the brick with a [V] powerup (shown on the MineMap; but I don't know if the screenshot is still correct or not. If that it is wrong, at least you know there are [V] powerups somewhere.) When you see a [V] released, unless the ball is not immediately falling down, purposely die so you can start a new life with a slow ball with the [V].
