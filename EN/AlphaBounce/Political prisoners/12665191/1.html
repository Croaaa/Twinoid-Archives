Motion Twin will not refund any lost or ... unfortunate but true...

If you get a connection error on finishing a level, do not refresh or leave the page, just let it sit and it will periodically attempt to reconnect and submit the results of your played level.

If you "must" browse around, just either browse the forums here, or open a new tab and go other places and check back with the alphabounce tab every so often.

About the "use a different computer" thing, that means that on your computer network, 3 or more people are playing alphabounce. This could be yourself with 3+ accounts (not allowed) or if you're in a public location or a location with multiple computers on the same network, if someone has already played on their account it won't let anymore than 3 play on that network per game day. If you're at home, and are on a wireless network, you should probably put a password on it so "joe user" that drives by and sees a free wireless connects and uses it... well yea, that's just not fun... if you're in a college where you're using the school's network, well yea, there are going to be MANY people there with the same connection to the WWW ...
