Drive yourself into an anti-radar asteroid before completing ambro-x, then find the nearest merchant to fix your damaged radar for a few minerals. While there is nothing to fix since you don't have one, witchcraft happens (bug if you will) and you walk out with your first radar. I actually recommend new players to do this as early as possible, maybe along comleting the Soupaline mission (a new colony), don't remember if there are other missions in the area, but either way there is an anti-radar field just north of Soupaline. If you acquire ambro-x while radar reads "1" it'll be upgraded to "2". After that, the bug will no longer work and jumping into an asteroid will only damage your radar, you'll need to have it fixed and you won't get an upgrade.

You can use bubuletz's map to navigate, note your options to the left.
You may also want to check moneymaker1's map, each map helps in a different way but you will find most players, including myself, use both.
