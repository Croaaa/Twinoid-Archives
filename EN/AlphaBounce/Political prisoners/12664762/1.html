Info about [ C ]:
- does not work with [ H ].
- keep mouse button pressed (or Ctrl or whatever key it is) to nearly cancel the effect.
- if something else is activated such as a Javelin, clicking will launch this without letting the stuck balls go. Click another time to release the balls.
- glue time is limited, be careful!
