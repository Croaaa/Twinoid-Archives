If you want to keep your current Twinoid account:

  • do not log out of AB, but do not leave it open in another window/tab
  • go here and unlink your new AB account
  • go back to AB (you should see the landing page with the demo on it)
  • click on the Play button near the top of the screen
  • make sure you pick the option that says you have played before or you have an existing account (I can't check the exact wording, you know)
  • enter your old login and password for AB

In case it isn't obvious, you can't log in to Twinoid using your AB credentials. AB is now a part of Twinoid, so you have to link your old AB account to your Twinoid account. That is the only way you can access your stuff
