[D] - Dynamo - you get an empty bar, rapid clicking builds up javelins, but the paddle can't move when you're clicking, and there's lag time. (After you stop clicking, you can't move for ~1 second)

[G] - Gild - All minerals become 'gold minerals' that don't give minerals.

[H] - Hole - Your paddle becomes two small paddles with a hole in the middle.

[J] - Jitter - The ball vibrates back and forth perpendicular to the direction it's moving.

[K] - Katana - Click on one brick to mark it with a crosshair. Click on another, and the line made by those two points is destroyed with a sword slash.

[L] - Legion - A [R]egenerate that, instead of creating new balls, acts like a [M]ultiball. However, each use speeds all balls.

[M] - Mimic - All balls blend into the background.

[N] - Nausea - The bounce physics of your paddle is inverted. For example, instead of bouncing far left when the ball hits the left tip of your paddle, it bounces far right.

[O] - Offside - The top row is destroyed and the paddle appears there. The floor becomes a wall and the ceiling becomes the floor.

[P] - Party! - For the rest of the level, whenever you use a missile it doesn't explode and instead sprays confetti. You still lose the missile.

[Q] - Quandary - Adds normal bricks where the [Q]uasar would remove. Basically a circle of bricks appears in the center.

[R] - Rockets - Lets you shoot rockets. They aim at where the little circle of your mouse is, and destroy 3x3 bricks. [S] - Spirit - Acts like the Halo upgrade of the KadoKado game.

[T] - Terraform - Same as [T]ransform, but the beam shoots where your circle pointer is in a 2x2 area around it. Regenerates slower.

[U] - Underworld - Click to send your ball into Halo mode. Click again to bring it out. Has a usage bar, each click drains a bit.

[V] - Venom - Acts as a glacier that, instead of freezing, makes bricks act as mines.

[W] - Weld - The ball turns white-hot. Hitting a copper brick generates a new ball, and hitting a silver brick generates another welder ball. All sentinel-types become blue balls that, upon contact with your paddle, shoot a beam that destroys a random brick.

[X] - X-ray - the contents of all bricks are revealed.

[Y] - Yoke - The ball is connected to your paddle by a string that auto-extends. Click to instantly pull the ball back to your paddle and make it stick.

[Z] - Zap - The ball becomes like a plasma ball and is able to extend up to three zap rays to random nearby bricks. The rays jump off bricks three times, and destroy bricks at a much faster rate than [V]olt.
