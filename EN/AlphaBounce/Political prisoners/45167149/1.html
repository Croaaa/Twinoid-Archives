Transports are ok if you happen to be in the area and you're heading where the aliens need to go... If you look around the forum there are some threads with mineral rich routes.

What I used was captain1821's path. You can see it highligted in the map if you click it from the left menu. It's mostly circular. You can click the "Editor" button there and it will open a textarea with the exact coordinates (in Google Chrome or Firefox you can drag the lower-right corner to resize the area and make it bigger so you can scroll easier). If you make the route, you can buy and have extra and you can repeat as much as you want, because the route is long enough that minerals will regenerate.

The only "problem" with it is that you need to have a good engine and radar for it to be efficient... Maybe you should wait a bit for it... other than that, you can still check the minerals option and it will show you the mineral rich areas... this also has a "downside", mostly that it's away from the origin...

Maybe the best thing for you right now is to continue with your missions, and in the process you'll get some minerals (use the maps to see what's the best path from one mission to the other) and you'll get better upgrades and eventually you'll be able to "mine the deep universe" in a more efficient way...
