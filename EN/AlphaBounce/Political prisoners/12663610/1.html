I know this is a slightly worthless topic, but hear me out

Beginners that just got into this game should definitely take a peek here.

Lycans is a great planet. While you don't get any rewards for completing it, there are several pros that make it's exploration worthwhile:

1. Shiny completed planet. You won't believe what this does to you self-esteem

2. Early on, this is a great place to get minerals. I went from like 100-200 Minerals to 769 in like a week. Even quicker if you have Meaning, if you're looking for money to get more envelopes/missiles/drones/othe r upgrades, you should check this planet out.

3. Experience. Lycans goes from easy to normal (I cleared Arkanoid II: Doh it Again. All 99 levels. I have skills at brickbreakers ) This is great for practicing and for obtaining experience. The levels are just right to prepare you for harsher levels of space.

4. Missiles. Two missiles lying around the planet. 'nuff said.

5. Future. Most likely, there will be a mission for Lycans later in the future. Although it might be a while, having a fully explored planet never hurt.

So exploring Lycans is definitely worth it . Although it might take some time, the pay-off is very sweet. I'll write some tips in a bit, if people want them.

Anyway, some tips for Lycans:

1. Preparation :Even though this is only a beginner's planet, you should definitely prepare.
a) By now you probably lost the three lives. You probably have a missile, but you should get another one (Both life and missile) as quickly as possible. Complete the Distress signal mission and then just find a couple missiles lying around
b) It would help if you had the envelope extension, but if you don't, consider getting it. An envelope with a permanent [E] is always good.

2. Tips : Lycans is famous for its orange TNT-like blocks. Make use of them. Some can finish levels for you (Really. I'm serious. Plenty of people can back me up on this)
If you have a level with tons of brown two/three-hit blocks, try to get the ball to hit an orange block or get the [F] power-up. Actually, most of those types of levels have [F]. Still hard?
Use a missile. They destroy brown blocks instantly.
Still can't do it? Well, there HAS to be a helpful power-up in there somewhere. Dig it out! and keep the ball going. If all else fails, get your trusty Javelin to wipe them out.
Just keep practicing. This is practice for the real game
