My advice would be to finish Salmeen step 1, then head to Moltear.

At Moltear you can complete your "Drilling Ball: OX-Soldier" mission as well as another mission which takes place on Moltear (this mission will unlock when you get near Moltear) by the time you get there and do those two missions you'll have enough to buy two drones (two drones is advised for Balixt).

Balixt is a hard level so it's best to leave that mission and head to Moltear first as it'll build your experience in the game.
