I collected several missles before I created my Wikalpha page. But, I'm keeping track of which missles I know I have, at the bottom of that page.

When I get close enough to a missle location shown on the MineMap, I check it against the game screen, to see if I've already been to those coordinates. If I have, I add it to the list on my Wikalpha page. If I haven't, I go to collect it, then I add it to the list on my Wikalpha page. (I know I successfully collected any missles I attempted to collect.)

Whether it's an online page, a file on your computer, or just hand-written on a paper near your computer, you might want to do the same type of thing.
