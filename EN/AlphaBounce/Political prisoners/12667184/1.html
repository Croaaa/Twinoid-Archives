MrBlue.

1. You can now leave the belt now that you've done 52 cleanups.

2. The mission where you have to get to [64,154] is for FURI only, you only get that mission if you leave the belt before 52 cleanup missions. So you don't need to worry about that. The Spy mission which you have now means you have to clear every sector on Nalikors just like you did on Balixt.

3. You need to pick up Salmeen on Tiboon to get 3 major upgrades. Luckily, you don't need these upgrades to complete the spy mission, but you need them for pretty much every other mission from then on in the game.
