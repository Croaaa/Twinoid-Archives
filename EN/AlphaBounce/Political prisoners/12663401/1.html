These data feed the alphabounce map, two Excel files ABCazutil and ABCuriosités and the website G.A.I.A..

I wish to regroup extended cell informations.

All you have to do is to post your explored coordinates and specify, depending on your will and possibilities:
(from most to least important)

0) "RAS" if nothing to report (no unknown planet in sight, no black hole, no mission item, no antiradar etc).

1) Count of green/blue/pink ores and missile recharges.
Do detail each count and not total value (in case value changes), and put 0 if none.

2) Count of bricks of: Life(red cross), extra ball, guardian, sentinel and other baddies.

3) Presence of precious pills such as Kamikaze, Regeneration, Sauvetage(Life), Volt, Laser, Javelot.

4) Comment for anything special: vary easy or hard, peculiar shape, abundance of pills...

5) If the cell is editable.

Don't worry about doubles in posts for now.
