I'd say:
First, travel a little bit farther below moltear to near where the turqoise star is. You won't be able to get the star yet, but it will put you far enough away to get the spacesuit mission on Cilorile. Next, head straight east (Right) Get the first antimatter, go around below the antiradar asteroids, keep going east, and get the second anti-matter, curve up to Cilorile, get the spacesuit, grab the aliens there that go back to near the origin, grab the two anti-matters on the way back toward balixt, drop off the two aliens that go near the origin, get a few more drones/upgrades for them, then head up and clear balixt. Then if there are a couple levels you are really struggling with, you can just go near soupaline, get the landing pods (you won't lose your until this point) and use [Q], [G] , [V] capsules which are easy to get from the surface merchants on balixt to clear them much easier
