and Yak, my hubby replied to another thread before explaining the whole bit of why your mouse will travel up off the screen ...

You can sit there on any page ... or better yet, open up paint, hold your mouse just like you would when playing this game... now ... draw a straight line ... back and forth (while holding the left mouse button and on the pencil) ... see how the line ISN'T straight but an arc? ... and see how that arc can sometimes (read most of the time) not really end up at the two points of your arc? ... most of the time it'll go upwards just because that's the nature of our wrists ...

Now, lift your arm up off the desk you're playing on ... yes ... stop being a little lazy ... keep your wrist off the desk too ... now, draw a straight line .. use your arm, not your wrist ... see how much easier it is to draw a straight line? ... use that method while playing
