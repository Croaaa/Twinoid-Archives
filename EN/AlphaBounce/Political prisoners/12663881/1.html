I just can't take it anymore. I'm, quite frankly, screwed up. I had checked my sanity at the door when I was imprisoned. I forgot to reclaim it as I left for the mines.

ESCorp, you've been good to me, except for those times where you played with my hopes, telling lie after lie until I was numbed enough where I'd just take anything you say. Class act.

You've praised me often for my apparent skills in the art of space mining... Too bad. You're about to lose a valuable worker, and the world's about to lose a good person. I hope you're all happy.

Goodbye, cruel world.


...


Oh, maybe I should clarify... by "cruel world" I just meant "idiotic corporate monster". And the only reason the world's losing a good person is because I'm giving up on 'good'. It got me nowhere, got me falsely accused, got me punished for something I haven't done. And now I've been beaten, abused, and taken advantage of for all the wrong reasons.

I'm sick of trying to hold myself to any moral standard among the greed and corruption that has become of the universe. I'm tired of being lied to and being treated as a statistic.

As I write this, I'm speeding off to where you'll never find me. I've already hacked the onboard reciever, don't even bother to think that the position shown above is anywhere near where I really am. (Too bad it isn't, though; Moltear makes for quite a nice view.)

The universe is beyond saving at this point. I'm acting on revenge alone; a rebel without a clue. I'm going to help by chipping away at your foundations with my personal pocket hammer of justice. I'm joining the opposite side, and they'll be happy to have me.

Slowly, with a rebel yell, you'll die. More, more, more. In this glorious midnight hour, more, more, more. You'll disappear. The black wind begins to blow. Give me your best shot... if you're prepared for the void.

This is the last you'll hear from me as ESCorp prisoner #87,221. With this writing, I'm setting you up the bomb. I'm taking off every 'zig'... for great justice.

Ha ha ha ha ....
