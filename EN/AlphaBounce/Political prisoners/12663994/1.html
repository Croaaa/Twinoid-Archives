You only get the Mines and the 4th missile if you stick with ESCorp.

The missiles destroy a 5x5 area.
The mines regenerate at the start of every level and are placed on random bricks each time.

The mines cost way too much and you can buy up to 4.

To get the missions for the 4th missile and Mines you have to do many ESCorp clean-up missions, and this can get really, really boring.
