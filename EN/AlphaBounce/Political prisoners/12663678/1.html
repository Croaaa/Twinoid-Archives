The coordinates I'm posting from are near the far southwest 'corner' of Moltear, if "up" on the map were north. So I'm in the bottom left quadrant of it.

The difficulty with Moltear is that some areas have many "flash your screen" molecules, or many "speed your ball up" molecules. You can kill these with missiles or with Javelins (or autojavelins) if you have them available. The other molecules are mostly not too bad, except for the ones that were Blue/Blue/Black, I think.... those seemed to affect the ball much like a W powerup whenever it got reasonably close to them.

I failed about 5 or 6 boards in the process of flying across Moltear to pick up the OX-Soldier and the Ambro-X. Don't have the sunglasses, but it's not as big a deal after you get the OX-Soldier so you destroy the flash-creating molecules the first time they get hit.
