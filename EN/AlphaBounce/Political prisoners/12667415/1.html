The choice is between quantity and quality

Go ESCorp if you want to have more upgrades to play with (some will make it a lot harder to fail levels). The zone cleanups get bigger, so those missions will get more tedious

Go FURI if you want to play more, as you'll get an extra per day. The other exclusive upgrades only help on levels that were easy to begin with. Also, FURI missions only require you to clear one coordinate
