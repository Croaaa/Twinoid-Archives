It is up to you what to decide, here are the outcomes of the two choices after spending LOTS of time travelling:
Getting the extention way:
-You will be able to land only on breathable air planets
-You will get a space suit that will allow you land on non-breathable air planets
-You will loose an emergency
Continue on to Spignysos:
-You will get a new missile type (up to 9 damage)
-You will get a new ball type: OX-Soldier ball
-You will have Zarkonian Medallion ( better stability and movements)
- You will have Ambor-X (+1 to radar radius) because Salmeen is with you.

My advice is to get things done in Spignysos and Moltear BEFORE going for the extensions because:
1) You will have lots of upgrades, get lots of and buy stuff from shop (Drones, generators etc...)
2) The new upgrades make some hellish levels pretty acceptable.
