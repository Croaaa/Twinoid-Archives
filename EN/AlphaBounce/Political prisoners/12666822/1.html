I was thinking about it in my case and I decided to:
1. Do 20 Free A Prisoner missions,
2. Circle Karbonic Belt from the bottom (Documents, Tablets and also collecting stars when being near them),
3. Go to Nalikors and finish missions,
4. From Nalikors go to Holovan and complete Saumir's Twinner mission,
5. Go get the OX-Delta ball.

When joining FURI I was doing this route that people advised me - dropping off Salmeen and taking passengers so I have a little advantage - the path from Nalikors to Holovan is explored so I can use my engine on full power.

Yes, I have dropped him off before joining FURI .

If I were you, I would now do twenty liberations, then start collecting Tablets and Documents on North-East (definately I would start from part located at [103][-73].

Then circle Karbonis Belt and while you will end doing this circle at [65][-102] I would definately go drop Salmeen (50 ) off from there. After You drop him - take passenger from Khorlan to Holovan. Use wormhole to get there and get passenger from Holovan to Nalikors (30 ).

Leave Holovan and head from there to Nalikors. Making it in this order gives you a few advantages:

a) three of your missions will end on Nalikors (Documents, Tablets, Transport).

b) you will have explored way from Holovan to Nalikors.

c) transportation shouldn't take too long because of free 80 that you will earn from Salmeen and Brifuk, if I remember correctly .


After finishing missions on Nalikors - head to Holovan and complete it. And then - OX-Delta mission for which you will have 4 extra envelopes, Saumir's Twinner and 4 per day .
