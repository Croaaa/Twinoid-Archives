There is a kind of cheat to help get rid of the guardian lasers:

The moment your ball hits the guardian and it starts to fire, press P. Now during the game is paused move your mouse a little aways. Press P again and your envelope will have moved and the shot misfires.

This isn't as easy as a real cheat, but it still helps avoiding the shots. It's easier still if you have the Medaillon, but works without it as well.
