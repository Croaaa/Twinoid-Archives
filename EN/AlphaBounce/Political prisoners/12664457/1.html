the clear text recommends soupaline because it has breathable air. The only other planets like this are pofiak and cilorile. beyond that youll need the spacesuit to walk around (you get that in a later mission).

All i can recommend is practice, i was awful too at first but you get used to it. Just make sure your envelope is totally in line with the ground as you're landing. Use small thrusts to make sure you descend slowly and when youre close to the ground try and make your envelope stop just before hitting the ground. That way when you let it fall it'll be very slow and the legs will come out.

Trust me, it becomes a LOT easier when you get longer landing pods. But you neednt worry, you wont need to be landing for missions anytime soon
