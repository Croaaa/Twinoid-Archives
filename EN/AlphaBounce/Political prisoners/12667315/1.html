First, read the FAQ's, there is a point about Spignysos.

Also, looking at your profile, it's important to note that you are one of the few who've experience the radar bug. This means your radar will have a range of 1 more than it should be. When you upgrade your radar, it will always be 1 more than it should be.

There's the good news, now for the bad news. The main reason why you're having issues with this planet is you have one less life than you should have. You have to converts one of your lives for the landing pods, you've done that too early (should be done after Balixt as you're reward for the Balixt mission is an extra life) so now you're only left with one extra life.

Unfortunately, the only thing I can suggest is buying some with really money. By doing this for your first time you get get an extra life that you keep forever. Otherwise, you're going to have a really hard time continuing from where you are now with one life.

If you decide not to buy some then the best thing you can do is follow the info on the FAQ to get the blue rock you're trying to get, then go straight up to Balixt and do that mission.
