Hey. I just wanted to share my thoughts on Alpha Bounce.

For one thing, it's an excellent Breakout game, with precise ball-meets-paddle physics, great visuals, a good selection of powerups and powerdowns, and a unique storyline. Though I'm not certain if anyone would agree, but I think the admins should come back and spice up Alpha Bounce a bit.

The thing is, I make suggestions for a wide variety of different games on other sites, and since I myself have gotten very into Breakout/Arkanoid clones, I know how one could be made with little to no flaws. The current version of Alpha Bounce is fun to play , and future versions, if they are to come, will possibly hold up and drive the success of the title to new levels of greatness. These features to improve Alpha Bounce I recommend strongly, though none are required.

When you enter Nightmare mode, allow the player to switch back and forth to Normal and Nightmare modes, so that the player can still continue playing on normal difficulty so they can gain 100% completion after finding and completing Earth.
If it isn't possible already, when being fired by the ESCorp for traveling beyond the Karbonis Belt, the player should be able to find retail sellers that sell envelope extensions that were previously given up due to deactivation.
I think that the lowest number of lives you can do a level with should be 3, not just one. It's not to decrease the difficulty, but to make the game seem a little more fair to everyone.
Therefore, the game's max starting limit of lives would be seven. No more than that.
When it comes to gem/mineral blocks, only the ones destroyed and collected would become inactive defunct ones on replays of the level. This includes beating the level without all of them being destroyed, or eliminating them with powerups that would prevent the (s) from being collected inside, mainly [G], [O],and [Q].
Rather than only having set powerups to appear in set levels, one of all powerups should have a 30% chance of being spawned from a normal brick, with the more useful ones (like [K] or [P]) having a lower spawn ratio compared to others.
The ball's acceleration rate in levels I feel is a little too fast. Maybe slow it or wait until 3/4 of the normal blocks are destroyed before the ball begins speeding up.
*If the [U] powerup ever becomes available in this version of Alpha Bounce, it should stand for "U"ltra and allow the ball to rip through any brick, like in many other brick-breaking games.

This should hopefully cover it for now.
