Another valuable method for using attraction is as follows:

Send ball through narrow hole nearly vertically (much easier than trying to get it through at a steep angle. Hole can also be one that you drilled yourself). Move to one side, and just barely tap the mouse button, while the ball is on the way up.

The ball will curve towards your , and if you tap for just the right length of time, it will be moving nearly horizontally. A horizontally moving ball bouncing back and forth in a narrow tunnel = lots of broken .
