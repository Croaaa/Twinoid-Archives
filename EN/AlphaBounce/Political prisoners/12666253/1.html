On october 18, 2009
npfan
The 4th missile can break up to a 5x5 area like the MAS-Z can do up to a 3x3 area.

The 4th ball can go through about 3 bricks (depending on the thickness on each brick) and then start hitting bricks like a regular ball. If a brick is too thick to break through, then the 4th ball will just do 3 damage like the OX-Delta would do 2.

Mines are places randomly on normal bricks, but are not counted as normal bricks. Therefore, you don't have to hit these bricks to complete the level and they won't add to the time it takes for the auto-javelin to charge up. If you do hit a brick with a mine, it destroys a 3x3 area of bricks around the mine.
