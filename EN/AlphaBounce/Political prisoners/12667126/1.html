Drones will disable sentinels (if any) in the game.

Each drone will launch each time the ball hits your , depending on how many you have. Buying an extension will apply it to all drones at the same time (even if you buy new drones later).

The collector add-on will make your drones collect minerals for you once all sentinels are disabled. If there are no sentinels, they will still go for the minerals.

The perforation add-on will make drones twice as effective.

Engine support will make drones travel faster across the game area.

The converter will...convert (duh) sentinels to minerals instead of destroying them.

You do not need to activate anything, the drones are fully automated.
