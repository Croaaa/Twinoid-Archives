Hi,

I think you are confused by 2 kinds of extensions :

Envelope extensions. These are the ones we are dealing with here ... the first is gotten on Lycans and lost if you leave ESCorp, and the second is gotten after 100 ESCorp cleanups, which implies you cannot get it if you are FURI These extensions cannot be bought at shops. BTW the [E] or [D] powerup is equivalent to a temporary gain or loss of two envelope extensions

Pod extensions. They give you taller "legs" when you're landing (there are 3 kinds of them). Those can be bought at shops by both ESCorp and FURI members
