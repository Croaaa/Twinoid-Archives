Make sure that you do this steps by order.

Go to [9][-10] and complete the level. You will turn on the mission "Strange Symbols".
Then go to [7][-10] and complete the level. In that moment Salmeen will start follow you but you won't understand his language.
When you get out of Tiboon and play some levels, you'll understand what he's saying and "Salmeen Mechanic Part 1" mission will appear.

Good luck.
