What does the new drilling ball exactly do?
There's some things that float around the playing field when you break their bricks called molecules. You probably encountered Flash molecules at Spignysos. The OX-Soldier destroys these molecules when it hits them.

and is it hard to get there?
Moltear, the planet it's in, has loads of them. So you'll have to play one or two levels with molecules before you can destroy them. This can increase the difficulty either by almost nothing, or to near impossible levels depending on the type of molecule on the level (for example, there's a type of molecule that creates new bricks in it's path, and without missiles to destroy it, it can easily make the easiest level impossible to beat).

As for other missions? Go read the FAQ in the Important messages section, and read the instructions on how to get Salmeen. It will be very useful to you if you visited Moltear with Salmeen.
