With 850 minerals, you can buy both the extra envelope from a shop when you reach one that has it, and the Sunglasses that are helpful for Spignysos.

As for Salmeen, get him, after Spignysos you will be told to go to Moltear for the next ball, with Salmeen will be able to get the AmbroX, which increases your Radar range making movement in unexplored space easier, and assemble the Zonkerian Medallion as well.

Oh, also, if you are using Keyboard control (which I am using, since I am using a laptop) change it and use the pad for Spignysos. At least that way the envelope will move decently fast.
