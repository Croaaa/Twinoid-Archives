On keyboard controls, Ctrl launches the ball and activates various things (like most pad-based powerups).

As for the cursor thing, that's the Sensitivity option. It makes the in-game cursor's movement speed higher than the mouse censor (or even lower if it's set to low levels? not sure), and thus if the mouse censor were to appear during play, it wouldn't be directly over the in-game censor.
