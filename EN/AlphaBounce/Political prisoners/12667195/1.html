The decision on whether or not to skip the v.4 generator is not always clear-cut -- I knew it would be quite some time before I saved up to get the v.5 generator (my focus was on getting PID pieces as quickly as possible), so it actually made more economic sense to get v.4 than to skip it -- v.4 lets you travel 5 spaces instead of 4, amounting to 1 extra worth of travel per 5 plays (5 x 4 = 20 spaces with v.3, and 4 x 5 = 20 spaces with v.4).

Considering that each is worth 107 minerals (if you buy from the cheapest merchant), then if you plan on playing more than about 200 plays between now and when you plan to buy the v.5, it is actually cheaper to get the v.4 than to skip it!
