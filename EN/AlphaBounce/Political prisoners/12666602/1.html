Completing 100% of planets is just to have them on your page, except if you have a particular mission associated with it.

That is for Soupaline, Balixt, Cilorile for the ESCorp or Holovan for the FURI. I think the ESCorp also has Nalikors.

The places for «stones» are for the tablets. It's for a particular mission. I invite you to consult external sites to know more about them (karbonite tablets).

The most efficient way to get minerals is to play some levels with a lot of . You'll find them on the MineMap (and maybe other maps). There is no better way, as far as I know. You may also take some crystals on the planets, but I wouldn't suggest this as long as you're not use to land, because you would lose the crystals.

The less expansive perforation tools are
ON Nalikors, if my memory don't deceive me

So you wont be able to buy these until you complete 52 cleanups or definitevely quit the ESCorp.
