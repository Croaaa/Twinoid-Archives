Sorry, didn't focus on making any zoom functionality, it would have been too complicated / time consuming. But you can still use the browser zoom function and then scroll around there to center on the inner belt. Both for Firefox and Google Chrome you can easily zoom by pressing Control and using your mouse wheel. With what you have now, you should:

Get the Space Suit. Now. Well, it's not THAT important now, if you're closer to Nalikors at the moment skip this step and leave it for later if you want.

Go straight to Nalikors (located at [66][152]) and finish your mission there (if it hasn't yet appeared, move one or two sectors and it will, at the same time as the Karbonis Belt mission dissapears).

If you want, you can then (or before, if it's more comfortable for you) deliver Salmeen to Khorlan. I would recommend getting the spacesuit first and take the Wormhole from [121][-50] to [334][-192], it puts you south-east of Khorlan (although still far away, but a little closer), and there's a missile to the way there too in [259][-195]. Khorlan's coordinates are [180][-191]

Don't bother to collect the starts. they're pretty much decoration of your profile. Get them only when you pass nearby one.

The OX-Delta... Well, it's really far away. Maybe you should do more cleanups and unlock more missions before going for it. Your desition there. Missions gotten from ESCorp appear in intervals of 10-15 cleanups though, so consider that.
