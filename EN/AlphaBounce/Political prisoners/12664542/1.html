On october 1, 2008
npfan
Aim for the guardians while the ball is slow. Just as the ball is about to hit a guardian, move the pad as far away as possible. Once the guardian strikes, move the pad back to the opposite side of the screen, catching the ball. This way, the guardian has almost a second to hit you instead of in the blink of an eye if your pad was right under it.
