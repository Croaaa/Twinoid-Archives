There's two things for upgrading your envelope's travel distance. Engine and Radar.

Engine determines the maximum distance travelable through explored (cleared) levels and into radar-touched space.

Radar strength determines how far into open space you can see around a location that you have cleared. You can see the "radar touched" area as a lighter blue than a completed square, but not as dark as "deep space" (untouched by cleared levels, and outside radar range).

You can only ever travel into "deep space" one space from your radar touched area. So for you, even though you have a larger engine than your radar can touch, going to explore new areas will be at the same speed, but going through already explored and radar touched areas will allow you to travel a bit faster.

There are a couple different radar (actually 3 if I remember right) upgrades that you can find.

1. Salmeen taking to moltear and collecting what he points out to you.
2. collecting the karbonis tablets and taking them to nalikors (only do this after you decide if you're going to stick with ESCorp or going to FURI as gathering some of these tablets will kick you out of ESCorp, and turning them in would certainly get you kicked)
3. finding your Earth and choosing to stay in the current mode will grant a radar and engine upgrade
