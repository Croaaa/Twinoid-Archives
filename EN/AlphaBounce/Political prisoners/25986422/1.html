That may be, but it's easy to be confused by fluff/roleplay text when it refers to real game items and effects.

To answer the question, their use of the term 'radar' doesn't have anything to do with your radar. It's just fluff/roleplay text. If you cross the belt before you gain permission to do so you'll be punted from ESCorp, and that has nothing to do with radar despite the text, it's just game code.

There are ESCorp only items which you will either lose or lose access to the quests which would let you gain them. But in exchange you'll gain access to FURI only quests and items. That's what is covered on the wiki.
