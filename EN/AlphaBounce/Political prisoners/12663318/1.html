I will try to suggest this, but I don't think it will be done...

For Vista users, you can add a secondary clock to your computer, by doing this:
-Click on clock in taskbar, then on "Change date and time settings"
-Go to the "Additional clocks" tab
-Check the first box and select GMT +1 in the list.
-Name the clock to whatever you wish (e.g.: Alphabounce)

Now when you want to know what the server time is, all you have to do is hover the taskbar clock!
