The Anti-Matter nucleus is an item you'll need for an upcoming mission (should be unlocked as soon as you get the OX-Soldier).

The Zonkerian Medallion, you'll hate if you are playing with the keyboard, you won't care about much if you are playing with the mouse, and you'll love it once you realise how goddamn cheap it is.
It's supposed to make you pad move faster. With keyboard controls this translates to MOVING TOO FAST FOR YOU CONTROL OH WHOOPS YOU MISSED THE BALL BECAUSE THE CONTROLS ARE SO STIFF. With mouse controls, it translates into ZERO delay time to get from wherever the pad is to your cursor's position.
The latter means that when you are playing, you can pause the game, move the cursor away, and TELEPORT. This has numerous applications, from dodging 4 Kashuat sentinels shooting at you at the same time (I call this Pause Dodging) to catching the ball when it's moving way too fast and you can't keep up.
Just remember not to abuse this, the pause limit is, what was it again, something between 15 and 20 pauses?
