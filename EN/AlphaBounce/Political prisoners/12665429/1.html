When you use the ice capsule, your ball becomes the ball that you get when recieving G power - up. With this ball every brick takes 2 hits to break, 1st to freeze, 2nd to break. If you break minerals, you don't collect them. NOTE : even bricks that you usually break with 1 hit, takes 2 hits.

Fire : Same as F power-up. Your ball breaks bricks that usually take 2 hits, in 1 hit.
