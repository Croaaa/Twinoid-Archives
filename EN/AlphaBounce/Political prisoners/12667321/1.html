There is always a chance, but sometimes it's hard(er)


First, have you picked up a guy called Salmeen? If you haven't, read the stickied FAQ - you'll find helpful info about him there.

Second (I'll assume that you haven't picked him), go to Moltear. You will find one very useful artifact there, which you cannot acquire without him.

Third, do Salmeen's missions. You will get your first surface engine and landing pods (in that order). After you get them both, you will be able to land on planets and asteroids.

Fourth, to get the Gregune's Spacesuit mission, you have to move some 50 sectors from [0][0]. After you get it, you can grab your spacesuit.

As a sidenote, have you bought (also called MTPass) with real money? That will help you a lot, because with first purchase you will get an extra . If you don't want or simply can't spend money on that, there is a way to get it for free, but it takes a while. Unfortunately, the site is only available on French, but you can find the instructions and translations somewhere on these AB forums.


And a question: Do you have a broken radar? If you do, go to a merchant and fix it. It'll help a lot


I hope that helped
