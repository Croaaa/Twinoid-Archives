There are tips for this planet, but first, I will ask that you try and keep cool when you post here

1- Your will be slower than usual, so you will have to train a little before managing to go through this planet. Everyone loses a few around there...

2- try anticipating the position where your drilling ball will hit your envelope as soon as possible.

3- get the sunglasses from a shop to cancel the flashing parasite's effect

4- explore Moltear and find all 3 pieces of the Zonkerian Medallion (locations described in the AR-57 mission topic), this will reduce the ice effect significantly.

5- if you just want the piece of rock, go on [-39][-7] (i think), break the orange parasite and let your ball die. The parasite will finish the level for you if you don't hit it, so don't launch your next ball. Once you win the level, play the one for the mission, break the [ ? ] brick and grab the piece of rock, and once you have it, LOSE the level. You will be back to [39][-7] but with the piece of rock. You can then easily leave the planet...

That should do it I've done all of that planet and lost about 1/4 levels on first try but all good on second try.
