You can delete conglomerates, but I think this is a somehow new feature of the editor.

Edits are pending until validated by a moderator.
They have to actually play that level with their account, so if done on the middle of nowhere if might take very long to be validated.

A level with edit pending acceptance can not be re-edited meanwhile.
But after that it may be edited again, and reset to original too.


There are some levels that are not editable (for whatever mysterious reason), some of which even moderators can't edit (presumably edits done by admins).
