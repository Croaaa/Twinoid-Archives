The others have pretty said it all. Use wormhole, get space suit, join FURI, do liberation missions, reach 20, start circling the Karbonis Belt collecting tablets and parchments (change your course a bit to pay Khorlan a visit).

I will suggest that you start collecting the parchments and tablets from the upper middle part of the belt. That way, when you are done (at which point you should be close to where you started), you can go straight down to the drone merchant and increase your numbers to 3 or 4, and then when you move down back to Nalikors you'll also have enough money for the Perforation Tools, at which point you will be fully prepared to take on Holovan, equiped with 3 or 4 super-drones, a better radar, and an extra LHC per day.
