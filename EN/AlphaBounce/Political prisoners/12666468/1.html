One thing that you can do, if there are no flat surfaces available, is to land atop a domed house. It is possible, though a bit tricky, to do so with just the basic landing equipment. The advantage is that the dome houses are the same everywhere, and are always part of the foreground, so you always know what to expect.

To land atop one of the houses, position your envelope directly over the house, and fire your thrusters in little bursts so you descend as slowly as possible. Keep the envelope level as you descend. Just as you are about to hit the house the landing legs should come out. But you must be moving very slowly for it to work.

If there is any flat patch of ground available, I'd land there instead -- this should certainly be the case on Soupaline -- but if you can't find one, the house method might be worth a try. It is my landing site of choice now that I've got the first pod extension.
