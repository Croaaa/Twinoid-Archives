I thought the same as you did, but once you purchase an extra envelope from one of those merchands, you won't be able to purchase another one, no matter which other merchands you'll visit. So choose the one that's closes/cheapest to you and buy the envelope. No point in visiting others. The only way you'll be able to have more than 4 lives during a level is to collect "S" powerups or break "medical" bricks, but those are only available in that level of course.
