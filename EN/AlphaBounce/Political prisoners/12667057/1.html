When the last "normal" brick is destroyed, you will get a MISSION OK no matter if a new normal brick is created afterwards.

In your case, when the last brick is destroyed, the MISSION OK pops up, but when the new brick created is destroyed immediately, the server recognizes this as a new MISSION OK for that level. It will continue to go through that loop until the "last" normal brick is destroyed and no other normal bricks being made in the process are destroyed.
