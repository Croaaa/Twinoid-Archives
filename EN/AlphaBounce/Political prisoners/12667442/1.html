What will disappear

- The Ki-Wi antenna should become obsolete (forums will be acessible to all).

- Player ranking next to the username will disappear.

New stuff
- Twinoid avatar next to your username.
- Possibility to edit posts within 10 minutes of original sending time, if no one replied yet.
- Possibility to edit first thread post if you're the author.
- You can hide some posts if you're the thread's author (for hiding ads, etc.).
- Adds Twinoid smileys to current ones.
- You can cite a message without having to copy it.
- You can share a user's message with a direct link to that post.
- Icon showing where you were last time you read a thread.
- Icon for new messages since you last read the thread.
- Icon that shows threads with MOD/ADM messages.
- Quick access to other Twinoid game forums.
- You can follow threads (new post = message in inbox).
- Better search.
- Probably more fun things!
