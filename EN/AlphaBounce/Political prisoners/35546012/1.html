I just simply decided that I wanted to make the perfect profile. I mixed lwxtz2004's and fallingballs's profiles (both of which are complete for their own faction), and I added the missing missiles (for a total of 49 missiles filling the whole table). I added the complete explored Karbonis image from micmac59's profile, and added the 4th Radar (which is achievable by a bug, but not many people, if any, have it). Also I extended the bugged showcase of the Envelope's lenght (making it the size of an actual grade 100 ESCorp's envelope in-game).

I then made a mix of ESCorp's and FURI's last rank icon: Actually, there's the maximum rank ever achieved by users, and then there's a last rank that's either not accesible or not yet obtained by anyone, but in the game files. I made a model with both and decided the later.

Finally, I had to decide who to put as the user name. I selected bubuletz because he's the amazing creator (and still active user!) of the ABMap (Sorry moneymaker1, you deserved it, but I haven't seen you in a long time and I'm not sure if you're still there).

And these are the rank icons I experimented with





But no, that's not enough. We still have a missing map that's not implemented (It was most probably meant to be either a Wormhole map or a surface vendor/PID map vendor map), and 2 missing item slots.

One of the items could have been a sort of "Advanced AI chip" that made your drones smarter. The last item could be some sort of shield to protect once per life the attacks from dangerous conglomerates. Those are my ideas for now (and those that could have fit best in the game).


I'm not very good doing pixel art, so maybe a creative mind in this (dying) forum can make arts for the missing map and missing items.
