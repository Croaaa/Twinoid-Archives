Do not think of this as a game that you can sit down and "beat" in a small span of time. Even your 100 will not get you very far.

This game is about space, which is a very big place. As such, it takes a long time to get from one point to the next. It's more fun if you approach it that way - enjoy the journey and don't just focus on the destination, which could take weeks/months to get to.
