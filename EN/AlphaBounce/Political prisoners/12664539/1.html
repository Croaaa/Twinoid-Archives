I think "gimmick" is supposed to mean "some game-play element that makes the levels on this planet special".

In most cases this is some specific type of brick that is extremely abundant on this planet, thus presenting a unique challenge.

However, I don't think that all plants really qualify for having a "gimmick".

Based on Moneymaker's MineMap, here's the theplanets' gimmicks as I see them:

Balixt: Reducine Sentinels
Balmanch: Orbital Nuts
Chagarina: Killer Bricks and Ferrous/Steel Conglomerates
Cilorile: Guardians
D-tritus: Parasites
Douriv: Multiple dangers, but none so abundant that I would call it "Douriv's gimmick"
Folket: Reducine Sentinels and Regenerating bricks
Grimorn: Steel Bricks and solid brick walls, No Minerals
Holovan: Kashuat Sentinels
Khorlan: Orbital Nuts
Lycans: Explosive Bricks (more of a help than a challenge)
Moltear: Molecules of all types
Nalikors: Ferrous/Steel Conglomerates
Pofiak: Few and scattered dangers, none particularly abundant
Samosa: Gluante Sentinels and Ferrous/Steel Comglomerates
Senegarde: Molecule Generators
Soupaline: Regenerating bricks (but these are hardly "abundant", especially when compared to Folket)
Spignysos: Flash Molecules and slow Envelope movement
Tarciturne: Ferrous/Steel Conglomerates
Terre: Hidden Location, Abundant in Mierals, No special bricks whatsoever
Tiboon: Ferrous/Steel Conglomerates, No Minerals
Volcer: Reducine and Kashuat Sentinels and Ferrous/Steel Conglomerates

An abundance of Ferrous and Steel Conglomerates seems to be the "gimmick" of multiple planets, and is a condition that can be found rather commonly in deep space as well, so one might argue that it doesn't really count as a Planetary Gimmick.

If this were to be excluded from the list, then Douriv,Nalikors,Pofiak and Tarciturne would be left without any specific Gimmick, and Tiboon would stand out only through its utter lack of Minerals.
