To denounce FURI members you need to unlock the mission "The Fight" which you get after another 10 more cleanups (62 total.)
I would reccomend not clearing Nalikors until you have "The Fight" mission unlocked because there are a lot of FURI members on the surface, so your best option is to do another 10 cleanups and go over to Cilorile, clear it which will give you the first FORA mine (58 cleanups) and land and get the spacesuit so that you will be all ready to go to Nalikors
If that sounds too depressing then you can just go round the belt for a bit to get the new radar, it's really up to you.
