There is only one drone for sale at a time. All stores that can sell Drone #1 will sell that one until it's purchased and then stores that offer Drone #2 will sell that one. It doesn't matter where you purchase the specific drone. So if you have 4 drones in your inventory, all shops will be selling Drone #5, if available.

The price difference is to keep the difficulty of the game up (not to mention a massive money sink)... the cheapest drone shop can be found on your way to the ice planet from [0,0] ... I can't remember the exact coords.
