Drones on their own disarm sentinels by turning them into normal blocks. There are upgrades for them which mean that they turn the sentinels into minerals to be harvested and also an upgrade which means they can harvest minerals too. Early on the most useful place for drones is on Balixt to disarm the reducines.

What do you mean by "not effective against Reducines"? It is effective against reducines, and every other type of a sentinel brick.

Reducine is a type of brick, that when hit by ball shoots in direction of your paddle long bright laser-thingy than when toutched decreases size of your envelope. Is that what you encountered?

And for other questions - it activates automatically. It launches from your envelope when ball hits it (you start the game, ball bounces off the and comes back). And it launches only when there is something for it to disarm/gather.
