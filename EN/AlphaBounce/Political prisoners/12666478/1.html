Collecting missiles in a level (grey boxes, quite common), getting the [p] powerup and reloading ammo from stores will replace the missiles you've fired, but only up to the max you can carry.

To be able to carry more missiles, you need to get items from [?] bricks, or buy then from the store (there's only 3 the rest have to be found).

It is kinda confusing when you think about it actually.

The 'undefined' is a known bug that doesn't effect gameplay.
