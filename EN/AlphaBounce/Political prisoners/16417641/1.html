Thank you for making a picture. Much easier to understand than text Speaking of which, it's simpler to make (and follow) a list. Well, in my case, at least.


Why clockwise is bad?
Let's say you do 20 liberations prior to circling the belt (that way you unlock Holovan and Documents missions).
If you collect Tablets and Documents in a counterclockwise direction, you will (or at least be able to):
Grab two to three missiles; one in the belt, then sidetrack to grab the second, and get the third one on your way to Khorlan.
Make a detour to Khorlan (right after [103][-73], where you will get an extra envelope, the one you gave to Salmeen so he can create Landing Pods.
Continue the circling the belt, possibly making a (slight, as you won't have suitable landing equipment) detour to Douriv.
If you do, you'll want to grab enough minerals there for the cheapest Engine v5, which you could grab (as you'll pass through the neighborhood) on your way to OX-Delta.
Grab OX-Delta, a very helpful ball.
Continue towards Holovan, where you'll get Saumir's Twinner. This is the place where you will need the extra envelope from Khorlan/Salmeen.
Continue the circling, end at Nalikors, grab Syntrogenic Accelerator.

On the other hand, if starting equipment is the same, this might be your path:
Belt to [-67][66]
Holovan
OX-Delta
Belt to [-80][-20]
Engine
Belt
Avoiding Douriv as you had enough minerals for Engine
Khorlan
Belt, Missiles, Nalikors.

I'm sure I overlooked or forgot about something, but I still think this is a solid breakdown.


PS: When you go to Nalikors, don't forget to buy Perforation Tools at [66][155] (possibly surface merchant!). It's a must for Holovan, and helps everywhere else.

PPS: Not sure if I mentioned it already, but 5 drones and Perf. Tools are the minimum for Holovan. Even with that, it was hard. Kashuats are nasty, simply said.

You are not going to like me for this, but...

I think you should definitely consider this (It starts from the point when You get Space Suit):
http://images48.fotosik.pl/403/537e24...

1. Go to Khorlan before joining FURI. Collect all the stuff You wanted on your way there. You receive 50SHC and an extra envelope after getting there. You cal also buy some useful stuff on Khorlan.

IPORTANT: Pick up a passenger from Khorlan to Holovan (+3000 minerals)

2. Go to Holovan using a Wormhole - it shortens the route significantly.

IMPORTANT: Pick up a passenger from Holovan to Nalikors (+4000 minerals and +30SHC)

3. Go to Nalikors trying to create a straight path (You will collect some useful things and speed up future journey to Holovan).


Advantages:
Thanks to this route You will not have to worry about the money (+7000 minerals almost for free). You will save A LOT of time and SHC, since this journey will take just a little longer than going from Cilorile to Nalikors without bonuses. And the point is, that in the future, while circling the Belt, You will not have to make a detour to Khorlan.


While circling the Belt - do what You wanted to do. But obviously don't go to Khorlan (there will be nothing left for You there) and what's more important - forget about the OX-DELTA and Holovan. Instead, try to get to Nalikors as fast as possible to complete Syntrogenic Accelerator. Because it gives You more LHC - the sooner You get it, the better.

After getting Syntrogrnic Accelerator - go to Holovan using previously created straight path. It will speed up your journey. After finishing Holovan - go for OX-DELTA trying to make another straight path (it will make your journey back much shorter). Eventually, make a detour for nearby missiles.

Check it out!
