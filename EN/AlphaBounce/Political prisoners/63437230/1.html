So I had an intricate plan for alphabounce. I had both the rest of the game figured out as well as my retirement after the fact. When I first learned about flash ending at the end of 2020, I realized that I had to get a move on and needed to mine minerals so that I could have enough SHC to comfortably finish. On the other hand, knowing that it was going to end soon caused me to lose a lot of enthusiasm for the game because I wanted to finish it and have a post-game. So when doing the mining route got boring (I didn't even finish it once) and my life got busy, I just stopped playing. Now, at this point, I've lost so much time that I don't think it's realistic to get back into it. Technically, I could do a bunch of mining routes and still finish the game, but that requires both time for the mining routes and time to spend my excess SHC. Time that I don't think I have. So, for all intents and purposes, my AB character is dead. My character planned out how he was going to live his life and retirement, but death caught him before he had the chance.

If there is ever any news that Alphabounce will continue after 2020, please send me a message. I would very much like to play again, but I want to do it slowly at my own pace.
