I have no idea what it's power is, to say the truth; but you shouldn't concern yourself much about it, because rather soon after you get it, you'll also get a mission that vies you the MAS-Z missiles, which destroy all bricks with one hit; even Silvers! The purple ones and those yellow ones that return after your ball goes above them are exception though.

In addition, as for the description, saying it destroys up to 9 bricks; that's wrong. Imagine a layout like this:
BBB
BBB
BBB

Where B, are Bricks. Imagine that you fire a missile aimed at the lower center one:
BBB
BBB
BBB
M

The bricks that will be destroyed will be those marked with X:
BBB
XXX
XXX

However, a 3x3 area is affected; imagine the following layout:
BBB
BBB
B B
Imagine that you fire a missile in the gap:
BBB
BBB
BMB
Then all bricks are destroyed:
XXX
XXX
X X
So, while a 3x3 area is affected, up to 8 bricks can be destroyed. You may want to keep the pattern in mind, especially when you get the MAS-Z, when you'll want to maximize your effect against Silver bricks.
