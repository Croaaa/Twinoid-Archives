If it says "1.50£/APPEL", it means the call will have a total cost of £1.50. Nothing more

If it says "1.50€/MIN", then you will just pay 1.50€ per minute on the phone. Usually a call lasts under 2 minutes and the average price is said to be around 1.80€.
