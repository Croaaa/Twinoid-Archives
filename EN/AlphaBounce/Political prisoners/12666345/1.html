Hi,

for me, things are very simple : the two factors to take into account are
1) the number of inside the -rich level and 2) the distance in between you and it .

Assuming you have the drone collector, you can bet that even if you won't beat the level, you'll have at least the time to collect all in it (this may be false for hard levels with decoys or lots of sentinels though, so if you have doubts about your skills, better look first at the screenshot of the level if available).

To take my decision I compute the ratio between the two factors above, which gives a number in /. If it exceeds 120, I make the trip towards the -rich level (compare this value with the lowest price at which are sold in shops). You may take a lower threshold if you need money quickly to buy some upgrade for instance .

Note also that this ratio increases with your version of engine or radar, because the more powerful they are, the less you need to reach -rich levels. Therefore I'd advise you to upgrade them as quickly as possible .
