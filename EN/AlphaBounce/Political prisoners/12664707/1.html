On the MineMap (by moneymaker1), there's an icon which shows where there are mission objects. If you hover over them, the pop-up will tell you which object is there. The ones on planet spaces show the locations of PID pieces.

You can easily find PID pieces with the MineMap, by clicking on a planet name, (which centers the map on the planet you click on,) then hover over the spaces with the mission object icons. (Repeat for each planet.)
