Friday, March 13th,

I was arrested for something I now dont want remember, Placed in an over crowded prison with twenty back to back live sentences for one small computer virus...

Well the Virus was unique so to say, it was designed to be passed by atm cards, stick an infected card in an atm machine effect the machine, swipe an infected card in a credit slot boom the terminal was infected. It was a glorious virus in that aspect. The AI of the virus was designed to hide and spread until the turn of the year or it had infected the entire planet which ever had came first.

I was not expecting the virus to infect the planet in three weeks.

Its payload was to disrupt the entire banking system of Mars. It never was to touch Terre, but it did. Oh lord it did. I was found a week after Mars collapsed under the decimated monetary system. Because of the one bank that went down on earth.

Terre Authorities were able to trace and locate my tags in the programing which lead to my Arrest. I will say the trial was almost a sham, not even my lawyer tried to defend me he sold me up river because his brother was an owner of one the Mars banks.

I spent the first part of my term on the over crowded IO prison Moon, When ESCorp was looking for ahem gunny pigs to try their mining platforms for Rehab of Criminal Offenders. I turned down the offer multiple times.

My Two cell mates Khelben and Bloodtears took the chance at their first opportunity. I while stationed at the IO prison Moon waited, ESCorp had made some of the logs from the miners available. Two days ago I had seen an entry that Khelben had posted, apparently he has found a way to get away. Now I think I may see a glimmer of hope here. If he was able to find information on how to get away from the system then I may find a way to return to Terre and Extract my revenge on the Banks and the Courts.

Ok since the other guys have bore their souls I will also. I am no stranger to the Mining envelope technology. I was at one time a member of a Geological exploratory mission trying to find new worlds for Us humans to move on to. The Ship we were assigned on while in deep space was attacked by an Alien life force, Having no real weapons other then the Old style Ping Pong miner systems, otherwise Known as Vehicular Assisted Utility System or the VAUS for short. We had defeated the Life force at least three different times on our journey back to earth. But we had not realized it had not died only hidden and followed us.

During our journey back we had found alien tech that had upgraded our Vaus Systems but only one at a time. In the time between our return and the day the life force had found its way to earth we had sold the tech to some of the Mining companies for them to reverse engineer.

The day the life force had attacked no on on earth had known about it. The life force had built a massive wall around the system to start consuming the life trapped with in its walls.

The mining companies who were out testing the Reverse Engineered capsules were the ones to discover the wall and destroy it. Those mining companies merged into ESCorp... The newly formed ESCorp had informed the government of what it had just defeated... This cause the whole Crew of the ship I was on to be Quarantined and arrested for bringing an interstellar monster to Earth.

What is now know as the Envelopes is the Reverse Engineered VAUS system. The ESCorp had found the new systems doubled its profit tenfold and soon had grown big enough to that the areas to mine were more then the trained crew they had.

During the Battle with the life Force the ESCorp had reverse Engineered one piece of tech from the remains of that life force, the Indirection Engine., an Engine so powerful it was able to Randomly Move a solar system and planets around. They traded that tech to the Earth Government for the protection of the Planet in exchange for the use of its Prison Population to Keep its mining profits up.

The Earth now Randomly moves around the Galaxy due to this deal but always in a predefined Interstellar Direction or a P.I.D. mapping. ESCorp being the ones who gave Earth that tech always know where it is to Acquire more Prisoners but those of us who are in the Envelopes there are only rumors on how to get there.

All of this History happened while I was Imprisoned in the IO correctional Facility where Eventually I was joined by Vasq and Bloodtears.

Knowing the basic prehistory of ESCorp and how to handle a Primitive Envelope I personally jumped at the opportunity to get back out in space.

Now this is where people are going to be scratching their heads after all this question is asked over and over "ESCorp has been in business for a couple hundred years at this point, how could you have been a miner in those Ancient times...?"

I/O prison is very unique in its treatment of prisoners, If you are to say suppose to serve 25 consecutive life sentences for nearly bringing Ruination to Life as we know it when you get old they have found ways to Rejuvenate your body via Nanite technology injected into the blood stream to the Approx age of 18 so you can serve the next sentence. I was on my sixth Rejuvenation cycle when my Cell block was offered the chance at the ESCorp miner Release Program. So I dont fear growing old any more the Nanites are programed to Restore me nineteen more times, to ensure I serve my full Sentence.

Hence why I want to Escape ESCorp and hit the wild Edges of space, maybe if I am lucky I can find a way of Ridding my body of the Nanites and finally getting the Peace I want...

Unlike Blood Tears who has only to serve his sentance all the way out and earn redemption, Or vasq who wants revenge against Earth.
