First, after defecting from the ESCorpians (as I have) and joining the FURI ... you do a ton of missions, then eventually you'll get the tool that gives one extra a day ...

Secondly, if, with your three , you fail all three, you will be granted a fourth ... but again, this isn't that great, as you won't make much progress.
