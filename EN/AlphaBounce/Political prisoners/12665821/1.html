After getting Salmeen, you get a mission "Salmeen the Mechanic Step 1. After finishing that, you'll get a mission called Salmeen the Mechanic Step 2. That misssion, basically, tells you that you can buy the landing pods at a merchant near Soupaline (don't remember exact co-ords) for the cost of 1 .

2. I know It's best to go to Moltear BEFORE Balixt (and after you have Salmeen on board.) However, since I am on Tiboon, what would you guys recommend doing? Should I complete some Salmeen missions first BEFORE going back towards Moltear, or first go to Moltear?

Depends. If you're willing to sacrifice a for pods, do the Salmeen missions first, then Moltear. If not, then do Moltear first, then Balixt to get a , then the Salmeen missions. However, if you do the S-missions first, while you're up at Balixt you can head over to Cilorile and pick up your anitmatter nuclei on the way. If you don't, you'll have to get them at a later time.
