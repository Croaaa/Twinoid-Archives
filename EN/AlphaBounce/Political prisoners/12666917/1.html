Rather than thinking that Terre will give you the '7th generator' it's better to think that all it does is +1 onto whatever generator you have now.

Lets say you got to Terre with the level 3 generator, it'd turn it to 4. But you'd still be able to buy the 4th,5th and 6th gen.

Skipping is fine, there's no way you can do it that'll mean you can't get the full 7th level. (This didn't used to be the case a couple of years ago untill they fixed it now).
