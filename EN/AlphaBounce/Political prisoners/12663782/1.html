The extra envelopes you get are:
1. Douglas's, when he dies.
2. One you can buy from an in-game store.
3. You get one when you first use the interstellar bank (Carapass gained MTPasses give you this).
4. You get one when you finish the Invasion mission (the mission to clear out Balixt).

Once you finish the Salmeen missions (including the one in which you give up an extra envelope):

5. An extra envelope given to you by Salmeen, when you return him to his homeplanet.
