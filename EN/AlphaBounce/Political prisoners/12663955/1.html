You need the space suit to leave your envelope and walk on planets where the air is unbreathable. It does not let you leave the belt without the ESCorp firing you.

You can get the space suit in one of two ways:

Complete the mission "Salmeen the Mechanic Step 2" and fly at least 70 sectors away from [0][0]. You'll get a mission to go to Cilorile to claim the suit.

or

Complete "Salmeen the Mechanic Step 2" and leave the belt, in either order. But be warned, the ESCorp will fire you, disconnect your extension upgrade, and you'll have to join the rebel organization FURI. Once you've been fired, Salmeen will tell you of a cousin of his on Soupaline; go visit him to get the suit.
