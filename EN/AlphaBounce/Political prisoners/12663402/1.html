Hi all!

There are a lot of lists out there detailing what each of the 25 as-yet-discovered powerups do, but there doesn't seem to be a list of all the different brick types; according to the front page, there are over 50 different types. Here are the ones I've found so far:

1. Plain--1 hit
2. Copper--3 hits
3. Silver--indestructible
4. Purple--regenerates after a certain time
5. Yellow--regenerates once your ball goes above where the brick used to be
6. Volcanic--when hit, destroys surrounding bricks
7. Dragon--when hit, destroys all bricks to the left/right of it, depending on which way the dragon is facing
8. Green mineral--provides 1
9. Blue mineral--provides 5
10. Red mineral--provides 25
11. Item brick--marked with a ?, when it an item (usually for a mission) drops down
12. Missile brick--when hit, a missile drops down which will refill one of your missile slots
13. Extra life brick--marked with a red cross, gives you an extra life when hit
14. One-shot laser--marked with a orange-yellow circle, when hit it sends a laser toward your paddle; if the laser hits your envelope it explodes
15. Sentinel--looks like Reducine, but yellow instead of blue. Dunno what happens if you hit it yet, but I'm not sure I want to find out
16. Reducine--marked with a blue oval, when hit, it sends a laser toward your envelope which shrinks it
17. Plague--marked with a skull and crossbones, when hit, it destroys the ball that hit it. If you don't have multiball, this makes you lose a life
18. Molecule (accelerator)--red, white, blue. When your ball hits it, the ball gets faster
19. Molecule (destroyer)--orange, orange, blue. Molecule moves around the screen destroying bricks
20. Molecule (flash)--white, light blue, dark blue. When your ball hits it, the screen flashes and unless you have sunglasses your envelope shrinks.
21. Molecule (populator)--I forget the colors...moves around the screen adding new bricks
22. Molecule (repeller)--light blue*2, dark blue. Exerts a repelliing force on your ball
23. Jumping brick---Orange with three green pentagonal regions. Each time you hit it, it moves to a different location on the screen and one of the regions changes to red. When all are red, it can be destroyed with one hit.
24. Ball-in-brick--clear-looking brick with a white circle in the middle, when you hit it a new ball is released.
25. Factory brick--green brick with a silver X across it. When you hit it, a new brick is produced; e.g., if you hit it from below, a new brick will appear above it and push any other bricks up. I think it takes 8 hits to destroy it.

Hope this is useful or at least interesting. Also hope it's not too long.
