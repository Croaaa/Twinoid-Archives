The ESCorp specific items you can't get with FURI are:
-The Retrofusor (sends you back to 0,0; unlimited uses).
-The mines (they act like exploding bricks and are expensive as hell).
-The 4th missile (larger damage area, can destroy up to 25 blocks).
-The envelope extentions (including the one you get after the Lycans mission, you lose it if you leave ESCorp).

But the FURI also get, aside from the 4th ball...
-The Syntrogenic Accelerator (gives 1 more per day).
-A permanent Multiball (compare to how ESCorp gives permanent Extentions; however, a second ball has a much higher value, considering we are talking about the Asphalt Project. One could say that FURI doesn't just give you the best ball in the game, but 2 OF THEM instead).
