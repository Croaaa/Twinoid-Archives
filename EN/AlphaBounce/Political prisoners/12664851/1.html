Hi. Some of you may remember me, some of you may not. This is my first post in a long time (heck, the first time I accessed the Alphabounce site at all in a long time).

A few days ago, I came across a non-Motion Twin MSORPG that similarly allowed only x number of games per day unless you paid for more. It's kinda funny, but that fact alone made me really nostalgic and I decided to come check the site out again.

Well, it seems that everything is exactly how I left it. I'm still stuck on Holovan, I'm still (in the realm of the game) an active FURI member, I can still barely see the forum, and I've still got 270 sitting in my account doing nothing. I'm not sure what else I was expecting, but it feels weird to know that the game was waiting for me so long. And here I am now, with my green movement range blinking like it's glad to see me and wants to be friends again.

Anyway... I tried playing a round or two and... wow. I suck. D= I think part of the reason I became FURI was that I thought I had the skill to work with a small envelope for the whole game. And I probably did, at the time. But now time has dulled my drilling instincts, and I can't really move from this spot. XD

Still... it's odd how immersed in this game I was way back when. Before I was addicted enough to buy , 15:00 US Pacific time was the highlight of my day. Even when I had no and no particular urge to buy , I'd check the forum multiple times per day just to see if anyone had uncovered anything new. (Also, if anyone came up with stupid reasons as to why this isn't an excellent game, I'd be able to rant - something I like doing. )

Anyway, this post is mainly just a reflection, and a belated thank you to Motion-Twin and everyone here at Political Prisoners for brightening up an otherwise very dull Spring/Summer 2008.

Someday, I may feel the inclination to take up the OX-Soldier once more. But until then, happy drilling.

(One final question though - if I were to reset my account at some point just so the difficulty became managable once more, would I be able to keep my purchased ?)
