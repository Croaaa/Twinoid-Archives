if you have a mission involving space suits, you should be told to go to a planet to collect it. these planets have low gravity and are easy to land on.

let your craft fall for a whle until its about 2 inches (about 50 millimeters) from the ground, then slow it down, once its just over the surface, just keep tapping the boost button to sort of hover your craft over the ground and the legs will eventually eject. i find that it often comes down to luck.

on harder planets, you have to start slowing down a lot eariler
