What do you think about a journey around Karbonis belt? You'll get radar 2, several missiles and stars. Karbonis cells are richer so you will have some money that you can spend on better generator.

Contrary to what I've done, I recommend flying in the Nalikors direction to the Karbonis belt and start collecting tablets anticlockwise, fly to Khorlan by the way, fly back to belt, then collect remaining tablets. Having collected tablets in order to get radar 2 you'll need to land on Nalikors, and after 52 cleanups you will also have a mission on Nalikors.

This way you won't have excessive flights. If you travel to Khorlan first, your Karbonis journey begins near the Khorlan. Naturally, it will also end near the Khorlan, and having collected every tablet you'd better be near Nalikors where you can get radar 2. So, if your Karbonis journey needs to be finished near the Nalikors, it should also start near the Nalikors.

I don't take into account zone cleanups. I don't know whether it's better to clean some zones before or after other missions. Personally I have decided to postpone zone cleanups.
