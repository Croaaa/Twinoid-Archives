I just checked how the level looks like: Yeah, it has a hell of a lot of Kashuats, but there's also 2 extra lives, and 2 extra balls close to where the few normal bricks are...

You may not be aware of this, but there's an easy trick that can help LOTS for avoiding Kashuats, as long as you have the Zonkerian Medallion (which you do):
When most, or all of the red shots have been fired, pause, move the cursor somewhere else, and unpause: instant teleportation (as there's no delay between cursor movement and pad movement with the Zonkerian Medallion)! That way you can easily avoid Kashuat shots after diverting them from your balls' estimated position when it reaches the bottom of the screen. You can even use it when you are not facing against Kashuats but the ball is just going too fast or following an unpredictable course or whatever. Just take note, you have limited pauses per level (somewhere between 15 and 20, but they should still be more than enough).
