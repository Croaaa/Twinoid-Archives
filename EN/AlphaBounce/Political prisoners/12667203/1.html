Greetings to you


Basically, you can now only do clean-ups. As you are staying in EsCorp, it would be a good idea to do at least 73 clean-ups because you will receive the Retrofusor mission.

You should also get some more missiles and at least 1 or 2 extra drones.

Liquid Cooler is really optional, but if you stumble to the cheapest one, it would be a good idea to buy it

Landing pods and Surface Engine upgrades are a must if you plan to land on most planets. You will get the minerals from clean-ups. Also, don't forget to check for nearby mineral-rich squares on one of the maps.

You will soon get a mission on Cilorile for the space suit. Do it when a clean-up brings you close to there.


That is all info I can give you. Apologies if it's not helpful
