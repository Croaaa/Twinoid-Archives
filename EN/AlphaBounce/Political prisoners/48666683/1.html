I have to admit that the twin balls is gimmicky, not very useful objectively, but fun. I can't wait for those. The extra is interesting. The paddle of half the size is painful, but it makes the E bonus so much more significant, and aids with evading conglomerate attacks (Nuts, kashuats, guardians), so it's not an absolute disadvantage. The Asphalt is very far down the road, but as I see it, yes, it is impressive. It might be worth it joining FURI and going the 100 liberations just to get it, even if you don't like the rest of the features native to FURI supporters.

I like to think that for ESCorp prisoners, the ball is a tool. With it, you mine bricks, make space for AR-SRX launches, activate FORA-7R-Z mines, and have control over with your big paddle. For FURI members, the ball is a weapon. You use two of them at a time, and you demolish the playing field with them. Your missiles will open some space for them to take action, and your small paddle will evade enemy attacks that are resultant of the destruction.
