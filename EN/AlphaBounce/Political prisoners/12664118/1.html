To make things easier with those nasty flash molecules...Go to the Merchant on the south east from Spignysos the one that is close to the planet...He sells sunglasses for 200 , the flash effects of those molecules will be a joke... Then try searching the forum there is a trick to get the rock easily.

One last advice: Search the forum, you will find lots of hints and guides how you can pass, this cursed stage of your quest for the AR-57 missile.
