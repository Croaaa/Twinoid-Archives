Additionally, I only explained the differences between the last ball and the last missile, not the differences between ALL the exclusive items.

So:
ESCorp, aside from the last missile, also gets YET another pad extension, an item that brings you back to [0][0] at will, and mines which are randomly placed on the level and make the brick that they touch act as an explosive brick when hit; you get he first for free but have to pay quite a few minerals to buy the other 3, one-by-one.

As for the FURI, we get a Ball Duplicator (every time your start a life your pod has two balls on it, as if you had picked an [N] power-up right from the get-go), and an item that gives you an extra each day.

Yes, the ESCorp has one more upgrade than FURI, but eh. I much prefer both the last ball and the extra per day to anything the ESCorp offers.
