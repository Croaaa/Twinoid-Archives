I know your original question wasn't about who to side with but since you're still a little bit unsure I thought I would throw my opinion into the mix. I think lots of people are quickly drawn to the FURI by the promise of the extra and the asphalt ball.

As far as the goes, if you diligently play every day you net an extra 365 free plays (366 on a leap year) per year. For 15 euros ($20USD) you can buy 350 and be roughly equal. I know it involves spending real money but think in the course of a whole year what you spend $20 on without even blinking (fast food, coffee, etc). All of a sudden it doesn't seem like such a big deal.

The twin asphalt ball looks very appealing and there are many levels where I would love to have it. However there are many other levels with lots of sentinels where the extra time my drones have had to defuse them has been the only thing that got me through. You can't bide your time as well when the ball is so powerful, not to mention how much smaller your envelope is. See this page for size comparison.

The last thing I think is often overlooked is how awesome the AR-SRX missiles are. I didn't know useful they were until I got them but their path of destruction is devastating (and so helpful), nearly three times larger than the previous missiles.

Also, warping back from far away places Chagarina, Balmanch, or even the OX-Delta ball with just one click is pretty nice too. The mines, while not amazing, have also been more useful than they seem.

Good luck with the decision, just make sure you don't just superficially look at the advantages/disadvantages of each side. You'll have fun either way so in the end it doesn't much matter
