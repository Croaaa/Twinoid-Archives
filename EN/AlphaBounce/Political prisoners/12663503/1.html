If you're like me, you've spent about 600 doing really easy levels for cleanup missions before heading to this planet. You may have gotten used to it. Fortunately Nalikors isn't a huge step up, as long as you have plenty of antimatter missiles. Fortunately there are several [P] powerups to keep you supplied.

Remember those missiles you've been carrying around since the beginning of the game, that have been only marginally useful? It's time to find out what they're for. There are a few easyish levels on Nalikors, but by and large they're mazes of highly-reinforced brown bricks, unbreakable bricks, and skulls. Thankfully most of the skulls are offset by red cross bricks and [S] powerups on the same levels.

Take a little time at the beginning of each level, before you launch your ball, to figure out where to use missiles to let you finish the level more easily. Usually that will be killing skulls or silver blocks, but there are often way too many silver blocks to destroy them all, at least if you don't want to be running to shops between every level.

Nalikors is a little smaller than it looks on the map. You can save yourself eight levels of trouble by making sure the little green pointer has the name of the planet on it.

You'll want to use the ABMAP, and navigate to make sure you hit the levels with [P] powerups at the right time to get the best use out of them. The map is wrong about one of them, though: the [P] it lists at [69, 149] is actually at [66, 149]. This is a very easy [P] to get, it's low on the right side of the level, so if you have to get one twice, it's a good choice.

Good luck!

I had the same problem, and it fixed when I upgraded Flash. If you have Flash 9 it will generally tell you your version is current, but not all versions of 9 work with the ABMAP. You actually have to be running the latest build, which is 9.0.115.

If you right-click on the AB play screen and choose "About Adobe Flash Player 9" it will show you which build you have. If it's not 115, upgrade and hopefully it will work.
