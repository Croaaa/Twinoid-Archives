For the Salmeen the Mechanic missions, there are four stages or "parts". The first of these, the one that ottottott and I are doing, is to collect the four parts required for Salmeen to build the surface engine. There are four necessary pieces that Salmeen needs in order to build this engine. These are displayed on the map by the flashing images, showing which sectors must be reached in order to collect the pieces of the engine.
The main reason for the confusion is that the mission only indicates that you must go to (44,-5) to complete Salmeen the Mechanic part 1; however in reality all four of the flashing pieces indicated must be collected to complete the mission Salmeen the Mechanic part 1.
Next time, when in doubt, just collect the pieces that you see flashing, as it is likely that they are going to help!
