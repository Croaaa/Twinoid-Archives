each of those marks are what's called Chevrons ... commonly seen in military ranks. Here, it is to represent ranks as well, but on a minor note... the more cleanups you do for ESCorp, the more chevrons you'll get, also you'll get a coloured blue background eventually as well to go along with it.

Those (like myself) that have no chevrons or identifying marks are either interstellar fugitives (left the belt before doing the 52 cleanups for ESCorp), or are already FURI members and just haven't gotten to their first rescue mission.

Those that have skulls and stars, are FURI members that have rescued a fugitive.
