There are 25 different powerups (or powerdowns) bearing the letters from A to Z (U is missing).

If you take all of htese powerup capsules and sort them by alphabet, their colors create a rainbow.

Look here to see it.

That is why the capsules [D] and [E] are so similar in color.

Similar care in identifying capsules must be taken in the case of [V] and [W]. [V]olt is a very powerful bonus, while [W]hiskey is a weird effect that usually ends up being quite a nuisance.
