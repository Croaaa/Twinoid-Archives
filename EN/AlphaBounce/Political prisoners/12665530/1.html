The Karbonis Belt mission DOES give you a greater benefit the earlier it's done, but I wouldn't call the Twinner "lousy".

At first, yeah, it will be next to useless, you'll probably be losing the second ball too quickly.

But when you get used to it? Not only will you get better as a player, you will also have an second efficient weapon. And as you get the next two ball upgrades, it's a weapon of rising value as well.

And there's also the nice addition that on levels with death bricks, if you manage to maintain both balls alive you can destroy one with each of your lives without worrying about losing an actual life to it.

[M] and all ball power-ups also become of increased value (what's better than a [V] ball if not TWO [V] balls?).
