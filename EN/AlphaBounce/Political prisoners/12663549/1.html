It seems like every game nowadays has some sort of "Achievement" system. Basically little goals that give no other rewards than bragging rights. I find these to be very fun to have as they are like little mini games inside the game itself. I know some Steam games (Half Life 2, Team Fortress 2) that have some really entertaining achievements.

I don't know how difficult it would be to implement this into Alpha Bounce, but I have a few ideas:

Bounce Adept - Destroy 10 bricks on a single bounce.

Bounce Expert - Destroy 25 bricks on a single bounce.

Bounce Master - Destroy 50 bricks on a single bounce.

Sniper - Destroy all bricks in level while leaving molecule blocks intact.

Wasteful - Destroy all bricks in level without gathering (once you get drones and collection it's pretty much impossible to get this one I guess.)

Greedy - Destroy all before any other block.

Opening Finish - Finish level by getting an Opening powerup.

Quasar Finish - Finish level by getting a Quasar.

Multitasker - Finish level with 6 or more balls in play.

Light Show - Have Volt and Laser powerup at the same time.

Juggler - Keep 3 or more balls in play longer than a minute.

Arms Collector - Collect 5 missiles.

Arms Dealer - Collect 10 missiles.


... and so on, I bet I could think up more. But it would be something cool to have on your My Page thing that showed all the unique things you have done. Some of them could be really easy, like "Fresh Start - Collect Alpha, Beta, and Ceta Licenses" and some of them could be very difficult...

I dunno, something to think about
