Just my two cents ...

Someone said [ P ] (Provision) doesn't drop from ordinary bricks, but that's precisely what it does.

Also, you can in fact die while powered up with Kamikaze or Regeneration.

Regeneration - If the only ball on the screen (as in, you don't have a back-up one ready and awaiting launch) drops below your and you haven't charged up enough energy to make another ball materialize on your pad, you'll get all dead and stuff. There must always be a ball on the screen.

Kamikaze - When the ball hits a brick, it changes direction and arcs its way to the next brick. On the very rare occasion that the radius of that arc is wide enough to bring it below the bottom of the screen, your a splodes. It's pretty rare, but it's worth following the K'd ball ju-u-u-u-u-u-sssst in case.
