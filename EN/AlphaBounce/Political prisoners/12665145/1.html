That's because the connection with the site at some point was lost and (I assume) the game couldn't connect to notify the AB server that the level was successfully completed. As such it counted as a loss.

In cases of such errors, wait a few minutes before reloading the page; the game will continue to attempt communication with the AB server. Reloading the page, however, will result in what happened to you.

So just wait a bit, and if the game does connect, and you have remaining fuel to spend afterwards, wait a couple of hours, and most likely the connection problem will be fixed by then and you won't risk losing any more lives due to things like this.
