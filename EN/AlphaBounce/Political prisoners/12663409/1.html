Auto_Javalin info:

-Starts after not hitting blocks for a certain amount of time
-The timer starts after a set time. the time depends on the blocks you have left
-The auto-javalin can destroy almost every type of block, including the silver "indestructable" ones
-The javalin resets everytime you hit a standard block
-You can still reap the rewards from a javalin hit (minerals, question blocks, extra saves, extra balls,etc.)
-If your ball dies, the timer pauses. It picks up again when you launch another ball.
-If the timer is activated, and the ball sticks on your paddle due to "Control" the timer keeps going.
-The auto-javalin shoots auto or manual
Manual: click when the envelope turns white to shoot it
Auto: the javalin shoots after not being shot for a while
That's all i know
