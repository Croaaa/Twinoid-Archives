For the hardest levels, If you start with a [Q] and then a [G] immediately, you'll have the middle cleared out, and no worries about sentinels shooting at you as well as destroying any of them that you hit, then once you miss that ball and die, start with a [V] and you still won't have to worry about the sentinels shooting at you, and you'll break all of the regular blocks quick. you'll have to use alot of turns to restock them though.
