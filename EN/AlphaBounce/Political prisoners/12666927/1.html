Hi Eightball, I know everyone plays this game differently, but you're playing it very wrong.

The idea is you follow your missions. The problem you're having if you have virtually no upgrades and you're quite far away from the starting point (the further you go out, the harder the levels get). Head back towards 0,0. Follow the missions you've been given (ignore the cleanup mission for now but do the others).

You haven't picked up your first generator yet which means you can only travel one space at a time. You need to complete the 'A new generator' mission which'll alow you to travel twice as far with one and it'll also allow you to visit other merchants so you can buy an extra life. Until you get your first generator, you can't visit merchants.

No on can alter the levels for you, the level you're on is considered very easy, the reason you're having so much trouble is because you have no upgrades and only 1 extra life.
