This game will always be flawed if the controls can't be changed to allow keyboard arrows for the left-right movement of the paddle. Traditionally, all of these block/paddle games were always played with either a two button left-right movement, or a "wheel" (knob). I know - I played the first one built.

The use of the mouse or keypad (laptop) creates situations where the cursor location off the gaming area disables the left-right movement. That is simply a non-starter for most gamers and I'm surprised this game hasn't been modified for a keyboard option yet.
