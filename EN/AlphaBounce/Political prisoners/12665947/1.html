Another bit of slightly off-topic and maybe unwanted advice:

You have taken quite a lot of passengers, before doing anything transport them to where they want to go. With just one life playing gets rather tough and probably impossible outside the belt as FURI.

After that, I'd propose you leave the belt and join FURI on Nalikors (because you already left ESCorp anyway). But instead of going straight to Nalikors you can do some things on your way:

1) Go to Khorlan 180|-190 and return Salmeen home, gives you the last extra life
2) Go to 63|-142 a wormhole to Holovan.
3) Pick up the alien at -154|108 he offers 4200 and 30 for transport to Nalikors
4) Bring him to Nalikors at 67|153 and become a FURI member
-> on route to Nalikors pick up the star at -37|176 and the missile at -101|151

I didn't come up with this by myself, Gibbles posted it in an earlier thread. It really saved me a lot of time and so I just want to spread the good news. And I added the star and missiles pick-up en route...
