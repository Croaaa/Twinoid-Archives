Bare in mind that when you are going towards those parts that Salmeen wants you will most likely encounter Sentinels and molecules. Since you have V3 generator, having Ambro-X makes traveling faster because your radar range will increase by 1. Not to mention that the Zarkonian Medallion and the OX-Soldier ball are sure handy upgrades when you traveling to THE EAST ( Courtesy to Diablo II fans )

Landing is a very interesting thing to have in exchange of an emergency sacrifice, however, I think, that there are many upgrades that can be acquired BEFORE heading east and WITHOUT exploring the surfaces. In addition, you cannot land anywhere just like that. You can only land on Cilorile, Soupaline and another planet. But not on the major planets (Lycans, Baltix, Tiboon, Moltear, Spignysos) For that you must get the space suit that will take additional time traveling forth (to get the mission) and back to Cilorile (to land and get the spacesuit)

Comparing both situations: There are lots of things you can do when you go west that when you head east.
