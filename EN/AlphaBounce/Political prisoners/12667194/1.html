You have all the things you need to land. Might be worth youtubing a how to land video, there's a couple of them.

You have land on a very lat surface, very slowly - you need to be almost stationary.

You can only get the Spacesuit on Soupaline if you have the mission active which you don't. The spacesuit on Souplaline is only for Interstellar Fugitives. To become an Interstellar Fugitive you need to leave the belt before doing 52 cleanup missions

You can however go get the spacesuit on Cilorile even if you plan to join the FURI.
