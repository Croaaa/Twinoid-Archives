1. i wanna buy 2 drones and had enough minerals for both but i could only buy 1 . WHY?

Exit the shop and enter it again, this is a bug I have heard other people encountering. I am pretty sure you do not need to leave the square to do this.

2. to get the spacesuit i have to cross karbonis belt and es corp disconnect all upgrades (perm. extenision)
even extra lives?

If you leave the Karbonis belt before doing 52 cleanup missions, the ESCorp will attempt to disconnect all your upgrades, but all they will be successful in disconnecting are your length extensions, so your will be the same size it was at the very start of the game.

3. is there any other way to get the space suit except being an intersellar fugitive or when i did the 52 ES-Corp Missions?
There is not much reason for the space inside the Karbonis belt, but you can get it before doing 52 cleanups. Here is the wiki page about it. Long story short: get your landing pods, and head over to [76][-20] before you get there you should be given the mission to land on that square to get the spacesuit from the alien living there.

I would however wait on getting the landing pads until shortly before exiting the belt. The top priority things for you at the moment do not need the spacesuit at all.
