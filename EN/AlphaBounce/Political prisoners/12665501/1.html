I do believe that hitting Enter automatically activates the rightmost capsule as seen on your screen. So when you have multiple capsules, you may want to pause the game and select it from there instead. Not to mention that if the balls are going fast and you don't have time for moving the mouse, looking at the capsule display, and pressing a key, you might as well pause anyway.
