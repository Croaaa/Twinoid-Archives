Huh. This is weird. Searching for "ESCorp vs FURI" doesn't bring any of the old, big topics on the subject up.

Anyway, I'll try to keep this short.
FURI gets liberation missions that are the equivalent to the ESCorp clean-ups. These only require you to win a specific sector, and always give you 75 minerals.
In short, Liberation missions are much, much shorter than clean-ups, and give more minerals per levels played, but less minerals per mission (unless you keep getting 2x2s).

As for the items each faction gets:
FURI:
-Saumir's Twinner (5 lib. missions, mission is to clear Holovan)
At the start of each life you start with 2 balls. Their position on the paddle is random/independent of each other (like picking an [N]).
-The Syntrogenic Accelerator (20 lib. missions, mission is to get 7 tablets scattered on the Karbonis Belt)
Each day you get 4 LHC instead of 3. You still get an extra one when you lose all 4 times.
-The Asphalt Ball (100 lib. missions, mission is to simply pick up the ball from a very far-away location (I do not recall exactly where))
Kinda hard to describe. It basically gives your balls penetrating power which deteriorates with every brick the ball breaks, and bricks that take more than one hit to break reduce the penetrating power more in proportion to their strength. Penetrating power is recharged when the ball bounces back off the paddle. Balls have 4 (IIRC) penetrating power at the start and after each bounce off the paddle.

Note that FURI loses permanently the extension gained from the Red Lycans mission.

ESCorp (I don't remember the clean-ups needed for each mission or the exact names of most things or the missions, so someone else can fill that in):
-The mines
When you do the mission for these, you gain one mine. Whenever you start a level, the mine places itself on a brick, and when that brick's broken it causes a 3x3 explosion around it (which acts like a MAS-Z missile, I believe). If there was a powerup in the brick the mine's placed at, the power-up will dissapear. You can purchase another 3 mines from shops, but they are very costly.
-The 4th missile (mission is to gather 5 pink crystals which are fairly close to each other)
This one is JUST like MAS-Z, only the explosion range has been increased from 3x3 to 5x5, centered around the brick the missile hits.
-The Lithium Retrofusor
With this, you can go back to [0][0] at will, at any time, from any place, unless you are in the middle of playing a level. Has unlimited uses.
-An extra pad extension (100 clean-ups, only thing mission requires is to pick the thing up from, IIRC, [0][0])
Just like the Red Lycans extension. I'm fairly certain each of these act as half an [E] powerup, so with both it would be as if you started a level with an [E] power-up.


These are the main things you should consider. There's also a bit of a difference with the FURI supporters (after a certain mission ESCorpers can denounce them for 5 SHC each, and they give FURI members either a number of SHCs or minerals, typically in the range of 3-4 SHC or ~70-100 minerals), but that's not nearly as important as everything I've laid down above.
