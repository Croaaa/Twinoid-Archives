It seems that AlphaBounce used to be a crappy minigame on this site www.kadokado.com . It's a Motion-Twin thing with a lot of minigames, and one of them is AlphaBounce, but without the story and worldmap, just levels. If you think the acceleration on here is bad, play it over there where in three bounces it's going as fast as it does in 15 bounces here. Also, the playing area is even smaller and a lot of the power-ups are different. [M] is multiball, but [X] is Xenophobe (not sure what it did) and [R] is like our [X]. [P] gives you the power to basically make your paddle inifnitely wide for a few seconds (its a rechargeable thing like [L])

Anyway, not sure if that was common knowledge, but I thought it might provide some amusement for you guys. Doesn't seem like the plays are limited either (Can't really read the french), but you'd probably need to be kinda desperate since it does suck a bit compared to this one .
