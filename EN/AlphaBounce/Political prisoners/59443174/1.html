The block simply doesn't appear if you haven't got all the Karbonite tables yet.

If you check your profile (under My Page in the game interface or right here if you click your username), you'll see that you only have 7 out of 12 tablets. The block still appears in the level editor, but you can't obtain it because you don't have the requirements for it to appear when you actually play the level. The reason it appears in the editor is so that there aren't two different versions of a square on the map.
