When I completed 52 missions, I maid a flight to Khorlan to get extra life and then started collecting Karbonis tablets. I recommend:

open EEBKiller's Global Map
turn on Karbonis tablets, rockets and stars

this will be your target points. Not all of them, just those ones that are close enough to the belt. You can also visit Nalikors by the way. Belt is circular so having retrofuser isn't helpful IMHO. Quite opposite, given that belt is well mapped, you can become richer rather quickly and eventually buy good generator. Landing Nalikors also helps you get richer. The richer you are, the better your equipment. It might help you in cleanup missions (e. g. not to lose game, travel faster). And, after all, it is pleasant to buy upgrades.

Personally, I was so tempted to get OX-Delta that I have decided to stop collecting tablets and maid a flight to the ball. I don't regret since I have visited several shops and got a lot of money. There are many rich squares far away from origin. I've got KiWi, Surface Engine X1, Generator V5 and another upgrades.

Of course, it depends on you. It depends on how do you optimize your game. One might want to minimize amount of and find Terre as quick as possible (or make another accomplishment). I try to maximize average game experience. So, I buy anything I can afford and get all the upgrades even if I could waste less , but play with worse equipment.

In either way, I think, travelling across Karbonis belt is worth considering.
