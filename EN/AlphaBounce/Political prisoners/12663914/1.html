There's a trick for getting that rock sample from Spignysos.

Go around the planet though open space until you can reach [-39][-7]. That level includes a brick destroying molecule on the lowest line of bricks.

Break that brick! After that you can let the ball drop (though you don't have to, if you can keep it in play...). DO NOT LAUNCH THE NEW BALL! The molecule will eat up all the bricks for you and the level will count as won.

From there you can reach [-39][-9], where the rock sample can be found. Keep the ball on the left side of the level as long as you can to clear a path towards the [?] brick. Once you have collected the rock sample, LOSE THE LEVEL! That will take you back to [-39][-7] and from there you can leave Spignysos and head for friendlier regions...
