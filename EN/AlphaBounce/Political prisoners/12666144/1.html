I cant see the level, but i'm assuming what you're talking about are guardians, which aren't classed as sentinels. The difference is that they are destroyed upon contact with your ball, so you only have to dodge one hit from them. Wheras sentinels can't be destroyed by the ball and must be dismantled by drones.

The signal will become weaker as you get further from the origin, at 50 sectors from the origin (i.e [-50][0] or [50][-10]) your signal will show as weak, at 100 from the origin it'll become much weaker, at 150 it'll disappear altogether, this can be remedied by buying the Ki-Wi antenna, although you can still view the forum by logging out and clicking the forum tab at the bottom of the screen.

Yes, there are guardians:

level

At first I found them hard to deal with. Best way is, shortly before your ball will hit them, to start moving your pad slowly to one site. And keep moving once the guardian starts firing. Then he will miss. Important is to keep moving, don't stop and don't go back.

Or there's kind of a cheat:Press P right before a guardian gets hit, move your mouse away while in pause mode, unpause. Your pad sort of teleports (works best with the medaillion but w/o as well) to the new location.
