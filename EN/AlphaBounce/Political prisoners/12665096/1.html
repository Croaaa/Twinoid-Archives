Radar upgrades are not available in shops. Each of the 3 radar upgrades are automatically given to you when:
1. You collect all 3 parts of the Ambro-X;
2. You collect all 12 Karbonite Tablets;
3. You land on Terre.

See the Radar-(Item) page of the AlphaBounce Wiki for more information.
