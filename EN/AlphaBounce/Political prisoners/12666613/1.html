Just one word about Cilorile if you plan to stay ESCorp :
if you clear it right now, you won't have to return there for the mission after 58 cleanups. Indeed, in this case you'll only have to play any square (on Cilorile or not) to complete that mission

That really all depends on what your goals are. But, if you really want to be efficient, you have to do some calculation with how far you can travel per . If using worm holes becomes an option, you would need to compare number of capsules used for either going straight or the worm hole. You would also need to consider what direction you're going (straight north, diagonal southeast, etc) and whether you would be going through explored or unexplored spaces.

Maps and help files are your best friends in making the best routes.
