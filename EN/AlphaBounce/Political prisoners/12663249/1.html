You will learn in time to play at least for a few moments even with a very fast ball. Only other help is to obtain more envelopes and try to find a way to win a hard level in some easy or fast way. Some can be won in literally few seconds with specific powerups (H, V are usually very good, if you get R you can't lose), in some levels it can help to get ball through some very narrow opening behind all bricks so it won't drop down for some time and destroy many bricks from behind without any help from you. In most seemingly difficult levels, there is a way to win them fast or more easy than it seems at first look.
