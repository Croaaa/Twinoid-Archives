It all depends on where you're going really.

For transport missions i dont look in advance, but say im around a planet i'll look it up and see. That way if i find a transport mission that's in the same direction as where im headed i can take it.

Use GAIA to look for transport missions, you can only do it planet by planet though which is why i use the above method.

www.gaia-qg.info/Balixt - this for example shows that there's a transport mission at -5, -40 on balixt to soupaline. So if you were on your way to soupaline, you might take that mission on the way.

Otherwise i dont think it's really worth the / to make any sort of diversion for a transport mission.

Oh, and as a side note: If you do go get the spacesuit it may help your progress on balixt, as you can stop off at surface merchants for volt/glace capsules.
