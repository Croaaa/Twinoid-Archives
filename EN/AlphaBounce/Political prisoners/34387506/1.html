This topic was well discussed before, the pro and against arguments for each faction can be debated all day long... You're not gonna get a clear answer from anyone. It's enough to look in the rankins and you will see players on both sides. It's really just a matter of personal preference...

You can go for FURI if you:
- want to play 4 per day instead of 3
- want to play with the asphalt ball
- want to play with two balls
- don't mind playing with a shorter

You can go for ESCORP if you:
- want to have a larger (about the same size if a FURI player grabs an E powerup)
- want to have a stronger missile
- want to have the FORA-Z mines
- want to be able to travel instantly to the origin with the Lithium retrofuser

That's about it... It really boils down to which set of "desires" from above you have.

Personally I don't mind traveling longer (can't warp to the origin from far away) but I prefer the 4 and the asphalt ball (I don't use mines that often, and the 3rd one I have is enough for what I need).

ESCORP people will tell you their own reasons for choosing that faction, and each choise is fine as long as you're ok with it .
