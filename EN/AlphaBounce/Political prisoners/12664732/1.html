Erg... I played this game in multiple tabs to see the effects of all the upgrades. I also got everything to max last night (left the computer running).

Features:
I see nobody mentioned you can help the little guys by grabbing the boxes and throwing them to "Global Switch" this can greatly increase income.

Upgrades:
1. The Worker
- Initial: 4
- Max: 20
- Each upgrade level gives one more worker.

2. The Shoe
- Initial: 0
- Max: 4
- Each upgrade level increases the speed with which workers return to "RedBus".

3. The Muscle
- Initial: 0
- Max: 4
- Each upgrade level increases the speed with which workers go to "Global Switch".

4. The Flying Box
- Initial: 0
- Max: 4
- Each upgrade level gives a higher chance that workers will hurl the box they are carrying to "Global Switch".

5. The Steel Box
- Initial: 0
- Max: 1
- Prior to getting this upgrade, boxes hitting the ground would break (when you threw them with the mouse). After getting this upgrade, boxes get a "bounce" effect once they hit the ground.

I have yet to calculate the cost for each upgrade as well as the total cash necessary... maybe in a next post.
