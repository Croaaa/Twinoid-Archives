On Terre, once you land, you have the option to continue in normal mode, or to start over in nightmare mode. If you choose to continue in normal mode, you get a permanent engine and radar upgrade as soon as it sends you back to [0][0]. As for the Twinner, you start with two balls everytime you start a new level, and everytime you die.
