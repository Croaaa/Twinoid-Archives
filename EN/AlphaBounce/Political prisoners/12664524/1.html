The stardust mission will also require you to go beyond the Karbonis Belt...

Basically, you now have two choices:

1) Defy ESCorp's orders and go beyond the Karbonis Belt. This will cause you to be kicked out of ESCorp and become an Interstellar Fugitive.

2) Do as the OX-Delta mission tells you and resume your scheduled mining duties. This means doing Zone Cleanups - lots of them!

Once you've completed 52 CUs, you will be given another mission which wioll require you to move beyond the Belt, but in this case, you'll be given an official license to do so. After that, you're free to leave the Belt at any time without risking ESCorp's wrath.


Also, there are a number of missions that are not transmitted to you from ESCorp, but need to be found by travelling to the correct coordinates. In your current situaion, you would have quite a lot to gain from taking a closer look at the planets Tiboon (first) and Moltear (second).
