As it says in the notes section, you should acquire a space suit before you try to go for it because you won't be able to complete the last step of becoming a FURI member.

I do suggest getting the MAS-Z missile first and increasing your max missile capacity before venturing too far out. A few (try to reach 3-4) extra drones wouldn't hurt as well. If you do the first two steps, Balixt should also be no problem to complete ("Invasion" mission) and is well worth the trouble.

You might want to read the FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions) thread pinned at the top. There's also plenty of useful information on the wiki and AB map.
