Q: I lost my lives and only have one on each level now, what happened?
A: You are out of ESCorp’s “newbie protection”, so to speak. That means that you emergency envelopes, aka your extra lives get taken away from you. Do not worry, you will find more extra lives as you progress the game, 2 of which are possible to get very early (one automatically obtained a few levels after you complete the Distress Call mission, another is buyable at an in-game shop for a rather small amount of minerals).Where is this shop located?
