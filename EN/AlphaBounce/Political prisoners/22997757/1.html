Launcelot

Probably Douriv. Some locations are average but quite a few require very delicate maneuvers. Strong gravitational pull but manageable, your problem lies in the surface. I recommend you don't attempt to land there without 6 meter pods and engine 2. That's the engine I used but with 4 meter pods and I must've lost some precious minerals, though I think I got about 80% of it all. In the toughest locations most of the time I was able to pull it off with practically no fuel left, so I was unable to take off but that didn't matter because the job was done (minerals/aliens/denouncing). Also many times I must've put my pods suspension limit to a test because I had to land pretty fast, having already spent most of my fuel trying to find a spot. If I did it again I'd try with 6 meter pods so I wouldn't have to be so picky and waste fuel. Btw most of the time you'll be landing on trees, never reaching the ground. Ton of minerals nonetheless, worth the effort.
