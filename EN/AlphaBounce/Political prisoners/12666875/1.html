Sorry for bringing up a locked thread, but I have a couple of important notes to make.

The number represents which drone you buy, not how many.
If a merchant sells a drone, it sells all drones. For example, if the price for drone #10 is not listed even though drone #9 is, the merchant does sell drone #10, but we don't yet exactly how many minerals the merchant is selling it for. However, you can get a very close estimate knowing that all drone prices increase in the same rate. (In other words, price of 10/price of 9, price of 9/price of 8, and so on are the same no matter what merchant you go to.)
