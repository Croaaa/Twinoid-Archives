I don't mean to rile anyone up or cause controversy, but I don't like how the concept for editing levels is handled, whereas you can only modify existing levels and can't add any new blocks than the allowed limit. And also, you have to unlock it first.

I was thinking that players should be able to create and upload levels to a separate level browser area that is not part of the main game (plus able to modify and re-submit existing levels by the user). Here, players should be able to create their dream level, using every brick/brick color/object/background in Alpha Bounce, with no restrictions on the # of blocks the level may contain. Also, playing a user-made level on the level browser or creating one to upload does not consume the user's supply of . Also, you can use your various equipment and upgrades from the main game in the level browser levels (but the player cannot obtain additional minerals from breaking mineral blocks in the level browser levels).

I'm very into games of the same genre as Alpha Bounce, and it is extremely rare to find one, even nowadays, that includes an easy to use level editor available right from the start, allowing absolute freedom for the player.

Of course these levels would be monitored just like the ones in the main game, and have a five-star rating system with a policy to remove inappropriate and/or low-scoring stages.

Of topic: I sometimes wish this game had a scoring system.
