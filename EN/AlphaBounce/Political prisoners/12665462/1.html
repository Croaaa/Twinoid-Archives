npfan
It is good to think about, but it is also very complicated.

You have to assume lots of variables before you can mathematically prove which one is better.

Let me just give you a synopsis:

During play when you're completing missions, ESCorp is much better as far as completing missions in fewer plays than in FURI.
When it comes between which one gets the more minerals (and hence more bought from merchants) in terms of cleanups/liberations, it depends on the distance between two cleanups/liberations and where the two are. Some cases FURI would be better, others ESCorp.
One argument about FURI is that players can collect minerals while traveling what ESCorp players save using the Retrofuser. While this is true, given that levels on average only have 22 each and each cost at least 107 , this is not a very strong argument unless you (almost) never buy with real money.
One key thing to remember about who gets more plays is that also depends on how long you play. No matter which faction you are with, the number of days you play can heavily influence which faction gives you more plays.

Since there are many factors involved, I can come up with hundreds of scenarios where ESCorp is better and other hundreds of scenarios where FURI is better.
