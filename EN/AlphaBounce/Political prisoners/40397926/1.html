There's no way of knowing if you landed or not. But the good news is that you don't need to land in order to finish your mission, you just have to explore all the sectors of that planet...

If you happen to know that a certain surface area has a lot of minerals or some other reward, you can land on it of course... But you don't have to land on all the levels of the planet to complete your mission.
