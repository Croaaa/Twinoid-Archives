If these are the only two missions left, you have 2 options:

1. Continue doing clean-up missions for ESCorp. Once you hit 52 of them, you will become a spy for them instead of being a prisoner, and be allowed to move outside of the Karbonis Belt.

2. Leave the Karbonis Belt despite ESCorp's warnings. Once you do so, ESCorp will take away any upgrades they have given you and you will be labeled an interstellar fugitive. At this point you can go to Nalikors, south-southeast of [0,0] and join the FURI, a rebel organization against ESCorp.

Once you become a spy for ESCorp or leave the Karbonis Belt, you can't switch sides again, EVER. So make your choice carefully. Each faction has some upgrades unique for it's members, search the forums for these, you are bound to find information on the advantages each faction has over another.
