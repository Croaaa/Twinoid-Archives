I would get the third ball, it makes a big difference. It looks like you're on your way out there anyway and with the retrofusor it should be a pretty easy trip. Do the required cleanups to trigger the final missile upgrade mission 87 but don't bother doing any more than that until you're really pressed for things to do. The last cleanup reward is nice but not really worth the effort compared to other things you can get.

At this point in the game whenever you get out to a distant planet do yourself a favor and pick up the PID pieces there. You may think you're not interested in them and don't want to bother with that part of the game but at some point that will change and you'll regret having to make those trips twice. It doesn't have to be top priority but when you get to the pink crystals stop by D-Tritus on the way and then swing by Samosa after. (also at Samosa, pick up the cheapest surface turbo #2 (you need it) close by and then get all the pink crystals on the surface for 11105 (practice landing on a different space first). When you're ready to warp home, there's 2 (3 if you're brave) transport missions you can do that will pay you 20764 and 121 [one to Soupaline, Spignysos, and -71,3] See the wiki. I'm just sayin' )

Speaking of planets, make sure you do every "The Fight" mission and denounce the FURI supporters to get your 5 . Even if you weren't planning on doing that square it would cost you 2 / and you would end up with 3 extra when you were done.

Also, as already stated, the PSM makes a big difference especially once you have the AR-SRX missiles, which are also amazing.
