Are you using Windows? If so, here's a tool to help narrow down the problem: get to command prompt, and run TraceRt. It will report to you about each node between your computer and www.AlphaBounce.com, or whatever location you want.

To get to command prompt, open the start menu, click on "run", and then type CMD and hit enter. A small black window should appear, with a dos prompt. Then just type "tracert www.alphabounce.com". Do this a few times and determine if the delay is on your end or elsewhere.
