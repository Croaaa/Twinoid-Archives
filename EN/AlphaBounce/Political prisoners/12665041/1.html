and ... here is a spoiler tagged copy+paste from the wiki about the P
Provision

Colour: Bright Pink

Helpful powerup

Rare

Affects Envelope

To use: Automatic

Effect: Completely refills your missile capacity

Useful Situations: When you're low on missiles. Levels with this powerup are marked on abmap for if you are far from a merchant but in need of a missile refill.

Beware of: Not anything really, except if you see it falling and you have lots of missiles you may as well try and use a few before getting it. Not worth going for if you have lots of missiles and good powerups in play.
