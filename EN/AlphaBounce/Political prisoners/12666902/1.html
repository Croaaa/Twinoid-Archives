Having the cursor moving off the screen (especially the top) is a new users nightmare, however it doesn't take very long to get used to.

It designed to move off the sides so you can have better control of the ball when it comes down at the side of the screen. If you envelope is flying off one side hit the spanner / wrench and play with the mouse sensitivity settings, this may help you.

As for the cursor going off the stop of the top and bottom of the screen there isn't anything that can be done about that other than practice.

A joystick isn't going to help, and would probably make the game much harder.
