You get kicked out of ESCorp if you move across the asteroid belt for any reason before you've completed 52 Zone Cleanups. There are a couple of missions that require you to leave the asteroid belt, so these can only be fulfilled after the 52th CU, or by defecting from ESCorp.

If you choose the defect, the envelope extension that you gained in the Red Lycans mssion is taken away from you, you lose your ESCorp rank and can no longer get any new missions from ESCorp.

However, you do get a chance to joint the rebellious F.U.R.I. group and do missions for them instead.

Basically, it works like this:

To join F.U.R.I. you must first become and Interstellar Fugutive.

The only way to become a Fugitive is to be kicked out of ESCorp.

Once you completed those 52 CUs and are allowed to leave the asteroid belt, there's nothing left that you could do to get ESCorp to kick you out.
