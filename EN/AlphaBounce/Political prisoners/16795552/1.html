BluRage, read this wiki about merchants. It will tell you what mission you have to finish in order to get the merchants map.

Also, the Moneymaker's map has all of them marked, really useful tool for detailed exploration. It also shows the merchants that are located on the surface of the planets (that's later for you, when you'll do the necessary missions that will allow you to land).

Then there's also the Overview map, and if you tick the "Merchants" checkbox, you'll see them all marked with dots. It also has the "Cheap items" select where you can choose the item you want and it will give you the best prices for it and the merchants' location where you can find those items.

Good luck in discovering / exploring the Alphabounce universe .
