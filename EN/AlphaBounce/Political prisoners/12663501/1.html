Aim for the clear brick with the orange-blue-orange molecule in it.

That molecule will start destroying all the bricks in the level, so you no longer need to worry about the ball or your envelope (unless you're on your last life).

Just drop the ball and don't relaunch the new one. The Molecule will win the level for you.

how do you just "go for the ?" as it is not reachable easyly?
You'll probably have to try a few times.

But just concentrate on aiming for the [?], because whatever happens, you do not want to win the level! If you win the level, you'll move into its position, so you'd have to move away again before trying again (if you didn't catch the [?]). And if you did catch the [?], losing the level will send you back to [-39][-7], and from there you can leave the planet with only one more move.

And one more tip:
There's an [O] Powerup in the level with the Rock Sample.
Do not catch that Powerup!
It will move the [?] further to the side of the screen where it is even harder to hit than before!
