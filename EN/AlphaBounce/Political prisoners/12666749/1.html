I have no idea what question you're asking. SHC = solid hydrogen capsule () these last forever and never expire, you can buy them with real money, or get some from in game missions. You can also buy them from merchants for minerals, however this is always advised against as the money should be spent on other upgrades.

LHC = liquid hydrogen capsule () you get 3 of these free a day at 00:00 GMT+1. To find out the server time, look at the time on your cleanup mission, this gives the current time. Players all over the world get their free at different times depending on what timezone they're in.

You can only have 3 at one given time. If you don't use them by midnight GMT+1, you'll lose them and you'll be issued with 3 new 's.

I think what you're asking is where are your 3 free LHC () and you have probably not realised that they renew at midnight French time.
