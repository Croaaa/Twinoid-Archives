Hello

I have made a symbolic map of Alphabounce using Excel's graph feature.
It started for my own use, then I decided to improve it so others could use it.

Here is a preview.
It is available here and works with Excel 2000 (possibly 97).
I regret it is unusable with OpenOffice . The data tables are still usable though.


Oh, but what does it do ?
•Display levels with more than 50 minerals (the higher the value the more highlighted).
•Display your exploration path if you have input it, in shades from most recent to oldest, and then darker when ores have regenerated.

So far no other map does that, that is why I did it.

Also displayed:
•Landings (color coded for ores and special stuff).
•Merchants (best prices highlighted and tagged).
•Missions (color coded for accessibility).
•P Bonuses
•Antiradars
•Black holes
•Missiles
•Various notes, which you can add.

Of course to be able to display it all, it includes all the latest data collected by users.

And various toolbars, settings, formatting and guided input to track your mission & missile progress and better use and mine the whole thing.


On the downside, it is a bit heavy and slow... it is only basically a spreadsheet tool while waiting for someone to make a real online map.

Coming next:
-Better tooltips.
-Missions organigram.
-Fix the scrollbars.
