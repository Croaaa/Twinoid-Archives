Most importantly:

Don't try to complete Lycans now!

It's a common beginners mistake. You'll hardly manage it now with that few lives and it will get boring soon because it'll take ages. You'll also get nothing for completing it.

Just do what the missions tell you. Or read the FAQ and beginner's help.

Most important thing is getting more extra lives:
1) complete the distress call (if you haven't done that yet)
2) buy an extra live
cheap one at -13|8
3) buy capsules, if you have no CC or paypal use SMS - it will get you an extra live

After that you'll find things much easier. But still don't just try to complete planetes but for now stick with the missions. You will get rewards for completing them.

And, again, read the FAQ and Help posting.

The Map is quite helpful also.
