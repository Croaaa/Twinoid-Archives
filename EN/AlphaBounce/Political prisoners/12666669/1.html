you really do need to read the FAQ thing... there's a specific level reachable with your engine and radar that has a orange molecule in a brick right at the bottom... if you hit that with your very first ball, then loose that ball on purpose, it will have released that molecule ... that orange molecule is a destruction one, wherein it will bounce around and any time it hits a brick (or a wall) it will destroy some bricks around itself. just sit back, enjoy the easy easy level that requires only properly placing your first shot and never needing to release your second shot.

Once that level is complete, you'll be within shot of the level with the piece you need, try to shoot for that ? block which will be on the left side. once you hit it, make sure to catch the falling object. once you've caught it, PURPOSEFULLY loose the level, which will put you back at that easy level, but saving a fuel. You are then free to move outside of the planet and move on with your missions.

Hints: Try to always hit the ball off the centre of the pad. Once you've gotten your part you need, re-read the FAQ about the next mission you will have been given.
