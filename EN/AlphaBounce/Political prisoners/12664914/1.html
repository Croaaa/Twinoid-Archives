I'd say the medaillon is completely useless for landing

For landing safely, you have to reduce your speed as much as possible and always keep your ship paralled to the ground .

To to this, click on the mouse button to brake and always keep the cursor right above the middle of your envelop to stabilize it

As said, this requires practice, but some items (reactors and/or longer legs) are available at shops, which makes landing much easier
