Don't skip the V5 engine. it'll be a LOOONG time before you can afford the V6

As for what to do, here is what I would do in your situation:

After your cleanup, head to Karbonite tablet at [119][65] then circle the belt counter-clockwise, poping up to Grimorn for the Retrofuser when you get nearby.

Also you might want to take short diversions for nearby stars and missiles.

Once you get the tablet at [58][106] head south to Nalikors. There you can complete the Spy mission and by landing on [70][151] you complete the mission that gives you the Radar level 2.

After that, you could retrofuser back to the origin then head northeast to Cilorile, do that mission, continue northeast to Khorlan to return Salmeen.

At least, that is what I would do. Feel free to change it up as much as you want. Personally, I circled the Karbonis belt right when I got to 52 cleanups, so yea. To each their own.
