Well, you see, Clean-ups are for the most part unimportant; until you get close to doing 52 of them. Doing 52 clean-ups means you are going to be stuck with EScorp, but before that, you have a choice.

Now, there is an asteroid belt in the game, and all missions early on take place inside it. If you leave the belt before doing 52 clean-ups, you get kicked from ESCorp and can join another faction, FURI. ESCorp and FURI get some different upgrades and there's some other differences between them.

People tend to not do Clean-ups early on for two reasons: one is to give them time to decide whether they want to go FURI or ESCorp once they do all major missins inside the Belt they can.

The other is that they are just plain boring.
