There are two different missions for the space suit, depending on if you get the mission while still an ESCorp prisoner, or if you get the mission while defected and between ESCorp and FURI (seeing as you can't join FURI without the spacesuit anyway)

Either way though, it's just to be able to have two ways of getting the suit, a "right" way so to say as you stick with ESCorp and never leave the belt, or as a harder way, whereas you leave the belt and become an interstellar fugitive (like I am at the time of this posting) ... then go get your envelope's pod legs then travel ... meh, spoiler reveals finer points of that

Ok, I'll try to make it all simple.
Both ways require you to have finished the mission Salmeen Part 2.

The first way to get the spacesuit is to get far away from the origin (approximately 90 sectors). Then you get a mission to go and get it from Cilorile.

Second way is only possible if you get kicked from ESCorp. Once that happens, you get a mission to get the Space Suit on Soupaline.

Now, you say that you are planning to join FURI. The second way looks like it involved backtracking to outside and inside the belt, then again outside to join FURI (since you'll need to Space Suit for that). While the first way seems faster, I believe that some people here have a posted about using a wormhole to transport to outside the belt, playing a level there and becoming Interstellar Fugitives, then returning and getting the Suit from Soupaline.

I do not know if this way is the fastest, as it would also depend on your location in regard to the wormholes. I do not know much more about this, so you may want to wait for someone who did it this way to come and reply, or someone with enough time and willing to check it out.
