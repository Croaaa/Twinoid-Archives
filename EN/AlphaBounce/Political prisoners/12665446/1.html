First of all, it's FURI.

FURI:
1- Syntrogenic accelerator – it will give an extra play per day, you stay with 4 besides of 3
2- Saumir's Twinner – gives you an extra ball and the start of each level and new life
3- Asphalt-project – better known as the 4th ball, has a powerful effect of going through several bricks (except thick ferrous bricks where the ball will just give three hits) at once before turning into a normal ball
4- Liberations – 1 square each with 75

ESCorp:
1- Lithium Retrofuser – returns you back to the origin at any time at no cost
2- FORA 7R-Z mines – randomly placed mines that destroys a 3x3 area of bricks when hit
3- AR-SRX missile – better known as the 4th missile, has a power of destroying a 5x5 area
4- More cleanups
5- Pad extensions
