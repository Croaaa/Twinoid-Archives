To get any suit you need to have done the Salmeen the mechanic step 2 missions.

The FURI suit mission triggers automatically if you turn FURI.
The ESCorp suit mission triggers if you are ESCorp and farther than 70 from [0,0].

It is possible to get both: frst trigger the ESCorp one, then turn FURI and trigger the FURI one, and only then complete both.
Not much point for the suit as it is the same one and only suit, but the ESCorp side also gives 20 SHC.

Not to be neglected even you plan on going FURI.
