You can only hold a certain amount of missiles at any given time. To raise your maximum number of missiles, you'll have to find them in their various locations, either randomly, by buying the missile map from a merchant to know precisely where to find them, or check one of the several player-made online guides that provide you the coordinates.

You could catch hundreds of those grey missile capsules, but without bumping up your carrying capacity, you won't benefit much from them.

The missile slot booster thing, once you find one, is visible usually at the very top layer of , inside a green-colored [ ? ] block. You don't wanna let that fall past you without catching it (even if it means letting the ball go by and losing that life) or else you'll have to do that level again.
