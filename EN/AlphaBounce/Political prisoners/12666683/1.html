Hello,

the following rules are written for people who completed the "Star Dust" mission. This mission makes it possible to change the layout of bricks inside a level. An edited level, also called an anomaly, appears as such for everyone; it is not possible to edit for oneself only. Therefore, a proposal for an edited level will become effective only if a moderator approves. For that to happen, you must respect the rules detailed below...

• Be creative! Edit only if you think you can significantly improve the original level, especially if the level is located on a planet or an anti-radar square or contains a mission item.

• The difficulty of the original level must be maintained. In particular, foolish proposals consisting "annoying" bricks put at the top will be immediately denied. Moreover, in general, all bricks must be used in the edited levels, especially as far as special bricks are concerned ([?] brick, minerals, red crosses, dragon heads, balls, molecules, or obstacles like sentinels, guardians, unbreakable bricks, or parasite bricks). However, the number of ferrous or normal bricks may vary for aesthetic purposes. The bottom of an edited layout shall not be too far below for a level inside the Karbonis Belt in particular.
In a level located inside the 7x7 square ranging from [-3][3] to [3][3], the barrier of ferrous bricks must absolutely be kept as is and all other bricks must be put back above this barrier (to prevent beginners from escaping the tiny area they are imprisoned in, until they complete the mission "Drilling License").

• In edited levels, always keep a way for the drilling ball to destroy all regular bricks.

• Exception: Terre level edits do not have to follow the first three rules.

• Any edited level revealing an offensive or unauthorized word or picture shall be systematically denied and its author punished.

• If you suspect there is a level that did not comply to these rules, please report it in that thread (English is allowed there); moderators will take adequate measures.

• Moderators' decisions are not to be discussed.

Any player breaking any of these rules will be punished by a temporary withdrawal of editing rights depending on the offence. There might be no warning.

These rules are subject to change at any time.

The moderation team
