Launcelot

Also go to [-10][-8] which is the nearest anti-radar asteroid field, pretty close to where you are, play that level and upon completing it you will get a message that your radar has been destroyed. But in reality nothing was destroyed because you don't have a radar yet. It's a bug which you can easily exploit and earn your first radar with only a minor cost. You just have to visit a merchant after that, say [-12][-1] or [-5][-3] basically any merchant will do but these 2 are the nearest, who will "fix" your non-existent radar for about 55 minerals. And magically you have a radar! Just make sure you do this before you acquire Ambro-X (an item which you'll get later) otherwise the bug won't work.

After you get this done, avoid getting into those anti-radar fields again because you won't get any more upgrades by doing this, you'll have to visit a merchant to fix your radar and that will only cost you minerals everytime.. so do it the first time only and never again.

Take this map by bubuletz to help you navigate, you can see the fields I'm talking about.
