I don't have the mines which definetelly help clearing a level, nor do I have the 'Lithium retrofuser' to bring me back to the origin in the blink of an eye. On the other hand, I do have the asphalt ball which I really like, the 'Saumir twiner' that will give me 2 balls each time I start a level / life, and I'm really used to it and find it usefull for clearing levels faster or getting rid of death bricks. Just keep in mind that many/most of the advantages people are using as the draw for one faction or the other come very late in th game. I'm ESCorp and have been playing for 933 days. That's 2.5 years... I have the OX-Delta and the Retrofuser, but only one mine as they are incredibly expensive. I'm a long, long way away from the AR-SRX Missile and the final envelope expansion.
