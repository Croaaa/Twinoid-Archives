My liberations reach Samosa now. They can go out further and further the more clean-ups/liberations you do. There is a limit but it grows with each clean-up/liberation. As far as I know neither coordinate goes beyond the number of clean-ups/liberations. So with 55 clean-ups the next would be anywhere between [-55][-55] and [55][55], though the actual limit seams to be more circular.
