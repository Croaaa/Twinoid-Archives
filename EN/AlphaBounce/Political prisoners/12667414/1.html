A long post ahead!

1) To get another mission for ESCorp, you need to do more cleanups. From the top of my head, i guess you have 3 more missions, with the last one assigned at 100th cleanup.

2) Salmeen: maps will help you with that. Search the forum a bit, you'll find them

3) Missile map shows you if a missile is in your "field of vision", on any sector you can currently see (is in your vicinity). Not sure how else to phrase it, sorry.

4) I take it that with "space unit" you mean space suit. Yes, you need it to get out of your pad on most planets, when you land.

5) A waste or not, take a look at the maps. Some landings are worth several hundred to several thousand . You'll also need to land to buy PID map pieces (to get coords of your Terre), which cost approximately 100k .
Plus, some landing spots have transport missions, a great way to earn a bunch of AND

6) You should go back to Nalikors and buy Perforation tools. They're cheapest there. Then do more cleanups (to get Retrofusor mission). Then go circling the belt (for the radar upgrade given by the Tablets).
Later on, you could do 100 cleanups (for the final extension). And, in any case, earn more minerals, buy more drones (up to at least 5 for some levels), do transports, collect pink and blue crystals (on landing), and buy the PID map.
