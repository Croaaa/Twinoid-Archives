[1413387]
All powerups are the same apart from...

A - Attraction is much better in KK AB than here. Get it at all costs.

G - G does the same in KK as here but you get 125 points of every frozen brick broken so is beneficial if there arn't many minerals about (you get 125 point for any frozen brick).

H - Halo acts as the asphalt ball does here.

I - Bad in both games but worse in KK, left means right and visa versa.

J - Same but it doesn't give you a few seconds to click and fire, it fires as soon as you get the powerup. You have to line yourself up right before you collect the powerup.

N - The one that catches everyone out, it makes your pad vibrate left and right making it hard to hit the ball, but it's not a too bad powerup and can be lost by collecting many other powerups such as the common T.

O - Blackhole, You get points for the bricks and the minerals sucked it.

P - Very usefull powerup send out a pink horizontal beam to stop any balls being lost. Useful to do when you need to grab a good powerup, or avoid a bad powerup while keeping your ball in play.

Q - Opens the exit to the level. Can be good or bad, you may not want to leave the level just yet but forced out of it due to a ball needing to be hit close to the exit.

R - Slows down the ball.

S - For a limited time period if you miss the ball it'll bounce right back if you miss it.

T - Slows down the ball for a couple of seconds when you click.

U - Turns every brick into green minerals. However, you get no powerups from any brick and if there are many green minerals it's hard to finish a level as you have no powerups to help you. Also, if there are more valuable minerals (blue and red) it'll turn those to green ones too.

V - Takes one hit from every brick.

X - Turns your ball back to a regular ball.
