If a surface has a blue or pink crystal, you have to get it the first time you land or you lose it forever. For normal surfaces, surface minerals come back after 200 / used. For special surfaces, like ones with crystals, missions, etc., the surface regenerates an amount that is different from the amount available during your first attempt.
