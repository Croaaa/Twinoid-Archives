To answer your question about the drone:

A drone is a little bug creature like thing that gets ejected from your envelope whenever your ball hits the pad if certain bricks are on the level, and depending on the upgrades you have for your drones. I myself have a few drones, and the collector upgrade, which allows my drones to go out and pick up minerals for me whenever there are any on the level (after their other targets have been dismantled of course ... which sucks because a recent level had many many fake targets for the drones and I lost out on a 5x5 brick area of pink minerals )
