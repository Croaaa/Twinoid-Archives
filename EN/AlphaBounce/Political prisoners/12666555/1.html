thanks what is a drone for????
There is a kind of brick known as sentinels. These sentinels are grey with a coloured circle in the middle. Drones are used to turn these sentinels into normal bricks. If you have the collector, drones will also collect minerals for you after all the sentinels are gone. You can also purchase the converter, which transforms all sentinels into green minerals. However, the converter is very expensive, and I do not recommend buying it ever if you do not plan to have a completed profile.
