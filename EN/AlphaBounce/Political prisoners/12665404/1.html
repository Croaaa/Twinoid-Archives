And it is at "move X pixels per frame". The problem is, the default framerate is so high (I think 60fps) that older computers can't run it at the full speed. If, for example, it ran at 30fps instead, their ball would move half as fast.

The same thing happens if you are using up a majority of your processor. If you are using 95% of it, AB doesn't get enough clock cycles to operate at the full 60fps, slowing it down.
