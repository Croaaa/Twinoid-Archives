[None]
A - I'll pick it up if there's no other 'paddle' capsule in effect, or if the one I currently have is pointless (like a T when all the blocks left are normal). I've actually lost balls to A by trying to fire an autojavelin when the ball was too close to my paddle. (the click triggers both things at once)

B - Avoid at all costs. I will lose a ball before risking this powerup, in the early stages of a large board.

C - I love it. I think L and R are the only 'paddle' capsules I'd replace it with, and that depends on the specific board.

D - Avoid, but don't lose a paddle doing it.

E - Collect them whenever reasonable.

F - Nice if there are multihit bricks on the board -- brown conglomerates, teleport bricks, brick generators, etc. Easier to see the ball, too.

G - Hate. Almost never collect it. Will avoid an otherwise useful powerup if I'd be forced to collect a G at the same time. Still, I carry a Glace capsule for certain situations.

H - Usually like it, but on some boards it can be very difficult to hit some of the blocks with H. I've sometimes intentionally missed the ball in order to get rid of H. Could also just overwrite it with a store-bought V/F/G capsule.

I - Avoid. Worth losing the ball to avoid in certain board positions, but OK if forced pick it up if the end of the board is near and I have lives left.

J - Handy, but sometimes I don't have the time to aim it if the ball is moving too quickly.

K - The instant win powerup.

L - A great paddle powerup on most stages.

M - I'll grab it if convenient, but I usually end up losing all but one ball within 10-15 seconds anyway. Part of the reason I'm not too excited about joining FURI -- you mean I get a smaller paddle and start each life with an automatic M? I'll just lose it.

N - Unless the current ball has an important powerup like K or V, an N is always worth collecting if you can reach it before your current ball leaves the screen and kills you. Slow ball = good.

O - Will always grab it if there's nothing important in the outer 3-4 columns of the board.

P - Saves me a few minerals at a shop. No big deal, but will pick it up if I see it.

Q - Always worth grabbing if there's nothing important (minerals, ? blocks) in the middle of the board, unless I'll lose the ball by doing it.

R - Another 'instant win' powerup, it just requires a little more personal effort than K does.

S - Hard to argue with an extra life, even if you'll lose the current one collecting it. See 'N'.

T - Decent if there are a lot of nonstandard bricks, or some minerals that are hard to reach, but otherwise not terribly useful.

V - Almost another 'instant win' capsule. Especially if some Ms show up later.

W - Avoid unless it'll cost me my current life.

X - Always collect if it won't be fatal.

Y - Avoid unless it'll cost me my current life. This one drives me batty.

Z - Avoid unless it'll cost me my current life. On rare occasions I'll pick it up intentionally if there are just a few bricks left on the screen and I have lives to spare.
