I found this the most important things at the beginning:

Get as many extra lifes as possible!

You'll loose all extra ones soon. You can get new ones early on by 1) buying one at a merchant. 2) Finishing the Distress Call mission 3) Buying for real money at the Bank.

Do the missions!

Some people try exploring planets at first. Which doesn't get them anything and also is very hard at the beginning without all those items.


And like YF23 said, read the FAQ.


General advice:

Use maps: The Map or MineMap

And the Global Map for finding items and the cheapest merchants for all stuff. Finding cheap merchants is important, because the prices differ hugely.

The AB Wiki also has lots of useful information.


So for now just follow the missions/FAQ and don't forget getting Salmeen. And once you get the message regarding Moltear and Balixt ask again how to do this most efficiently.
