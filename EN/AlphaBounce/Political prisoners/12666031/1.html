I did this and would certainly do it again, because it finishes two missions (Salmeen and joining FURI) while earning you a few thousand and quite some on the trip plus a missile and a star.

1) Go to Khorlan [180][-190] to return Salmeen. You will get 50 and most importantly the 4th extra .

2) Pick up the alien at [177][-195] he wants to go to Holovan for 3270. Holovan is far, but there's a wormhole nearby.

3) Go to the wormhole at [62][-142] which brings you near Holovan. On Holovan pick up the alien at [-154][108] he wants to go to Nalikors for 4200 and 32.

On your way to Nalikors pick up the missile at [-101][151] and the green star at [-37][176].


On Nalikors join FURI, then return to the origin for the liberation missions. Do 20 of those to enable the Accelerator mission and then travel the belt to collect both the tablets and the scripts on one trip.

Then you'll have 4/day and also a better radar probably the V5 and are well equipped to make the travel to the 3rd ball.
