Hi,

the mission for the spacesuit on Cilorile is for both sides. You need to be able to land and go far enough (~70 sectors away from the origin).

In order to get the mission for the spacesuit on Soupaline, you need to become a fugitive first.

So in conclusion, ESCorp members can only get the first suit, whereas would-be FURI members can have both missions at the same time
