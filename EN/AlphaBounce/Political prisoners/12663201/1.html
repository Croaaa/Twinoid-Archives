A reducine shows up as a brick in the level. If you hit this brick, it will fire a laser at you. If this laser hits, it will shrink your envelope by one size. If you lose a life, your envelope resets to normal size, or you can always grab an 'e' powerup to make it bigger. If you get a drone, it will fly out and start turning the reducine into a regular brick. If you can avoid hitting the reducines until they are all converted into bricks, then you should have no trouble. Also, once the reducines have shrunk your envelope to a certain size, they'll stop firing at you, but of course, it's very difficult to play this way. Also, when they say the envelope will be shrunk until the end of the mission, they only mean until the end of the level. I hope that clears up any questions you have about reducines. I completed the Balixt mission before going to Tiboon or getting most of the things on Moltear, but I think I would have found it easier to have done so. I say, complete the Moltear stuff and save up some money to buy a drone along the way, then Balixt shouldn't be too difficult.
