I've been using Minemap's Merchant list for a while, but I've been overwhelmed by how hard it is to actually use. I mean, the table is too big even for my 1920x1200 display, and looking for merchants near a certain location is just impossible (because you have to scroll back up to find out what a column corresponds to).

So I made my own.

AlphaBounce Merchant Search allows you to find the cheapest items that are close to your location, and lets you search for the top ten closest merchants and their inventory.

If you have any requests for this tool, please don't hesitate to post them
