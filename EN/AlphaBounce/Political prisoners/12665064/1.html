Well, here's the "tips" which may help you to understand the game:

To get to next level one must hit all turtles with the white ball to turn them into 1-point, 5-point, or 20-point balls (green, blue, pink).
It is not necessary to get all points.

Hit turtles on the side to minimize speed loss .
A collision works like in pool: the more frontal, the more speed is transfered to the turtle. A full frontal hit will stop the white ball dead in it's track.

Hit 1-point, 5-point, 20-point, or bonus (yellow) balls on the way to extend range.
A ball collision is like hitting a flipper bumper: the white ball bounces with a boost, regardless of the hit angle.
Note: The boost is insignificant at low speed.

It is better to reach next level than to pick-up all pointsbecause higher levels earn significantly more points.
In particular 1-point balls are worth tool little to waste shots getting them. It is better to leave them littering the playground, increasing the chance of getting them at random, with a speed boost as a bonus.

Use corners and walls
Smartly using them allows to hit a turtle and bounce the resulting ball back to the white ball, getting points and a speed boost in the process.

Learn from the demo
One can get familiar to game behavior by watching the game demo running when not logged in.

Train with the bug
1) Log in and get to where you must click to start game - but don't click yet.
2) Open new windows or tabs on the same location.
3) Start one of the games (no need to complete it right away). This is the real game that will register score.
4) Start the other games. These are fake games with only 2 turtles that will not register. Use them for training.
However, don't let a fake game try to register first: it would register as a 0 point lost game.

Strategies for the death ball (black ball)
-Adjust shot power as needed. Hitting full power raises the risk of inadvertently hitting the death ball.
-Try to send turtles or balls near the death ball, so they form a kind of protection.
These levels require lots of skills, and luck too.

0 points and restart
If one has too little points left after buying a MTPass, one might want to delete account and re-create it to have 3 free games again.
Careful though: I don't know if this is allowed.

Turtles look (or are) slightly bigger than balls.
Don't let that make you miss shots.
