Ok, moneymaker's guide is a little ambiguous in some areas, so I thought I'll post a guide that makes each of the powerups as clear as possible.

A: Attract - You get a green bar in your paddle. Hold down the mouse button to attract or change the direction of your balls. The attraction must recharge after 3-5 seconds.
B: Boost - Adds 2 or 3 (can't remember) to the number of hits needed to break every brick (never seen it)
C: Control - Adds green goo to your paddle which catches balls when they touch your paddle. Click to release all balls.
D: Diminish - Diminishes the size of your by one increment.
E: Expansion - Expands the size of your by one increment
F: Flame, adds 1 to the power of the ball (a 2-hit brick will be broken with a regular ball). Makes a fiery halo around your ball.
G: Glacial - Ice, hit a brick one to freeze it, a second time to break it. Sentinels won't shoot when Frozen. No collected from frozen blocks.
H: Halo - ball can go through bricks while going upwards. When it hits the ceiling, it turns solid again.
I: Indigestion - Adds a brick around every other brick.
J: Javelin - Javelin, destroys a whole column of bricks, molecules, etc. when clicked.
K: Kamikaze - Ball (s) turn red and don't fall down. They hover near the screen and chase down bricks.
L: Laser - click to fire. You shoot 2 lasers per click; they come out of the edges of your paddle.
M: Multiball - duplicates every ball in the game (there is a maximum limit) Slightly changes trajectory.
N: New ball - that's self-explanatory. The new ball appears on your paddle and is launched after 3ish seconds.
O: Opening, All bricks separated from the center, 3 bricks to the outside (leaving a 6-brickwide gap in the middle)
P: Provision, fills up your missiles (e.g. you have 3/11, then take P, then you'll have 11/11)
Q: Quazar, makes the center bricks disappear in a black hole, and others will be lost.
R: Regeneration, creates a new ball on click. Has a little red bar that refills.
S: Safe - Rescue, grants an extra
T: Transformation, click and hold to transform any brick to a regular one.
U: Still unknown
V: Volt - turns your ball into a yellow ball of death. Can't break bricks, but bricks glow with electricity when the ball comes close. When the brick is exposed to enough electricity, it explodes.
W: Whisky, ball(s) get drunk and can change direction when they want. They can arbitrarily change direction and speed.
X: Xanax, slowing effect
Y: Yoyo, ball goes faster when on top of screen, slower when at bottom.
Z: Zeal, acceleration effect
