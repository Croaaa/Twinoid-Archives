Before Earth/PID/Hard Mode update:
A few people high in the rankings have both FURI and ESCorp items. All planets except for last are discovered one after another. In the end, only that one is left.
After update:
Bug fixed, people with items from both sides lose those that belong to the other side and keep those of their own faction. Shortly after the update, the PID map pieces are discovered. In the following days, the high ranking people collect them. It is obvious we are talking about the Earth even before the map is complete. Eventually, Acopis is the first to collect all the pieces and heads to Earth. He is the first to enter Nightmare Mode.

Don't think the PID pieces were found and collected overnight. There was enough time for the bugfix to be noticed.
