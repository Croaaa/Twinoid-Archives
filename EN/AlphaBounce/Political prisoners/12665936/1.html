Please do not bring up old threads.

As for your question, if you mean the controls, then you need hold left click for a little bit to slow down your pad. Once you find a flat surface (or at least until your pad is parallel to the surface if the surface is a bit slanted), your landing pods will come out and you can step out of the planet. However, very few planets have breathable air, so you really can't do anything useful on the other planets unless you have the space suit.

If you already knew all that, then all I can say is practice.
