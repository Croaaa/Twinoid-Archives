Oreocreme, indeed, in levels with lots of sentinels it's a disadvantage, but you'll love it later.

As a tip, when you have two balls and you only want to launch one at first, you can use the sides of the level to only launch one without clicking. Just slowly move to one side until one of the ball goes out of the screen. At that moment, one ball will launch while the other will still be on your envelope.

Just remember that after a while the other ball will launch automatically, but still, this tip might be useful for you. In fact, I use this almost all the time, otherwise there's a pretty big chance you'll lose one of the balls if they both go up at once and come down in different places at once...
