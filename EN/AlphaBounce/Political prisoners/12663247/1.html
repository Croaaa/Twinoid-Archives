[None]
The [ G ] and/or ice capsule will freeze any block allowing you to destroy it with a second hit, including the purple reappearing ones, iron conglomerates and the metal drilling-proofed ones (with the exception of a few ... for example, it won't freeze and obliterate parasite molecule blocks, they're still released once the block breaks). It saved my life a couple times when I encountered the black 'instant death' skull-and-crossbones blocks a while back ... I took them out with ice which prevented them from activating and instantly killing me.

The downside of these capsules is that it takes 2 hits to destroy even the regular bricks and if you freeze and break a mineral block, you won't get the credits for it. But they're indispensible for getting rid of those annoying permanent metallic blocks.
