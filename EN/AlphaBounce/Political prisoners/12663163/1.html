We have received a new transmission from our Headquarters:

• English is the only language allowed in this forum. It is no longer possible to write in any other language even if an English translation of the text is included in the same post.
Change made Aug. 12th due to too many players not following the rule. All threads in other languages will be progressively closed.

• Flood (sending multiple messages with no use to the subject) is forbidden, as well as abuse of bold, italics tags, capital letters and smileys. Topic reviving (answering a topic with no answer for at least a month) should be done only if useful.

• SMS language is forbidden on this forum. Take care of your spelling, don't hesitate to reread yourself several times! Use an spellchecker to reduce the number of your mistakes!

• Advertisement (MSN, blogs, websites, ...) is forbidden. Sites linked to the game are allowed, as well as all other Motion Twin sites, without supporting links. Links to myminicity and mybrute are forbidden. Links to specific Brutes/Minicities and sponsoring links are forbidden as well.
As of May 2011, Minitroopers links are also forbidden.

• Courtesy is compulsory : insults and potentially hurting words or phrases are to be banned.

• It is forbidden to release personal information, and private life about other players. Concerning mail addresses, please do not reveal them on the forum, use your personal page, via "My page" button > "Edit my infos"

• Please answer only to leave an in-topic answer. Avoid being off-topic! Do not answer to someone not following the rules! Please do not use a thread to speak about another one!

• When unveiling information related to the game that may spoil the discovery (object, planet coordinates...), please use a spoiler tag.

• Mod decisions are not to be discussed.

Any player breaking those rules will be punished more or less severely accordingly to the harm done. There might be no warning.

These rules are subject to change at any time.

-- the moderating team.
