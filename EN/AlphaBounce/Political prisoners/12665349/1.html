First of all, you don't need a radar upgrade to upgrade your engine; however, engine upgrades are only really useful for already explored areas if your radar level isn't high enough.

As for radar upgrades, you get one when you bring Salmeen to Moltear and complete a level (which will be marked on your map).
You get another one when you gather all 12 Karbonite Tablets scattered around the Karbonis Belt and bring them to the mechanic on the surface of Nalikors (if you are going for this radar upgrade, make sure you have decided whether you want to go FURI or ESCorp and have done the necessary preparations).
The last one, you get when you complete the PID map, go to the specified location, land, and choose not to restart the game in Hard mode (this one will be much, much later in the game- in fact, it's going to be pretty much at the end of the game).
