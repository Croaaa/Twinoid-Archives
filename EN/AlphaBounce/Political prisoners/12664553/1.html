Actually there is, but you'll need to go to Moltear to get it, and it's really more practical to go to Moltear after collecting the rock sample from Spignysos (because you'll be given a mission to go there anyway)

For the moment, you can go to [-39][-7]. There you will find two blocks containing orange-blue-orange molecules. Break one of them to release the molecule. These molecules destroy bricks, so they will clear out the level for you. You can even let the ball fall off the screen (if you have an extra life left). With the next life, don't launch the ball again. You can just sit back and watch the molecules eat up the level for you.

Form [-39][-7] you can reach [-39][-9], where the rock sample is. This level might require a couple of attempts to get the sample, but it's certainly doable. In any case, you do not want to win thios level! Any time you lose the level, you'll just go back to [-39][-7], so you can immediately try [-39][-9] again. And if you collect the sample and lose, [-39][-7] will allow you to get off Spignisos in only one move.
