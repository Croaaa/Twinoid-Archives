Your radar and engine are two different things.

You radar allows for travel in unexplored space. As you can see on the game there's 3 different shades of blue. The darkest blue is unexplored space. The medium blue is explored space, and the lightest blue is levels you've cleared.

When traveling through explored space, the distance you can travel is dependent on your engine. You have engine 5 so this means you can travel 6 spaces (if you're going completely vertical or horizontal). But remember this is in explored space only.

In this game, you can only travel one space past explored space. However having different radars increase that explored space.

Having no radar like you start with allows you to travel two spaces at a time in explored space. Getting the ambro-x will allow you to travel 3 spaces, and the karbonis tablets will allow 4 spaces (this is where you're at now). There is one more radar upgrade which'll allow you to 5 spaces in unexplored space and you get them from Terre, providing you don't go to nightmare mode.

If you have engine level 3, and radar level 2 (which is an unusual combination to have as most people have a better engine at that point) then your engine will not be limmited by your radar. Level 3 engine allows you to travel 4 spaces and the level 2 radar lights up 3 spaces around your and you can move ahead of those lit up spaces meaning you can move 4 spaces at a time. In this situation, your engine is not limited by your radar.

However, not that you've got a better engine when traveling through unexplored (darkest blue) areas you can still only move 4 spaces at a time.

The only advice I can give you is get to Nalicores and join the FURI (if you haven't already) and then do prisoner freeing missions. At set numbers you'll unlock other missions that'll give you upgrades, the upgrades are a device that'll start you off with 2 balls instead of one, a device that will give you a day, instead of just and you can also get the 4th ball which is awesome

Also, when traveling try stick to explored areas of the map and then you'll be able to use your engine to it's full potential.

Yes the game is expensive however you do not have to pay to play. Just use your free and be patient.
