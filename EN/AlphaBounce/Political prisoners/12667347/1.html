Start from the "south" and move in whichever direction you want (toward the northeast or northwest), it doesn't matter much.

You will do two missions along the way (three to four if you take a detour). They are (in the order i recommend you to do them):
Karbonis' Memory (hidden mission) and Syntrogenic Accelerator, at the same time
Detour for Holovan
Detour for OX-Delta ball
a truly optional detour for the better engine (i am not sure if you took it)

You can find all the info you need on AB The Map (take a look at the search interface on the left; you can zoom in by clicking) and [lien=alphabounce.pbworks.com] AB wiki[/lien] (here's the Mission page).

Hope that help.
