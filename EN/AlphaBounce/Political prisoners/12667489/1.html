These capsules can be used just like you use some of the powerups from the game:

- ice capsule: just like grabbing the G powerup
- fire capsule: just like grabbing the F powerup
- volt capsule (or 'foudre' in french): just like grabbing the V powerup
- black hole (or 'trou noir'): just like grabbing a Q powerup

These capsules are very useful in some of the more difficult levels where there are a lot of sentinels or things like this. Usually I use them only on planets, where I really must clear a level for some reason and can't normally finish it. Otherwise, no sense in wasting them on regular levels.

In order to use them, just pause the game then click on the one you want to use, but be careful, because as I remember, when you click on it the game is unpaused and you'll carry on.
