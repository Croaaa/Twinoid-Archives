You WILL get back up to 3 envelopes, and perhaps even more. You WILL improve your skill at the game. Just because it's a simple breakout game doesn't mean that it's trivially simple. You'll get better at the angle and aiming of the drill, recognizing the best power-ups for the level you're in, avoiding catching bad power-ups, and planning your movements across the galaxy map to either get to an objective (such as a merchant who sells an extra life) or to maximize your collection so that you can buy a nice, permanent improvement to your envelopes.

And, read the FAQ!

Stick with it and you may even find that most levels are a bit boring, and that you welcome the planets where you're being actively shot at as you attempt to clear them.
