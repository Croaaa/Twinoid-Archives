ANS1
Ball changes:

[F]lame: Adds 1 to the power of the ball; i.e. blocks that would normally take 2 hits to break now only need 1 shot.

[G]lacial: Makes it so that any block can be broken, but it takes 2 hits to do so. Effect blocks won't activate, so [G] is a bad level on any levels with an item or , but a good item on levels with blocks like sentinels, guardians or death-blocks.

[H]alo: The ball doesn't hit blocks on the way up, but it does on the way down.

[K]amikaze: The ball is attracted to bricks. Usually results in an automatic win.

[N]ew Ball: A new ball appears on your .

[M]ultiball: All balls onscreen split into 2 balls.

[V]olt: Bricks around the ball are weakened and eventually broken, but the ball itself doesn't break any bricks.

[W]hisky: The ball curves as it travels. Very confusing.

[X]anax: Slows the ball down.

[Y]oyo: The ball is slow at the bottom of the screen and fast at the top.

[Z]eal: Speeds the ball up.
