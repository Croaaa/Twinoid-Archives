The Guardian bricks activate in 2 instances:
1. When your ball hits them.
2. When you fire a missile.

When they are activated, a little core they have at the center comes flying straight to your envelope, and when it hits it the envelope gets destroyed. That is absolutely unavoidable if you are firing a missile, and if your ball hits one, well, you have to see it coming to actually manage to evade.

They are found in random areas in space too, but mostly at the planet Cilorile.
