Ok, I will clarify things now because I can see the level of misunderstanding in this thread has reach an entire new level.

To join the FURI, you need to be able to land AND have a Spacesuit.

Salmeen Part 1 and Part 2 give you the ability to land. This means you need to do them in order to be able to join FURI.

After you finish Salmeen Part 2, since you have left ESCorp already Salmeen will give you a mission for the space suit. You will need to land on Soupaline for this mission. You need to do this in order to be able to join FURI.

After you do these two missions, start heading towards the planet Nalikors, at [66][152]. Only when you get close enough will you get the mission to join FURI.
