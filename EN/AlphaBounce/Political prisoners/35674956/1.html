The Fire capsule is something you have in your inventory that you can use at any time to instantly activate an [F] powerup. You can only have one of each type of capsule at a time, and there are four different types in the game.

And to use capsules, you pause the game (with Esc or P) and select the one you want. The Fire Capsule specifically increases the power of the balls on your screen by 1, so, if for example there are brown bricks that would need 2 hits, they would only need 1 hit to be destroyed. It's not the best bonus, and it lasts only 1 life (as any power-up), but it can help.

The other three capsules that you can buy from the merchant are for the V (volt), G (ice) and Q (void) powerups. These are much more useful than the F powerup, especially on difficult levels on some planets, where there are a metric ton of sentinels. Whenever you start exploring a new planet rich in sentinels is a generally a good idea to have all of them. They can really help on the seemingly impossible levels. I usually don't use them all at once, and only on levels that are really hard.
