everytime the ball hits your pod, a drone will be released. The drones go to sentinels first, and after that to the minerals.
They just need a period of time to disable the sentinel or collect the mineral.
You dont have to do anything, they do all the work for you.

Just one thing, if u lose the ball, then your drones will return to your pod. So you have to launch the drones again.
