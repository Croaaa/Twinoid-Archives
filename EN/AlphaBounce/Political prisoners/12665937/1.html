I have heard that getting all 7 rainbow stars lets you "edit levels". Can someone explain what this means please?
You can rearrange the bricks of a level you are on. A mod must approve the new level layout before it applies to everyone.

Also, what are the P-levels and K-levels referring to on the AB map?
Those are levels that have either the [P]rovision or [K]amikaze powerup.

Finally, what do the Pink Crystals and Documents refer to on the AB map?
The Pink Crystals are for ESCorp to get the 4th missile. The Documents are for FURI to get the extra a day.

1."Editing levels" - when you gatter the 7 stars a button in the bottom left corner will appear and then you can change levels ( you can only change the position of the existing ones ) by the way you like... and then a moderator must complete that level and decide if it is OK .
2. "P"-levels means that there is Provision power-up ( which refills your rockets )
"K"-levels means that there is Kamikadze power-up (the ball destroys the level by itself but sometimes you may die so watch out )
3.The Documents - These Artefacts are special for FURI members and are spread all over "The Karbonis Belt". When you collect all 8 of them you recieve one extra per day
The Pink Crystals - These Crystals are special for ESCorp members when you collect all 5 of them you get the pink rocket ( the 4-th rocket I don't know it's name )
