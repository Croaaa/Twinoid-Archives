When You will pick up new ball, things would definately go better. But as for now - You don't have it, so try something like this:

1. Go to a shop located in [-37][30].
2. Buy Quasar Capsule there and refill your missiles.
3. Do everything You can (without using Quasar Capsule) to beat level [-53][28]. It is quite easy level, because it doesn't have any speeding molecules.
4. Go to level [-53][30] and press the Pause Button (P) at the beginning of level and use Quasar Capsule.
5. After using Quasar Capsule there will be only two molecules (not the speeding ones, that You hate ) and only a few regular bricks left. You shouldn't have any problems with beating this level.
6. Get to level in [-54][32] and concentrate on collecting item from "?" brick. You may even fire all your missiles to get this item.
7. That's it! You don't even have to beat level. I would even advise You to lose this level on purpose so You won't lose additional on going back from that sector.

Remember! You don't need to collect any items (excluding new ball) from Moltear now, because You will have to come back there later to complete Salmeen missions (visit FAQ if You don't know what am I talking about).

Good luck.
