Just go towards Nalikors and you'll eventually receive the:
Life Signs mission. Follow its intructions and you'll get The FURI mission.
Complete it and welcome on board the FURI ship.

Suggestion: Have ~3000 with you to buy the cheapest Perforation Tools on Nalikors [66][155] surface. When you join FURI is the best time to buy it as you'll likely return to Nalikors only after a really long time.
