You will need the Zonkerian Medallion. You obtain it by collecting some pieces, but don't remember exactly where they were, it's been a while since I have obtained it. Think you can easily find the information online.

Anyway, think it's harder to actually get the medallion (which improves mining on Spignosys) then it is to get your current mission done. The trick is to go as little as possible over the planet. You can go around it in such a way that you only need to do one level on Spignosys (which is easy since it has an orange molecule), then from that one you can reach the level you want.
