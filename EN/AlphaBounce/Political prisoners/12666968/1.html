You missed out a couple of things

First, to Moltear again. You have the Medallion, but your friend Salmeen will get you something else from there

Second, you should get MAS-Z missiles. With them, the level won't be a problem. Their location is written in the FAQ

Third, you could also buy Sunglasses. They will help you with the flash molecules. You will encounter them sooner or later, and the Sunglasses are helpful.

And fourth, go to a nearest merchant and restock on you missiles.

And in case you don't know it already, click on the Smiley . It links to The Map, a free Alphabounce map. There you can find the cheapest item, the locations of Stars, Antimatter, Pink Crystals, ... You can even find levels with many near your location
I hope that helps
