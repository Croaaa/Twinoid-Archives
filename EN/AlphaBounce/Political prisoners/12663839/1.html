It is difficult to know exactly what to do if you dont read french, but with some trial and error, i figured it out.

In the clan, you have two options. You can either make ONE attack against another clan, or you can defend one attack from another clan.

Easiest way to describe for me, is to click the SITE link after logging in, then click on the clan.

If you want to attack a clan (which I generally do), just click on "classment" or however its spelt, to show the clan rank, then pick one of the other clans.

You will then see a link on the left that says attaque d clan, or some such...

Clicking that then shows what games you can attack them with. Pick the game of your choice and play away.

Once the game is over, it will ask you to confirm the attack. Click that, and its done.

You can then go back to play the other three of your turns on any game you wish.
