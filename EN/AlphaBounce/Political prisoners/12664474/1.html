Maybe you had just used up your missles. When you have at least 1 missle, while playing a level, it shows how many you have available in the bottom-right of the playfield, right above the help button. a/b, where a = # available, and b = maximum you can carry. (If a = 0, the numbers don't appear at all.)

On your "My Page", available missles are white with a colored tip; used missles are light gray; and missles you can't hold yet are dark gray.

(The color of the tip depends on what type of missles you have.)
