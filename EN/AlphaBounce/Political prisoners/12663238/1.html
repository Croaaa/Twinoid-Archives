If the ball is going in your direction, using A makes the ball turn more vertical. If it's going opposite you, using A makes the ball go down. Making the ball go sideways is useful if the ball is near the top, as you can get lots of combos that way. It's also an extremely good upgrade if you have 4-5 balls on the screen at once. And as mentioned above, it's good for keeping the ball above blocks.
