Extra Lives: At this point in the game, I believe there are two ways you currently can get an extra life:
One: Pay real money to get 's (extra games), and you'll automatically get an extra .
Two: I see you have a merchant map. Check EVERY merchant you can get to, one should sell you an extra ... for a price.

Capsules: Capsules are VERY USEFUL and very easy to use, they can make many levels easy by giving you amazing powerups. The Fire capsule is the equivalent of an activate-at-will Flame [F] powerup, while the yellow Volt capsule is the equivalent of a Volt [V] powerup. To use a capsule, buy it, then during a level, press ESC or P to pause the game and click it at the pause screen (obviously, it is a good idea to make sure the ball isn't going to fall past your pad and kill you while you're doing this).
