Luckily, the way this game is designed, reaching a high ranking does not need to be anyone's priority.

This is the knid of thing that turned me away from browser games such as Travian or Puzzle Pirates. Those games have a very large PvP aspect, and they also allow you to play for free, but pay to get more things done in less time.

Thus, as a "free" player, you don't really stand a chance to defend yourself against attacks by player who regularly pay for better performance.

But Alphabounce is strictly PvE. You're not playing against others. Every single player is only out to beat the challenges the game sets before him. You may get things done faster if you buy from the Bank, but even with just your three free per day, you can succeed at any given task eventually.
