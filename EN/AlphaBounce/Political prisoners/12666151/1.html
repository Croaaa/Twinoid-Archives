Landing engine lets you fly above the surface of the planets.
Landing pods let you land safely on the planet.
Space suit lets you go outside of the if the air is non-breathable.

To get the mission for the space suit, you have to go (70 or 90, cant remember) spaces from Karbonis' center. Just start heading towards [76][-20] (where you pick it up) and the mission will appear along the way.
