Let's see if I can answer some of your questions for you..

missions are those in the green box clean?
how do you know how many I have completed?
The missions are the things in the right side of the game screen when you are on the missions tab (if you are looking at this, then you are on the forum tab). You can see your previous missions completed by looking at the missions page and clicking the "history" button near the bottom.

To tell how many cleanups (the green square things) go to your "my page" tab, and hover over the icon next to your name, the number of cleanups will be shown in the tool-tip.

how do you choose to go with the FURI or theEScorp?
To stay with ESCorp, you continue doing cleanups (the green square missions) until you are allowed to leave the Karbonis belt. which if I remember correctly isn't until your 52nd or 53rd cleanup.

To go to FURI, you just have to leave the belt, and land on the FURI homeworld somewhere to the southeast of the belt.. PLEASE make sure that you do all missions within the belt that will not kick you out first for the easiest and safest way out. (all you should have left is the star, 3rd ball mission, taking salmeen home, and possibly the "return to origin" warning 'mission' which you should ignore when you see it, just don't go out of the belt before you're ready)

which should really more?
If you're asking, which one is better... that's a matter of opinion.

If you stick with ESCorp, you get the 4th missile which can destroy a very large portion of a level, an item that can transport you back to the origin (0,0) at will, a further pad extension upgrade, and some mines you can purchase.

If you choose to go with FURI, you get the 4th ball which has some special properties to it, a 'twinner' which gives you two balls on your pad whenever you come to a new level, or when you respawn after dying, an item that generates a 4th each day. you do however, loose the envelope extension from ESCorp that you get from some mission near the beginning once you leave the belt. You also will NOT get the last extension. In other words, you get reduced to, and stay at the original pad length from when you started the game.

If I missed anything, anyone else care to mention it? ^^

Hope that helped
