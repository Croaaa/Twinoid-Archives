I hadn't reached the landing part of the game at the time I wrote the FAQ, so that's why there's no mention about landing.

Anyway, on the landing screen, there is, of course, the envelope and the planet, but also on the upper right corner a fuel gauge. By clicking the mouse button you activate your envelope's thruster, which slows you down. You need to reach the ground at a slow speed and be aligned with it for the landing legs to extend so you can land.
