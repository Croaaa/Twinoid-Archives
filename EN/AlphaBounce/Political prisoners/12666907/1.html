check "my page" on the top. you have one missile slot. That is, you can hold a maximum of 1 missile. No more.

Press the spacebar to fire it to destroy bricks. As you upgrade your missiles, you can blow up more things. Sometimes you have to hold the space bar to fire the missile.

Replenish missiles by:
-hiting missile blocks in missions and collecting a missile refill, which refills one missile
-buy refills from a merchant. 1 mineral each.
-pick up the [P] upgrade to refill all your missiles for free

find more missile slots around the map.

check the alphabounce wiki and maps for more info.

last thing: keep a tab of where you found your missles so you dont accidentally go back there again
