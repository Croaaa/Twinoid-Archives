I assuming that there's going to be a big influx of new members joining so I thought I'd make this thread to direct new players to resources.

Most importantly, at the top of the forum page are the 'important messages' this is where the FAQ's are located. Read this a couple of times, it will clear up 90% of your problems.

Secondly, the Alphabounce Wiki goes into very fine detail about everything the game has to offer.

And lastly, 'The Map' and 'MineMap' are two maps of the known AB universe. They will show you how many minerals are on zones, where the shops are and even show you what the zone actually looks like!

For now those things will get you going.

If it's ok with the mods, I'd also suggest if you have any basic, quick or beginner questions or on something that you can't quite get your head around, go ahead and ask here. But for anything more detailed, don't be afraid to make a new topic.
