It's more warping your paddle out of the way and then warping it back after the danger is gone. When you pause, you can still move the cursor. If you move the cursor to the other side of the screen and then unpause, your paddle is now instantly in a new position. If you pause and move your paddle as soon as you hit a guardian, the laser will shoot the old position and you'll be safe. If you're close to losing the ball when dodging the laser you can use the same pause technique to move back and catch it before you die.

As you continue in your journey you really should work on honing your skill at dodging them and only use that technique in desperate situations where you're down to one life or there are a lot of guardians in the level. With practice it becomes quite natural to avoid them
