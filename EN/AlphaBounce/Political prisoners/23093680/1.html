You need to use your jet pack to fly back to your envelope. Click to jump, click again while in the air and hold it, that makes you fly. While flying use mouse to navigate. You can always get back to your envelope no matter how high you've landed above the ground. Good luck with landing, sometimes you'll be tight pressed to find a spot just before fuel is gone, like I mentioned in another thread, I did it with 4 meter pods and engine 2 like you, but if I did it again I'd go with 6 meter pods so I wouldn't have to be so picky trying to find a spot and save fuel for more gentle landing.

It just takes practice, but you'll get used to the jet-pack.

In order to get the 6 m landing pods, you have to buy them from a merchant, just like you did in order to get the 4 m landing pods. Easiest way to see the merchants that sell them, go to the overview map and from the "Cheap items" dropdown select the "Special pod extension". It will mark on the map the merchants that sell them the cheapest.

Good luck !
