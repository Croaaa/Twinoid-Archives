How do you get it in the FURI?
You unlock the missions by doing 20 liberations. Liberations are the FURI equivalent of clean-ups, but require you to clean up only 1 spot and each one gives you 75 minerals.

After you unlock it, you have to travel around the Karbonis Belt, and get 8 parchments.

What are the requirments for the syogenic accelator?
As I've already said, after joining FURI, it's doing 20 Liberations and then completing the mission given to you.

What are the other benefits for FURI and what are the requirments for them?
Once you complete the Holovan mission, you get an item that gives you an extra ball at the start of each life.
Once you get the OX Delta ball and finish 100 Liberations, you can get the best ball in the game which passes through 5 or so bricks each time it touches the envelope.
