What are the consequences of doing this missions?

You get the ability to flight over and then land on planets.

Will I be fired from ESCorp?

No.

Will I lose my extra life?

In order to complete part 2, yes.

Do I need Salmeen to get zonkerian medalion and ambro-x?

You need him for the Ambro-X.

What, and in which order I need to do, if i want to get Ambro-X, Zonkerian Medalion, Landing Pods and Space Suit and do Moltear and Balixt missions, without being fired from ESCorp, losing an extra life, and wasting any hydrogen capsules?

I suggest doing this: after you get Salmeen, go to Moltear. Get the Zonkerian Medallion, the Ambro-X, and the OX-Soldier Ball. Then go get the Anti-Matter that is close by. Then go to the Anti-Matter that is south-east of the origin (both coordinates positive). Then move North, once you get to the shop at [17][4], you should have enough minerals to buy one or 2 drones. Then keep moving north, towards Salmeen Part 1 mission. Collect ALL 4 parts. Then move northwest towards the 2 remaining Anti-Matter nuclei, then keep moving northwest or west towards Balixt.

To confirm the coordinates of planets, the shop and the Anti-Matter nuclei, I suggest using this map:
Map haXe
