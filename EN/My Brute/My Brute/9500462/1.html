I have an advice for everyone to read, if you just joined to the game and if your brute is newly to game, if you get sai as your first weapon, don't take any another weapon after sai unless it's some enormously powerful weapon like hammer, whip or shuriken. Sai has a natural impact effect and high possibility of disarming any opponent, therefore is a giant opportunity for beating an opponent even several levels higher than you. If you choose another common weapon over sai, your brute will have to be make a choice between sai and other weapon, and results should be unhappy.
I had sai at first, I would win 10 fights everyday. But then, I chose sword and I reach 10 rarely.
