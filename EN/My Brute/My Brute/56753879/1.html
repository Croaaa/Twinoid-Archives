Each Endurance point equals 6 HP.

Bear decreases Endurance by 6 points, 12 if you have Vitality, 28 if you have Immortality and 32 if you have both.

So if you have Vitality, it reduces a total of 72 points, and remember every brute gets 1.5 HP per level up, so your brute lost 72 HP, but gained 2 (one level it rounds down to 1, the next it rounds up).
