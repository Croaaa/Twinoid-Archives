NidoKing
Technically speaking if you do take over 20% of damage every hit you'll only last 5 hits, which isn't all that special considering the only way to make Resistance usable is to forcefully keep your brute in a glass cannon like state. Overtime the resistance will become obsolete because either (A) You intentionally keep your brute's health low so you can abuse Resistant, in which case you might as well have just increased hp because it takes more than 5 hits to KO a brute with 200+ hp. or (B) You get hp when offered and you get so much hp that Its hard to even USE resistant, I've seen both cases. Great skill just not near the top 10 imo.
