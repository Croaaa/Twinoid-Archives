I thought maybe someone would benefit from this trick I use whenever I stop 1 to 10 exp short of leveling while training.

When training you would normally train your brute if he/she is near leveling up and then you would enter him/her in the tournament after you've either come up short from leveling or leveled up & upgraded your brute. Sometimes ( often for me ) your brute always come up 1 or 2 exp short cause you magically pick perfect counter brutes that stop you dead in you tracks, sucks, don't it? When that happens, don't enter the tournament ! Wait until the server is about to reset ( anytime within the time needed to complete your tournament ) E.G our brute is 25/30 exp from leveling up, wait until the server is about to reset, before it does enter the tournament. The tournament try you get from the server reset will carry over until the tournament you're in currently is over. While waiting for the 59 minutes until next match waiting period to pass, you're able to use the 10 training rounds you get from the server reset to train your brute and level up him/her during the waiting period of yesterdays tournament allowing you to fight THAT tournament at the level you would have been had you been able to level up the previous day.

Maybe it's a bug? You're able to level up and have your brute's current level change during tournament waiting periods.

I wouldn't say this is a bug ... You're just entering the Tournament after you've already trained for that day, so it's going to count for that previous day. The server resets and you are given another tournament to enter and your 10x's to play or 3 deaths.

Day 1 -> Given 1 Tournament and Train of 10x's or 3 Deaths
Day 2 > Given 1 Tournament and Train of 10x's or 3 Deaths

Regardless of when you enter the Tournament you're still given 1 enter per day basically.
