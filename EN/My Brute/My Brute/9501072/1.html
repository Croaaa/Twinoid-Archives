Immortal is -25% str -25% agi and -25% spd, forever. Let's say you have 4 strength now, when you get immortal it becomes 3 strength. Not so bad right? Let's say you have enough +str to get 16 strength, with immortal it would be 12. Let's say you're in brutal legend and you're level 30 and you got enough +str to get 100 strength! Well, since you learned immortal, your str is only 75. It will stack up and eventually what you could have had in stats will make the special a huge liability.

Of course, if you're trying to rank up fast it's good but, once you finally get that last rank...you're SOL
