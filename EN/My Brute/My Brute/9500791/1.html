Hello,

When you win a tournament you get two options
"Same Destiny" and "New Destiny"

If you choose New Destiny you will be offered new choices right from level 1
If you choose Same Destiny you will be offered the same choices until you choose something different from your last set of choices.

e.g. If you chose "+3 Agility" at level 1, "Sword" at level 2, "Herculean Strength" at level 3. Then when you hit level 4 and your choices are "Baton" or "Dog A" and you choose "Dog A". Then your next set of choices are "Feline Agility" or "Knife", let's say you chose Feline Agility. Then you won the tournament and chose same destiny, this can happen... You choose "+3 Agility" at level 1, then "Sword" at level 2, then "Herculean Strength" at level 3. Then this time you choose "Baton" instead of "Dog A". Because you chose Baton your next set of choices will NOT be "Feline Agility" or "Knife", they will be something else.

Hope this helped,
