Hey guys, so I know that not many of you might not be aware so I decided to post about it here.

So basically, it's been announced that Twinoid, and all its games will be gradually closing by the end of 2020.

This is from a post from the Spanish forum that I translated for you all.

It is now impossible to purchase anything from web games.

The games will be available until the end of 2020, coinciding with the cease in support of Flash. After that, they will be closed depending on the user base.

Those who invested money will have time to consume what they bought, but, soon they will make paid benefits available to everyone, so there will be some partial refunds (referring to games that sold weekly or monthly packs such as DinoRPG, Die2nite, and Mush)

Changes will be applied gradually, so not everything will be reflected right away.

MTPasses cannot be purchased, but can still be used.

But most importantly....

You can obtain unlimited from the old payment interface for free.

Please, go and make dozens of brutes and train your current brutes to lv 100, maybe they'll realize that it's not a good idea to let it die like that :p

I will be playing daily until it dies and maybe even later since there are people who are trying to remake the games :0

Good luck to everyone, I made some really good friends in this website over the past 9 years and will miss all of you.
