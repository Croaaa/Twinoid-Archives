coro

This thread is just to let the community discuss additions that would make the game better. It is unlikely that any changes will happen, but this thread will let the admins know exactly what you guys really want! MT loves making new games, so your suggestions may appear in a future game.

Don't make any suggestions that would affect the actual fights (e.g. new skills/weapons, modifications to current skills/weapons), as these kinds of changes may cause compatibility problems
Key:
Free
Fee charged

  • Preview of your next level up choices
  • / Private tournaments (assuming that there's no benefit other than bragging rights)
  • "Master" page that allows others to see the Brutes that you own
  • "Challenge Fight" - Like a normal training fight, but your opponent choices are at a higher level. Usable once per day. Only available if you have no deaths. Winning gives you 5 XP, losing gives you 1 XP and 3 deaths
  • / Sparring matches, where you can choose to fight any Brute you like (no XP gain)
  • Channels for Brute TV
  • Dojos for groups (people in a group can add specific Brutes to the dojo)
    • / Tournaments between two (or more?) dojos
  • Allow a Brute's appearance to be customized
  • "Elite" tournaments - something for the high level Brutes, to allow them to participate in a tournament that doesn't increase their rank nor give XP. Would be split up by specific level ranges; open to all Brutes, regardless of rank
  • A Nexus post when a Brute wins a Tournament
  • / Access to more robust information about one's Brute. Things like general statistics, win/loss records against specific Brutes, destiny trees, etc. (If not free, it should be a permanent upgrade)
  • The ability to pause a fight
