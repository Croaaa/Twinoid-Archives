The time resets at Midnight GMT +1

Thus

GMT +1: 00:00
GMT +0: 23:00
GMT -1: 22:00
GMT -2: 21:00
GMT -3: 20:00
GMT -4: 19:00
GMT -5: 18:00 (Eastern United States 6:00pm)
