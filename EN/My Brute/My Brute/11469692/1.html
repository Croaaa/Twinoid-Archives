Jump in as soon as possible.

The tournaments are kind've based on luck. Sometimes it'll be Lv4 vs. Lv4 or Lv4 vs. Lv12.
The more tournaments you're in, the more chances you have to score some extra XP since you get +1 per round that you win.

If you accidentally win the tournament - No biggie. Just start over with the same destiny (if you liked what items you got per level) or start a new destiny which gives you new options (beware you won't get any of the same choices again).
