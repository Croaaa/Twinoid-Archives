SoItBegins

>Weapons<
 
Whip or Flail: Whip. Definitely. Add 6th Sense, Shock, or Fists of Fury for maximum results.
Leek or Trombone: Trombooone. The Thrumpet is undefeated!
Sai or Axe: Sai. 100% knock rate, and you can block with it.
Knife or Broadsword: Uggghh... I'd choose Knife if your brute is fast (or has Shock, because it often multihits), but Broadsword is better if you have WM or shield.
Noodle Bowl or Shuriken: Noodle Bowl! Higher damage than Shuri. It's just a lot rarer, is all.
Trident or Lance: Trident. More damage, and I think it's a greater knock chance, too.
Sword or Morning Star: Sword. Stacks with WM, can block, is sharp so Lead Skeleton won't null it, and is generally cool.
Scimitar or Hatchet: Ooh, a challenge! Both are excellent weapons. My personal inclination is to go Hatchet— even though it's not stackable with WM, it's speedy and a brute can do some serious damage, with high Str.
Mug or Frying Pan: I have absolutely no experience with either.
Halberd or Fan: Hmm... while I have no experience with the Halberd, I have recently got the fan. It's interesting. Its temporary Agil boost gives some surprising advantages, but its damage is low and stays low. By comparison, what I've seen of the Halberd has it knocked easily and dodged easily as well... I'll go with fan.
Keyboard or Tennis Racket: Again, no experience.
Mammoth Bone or Bumps: Mammoth Bone— it's less powerful, but almost always hits.
Baton or Piou-Piouz: Baton. It's not powerful, but will knock and can keep opponents away from your hide.
Best weapon of ALL: Sai.
Worst weapon of ALL: Hmmm.... I'd say Bumps. It's so inaccurate that you MUST have the right skills, or you'll be hosed.
 
 
>Pets<
 
Wolf or Bear: Hmmm... wolf, because it can multihit and do some damage (pets get taken down quickly at higher levels, so bear's health is less of a benefit).
3 Dogs or Wolf: Wolf.
3 Dogs or Bear: Bear.


>Skills<
 
Herculean Strength, Feline Agility, Lightning Bolt or Vitality: Hmm, tricky choice... Lightning Bolt isn't as useful as the other three. On average, though, I'd say all three are useful in different ways. Perhaps Agility requires more care to use, but they're all good.
Weapon Master or Martial Arts: Martial Arts, under normal circumstances. If you have many sharp weapons, you might be better off with WM.
6th Sense, Hostility or Fists of Fury: 6th Sense first, Fists of Fury second. Hostility doesn't activate enough to be useful unless you have Shock, Sabotage, Martial Arts, etc.
Shield and Counter Attack or Armor and Toughened Skin:
Untouchable or Relentless: Untouchable, though I've no experience with either.
Shock or Sabotage: Sabotage, if your brute is fast. Shock is more dependable, though. Both, if possible.
Bodybuilder or Lead skeleton: Lead Skeleton! Damage reduction is better.
Survival or Ballet Shoes: Survival. Can't be stopped by Net.
Determination or Resistant: Resistant! Nice armor-y effect.
First strike or Reconnaissance: First Strike.
Iron Head or Shock (yes again): Tricky choice! I'll go with Shock, as it's more common and keeps the ninjas away.
Relentless or Determination: I recommend Determination, as you can keep going until you (hopefully) hit.
Thief or Hammer: Hmmm.... tie?
Fierce Brute or Bomb: Fierce Brute. Has greater potential than Bomb.
Net, Cry of the damned or Hypnosis: Hypnosis as a pure anti-pet skill, but Net can also be used with Axe, Fierce Brute, etc.
Tragic Potion or Tamer: Tragic Potion! Unless you have many pets.
17. Flash flood or Fierce Brute (yes again)
Regeneration, Chef, Spy, Saboteur or Backup: Backup if your brute's doing poorly and you want to write off this go-round. Otherwise, Regeneration.
Best Passive Skill: Ohhh... Vitality.
Best Super: Hammer!
 
 
>Misc<
 
Choose stance or choose your brute's hair style: Hairstyle. Definitely.
Best Male armor style (styles): Where you've got the metallic armor with the 'wings' that cover your shoulders and ankles.
Best Female armor style: Long catsuit... or maybe shoes and skirt.
Being #1 Padawan forever or Being Brutal Legend but having to return to level 1 once you get to level 15 and get another ear: Brutal Legend! Legends have been optimized through many iterations. Padawan, you only get one chance to build and stat your brute.
Best Pony (Obligatory): Pinkie Pie 4-EVER!
