Enemy attacks you:

First 6th sense has a chance to proc. If it fails, the enemy hits you.
Then Hostility has a chance to proc. If it succeeds, you strike the enemy.

Of course, if 6th sense procs, there won't be a hit from your enemy, meaning, Hostility won't be triggered.
