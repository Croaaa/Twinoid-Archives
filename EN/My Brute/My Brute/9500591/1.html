Yes, I think there should be some form of reset.

However, it would have to be implemented in a way that wouldn't allow it to be abused by players. There are a number of ways to do this, but here are two quick ideas:

1. Cap the number of times you can reset a Brute to no more than 2.
2. Or... don't cap the number of resets possible, but make Token price go up exponentially each time. Something like 150 for the first reset; 300 for the second, and so on. This will weed out the multi-accounters and keep things legit. Also, it would be another form a revenue for Motion-Twin.

Crappy Destiny after crappy Destiny, and next thing I know, my Brute is a Plaster Puller with ZERO hope of ever winning a tournament. My only option is to keep leveling her up, slowly, and pray for a miracle tournament victory. Or, if this is possible, pay the bulk price and reset her.
