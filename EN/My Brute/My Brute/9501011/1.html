Cripto
Call me crazy but I'd go for increasing my endurance

The halbard is one of the two weapons in the game (the other being the whip) that is able to counter even all other long weapon before they hits you, and is also able to multiple attack. The halbard is probably almost as reliable as the whip at both these things.

All this is well and good, right? But its one disadvantage is what kills it for me and that is its accuracy, thus rendering it's ability to counter and do multiple attacks redundant if it's not able to hit it's target.

Let me not lie, this weapon is great but it needs skills and the right stats to make it work for your brute. It's not as blessed as the flail and the whip that can turn a weak brute into Hercules in one level on their own.

So here's advice. If you're willing to invest in your agility stat, then get the halbard because I've noticed that having a higher agility than your opponent will reduce their chances of dodging.

If you're impatient and you just want a weapon, then pick the halbard because it is admittedly a very good first weapon.

If you're betting on the possibility of getting determination somewhere along the line, then get it and good luck
