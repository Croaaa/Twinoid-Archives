Seriously:

If you pick Vitality, it's the equivalent of getting a boost on your stat endurance (health). Vitality is not an active skill or something like that, it just boosts your HP. Any further modifications on your HP you choose will reflect that, your vitality does not stay constant.

Since you got vitality, and got an increase from that ability, then picked Bear, your HP will go down. Any further HP stat upgrades you get will increase by THAT amount, not by a "vitality skill" amount.
