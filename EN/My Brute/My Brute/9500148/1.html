Counter-Attack : Useless..."
Yeah, I haven't had much success with this either. Pairs best with shield, huh?
"Spy : Good/OK for Training..."
Spy is more than just OK for Training. Thankfully, one of my secondary Brutes is a Spy, so none of my main characters take the loss penalty. Otherwise, it's the first ability I use, daily. Being able to scout your opponents and pick the easiest (or hardest) match is damn near priceless. Especially at higher ranks, surrounded by wolves, bears and flails.
"Regeneration : Get to die 4 times..."
Oh, is that what it does? That's sorta cool, I guess... but I'd still rather have a +stat.
"Anything involving Speed."
I've heard the same thing said about Agility! On a day-to-day basis, my Brutes definitely seem to be more prone to the random extra attack more so than dodging. Ultimately, I think that by themselves these stats are fairly insignificant, but paired with the appropriate weapon or ability, either can shine.
"Well, guess I won't take Ballet Shoes if I get to choose it..."
I dig Ballet Shoes, especially if RNG allows you the first hit.
"First strike is rather useless..."
Depends on the Brute, but I still more than likely woudn't skip it (especially if given the alternative of Agi or Spd).
"Chef"
Yeah, I'm not a fan of Chef. I can see where it might come in handy if your Brute is heavily-armored with low attacking power and you're lucky enough to unlock it. But even then... don't think it's worth the loss penalty and absolutely can't hold a candle to something like Saboteur.
