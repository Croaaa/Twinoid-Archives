My brute don't have weapons at lvl 9 and he didn't even got chance to pick one!

Is that normal?

Well, you see, here's the thing. It's not really a lottery card. A lottery card is a one time draw out of millions/billions/trillions of other lottery cards. It's a tree. And at level 9, you basically pass up 500+ potential other choices that you could have had. Your choices are what led you down the path in your tree that had no weapons. Which I say is special, since that means that out of 250 possibilities of being presented with a weapon, you received none. What I'm saying is, you had as much of a chance of getting a weapon as anyone else. Hell, there was probably a flail somewhere in your 250 chances (I divide it by two since there's always a +stat option every level). Make just one early level different choice on your next same destiny, and the odds of landing an earlier weapon sky rocket. For me, my worst Brute was called perish. It took her over a month to finish the padowan tournament. Now she's consecutively a finalist in the fist of steel tournaments at level 9, level 10, level 11, tied with what used to be my best brute, who was once lightyears ahead of her. She used to have crap, but subtly hidden somewhere in your tree are the amazing things you wish you had. Flail, bear, sword, axe, determination, hammer, leek, panther, resistant, whatever it is that you really like, I guaruntee it is somewhere in your tree. But beware, for you only get so many restarts...be as wise as you can, and I strongly urge you to keep notes
