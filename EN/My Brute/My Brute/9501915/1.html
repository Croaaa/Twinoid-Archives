More specifically, strength vs. speed for pure damage dealing (endurance is independently important, and agility is complicated). Its no secret that strength is better than speed at dealing damage (particularly with martial arts).

So given 10 level-ups, is it ideal to have 10 of them in strength and 0 in speed? Or are there diminishing returns when increasing just strength? So more ideal would be 8/2 or 7/3.

I'm in favor of the diminishing returns argument. It would be true if damage can be directly mapped to Strength * Speed. I don't see why this wouldn't be true, as with 100 strength and 2 speed you're better off increasing your attack rate than your damage per attack.

Am I missing something? Something to consider: 4 speed is certainly not twice as good as 2 speed, but I feel like something similar could hold true, where its good to have high strength small amount of speed just to make the brute not unbearably slow.

Thoughts?
