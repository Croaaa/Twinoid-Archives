Lead Skeleton stacks with armor and other defensive skills. A brute with Iron Head will enjoy having the skill because you'll be taking 30% less damage from a flail while giving your iron head 's 30% shock rate a chance to knock the weapon out. Lead Skeleton makes tough skin a better value to a brute while fighting blunt weapons since they work together to cut damage. Hostility brutes will get extra attacks in versus blunt weapons, counter attack brutes will have extra chances at blocking, recon brutes will be more likely to use their speed if they can survive the first 2 attacks by heavy weapons like axe, flail.
Resistant and Lead Skeleton will work together but you'll need lots of HP to make it worth it. if you're trying to make a brute go @25+ levels i'd surely take both if offered.
