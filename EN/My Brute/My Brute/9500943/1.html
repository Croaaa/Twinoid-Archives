Path for the First fight:
Baton, Dog, Lightning Bolt, +3 Strength

Path for second fight:
Lightning Bolt, Fists of Fury, Scimitar, +3 Endurance, +3 Endurance

So Lightning Bolt if the first thing you're offered upon reaching Level 2. After reaching Level 3 you must have had the choice between +3 Endurance/+3 Strength or +3 Endurance/Baton. Whatever it was you went with endurance and your destiny forked, leaving you with a completely different brute.
