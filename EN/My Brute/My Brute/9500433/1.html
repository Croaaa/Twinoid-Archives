Hm, I've never seen a noodle bowl being as effective as a shuriken in the arena, but then again, I haven't come across many brute that own, or get to use it in battle. Thrown weapons are most effective when it is a brute's only weapon, but the noodle bowl is a very rare weapon, so brutes are mostly given the option to get it at later levels when they have already acquired a few weapons already. By that time it would have lost its advantage, whereas it is more likely for brutes to be offered the shuriken very early on when the brute still has no weapons, so it is at its most effective. This is why it seems as though the shuriken is better, when in actual fact, the noodle bowl is just kinda unlucky to be rarely offered to a brute at a time where it could shine

There is also another thrown weapon which is equally, if not more rare than the noodle bowl. It's the piopio.

The piopio is better than the shuriken because it seemingly has the same multiple attack ratio and is also more powerful.
