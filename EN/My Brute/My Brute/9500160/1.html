Yeah.. when you win a tournament, you can continue to train your brute regularly and level him up. But you won't be able to join another tournament. Only thing you can do in the tournament area is click on the Click Here button and rank up (moving back to level 1 again)

So if you want to continue participating in the tournaments after you win one, you must reset and increase your rank. But it doesn't stop you from leveling your brute further (some might prefer to level up for a while to see what skills will be offered to them so they can decide whether to keep the same destiny or not).
