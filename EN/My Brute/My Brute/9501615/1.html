Every weapon has its good points and bad points. Examples:

Baton: Low damage, but chance of knock/block/counter.
Flail: Awesomely powerful, but blunted by Lead Skeleton and god help you if a brute with Thief comes along.
Bumps: Misses a lot. Needs special skills for proper use; however, high damage.
(Long)sword: High damage; bladed weapon... however, it's heavy.
Knife: Bladed weapon, but low damage. Can multihit.
Shuriken: Low damage, but... &#$@ NINJAS.

And so on. As said above, there are benefits and drawbacks to all paths.
