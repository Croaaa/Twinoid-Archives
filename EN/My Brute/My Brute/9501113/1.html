MadMooney
In very seldom cases it might make sense to chose STAT+ over a special ability.
Example: if your brute is already doing the maximum 10 (or 20) fights in training, an AGI upgrade might make more sense than Regeneration (which gives you 4 deaths instead of 3). AGI helps in a Tournament, too, while Regeneration, simplifying it a bit, only helps in training.
There are other skills that may or may not help your current configuration, and if you have chosen them before in this destiny and if you were not satisfied with the outcome, you can chose the STAT+ to branch within that destiny.
