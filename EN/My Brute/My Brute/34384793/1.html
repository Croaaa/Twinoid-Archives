VOTING
There are 3 options on the table:

  • External polls:
    - Community members nominate brutes and an external poll is put up (via Google, for example). Over a period of time, a public voting process takes place and a new poll is placed once nominations are made.
    - Pros: voting process is easy to moderate.
    - Cons: open for exploits (multiple votes, etc.)
  • Internal voting:
    - Community members nominate brutes and votes are counted manually. All nominated brutes are posted in designated list with their votes, and each is added once it gets enough + votes.
    - Pros: a transparent, constant and updated voting process.
    - Cons: requires frequent, manual editing.
  • Power voting: (+3)
    - Community members nominate brutes and a selected group of members decides which are the best by itself.
    - Pros: quick and efficient.
    - Cons: not a democratic process, selecting members is problematic.

MODERATION
A combination of 3 threads will be deployed, in accordance with the Mod:

  • Sticky thread:
    - Updated only by mod or only with mod's approval.
    - Includes introduction, description and links to 2 normal threads.
  • Lists thread:
    - Updated by a user-group (several users).
    - Includes updated lists, nominations and voting data.
  • Discussion thread:
    - No modding necessary.
    - Includes open discussion, members' nominations, voting posts, etc.
