You know, you don't actually have to watch a fight for the W/L to go through. Wins and losses are decided even before you watch a fight. It doesn't play out as you watch because they are all predetermined.

How do I know? Try this out: on your training screen, don't click to fight a single Brute. Very quickly click all four in the top row. You will know if how many you have won against or lost against if you go back to your Brute's homepage. When you click the "Select" button below a Brute in the training page, all you do is let the game automatically give you the predetermined win/loss as mentioned earlier. So by spam-clicking the "Select" buttons below multiple Brutes in the training page, you can get multiple fights done within a few seconds.
