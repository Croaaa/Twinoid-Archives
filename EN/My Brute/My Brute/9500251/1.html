When you win a tournament you can basicly be "reset" or "reborn" with all your stats start over again like you are level 1?
Yes, when you win a tournament you go to the next rank. with this you are set back to level one with basic stats, no weapons, and no skills.

And your choice is to level the same way again in next rank or get new randoms..?
With ranking up, you can choose "same destiny" or "new destiny". If you like your current level up with your brute before you won, you choose the same destiny. You'll level up and will be able to choose the same stats, weapons, and skills if you want. With new destiny you'll get a randomized tree, meaning all choices are different. Think well before choosing!
