NOTE: You must have a level 1 brute to start posting in this thread

This thread is to post your brutes choices in life so you may reflect on how to change your choices in your next destiny.

- Rules -
1) Do not post unless you are posting your brutes stats or discussing next destiny possibilities after you've won a tournament.
2) Anyone can post when giving advice about someones next destiny but only after that person wins their tournament (i.e. Do not complain about someones recent choice, if you think they should have made a different choice. Talk about that after they win their tournament, so they can do something about it!)
3) No nasty posts (e.g. "You should have picked this, not that, stupid")
4) You may not ask for help on which option to choose when you level up. Go to this thread for that
5) You must state your level, skill choices, what you chose, health, strength, agility, speed for each level up.
(You do not have to do it the exact same was as the example, just make sure you supply all the information. You can say what rank your brute is, what your brutes name is, the link to your brutes page etc.. if you wanted.)
