I had this idea which I thought could be fun...

Someone could create a mybrute account, and make the account public. Everyday each person can only choose one fight for "training." And every time it reaches a new level, we start a new message/poll where people can vote on which skill to take. The poll would be open for 24 hours, at which point the skill with the most votes gets chosen. When tournaments are won, there is also a vote on new/same destiny.

I haven't figured out a way so that we can limit each person only being able to pick one fight a day. Maybe instead of it being open to everyone, there are only a few select few, who can choose fights. This could also solve the problem of someone just choosing a skill without waiting for all of the votes.

It could be a stupid idea, and I realize that it has a lot of holes as to how it could be accomplished, but if you like the idea, or if you have any ideas for rules/guidlines, I'd love to hear them.
