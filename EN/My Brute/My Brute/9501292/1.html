BabyVaso
Having high strength alone won't guarantee you the win, its pretty simple really.

Sure you hit alot harder than everyone else but everyone else also have an advantage against you. Speed brutes will have consecutive turns far more often than you will, which compensates for them having less strength. Agility brutes will attack more times as well as dodge more, which compensates for having less strength. Health brutes will just outlast you with their much higher health, which compensates for their lower strength, now since damage can be easily modified by weapon they don't technically require high strength to do as much damage as you.

It really just comes down to health, speed only brutes with no health will do bad, agility brutes wih no health will do bad, and its no different than strength brutes with no health.

ZombieHero
This is how I see it. The best brutes need two stats: 1 offensive stat and 1 defensive stat. The offensive stats are strength and speed. The defensive stats are endurance and agility. A brute that is all defense but no offense is no good, and vice versa.
For offensive stats, Str> Spd because one is definite while the other is a chance. Likewise, for defense Endurance> Agility. However, note that Agility is also a bit of offense because it improves multi-attack and range damage. So this depends on the build as well.
Of course none of this matters at low levels. Low levels it's all about weapons, pets, and skills. But once at high levels, stats matter.

TLDR :p

Don't go assuming that Strength is the worst stat because your brute lost one fight with a lot of strength.

Your brute's power is determined from it's overall status, meaning that you having a lot of agility could add to your strength's power.

Yes, strength, in my opinion, is the most important for a lot of people, but only because the more strength you have, the more damage you will produce, but you need other stats...

You say you always lose. In order for this statement to be correct, I need to see more fights with your brute.

Yes, your brute was obviously more powerful than your opponent, but here are your differences:

You have much less agility. (Get more.)

The weapons of the two brutes are different. (Perhaps get more weapons.)

The skills. (Get more skills.)

Upon levelup, your choices may be totally random, but remember this.
