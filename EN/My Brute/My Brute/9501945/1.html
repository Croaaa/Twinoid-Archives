Cripto
Advancing through the ranks gives you more experience whilst also giving you numerous opportunities to rectify wrong decisions. By the time you reach brutal legend, you would have a more refined brute than you would have had if you had indefinitely stayed in padawan. The ranks get progressively more difficult, so it demands you to keep changing your brute to make it better in order to 'survive'. It's like a an evolution process in mybrute; The better brutes win the tournaments, and the weaker ones lag behind until they are good enough and brutal legend is essentially the height of their evolution.

On top of the advantages of ranking up, eventually you wouldn't have to any more and you'd be forever levelling up your brute just like the permanent padawans. So patience is the key. It's tempting to just keep level up your brute to see what he is gonna get at high levels, but why not wait so that you can do so in a more reputable rank.
