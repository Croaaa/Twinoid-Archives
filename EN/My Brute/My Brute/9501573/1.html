SoItBegins
never choose baton unless you have shock and 6th sense and shield
I wouldn't be so sure. Baton has a slight built-in chance of knock and counter, and it can keep an enemy with a flail, or somesuch, away from you. Admittedly, it's not the best weapon for high damage, but if your enemy can't land a hit on you, it doesn't really matter.

never choose knife unless you have weapons master, lightning bolt, herculean strength
Knife's chance to multihit actually works based on agility. While it is a lousy weapon to take at a low level, you might want to consider it at a high level if you have high agility.

never choose broadsword unless you have weapons master and shield
Broadsword's main weakness, like Mammoth Bone, is that it won't do too much relative damage at higher levels. However, it does have a chance of block, and its damage can be multiplied by Weapons Master. I'd still avoid it, though, unless you have one of these.

never choose bumps no matter what.
Normally, I'd agree with you. However, when combined with Determination, the bumps becomes absolutely lethal. It doesn't matter if you miss half the time, if you keep swinging until you hit...
