Flash sometimes doesn't honor the zoom settings of the browser.
Seems you zoomed out a bit, the frame around the fight shrinks, but the fight itself does not.

If you're using Windows and a Mouse:
press the control button on your keyboard and hold it, and then try to scroll your mousewheel.

If you are on a notebook without a mouse or not on windows, try your browser's menu, there have to be zoom settings somewhere.
In Firefox it's in View/Zoom, and pressing Control and + or - zooms in and out, too.
