galeru
OK, I admit, my answer is not original, have been said a bunch of times, but - flail is the best.

No weapon is absolutely the best though - under certain circumstances every weapon can be great (obviously) but I'd say flail is the best in terms of strengths to weaknesses ratio, which makes it great in the most number of different circumstances.

IMO axe and whip are closest behind - axe has higher dmg then flail, but flail's high multihit makes up for that, and axe is more prone to dodging: whip has all the special bonuses (multihit, disarm, 6th sense etc.) but very blockable and quickly thrown away (which are big weaknesses, brutes with shield are almost unbeatable) plus lower dmg.
Sword is both blockable and dodgable and it's dmg is not stellar.
Flail is practically unblockable, only weakness is that it can be dodged, but not as easy as axe, halberd, sword?, bumps...

Of course, there are plenty of set-ups that beat flail, but there are plenty more that flail beats.
