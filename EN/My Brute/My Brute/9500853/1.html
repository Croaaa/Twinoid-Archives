Usually there are enough brutes in your level and around it (there's some leeway about being same level), so that's not really an issue, but
- if you are one of the first to enter a new rank, so there are less brutes than necessary in that rank to supply you with enough opponents during training, you will get some opponents from lower ranks
- if your level is so high that there are more than just a few levels between you and the rest, then your luck just ran out. No more training until someone levels up near you. I think this happened to Q.i. once, (ranking) he posted about it somewhere.
