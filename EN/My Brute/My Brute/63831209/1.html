So there are 3 benefits that come from ranking up, but one of the three is pretty irrelevant in most cases.

1. You get to change the options that you took in previous levels, and when you do, you actually discover that in later levels, the options are different. So basically you're going down a new path in your destiny that could possibly provide better weapons, pets or skills. This is probably the best reason to rank up.

2. Being a higher rank means you are closer to being in the rank of Brutal Legend. This is the most active rank along Padawan but has brutes that are generally more optimized than those in lower ranks. You also obtain the suffix legendary that can be displayed in your profile and in forum posts.

If you win a tournament in Brutal Legend, you obtain ears that count how many times your brute has won a Brutal Legend tournament. These also give you trophies ( at 1 ear and at two ears) and the prefix Nero when you obtain 10 ears.

3. You can only fight brutes that are either 9 levels above or below you, so when you're at a high enough level, you start to see less opponents and might even be unable to gain the max amount of EXP per day. When you're in a higher rank, you can challenge brutes that are in lower ranks, thus giving you a wider opponent pool to choose from. Brutes in lower ranks can not challenge brutes in higher ranks. This is probably the least relevant reason since most new players are going to take a long time to actually get to these levels.

I also want to note that since there's been a high drop in activity in the last few years, some tournaments near the mid to high ranks (excluding Brutal Legend) take a very long time to begin, and have a lot of high-level brutes that will almost certainly win. This has made progression in ranks really slow so leveling is pretty common.
