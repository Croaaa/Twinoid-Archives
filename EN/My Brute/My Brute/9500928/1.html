Yes, yet another thread contemplating the usefulness of these two stats.

Here is my observation

Agility doesn't get great defensively until 20+ but i can see no major difference between 20+ and 40+.
With that being said I don't see why people get more than 20-24 agility. The same applies to speed which has no major difference between 20+ and 40+. Agility and Speed in the 10-14 area seems to provide only a 10-20% significant difference in their respective purpose, whereas I see about an 50-80% difference in the 20-24 range (these are estimates). Then when we reach the 40+ agility and speed there doesn't seem to be any major difference, it stays in it's 50-80% effectiveness range. The only real reason to increase your speed or agility any more than 20-24 area is so that you have the edge over another person who is also in the 20-24 region. This is wasteful in my opinion, you could be getting your character better all around, rather than specializing in a particular stat which shows no great benefit after it reaches a certain point.

Am I wrong?
