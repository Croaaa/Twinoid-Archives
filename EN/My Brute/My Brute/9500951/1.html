immortality does not increase your base health by 250%. It increases your base endurance by 250%. Your endurance is not the same number as your health.

Example
If your character was level 1 with 62 health, you had 2 endurance. If you were level 1 with 68 health, you had 3 endurance. If you were level 1 with 74 health, you had 4 endurance. So if you got immortality at level 2 and you have 74 base health, you would only get 250% of 4 endurance, which is 10 endurance (plus your standard 1-2 health per level up) and your level 2 health would be 111 or 112. Do you understand now?
