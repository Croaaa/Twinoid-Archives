Blaze
I had the same problem. Fixed it by refreshing the Shockwave Flash Plugin on Google Chrome
1. Click on the tools icon in the right
2. Go to View Background Pages
3. Click on Plug-in: Shockwave Flash and then click on End Process.
4. Refresh mybrute page with Shift+F5

The Flash Plugin will start automatically and you should be able to watch fights.
