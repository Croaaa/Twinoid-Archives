think of hostility as a 33% to hit the opponent after he is done with his ATTACK RUN. if player Abel (high agility and fist of fury skills with knife out) runs up on Vasey and Vasey has hostility, Abel may hit once, twice, three times, etc. Hostility is triggered at the end of Abel's attack run, just before Abel would normally run back to his side of the screen. Shield and CA would be the best way to win vs a multi-hit brute, but if you're up against a Bodybuilder/axe or flail brute you would be better served by Hostility/Feline Agility where your brute could AVOID the hits, then hostile back an attack. especially if your brute has shock/thief.hostility doesn't work that well with iron head. CA+Shield brutes and armor brutes are about the only brutes that can successfully use iron head as a weapon destroyer (what good is taking a shot via an axe and iron heading the weapon if your brute is dead?).
