So I just started playing this game again and I really do think its a fun little game but by god is the lack of control one of the most rage inducing things ever. I genuinely have no problem if I loose to an opponent whos actually more powerful or more skilled than me but it just makes you want to spit blood when you get your ass handed to you because your Brute started eating lead paint chips while you weren't looking. I've heard no more changes are going to be made to this but I still have to ask, is there any way you could put in a base rule that if you pull out a weapon then at bare minimum your Brute is not physically capable of throwing his weapon away for three turns after he gets it out? It's enough to drive you insane when you know you could have won your match with laughable ease if your Brute had just held onto his weapon a bit longer. Also maybe something about that if you have a weapon then if you don't get it out by your third turn you automatically draw it?
