This would be just a little feature which could make some money for Muxxu. The idea is to have a fortune teller, that can tell you the upcoming destiny of one of your brutes for a small price (5 or 10 tokens or so). The fortune teller would show you what skills you will be offered upon leveling up as well as what skills you will get on the next level depending on which one you pick.


Example: You get your level 5 brute's fortune told

It shows that on Level 6 you will be offered with a Shock skill and Speed + 3.
If you pick a Shock skill, on the next level you will be offered with a Lance and Endurance + 3.
If you pick Speed + 3, on the next level will be offered with a Martial arts skill and Agility + 3.
