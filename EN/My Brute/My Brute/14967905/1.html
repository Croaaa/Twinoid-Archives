Shield increases Block Rate by 45%, Counter Attack by 10%. Shield isn't required for Counter Attack to work, but it is required for it to work to it's full power.
Block rate is the stat that determines how ofter your brutes parrys a strike from your opponent. Shield increases your Block rate by 45% and is reduced when disarmed. Counter Attack increases it by an additional 10% and will automatically hit back whenever your brute parrys. Weapons such as Broadsword, Frying Pan, Sai and Baton also increase Block rate. In addition, some weapons like Whip and Sword are easily parried. Weapons like Flail and Leek will never be blocked.
