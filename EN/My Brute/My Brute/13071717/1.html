This thread was inspired by Dragon King's "Shock,Sabotage, and Iron Head" thread, though I've wanted to discuss this before I thought maybe it'd be a bad idea, that being said moving onto the discussion.Considering the developers abandoned mybrute it's fruitless to discuss this, but for the sake of argument there is quite a few skills/weapons in mybrute that either need a major nerf or rate of appearance should be severely tweek'd. MA is one prime example. People tend to complain about Flail, but as luck would have it both of my brutes receive Resistant after level 10 so it's never been a big issue. People tend to believe that players who have Flail as their only weapon and believe me it is VERY common in the higher levels are people who cheated to get their brutes, this maybe true but for the most part it's doubtful, the problem lies within the overly high chance of receiving the quite arguable the best weapon in the game following that is Sai, Whip, Shuriken.Here's a list of skills/weapons I think should be nerfed and why.Weapons
1. Flail - Its rate of appearance is just way too high, so high that everyone above Furious soul has it, its really ridiculous. Why is Fan/Frying Pan/Trombone/Tennis Racket/Pioz Pioz/Keyboard so damn rare and Flail isn't? These weapons pale in comparison to flail.
2. Sai - People will argue that the weapon is weak and therefore may not have the OP'd nature of flail but it's actually the opposite, unlike Flail, Sai can literally beat any weapon it comes up against therefore ( in the hands of stronger brutes ) is just the ultimate killer if the opponent has no MA. It should either be relieved of its high blocking or should be more rare.
3. Shuriken - People will say that Shuriken isn't great due to you having to have a single weapon brute in order to make full use of this weapon, however, it has been seen numerous times that there are a handful or even moreso of brutes that are Shuriken only and dominate tournaments. Without a disarming skill you are DOOMED against a shuriken brute, the amount of times they are thrown by even the slowest of slow brutes is really questionable for its high appearance rate.Now this list will maybe be a bit long due to the overwhelming amount of common skills are just way too powerful.
Skills
1. Shock - An incredibly common skill that is byfar one of the most potent skills in the game, it renders brutes without MA useless as most of the time each turn on your opponents side will likely result in you dropping your weapon thus drastically lowering your chances of winning.
2. Sabotage - not as rare as shock but rarity should be increased, it is a very questionable move on Motion Twins part to make this a always active skill and not Saboteur, the opposite should've been implemented.
3. Shield - Enough said.
4. MA - Another skill that is just too common and too powerful, this skill alone sometimes make a brute unstoppable when backed up with disarming skills and should be more rare.
5. Untouchable / Relentless - Having these skills almost calls for NO reason to get agility whatsoever, a skill that instantly makes up for a large portion of agility should not even be a skill, however, their rarity are fine.
6. - A more reliable version of relentless but more common, it should be more rare. As you can tell for the most part its just rarity that is the issue. Making countless brutes will not guarantee you certain weapons ( Leek/Trombone/Immortal/Hypnosis ) but on the other hand, it makes it very likely for you to run into these obviously more powerful skills which makes it a backward system.
