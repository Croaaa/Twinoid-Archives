You're level 9 without a weapon. Of course you have martial arts and a bear. But I'd say personally the fan. If my knowledge is correct, it does about 11 damage medium. And has a high change of frequently hitting.
But You can't go wrong with the strength as well. With martial arts and your strength as it is, You probably hit around 19. So if you got more strength, then it would definitely help to raise your attacks. You could probably be hitting around 23 if I'm correct, but to be on the safe side and to be sure that you always have the advantage I'd definitely stick with getting the weapon, even if it hits low, With your strength as it is it will help, And plus with the bear. Even if you only hit a 12 but you hit him again and hit a 11, that's a 23 right there. And if your bear attacks, Then that's about a 15 right there. So the damage is at 38 right now, But with the fan, You are likely to attack again, See the pattern?
Simply put.
-If you choose the +3 strength you will definitely hit high on the opponent every time you get a hit on, unless they have armor or toughened skin. Then it won't be as high.
-If you choose the fan, You will have the chance of hitting multiple times and since you have a bear the damage will add up once you start with your combo of attacking in sync with the bear.
