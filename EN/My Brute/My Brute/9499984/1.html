A few ideas for Motion-Twin regarding the token system that I think would be cool and people would pay for.

1. Full character customization — People are only going to waste so many tokens refreshing potential recruits... but some WILL pay a bulk price for FULL customization. Keep the 5 token refresh and add a 50 (or 100) token option for full customization; just aesthetics and clothing, NOT base stats.

2. Cool DLC stuff! — Put those Flash developers and graphic artists to work! Tokens could be used to purchase additional backgrounds, NEW clothing (with NEW colors), NEW hairstyles, etc. The graphics havent changed in years; add NEW stuff. Freshen up the game!

3. Rewards — Consider token rewards for tournaments.Even if it's just 5 (Semi) or 10 (Final. People will appreciate it. Why not?

4. Gifting — The ability to gift tokens to other characters (friends, girlfriend's Brute, etc).
