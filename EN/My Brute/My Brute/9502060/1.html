So, I'm kinda bored right now and I like numbers/statistics, so I was thinking: "How efficient is my best fighter at winning versus losing?"

Simple! I've been using him for 9 months (about 260 days). He has 1050 victories. Now at 3 wins and 3 deaths per day, you are completely even, given you are just as good as the opponents you fight. That is a 1:1 probability of winning/losing.

To get my ratio, I found out the amount of wins per day: total victories divided by total days (can be estimated 30 days per month). So mine was 1050 victories divided by about 260 days play time which = ~4 wins per day for 3 losses. Divide your calculated wins per day by itself+3 (deaths ending the day) to get your rate of victory. By this, I mean my 4 wins per day divided by the 7 battles total to get a 57% chance of victory (4/7).

57% chance of victory is definitely above a 50% average fighter. How are you guys faring?

If you couldn't understand what I said and want to find your victory rate, use this:

1. Victories / Time (in days) to find victories per day. Should be between 0 and 10.

2. VictoriesPerDay / VictoriesPerDay+3 (if under 7 (VictoriesPerDay+3 cannot be over ten unless you use tokens regularly for hospital visits))

3. This^ gives you your VictoryRate. An average fighter = 50%. <50% is less victorious than peers. >50% is more victorious than peers.
