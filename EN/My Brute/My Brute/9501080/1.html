Out of my 8 brutes only one got Spy. She ended sucking terrible because Spy is a worthless skill for fighting. It's as if she was always one level below normal (if she was level 10, she was only level 9 in power), and she had to give up one death a day. It got to the point where I stopped using it even though I had it, just so she could get more than 2 exp a day. Needless to say, I got rid of it when she finally won her tournament. She's turned into a good brute now.

It's useful if you are an insanely meticulous person with all the time in the world to carefully evaluate each and every brute you want to fight. I can barely muster the motivation to train my brutes for 10 minutes, with Spy it becomes an hour long adventure and that's with skipping fights.

So in short
If you plan on evaluating everyone all the time - Spy is great since it works for all 8 brutes
If you don't plan on it - Spy is literally worthless
