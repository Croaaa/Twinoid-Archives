Cripto
Not really, The ability sabotage would make it useless unless they immediately pull it out and iron head would deem it useless after the first couple of hits.
Well that goes for 100% of the weapons available in this game. No weapon is immune to the ability of sabotage. What I'm saying is obviously once the whip is out, it can oppose just about any weapon or skill that the opponent could have. It can even counter long weapons like the baton, which is something that, from observation, no other weapon could do. A long weapon that has a counter ability like the baton, is not able to counter another long weapon before it hits you, so it's countering ability is nullified when up against another long weapon.

That is also why I say that the whip can just about oppose any weapon or skill. No other weapon can make it less useful.

Also up against the whip i believe the sai would beat it, considering that it has a much better disarming rate.... At least, as far as i have seen.

That comes down to whether or not a brute with a sai is lucky enough to not get hit by the whip's counter ability before the sai could even land the attack. The whip could then also possibly disarm the brute of his sai. That all comes down to luck.

Like I said before, the sai specializes in its ability to disarm the opponent and its multiple attacks ratio. Because it is not a long weapon, it is disadvantaged against other long weapons. The whip doesn't have such weaknesses.

Winning with the sai is basically heavily dependant on the type of weapon or skills your opponent has.
