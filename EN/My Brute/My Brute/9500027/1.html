I also think it would be nice to be able to delete your brute, but for example to have a time requirement for it (like having to put it in retirement for 7 days first).

But.. if you win a tournament, are you able to get a completely new brute (replace the one you used)? If you are, then I think that delete feature isn't really that necessary.

Also, currently you can only hire 2 Brutes for free and the rest have to be bought. Being able to have multiple brutes is a really nice way to keep people from making multiple accounts - so I think it would be nice if you had the ability to unlock more brutes for your team as you play (by getting X number of wins).

For example to be able to recruit a 3rd brute you would need a total of 600 wins. After that, to gain a 4th slot you'd need another 1200 wins for instance, and then finally another 2400. And that could be a limit of how many new brutes you could acquire for free by winning. The rest would have to be bought with coins.

To amass 600 wins with 2 brutes you'd need at least 40 days where you always won 10 times with both brutes (this is very unlikely to happen - so I'd assume to get those 600 wins you'd need 2 or maybe even 3 months which I think is reasonable)

Same goes with 1200, since you'd have 3 brutes which could get you up to 30 wins a day - meaning that you could amass them in... 40 days again providing that you always win 10 times with all 3 brutes - which is even less likely.

The requirement doesn't have to be 600/1200/2400 this is just a pure example of how the feature could work.
