Ranking up will always reset a Brute back to Level 1, allowing the user to either repeat the previous Destiny or choose something new.

— For Brutes that begin very poorly, this is good.
— For serious players that want to explore various branches of the same Destiny tree, this is good.

But for those of you that would prefer to level up unscathed, free to unlock all new abilities and weapons, and without having to reset, I would recommend you remain a Padawan.
