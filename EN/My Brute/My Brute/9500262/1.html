You may be unlucky and were given crappy choices so far... but I think it's rather bad choices you make when selecting brutes to fight and incoherent choise of path when upgrading. Believe me, even with weak brute there is a great chance not to loose every fight, BUT... You have to know what opponents to choose. Try to compare stats and hp (hit/health points) to choose properly - on low levels for example there is a high possibility that brute with high endurance and not basic stats (that means one of them is higher than 4) doesn't have any weapon yet. If hp is lower than 60 it means that the brute has a pet. Remember, that this game isn't based only on luck. Hell a lot depends on how smart you choose when levelling up and what opponents you choose.
