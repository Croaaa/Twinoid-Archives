I've been mostly taking my brutes up til they win a tournament (usually level 10-20 depending on the rank... haven't made it past cheeky slapper yet), and then looking back to see if there is a good fork point where I could change next time (assuming I've found a destiny where the first few levels get me off to a good start).


If there's a fork point I want to explore, then I go ahead and rank up immediately and try that path. If I'm happy with my brute and there's no obvious fork point yet, then I just keep going level-up at the same rank until I get a "cold streak" of crappy level-ups and then go back to try a diff. path on the next one.


That means every time I rank up, I'm usually having to go higher and higher level to find a new fork point. That slows down the process of ranking up.


I guess what I'm wondering is, how do I decide when to stop ranking up and just take a brute and keep leveling him? At some point, ranking up and going through the same early levels over and over seems like it will get boring (I guess that is happening about now for me)... but I had always thought my objective should be to get to Brutal Legend and THEN level up. That seems like it will take years at this rate.


Anyway, I get that it's a matter of personal preference... there's no right answer, depends on your style, blah blah blah... but I guess what I'm asking is for other people's opinions:


How do YOU decide when to stop ranking up a brute and start leveling up at a given rank instead?
