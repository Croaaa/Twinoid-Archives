There are apparently weapon classes though that give a basic description of how the weapon operates, and are empowered by certain skills that raise specialization in that weapon class.

There is Fast, Slow, Heavy, Thrown, Melee
Fast weapons attack more often, and are harder to dodge
Slow weapons attack less often, and are lower your ability to block attacks
Heavy weapons simply seem to classify the weapon for purpose of skill empowerment.
Thrown means the weapon is a projectile, so it can not be countered or first striked
Melee classifies the weapon for a skill empowerment.
