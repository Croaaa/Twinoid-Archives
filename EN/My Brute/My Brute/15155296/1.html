1) How many Brutes can I have?

You can have an unlimited number of Brutes! However, only the first two are free. Each additional Brute costs 100 .


2) Can I delete my Brute(s)?

No. There is no way for users to delete their Brutes. An admin will not entertain any requests to delete a Brute, so do not ask.


3) Can I transfer a Brute between accounts/servers?

No. There is no built-in functionality to transfer a Brute between accounts/servers. An admin will not move your Brute(s), regardless of your reason for requesting the transfer.

In addition, Brutes from version 1 can't be copied over to version 2.


4) I don't like my Brute, what can I do?

There are several things you can do!

Win a Tournament, and pick a new Destiny.If you do not like how your Brute looks, this will not help. Read this thread for more information.Unlink your Muxxu account and start over with a new one.Doing this will reset all your progress in all Muxxu games. It can only be done once. You cannot unlink an account if it's more than 15 days old. To unlink an account, please go to this page.Switch your Twinoid account's languageThis can be done multiple times, but the cooldown period lasts for several months. You will default to the new language in places that are multilingual, so please do not do this if you do not understand more than one language. By switching language, you can register two new Brutes for free. In MyBrute v2, each language has it's own "server", and they do not share any data on Brutes that you own. To change language, go here.Unsubscribe (delete) your Twinoid account and start over with a new one.When you make your deletion request, you will have some time to change your mind. You may make your new account during this time, but please do not use both at the same time. You may submit your deletion request by going here.

5) What does Retiring do?

Retiring a Brute will let you remove a Brute from your active lineup. This feature is mostly useful only for people with a large amount of Brutes.

Retiring a Brute does not let you get more free Brutes.
