Hidden Stats(Source)

Endurance(not to be confused with HP)
Determines the ammount of HP you have (6 HP for every endurance point)
Modified by Vitality(+50% for every endurance powerup) and Immortality(+250% endurance)

Armor
Determines the ammount of damage that will be reduced in contact. Doesn't Affect Thrown weapons(shuriken, Pio-piouz. etc) or Supers(Bomb and MAYBE Hammer)
Modified by Armor(+5 Armor) and Toughened Skin (+2 Armor)

Iniciative
Determines who acts first in combat
Modified by Recon (-200%) First Strike (+200%) and Sabetuer (-100%)

Dodge(not to be confused with Agility)
Determines the posibility of dodge
Modified by Untouchable(30%) and Agility

Block
Determines the posibility to stop and attack
Modified by Shield(45%) Counter-Attack(10%) and some weapons(Sai, Broadsword, etc.)

Counter
Determines the posibility to block an incoming attack with another attack
Modified my 6th Sence (10%) and Long Range weapons (Lance, Trident, Whip, etc.)

Hostility
Determines the posibility to hit after being hit (Isn't affected by Supers and Thrown Weapons
Modified by Hostility(33%?), Leek(100%) and Tennis Racket(100%)

Disarm
Determines the posibility to disarm and opponent
Modified by Shock(50%) Iron Head (30% when hit) and some weapons (Sai, Baton, Whip etc.)

Combo
Determines the posibility to hit more than once in one attack
Modified by Fist of Fury(20%) Agility and Some weapons(Leek, Whip, Fast weapons, etc)
