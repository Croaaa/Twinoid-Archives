Toorc
For those of us who AREN'T lucky to have an Uber early skill/weapon, a good balance of defense and offense is what I've found to help CONSISTENT winning and ranking up.

The best Brute I've ever had has a Shield, which I feel is probably one of the biggest game-changing items there are. Also, Counter Attack gives me lots of swings with my Morning Star that I wouldn't normally get from just waiting for my turn. Martial Arts is very helpful to have as MANY MANY MANY of the Brutes at higher levels have Shock/IH/Thief.

Combo all that with a decent amount of HPs (140 does sound like a very solid amount), Str, and some decent skills, and I feel like you can really make a Brute that's a force to be reckoned with.
