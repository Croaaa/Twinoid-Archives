DanielHow do you know what seed to use?

It doesn't matter. If you want to know how you'll do against an opponent in the real game, you should use 10-15 different seeds (the more the better) and the outcome will give you an idea what your chances to win in the real game are against that opponent.

DanielEverytime I actually fought on mybrute (old version) the outcome was always me losing even though in the simulations I always won... Is this simulator really reliable?

You may have noticed, that you sometimes win and sometimes lose against the same opponent? The outcome always depends on the seed which is chosen by the game randomly. If your brute is stronger than your opponent, there are many more seeds which make you win - but there are still seeds that make you lose sometimes.

The simulator works perfectly.

But it's not a tool to see how you'll fare against an opponent, as the seeds are chosen randomly. It's only a tool to give you an idea what your chances are!
