In your opinion, which is better: Ballet Shoes or Survival?


Ballet Shoes
Advantages:
Good with First Strike
Good with Resistant
Dodges first Sabotage
Overcomes part of First Strike
Brutes tend to use Fierce Brute first


Disadvantages:
Overcome by Determination
Obsolete with high agility
Net, Hammer, and Flash Flood don't care


Survival
Advantages:
Good with Resistant
Guaranteed survival even if hit to death (disregarding glitches)
Great with Tragic Potion and Tamer for comebacks


Disadvantages:
Basically not stacking with things like Ballet Shoes.


It is all very much opinion. I think with optimally high agility that survival is overall better. Guaranteed 6 hits with resistant is my primary reasoning. The hardest argument to overcome is BS+FS yet high agility can compensate.

Opinions, more arguments, comment away!
