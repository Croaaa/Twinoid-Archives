The skill descriptions are a bit strange and misleading. 6th sense provides your brute with a 10% chance of reach hit. This is where your opponent tries to hit your brute, but your brute hits them as they approach and the opponent does not hit you. It's a good skill and it stacks well with weapons that have some reach chance, especially long weapons.
