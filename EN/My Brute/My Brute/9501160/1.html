The fact of the matter is agility increases dodge rate, accuracy, damage of range attacks, and multi hit.
Speed increases rate of consecutive attacks and multihit.
The difference between consecutive attacks and multihit is the former is when you go up to your opponent, hit them, go back, then go up again to hit them. While the latter is when you go up to your opponent, hit them, and hit them again without moving back.
