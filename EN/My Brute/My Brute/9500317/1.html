Because this MyBrute forum is flooding so much right now that I can never sift through all of my unread threads, we need a rule or two to hold back flooding this forum with unnecessary or misplaced MyBrute content. This has been suggested by many other players.

Do not misplace fights.
This has been happening so many times that I lost track of how many times. If you have an interesting or weird fight to show, place it in it's proper location which is the "Post your Fights" thread.

Do not produce questions that have already been answered.
This might as well be called "Use the Search button." There is nothing worse than flooding our forums with previously answered questions. Search for keywords of your question before posting, or at least post your question in the "Ask Questions Here" thread. Even that is better than creating individual threads for already answered topics.

Pertinently title your threads.
There are currently about 3 groups of threads with the same title. Over 98% of the Forum has threads that have a title that has nothing to do with the content in the thread, doesn't form a question, or is very vague. (e.g. "Question about MyBrute")

Failure to follow these regulations will result in a report filed to the French Moderators until we get moderators. A suggested ban is for two days. Post any more time period suggestions you may have.

Have a good game!
