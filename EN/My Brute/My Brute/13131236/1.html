The stat booster will give you an additional +3 of the stat it represents, lets say you have 7. The stat booster raises it by 3 and then an additional .5 of your whole stats, so that would raise it by 5. You'd have 15. Every other Boost, you get 4-5 if it's a +3 boost, 3 if it's a +2 boost and 2 if it's a +1 boost.
