Game FAQ Question 10

Each brute's destiny is determined randomly using a 'tree' of destinies comprising skills, weapons, pets and bonuses.

So, if you select Option A at level 1, you will not get the same propositions at level 2 as you would have had you chosen Option B.

Furthermore, rarely will a skill be offered twice in the same destiny: think carefully before making your choice!

In other words, if your level 1 options are bumps or +3 Strength and you choose bumps as padowan, your next level choices will always be +3 Agility or Dog A (in this example). If you later rank up to Finger Flicker and chose same destiny, if you chose +3 Strength instead of bumps, your level 2 options will be different, let's say they're Bear or +3 Endurance
