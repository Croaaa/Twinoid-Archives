A translation for those needing , such as myself.



Hello Twinoid!

We have important news to announce:

Twinoid and all of Motion Twin's web games will definitively close their doors at the beginning of November 2023.

We know that this news will sadden some of you, however when we ended paid options on our web games, we indicated that they would remain online at least until the end of 2020. deadline has been postponed many times, but there are gradually fewer of you taking advantage of it and keeping the games online is becoming more and more complex to ensure.

Concretely, all the sites of our web games will therefore become inaccessible in the first days of November.
Until then there should be no particular change.

This is an opportunity to highlight the existence of several community projects that have emerged to recreate some of our web games, some of which are gathered within the Eternal Twin project.

We had also made available the sources of a good part of our web games a little over a year ago. You can access it on GitHub.

Finally, we want to thank all the players and we hope to see you again on our other games, current or future!
