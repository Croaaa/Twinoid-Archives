Here is a list of Frequently Asked Questions. It contains questions that aren't in the FAQ but should be, because they are, well, frequently asked. Any further questions should be asked in the Ask Questions Thread.

I have a problem with MyBrute.com!
How can I import my brutes from MyBrute.com?

MyBrute.com and MyBrute.Muxxu.com are two seperate sites. They are not connected in any way. Therefore, you cannot import your brutes from MyBrute.com into this version. If you need support for the old MyBrute, go to the MyBrute support page.

How does a brute move up a rank?
I'm back on Level 1, help!!!!

In order to move up a rank, you must win a tournament. However, once you win the tournament, you must start back on Level 1 having lost all of your weapons, skills, stats, and pets, but on the new rank. If you do not want to restart your level, do not join tournaments.

What is Same Destiny/New Destiny?

When you win a tournament, you are asked to start from the same destiny or from a new destiny. If you choose Same Destiny, you will be offered the same choices upon levelup for each level. (The same from last time.) If you choose New Destiny, your choices will be totally new. For more discussion and debate about the destinies, see this thread.

What does it mean to retire a brute?

If you retire your brute, it means it is no longer part of your team. This means, that it cannot be fought with by you or anyone else. To retire a brute, go to your Team page and click Retired Brutes. You should be able to retire your brute from there.

Why do you have to pay for some things?

Yes, it is a shame that you must pay for some extra options, but Motion Twin needs some money for lunch! If you want to play MyBrute for free, you should play the old version at MyBrute.com Sometimes there are competitions to earn Muxxu Tokens and MTPasses for Muxxu Tokens.

A player has so many more good weapons and skills than me. That's not fair!

The destiny generated is totally random. If there is a Level 1 brute who has a hammer and you are at Level 10 but still don't have a weapon, don't think there is something wrong with the game.

What are deaths?
My brute can't fight anymore! What happened?

After your brute loses a battle in training, it gains one death. After your brute has died three times, you cannot fight anymore until the next day. (GMT+ 1, server time) However, you can take your brute tho the hospital which will cost 15 Muxxu Tokens, and your brute will be able to fight again. Also, if you win ten times in a day, your brute will get tired and you won'tbe able to fight anymore. You can only go to the hospital once a day per brute.

A brute's name is inappropriate, will he or she get deleted?

Alert a moderator right away. Be sure to include the link to the brute in the private message. They will let an administrator know, and the user will be deleted.

Can I talk about stuff unrelated to the game?

If you want to talk about unrelated things, there is a spot where you can do so. Click here to access the General Chat forum. For more information, see the forum rules.

These are awards you can win in MyBrute by accomplishing tasks. Here is a list of all of the awards currently discovered:

How do I delete a brute?
How can I get more than two brutes for free?

Sadly, you cannot delete your brute. However, you can retire it, but this will not make room for new brutes. (You can only have 2 free brutes, and retiring one of them will not allow you to get another free brute.)

How do I change the appearance of my brute?

Unlike in the old MyBrute version, you cannot change the appearence of your brute upon recruitment, and you definitely can't cheat to make it black. (Unlike in MyBrute v1)

Is experience gained in the tournaments?

Yes. You win one experience point per round won by your brute.

Is experience gained when another brute challenges mine in training?

No, experience is only gained when your brute wins or loses something. In other words, experience can't be earned behind your back without you knowing.

Are deaths gained in tournaments?

No, if you lose a round, you just lose a tournament. You can enter a tournament having died 3 times that day.

How powerful is weapon X?
What does skill Y do?

You can see the complete list of weapons here, and the complete list of skills here.

Why can't I see the weapons, skills, and pets on a brute's profile? (Unless it belongs to me.)

If you could see this information, it would give you a huge advantage. There is a skill called Spy that allows you to see this information. However, if you want to use it, you have to gain one death. (Not for each time it is used, but for the whole day.) MyBrute does this because it is very powerful and violates other brute's privacy.

What are points? (Below a Muxxu user's name in the forum.)

Points are calculated based on the value of all the awards you have earned. (Including other Muxxu games.)

Can I have more than one account?
I see a multiaccounter! What shall I do?

Having more than one account for yourself is forbidden, because you could easily cheat in the games. If you are a multiaccounter, delete all except for one of your accounts before the administrators delete all of them. However, if you have siblings who want to play Muxxu or MyBrute, they can have an account, but they cannot share an account with you! This is strictly forbidden as well.

If you come across a multiaccounter, tell a moderator in PM. Be sure to include the name of the player, and all of his other accounts that you know about. The moderator will let an administrator know, who will remove all of the accounts.


Can I change my brute's name?

No, unless you start over with a new account. (And don't forget to delete the old one.)
