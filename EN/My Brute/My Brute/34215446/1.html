Flash Flood/All weapons in game +5 pts (this is just for fun not happening anytime soon :p)

Penalties
Martial Arts/Shield/First Strike are mostly considered must-have skills for any top brute.
-10 pts for lack of each of those skills.
No disarm skills. -10 pts

Any thoughts, suggestions, ideas?
