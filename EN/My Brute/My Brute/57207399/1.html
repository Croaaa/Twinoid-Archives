sendosI still seem to be ignorant of BL and ears. So every time you win a BL tourney, you just get ears and then a level reset, right? I thought I remember hearing someone say you get an xp boost on winning. I guess they meant not winning, but getting to runner up stages and then losing.

Once you win a tournament in BL, you have the option to click on Click here, which will reset your brute to level 1. You only get a severed ear after you do this, so if you win a tournament and continue leveling, you won't get the ear.

You do not get EXP if you win a tournament, you only get 1 EXP per match won in every scenario except when you actually win the final. That means if you get to the final match but lose, you obtain 5 EXP.
