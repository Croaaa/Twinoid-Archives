Hi, so i was thinking, what with the fact that there are so very many new abilities out there, when compared to the original mybrutes, that it would be smart if we all got a list together saying what each one does. To my knowledge, such a list doesn't already exist, but if it does, link me :P. I'll start things off with what i already know:
[i'm including everything, just in case there are some noobies out there]


Herculean Strength: +3 str +50% str
Feline Agility: +3 agi + 50% agi
Lightning bolt: +3 spd + 50% spd
Vitality: +3 end (6 hp each) + 50% end
Immortality: +250% end; -25%/-50% (I don't remember this) str,agi,spd
Weapon Master: +50% dmg for sharp weapons (broadsword, knife, sword, scrimitar(?), halberd(?), hatchet(?))
Martial arts: +?% dmg for fist attacks

6th sense: Offers increased chance (+10%) of hitting your opponent BEFORE they land a hit
Hostility: Offers increased chance (+?%) of hitting back AFTER your opponent lands a hit
Fists of Fury: Increases your chance (+20%) of hitting multiple times in a row per charge
Shield: +45% to block an attack
Armour: reduces the damage you recieve (armor+5)
Toughened Skin: reduces the damage you recieve (armor+2)
Untouchable: Increases chance to dodge (+?%)

Sabatoge: each hit you make will deplete their weapon pool by 1, not counting the weapon they're using
Shock: each hit you make will have a significantly increased chance (+50%) to knock their weapon out of their hand
Bodybuilder: Heavy weapons (in example, long swords, morning star) no longer slow you down
[more missing data]:Increased Accuracy (30%)
Survival: Any hit to your brute while he is above 1 hp will leave him alive, with 1 hp remaining (not sure what'll happen to a brute with exactly 1 hp left)
Lead Skeleton: Any dmg by a blunt weapon (baton, bumps, morning star, axe, any more?) will be reduced by ?%
Ballet Shoes: The first attack against your brute will always miss

Determination: any time you attack and miss, you have a 70%(+70%?) chance to attack again
First strike: +2/200? initiative
Resistant: No hit can do more than 1/5th of your hp (rounded off). This means that at least, depending upon your hp, it will take 5 or 6 hits to kill you
Reconnaissance: +5 spd +150% spd -200 initiative (spd 2 -> 17, 3 ->20, 11 -> 40)
Counter Attack: if your brute blocks an attack, they will get a free attack on the enemy and end their charge
Iron Head: each time your opponent successfully lands a hit (does misses count?), they have a 30% chance to drop their weapon
Thief: (x2) steals your opponent's current weapon

Fierce Brute: (x1) +100% dmg for 1 charge
Tragic Potion: (x1) heals your brute for 16-40hp (or is it percentage based?)
Net: (x1) Randomly throws a net at a target (animals have first priority). If it is an animal, the animal is removed from the fight until a bomb is thrown at it. If it is a brute, they are disabled and cannot block or attack until they are attacked.
Bomb: (x2) Randomly throws a bomb that hits aoe damage to the entire team. 17-25 dmg
Hammer: (x1) An undodgeable move that hits for 60 + 2x the user's base damage, and hits through armor (may hit pets)
Cry of the damned: (x4) Scare 1-4 pets away from the battlefield
Hypnosis: (x1?) Steals all of your enemies' pets indefinitely

Flash flood: (x1?) Removes half of your weapons (rounded down, max 3) to deal their thrown dmg to your opponent in an undodgeable attack. Cannot hit pets.
Tamer: (x1?) Allows you to eat pets to regain their hp. Works on dead pets only, including your own.
Regeneration: Allows up to 4 deaths a day, instead of 3
Chef: (24h, use 1 death) Deals dmg equal to 2%? of your opponent's max hp each time they strike or throw a weapon. (Can be used to counter shurikens?)
Spy: (24h, use 1 death) Gives you a preview of every brute's weapon and ability pool before you select which one to fight
Saboteur: (24h, use 1 death) Remove one random weapon from your opponent once they draw it out
Back up: (24h, use 1 death) Let you help your other higher-levelled brutes to fight.

Regeneration, Chef, Spy, Saboteur, Back up work in trainings only, not in tournaments.

vel
All skills that give percentage bonus apply always when you gain points. The total value is counted something like this: first all fixed bonus values are added and then multiplied by percentage bonus. This means that when you have X strength and herculean str (which is +3 +50%) and you gain another +3 str it is counted this way: (X + 3 + 3) * 150%.
