"...but there's lazy developers too."
I hate using the word lazy, but there certainly are things that have been broken and remain unfixed dating back from MyBrute 1.0 (Fierce Brute Shield animation). Also, with the new Muxxu backbone, and Token system, they could be putting their developers and graphic designers to work and coming up with a plethora of new ideas (beyond the Hospital) to make even more money providing users extra bonus material that isn't directly related to core game functionality (subsequently making us happier). For example, things like additional clothing, colors, accessories; the ability to pay a bulk price (perhaps 100 Tokens) to fully-customize a Brute's appearance; paying a fee to change a name, perhaps. Stuff like that.

— Also, there are English translation errors that still remain unfixed.
— Forums are still missing functionality.
— Would also like to see some tweaks to the code / AI to include: capping Shuriken throws; adjusting the combo rate of low-level wolves; lowering the +initiative of Brutes with (more) pets; increase Chef damage (a lot); inclusion of prompts for receiving MyBrute trophies (and Muxxu achievements).

Having said all this, I love this version of MyBrute. I love that there are no cheaters and/or people spamming for recruits 24/7. And if there still are, they're easily Blacklisted. The Hospital was a great idea and I'm glad they implemented it. The integration with Muxxu is awesome with all the social feeds. For me, this version is a homerun, but has the potential to be a grand slam!

My current rating: 8/10
