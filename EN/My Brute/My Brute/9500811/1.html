Timinator
We're going for the top five weapons overall, not the top weapons "if you have xxx skill". Obviously with certain skill combinations certain weapons will be better than others. But if you have no skills and balanced stats...

1. Flail {Scourge} (high damage and combo rate)
2. Whip (moderate damage with awesome combo/cancel/counter rates and good disarm rate)
3. Stone Hammer {Axe} (ultimate damage and good all-around)
4. Trombone (high damage with great combo/counter rates)
5. Sai (low damage but awesome combo rate and ultimate disarm rate)

Other noteworthy mentions:
Noodle Bowl (long-range thrown, moderate damage with awesome combo rate)
Leek (moderate damage with ultimate combo/counter rates)
Greatsword {Sword/Longsword} (high damage and good all-around)

Award for most annoying weapon to fight against goes to:
Halberd (unbelievably high cancel rate)
This weapon, while even less accurate than Bumps, is nearly impossible to approach at all because of its insanely long reach. I wouldn't consider it a great weapon as it is highly unreliable, but MAN it is SUCH A PAIN to fight against.
