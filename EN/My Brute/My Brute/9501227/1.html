I will be posting this thread to suggest ways to make things more fair. Mostly trying to remove extreme luck things (bears at low levels, baton+shield+high speed or countering skill, etc.)

1. Level requirements on pets other than dogs. A low level shouldn't be allowed to have a bear.
2. Pets will take away hp from you equivalent to their own health. Example: Dog has 20 some hp, you lose 20 some hp from your bar. So this means if you get a bear, you'll be losing more hp than if it was a dog.
3. Shield counts as a weapon and can be broken if damage is high enough. Sharp weapons do extra damage on shields. Heavy weapons deal recoil damage to shield wielder when a heavy weapon is blocked.
4. Weapons can be used to block but less likely than a dodge and has a chance of breaking depending on attack from other brute. Example: Baton can be used to block but if an Axe is used then chance of Baton breaking is higher than a non sharp weapon striking it.
5. Long range weapons will lose out on at least one stat area. Example: Shurikens have high attack rate but do little damage.
6. Strength should affect fast weapons(knife, leek, etc.) at a much slower rate.

I will be adding more to this list if I can think of more(I know there are plenty out there that needs fixed still but few are coming to mind right now).

The Dog
The best friend of the gross. Acquiring a dog reduces the endurance of its gross 2 points or 12 points, 18 if you have the competence Vitality, 42 if you have the competence immortal, and if you have 48 + Vitality immortal.

The dog is useful mainly to a low gross.
After ten levels devienent damage its ridiculous and it gets killed by a single stroke of almost anything.
His worst enemy: Bomb that can easily 3 killed at once.

There are actually three different dogs called "Doggy", "Dog B" and "Chien C.", corresponding to the three dogs that you can have a maximum. They can be acquired in any order.
They do not seem to be really different, several theories about them roam the streets.

Panther
Lightning fast. Acquire Panther reduces the endurance of its gross 6 points, 36 points, 54 if you have the competence Vitality, 90 if you have the competence immortal, and if you have 135 + Vitality Immortal .


Very fast and agile, it is typically combos of moves in ten damage, often by linking the attacks.
It is not too tough with at most 27 points, but she dodges a lot.

The panther is a solid, useful at any level.
It is vulnerable to bomb it can not avoid and whose two shots will kill probably, and features cons and cons-attack that may inflict more damage than assaults suffered.

Despite its resemblance to a wolf, this animal is well-named "Panther" in its acquisition.

The panther is more effective than the bear, because it often carries a combo of three shots and resumes immediately after a 2nd combo, while the Bears hit with an attack rate much slower.

The Bear
The bestial nag. Acquire Bear reduces the endurance of his gross 8 points, 48 points, 72 if you have the competence Vitality, 168 if you have the competence immortal, and if you have 192 Vitality + Immortal.


Slow and very powerful, it deals blows to the unit between 20 and 35 damage.
It is very durable and has between 106 and 108 points, but very little dodges.

The bear is far more powerful than the raw low-level, allowing it to easily overcome higher raw 5 levels.
One level from 10 to 15 raw speed of take over, and from there, it will often fighting ended without attacking once, using just punching bag.
