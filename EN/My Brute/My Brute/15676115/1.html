Pets are really great for the most part. You can look at them in a few ways: each can count as "multiple ballet shoes" because they can dodge and absorb hits coming towards them, which, of course, do not come your brute's way. They can give massive amounts of damage, mostly from wolf and bear. Sure, they drop your brute's health and make him personally more vulnerable, but I'd say its never a bad thing to accept a wolf/bear when you're level 15 or under. From my experience having wolves and bears, they help you win many more fights per day.
