I decided to log back into my old French account that I made before the English Muxxu opened to check out what happens after one becomes a Brutal Legend. From what I've seen there and found on their forums, they have something that is called a Cropped Ear (see image below).

After one achieves Brutal Legend ranking, they may still compete in tournaments and acquire a Cropped Ear and reset their brute again. Not only does one get a Muxxu trophy for this, but I also found a Muxxu trophy for getting three Cropped Ears (see image below).

I suppose this would allow one to keep testing out different paths for as long as they wanted, but eventually I am sure one would like to settle upon a path and start leveling up. It would nice to have the x3 trophy, but after that I'm not so sure I'd keep participating. I guess it would be useful for brutes that I feel have become weak as they've leveled up in comparison to other brutes. This means we'll always have the opportunity to start fresh.
