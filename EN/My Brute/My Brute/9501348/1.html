wangthu
You can't be superman at everything but if you are weak in an area it's a problem.

It's senseless to talk about skills like it's a category since they all do different stuff but obviously the skills are best overall at improvement.

So what you need is:

1. deal damage
2. survive damage
3. dodge
4. counterattack and block
5. survive pets

If you can't deal damage then your guy will always lose, that's all there is to it. But there's lots of ways to achieve that.

If you don't have at least 100 hp then you can't survive a typical brute barrage so you will lose at least half the time no matter how much damage you can deal, even with armor.

Dodge can be some help but it's not reliable. For every fight where a guy dodges 9 times in a row I've fought a high dodge guy and toasted him many times, sometimes with zero dodges. Plus my dopy low agility guy still seems to dodge quite a lot sometimes.

Counterattack/block I like a lot but again it is no guarantee of anything. I get a lot of blocks but then will go whole fights with nothing.

So the moral is you can't really rely on one thing. Every SUPER AWESOME COMBO some guy posts I have also seen fail very badly. So you want to have a little of everything. So if your dodge isn' working out you still deal enough damage to win most fights, and if you don't pull your super awesome weapon you have enough ability to take damage a while without getting toasted.
