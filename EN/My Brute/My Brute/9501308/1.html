How to make an exceptional brute.

Picking the right brute.

First thing to consider is which type of brute would assure you that every time you restart at level 1, you will reach at last level 3 before dying 3 times. They're are 3 different types.

Tank - 72 endurance
Balanced - 68 endurance
Rogue - 62 endurance

When choosing balanced or rogue type brutes I recommend that you make sure they have 3 speed ( for balanced ) or 3 ~ 4 speed for rogue. Speed is the most crucial stat under level 5 as most brutes will still be very similar.

Agility + 4 brutes are not recommended.

Choosing a build.

Deciding a build, as you know level offerings are for the most part "random" but you can still try to stir your brute in your desired direction. Here's a few things I recommend you should keep an eye on.

1. Having high strength, agility, speed, endurance, everything else is abysmal. Simply being much stronger, faster, or more evasive than your opponent won't guarantee you a win

2. A brute with too many skills - Yes it is good to have a brute with as many skills as possible to best assure a likely chance at victory but its wise to keep track on which stat your brute boast in and choose skills accordingly based on that brutes build E.G A low health brute will not benefit from lead skeleton as they are likely to die from multiple blunt force attacks anyway( same with thick skin ) Learning the skill "Body Builder" This skill is misunderstood from some, "it increases the speed of all heavy weapons by 25%" that being said a brute that is slow are not likely to see the effects of this skill. Reconnaissance is a skill that can very easily turn the tide in battle of the opponent's speed is 20+ lower than yours, however, a brute whose speed is fairly low or health is low will do more harm to themselves than they would to their opponents having this skill.

Every brute should get at least 1 of these skills Net, Hypnosis, or Cry of the damned.
See http://muxxu.com/forum/thread/790606/?pa ge=1

3. Having too many weapons - Some players fall victim to this, choosing weapons over stat each time a weapon appears up ( with the exception of bumps & keyboard ) is a bad way to come about building a brute. Granted it is a very effective counter to disarming brutes E.G sabatuer, sabotage, shock, thief & iron head. Besides "shock", the other skills are fairly rare in comparison to common skills such as 6th sense, and lightning bolt. Here is a few weapons I think should be ignored unless the necessary skills are present prior to obtaining these weapons.

Knife - Weapon mastery ~ Sabotage
Broadsword - Weapon mastery ~ Shield ~ 6th sense ~ Counter attack ~ Hostility ~ Iron head
Baton - Shield ~ 6th sense ~ Counter Attack ~ Hostility ~ Iron head
Mammoth Bone - 6th sense ~ BodyBuilder ~
Bumps - 6th sense ~ BodyBuilder ~ Determination ~ Feline agility ( or high agility )
Fan - Fist of fury ~ Sabotage ~ Herculean strength ( or high strength )
Keyboard - Sabotage + Fist of fury

1 Weapon every 4 levels seems ideal ( with the exception of uber weapons )
See http://muxxu.com/forum/thread/859003/?pa ge=1

Training & Tournaments.

A few tips on increasing the likelihood of victory in battles.

Comparing their stats to their level
To lessen possibility of occurring a weapon/skill buffed brute its a good idea to pay closer attention to their stats and try to guesstimate how many of his/her levels were used on weapons & skills E.G

FlowerPedal
Level 10
Endurance 102
Strength 24
Agility 3
Speed 12

DarkerThanBlack
Level 10
Endurance 56
Strength 10
Agility 2
Speed 1

We can conclude that FlowerPedal used more levels up on stats based off her high strength & Darker used more on skills seeing that his stats are so average. Also, brutes with health lower or very close to his/her stating endurance is very likely to have a pet. Brutes with stats at 1 is likely to have armor, more than 1 stat being 1 is likely to have immortal.

Joining tournaments
Tournaments are quite tricky, luck is a prime factor in who reigns victorious. Keep in mind that the most hardcore of my-brute players will login as soon as the server resets, this will make winning a tournament unlikely.
I normally enter tournaments around 5am server time.

New destiny, Same destiny, or Forked destiny?

Winning tournaments is a rewarding accomplishment because you're THAT much closer to reaching brutal legends, or cropped ear (eww) but this is where some people often make it more difficult to win the next tournament ( I learned the hard way ) Bare in mind that winning Padawan, Finger Flicker, Plaster Puller, Cheeky Slapper & Fist Of Steel isn't something to celebrate as you're still in the training stages. Take these times to experiment with your brute and see how they fair against higher level brutes of that same rank before moving on.

Some players think their brutes are the best in the world because he/she won their tournament at a fairly low level 5 ~ 10 ( relying on a golden weapon ) and then immediately choose same destiny to have the same probability at victory the next time, and the next. This is a bad strategy, they are plenty of strong padawans and Finger flickers that you can test your brute against before moving on, sure this may make him/her rank up slowly but you have to think ahead. There are brutes at high levels ( 15 ~ 20+ ) in low ranks E.G Plaster Puller & Cheeky Slapper because they grew over confident of their brutes at lower levels and decided to stick with the same ol tree, relying on their golden weapon or skill and then found themselves in the later levels lacking.

New Destiny
If you find your brute having to wait until, say, level 10 ~ 15 to start doing some real damage to opposing brutes, even if you receive martial artist on top of your already high strength brute or determination on your heavy damage weapon brute you may want to consider going a new destiny since you're losing allot of valuable exp earning 4 ~ 6 exp every day.

Same Destiny
If your brute dominates all else from gaining multiple higher tier skills or weapons you should consider going same destiny.

Forked Destiny
If your brute received a great weapon or skill at a low level that's awesome ! But their later levels are quite average in contrast, take that time to fork your destiny immediately prior to the level up receiving the great weapon or skill, you may find yourself making a uber brute. If your brute again fails to get better, or get worse you should consider going the same destiny again but this time forking your destiny a level or 2 later than you forked it the previous time and hope for the best ^ ^

Feel free to add on if I let something out ^ ^
