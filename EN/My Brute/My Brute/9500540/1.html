ZodiacN64
I don't think some of the players have the right idea of what "First Strike" means...

First of all, this game is completely random. So having more "Speed" or having "First Strike" does not mean that you will be the first one to attack. Every player has a small percentage of "first strikes" or "counter attacks." But if your player has the "First Strike" skill, that skill means you have a greater chance to strike the opponent first when it is THEIR turn to attack. If you have the skill "Counter Attack", then that will increase your chances of hitting your opponent RIGHT after they strike you. The more "speed" your player has then the more turns your player gets to attack in each round.

I hope that clears things up...
On july 7, 2011
ZodiacN64
First Strike Skill = Hitting your opponents who are charging you during their turn, which makes them run back to their stance position.

Counter Attack Skill = Hitting your opponents during their turn right after they hit you, which makes them run back to their stance position (which works great during combo attacks).

If you do not have either skill, you will STILL be able to "first strike" or "counter attack" but the frequency is less. And if you have either skill, then that will "increase" your chance to activate that method of attack, but having that skill does NOT guarantee each time it will happen when you each take turns attacking.
