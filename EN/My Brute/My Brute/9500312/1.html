I see you're trying to build a monk.
Except Vitality (good) and strength and endurance (good only if combined with vitality), your brute doesn't have anything noteworthy. (I'm not talking about weapons but skills)

Vitality is good but actually quite common. And STR and END are very common.

So my advice would be : keep the same destiny, and fork (by making a new choice) as soon as possible, in order to study the possibilities of your current destiny tree. If you end up with nothing cool this time, you can pick new destiny.
In fact ... you could already.
So do what you want, in my opinion it won't be much worse than your current character.
