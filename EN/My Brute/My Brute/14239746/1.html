Aardvark

In general I´d put pets above stats I have to say. At start panther and bear are great, both for soaking up damage and attacking, so would certainly take either. Dogs are a bit useless, but on balance I´d probably take one.
At higher levels, I might not take a bear or panther due to the large endurance loss, but I´d definitely take a dog later on. You only lose around 10 endurance, and the dog can soak up a massive amount of damage for you eg: fierce brute multi hit flail, hammer etc. Even if it just soaks up one hit, by level 20 or so there´s a good chance that one hit was still fairly substantial and certainly more than 10. And it can also waste your opponents net, which could otherwise be used to pin you down for a big hit. For me that has to be worth more than a +3 strength, agility or speed, and certainly worth more than +3 endurance.
The number of times the dog is bombed, scared off or hypnotised will get less as you go up in levels, as will more than likely have taken a fatal blow before your opponent gets round to using any of them.
