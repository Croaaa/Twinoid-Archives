Fun weapons.
Noodle bowl - Average damage, 3-5 hits most of the time
Piou Piou - Low/Average damage, many hits
Leek - Low damage, always multiple hits, with built in 100% Hostility, unblockable
Mug - Low damage (except when having martial arts), very high chance of multiple hits
Keyboard - Low/Average damage, chance of multiple hits
Trombone - Average/High damage, chance of multiple hits, counter and block
Fan - Low damage, very many hits, improves your agility when wearing it (invisible)
Frying pan - Average/High damage, hich chance to block
Mammoth bone - Average damage, chance of counter
Racket - Low damage, chance of multiple hits, built-in 100% hostility

Knife - Low damage, multiple hits, little chance to block
Broadsword - Average damage, chance to knock out weapons, chance to block, counter and with a built-in hostility
Hatchet - Average/High damage, chance of multiple hits
Scimitar - Average damage, chance of multiple hits, chance of block
Sai - Low damage, 100% knock, chance to block.
Medium range weapons.
Trombone - See Fun weapons
Morning star - Average/High damage, easy to evade, chance of multiple hits
Sword - High damage, chance of multiple hits, chance of counter, easy to block and evade
Long range weapons.
Trident - Average damage, high chance of counter, chance to knock out weapons
Halberd - High damage, high chance of counter, easy to evade
Lance - Average damage, similar but weaker to Trident
Whip - Average damage, high chance of multiple hits, chance to knock out weapons
Baton - Low damage, chance of knock and block, high chance of counter
Heavy weapons.
Bumps - High damage, chance of counter, easy to evade
Halberd - See long range weapons
Sword - See medium range weapons
Flail - High damage, chance of multiple hits, unblockable
Axe - Very high damage, easy to evade, chance of counter.
Throwing weapons. (Throwing weapons are influenced by agility, not by strength)
Shuriken - Low damage, very many hits
Piou Piou - See Fun weapons
Noodle Bowl - See Fun weapons
