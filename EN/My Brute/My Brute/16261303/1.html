This would make it so that everyone would be evenly matched and so matches would remain hectic, fun, and most of all, never 1 sided because one brute or some brutes got lucky enough to say, Sai, Flail, Counter, Shield which you'd seem many BLs having. Each class of brute have their own advantages and disadvantages making choosing an opponent a bit more strategic rather than bullying black listed bad brutes *cough* clay9.

Warrior brutes
AdvantageHave an advantage because weapons are very likely to appear when they level up, thus making obtaining OP'd weapon combinations such as flail, scimitar, sai, trombone etc much more likely because they're always getting weapon options.Disavantage
Because weapons appear so often, they tend to rely mainly on high tier weapons to dish out great deals of damage and overcome their opponents.Specialist Brutes
AdvantageFor the most part specialist brutes are the most neutral of the groups, thus making their build diverse allowing them to branch into sub versions of other classes or even mixing other classes.
Disvantage
Because they have no set build they can often become good at everything, but great at nothing, would could allow others to exploit their jack of all trades nature.
Street Fighter Brutes
AdvantageBecause their main source of damage output is based on their hand combat they have the most consistant and reliable damage, being able to down the competition without fear of being hindered by disarm abilities.
Disvantage
Because they'll often be unarmed it allows for others to exploit their lack of range by using long ranged weapons to parry and counter.
Brutal Knight Brutes
AdvantageBrutal Knights are more than naught, similar to specialist brutes but with a much higher priority on endurance. Because their not so much as focused on any main type of build via weapons, skills, stats it allows them to branch into other classes while still maintaining the tankibility to outlast their opponents.
Disvantage
Because endurance is such a huge focus with brutal knights it makes them weak, and very vulnerable to heavy weapons making their endurance haxing nature a double edged sword.
Ninja Brutes
Advantage Ninja brutes are able to exploit those who are very low speed and are reliant on counters such as Halberd, 6th Sense, CA, Iron head, etc. With reliable consistent damage and a uber high multi attack rate they can be a nuisance to everyone. Or even, a troll.
Disvantage
Because their main weapons are throwing weapons such as Pioz Pioz, noodle bowl, and shuriken, it leaves little to improve on. Their extremely vulnerable to shock.
Just a little input in case someone thinks any one class of brute may be overpowered.
