The more I play this game, the more I realize that the game developers did not apply logic toward this fighting game. As example, once a Brute has earned a weapon, that weapon (or random weapon if more than one) should be drawn to use during the first attack of the fight, always. To randomly start each fight with Brutes using fists is ridiculous. No one would logically fight without a weapon, if one is available. This doesn't mean that the best weapon of the inventory must be drawn first, it could be a random coded command, but to not draw a weapon for the first attack run doesn't make sense.

Same thing with weapons being thrown away and leaving the Brute weaponless, when it is the last or only weapon that a Brute possesses. If it is the last weapon, then the game should be coded so that the weapon is never thrown away by the Brute, but used until it is knocked out of their hands (Iron Head or Shock by opponent), as it is illogical for a Brute to throw away a weapon and then be stuck bare-fisted.

If this change can't be coded in, then make a new skill called "Strategy" so that the Brute does this, as there is very little strategy and logic in this poorly coded game.

Scrimshaw

A fighting game normally runs down the line of weapons or no weapons. For the games with weapons - you choose your weapons and then employ them. This is the only fighting game (I know of) that makes your weapons a random decision and may even have the fighter not use them, even if it would have been to the fighter's advantage. Regardless the other things (yes, a leek is a silly weapon), the fact that items coded as "weapons" are only randomly pulled out and thrown at the opponent with no apparent pattern or strategy, but instead based on totally random code, is what makes this game very flawed. You should be able to assign priority to your weapons, or something other than the randomness that this game forces one into. It is so random that it loses value regarding strategy, as you may as well be staring at a slot machine in Vegas rather than watching your Brute fight - both are way too random, with little control from the player.

Don't confuse this as me being entirely against the game - it has potential, but it needs a coding update to improve just what I stated. And - if a player wanted to fight barefisted, always, then never select a weapon on level up.
