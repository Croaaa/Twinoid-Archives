I too have the same theory for a while now.

Some of my brutes get many of the same choices when I choose the other path...
But also some dont... It may all be a big coincidence.

Someone called it jellybeans theory.
You get a brute that is like a pack (jar) of coloured jelybeans.
Each jellybean is a different skill or weapon.
Each levelup you draw one jellybean from the pack. Different path means that you draw from different place, but from the asme pack (jar) - so your choices are a bit different but if you were to reacj lev 999 - it odes not matter which path you would choose - your brute would have the same set of skills/weapons.
And when you choose new destiny - you get the new pack (jar) of jellybeans...


Of course that is just a theory, and more people support "complete randomness" theory...
Just what buggs me is the storage space for each brute that would be required by the random theory. Because then each rankup and each different path - the game would have to write down more and more choices... and with jellybeans theory - it is all "composed" into your brute when you adopt it and I think it lowers storage (and since this game is about earning money to the developers - and storage of data is expensive... well)
