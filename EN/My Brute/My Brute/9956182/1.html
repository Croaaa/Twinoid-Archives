Hammer is everything "flash flood" wishes it was. or more accurately, it's what the fanatical supporters of flash flood want it to be. Hammer cannot be dodged, blocked, 6th sensed, countered, or avenged by hostility. Hammer damage depends on how much your brute's strength is, so unlike bomb, the damage will evolve when your brute adds strength. Does a brute throw away their weapon to use it? yes. there are a couple solutions to this: A. have more than 1 weapon B. have martial arts C. have ridiculous strength so your brute wins after the Hammer is used. This is probably not the best skill for the mybrute cheaters out there (shuriken and flail comes to mind) but for every skill and weapon there is a counter and Hammer is no different. Tough skin and especially armor will decrease the damage of Hammer, and Resistant will cripple the damage to 20%. The worst outcome for Hammer is when it is deployed on a dog.
My main brute is a Brutal Legend. This skill, combined with Herculean Strength and a Bear, have been the main reasons why i've won tournaments by level 21. This is an auto accept skill. example of the best Hammer since M.C.
