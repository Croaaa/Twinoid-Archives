Okay, now you got me mind runnin.


This version of myBrute has 42 skills to learn. Removing the 4 non-combat skills (since cook doesn't work in tournaments anyway) there are 38 combat skills. Now there are some that are only useful in certain situations, like Survival or Lead Skeleton. Let's say we effectively have about 35 useful combat skills. (I'll just ignore the fact that Feline Agility, Lightning Bolt and Herculean Strength are only useful for a brute with much of the according stat at higher levels to have an impact on the fight which is worth mentioning - that would leave us with even less useful skills)


Then, each level up he has a 33,33% chance to get a skill, since he can get a weapon, a stat or a skill on level up. With level 99 a brute had 98 level ups.


I've written a small program to calculate the average number of skills a brute could have with this system and let it run 10.000 times. The average number of skills for a brute to have at level 99 is approximately 33,961. It means, if a brutes owner choses a skill every time he sees it after leveling, it is likely that he will have them by level 99.


However, this is not the point. The point is, the higher level brutes become, the more similar they become, making fights at higher levels more and more depending on luck. And the game depends on luck very much already since the brutes have no AI and use skills and weapons randomly.
