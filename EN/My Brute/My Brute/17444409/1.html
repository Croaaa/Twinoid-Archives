Do remember all values are rounded down after all calculations. That means if a brute with 3 speed gets armor, the speed instantly becomes 2.7 (2), and if he gets +3 speed (2.7 with armor), it becomes 5.4 (5).

Now consider a brute with lightning bolt, recon and armor, with 4 base speed and no +speed level ups.

His speed now = (4 + 3 + 5) * 150% * 250% * 90% = 40.5 (40)
Without armor it would be 45 speed.

If he gets +3 speed now (10.125 for the boosters and armor), his speed will become 50.675 (50).
