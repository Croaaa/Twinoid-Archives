But this is what I'm saying though. With the high rate of countering via, Iron head, Counter Attack + Shield, Sixth Sense, the abundance of long ranged weapons you can do more harm than good being incredibly fast. Strength is something that is mandatory, you won't win if your brute is weak and relying on weapons is bad because most people above 30 ~ 40 will most diffidently have either Shock or Sabotage, a few Thief and some Iron Head, relying on weapons is backwards. Agility is also mandatory because you don't want just anyone swinging heavy weapons and blasting you full of attacks. Also you'll seldom be able to multi attack. Hp is self explanatory. But what about speed? Well, you do more turns which is good for fast weapons ( only fast ) a few moderately fast weapons like baton and broadsword may work well with it. But for the most part multi turning can be just as harmful as it is beneficial.
Also, bring up any mybrute emulator, even brutes in their level 10000ths don't have all 4 stat boosters, for the most part, it's a semi confirmed fact. But yeah, I believe if you're going to stay your rank with no plans of ranking up, it's a wise decision to skip Lightning Bolt, who knows, next thing you know you'll be missing out on Herculean strength or Vitality.
