Pure Strength Brute

15 ~ 20 or more STR, with anykind of wep (more agile) to do strong blows with a nice speed. Dangerous, yeaaaah, some of them use stone hammer, or bumps combined with fierce brute, sometimes 1 hit kill. But, without the right wep are just smashed by other builds

Pure Agi Brute

Bad Str, not soo great speed, but VERY agile. Dodge a lot, and in more advanced levels, are annoying to anyone who isnt wielding light weapons. Anyway, atk and speed isnt that great... doh

Pure Speed Brute

Isnt the great dodger, isnt the greater hitter, but with a light weapon may hit 3, 4, 5, 6 consecutives... WOAHA, some of them are really strong, but some brutes combine it with heavy weps, bumps mace or something and kill all the speed. =/

Wep builds, well, few ppl do that, like me, nice part is, combined with the stats they are ultimately KILLER, bad part is, if u dont pick a wep, you are dead.

Agile

Combine with most part of the stat builds, with high str are xtremely high damage in a single round, with high agi, the dodge go to the sky, with a nice speed, the amount of hits KILLS. EX: Shurikens, Knife and Sai.

Defense

Batons, Shields(yeah i consider this gear), Spears, Whips, Halberds, theres a lot of them. Block, hit, block, hit. Thats the life. Most of them hit a lot, except Baton. Many ppl like it with Strength stats, but the defense of them is beat down. Speed isnt te most recommended, some of them are slow, except Baton and Whip. I think agi is the most reccomended, letting the opponent most time without reaction, hes attacked in his turn, hes attacked in your turn, i like them HAHA

Massive Damage

Stone Hammer, Axe, Bumps, Mace and these wonders..

These ones have the higher damage in the game, but less speed and agi. The only recommended stat with them is STR, since agi and speed are lowered by the weps. With fierce brute they can 1 hit kill the enemy... Woah. The bad part is... the accuracy isnt good. Hit high, but they are very slow.

Mixed

Sword, Broadsword and others...

They have a decent block chance, or counter, some of them hit hard (SWORD) but they arent that speedy. All builds can be used, i think.
