Heinz
i´m playing a while now and i came to the conclusion, that its not very smart to wait for the perfect weapon, because:

1. You lvl up very slow (assuming you dont have skills like MA or Thief) which is boring and frustating
2. A mediocre weapon can become very powerful with the right skills - for example knife is devastating with sabotage, WM and some decent stats -> accurate Multihits with 25+ dmg and everyone destroys a weapon, not bad eh?
3. If you rely to heavily on one weapon you wont get far - there are so many ways to lose it right away (sabotage and thief again, but also Sai, Shock ect) Chances also are, that your brute throws it right away...

so bottom line: even "bad" weapons can become very, very nice.
