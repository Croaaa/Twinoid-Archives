simple, the higher your speed compared to your opponent is, the more throws you get.
let's say you have 2 speed, and your opponent has up to 10 speed, u'll usually do around 5 throws only. (~ x5 damage each throw = around 25 damage every turn if you don't miss).
if you have 13 speed and your opponent has less than 7, you will do around 13-17 throws a turn. meaning you can do around 100 damage easily in 1 turn.
also, if you have first strike, you can do x1.5 - x2.0 more throws in the first turn. let's say you have 8 speed, then you can get up to 15 throws in first turn !(again, if the opponent has low speed. it's always compared to the opponent's speed) if the opponent has reconnaissance, you can do x2 again, meaning you can do up to something around 30 throws in first turn (you can do ~150 damage O_o considering you do ~5 damage a hit. you can do higher damage with higher str and agi), its rare though.
if the opponent carries a heavy weapon you can throw more shurikens as well.
