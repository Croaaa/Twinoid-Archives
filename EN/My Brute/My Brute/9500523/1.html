Firstly, chill out and stop whining like a baby.

Secondly, the Saboteur ability will sabotage ONE of your opponents weapons. Doesn't have to be the first. Could be the last weapon he draws. My point is: it's possible to kill your opponent (or vice-versa) before he pulls out the weapon that could be sabotaged. Get it?

Lastly, Saboteur works GREAT with Spy. Using both, you can easily find and single out ninjas for quick, easy wins. Since they only have one weapon in their inventory, it will sabotage every time. Also, with Spy, you can single out other players with, perhaps, only one or two weapons. Another way to increase your chances of victory.

Just keep training; you'll get a feel for how it works.
