The Ranking, as seen in the Ranking pages, is by level, and then by the EXP the brute has in that level.
A level 18 with 10exp is higher than a lv18 with 5 EXP, for example.
I have not yet found out how the system determines the order when there are 2 identical brutes, level/exp-wise.
Say there's a lv18/10exp on slot 4 and another brute is reaching that point in training - might be that the newly arriving brute-in-training bumps away the one who's already there, or that the newcomer has to be happy with being #5.
That part I don't know yet.
And so this is not really connected to Tournaments or their previous outcomes. Couldn't be, in fact, for a Rank where there were no Tournaments yet, as in Furious Soul right now, and using Tournaments for calculating ranking for brutes who don't do them wouldn't work either.
