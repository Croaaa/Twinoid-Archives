So the resistant skill says it reduces the damage of 5 attacks. I figured after 5 attacks, the skill would stop working.


This is not the case for the resistant specialty
Resist is intended to make it so that you can not die in less than 5 attacks.
Ergo, you can only take 20% of damage to your health bar at the absolute most.
(e.g. Your opponent draws Flail and they have 35 strength. You have 80 health and your only specialty is resistant, your opponent can still only hurt you for 16 damage per attack.)
