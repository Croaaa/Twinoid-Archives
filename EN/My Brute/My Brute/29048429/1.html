you will definitely know right away once you've found a keeper.
But in general, I would say if you have good skills within the first 7 levels, then the destiny is worth keeping and exploring.
For example, in your current path if you get HS and FoF within first 5 levels I would keep that destiny. but if you get those skills later like lvl 10 and 11, then hit new destiny.
Also, I wouldn't just look at weapons to determine if a brute is a keeper. Instead I would look for good skills like MA, FS, shield (+CA), or the rarer untouchable/immortality to determine if you should keep the destiny.
