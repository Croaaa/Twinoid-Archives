My assumption is that, at 0 speed, fist attack takes 100 time units in average, but has a range (like 90-110 time units). Weapons attacks take different amount of time for different weapons (lowest being shuriken), and vary with speed.

As there's a range of time for each attack, a series of test is needed to take the average of time taken. And to make accurate results, we need to derive the formula of speed first.

Alternate method: only take relative speed of weapons from fights of both brutes having the same speed, and no speed multipliers like bodybuilder on heavy weapons. This way we can skip the speed calculation part but only have the relative results.
