So many posts and not verry helpful answers...

So first go there:
http://muxxu.com/group/list

It is a list of all clans existing on muxxu.
To create each clan it costs 200 tokens (you get those for donating to the site)

So choose one clan that you like and has less than 50 people. Click it. Then you would see a button "Request to join this group".
You can request to join one group at one time. So to reqest another group you have to be either accepted, denied or delete your request.
When you request to join a group - you have to wait for the owner to accept you (you sometimes have to PM him - check requirements, most groups have them).

And if he accepts you you are in!

As for benefits:
- you get special smileys to post:
- if your brute has kingdom map - you can play on the almost deserted kingdom map of your group and there it is easier to start.

that is all that I could think of for now.
