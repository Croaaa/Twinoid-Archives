lucaswhit
http://mybrute.muxxu.com/fight/43604430? chk=7859

two even brutes, the one factor that decides the victory is that my opponent caught my bear in a net. the net takes away 110 damage points from your opponent if he has a bear, plus the bear hits for 17-26 damage points after it starts on the 3rd attack. even if your opponent has a dog, you can remove the dog to free up an important attack (hammer/fierce brute/flail hits). Vs wolves , a net comes in handy if it's activated early.

bomb, brilliant skill for the first 20 levels. you should pass on bomb if you get net, as the bomb can free up wolves and bears. i've combined the bomb with my bear brute and it contributes to his victories, especially vs armor and RESISTANT brutes as neither skill offers protection from bomb. it's also awesome to remove 2x dogs in one free attack. i win 8/10 fights if my brute activates both bombs.

hypnosis, super rare skill , in my opinion always get this. turn the wolf or bear on your opponent. add 26 or 110 damage meat shield to your brute, to me it's a no brainer for anyone.

cry of the damned, you can certainly add this to any brute. only drawback for cry of the damned and hypnosis is that if your opponent has no pet, these skills have no value. anti pet skills contribute to deep tournament runs in my experience.

1. hypnosis 2. cry 3. net 4. bomb in that order. bomb will lose value over time, net can be used on non-pet brutes especially setting up a heavy weapon attack.
