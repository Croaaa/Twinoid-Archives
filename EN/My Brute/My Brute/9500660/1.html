1. Makes a ton of difference. It reduces heavy weapon damage by a lot.
2. Not that I know of. I've never seen it give a con, and if it did it would say so in the description like it does for recon.
3. No.

Whether you pick the skill is dependent on what you already have. I personally think high HP + Lead Skeleton is a great mix, which is what my brute has. She can take a beating. But if you notice that your brute dodges a lot, then having more agility would probably be better. It's up to you. Personally I think it's a great skill to have for anyone.

Resistant skill is better, but it's only limited to 5 hits only. And you don't chose the 5 hits, your brute does. So it can be wasted on dog attacks, un-armed combat, etc ...

Examples:
1) http://mybrute.muxxu.com/fight/5375731?c hk=29ec - Notice how much strength he has, and notice how much damage his hammer did.
2) http://mybrute.muxxu.com/fight/4024444?c hk=cdeb - high HP + skeleton is good damage soaker.
