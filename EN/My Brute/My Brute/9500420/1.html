2 - What does the skill "back-up" do ?
The brute owning this skill will come to fight with his teammates (other brutes. same account) if their level is strictly superior to his. Rank doesn't matter. This will work only in training, DURING 2 turns, and NOT in tournament.
3 - How do you use the skill "back-up" ?
You should go on the the "back-up" skill's owner cell. There, you will see a new button called "Reinforcements". Click on it, and for the cost of one injury from the brute who own the skill, it will be activated during each training fight during the current day.
