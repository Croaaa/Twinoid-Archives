Even if I have MA, I think I would always take flail. Most brutes don't have thief, and even if they do, shock and sabotage are much more common so it's possible you will be disarmed before they steal it.

The fights you win with flail will be higher than the fights you lose due to flail being stolen.


Of course it needs to fit in your build. If you have low agi and speed, maybe no 6th sense or hostility, don't take flail.


Other good weapons are sai, knife (with weapon master, shock/sabotage, first strike), mug (with MA), frying pan, trident, sword (with counter attack and shield). Axe is good too but you need the right build (6th sense / hostility / determination).
