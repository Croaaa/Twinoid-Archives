I guess the reason for the question is that in many browsergames you get a very small amount of whatever they call the stuff you pay for by doing different tasks/achievements or at random times.

Like getting one token for every 50 won fights or every time you reached a tournament final. The idea behind this is to get people "hooked" on tokens and make them see the benefits.
For this reason some games let you start out with a premium account for the first two weeks and take it back later. This way you actually miss the stuff you had at the beginning and are more willing to pay for it.
