This is because you have Vitality or Immortal both skills add flat Endurance + %. If you reduce your flat Endurance you will lose more HP from the percentages.

Here comes the trick. Does the Pets Affect the HP or the Endurance since Vitality and Immortal affect the Endurance and trough that the HP (1 Endurance = +6 HP).

So if it affects the HP you will lose as stated above 10, 23 30 HP but if it affects the Endurance it will be more.
