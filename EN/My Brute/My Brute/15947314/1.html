Spray is my 2nd brute, he's a brutal legend

this is post 1 out of 2 that i'm going to make this week. there is a lot of misleading information floating around the forum that is very confusing to new players and i'd like to get the real word out on Flash Flood.

in this example fighter Jorre uses flash flood to get a 100% guaranteed hit on brute Spray. unfortunately for him he throws away the sai (ridiculously overpowered) and broadsword, and neither weapon makes that much of an impact. this action sets up Jorre to be disarmed by sabotage, and now Jorre's offense is severely crippled. Determination with Sword or Sai is an amazing combination and Jorre would likely be better served without the Flash Flood skill.

Hypnosis is also a rare super that many fighters have never had. i would recommend auto accepting this skill and then adding a bit of speed to assure that the skill gets activated early in the fight to prevent a wolf from putting up excessive damage or a bear absorbing too much of your offense. opposing fighters with multiple dogs will also be easy targets for you sense they often take fierce brute, nets, and overpowered weapons.

for the Flash Flood promoters i have a simple question for you: how many levels does it take before the skill is worth anything? you need several levels to amass the right heavy weapons, then you need the support skills to balance your brute while he's unlucky enough to be stuck using a heavy weapon. so you'll need bodybuilder, determination, and likely an ungodly amount of agility to use them effectively. you'll also likely need armor, shield, 6th sense, or untouchable so your brute isn't a sitting duck on defense. and what happens when you toss a super weapon like flail? that is settling for 75 damage for one toss when you likely could get multi-hits out of it.

FF is a mirage.
