vel
recon is a dangerous skill - you could ruin your brute with it. My advise is to avoid recon unless you have iron head and some good amount of hp. Iron head is crucial here because with recon you'll have to withstand multiple attacks before your first move. Other very helpful skills for recon are feline agility and untouchable or shield with counter attack - in other words every good defensive combo is highly advised if you want to use recon with your brute. Otherwise you'll often get yourself killed without even single move of yours.
