MadMooney
Each attack is a programmed sequence of (Abilities*BaseChance*smallRandom) for every step of the way.
calculating attack vs first strike, who goes first?
has net? / use net?
has fierce? / use fierce?
Take weapon? Which one?
go
Opponent has weapon? can intercept/counter?
strike(strength?) ... opponent: can block? can avoid? can counter?
....rinse/repeat.
The logic is pretty straight-forward, actually, and the whole sequence prolly gets calculated within milliseconds, right before fight-start, and the result saved under a unique fight ID. This sequence gets fed to the renderer, which can determine where to place the brutes (and pets) on his own, so each fight looks a bit different each time you watch it, even if the basic actions stay the same.
