Level up bonuses don't follow a consistent pattern, but I can say this:

1) Write down your choices
2) (after a level reset) Try picking the stat boost on first level up, if you didn't already do that
3) Go with a new destiny as a last resort (some destinies can fork in surprising ways)
4) Don't get every weapon you're offered

X) Don't make multiple accounts, unless you're going to delete them relatively quickly
