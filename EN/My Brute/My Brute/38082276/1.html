I took trident on my brute which had whip, sword and frying pan with no regrets, but would never take morning star.


Trident is a nice all round weapon. Instead of being the best weapon for a particular purpose it is decent at everything.
Morning star is not a bad weapon with decent agility, but for me is a worse version of trombone. I also rate broadsword+weapon master as better than morning star+body builder but this is another argument.
Trident is a better version of lance. I don't see it as a worse version of whip as whip is blocked too often.


Both trident and morning star have some shock and sixth sense, but trident seems better. Trident seems faster than morning star which is more important for me than the additional damage morning star has which is often reduced by lead skeleton/resistant.
Both multi hit often enough. Trident seems more accurate to me.

Morning star probably blocks better and is more difficult to block, but I believe trident is the better choice for most brutes.
