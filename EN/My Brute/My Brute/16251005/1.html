Hey guys, i saw a similar thread 'bout a month ago, but since it was full of spam, lets start it again. I think the difficulty of getting this title is worth a separate post.

It's about the title Peacemaker (Show x1). Original thread here for the ones who want to suck up every bit of info.
Intro: For the ones who are not familiar with it: you need to shock 8 weapons from your enemy that may include his shield. It can be done via the skills Shock and Iron Head, the skill Sabotage doesn't count and Saboteur is not confirmed. As far as i know Sai isn't confirmed either.

Tips: To get this title your best bet would be to have a high defense brute (hp or agi, better if mixed) with low damage. The low damage gets handy not to kill your opponent before he pulls out enough weapons.

My goal with this thread would be to help each other out getting it (it won't involve many ppl anyway as the forum is deserted). So if you encounter a brute with potential title giving capabilities let us know
Till now i've seen only 2 and they just die too fast, but here they are (if we keep track of them maybe some day...): nvcnxijvbdnigh and locozero
