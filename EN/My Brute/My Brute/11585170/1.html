How do you get animals fighting for you.
On may 9, 2012
[4398]

Pets are upgrades you can get during level ups, there are 3 kinds of pets, Dogs, Wolfs (Panthers) and Bears. You can get up to 3 dogs that are called A, B and C respectively (all of them are the same) and a Wolf or Bear (not both). Getting a pet decreases your HP by a certain amount (Dogs<Wolves<Bears) and having HP stat boosters makes the reduce of HP even higher (Vitality<Immortal<Vitality+Immortal)
DogsHP loss: 12, 18 if you have Vitality, 42 if you have Immortal, 48 if you have Vitality + Immortal
HP: 14
AT: 3-5
It is speedy.
WolfHP loss: 36 points, 54 if you have Vitality, 90 if you have immortal, and if you have 135 Vitality + Immortal.
HP: 26
AT: 9-12 usually multihits
It is quite speedy and agile
BearHP loss: 48 ​​points, 72 if you have Vitality, 168 if you have Immortal, 192 if you have Vitality + Immortal
HP: 110
AT: 24-26
It is slow, not agile, but strong and enduring
It has very low initiative so he moves around at round 3 or so.
