More or less depends upon ur built and ur experience with ur brute . An example is that even if ur opponent has low HP , u still might wanna fight him/her if say u have Hammer/ Bomb skills [Bomb is AoE which hammer will go through defenses].

Also for say a high on defense type brute , u might u wanna fight someone who has low str since they will eventually tend to either throw their weapons or just not do enough damage . Either way , w/o enough Str its very difficult to beat a high vit brute especially if he/she has a shield and or spear type weapon .

Then say u have recon , u want to survive the 1st wave , so u might wanna avoid high str again .

Then say ur a very high on countering [shield + lance or something] , u wanna target high speed people since that way u will most likely trigger that counter more ofter and for the timez it gets through , the damage isnt that high either .

Now lets assume that you are a completely balanced type brute who has some weaponry , some stats , some good skills etc. , then u will pretty much have to pray to god that ur opponent dies b4 u do since then u will generally always have a 50-50 chance of winning [assuming both builds are on par] .

Now lets assume ur a str based brute , with low HP , u might wanna fight people with mid-low HP since they will tend to not have pets and/or will die quick enough for ur HP to not make too much problems .
