After 3 days spent with this game, here it is one build for a good brute, i estimate a win ratio of 85%. It needs 27 levels for skills (optional included), 2 lvls for weapons, the rest are for main stats.


0. DON'T PICK: pets, the remaining weapons, Shock, Thief, Cry of the Damned, Flash Flood, Ballet Shoes, Reconnaissance, Lightning Bolt, Toughened Skin, Weapons Master, Bodybuilder, Resistant, Survival.

1. MAIN STATS: speed is ignored as much as possible, and an ideea about the remaining stats: agility = 1/2 * strentgh.
First Strike to compensate lack of speed.
Stat boosters are picked as fast as possible (Vitality before Immortality).

2. DAMAGE MODIFIER: Martial Arts, Lead Skeleton.

3. DODGE AND BLOCK: Unctouchable & Shield, Counter-Attack.

4. COMBO RATE: Fists of Fury, Determination, Flail, Leek.

5. COUNTER RATE: 6th Sense.

6. DISARM: Sabotage.

7. REVERSAL RATE: Leek, Hostility.

SUPER: Bomb, Net, Fierce Brute, Tamer, Hypnosis, Tragic Potion, Hammer.

TALENTS: Regeneration.

Optional: Relentless (accuracy 30% for flail), Armor (-5 dmg received, -10% speed - wich is negligible, Hammer dmg reduction, cool visual update – expecially Saiyan, Legionary and Tight Dress), Immortality (best to take at range of levels 5-20), Iron Head (usefull, it's up to you if it deserves a level).


IMPORTANT: Note down the skills/stats you choose at every level-up. After you win a turnament, you have the possibility to fork your build for an improved one. For more information click here.

COMBO BUILDS FOR MEDIOCRE PLAYERS


So, you recruit a new brute, free or for 100 tokens. There are 12 ranks in mybrute, so you got 11 forks for your destiny. For lvl 100, that means 1 fork each 9 lvls, on average. But the 1st and the 2nd forks should be around lvls 15 and 25.


What that means?


It means if you win a turnament at lvl 9, don’t rank up, wait and see what choices you have at later lvls; if 2-3 lvl-ups are not suitable for your build, again, don’t rank up, just keep lvl-ing up another 4-5 lvls to see if you can benefit so much more on your build from them than those 2-3 unsuitable lvl-ups.

THIRD BUILD: Tasmanian Devil


So, you got a common weapon in the first 4 lvls, a knife. Knife has 2 attributes, sharp and fast.
Other sharp weapons: Sword, Broadsword, Halberd, Scimitar.
Other fast weapons: Sai, Leek, Coffee Cup.


This build is about one fast Tasmanian Devil brute.


Tactic: be fast as wind, disarm opponent very fast, 1st Strike, Sabotage and a lot of Speed. You will 100% start the fight first and with the help of luck, and maybe 1 weapon, you’ll attack 2-3 times your opponent, 2-3 hits each time, plenty to get a slight advantage.


Worst opponents: the Nostradamus build (used by all pro players) brutes (soon to be posted)


Ratio on sts: medium Strength, decent Agility, as much Speed as possible, full Hp (stat booster on str, speed, hp).


WHAT TO PICK: Fists of Fury, Relentless, Determination, Untouchable, Immortality, Shield, Shock, 6th Sense, Hostility, Counter-Attack, Martial Arts, Lead Skeleton, Iron Head.


WHAT NOT TO PICK: Ballet Shoes, Reconaissance, Feline Agility, Armor, Toughened Skin, Weapons Master, Bodybuilder, Survival, Thief, Flash Flood.


SUPER: Bomb, Cry of the Damned, Net, Fierce Brute, Tamer, Hypnosis, Tragic Potion, Hammer.
TALENTS: Regeneration.


Optional: Resistant.


4 lvls for weapons, 28 lvls for skills, a build that requires 32 lvls.
