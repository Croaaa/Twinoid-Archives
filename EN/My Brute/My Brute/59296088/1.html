Alright, so I'm guessing you understand that regardless of you choosing Same Destiny or New Destiny, you go back to Level 1. This aspect of the game is also present in Brutal Legend.

The way people get to higher levels without resetting their levels when winning a tournament is simply by not clicking on Click here to move up a rank! or by not entering tournaments all together. Keep in mind, by doing this, you cannot participate in future tournaments and you cannot exit the one you're currently in, so you'll be stuck in the rank.

Many people, such as myself, like to have some brutes level up in the same rank, and have other brutes rank up until Brutal Legend. It's just a matter of choice.
