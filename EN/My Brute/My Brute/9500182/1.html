My Brute doesn't suck, most of the topic were misnamed this way by impulsive kids.

You asked for suggestions, so here's my answers : I read the forum, and the two ones which sounded the most reasonable both for the players and muxxu were :

1) a graphical customization option when you create your brute. (should be accessible with tokens, if you ask me)

2) the possibility to get rid of brutes, to free slots and get another ones.
It absolutely needs a limitation, like having to wait for 24h or 48h (72h sounds too much to me) after recruitment.
This go without saying that if people buy something, it wouldn't be brutes, but slots (just imagine you buy a brute, and discard it, you could be frustrated to pay a second time in my opinion)

Very Important : the deletion shouldn't be effective immediately, but has something like a counter before the character is sucked up into oblivion. That's in order to prevent accidental deletion from unauthorized users (happens all the time)

So let's summarize 2) : The player decides he wants to free one slot to get another brute. He clicks the delete brute button, then a message warning him his brute will be deleted in two days is displayed.

Hope this message won't be in vain
