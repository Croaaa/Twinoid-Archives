For level 18 too vulnerable I would say.
Either you need more health, better defensive abilities (resistant, survival, untouchable) and/or more abilities to increase hits and finish the fight quicker (counter attack, hostility, 6th sense, first strike).
You also have no real pet defence (in this I would include 6th sense, hostility, counter attack, net, hypnosis or cry of the damned) so I would expect panthers to give you issues due to your low health and speed, and bears due to your relatively weak fire power (you´d typically waste several attacks to kill them I´d say).
I would expect that brute to struggle at brutal legend.
Having said that most Brutal Legend tournies are not won at level 18, so it depends what you get in the next 2-3 levels
