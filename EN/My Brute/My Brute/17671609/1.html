I've considered this myself lately and it's not impossible that 2 options r calculated randomly & presented to the player only when he reaches level-up & rank-up situations.


There r limitations, of course, like "if he already has stat-booster.. ..less chance to get again" etc., but essentially, they r all the same chance. Completely random.


Choosing a new-destiny simply let's u roll a new 1st-level-up choices... no more. The rest is still equally random. Every fork is, essentially, a new destiny, just like a new destiny is technically a fork at level 0.


We r left to imagine patterns of choices (like, if I choose this I'll usually get that afterwards) but I haven't been able to draw a distinctive conclusion.


What is remarkable to me, tho, is that out of 8 brutes I have, only 1 has had Hammer (the weapon) offered. At one instance, I chose to decline it and forked, only to have it offered to me again a few levels later. That's either a highly low probability OR ... there's more to it
