These are a few tips that I use on My Brute:

1. I always seem to choose brutes that have high Agility, life, speed and sometimes strength. Its best to choose them when your a lvl 6 or lower, because I realized that by now all the brutes would probably have weapons or pets or skills by lvl 7.

Why would you choose them?
-Well, an example would be the skill Feline Agility. That only increases the chance of your brute dodging a weapon and things like that. I sometimes guess that other brutes with high agility would have chosen that skill. They wouldnt exactly have a weapon or pet (Sometimes not even skill) would they?
They would be easier to fight and defeat than others with low strength and all.

2. Before you enter your brute in a tournament (If you want to, that is) then the best thing to do is either to train your brute or not. If it is 10 exp or lower, from leveling up, I'd take the chance and train my brute. But if its not close to leveling up, I would wait till the all the fights finished and check to see if my brute won the tournament. If they do, you would be able to train them so they can level up, while if you dont you can train your brute and wait till the next day to enter your brute in a tournament again or train it, sometimes even both.

Thats pretty much all I know. Feel free to post a few tips of your own. Thanks!
