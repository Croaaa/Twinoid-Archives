This game is mostly luck-based. Some people get amazing destinies, while others get junk. Having a choice at level up has significantly improved the game by allowing some actual strategy with what your brute has, but no one has cracked the algorithm that determines what you get

So my advice is "don't stress out about this game that much"

But if you want to get a decent brute, write down what you picked for each level (along with the other thing you didn't pick), and occasionally ask the forum what they think of your brute. There are enough people around here, so you should get a pretty good evaluation of your brute

Also, this game is not for impatient people
