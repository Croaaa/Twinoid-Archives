IMO speed and initiative are the hardest thing to get an accurate value of. Damage is way easier, we won't need to take statistics at EVERY fight we watch, as it doesn't involve anything about "average value".

But when taking stats of speed and initiative, we need to take an average value of a large number of "random" fights (completely random, so every fight that we watch must be taken into account), as the required initiative for a weapon attack has a range of values, AND we can't measure initiative directly. This goes with block rate, disarm rate, counter rate, and nearly every other stats, which average values needed to be taken.
