A guide for each individual weapon made by me, just because I love you guys
Whip
Whip is a semi-rare weapon that deals around 18-20 damage per hit. It is really easily blocked, it has a high amount of counter rate, multi hit rate and a decent amount of shock rate. It works well with 6th Sense, Relentless, Shock, Sabotage and First strike.

Trombone
Trombone is an extremely rare weapon that deals around 23-25 damage per hit. It has a decent amount of shock rate and multi hit rate. It works well with Fist of fury and shock.

Trident
Trident is a rare weapon that deals around 20-23 damage per hit. It has a high counter rate and a decent shock rate and block rate. It works well with 6th Sense, Counter Attack and Shield.

Tennis Racket
Tennis Racket is an extremely rare weapon that deals around 10-12 damage per hit. It has a small block rate and has a 100% Hostility that means it will attack right after your opponent attacks you. It works well with high strength, Shock and Sabotage.

Sword
Sword is a semi rare weapon that deals around 40-45 damage per hit. It is blocked easily and has a decent counter rate and block rate. It works well with 6th Sense, Fist of fury and Relentless. It is influenced by Weapon Master.

Axe
Axe is a semi rare weapon that deals around 80-85 damage per hit. It is dodged easily and is very slow. It works well with Determination, Fierce brute and Relentless. It is influenced by Bodybuilder.

Sai
Sai is a rare weapon that deals around 12-15 damage per hit. It has the highest disarm rate of all weapons. It also has a high multi hit rate, a decent block rate and is really fast. It works well with Sabotage, First strike and Shock.

Scimitar
Scimitar is a semi rare weapon that deals around 15-18 damage per hit. It has a decent multi hit rate. It works well with Weapon Master, Fist of fury and Relentless. It is influenced by Weapon Master.

Piou-piouz
Piou-poouz is an extremely rare weapon that deals around 8-9 damage per hit. It works well with high agility. It is a thrown weapon that deals more damage than Shuriken, less than Noodle bowl and is thrown less than Shuriken but more than Noodle bowl.

Noodle bowl
Noodle bowl is an extremely rare weapon that deals around 15-18 damage per hit. It works well with high agility. It is a thrown weapon that deals more damage than Shuriken and Piou-piouz but is thrown less than Shuriken and Piou-piouz.

Shuriken
Shuriken is a semi rare weapon that deals around 5-6 damage per hit. It works well with high speed, First strike and high agility. It is a thrown weapon that is thrown more than Piou-piouz and Noodle Bowl but deals less damage than both.

Mammoth Bone
Mammoth Bone is a common weapon that deals around 15-18 damage per hit. It has a small chance of Counter. It works well with Determination and high strength. It is influenced by Bodybuilder (?).

Leek
Leek is an extremely rare weapon that deals around 10-12 damage per hit. It has a 100% chance of multi hit, a 100% chance of Hostility and it never misses (with the exception of Ballet Shoes) or is blocked. It also has a small counter rate. It works well with Sabotage, Shock and First strike.

Lance
Lance is a common weapon that deals around 17-18 damage per hit. It has a high counter rate and a decent shock rate. It works well with Shock and 6th Sense.

Morning Star
Morning Star is a rare weapon that deals around 25-30 damage per hit. It has a decent counter rate and a small multi hit rate. It works well with Fist of fury, 6th Sense and Shock. It is influenced by Bodybuilder.

Knife
Knife is a common weapon that deals around 10-12 damage per hit. It is fast and has a decent multi hit rate. It works well with Weapon Master, High speed and Fist of fury. It is influenced by Weapon Master.

Keyboard
Keyboard is an extremely rare weapon that deals around 13-15 damage per hit. It has a decent counter and multi hit rate. It works well with Fist of fury, Shock and 6th Sense.

Halberd
Halberd is a rare weapon that deals around 25-28 damage per hit. It is VERY easily dodged and has a high counter rate. It works well with Relentless, 6th Sense and Weapon Master. It is influenced by Weapon Master.

Frying Pan
Frying Pan is an extremely rare weapon that deals around 20-25 damage per hit. It has the highest block rate of all the weapons and has a decent counter rate. It works well with Shield, Counter Attack and Iron Head. It is surprisingly influenced by Bodybuilder.

Flail
Flail is a semi rare weapon that deals around 60-65 damage per hit. It is unblockable and has a decent counter rate and multi hit rate. It works well with 6th Sense, Fist of fury, High strength and Shock. It is influenced by Bodybuilder.

Fan
Fan is an extremely rare weapon that deals around 5-10 damage per hit. It has a 0% shock rate even with Shock. It also has a high multi hit rate and is very fast. It works well with Sabotage, Relentless and Fist of fury.

Coffee cup (aka Mug)
Coffee cup is an extremely rare weapon that deals around 20-22 damage per hit. It is influenced by Martial arts, making it extremely strong. It is very fast and has a high chance of multi hit. It works well with Martial Arts, High strength and Fist of fury. It is influenced by Martial arts.

Bumps
Bumps is a common weapon that deals around 30-35 damage per hit. It is easily dodged and has a small counter rate. It works well with Relentless, Determination and Bodybuilder. It is influenced by Bodybuilder.

Broadsword
Broadsword is a common weapon that deals around 15-17 damage. It has a high block rate and a small counter rate. I works well with Shield, Counter Attack and Weapon Master. It is influenced by Weapon Master.

Baton
Baton is a common weapon that deals around 12-15 damage per hit. It has a high counter and shock rate. It works well with 6th Sense, Shock and Sabotage.

Hatchet
Hatchet is a common weapon that deals around 20-22 damage per hit. It has a small counter rate. It works well with 6th Sense, Shock and high strength. It is influenced by Bodybuilder (?).
