So imagine that you, as the user could carry up to 3 extra skills. These skills are the same as the ones that you can get when one of your brutes level up. When you hold a certain skill, that skill will be offered as an extra level up option for any Brute that levels up. As soon as you select that option, the skill you held will vanish.

For example.. say, you got a Broadsword as a skill in your inventory. Every time any of your Brutes level up, you will be able to pick Broadsword as your 3rd level up option. As soon as you select it for someone, the Broadsword skill will disappear from your inventory (you used it to allow that Brute to gain a Broadsword).

How would you obtain these? Imo the best way would be to get 1 random extra skill each time you win a tournament. Optionally players could receive 1 random extra skill after every XXX wins. They could even be sold for coins (some packages that grant you 1 random extra skill - purchasable once per day etc)

This would allow you for a much better customization, especially if you have more Brutes, and also make Tournament wins more rewarding.
