You have to choose the same options starting from level 1. If you chose something different at level 1 than you did last time, you will be down a different branch in the tree

There are 2 options at level 1
4 options at level 2 but you can only see 2
8 options at level 3 but you can only see 2
16 options at level 4 but you can only see 2
32 options at level 5 but you can only see 2
64 options at level 6 but you can only see 2
128 options at level 7 but you can only see 2
256 options at level 8 but you can only see 2
512 options at level 9 but you can only see 2
1024 options at level 10 but you can only see 2
and so on and so forth

It's a tree, to receive the same options you did last time, you have to choose the exact same options in the exact same order
