I like trident a lot.
It has some shock, 6th sense and it blocks well with shield too. Its damage isnt bad.

But with my brutes I always go first with the path without trident (unless I am lev 10+ with no good weapon). And sometimes It pays off and sometimes not and next rank I take trident.

TO find the best brute you have to get the good "base" - and then experiment with your paths a lot. So now you took trident - see where it takes you, and next rank try declining it.

Actually it is not that rare weapon and on brutes that I declined it it usually shows up again in the destiny.

If you have shield, hostility, Counter, 6th sense or shock or verry low hp - definitelly take it. If not - you can try other paths first

This weapon definitelly does not spoil your brute. But sometimes it is not amazing and great, just normal. Depends on other stuff you have along with it.
