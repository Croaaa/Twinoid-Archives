Abel was the one who told me what forking meant, although now I see that it's mainly remembering your old build, resetting brute on winning a tournament and then "forking" when you think your brute has had bad levels. I initially attributed it to a choice between a very ordinary skill/weapon and stats where you'd pick stats, but it mainly concerns destinies.

So at the moment for OP, you're still examining what choices you have over the course of leveling up. I recommend writing down each of your choices when leveling up and then when you win a tournament, you can reset the brute to level 1 with same or different destiny. Same means same, so you'll get the same choices, but "forking" is when you make a different choice among the same destiny. If you pick new destiny, the tree consisting your choices becomes moot and you must start a new one.

Then, when you reset your brute again by winning another tournament, you can choose 2 certain skills at different forking choices. Again, if you write all your choices down in the form of a tree, with a line representing the choice you made, you can basically highlight all the skills and weapons you wish to pick, which cuts out the randomness in the game to an extent.
