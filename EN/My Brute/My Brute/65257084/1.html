“After a strong launch in many countries around the world, Storytel has plans to continually expand its customer portfolio to the Asia Pacific market. In this regard, Thailand is one of our goals. Storytel has promoted to be a world-class audiobook using the 'Glocal' strategy, focusing on delivering a wide range of Thai language content along with English language content to the Thai market. ”

Storytel Asia Pacific Manager, Erin Thorstenson, added: “We are pleased to create a new experience for Thai people to know the world of digital audiobooks. The launch of the Storytel app will certainly excite consumers.

“We are confident that Storytel will create a new phenomenon in Thailand. By giving the e-book platform the option for consumers to discover a wider range of reading experiences, ”said Pavarnana Suwanjinda, Director of Storytel in Thailand, adding,“ Storytel is packed with thousands of content. Whether it's books on business, fiction, children's books, and more.

As Thais are the population of mobile phones and are ready to embrace new digital platforms. As one of the top players in the region, we see the potential of the Thai market to create a different reading trend. We are confident that Storytel will quickly become a part of the Thai digital lifestyle. "

Storytel made a strong debut in the Asia Pacific region in 2017, penetrating the Indian market first. Followed by Singapore and South Korea in 2019, as part of the launch of its service in Thailand, Storytel will launch a booth introducing audiobook applications and eBooks to Thai customers for the first time at the Winter Book Fest.

Which will be held on December 10-20, with a content library, audiobooks and Thai-language e-books for readers to experience the new reading world that can be easily accessed on hand applications. Hold Storytel will be officially launched in Thailand on January 27, 2021 with the launch of the famous brand ambassador.
