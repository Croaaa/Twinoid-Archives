Blocking and evading have two different rates.
The evading rate is improved by agility, untouchable and some weapons (e.g. fan).
The blocking rate is improved by shield, counter-attack and some weapons (e.g. broadsword, baton, sai, morning star, frying pan).
Therefore, without shield, Counter-attack is inferior to 6th sense.
