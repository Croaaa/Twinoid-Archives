I had bear but even so I would take wolf over bear. Wolf has a greater chance to dodge and hit multiple times. Takes less of your brute's HP away (notice in the fight that I had vitality but only had 96 hp). Gives less HP back to an opponent's Tamer skill (side note: if you are the one with tamer than bear>wolf). If opponent has Hypnosis wolf means you do not take as much of the double tap of having less HP and not having the meat shield. At low levels any pet is fine - At medium levels wolf>bear>1 dog (multiple dogs is a different argument altogether) - At higher levels having a meat shield is a simple trade off (depending on what skills you have to compliment them up i.e. tamer, hypnosis, vitality, ballet shoes, first strike)
