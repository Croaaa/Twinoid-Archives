Rolandesch
Cool thread man. I'll try to give my opinions.

Q 1 : Does having more weapons increase the chances of brutes withdrawing weapons? Haven't noticed.

Q 2 : Does having more weapons increase the chances of brutes exchanging weapons / throwing? Haven't noticed.

Q 3 : Does have more weapons increase probability of winning? E.G having a weapon for all types of situations such as 2 heavy weapons, 2 Medium weapons, 2 small. I'd say versitility is a good thing, but 2 in each area doesn't always work. Let's say you have shurikens. On their own they are kick ass! With random other weapons they are completely worthless, unless they are the last item with no others in stock. Or if you have a broadsword with weapons master, you might just want to specialize in the WM area like knife, halberd, etc.

Q 4 : Should I always get stats over a weapon even if my build doesn't support the stat being offered? Depends on the weapon and your current need for one. Typically if I dont have a weapon by 10, I take even crappy ones because I hate waiting on a decent one. This makes me end up with lots of batons and broadswords...Nooby right?

Q 5 : Why do people say getting lots of weapons is bad? Well, in most fights a brute only gets to make use of 1-3 weapons. Having any more than you need can hurt winning chances because you could have taken strength or whatever instead. One of my brutes was offered a new weapon each level for the fight five, I think. She sucked!

Q 6 : How many weapons should my brute have ( minimal ) I get along just fine with my top brute at TWO, because he is highly specialized. Know why? He has Knife AND Weapons Master with a Flail. That makes it very powerful with each weapon. He also has Martial Arts for when he is using neither. He ALSO has Thief Very helpful... I would say just keep trying new destinies until you're ready to keep your weapon set. Experiment.
