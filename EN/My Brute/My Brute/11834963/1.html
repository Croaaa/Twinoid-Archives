ANSWERS :
When can I join a tournament ?
As soon as you're level 3, you now have the occasion to join a tournament.

How many players can/must join a tournament ?
When you engage in tournaments, you have to wait for certain number of players to join the tournament.
For Padawan and Finger Flicker ranks, the total number of entrants (including your brute) is 16. In Plaster Puller and Cheeky Slapper ranks, it's 32. From Fist of Steel onwards, there will be 64 brutes per tournament.

What happens in a tournament ?
Fights are organized like in a tree structure, meaning A fights B, C fights D, E fights F, and G fights H.
Next, A/B's winner will fight C/D's winner, and E/F's winner will fight G/H's.
etc, until the finale, where there is only one winner.
There will be 60 minutes between each fights. You don't have to be there, the fights will be automatic. You will also be able to watch them later.

What happens if I loose during a tournament ?
Do I get XP in a tournament ?
You will have the opportunity to watch the tournaments until it ends, or simply leave it.
Once you leave it, you will get 1 XP per fight won.
You get XP only if you loose during the tournament - you won't get any if you win it.

What happens if I win a tournament ?
Once you've beaten everybody, you're offered to rank up.

What is ranking up ?
When you rank up you are offered a choice exactly like when you level up.
But instead of having skills, weapons, pets, or attributes, the two options will be :
- Same Destiny
- New Destiny
BOTH choices will get you back to level 1, but this time, you will fight more advanced players, and new tougher brutes, in the new rank.
View this as leagues.

What are destinies ?
A Destiny determines the options your brute will be offered during each level up.

What is "Same destiny"/"New Destiny" ?
Both will reset your character at level 1.

-> Same destiny will reset your stats the exact way you had them at level 1. Then, at each level, provided you're making the exact same choices during level up, you will have the exact same options. If you ever happen to make a different choice, the destiny of your character will fork, and you will have new choices.

-> New destiny will build a totally new character, even for the stats, and you will never see a glimpse of the old destiny again. The only things you will keep will be its appearance, and its name.

What kind of destiny do I choose ?
Based on the explanation above, you will have to think for yourself what's best for your character.
However, I would suggest to keep the same destiny, and fork as soon as possible when you've been offered a bad choice.
This way you will be able to come back during the next rank up if you miss what you had before

How do I rank up ?
No other way than winning a tournament

I don't want to rank up and restart at level 1 ! Is this possible ?
Yes.
You can avoid ranking up, once the tournament is over, by going back to the cell and continue to play normally.
Just do not click on the link telling you to rank up when the tournament is over.
You won't be able to participate in any new tournament until you rank up with this one, though.

I won the fight, how come I didn't move on in the tournament ?
This is a known bug. Sometimes you don't get credit for your brute's victory.
Just enter a new tournament if this happens.

What is the highest rank ?
Brutal Legend.

Are there tournaments in Brutal Legend rank ?
Yes.

What happens if I win a BL tournament ?
Instead of ranking up, you get an ear. Otherwise, it's the same as winning any other tournament.
There is a separate ranking for brutes who have won the most ears.
