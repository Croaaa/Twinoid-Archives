As we all know, when you get armor, you're brute gets a little modification in his apperance and since fighting armor brutes isn't that easy ass fighting normal brutes, I'm gonna help you how to identify them.
1. If the brute has 1 Speed and does NOT have a high HP (because Immortality also drops stats) they might have armor
2. If they're apperance is slightly alltered. Here is an image showing which brutes are with armor and which are without.
Difrences
Men

Barbarian: Gets a kind of boots, fur on it's back and pads on its shoulders.
Ranger: Gets gloves and patches on it's pants.
Legionary: Gets a cape and plates on it's shoulder.
Heavy Armor: Gets more armor on it's arms and waist.
Saiyan: Gets pads on it's feet and shoulders.
Robot: Gets more armor covering it's arms and abdomen.
Boxer: Gets protectove gear on it's torso and arms.
Women

Barbarian: Gets shoulder pads and a type of boots
Ranger: Gets gloves and patches on it's pants
Fur Girl: Gets more fur on wrist and feet.
Light Skirt: Gets boots.
Tight: Outfit longer
Heavy Armor: Gets more armor on it's shoulders and legs
Boxer: Gets a kind of proteccion on it's wrist
