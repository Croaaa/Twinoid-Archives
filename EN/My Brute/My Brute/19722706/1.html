Sword is much better. Take it if you are offered.

Broadsword is very common. It is decent, but nothing special.

Sword is rare. It hits very hard and even harder with weapons master. At higher levels it gets less good though, as opponents start to dodge it frequently. You should try to combine it with fists of fury, relentless and determination if you are offered those.

by all means take broadsword if you are given Weapons Master or a Shield. Counter Attack and Hostility also work very well with the weapon. Only take it if you have a brute that can utilize this weapon in combination with your others. Example: don't take it if you're stocking up on heavy weapons, throwing weapons, long reach weapons like trident and whip. broadsword can be good but you're going to have to build it up and be extremely lucky
