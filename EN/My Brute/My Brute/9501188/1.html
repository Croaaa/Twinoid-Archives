You have to keep in mind that there's a slight chance that the same skill/weapon will be offered again if you skip it the first time. Also you're often faced with the option of choosing between 2 set of stats and the order in which you get them can totally change your destiny.

Say your brute has a base Strength stat of 3, you remember it having 6 Strength in a previous destiny so when you're given the option to choose between 3Strength vs 2Endurance & 1Strength you jump in and choose 3Strength. At first it seems like everything is ok but on following level ups you're given entirely different choices. Why? Because in your previous rank you chose 2Endurance & 1Strength and then 2Strength & 1Agility on the following level up. See where I'm getting at? It's really easy to mess up your destiny because of a simple mistake like this.

It's called forking and it has been explained 100 times but people still seem to be making the same error over and over again. People have suggested favoriting the last fight before ranking up. This isn't the most ideal solution to keep track of your brute's progress because of what I've mentioned earlier. The easiest way is to note down your choices in a .txt file. It's hard to remember all your choices by heart, especially when it sometimes takes months to rank up.

I'm not saying this couldn't be a bug though without any real proof I'm 99.9% it can be avoided by paying a little more attention. I'm yet to see someone who has taken notes of their choices and had this problem happen to them.
