I don't know how you people get to those numbers, but I'll try to explain the mechanics again, okay?

- as a non-paying user you can have up to 10 fights per day, but fighting stops after 3 deaths.
This means you can have up to 10 Experience Points per day. If you die the third time in your 7th fight, that's it. If you fought 10 times and won them all, that's it, too.

- as a paying user you can send your brute to the hospital once per day. Then the above procedure can be repeated once more. This means you can get up to 20exp/day.
