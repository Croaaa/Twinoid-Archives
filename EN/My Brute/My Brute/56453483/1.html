Something I wanted to address is that Immortality raises Endurance, not HP in itself.

Before I continued to answer, I wanted to explain the differences. A brute begins with a base HP of 50 HP, and any additional HP is due to Endurance points assigned to the corresponding brute. So a brute that has 68 HP to begin with has his 50 HP and 3 Endurance points (each point equal to 6 HP).

The increase of 250% endurance is based on the amount of Endurance points you have, which are later translated into HP. I would like to see if you could tell us how much HP your brute began with.
