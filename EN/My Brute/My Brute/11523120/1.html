So I used this method back when I actually watched all my fights and I think this method would work well for you guys too.

When you login, just leave your account signed in and refresh in your cell periodically while paying close attention to your challenger list, when new people fight you simply review the match and decided weather or not that person is beatable for your brute. Once you've ratted out the brutes that have a advantage over your brute simply jot their names down in notepad and avoid those brutes once you see them in the challenger page.

I normally fight the brutes just after a brute loses to me so eventually you'll have plenty of brutes names down to better your chances of getting 10 exp.
