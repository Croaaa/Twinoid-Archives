Lean here some info for you about pets:

- When you receive a pet, you have an extra AI player that helps fight alongside your brute.
- When you receive a pet, your brute's total health decreases by a certain amount. I don't know how much but the bigger or stronger the pet, the more total HP your brute loses. You can get more HP later by picking Endurance in your destiny.
- All pets are good as an added distraction, or added damage.

Regular Dog:
- Brown colored dog.
- Total life of 14 HP.
- Can hit 1-3 times per turn.
- Pretty fast.
- Low damage, average damage about 3-4.
- Low evasion rate.
- Best used when you have multiple dogs.
- Can have up to 3 regular dogs.
- Most common pet.

Wolf/Panther:
- White colored pet.
- Fast pet.
- Can hit about 2-5 sometimes 7 hits in one turn.
- Moderate damage, average 10-11 damage.
- Has about 20 HP.
- Can only have 1 wolf unless you have the Hypnosis spell.
- Moderate evasion rate.
- Rare pet.

Bear:
- Brown bear.
- High HP, I think almost 200 HP.
- High damage, average damage 20-30.
- Very slow pet. Usually attacks every 2-3 Brute turns.
- Low evasion rate.
- Very rare pet. Mostly likely more rare than wolf.
