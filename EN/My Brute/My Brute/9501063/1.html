Alicion
Well, it depends. Theres always a surprise when you level up.
It gets harder when you level up to a different rank. It starts to get tougher and that means you have to choose the best stuff for your brute.
I'd recommend these skills, weapons and pets:

Skills:

Hammer
Tragic Potion
Shield
Cry of the damned
Hypnosis
Armor
Thickened Skin
Bomb
Herculean Strength + Martial Arts (Your brute does a lot of damage when they punch, seriously).

Pets:

Bear
Wolf
(Dogs are great sidekicks, but the usually die when your opponent uses the Bomb skill, if they have it).

Weapons:

Flail
Whip
Shuriken (This kinda helps).
Stone Hammer (I think thats what its called).
Celery thing (With this your opponent can be hit several times!)
Hatchet (Kinda good.)

Things I would stay away from:

Bumps: This weapon is easily dodged, even with brutes that have 2 agility.
Net: This is useful sometimes, but if you have bomb skill you wont want to touch this. (It releases the opponent or pet from the net...VERY bad!)
Vitality: It is good, but trust me, doesn't work that well when it comes to higher brute levels.

If anyone else has any other weapons, pets or skills they think are good or bad, please post below if that is okay.

I'm glad I can help! I understand that it gets tougher when your brute reaches a higher rank, because I currently have 1 brute at Plaster Puller and the other at Finger Flicker.
Hoped this helped!
