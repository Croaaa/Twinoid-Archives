wangthu
Most the weapons have their uses.

The fan is great if you have high strength and something like shock or sabotage, otherwise it's stupid and pointless. But then if someone has a lot of speed and can counterattack a lot it's worse than useless.

The bumps is not so bad as people make out. If you go against someone with a lot of speed it's better to have fewer attacks that hit harder. But if your speed is already low and your strength is high then since it misses quite a bit it's a bad idea for you.

So the basic idea as someone put it is strength gets beaten by agility/dodge, agility gets beaten by speed, and then strength beats speed, in an endless circle. So that fan or shuriken might be awsome for some guys, character ruining for others.
