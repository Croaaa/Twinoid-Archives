Cripto

I think minitroopers is better
*
1. There are 140 perks in the game. That's a hell of a alot of perks. What is great about this is that not only does this mean there are countless skills to look forward to and ones which are more likely after reaching a certain level, but it also makes it so that everyone's armies are very different because of the vast amount of perks available. It gives each army a sense of uniqueness and makes things much more competitive. Whereas, in mybrute, hardly any brute seems 'unique' anymore. For every brute we've seen, we've probably seen a dozen similar ones before it doing the exact same thing that one is doing. There are a few brutes that I can truly say are 'unique'.
*
2. You also get a perk at EVERY level. The levelling curve in mybrute is designed so that the higher your level, the less likely it is to get a perk. Whereas, in minitroopers, you are always rewarded with a skill or a weapon at every level. This way, levelling your troopers is always fun.
*
3. The troopers are not complete morons too, unlike brutes. Everything in the mybrute fights are determined by luck. They take out their weapon, throw their weapon and use their skills whenever the outcome of the dice requires them to. However, the artificial intelligence in minitroopers is designed so that the troopers are able to act differently and actively respond to what is going on in the fights, to an extent. It's more obvious at higher levels and with more troopers.
