It seems the game defines the brutes for animations by its looks. If both brutes look the same, the game does every combat animation (by both brutes) on one brute, so it looks like an imaginary battle. If you notice, in the first fight, the left brute has no dagger, but pulls one out. In reality, it's the other brute, who pulls the dagger, only the animation is played on the left one. Not the left brute attacks its pets, but the right brute does - only the animation is played on the wrong brute. That's also the reason why both sit down at the end.

Very interesting glitch indeed.
