Noodle Bowl - Really good when drawn, but has a low draw rate.
Tennis Racket - Pretty good with decently high strength about 15+ but has a high discard rate.
Scimitar - Really good with agility FOS and WM.
Mug - Excellent with MA which is common so if you get mug hold onto it !
Keyboard - Mediocre but not bad, not great either, has a decently high multi attack.
Piou Piou - Mediocre but isn't bad, has a high discard rate.
Trombone - Really good with 6th sense.
Leek - Excellent with high strength about 15+ but has a low draw rate.
From what I've observed.
