Hello people.

I don't know how many fellow players from the old days are still playing this game (if any), but I just had to laugh about something with my "First-BL-Chaser-Brute" Blender22 and I wanted to share it intentionally just to tell it somebody who knows mybrute.muxxu. Not to brag about, but to tell how long I am here (with 3 breaks): Although my brute Blender22 is number 80782 which means she was created as brute number 80782, she was able to quickly rise due to her Flail which she gets as her first choice and then I was able to fight for the title "First BL". Now to finally get to the point: One reason I had diffeculties to be confident in Blender22 and "her" attempt to win was that Blender22 was missing something important: Martial Arts. We all did 20 fights the day - if possible, but mistakes were expensive rankingwise, trained tons of brutes to find reinforcements and a spy and nobody ever missed one day and we all paid.

How she won Haemoglobin: http://mybrute.muxxu.com/fight/221123...

In my opinion you only have a top brute when it has MA. Without is a fail, you NEED it.

That's how I always planned my brutes, MA is a must and when the brute don't has it until 20: New destiny or fork. And the brute that had to try to win the title (inofficial I think) was fighting without it and there weren't many possebilities to fork and I coulnd't risk new destiny as the Flail worked great.

No question, I tried everything to find it - we had time to search, as we had to wait weeks until the next tournament started, once you won yourself to the next title. No chance, it never came to me on Blender22...

Now I returned here to train 10 of my 29 brutes, some, because I think they grow strong and some which I want to find a destiny and to finally find immortality and the mug, the only 2 things I never found.

And today Blender22 found Martial Arts. Phew, finally I can level her although she would be a nice ear-cutter, but I have another ear-cutter and I think he will pass her quickly so her long deserved rest can start today. But she wants to grow and she will.

I don't have time like years ago, so normally I'm only here for 5 minutes skipping through fights and 2 or 3 tournaments.

Short version: Today after many years my first brute Blender22 finally found martial arts - she went through a lot to achieve it, so in dedication to my little girl I wrote down that boring story xD
