TALENTS:
These skills are activated manually before fighting and cost 1 death, and they stay active for 1 day/period.

They are not active and do not work in tournaments nor are visible in the description in combats.

Cook
Your opponent suffer 2% damage (rounded up) at the end of their shares. Glows green like being poisoned.

Spy
Can see the weapons and the skills of of any brute.

Backup aka Reinforcement
This let your brute join in any other of your brutes fights, remains for 2-3 attacks. Your other brute must be higher level then the guy with backup aka reinforcement skill.

Saboteur
Your opponent will lose one of his weapons as soon as he draws.

SUPER Abilities:
These skills are special actions that can occur a limited number of times during a fight.

Bomb
Your brute throws a bomb, dealing 15-25 damage. Can not be dodged or reduced by armor. Can occur up to 2 times during a fight.

Fierce Brute
You make a powerful charge in combat. You double the damage of your next assault. No effect with throwing weapons. May hit on weapons with several combos. Can only occur 1 time during a fight.

Cry of the Damned
Scares animals away. (Dog's, Wolfs, Bears) 50% chance of success. Can occur up to 2 times during a fight.

Flash Flood
Your brute jumps in the air and throws half of your weapon arsenal at your opponent. Can only occur 1 time during a fight.

Net
A net that can be thrown at your opponent, that prevents him from move/dodge/parry until your next blow. Can also be thrown at his animal/pet that will last until the end of the round. Can only occur 1 time during a fight.

Hypnosis
Hypnos your opponents animals, making them join your force instead. Can only occur 1 time during a fight.

Hammer
Grab your opponent, jump up in air, wrestle him down. 60 base damage. Is effected by Fierce Brute and Strength, not reduced by armor, can not be avoided. Can only occur 1 time during a fight.

Tragic Potion
Heals your brute for 25-50% of his health. Also cure poisons from Cook. Can only occur 1 time during a fight.

Tamer
The brute can eat a dead pet. Even his own. Your brute recover 20-50% energy depending on the quality of the pet. Can occur 4 times during a fight.

Thief
This skill allows you to steal the weapon off your opponent, and use it against him.

SPECIALITIES:
Specialities are passive skills.

6th Sense
Your sharp sense allows you to hit your opponent before he hit you. Odds: 10%

Feline Agility
Adds +3 agility and +50% agility. The 50% increase also acts on increases stats to level changes.

Armor
It's an armor. +5 armor, speed -10%, slightly changes your brute's appearence.

Martial Arts
Chuck Norris classes paid off! Unarmed damage increased by 100%

Ballet Shoes (Ballerina shoes)
Your opponent can not reach you. The first shot of each is automatically avoided. If the first shot is a great non-evasive, the following will be avoided.

Shield
A shield to parry the blows... Parry chance: 45% (can be lost during a blow)

Shock
Chance to disarm your opponent for each hit. 50% chance to disarm.

Counter Attack
Each hit by your opponent, you have a chance to automatically hit him back when you parry. Also increase parry chance 10%

Herculean Strength
Adds +3 strength and +50% strength. The 50% increase also acts on increases stats to level changes.

Lightning Bolt (Bolt of Lightning)
Adds +3 speed and +50% speed. The 50% increase also acts on increases stats to level changes.

Bodybuilder (called Strong Arms in old version)
You are the master with heavy weapons. -25% interval between shots of heavy weapons.

Immortal
Your health has increased humongous. +250% endurance, -25% speed, -25% strength, -25% agility.

Relentless
Makes you hit that destination. +30% chance to hit.

Resistant
By every blow, you can not lose more then 20% of your health bar. Every blow inflicts maximum of 1/5 of life, rounded to nearest. The brute flashes yellow when it takes effect.

Untouchable
You avoid more easily. +30% chance to dodge.

Weapons Master
You cause more damage with your sharp weapons. +50% damage with sharp weapons.
Sharp weapons include: knife, broadsword, long sword, scimitar

Reconnaissance
Your ready to take your time, but when you start nothing stops you. +5 speed, +150% speed, -200 initiative.
2 speed --> 17 speed. 3 speed --> 20 speed. 11 speed --> 40 speed.

Toughened Skin
Armor +2. Does not work against projectiles and bombs.

Determination
When hit does not blow, you make another one. +70% chance to attack again.

Hostility
When you are given the chance, you strike back for free! +33% chance to strike back against target.

Regeneration
Allows you to have 4 deaths per day, instead of 3.

First Strike
You are always the first to strike. Initiative +200

Lead Skeleton
Reduces the damage taken from blunt weapons. -30% damage.
Blunt weapons include: morning star (mace), flail, hammer, bumps, mammoth bone, leek

Survival
The fatal blows leaves you with 1 life instead.

Iron Head
When you take a shot, your opponent have a chance of losing his weapon. +30% chance of losing weapon.

Fists of Fury (called Tornado of Blows in old my brute)
And you hit, and you hit, and you hit.... Chance of combo: +20%

Sabotage
Every shot allows you to destroy your opponents weapons, even weapons not wielding.

Vitality
Your life increases! +3 endurance, +50% endurance, the 50% also increases on stats and level ups.
