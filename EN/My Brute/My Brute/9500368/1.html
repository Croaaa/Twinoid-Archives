Reconnaissance was the worst thing that could have ever happened to one of my brutes. Although you get awesome speed boost, your initiative is decreased considerably which means that any opponent you face will be allowed to attack multiple times before you are finally allowed to attack, basically giving them the skill first strike for free. Against a brute that actually has the skill first strike, this would of course be disastrous because the effect will be doubled.

Having reconnaissance also almost certifies a loss against a brute with a wolf. Seriously, your chances of winning at a low level against a brute with a wolf would drop down to a wooping 10%. The wolf is already able to attack multiple times. Add the fact that reconnaissance will give them addition multiple opportunities to attack, the battle would be over before it has even begun.

Of course, there are skills such as immortality or armor which would best compensate for the fact that reconnaissance would turn your brute into a helpless puppy at the start of a battle. High agility stats may also help to survive the opening barrage of attacks from the opponent.
