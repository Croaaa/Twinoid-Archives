After all this time playing the game, my buddy Francisco and I have been working on the Spanish version of the wiki and he found out the exact data values that affect the weapons in this game, as well as the exact values for other skills like Bodybuilder and Lead Skeleton.

These statistics include stats like interval, disarm, multi hit and base damage, and also apply to the use of fists in a fight. These statistics have been implemented to the Spanish wiki in a cute little table here, although I've been too lazy to implement them to the English wiki. We've been busy doing different updates to the wiki like adding actual embed shockwave fights to pages like this one, or trying to add a GFX generator to the appearance page.
