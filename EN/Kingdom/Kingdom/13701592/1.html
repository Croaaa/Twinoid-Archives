Because you're playing on a private map. The one who set up the map in your group has set map speed to slow. Normal speed is 30m and fast 15m. Time differences apply not just to turns but wherever time is involved, including how fast rare resources become available to collect, how fast you travel from one node to another, battle progress etc..
_
There are also public maps you can play. To play these maps you don't have to be in a group. Public maps are always at normal speed (30m). These maps are shown on the map selection screen as the private map you joined. Private maps appear in a parenthesis.
