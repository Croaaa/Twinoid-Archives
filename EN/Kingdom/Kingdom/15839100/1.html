In my book the best unit is the one that beats most enemies. Mounted Archer is the best together with the war machines because that unit loses only to Knight. War machines the same because they lose only to Mounted Archer (provided they are used on the correct side). Knight comes third, because he loses only to the war machines and Archer (although barely vs Archer).


ALL other units can be replaced. You can have Pikemen's bonus against Cavalry (minus Mounted Archer) by using war machines, and even better bonus too. You can have Archer's bonus against Pikemen and Knights by using war machines here too. You can also replace Mounted Knights strength against soldiers by using, what else.. war machines..


But what you can't replace? Mounted Archer's ability to neutralize all enemy bonuses, hence losing only to Knights due to their larger health, you also can't replace Knight's huge health, being the only one to beat Mounted Archer, and of course you can't replace war machines, with their huge bonus against ANY one but Mounted Archers...


Now, why make other units then? Why make Pikemen, Archers, Cavaliers.. etc? Simply because, not everyone is capable of mass producing war machines.. if you are, then believe me, you DON'T need any of these units, just those 3 I mentioned above. Which means, when you're still a small player with limited strength, then it's nice to have resources available and upgrade to ANY thing possible, it will instantly give your army more strength.
