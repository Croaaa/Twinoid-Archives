Basically, you should place just enough troops so it's not possible for him to win in a battle on the capital should he revolt. Catapults are recommended, as they count as attacking when the follower revolts. Another option is to place a lot of MKs or knights in the town, as they have a large "scare factor" which can prevent the follower from even starting a revolt.
