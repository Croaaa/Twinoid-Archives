Question about negative trade, should i better leave catapults or balistas rather than soldiers?do balistas abandon if theres no money in your capital?
I wanted to mention something here when I read this. Never garrison a village with catapults if you can help it. They are little better than basic soldiers at defending. Their true value comes when attacking locations as they have a 200% bonus. Assign any and all catapults to your generals.

I would also suggest assigning balistas to any villages that border aggressive players as by doing so, you'll benefit from their 200% bonus when defending locations. While they make no difference in trade, they can have a benefit if the location is attacked. Having them stationed at crucial locations could mean the difference between remaining independent or becoming someone's follower.
