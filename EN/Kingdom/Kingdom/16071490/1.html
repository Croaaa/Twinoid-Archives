It's the Title you get for 10 Prestige. So you need to take some villages to achieve this.

Titles are given as you take villages & earn Prestige & are as follows:

3 = Sir
7 = Baron
10 = Viscount
15 = Earl
20 = Marquis
30 = Duke
50 = Prince
75 = King
100 = Emperor
