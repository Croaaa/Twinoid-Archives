You will be once your citizens die off too. This may take from a few hours to a couple days, depending on population size and stored food. In your case, a few hours will be enough.


How it works:

Game will be taking turns for you without producing anything in return, so you'll be losing food and gold to feed your citizens and pay your troops. Eventually you'll run out of both and your kingdom will start collapsing. You'll lose your troops and your capital will go from tower to shacks until it's done. When you're down to the last citizen you'll die.
