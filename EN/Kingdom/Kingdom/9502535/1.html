Go to Capital, under ACTIONS click Build then you'll see a list of buildings. Those in grey shade are not available because you don't meet the requirements. The requirements are shown in each of the boxes respectively for every building. The first building you always start with, is palace, then the rest of buildings if you meet its requirements. So you'll notice that you need horses to build stables, these you find on the map.

As far as Cotton you can't make that on your own, you've got to find it on the map. Metal you can make on your own, but you'll need a building called Cauldron before you can do this, and the way it works is burning wood in exchange. But be prepared to consume so much wood for it that it's not worth it, unless your Cauldron is level 4 or 5. Otherwise, just like cotton, metal can also be found on the map. Speaking of levels, each time you make a building, that's not the end, as you can further upgrade it down the road, which in return makes it more efficient. Highest level is 5 and you need to progress quite a lot before you reach it.
