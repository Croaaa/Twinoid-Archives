A master doesn't get any information about planned revolt. When one gets an info about it, the revolt starts immediately so there is no time for a master to prepare.
When diplomacy fails to prevent revolting, the only way is to flood the follower with soldiers which can be very expensive. Because you either use the army to keep followers by force or help them expand and protect in case of need.
