Wriggly
Taxes are automatically subtracted from the positive trade of a follower.
These taxes are no longer accessible to the follower.
Not collecting has no positive benifit whatsoever for your follower.
The only way to reduce the taxes is to sever the direct links with your follower.
These means there can be no direct paths to your follower's kingdom that are completely under your control.

What I read and understood from the french forum is that tax is always automaticly taken from the follower after each turn he takes, so when the master collects the tax it isn't taken directly from followers treasury. The follower only gets a message that his master collected the tax and how much was it. That means that even if you don't collect the taxes, your follower is paying it anyway
