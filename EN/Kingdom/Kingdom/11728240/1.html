The first and most important thing is that you see that there is opportunity to make Sir, that means conquering 2 villages. As Master one cant play the game at all in my opinion..the difference to Sir is huge.

If you find yourself in the nearest capital to a big player and no savage near - bad spot
If you find yourself with 1 savage near and your master has all villages around you then you can think about gambling. A good master is often willing to give his follower a village to make Sir.

Age and health of your closest 3-4 neighbours also play a role, if you have little space but 3 neighbours in bad health then you might be sitting in a golden opportunity to become a big fish later on..

And basicly like Djisas says, there are more savage land than one might think..remember even big players die, when you join a map surrounded by an emperor just imagine the savage lands that appear when he dies

Patience is good to have sometimes..
