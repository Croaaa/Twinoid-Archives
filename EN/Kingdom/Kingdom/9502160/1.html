Launcelot

Depends on what makes it better for you. I mean if you wanna score high in the pantheon you need a spot that gives you room to grow but at the same time you want other players relatively close that you can possibly conquer. Perhaps you could even make a deal with a weaker player, offer him protection in exchange for his loyalty. He may not even have to pay you taxes if his kingdom isn't connected to yours, which is also in your best interest if you wanna score high. IMO you can earn much more from a strong follower who's using all his resources to expand for you. Everyone has his own playing style, I like to rely on my own strength and as such, I don't need taxes so I try to make it easier for my followers if I can. Using diplomacy you can achieve great things.


If better means staying out of trouble, then you can try finding a sparse map, there are a couple normal maps you can join.. but sparse maps will significantly limit your final prestige due to lack of followers. If you're ok with that, then this is your map.
