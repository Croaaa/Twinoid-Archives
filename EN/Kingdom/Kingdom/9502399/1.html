All the health potion does is increase your health from bad to good or good to excellent. It doesn't increase your chances of dying faster or anything, it just gets more expensive with each use. One thing for sure is that it definitely does give you more turns. I should be dead twice over by now, but I'm still alive and kicking because of these potions.

The time you have before your health degrades is really a matter of luck, and these potions don't appear to influence it at all. I managed to last for what felt like forever after taking my second potion. I then went through two more in no time after that. The only reason I'm not buying another is because it's now really expensive.

tl;dr: A potion won't affect how long you have until your health further degrades. The only decision you have to make is whether or not it's worth that many tokens to buy it.
