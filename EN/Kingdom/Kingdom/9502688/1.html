Famine and bankruptcy has nothing to do with how long you'll live, if you've read in the help section that they are related, you can just pretend like you never read it. It's a myth. Starve all you want, go broke 100 times you may still make a new record.

If you buy potions, you will extend your life on your current game and things will be great, but come next map you're gonna regret it and if not next map then the maps to come. To answer your question though, first potion costs 100 tokens, second is 150, third is 200 and so on, add 50 every next potion.
