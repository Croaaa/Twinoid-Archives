It's pretty much obvious but I'll try and translate it for you.

Commerce nul dans X1 tours
Trade = 0 after X1 turns.
Territoire rentable dans X2 tours
Territory becomes profitable after X2 turns. (you don't lose any more gold for that territory).
Commerce stable (Y or) dans X3 tours
Trade stabilizes after X3 turns, you gain Y gold per turn.
Et Z or dépensé (dans X2 tours)
Total amount of gold spent = Z (in X2 turns).
