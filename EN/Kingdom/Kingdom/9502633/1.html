Launcelot
Wasting food is good it means once you upgrade Granary you'll go above the square of population in a heart beat, so you'll get tons of citizens very fast. Also even if you don't upgrade yet, the more you keep food at full, the more citizens you will be getting of course not as fast as if you had upgraded, but still faster than if your food was half-full, so you may notice that while you should only have like 24-25 citizens with a level 2 granary (25x25=625), you can have 30-32 which is way above the square of a level 2 granary. But if you never have a full granary, regardless of its level, you will hardly ever see new citizens.

Wasting gold or wood on the other hand, that is bad because you could use the extra gold to recruit more troops if your economy is so strong, or you can capture more villages.. and you can use the extra wood to make war machines, buildings, metal.. of course if you're after the trophies, then the end justifies the means, so happy wasting.
