thought it was based on villages taken over

It is - you get 1 Prestige point per village captured, unless you start losing villages. Prestige does not go down if you lose villages. So it is really the maximum of total villages you ever commanded at any one time. Note that your Capital counts in this total.

If you have followers, their Prestige is added to yours. If you like, you could say that you also command the villages in their kingdom, but that is not really true. They are still in your kingdom though.
