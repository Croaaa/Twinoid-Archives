Yes grid resets when it runs out of moves.

Sometimes it's for worse and other times for the better. Sometimes when the grid doesn't help you (i.e. you need gold and there isn't much of it) you may find an opportunity to end it and make it reset. Doesn't always work because it's not so easy to run out of moves, but it's possible. So if you ever come to this situation and the grid is likely to run out of moves, you can try and help it. I've done it a couple times, made my last turns in a way that the grid would have to reset.
