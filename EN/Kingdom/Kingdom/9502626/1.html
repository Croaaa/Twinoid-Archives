You get the first unit of every five units of gold a gold a connected follower earns. What that means is that a follower with 5 trade pays one gold in taxes. A follower with 6 in trade will pay you two gold per turn. You always get your tax before their earnings go up.

I noticed this when I was Melian's follower before she died. When I reached 6 gold, I was paying two in taxes. If she were still my Master, she'd be earning the first 16 units of gold I get from my 80 gold in trade.

MagicDragon
The gold she earns on the production grid does not get taxed. What gets taxed is the gold she earns through the trade generated by her villages with each turn she takes on the production grid irrespective of what she actually produces. Look at her trade. Whatever that number is, divide it by five, and that is roughly what you'll get. Alternatively, you can look at your followers tab at your kingdom home page. It'll show how much gold you take with each follower's turn.
