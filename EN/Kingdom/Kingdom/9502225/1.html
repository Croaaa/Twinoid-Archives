If you want to raise trade, stacking up soldiers on less locations is better. For example, if you have 10 locations with 10 soldiers each, you will max out at 190 trade. If you have 20 locations with 5 soldiers each instead, you will only max out at 180.

However, it might be worth having a little less trade (in this case only 10 less), to add to your prestige. Prestige seems to govern more where you will place in the Pantheon than trade.
