If they haven't built a wall you can pass through their kingdom freely. That however is not enough to let you farm resources. Like GunnarD you need to take over the village or if it's already taken ask the owner permission to collect their resources. Either way you'll also need permission from the player who owns the town in between, so that it connects with your capital.
