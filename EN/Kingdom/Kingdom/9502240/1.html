After 89 years and 7 months of fighting battles, conquering villages and gathering resources I have finally passed away. Funny thing if you ask me since I have that number in my nickname. Since other players shared screenshots of their kingdom I thought I'd share some too, maybe some of you will find them interesting.

First I'll mention that my health turned Good at the age of 34, used a potion at 54 to bring it back to Excellent, Good again at 69, turned Bad at 86 and died only 3 years later. I've had a couple of famines at the beginning, also my soldiers deserted me a few times due to negative trade but all in all I think I was lucky to live this long.

This is my capital called Aberam in the land of Camiton and this is a map of what my kingdom used to look like. I managed to build every building and have 100 citizens, finding rare resources wasn't a problem either. Here you can see my generals, I've had one named Gluttony who sadly passed away in a very tight fight against a large army fierce barbarians. I've had an army of ~1000 troops that I used to carry around, conquer villages and raise their trade with (0 to 60 in 20 turns). Thanks to the lands I've conquered I was able not only to pay my army but actually make a profit. I haven't fought many battles but the ones I've fought were pretty great, ending up with -300 trade, soldiers deserting but never giving up until those capitals were captured. As I became older and my army would grow bigger my battles would become less and less interesting.

From now on it will only be a dream to have a kingdom like this again, there's a lot more competition on higher difficulty maps, fighting over a small piece of land and winning is a lot more rewarding now. That's what makes it fun though.
