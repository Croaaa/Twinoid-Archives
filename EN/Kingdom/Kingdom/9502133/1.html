Or, put another way, is the wall just around the capital or the whole of your domain?
Its around the whole of your kingdom - it prevents people from walking straight through your territory. They'll either have to fight their way through or ask for your permission.
And as far as I can tell, the only way to tell if someone has a wall is to try to send your general through their territory. When you click on "Move", it won't let you select something beyond their first village.

Upgraded walls slow down battles, meaning you have time to call your allies for help and gather more reinforcements.
