LauncelotA unit with 100% bonus causes twice as much damage than the damage it sustains. 150% bonus causes 3 times as much and 200% is 4 times as much.

That last one isn't quite accurate. The 200% bonus allows machines to inflict five points of damage for every point of damage sustained. It is a bonus on top of the one unit of damage already inflicted, thus they are five times more effective when used right.


Also, a bit of a tip for those who might not be aware, ballistas and catapults when used correctly against each other do not cancel out their bonuses. They counteract them. In other words, they inflict five damage for every five damage sustained when used correctly. As such, if you can afford it, it is best to attack machines with the counteracting one if you have numbers on your side.
