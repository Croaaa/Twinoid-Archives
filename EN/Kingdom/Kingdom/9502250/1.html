This is a suggestion to help improve and add an element of fun to the game. I realize that there is not much development going into this game currently, but it is an idea that I've always thought would be neat ever since my first day at Muxxu.

If a ruler has, as part of their kingdom, a village that sits on the banks of a body of water, it would be neat for them to be able to take their generals across the water on boats at greater speeds than on land to conquer other territories around the water. If the ruler owned another village, say, on the other side of the water, they could move across that much faster than already walking around.

This would give greater importance to conquering village situated around water and give players an advantage if they can successfully do so. In the real world, before flight, access to water was always highly contested and was responsible for involvement in wars.
