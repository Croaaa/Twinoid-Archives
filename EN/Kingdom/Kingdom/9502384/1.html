The barbarians control the location where your general is, not the places around it (including your capital). The barbarians are not friendly towards you which is why they won't let you pass through their land and you can only go back the way you came. Your options are:
defeat the barbarians so you can continue your journey;
turn around and find another route back to your capital;
send another general to defeat the barbarians;
ask another player to help you defeat them.

The lesson here, which I also learnt the hard way, is don't rely on the game to choose the best route for your generals. It has an annoying habit of taking them through barbarian territory when there's a perfectly good (and often quicker) route through your own kingdom. You sometimes need to break the journey down into two parts to ensure the general goes the way you want him to.
