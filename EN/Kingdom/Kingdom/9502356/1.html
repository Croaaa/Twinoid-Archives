Whether you find joining a map already in progress enjoyable or not depends on your attitude. If you expect to be able to conquer everyone in sight right from the beginning and are unwilling to co-operate with other players then you will find it very hard, if not impossible, to make progress and you won't enjoy it much.

If your capital is conquered early on, you don't have to regard your new master as an enemy. By working with them rather that plotting against them, you may find you progress a lot quicker in the game than you would otherwise, although that depends as much on your master's attitude towards you as a follower as it does on your attitude towards them. That's where the diplomacy options and PMs come in.

Part of the fun of the game is adapting to the changing circumstances and taking advantage of opportunities when you see them. Isn't it more satisfying to reach the rank of Marquis after a period of struggling for survival than it is to get there quicker and without opposition?
