Mounted Troops are available when you bild a Military Academy. That includes Mounted Knights and Mounted Archers. It doesn't matter whether or not you have an Archery or a Guard Tower first. They are completely differen troops. Yeah, the name implies that a Mounted Knight is simply a Knight with on a horse, but it's not. A stand-alone Knight has more health than a Mounted Knight, but lacks the bonus of +100% against regular soldiers. A Mounted Archer has slightly more health than an Archer, but lacks a distinct bonus against certain troops (like a regular Archer has), instead nulling out all bonuses against it.

tl;dr: They are different troops. The names are the only similarity. Oh, and as stated above, you need a Guard Tower to train regular Knights.
