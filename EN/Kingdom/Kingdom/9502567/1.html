Food production is always extremely difficult to manage. There are times when a certain resource will be very scarce on the grid, and food just happens to be one of them. I'd recommend keeping a large number of peasants and trying to keep your stock as close to full as possible. Also, avoid starting construction on anything until food starts showing up again. Don't be afraid to spend a bunch of turns on nothing but keeping enough food in stock. It's just one of the things you have to deal with in this game.
