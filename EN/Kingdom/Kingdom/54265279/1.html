There are two stages to a revolt. The first stage involves some in game calculations to determine if your troops are 'moralized' to start a revolt in the first place. The second stage is the actual battle.


The calculations are based on number of your troops in your capital vs your master's, number of your troops in total vs your master's and RNG. Even if you outnumber his troops stationed in your capital, if he has a vastly larger army overall, you are still likely to fail. In this stage, only quantity, not quality of troops are considered. Unit bonuses are only applied during battle (stage 2)
