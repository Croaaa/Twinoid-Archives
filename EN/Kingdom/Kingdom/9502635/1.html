General rule of thumb, don't bother upgrading the archery range at all, because archers are almost never worth it and the storage building is the factory, which doesn't cost any cotton, and that should be built up whenever you get close to cotton cap. Next, only bother upgrading stables or forges for the increased horse/metal storage capacity. So basically, ask yourself whether you're prepared to pay a little rare resources so you can hold more at once. After that, you should be looking to get military academy as high as you can possibly get it, again it costs no rare resources and MAs are vital units that counter bal/cat spam. MKs are helpful, but I find that bals/cats do a much better job against soldiers, and a lot of other things as well. Only bother getting guard tower if you actually have so many resources you can build it to level 4 or 5 where it is actually worth it, and have so much metal you can produce a large amount of knights. However, I do believe that you can do without knights in most situations...
