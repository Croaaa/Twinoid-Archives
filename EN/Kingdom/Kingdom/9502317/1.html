All soldiers in villages cost you 1 gold. The first soldier in a village earns you 1 gold, each one after that earns 2, shown as the Trade value per village. That Trade has to build up to that maximum value. Add more troops temporarily to speed up that growth

Some soldiers in your Capital are 'free', this depends on the level of your Barracks. Some soldiers with your Generals are free, this depends on the Reputation of the General.

Look on your Home Page. The Generals tab shows the costs of your Generals troops, the Kingdom tab shows the costs and income from your villages
