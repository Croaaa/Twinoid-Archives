It is unlimited until you line up your 100th citizen. There will be no more icons after that, except those you may already have on the grid. If they are 3 and you manage to line them up before grid resets, you'll have 101, if they are 6 you'll have 102 and so on. I have confirmed that on 2 different maps where I didn't get another icon for a good 50 years after my 100th. On my beginner map I didn't know there was a limit so I remained with 100 and didn't try, but on my next Emperor run I tried to accumulate icons before lining up my 100th, managed to accumulate about 6 and then grid got reset and they were all gone. Again didn't get another icon for at least 50 years after that. And then on my last Emperor run I stopped at 97 or 98 citizens and died 74 since I decided it wasn't worth my money, although there was great potential. That being said, no more pots for me, unless I am only few villages away from making Emperor and I get bad health, in which case I might take a pot or two just so I make Emperor and that's it.


So technically, until proven otherwise, it is possible to reach 104 or more, as long as you have the patience and luck to save your icons, while still lining up food and gold. Get 15 icons on the grid while you have 99 citizens and you'll reach 104. And I said until proven otherwise because for all I know there may be a limit to the number of citizen icons on the grid. But they do stop coming after your 100th citizen, no doubt about it.
