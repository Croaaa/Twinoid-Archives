You've obviously attacked a village with your General & won, your General then will automatically defend (which he's doing now).

Click on withdraw & it will give you the options of 'move', 'defend' or ' troops/soldiers'.

Move, is just that. allows you to send him where-ever.

Defend, put's him back to defensive state.

Troops, allows you to deploy or remove your troops from that village.
