Well, as with everything, from food and gold production, to wood and recruiting, they tend to increase exponentially the further you progress into the game. More food brings more population, more population brings more of everything and everything means more gold, more wood, more recruiting and more trade. But you're right, you will have to make more recruiters down the road if you want to boost trade further and you will also require a decent amount of swords, but they will come up, sooner or later. So in other words you may struggle to build your first 200 points of trade, but the next 200 will come twice as fast, then the next 200 will be 4 times as fast, next 200 will be 8 times as fast, etc.. typically that's about how it happens, but then again a bad grid, or bad management may slow you down.

Getting into tough wars will also slow down trade progress, especially if you require reinforcements, mainly on harder maps. But that's the beauty of the game, you may have to change plans 100 times you can never be sure about what and how you're gonna do it.
