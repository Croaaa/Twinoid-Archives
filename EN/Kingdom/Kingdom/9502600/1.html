Lvl 2 for the following is enough in the short run Forge, Stables, Factory, Archery Range it allows you to have 100 of each rare resource and lowers the cost of unimportant units...

Tower upgrading is a huge effort, a lvl 2 tower allows making knights at 10 iron cost or 12 at lvl 12, but you have so much iron that a lvl 1 tower should do for a while...
Workshops drops price of machine and increases workers production, an early upgrade to have...

The academy allows MA's and MK's at 6-5-4-3 cost with each level, make you own calculations and see if they are worth...

Wall lvl 1 prevents free access to your capital, lvl 2 and above just prolong the battles duration in your capital, not really worth it when there is no one to attack you, just lvl 1 is fine for starters...

Construction site is crucial, it produces food and gold when not building anything, and trust me its worth it, it pays having a lot of workers, more than even traders...

Only upgrade the cabin when you start wasting wood, before that you are delaying other construction...

With 3 iron mines, who needs a cauldron??
You will never have enough wood anyway...

Hope it covers everything in a reasonable and understanding way...
