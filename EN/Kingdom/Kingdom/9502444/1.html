The way I see it my Character is the King of the land and he sits comfortably on his throne while he has other nobles and family members go out and fight his wars as Generals.

The only reason I came up with this idea is, that this way we will be making some history and we will have heroes and villains who will never be forgotten by their names...
The whole "king and his family" idea was developed by me, because it will allow players to change their style of playing with every character... and we will have living world instead of the world, where someone dies, and reincarnates somewhere else... it was logic for me, that when there is age implemented in the game, we will be playing mortal characters represented by the generals, instead of some universal entities ruling their kingdoms...

And another thing is, that you will be more careful to go into a war, when there will be risk, you will die, instead of wasting some random generals... When we want to play with more diplomacy and role-play, then we should try to act more realistic... in old times it was quite common that king was leading his army into the battle... but he wasnt throwing his armies into a fight, he couldnt won, or where he could die....
