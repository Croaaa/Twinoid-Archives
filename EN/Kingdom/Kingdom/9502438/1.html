Your health decreases throughout the game, and it's pretty much impossible to know how long you have until it decreases again. It's true that famines and bankruptcies will accelerate the deterioration of your health, but it will still go down without them.

I'd recommend just not letting it get to you for now. Keep playing the game as normal, and you'll eventually get a message saying that your health has dropped again. You really don't need to be concerned with your health until it's Bad.

Scorpian
Bankruptcy and famine influence the deterioration of your health. They don't determine it. Your health level actually deteriorates at random outside of that. It's possible that you can live for twenty years before your health decreases to Good, but only for another two years before it drops to Bad. That doesn't mean you're going to die after you take your next turn though. I have seen people live for around forty years on Bad before they finally die. I've also seen them drop off after two or three. There's just no way to know for sure.

Here's something to think about: the ability of your potion to keep you alive, and the supposed "randomness" of the next decrease in your health, may be affected by any past periods of Famine or Bankruptcy.

So, if you had four or five of these incidents, it's possible -- not proven, of course, but possible -- that your particular Health Potions may be less effective.
