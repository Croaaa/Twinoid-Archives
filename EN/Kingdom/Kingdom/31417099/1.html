The unit you speak of is called the mounted knight.


The names are just screwed up in this game because it's more or less all translated from french. The game uses both knight and paladin to describe the same unit, so there's no "we call it, you call it". It's all the same.


If you're unsure of anything, your safest bet is to refer to the french wiki and match up the unit pictures in-game.


Anyways, you needn't be too concerned about knights of either kind if you're researching tactics on these forums. All you need to know are catapults "cats", ballistae and mounted archers "MA"
