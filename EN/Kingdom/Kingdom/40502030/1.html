Hello,

How can I reassign new troops to my new villages?

Take a general with more than 1 recruit / machine / other unit to a village you've taken and press the button "Troops & equipment on the right side of the screen. There you will be able to set the needed ammounts of troops in a village and general. Don't forget to click the "OK" button.
