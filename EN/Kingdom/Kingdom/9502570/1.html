I am assuming this means the following:

If you line up 3 and get 1 free turn, the turn does not age you and your turn count does not decrease. Your rate of aging in real time is unchanged.

If you line up 4, 5, or 6, you get 2, 3, or 4 free turns. The first free turn does not age you. The additional free turns go into the regular turn pool and do age you normally. Your rate of aging as you take those turns is unchanged but since you earn turns faster your rate of aging in real time is faster.

That is what they mean by "extra turns age fast". You age just as fast in game time but are able to take turns more quickly and so age faster in real time.
