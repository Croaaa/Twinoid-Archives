It's very possible to have a master that's not even connected to you. Some people go through savage villages far away and just conquer barbaric villages in the hopes of other players being assigned those villages as their capitals.

Also, the answer is yes, you can be placed in a village with a master from the start. In fact, as you progress through the game and start playing harder maps, the likeliness of getting a capital that isn't already owned by somebody else is very small.

That connection error you're getting is likely related to issues on your end connecting to the site. Clear your cache and restart your browser. See if that helps.
