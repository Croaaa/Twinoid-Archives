Launcelot
Been there before, until I decided that soldiers are kind of useless for battle except for raising trade and since they're easy to make. When you're not in a hurry and don't mind spending a lot of time in a battle, then you can bring soldiers, to fight opponent soldiers. When you wanna finish it quickly, bring catapults and catapults only.

Example 1: 20 soldiers vs 30 soldiers. You have the 30, so you're gonna win. But there is no way the battle is gonna end soon, could take hours, and give your opponent plenty of time to recruit more and make your life a living hell.

Example 2: 20 soldiers vs 10 catapults. You have the catapults, so again you're gonna win. But the difference is, that this battle is gonna finish in 1 hour and 15 minutes, which gives your opponent very little time to recruit. And note what I said, you don't even need 30 units, 10 cats are enough and you won't lose any either.

Bottom line... when you wanna finish it quickly, only catapults. You bring in 50 catapults, he'll need 300 soldiers to beat you, less if he has ballistas but, how many units do you really think he can bring with 0 trade? Screw up his gold eventually? End of story.

And did I say only catapults?
