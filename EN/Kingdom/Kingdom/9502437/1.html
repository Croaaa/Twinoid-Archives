You can conquer ANYthing! (:^D) Except, that is, your own Followers, whose generals appear as Green to you. Any villages you conquer will be added to your Prestige, as well as any capitals of your Followers (although their conquests do not count).

The difference between Savage and Barbaric villages is that Barbaric ones are once-conquered, and still have troops left in them (which can be returned to their previous King if they come and attack, and then get an option to have them rejoin). So, you'll have a battle to capture Barbaric villages (if you're not their previous king), based on the number of troops still there.
