Here is a tip for you...

Trade is calculated when you click on your grid, i.e. take a turn (it seems it is also calculated when you change the number of soldiers at the village, but this seems logical to me, not a bug).

Your General(s) move in real time.

So, gather up spare soldiers with the General(s) and move them to the trouble spots. DO NOT take any turns until the soldiers are stationed at the village. Then catch up with the turns you did not take, as now the new soldiers are included in the trade calculation. In other words, be VERY patient!
