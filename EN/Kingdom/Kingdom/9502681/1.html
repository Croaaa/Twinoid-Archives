Your friend selects view battle battle comes up, right side options, join attack/defense. Always remember on the left are the attackers, on the right the defenders; you can see that on the map as well not just on the battlefield; the general who wields his sword to the right is the attacker, the general who wields to the left is the defender.

Reputation as it was well put, is indeed divided between winning generals but few days ago there was a fight going on between 4 generals, it was 3 vs 1. The 3 generals belonged to 2 players, player A had 1 and played B had the other 2 and they were the defenders. Defenders won, player A earned 5 points, player B earned 7. I suspect that was because player B brought 2 generals to the fight so he had to have a bonus. Not confirmed, only a guess. But normally reputation is divided equally from what I have seen, at least when both players have brought the same amount of generals.
