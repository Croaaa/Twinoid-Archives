I had to find out that provoking affects all generals standing in one point, there is no option to choose the general you want to provoke. This rule applies even to generals of your declared friends (!), so make sure all your friends are gone by the time you want to provoke, or you will fight against friends AND enemies combined.

e.g. if a party of two generals (from two different players) want to force a battle by provoking, one has to leave, the other one has to provoke, then the general who left has to cancel his movement again and can join the battle.
