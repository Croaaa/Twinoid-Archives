You have to match up the three pieces of wheat to create food. More peasants just means that you create more every time you match them up. Having a lot of peasants at the start is good though. Scorpian
The tutorial sets you up with the basics of the game. It skips over several details that help your kingdom flourish. I guess it just assumes that you'll learn those on your own and try them out in the next kingdom or something.

Anyway, a farm is more important than a barracks at this point. The farm will increase the productivity of your peasants. That should at least keep your citizens from dying. At some point though, you'll need to build a granary. That will increase the maximum amount of food you're able to store (I believe to 250). Try to focus on keeping as much food as possible in store at all times. That way, when food stops appearing on the grid, you'll be able to last a while.
