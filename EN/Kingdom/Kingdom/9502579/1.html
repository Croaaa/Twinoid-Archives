Seems like you are attempting to send a message through the "write to them" button which appears when you click on someone's capital. Yes this will not work.

To send a message you have 2 options.

First, go to the player's information page, it's the one that comes up when you click on their name in the map. That page displays their age, health and other info. Above that you will see some icons, one of them is an envelope, click that and it will work.

Second, the same envelope icon in their profile page (either muxxu or twinoid profile, both will work).
