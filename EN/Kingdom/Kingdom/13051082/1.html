So just wanted to share what I've found regarding how resources respawn. To my understanding, every map has a specific number of resources per type (metal, cotton, horses) and it always maintains that number. That means when you deplete a resource point, it will be respawned somewhere else but in random quantity not necessarily the same. I've also found that usually they respawn in horizontally opposite directions across the left and right side of the map. So if there's cotton on the left side and you're somewhere on the right, as soon as it's depleted by the player extracting it, there's a good chance it'll be thrown around your area, but of course how close it's dropped depends on the size of your kingdom. I've suspected it worked something like that a long time ago but just recently confirmed it. Check it out in your map.
