Launcelot

Sir 3 (level 2)
Baron 7
Viscount 10 (level 3)
Earl 15
Marquis 20 (level 4)
Duke 30
Prince 50 (level 5)
King 75
Emperor 100


King and Emperor you earn trophies which you can show off.


Barracks apply only for those troops you have in your capital.


level 1.. 5 free
level 2.. 10 free
level 3.. 20 free
level 4.. 50 free
level 5.. 100 free


So let's have an example.. Suppose you're level 2 and you currently have 15 troops in your capital. You will be paying 5 of them only and those are the "extra troops" that cost after each turn. Now suppose you upgrade to level 3.. suddenly you will stop paying them all and not only that, you will be able to recruit another 5 for free.


Another one.. Suppose you're level 3 with 60 troops in your capital. You will be paying 40 of them. But if you upgrade to level 4, suddenly you will be paying only 10.


You see how upgrading barracks helps keep costs down.


Finally be aware that free units isn't the only thing you can do with barracks. Barracks level 1 enables you to recruit troops and level 2 enables you to make recruiters. With recruiters you can recruit more troops for every set of swords.
