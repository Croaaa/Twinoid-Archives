Scorpian
You don't necessarily have to hunt for rare resources. They can come to you. Every village (excluding the ones that are barbaric or capitals) can spawn a rare resource. It's a matter of luck, but it does happen. It's completely feasible that you could wake up tomorrow morning to discover a meadow right next to your capital. It happens a lot. That's an advantage to having a large kingdom. The odds of a rare resources appearing within it are pretty good.
