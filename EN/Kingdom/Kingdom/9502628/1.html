When you health starts to decline it will go from excellent to good and then to bad. The time (turns) it will take for every drop is based on luck so you can't really know when it's going to happen, but you'll see it. With every potion you can restore your health to the previous level, meaning that if your health is good you can make it excellent, or if your health is bad you can make it good with one potion or excellent with two.

You can purchase potions on the home screen, next to the buy more turns option, but it won't show up until your health goes good. Every potion you take is +50. Iirc the first one is 100 tokens.

I only take potions if my health is bad and make it good again, I don't bother doing so if it just drops to good.
