Did you not read the FAQ? The maximum number of allowed turns is 100. If you go over that limit you have a set number of wasted turns that you take where you are not penalized. Once that's used up, the game starts taking turns automatically for you without any production taking place.

For your reference, the number of allowable wasted turns is equal to five percent of total turns taken. For instance, if you took 100 turns, you have 5 allowed. If you've taken 2,500 however, you get 125 wasted turns allowed.
