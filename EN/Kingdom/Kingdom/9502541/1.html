Launcelot
There is no pattern. Actually if you call it a pattern, generally the game tries to preserve a balance in the map where resources are spawned but the balance is so general that the small part of that map which is yours, may easily lack any resources for quite a while and yet your neighbour have it all. For this reason it's very handy if you own a large kingdom, easier to get resources. There is no time pattern though. Only thing that goes by time is resource units that become available to collect and this is 1 unit per 30 minutes as you probably know.

Also it's totally random the quantity of each resource. So far largest I have seen is 390 of one single resource.

Just wanted to let you know that Catapults for attack and Ballistas for defence are actually among the 3 most powerful units in the game, so don't be disappointed that you can't make archers or pikemen or cavaliers or mounted knights.

Most powerful units in the game:

Catapults/Ballistas. Why? They lose ONLY against Mounted Archers, and even against them they give a good fight. Mounted Archer walks out very injured (1/4 health left). Against all other units, Cats/Bals totally rule and I mean totally rule!

Mounted Archers. Why? They are the ONLY ones that can beat a Catapult and Ballista. Not very easy, walk out very injured yes, but they win.

Knights. Why? They are the ONLY ones that can beat a Mounted Archer. Not easy, walk out very injured yes, but they win.

So bottom line, if you can't make any other units, at least make as many Cats and Bals as you can, they are VERY powerful units that completely annihilate soldiers/pikemen/archers/cavaliers/mount ed knights.
