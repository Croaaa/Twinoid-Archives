how can we place the soldiers to villages ?
When your general is at your capital, click on him and find the "troops and equipment" button. Move some soldiers to your general, then go back to the map.
Move your general to a village.
When your general is there, click on him and hit the "troops and equipment" button again. Here you can move soldiers into your village.

how can i gather all resources??
Some resources (metal, cotton, horses) are not available yet.

Kayin
A spot which isn't a castle, will have at the beginning an initial trading power, this power varies by distance from your capital. So distance = x, trading power = -x².
That power can go up to twice the standing soldiers, minus one.

There's a theory for calculating how long your trading power will go from one number to another (so could be false).
nb_turns = ((10 * X * (X + 1) / 2)-(10 * Y *( Y+1) / 2)) / nb_soldiers
While X and Y are two different trading powers, and X > Y.
