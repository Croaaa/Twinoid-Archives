The negative trade after conquering a village is counted for the shortest distance from your capital. But if it goes to trade route/connection it doesn't have to be the shortest way. You just need to have a connection with the village either through your own villages or any other player's who gave you the right of passage (followers have an instant right of passage through their master's territory and vice versa).
