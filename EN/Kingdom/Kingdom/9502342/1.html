When you become a Sir, you can build a level 2 palace. Once that's built, you can upgrade all of your other buildings to level 2. Each one has its own advantage. A level 2 barracks lets you have 10 soldiers stationed at your capital for free. It should also allow you to assign new citizens to be recruiters, which lets you build a larger army faster.
