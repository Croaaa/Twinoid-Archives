Launcelot
It's preventative measure; you can't have 70 citizens with a level 2 granary, I mean that's too early in the game. So at some point you are going to need a higher level, which means you need to advance title first. Then you are able to store more food, and that's when you will be able to make the square of population and make it rain citizens.. until you have so many citizens that the square of population is again unreachable because of a smaller granary. So then you need even higher level, and it goes on and on.. Citizens will always appear though no matter what, just much less often.

Tip: each time you upgrade palace you need to think; do you have enough peasants? is your food production enough so that you waste food? if not then you need a better farm.. but if yes, then the first building you need to upgrade is granary, because that will make citizens appear, who in return can produce more food, more gold, wood, build faster and recruit more. And then you can apply all these improvements to doing everything else, including upgrading the rest of buildings.
