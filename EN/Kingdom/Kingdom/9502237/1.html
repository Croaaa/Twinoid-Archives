norby89
TheCreator is right. If the attacker lives very far from you and you have many villages around your castle, by the time they get to your castle their trade will be so bad that their general will refuse to fight. This means you should only fear the players close to you.

Having a high level wall will reduce the amount of simultaneous fights (in the capital only) which gives you plenty of time to gather more troops there and/or hire new ones. When the level of the wall is high enough (lv 4-5) you should feel safe against any size of army, because the battle will take so long that the attacker will probably die by then.

So the best defense is to take as many villages around you as you can. Not only will this slow down your attackers but it will also help fund your army.
