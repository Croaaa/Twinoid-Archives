If in the next few turns you don't get some gold then some soldiers will be unsatisfied and desert you.

You should raise your trade so that you don't have such problems, leave soldiers everywhere and start by taking only villages that are near you because the starting trade is equal to the square of the distance from you.

If your problem is too big and you can't raise your trade and lost most of your troops, then you will start the villages with negative trade.
