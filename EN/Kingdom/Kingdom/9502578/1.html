When all your wasted turns run out, you will begin to lose food and gold each 30 minutes, as if you were taking your normal turns without aligning food and gold. For gold I have to assume it's a number equal to your total troops minus barracks minus reputation minus trade, if you have 40 troops and level 2 barracks (10 free units in your capital) with 4 reputation (4 free units on your generals) and 12 trade this means 40 - 10 - 4 - 12 so you will be losing 14 gold per 30 minutes. If you run out of turns to waste, the game takes your turn automatically (I believe it just used the "suggested" turn. You have expenses of grain and gold normally but you do not earn anything from whatever may get lined up in the grid and you do not earn trade.

Once you run out of grain, you start to loose citizens. Once you run out of gold, you start to loose soldiers, then you villages. I believe both of these are lost farthest first but villages may be lowest trade first. I do not believe you loose soldiers that do not cost gold (with general with reputation or at the capitol with barracks).

Eventually your kingdom collapses. I think the trigger is when population hits zero.
