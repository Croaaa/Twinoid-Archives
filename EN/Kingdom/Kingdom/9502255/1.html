Owyndaw
Trade applies if that Capital is connected to your Capital. That means that you can follow a route between the two by only using your Kingdom, your Followers Kingdom(s) and your Friend(s) Kingdom. If there is a savage village or an enemy (someone who has denied you passage through their lands), they are not connected and you will not get any trade.

The soldiers are only there to defend the capital from attack by others, or to guard against revolt. They do not affect trade.
