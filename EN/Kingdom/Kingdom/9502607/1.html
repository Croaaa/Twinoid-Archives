Mounted Archer cancels bonuses of ALL enemy troops, and all means all, including catapults, ballistas and of course Pikemen to answer your question. Now since Mounted Archer has more health than Pikeman, he will win but don't expect an easy victory because their health difference is awfully small. To put it into numbers, Mounted Archer will win after approx. 1 hour and 30 minutes and will be injured having about 20% of his full initial health, or 1/5 to put it differently.
