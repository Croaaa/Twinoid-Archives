What happens to my troops after I die?
On january 24, 2012
MagicDragon
It depends. If they were still under your control, they disappear as if they never existed. If your Capital is taken and kingdom destroyed, they become barbarians unless you reclaim the village and accept them back in your service. Any troops that were in unreclaimed villages when you die stay there.
