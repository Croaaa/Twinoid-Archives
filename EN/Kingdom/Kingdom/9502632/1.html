Launcelot
The most important building upgrade for your faster growth is Granary, because that will instantly increase the place where you stock up food, you will be able to stock more food in other words and when this happens, you will see on your production grid that the appearance of citizen icons will become a lot more frequent. So Granary will enable you to increase your population in a very short time, and what more population basically means is you're stronger. You can make more wood, more gold, more troops, build faster and of course make more food to feed your population.

Now.. personally, I would strongly advise you to build a Granary right after Palace, ONLY if by that time your food stocks are usually full, meaning you waste food. BUT if you're having a hard time with it, if you're in danger of famine, or you barely make it to half-full, then start with a Farm, and then Granary (and make peasants!!)
