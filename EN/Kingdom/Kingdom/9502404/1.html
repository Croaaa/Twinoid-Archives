If it's five soldiers vs. five soldiers, you're pretty much rolling the dice. Both sides are evenly matched, so there's no way to predict who will win. As stated above, it's likely that the winner will only just barely survive, but it's also possible that he might still have all five soldiers, just with one health point each. Either way, even though it will be by a very slim margin, there will be a winner. You just won't know who it is until the battle is done.

If you mouse over the soldiers while they are fighting, you can have a general idea of their health, and you can clearly see how upgraded troops have more health. However there is no exact number for hitpoints that we know of, and if you have troops going against an equal opponent, i.e. soldier vs solder, catapult vs ballista and so forth, its just a roll of the dice who will survive.
