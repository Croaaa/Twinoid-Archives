if your turns go over 100 they take a turn automatically
Actually, when your turns go over 100 any further turns are put into your Wasted Turns, which is 5% of total turns taken, if I'm not mistaken. You can see your wasted turns amount when you move your mouse over the text above the grid which tells you how many turns you have left (in your capital). If your wasted turns are full, then any further turns will be automatically taken: food and gold disappear without you producing anything. It has happened to me quite a lot when I go on a mini-vacation to a place without Internet When I go back home I have to deal with lots of people starved and a rather bad famine and gold shortage.
