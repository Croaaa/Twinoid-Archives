If you can afford it economically, you should definitely attack him back. Is the player his own master? Is he a follower to a player much more powerful than you? Both factors should be considered before attacking the other player back.

For instance, one of my followers took a village from another player. That player hasn't responded to the attack of aggression. I think his lack of a response is largely due to the fact the player is my follower. My kingdom is the largest there is on my map. To make matters worse for the other player, my economy is the largest by far. If he did respond, I could afford the cost of building a large army to smack him down a little.
