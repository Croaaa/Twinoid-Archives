So nearly 3 years after "kingdom sim" went permanently down, I have found another one that goes by the name of "kingdom tools". I've been searching for a long time on google but nothing ever came up so I eventually stopped looking, until recently. I just had to find another way.


So few words about kingdom tools. It includes both a trade calculator (that actually works) and a pantheon score calculator. You will find that the trade calculator is a little different than the one we used to have, far better actually. I have the link at the bottom if you want to skip the rest of my post, I'll just write few simple instructions first.


Trade Calculator


It's the default page ("commerce" highlighted in the top right corner). Here you need to type the village's starting trade in the "commerce initial" box, the village's desired trade in the "commerce désiré" box and the troops you have in the village in the "garnison initiale" box. What is different here compared to the old calculator is the desired trade, which didn't exist as far as I remember. What you want to calculate here is how many turns you're gonna need to take, so that trade moves from initial to desired, and initial isn't necessarily negative. Of course you don't have to type anything in the desired trade box if you don't want to, in which case the results will be almost infinite and you will have a hard time going through the graph, that's why you want to narrow the results down and that's what desired trade is for.


Right under the graph you have 3 options in different color, all enabled by default and I suggest you keep them like that. Red represents negative values (if any), blue represents positive values (if any) and green shows values that exceed the number of troops, which means the village is actually earning you money. Now green is probably the least useful but why not have it displayed. So with all colors combined you have the complete graph.


Pantheon Score Calculator


That didn't exist in the old calculator, but is just as easy to use. First click "score panthéon" in the top right corner and you'll switch to the final score calculator. Then type your army size in the "nombre maximal d'unités" box, your reputation in the "réputation maximum" box and your prestige (at death) in the "gloire actuelle" box. Next select your map level (on the english servers we currently only have "soldat" and "archer" that represent beginner and normal respectively) and finally select your title from master to emperor. Your final score is displayed under the boxes and it's actually incredibly accurate if you just go to the pantheon right now, pick a player and test it.


http://kingdomtools.tk/
