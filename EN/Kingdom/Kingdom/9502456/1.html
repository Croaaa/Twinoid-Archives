my territories wont be connected to newly conquered ones...The game will consider your newly-conquered territories to be connected to your capital if there is a route between them through your master's kingdom. (But if he should die or be conquered by another player, your territories will no longer be connected.)
if I go past his domain will I receive taxesI'm not entirely sure what you mean by this. You only receive taxes when you have followers of your own. If you mean trade, then yes, you will receive trade from the new territories and your master will tax you on that trade at a rate of 20%.

The problem you have with the nearest conquerable village being so far from your capital is that you will start off with hugely negative trade which will take a lot of troops to raise above zero and you will be hit hard not just by the negative trade but also by having to pay all the soldiers needed to raise it.

You could try sending your master a PM asking him if he would consider donating one of his territories around your capital to you - which is simply a case of him moving his troops out and you attacking the undefended village - which would help you build up your trade before you go conquering further afield.
