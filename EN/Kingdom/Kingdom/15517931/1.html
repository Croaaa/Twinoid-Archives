That's definitely not a good spot. A village ten nodes away at your kingdom's state of development is suicide. A player at Carl's level (he's an Emperor in Killimond with over 4,000 trade) can take the hit. You can't. Here's a a bit of advice before removing your troops from the village. Make sure to use up all your turns before doing so. If you don't, and remove them before hand, taking turns will make the trade hit even worse as villages without a garrison have their trade drop like a rock.


Using up all your turns will allow you two days to hold off taking turns without risking your precious supply of wasted turns (equal to five percent of all turns taken).
