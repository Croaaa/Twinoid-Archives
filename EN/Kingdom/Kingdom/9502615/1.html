dDShockTroop
After a duel finishes, the victor returns to his army. Then both armies will randomly pick a unit to fight in the new space that just opened up. The chance of the unit that just got sent off to come back into the fight is very small if you have a large army, and high if you have a small one. However, in a very long battle your injured troops will start to build up, and the chances of an injured one being thrown out to die increases. So yeah, MAs are really, really good in short, one-sided battles in your favour, because they are highly likely to not die.
