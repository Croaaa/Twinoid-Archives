http://www.smoggstudio.com/kingdom/

Commerce = trade, Garnison = troops

Commerce field: type the current trade whatever it is for example 15. If it's a new village it will start from a negative value, type it as it is for example -9.

Garnison field: type the number of troops in the village for example 50.

A graph comes up, mouse over the line and you can see what trade is going to be after X amount of turns.

(tour = turn)

General rule: the more troops in the village and the closer the village to your capital, the faster trade goes up (the faster means the less turns you need to take).

The calculator in the above link, unfortunately, does not give you a distance option so that is not included in the estimation. For this reason the results are not entirely accurate, but you can have an idea nonetheless.

About your last question, trade stops rising when it reaches 1 point below twice the amount of troops in it. For example if you have 30 troops in it, trade will keep rising until 59 then it stays there forever until you add more troops. Similarly if you leave less troops than half of the trade, it will begin to drop. For example if trade is 60 and you remove troops leaving only 30 or less, trade will start to drop after each turn, and from then on the less troops you have in there the faster it will drop.
