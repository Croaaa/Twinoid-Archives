You have a lot of prestige thanks to your followers who also linger in bad health like you and you want to die before they do in order to keep your prestige. If they die before you, you will lose their prestige and that will result in lower score when you enter the pantheon. The higher your prestige, the higher your score at the end. The factors that affect your end score are:
_
1. peak prestige
2. prestige at death (where poison comes in handy)
3. army size
4. trade
5. reputation
_
For numbers 1, 3, 4 and 5 it's the highest achieved that counts so you don't care what your followers do, but number 2 is the only statistic that counts at death and therefore if it gets damaged because of followers dieing, so will your score. I've never used it but that is the only good reason I can think of. If you're just bored and wanna start over you can just wait few days you don't have to pay for it.
