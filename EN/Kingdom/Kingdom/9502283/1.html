You probably have more than 15 citizens. In order to be able to recruit new ones you'll have to upgrade your Granary which lets you store more food. The square root of your food roughly gives you the amount of citizens you can have until citizens icons will stop appearing on your grid, in your case that's ~16. If you can't upgrade your granary you'll probably have to upgrade your Palace first, you can unlock this upgrade by taking over villages and reaching a certain rank.

Fail game isn't fail, you just need to read the FAQ.
