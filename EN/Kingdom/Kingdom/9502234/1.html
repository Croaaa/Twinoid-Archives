You do know you should only have one account on this server? (you can have another account on another server / language). You should delete one or risk losing both (only 2 accounts, right?)

Your troop distribution should probably reflect your military strategy, rather than the economic one. The first soldier in a village earns 1 trade, each one after that earns 2. Choose where you want troops stationed to protect your kingdom, particularly your capital, and concentrate your troops there. Once an 'unimportant' village is trading at a reasonable level, reduce the troops there to the 'stable trade' level and move the rest somewhere else.

Of course, getting your villages up to that maximum is the challenge, and balancing trading troops with having enough troops to capture far away villages and quickly reduce their negative trade.
