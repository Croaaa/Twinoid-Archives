The trade starts as a minus number. Count the number of moves it takes to get from your capital to the village, then multiply that number by itself. That is the starting trade (i.e. -1, -4, -9,... for 1, 2, 3,... moves away). Try to capture as many of the closer villages as you can before taking those further away to keep the negatives as low as possible.
