The appearance of swords on your grid is directly influenced by how much gold you have in stock. If you're having to constantly work at it to keep enough gold available to pay your troops after each turn, you can forget about seeing any swords appear. However, if you always have a lot of excess gold and don't really need to focus on specifically looking for it on the grid to avoid bankruptcy, you'll find that swords will appear more often.

I'd recommend making sure you have plenty of traders before hiring more recruiters. However, if you have a lot of both and are carefully managing the trade in your kingdom, you'll find that building a massive army is easy.
