Zanarkand I'm against auto attacking upon arrival in general. I don't like that option, I wish it was removed.

MagicDragon reputation points are given to a general depending on how many enemy troops his army kills. I have good reasons to believe that time is also a factor and the faster you finish a battle the more points you earn, but I have yet to confirm this. But I am 100% positive that kills are a factor. Your 3 generals will not necessarily earn the same points if they all survive. Each general brings his own group of troops, the more enemies each group kills, the more reputation their general earns.

Tip: if you overpower them and you want to provoke them to earn reputation instead of allowing the enemy general to start the fight against the village, it's a good idea to go there with your general and before you provoke, put ALL the troops on him, leaving the village undefended. That way your general fights alone, with no other group alongside his. If you do that and you win, you will get the most reputation out of that battle. If you leave troops in the village and you provoke, in the fight you will have 2 groups, your general's and the village garrison's. That means you will waste some reputation points.
