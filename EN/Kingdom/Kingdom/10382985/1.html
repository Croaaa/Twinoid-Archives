You stop taking turns and after a couple of days you will die, it is the only way.

More details:
First you must reach 100 turns, then a certain number of wasted turns (you can see this number by hovering mouse over "## of turns" in capital). Then after wasted turns is "full" turns will start to happen anyway, food and gold will be used.
Then you die.
