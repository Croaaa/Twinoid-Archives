Scorpian
When you revolt, any soldiers that your master assigned to your capital will automatically attack, even though your position as "follower" is immediately revoked. If you win against those soldiers, the revolt is successful. If not, you are recaptured and your kingdom is destroyed again. That's why you don't appear to have a master, even though you're being attacked.

As for how long you need to wait, I took a look at your profile. Looks like you were able to revolt. To answer your question though, I believe the wait is 24 hours. You get one chance to revolt per day, though it very well might be less (I haven't been a follower in a very long time, so I don't remember for sure).

That's why you should be careful when you revolt. If you know for sure that he has an army large enough to shove you deep into the ground, revolting may not be a good idea. It will just result in needless losses and the continuous destruction of your kingdom. I'd recommend just trying to get along with your master. That way, he can help you expand instead of blocking you. And, in the unlikely event that he refuses to help, just silently try to grow more powerful than him. That, or wait until he's struggling. THAT'S when you revolt.
