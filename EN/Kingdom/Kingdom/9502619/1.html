This is not possible, because that is what a bonus does, it speeds up damage inflicted to the enemy unit. Without a bonus both units in the duel will lose the same amount of health in the same amount of time, making it irrelevant whether we will have a winner because that may be due to different health, but.. fact of the matter is, they will both lose the same amount of health and in the same time.

But WITH a bonus, the unit that has it, will inflict more damage to the enemy unit, equal to the percentage of its bonus. But what more damage means? It means the unit with the bonus will cause damage faster, that is why in the end it will have inflicted more damage than the one taken, again making it irrelevant whether the bonus will actually be enough to win.. because health bar is also a factor.

MK bonus 100% vs soldiers
Cat bonus 200% on attack vs everything but MA's

If you were on attack, your cats should be able to kill a soldier 2 times faster than the MK. So if it was actually the other way around, it means your MK's were drawn to a duel with very injured soldiers, while your cats had to fight healthy soldiers. Or you were on defence.. with those cats, so no bonus applied.

Just telling you, not possible. That's what a bonus does, speeds up damage inflicted to the enemy.
