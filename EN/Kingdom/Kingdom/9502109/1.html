Here is a copy of what the linked thread says for those who don't have access:

The Gentry is an aristocratic government in the Kingdom of Liveruchorne.

It's founding members are Tillia of Kirking, Niewidomy of Dartave, and Cuchullain of Stainingham.

As members of The Gentry, we have agreed to follow two simple rules:

1) Members of The Gentry must allow all other members passage through their lands.
2) Members of The Gentry will not attack lands under the protection of other members of The Gentry.

Any member of The Gentry who attacks lands under the protection of another Gentry member will be permanently removed from The Gentry.

Any enemy who attacks lands under the protection of a member of The Gentry risks retaliation from other members of The Gentry.

All those interested in joining The Gentry must submit an application here and be voted in by at least a two thirds majority of the current membership. The application must include the applicant's name and title as well as the names of their followers (if any).
