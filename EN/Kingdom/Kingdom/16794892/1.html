I want to make one point about knights that I think needs to be said. If you have the level four cauldron and cabin, you can essentially produce two units of iron for every one turn of wood production if you have at least 14 lumberjacks (it might be more, might be less). With 15 lumberjacks and level five cabin, I believe 150 units of wood are produced. If you have a level five cauldron that amounts to three units of iron.


At that level of production, you can essentially train knights on demand. They are the only unit who's ability to produce is not dependent on control of rare resource points. All other units require you to have control of a cotton field or horse meadow in order to be able to have a steady supply of resources to make more units. That, to me gives the knights an enormous advantage.
