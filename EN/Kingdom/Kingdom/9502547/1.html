To gain titles, you will have to gain prestige. To gain Prestige, you will have to gain territories. Here is a list on how much prestige you need to be awarded with a new Title:

Master: 1
Sir: 3
Baron: 7
Viscount: 10
Earl: 15
Marquis: 20
Duke: 30
Prince: 50
King: 75
Emperor: 100

you can only upgrade your palace when you gain the Title of Sir, Marquis and Prince. The last titles do give you trophies, though. you can even rename your capital when you die with the title of an emperor.
