TolbbyTo get to Marquis, how much prestige will I be needing?

You'll need 20 prestige for Marquis. To get to the Emperor you will need much luck with health or potions. I can't see how can you get to an emperor before 50 y.o. (unless you somehow convince a lot of people to be your followers). So you have to live to around 60. The second thing you need is a good start. If you will lose troops/territories at the start or don't use every single recruit to raise your trade - you lose years and years of your life. In my opinion to avoid this you have to contact just about every neighbour (or even every player higher than sir ) and make some agreements, offer help et cetera et cetera. You may be lucky to find some great allies and good followers (if you treat them well and help them develop). So diplomacy is important too.
I wouldn't expect some high results from your current situation, i'd rather start from the beginning and choose a good start place, as some prince or higher ranked master at bad health without potions, but without being forced to pay tax.
