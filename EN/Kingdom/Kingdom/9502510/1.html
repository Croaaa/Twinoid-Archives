Launcelot
It's not that the potion you bought lasted 20 turns, it's your health that was gonna go down in 20 turns anyway. If you hadn't taken the potion you would be dead. So what I mean to say is, when you take a potion, that alone does not reset the amount of turns that will make your health decrease, that keeps counting and it's completely random; could happen in 300 turns, 100, 20 like yours did, or even 1 single turn!

To give you an example, a friend of mine had a good health for quite a lot of turns, then suddenly she makes a turn and goes bad. Then makes one single turn and dies, so the transition from good to bad took 1 turn. If she had taken a potion before her health went bad, that wouldn't have changed anything beyond the fact that she wouldn't have died, but still.. her "good" state would have lasted the same 1 single turn... it's not that the potion would have reset the turn counter.

You take a potion to prevent yourself from dying, but it's still random whether your health will stay good (or excellent) for 20 turns or 200, this is always random.
