I had to leave the game for a day, but when I got back I still had 100 turns, food didn't disappear and troops did not revolt. Perhaps I was lucky?When the number of turns you have saved up reaches 100, you won't be able to accumulate any more, but the game doesn't start playing the turns for you straight away. You have a period of grace before the "punishment" starts, which is equal to 5% of the turns you have played so far. So the longer you've been playing, the longer it will be before the computer takes your turns for you.
