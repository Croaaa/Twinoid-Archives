I agree with Panter: larger, more active Followers will add more to your Prestige (and to your Taxes, if you do collect them) than a dozen one-cital capitals with players who come and go three times a day, and never expand.

Of course, you run a risk with Followers, who at some point, decide they don't want to give you 20% (whether you collect it our not). But if you can find some Followers who are willing to work with you -- and if you give them something in return for that 20%, like borders they don't have to defend, and perhaps the use of your resources, and maybe an army to call upon when they're being attacked -- then they may have more reason to stay with you.

Of course, you can't please everyone. Don't expect a revolt from larger Followers, but be ready for them (and have a reaction plan in mind) if they do.
