A few things you should known before you post :

  • Respect the etiquette (no flood, be polite, etc)
  • Before creating a new thread, be sure that the topic is not covered by another existing thread
  • If you want to ask a technical question to experimented users, use the question post-it
  • Before asking a question, be sure to check the FAQ
  • If you suspect some ill doing from another player, never drop name on the forum. Lynching is considered old fashioned, and from time to time the guy happens to be innocent. Instead contact a moderator. A moderation group will soon be created. Meanwhile you can contact ayalti or Breith.
  • If one of your message has been deleted, with no reason given :
    • do not take it personnaly. Unless you have been banned or warned, it does not automaticaly means that this is a sanction.
    • a reason is allways given. But if your browser is small, it may not be displayed, unless you explicitly check your event history (link "Hist" on the right panel ; or simply this link)
