But this A-hole south of me kept conquering my kingdom and imposing a 20% tax, so to help slow down his recapturing I build the level 2 wall to limit the number of duels, drag out the fight. Got home from workyesterday to find the battle was less than an hour from finishing and he had a pretty massive army against what was whittled down to a few troops and a ballista.

So I scramble my hero to collect as many troops as I could (without killing trade off completely) and raced for home while spending turns working on gathering wood and constructing more ballista while recruiting what troops I could (4 recruiters, so 3 swords = 5 troops a pop).

In the end I managed to keep the battle running for about 24 full hours. Might have won, too, if he didn't send another general with pikemen up my way. No point crying, figured I'd just go to bed and revolt after making a few recruits in the morning. Only, about 5 turns: The hour of your death has finally arrived...

Guess the stress of that last battle weighed too heavily upon my heart, but what a way to go. Stalling some jackwagon and killing off his troops for 24 full hours.
