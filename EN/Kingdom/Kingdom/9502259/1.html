Trade appears then you conquer village. Each village (except followers') gives trade to you. Always then you conquer barbaric village, trade is negative - in minus, the further is village the minus is bigger, but with troops you can raise into positive - plus.

When you conquer village trade formula is:
DxD=-T

(D=Distance
x=Multiply
T=trade}

e.g Distance=1 1x1= trade is -1
Distance=2 2x2= trade is -4
Distance=3 3x3= trade is -9
Distance=4 4x4= trade is -16
Dista...

For example press on picture for knowing about distance:

http://imageshack.us/photo/my-images/89/ tipsr.jpg/

Followers' villages' trade always is ZERO

But the troops affects trade too, but for final result you have to wait much turns, final result with troops formula

t + t - 1 = T

t=troops
+plus
-=minus
T=trade

e.g.

1Troop 1+1-1= trade is 0
2Troops 2+2-1= trade is 3
3Troops 3+3-1= trade is 5
4Troops 4+4-1= trade is 7
5Troo...

But this is just trade, troops in barbaric or followers' village minusing overall result too 1troop = -1gold
1troop 0-1=-1
2troops 3-2=1
3troops 5-3=2
4troops 7-3=4
5troo...

Please no offence, I know I'm not smartest, but I hope this will be helpful for lots of begginers
