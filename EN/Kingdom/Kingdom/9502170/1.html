I know some people are planning to stop taking turns when their health turns bad, and focus on expanding their kingdom as much as they can to get a good title, but there appears to be a limit to this:

If your trade is really low, your generals will refuse to capture villages until you stabilize it.

This has happened to my friend Keshiru - He was trying to get the title of Prince before he died, so he sent his generals to conquer as much as they could and stopped taking turns. Eventually, he got this message: "Your general is upset with the state of trade within your kingdom and refuses to fight until you stabilize it"
Keep this in mind if you decide to try something similar
