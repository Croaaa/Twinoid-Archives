I'd only use Declare this player a friend for a master with whom I was on good terms. They would already be able to see how many troops I have anyway, as well as move throughout my kingdom and collect resources, so it's more a gesture of good will than anything else.

For anyone else that I wanted to be on friendly terms with, I'd remain neutral and come to some agreement by PM. If I felt that I could trust them, I might grant them right of passage and/or access to resources but I can't see the benefit in changing my status towards them to friendly. Letting anyone see how many (or how few) troops you have is just asking for trouble.
