If you're away from the game for a long time, it's true that you'll gradually run out of gold and food. This means you lose your soldiers and villagers, and most of your kingdom (if not all of it) because the trade will become so bad they revert back to barbaric villages. However, you will always have at least one villager that will not die. This means, if you're away from the game long enough, you'll log back in to find only your capital with one villager (and someone may have conquered you by that point). Your health also may have decreased, but its still possible to progress further in the game, you'll simply be disadvantaged.

Basically, if you're away from the computer/internet for some time due to a vacation or something similar, its not too bad. But if you're away for long periods often, there isn't much point to playing.
