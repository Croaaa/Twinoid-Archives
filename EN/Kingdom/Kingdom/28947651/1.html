There are a few factors that decide whether a revolt succeeds to commence (you are the follower):


1. Force in your capital against your master's garrison.
2. Army size against your master's (including stationed troops).
3. Titles


Nobody knows which factor weighs more than others, if they don't even weigh the same but you can start by gathering as many ballistas as you can, paired with other units if they're not enough, and hope it works. If it doesn't it could mean that your master's total army is big enough or his title is much higher than yours to compensate his weak garrison, so you need to bring even more troops.


There is also a 24h margin between attempts to protect masters from revolt button abusers.
