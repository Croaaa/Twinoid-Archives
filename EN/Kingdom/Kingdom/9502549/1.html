LawKillar
Kill some of your soldiers.

This game is a balancing act. If you want lots of troops, you will either need to have the trade to pay for them, or spend all your turns on the production grid trying to get the coin to pay them.

Trying to get too many soldiers to fast, or in other words, get too big for your own boots, and your going to end up in trouble. You need to find a way to grow your trade and your kingdom, while balancing your military to ensure you don't end up with a huge deficit.
