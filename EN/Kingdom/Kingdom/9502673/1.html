dabster
Once you take over a village that border to your kingdom and you put some troops on the villages, trade will start to rise with every turn you take. At first it is negative because your troops cost gold, after a while trade will have gone up so that you make more than the troops cost.
There is a maximum trade and that depends on how many troops you have on the village. Troops times 2 minus 1, the more troops - the higher max trade can become.
