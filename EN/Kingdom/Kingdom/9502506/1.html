Can my followers generate and collect trade in the following situations?

1) Follower A's capital > my village > follower A's village
2) Follower A's capital > follower B's village > follower A's village
On november 10, 2011
vel
The village must be connected to the capital, which means that if you have a village which connects with your capital through another player's territory you have to get the right of passage from that player. As a follower you always have the right of passage through your master's territory. Your followers need to give the rights to each other so in the second example you've given, follower A and follower B need to give the right of passage to each other.
