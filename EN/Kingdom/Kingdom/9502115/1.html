It's not the people in the grid, but the population of your Capital. At the rank of Sir, your grid will rarely get people when your population is around 16. At the rank of Baron, your grid will rarely get people when your population is around 25. Of course you can try to load the grid with as many people as possible before hitting that effective limit, then align the people in the grid later.
The real trick is to keep your gold stocks as high as possible to keep swords appearing... that seems to be key in the long run.
