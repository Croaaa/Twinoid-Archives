Scorpian
Inactivity will kill you. You get 100 turns max. On top of that, you get a set number of wasted turns based on the number of turns you've taken so far. If that maxes out as well, it starts wasting your resources, which will quickly kill you off. To prevent that from happening, you simply need to make sure you don't max out the number of available turns. Just make it a habit to take a few turns every day, and you'll be fine.
