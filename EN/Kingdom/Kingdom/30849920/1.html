Yes and you've just hit it. To be more precise, technically there is no cap in that you will always get citizen icons, just not as often once you've hit the square of population in food. Level 3 granary allows you to store 1,500 food and you have 39 citizens. Square of 39 is 1,521 so until you upgrade to level 4, you will be getting citizens less often. Once you upgrade and bring food to 1,800 your grid will start popping citizens like it used to, just don't expect it to work right away you need to stay above the square for a few turns.
