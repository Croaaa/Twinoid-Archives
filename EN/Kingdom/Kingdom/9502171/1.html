The French version of Muxxu doesn't allow for multiple accounts at all. (Of course people still try to do it) It is near impossible to use this to your advantage in Kingdom in the French version anyway and it will probably become the same here soon enough.
It is impossible to control your starting location which might mean it could take a day or more to take over a 2nd account you are trying to use.
There are other players who might take over either of your places while you are trying to pull this off.
Maps start replacing the players that die off. This makes it harder to get two accounts onto the same map.
Joining maps that aren't 100% new means you could start off with someone already taxing you making it near impossible for you to do anything.

My best was 97 places under my control before dying off. Maybe 15 of that was due to people below me expanding their own kindoms. So 100 is totally possible without serious outside help. I will not say that it is easy, only that it is possible.
