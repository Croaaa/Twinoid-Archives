Further confirmation as requested:

Tax is deducted automatically from your trade after every single move you make. This gold goes into a "bank" and you will never, ever, EVER get it back. It is up to your master whether or not he takes gold out of the bank. If he doesn't take it, it will just disappear into thin air when either of you die, so he might as well keep the bank empty (assuming he isn't maxed out already)

If you revolt, the money is still in the bank, but your ex-master loses his PIN number, and will need to recapture you to regain access it.

Once again, you will NOT, EVER, get any of the tax back.
