Nah, diplomacy takes a lot of place, on older maps players don't start dying on their own, they're all of different ages and strength. You have to wisely choose your allies and enemies and it is hard because you have to use your own tongue to show the chosen ones that it is indeed in their interest to help you - no buttons "Bribe the character - +100 to influence".

I guess the developers never thought that the game would be this complex, the idea is quite simple, really - do the grid and bulldozer your enemy.
