Small difference between death and conquered... if you die, you are gone. There is no reason for the Troops to hang around, as there is nobody to be loyal to. If you are conquered, you are still able to rebuild your kingdom, whether you do it as a follower or revolt first. The Troops have reason to stay around and be loyal to you. If anyone tries to take their village they have reason to resist because of their loyalty to you. If you try to take the village, they are supposed to recognise you and return to your control.
