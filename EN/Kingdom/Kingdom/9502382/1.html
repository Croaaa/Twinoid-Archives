No you cannot attack like you said. Battles only take place in a village or Capital. You cannot attack a General, even if you take the village, but you can provoke the General who will then attack you. Be careful though, you will be the defender then, so Catapults will not use their attack bonus. Also, if you have a 'friendly' General at that village, that General is also provoked to attack you
