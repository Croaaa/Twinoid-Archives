Yes. What happens between them has nothing to do with you, it's the same like if you were not their master and they were independent. But since they are not independent and you're there as their master, you can impose some rules if you want like don't attack each other. After all, it's not in your greatest interest that your followers fight because it means they don't use their resources to expand and earn you prestige. Moreover when one of them wins, the other one loses his villages so your prestige is hurt. Also if you have troops at their capitals and war breaks out they are compromised and if you take your troops they can revolt. You need to talk to both and forbid this. And there are ways to put some pressure on them to make them hear.
