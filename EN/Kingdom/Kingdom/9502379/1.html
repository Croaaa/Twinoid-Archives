Scorpian
Depending on how much more powerful than him you actually are, it might not let your follower revolt at all. It will still notify you if he tries though. If, however, he is able to revolt, any troops you left in his capital will immediately attack to try crushing it. So, if you leave one troop in his capital, and he revolts with one ballista, he'll probably win. To ensure he can't revolt, it's best to leave a handful of catapults in his capital. Remember, during a revolt, the master is always the one attacking.
