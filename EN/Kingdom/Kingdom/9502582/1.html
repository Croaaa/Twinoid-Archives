April, the sparse maps like you are on require a different kind of play. Attacks from your neighbors are pretty unlikely. Attacks across much distance (certainly across more than 7 or 8) require a nearby follower to pick up the hugely negative trade villages as the attacker clears them. In a sparse map, that just is not going to happen. The only place you could really every expect an attack is through Leihall. If you just shove a LOT of soldiers and a few ballista there, then you have no worries while you concentrate on expanding East and South.

Once you can afford it, you can also grab some quick prestige by taking a general and just sending him to wander the map making followers of as many savage capitols as you can, dropping just a soldier or three in each. It is a drain on your income, but it will get you to higher titles faster.

Just remember this map is very unlike the maps you will get as an experienced player. They are very populated and far more violent and confining. You are playing this game just to get to those maps. This is nothing more than a relatively safe place to learn the mechanics.
