Summary: to start make sure that half of your citizens are Peasants (making food). Build the Farm and Granary.

Learn how to 'play' the grid. You match 3 tiles, but if you look carefully you can often match 6 or 9. Keep watching your Food and Coin stores, and prioritise those if they get low.

Capture 2 villages and become a Sir, then upgrade the Palace, Farm and Granary. When you are continually reaching the maximum food you can store, you can lower the percentage of citizens that are Peasants; as you get a bigger army, increase the number of Traders, while not forgetting about the other citizen trades

Learn this, then you can adjust your plans as you go along, depending on your surroundings
