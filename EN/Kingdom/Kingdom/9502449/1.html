What Jinacio was saying is that you have a Followers tab on your Home page, that shows what the taxation is available for collection. BUT -- if you don't have a connected line of villages (including Followers, or allies who have granted you permission to travel through their lands), then your tax rate is 0%, which means you can;t collect anything.

Otherwise, your Followers are automatically taxed at 20%.

Whether a Master decides to Collect that Tax (which is an option in the Followers section), is up to that Master.
