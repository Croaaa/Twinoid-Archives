According to your question - you can't abandon a village, but there are two ways of getting rid of it. First, the most painful is to leave the empty and take turns which will effect in high negative trade and loosing all your money and soldiers but eventually you'll loose the village because of too high negative trade. The second and more resonable way is to make an agreement with other player settled near the unwanted village that he will take it over from you. Remember that using the first option may seriously shorten your king's life because of bankruptcy.
