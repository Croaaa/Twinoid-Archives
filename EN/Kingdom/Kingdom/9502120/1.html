You do not pay to soldiers if:
1 They stay at the your capital. Number of soldiers that can be unpaid depends on barracks level. 1st level is 5 soldiers.
2 They travel with your general. Number of soldiers depends on reputation of general which rises when you win a battle. Reputation of 1 allows to have one soldier without paying.
Otherwise you have to pay to your troops, their cost is displayed at HOME tab.
