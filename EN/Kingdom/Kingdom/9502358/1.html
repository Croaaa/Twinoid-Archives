In order to construct any other buildings, you need a palace. To build that, you need to have 15 pieces of lumber in your supply and at least one worker. When you line up three people on the grid, you can assign him to be a lumberjack or a worker, in addition to a peasant or trader. A lumberjack is necessary to help you get enough lumber to build anything. A worker, however, is the key citizen you'll need to build anything at all. The "build" link won't even show up unless you have at least one worker.

My recommendation is to get at least two or three peasants, then get one lumberjack and one worker.

Scorpian
More workers also increases the bonus you get from aligning more than three hammers. For example, if you line up four hammers, but only have one worker, it still only progresses the work by as much as if you'd lined up three. However, if you have five or six workers, and you line up four or more hammers, you should notice the work increase by more than if you had lined up three.
