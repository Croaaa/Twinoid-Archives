Yeah. The margin of error is also rather small. With an ideal max population of 16 for level one granary, you'll run out of food in 16 turns if you don't produce any food for those turns. At level two with a population of 25, you run out in 24 turns. It gets better with level three as you run out of food in 38 turns with a population of 40. At level four, the margin of error is about 59 turns. If you max out food storage, production, and population at level 5, your margin is 100 turns.
-
The above is somewhat misleading since a higher farm will allow for you to increase food production significantly. With a level 5 farm and 30 peasants you produce 360 food for every three wheat icons matched. With that kind of food production, you should never dip below 8,000 or so if you manage your food production well.
-
This is why I think that if you have space to grow where you spawn, you should try to make Viscount as soon as possible as at that level, you can really start to branch out your citizens without worrying about famine overly much.
