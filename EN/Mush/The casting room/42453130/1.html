Welcome on our recruiting board. Are you looking forward to join a casting? So you should consider reading the following lines!

WHO WE ARE

We aim to be a roleplay casting, not only minor. Here are the ships you are susceptible to find in our launch bays:

Soft RP ships: Classic ships with emphasis on roleplay, of course
Hard RP ships: Killing the mush and reaching Eden remains the goal, of course, but the result doesn't matter: those ships are made to have fun through characters interactions. Murders are forbidden except in the course of investigation, and you have to illustrate your roleplay with gameplay as much as possible. Those ships should be quite rare however.
Scenarized ships: This category is quite vast, as it includes everything from classical ships with a different goal to totally different ships with shifted roleplay, modified background and characters and a MushScenario script.

We encourage members to offer their own scenarii if they wish

We might also launch optimized ships from time to time but this is not our priority

You can visit us here

WHO WE'RE LOOKING FOR

You speak a correct english, or at least you make efforts in this way
You are an active player
You have a mostly clean mush profile
You are aware of the netiquette rules and you follow them
You like roleplay

If you match the most of these points, then Art of Infection is made for you


HOW DO I APPLY ?

You can send a PM to myself (Calmacil), Autrui, Infection, RixDeTurcan and/or Fennec with answers to the following questions:

Why do you want to join us ?
What is your roleplay level? You may include a Roleplay example with your favorite character
Are you a rookie, a medium or an experienced mush player ?
Are you bronze, silver or gold mode ?
What was your favourite game? Please join the link to the end page
What is your favourite lightsaber colour?

We'll discuss your candidacy and give you an answer as soon as possible, within a few days at most.
