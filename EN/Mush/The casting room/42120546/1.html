Hey there. I think, hope this post belongs in castings. But it's not really 'about' a casting. It's about not casting.

Some players won't be joining the casting ships. Maybe you didn't learn about them until they all filled up; maybe the ones you wanted to join didn't have room for you. Some of you joined castings and found out that your ship jumping when 24 people are ready means that 8 of you don't join the jump.... Maybe you've just started and are not even sure how to get a casting ticket... just that you don't seem to have any yet....so now you random jump.

Yep, this post is about random jumping.

You won't be alone.

Not all skilled, creative, knowledgeable, pleasant teammates are going to be on castings. Sure, maybe most of the cool kids are. Hope they have fun.

But they're not the only ones going to have fun.

I'm pretty devoted to random jumps. I'm glad for castings, delighted at the excitement, hope that everyone plays with them as much as they want to -

But I'm going to be random jumping, almost always. Here's to you, awesome new players in your first game. Here's to you, folks who do your own thing (within the rules) and go your own way. Here's to you, who would rather take your chances on finding fun now randomly, than waiting until the ships jump...

I'm going to be right there with you. And I bet we're not alone. Some of my best ships have been with people just like you. We're not left out, because we don't group jump or Cast. We're just different, and our fun is how Mush fun started. Jump when you're ready - people like us are too.
