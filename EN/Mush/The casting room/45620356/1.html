OP overhauled as of September 6, 2018.

Hello forum-reader! As of this month (September 2018 ) This Is A Casting has changed its method of operation. Rather than launching a ship every month, we are instead posting a poll each month containing several possible "special rules" ships.

These ships include:

-Farm Ships with 1 hour cycles where the goal is simply to stay alive as long as possible for a nice boost in XP.
-Ships where everyone must drop their communication devices in the rear corridor, at which point no-one is allowed to take those devices out of that room.
-A ship specifically to test the Jukebox, whether mush can use the Lethargic skill, and other things that are still unknown.
-A highly experimental ship where the only way to win is to make it back to Sol, but also be the only human alive when you do.

All casting members may vote for as many ships as they would like but we will only launch ships if any of these options actually manage to get 16 votes. We will also add options to the poll as they are suggested, provided they have at least 2-3 people already in support of the idea. (This is subject to change as the poll grows in size.)

We're otherwise doing our best to perform organized jumps onto public ships. Especially during months with no current launches planned.

Even if you're not sure about joining us for a special launch, I encourage you to check out the casting page and apply: This Is A Casting
