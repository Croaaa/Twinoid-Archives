Hello!
First off, Towards Eden group: http://twinoid.com/g/tow-rds-eden
Casting: http://mush.twinoid.com/g/towards-eden

After having given this much thought and discussed it a bit with group founders and some longstanding members, I've decided to post this here to give people information on the group, and how to join if you are not currently a member but wish to be. First off though, some info about us.

Our goal is, at it's most basic, active, enjoyable players playing together. Our rules are simple - don't be a jerk and always do your best for your side, be you mush or human. We are anti intentional human proof but have not traditionally had many rules regarding mush play - although most of us do prefer conversions. Castings rules for mush play are currently being discussed. Many of us love roleplay and will talk your ear off shipside.

So, negative people, pro-human proof/mush test players, and those who get overwhelmed by too many things to read in general chat need not apply. Casting is for Towards Eden members only, so by applying to join the casting you are also applying to the main group.

We've traditionally done monthly group jumps, and still will even with castings. We've also only taken new members on referral basis thus far. However, I've seen lots of really cool people applying to the group who would probably make great additions. SO! If you would like to join and are not already a TE member, there are two ways to join.

Method 1 - (Recommended)
Look at our members list, see if you've shipped with any of our members. (If you are having trouble with this, you are welcome to message me asking which members you have shipped with - I have all in my contacts so can tell pretty quickly. ) Message them, and ask them if they are willing to give you a referral for an interview based on their experience playing with you. The interview consists of 8 questions designed to see if you would fit in well with the group, while giving you a good idea of the things we consider important.


Method 2
Send me a message stating your interest. Any additional information you wish to provide is welcome. If you do this, I will go based upon a few things such as your likes per ship and any knowledge group members have of you. If I think you would be a good addition based upon this, I will tell you when our next group jump is (most likely non-castings), so that you can join as a guest and we can get to know you better shipside.


Please keep in mind, although I do try to respond to twinoid messages promptly, I sometimes do not get to them for a day or two depending on how busy I am, so a delay is not personal. Feel free to give me a poke if you think I need a reminder. If, however, you apply to the group without messaging anyone, do not expect a response.
