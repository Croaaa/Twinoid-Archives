Good day, Mush community~

This thread is about the first and quite possibly the last AA-inspired MUSH casting.

Our games are based on three main ideas, that being:

1. RP (never leave your home without it!)
2. We do not mind new players in our casting, as long as they are willing to learn more about the game and their role. In fact, I will personally try to explain basic concepts of the game, if you ask. (Do keep in mind that I am not going to be online 24/7, though).
We do not mind experienced players either, so feel free to join.
3. Logic chess; that term actually comes from the renowned AA series. It proposes logical approach to any kind of problems, that means that during the mush hunt you'll have to actually prove that somebody is a mush, gathering the evidence and the like. Only after you prove your case in a free form, you are free to take active actions.
Sponsored by Praesumptio innocentiae. We do believe in you to the bitter end...


Right now our casting needs new players for its first launch. Hope to meet you here soon.


Sincerely yours,
deputy creator Yurarin.
