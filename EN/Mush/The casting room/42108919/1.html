Ferhago

Hi everyone. It's been quite a long time since our last game, and in the meantime we unlocked a shiny new option. So I thought, why not change things up? In celebration of Valentine's Day, this next ship is going to be a little bit different. Instead of a more serious/traditional game, something a little more zany, a Last-Man-Standing game with a Couples theme!

That means...
2 Mush (as well as Hidden Mush)
2 Hr Cycle
and a competition to become the last 2 people standing!

Rules:

- Humans cannot attack/kill other humans, as usual. They can kill the Mush/suspected Mush. The normal restrictions against Mush rampages apply. As well, no bed stealing by punching people!

- The medikit should always be dropped after use - it can't be held; but you can for instance, ditch it in a rear turret - though keep in mind, since the medlab is always available, the purpose of this would only be to stop someone from healing someone else.

- People are free to hide/hoard items like plants, food, fruit, drugs, and all kinds of 1-use items that can help them stay alive. But since the goal is to be 1 of 2 people left standing, you are going to need to cooperate with others to help keep you alive.

- Mush are free to convert as many people as they want; the humans are hunting them as normal and they need to fight them as usual. However, since Hidden Mush is enabled, they will have a lot more freedom. The Mush are allowed to take the ship back to Sol/Eden, but only after converting all remaining humans. This means taking the ship back to Sol the same cycle as the last human is converted; alternatively, of course, they may simply let themselves be assassinated by NERON.

- The Mush are supposed to be a team and the humans are supposed to play against them, but the co-op rules are a little more relaxed this time. Mush cannot reveal themselves to the crew, who would still be obligated to kill them. But ultimately each human is looking after themselves, and if someone you trust and who is cooperating with you and seems a bit Mushy, there is not necessarily an obligation on your part to get rid of them! Not if you trust that that Mushy person is going to have your back in the long run. "But wait a minute" you might say, "won't that just mean everyone will just let themselves become Mush". Ah - but being a confirmed/suspected Mush is the only legitimate reason to kill someone else, and becoming a Mush simply opens up the possibility for you to be killed outright.

- If it ever happens that organizing expeditions becomes too much of a problem (since no one wants to risk losing by dying on an expo...) then there can be implemented a lottery for people to have to attend a given expo. Or else...

- PILGRED should be completed as soon as possible. The Mush aren't allowed to just take the ship back to Sol while everyone's alive, and the option needs to be open so that when the last two are crowned, the game can end.

Overall, though the LMS element has some rules to stop it from becoming an unfun slog, the whole thing is supposed to be a friendly competition! The fast speed is meant t be difficult to keep up with; when four cycles pass in your sleep after D12, it comes down to luck if you wake up alive or don't wake up at all!
Now would be a good time to offer criticism/suggestions, before the ship launches. We currently have 12 people ready, so we're looking for 12 more. Ideally, a Valentine's inspired game would launch around that holiday, so a launch for next week should be our goal.

If you want to play, all you have to do is go here and join the casting! http://mush.vg/g/franglais-mush/center

Also, if you're already a member and weren't a part of the PM where this idea was discussed, send me a message if you want to be included when the next group PM goes out.
