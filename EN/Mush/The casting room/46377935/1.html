Let me introduce you this casting: Glow in the dark

Evolution created amazing and dangerous organisms.

Long ago, somewhere, one mushroom species acquired started to dominate all life form on the planet. Its strategy was simple: it was a parasite. Parasites use other organism to survive, reproduce and spread. But the Mush was somehow special. It was able to manipulate its hosts.
At first, little animals, but then, after the centuries of evolution, the Mush was everywhere. It was the force that keep the other organism alive, existing only thanks to the Mush's will to keep them alive.
The Mush spread his spore so efficiently that some escaped, by chance, the planet. The story after that is well known: First cases of contamination were reported on Naumachia... and more later on...
