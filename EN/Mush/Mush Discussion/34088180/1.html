what makes a mush performance memorable?I really liked the answer given by blackmagic, the main Mush dev, a few days ago : a good mush is a mush who had fun. It's not about glory, it's not about killing everyone, it's not about destroying the ship in one day, though sometimes it can be fun and certainly memorable to have the ship blow up and kill the whole crew in a few cycles.

What I prefer is staying hidden as long as possible, playing my part as a human while contaminating as many people as possible. It's a great feeling when you know no one suspects you, when they even trust you so much that they invite you in a special "human" channel with Chun to discuss the investigation, or arm you with a Natamy rifle to kill the mush.

For the end of the ship, it depends. It's nice not to have the same strategies on every ship, it can be going back to Sol, perhaps with transfers on Jin Su and / or Raluca to finish PILGRED, or trying to kill as many humans as possible and waiting for Neron to kill off the mush, or causing the ship to be destroyed... Whatever the strategy, the most fun is in planning the final blow with the other mush, coming up with crazy plans which usually fail or don't go as planned.

Whenever I play as a mush, my goal is always the same: force the humans to return to Sol with mush onboard. If that doesn't seem feasible, I just wait until enough humans have been converted and then we kill the rest.
