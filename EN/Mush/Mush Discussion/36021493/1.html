There's basically two problems here:

1) Someone tries the game, gets bored and never comes back
2) someone tries the game and simply for whatever reason doesn't like it

There's a few things that can be done to impact both of these issues

Bronze Character Limitations
Sadly, a few of the characters are not particularly enjoyable for a Bronze player, you click to play the game for the first time and select Roland, because it says he's a hot-shot pilot with mad skills. You jump into the ship and you have Sprinter, you immediately find there is nothing useful you can do bar moving equipment around the ship, which whilst an experienced crew will honestly appreciate, isn't the most engaging and fun play, there are several other characters with similar issues

Generalist Characters
Similar to the Bronze heroes, if you jump onboard as Gioele, Stephen and to some extent Chao/Eleesha, what you're meant to do isn't immediately apparent, which can lead to quick boredom and abandonment

For the poor crew left aboard
Now the remaining crew have a few issues:

1) The ship can find itself without any/few Pilots/Technicians, many ships I've been on have struggled significantly because there was no technicians and on one ship I was Jin Su, the only pilot

2) Inactive characters can directly harm the crew, Noob Jin Su who ran around the ship and left himself standing in Alpha Bay, the crew can't move the ship for 2 days without wasting a ton of AP hacking the command, Noob eleesha means you never get the list, Noob Finola means your research is hugely delayed, noom Chun outside the lab hinders research

3) Inactive characters can benefit the mush hugely, if you have two active mush and an afk Chao standing in a turret, turn him, anonymush, let the crew kill him and bam you're hidden.

Solutions
Solving all these issues isn't easy. Trial ships for example don't solve all the problems, they also would indeed mean less people would get drawn into the game, as a ship full of level1 characters who don't know what to do isn't going to be a massively fun experience for anyone.

Here's a few simple gameplay suggestions

Inactive regen bonus
For every character that's gone inactive, another random a ctive player onboard receives 1ap/1mp each cycle, to counter the loss of team AP/MP (the game should be balanced for 14 active crew with 7-9 in beds on every cycle change, so this won't imbalance things

Shove/Drag action
Allow players to shove/push players into other rooms, let the crew push Chun into the lab, shove AFK Gioele from the Bravo Bay into the dorm or push Raluca out of it.

Mage Book Drops
Say your Roland goes AFK on day one and dies of starvation, has him drop a pilot mage book when he dies, or Physisist book when Raluca commits suicide in the lab on Day 2. allow the crew to compensate for the lost specialisation, one specific core skill for each character, only dropped when their inactive (so an active suicided Raluca won't drop a book).

These idea's can work within the current mechanics, without the need for segregation.
