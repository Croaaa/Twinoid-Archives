Chime

Well...
1) the characters are more than just the sum of their skills. If testing their skills is all you do to discover their character then yeah you'll be done soon but they all have different personalities, histories, flaws and strong points. They all have something like a meta-play-style but you aren't limited to play them that way.

2) I can't see how you won in every possible way yet. Not well developed? It's pretty decent the way it is. You can't win in D2N - you die today or you die tomorrow. Here we got a possibility to save / doom humanity. How is surviving another day to ultimately be eaten by zombies better than surviving another day in the hopes of saving humanity? Besides, you can do your survival thing in mush too, people just tend to give up and return to Sol before dying.

3) They have a mission. Prevent the humans from winning. It's just that in most cases the RNG are doing a better job at it than the mush. If you need goals like the ones you just suggested why not make them yourself? Start a ship as Roland and make it your goal to convince everyone that Hua is your father. Make it your goal to convert Terrence on the next ship you are mush on. See how many people you can convert. Try to infiltration a Sol-returning ship or even Eden. Actually you can start at trying to reach Eden it's not that easy sometimes the RNG just hates you. Anyhow I guess you get the gist of it. The sort of missions you are asking for, given by the game - they would be listed by the community and the humans would be looking out to use those to find you faster.

4) Ships currently take quite long to fill that wasn't always the case. You get your additional xp anyway and setting mush aside, you do get to use your Skills before selection too. Or are you one of those people that just lie in bed till selection so they have full AP and can complain in what a bad state the ship is by the time the last wakes up? Hmmmm?

Lastly. Mush is not a single-player. It's a communication based multiplayer. You can't expect people to dance on the table for your amusement but you can get up and dance for yourself and try to get them to join you. It's hard and some people don't have the talent to animated others ( for example: I can only drive them mad whenever I try ) but...
The game has an infinite potential, yet each ship is just as splendid as it's crew makes be.
