This game is extremely fun when you get to have 2 or more skills, but pretty much cripples you with only 1 free to play slot. Now I get why it was made like this. Companies want their money after all, but what will be the point of leveling up if you are going to stay a free to play player forever? If you only have one skill slot, you will almost never get the opportunity to use a skill you acquired later, because most characters can't work without their first skill choice. It's simply essential to have that skill for you to do your job. If Chun, for example, doesn't take her first skill, everyone will lose 2 morale a day, which would be overkill. If Frieda doesn't take her first skill, she won't be as effective in planet scans...and since she needs those to earn glory, she will have to use it. That applies to almost every character.
With things looking like this, I think it will take all this leveling up fun away from people who wouldn't pay to win. After all, what's the point of having additional skills if you have to use the default anyway? It's kinda disappointing that you will be left out on all the fun.
I suppose being mush won't be all that glorious either. Only one skill will probably not leave you with much of a choice. But you at least aren't forced to take the same skill all the time. Unlike human players.

Also, because you will only have to have one skill, certain characters with a rather boring primary skill will be characters no one wants to play. I am talking about Stephen, Gioele and Terrence.
While Gioele doesn't need his primary skill (because it almost never seems to serve any purpose), he could easily take one of his level up options, but those aren't all that breathtaking as far as I could see (I haven't unlocked them all, though). I am not sure if Stephen needs his cooking skill, because he will hardly use it anyway. Does he need it for glory? If not, why take it? He will be lucky if he gets an interesting secondary skill he will get to use instead.
Terrence appears to be a VERY fun character to play...if you got 2 or more skill slots. With only his primary skill available, you will find yourself to be bored easily. If you are lucky enough for someone to bring in scrap metal and you actually manage to find your drone and pick it up, you might actually get to do something every once in a while. In contrast to characters like Roland or Frieda, who can fulfill their primary objective almost all the time. As far as I know, Terrence can't even dismantle stuff for scrap without the technician skill and that skill is NOT a primary skill for anyone. So if you won't be lucky and get a player with two skill slots AND a technician ability, you are going to have a hard time, right?
Furthermore, technician appears to be far more useful than Terrence's primary skill, because repairing stuff is what you will have to do almost all the time, yet Terrence can't abandon his first skill, because he needs it to earn glory. If he favors technician over his primary skill, he might actually be helpful to the team, but ends up losing all his chances of ever getting into the ranking. That's something only very few people would do, since they want to be listed as one of the best players, right?

Speaking of which. Is it really equally hard to get a certain score in the ranking? Roland can shoot the crap outta hunters almost any time, while other characters only get glory for killed mush players. I know that Roland will eventually get less and less glory for each kill, but won't he still get more than a character who needs mush assassinations? I can easily see him and Frieda dominating the leaderboards in the future. At least that's my theory.

Well, that's what's on my mind. Feel free to point out where I am wrong, if I am. I tend to over think stuff easily.
