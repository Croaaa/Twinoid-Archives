Look at it from a mush perspective, how many spores are you going to need with two Alphas? Two spores each without anti-mush is already 4+2 AP, not accounting the shower damage. With Constipaspore, it becomes 8+2 AP. Clearly, extracting all the spores you can with only two mush is suicide. If a Beta isn't produced by the time Constipaspore is done, they won't even be able to hit the 4-spore limit without being obvious mush. The level11 skills might be a partial remedy, but not by much.

I suppose you could hit the spore limit if one of you likes to phagocyte a lot for healing (like me!) but Constipaspore is still going to hurt more.

Consider what happens if the Mush team manages to spore someone on the day of selection and they take Anonymush (Either by coordination or being Silver, or both):

-Antispore Gas does much more damage, because it effectively means that, barring Mycelium Spirit, there will be at least one Mush which can't spore, meaning they will have to resort to a slower infection rate and/or taking the ship by force by killing the key crewmates.

-Constipaspore Serum will have a lower impact, since each Mush could just lay low and produce a single spore per day, and that would still be enough to convert one target per day. By that point, the pool of Mush that can be online at the same time to infect someone else in a coordinated fashion increases, and the ship rapidly goes to its doom.
