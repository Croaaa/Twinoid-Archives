The team glory idea is nice, but then again in a psychological way it also gives incentive and makes the game more fun to have an element of competition.

And considering the random nature of many events, it is also ok that some people have an easier time getting glory than others on particular ships, depending on how everything develops.
Just like it is ok that some characters get their glory quickly, but not as much, while others have the potential to score higher on the long run. So those characters will do better on bad to average ships.

So that should be fixed - and could be, with the systems already in place (values, diminishing returns etc.).

And, of course, glory should not be an incentive for harmful actions, like Ian deliberately killing plants so he can plant new ones rather than grafting, or Gio wanting the Mush to spread. That is just stupid.
