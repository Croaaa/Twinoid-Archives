So, this stuff has been on my mind for some time, and I doubt anything will come of it but I figured I'd like to share my idea's anyway.

I think mush is a fun game, and has a lot of potential to be even better, so here's a list of things I would change if it was up to me.

Random
Junk - Each time a ship start, 2-5 pieces of junk equipment spawn in different rooms that technicians can dismantle for extra scrap, for example, the broken patroller in AB2, a broken blender in the cafeteria, a broken TV in a dorm etc
Narcotics Dispenser - All ships now start with this item, the Research project becomes more difficult, but produces 4 drugs per button push instead of 1.
New Research: Fungal Detection Serum - Using Mush Sample and Chun, crew are able to make a detection Serum which upon completion will randomly name one member of the crew who is human and spore-free.

Oxygen
* 1 Oxygen is consumed per cycle per living crew
* 1 Plant produces 8 Oxygen on Day change.
* Maximum Oxygen is 512
* Starting Oxygen is increased x16
* Results from pumping Oxygen from an Expedition is x16

This makes Oxygen more interesting and Dynamic, as crew die off, less oxygen is needed to keep the remaining crew alive. This helps scale difficulty to some degree. A ship with 5 living crew is already extremely difficult to survive, this could mean on a late-game ship, 5-7 crew have less to worry about Oxygen planets and more about everything else that is falling apart around them

Water
* A Water tank is added to Front Storage
* A Water meter is shown next to fuel/Oxygen
* Each crew consumes 1 water per day on cycle change
* Watering a plant consumes 1 water
* Showering consumes 1 water
* Creating coffee consumes 1 water
* Water is now required to make a new extinguiser
* Water can be gathered from various different zones: Ocean. Low Temp, Desert (oasis!), Swamp
* On a cycle change where water is at 0 or runs out (5 water left, 6 crew alive), As many crew as there is missing water will become 'Dehydrated' causing all future cycle changes to cause -1 (Mush, being fungus also consume water and will become dehydrated just like humans)
* Survivalists have a 10% chance to not consume a water on day-change as a passive perk

Skills
To help balance out the difficulty of the game and make things more fun for both new and experienced players, Bronze players now have 2 skill slots, Silver have 3 and Gold have 4. No more Sprinter Rolands that drag the ship down for everyone.

Neron Projects
anti-grav thrusters - Renamed to Autopilot - "you remove Neron's backup systems and install them in the Icarus, it's not like Neron needed it anyway?" - The Icarus can now be launched without a pilot (always takes off and lands safely)
Auxiliary Terminal - Also doubles as a Navigation, Pilot and Comms terminal
* Convenient Sink will now wash all dirty crew at the end of an expedition without using up ships water, making it a far more valuable and worthy upgrade.
CPU Overclocking - Also makes scans always successful first time (unless planet scanner is broken in engine room)
Fission coffee roaster - changes coffee to create expresso, that gives +3 instead of +2
Magnetic Return - Renamed to auto-medic, system automatically heals all expedition crew at the end of an Expo +3 (increased by research)
Plasma Shield - All ships now start with a basic shield that provides 20 shield and regenerates 1 shield per cycle. The Plasma Shield project reinforces the shield, instantly bumping it to 50 (of 100) shields and increases regeneration to 5 per cycle as it does now.
Rerouted Charges - Also increases chance to hit by 30%
Spatial Wave Radar - Also increases neron upgrade progress on comms and improves decoding speed of signals

Gardening
* Hydropots are removed from game
* Hydrovats are placed in the Garden, these cannot be destroyed or picked up
* All projects that granted pots, extra pots etc add additional vats in the garden instead
* Hydrovats can become broken
* Ships, instead of Banana's, start with "Space Algae", this grants +18 per day on cycle change, but does not produce anything edible
* Other plants that you get from exploration now have varying amounts of Oxygen production, Jumpkins for example can produce 16 , Another plant can be very poisonous to eat, but produce 21 per day, whilst a different food that gives a huge amount of benefit such as health, action points, movement points etc only grants +5 per day.
* Players can click on any Hydrovats and select to cultivate Algae (no algae is required to start a new one)

Well, those are my idea's in general, I think they add more fun dynamics to the game and change scaling difficulty and add a little more complexity on some factors.
