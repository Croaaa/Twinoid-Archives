firefighter is top , not for putting fire out (well it can help for remote rooms or fire when people sleep) but because with that drones are immune to fire making sure they won't brake when in a room in fire

turbo is mandatory it allow drones to do 2 actions nearly each cycle (80% chance)
repair hard things faster (gravity simulator,) or repaire multiple things twice faster (electric spike on bridge or engine room )

pilot is clearly something you are not sure you want it
1) you need magnetic net before or you will lose drone and patrolship when you move
2) even with turbo drones are slow at shooting, you need time for them to be effective , and in late game you won't have time : pilot take off shoot araks land and commander move the ship

3) while drones are out (and they'll stay out as long as pilots won't take off to finish hunters)
they don't repair random stuff in the ship (doors, patrolship, turrets, fuel tanks... )
theyr patrolship don't reload
theyr patrolship take damage and will eventually explode
