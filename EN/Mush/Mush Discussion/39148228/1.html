Captain Baranov

Background: Captain Baranov was a replacement for Jin Su, send by The Federation. While in the "main" timeline he was killed by Chao as mush, in our version he was fast enough not to be caught. Role: Captain Baranov is the first commander of the ship. He mostly replaces Chao, as a lot of him skills are focused in dealing with mushes.

Trait: To run away very fast.... His arrival has shocked everyone in the team. While they understand, they were actually lucky with mush agents killed, it's very hard to be close to Captain Baranov. Nobody can flirt with him and everyone in same room loses 1 morale on cycle change.

Skills:
1) Shooter
2) Lighting reflexes - When any aggression action is made against Baranov there is 75% chance he will strike back. If Baranov has at least 1 ap he will try to hit the attacker with his blaster, then knife, then fists (other weapons are not good for reaction shoots). If he hits the attacker, his aggressive action will be failed. He cannot counter action he cannot see like transfer or spiking in a room without a camera.
3) Equalizer - Captain Baranov starts the game with his custom blaster gun, called Equalizer. It has 3-5 damage, max 4 charges and make more wounds then normal blaster. Others has just 25% to hit with this unusual weapon, when Baranov has 50%. Also it gives +2 to expedition strength.
4) Torture
5) Survivalist
6) The Dreaded - Sending mission becomes a free action. Accepting mission gives +5 ap instead of +3 ap. Nobody wants to fail The Dreaded and should understand what he wants as fast as possiable...

Empathy Fofalla's duties fall somewhere between comms work and mush hunting. Due to the loss of Gioele and Paola Rinaldo, and the arrival of Viktor Carlsson, Empathy fills in the the void left by Gioele's loss of Paranoid and also Paola's loss of Logistics and Radio Expert. Her other two abilities are interesting in that they help hunt the Mush, but require a degree of coordination to use.

Not to mention, an intelligent and observant mush can usually work out what's going on when someone who is usually supposed to be on the Bridge comes down to their Dorm and everybody slowly starts clearing out.

To make use of her Feed Freak ability requires some careful positioning for full effect.

When the Mush are taken care of and the comms work is done, she replaces Gioele as a pretty hardcore generalist, her IT Expert ability giving her the ability to hack should the Commander be slow, advance leftover research and projects, or help in planet scanning.

Replaces Paola Rinaldo

Unique Trait: None

Glory:
I knew it! I knew it!: +5 Glory for each Mush killed with a bug on them.


Skills

2. Radio Expert - A fervent listener and follower (perhaps too fervent) of Paola Rinaldo, Empathy went into Communications in a big way.

3. Logistics Expert - Being the head of her University's conspiracy uncovering club, movement, forum, blog and protest group, Empathy is no stranger to coordination.

4. Feed Freak - Empathy watches all the doors at all times and has no shortage of gadgets. First of all - why do I present a character like that - once I saw an idea of priest-character, and I liked it. - ability to cure disorders without Shrink (or too random pills and fruits)
- a character, where Self Sacrifice can be useful
- ability to feel the situation on the ship - no collect to evidence like Tracker or print the List, but to have overview of it
Role and abilities:
Maximilian helps his team to stay healthy. He can heal them, help them with morale, provide some ap/mp for those who cannot take beds. Also he can work in lab, but still is less powerful then Finola, Andie or Ian in that. He also helps in investigation, but indirectly.

Personal objectives
Serenity:
+4 glory for each day he got morale boost from his trait
Livesaver:
+4 glory when Ultra-Healing Pomade research is complete

Trait: Holy war
Maximilian feels how well things are going on the ship. Maybe he does not know what happened exactly, but he just knows if mushes or humans get advantage and if it is going to end well...
On day change Maximilian recieves a special message and morale boost or penalty.
-1 morale for each spore humans/cat got (direct spiking, trap, spored food, mush trap event)
+1 morale for each spore destoyed (trap triggered by mush/Chun, spored food eaten by Chun/destoyed, spores destoyed by super soap/spore sucker etc)
Spores eaten or vanished during transfer does not count. Just extraction does not count.
+1 morale for each mush killed
+3 morale for human cured with RFS
Humans turned to mushes don't cause extra morale lost, just regular -1 for third/fourth spore.
If the sum is below -3:
You are losing control of the ship, mushes continue to spread in the dark corners and looks like nothing can be done. You should do whatever it takes to stop them!
If the sum is -2 or -1:
Something bad happened today. You just hope, that crew will be able to endure that...
If sum is 0:
You don't feel anything to change today. Are they gone? Or just decided to lie low for a while?
If sum is +1 or +2:
Looks like everything is going to be ok, spores are being destoyed and everybody looks fine.
If sum is above +3:
Today was a big victory for humanity. Soon every single trace of those parasites should be gone!
Note: He see only result of all activities during the day, not every single action separately. For example: on D2 mushes made 4 traps, 3 were triggered by humans, one was triggered by Chun. Result: -2. But he does not see -3 morale +1 morale, just -2.
Note: This event does not replace normal -2 morale per day (-1 morale per day with MOH).

Skills

1. Self Sacrifice
His trait puts Maximilian in danger. Should mush team be good and he can be gone in few days. Having that skill is good to allow Maximilian to know what is happening, but never to give up, even if the ship is in grave danger. Skip it on your own risk, if you are sure in your morale supports.

3. Inspiration

The crew member will receive "Zeal" status with following effects:
If a crew member was in the bed, he will get up
+1 mp every cycle change
25% chance to get +1 ap every cycle change
25% chance to get +1 morale every cycle change
If crew member is affected by any disorder, every cycle under holy zeal status will count as staying in bed with Shrink for healing the disorder.
The effect will end, if crew member lie down, but he will be warned about this (like message when you take off for expo).
Mushes can be inspired, but receive nothing from that, they will be only forced to get up.

- When a crew member dies, there is 50% chance for others not to lose morale. Works on self
- Losing her little cat cause Raluca to lose 1 morale instead of 4
- If a crew member wanted to make suicide on cycle change, there is 50% chance for him to restore 1 morale instead. Does not work on self
