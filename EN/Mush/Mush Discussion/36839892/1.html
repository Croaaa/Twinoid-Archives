EXTRACTED FROM MUSHPEDIA AS AT APRIL 18, 2014. REFER TO MUSHPEDIA FOR THE LASTEST UPDATES

PLEASE NOTE IF THERE'S ANY CONFUSION OR DEBATES ON HOW THE ACTUAL SKILLS WORK, ALWAYS CLICK ON THE MUSHPEDIA LINK ABOVE FOR THE LATEST UPDATES. I'LL TRY TO UPDATE THE INFORMATION BELOW ONCE A WEEK.

Character changes

More information on the new characters, their traits and skills can be found in their respective articles.

New character: Andie Graham

New character: Derek Hogan

Roland: Glory is now gained for every 2 killed Hunters. (Before: Glory for each Hunter)

Skill changes

New Andie skills: Devoted, Skillful, Confidant, Expert
New Derek skills: Motivator, Strong Skin

New Mush skill: 5 Electro Slime

You produce a green, acidic slime in your armpits...
For 1 Action Point, you can cause an electronic item to break. It will take several cycles before it breaks.
The selected item breaks in 1-4 cycles.

New Mush skill: 9 Defacer

Your DNA permits you to modify certain sequences in given devices. Plenty of potential!
Once a day. For 2 Action Point, you can wipe the entire log history of a given room. The logs will resume to register from the following cycle onward.
Confirm: This may occur within 1-4 cycles, rather than instantly.

New Wrestler and Solid action: Forces the target character to leave the room.
Costs 2 Action Point. (1 Action Point for Inactive targets)
This action is not Aggressive, improving interaction with Ian's Pacifist and Chao's Crazy Eye.
As a result of this, Wrestler and Solid are now kept as Mush, as the Mush test would be too easy otherwise.
Bacterophile: Action Action Point cost adjusted.

Nightmarish: Action Action Point cost adjusted.

Firefighter: No longer needs the Extinguisher to extinguish fires.

Status changes

New status: Pregnant
Gained by female characters (not Andie) when doing the thing. 8% chance.
Gives an immediate bonus of 8 Glory.
If a Pregnant character dies, the entire crew loses more Morale.
If the father injects a Spore when the baby is made, the mother is affected by an extra Spore.
Gives various diseases like Slight Nausea.

New status: Grand Inactif ("Super Inactive"

"You're super inactive, not having connected in the past 48 hours. You are a punishment to your crew who has a right to dispose of you."

Violence against Grand Inactif players is not sanctionable

Inactive:
Now prevents the character from gaining Glory.
No longer lost when the character is attacked.

Item changes

Items can no longer be hidden before the Mush appear.

Mush Sample: Now spawns on the ship when the Mush appear.

Myco-Alarm: Now also detects the special actions of Fungal Kitchen, Trapper, Radio Pirate, Nightmarish, Bacterophile and Transfer, as well as Infectious Caress (on Schrödinger) and the act of mutating into a Berzerker.
To balance this, only 4 MycoAlarms will be produced from the research.

Miscellaneous changes

New feature: Alien merchant ships which allow the trading of items and even characters (for instance, selling off Grand Inactif players with no penalty).
A merchant always spawns on Day 0.
Merchants sometimes appear in place of Hunters.
Interaction happens via the Comms Console.
Being sold is not considered a death in terms of morale loss and Distinctions.

The chat tabs now update in real time without requiring a refresh.

Chat history scrolling seems to have been fixed.

Various other bug fixes.
