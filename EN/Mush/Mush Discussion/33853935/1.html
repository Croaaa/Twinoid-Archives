Are you in Gold Mode? If you have more skills than skill slots available, you will automatically have every skill that you can choose at the start of the game. If you're both Gold and level 5 or 6 with that character, then you'll have the option to choose your skills at the start of the game.

The same basic idea applies to Silver and Bronze players, but it's three or more skills and two or more skills for them, respectively.
