I've been thinking about the way Gold Mode is set up. I think it could be executed better. First off, the way gold mode is timed. I've seen people several times now commit suicide or entertain the thought of suicide because they don't want to 'waste their gold days'. And I don't think it'd be unreasonable to say that one's primary lure to gold mode is the skill slots, which is set the moment the ship begins. What would happen if someone paid a month for gold mode and made it to a Day 20 ship then a Day 11 afterwards? Only two ships with max slots. Gold mode is currently flavored as a subscription and not much cheaper than something like World of Warcraft.

What I'd prefer to see is paying for extra slots on a ship by ship basis. Maybe you want only three slots for your current ship instead of four, and have no interest in the double XP/+2 HP/Morale. I think that people would be more willing to purchase rewards at the microscopic level than to pay a monthly subscription. Let's say someone goes through a Day 10 ship, Day 7, Day 8, and Day 5 in one month, buying four slots at $3 each. Rather than the $11-$13 (I forget the exact value) for a month, they'd have actually helped the support the game with $28 that month. They'd also be able to make this decision as they go..imagine making it to Day 10 with your 2 slots, you start to think that you're with a good crew and should ramp it up to three. Maybe the other techs died but you're on Day 15 now and want to keep going, you can drop in slot #4. I don't think gold mode should -go away-, but I think that if this got added, there'd be plenty of lure to purchase first month for the free skill slot and continue to purchase the third skill for virtually every ship going forward.

I'm curious to see what you all think. Good or bad or are there better ideas?
