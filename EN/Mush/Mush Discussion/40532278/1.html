Has someone gotten a 0% boost to a Neron Upgrade(NU)? I've never seen that, but I have seen 1% increases very often.

If you have seen a 0% increase, was it only with very high NU numbers?

If so, it may not be a fail or a bug. I don't know how the NU is programmed, but if each attempt to upgrade contributes some 'points', and the total points needed are more than 100, then a 0% increase may happen because progress was made, but not enough progress to increase a full percent, or even enough of a fraction of a percent to show the change.


Example:

We're working on NU 10, and we need (made up #!) 1200 upgrade points.

We currently have 360, exactly 30% done.

Progress is made, giving 18 points (378/1200= 31.5%): that is 1.5% of the total needed but only whole percentage is shown, so it appears to be a 2% upgrade: we are shown that NU is 32% done.

Progress is made again, giving 7 points (385/1200= 32.1%): That is a little over a half percent, but we were already seeing 32% done - we still see 32% done. We think the progress attempt failed.

Creative giving an AP from progress on NU sounds like a bug to me.
