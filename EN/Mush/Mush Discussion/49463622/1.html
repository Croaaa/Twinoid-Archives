Hi All,

With the death of the Massive Mush Movement, there hasn't really been any avenue for larger numbers of experienced players to jump together. I feel that most of the experienced players are partitioned off into their respective Casting Groups. Although there are good casting members that jump on Random ships, most of the times, we don't get a critical mass of good players to have a really enjoyable experience with rare exceptions.

I understand that the Towards Eden do organise their own Random Jumps once every 2 months. What I propose is that the biggest Casting Groups such as MS, TE, SA and RP co-ordinate a random jump, say once every 2 months and post it here.

That way, we can enjoy an active Random ships with;
1. members of other casting groups that we might otherwise never meet;
2. give an opportunity to newer players to mingle with more experienced casting members; and
3. an opportunity for experienced members to select their favourite characters to play (as casting ships, your character is randomly selected).

I'm from the RP group, so I can handle things on my end.

Hope to hear some favorable response from the other experienced casting groups.
