So! Don't wreck your current ship, but when you are on a post-update ship, check stuff out as you play like you would normally. Talk about changes you notice, and lets create a player-verified 'translated patchlog ' for our own benefit, as well as get a head start on making the list of remaining bugs. Because they haven't forgotten us. The time will come when our Devs and Admins are ready to give some more time to MUSH. Lets be ready for that, so the power of our enthusiasm empowers them to give our game even more than they thought they could before!
