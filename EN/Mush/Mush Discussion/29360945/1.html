Basically the next information is available when you become a botanist due skill book or because you can select that skill and NERON guidelines:

Every day, plants need to be cared for. At the end of the day if they are healthy they will produce fruit and oxygen, see plant info how much they produce. (This is after cycle 8 )

Young plants do not produce any oxygen or fruit until it reaches maturity. This is over the span of multiple days...

They can gain three statuses:
Thirsty - They'll need some water, and will not produce any fruit after the day ends.
Sick - Will not produce either oxygen or fruits.
Dried - If they do not get watered, they'll die at the next day.

That is all the basic info NERON presents you. The rest is not spoiled, as it's the mysterious fun of being a botanist brings with him/her =)
