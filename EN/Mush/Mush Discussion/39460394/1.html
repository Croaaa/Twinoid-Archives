Since you're only allowed four (or six with the Large Bay Upgrade) people on an expedition, the game actually limits the number of people who can enter the Icarus Bay at all. If they have a full expedition team then you won't be able to get in there, never mind lay a Trap.

However, it can be a good place for it before an expedition, and you have the excuse of dropping off some item that is needed in there (unlike Engine Room, which may be off-limits for non-confirmed players).
It's not as great for targeting specific people, since any person going on the expedition will be picking up items and setting them off. The Nexus can suffer from that too, given anyone can relay, but can still be good if you are relaying for Raluca or a Tech on a bonus-needing project. AB2 is good for getting Pilots spored.
