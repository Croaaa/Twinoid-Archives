Ok ok, we've been reminded that we're overdue for a season change so we're going to try to address that.

Here's the caveat upfront, though. The last dev that can/will do season changes for us is knee deep in Dead Cells and we're not sure how much he's checking twinoid anymore. That's not to say we won't try (we will!), but it is to say that for all our best intentions, we may not get a season change anytime soon.

So now that that's out of the way, here's where we can discuss a new season! With just two of us, we quickly run out of ideas - both for themes and options. So in this thread, you get to play moderator!

If you've got an idea for a new season theme, shoot them out here. The more developed it is (i.e. we don't have to write the entire news blurb/thread/etc.) the more likely we are to consider it as an option. So here's what we need from you:

Code Name - a two or three word name for the season.
News Title - not crucial, but if you've got something clever, include it!
News Article - an RP announcement of the season, the theme and the options chosen. This appears in the pushed news to all Mush players so the more creative, the better
Description - a short (approximately one paragraph) description of the season
Options - Choose as many of the options below as you like, but make sure you work them into something cohesive that goes with your theme! Since we switch each season, the next season will feature Finola and Chao, that is not a changeable option.

  • Starting Injuries
  • Start Hungry
  • Start in Orbit
  • Starting Spore

Final note: Consider your options carefully! If we can make this season change happen, it might be the last we get. Make your options fun, but don't go "hard mode" just to have it happen for a season since it might be a tad longer than that

I agree with the idea of looking at each option's pros and cons in light of a probably very long season. I'd be happy if someone comes up with a theme if people like the choices... but before we get there, here's my two cents as a long time player and fellow dev who would really like this little gem of a game live on:


Starting Spores
A little randonmess would make the Mush hunt more psychological and less likely to be taken over by that one player running all the numbers, because nobody is entirely safe/clean.
Transfers are more likely to happen.

Advantage: Mush
Suggestion: On.
>Judging characters rather than just counting is the fun core of this game. Enhancning this is good. And transfers happen anyway.


Starting Injuries & Alien Fruit
Makes ships more varied by giving rather large random disadvantages/advantages. Makes it harder to hunt Mush by numbers.
Makes the game harder overall, some injuries can be frustrating, and standing in line for expensive surgery is not fun.

Advantage: Mush
Suggestion: Off.
>Spores and Planet do similar things with more agency and fewer downsides.


Start Hungry
Gives an excuse for being hurt early on, possibly dirty.
Makes the game harder by using up food. Makes low activity ships worse again because inactives starve before the crew can use the trade mechanic that was added to alleviate this. Makes them even worse because this dumps Morale.

Advantage: Mush / Inactives
Suggestion: Off!
>This is not a good idea.


Start in Orbit
Free planet gives and early opportunity for plants and O2 - some randomness for game variety, but still requiring choices. Early access to a fun activity.
Takes 1 fuel and some AP to get to the usual starting position. Incomplete crews who don't know how to hack are prevented from scanning. Suicidal Mush may crash the Icarus on the planet.

Advantage: Humans
Suggestion: On.
>A free random planet is nice to have, easy to ditch, and good to let new players see fun content early.


So in short: Start in orbit with some spores.
