You overestimate Motion Twin's capabilities. They're a small company, with a handful of developers, and only two of them are left from the original team. They not only have all the Twinoid games, with several international servers, but also a few games outside of Twinoid. The glass is not completely empty, however. Blackmagic made sure that he fixed almost all the major bugs before he left and that mods would have the possibility to prepare season changes with minimum help from the current devs, who can also intervene if serious bugs occur.

The problem is that it's not just a small tweak and can NOT be done easily.

We made this request in the past, shortly after the developer left the company (unfortunately, it was still in full swing while he was here so it wasn't necessary or something we ever thought we might ask for). The answer was that after looking at the code, it's too intertwined with too many other things and the one guy that can occasionally poke the code if something major goes wrong didn't feel comfortable making the change without breaking the entire game. Believe me, we'd like to see that change as much as anyone, but there's pretty much no such thing as a "small tweak" with Mush.
