Hi, all!

I recently created script that shows your friends in the flights page. It'll show your friends with whom you were aboard Daedalus on the flight, or, if you visit other user profile, it'll show you when this user played with your friends.

Casting ships are by default labeled with casting name (click on it to reveal friends list).

Screenshot:
https://cl.ly/0U3L3P052h1e/castings.png

It's in kind of a beta now. Please feel free to give it a try and give your feedback

Link for installation: https://github.com/tamtamchik/mush-sc...
