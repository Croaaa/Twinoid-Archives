I decided to post a small review to the IGN comments section.

thoughts?

great-games-you-may-have-missed-in-2013?
Mush
This is a great niche game but there are some important things you need to know before just diving in. Each player's activities require action and movement points (AP and MP) which generally regenerate 1 per 3hr cycle, so you won't actually be playing the game directly much over the course of each day,

the true gameplay really comes through the use of the ingame chat to organise the crew and figure out who the alien Mush are. This is entirely dictated by the players so each ship can operate very differently. The game only has a very limited and misleading tutorial so time does need to be spent reading forum topics, checking the mush wiki and learning from your mistakes, but when you do start to figure it all out you become an important crew member and it really opens up.

Add to that the random possibility that you may also be an alien Mush with additional abilities makes for a very impressive experience, albeit at a slow pace.

Now comes the important part. the core gameplay requires human characters to act for the benefit of the human team, and for alien Mush characters to play against the humans. This can lead to a number of ways to break the rules and be sanctioned and/or restrictions imposed, not something many players will be used to.

Logging in just to grab a weapon and kill some team-mates will likely get you reported and restricted from playing another ship, as will committing suicide, or just performing actions that are considered bad for your team. There are many of those as the alien Mush also need ways to bring about the demise of the crew. Revealing you are an alien Mush to the humans is a no no too.

And finally, remember that if you try out the game and give in at the start, you may doom the entire ship so at least give it a chance and stay until the ship has finished, which can be a done with a minimum of input.
