Following the annoucement of the imminent closure of Mush, we are working to back up your profiles so that they can be accessible someday on eMush, as part of the collective effort of the Eternaltwin project.

(as long as your profiles are saved somewhere, we can retrieve them at any time)

We currently have a program that retrieves the history of your games, your character's experience, and your skins.

We are also working on recovering rankings and end-of-game pages.

Tests on a few profiles are scheduled for this weekend, and we are making good progress. We will keep you informed as soon as everything is ready.

In the meantime (and as a backup), other options are available to you:

- Use LAbare's Histori(Mush) script, which will back up your games, your experience, and your achievements.
You will download a file ending in ".html" and a file ending in ".json". You can send me the ".json" file (and keep it safely on your side as well).

- Log in to Pictoid, which will save your Twinoid profile (mostly your pictos) in a database managed by Angelisium.

PS: For those interested in saving their casting forum posts, please don't hesitate to contact me. We have a way to back them up.
