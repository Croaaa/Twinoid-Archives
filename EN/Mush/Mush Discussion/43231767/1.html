One thing I've found, every ship is different. Sometimes only a little, sometimes crazy much. At least with random ships.... maybe you awesome casting folks sometimes have the same ship twice

There will be inactives, many or few. There will be rulebreakers, mild or severe. There will be awesome play, a tiny bit just from you, or cross the entire ship involving most of the players.

The best solace I can offer, those ships that have so much going against them, where the human team is in a desperate fight that does not appear possible to have a happy ending...

Do your best. Just do your best. Learn all you can for your future ships. Once you really know the game well, you might have 'the skills that pay the bills', I mean as a player, not character. Your skills as a player may well one day turn what today appears to be a 'hopeless wreck' into an incredible, almost unbelievable short-lived ship's human victory against crushing odds.

Until then - still do your best and learn all you can from every ship, and take solace in knowing that the ship dies fast if it is in so hopeless a position, even if you fight the best you can - unless and until you find ways to turn that steep decline into a win for your team.
