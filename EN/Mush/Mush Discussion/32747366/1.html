Glory is one of many things to play for. There's plenty others, here are some of my favorites:

Trying to earn the highest number of likes on the end page, as human... and also as mush.

Trying to gain game achievements and getting the highest possible 'mush game score' possible (View through your Twinoid profile, maxes at 1000, the highest I've seen anyone have yet is a little over 400)

Trying to get the most possible wins for your team as you can. You have to track this yourself, however the average wins at game end, for all time on this server is around 27% human win (made it to Sol or Eden with no mush alive, with or without the cure), 73% mush win (any other end game condition, from infection of Eden to destroyed in space... stats unfortunately include the initial test ships which were destroyed by 'impish administrators' ).

Simultaneously trying to kill the fewest members of your team as possible (harder to do as human than mush) and convert or kill the most other members to your side as possible (harder to do as human than mush).

Trying to reach Eden (as of a few days ago, that had been done only 46 times on this server, including Infected Eden).

Trying to survive the highest number of days possible with the highest number of players alive as possible.

There's other things to 'play for' too. Glory is one of many.

There's a few things you can do as a mush to gain glory as well. I was recently pleasantly surprised to discover that !!Finding Starmaps!! gives glory to mush as well as humans. Just make sure your ship finds at least 3 starmaps a game day, and you've completely counteracted your glory loss. Don't like all the humans gaining glory every cycle? Convert them all. Sure, they get (or lose) bonus glory based on how soon or late they were converted.... but as long as you are doing all the converting, you're getting all the extra glory for conversion. Think outside the box.
