The long awaited mushy update is here! Players will enjoy a variety of new features to enhance their Mush experience!

- With an enhanced Door Subsystem installed, a Commander can vent Oxygen out of the 4 Ship Bays and a random player in the room will asphyxiate the next cycle. Upon opening other doors, this effect will spread throughout the Daedalus each cycle until the doors are closed.

- To counter the effects of the Meridon Scrambler, Traxes now have Mind Control systems installed. These systems will allow any enemy Trax to control any player other than Chun, forcing them to sabotage the ship from within for 3 cycles.

- By replacing the Medlab with a Clone Bay, players will be able to kill each other without fear knowing that their comrades are just a cycle away from being cloned! However, each death will result in the loss of 1 random skill. If the Clone Bay is damaged or sabotaged, players in queue for cloning will die for good in the following cycle.

- In an effort to reach out to our griefer playerbase, players will now be able to play as *Lan*, a Lanius crew member who sucks out oxygen in the room he ends a cycle on. Crew members present will asphyxiate the following cycle. He has the special trait Alien, which keeps him from communicating with the rest of the crew. He learns the following skills: Solid, Technician, Shooter, IT Expert, and a new skill, Mankind's Worst Decision, a passive skill that reflects the consequence of bringing him on board which increases daily morale loss by 1. For a limited time, players who are able to unlock Mankind's Worst Decision before being quarantined will receive a free Cyanide Pill to use on their next ship!
- Cyanide Pills are now available in the vending machine for 4 Klix.

- The new Hacking System will allow players to hack into Hunter ships and adjust their bios to perform a variety of actions, including but not limited to deactivating their target's food destruction and vocoder announcements, enabling them to taunt the Daedalus every cycle the victim is in flight!

Please be on the lookout for bugs in this new content and enjoy your flights aboard the new and improved Daedalus!
