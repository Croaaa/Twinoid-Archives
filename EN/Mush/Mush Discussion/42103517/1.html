Game Bug issues:

◾ Anomaly detector not always showing broken fuel and O2 tanks; in general it seems to work rather randomly.

◾ Randomly Invisible narcotics distiller

◾ Raluca lost on planet loses morale from being too alone, and if someone else is lost on the planet with her, she also loses morale from being crowded that same cycle.

◾ Merchant ships drop goodies if shot once, don't have to be fully destroyed.

◾ Magnetic net project only returns a ship to the Daedalus if it has a pilot flying it; ships that were ejected from do not return to the Daedalus with the Net.

◾ Complex ritual required to pick up more than one drone.

◾ A carried cat continues to create noise where it had been picked up, but not where it actually is, until it is finally dropped again somewhere else.

◾ D1000, supposed to attack 3x per cycle, rarely/never do more than 5 damage and may only be attacking once per cycle.

◾ Research 'Perpetual Hydration' states it gives 3 glory, actually gives 0.

◾ The tooltip for CPU Priority claims this setting can be changed once every 8 cycles, however it functions variably across different ships (and sometimes varying on the same ship), allowing the setting to be changed once a day, once a cycle, and at will at different times on different ships. (Translation error versus bug?) (Bug in the variability)

◾ The Transfer skill tooltip claims the skill is covert, but it functions invisibly.

◾ Organic waste informs those with the Chef skill that it is cookable, though it actually is not cookable.

◾ Those with the Chef skill see that they need to spend a cooking point to consume items; however no actual cooking point is used when they eat. A chef point also appears to be required for free cooking-related actions, such as producing coffee from the coffee machine.

◾ When a merchant ship is present, all items, even hidden items in storerooms are visible through the trade screen, and can be traded as part of the deal.

◾ Meridon scrambler causes hunters to occasionally fire at traders, not only at 'each other'.

◾ The comm terminal in-game text states that inactives can be traded from the bridge, but actually can only be traded from the storerooms.

◾ A gold player with the apprentice skill whose gold days run out before apprentice is used is unable to use apprentice to gain a skill if they already have 2 or more skills.

◾ One report of Derek going on expo without a spacesuit, to a planet without O2, and dying the moment the expo started, cause 'abandoned'. The other players on the expo completed it normally, and even did a later expo to another airless planet with Kuan without a spacesuit - Kuan survived like normal.

◾ If the Thalasso vendor project is purchased and a shower has been removed, the illusion of a Thalasso shower is created where the shower had been.

◾ Missions have a button that allows you to 'hide' them, but clicking it does nothing.

Gold purchase bug issues:
◾ Players running IPv6 need to deactivate that in order to purchase gold. (Players running IPv4 do not have this issue)

Forum Bug issues:
◾ Casting acceptance notification has wrong url.


Working as intended?:
◾ The guarding skill does not return players 'Kicked out' with the solid or wrestling skills to the rooms they had entered from when they are 'kicked out' a second time.

◾ Pilgredissimo, a glory listing of "Earned by everyone when PILGRED is repaired." appears on each ship's end page glory list, always giving 0 glory to each member (it is intended for casting ships only, and should not appear on every ship)

◾ The algorithm that selects the end page 'mentionable traits' such as "Super Optimizer" works oddly for some traits. Inactives often win the Super Optimizer trait (because they never move, thus never convert AP to MP?). Occasionally the same player earns both Super Optimizer and Energy Dissipator... and occasionally Violence is Evil is earned despite using aggressive acts on a ship with other players who did not do any aggressive acts (example here, Derek's actions show he attacked, but he still somehow won Violence is Evil, as well as both Super Optimizer and Energy Dissipator: http://mush.twinoid.com/theEnd/4932)

◾ If the cat is killed while Death Announcements are off, a message about the cat's death is still created.

◾It is currently possible to finish Pilgred and jump to Sol (using the hacker kit) before all players have connected to the ship.

◾Players dead before mush selection are possible targets for mush selection, resulting in game starting with a dead mush, however the research options a dead mush unlocks are not unlocked.

◾Opening the 'sterile' survival kit can cause you to become dirty.

The first is the amount of AP being spent on your ship, no matter what it's being spent on; as most players probably know, the more AP spent (or wasted, as the case may be) the harder the game gets. This is why myself and others emphasize AP efficiency, and not wasting AP on the Mad Kube early on, etc.

The second factor is whether one of the events that gets called is a new hunter wave. If it isn't getting called to appear, then the number of hunters for the next wave gets increased, until it eventually does get called.

I have personally observed one other factor in relation to this phenomenon of "way too many hunters at once" - How many hunters your ship has destroyed.
