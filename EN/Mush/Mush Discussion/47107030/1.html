Re: useless captains, this is the (main) purpose of the Hacker Kit. It allows you to break into the Command Terminal and move the ship even if you aren't the Captain. The major downside is that it's quite an AP hog - it's not unknown to blow your whole AP bar without breaking in. It may also end up hidden by someone on your crew, to keep it out of mush hands. (Or, perhaps because the mush got there and hid it first!)

Green Jelly is not as widely used as it used to be, because it was pretty badly nerfed. You used to be able to use it on a camera in a crowded dorm with no one the wiser, but now cameras will spot its use, making it... well, pointless.

The only thing that I have to add about Transfer is that it isn't quite free because it requires the target to have a spore on them first. That means that the mush either had to set up their target in advance, or they may have to spike them right there first, often under a under camera. And to repeat WeakestLink: both involved players lose all their skills when a transfer happens. A Mush will often rechoose theirs if they'll be useful to their plan (Chao's Wrestler/Shooter/Crazy Eyes, for example), and if the human is online they'll usually start shouting about being a transfer victim. Otherwise, they'll stay silent and their skills will remain blank. Mush may try some more tricks regarding this though, so be wary!

It seems like we've been having an inactivity spike lately, but I've seen these come and go before. Hopefully you'll have the luck of a decent ship soon.

How to counter transfer? Well...
Joke: tell to Chun a code in case you got transfered so you may call it every time Chun asks... but seriously, who would do this?

You should remember that the victim of being transferred might be everybody who may carry a spore, so each person on this ship beside Chun, even, or especially, confirmed human who has got an illness/disorter, double-eaten, Natamy test, etc.

How to make sure you don't be targetted with transfer?
1. Be Chun... or Frieda with Antique Perfume skill and wash every 3 cycles.
2. Be in place where nobody can get you, so stay outside in Patrol Ship or Pasiphae.
3. Have all the Mush killed.
4. Be Mush.

When you cannot get to point 1. ...
The point 2. is very situational and I don't recommend you to follow this unless you realize that the Mush is going to transfer into you, for example you're in active rampage... or PILGRED repaired discretely and you're commander.
The main goal is to reach the point 3.
Even don't think about the point 4. as human.

Think there are 16 crewmembers and at most 13 of them might be transfer target. When they don't know the Mush they won't run away catching a Patrol Ship and staying outside.
Basically, you can't stop transferring, the Mush has open way to each of crewmates, they just pick one per each Mush.

Let's return to main question: How to counter transfer?
When you can't stop the Mush transferring, give them as few advantages as you can.
So the answer is: don't trust your body.
Remember that the answer excludes Chun herself.
What should you do to follow the answer?

1. Don't hold Blasters or any shooting weapon if your character has Shooter skill! You don't have to pick Shooter skill to let the Mush pick it. Hide the weapon somewhere to use in the further but remember that your body will see the hidden object.
2. Don't hold Knife or Grenade, nor Hacker Kit while expedition. Follow point 1.
3. As commander don't guard Front Corridor to prevent from accessing Command Terminal. If someone else guard, make sure you don't have access to Commander Terminal in the time the Mush may make much harm if had access to them, you may even get rid of AP/MP while exploring planet.
4. Don't leave the most important private conversations open, these ones with some information the Mush can use to harm the ship (for example about weapon location).
5. As Chao/Derek, don't stay at full AP, remember that you may eat a ration to gain same while big actions, but the Mush won't.
6. As comms manager, shoot traders giving PILGRED offers if 2 Mush are alive.

And more, more... just follow the answer and the advices I've given.
