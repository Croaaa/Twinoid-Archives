March 15th: Beware the Ides of Mush!

Friends, Mushes, Mankarogs, lend me your ears! On March 15th, Season Three will come to an end, and Season Four will be unleashed upon us. Chao and Finola will be (re)joining us during this season. Unfortunately, the Cryo Chamber had a major malfunction just before you woke up, and some of you will wake up to find yourselves injured. But that's not all that awaits you! Every ship will come pre-stocked with a few random fruits. Might there be...? Yes! If you get very lucky, you might even end up with a Jumpkin or two in the Refectory. Frugivores, rejoice!

Those who still want to play Derek and Andie still can - they'll be available for use in casting ships.

Also note that if you're on a ship during the time the season change hits, your ship won't count in the Rankings for either season. You have time to wrap things up if you have a longer ship in progress right now. If you're not concerned about the rankings, then carry on. The first ship you start after the season change will have the new content.
