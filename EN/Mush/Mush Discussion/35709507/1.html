The basic idea is a 17th playable character for people who prefer playing the Mush-side.
Let's call it MiniMush for now (name pending).

You can choose this character only when a ship has all 16 characters picked.
So you arrive on a ship with Alpha Mush already picked.

Here are your features:

  • A small furry creature that has a lot of MP, and a few AP
  • Have very low HP, like 5
  • You can be secretly/invisibly/privately (I'm inclined to choose private) picked up/dropped by Mush or make yourself be picked up/dropped by them
  • You don't get AP at cycle changes, only when you are in a Mush's inventory
  • You can't sleep in beds obviously
  • You appear as an easily distinguishable non-human entity on the Tracker
  • You might be able to pick up 1 non-heavy item (might be overpowered)
  • Can see the Wall, can't comment on it, but can Whisper to people
  • You can harrass people by using some cool skills:
    • facehug someone - to slowly asphyxiate them; the victim or anyone else can remove you from the face, but if they fail to do so in a set amount of time (say - 3 cycles), you will kill the person
    • cover your tracks (remove own movement entries from the room log)
    • make people dirty
    • cause illness
    • other awesome abilities you will come up with



Issues I'd like to work out some good solutions to:

  • Should MiniMush take up an inventory space or just be a "status" on the Mush?
