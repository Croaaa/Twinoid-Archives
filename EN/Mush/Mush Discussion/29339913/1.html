We are orbiting a planet with oxygen, and want to organize an expedition. How long will it take? Is it immediate with generated results based on characters and equipment, or do we have to move/fight/do something there (ie we need a lot of AP and movement points)?

And how to bring back the oxygen?

Gather your expo team in the Icarus Bay, and have at least 4 AP to launch. This AP expenditure is required by someone, but it doesn't have to be a Pilot - just note that having the Pilot skill on one of the team will make for a smoother ride, pun intended.

Some things you'll want to have in your inventory before you leave include: the drill for potential fuel deposits, rope, weapons, armor, a compass, spacesuits (if there is no O2 present,) etc. Drop all your Trackers and all of your Walkies in the Icarus Bay, except for one of them, so that you can keep in contact with the ship with that single Walkie. The list is by no means exhaustive, and you'll have to tailor your gear to the situation at hand; every planet has a different mix of potential threats.

The expo lasts about 90 minutes, but circumstances may force it to be shortened. All combat (if any) is autoresolved, and negative events can lead to team members getting lost or even killed. If they are lost, a second expo is required to find them, or else they'll die.

When your team gets to the area with O2, they may gather some of it, and it will be added to the ship upon your return. (A similar thing happens with fuel, if you find it.) You might need more than one expo to get to that area, however. Each member of the team allows two areas to be searched, so larger planets require two expos, typically. Each area can be searched once - this means that if you fail to get something from an area, you can't try there again for better results (but any unexplored areas will still end up being searched.) Make sure to let your crew know about injuries, and have someone on standby with the Medkit, just in case.

That should cover the basics...
