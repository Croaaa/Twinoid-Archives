It's been established that the longer you stay in one place, the more hunters will come after you. I also theorize that (at least before Day 10 or so) that the longer the interval between waves of hunters appearing in the same location, the more hunters will appear, when the next wave finally arrives. On my last ship, we hit a massive wave of 31 hunters on Day 9, and we only had 32 hull left. We didn't make it. We'd also been in the same place for a few days, with a long interval between waves.

I further theorize that if you're able to move the ship often enough, and clear the ones that follow you to your destination, the followup waves will be smaller, at least initially.
