Sadly, this can't work. Reasons:

  • Time spent on old games is time not spent on going forward and creating new games to attract new people. Even if paid at the right price, this is not something that helps the future of the company, which is quite a high concern at the moment.
  • Blackmagic, the main dev, has left Motion Twin without explaining how the code works, and it's a mess. Fixing even a minor bug would require to understand the structure of the whole game to find where the bug lies and to make sure the fix won't break anything (and it will break something anyway). We're talking weeks here.
  • This list is not up to date, most major bugs are fixed and the others are quite minor or impact only a few ships. Even if we were to pick only the top 10 in it, it would be quite a disappointing update for a lot of work and it would barely improve our game experience.
  • I think you underestimate a lot the cost of development and overestimate a lot the amount of money the community would put into this. Mush has always been barely profitable, I can't see people suddenly throwing money at the devs for this.

Crowdfunding is a nice idea. Fixing those bugs would be nice. But nice won't be of much help in this situation that everyone whishes would be better.
