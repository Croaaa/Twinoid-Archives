Lab Rat Felinophile

Since not everyone will have the luxury of being Gold whenever they want, and the game is meant for people to be able to play even when everyone is bronze, I think a list of what skills to pick for each character for each tier would be more useful.

Here is my suggestion:

Jin Su:
Bronze: Leader is one of the only skills to give morale, and there is a reason it's first level. I would stick with that. If you're high enough level and you don't pick your skill right away, and Janice seems very active or Kuan Ti has Leader, Logistics can be very good too.
Silver: I would go with Leader and Logistics, for the above reasons.
Gold: Leader, Logistic, Pilot, Shooter, or replace Shooter with Strateguru

Frieda:
Bronze: Astro. No questioning it.
Silver: Astro and IT. Free planet analysis is always useful.
Gold: Astro, It, Survivalist and Pilot. You can't scan for planets when you're in orbit, so if you have the AP, might as well join the expo. With Survivalist, you can bring back more stuff.

Kuan Ti:
BronzeThis one is tricky. I usually don't pick right away. Designer isn't bad, free Neron points and all, but Astro and Tech can be useful if nobody else has those skills on board. Wait until people have updated the skill thread before you pick.
Silver: I would pick Tech and either Astro or Designer.
Gold: Astro, Designer, Tech and Leader

Janice:
Bronze: Anything but Shrink is pretty much grieving.
Silver: Shrink and Diplomacy.
Gold: Shrink, Diplomacy, NERON's best friend and probably IT expert. If you fear for your morale, Abnegation can help, but it has limited uses, and free IT points are worth more IMO.

Roland:
Bronze: Pilot
Silver: Pilot, Shooter
Gold: Pilot, Shooter, Fireman, Sprinter/Optimist. If you go on expos, Sprinter reveals more zones, which can help, and you get to move around more... but Roland typically doesn't move that much. Optimist can help you if the Dorm for pilots and the Dorm for Janice patients are different (happens in a lot of my games).

Hua:
Bronze: Pilot
Silver: Pilot, Survivalist. Hua's glory is based on expos, might as well pick a skill that helps with that. Mind you, Pilot/Botanist is pretty good too is Ian is slacking of, but Hua has little reason to be around the lab in the first place.
Gold: Pilot, Survivalist, Tech and probably Botanist. Determined isn't that good and the going back one is very rarely of any use at all if people go on expos with full health.

Paola:
Bronze: Logistics, definitely
Silver: Logistics and either Radio Expert (if low level) or Rebel (if high level)
Gold: Logistics, Radio Expert, Rebel, Shooter. Paola has little reason to spend time in the lab so Biologist isn't that useful. Might be taken instead of Shooter though if the research team isn't very active.

Chao
Bronze: Wrestler or Torturer are probably best
Silver: Wrestler AND Torturer
Gold: Wrestler, Torturer, probably Crazy Eyes and either Shooter and Survivalist. Why Survivalist? Chao is second in command, and it's good practice for the commander to be on expos. If Jin Su dies or becomes inactive, might as well take advantage of Chao's presence.
I know Chao has a blaster in his pic, but without Pilot, Shooter isn't that great. This being said, if you are hoping to play on the long run, then most of Chao's skills aren't going to be useful once the mush are gone, so you might want to take Shooter instead, which at least can be used against Hunters from a turret.

Finola:
Bronze: Medic. It's everything Biologist is, but better. Finola's main job is research, so it should be her first pick. Only get Biologist if you're 1st level.
Silver: Biologist and Medic. The skills combine and that makes Finola very efficient at researching. That means you need less relays, less cooperation from others and less coordination with them. It can make up for an inactive Chun or Ian.
Gold: Biologist, Medic, OCD and Diplomacy/Nurse. It's not really Finola's place to go on expos, so Diplomacy isn't that useful for her. But nurse does nothing the other skills don't, except save her 2AP per day. Really not that good. I'd pick Diplomacy in case Janice is unavailable, but at this point it's your call.

Stephen:
Bronze: Chef. It allows him to read the properties of all food, which is pretty useful. The free cooking really is just a bonus. Or if you're high level enough, go for Apprenticeship and pick any skill anyone else has (Only hope doesn't stack with Chun's).
Silver: Chef, Apprenticeship.
Gold: Chef, Apprenticeship, Solid (useful for moving the freezer to Icarus Bay)... and probably Shooter. Stephen can become a Jack-of-all trades and fill whatever role is needed in late game, so it's good for him to be versatile.

Ian:
Bronze: Botanist. Definitely needed, Hua can't be expected to have it and you want to see how your plants are doing. The free points are definitely useful, too.
Silver: Botanist, Green Thumb. The extra Hydropots from Green Thumb can be sorely needed.
Gold: Botanist, Biologist, Mycologist, Green Thumb. Biologist is good to help with research. Mycologist is really awesome to remove spores but I've never seen a Ian doing much healing to begin with which is why I put it as a Gold choice and not a Silver one. IMO it doesn't beat extra hydropots.

Chun:
Bronze: Mankind's Only Hope. As with Janice, not picking it is almost grieving.
Silver: Only Hope, Lethargy/Sneaky. Regaining 3 AP per cycle change (after the first 4) is pretty good. Just coordinate well as you'll be needed in the lab for research and the Medlab bed should still go to Finola (she still has a better rate with 2 AP than you do with 3). Of if you're expecting aggressive mush, Sneaky helps with that.
Gold: Only Hope, Lethargy, Nurse, Sneaky.

Raluca:
Bronze: Physicist. Really needed for Pilgred. Might go with Tech instead if there isn't one yet.
Silver: Physicist, Tech/Genius. Genius is very powerful but a one-time thing. Tech is more useful on the long run, but there might not be a long run if it takes ages to finish the Plasma Shield/Serum. It's a matter of preference.
Gold: Physicist, Tech, Genius, Detached. Raluca has enough morale issues as it it, good to take all the help you can get.

Gioele:
Bronze: Paranoid or Caffeine Junkie. I have a preference for Junkie, it's RP and very powerful towards the end game.
Silver: Caffeine Junkie, Paranoid. Solid might be considered if you end up moving things around for everyone.
Gold: Junkie, Paranoid, Solid, Astrophysicist. The other two are really meh IMO.

Eleesha:
Bronze: Tracker. No other way to print the list and you'll be expected to have it.
Silver: Tracker, Observant. Searching every room for free and getting a chance to notice other people's actions are pretty useful.
Gold: Tracker, Observant, IT, Technician. After the mush detective work is over, IT and Tech are pretty useful for the ship's day to day life.

Terrence:
Bronze: Robotics or Tech. Tech if nobody else has it.
Silver: Robotics, Tech. I know some people go for Pilot but there usually are enough of those when you only have 2 slots to use.
Gold: Polyvalent Terrence with Robotics, Tech, Pilot and Shooter, or mechanic Terrence with Robotics, Tech, IT and Metalworker. IT isn't used that much, but Pilot and Shooter aren't as interesting without each other. Metalworker complements both Technician and Robotics well.

And that's it. Phew! What do you guys think?
