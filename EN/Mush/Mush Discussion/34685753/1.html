Rain

No, whether someone skips the tutorial or not, they are already on a real ship from the time they pick their character, and stay on that same ship.

Reporting inactives is a waste of your time, and the judges' too. Inactives aren't sanctioned because serial inactive players are unlikely - it's more likely that person tried the game, didn't like it, isn't coming back. So a sanction would serve no purpose.

Best advice I can give you is adjust your expectations. Have fun with the players who are active. See how well you can band together to work around what you're missing. Adjust your expectations, and your goals, to the ship you are on, and the game isn't in any way ruined.

Inactivity (new players, players not communicating or playing) will not be sanctioned, do not report them. We do not want to sanction new players who might not be engaged. Learning to work around these players is part of our game. You *can* succeed on as ship that has many deaths and/or a fair number of inactive players. Luck and team work amongst those left will be in order but it can be done. Thoughtful communication and patience can go a long way.
