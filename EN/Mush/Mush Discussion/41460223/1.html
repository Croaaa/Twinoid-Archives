My thoughts on Janice - shes one of the harder characters to play as a solo mush mostly since you only get 8 AP a day. Getting 2 spores and a secret shower is more than a full day's AP after C.Serum.

Why she looks mushy: Her active skill to give morale is generally not needed by most of the crew if coms does their job - generally just 1-2 comforts on Raluca & Chun at the beginning in case the mush try demoralizing them. In order to give a morale to everyone you need to stand in both dorms since standing in alpha dorm is generally a waste but experienced players will still probably accept that just to avoid the suspicion. Also she has very low AP gain which is made worse if you convert AP to MP to reach the nexus (works out to once every 3 cycles that she can actually do that)

Meanwhile when I play Janice I always have IT expert as a second skill, It expert is an awesome skill for her since it means you can advance research 4 - 8 times before standing idle in a dorm - very few people think you're mush if you knock out C.Serum and Antispore gas before walking to bravo dorm.
