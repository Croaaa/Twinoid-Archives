I would agree that most characters can do great with 2 or even only one skill (Janice-Shrink), some are much better with gold, but it depends on the character whether the extra skills of Gold are worth it, and you would also need the XP to actually unlock skill 3 and 4 as well. The deciding factor is not necessarily how good the skills are, but if they combine or stack in a powerful way. Two powerful, but AP heavy skills are only meh together, because you have only one AP bar, and it runs out all to quickly. Skills that give free 'points' stack much better, and skills that recover AP or morale passively stack the best with other skills, like Logistics.


My favorite Gold character has to be Terrence. His first four skills are all insanely good, and they stack real well, too. Shooter gives free shots for Pilot, and Robotics (in a manner of speaking) gives free points in Tech, because 2 turbo drones are 4 x as good for repairing stuff as 1 normal drone. (and tech also gives free tech points, so you can still tech out, even if you have to pilot all the time). The cherry on top is pilot can get you scrap for your drones and drone upgrades as well, making him totally self sufficient in his core skills. Honorable mention to any character with Logistics/Caffeine junkie, because, in this game especially, more AP is always better, and every other skill is better with more AP
