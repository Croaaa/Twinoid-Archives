A few remarks about that list...


Not real bugs :

  • Broken patrollers count as 2 objects on the alert bar. That's not a bug, actually. Patrollers are composed of two different parts, the hull (or the ship itself) and the inside. If you pay attention to Neron announcements, there are two different ones for broken patrollers : one saying that a patroller is broken, and one mentioning "patrol command" or something like that. Drones used to be able to fix the hull, but when you took off, the inside was still broken. Now drones fix the whole patroller.
  • We get more mycoalarms then the game says we should. No. The game says that we should get 5 mycoalarms, and that's what we get. The devs considered giving less mycoalarms when they were improved, but never actually did it. They decided to implement Defacer and Green Jelly instead.
  • Increasing of hunter waves after ship moving. It's just due to bad luck. And it's not due to Trail Minimizer, which works perfectly fine.
  • Distinction 'Days without sleep' counts sleeping on a sofa as a type of not sleeping. That distinction might be bugged, but not that way. It seems to increase by one for each long ship, whether you sleep or not. Some people think that "without sleep" means "without going idle", but if it's the case, it doesn't count the real number of days.

Bugs that are supposed to be fixed (either announced in the changelog or confirmed on the French server) :

  • Plasma shield doesn't work anymore after being traded
  • Finola/Chao or Andie/Derek can't be on the lists (confirmed)
  • Objects gained during expo now appear when the Icarus comes back
  • Open air brains and severe burns now kill you if you're dirty.

Bugs that were reported (or reported again) recently, just before the last update, This was a deliberately short list of the main bugs, those that really need to be fixed. Some were fixed, the following were not :

  • Anomaly detector not showing broken fuel and O2 tanks.
  • The Xyloph list can have no negative results (I'm not sure this hasn't been fixed, actually)
  • Right side Lateral reactor that can't be reported or repaired by players
  • Randomly Invisible narcotics distiller
  • One report of Derek going on expo without a spacesuit, to a planet without O2, and dying the moment the expo started, cause 'abandoned'.

Bug that should be fixed soon, when the devs have time, because it's a little complex and will take time :

  • No possibility to do an expedition for retrieving a lost crew member.

All the other bugs in the spreadsheet are confirmed and were reported at least once (some on many occasions).

The current PM bug has also been reported in detail and should hopefully be fixed soon.
