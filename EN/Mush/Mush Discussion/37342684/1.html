SupermanStuff

Well Chao's greatest strength is also in his weakness. His hyperactivity lends him to being really great at moving around and investigating, which I normally do during the nights. Mornings, and on occasional days, I keep him planted in front of the Shower and torture liberally - but that's besides the point. He is amazing at checking rooms people shouldn't be visiting and map awareness is key for a role like him, and eleesha. See a blip in a center storage? Check it out.

With Kuan and Chao I normally have a set cycle I walk through. Primary areas are the center storages, nexus, rear storages and engine room. I leave the front of the ship alone given it normally has heavy traffic and dedicated people hanging around it - in my experience mush normally operate in the middle/rear of the ship.

You'll often find ships will push out anabolics and then never use them, especially early on. While initial research shouldn't be diverted from the gas/serum early on if you can squeeze in some research into that (since everyone has great proficiency with it) it will be very helpful. No one ever touches anabolics even after they are made and it gives you a massive boost to your already impressive movement as Chao.

One thing I try to do, for both Chao and Kuan-ti - is start a channel with my "Department". For Chao this means Chun, Eleesha and possibly the captain (I normally don't include jin myself though). Take initiative to keep up with them and see their progress. This keeps you in the loop of their search and helps coordinate while also lowering the chance of one of them running off and killing a crew member on half baked evidence. You should take the initiative to start the mush hunting private chat but keep in mind that other than Chun no one is proven - but by gauging your teammates reactions and seeing their work you can get a feel for if they are trust worthy or not. Also don't get bummed if Chun and Eleesha aren't too talkative, remember they are nervous about trusting you too at first!

The same actually goes for Kuan Ti! Open a channel with Raluca, Terrence and Gioele and call them your Engineering department. Gioele is a stretch since he also may want to work with the mush hunting or help with planet scanning - bronze or silver Gioeles normally appreciate the added direction. I use this as a springboard to coordinate Pilgred's resets, Neron's relays and making sure the Pasiphae is getting used. Once again, this also lets me tally up contribution and notice if a team member isn't really doing much with their AP or is acting inconsistent - which I then forward to chun.

As far as actual work I do with Kuan, I am a level 5 gold with him so I mostly do a bit of whatever needs done most. I have a list of jobs that I rank based on priority and work from there. At the top is normally things like Planet Scanning if Frieda is inactive, then Pilgred resets and repairs if the ship is in danger or equipment is broken. After that I try to give speeches once a day in each dorm if I think we need the morale boosts. I always treat Nexus work as a side job to do at the end if there is nothing else major that requires my attention - the exception to this is if Plasma shields is open in which case that is the priority.

Kuan Ti can do so much it's easy to get swamped especially if you don't sleep often or eat. Claim a bed and stay rested and keep a list of top priorities based on the ship. Just my thoughts on him, im sure others have played him longer than I have can add their two clix in as well
