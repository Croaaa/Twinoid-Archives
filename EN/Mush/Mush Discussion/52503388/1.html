If you picked Sprinter+Firefighter as Roland, I'd probably assume you were either a griefer or had your "advanced mush" option turned off and let the RNG pick a bad combination for you. (Which is actually what happened to the poor Roland on my current ship.) That skillset relies on defeating the mush and getting a long ship, where regular expos and firefighting actually come into play. Now, if you were a transfer victim and were skill-less when the last mush was killed, with a surplus of pilots on your ship, maybe then you could consider that. At game start though, it would be a poor choice.
