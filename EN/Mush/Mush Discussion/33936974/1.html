AI Inhibitors are just a flair thing as far as I'm aware. They change the way Neron does announcements. Activated and it's like a standard automated announcement (e.g. something broken - contact technician)
With them deactivated, you see Neron's crazy personality. For example, when our oxygen tank broke down, Neron said
"It's just a little thing, but a problem which has arisen has forced me to recalculate the estimated asphyxiation cutoff point. As it happens, I regularly have to jettison your oxygen into space in order to avoid an excessive build up of pressure in a damaged reservoir. You could always repair it if you have nothing better to do... "

So in short, It just changes the nature of the automated Neron messages
