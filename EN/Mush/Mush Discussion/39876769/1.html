Some discussion of this new guide was in this thread:
Noob-friendlyness of characters
But as it became quite different from Zeebok idea and became separate project, let's also separate threads to make discussion easier for everyone.

Here is the wiki page people are working on: http://mushpedia.com/wiki/Job_overview
Main idea of the page: to make complete guide of every ship duty, which allowes to understand importance of every job, skill and crew member.

Work is in progress - everyone is welcome with discussion /suggestions/help and so on.

Few things I want to state:
- It's not the complete guide how to do every job - no need to state here what zones are better for astro or why you should go piloting with full ap or what settings NERON admin should set. We have enough tips on skill or characters pages, don't copy that. Tell a reader idea of the job, and if is interested he will open main page for details.
- Don't describe very rare situations. Of course Intimidating or Antique Perfume skills can help in mush hunt, but there is no need to write about that. A new player who wants to find his place of the ship should be able to read at least few articles he is interested in, not be buried under thousands of rare situations.
