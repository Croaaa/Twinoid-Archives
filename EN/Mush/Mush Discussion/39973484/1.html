i really like that paranoid aspect of the game, you can trust nobody and even proven humans can be infected at anytime.
but at the moment all ships are the same: humans rush anti-spore gas and mycoalarms and after that the mush team has only 2 win options -> rampage or rush pilgred... after 4-6 days all mush are dead and the humans start searching for starmaps or just go back to sol if the mush rampage was successful. the mush team has no chance with an active human team.

thats why i had the idea of "random spores" that are spread on mush selection, e.g. ->

- 1 person starts with 3-4 spores (1st alpha mush, sometimes he will start with a spore without having to extract one)
- 1 person with 3 spores (2nd alpha mush)
- 1-2 humans already have 2 spores on them
- 3-5 humans start with 1 spore

and to make it fair: max spores generated per day should be reduced to 3 (still 2 with anti-spore gas) and a new rule would be good:
the mush are only allowed to rampage if
a) they mutate first
b) they got identified as a mush
or c) to help another mush who is under attack


with this changes the whole game could be a lot more interesting and a good mush team could still win (without rampage) even when there is a good/active human team. they would have to check who has spores on them and could make a plan to infect the crew.
the mush team would grow faster, researches like pheromodem and patuline scrambler would be a lot more important (at the moment they are only good for glory, when they are finished all mush are dead or only 1 is left...) and the mush would be a real threat that you have to get rid of...
