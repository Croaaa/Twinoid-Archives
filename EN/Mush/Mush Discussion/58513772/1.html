I'm new. What is this all about?It’s an event during which active Mush players gather and start their game at specific time. This helps to gradually increase activity on board.


In short – this is an attempt to reinvigorate Massive Mush Move to something more focused and efficient.

How to join --> click here.

Last few launches were ruined by massive account spamming from griefers, this is why from now on these events will be conducted in a Twinoid group called Massive Mush Move community jump. Event dates and time will be only available for group members, thus, in prospect, helping us avoid such griefing in the future.

What are the rules?
A single and most important rule you’ll have to follow is not to share jump dates outside the group. In order for this group to be effective, this information should remain exclusive to it’s members.

What are the requirements to join?
Everyone will be accepted, as long as their account won’t give any suspicion to griefing.
Griefing is intentional activity that ruins the game for others, usually involves going inactive or massively killing players on board. To make this perfectly clear, this group is not dedicated to specific play style, behavior or personality. Everyone is free to join as long as their activity is legal.

Will I have to participate if I join the group?
No. Group only provides jump date information.

Can I spread the word?
Sure. Refer your friends to this post or give them the group link.
