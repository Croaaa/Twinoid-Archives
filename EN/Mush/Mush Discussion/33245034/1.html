Schnuuk

No - they play in different teams and have different (nearly opposite) goals.

In d2n the ghoul can live much longer if he is cooperative - and so he gets his points, too. Only disadvantage is maybe his sacrifice of premium days.
The humans there have no disadvantages, to keep the ghoul as long alive as they can (without feeding too much living food).

In Mush the Mushes get most points, if they turn or kill all humans (or reach Sol). The longer they wait, the few points they get.
The humans get most points, if they reach their goal (for example: reaching Sol) and wipe the Mushes before they reach it (they lose, if they reach their goal with Mushes left on board).
So even if they would cooperate to a specific point - only one team can win.

And last but not least: It is forbidden (please no nitpicking, we can follow the meta-discussion in the other thread).
