To elaborate a bit more: those who have played the French version or who have links to wikis or other sources of information about the French version of Mush should refrain from spreading that knowledge in their ships.

There are several reasons why this information should not be spread:

  • This would spoil the discovery part of the game for new players.
  • This would just copy the habits from another version of the game without letting this community evolve on its own and establish its own guidelines.
  • This would also make it less likely that any problems or things that are unclear for new players will be reported. It would be annoying to miss these issues during the beta phase.

So if you already know the game, please do not start by describing the different skills, the usage of each piece of equipment, what each person should or should not do, etc.

Thank you!
