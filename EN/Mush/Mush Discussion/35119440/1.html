Googleshng

Stephen strikes me as something of a joke character (fairly blatant Under Siege reference and all), which I can totally appreciate, but every other character starts with a (usually unique) starting skill which provides them a fairly intuitive useful role to fulfill every day (except maybe Chun and Giole). Stephen looks like he does, and having no real warning not to, every new player who picks him tends to immediately head to the refectory, spend all their AP cooking, and just waste a huge pile of food. Best case scenario, someone explains to them that their only skill is only actually called for once every 4 days (and even then, easy mush vector).

So they either get murdered for being a potential mush/griefer, possibly without any real explanation, or just drop out immediately because they feel totally useless.

Granted, there's some value even in having a nasty trap of a character like this because it provides a nice smokescreen to Mush players, but it comes at the expense of roughly 1 new player in 16 having a miserable first experience with the game and probably never touching it again.

Switching his level 1 and 2 skills around would fix things nicely, but then he'd be overlapping with Giole, and that's a weird skill to make redundant by default.
