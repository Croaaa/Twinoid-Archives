I know this thread is pretty old: But I'm pretty sure I found the reason this happens (and the fix). The cause of this is because your character tried to do an action while in a different room (For example: Trying to access comms terminal in Front Corridor. Or Pulling coffee from the coffee machine in central corridor). This either happens due to the game being very slow (D10+ lag possibly too), or, in a much more rarer case, every time you connect to mush it automatically tries to connect you to a url like this. How I fixed it (On my old laptop that suffered from this), is I just cleared my browsing history.
