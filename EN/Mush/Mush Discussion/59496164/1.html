I've been thinking about the state of the game, recently. The way some ships don't seem to fill until day 4. The way that some ships seem to take an entire 2 days just to find that Chun/Eleesha they so desperately need to finally get started playing the game.

And then I thought about all the ships I've seen on day 4, where at least one player said "lol what if we just returned to sol now?" Me? I tend to roll my eyes at the suggestion; Why return now, just to start another ship where we start waiting again? Yet, if I recall, it's been said that move is allowed so long as no-one in the crew is against the idea.

So why not go bigger than that? Wouldn't it really spice things up if players started trying to go to Eden pre mush?

Sure, it's really unlikely. You would, undoubtedly, require a rediculous amount of activity and luck. But if you really think about it? It's possible, isn't it? There's no real reason you couldn't collect three starmap fragments, research the calculator, find eden, and hack yourself to it before mush selection. I mean, hey, your pilots are already gonna be a lot less busy. You won't be stopped from moving by an arack. Just the fact that you need to hack the terminal.

And even if you never succeeded, wouldn't the game be a lot more interesting if we started taking some bigger risks before mush selection? Even just an injury or illness before mush selection would really shake things up. And heck, even if you only find a single fragment before mush selection, it'd mean post-mush-hunt you had one less to find!

So I'm proposing that we try it! I think we should try for a pre-mush Eden. That's a white whale I could REALLY sink my teeth into. Thoughts?
