Gallade

You haven't considered two important weapons that are aboard the Daedalus and could turn the tide of the entire war: the Mush Sample and Chun. Just think of all the research that is only possible with either or both of them.

Now, think of what happens on ships where Chun dies and/or the Sample is stolen: Mush can spore freely without much impediment, humans don't know who to trust until it is too late, and it is generally a cakewalk to infect some and kill the rest.

That is the situation on Earth if nothing changes. Even if you don't do much research, just the fact that you manage to kill the Mush agents and go back to the Resistance with the Sample and Chun in hand means that you've completed the mission by delivering critical information to fight them.

What about the virtual data who lets you do research without Chun or the Sample? Well, that also counts as a win thanks to the Daedalus being able to communicate with Xyloph and download the research data. Intergalactic communication is slow and unsafe (just think of the Rebel Bases, which take a long time to transmit for a short window of time for simple messages or upgrades), while downloading data from Xyloph is pretty much instantaneous. This means that the Daedalus must have some sort of security clearance to access that data, so even bringing it back to Sol with only one human survivor, no research done, no upgrades, all systems broken and all rooms on fire is a victory just because you let the Resistance access those resources and start developing the Anti-Mush research.
