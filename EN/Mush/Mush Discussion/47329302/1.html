[8281990]
MorgaineI would probably have killed you too, for wasting pills especially if you don't know what they do. Mush do know what they do and may well eat the good ones..

Bingo. Randomly eating pills doesn't really help the human team, because so many are fairly useless except in niche cases (and the morale penalty ones are outright dangerous). It usually makes it look as if you're trying to clean out the good ones. Now, if you'd communicated your plan with the crew and they agreed it was worth a shot, that would have been different.

As for pill use, I generally keep them a secret as a researcher, so they don't get wasted by selfish crew. Mush can always see the effects of course, but they have to 1) risk coming into the medlab to see them, and 2) will make me suspicious if they "luck" into eating the best pills, or even more damning, report effects that they shouldn't know.

As for pill use, it's usually need-based. MP pills are usually not worth the trip to the Medlab, and as a researcher I'd probably eat the AP pills for the early antimush work ASAP. The ones that only cure specific diseases, obviously, are situational, and morale pills are distributed in times of need.

True!

But.

Team communication is still key, to make sure those consumables are used in the best possible way. And some restraint can be useful.

I had a crewmate once who kept cleaning out the Jumpkin garden in order to scan for planets during a run at Eden. He was not an Astro, and I was. I had a plan laid out to take a chunk of the fresh crop to scan C1, then eat what was left at the end of the day if the first scans turned up nothing. He got frustrated that I wasn't taking everything at once, and went over my head to scan suboptimally. Even after it was explained multiple times by multiple people that the remaining crop was an emergency supply to aid fellow crew in firefighting or healing emergencies, as late-game ships often require. It was frustrating.

Same thing for pills. If there's a good chance your biologists/nurses/medics are hoping to catch Mush who report effects they couldn't possibly know, respect their strategy. If you think it's time to start rolling the dice, discuss it with the crew. But if you act alone and get chewed out for it, don't be surprised. Team game and all.
