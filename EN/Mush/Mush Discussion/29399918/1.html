OmniraptorWhat exactly does sunsky's mush helper script do?

It is legen-wait-for-it-dary!!

So first you may or not notice the new tab on the upper-right edge of your browsing window!! I didn't know what it does either until I just accidentally noticed that one. Just click on it to expand the app, it's right underneath beneath the Motion Twin or Twinoid cube that grants us access to the other games and also lets us checkout their official News stream.

Once have you click to expand it, notice the three small buttons on the top:
First one is a pin "Pin the script".
Second is "Reset the dimensions".
Third doesn't have the hover text, but it just toggles between the two different layouts. One covers partial character informations and the other one covers the Mush banner and some of the buttons underneath beneath. I ASSUME THE DEFAULT LAYOUT WHICH WAS THE FIRST ONE I MENTIONED AND TALKED ABOUT. It goes down the page a bit more than the second one which goes further across the screen, they both have the same setup just rearranged for your viewable funtimes.
Mouse to them, the buttons, for the quoted tooltips.
Oh, and there is the arrow (< or >) in the top-right corner of the app, just click it to hide the app and it will change directions to signify that if you click on it again then it will expand the app all over again.

Next down you may notice the INVENTORY button, this prints the current locations shelf with preformatted text. You will find the printed text within the textarea of the app.

Next down the list is FIND UNREAD MESSAGES, which does exactly what it says that it does. This refers to the coms channels.

Next, further down, is EXPAND MESSAGES, it expands all of the messages. You know, so you don't need to click Show reply or Show all x replies. Again, refering to the Walkie-Talkie.

Sunsky "generate texts (inventory, planet, researchs, projects, communication, pilgred)."

As stated above, INVENTORY is always available, atleast from within the ship (I have yet to venture out into the wilderness of the unknown) because there are shelves, although it may not appear if the room has no items in it.

I have found if you access specific equipment the other buttons will appear, just like for me I get my the RESEARCHS button in the location of this spoiler tag. But only when I Access Terminal for Research Lab.

The generated preformatted text printed in the textarea includes all relevant informations regarding the equipment you have accessed so you can just click onto the SELECT button and CTRL-C to copy and CTRL-V to paste into the significant coms thread.

Then there are three buttons on the next level, G and I and SELECT.
G makes selected text in the textarea into bold.
I makes selected text in the textarea into italics.
SELECT selects all text in the textarea.

And just under that is CLEAN, it just clears the textarea.

That big white box is the textarea I have been mentioning all over of along.

Finally there are emoji for the textarea, these expand and function similiar to in the way of the Forum emoji works.
