spacekadt

Active gold days allow you to have 4 skill slots. If you've purchased gold days at least once (even with MT Passes), you will keep 2 skill spots on all characters even when you don't have active gold days.

If you are gifted gold days from another player, you'll have the 4 skills for that time frame, but since you didn't actually *purchase* the days, you will not have the silver level unlock of the second skill.
