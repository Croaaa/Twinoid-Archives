Comrades of Mush!
Remember, remember the 5th of November!

After a fantastic beta testing phase, we've decided to forge ahead with the open launch. After the weekend we'll be opening Mush to players from all over the interwebs. You'll be able to freely invite your friends to join you aboard and infect torture show them what they've been missing!

As announced, all XP earned in the beta has been erased and the rankings reset. As beta players you'll have a few days head-start on the new arrivals next week. We really appreciate everything you guys have done here, so to say thank you, we've prepared a little something special:

  • Any crewmember who subscribed to GOLD mode and used some/all of their GOLD days has had them ALL re-credited to their account.
  • Any comrade who bought Klix during the beta has had them ALL re-credited to their account.
  • Anyone who participated in the beta has be given an additional 5 Klix.

Here's to the future, comrades... and to our future comrades!
