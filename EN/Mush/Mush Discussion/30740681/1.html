AbyssienHi, I active Advaced Mush Skill. Some ship ago, I see the map of genome. Someone know how it works?

It allows you to choose your human skills, in case you become Mush before you have picked your human skills. As an example: If you are the last to wake up, and become Mush, then you haven't picked your skills yet. This made it very easy to identify you as Mush.

Now that this module is active, you can still obtain your human skills, while you are a Mush. Just note that not all of your human skills will be available as a Mush player.
