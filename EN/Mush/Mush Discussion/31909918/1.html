AivarasYou could try changing number at the end of link till you find yours... Might take a while

I do this and have gotten pretty decent at it. I'll start with a random number in the area I think our expo should be and jump around in multiples of 10 or so.

There are three different basic messages:

This link doesn't exist means there's no current or past expo with that number and you guessed too high.

Haax and calling you a bad person means it's an active ship's expo, just not your ship. Chances are you need to guess higher.

Message about not having finished but you didn't take part means it's a current expo for your ship. You've found the right one if you're trying to search before the expo is done!

And of course, there's results which show up after the expo is done. If you leave the page with that last message loaded in a tab, you can just refresh after the expo finishes and get the results then.
