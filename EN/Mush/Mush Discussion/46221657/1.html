In before the inevitable "bitch about n00bs/idlers" thread

From what I've been hearing from friends, RL or otherwise, who have jumped in the past 24h, something strange has been happening on our servers. You may have noticed a massive increase of tutorials, idles and ships filling alarming fast

Is this some new form of griefer? Servers gone haywire?
DON'T PANIC, and always carry a towel this is all good news. A recent Youtube video has been posted yesterday on mush. It's had 12,000 hits and counting since it was posted roughly yesterday, which you may recognize as about 3x the amount of views your average PlumpHelmetPunk video gets in a lifetime. This is causing a massive increase of new players signing up, and as you well know, most new players don't stick around

While this seems like a bad thing at first, a nice chunk of those new players will be here to stay. New blood means a larger community and player pool in the future, which means higher not-newbie to is-newbie ratio
Let us take this opportiunity to revitalize our player pool! Don't be selfish and throw a fit over lame ships. Be friendly, patient and give these people a reason to stay. Build better ships for the future ( Although, this applies whether there's a new wave incoming or not )

The following few ships may be tough, but take it as a challenge and a service to our community. It can't possibly be as bad as the post-Beta days where only ~2/16 people had more than 2 ships.

By the way, you if happen to be part of that new wave :

  • There are a lot of errors on even the basic mechanics on that video. Consult the wiki or forums instead
  • Mush is better than SS13. If you don't think so, you're not doing it right
