[6826491]
EdgyVenisonI didn't know that you can disassemble the shower
But why would you disassemble the useful shower into a sofa?

The Sofa acts as an additional bed. If the person in that sofa has the Logistics skill (Paola or Jin Su) then one person in the room with them will also get 2 AP per cycle, as if there were a second bed in the room. This is why a lot of bridges in the beta had only Paola and Frieda stay up there - Paola would take the sofa, and both she and Frieda would benefit. If Jin Su also had Logistics, he could take the Medlab bed, and one other person could benefit from the "extra" bed as well.

You also need the Technician skill to disassemble anything, so it's not an intuitive process. But Cleef's point is also correct. If there is only one shower, it is easier to confirm who has (or hasn't) been using it.

Pay person X the same amount you of money that you can buy for 1 month of Gold Days. 1 Month equals 4 packets of 7 Gold days each that you can ask whenever you need them.

Pros:
- Person X profits a small percentage of the gold days

Cons:
- If you haven't bought gold days, no First Time Purchase bonus package
- You lose 2-3 days ( total-of-days % 7 )
- Requires external payment method


Refined Idea:
You buy your own gold days, then send them to person X. Person X then logs who sent him the gold days and resends them according the your needs.

Pros:
- You can still get your First Time Purchase bonus package
- You don't lose your gold days, but (total-of-days % 7) days will have to be used immediately
- Doesn't require any external payment method

Cons:
- No incentives or bonuses to person X who logs, resends, and manages the gold days.
