sperbi

If the human team is not very active in research, mushes should easily be able to convert other people. It just needs either 2 somehow active mushes or one active and experienced mush to pull out conversions then.

If human team is rushing research, you need an active and skillfull mush team, consisting of at least one mush who has at least lvl 11. Then conversion should be possible too.

Luck is always involved. A perfect human team, what is always checking logs everywhere, looking for ap usage, taking care to secure every room should always be able to protect themselves well (regarding conversions) and after some days narrow down the mushes.
But such a human team is rarely existing, especially rare on random ships.

Chances are just generally higher that all the active people are in the human team or at least only one active person in the mush team. (Let's say we have 8 active people on the ship, statistically chances are highest that after mush selection you have 7 active humans and one active mush with an inactive partner).
When human team is then rushing research, it is very difficult for the lonely mush, especially with low level.

Conversion is possible, but difficult to pull off. Rampage is much easier, but most players look down on it, because it skips the investigation part of the ship. Most castings ban rampages, and have more active/competent players, which is what makes being Mush harder on a casting ship. I have won many ships as alpha mush rampaging, even to the extent of killing every human through violence, or morale loss. The reason it is so successful is that certain skills are just not replaceable, do to game design. A ship without a shrink will die in 5-10 days almost certainly, its just too difficult to arrange enough sex and fruit sharing with random players to make it. Its much faster with 4-6 kills, which is fairly easy with weapons, combat skills, and transfer. The big power of being Mush is there are almost no rules about how you can be an agent of Mush, which means you can try all kinds of strategies to hurt the ship. If you like online chatting, you can even subtly misdirect the crew and lie your way to ship destruction, through humans killing other humans, or running out of air or fuel. I usually follow the other Mush's lead, so we are both on the same page and have the greatest chance of victory.
