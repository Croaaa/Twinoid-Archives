I think it's more of a "There's a possibility of a cure" angle than "Wow, she's not squeamish about needles" for her inspirational powers. They likely will admire her for her endurance but it isn't the main thing.

Chun would likely be quite withdrawn given her harrowing experiences on Tamina and the public scrutiny afterwards. The past few years spent living a relatively normal life aboard Xyloph may have helped her to ease up - especially around the longest-standing members of the project. They may not have talked to her much if the station was pretty sizeable though, she was just in maintenance.

The definite exceptions would be Jin Su, who new of her true identity as the survivor of Tamina, having worked with her or at least known of her before - they likely have a rapport. Nils Carlsson is the other one, as he helped her escape her persecution.

Depending on how much she knows of Gioele's attempts to have him removed from the team in place of Paola, she may distrust him. Distrust would be directed at Eleesha too, given previous bad experience with journalists and even her in particular (despite it saying "his questions" for the 3142 entry).

There would be opportunity to get along with those who work with her in the lab, particularly Finola, at least depending on her bedside manner. Janice is everyone's friend of course, and Frieda might have something in common given their shared survivor attitude and time spent in cryo (admittedly a vast difference in length of the freeze).
