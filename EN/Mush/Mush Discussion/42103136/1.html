Mush EN gets castings! Finally!

If you click on the castings link you should now have access to this feature!
We know you've been waiting for a long time so here it is!

In other news blackmagic, your resident Mush developer is going to give Mush a once over and fix a ton of those pesky bugs that you've been dealing with for so long.

With this in mind please raise all bugs in the appropriate places this week!

If you're not sure where you should be doing this then talk to your friendly moderators, the'll be only to happy to help out.


Ok so that's the good news, now for the bad:

This will most likely be the last major set of updates that Mush will receive for a while. As many of you may know there was an announcement made on the French forums last week regarding future updates.

The situation is as follows:

Times are tough for Motion Twin. We need to focus our energies on new sources of revenue (yes it's the sad reality of being a game dev) and therefore beloved games like Mush are receiving less dev time than they have in the past.

As such: Mush won't receive any major updates moving into the future. If we have another hit game soon, you might be able to hope for a mobile version and new updates, but don't get too excited...

Bug fixes and support will continue!

All bug fixing and support will continue as per usual and the game will continue to be interesting and fun for a long time yet.

So enjoy your games and keep reporting any griefers or bug as per normal.

Should you need anything my name is buzzard and i'm here to help.

Good luck people!

angrysnail

Avoid making too many castings, ill explain why:

- at first you need at least 24 persons ready to launch ship, meaning that 8 are left behind, since a ship can only contrain 16. If there was 32 or more persons ready, then 2 ships are launched (etc, if 36 persons are ready, 4 are left behind.. if 48 are ready 3 ships are launched, shuffling people better) For this reason, its much better to have a big casting than one that barely get 24 people ready then ditch 1/3 of them, really frustrating as its sometime a week long wait until enough people are ready.

-castings can levelup and unlock new options, such as hidden mush, 3x mush, blitz (1hour cycles) and 16 persons launch that really speed up the waiting time for players. Most of these options are long and hard to unlock and are gained by launching ships (donating tickets too, but launching ships is more effective), so having big castings mean that players will access these options faster, eventually, castings can unlock the 16 persons launch but its very long, in french mush most castings die long before they do and i guesstimate that only half a dozen got it as of today and only one reached the highest option (blitz cycles).

-after playing a few ships, you will recognise some people easily (if you launch with only 16, you can see who will be on board before just by checking who's ready too, having 2 ships launched at once is good to shuffle things a bit)

-casting tends to have the same rules, no rp tests, rp chat.. a few themes ships and optimisation ships here and there but mostly no big differences, there is no need to have 10 RP castings, because of the above points it will be detrimental to the community.

So be aware, dont create too many casting and donate tons of tickets unless you know what you are doing, small castings tends to slowly die because they at first barely get 24 persons ready after a few weeks, then the 8 who got ditched leave or dont bother the next time..
