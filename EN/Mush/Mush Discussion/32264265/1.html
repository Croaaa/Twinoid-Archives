So, this game has a pretty steep learning curve. There are quite a lot of ways that new players can mess up, and quickly be discouraged from playing, given the likelihood of either not knowing what to do or doing something that gets them killed. I would really like to see actual tutorial ships. Here's my idea for what this would look like.

All new players go onto a tutorial ship their first ship. A certain percentage of this ship (perhaps 2 members?) consists of mentors - experienced, helpful players who apply for the role beforehand. Perhaps some sort of established rule among mentors that if they end up being mush, they play mush, yes, but must still advise the crew from a human standpoint. I think this would be a good thing for new players learning the game some among other new players, but with some guidance. And perhaps the mentors could get some sort of bonus like gold mode for the duration of the tutorial ship, which would serve several purposes - motivate people to mentor, help combat the level of inefficiency and inactivity these ships will have, and show new players what gold mode can do for them, driving more purchases.

This is just a rough idea, I'm sure it's with it's flaws, but I really think something of this nature would be a good thing for the game long term.
