Regardless of Glory, people should definitely be open to swapping roles within a ship as the need arises. A lot of players will get bogged down with "how things are always done", which can limit the potential of a ship.

Deaths and Mage Books will have a pretty large effect on this. On a recent ship of mine we had Jin Su take an Astro book while Frieda became a Pilot, and on my last one we also had a full-time Piloting Frieda (via Mage book, although we abandoned Astro entirely). On that same last ship, I was a Paola with Biologist, Rebel and Medic via book so after the Day 5 base simply moved to Research (having also killed two of our three Mush).
Your Frieda example is a good one, especially since Astro and Pilot are both played as "save up loads of AP for one go", she can choose which to do depending on if a planet is found or there are expos/Hunters/scrap to take care of.
