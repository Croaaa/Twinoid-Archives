Currently Defacer is so over-powered that it will destroy the game for humans. Defacer lets a single, medium level mush start a rampage as soon as they're infected. They can continue the rampage indefinitely, infecting and killing 2 or more humans every day with impunity.

Defacer wipes all the historical logs in a room, it hides every action anyone takes in the room for the rest of the cycle and it suppresses Neron Alerts coming from that room. Finally, if you use Defacer and then Transfer, Defacer gets refreshed allowing you a second one the same day.

This means that a mush in a room full of active players under a camera can kill one of them and the only sign will be one human ticked off of the death tally. If humans even notice the death they're left with a room full of suspects. Once humans do start to narrow down the suspects, the mush can simply transfer to a human who was not on the suspect list, Deface the logs and kill it's old body. The humans will spend all their MP looking for the old body because there is no log of it's death anywhere. The only way for the humans to counter this tactic is to start killing every person in the room that was Defaced and hope that they are lucky enough to hit the mush early in the death order.

I will single-handedly cripple any crew, no matter how active and skillful. Given an active mush partner we'll destroy the ship.

I suggest Defacer be scaled WAY down. IMO Defacer should allow a mush to erase an individual entry in a room for 1 AP. A message indicating the entry was
erased should be show up in place of the entry. Limit the use of this skill to once per day. This would still leave Defacer far more powerful than most of the other mush skills

If not the above, then at the very least:
Neron Messages should be enabled so the humans know who has died and when.
Transfer should not grant a second use of of Defacer on the same day.
Only erase historical entries, do not continue to scramble entries for the remainder of the cycle.

These lesser changes will still allow me to decimate a crew, but would slow me down somewhat. Please take immediate action on this problem before it ruins the game for everyone.
