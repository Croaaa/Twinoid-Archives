wLink

It's very situational. For golden Frieda it can be alternative to survivalist. If there's no camera/mycoalarm in Icarus Bay Frieda can just wash herself to not risk getting converted before/after expedition.

It seems to be more useful for pilot Frieda than standart Bridge sitting Frieda because the latter can always avoid getting dirty. If you're the only pilot and you have a bed in BD you may consider taking Pilot+Antique Perfume if you're at least silver. For a price of 1 AP you will be able to protect yourself from potential traps in bays and Refectory. Not the best thing in the world but isn't totally bad either.

You can also protect yourself from brute conversions in a room with camera for a price of 1-2 AP if you're excepting it. It only lasts for 2 cycles though.

Another upside or downside is it will confirm your humanity if you wash yourself and someone will notice. I'm guessing that's more of a downside because you're able to food test without any skills anyway.

Overall I think the skill is conditional and even when it does something it's usually rather mediocre effect. There are better ways to spend 500 xp unless you have too much already or you're playing a lot of Frieda and you're golden.

I never saw Frieda with Antique Perfume by the way,

I wouldn't and couldn't quantify it like that.

It's quite similar to Chun's Lethargic. Most people I know don't pick it for the additional AP, but simply for the huge AP-bar. They fully expect to have to get up in emergencies before Lethargic kicks in and just take the AP as bonus, if they happen to sleep 5 cycles or longer.
Self Sacrifice allows to completely ignore the morale-bar and take morale decreases with little worrying. It also shuts down any attempt at attacking your morale by rampaging and making cat+fruits+pills inaccessible or simply using the Demoralize skill. The threat of a Mush having that skill tends to be bigger than its actual use. Shrink/IT/Diplo/SS is definitely the strongest set for the PvP part of Mush.

Furthermore it helps surviving on good and bad ships alike. The former is quite obvious(lots of people die, a lot of morale lost). On good ships you may be hit by several panic attacks in a row and while it's possible to delegate someone for healing(that's necessary anyway), which everyone can do, it's not possible to delegate morale stuff that good. There's no indicator for lost or full morale. Others may end up overcomforting you or don't comfort, because they don't know the last 3 cycles with 3 panic attacks brought you down to 3 morale and the next panic attack will definitely kill you.

And back to your question: I couldn't answer how often I was saved because of Self Sacrifice, but the 1 fatal depression I had happened because I picked Shrink/IT/NOF/Diplo too early and ended up with a Spleen, which killed me in the end.
