Season Four of Mush has landed and Derek & Andie are back!

Greetings Mushlings, and welcome to the new season, "Spores Galore"! In this season, Derek and Andie return to the ship, and you start in orbit of a random (not analyzed) planet! You can't expo until the ship fills up, though. We did away with random injuries and alien fruit, but as the season title implies, humans (except Chun, naturally) have a chance of waking up contaminated with a single spore. Can you imagine the potential chaos? We certainly can!

(In case you're wondering, the Mycoscan research is not considered a violation of Neutral Play, and is the sole exception to it in regards to anti-Mush research pre-selection. We expect more ships to finish it before Mush selection, and it's a smart idea to consider it!)

Note: The new season will activate at the beginning of the next cycle. So sooooon!
