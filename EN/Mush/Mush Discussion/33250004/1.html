They're all from pulp scifi and antiquated comic books. I do like the character mixes, but boy they are a bunch of stereotypes. Now, I'm not against archetypes, because that makes it easier to RP. I do approve of the anime hair color deal, which is quite practical for identifying tiny sprites at a distance.
