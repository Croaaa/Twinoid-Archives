Here is a google translation of the message they posted on the French server.

Hi all,

I owe you some explanation.

Motion Twin today undergoes a deep and prolonged crisis, it's been several years since we cumulate production problems and departures.

This is part of a global context on course web game, we are affected by the crisis, competition and other endo and exothermic phenomena that you can imagine.
In this context, my mission now is to support Motion Twin technology transition and focus on income-generating projects. I would continue to remain attentive to the bug and other gaping flaws but I would not have the same sound quality.

As you know, Mush can neither be promoted nor mobile web game become profitable, it is structurally impossible for this project. This is a project that has been very successful and passionate about, its scale and of course probably one of my best adventures with AlphaBounce DSi.

Today, I no longer have the means to "maintain" Mush, ie to update on content additions. I will spend time on Mush in October to further improve a little more automation and maintenance by the community.

This is not gaiety of heart but it is a fact, Motion Twin has (over) live and this requires a disengagement from me on my side projects including Mush belongs.

This refactoring Motion Twin occurs at all levels and in all sectors, the most committed of you already know the situation.

So in mid-October I effectuerais the most important tasks for Mush effectuerais and the change of season. It will then become automatic.

Imagine how the situation saddens me.

Thank you for your attention and APERO!
