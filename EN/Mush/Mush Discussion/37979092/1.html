Jart

Is it just me or does MTPass give more days than paying a monthly subscription.

Can someone tell me the advantage of buying subscription instead of buying MTPass?


MT pass gives 33 % less days, but you have MUCH more control over when you go gold, and going silver after 7 days isn't so bad, because you keep your extra skills over two, until the ship ends. You can usually get a good feel for a ship in the first few days, and many are not worth Gold days, similar to random Die2Nite towns, where some lose 3 the first night, others 15 + Also, if you are into it, gifting MT Passes to a Bronze is is MASSIVELY better than gifting gold days, because the passes give an extra day of Gold, permanent Silver, AND 4 skills for the ship's duration, if the player is at least level 4 when Gold ends.

The advantage of Subscription is more total XP over the same period of time, because of the net extra Gold days over the MT Passes (6 months 2 weeks, vs about 4 months with passes), If you ALWAYS want to play gold and/or never gift Gold. IMO, most skills over level 4 are not that good, with the occasional exception, Stephen being the most obvious, but if you are usually Gold, you will want 4 good skills, which can push you to level 5-6 with some characters.

On a side note, you can also win 'free' MTpasses in other Twinoid games as prizes.
