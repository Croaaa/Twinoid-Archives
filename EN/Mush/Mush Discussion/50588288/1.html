I'm sure I'm not the first person to mention this, but Radio Expert has always seemed like a lame skill to me. I mean, as a first skill for Paola it makes sense. What doesn't make sense is why two OTHER characters have it as their level 5 skill...

That and getting it as a mage book has always been annoying. I almost want it removed as a magebook, but more than that...

I'd actually really like to see some extra utility added to the skill. Perhaps for 1 AP the Radio Expert can get a piece of information about a random private conversation going on in the ship. Perhaps the last few messages sent from one of the chats, or a list of all the people involved in a random chat.

OR it would make more sense as a Janice/Frieda skill if it made it more likely to hit zones with alien life in them (zones with pink icons.) It might even give Paola a reason to not spend the entire first week of the ship locked on the bridge. (Makes playing her as a mush nearly impossible without pretending you're a total noob.)

It's just a skill that's always struck me as "meh." And I kinda want to know if other people feel the same way.
