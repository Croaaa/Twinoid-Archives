Maybe it's because of how I play her, but I don't recall the Mush ever targeting me when I've been Chun. I rarely get assassinated regardless of who I am, though. (Just twice, and one of them was my first time as an Alpha.)

I often have Chun as the ship's Biologist, or if that book isn't available, its Chef or Botanist. Having Sneak plus the Armor, along with Ian in the Lab, just adds to the difficulty in killing me. Sure they could use a Mush with Shooter, and get a critical, but if they fail...I back that up with Nurse. Potentially free DIY surgery, if she gets injured! Chun is a great character for someone who is Gold, and knows most of the things Mush can/can't do. The more she knows the roles of the other players, the better she'll be at directing the newbies to where they're needed. She's a natural leader, and if played well, she can keep the player morale high, even if the ship is falling apart around you.

If Chef is the other skill she takes, then she can still freeze the steaks after an expo, and determine which plants are safe/best to graft. I rarely use Chun on expos, but she's quite useful for healing the team afterward, in addition to this.

She's also great for taking the lead in organizing the Mush hunt. I've usually had two or three separate Mush hunt channels simultaneously, and kept information flowing. This split tends to happen when certain crew don't trust each other, but they still trust Chun...so their information gets filtered through Chun, and then back to the players who need it. Of course, Chun can't always be sure of who to trust, so occasional misdirection on her part is required. A certain (somewhat paraphrased) axiom applies here: "Just because I have told you everything that you know, does not mean that I have told you everything that I know." As long as this is being done for the sake of the human team, and not getting innocent people killed, Chun must keep certain secrets safe, and she is in a unique position to do so. Wheels within wheels.
