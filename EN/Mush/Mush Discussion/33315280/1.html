If you want to have got a higher chance to join the same ship then your friend (or you) should jump first on a ship with 4 possible chars. Then you tell the other one whats chars are already awake (you can see it in the roster at the lab) and then the other one can compare his possible chars with the chars that are still available on your ship.
So if e. g. Paola, Frieda, Raluca, Roland, Hua and Gioele are not already chosen and your friend has got a groupt of 4 of this 6 chars then there is a high chance that it is your ship.
If there a another char which is already awake or less then 4 chars then it cant be your ship and then he can wait and hope to get another choice. (ofc the chance is higher if there are only a few people left on your ship to be chosen)
