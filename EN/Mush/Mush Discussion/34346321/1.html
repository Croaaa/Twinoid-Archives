So, I've been playing Mush for around 1.5 months now and there's one thing that slightly cramps my gameplay.

As all experienced Mush players know there are important things going on in the first cycle of each day: skill APs resetting, Coffee Machine resetting, a Mush can extract two spores in a row with showering only once etc.

The problem is: Cycle 1 of each day in public games is starting at 01:00 CET.

For anybody that has a job, that is quite a big issue as most of us wake up at 07:00 or 08;00 in the morning or earlier and we certainly don't want a browser game to dictate our lives.

I don't have access to the traffic stats of Mush of course but I assume the two largest player groups are from Europe or the Americas.
Cycle 1 starts at 19:00 EST or 16:00 EST in the US, so moving the time wouldn't affect anyone hugely, especially since you can occasionally log in from work or school anyways.

My petition and point of discussion would be: Is there a possibility to move Cycle 1 to 23:00 or 00:00 CET?

I realize that this is a huge change affecting absolutely everybody but I would like to hear what the devs or other players think about it and what the reason for choosing 01:00 was in the first place.
