Trading inactive players is a very good idea - gets rid of the useless players, and the active crew gets something in return that makes them feel a little better about losing that character to someone who isn't playing.

However, getting them into a storage room is a pain!

Moving players with Solid and Wrestle is also a good idea as it mixes things up and, hey, coincidentally, would help with moving Inactives around to the storage rooms they need to be in. Except that you can't pick the direction you're booting them towards. It feels like you should be playing "Yakety Sax" (i.e. "The Benny Hill Music" as you do it, moving around to random rooms. Even coordinating Guard won't help as it doesn't block entrance to a room, just lets you move the person back out to the previous room for another random chance to knock them into the right room.

So unless you get really luck AND have someone with Wrestler/Solid who is willing to spend the AP on the ability OR for some reason your Inactive stopped in a storage room themselves, it seems almost ridiculously hard to trade an Inactive player.

I can't seem to find a thread addressing this issue, and I'm surprised it didn't come up during the Beta testing of the newest episode. Maybe I'm missing something important?
