One answer: Go to a bank, and if that doesn't work, search your area for a business which specializes in financial transfers, and explain that you need assistance in finding a way to use a bank card, credit card, or gift card to send an online financial transaction in Euros. If the bank says they cannot help you (it is -possible- for microtransactions one might refuse.... but I assure you, if you were trying to move many thousands of dollars this way, the bank would find a way, heh - that's what banks specialize in), then find your local private business that does financial transfers.

Check cashing stores, payday loan advances, and the like are the private businesses likely to specialize in this - and it should be as simple as purchasing a gift card with money in Euros.
