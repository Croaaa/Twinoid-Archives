I'd say any generalist or a crewmate with a low-AP-demanding job (like, say, Janice) could be the character you're asking about.

There're characters that have a resource-demanding and specific job (like pilots or researchers or astros), that just don't have enough energy to do all the other tasks that need attention, or save AP and step in when emergencies happen. So it happens often that there's no one to help with emergencies or chores with less priorities, which can hinder the ship badly. Generalists are those who can take that on their shoulders, as well as take important roles of disfunctioned (dead/inactive) characters.

The generalists are Gioelle (can be very helpful later game when Pilgred is fixed, if has caffein junkie), Stephen (is an even more amazing addition to the crew if has Apprentice), Derek/Chao (though they usually don't get a bed, unless there's a player with extra Logistics Expert). Also Eleesha, Kuan Ti could be those guys. Possibly Chun, but she's mostly occupied in helping Finola/Andie/Ian with research and focusing in mush hunting.

I might be missing someone. But it depends on skills available to you for this or that character a lot, as well as on your overall activity and the needs of a particular ship you join.
