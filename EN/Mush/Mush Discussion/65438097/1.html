A couple years ago, I started a project: a text adventure which aimed to play out mechanically the same as Mush. Most of the game logic was coded out, though it remained largely untested, partially due to it being a standalone application that was a pain to update and distribute. I kept it to myself because, though I love this game to death, I'm not a very vocal member of this community, and I didn't want to get overwhelmed by its more enthusiastic members over a project which was not very playable and needed a ton of work.

Fast forward to a couple of weeks ago, and after going on a nostalgia trip over Mush and mourning its tragic slow-burn death, I became inspired to revisit the project. Only this time, I'd use a medium that was more accessible: a Discord bot. It was surprisingly easy to plug all of my backend code into the Discord bot API, and before long I had a game that was playable. I invited some friends and members of my old casting to help test and the results were much better than expected.

The server has grown considerably, the community now consisting of many veterans and enthusiastic new players alike. Ships are public and launched frequently, often filling completely in a couple of days. And there is plenty of room for expansion!

If you're interested, you can join the server here: https://discord.gg/9ySYFyRjkj
Information on how to play this version of Mush can be found in the #how-to-play and #commands-and-legend channels. There's also a (WIP) wiki: https://mushrespored.miraheze.org/wik...

The goal of this project is to provide a faithful recreation of the spirit of Mush, translated into a MUD-like text adventure format - along with any gameplay tweaks this necessitates. New content and ways to interact with the game's environment are being added all the time. The development team is just one guy (me) but I'm very active and quick to respond to feedback, so don't be shy about suggesting stuff! (My intention is not to steal the thunder of other similar projects - you guys are doing amazing work, keep it up! Instead, I plan to create something that could be considered a unique spin-off of the original. So if something isn't exactly the way it was in the original, this is likely on purpose.)

So yeah, that's it! Hope to see you aboard!

Popping back in to say that much progress has been made since I first announced this project! It now has an official name: Mush: Respored.

Hey! Just a small bump to let you all know Respored is still active, and updates are still rolling in.

A recent addition is a Roguelike mode which is entirely PvE/co-op and pushes the game's survival engine to its limits. The ship starts out with many things broken and lights off; the ship needs to be fixed up before crew operations can be carried out as normal. There are other major differences which makes the act of completing projects and repairing PILGRED more of a team effort that spans multiple sectors and planets.

Roguelike is feature-complete but still needs heavy playtesting to balance out (it's essentially a whole new game after all). All are welcome! https://discord.gg/9ySYFyRjkj
