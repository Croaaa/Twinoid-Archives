The smallest number of MTPasses you can purchase is 10, for 15 euro, or roughly $20. Only 2 are needed for the week of gold days, so that does work out to about $4 per week, and you have silver after the first time it runs out. So while you will pay more for 30 days of gold mode if you go the MTPass route, you will also be able to use them at your discretion, rather than in one consecutive streak.

Edit: And yes, you can send them to anyone you want.
