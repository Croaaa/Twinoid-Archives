With the Daedalus knocked out of hyperspace by hostile vessels, NERON in his wisdom has diverted the life sustaining cryogenic fluid through the ship's sprinkler system to save hydroponics. It worked, but now the sprinkler system is toast, the crew are thawing, lacking nutrition, and most of the ship's oxygen giving flora is gone. With the ship still under attack and adrift in open space, the crew are going to have get to work quickly if they want to save humanity...

In this season the crew of the Daedalus awake with the Mush's greatest enemies among the crew, Finola Keegan and Chao Wang. Things are a little complicated however as NERON's snap decision has left some of the crew starving, a weakness that has let some latent spores from Xyloph develop into the early stages of infection...
