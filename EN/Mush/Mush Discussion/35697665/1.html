Derek's full biography has been revealed on French Mush alongside his second-in-command Andie. They are two new characters that can be chosen to play in the alternate reality where Chao was killed.

Detailed biography:

3109 Birth on Earth.

3121 Derek cuts himself trying to trim his shaggy beard, which causes a scar. He swears to anyone who will listen that this injury comes from the battlefields of Tau Ceti.

3139 Derek is enlisted into the SDF (Sol Defense Federation) after receiving a propaganda leaflet. His mother wishes him to return home to visit, but Derek decides to cut ties and now flies alone.

3145 Derek slowly climbed the ranks, and acting as a willing and obeying servant it does not take long for him to get noticed by his superiors. He is sent Sol after a promotion and participates in a great battle. Sent as a scout on the ground, he surprised his superiors by taking over an advanced enemy base by himself. By his own admission, he didn't see the enemy communication tower before he accidentally crashed into it and disabled the base...nonetheless he is rewarded for his bravery on the battlefield and promoted sergeant.

3146 After a few missions Derek is posted on Xyloph-17 to oversee the Magellan expedition and report any actions of scientists working in the orbit of Jupiter. He meets Chao Wang, but maintains no relationship with him, preferring to keep his distance and attempt to flirt with the women of Xyloph-17. His reports gradually worsen in quality and his contact with the female staff of the station becomes increasingly problematic and so he is quickly transferred to the field.

3151 Derek runs small missions and manages to complete each one. Colleagues often complain of lamentable decisions, but recognize that the man is lucky. He manages to form a small squad to rescue Chao Wang on a mission on Enceladus . In the upper echelons of the FDS, some see him as a rising star, others fear his risky methods. Alas, they are more numerous and better ranking, so he finds himself sergeant in one of the many schools affiliated to the FDS.

3151 Before the catastrophic results of failing students under the responsibility of sergeant Hogan, he must leave the school.

3153 Commander Jin-Su takes Derek under his wing, and before long entrusts him with an "informal extraction mission" with Chao. He is unfortunately unable to defuse the security system of scientist Finola Keagan's home. Fortunately Derek is able to knock the scientist unconscious and escape. But his partner in this mission, Chao Wang, died after being pursued and shot by a group of security guards.

3154 Derek manages to board the Daedalus along with 15 other crew members.
