I've seen this on occasion (people thinking certain crew are more prone to being mush than others), so let's explain:
First, on any given ship anyone who isn't Chun has about a 13% chance of being Mush. If you're not always picking Chun, statistically speaking, after 7 to 8 ships you should be Mush at least once. Assuming you have a Mush Stephen one ship, the Mush Stephen the other ship isn't necessarily the same player as last time -- if you take the odds of being a mush as a function of the player rather than the character (say, the second Stephen had been on 7 ships without being Mush), the idea that they were chosen as that ship's Mush shouldn't be too far fetched. My own personal experience is that Jin Su is more likely to be mush, but it's a matter of sample bias more than anything else.
