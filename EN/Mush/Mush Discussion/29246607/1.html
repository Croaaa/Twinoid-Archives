Hey guys!

Started working on a Twin Points Tracker to make life easier in the future, when we actually get this introduced.
It was not trivial to figure out the units used for the points, but I think I cracked it.
There are currently about 1080 units unlockable in the french version, based on previous games I expect most of the titles to transfer without a change to the english Mush.

For the simplest titles you get 1 unit, which is worth 1 TP (it gets rounded to the nearest 0.5 value).
The higher tier titles worth the following unit-TP point values:

  • 2 -> 2 TP
  • 5 -> 4.5 TP
  • 10 -> 9 TP
  • 20 -> 18.5 TP

    Didn't find any higher tier so far, but there might be (for example die2nite has a title worth 60 units - and there are only about 300 units distributed there) .
    So far I've found about 400 TP worth of titles, there are a lot of 1 TP ones (a lot of character-specific ones), it'll be fun to hunt all these down in the game.
