Part of the problem right now is that there's a huge influx of new players and a lot of them go inactive. The game is usually much better than it's been for the last two weeks or so. The good news is that it *will* die down and get better again.

There are castings you can join (I'm honestly not following many of them so I don't know which way to steer you if there are any that accept newer players that aren't referrals from people already in the group) and that'll help some too. But that shouldn't be the end of the answer by a long shot.

Early mush rampages are usually a sign of 1) someone that doesn't want to be a mush, 2) someone with an inactive partner or 3) someone new that doesn't really know how to play the long con. Two and three are scenarios I can forgive, though I'm still not fond of the early rampage. I don't think there are many scenario one folks out there, but there are a few.

As far as how to get your morale back up after that happens? The only thing I've got is to try to rally the crew to see if you can at least get PILGRED fixed and get back to Sol. It takes a decent crew, but it can be done!
