So I think this suggestion is pretty simple: allow young plants to produce oxygen but not fruit. This would be a double edge sword given Ian (or someone) would have to take care of more plants sooner as, just like adults, this oxygen would only be provided if watered and healthy.

The reason for this suggestion is some early ships can take almost two days to fill depleting about half the oxygen. This situation can lead to the Mush just instantly rampaging and screwing over the crew which is especially terrible due to the long wait for the ship to fill. This, in my opinion, breaks the game balance some. By allowing a small source of early oxygen production greater than two plants, it might make the game slightly more fair for ships that take longer to fill. I don't see this as too much of a help considering someone would still have to take care of the plants; thus, costing AP that could be used on other tasks.

Lastly, if implemented, it would stop the silly practice of not treating or watering (half the time) young plants till they grow up. Purely realistically speaking it: unhealthy and under watered plants wouldn't grow so well and young plants still perform photosynthesis before baring fruit.
