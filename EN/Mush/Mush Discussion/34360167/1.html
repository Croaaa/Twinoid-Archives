Why can't we put cameras on drones? That already happens now.

This will sacrifice a drone and a camera.
The camera must be partially dismantled, costing corresponding AP.
This requires the Robotics Expert skill (so it's an upgrade);
and permission from NERON (so it needs a NEXUS research permission);
and a new blueprint.
The camera will only record once per cycle.
It will escape at the end of each cycle as per drone rules.
It can be sabotaged exactly according to camera rules.
It will continue to record even if picked up, unlike a mycoalarm. (Also these people don't seem to have shirts big enough to block a lens.)
Unless you have a Dirty T-Shirt, which can be used to obscure random parts of the log (I need my fun somewhere.)
Drone cam will record itself being picked up, just as all objects being picked up are logged.
Pick it up in a room with a Trapped shelf, and Trapper rules are in effect.
A drone cam's log will not indicate location.

Basically, the drone-cam would be hard to build, so a ship won't have it right away, nor would Gioele's Paranoid skill be an automatic over-power move.

Further, because it records in chunks that are one cycle in the past, if you want to know what happened in a room at that moment, you have to waste points chasing after the drone and picking it up. (It would be even more evil if downloading the log came with success percentages.) The best deal is to come upon it in a room and check its log, then try to figure out which adjoining room had this action.

And because it ONLY records one cycle in the past, it doesn't totally replace the human trackers on the ship.

In some ways, it even gives the Mush a potential element with which to wreak havoc.

Discuss!
