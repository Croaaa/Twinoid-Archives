I find Finola to be a quite good Mush. But you have to play a short-term strategy with her. Spike someone immediately before and after the day change, no matter how publicly, so your partner can finish turning them, spend all of day 2 saying you're going to be saving up AP to work on research, then do your spike on transition and kill the hell out of Chun and generally send the crew into a tizzy.

In the ensuing chaos, you'll undoubtedly be dragging people out of the dorms to fight you, the perfect opportunity for your partner to stealthily and safely finish the conversion of your chosen target, leaving the ship on day 3 with no Mush research, 2 mush, and without at least their primary Mush researcher and very likely without Chun as well.

A Finola Mush is incredibly well-positioned to really screw a ship over, probably one of the most effective positions you can be in.
