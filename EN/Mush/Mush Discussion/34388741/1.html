Mush also get to pick who they want to convert. An extremely skilled Eleesha mush hunter becomes an even more powerful Eleesha mush.

But skills are there just for fun. A lot of characters - Chao, Jin Su, Frieda, Chun, Hua, Ian, Eleesha, Gioele, Stephen are perfectly fine bronze. A month is just 10 Euros anyway and doesn't run out when you're not on a ship, unlike a lot of MMOs where it runs even when you're not playing. It's a good deal.
