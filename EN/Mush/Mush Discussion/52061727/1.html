Although this is a major tangent... A mention of the concept of the division of labour. Essentially, if you divide up the labour to just do 1 job, then they'll do that job very effectively. Say if Hua only did piloting and didn't also do tech and gardening work, she'll be doing a lot better a being a pilot since all of her AP is invested into it.

It's not so much whether it's better to be a generalist or a specialist, but working out the correct balance to keep the ship in the best condition.
