If you're human your goal is to find and eliminate the mush and after that repair PILGRED (the ship's warp drive, essentially) and return to Sol.

If you're mush your goal is to destroy the ship or kill all humans. It's alright if all mush die in the process as long as all humans die or the ship is destroyed before the humans manage to repair PILGRED. Alternatively, you can also win by infiltrating Sol i.e. returning to Sol before all mush have been eliminated.

There's also Eden but that's essentially like returning to Sol with some RNG-heavy extra steps.

I recommend you check out the game's wiki Mushpedia, in particular all the articles under overview.
