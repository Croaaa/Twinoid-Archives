This is an idea I've tossed around in my head a bit but the desire to flesh it out hasn't been strong enough for me to dedicate the time...

A lot of us check up on mush habitually, then when there's no new chats or developments, we're like, awwwwwww...

So I was thinking, what if we created chat or forum games within a special channel and the crew members played games within games over these special channels? It would not be mush-immune of course but I don't think it would throw off the game's balance either.

A similar idea I had was to create a roleplaying thread in the main discussion where people could iterate or improvise on the character they've selected.

The challenge would be coming up with proper forum/chat games to play. What do you guys think? Like it, hate it, got some ideas?
