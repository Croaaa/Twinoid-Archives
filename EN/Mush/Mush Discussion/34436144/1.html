One thing I've noticed a lot of people mentioning on this forum is that Stephan's first skill, Chef, is considered mostly useless. Due to the initial settings on NERON BIOS, cooked and unfrozen food and fruits are destroyed at the start of each cycle, rendering it a fantastic griefing tool, and most people refuse to eat and cook their own food for the first few days for fear of Fungal Kitchen.

What many of those people fail to mention is how damn USEFUL Chef can be once one gets past those vital first few days. While the chef points are not very useful initially, once you get the +1 ration AP rebel base rations become VERY good for AP generation. If your crew goes on expeditions it becomes possible for a chef to convert up to 4 alien steaks a day into proper rations, and if you trust your chef you can even eat them without losing morale!

However, the least-mentioned part of the chef skill that I find the most useful is the following:
1. Chefs can see the nutrition information of various foods, including the properties of plants.
2. Chefs can see how badly spoiled food/plants are!

The former allows a chef to help the crew's botanist (or allows him to function as a poor botanist if you do not have one for whatever reason.), the latter lets him warn crew-mates which fruits can make them sick, and what they will end up sick WITH.

Personally, after trying Chef I've found it to be an incredibly useful skill once you get past those crucial early days. It's one that is highly underrated and in the hands of an experienced player can be a massive help to the crew. Easily on par with leader, botanist, and shooter.

What are your thoughts?

Another surprisingly useful and under-loved skill: Mycologist.

While Mycologist doesn't give Ian a lot on it's own, or help him perform his primary job, it's the singular skill on the ship that allows the crew to remove a spore essentially for free. Better, actually, as it can be combined with an (often required) healing action.

Mycologist combined with Medic (Making a gold Ian Finola's equal in research and giving him crazy strong heals) or Nurse (1 free spore removal per day!) can really make Ian a viciously powerful anti-mush character, in addition to his normal duties of 1. keeping the crew oxygen supply from running out. 2. Researching. 3. Keeping crew health and morale up via fruit.

Combine it with Pacifist for incredible returns!
