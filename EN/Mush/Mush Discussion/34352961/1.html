On all three of my ships, I've found the best way to win is to actually... do my job and encourage others to do theirs.

Paola and Jin Su, for example, shouldn't really give two farts about the Mush aside from: 1. Making sure Eleesha/Chao/Chun are hunting, 2. Making sure they don't put THEMSELVES at risk to be infected. They have jobs to do that will take up a good deal of their AP, failing to do that job means the crew will die regardless.

In my second ship, one that had 15 human players at one point and lasted into day 12 (we lost one or two to inactivity after curing our Mush though Y.Y) the very first thing I did as Jin was simple, and turned out to be fantastically effective. I opened half a dozen private chats few people who I expected would need to work with each-other. I almost immediately got our three major Mush-Hunters on the same page and working closely together, had Chun coordinate Finola and Ian's work in the lab with the hunt. I had Janice, Kuan, and Raluca in one chat for Nexus work, and another two for Bridge staff and pilots. We had our Eleesha, Terrance, and Raluca in a tech chat (Eleesha was a latecomer to that, but she did a lot of repairing on that ship since we had NO techs at all.) Even Gioele and Stephan were placed in a "Generic" group to coordinate their efforts (the former was inactive, the latter Mush and assassinated by Terrance). While we DID have everyone contributing to the Mush hunt, each person's role on the ship came first, and in the end we did pretty damn fantastic for a mostly bronze ship and no technicians at all.

I won't say who I am on my current ship (I'm not Jin), but I've been advocating people focus on their roles first and the Mush hunt (which is probably over. We're pretty sure the mush are gone) second. I've had to shout down and stop our more violent players from going too crazy on the mush-hunting, but overall things have really went well for us despite loosing a lot of people to inactivity. I'd say we have a real, if slightly long, shot at Eden on this ship if everyone keeps working so well together!

The real advantage Mush have is their ability to spread fear and paranoia among the human crew. In short: Fill your role and you can easily win this as human.
