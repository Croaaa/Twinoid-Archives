Hello, I'd like to make it aware for us the English users that Twinoid will shutdown their games in 1st of November 2023.

Twinoid's message about this is here:
https://twinoid.com/fr/article-fr/686...

Twinoid's translated messsage:

Hello Twinoid!

We have important news to announce to you:

Twinoid and all of Motion Twin's web games will permanently close their doors at the beginning of November 2023.

We know this news will sadden some of you, however when we ended the paid options on our web games, we indicated that they would remain online at least until the end of 2020. The deadline has been postponed many times, but gradually fewer and fewer of you are taking advantage of it and keeping the games online is becoming more and more complex to ensure.

Concretely, all of our web game sites will therefore become inaccessible in the first days of November.
Until then there should be no particular changes.

This is an opportunity to highlight the existence of several community projects that have emerged to recreate some of our web games, some of which are brought together within the Eternal Twin project.

We also made the sources of a good part of our web games available a little over a year ago. You can access it on GitHub.

Finally, we want to thank all the players and we hope to see you again on our other games, current or future!

So we only got 2 months left to enjoy playing Twinoid games for the sake of nostalgia, or try out new Twinoid games that you have not tried before while they are still here. Make these last moments memorable for yourself!
