IMO, if someone's not responding in the public feed, try opening a private conversation with them. Maybe they'll be more comfortable in a one-on-one. Or, at the least, any tips you try and throw their way for their current character won't be lost in a sea of text.

Hm... can "waste of AP" give "Playing against team" ban?
I think so. Cube in day 1 for Chao is 100% waste.

Not in this case.

The sole purpose of the Kube is to sit there like forbidden fruit, tempting players to spend AP on a selfish purpose rather than helping the team, just like every turret invites you to gamble for some by inefficiently shooting hunters.

So it is a deliberate part of the game.
- It adds a form of prisoner's dilemma, which makes it more interesting.

Likewise, you cannot sanction a crew who decide to punish (kill) someone for doing this excessively, as this is the risk they are taking, besides the ship not getting very far due to their lack of participation (group benefit vs. personal benefit).

Killing them in order to make them behave differently in other ships is of course no help to the current ship, but still a reasonable consequence if you look at the "game" as a whole (enforcing norms, even at a cost, for future benefit). Pretty meta.
