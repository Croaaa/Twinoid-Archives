My thoughts and suggestions about how to deal with griefers... don't lose hope.

My ships 2-10 were plagued with griefers, every one of those ships had at least one, and on some of those ships I reported 4 different players. I was getting rather incredulous on the forums, as some people were posting about how griefers didn't seem to be much of a problem anymore, heh. I posted about my experiences.

Turns out I actually was repeatedly jumping after dying or the ship ending... and so were some of those griefers. In a few cases, the griefer I was reporting on one ship was the same griefer I was reporting on the next ship. Eventually judges caught up....

So, my suggestions. Report them. Try to organize your ship to remove them. Don't worry if killing them on your ship frees them to become a new griefer on a new ship. It's your ship. Don't let griefers stay on it. Work with your fellow players if possible, that's great for your own morale as a player, a great way to learn the game, and a great way to have fun - even if you're having fun teaming up to stop a griefer, and then continuing to have fun the rest of the ship with the teamwork created to deal with that.

You don't need a griefer to build a team, of course. Building a team is a big part of many successful and really fun ships. But that strategy works, as far as anything works, against griefers too.

And know that every ship can be really, really different. You may end up with some of the same players on your next ship - if they die about the time you do, they may start ships about the same time as you do - but many of the players will be different. Most ships are different from the last. Keep playing, keep trying, and learn as much as you can, enjoy as much as you can, so you're ready for the good ships you get - you will get good ships too.
