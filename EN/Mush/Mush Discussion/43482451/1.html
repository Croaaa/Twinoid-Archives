Inactivity or Idlers
Inactivity (new players, players not communicating or playing) will not be sanctioned, do not report them. We do not want to sanction new players who might not be engaged. Learning to work around these players is part of our game. You *can* succeed on as ship that has many deaths and/or a fair number of inactive players. Luck and team work amongst those left will be in order but it can be done. Thoughtful communication and patience can go a long way.

There are a couple, very specific, instances of inactivity or perceived inactivity which can be reported. If there is proof of idling for XP farming without actually playing, this can be reported. Proof though would include an admission of this type of activity. Widespread idling for XP gain isn't happening and it shouldn't be assumed that someone inactive is engaging in this activity. Over reporting this will result in a sanction for the unnecessary reports if there isn't solid proof of this behaviour.

The other type of inactivity that can be reported is surrender or giving up. For example, a player has had a disagreement on the ship and stops playing. This is not allowed, ships must be played out.
