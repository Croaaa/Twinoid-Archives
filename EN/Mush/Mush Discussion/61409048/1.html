In Die2Nite, a coalition is when you make a group with your friends or whoever you want to play with and then when the leader of the coalition joins a new (random) town, everyone in the coalition automatically joins the same town. That way you can ensure that everyone in the group will always be playing together.

There's no equivalent feature in Mush, but the closest thing is the random jump thread. Castings are more like making a private ship as opposed to ensuring that all your friends simultaneously join the same public ship.
