To sum up, there are two main goals.

  • There are 14 humans on board the ship and 2 Mush. The Mush must contaminate other crew members, sabotage the ship, kill humans. The humans must discover who the Mush are and kill them. They must also do anti-Mush research to make contamination more difficult.
  • The other goal is to survive. The ship isn't very well designed, it isn't finished and it falls apart. So the crew must complete projects to improve the ship, and they must face many dangers (fires, broken equipments, hunters that try to destroy the ship, lack of oxygen, hunter, morale problems, injuries...).

The ultimate goal is to go back to their home planet Sol after repairing the PILGRED, a powerful engine, with no Mush on board and with the serum that will enable them to cure infected humans on their planet.

As for details, you'll learn everything progressively. Try to find someone on your ship who is experienced (but be careful, they might be Mush ) and ask for advice or explanations every time you don't know what to do. You can also ask here on the forum.

As the captain here's some important things to note:
Communicate with the crew and work closely with the radio expert and the scanner at the bridge. Keep close ties with Raluca to instruct her on how much fuel (located in two chambers, one in each rear storage) in the combustion chamber (must be exactly the fuel amount not 6 fuel capsules for a planet 3 fuel capsules worth away). Whoever is the radio expert you should tell what to announce with announcements.

Don't move the ship when Paola is signalling other headquarters or when there's HUMAN pilots outside (there's a Nexus project that uses magnetism to move fighter ships, but keep in mind projects diappear after one of the three are done).

I recommend creating tabs like "Scanning Threads", drug property threads (Finola needed to read them!) and item threads that you should make as captain. Work with Chao and Eleesha + others to track the Mush. Issue missions (costs 1 AP and gives 3 AP when accepted) to those who need them. Annnnnd...

When the Mush are eliminated, get projects flowing as the captain and continue to work with others on things like PILGRED, expeditions (oxygen, hydrocarbons are key or ruminants and insects and oceans which supply food if you kill them or find food plus mountains and shipwrecks which can give fuel, and forests which give plants at times). As the captain, try to find intelligent life to get starmap fragments to create the map to get to Eden (a more secure Sol. with Mush-Free style .
