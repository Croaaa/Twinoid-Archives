By moving between rooms, when you are out of MP (but still have AP).
It will automatically be converted. 1 AP => 3 MP.

  • +2 MP if you have Scooter (with remaining charges)
  • +2 MP with Sprinter skill (doesn't stack with Scooter)
  • +1 MP on even cycles when holding a Boulder artifact
