SummerMcLovin

As others have mentioned, Chun is the only confirmed human at the start of the ship. This is her most important characteristic, as you will have to coordinate the early suspicions of the crew in regards to who is Mush, rather than a possibly-Mush Derek or Eleesha. The Mush hunters can do without you, but it is much easier when there is an active Chun ready to give them credence in case of a mistaken dead human. She also should protect or hide dangerous and important items such as aggressive Mage Books, weapons (especially Grenades) and the Hacker Kit.
For that reason, I wouldn't recommend her to a new player, at least until they have had some part in a Mush hunt before they try to lead one.

Most of her work will be in the Lab though, watering plants (diseases being treated by a Botanist if possible) and relaying Research.

Addem-Upmost of the good research aboard the ship requires her presence People seem to make a big deal of this, but MycoScan is really the only one you would want early, then the Cure if you have several Biologists/Medics/Polyvalents or a Genius to get it finished quickly to take care of your second (or further) Mush. The Soap can be useful, but the Spore Sucker can be used in its stead and only requires the Mush Sample. By the time you are working on Glory Research, it is likely that you already have her Genome map from Xyloph.

MOH is definitely her most useful skill, but doing without it isn't really much of a problem if you have at least one Morale skill-holder left alive (who can do the thing and eat fruit to regain their own morale).
