[1826688]

Ian is a pain if you don't have another active mush to back you up. 3ap to spike makes it almost impossible for him to regularly spike people without it being noticeable. Basically it means there's a 6ap cost to spike with a single spore or 8ap with constipaspore. I'd rather take Paola mush tbh.

The first few days there's a simple task you can do to reduce the effectiveness of the humans. Keep planting banana trees.

If you can, take "Green Thumb" along with "Botanist." Getting the humans to waste ap on researching "Extra Hydropots" is a great way to keep them busy on an activity that will never provide them benefit because you will kill the plants right before they are about to provide fruit/o2 anyway.

While at first glance growing plants seems counter productive, this actually provides mush Ian with a few benefits:
First benefit: by doing your job people will be less likely to suspect you.
Second benefit: A banana planted is a banana that didn't gain the humans ap and wasn't used to mush test anyone.
Third benefit: While doing your job, you can freely come and go to the lab. If the humans don't have a camera/mycoalarm in there this can be a spikeathon.
Fourth benefit: By looking active as a botanist you are almost certain to get invited along on expeditions which will give you the opportunity to spike people in icarus bay.
Fifth benefit: If you have "botanist" planting won't cost you any ap, so this is an activity you can do while leaving you free to conduct your nefarious sabotage of the ship.

If you are planting both banana's every day the first two additional plants will mature on day 6 so it's a good idea if you are planning on trying to spike/kill active players to plan the big raid for the day 5/6 cycle changeover (at the latest, earlier is obviously better if you can manage it).

If you aren't being monitored closely, let all your plants get to dry the day of the planned mush activity and then fail to water them cycle 8. Then at least if the humans stop your nefarious scheme, all the plants will die anyway, leaving them with a lab full of empty hydropots and no fruit to plant.
