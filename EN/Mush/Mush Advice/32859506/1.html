I have come to the conclusion that it really depends on the ship you're on. (Before you say "duuuh", listen.)
Question. Why do you want to camera a specific place? To prevent Mush interfering with the ship or the personel. Since spiking can only be detected by cameras, not people, your important or most visited places are the best places. Corridors are bad in my opinion, as it is easy to only move trough them, people rarely stay there, so the safety it brings is minimal. Extracing a spore can always be done in other places.

At first I would have said the bridge, since early or even midgame there is no sofa there, aka most people go to the dorms, but there are always the ones who stand and wait alone there. Since bridge personel usually have high rankings, they can be prime suspects.
The other part would be where the mush sabotages often. Oxygen tanks are a prime example, although the room is rarely used most of the time, so tracking back may be easy, depending on activity.
Apart from these, the biggest question is, "Which person getting mushed will hurt our ship the most?"
It can be researchers, bridge personal or even Ian, as these can be found alone for valid reasons.
So again, apart from dorms, I think it depends on you, and the ship. Remember, cameras can awlays be moved!
