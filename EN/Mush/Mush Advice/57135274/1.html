I think only bold works for post-its, at least from my experience.

* is standard for me for in-ship chat, it's why you'll see the occasional forum post with *omg* instead of omg.

There are a few little quirks in the chat system, strike doesn't work in the main chat if I remember rightly but it does work in private chats.
