I actually tend to do comms officer a bit different than has already been suggested here. The day 2 rebel base only gives glory for the ship. That's all it does. So when playing Paola, I skip it and focus on xyloph databases out the gate. There are some really useful ones that would be good to get sooner rather than later, namely the Kivanc contact which halves the AP rebel bases take to decode. If I don't get that before the day 3 base, I typically focus on getting the day 3 one (it will take approximately a full AP bar without anything that makes it easier), then ignore the day 4 base as well, since, while the effect is nice, I don't see it as crucial. So I do xyloph day 4 if I didn't get Kivanc yet.

Also, note that the rebel bases will contact you at the same time each day, and plan AP use accordingly.

French wiki link about comms - will show you the order and effect of the bases and other info, need browser to translate though: http://www.twinpedia.com/mush/communi...

And... it's pretty important to the comms officer (and the ship as a whole, since an effective comms officer benefits the ship a ton) to get the sofa built on the bridge. This takes the coordination of multiple people though - you need a tech to dismantle a shower for one of the parts and possibly a pilot to get you a scrap metal (although there are other ways to get scrap metal, this is probably the best if it can be arranged), and someone with 3 AP to build it once parts and blueprint get to the bridge.
