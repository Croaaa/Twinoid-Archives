If you have used the same IP you won't both be able to play on the same ship. I convinced my husband to make an account once but because we live in the same house, there's a thing on our accounts that prevents him from seeing ships that I've jumped on and vice versa. I understand why but it does kind of stink, we both play a lot of traitor games IRL and our desktop screens aren't visible to each other (our desks are back-to-back) so there wasn't an issue in our case. Hard to refer people to play the game when you can't refer anyone who might ever use your wireless network. Either that or you tell them you can never play together which sort of eliminates their main reason for trying the game.

As for the email address, if he doesn't delete his emails (I archive mine) then he should check to find the old signup email. He might have signed up with a variation (e.g. Gmail has plus-addressing and I'm not sure if Twinoid checks case sensitivity of email addresses on login). If that doesn't work you might try contacting Twinoid support here: http://twinoid.com/support/twinoid
