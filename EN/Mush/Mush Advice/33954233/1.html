[1757645]

Hang out on the bridge. Hope people help you to establish Comms. Then spend your AP on various Communication endeavors.

You should focus on Rebel Bases first. Those are offered once per day (until there are no more) and will become unavailable at the end of the day. Each gives a different effect upon completion.

Then there is the Xyloph stuff. Each time you do one, a random item is picked. One of them is the MUSH Genome Disk needed for the Anti-MUSH serum needed to win the game (if you return to Sol.)

You also can update NERON. Each time you do, a random NERON project is completed from the pool of those not offered yet. This gets harder as time goes on. This is basically a free Project, and it doesn't block you from other projects like working on them in the Nexus does.

After that, you can help with various other things. However, the Comms job is very AP intensive and you'll find yourself just sitting on the bridge. If the crew is nice, they make a Swedish Sofa for you and Frieda on the bridge. Hope that helps.

Note: If you are a MUSH Paola, good luck. You don't have an excuse to get dirty or leave the bridge.
