As part of future guides I'm "working" on with some peoples, Rain made me think that it could a nice touch to add some suggestions about : What skill should a silver player take? For each characters.

So it's in this idea I'm making this list, please feel free to make comments and share your thoughts on it.
Take notes that most of these suggestions are situational and aren't the best ones to do in function of the ship you're in.
These suggestions are assuming that you're playing as a human.


Jin Su Leader / Pilot
• Leader ; allows you to do Janice's job in case she's dead.
• Pilot ; if no planets are found, you'll have some extra AP, you can go help the pilots to kill hunters and collect some SM. Can be useful for an expedition too.


Frieda Astrophysicist / IT Expert or Pilot
• Astrophysicist Well, that's your job.
• IT Expert will give you 2 free scans per day. Note that IT Expert only works for scanning, not for searching a planet.
• Pilot if you feel like pilots gonna need an extra hand. But you probably won't have the energy for.


Kuan-Ti Technician / Astrophysicist or Leader
• Technician Will help you a lot for doing most of the projects. And when you've completed all projects, you can still be very useful.
• Astrophysicist Same reasons as above.
• Leader if you feel like your crew will need some moral boost.


Janice Schrink / Diplomat
• Schrink It's one necessary skill. Don't need to explain why.
• Diplomat Very useful skill, will helps you a lot during expeditions AND it will make it a lot easier to find the Eden. And that will make you necessary for expeditions, which is how you gain your glory . [/braggin' off]


Roland Pilot / Shooter
• Pilot Well that's your job. Always useful to collect scraps, go on expe or of course killing hunters.
• Shooter Makes you way more efficient on your primary task. And can be use to shoot down some mushies.


Hua Pilot / Botanist or Technician
• Pilot Same reasons as Roland.
• Botanist Ian can have a hard time to take care of all the plants, you can take some with you in the dorm to take care of them. Plus it's an useful skill for expeditions, which is how you gain your glory.
• Technician An extra technician can be very useful too... I'll go for Botanist but the choice is yours.


Paola Logistics Expert / Rebel or Radio Expert
• Logistic if no SOFA is made on the bridge, you can use Finola's bed in the medlab, so she won't have to lie down to gain 2 AP per cycle. If it's made, then Frieda doesn't have to use a dorm.
• Rebel With this skill, you'll save a lot of AP which could be use in some Neron UPDATES, your crew will love you.
• Logistic Expert In some games, it's hard enough to get the connection so without that skill it can be harder... again, your choice.


Chao Wrestler / Torturer
• Wrestler Will make your kicks very efficient, good combo with Torturer with one kick plus one " torture " action, you can see minimum the last 4 actions of the person.
The bonus of Wrestler doesn't apply to the knife.
• Torturer Best way to find a sneaky mush is with a huge book on his face!


Finola Biologist / Medic or Diplomat
• Biologist will make you way more efficient for the researches.
• Medic Same plus some free healing points.
• Diplomat if Janice doesn't have this skill, can be very useful. Even if you have a high chance of geting back dirty from expeditions. Be sure to be ready to wash yourself.


Stephen Apprentice / Shooter
• Apprentice you can fill any blank.
• Shooter because the blank you'll have to fill will be pilot for most of the scenarios.
NB : Chef is a skill book which will appears every game if Paola does her job.


Ian Botanist / Green Thumb
• Botanist Someone has to get some dirt on his hands.
• Green Thumb Gonna make your job harder but it will worth it, if every plants is fine you'll gain 7 O2 / day, which will allow your crew to look for other planet than that the ones that are containing o2


Chun Mankind's only hope / Premonition or Lethargy.
• Mankind's only hope without that skill, your crew will lose two per day instead of one.
• Premonition if you work with Janice, it can help you to find a mush.
• Lethargy if well organized, this will give you a huge amount of AP that can really help the crew.
NB : We usually give a skill book to Chun since she's the one we can trust.


Raluca Physicist / Technician
• Physicist Will help you work on your PILGRED and for the project that all wants : The Plasma Shield tinrintiiin
• Technician Always useful. And it will make your work on the PILGRED easier.


Gioele Paranoid / Caffeine Junkie
• Paranoid will give you two cameras. Handy if you want to protect your mates!
Tip : If you take that skill with only one inventory slot available, the camera will automatically be installed in the room you're.
• Caffeine Junkie minimum +4AP per day, that's not nothing ! With the PILGRED done, you'll have 4 coffees per day.


Eleesha Tracker / Observant or Technician
• Tracker allows you to print a list that contain at least the name of one alpha mush. You can also track down people, to see if they're up to something.
• Observant will give you 25% of chance to detect a secret action. These two skills combined are awesome for the investigation.
• Technician if you feel like the investigation won't last long and you think it would be nice to have a skill that will allow you to remain useful even after the death of the mushs.


Terrence ( this one is tricky, very situationnal )
1. Robotics Expert / Pilot
• Drones can be very useful and make the crew economize a huge amount of AP. Pilot will allow you to collect your own scrap. You can take this combo if you see that there is already some technician who took care of some primordial action like dismantling one shower.

2. Robotics Expert / Technician
• If you think you can trust the pilots to get you enough scrap to do your job.

3. Technician / Pilot
• With this combo, you won't win personal glory and won't be able to upgrade your drone(s). But you'll be multitasks in the ship.

4. Pilot / Shooter
• Hua and Roland are already dead.
