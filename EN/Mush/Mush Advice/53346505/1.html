You have several solutions :
- Whisper (i.e. start a private conversation) with people in the same room.
- Go to the bridge, which allows you to read the main channel and speak in it.
- Wait for D6 and hope that the comms officer decodes the rebel base that allows people to speak without their walkie.
- Use post-its to write messages.

And next time, remember never to take your iTrackie (or any important item) on a planet with Intelligent life or Strong Winds zones.
