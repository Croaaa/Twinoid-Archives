Hi, vaultdweller123, it's not illegal to share your Twinoid username. We considered that doing so might spoil the game for others but in the end felt it was too punitive. It's up to you, especially if you are indeed asking questions on the forum or just want to keep anonymity, to break that anonymity or not.

Game end you can always see the names of your team mates, after the ships are no longer anonymous, and you can PM those people, become "friends" by adding each other to your contact lists or join groups together. When Castings come, you can join those together as well.

Please use the Q&A thread for these types of questions in the future. There is also a suggestion thread for suggestions. You can also read this first post here to get some clarification on what is indeed allowed in the game you can ask questions in that thread as well.
