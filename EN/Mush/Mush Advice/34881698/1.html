TBH, I don't even like putting planets or projects or research all in one thread. I especially don't like consolidating on an active ship.

The threads get too long and too unwieldy too fast. I'm sure I'm not the only one that hates expanding a 50-message thread just to find the one message that's hiding behind the consolidation.

Inventory, dirty, stuff like that that doesn't have to be referenced quite as often - by all means do one big thread for that stuff.

[1384452]

Not fond of threads now that I think about it. Damn lag.

Research, projects, planet results should not be kept on threads.
Fires and breakages shouldn't get threads.
Time-intensive things like planet scanning.. yeah, because sometimes I log on 30 minutes before cycle change and don't want to spend half an hour looking for it.
Fruit and pills definitely get their own thread.

Inventory threads should be only up to like 5-10 days while people haven't found/built everything or dumped things out of the storages. Then they should be split into things like medikit, wrench, drone, and scooter threads. Because it takes forever to dig through an inventory thread for one small thing.

Research and projects are a controversial one because they're important. But the "20%, 21%, 29%" relaying bulks up a thread on an active ship really fast. Good thing is that you can dump them later when they get too fat.

I'm not really sure what to do with injuries. On my current ship, we have a nice format of "12 :pv: 10 :pmo:" (12 10 ) which is really easy to read and doesn't seem to lag the game for some reason.
