OsiuOK i got it... now where to hide it

On most ships, the front and central storage rooms get cleared of any useful gear. As such, they don't get a lot of foot traffic, unless an O2 tank breaks or a fire starts. The downside is, the room logs tend to be easy to read through, and the list of potential suspects can be rather small. That said: If your Eleesha is dead (Don't respond to this post, else you might reveal who you are to your shipmates) you might have even more success if you hide more than one item in the same room. A search will find the last item that you hid. But if you hide the disk first, and then another (useful) item after that, most players won't want to waste their AP searching the same room a second time. Eleesha can search rooms for free, which can undo your plans to keep it hidden rather easily.

Fires can actually hurt you as much as they hurt the humans. Hidden items can be damaged by fire, and unless the item has been hidden for at least 24 hours, and you haven't been on the room since then...it might make you a prime suspect.

Also be aware that if Chao has Torture, your efforts might be for naught.
