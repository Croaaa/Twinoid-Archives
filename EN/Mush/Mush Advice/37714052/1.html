SummerMcLovin

Good general advice for being Mush is to stay hidden as long as you can, only acting when your victims are unaware, offline or otherwise vulnerable, or when you can do enough damage before anyone can react fully. Expeditions can be useful for this, e.g. you have Traitor and skills/item to make sure it isn't you that the bad events hurt; attacking people who are significantly damaged after an expedition; using the Command Console to move the ship and kill all those off the ship.

For infecting rather than killing (which you should try to do at first since anyone who survives your rampage will deal with you soon afterwards), you'll need to be able to extract spores. This will always make you Dirty, so make sure you have a good alternative reason for how that happened. Spiking people can be difficult, even with Trapper and Fungal Kitchen to set traps for people. You'll need to get people when they are away from Cameras, but also have a good reason for why you were there. For example, Raluca is usually unprotected but anyone entering her territory will be very suspicious.

- moving the ship as commander, while somebody is in space
