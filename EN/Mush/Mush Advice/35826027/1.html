The RNG doesn't pick objects the way it picks one object during an expedition. It decides for each item individually whether it will be broken or not. If 5 plants each have a 50 % chance of burning (totally random figure, I have no idea what the chances are), they will have a 50 % of burning if there are 10 or 100 other objects in the room.

From what Blackmagic said, he tolerates the post-its method for expeditions, but he designed fires specifically so that players can't "cheat" like that.
