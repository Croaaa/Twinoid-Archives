This is a Casting
We operate on a monthly launch window, meaning that we ask all available members to "Ready up" from the 1st of each month until the 10th of each month as and when their public ships finish. This allows people to enjoy their random ships without pressure and still be able to participate in the Casting launches, gaining the best of both worlds!

We (by default) launch with the opposite crew loadout to the one in the current season.
