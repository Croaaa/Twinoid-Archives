If you wait until you're close to depression, you've waited too long. When the Mush are still alive, you know that they're going to be killed, or that they're going to kill people, or both. They often target the morale sources (Jin Su, Janice, Chun, Schrodinger), so not only you might lose morale when they're killed, but gaining morale after that could be a little difficult.

In late games, the real danger come from panic attacks, which become more and more frequent. They make you lose 3 and you can have 2 or 3 panic attacks in the same day (sometimes in a row). So keeping your morale as high as possible is a good idea, otherwise you might never wake up.
