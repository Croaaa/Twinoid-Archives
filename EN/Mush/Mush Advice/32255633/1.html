Roland Zuccali Your job? Easy, get on a patrol ship and shoot down those hunters! You can also use the Pasiphae (when there are no more enemies) to get some scrap metal, could be useful to build some stuff, and to repair the ship if you're not doing your job right!


His skills :

Sprinter : You earn an additional 2 MP every time you convert AP to MP.
Does not stack with Scooter.This competence is useful for a lot of things; to move stuff around, to do a little investigation on your own etc... it's useful in any condition.
It also allows you to visit one more zone on expeditions.

Pilot : Allows you to use patrol ship, be the terror of the hunters! This competence fits him since he wins his glory by killing hunters.

Shooter : Perfect combo with Pilot since this competence gives you 2 free shots per day. You can use them from a patrol ship, from a turret or to shoot down those mushes who're infecting your ship!

FireFighter : The Extinguish Fire action is always successful and some bonus for some projects. This isn't his major competence but it can be useful. It fits with Sprinter, finding + extinguishing fires can be hard sometimes.

The Optimist : You're the soul of the ship, you see the bright side at any time! You'll lose one less morale point per day

Creative : Now we're talking! This skill gives 50% chance to gain 1 AP after each failed action. Very useful when you miss your shot or even for killing some mushes.


Personal glory
Roland earns 1 for each hunter he shoots down. After 8 gains, these gains will be less frequent.

Some tips.
- Check your patrol ship before taking off ! You don't want to end up vs a swarm of hunters with no ammo !
- You can select your target by clicking on it.
- Never use the Pasiphae if there is still some enemies.
( Even Asteroide can hit the Pasiphae ).


Conclusion : His major capacity is to take down hunters, but Roland can be useful in many more situations. With his capacity for moving around, he can investigate, do relays, take care of some intern issues etc...
