U-Turn enables Hua to send the exploration team back to the Daedalus at any time during the expedition. It's quite useful in several situations :
- when you've found what you needed (oxygen, food, fuel...) and you don't need to explore the rest of the planet, especially if there are dangerous places left
- when someone is nearly dead and you still have several places to explore
- when you lose something or someone valuable, for example when the diplomat dies and the team find themselves with no diplomat and no weapons
- when the Mush take advantage of the exploration to kill or sabotage abord the Daedalus
