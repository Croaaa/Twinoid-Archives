I didn't mean to imply it was illegal on randoms but I would like to do my best to discourage people from using it even so, if not in the name of FUN, then in the name of intelligent gameplay

This tactic guarantees your death, while not being difficult to counter. There are a number of things your humans can do :
- Build spare extinguisher from BP
- Move the ship to kill the mush and rush PILGRED
- Complete Magnetic Net, move and kill the shroom for the items
- Use the firefighter skill found on three different characters or magebook
- Rush comms for extinguisher BPs and/or firefighter magebook
- Upgrade a firefighter drone, possibly using dismantled junk (like weapons) if mushy took the scrap ship

> God forbid your humans were extra paranoid like me and hides one extinguisher at start to prevent these things altogether

Also to keep in mind :
- Using the skill triggers an a NERON announcement instantly. A refresh manic human could possibly attempt to murder, pick up extinguishers and/or guard. While there are patrol ships everywhere, a well placed guard could waste enough of your AP for you to not be able to take off.
- This skill costs a whopping 4AP to use, which is more than any other action in the game. Between locating the extinguishers, taking off and gaining yourself the permission to do so, you're not going to be able to seed many rooms for spread
- Pyromania is level16 on the mush skill tree. By the time you unlock this, all but two mush skills will be available to you. You have many, many better options

It just doesn't look like a worthwhile risk at all
