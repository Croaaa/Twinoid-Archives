I still think Diplomat can be a good alternative for Medic Finola
Medic gives 2 main bonuses
Research Boost: Diplomat ceasefire may save your life if mush attack the lab early on - if the mush manage to kill/spore Finola then the research boost of medic won't help the humans anyway
Diplomat can prevent aggressive actions (both attacking and sporing). the advantage here is that you can declare a ceasefire on your own, without need for relays, disadvantage is that it is single use
Healing Bonus: there are only a few things that cause health loss where you would need a Medic
actually most of those situations can be prevented with a Diplomat:
- Combat (rampaging mush) - Diplomat can declare a ceasefire to minimize damage and have enough time to prepare for fighting back
- Random Steel Plates - you don't get steel plated that often in a game unless you are really unlucky, can still easily be healed by anyone with a medkit
(1 plate = 4-6 Damage = 2 Medkit heals)
- Expeditions - Accident and Fight event, Diplomat will prevent the Fight events, so you save a lot of damage here - Accidents only give 3-5 damage -> can be healed with 2 Medkit heals

generally Medic Finola is great against Mush who are sporing/infecting (most research is targeted at slowing down mush ability to infect or making the infecting more risky for the mush)
but theres only 2 researches to counter the mush who prefer hand to hand combat over infecting.
- Constipaspore Serum (nullifies the AP gains from Phagocyte)
- Natamy Rifle (makes it easier to kill the rampaging mush - if he is hard boiled/wearing plastenite/ hiding behind Ian)
Diplomat Ceasefire is probably better suited for dealing with the type of mush who rather goes around slaying humans instead of infecting them.
or if you cant get any research done anyway, because Chun and Ian are inactive and nobody else feels responsible for relaying.
