From a roleplay point of view, the mush sample needs to be in the lab so you can conduct tests/extract samples from it in order to help with the research. As for gameplay all it does is unlock research options while it is present in the room. You don't need to hold it while you research as it's a passive item.

On the other hand you need to hold the apron if you want to avoid getting dirty while doing w/e action. I wish it was passive just like the sample but it doesn't really make much sense ( I thought the same when i started playing)

MrGrunties

Certain items and/or Chun need to be present in the lab to have various research options available. In some cases, like the cat for example with research of the lenses, the item has to be held. The mush sample does not have to be held.

There is a good chance when you research that you will get dirty. It is not 100% guaranteed, but is highly recommended that you hold the apron as it will ensure you don't. You do have to have the apron in your inventory for it to protect you. Since you can't participate in research when dirty, it should be a given that you hold it except under the most dire of circumstances, especially if you are planning to research multiple times per visit.
