Why this thread? I've seen entirely too many people claim things that just isn't true. I still have friends who are experienced players ask if such things are true. I want to make this thread be a reference area that people can grab from when such things come up on their ship.

This is NOT a Q&A. If you post here only include a rumor and proof of it. Do not ask about rumors here, questions go to the Q&A. Only post if you have information regarding a rumor listed or not listed and if you don't have proof then don't post here. This is here to be a quick reference for debates happening on ships.

I removed rumors with unknown results so that people wouldn't get confused or influenced. Feel free to pm me with any unknown rumors to help make me aware of them. You could make reference to them in the thread but please do not let it turn into a debate.


  • Rumor #1: Bumpjunkinitis doesn't count against Eden glory.
    Conclusion: False. It does just like any other disease.
    Proof: http://mush.twinoid.com/theEnd/5765 6x Microbe Pollution. None of us were sick with anything else.

  • Rumor #2: Fires have less of a chance of burning important items if there is a bunch of items in the room.
    Conclusion: False. Every item has its own burn roll in the event of a fire.
    Proof: http://twd.io/e/0MtM0g/44

  • Rumor #3: Mycoalarms will go off when a mush extracts a spore in the room with it.
    Conclusion: False. They do not.
    Proof: Here
  • Rumor #4: The more AP used in a cycle, the higher chance of difficulties at cycle change.
    Conclusion: True. The more AP the crew uses the higher chance things are going to break at Cycle change.
    Proof: http://twd.io/e/tAn/79 and http://twd.io/e/RhAX0g/125 . This is in French but it's from the creator. If someone could offer a translation then that'd be grand!
  • Rumor #5: Using RFS will give you less glory at the end of the ship.
    Conclusion: False. Who even came up with that?
    Proof: Check any glory history of any ship who used RFS. There is clearly no penalties for such listed at the end.

  • Rumor #6: Eating a food item with 0 satisfaction (coffee, jumpkins, etc) will not remove starvation.
    Conclusion: False. If someone were to start with 0 satisfaction it takes 24 cycles for them to begin starving. Eating ANYTHING resets that number and any satisfaction gained from the food is added to the timer. (Example: rations give 4 satisfaction so eating would give you 28 cycles until you starve as you lose 1 satisfaction per cycle). Pills are not considered a food item and will not reset the starvation timer (But it will still make mush full).
    Proof: Difficult to prove beyond giving a before and after screenshot. I'll try to get one eventually.

  • Rumor #7: Spiking someone with other people around will cause the log to be revealed.
    Conclusion: False. Spiking is a Covert action, not a Secret action and can only be revealed by cameras and have a chance to be revealed by Eleesha's Observant. This rumor is a result of the terrible game tips.
    Proof: http://mush.vg/tid/forum#!view/67061|... (Requires translation)

  • Rumor #8: Inactives/Fires consume more oxygen!
    Conclusion: False. You always lose 1 oxygen per cycle. A broken oxygen tank or Oxygenated Ducts are the only things that can change that number.
    Proof: Do I really need to? This should be obvious considering that you don't lose more than 1 at the very start of the game when you have 16 people. For the fires I can try to get a before and after screenshot soon. You can just pay attention to your ship's stats, though...

  • Rumor #9: Pregnant women have a chance to vomit!
    Conclusion: False! Pregnancy does not cause vomiting or other illnesses! Just gives glory and causes a higher morale penalty upon death.
    Proof: http://mush.vg/patchlog Patch 1.58 (requires translation)

  • Rumor #10: You will only receive a Day distinction award (10 Days, 15 days, 20 days, etc) if you are on the ship the required amount of days (and cycles) since you've awoken.
    Conclusion: False. The distinction reward system is based off of the base number of the days the ship is. Your records might say you made 19 days but if you get to D20C1 then you are getting the Day 20 distinction. However this rumor DOES apply to receiving a gold ticket on Day 10+ and you must wait until 10 days after the cycle that the first person awakens (72 Cycles).
    Proof: 7645, 5765, and 4506. I've received 3 D20 as you can tell from my profile. 7645 and 4506 are both listed as "19 days" on my ship list. The other D20+ in my profile are all casting ships.

  • About fires : http://twd.io/e/0MtM0g/44

    The overal equation is fairly complicated but all that you should know is that an overactive crew will have a little more trouble, while an inactive crew will have a little less.

  • About pregnant women : http://mush.vg/patchlog
    Implementation of the pregnant status, which gives glory, and, if the pregnant woman dies, inceases the crew's morale loss.

There is seriously nothing wrong with Trail minimizer. The escape mechanism is designed to help crews deal with big waves of hunters (or even smaller waves, in the case of ships with pilot problems). This mechanism works perfectly : if you escape hunters, you'll get only half of them when the ship stops. TM only improves that mechanism by 50%. If there really was a problem, then it would have been detected and fixed long ago, because ships would have had endless problems with hunters. And I mean all ships or most ships.

Some ships can have bad luck and get two or three waves without moving (I mean moving when there are no hunters, not moving to escape). If a ship is really unlucky, this can happen several times (or days) in a row. But it doesn't happen every single day. And on most ships (those that don't do lots expos, at least, since that changes things a little), what usually happens is that you get your daily wave, escape it, then finish the rest when the ship stops (25% with TM, 50% without it).

Most rumors on TM I've seen were based on one or two ships only. But knowing the big part played by chance in the game, you can't draw conclusions based on so little evidence. It's a bit like people claiming that there was a mush traitor in their expo team. Most of the time, expos go horribly wrong because of the RNG and bad luck, without any help from mush traitors.
