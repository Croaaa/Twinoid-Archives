These things are usually client-side, for future reference and bug reporting. Disappearing items, freezing up, often clearing your cache, restarting browser or as a last resort restarting the computer will fix it. If it's a slowness problem, it's most likely also client-side: Try using Task Manager if you're on Windows to close programs. :-)
