Be warned, if you try to use the command terminal while there are Arack around and you have at least 1 fuel loaded - you are allowed to spend your AP to try and move the ship - it's only after you try that the terminal will inform you that Arack are around and you cannot move.

However you can still jump to Sol or Eden (if requirements are met) even if Arack are present.
