As I'm quite tired of watching mushes on my ship dying without making any troubles and giving free wins, I'll post here this guide. It's quite easy to perform, it's ok to do as bronze, with low lvl mush and low lvl host body, it's very flexible, depending on your ship.

Remember - this tactic like every other mush tactics is not the only one and is not best for every game, it's situatinal.

Main idea - one of the mushes do several things, that will make human life hell and his partner life perfect. Humans detect these and kill him for them, but it worth it and his comrade(s) easily win from now on.

Preparation:
- You will need full ap. Easiest way - don't do anything at all after joining the ship - just stay in lab and wait mush selection. If you happen to be human - go and play as human, if you happen to be mush - speak with partner, analize the situation, if you have decided to use this tactics - wait up to 4 cycles. You can go without full ap ofc, but why should you limit your possibilities?
- Best time to use it - just on day change. It will allow you and your partner to spike twice without infector, use extra spores and probably recharge some free points like shooter and tech.
- If you are Ian, some parts of the guide are not possiable. Still there is enough things to do, but I don't recommend it. All other characters are ok.

Why should mushes become less then 2?
You will be killed for your actions, but you can make just another mush for your partner instead. For that follow simple steps:
- Just before day change remove camera in room you want to infect a human, spike him
- Your partner also spikes him
- To confuse humans much more - remove cameras in both dorms and bridge - so they'll have no idea who was infected. Also they will not be sure about it at all.
- After day change either you or your partner (better partner) spikes again and takes anonymush

Knocking over cameras into oblivion
I still don't know is it a bug, or mushes just hate cameras so much, but if a mush drop a camera in the room with one installed (taking Paranoid with full inventory or dies with cameras in inventory) - it's lost forever. So after you took all cameras you can destoy them for free. Just stay in a room with another camera (if humans have 5, thanks to Gioele) or just any room (if they have 3, and all 3 are with you). When humans kill you, they'll have such surprise...

Too much unnecessary items
If your character has access to Tech skill - take it. If he does not - you can transfer to anyone who has. Then you can use your 3 tech points (3 because of day change) to dismantle:
- Tabulatrix - so cool option! No list to find your partner or check anonymush, no mage books, no BPs, no genome disk for RFS of pheromodem! Best thing to target
- Second shower - makes spore production so,so easy, and investigation much harder. Add some slimetraps and take emergency shower on the beginning of C1 to make things even better.
- Genome disk. If they have done it already - destroy it. No pheromodem, no RFS is good.
- Extinguishers. Not as powerful as full pyro-mush plan, but losing those will make their life harder

Forbidden professions
You can also leave ship without teches/pilots/morale support. On most of ships you'll find up to 3 people with each profession (quite often only MOH+Shrink and just 1-2 teches really). You can infect one of them, transfer to second and kill the third. Just make sure humans needed those a lot. Your goal is to crush them and win, not just make them angry a little.

Fighting the fighters
Well, what problems you partner and new mush will have? Most dangerous are:
- The list - has ~50% to give name of your partner, will reveal your partner under anonymush if they find your new infected friend
- Pheromodem - very powerful tool, that can reveal somebody was infected and will detect any mush actions. They got your mush body and can make it now...
You can stop both with dismanteling Tabulatrix. List - by infecting, transfering to Eleesha or coping Tracker with Stephen, printing and shreding list. You can also target genome disk to stop pheromodem.

Burning all fuel
If you happen to be the commander, or you just sure he has a lot of ap - you can burn up to 18 fuel with 2 jumps (just before and just after cycle change). If ship has less then 18 fuel at the moment. I don't want to write here all details how to make sure, humans will not be able to restore any - I did it in Q&A and there are quite a lot. Resulf if you did everything well: ship has 0 fuel and no way to get any, it cannot run from hunters, abadon any mush in patrol ships or do any expo. It will die from O2 loss or hunters.

Conclusion
You will need an active partner to do it. I did it several times myself and they turned to mush victories. Some of my partners were playing as mush for a first time, but after we discussed the plan and they followed steps I wrote, everything worked as planned - so don't be afraid about unexperinced partners.

If you want anything to add/correct or give advices to make plan better - you are welcome!
