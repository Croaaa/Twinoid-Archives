Haha, this is a classic mistake!
There are many ways you can communicate to your crew members if you loose your walkie-talkie..
First off, if you can find the 'Post It Notes' you can make notes which other people can read.
Secondly, if you are a communications officer you can still communicate with everyone without a walkie-talkie.
Thirdly, if your communications officer has been doing their job, around day 6 or so a base can be decoded which gives the ability to 'brainsync', meaning you don't need your walkie-talkie any more!
But you should take any steps necessary to make sure this doesn't happen in the first place!
