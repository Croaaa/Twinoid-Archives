Let me explain what you're doing here. And mostly... how to be useful, it won't be easy.


Kim Jin Su
As Kim Jin Su, you're the captain of the majestic coughcough Daedalus! As the Commander, you can give daily missions to your minions, for one AP you can give three AP to anyone.

You also have access to the Command Console, which gives you the huge responsibility of moving the ship.
Once someone has scanned an interesting planet, they'll give you the details. Someone will have to put the right amount of fuel needed in the Combustion Chamber and then you'll have to move the Daedalus in the right direction and activate the trip. Once the exploration of the planet is done, for 1 fuel, you can leave the orbit which will allow the crew to start scanning again!
When the PILGRED is done or Eden calculated, for 2 AP you can decide to do the trip. Be sure to ask for their opinions before doing it, they may not appreciate an early returns.


His Skills :

Leader : A very important skill! Invite all the crew to gather at one room and make them remember why they're here. For 2 AP, you'll give +2 to everyone in the room. ( Except yourself ).

Pilot : What's a good Captain without his navigation skills? This skill allows you to support the main pilots of the ship if they need a hand. It also allows you to pilot the Icarus.

Cold-Blooded : As a Captain, when a member of your crew dies, instead of crying over them, you act! This skill gives +3 AP each time after the death of a member of the crew.

Shooter : Perfect combo with Pilot since this competence gives you 2 free shots per day. You can use them from a patrol ship, from a turret or with a blaster to shoot down those mushes who're infecting your ship!

Logistic : You know how to organize your crew! Each cycle, one AP is given randomly to a member of the crew who share the room with you.

Strateguru : You know how to fool those hunters! This skill gives you 50% of chance to reduce the next wave by 33%
The effect does not stack if multiple Strateguru are in the crew.


Personal glory
Jin Su earns 3 each time he moves the ship. There is no gain when you move out of orbit. After 6 gain, this will happen less often.


Some tips :
- Use your daily mission!
- An early Impassioned Speech can be a good idea to make sure all your crew is prepared for the long journey.
- Check there is no one in flight before moving the ship! If the project "Magnetic Net" isn't done, everyone in flight will be lost.
- Be sure no RebelBase is trying to contact you before moving the ship. The signal will be lost if you move it.
- Check (Examine) your patrol ship before taking off! You don't want to end up vs a swarm of hunters with no ammo!
- You can select your target by clicking on it.
- Never use the Pasiphae if there are still some enemies.
( Even Asteroid can hit the Pasiphae ).


Conclusion
Jin Su is a very valuable member of the crew. By playing him, you've got to be active and be ready to move the ship at any time.
Your daily job will be to help the pilots to defend the ship or to do some relays where it's needed if there is no need to move to a planet.
If Janice is dead, your "Impassioned Speech" will become very important, be sure to organize your crew to make them more efficient.
