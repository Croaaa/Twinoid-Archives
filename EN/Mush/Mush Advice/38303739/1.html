crawlkill

Derek is less good for the mush inquisition, but mush generally expose themselves eventually anyway, and he's much better for general use because he's not limited to one action point a cycle. He's arguably even a better investigator than a non-Torture or non-Torturehappy Chao, because two AP per cycle is a lot more MP per cycle than Chao's MP bonus gets. But I don't think Derek's meant to be an investigator: I think he's meant to be a second Gioele, someone without a specific role who can fill in anywhere but who shines when trusted and there's someone to be destroyed.

As to Andie vs Finola, a leveled Finola will probably be a Biologist and a Diplomat anyway, and the extra healing actions are invaluable on a late-stage ship where sliding plates are as much a danger as hunters and research is done anyway. I think they're mostly parallel, and both are characters with strong character play potential right out of the gate, so they're certainly both interesting.

On balance, yeah, Chao is probably a little better than Derek, and Andie is probably a little better than Finola. But every ship comes down to circumstance, and there will be times when you're howling, "If only I could give everyone 3 extra MP instead of having Torture!" and times when you wail "I wish I could heal 12 points for free instead of getting 3 extra AP from our inactive leader!" All characters are situational, and some characters are more situational than others. But three of my favorite four characters, Chao, Finola and Andie (and Jin Su), are on the cycle, and I'm happy to see any of them turn up as my options.

EDIT: I said Paola instead of Finola several times. Sorry, I've had Paola envy my last few ships.
