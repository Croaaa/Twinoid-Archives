A smart crew will always verify the planet they're heading to by checking the Astrophysics terminal as they're flying toward it. Anyone can verify the planet, or complete any remaining scans on it, while you're in transit there, too. So give them something every now and then, or they'll kill you. The room logs will name the planets you're scanning, each time you scan one of them. This can make maintaining your lie difficult, so bear that in mind.

To be clear, scanning planets (finding them) doesn't leave a room log. However, analyzing them (finding out their zones) does, and your crew might get suspicious if you never analyze any planets. Especially if you claim to have found planets, along with their zones, without actually analyzing any.
