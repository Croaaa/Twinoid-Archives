Make sure you:

1) Report it first. and then

2) PM the mod team immediately after the report is filed. A group PM is best, since it increases the chance one of us will see it.

It's a two step process. Follow both steps in order.

Don't tell us, "someone else reported it" in the PM. Even if someone else says they reported it, you have to report them, too. And then, you wait for a reply from us. More often than not, the situation will be resolved quickly (within the same cycle.)
Messaging the Mod Inbox has worked in the past, but we don't suggest messaging us there, at least for this purpose. You will get better results with a group PM to the mods.
