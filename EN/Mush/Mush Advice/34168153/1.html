Your character isn't permanent, this game works on a round-to-round basis. (a round usually lasts around a week). Success depends very heavily on players working together and making the effort to read and learn how the game works, because the ship crashes and burns very quickly when characters aren't fulfilling their roles.

There is an in-game FAQ you can access on the bottom right of the page, there are many, many guides on this Mush Advice forum, there is a Questions/Answers thread here for any questions you have that aren't answered anywhere else, and there's a growing resource of info in form of the Mushpedia.

Please take the time to familiarize yourself with how the game works instead of rejecting it outright, like you did in your last thread. What you perceived as "rudeness" towards you was rather disappointment that you didn't even try to read up and inform yourself, instead expecting everyone to spoonfeed the most basic info to you.
