Kompera

For RosenbergAtom, and anyone else it may help:

Human:
Cooking tons of rations - Food goes bad over time, and is in short supply.
Eating every time you can - Food is in short supply, and you only get the 'hungry' status if you don't eat for three days (accuracy check the day count, please)
Working on random neron projects - Only one of the three current NURON projects can be completed. Any effort (AP) spent on advancing the others is wasted effort. Also, the other two projects are gone forever, so choose the one to advance wisely and with the consensus of your teammates.
Going offline in random rooms - There is safety in numbers and under cameras. The Mush might get you!
Wandering around clicking all the things - (I'm not sure why this is bad. It can teach a new player a lot about things and doesn't cost any AP).
Staying dirty - You cannot take most (all?) actions involving consoles while dirty, and anyone who is dirty is a Mush suspect. If you do not or cannot explain why you are dirty, you may be assassinated by your own team!
Letting health or morale get low without saying anything - You teammates can help you, but only if you ask for help. This pretty much falls under the "Not communicating" tip, below.
Ending a cycle in the same room as Raluca - Raluca has the Antisocial special, and will lose 1 Morale per cycle she ends with another person in the same room as she. Don't screw your teammate!

Mush:
Sporing in front of a person or a camera - It will be logged and you will be revealed and killed.
Infecting someone in front of a camera - It will be logged and you will be revealed and killed.
Staying dirty - with caveats - Same as for humans.
Washing too often - It damages you without the skill which prevents the damage. And, you will be suspected of being a Mush.
Sabotaging things in rooms not many people go to - It will be logged and you will be revealed and killed. Sabotage is not a good Mush action in most circumstances. Converting humans is a much better goal. Late in the game when the ship is falling apart, it can be a nice added nail in the coffin, but before then it's most likely going to be a case of " - It will be logged and you will be revealed and killed," because even if the sabotage isn't logged (no camera or humans present), your presence in the room is.
Adds:
Mutating under almost any circumstance - You lose ALL human and Mush (yes, you read that right) skills. You cannot carry or pick up anything. You cannot infect a human. You cannot sabotage or interact with anything on the ship. You available actions are moving, attacking in HTH, and reading the Mush channel. You are essentially a short lived punching bag. Go down fighting!
Choosing skills without thinking about the ramifications, or too early - Mush get a lot of very good skills which take a high level to unlock, and filling up on low level skills isn't a good move.

Human or Mush:
Not communicating! - Not much to say here. Teamwork is stronger than solo play.
Doing whatever the tooltips say- The tool-tips suck. Seek enlightenment on the forums or from your fellow players, be they human or Mush.

Enfilade
DragonDo you lose fuel put in the combustion chamber in x cycles if the ship doesn't move? Can't you leave it there for future movement? If you put more fuel than required do you lose the extra ones?

When you move the ship, you move a number units equal to the amount of fuel in the engine. If there is more than zero in the tank before a planet is chosen to visit, whoever loads the fuel must be careful not to overfill the chamber lest the destination be missed. Essentially, (a) the engine fueler must be observant and possibly have to cart fuel back to storage, and (b) it's an easy invitation for mush sabotage ("I thought it was empty to begin with!" ).

The second point is about conserving fuel: you only need one fuel to leave orbit/avoid hunters, so adding any more is just a waste.

Actually... both suck pretty hard. We have limited stores... and everyone is expected to have a reason for cooking or eating.

Favorite this thread *Planet scan thread* This is important since everyone has their own planet scans... we need top use this to communicate with each other what's currently available to we can decide as a crew where to go next.

Favorite this thread *Pill description Thread* In medbay.,.. there are a wide assortment of meds. What they do is randomized every ship.. we need a medic/nurse to read and post the descriptions of these meds in this thread so people affected by disease know what to take

Favorite this thread *Current research thread* use this thread to ask for advice, as well as post current research projects. This can be used to work out priorities with crew mates to makes research more efficient and to keep us on task for relevant research tasks

Favorite this thread *dirty thread* post here where, and how, you got dirty... this is important as being dirty without a good reason can be a telltale sign of malicious activities

Favorite this thread *Item Thread* use this to discuss item locations... and item movement. This way the crew knows where everything is. This not only saves crew AP/MP... but can be absolutely essential in the event of an emergency (ie: finding an extinguisher when there is a fire)

Favorite this thread *nexus project thread* use this to communicate the current projects in the nexus. This is important since once one project is completed... the other two disappear. This means AP used in the other two are effectively wasted

Favorite this thread *Newbie Thread* anybody who is new to the game... or wants to learn more about how to play a new character. Feel free to post here... I will invite you into a newbie channel. The only rule here is you can only ask questions that don't involve trying to figure who is or isn't a mush. This is a channel that can only be used learning about the game. I will invite (or anyone that is currently in the channel can invite them) anyone that want's to learn :3
