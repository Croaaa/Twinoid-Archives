Unfortunately there isn't anything you can do. Eventually hunters will shoot them down or you will move the ship and cause them to die. If they die and are Mush, their body will act as a Mush sample automatically. If they are a griefer then your ship is just screwed. Only thing you can do is report them.

By the way, losing the sample doesn't doom the ship, just makes it harder to do anti-mush research.
