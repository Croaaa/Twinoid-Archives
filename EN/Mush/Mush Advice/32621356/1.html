Character: Frieda Bergmann
(She doesn't like being called Freida.. I will add helpful projects tomorrow.)


Role: She scans planets, and if you are silver or gold can also explore said planets quite well. She is an extremely useful crewmate for the whole ship, given her ability to find planets well. Never underestimate her skills at flying ships too if you decide to use pilot too. She DOES use a lot of AP though, be warned, so it is extremely useful to have Paola on the bridge with you. Her Logistics Expert randomly gives 1 ap to somebody else in the room at cycle turnover, so you can have a scan action every single cycle if your ship is efficient. If the priority is set to scanning, you will reveal yet ANOTHER planet property, on top of the one from her signature skill, Astrophysicist. 3x better than other crewmates at scanning! Want to go to Eden? Get starmap artifacts. Frieda can help and be a very good teammate in going to Eden.

Skills:
Level 1/Starter: Astrophysicist
This skill is vital for her, simply. It allows her to discover 2 properties per scan action. This makes her 2x as good as other people for scanning planets at the beginning, which is very very useful to have. ALWAYS use this. You will be responsible for finding a planet with oxygen, which is very important. Also, scanning to outright find a planet only costs 2 ap with this.
Level 2: Pilot
You never miss a landing or takeoff. If you miss, you may crash into the daedalus. Your ship may even have it so ONLY pilots are allowed to takeoff on the ships. If someone in the party for a planet expedition has this useful skill, you will get the bonus. Pretty important as it allows Frieda to both search and go on expeditions without using AP on icarus.

Level 3: Survivalist
All damage taken on explorations by THIS player is reduced by one point. This allows Frieda to once again explore planets very efficiently. The second use of this skill is that when you get alien steaks, you get one more. Your Stephen should be able to freeze those steaks into rations. Rations are not always in the best supply, so it's quite useful to have a Survivalist on your expedition. The final thing is that you are last to die if a fatal event occurs. What that means is if one of your team randomly dies, you will be the last one to.

Level 4: IT Expert
The owner of this skill has an increased chance to hack things with hacker kit, for one. The second use of this skill is it gives two free computer action points per day, which are used to analyze planets and for NERON projects. If you happen to be silver or gold this is quite the useful skill given as how it allows you to do your job faster.

Level 5: Radio Expert
The radio expert enjoys doubled success rate when attempting to contact sol. In addition, just by being in the room they increase the chance of contacting sol by 50%. This is a so-so skill. If you have an active paola you should really have no problem with matters of contacting sol, given as how she is by default comms officer. I'd focus more on planet scanning. Worst case, mage books give this so you could always use one of those..

Level 6: Antique Perfume
Every time you shower, you're immune to mush infections for 2 cycles. I presume this is like chun's passive, in that you literally cannot be infected during those 2 cycles. It is very underwhelming for a 6th skill compared to some other skills and Frieda is much better off doing other things, rather than wasting their time with this skill. Don't bother with it.

The Skills to choose, for your Frieda

Bronze Mode:
Astrophysicist (Enough said. You're a simple planet scanner. You help out a ton earlygame and even lategame.)

Silver Mode:
Astrophysicist (This skill is extremely useful no matter what mode you are. It is your bread and butter and helps you find oxygen planets.)
IT Expert (Free planet analysis and NERON projects.)

Gold Mode:
Astrophysicist (Once again, you scan for planets. This time you'll be helping out a lot with them, however.)
Pilot (This allows you to safely take an expedition to the planet, very useful especially if there are no other active pilots onboard.)
Survivalist (This allows you to stay alive on those planets much easier. You also get an additional steak, so more rations for the team.)
IT Expert (Allows you to analyze planets for free, which is amazingly useful when you happen to be a planet scanner.. also NERON projects which is quite good.)

Scanning for Planets
As I said, as Frieda, your main job will be scanning for planets. Early on what you really want to find is an O2 planet. Later on, you should try to look for intelligent life, but oxygen is always a help. Hydrocarbons is good too, for fuel. As for what you DON'T ever want on a planet: Mankarog, Volcano, or Seismic. If you get any of those 3, trash the planet unless you are absolutely desperate. Losing an (active!) crewmate, especially one with pilot, will not be helping you at all. This will basically be your role for a lot of the ship, so please learn it well and you will help your team well.
