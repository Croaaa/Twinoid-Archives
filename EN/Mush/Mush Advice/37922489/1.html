Antispore Gas and Constipaspore Serum is crippling. The counters to these two researches are Mycelium Spirit and Fertile. Rather frustrating that it takes so much xp and time to unlock them, but once you have them, not even the pesky research team can stop you.

Two spores a day and really heavy AP costs make it so that it takes about 2 days to convert one person, and it's a huge risk as losing 4 AP is hard to cover up. Once one Mush has M. Spirit they can once again convert one person a day, which is really useful if almost necessary if you wish to have a Mush conversion victory.
