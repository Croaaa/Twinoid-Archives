Mush have two different means of giving illnesses :

- The mush skill Bacterophile allows them to make someone ill. They have to be in the same room, they can choose their victim, and it's a secret action (cameras will reveal it). According to French wiki, the only illnesses they can give are Cold and Flu.

- Catching a spore can sometimes (but not very frequently) give you an illness. Afaik, the only possible illnesses are Cold, Mush allergy and Fungic infection (not sure of its English name). Maybe also migraine, I think I've seen it happen once. The illness is not immediate, it appears 2 to 4 cycles after catching the spore.
