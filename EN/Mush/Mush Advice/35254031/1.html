She alone holds the key to hindering the mush advance! She alone, has the difficult task of thwarting humans' advances by sporing... and staying extra-clean at the same time! (while being far from the nearest shower) She alone holds the key to the crew's health (most of the times)!
Everyone, a big hand for Dr. FINOLA KEEGAN!!! Also known (as of now) as the Dark Researcher

Starting with the common traits for every Evil Finola player:
1.- You CAN'T be dirty: a) you're supposed to lose morale if you are dirty, so it's *EXTRA* suspicious on you; b) you have the apron with you at all times, so you have *no* excuse for being dirty
2.- You are the Lead Researcher, so mush research depends (mainly) on your collaboration
3.- You are usually very trustworthy (you're the Science Lady after all!), and that trust can be useful when a mush partner is curious about where the mycoalarms might go
4.- You have the cat at hand. It's useful when a low morale Raluca is on the ship... and accidents happen. And they happen oh, so suddenly, even more on cats
5.- You have the mush sample at hand, more than anyone. My, my... imagine what would happen if it were to disappear...
6.- Oh, and lets not forget, last but not less important... sometimes you are THE doctor. Which means that brain surgery is a piece of cake for you. Unless you're in one of those 36 hours sleep...

Of course, I've only been *once* mushy Finola, so other players are most welcome to add their own tips and strategies.
For example:
- Sporing is extremely difficult for you. But that doesn't mean you can't spread around rumors about others mushiness, or hide stuff, or use up stuff (i.e.: books )
- Your partner will appreciate greatly to know that mush research has been delayed about 1 day more because Finola seems inactive. Or because she thinks it's better to research Natami Rifle instead of Mycoalarms... hey, she knows what she's doing... she's a scientist! You're not a scientist, aren't you?

P.S.: I'm not explaining skills because those are common to both good or evil Finola. So you can go search that guide
