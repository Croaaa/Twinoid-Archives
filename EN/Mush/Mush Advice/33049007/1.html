Here's a summary:
_1 O2 unit consumed per cycle, no matter how many people are alive. Fires do not burn oxygen.
_1 extra unit consumed per cycle per O2 tank damaged (so up to 3/cycle if both are damaged).
_If O2 is at zero, one random person is selected to die per cycle.
_Having an oxygen capsule on you saves your life if you are selected to die when O2 is at zero. The capsule is consumed.
