Playing with a person with the same IP is not against the official rules. However, the first time a person is detected to have the same IP as another on the same ship, they will both be tagged as multiteamsters. This prevents you from joining another ship. To remove the tag, you need to talk to the mods and explain that you are not the same person (I suppose contacting them in advance stops it from ever happening). This safeguard is in place to prevent griefers and ignorants from breaking the game.
