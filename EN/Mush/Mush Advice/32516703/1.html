Rain

It's per mush. You can directly infect one person, and your partner can directly infect one person, period. So if you have 2 mush, you can directly infect 2 people once, or one person twice, and that's it in a given day. To do more, you'll have to use mush skills that allow for indirect spore delivery, such as booby trapped rations, or once you level up a bit, trapping shelves.

Enfilade
AaeniWhat is the best skill for a starting mush level two?

Yeah, there's not a clear "must-have" skill at level 2, but I would recommend anything but Anonymush. Phagocyte gives you a way to heal and regain energy (at the cost of slower conversion); Fungal Kitchen gives you a way to convert faster than daily manual infections; and Traitor makes expeditions more dangerous. The first two can be useful no matter who you are, but only take Traitor if you're likely to go on expeditions.
