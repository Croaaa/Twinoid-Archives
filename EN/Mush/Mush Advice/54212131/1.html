Yes, it's because there is a risk of losing the entire expo team. The other zones that are generally avoided are Mankarog (because there is a high chance of someone dying) and Seismic (the chance is lower, though). Of course sometimes you don't have a choice because you really need to explore, for instance when there is oxygen on the planet and you badly need it.

Lots of people also dislike MIA zones (Forest, Cristallite, Oceanic, Cold temperature). They are almost unavoidable as it is very rare to find a planet without at least one or two of these zones, but often people tend to avoid planets that have too many of them. Getting lost and then being found dead is very frustrating, and it is even more stressful when there are several possibilities to get lost on a planet.
