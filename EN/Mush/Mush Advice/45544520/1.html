You can play together if you create your own casting and get enough members. For a ship to start you'll need 16/24/32 ready members(casting ranks).

Therefore I'd highly recommend to join an existing casting as you could have difficulties to even get the 32 necessary players and 50+ necessary casting XP for a launch.

Another possibility to get on a ship together (if you don't want to play in a casting) would to try to jump on the same random ship(for example if you have the same selection).
