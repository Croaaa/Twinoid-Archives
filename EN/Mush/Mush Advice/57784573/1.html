westas12) How does character selection work? We all know that if you don't select at once you may get other roles later. But how often does it change? Is it each cycle?

Your selection changes whenever a character wakes on the ship you're looking at or when an entire ship fills. In first case you will be aiming for the same ship with different selection, in the latter most of the time you'll be looking at some other ship.

westas13) If there is a simultaneous jump what are the chances to end up on one ship? Is it better to jump at one time or someone to go first and report what roles are missing in the ship so that others may look for them when are still in "choose a role" phase?

It's better to let 1-2 people jump and then see if your selections change whenever someone wakes on their ships. It may sometimes take a lot of time though.
