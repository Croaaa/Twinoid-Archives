Before there is any continued discussion about neutral play, let just remind you why it exists in the first place:
Before the update, it was possible for humans to research one or more researches before Mush were even selected, crippling the Mush before the game even starts. Also, this rule was made during a time when it could take a day or so to fill up a ship, and it was mainly a way to preserve fairness for a normal ship that is taking days to fill or for MMM or MMM-like ships. It was also possible for someone to hide important items just in case they became Mush. Now that both issues have been addressed by the devs, this is a rule that will most likely not affect anyone since it's now very subjective what is neutral and what isn't. Holding onto important items is still a bit of a problem since you're now forcing the human crew to either wait to get the item back when needed or kill someone for it or forcing a ship-wide search if the Mush manage to immediately hide the items they were carrying 'just in case'.
