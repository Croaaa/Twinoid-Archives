This is a translation of the French twinpedia wiki about Castings due to questions that have come recently at the forums. Mind that we do not have Castings yet, and that at the French Mush side it recently moved out of beta too.

Create a group
Costs 3 tickets. You must choose a name or accept the proposed (maximum 50 characters).
The creator is called "Creator."
The current limit is three groups joined by a player with Gold Mode and a player that has no Gold Mode is restricted to one group.

NB*: If your Gold Mode expires you cannot jump with any of the three groups unless you leave two out of three groups.

Casting and funding
Everyone can invest the number of tickets he wants in a Casting. At 50 tickets, the cast is created. Early investors are noted on page the Casting homepage.
A forum for the Mush Group is created.
Only members of the casting have access to the forum.
Each casting a fixed number that represents their group.
You can apply at the Casting group.
Application notice:
"The player wants to join your playername cast Nom_du_ cast. Go HERE for you to accept."

Forum
The Creator of the casting becomes moderator of the forum and all the members.
At present, even if the cast is disbanded, we can see the names of the groups, but the group cannot be accessed.
The Creater may appoint additional members with the rights of moderating their own group forums.

The cast is created at the 'beginner' level.
You can register on the cast or request a listing. Max 48 players at the beginner level.
The first level is 25XP.
1 ticket can be exchanged for 1XP.

Join a cast
By clicking on the casting → ​​"Apply" or "Join" - there is an option to create an automatic/manual accept of people that apply.
Atleast 32 players must set ready (cost 1 ticket!) before the ships launch.
The maximum number of players permitted in a casting is scalable depending on the level / rank of the ship.
Mush announcement:
Application: "The player wants to join your player name cast [Name of Casting]. Appointment [Link page casting ] HERE [/ link] to accept it. "
Accepted: "You have been accepted into the cast [Name of Casting]! Our most sincere congratulations!"
New member joined the casting: "Ale'rt! [Membername] joined your cast Nom_du_ cast."
Volunteer leaver: "You left the group [Name of Casting]"
Application Refusal: "You have been rejected by the casting [Name of Casting]. Your performance does not convince them... "
When the player leaves the casting, the invested tickets are not refunded. Be very mindful who you join...

Levelling up a casting
Each part of, the cast gaining experience points: 25 per vessel (depending on the level of casting ) + 1 per ticket invested.
For X experience points earned (in part and / or investment), the cast levels up. For example, from 25 to XP, you unlock "Garden Gnomes" which doubles the size of the Mush group.
Some ranks will unlock game configurations specific bonuses...

The creator of a cast is able to disband.
When the cast is disbanded, any player (Creator and stakeholders) will get their tickets refunded that they had invested in the Casting.
When the Creator of the casting decides to disband all the players part of the casting will receive a notification in their Twinoid bar Mush: "Your group [Name of Casting] was dissolved by Playername. "

Get tickets
You can buy tickets from the vending machine (I believe this was 10 Klix = 1 golden ticket.
In addition if you buy a subscription you will get a bonus amount of tickets per month, 6 monts will net you an addition of 10 golden tickets!
Surviving in an exceptional ship with grand goals of long-term survival will reward you with blue or golden tickets!
*Note: Blue tickets are limited up to a certain number, Gold time users will have an increased pool of the amount of blue tickets they can have at any time. Golden tickets do not count towards this limit.

Rights of Members
All members can invest in the casting.
Ready: You will put in a ticket to be ready - if enough members are ready your private ship will start! (You must not be in an active ship for this to happen)
The Creater and it's appointed Managers can rename forum ranks and roles and also change associated rights. They are also the ones who can start parties earlier than the appointed time if all members area ready and not in an active ship.
