Click on your character in the room to see your skills. To pick a skill, you need to click the icon at the bottom of the set of Mush skills. You also have to have "Advanced Mush" turned on, so your human skills won't be automatically picked up once you become Mush. To switch the option go My Account -> My Settings and tick the proper box.
