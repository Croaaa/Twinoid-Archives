The skill is supposed to be a covert action.
Covert action logs are revealed to all if committed in the presence of an installed camera.
And since camera's can't detect this skill, nothing will be saved in the room logs for others.
However, the Observant skill has a small chance (25% i think) to reveal the room log if the one who has it is in the same room as the one who commits a [i]covert[i/] action.

So it is possible to dismantle a camera, spike someone (as long as the only ones in the room are yourself and the target) then re-install the camera if the target is afk or before the target realizes.
