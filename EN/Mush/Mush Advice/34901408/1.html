They start at 14 (or 16 in gold mode), like the mush, and with 7 (8 in gold mode). They get 3 when the connection to Sol is established for the first time, 2 for each speech made by Jin Su, 3 if they cuddle the cat, 1 if they're sleeping in the same room as Janice at cycle change. And they lose 1 at day change (2 if Chun is dead or doesn't have Mankind's Only Hope), 1 for each death, and 1 if they eat an uncooked ration.

This should give you a good approximation of what your morale would be if you were human, depending on what you've done so far.
