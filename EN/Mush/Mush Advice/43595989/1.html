[6826491]

People can enter IB while an expo is in progress, even if this puts it above the normal limit (4 without the Large Bay Upgrade, and 6 with it.) Once the Icarus returns, the room will be inaccessible to any new people until the number of people in the room drops to below normal capacity.

If another expo is launched while IB is above normal capacity, then people are randomly chosen from those present. This could be problematic, if they're injured, or don't have the required equipment (spacesuits, for example!)

That, I can answer. You can launch regardless, but anyone without a spacesuit will stay in the Icarus during the expo. You probably know that much already.

If nobody has a spacesuit when you go to a planet without O2, the entire expo team will safely return to IB after landing.

I actually had this happen on an Eden ship of mine. In this case, it potentially saved the whole expo team, and our chances for Eden.
