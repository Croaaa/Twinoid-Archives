This might've been better directed at Q&A Thread. Use that thread in the future, please.

It takes 24 cycles for someone with 0 satisfaction to begin starving then you start to take 1 hp damage per turn. Tutorial newbies will start starving which is likely why your Terrence died earlier than the others.

Most of the time inactives will be silver or bronze making them have a max of 14 health. So it'd be about 38 cycles (from the moment they awoke) for them to starve to death, maybe a bit earlier if they take room damage. Tutorial newbies would die in 14 cycles if they failed to eat.

Also it's a good idea not to mention any specifics about ongoing ships in the forums. Every ship with a starved Terrence is probably glancing around right now.

An inactive starts starving when he hits 0 satisfaction (which is 24 cycles from the cycle they woke up if they didn't eat; each time they ate, the counter has reset and satisfaction becomes 24+satisfaction points from food consumed). He starts losing 1 health on the cyclechange after starvation started (starvation happens on c4 => -1hp on c4 to c5). Every daychange restores 1 hp. The starting hp is 14 for bronze or silver and 16 for gold. The end.
