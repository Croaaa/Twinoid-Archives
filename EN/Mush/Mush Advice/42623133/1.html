Irishpolak

As a bronze Derek, it's a good idea to keep skills unselected until you see how the ship runs.

  • Shooter is nicer for the +1 expo strength without a diplomat in combat zones, plus shooter points are amazing if a pilot book is around and the crew trusts you. Also, having free shooter points are nice to shoot at mush Ian or a mush hiding behind Ian.
  • Wrestler is nice to be able to shove people out of rooms, and is amazing for doing damage (just make sure to drop the knife first, you are more powerful without it). Good for killing someone you know is mush, and definitely a great choice if mush and forced into a rampage.
  • Firefighter is a good backup to keep in mind in case mush are dealt with too quickly or flee with the extinguishers. 100% chance to extinguish a fire with an extinguisher and the ability to try and extinguish without one makes this the best choice for a long-lasting ship if no pilot books are available, but really only comes into effect post-Day 10 or if extinguishers are gone.
  • Motivator is decent for giving people MP, but as bronze really isn't something you probably don't want to bother with.
  • Hygenist is a great skill for human or mush, but a bit hard to obtain due to XP cost. (1 month of gold or 2 months bronze/silver). Makes mush less likely to target you for transfer, makes you sick less often, and as mush gives you a valid reason to not get ill if you join in on an expo. Only problem is you lose damage bonus of wrestler, expo strength of shooter, and fire fighting abilities of firefighter.

ONE REALLY IMPORTANT THING TO KEEP IN MIND: Keep AP as low as possible in case mush target you for transfer, and try to avoid taking beds if at all possible. You may not have a bonus but relaying research or NERON projects is always a good idea.

One other thing: You make a good candidate to do some mush hunting yourself, as your AP isn't as valuable as some others. You can double check room logs behind Eleesha, just make sure to avoid the danger rooms as you are still open to investigation yourself. A good idea to keep in mind is maybe grab and hold a ration, as it's free AP as human if you need it.

Great guide already from Irishpolak and he is totally right with waiting until picking the skill. That is especially important for characters who have skills which are useful only in certain circumstances.
Since Derek's job is often to kill the msuhes you will mostly have to choose a skill to kill them when they are found out and you are active. And the best skill to kill mushes is wrestler (3-5 damage with every successfull hit) unless the mush is Ian, then it would be more wise to choose shooter for killing purposes.
But it can also happen that mushes stealed the extinguishers, then firefighter would be almost the only hope for humans to do something against fires.
Sometimes it can happen that the RNG is giving you items which are really helping you if you chose the shooter skill.
For example it can happen that you have a ship with sniper helmet blueprint, researched the lenses and your crew found an invertebrate shell. Then derek can always be a big help to kill hunters only with his shooter skill and these items. Especially to kill only some aracks before moving of the ship. Or your ship got a pilot mage book which would enable Derek to be a great pilot.
But you can't know in the beginning what that will happen. That is what makes it actually interesting to play Derek and should encourage more good players to choose him
