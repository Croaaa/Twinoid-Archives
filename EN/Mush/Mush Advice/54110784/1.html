As everyone said, suicide is forbidden by the SDF rules and can be reported, for several reasons.

If it was allowed, people would kill themselves as soon as they "don't like their ships" for any kind of reason : because they're mush and wanted to be human or the reverse, because they don't get along with another crewmember, because they think that their crew don't have the right skills, because suddenly they think "oh, in fact I don't like this character"... Back when it was allowed, players kept suiciding for the silliest reasons.

There's also the fact that some players consider that a ship is doomed and want to leave it rapidly. But then again, some people consider their ship "doomed" if it is not perfect (16 active players with all the right skills, Mush who get nicely killed or cured without causing too much damage...). If there is the slightest problem, then they decide that the ship is not worth it. Sometimes they even decide it from the very beginning.

It is not all right because almost any ship, as long as the crew is willing to give it a try, is worth it and can succeed. There's nothing that kills a ship better than a crew giving up, while a crew that refuses to give up can survive and even have more fun than on "perfect ships", because there is a higher challenge and it's more rewarding.
