On november 12, 2013
Enfilade

Usually the antispore gas is a good start - it cuts spore production in half, which makes it harder for the mush to spread quickly. After that, you've got lots of good choices: mycolarms can help protect against manual infections; the constipaspore research makes it more costly for mush to extract spores; and the mushavore vaccine makes conversions harder.
