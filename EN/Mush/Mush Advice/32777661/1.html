So you're playing mush and you suddenly see this dapper fellow:

Congratulations! You get to play as Lai Kuan-Ti, literally the best character in this game and possibly any other game in the world. Before you can play as Kuan-Ti, however, you have to get into the Mindset of Kuan Ti

1.) GETTING STARTED AS KUAN-TI

Kuan-Ti is by far the most important character in this game and/or any other game. If you aren't Kuan-Ti right now, you might as well try to kill yourself, as nothing will ever compare to being Kuan-Ti.

Kuan-Ti has access to the following skills:
Designer- Your first skill and one of the best, Kuan-Ti gets bonuses to making certain NERON projects as well as having two free points a day. A pretty good skill to use.
The Optimist- The second skill loses one less morale point per day. A decent skill, if a bit underwhelming. Great for not worrying about morale though!
Astrophysicist- Kuan's third skill gives him two properties per planet scan instead of one. Great skill and completely/utterly replaces the need for Frieda, might as well shoot her now. No seriously. Go grab a blaster and kill her.
Technician- Fourth skill is one of my favorites. It gives you FURTHER bonuses to developing NERON projects as well as overall a ton of additional points to repairing stuff. Now you can fix your ship while you fly it!
Leader- Kuan's fifth skill is leader, which gives EVERYONE a morale boost with a charming speech. This skill should be innately in Kuan's arsenal but I'unno. Guess he'd be TOO broken.
Being Kuan-Ti- The sixth skill not in the game yet, Kuan-Ti automatically is the #1 player in the game and will get glory for doing anything. Sneeze too hard? KUAN-TI POINTS! Take a shower? KUAN-TI POINTS! Being Kuan-Ti? DOUBLE POINTS!

In addition to these skills, Kuan-Ti gets glory for every NERON project completed.
_____
Here's a Kuan-Ti(p)! Try to communicate with the crew before selecting skills! This will maximize your potential!

Now that you have all your skills figured out, let's move on to the RP aspect of Kuan-Ti

2.) DAPPER AS F__K, BEING KUAN-TI ON A ROLE PLAYING SHIP

First of all, Kuan-Ti is the creator of the Daedalus and, as such, should make sure everyone is properly admiring your ship. You're intelligent, dapper, and you are always in a "Captain Morgen" pose when the situation allows.

However, Kuan-Ti is not without human flaws. Because of how dapper and amazing he is, he did not notice the Mush menace until it was too late. A sad side effect of being so spectacular. Further more, Kuan-Ti might just be TOO perfect, meaning that he has to associate with beings far under him.

If you must associate with people, try to hang out with Kim Jin-Su or Raluca, as these are people you've met and collaberated with before. Not to say you can't befriend ANYONE, but these are the people you know best. You are the Magnificent Kuan-Ti after all!

_____
Kuan-Ti(p) time! If you're roleplaying as Kuan-Ti, you are one step closer to actually BEING Kuan-Ti! Go you, you dapper space king you!

3.) PLAYING ON YOUR BEAUTIFUL SHIP

As you are playing a game, you will likely have things to do and places to go. Here are some tips that apply, though keep in mind these also apply to EVERYONE as well!

1.) Focus AP on what you're good at. If you can toss some points to better creating a NERON project or scanning planets, go for it! Kuan-Ti's skills in repair, NERON, being dapper, Astrophysics and being even more dapper go hand in hand with this!

2.) Save major projects until they're needed. For example, don't rush to communicate with sol until everyone is awake or risk losing a morale bonus for the entire crew!
Try to communicate with your crew about what you should wait. You -are- Kuan-Ti, you know best for everyone!

3.) Try to establish an early network of friends or people you can trust. Zhong Chun can ALWAYS be trusted so it's recommended to start with her, as she is immune to the Mush menace.
Because you're Kuan-Ti, try to make a romantic relationship with her! Though it will break everyone elses heart, you deserve the best. As you ARE Kuan-Ti!

4.) Sort your items so they're in easy-to-reach places and collaborate to make an item list for everyone! It'll also be easier to see who's using what!
Kuan-Ti should have first pick of all items ever. EVER.

5.) Save your AP and MP until you're ready to act. You can do a lot more with 3 MP and 3 AP than you can by spending 1 AP per cycle!
Seriously though, Kuan-Ti should have infinite movement points. He also deserves a motorcycle or a convertable.

_____
More Kuan-Ti(p)s for you! Try to eat/drink/sample everything you can, as you must keep your energy up and if you die, it's game over for everyone. Seriously, if Kuan-Ti dies you might as well surrender.

4.) WHAT IF I AM A MUSH?

In the two several hundred times I've played Kuan-Ti, I have never gotten to be Mush. I must conclude that Kuan-Ti is above being a mush and will never ever betray you. Period.

_____
Kuan-Ti(p) for you all: Although you might be above being a mush, you're never above murder! Mush organs strewn all over the deck makes for Daedalus war paint!

5.) CLOSING COMMENTS

Kuan-Ti is amazing. I mean really. He has a cape and a sick hair style. If you're not Kuan-Ti, you're missing out.

Please don't take this "guide" all that seriously. Mostly it's general tips about playing Kuan-Ti and overall everything I said should be taken with a grain of salt!
