5 or 6 diseases are worth a full bar of XP, which in turn is:
- approximately the amount of AP needed to kill someone
- enough to convert someone
Also the illnesses won't start immediately, but ~4 cycles later. Look them up! The illnesses are all pretty much unusable and more of a nuisance. Disorders are a bit more annoying, because of the difficulty to remove them(Shrink or special pill/fruit needed, takes several cycles/applications). Injuries however are pretty nasty and can really maim someone: Restricted (or even no) movement, incredibly expensive actions and HP drain are likely to make that human useless for quite some time. But if you can inflict those injuries sou might as well go for the kill anyway...
