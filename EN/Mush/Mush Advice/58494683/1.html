Do you love Mush? Do you have the time and desire to help the community? We're looking for a few good people!

If you're interested in becoming an SDF Judge, please send a message to the Mush Mods Box explaining why you'd be a good choice. Candidates may be sent a link to an application with scenarios and/or actual logs from previous ships to judge and explain their rationale. There is no definitively correct answer for judgements, we're looking mostly for the rationale behind your judgement with this application.

Here's what we're looking for:
- Someone that will stick around and continue to judge reports.
- Critical thinking skills
- Familiarity with Twinoid T&C's and Mush rules.
- Relatively clean SDF history

"Do I need to log in every day?"
Not if you aren't already. If you even check once a day for reports and take care of them when you see any, that's plenty. We'd even be happy if you did it every other day or so.

"How many hours per day will I have to spend?"
It really depends on the reports. If you're looking at them often and judging them as you see them, average is maybe 5-10 min per report. Some are super easy and some are more convoluted, but that's a good ballpark estimate.

"Do I work individually or do I have to be here at the same time as other judges?"
There is no need to schedule time to be online so that you can collaborate with other people. In fact, it's better when you don't! We certainly have a forum for discussing some of the more convoluted issues, but it's better to judge reports without the influence of other judges.
