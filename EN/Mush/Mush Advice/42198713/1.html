If someone randomly picks you for a 'test or die' situation, then you're a random kill if they kill you for that reason alone. Refusal to take a food test is not acceptable evidence that you are mush to SDF judgment. I recommend not being a jerk about it if someone challenges you to take one or else, but letting them know it's against the rules to kill you because you refuse if that is the only evidence against you, or if there is otherwise weak evidence that you are mush is totally appropriate, heck, link to this post in your ship's chat or the PM if you want.

it's only acceptable to 'force' mush testing when you already have enough evidence that they are mush that you don't 'need' to test, that a kill of them without the test is not going to get you sanctioned if you were reported for 'randomly killing' that person. People have been sanctioned for trying to forcibly test others without reasonable evidence. Exactly what reasonable evidence is, that is a judgment call, so I cannot give you blanket 'fits every case' rules. However, forcible mush testing is rarely a 'safe' choice to avoid sanction.

Never try to die though. Remember your human team has many humans on it, and even if a few of them are trying to force testing, report them and keep playing to win. Talk it out, but remember your team needs you, and its a waste of your team's resources to kill you. Don't encourage them to do that, don't try to get killed (suicide is sanctionable).
