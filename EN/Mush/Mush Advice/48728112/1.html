In "My account" you have "My settings" part. Under that, in "My configuration" part of the site, you'll have a box called "Advanced Mush". As long as it stays unchecked, game will randomly choose your human skills whenever you end up becoming mush and didn't pick them up yourself before. if it's checked, you can pick them up manually even after becoming mush.
The problem is it's unchecked in default. This is a big problem for most new players/players who are not mush very often and don't have much experience with that. I'm not sure why devs even put something like that into the game, especially that part about being unchecked on default.
Anyway, keep it checked in the future, so it won't happen again.

It's a fairly common mistake, actually. Don't be embarrassed it happened. I remember a ship I was on when it happened to a Gold player, and believe me, that was embarrassing for him.

To fix it, go to "My Account", "My Settings", and then tick the "Activate Advanced Mush."

It is rather discouraging, but it's in the game to ensure players get skills when they become Mush. As I hope you've noticed, the skill slots are replaced by Mush skill slots, making it impossible to choose human skills. Activating Advanced Mush puts a little symbol under the Mush skills, where you can choose human skills.
