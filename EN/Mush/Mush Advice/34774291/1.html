Pyrrhus

A 'title' is a Twinoid achievement-esque feature that is given when something happens to you of some interest. You get one for consuming coffee for instance, and you can use them to construct titles for your profile. For instance, mine is Protector of the COFFEE! which you can see underneath my name on this post.

It DOES mean you were spored, but not necessarily at that time; titles are handed out at odd times and not always at the same time as something happens or even during the duration of the game in which that happened.

However, it is in your human player interest to get it checked out if you have the mycoscanner research on the ship yet, though that's the case in general!
