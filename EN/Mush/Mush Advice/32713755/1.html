First you need scrap metal. This can be gotten by dismantling certain items or some NERON projects, but the most common way to get it is for pilots to take the Pasiphae in alpha bay 2 and collect scrap after hunters are shot down. Once you have scrap metal, select a piece and you have the option to reinforce the hull. Anyone can do it, and the wrench will increase your chances slightly.
