It's a level 6 mush skill. First you must choose a human who has already been infected with a spore (it doesn't work on other mush or on Chun). You must been in the same room to do the transfer. It doesn't cost ap, except if Ian is in the room, and it is totally invisible (cameras and other people can't see it).

Transfert exchanges everything. You will have the other person's ap, mp, health (hp and even morale, which means that if they had 0 morale points before the swap, you can die of depression, even if you're mush) and glory. You will have their inventory and they will have yours, and private conversations are also exchanged.

Of course you must be very careful when you use it, because if your victim is active, the first thing they will do is to warn everyone. You must make sure they can't do that, either by dropping your talkie far away from the room where you do the swap, or by killing them. It's a good idea to do it when people are trying to suspect you, it will give you a reason to kill your old body (make sure you are injured before the swap, so that killing your old body will be easy). Or you can transfer on an inactive, but it can be obvious if someone idle starts to act and talk.

What happens with characters' skills after transfer? E.g. if human character had lvl4 skills, while Mush player hadn't levelled respective character past level 2, will he have only level 2 skills in a new body?
On january 6, 2014
[7898260]

Yes.

Once in a new body, you mush skill will not change. But you'll be able to choose your human skills via a special tab below your regular mush skill tab.

Be wise what human skills you choose though. Only skills available for character you leveled up will be available. That means if you transferred into Kim, and your Kim Jin Su character is level 2, only first 2 human level skills will be available for choosing. So be sure to check your targets specific skills, or others can trace your conversion.
