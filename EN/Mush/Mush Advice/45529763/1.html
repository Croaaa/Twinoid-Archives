Kyubey

After you have sent a report, and your game has ended, it will become visible to SDF judges.

Note 1: Only the game has ended, we don't anything to rule breaker during the game he breaks anything. It's because a lot of stuff can be judged only having full picture
Note 2: If nobody has sent a report, we will not be able to check the ship and to choose any punishment. So - if you see somebody who should be reported - report him. And don't worry about somebody else already said he reported him - first he could just say that, without sending a report, second - when 3-4 people report one person with good explanation, it's much easier to judge.

SDF judges will see your whole ships - all actions of every character, room logs, mush and Private channels. Having that information a few judges will say - if there was a rule breaking, and how bad it was. Then the system will give that person some number of SDF-points based on judges votes. Reaching certain numbers of point lead to warning and bans.

Don't worry to send reports when you are not sure if there was any rule breaking. Like reporting somebody who killed 3 humans for no reason and happen to be human when he died. Maybe it was a mush, who used transfer in right moment, and if you report him as griefer, and we see that situation, we'll just clear the report.
