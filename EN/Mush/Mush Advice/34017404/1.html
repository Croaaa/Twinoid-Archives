spacekadt

Once that research is completed, humans will be able to see the same information on the Mush channel that Mush do... which is the last 24 hours.

Technically, the mush could avoid using that and only use pm's, but the mush channel also automatically announces any time someone gets a spore. (something along the lines of "Frieda has been infected by Jin Su. Frieda's infection level is now 1."

However, unlike all other means of communication, the mush channel is the one thing that you don't need a walkie talkie for (there are a few ways that people can communicate, but it's either mid/late game or occasional).

It's not the *most* helpful piece of research, but if you can get it done without the mush knowing, you might be able to catch them
