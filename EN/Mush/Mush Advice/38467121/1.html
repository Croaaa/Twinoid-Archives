[1820396]

Antenna - Interferes with communication. Actions on Comms Terminal cost 3 APs instead of usual 2.
Combustion Chamber - You can't load or unload a fuel.
Emergency Reactor - Daedalus can't move (still can jump back to Sol or to Eden, if Pilgred is repaired).
Lateral Reactor - Daedalus can't change direction.
Planet Scanner - The chance of succesful scan for planet is only 50% instead of usual 80%.

There is also Pilgred Reactor Terminal, which can't be broken and, if you have a project for it, Auxiliary Neron Terminal which broken makes the Neron not accessible from engine room.
