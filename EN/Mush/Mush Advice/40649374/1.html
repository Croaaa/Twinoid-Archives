There are several pages on the Mushpedia wiki that might help you out. Check out the individual characters for recommendations on skills for each pay-level (bronze/silver/gold). This page is still in progress, but it will give you an idea of what is expected of you for which skills you take.

Kuan Ti is a great beginner character - his first skill gives you free points to spend helping on projects and keeps you close on those, learning which projects are considered better than others. It also teaches you to obsess about keeping clean, because as Kuan Ti you have very little reason to get dirty and it blocks you from doing your job.

Paola and Frieda have a little more difficulty, as their jobs are complex, but actually carrying them out is pretty easy. Stay clean, stay on the bridge (unless you are Frieda without a Logistics Paola buddy), and keep the crew posted on your progress. You can use the wiki or a newbie advice thread to figure out what is important.

If you're super busy in real life, consider Frieda, Hua, or lvl2 Roland - they have jobs that are easy to do, but require a lot of AP, so you can check in a few times a day and still be effective (I would check in at least once a cycle just to know what's going on, but it isn't necessary.)

In short, you can be effective with any character. The key (I'm going to quote Spazticus here) is COMMUNICATION! That is how you'll know if you're doing well or if you're making mistakes, and how you'll learn what things are supposed to be like.

General picks for bronze players on skills-
Jin Su- Leader (possibly Pilot, depending on your ship)
Stephen- Apprentice (this lets you copy somebody else's skill, filling in for inactives or somebody who is about to die.) or Solid (if you aren't high enough level)
Gioele- Paranoid (extra cameras), but Solid is just as good.
Frieda- Astrophysicist
Paola- Logistics Expert (basically a free bed for Frieda)
Hua- Pilot
Roland- Pilot
Ian- Botanist
Janice- Shrink (SUPER IMPORTANT)
Eleesha- Tracker (without it you can't print the List.)
Kuan Ti- Technician (Designer is okay if you aren't high enough level)
Raluca- Technician (Physicist is okay if you aren't high enough level)
Terrence- Technician. If there are enough other techs, you can choose Robotics Expert or Pilot as needed.
Chun- Mankind's Only Hope (if you take anything else you will be killed for griefing, even if that wasn't your intent)
Andie- Polyvalent (acts as diplomat/botanist/biologist all at once. It's level 3, so save up points before playing Andie- the research team is really crippled without it)
Derek- Wrestler. Shooter is also good, but Wrestler doesn't require a blaster and lets you move people out of a room)

Finola- Medic (same bonus as biologist to research, but now you can do surgeries)
Chao- idek for Chao, I never got to play him. Wrestler/Shooter seem a good choice, like Derek.

From the easiest characters to play
1. Raluca - just relaying PILGRED and stay in one of rear turrets, that's all
2. Kuan Ti - look at Project which is the most voted and take care of this
3. Andie - (but you should put level 3), carry on research and do it as fast as you can
4. Ian - take care of plants, plant fruit if possible, be active on cycle 8 every day and don't let them dry out, but you must know, that young plants aren't required to be treated and watered (if they don't have status Dried out)
5. Paola - remember, lie down on sofa at the Bridge if built, upgrade NERON 2 times, decode rebel base which appear on day 3, 5, 6, 7, and then Xyloph. About traders, ask the crew if it's needed.
6. Hua - shoot hunters, later just aracks and traxes. Do it just if you have full PA.
7. Roland - (level 2 required) such as Hua
8. Janice - ask the crew for morale, access the Bios Terminal in the Nexus, foodstuff destruction must be off, and CPU Priority at Astronavigation (unless the crew wishes differently), free PA -> help with projects and research
9. Stephen - assist the crew: building the Sofa research or projects, maybe PILGRED
10. Terrence - (level 2 better) - repair what's broken, dismantle shower if it isn't dismantled (in Alpha Dorm better), but don't move between rooms too much
11. Gioele - (level 2 better) such as Stephen, with 2nd level put the cameras where they are needed, but be careful, because if Jin Su dies or goes inactive, you should be more active
12. Derek - kill enemies (so Mush if you're human), listen to Chun's orders to murder somebody, free PA -> assist
13. Frieda - go scanning, find oxygen planet at first, and then some planets with food, artefacts, fruit and fuel
14. Chun - stay in the Lab, hear the crew and find the Mush, hide important items (grenade, knife, hacker kit), hold plastenite armor and remember: everybody who attempts to kill you is Mush
15. Jin Su - (high activity required) jump the ship to avoid hunters, issue missions to get PA for one of pilots, Paola or Mush-partner
16. Eleesha - go around the ship and tell Chun all your suspects, find the Mush
