Get it here! - v2.10 has GUI additions for less tab-clicking!

Hello, ladies and gentlemen of MUSH. I was lucky enough to get on board of a MMM ship, which had many very active crew members. One of them, now passed away Ian, took upon himself the role of expedition organizer. He took the planets, researched the zones, told us what we should take with us, what we could find on the planet. I was interested in his work, I wanted to help him make it easier...

Thus, I present to you the result of many hours of work in Excel. This sheet will take in the zones on a given planet, the items that a crew member will take to the Expo and will calculate the following information:

  • Which gains are possible on this planet?
  • Which risks are involved in this expedition?
  • What items/skills are recommended?
  • What will this crew have in terms of strength and bonus gains?

Moreso, it will comprise a formated post for you to copy-paste into the ship's forum for people to see, listing the chosen crew, listing the gains in short.

PLEASE, download, check it out, tell me what you think? What changes would you like to see? Anyone interested in turning this into a web-based calculator? Share it with your friends! Comments and suggestions always welcome.

A few things I've noticed while using this.

1) Ropes don't cancel ALL accidents. Only mountains, caverns and seismic are cancelled by ropes. All other accident events are possible to take damage.

2) Though rare, it's worth having a pilot skill on the list, I think. Lack of a pilot opens up more chances to take damage and if that's overlooked on a crew that doesn't have any pilots left, it could lead to possible death if someone's just barely healed enough to guarantee not dying otherwise.

That said, now that I've finally found and started using this tool, it's pretty damned awesome for figuring out how many we need and what we need to bring on expo, thank you!
