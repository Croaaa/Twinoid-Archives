Dear NERON Admin:

Congratulations! You have been entrusted with the maintenance and care of the most advanced AI known to mush...mankind. It is a large responsibility that, when performed diligently, can make life aboard the Daedalus much easier.

Regarding the DMZ PeaceCore setting, it determines the acceptable level of violence aboard the ship. When set to On, all grenades aboard the ship will be unable to be used. When set to Off, explosives may be primed and detonated by crew. We do not recommend changing this setting unless circumstances are dire and all grenades are with trustworthy people.

As for general suggestions, most of your interaction with the BIOS will deal with setting the Priority (which makes one particular type of activity more effective - either research, projects, or scanning) and the Crew Lock (which restricts one type of activity to only certified people).

It is recommended to consult with your crewmembers as to what settings would be most helpful given your circumstances; for example, scanning priority is best when you are really low on oxygen and need to find a suitable planet ASAP. The crew lock should usually be set to Piloting so that anyone can help advance the projects and research; however, should all pilots be unavailable or dead, you may need to allow nonpilots to fly craft.

We hope this crash course has given you the advice you need to begin your new career. There's more to learn, but these tips will cover the pressing items.

Regards,
Xyloph Research Team
