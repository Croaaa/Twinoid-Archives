So... from previous Mush experiences, of which I have had two, I have come up with a suicidal but possible effective tactic.... perhaps.

This effectively guarantees that you will die. Use with discretion.

Best situation to use in: You're suspected of being Mush, low HP (don't use if HP is at 3!), or want to cause EXTREME havoc. Even better on Cycle 8, where you could get an additional recharge on your blaster and extract four MORE spores!

This requires the following procedure:

1) Become Chao and select Shooter. Get full AP bar. If you become Mush, then...
2) Pick up the Grenade, Blaster, and Knife. Select Phagocyte as your ability.
3) Extract two spores wherever you want (this is essentially a suicide run), although preferably out-of-sight/when players are not online at the moment.
4) Toss the Grenade in a filled room, then use free blaster actions to shoot the poor weaklings. Use the knife when both the grenade and blaster charges are all used up.
5) Use Phagocyte both times (you have two spores, remember!), extract two MORE spores, and Phagocyte two MORE times and finish off whoever remains.

This tactic is less effective completely useless if:
-Ian is in the room, thus reducing your attack count.
-Someone in the room is currently ONLINE.
-Your fellow Mush have spored, thus giving you less spores to use with Phagocyte.
-RNG screws you over.

Effective against: Janice, Jin Su (so you become Commander! If you survive...), and Chun.
Guaranteed number of attacks if all goes well: 1 (Grenade) + 3 (Blaster) + 12 (Knife, AP Bar) + 8 (Knife, Phagocyte) = 24 attacks!
In a perfect situation (Cycle 8, Day X to Cycle 1, Day X+1), save one AP for extracting spores when the next day begins, thus allowing 23 + 1 (Blaster Charge) + 8 (Phagocyte) = 32 attacks in two cycles!

Suggestions? Improvements?
