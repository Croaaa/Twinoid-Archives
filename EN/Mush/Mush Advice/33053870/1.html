Annette

We're still figuring this system out. So, these players are tagged as muilties on this ship, they are permanently tagged "multi" and will not be able to play the next ship. They will have to message us mods. We explain that you cannot play more than one account and if it's a situation like with Rain and KC (who I know personally and know they are indeed two people in the same household) I mark their accounts "okay to play". Mostly people are saying, "I have more than one account, what, this isn't allowed?" I ask them to delete the 2nd or ahem 3rd account and when they are gone, to PM me again. I will then take the multi tag off their account and they can play again.

So, mulites on a ship, we can't do anything about them because as mods we have no mechanism to check their IPs or ban them for this tag so it's not worth reporting. That tag shows up automatically, we can only take it off the player, not add it. The next time this person tries to log into the game though, they will be told to PM the moderators and have their mulit status resolved. Either way, they will not be allowed to play with multies even if you aren't reporting them. I hope this makes sense, it's taken us a bit to figure out how it all works.
