Here's a neat secret hint to do if you don't like the roles availables to play: DON'T PLAY IT
Seriously, just keep refreshing the screen and wait till it pops up someone you like to play. It might take a while, yes, but if you're going to play someone you don't like and just go AFK for the whole trip so you can die and play another.... well, you'll be ruining the game for the other 15 players.
