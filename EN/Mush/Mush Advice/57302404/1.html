Aceb1) Do not panic.

This is the by far the most important point.
2) Wait for opportunities.I'd even say search for opportunities. Gathering information can be the key to victory: Starting spores, skills, activity,...
3) Rampage only as last resort [many players just rampage or do something close to it so often...]I wouldn't discourage rampages. Some ships are better dealt with a well-timed rampage. Especially for rather low level Bronze(and Silver) Mush a rampage can be more promising and easier to execute than the conversion game. Phagocyte and Transfer are two of the strongest skills and unlike most skills for the conversion game available pretty soon.
Just because some people frown upon rampages doesn't mean they're bad. No Techs and/or Pilots leaves the human team usually in a bad position where the loss(explosion of the ship) can be inevitable.
Takes some experience to assess the situation right. Rampages can do little to no damage or utterly destroy a ship. Obviously you shouldn't rampage if the former is the case.
