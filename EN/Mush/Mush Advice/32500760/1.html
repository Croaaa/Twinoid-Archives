When I was commander as Gioele, I could *usually* give myself a mission. The option was there sometimes and not others. Didn't figure out a pattern. Dunno if that's different for Jin Su or not. Will say though that giving someone else a mission is a great way to give other players direction without being overbearing about it - say, giving your pilot a mission to go kill hunters functions as a request and a 3 ap boost.

Basically look for what's not being done, hunters is a good default, research is a good one if hunters are actually being taken care of. Mush killing is another good one if that comes up. You'll get a better feel for what exactly your ship needs over time, but as long as you're giving the missions to active players who you're fairly certain are on the same team as you (mush or human), it's never a waste.
