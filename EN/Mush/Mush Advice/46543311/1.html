You can make a password, and tell it to any other player.
- You can use it to prove there was a transfer, saying your password from new body. But it's quite unnecessary, as humans will be able to see a transfer as skill reset.
- The main use of password - mush transfer into you, kill you in your new body for any reason, your comrades ask mush in your body a password, he fails to say it and get revealed.
- But if you are turned into mush, you play on mush side now. You have all information you had as human and should use it to win as mush.
