Overall - If you identify a mush, your options are really quite clear:

1. Do you have a dead mush already
No - Kill them.
Yes - See 2
2. Is the serum a possibility?
No - Kill them
Yes - See 3
3. Can you get the serum out before they rampage?
No - Kill them
Yes - Cure them See 4.
4. Did it work?
No - Be glad you didn't fuck up.
Yes - Try and get them to sell out any betas they have. If so, see 5
5. Is their beta Chun?
No - Kill the beta
Yes - Kill the ex-mush griefer.

An alternative to murder is getting someone else to murder for you, but if you can't convince anyone else... your AP is as good as any other for the ultimate confirmation of mush/humanity.
