It costs 2 AP each time you try to establish connection with a Rebel Base.

The base rate is around 20% each time, so you'll need to spend 10 AP, or 8 AP if lucky, for each Rebel Base.

Paola's last skill can double this rate.

One of Xyloph DB entries can also double this rate, and I believe it stacks with Paola's.
