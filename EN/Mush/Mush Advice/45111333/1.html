I'll add that using external communication to get around Torn Tongue, a similar injury, or the loss of their WT/iTrakie was ruled to be highly discouraged early on by the SDF. It just hasn't come up much in recent months, but the ruling definitely exists. That definitely counts as using outside communication to circumvent the in game systems. Injuries are less of an excuse in particular this season, given that most ships will have access to a Medic.

One of the reasons we encourage the use of on-ship communication is due to the nature of reports, if one is filed. If your Mush hunt channel only exists on IRC, for example, and the reasons for killing someone aren't discussed somewhere in the ship's logs, it could be reasoned that you may not have had enough evidence to make that kill. Since we can only reasonably review the communication and actions logged by the game, anything you want filed as "evidence" in your favor (in case you are reported) better be found in those logs.

I've left logs for the judges in a PM to myself, whenever I'm about to do something that could be potentially considered playing against the team. On a recent ship, I was Mush, and I killed my partner in order to keep my cover. He'd dropped his WT and spored literally minutes after selection, and was caught. I didn't even have the chance to PM him to work out a strategy, and I rarely ever use the Mush channel. I knew I might get reported for being the one to kill him, so around the time I attacked him, I left a PM with my motive for doing so...just in case. The Mush team won in the end, even though I didn't live to see it.
