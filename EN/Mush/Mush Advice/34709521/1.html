Usually you start getting fires around Day 3-4. It's easy enough to keep track of where the extinguishers will be, with an active crew (usually in Rear Corridor, and the other in Front or Central Corridor.) Then it's just a matter of having the AP to make your move, and preferably before Magnetic Net shows up. If Hunters or even an asteroid are outside, you can get damaged by collecting scrap, until the Pasiphae goes boom. Without the Pasiphae, they can't go out to collect the extinguishers from the area...

Granted, this one is a really cheap tactic, and all too easy to pull off successfully. So try this one instead:

Alternatively, if you manage to get to the location of an extinguisher before anyone else enters that room (and Mush have already been chosen...) Don't pick it up, just hide it, and at least one other item if possible. Most people don't think to search a room more than once, if they find something the first time. If they only know the location of one extinguisher, that will cause extra AP to be spent later. As long as Eleesha doesn't have her third skill, and the crew didn't go all out in searching the ship initially, you'll have a decent shot at pulling this one off.

On one of my ships, I was a beta Mush, and managed to get into one of the storage rooms before anyone else had entered it. I hid four items, including a blaster, and then reported the items I didn't hide, in the Inventory thread. That blaster came in handy for the killing spree later on.

EDIT: Regarding Traitor, just make sure you're always the one to hold the Compass and Rope, so you don't get lost, and your potential damage from certain zones will be nullified. You can always justify it by saying that if the Pilot is lost, they'll will have a tougher time organizing a rescue party, but you would be able to launch a second expo if it were needed.

Hua's one of the hardest as mush IMO. Especially bronze Hua. No choice but flying ships. If you're active around the ship and there's hunters out there, you'll be suspected (even if you're not mush). You still have to kill all the hunters and get enough rest for the next wave.

There's not a lot of chances to get dirty and infect because a good Eleesha will make sure you're only around in the middle of the ship (bays, dorms, refectory, CC, RC). If you're the only pilot on the ship, ask permission for rations in order to get dirty. If there's another pilot, let them hog all the glory, and take off and return on ships, spending only about 8-9 AP before sporing.
