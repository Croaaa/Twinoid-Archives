The key rule is that you have to play for your side.

So it's strictly forbidden to turn yourself in to get cured. You are mush and must act against humans until the end. If you are discovered and humans are out to cure you, you are supposed to be a sport and try to avoid getting cured as much as you can.

If one of your mush comrades gets identified and will get/is getting killed, however, you can of course join in hitting them to blend in and reinforce your own cover. This is playing for mush interests. Since you're losing someone anyway you may at least try to make it somewhat useful.

If you are cured, don't hesitate to say everything you know. You are human now and play for your new side. All in all, it's a pretty rare situation.
