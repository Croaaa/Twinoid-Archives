On regular ships, dead humans are red and dead mush are orange (even those who had Anonymush). On casting ships with the Hidden Mush option, dead humans and dead mush are red.

When someone dies, there are several ways to know if they were mush :
- Gioele gains 3 through his personal objective
- The person who kills them gains 3
- When the first mush dies, it unlocks new research (Meridon scrambler, Mushovore bacteria...)
