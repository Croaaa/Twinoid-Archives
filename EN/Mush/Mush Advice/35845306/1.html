[1403405]

they are two cases when your skills are autopicked:

1) you are human and the number of skills unlocked is below or equal to your number of empty slots. This is because the game considers it is not ambiguous which skills you will pick: you have more slots than skills... but it is very bad because if you are mush, you would like to NOT pick and fill your human skills to have something helping the humans.

2) you are mush and you did not desactivate the autopick in your profile. In this case, regardless the numbers of skills avaialble (even if you have more skills than empty slots!!!!), the game will pick randomly enough skills to fill your slots. Many many mush get caught at the seconds they are chosen because of this.
