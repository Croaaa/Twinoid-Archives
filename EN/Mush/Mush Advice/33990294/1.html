On june 20, 2014
SummerMcLovin

Those crew members who have got around to it at some point in their lives before the ship still gain 2 morale from it. While it might not seem a lot if you've been on fairly short ships so far, on a long voyage and/or one where you don't have morale skills it can be a big issue. The increasing number of panic attacks (minus three morale on the start of a cycle) can quickly cause your demand for fruit to overtake the daily supply from your garden (if you have a well-tended one). Even if you have Janice's Shrink or Jin Su/Kuan Ti's leader, they'll still need a source of morale and the thing is a cheap way to get it.
