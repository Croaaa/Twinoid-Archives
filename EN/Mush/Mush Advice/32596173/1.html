First of all : You have to have the skill Technician ( lvl 2 Terrence, if you're not silver/gold you won't be able to have this skill for now )
2nd : You've to have one scrap metal, either by dismounting the microwave or with the help of a pilot with the Pasiphae.
3rd : Dismounting the shower will give one thick tube and one plastic scrap. In order to build the SOFA, you've to take with you the thick tube, one scrap metal and the BluePrint : SOFA on the room you want to SOFA to be.

NB :

  • Once the SOFA is build, you CAN'T move it.
  • Use the wrench to dismount the Shower / Microwave.
  • Once everything to build the SOFA is here, everyone can build the SOFA. Don't have to be specially a tech.
