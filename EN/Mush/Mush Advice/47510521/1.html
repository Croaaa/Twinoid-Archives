Kyubey

I like more active and brutal approach. Just an example:

Take Defacer+Transfer, optional Bacteriophiliac.
1) Wait full ap
2) Drop-Pick up your walkie until 1-2hp
3) Kill some important humans in any rooms, but save 3-5ap
4) Find a human with illness in a room with lots of people. If you don't have one, use Bacteriophiliac after step 5 on your target
5) Use Defacer in the room
6) Spike the target
7) Use Transfer
8 ) Kill old body. Kill some more humans in the room if you want to.
9) Self-heal the illness, (not in the room ofc, you need to leave logs)

And now all you need to do - to confuse people even more. While keeping your ap, convince them, that you need to kill mush asap and he is one, who was in the room, but not you (illness from 'killing' should be strong protection on many ships), make them kill another 2-3 humans.

Who to target:
- JS is a good target for transfer - you will be able to fail expoes later and you make human -1pilot
- Chao is good target, if you plan to kill manually some more humans, after they stop killing suspects themself.
- If you want to use spores later, and use this plan on first days, you can kill Finola
- Janice can be good target - her death limits morale gain, and disorders people get will not confirm them as humans
- Or just go for pilots and teches - if your random ship has just 2-3 pilots and 1-2 techs, you can kill/accuse them all.
