Kyubey

2 zones planets can have good zones, problem is - there 2 zones should be really very great to worth time and fuel to come there. Most of time better delete 2 zones planets, but if you need for example only O2 fast - you can scan them and they can have it.

What planet to visit is a good question, most of the time you need to count possible benefits and dangers. Here is the list from worst to best zones, and you can count the sum:

Volcanoes, Mankarogs, Seismic activity - most dangerous zones - can instant kill one human, or everyone in the expo. Visit planets with those only if you really have to (like you have 2 units of O2 and planet has O2)

Low Temperatures, Strong Winds, Desert - useless zones. You can not find anything here, but you can take damage or/and lose items.

Swamp (fruits), Oceanic (meat), Mountains (fuel, fruits), Forest (fruits), High Temperatures (fruits), Predators (meat), Insects (meat) - normal zones. There are chances here to find something useful, but in the same time there are chances for MIA, damage, illnesses and fights here. To visit or not depends on the situation. Btw illnesses are good, not bad. They can be healed on same cycle just for 2ap, and will confirm humans/reveal mushes,.

Hydrocarbon Deposits (fuel), Oxygen (O2), Orchard (fruits), Ruminants (meat) - free loot zones - quite no dangers and big rewards. Always try to visit.

Caverns, Ruins, Shipwreck, Intelligent Life - these zones can give you an artifact. In the same time - they are quite dangerous (especially Shipwreck). Having diplomat/white flag/babel module will help to low the risk and rising the chances.

Cristalite - same as artifact zones. But the risk is even higher, but reward is not a random artifact, but Eden map piece.

I'm not an advanced player, but I'll give you some tips:
1. Scan planets between 5 and 12 zones.
2. Don't analize planets when CPU Priority isn't at Astronavigation as with it you can reveal 1 additional zone. Ask the NERON Administrator for it before.
3. At start you should find the planets with oxygen asap. Until many plants doesn't mature, delete planets with without oxygen.
4. Avoid the planets with death events, like Volcanoes, Seismic Activity or Mankarogs, except if you're lack of oxygen (6 units or less).
5. You need a lot of AP. Ask for free space or take Paola's "Logistics Expert" skill.
