There is always one alpha mush on the list. The second alpha mush can be on the list, and so can the infected mush, but it's only by chance. The list picks one of the alpha mush and then picks 7 names at random among the crew.

The list contains at least one alpha mush and then a number of random people from the ship. Among those random people there can be the second alpha mush being listed (also Chun or dead people can be listed).

You can only print the list once per game. If you never printed the list and you think you killed all the mush you can go try to print the list. If you can print it, it means there are mush left, if you can't print it (the option is scratched, it means there are no more mush on the ship.
