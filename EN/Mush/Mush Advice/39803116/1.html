spacekadt

This all becomes very obvious once you make the purchase, but let me give simple answers for now.

1) Yes, two passes = one week. Yes, you're silver afterward. It works by going into the payment system and selecting MTPass as your payment option. You'll see where you need to enter two codes.

2) MTPasses are *easily* shared. In fact, there's an interface built into the MTPass system that allows you to check off x number of passes and send those codes to someone else to use themselves.

3) You wouldn't be breaking any rules at all. It's actually very common and recommended way to share the cost.

SummerMcLovin

You can buy MTPasses through the Twinoid 'app', which should be here but there's a link when purchasing Gold in the MTPass section. Depending on how many you buy, you'll have a list of codes on the History tab there, which are entered on the Payment screen.

Any that you haven't used can be saved indefinitely. If you have a really promising ship that you want to help out even more as Gold, you can use them for yourself; if you want to give them to others, it is as simple as giving them the nine-digit code for two unused Passes. Alternatively if you want to play other Twinoid games (especially Arkadeo and KadoKado to earn more Passes), their version of Silver bonuses can help towards that: e.g. KadoKado gets you double the points used to unlock further games or spend on Passes.

There aren't any rules against sharing Passes, all you're doing is depriving yourself of the benefit. I don't know if it is that common but they've been used as clan prizes in some other games on here.

Actually, I'll even go a step further. People who have met me on ship know me for always wanting to help new players out to get them more engaged in this wonderful game. I give out passes constantly. In my mush group I created a mini guide that am going to share with everyone now.

MTpass.com - It's an official MotionTwin site. Those codes work for ALL of their games. Go to that link while logged in. You can highlight your account tag to open the pop-out menu and then click on "shortcuts" in the bottom right corner in order to "add it" on your Nexus Bar.

I learned about them from another player, now a good friend, on a ship that I gifted regular Gold days to our Commander. Up until that time, I kept renewing my account the normal way. Now I simply buy the passes, it's more efficient and it's a chance to do something good for a just starting new player.

After you buy a bunch of MT Passes, you will get a message on your Nexus with "Access Codes" equal to the number of passe that you buy.
Each code can be "verified" to see if it has been used, if you decide to give some out....
To check how many passes you have available at any time - you go to mtpass.com and click on the History tab.

The way you access them for use in MUSH is at the popup window with the payment information after you click to either "Renew Gold Days" or buy more "Klix".
The top choice is "Credit Cards and PayPal".
The one below says "Phone and MT Passes"
You click that and a few empty rows will appear.
Enter the desired number of codes there to finish your transaction.

It is essentially cheaper that way, because if you are on a good ship that will last over 10 days, you only spend enough passes for 7 days.
If the ship turns out bad, don't even have to bother activating them.

They can be used for "Klix" too. 1 MTPass = 10 Klix.

When gifted, [must gift 2 passes] they will permanently unlock "Silver+i'Trackie" for the person that receives/uses them.

Another interesting feature will allow you to gift MTpasses to someone that is bronze, let them activate the week, then give the gold days back. 1st day is deducted automatically upon activation, the 6 remaining can be returned. This will unlock Silver for the receiving player, and ultimately only cost the generous donator 1 day of their time!

Hope that sums it all up and answers most questions. http://mtpass.com/
