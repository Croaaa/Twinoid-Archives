Starvation damage occurs at the end of the cycle 24 cycles after you run out of satisfaction points.

In the tutorial I'm pretty sure you eat a ration (4 satisfaction points) so if someone actually completed the tutorial, then went inactive you are looking at 29 cycles after they began play before they take damage. So if they began play D1C1 they would have taken 1 damage at D4C5 and be dead by D5C8.

If they joined the game after D1C1 you need to add those cycles to the end point. It usually makes it easier to just wait till the end of day 6 and then kill any inactives left alive.
