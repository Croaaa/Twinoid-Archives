It is the only room that's limited, because only four people can go on expeditions. If you have Large Bay Upgrade, six people can go on expos, and so six people can enter the room.

That limitation has always existed. It makes a little less sense now, because if there are more than four people in the room, the game picks four of them at random if you launch another expo.

But it used to be possible to launch an expedition with four or six people, have four or six other people enter the bay during the expo, and then launch another one with eight or twelve people. That's why now four (or six) people are chosen at random, to avoid having overpowered expo teams.
