1. I select a turret (Generally the one next to Alpha Bay 2) and I stay there. If anyone comes in I would denounce them as mush.
2. I ask politely for a camera, and when denied I have a hissy fit.
3. I ask Ian for one banana plant. It's a maximum of 3 AP to tend to it (If it gets diseased) and you get +1 AP, +1 and +1 . This offsets the daily morale loss (provided Chun is still alive).

So using these tactics there is no need for me to eat rations or get in contact with other feral humans. Hope it helps!
