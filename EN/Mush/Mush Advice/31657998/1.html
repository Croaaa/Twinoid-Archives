[6826491]
IatotsAny tips for chefs? Am I just supposed to warm up the food for the crew and make coffee or...?

I would say wait until the crew actually needs it (nobody should be eating before Day four, barring any ship emergencies...save it for those.) Rationing your food resources carefully is a good way to increase your chances of survival. I was on a ship where our Stephen cooked eight rations on the first day, without bothering to even ask if anyone needed them, and we had severe food problems for the remainder of the (significantly shortened) voyage.

One of the best uses for your food points is freezing the alien steaks obtained from expos. Since you can freeze them for free, the expo team won't have to waste their AP freezing them. Just get to them by the end of Cycle 8, so you can freeze them at the peak of their freshness. Spoiled food can cause illnesses, so it's a very good idea to notify them of any spoiled food, so they won't get sick.

You can even freeze fruit and coffee for preservation...however it costs you AP to consume frozen fruit and coffee. So how do you defrost it? That requires using the microwave specifically - and using that is likely to get you killed. So the second lesson here is, don't bother freezing any fruit or coffee.
