Gadget57Okay I have a huge problem the game changed my name to Kuan TI how do I keep it as Gadget57 so people can recognize me?

One of the features of the game is that you're entriely a ship's character. Think of your ship as of a movie in the making where you're an actor in the role of Mr. Lai Kuan-Ti. One of the enjoyable parts of playing Mush is roleplaying. It is not against the rules to ignore the roleplay side of the gaming process.

However, it is against the rules to reveal your Twinoid name (if you already did it, don't worry, I think you'll pass once with it as you're new).

There is a reason. All the anonymity in the ship, all the hiding behind characters is so that no one can know what to expect from each other and to avoid prejudice about each other's possible actions and the playing quality. Besides, it helps to set up the athmosphere, even if half of the crew is not roleplaying.

But don't worry, after the ship ends there will be endscreen, and after some time it will reveal all the Twinoid names behind the roles. All the endscreens will be saved in your profile and you'll be able to re-view these.
