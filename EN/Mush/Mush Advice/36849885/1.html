The cause of death is what removed your last health. If you are at full health and start to starve, it'll take 14 (for gold players, 16) cycles to die with the Starving death message. If someone gets impatient and hits that player the cycle before they would die anyway, instead the player would die with the message from being attacked. That could have happened in the case you saw, but instead there was 1 health left, and so she got the starved message instead.

Starving doesn't cause death. It causes health loss. 1 per cycle. The death is caused by getting to 0 health.

What must have happened, as already said, is the player didn't get to the point in the tutorial where you eat a ration. (You start it starving, and eat a ration partway through.) The attacker made his health pretty low without actually killing him, and the -1 health per cycle finished him off soon.

Not really important to this, but worth knowing: The starving mechanic using health means it's possible to keep a starving character alive indefinitely with regular healing. This can be useful for keeping an inactive Chun alive, just heal enough to counter the starving loss of health, and have Janice comfort her every now and then to prevent death from depression.
