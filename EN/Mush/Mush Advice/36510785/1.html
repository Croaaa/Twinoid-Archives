Depends on if you are a lone MUSH or not. If your partner is another character who can't get dirty, or it's just you, then go all out on research. It's not like you will be able to make spores anyways, so the research really doesn't hurt you. Then just lay low and accuse anyone that doesn't do their job of being a MUSH.

If your partner is active and needs spores, then try to work on research when noone else is on. (Guys, I asked for a reset hours ago. Oh well, I guess I'll go offline for awhile.) Generally be inactive and buy your partner time to work.

A rather risky play if your partner was killed off, is to try to finish the serum. No, not for the reason you are thinking. If you are lucky, then you will have an inactive player somewhere. If you can convince the crew to use the serum on him (because obviously the other MUSH is inactive) then you can give it to him and use AnonyMUSH to make the yellow guy disappear and show that the inactive was 'cured'.
At which point you have the run of the ship. Hopefully they finish the Pilgred, and then you can hack (or use Transfer) and take the ship back to Sol.
Warning: If Eleesha is alive, then she can use the Tabultrix to see if there are any MUSH left. If she is inactive, dead, or already printed the list, then go ahead and try it.
Second Warning: If you make the serum and someone grabs it, leave the room, if they follow you, then murder is the only option.

Also, keep in mind that a lot of inactives will just stay in the Lab. So, if you have Transfer, and are sure they are inactive, use it on them and then you don't have to worry about being Finola. (Plus transferring will mess up the research progress.)

I think as Mush Finola, your best card is that you can deprive the humans of yourself, and thus Mush research. - Delay the Antispore Gas until the first day change if possible, find out where the knife is, then go on a 8x Phagocyte rampage.

Kill Chun, then kill Paola if she is a Biologist, then Janice and/or Jin Su if you can get to them. Then spore and transfer into someone: Biologist Ian to put research to a standstill, the other of Jin or Janice to get rid of the last morale booster, or a pilot in order to grab some items and take off. Your Mush partner will thank you.

If you would like to live longer, you can be sneaky and try to just get Chun and maybe one other person as a "griefer" into transfer trick rather than an obvious Phagocyte run. If successful, that would leave you with 2 Mush, a different body, no research team, and none the wiser.
