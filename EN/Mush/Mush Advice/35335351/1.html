Let's discuss the goals which every character (human play) want to achieve for the benefit of the ship and maybe give some hints to achieve it. Plz don't make another tutorial for each character, but maybe you can mention some good strategies for characters you know well.

I will start with the characters I know a bit.

Ian: Every Ian should hope to get as much good plants as quick as possible. The ultimative goal would be to have only jumpkins - as much as possible. So you need the extra Hydropots as project and you should participate on expeditions where fruits can be found. Once you have a jumpkin cultivate him good and quickly and do the crossbreeding of all other plants with jumpkins at cycle 1 of a day. Once you have a big garden also the NERON project "heat lamps" (additional fruits) would be a great benefit.
Taking care of plants is also using lots of aps, but if you have made it that far, the bonus APs for the ship are comparable with the aps a coffeine addicted Gioele can get once pilgred is finished. And think about the free health points and morale points and the oxygen ;-) - incredible!

Terrence:
His ultimative goal has to be to get as much drones as smart upgraded as possible. So at the beginning of the game you really have to take care that you get lots of scrap. To fulfill your goal it is really useful if you are robotics expert, pilot and technician at least (so be a gold player)... or skip pilot skill if there are enough other pilots on board of the Daedalus as silver player...
You should vote for the NERON project extra drone and build your blueprint of the extra drone before you start upgrading them.
useful drone upgrades are turbo and firefighter. The more drones you have the higher the possibility is that there is a drone in your reach which can be brought to a broken equipment which is difficult to repair or that one of the drones would find the broken equipment on its own. A drone with turbo will repair a broken equipment with a starting chance of success for repairing of 5% probably within 2 cycles.
pilot skill should only be chosen with magnetic net or if you are extremely careful that the drone doesn't reach a bay to take off...which is really difficult to prevent currenty (actually a drone with pilot skill is really helpful if you take it with you on a patrol craft- after firing on enemies with all of your aps and if no charges are left, the drone will land at cycle change-so you saved 2 ap to shoot more enemies ;-) )

I think there are currently some bugs (like Pathfinder) and problems with drones which should be fixed (the permanent moving of the drones is annoying and if you have to search for them it is using sometimes lots of aps...). I would appreciate an update which would make the drones even more awesome.

So if you are a silver Terrence and there aren't enough pilots it is probably better to to bury your dream of the ultimative drones. It would be more useful to be a pilot and a technician- your new ultimative goal would be to fix broken Items, get as much metal scrap as possible, fight hunters, build the oscilliscope and fix the hull with all the metal scrap ;-) -> which is indeed also very useful for the ship.
