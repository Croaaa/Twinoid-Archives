The 5 different types of actions
Invisible actions : Never logged in the room events. Totally invisible to yourself, to cameras and other people.

  • Giving a mission
  • Advancing a research
  • Injecting the serum to a Mush
  • Phagocyte (the only visible message is the gain of hp and ap)
  • Fungal kitchen
  • Infectious cuddle (totally identical to a regular cuddle)
  • Transfer

Can be revealed by Premonition or Torture.


Personal actions : Only visible to you, written in purple italics. Never visible to other people or cameras.

  • Most failed actions (repairing, dismantling, searching, extinguishing a fire...)
  • Injuries
  • Taking a shower (Mush or human)
  • Getting dirty
  • Healing yourself
  • Watering and treating plants
  • Dropping or picking up a walkie talkie, iTrakie or tracker
  • Advancing a project in the nexus
  • Accessing a terminal with the hacker kit (whether you fail or succeed)
  • Modifying the Bios parameters
  • Actions revealed by Chao's torture (only visible to him, not the victim)
  • Chun's Premonitions
  • Actions revealed by confiding to Andie (only Andie sees the actions)
  • Scanning a planet
  • Hyperfreezing food
  • Catching an illness
  • Recovering from a physiological illness
  • Mycoscan results
  • Using the spore sucker
  • Being cured by the retro-fungal serum

Can be revealed by Premonition or Torture.


Furtive / Covert actions : Only visible to yourself, in a blue frame, if there is no camera. Visible to everyone, in a red frame, if there is a camera.

  • Direct spiking
  • Demoralize
  • Slime trap
  • Nightmarish
  • Bacteriophile
  • Radio pirate

You do not need to be alone with your target to do those actions.
Can be revealed by Eleesha with the Observant skill if she is present when the action is performed
If Gioele gives you the "Pariah" status with the Persecutor skill, your furtive actions become secret.


Secret actions : Only visible to yourself, in a blue frame, if you are alone and if there is no camera. Visible to everyone in a red frame if there is a camera or if someone else is present, including other Mush.

  • Eating, drinking coffee, taking a pill
  • Extracting oxygen or fuel from the tanks
  • Unloading fuel from the combustion chamber
  • Hiding an object
  • Extracting a spore
  • Removing or installing a camera with the Nimble skill
  • Trapping a room
  • Pyromaniac
  • Sabotage . (note that cameras do not reveal their own sabotage)

Can be "noticed" by Eleesha's Observant skill several cycles later if they were already visible.


Public actions : Always visible to everyone, written in blue.

  • Attacking someone (even failed attempts). With the Ninja skill, unarmed attacks are anonymous but are still visible. * Picking up or dropping an object
  • Accessing a terminal
  • Repairing PILGRED
  • Flirting and doing the thing
  • Successful actions (repairing, extinguishing fires, searching, dismantling, destroying a hunter...)
  • Throwing a grenade
  • Mutating
  • Lying down and getting up
  • Cuddling the cat
  • Psychological illnesses cured by a shrink
  • Janice's comforting
  • Healing someone
  • Surgery (DIY surgery or surgery by a medic, whether successful or failed)
  • Curing someone (or yourself) of a physiological illness
  • Removing and installing a camera
  • Taking off with a patrol ship
  • Analysing a planet
  • Finishing a research
  • Loading fuel into the combustion chamber or into a tank
  • Symptoms of psychological or physiological illnesses
  • Confiding to Andie

Eleesha's Observant skill

  • Eleesha has a 25 % chance of acting like a camera and revealing a furtive action if she's in the room at the same time. The message "Eleesha has noticed something interesting" will appear in the logs and the furtive action will be visible for everyone, in red.
  • For secret actions, the same message will appear when someone has done a secret action in the room. The message can be simultaneous or can appear several cycles later, when Eleesha refreshes the page, when she does something, when she enters the room, or when someone else does something. It is always an action that was already visible in the logs because the action had been done in front of someone or before a camera.

Screenshots :

  • Eleesha noticing a secret action : Terrence eats, and the action is revealed because Ian is also present. Eleesha enters 30 minutes later and the message "Eleesha has noticed something interesting" appears.
  • Eleesha revealing a furtive action : There is no camera in the room, but Terrence's spiking became visible.

Eleesha cannot reveal private or invisible actions.


Mycoalarms
They detect the following Mush actions :

  • Direct spiking
  • Infectious cuddle
  • Fungal kitchen
  • Bacterophiliac
  • Transfer
  • Trapper
  • Nightmarish
  • Radio pirate
  • Mutating

They do not reveal spore extraction.


Torture, Premonition and Confident

  • Chun's Premonitions reveal the person's last action.
  • Chao's Torture reveals one action per mission hp (for example : if Ian has 10 hp out of 14 and Chao tortures him, torture makes him lose 1 hp and reveals his last 5 actions).
  • Andie's Confident skill reveals the person's last X actions (X = Andie's morale).

Only Andie, Chun and Chao can see the result. These skills do not work like cameras. They do not reveal the actual log but the name of the action : Search, Extract a spore, Consume, Wash... Which means that they can reveal invisible and private actions like transfer, fungal kitchen, showers, etc.
