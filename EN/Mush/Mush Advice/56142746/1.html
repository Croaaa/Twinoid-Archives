Welcome to the Daedalus Emergency Room. Have you suffered an injury during cryosleep? Don't panic... we have no doctors, but here's what you should know.


Some injuries do not need to be healed.
- Broken Nose has no effect, except making you a little more ugly less attractive.
- Bruised Shoulder only handicaps pilots or shooters. Other crew members don't need to heal it.


How to perform surgery
- Take the apron to avoid getting dirty, unless your finger is broken (see below).
- Go to medlab and lie down on the bed
- Click on the surgery pod and on DIY Surgery.
- If you fail, lie back down and try again.


Make sure you have enough AP to perform surgery
- DIY surgery costs 4 AP (or 1 healing point) for each try.
- If the operation fails, you catch Sepsis, a physiological illness, which must be cured as soon as possible. It is better to ask someone else to heal you (2 AP) instead of self-healing (3 AP).
- If you have a Broken Finger, most actions cost +1 AP, even free actions. Don't take the apron, it will cost you 1 AP. However, DIY surgery always costs 4 AP.
- If a Medic is present (Finola or magebook), ask them to operate on you. Medics can perform surgery for 2 AP on a standing patient, in medlab or anywhere with the medikit.


There you go, you're good as new!
