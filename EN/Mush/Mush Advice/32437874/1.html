Need to know how to go on an expedition? Here's the few steps to go through to go on an expedition :

  • You have to find a planet with the Astro Terminal.
  • Someone has to put right amount of fuel needed, if the fuel isn’t correct and the Daedalus moves, the planet will be lost forever.
  • Your Captain has to turn the Daedalus in the right direction and then engage the trip.
  • Optionally finish the scan if you didn't already in order to make sure you won't encounter things you don't want to see (generally these are volcanos, mankarog and seismic).
  • Get ready in the Icarus Bay. And take off.
  • The expedition will happen automatically.
  • When you do an expedition, all objects, fruits and food appear automatically in the Icarus Bay.. From a patrol ship they appear when the patrol ship gets back.
  • Once the expedition is done, someone will have to put one fuel in the combustion chamber to move off the orbit. If the Icarus is still on orbit during the process, the Icarus and all the person in exploration will be lost forever. ( Except with the special project ).
  • Note that if someone dies during the expedition, everything they were carrying is lost permanently.

You can't launch an expedition under these circumstances:

- The planet is fully explored
- An expedition is currently happening
- If the Lock is on [Pilot], the one who launches the expedition has to be a pilot.



Expedition skills:

  • Botanist : +1 to the fruit from Harvests.
  • Diplomat: Cancels the probability of the outcome Combat. It won’t protect you from accidents or from getting smashed by a Mankarog.
  • Sprinter: +1 area visited per sprinter.
  • Survivalist:
    • Personal damage to the survivalist is reduced by one point.
    • The event "Provision" gives one more steak.
    • Last to die in case of fatal events. It means that if you had to die (in case of some fatal event) someone else will.


Equipment :

  • Quadrimetric Compass:
    • Protects the group from the event " Wandering ".
    • Protects you from the event "Lost".
  • Spacesuit: Allows explorers to go on a planet with no oxygen.
  • Rope: Cancels personal damage that can happen to you in the areas "Seismic", "Cave" and "Mountain". Does not prevent sudden death.
  • White Flag: Cancels the chance of Combat with "Intelligent Life".
  • Echo-Locator: Chance to explore a "Hydrocarbon" zone x5
  • Drill: Double the gain from the event "Hydrocarbon".
  • Babel Module : Chance to gain artefact in "Intelligent Life" are x2.
  • ThermoSensor : Chance to go to one of the remaining sections "Ruminant", "Predator", "Intelligent Life", "Mankarog", "Insects", "Lost" x5
  • Post-Its: Can be used to fill empty inventory slots to reduce the chance of losing an important object.



Weapons:

  • Blaster: +1 added to the strength of your team during Combat.
  • Knife: +1 added to the strength of your team during Combat.
  • Natamy: +1 added to the strength of your team during Combat.
  • Lizaro Jungle: +1 added to the strength of your team during Combat.
  • Old Faithful (Machine Gun): +2 added to the strength of your team during Combat.
  • Rocket-Launcher: +3 added to the strength of your team during Combat.
  • Grenade: +3 added to the strength of your team during Combat. (one time use and consumed at the 1st Combat). If a crew member carries more than one, one will be used per fight.

Steps:

  • The exploration occurs automatically, one step every 10 minutes.
    • There are 9 steps planned in Icarus, 3 from Patrol, and 1 more per Sprinter in the group.
    • They correspond to the characteristics of the planet.
    • Each can zone be explored once, but a visit may fail to explore.
    • There are four possible outcomes at each area.
    • Gains (Steaks, O2, Fuel, Fruits) reported by the game include bonus skills or objects mentioned above.
  • Combat takes place like as follows :
    • The creature has his strength fixed by the area, except for the Shipwreck.
    • The strength of the crew is one per member plus bonus and objects.
    • If the group's strength is inferior to the creature Strength, all members receives the difference in strength in damage spread on all of them. (Not necessarily in an equitable way)
    • Nothing happens if the crew wins. They just don't take damages.

Landing
Always successful if there is a pilot among the explorers, even if it's not the person who piloted the Icarus.
If the planet has no Oxygen, the explorers without a spacesuit will stay in the Icarus Bay during the expedition. Their skills and equipment won’t contribute to the expedition, but the Mush’s skill “Traitor” will.
