Then they are not "synchronized".

Besides, players on the English server come from all over the world, so UK time means nothing to them. Cycle changes were not based on what time it would be in the UK, they were based on French time, so as to avoid having all cycle / day changes happening at the same time on all servers.

It's the same thing for Hordes and Die2Nite. Day change doesn't happen at the same time, or the servers would crash.
