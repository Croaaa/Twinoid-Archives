MT recently fixed Lethargy to remove the bug that made it useful. Now you have to stay in bed performing NO actions that would wake you up, and the bonus is only occurs once every 4 cycles that you lay down. Essentially the only bonus now to lethargy is the fact that you have double the AP you can store, as doing anything that will wake up the character (even moving) will reset the cycles slept counter.
