Under the 'my account' tab, you should select 'my settings', then checkmark Activate: Advanced mush.

That will allow you to pick your skills, if you have more skills than you have skill slots.

Under all circumstances, if you have fewer or equal skills as you do skill slots, your skills will be auto-selected to fill those slots, ranked by level.

What you described sounds strange to me, unless you leveled up to 4th level after the game started. If you gain a level in play the game does not automatically try to fill that skill slot for you, unless you become mush and do not have Activate: Advanced mush selected.

What you described does make sense if you were level 3 Andie, joined the ship, had skills 1-3 picked for you, then leveled after joining the ship.
