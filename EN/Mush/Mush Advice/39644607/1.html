I ran this experiment on my last ship as a Jin Su with LogEx and it rocked my world. We had Frieda take a bed in a dorm (should've been medbay but she was semi-inactive and our paranoid Gioele didn't relocate a spare camera there until around day 3) and had me and Paola sitting on the bridge, with her getting 3 action points a cycle. It basically pushes out her NERON upgrade capacity by 50% (a little less, I guess, since not all of her AP goes into that, but), and our Paola pulled only useful projects. It was kind of stunning.

Has anyone got a counterargument to this? We DID kind of suck for scans. Frieda ended up going idle about seven days in and dying, and we did very few explorations. No starmap fragments in spite of surviving to D11C1 with Andie going on every expedition we did mount. I know scanning is important, but free NERON projects are literally irreplaceable, and the earlier in the game they happen, the healthier they can be for the ship.

But Paola got us the +1 zone-per-scan upgrade, too. I'm just not convinced that having one character who gets 4 zones instead of 3 is worth losing out on the potentially crazy benefits of a ballsload of NERON updates early in the game. Okay, so on some ships it'll be 3 zones instead of 2, which is, y'know, 50% better scan results rather than 33%. But I've been on ships with normal Friedas who scanned religiously the whole ship and never found a single planet worth visiting, too.

So which way is better? At this point, I just leave it up to my Paolas and Friedas: I point out the benefits and ask Frieda if she's okay with taking a dorm bed (or medlab if we've got a camera). I'm not at the point where i'm so convinced that superPaola is the best choice that I insist. Not yet, anyway. Should I be moving more in that direction, or am I just trading one RNG for another?

I guess it depends on what you're going for. Paola's upgrades are RNG with diminishing returns which means you can get a day 1 plasma shield or you can end up with some of the most useless projects and spend 2 days of AP to get Dismantler. Having a Jin Su with logistics is fairly rare, but I would assume early research would be priority over both scanning and coms.

If the mush are dead and you're going for eden then you'll want as many planets scanned as possible before things get hard - the projects just make things slightly less hard. If you're not going for Eden then I suppose it could compensate for a mostly inactive crew, but you'll probably just do an early sol regardless of what neuron upgrades you got beyond plasma shield.
