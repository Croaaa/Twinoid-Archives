One thing you should know about wounds is that they also decrease your max hp, even if it's not written. For example, with torn tongue, your max hp is 15 in gold mode and 13 in bronze or silver. And it occurs before the loss of hp due to the actual shooting or stabbing. If someone stabs you and you get torn tongue, first you lose 1 hp because your maximum is lower (and there is no message telling you that) , and then you lose hp due to the knife attack.

This explains in particular why the natamy rifle can be dangerous for humans too : even if they only lose 2-4 hp, they can indirectly lose much more because of the wound(s). And it also explains why sometimes people have less than 14 (or 16) hp and can't be healed.
