Evalcat

Certain human skills are still active when you are mush like pilot or shrink, since losing their effect would make finding you way too easy. Other human skills, like things dealing with food, become inactive, as either the effect is already nullified by being a mush or, as for the case of cold blooded, would just be too strong if it was kept as mush
