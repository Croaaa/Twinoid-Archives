This [Tutorial] Agent of MUSH help should help you.

At the start, if an entire ship is filled or another condition is met, two people will turn into Mush. You will get a special popup, and a pink next to your character avatar.

The two Mushes that start the ship with are called the 'Alpha Mush' or also 'starting Mush'. The rest of the Mushes that get converted are all 'Standard Mindless Mushlings'.
