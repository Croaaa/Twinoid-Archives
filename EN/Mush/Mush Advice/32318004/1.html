SpaceShipRatJust click personalize, and remove the game from your list. It won't delete your account or anything, but what difference does it make, no one's forcing you to play.

Yeah, that's one thing that will help hide any Mush-related things. Another is unsubscribing from the Mush news feed. The emoticon box for Mush will stay on your post screens, as you can't get rid of that thing

So here are links to the things you can do:
Personalize your bar - On the left side, there should be some game boxes. If Mush is in the group on the top, drag it to the bottom group. You should also uncheck the box that says "Automatically add games I play"
News subscriptions - Make sure that Mush does not have a checkmark next to it, so that you don't get any* alerts about the game
* I think MT can still override your settings, as I am not subscribed to Mush, yet I got an alert about this post


That's all I'm aware of, so hopefully that's good enough
