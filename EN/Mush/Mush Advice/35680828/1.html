[7904646]

If you have the Mush Skill "Nimble Fingers", you can do precisely that- Nimble Fingers prevents any interaction with the cameras (removing and installing) from appearing. However, doing so is incredibly AP intensive, and Neron will still announce the death. Also, anyone can walk in and see you punched/shot them anyway, it's a public action. Cameras only reveal Secret and Covert actions.

What you CAN do is remove a camera, infect the other person in the room, then reinstall the camera- or do some other activity that the camera would have otherwise revealed like giving someone a disease or trapping a shelf.

The QA thread is simple, dude. Number your question, look out for your number when scanning through answers. It's massive so people don't mind repeat questions. It makes for some pretty good reading if you're bored, though.

Having a camera in the room or not doesn't change anything when you kill people, because attacking crewmembers is always visible.
