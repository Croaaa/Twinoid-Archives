I can't particularly recommend SS13, even after playing it for 1-3 years on a few different servers. It's very prone to griefing, requires a lot of knowledge you'll have to build up over weeks, usually has a lot of metagaming going on and unless you're quite lucky in your choice of server/company, decent RP. Rounds tend to end quickly on the more populated servers, too.

...Playing it was fun while it lasted, though. Especially if you manage to find a quiet server with people that don't instantly want to break into the Captain's room, steal his ID, send Beepsky after everyone, release the kraken that is the singularity, robust you in the corridor with an empty toolbox, tie you to a chair and throw floor tiles at you, run over you with one of the cargo bay's delivery machines, forcefeed you acid so your face melts off, put you to sleep and take you to genetics to give you a whole new identity - and so on.

That said, some of those times were fun, too. Not if you're the one having your face melted off, granted...
