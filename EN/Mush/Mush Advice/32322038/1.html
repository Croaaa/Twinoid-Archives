Hey newbies!

I remember how freaking difficult it was when I started and I expect you're all going through the same frustration.

As the more experienced folks are working on getting guides up and running and I think there's even preliminary work on a wiki (though I can't remember where and don't know how complete or accurate it is yet), I wanted to at least give you something.

So here's the French wiki in google translate speak.

For those that want to learn and experience by poking around yourselves, that's absolutely fine and I encourage that... sometimes it's the best way to learn.

For those that are looking for fast answers, the wiki can help.

Also, there's an IRC channel set up where a few of us idle lots and lots, but if you can catch one of us (hell, ping my name in there and I'll likely be around!) sometimes we'll have an answer or can give some help. You can find that on the mibbit irc server at #mush-en or click here for a shortcut
