Mystaira

Mush aren't always dirty, just like humans aren't always clean. Click on the character, there will be a icon to indicate they are dirty.

Mush tend to get dirty more often than humans due to extracting spores, but some humans are almost always dirty due to their jobs (Like Raluca). Some jobs don't make you dirty, so those characters are highly suspicious if they are dirty.

And if the Mush wash before you notice they are dirty (especially if they don't use the soap), you won't even be able to tell that they were.
