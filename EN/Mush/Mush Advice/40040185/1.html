Sorry, SummerMcLovin.

Two more things :
- Bandages work differently, you can click on them while they're in the shelving unit and you will get the "Self-heal" option. It's very useful when your health is so low that picking up the medikit could kill you.
- Some injuries or illnesses lower your max hp, which means that the "Self-heal" option doesn't appear even though you have less than 14 (or 15) hp.
