In my opinion: Lethargy is good because you can store 24 AP, not because of the +3 AP after four cycles. If you spend resources wisely, you can ALWAYS be prepared to kill a mush if they expose themselves, even if they do it 5 minutes after you relaying, it can be good for rushing RFS to cure a mush too (assuming you have biologist book). Don't expect to benefit from lethargy if you are the ship's healer though. If you want to utilize the +3 AP while sleeping, you can either spend your energy on scanning (since you can stay in the bed until you have max AP), or if you are lucky, you can be the ship's pilot if there's a pilot magebook (Storing 24 AP is better than Shooter or caffiene Junkie Pilot in my opinion).
In short: I think it's only useful when there are magebooks that synchronize with it. And if there are no books, then you can still do a good work with astro scanning on a ship.

There's also the unintended side effect where it tends to encourage Chun to stay offline for longer, because players know they can't do anything for a certain amount of time before it kicks in. Thus, nearly every Lethargic Chun I've flown with has been near useless during the mush hunt. Even when I know the player is decent and not your average inactive newbie.

It doesn't have to be that way - chatting and such doesn't count against your bonus. But in practice the players also slack off a bit, and you may as well not even have a Chun until the post-mush phase.

The increased AP pool is great for things like late-game piloting, visiting fury upon an outed mush, late-game healing sprees, and astrophysics with a huge jumpkin garden. But the poor play it encourages in early game makes me not like it all that much :V

Let me forward what Kobb said ones more. A good Chun is an active Chun. And during the mush hunt phase she needs to do a lot of things: like securing items, distributing alarms, checking lonely crew in the turret or in Medlab, checking logs and keeping an eye on the ship. She is also the best guardian on board as she cannot be converted or transferred into, thus is immovable if mush have no access to solid or wrestler.

So as lethargic Chun, consider that you will be tempted to stay in bed more, and assist the mush hunt less. In early stages of the game one really needs to do their best and resist the temptation to abuse this skill and instead attend to pressing matters, which will benefit the ship more than any additional ap Chun may gain.
