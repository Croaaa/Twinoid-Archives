Haven't played the new episode, but from my experience with the last season...

Eleesha: Very High at first, low later
Mush hunting is extremely important early in the game and she's dedicated to it. If you've seen her do her job properly, it's probably one of the hardest things to do in the game. Later on she's just support.

Frieda: Low-Medium
Her niche is scanning planets and doing expeditions. She needs to stockpile APs for this so she only really needs to log on once a day, and right before an expedition.

Gioele: Low-Very High
He's near useless early on, only good for support. After PILGRED is complete, he's the most important member of the team, especially with Astrophysics and/or Pilot. Caffeine junkie means he has to get on every cycle. He's third in line for commander role, which happens often because Jin Su gets mushed early and Chao ends up a griefer.

Ian: Consistent, but low
He only needs to get on at the right time to keep the plants alive. I heard it's one of the easiest roles on the ship, but he has to consistently get on every day because most people won't even look at the plants and expect him to do everything. He's not someone who will get 'covered for' easily.

Janice: Low-Medium
Most of her skills are passive - she's useful if she just stands in a dorm all day. With Diplomat, she's expected to go on expeditions, but that's about it.

Hua: Medium
She's the main expedition pilot if Frieda isn't. She needs to stockpile APs for hunters so she has an excuse to log on only once a day.

Jin Su: Very High
Gives out missions, expected to jump the ship around, bosses people around. Has to be in contact almost every cycle.

Kuan Ti: High
Early game, he relays research, meaning that he has to get on every time someone does any research. Late game his astrophysics and technician skill is in high demand.

Paola: Consistent, medium
Her AP is vital in the early game, she's expected to dump a lot of it into comms and should be around enough to optimize that.

Raluca: Medium
During the mush hunt, she can't really do much, but fix things in the rear of the ship. Later on, once things start breaking down the technician skill becomes really useful.

Roland: Medium
Pilot.

Seagull: Low
Doesn't really need to do much unless you have the apprentice skill and took something vital. But if he took the skill via apprentice, chances are that someone else has the same role.

Terrence: Medium
Drones, tech, pilot... all vital skills. But rarely does he have the AP to do all of the work.

Chao: Low-Very High
Only useful for the mush hunt for most of the game. But Jin Su is often a high priority target for mush which leaves Chao as the commander in many games.

Chun: Low
She's core to the mush hunt early game. Later on she has to run around healing people. But with the Lethargy skill, she's expected to stay in bed for extensive periods of time.
