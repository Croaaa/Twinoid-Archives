If you want to see all the icons that you have unlocked on the different Twinoid games, you have the list just below the text window when you're writing a message on the forum. Just click on the icon to see the Arkadeo icons, or on to see the Mush ones.

Mushpedia also gives a list of icons that can be used in game, including a few that can't be used on the forums.

You can also see all your distinctions on your Twinoid profile, in the statistics part.
