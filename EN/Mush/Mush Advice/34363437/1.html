There are two different Xyloph entries that make you lose the signal with Sol. The microwave can also cause a loss of signal. And if the signal is particularly weak, it can drop semi-randomly.

There's actually nothing that anyone can do to *make* the ship drop the signal other than microwaving (which has a chance and is announced by NERON) or sabotaging either the comms panel at the bridge or the antenna in the engine room.
