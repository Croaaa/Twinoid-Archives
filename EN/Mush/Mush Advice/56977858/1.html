Definitely Quantum Sensors for me. It makes scanning so much easier, and saves a huge amount of AP, especially in the long run, when planets get bigger and bigger. For an astro, a 12-zone planet takes 3 analyses with QS and CPU priority, instead of 4. For non-astros, the difference is even bigger : 4 analyses instead of 6. That may not seem a lot for one planet, but considering the number of planets scanned, it makes a huge difference. It can even be a life-saver when the crew desperately needs oxygen and everyone has to scan because the RNG has decided to be annoying.

Auxiliary Terminals are not as useful, because Raluca often stays in the Nexus in order to benefit from the protection of a camera or mycoalarm. Even when she stays in a turret, relaying in the engine room would not be safe. As for the crew members who stay at the front, they work mostly in the lab and have less opportunity to work on projects. I'm not saying that it's completely useless, but it's really not that great.
