SummerMcLovin

While Hua's main task of Piloting is AP-intensive, random repairs (especially with Tech points) is not. You can easily work the two non-related skills separately on a Silver Pilot/Tech Hua. A ship can never have too many Techs, so if you have level 4 Hua it is usually a good choice.
That being said, a replacement Botanist for an inactive/Mush/dead Ian would normally be preferable (although technically the only thing it is needed for is fruit properties, which even then can be seen using the Chef Mage book), and if you have a few Techs the extra fruit can be a better bonus. At least in my experience, expeditions tend to source more fruit (events) than steaks (events) so you could get more bang for your buck with Botanist than Survivalist. The reduced damage is not to be sniffed at though, especially if you get a lot of Fatigue events where it has the most significant difference.
U-Turn is only useful in very rare situations and I wouldn't think it worth anyone's time to sink that much XP to replace any of skills 2-4.

Unlike Tech tasks, Astrophysicist work is very AP intensive, so generally Frieda won't be able to get much use out of Pilot. However, Survivalist is entirely passive, so Frieda only has the MP cost of going down to Icarus Bay to deal with. If your NERON Admin is active, swapping the Pilot lock away from Piloting can have someone else launch it while you still act passively to not crash the Icarus (and saving your AP for more scanning).
If your ship is lacking Pilots though, get out there and shoot the Hunters. Hull breaches are more deadly than the slower finding of good planets.

Most of the above is considering Silver, which I assume is your situation. If you are Gold, both characters should take all of their first four skills.
