Welcome to the Mush forum! To keep it comfortable for everyone, we ask you to follow the TOS and make good use of the forum:


I. Search before posting!


* Before posting a question, use the Search function to verify that a similiar thread has been made.
* Before posting a question, check that the answer is not in the Help or the support site (see list below) .
* If you still cannot find it, use the thread Questions and Answers.

II . Write correctly and without abuse.


* Make sure your spelling/grammar is correct and avoid SMS-code, spamming smileys, capitalization or punctuation.
* Being polite goes a very long way, and makes your post more enjoyable to read.


III. Post intelligently.

* In general, avoid spamming, offtopic posting, 'bumping' for no reason, etc.
* If a thread has gone off topic a lot, report it. Do not post (Unless it's a fun thread!)
*: To report a bug, or translation errors do it in their respective threads.
* If see a bad message on the forum, you can report it using the "Block/report" function. You will not see the message and the moderators will be notified. You can also contact the moderation through the page "Contact an official group" that allows you to send a message to "Mush EN Mods" group.


IV. Leave the moderation to the moderators.


* Do not provoke the troll, contact moderation by reporting the content or through the method described above.
* Note that moderation is also there to ensure the stability of the forum. As such, you can ask the closing, moving, editing or creating sticky threads. Similarly, if you have ideas for events and want it to be more notable you can notify the Moderation.
* The "Block" feature alerts the moderation team for each reported message. Do not use without reason because just like the complaints system, it could turn against you.
* It is forbidden to hide a message of the Moderation.


V. Respect the players and staff.


* Whatever the difference in opinions, it is imperative to remain courteous at all times and to have respect for others, whether the other players, including the MotionTwin network, Moderators or Administrators.
* As such, teasing, ganging up and personal attacks, name and shaming, challenging penalties publically, petitions or other discourteous event are not allowed on the forum.
* Any discussion held by private messaging must remain private. Lies and slander is not tolerated.
