Those exclusive smileys appear in two places because you can (or could) get them by two different ways. Those under the icon could be won when you did the questionnaire on the main Mush page (the one that told you which character you would be).

The smileys obtained when you buy gold mode are under the icon and you "only" have 1000 of them. When you run out of them, you can't use them anymore (just like the picture). But 1000 is a lot.

For those who play on the French servers, the smileys that you see are totally identical in all sections, and they're the smileys obtained on the French server only. But you can still use the ones you gained on the English or Spanish server if you know their code.
