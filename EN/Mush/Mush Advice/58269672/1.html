Hello everyone , as many of you know, picking up multiple drones in a single room is by far the most frustrating things to do in this game, normally when you try to pick up a 2nd drone in a room, the game acts as if you selected the drone in your inventory, with no "Pick Up button!" Well, I found a way to "trick" the game to prevent the "Pick Up" button on screen from disappearing when picking up drones, and I'm going to tell you how to do it here!

Steps:

- 1. While in the room with the drones you want to pick up, open 2 separate tabs of your game.

- 2. after both of your tabs are loaded, go back to your first tab, click on a drone, when the option to "Pick Up" a drone appears, Right click on "Pick up" and press "Open in New Tab"

- 3. Now click on your third tab in the browser, give it time to load and make sure you have 1 drone in your inventory ONLY on your 3rd tab.

- 4. Go to your 2nd tab, select a drone, and left click "Pick Up" on any drone

- 5. At this point the game knows you have a drone in your inventory already, but doing this tricks the game in leaving the "Pick Up" button on the game after having 1 drone in your inventory.

- 6. Click "Pick up" another time in the second tab, let the game put the drone in your inventory before doing any other actions.

- 7. Now you have 2 drones in your inventory! If you want to pick up a 3rd drone, the "Pick up" will still be there, you just click it again and Viola! 3 drones in your inventory!

Notes:
- I tested this 4-5 times on one ship, and every single time I tried the method I described, it worked. A proper test would involve testing this with different characters, but I was unable to do that, so please post here if this guide does or doesn't work for you.
- I've only tested this on Google Chrome, so if anyone gets the oppurtunity to test this on other browsers, please let me know here!
- I do not know why this method exactly works, but if you are curious, my assumption is that Drones work like stackable objects, so pressing the "Pick Up" button picks up the next drone in the "stack", and the reason it's so buggy is because drones don't utilize the shelving unit when "Stored" in a room. It's a weird thought but that is what makes most sense to me!
