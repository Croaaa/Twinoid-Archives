A good ship doesn't need to have Terrance with a scooter, actually. They can form a chain and send him flying all over the ship. A chain from one end to the other can allow Terrance to move all the other items to wherever the ship needs them.

And some characters (like Eleesha) can use the scooter also. She can use it to better follow where players went and see what suspicious people are doing.
The thing is, Terrance NEEDS his AP. Using them to move around is kinda a waste. Especially with a bit of communication you shouldn't even need to spend MP to get where you need to go. While other characters (like the a fore mentioned Eleesha) don't need their AP as much and need to get around the ship more. Most characters don't need the scooter.
Why Hua would us it is beyond me. All he needs is MP to go to the Bay and back to the Dorm. That's 2 MP to go to a Bay and back. 4 if she's collecting scrap.

As for drones. They are really useful. However, they are really stupid and will usually just wander around. Sometimes you just need to grab one and take it to what you want fixed. Let the drone use it's AP to fix something, don't waste your time.
