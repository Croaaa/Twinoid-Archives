It depends on the ship but "Generally" you should always have a bed in Alpha Dorm (with priority over most of the rest of the crew) and you only really need to enter Alpha Bay and Refectory (think about carrying rations with you).

When it comes to scrap runs, you take the Pasiphae from Alpha Bay 2, just keep in mind retrieving scrap will take 2ap each time.

You have very little cause to be anywhere else on the ship (though with sprinter you may occasionally get asked to move things or help connect to Sol)

Well, most of ships have more then one pilot.
So first pilot: gets 10-12ap
- take off in patrol ship 2ap
- shoot at least 6-8 times for 6-8ap
- better use ration/coffee/mission while piloting to get more shoots
- land 2ap
and goes to rest.

Then second pilot: also gets 10-12ap
- take off in pasi 2ap
- collect 3-4 times (2ap per collection)
- better use ration/coffee/mission while piloting to get more scraps
- land 2ap
and goes to rest

Ofc if you did job of "pilot 1" and then 5-6 cycles passed, you got full ap again and no hunters have arrived, then - go to "pilot 2" job. But teamwork is quite always better.

You can kill hunters without taking off:
- if somebody kills them with turrets, but low accuracy and low damage of turrets make it quite bad way in most of situations
- if you upgraded some drones to do piloting stuff. They are quite difficult to use, and some ships are too lazy for that.

Still don't think then you have to do only this, if you are playing pilot. Ofc wasting ap on random stuff is not good idea, but there are emergencies to look after, and finding mushes is everyone's job, not just Eleesha-Chao-Chun.
