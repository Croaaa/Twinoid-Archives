PerpetualSin

At bronze level (which most new terrences will be) hes crap.

A robotics only terrence is dare i say... "crippled" and lacks a tonne of utility that gold mode beta crews would have been used to.

Terrence's main job is create an army of upgraded drones, that when done correctly can save the crew a large amount of ap.

However, to do this he needs scrap metal.

To get scrap metal you need to either dismantle certain things with the tech skill or salvage destroyed hunter parts with the pilot skill. A robotic bronze terrence doesn't have these skills, so he falls to the mercy of other pilots / techs to obtain it.

However most pilots are going to have their hands full with the hunters themselves and won't have the ap needed to salvage scrap for you.

The alternative is to ask the neron admin, (At game start, this is Jandice) to turn off the setting in the neron bios, so that anyone can pilot patrol and recovery craft.

Now you could fetch scrap, but at the risk of damaging the ship during take off / landing, providing an easy platform for mush to grief, and providing everyone else a viable suicide method.

The other alternative is to hope the crew completes the engine dismantler neron project for 5 scrap metal.

I believe it costs 4 scrap metal and 1 plastic to create a new drone from a robotics BP.

Then 2 scrap metal for each upgrade.

There are 5 upgrades for each drone.

Pilot - can fly patrol craft.
Fireman - immune to fire and can put out fire
Technician - fixes broken stuff.
Turbo - 80% chance of 2 actions per cycle.
Sensor - Some kinda better AI pathfinding, that determines what events the drone should give priority. (think this one is bugged)

Of course, without scrap metal. None of these really matter. But turbo and technician are good first picks.

The ship starts off with 1 drone already, so you could attempt to upgrade that one.

So unless you are working with someone else to obtain scrap metal, your best bet as a robotics only Terrence is to work on robotics specific neron projects, of which there are 5.

Have fun.
