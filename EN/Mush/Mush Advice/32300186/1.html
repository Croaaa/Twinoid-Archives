Mush Character Guide : Chao Wang

Special Trait : Hyperactive. Recieves no bonus AP (Action points) for lying down. (Using a bed) However Chao recieves 2 MP (Movement points) each cycle.

(Other characters only get 1 MP per cycle)

AP/MP Yield : Max 8 Ap per 24 hours. Max 16 MP per 24 hours. (Keep in mind the AP/MP bar can only hold 12 max.)

Because Chao MP is at a premium and AP is not, you should avoid converting AP in MP at all costs.

Activity level required : Medium.

(Transfers to activity level high, if promoted to the commander title. Of which, Chao is second in line to recieve.)

Most effective when played by someone who can log in every 12 hours (4 cycles) to make use of his daily 16MP.

Knowledge level required : Medium.

Role: Primary : Investigation / Patrol, Expeditions, Ship Turret Defense.

Secondary : Item Management / Transportation, Neron upgrades / research resets.

Gains Personal Glory : By having the killing blow on a crew member (includes the cat).

Best achieved by seeking out and killing the mush. (Unless you are mush yourself)

Room Preference. None. Although its beneficial to leave Chao in a room where a camera is located, when hes unattended. This avoids making you an easy target for a mush spore.

If Jin or Paola have the logistics expert skill and they are alone, Chao can benefit from their ap generation by being in the same room as them. They are effectively Chao's version of a bed, however their skill is best used on other more AP intensive characters.

Characters to "do it" with for morale generation.

Chun - (First choice, no risk of mush infection.)
Finola, Paola, Hua, Janice, Frieda, Raluca, Eleesha.

While you can lie down in the same room as Janice for morale generation, this shouldn’t be your first choice. If you do choose to do this you shouldn't be angry/surprised if someone kicks you off the bed, to make better use of the AP generation.

Titles :

Commander :Chao is second in line to recieve commander. A large responsibility.
Admin : Chao is last in line to recieve this.
Communications : Chao is second last in line to recieve this.

Ration Use : Low.

Besides eating fruit / rations to avoid starvation. You have little reason to consume rations for bonus ap.

While your AP generation maybe low, you don't have many ap intensive tasks that require your immediate attention. Alot can be achieved with just your MP. (Patrol, observing rooms, item transport, etc)

The best excuse for ration use, would be to chase, kill, persue an identified mush to stop them doing any further damage to the ship and crew.


Skills and Levels

1 Shooter – 2 free shots per day. Starts the ship with 4 free shot actions. (Good)

2 Survivalist – Expeditions, 1 less damage, 1 more alien steak (food item), last to die in case of fatal event. (Good)

The planet sections the survivalist skill benefits you most on are : insect, predator, ruminant, intelligent life, and ocean. You should consider going on all expos with large amounts of these sections. Even better if you go there with someone who has the diplomat skill.

The reduced damage taken will benefit you on most planet sections.

3 Wrestler – 2 more damage on hand to hand attacks. Makes your punch as strong as a blaster. (Average)

4 Torturer- For 1 ap, allows you to torture a crew member to reveal their previous actions.
Up to 8 actions based on the amount of health they have lost. (Good)

5 Crazy Eye – attacks against you cost 2 more ap, for the person attacking you. (Average / Bad, highly situational)

6 Intimidating – Debuff, 1 Ap. Once per day targeted player loses 2ap and 4mp. (Bad, Highly situational.) Might be effective to trap a potential mush, but Chaos worst skill by far.

Suggested Skills :

Bronze : 1 Skill.

Bronze : Shooter. Gives you the most utility onboard the ship. 4 free shots at the voyage beginning and 2 freeshots, per day.

These can be used for 2 free blaster shots, 2 free turret shots, or two free shots onboard a patrol craft.

In an enviroment where the number of hunters and asteroids increase over time, this skill is very important to have.

Another benefit of this skill is the free shots ignore Ian's passive skill.

Bronze Alternatives : Torturer. - Not recommended.

While you are still able to investigate mush. Without wrestler/shooter to back it up its not very effective.

Your hand to hand damage will be weak, you will be inaccurate with a blaster and risk damaging yourself.

It also comes with the risk that the mush might reveal themselves early and get killed, rendering this skill completely useless to the surviving crew for the rest of the voyage.

If you insist upon this build at bronze level, its best used in tandem with someone else that can do damage.

Silver Builds : 2 skills.

Shooter / Torturer - The utility of shooter, combined with torturer, allows you to investigate mush aggressively with a blaster in hand.

Shooter / Survivalist. - Utility build. Benefits the entire crew with shooter, and survivalist for expo's. Leaves the mush investigation up to Eleesha and Chun.

Gold Builds : 4 skills.

Shooter / Survivalist / Torturer / Wrestler. Combines great utility skills with great powers of investigation. Your punches do blaster level damage that easily chip peoples health down for investigation.

Shooter/ Torturer/ Wrestler / Crazy Eyes. Makes you the most threatening / defensive investigator. With an extra 2ap being added onto attacks against you, it makes retaliation less effective and less likely.


Roles / Strategy.

Chao's extra MP is a benefit incase you don’t have anyone that can carry heavy items with the solid skill. Chao could do this instead. Using his vast MP generation to offset the heavy item MP penalty.

For example : Moving the hyperfreezer to the Icarus bay, for easier steak to ration conversion.

Chao's extra MP is great for running around the ship collecting items, and hoarding them in specific rooms. The more items you have scattered about, the easier the mush can benefit from it.

Just be aware, fires in those rooms can ruin your day.

Investigating.

Being an effective investigator requires that you know what the roles of other crew are, and where they should be located. You should be on the lookout for logs / forum posts / dirty crew events, that don’t make sense.

You should know what items are where, check to make sure things haven’t gone missing / wandering.

You have a large amount of MP to burn, so you can easily patrol the ship looking for mush activity or strange actions that might give a mush away.

New mush tend to take damage when they shower, you could use this to your advantage by applying torture before you dish out damage.

If the character you tortured is revealing more than 1 action, it could indicate they are mush if they can't explain their injuries.

Chao's beneficial bonus skill books.

Pilot – Combined with shooter makes Chao great for using patrol craft or for piloting expeditions.

Fireman - With your 16 MP per day, combined with fireman you are the perfect character to go searching for fires and putting them out.

Technician : Same reason as fireman, except for repairs. Remember to take the wrench! This also opens you up to be a far more effective neron project researcher and pilgrid repairer.

Chef : Cooking and transport of rations to characters that need it the most.

Beneficial Character Combos : Ian - The crazy eye skill stacked with Ian's passive skill will cost an attacker 5 ap to attack you. - Remember this doesn't work on shooter skill actions.

Terrance - With 16 MP per day, Chao becomes a viable way of helping Terrance move around for free. Best if they share the same time zone.

Gioele and Steven. - If they have the solid skill, they can help Chao beat peoples health down.

Finola and Chun - Best potential healers, to undo your torture damage.

Chun and Eleesha - Working together in private to check for mush activity, pick torture targets.

Jin and Paola - If these two have the logistics expert skill and haven't organised themselves to use it on anyone specific. Feel free to use them for an extra 8 AP per day.

Beneficial Items :

Blaster, Blaster Rifle Natamy - For investigating and torturing Mush. The Natamy can kill a mush in 2 shots.

(Blaster Rifle Natamy - May currently be bugged and also two shot, humans.)

Snipers Helmet, Contact Lenses, - Bonus accuracy when using turrets, blasters and patrol craft. Just remember to drop when not in use.

Invertebrate shell : (found on expo's) : Move this heavy item to a turret, and combine with your shooter skill for 2 free shots with double damage.

Medikit : Be nice and heal your victims back up.

Neron Research :

Specific Neron projects you should consider investing AP into for max efficency.

Blaster Upgrade- Increases damage of patrols by one point - Shooter / Pilot skills.
Yep... Chao ain't the best for working on neron projects.
