Have a look at the blueprints available on the ship and see if you can build weapons. They don't necessarily cause more hp damage than blasters or knives, but they cause frequent wounds. Even better, even if it takes longer to do it, you can do the Natamy rifle by bringing a blaster to the lab. It is extremely powerful against mush and you only need to shoot them twice.

There's also another way, if you have already killed the other mush and if you have finished the retro-fungal serum : instead of killing Ian, you can cure him.
