Since the moment I learnt research, I didn't think Antispore Gas was powerful. It says 2 less spores but on day chance it resets. First, I didn't care as I thought Antispore Gas was the quickest to do, quicker than other part of research which I saw as more powerful: Constipaspore Serum and MycoAlarms. The plan was to make this quick anti-Mush research then make more powerful ones and be safe. But once I learnt Constipaspore Serum and MycoAlarms are made as quick as Antispore Gas, I was disappointed with the crew.

Antispore Gas is made because some crew is afraid that with 4 spores Mush can convert a whole human and even more and they believe this research will prevent them from converting and they're safe. Rampage with Phagocyte is the most efficient, the Mush can fill themselves with spores very quickly. The most commonly used reason of why Antispore Gas must be done is simply a habit - it was always in previous ships so this ship will also be filled with Antispore Gas.

Extracting 4 spores per day doesn't mean they'll use them all. Let's say Mush extracted 4 spores because human crew delays Antispore Gas. Now they're going to convert. How? They can infect directly. They can also do the thing carrying a spore. They can use a slot for Trapper to leave a spore in a room or Fungal kitchen to leave them in the food. Antispore Gas does not prevent infection, it just reduces spore extraction. All the actions above might be done with spores made yesterday and you can extract some today. You need MycoAlarms so you can put more places under security. Only MycoAlarms can detect food contamination, which is the most efficient way to convert putting 3 spores into pill or fruit. Let Mush extract spores, it's difficult to take control on it, but with MycoAlarms the Mush won't be able to use spores and they'll be forced to keep them with themself.

Rampages are efficient with Phagocyte and Antispore Gas will make the rampage less efficient but only by 4 AP. It might cause that the Mush will kill one less person among the most and crew will save one morale point or one room less will be ignited before hijacking a Pasiphae. The biologists might boast their job done, but it could've been better. Yes, Constipaspore Serum will prevent this kind of rampage as the Mush won't gain AP eating freshly extracted spores. Of course, they can eat spores made earlier, but neither Antispore Gas or Constipaspore Serum affects eaten spores. Nevertheless, Antispore Gas won't prevent from making new ones if the limit hasn't been reached yet, Constipaspore Serum reduces their purpose to healings, which makes Phagocyte not worth the Mush slot.

Remember, no Antispore Gas doesn't mean that extracted 4 spores will be end up in human bodies everyday. If Mush doesn't use any skills, the limit of used spores is only one per Mush. They can only infect once per day, excluding converting Schrödinger which can be made once in voyage and it might be done in the start, before any research being done. The Mush can also do the thing with human but no human will agree on this with Mush on board. It means 2 Alphas, 2 spores per day, so Antispore Gas doesn't affect the Mush that doesn't use any skills, of course when there's human outside camera, but human crew makes sure all are secured so there go MycoAlarms.

When I tell people that Constipaspore Serum is better than Antispore Gas to prevent Phagocyte rampage, they say that Constipaspore Serum doesn't stop Mush with Fertile so Antispore must be done. Wait, but Antispore Gas doesn't stop Mush with this skill as well, moreover they can make a second spore and eat to gain AP. With starting 2 Mush they may connect Fertile and Phagocyte do bypass this research, but Antispore Gas doesn't limit the two free spores they used. Maybe if there were Betas, but then either they rampage in the moment of conversion, or they activate Anonymush which requires Gold Mode and they sneak silently then attack, as Antispore Gas not done they have 4-8 AP more, everybody Fertile, Phagocyte and some Anonymush. But rampages with a few Betas mean game over even without Phagocyte.

Constipaspore Serum is preferred by people reading AP threads and other parts of forum. It was mentioned here why Constipaspore Serum is the better one, these ones who said that Antispore Gas left it without argument because there was nothing to say. With my words and what I didn't mention, Constipaspore Serum really helps with investigation. Mush using their limits Antispore Gas puts is least likely to be detected. Constipaspore Serum makes it difficult to extract more than 2 spores without being detected. With decent investigation, the Mush will most likely get killed if they spend their 8 AP on spores adding 2 AP to wash. Now imagine more than 2. Of course, Fertile is a thing to consider if it's worth spending a Mush slot, but still to wonder if the Mush is going to make more than 2 spores per day if they have only one free to make. And what would they use their spores for?

MycoAlarms are so important as they should knock Antispore Gas to the end of the queue. They prevent secret infection. Some biologists might argue that the number of MycoAlarms is limited and they only protect several places, but who would tell the Mush where MycoAlarms are? It's enough Chun walking around the ship and you don't know if it's in Engine Room, Refectory or one of the Bays. Mush also doesn't know, so Trapper and Fungal kitchen are unable to be used or reveal. Moreover, MycoAlarms remove the paranoia which inactives bring staying in corridors and being an easy target for Mush to make them look Alphas while the original one is becoming Anonymush and silently winning the game. The ship is limited to 6 rooms where crewmates meet each other: Bridge, Bravo Dorm, Alpha Dorm, Laboratory, Nexus, Icarus Bay. With Paranoid Gioele, 5 cameras usually secure each of these rooms except Icarus Bay. And people are staying in dorms and wait until they make Antispore Gas and Constipaspore Serum so they can finally make MycoAlarms to put one in Icarus Bay and launch an expedition safely. Or they get impatient and come to Icarus Bay waiting for a launch, human with a starting spore and 2 dirty agents of Mush, so 2+1=3.

What's useful with Antispore Gas? Ask crew. They hold tradition. But on serious note, they're useful when there are Beta so they won't make too many spores to convert. It mostly happens when you underestimate security and the true priority of research. It's so useful as Gioele's Panic skill, which you can see here. Antispore Gas might be really situational. If you see Mush extracting 2 spores in public when Antispore Gas is done, you might assume the Mush is unable to make them more, unless you have Mycelium Spirit. It might mean that people visiting shower are most likely human because Mush avoids shower which gives nothing but suspicions, unless it's psibertus with overthinking investigator like me who is convinced that the Mush is human at the moment of visiting Bravo Dorm with mentioned event having happened.

From what I have experienced, I've seen many Phagocyte rampages with Antispore Gas done. I've spent hours lecturing why Antispore Gas is useless. They had counterarguments which I managed to overthrow, despite this there was a few people who wanted to make Antispore Gas done for no real reason and they agreed, the Antispore Gas was done. I've been Alpha Mush about quarter of times and surviving long voyages I've never thought "I need more spores but Antispore Gas doesn't allow me to". My Mush team has never done more than 2 spores after first anti-Mush research made regardless of what research that was. I only convince people to make Antispore Gas when I am Mush, to gain trust for nothing. Once I was the head of research and made most of important anti-Mush research, but not Antispore Gas, with both Mush active who had regular access to shower - one got killed, second did an inefficient rampage.

Making an Antispore Gas with one Mush alive is an absurd. Why would one Mush make more than 2 spores or even use more than 2?
