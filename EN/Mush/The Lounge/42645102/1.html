First of all, I'd like to give a big HUGE applause to my surviving crewmates, which were amazing and were probably the creme of the creme of the Daedalus.
Brief summary (although I don't remember a lot, so anyone feel free to correct me):
Ship starts finishing to wake up around Day 3 if I'm correct.
Then griefer Derek murders Andie, and gets killed by Eleesha.
Later on, Paola hides stuff and gets killed (she was human griefer though).
Gioele gets killed for killing Chun (and I think he also hid some things), and Kuan Ti removes a camera and demoralizes (someone?) in Alpha Dorm. Those were the mush. Not bad, actually... fair skills mean fair players. I guess they acted based on the circunstances on the ship.

Anyhow, once we're mush/griefer free, we were left on a ship with... ehh, about 3 or 4 inactives, I think they were Jin Su, Stephen and Terrence, which they soon die of massive depression.
So they were gone too. Shoo.
That done, we devoted ourselves to just stay alive, since we were low on O2 and the RNG didn't want to give us a single O2 planet which (which didn't include a massive death threat)
Nevertheless, and in spite of all the bad odds, we did manage to survive for about 6 days (we were so close to D10!), but when the wave of hunters got too big for our only two pilots (glory-hogger Roland and Hua, because Frieda was the only one that could find us that O2 planet), it was clear that we needed to return to Sol.
So... yeah, that's that. Amazing ship, though. Not a single regret.
