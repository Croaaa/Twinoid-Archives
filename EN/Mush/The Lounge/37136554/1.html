Wasn't on the ship, but TSRD in case you didn't know; white flag cannot replace a diplomat at all. It's nowhere near as good as one - it's like being a diplomat for only intelligent life.

Your diplomat skill prevents fights with everything from insects to crystallite monsters; it effectively increases the odds of getting good results (or at least non-fight results) from every zone that could have resulted in a fight. It allows your crew to visit planets that probably would kill them without your Diplomat skill, to take less damage thus heal less on almost every planet, and to get more good results than otherwise possible.

For me, if my ship has a diplomat (or someone who can become one that game), I'm actually more worried about that person's survival than I am Chun's; that's how very much a diplomat is worth.
