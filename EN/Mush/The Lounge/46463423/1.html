As the one who just had his very first game ended I decided that this thread could have some importance. Well I don't know if my story deserves a full thread so let this one be continualy updated with many more newbie stories. Ofc most of those would be "meh" for an experienced player but not for us.

So when I started the very first ship I already read about half of mush wiki and some of the forum suggestions. I came prepared. Somehow I ended being Raluca. Maybe it was not a very good choice since her trait is so restrictive. And thats why I made a couple newbie mistakes which made me a suspect of being mush for sure. Hopefuly I managed to shrug it off by being helpful. And there was Hau almost certainly a mush. Forgeting to use apron one time after another, fast talking some ridiculous things... Even claimed to be pregnant by doing it with Stephen. Finola, a good experienced player, had enough and killed her for good. Though the visible noobish of this certaing mush backfired... The very reason that got Hua killed had converted Stephen to the mushroom cause. And he managed to conceal himself just in time. Second alpha mush was inactive Eleesha. Which is a shame. Soon afterwards Raluca, which is me as I said above, were converted too. Being a bit afraid of loosing the game I instantly suggested to lay low since we could easily drop all suspicion by killing inactive Eleesha. Next I thought we could rush Pilgred. And while we were still deciding our next moves major luck struck. Half inactive crew members began to die while ship was falling appart. And that made Stephen a captain. And thats it. You may guess what happened next. It was easy to convince crew to get Pilgred repaired asap... for coffee... While serum was far from being completed... So mush officially took over the ship, got help from remaining crew and joyfully pushed a button making jump to Sol as soon as Pilgred was online.

This game overall is great. Current ship was great. The crew was great (even with some inactives). And thats how I express myself about it. Had much fun. Thank you Stephen, Finola, Janice, Therence, Roland, Jin Su, Hua and others And sorry Finola. I was made mush only on day 7. Not earlier.
