Helloooo dear fellow crew mates, this is Eleesha Zhong-Williams speaking. What a journey... vampires and a kidnapped Teddy bear, mush traps, a sneaky mush, a Houdini cat, mush traps again, heroic drones, a friendly Mankarog and mush traps again...

Our trip started 20 days ago, and our first alpha mush, Kuan Ti, was killed rather quickly after a courageous attempt at defacing the logs in Alpha dorm and spiking me. Then followed a maddening hunt for the second mush that lasted about 10 days, and which nearly drove us mad from trying to decide who to cure and who to kill.

"So, should we cure Raluca ?"
"But she geniused the serum, so either she's not mush or she doesn't deserve to be cured."
"Should we kill her, then ?"

"Well, I don't think that Roland is mush."
"What about Frieda ? What do we do about her ?"

"Ok, so we cure Frieda, and if we're wrong, we kill Raluca, Roland, Janice and Hua."
"No, we should cure Hua and then kill Raluca, Frieda, Janice and Roland."
"What if we started by curing Roland ?"
"As for me, I don't think we should cure anyone."
"But we might kill innocent people."
"Then we cure someone, but who ?"
"How about Stephen, by the way ? No offence, Stephen, but you're not confirmed human."
"No, I'm not offended, but I rather suspect Eleesha."
"But I told you, I can't be mush, I killed Kuan Ti."
"Okaaay, then should we cure Roland ?"
"No, should we kill Raluca ?"
"I think we should cure Frieda. Or Hua. Or... or Frieda, actually. Or maybe Hua ?"

By some miracle, we ended up curing Frieda, who was our second mush, without killing anyone. But with a few migraines.

Then we began our quest for starmap fragments, exploring countless cristallite fields and encountering mush trap after mush trap... after mush trap (5 in all, I believe). We had almost given up hope when we decided to choose between two cristallite planets, one with a Mankarog and fuel, or one with no Mankarog and no fuel. The beast turned out to be a fluffy, friendly monster who gave us an artefact, but Hua got lost trying to find the last starmap fragment. We rescued her, fled the planet (who knows what fluffy monsters become after their nap ?) and decided on a last-chance planet, our worst expedition, which did allow us to find the last fragment, but also proved to be deadly. We scraped together all the food left on the ship, jumpkins, rations, coffees, fruits, even the remains of poor quarantined Derek, in the hope of calculating the coordinates of Eden and moving the ship before the end of the expo, but failed and couldn't save Andie. Frieda eventually hacked the command terminal and launched the trip to Eden after the expo, without waiting for our vampire commander to wake up (what kind of a vampire sleeps at night anyway ?).

And now that we've landed safely on the Promised Land, Chun and Eleesha have the honour to invite you all to their wedding, after which they plan on living a long, happy life with their baby daughters.
