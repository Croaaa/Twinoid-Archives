The Daedalus from Hell!

Well, I couldn't think of a better title... I suck at picking headlines. But I thought this ship deserved an AP thread after having such an abrupt and unexpected ending.

We were only 16 normal people on a normal ship... but we soon found it there was nothing normal on the crew, and that the ship it wasn't normal (sometimes it felt like a MMM sort of crew)... it was MURDEROUS!!!

Summary:
It was a great ship... really great. I mean, for any ship surviving till D17 is an awesome accomplishment! And we were no MMM nor Mushday ship! We had a really good crew, almost no inactives (and those who were, it was due to RL circumstances that couldn't be avoided).
There was a good synergy among all, despite some heated encounters between some crewmates, due to the stress we were under.
Yet, the RNG saw this (around D7 or so) and said... "Thou shalt not live!" But we did; for 17 days, until a planet went totally bananas on Derek and murdered him on C8. Afterwards on cycle (and day) change, 3 more deaths due to fire/injures/depression made it clear that we were not welcome anymore.

RIP Derek, Andie, Janice, Stephen... you will all be missed. You deserved to return to Sol with us.
Yet we got greedy, and that outcome wasn't expected at all.
