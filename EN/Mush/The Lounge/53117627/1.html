I was Paola on this ship, and things started out looking pretty good. We filled up by the middle of day two. There were three new players, Derek, Ian, and Hua, and we only had one inactive player. I started out by roleplaying a radio announcer and writing the show "Welcome to the Daedalus". Unfortunately real life got in the way and I had to abandon the role play.
We didn't make much progress over the first several days.
Our mush rampaged early, killing Janice and dismantling our second shower. They were caught before too much more damage was done. As a result, research was abandoned pretty quickly.
However, we got incredibly lucky on a couple points. Our magebooks were Pilot and Shrink, and that made up for losing Janice so early. We were also had really good luck finding planets and starmap pieces.
Unfortunately, the expedition to our most recent planet was only launched with three crew members, and it was kinda a dangerous planet... Ian, Frieda, and Jin Su were killed, with Derek dying of morale loss.
However, Jin Su managed to return with the last starmap piece before he died, and we made it to Eden with half our crew.
