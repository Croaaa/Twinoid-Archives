So for those who don't know a story game [SG] is where its the people who comment in this thread who decide what happens in the story, yes that means YOU!

I'll write a couple of introductory paragraphs and then you can decide what happens next!

I'll write the second part in a couple of days!

RULES:

1) Each person can contribute up to three times at each decision point [DP]

2) Each contribution must have its own d100 rolled.

3) The highest roll will win and that contribution will become part of the story!

4) The author (me) will be the deciding vote in a draw

5) Try to keep foul language to a minimum but that doesn't mean it needs to be PG!


The Storyteller is the fictional narrator of this story.


The Storyteller In the land of Araknosh, before the Age of Heroes, there was a great and terrible evil. Hordes of goblins and their orc overlords swept the southern plains, raiding human towns and massacring innocents; dragons prowled the northern mountains, slaughtering at a whim; to the east, elven war-bands judged all before them unworthy and exacted swift retribution; in the west...there were rumours of something far, far more terrible, a demon infestation had taken hold and was slowly moving towards the Citadel.

The Citadel, once a place of elegance and beauty, was now fearful and lifeless. The last remnants of humanity had fled there, hoping for a safe haven, instead they were conscripted and forced to serve King Hroth in his Royal Guard. Hroth, out of fear of his enemies, began to rule with an iron fist. With enemies drawing closer on all sides, capital punishment became commonplace...as did suicides, and every soul in the Citadel knew fear.

And now our brave hero enters the scene.

A figure stirs in one of dungeons of the Citadel, clutching their head, they groan and sit upright. They stare round at the grimy walls of their cell; water drips from the ceiling in one corner and mould appears to be a common theme. Mortar between the bricks crumbles at a touch, the cell obviously hasn't been repaired in a while. Where am I? They wonder How did I get here?

[DP]

Decision Point!
Lets find out who our brave hero is and how they ended up in the Citadel's dungeon!

How old are they?
Are they clumsy?
Are they clever or stupid?
Are they a talented swordsman or a wannabe archer?
Were they arrested or captured?
Are they inured or sick?

Have fun!
