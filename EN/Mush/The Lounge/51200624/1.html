Alright! Lets start Part 1 of this story! I must warn you that this was a 20 day ship filled with a ton of micromanagement and pre-planning. I have forgotten a lot of the early game, hopefully other members can fill in.

The ship started different from any other ship. I was trying to jump with fellow TE members, they all had Chun in their selection so I decided I would take the dive. I woke up as Chun on D1C1, alone. They didn't match my ship. I did a bit of inventory while the ship SLOWLY filled. Izpodtishka and Fennec eventually were able to get in, then a few other TE I didn't expect.

My rp was a bit different from most Chuns. I started with the cliche "I'm a nice and caring person" kind but, underneath, she was only doing it to get what she wanted. She was actually a psychopath, fairly uncaring, and completely insane! You'll find out why later... I saw the personalities pop in and the ship was EXTREMELY active! I knew it was either going to be a very hard ship or a very easy one depending...

Selection happens. I rush around looking for weapons to hide/grab. Paola had grabbed the grenade and Eleesha had grabbed the knife. I immediately asked for their locations. I found the knife in RC and Paola said she was still carrying the grenade. I decided to allow her to hold onto it. Sure she could've been mush but, if she wasn't, then the mush would probably never get a hold of it (Paola isn't known for being a prime target). I hid blasters when I was able to, kept the knife on me and picked up my armor.

A day passed, research was fairly active with little help from me (inventory can drain a lot from you). I had medlab bed as a lethargic Chun. My preparations were still on doing whatever I can to prevent a rampage. Derek was standing in the refectory, alone. Given the fast research and unprotected powerhouse, I decided I would contact the most trusted tech, Terrence. Raluca was helpful but it's always difficult to predict a Raluca mush since she's so hard to watch. Kuan Ti was... Messy... We'll get to that soon, though. So I contact Terrence and arrange a meeting in CAT where I would hand off my armor to him. I'm the kind of Chun that sees little value in protecting myself. MOH dies and the ship can still crutch home with a tech but if the techs die then the ship's chance of winning drops tremendously (besides, people usually assume Chun is protected, hence the secrecy).

Terrence was quick to go the turret at the start of the cycle and I quickly unite with him and hand him the armor... Then I waited for him to leave... And waited... And waited... Damnit Terrence go already! Finally he does and I happily start making my way back to medlab. When I got to CC I saw Stephen had arrived there just a moment ago. Oh okay. I'm going to check if he's dirty real quick. Hm, not dirty. I refresh and... this happens. I didn't even hesitate, I ran in, ate a ration, and ran out. To think, I would NEVER have caught this if Terrence wasn't dragging his feet (literally and figuratively).

Our first mush was dead (poor unlucky Stephen). The crew was more than a little confused at the sudden death of a crewmate. It was difficult to explain its simplicity and sheer randomness. The commander presented me with a reward. I then became known as a yoga master (master of the not-move-for-half-of-a-day-on-the-bed pose) and ultra ninja!
