What a trip! Kuan Ti here, and I would just like to say that this is what Mush is all about. Success and failures, drama (maybe a little too much), and working together. But let's break it down for the people who didn't come along.

End Page

-BEGIN TL;DR-

The start was a little rough. We had a wonderful fresh newbie Ian, who was already eagerly going about his duties. However, he made two mistakes: posting that he was dirty and had no idea how he'd gotten that way, and left out part of his actions that seemed very suspicious. I'm sorry that we killed you, but that's how the game goes sometimes. Better luck on your next ship!

Then our apparently inactive Derek woke... and turned out to be a griefer. He grabbed all the weapons he could get his hands on and went for Chun. Unfortunately for him, she was online and had the knife. He seriously hurt her and deafened her, but she survived and was able to receive help.

Next, was the first Bed Tantrum. Our pilot Andie, also fairly new, staked a claim on a bed... and was very displeased to find it occupied when they returned from shooting down hunters. The first we knew about it was a chat message that went something along the lines of "HOW COULD YOU WAAAAHHHH" (yes, actually 'waah' ). It got obnoxious very quickly, but eventually it blew over. (Andie later proved themselves fairly useful and pulled their weight. As such, I'd like to apologize again for being cranky while I thought you were being an entitled brat.)

Then, the Bed Tantrum. Hua returned from Piloting and,seeing Janice in a bed, was displeased. Janice should not require a bed, she thought, oblivious to the hard work Janice had been doing in Neron research and coordinating morale. Janice is only good for standing around to help boost people's morale, she thought. So she sent Janice a message asking her to get out, and when Janice declined, she punched Janice out of the bed and took it.

We did not learn of it through Janice. Eleesha, who was sleeping in that dorm, reported it and asked Hua to explain herself. Janice's response was to beat Hua to within an inch of her life. An argument broke out in the chat, with Hua and Janice screaming at each other, and Eleesha and I asking people to calm the heck down. Hua eventually subsided, but not before knocking Janice out of the bed again to prove her dominance.

Janice lost it, laying into Hua again and taking away the sliver of life she had left. When Eleesha and I disapproved and told her that the reaction was completely unjustified, she raged further. She spent the last of what had apparently been a full AP bar (contrary to her claim of needing the bed more) throwing punches at Eleesha, telling us that we should go ahead and kill her; she felt that without her, the ship would die from lack of morale. So we did. While her skills were extremely useful, we did not want such a toxic attitude as part of our crew.

Things started to look grim, then. We still hadn't done ANY anti-Mush research and had no leads on any suspects, meaning there were a lot of possible spores out there. Eleesha got to work, and finally found something odd: Raluca had barely done any work on Pilgred - only as much as the free Physician points would do, yet she hadn't put any AP into Neron projects. Not only that, but of all the people that could have relayed her, Jin Su had - apparently on private request, since he made no mention of the fact that he was back there. Eleesha scoured the logs until we had enough proof, and sure enough, Raluca was Mush. Unfortunately that meant that Schrodinger had to go as well, since he had been cuddled since the Mush were selected. Additionally, it put further suspicion on Jin Su, as he had not only relayed with Raluca without visible request, but he had been the one to bring Schrodinger to where he was accessible to the crew.

We knew Jin Su had to die, but our oxygen was critically low. So we played off like we didn't know, and organized an expedition. Jin Su "went offline" in Icarus Bay, willing to be a body especially since we knew there was still a mush out there. Stephen joined him, and Paola and Terrence were on their way together after a morale boosting session. So it caught us all by surprise when the expedition launched - with only three people. Jin Su had waited for Terrence to enter the room and clicked the button before Paola could follow. This made us worried - Stephen had been sarcastic and rebellious in chat, objecting how the Mush Hunt was being handled, and thus was suspicious, Jin Su was almost certainly a Mush. That meant that if they had the right skills, we could lose our only technician to accidents. Imagine our surprise when Terrence came back, alive and fine, the ship had gained full oxygen.... and it was Stephen who got lost planetside.

Frantically, we tried to plan a rescue, hindered by the same poor coordination that had hindered the Research. Stephen even requested that we leave him if we couldn't be bothered coming down to save him. We were sure that Jin Su would try to leave if we went down to get Stephen, dooming the ship, but if we killed Jin Su then, Stephen would have died from morale loss.

Then, Jin Su made his move. He grabbed the soap, a fire extinguisher, and a container of fuel, and flew off the ship in a fighter. Demands that he return were futile, since not only had he resigned himself to his fate, but he had dropped his talkie and couldn't hear us. We hurriedly launched the expedition to save Stephen while having the Front Corridor guarded, so he couldn't land and jump the ship, but he didn't even try. After we found our lost cook and a bit of discussion, we jumped the ship, leaving Jin Su behind to his small victory.

After that, things fell into place. We were gifted with the best mage-books: technician, biologist, and diplomat. Terrence and I (with the help of the tech book), started working on Pilgred. We started researching again, led by Chun (with the Biologist book) and Eleesha, who had plenty of AP to spare since she wasn't tracking mush anymore; working on Pheromodem to be sure that we were mush-free (it would at least inform us if people were getting spored). Mycoscans showed us all to be clean. Andie took the Diplomat book to help us in the quest for artifacts, extra drones were made, Neron updates gave us Plasma Shield and other wonderful toys. Unfortunately, it was too much. Things were breaking down at an incredible pace, the hunters were swarming, and our new commander, Gioele, was having trouble getting online. Without a regularly active commander, we didn't have a good chance at Eden, so we finished the serum and returned home.

-END TL;DR-
