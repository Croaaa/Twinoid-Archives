So I am the Stephen from the voyage and I have to say that from the Mush point of view this ship was quite the laugh but let's start at the beginning.

Well at this point I know that it's only a matter of time before the fun starts.

At this point I've spored once and stealthed washed myself in Bravo Dorm playing inactive. A few cycles later I come back to Paola announcing over the General Announcements that her tongue was bitten off and that she was dirty. After clicking this away I see that both Paola and Janice were both dead. OH THE JOY!! Later I found out that Gioele killed Paola. I still don't know who did Janice in, but I have a feeling it was Gioele. Three Down

Terrence who was Silver I believe questions why Gioele had been so rash and suggests getting rid of him. After reading through all the chat decided to take a swing at everyone in Alpha Dorm including me then hides the soap in front of us. Obviously he just wanted to quit the ship after what Gioele's rampage thus far. As you could probably guess Terrence is dead within 45 minutes with Gioele shouting in general chat about how dumb it was to talk about killing the only 'confirmed' human. It turns out that Gioele had the skills Paranoid(YES HE WAS) and Coffee Junkie had bought a coffee thermos which had been solely used on killing teammates so far. Four Down

Derek goes and grabs the knife which is used to end Gioele. Turns out to everyone's surprise but mine that Gioele has been human this whole time. Five Down

Well considering that this ship was so crippled I felt that there was only one humane thing to do and that was to spore one more time and lay down in the mostly empty Bravo Dorm to rest up. Of course I am just going to use Phagocyte and try to kill off the rest of the crew as quick as possible. I don't think the humans that remained really wanted to continue.

So after resting a few cycles I was going to kill the most important active crew members in the order of: Raluca(Only chance for PILGRED left, Derek, Jin Su, and etc. Then I noticed my Mush brother Andie had awoken. I had been continuously keeping a log of the events summarized in the mush channel to inform her of what was going on as it happened so she could easily catch up. After stating how happy I was to see she was awake and that we should be able to quickly rap up this ship I walk away from my computer for a few minutes. What I come back to left me gobsmacked. I was dead and my killer was Andie who went out to grab the knife and kill me then Jin Su. Seven Down

Well after that I don't know what really happened. From the messages left in the wrap up screen it seems like Derek might have attacked or killed Raluca. Which I feel makes zero sense given how the ship was when I died. Of course the ship fell apart and only a total of seven crew members died of something other than assassination.

KobbFunny, I was just involved in an argument over whether it was ever okay for mush to kill mush.

To clarify things, because I see a lot of people who don't really know the rules : it is not completely forbidden for a mush to kill their partner, as some people believe. Of course, you can't denounce your partner, draw attention to something that no one else would have noticed, or kill them out of the blue.

But if the other mush has done something very obvious and humans know that you must have noticed it, then you have to report it. For instance, if you walk in on your partner extracting a spore, failing to report it will get you killed too. You can also be asked to kill a mush, and if humans know that you're online at that time, refusing will only make you a suspect (or get you killed).

As for the ship discussed in the thread, as npfan pointed out, Andie didn't directly kill Stephen, and it's impossible to know if he meant to kill him. The grenade was apparently thrown to injure Jin Su before killing him. Stephen only died of injuries because his health must have been below 8, and that wasn't Andie's fault. You should never let your health be so low.

Maybe Andie was careless, maybe he didn't care about collateral damage, even if it was his partner. But maybe it was accidental, and at any rate, he didn't hit Stephen directly.
