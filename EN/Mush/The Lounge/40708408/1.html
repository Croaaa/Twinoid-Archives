Not only that, because Jin Su got himself killed early on, I soon find myself a solo Pacifist mush, who is already a suspect thanks to that MycoAlarm fail!

Fail number three

Thanks Jin Su's death, RFS is now a possibility, so it becomes impossibly hard for me to get any humans killing each other. I lost the trust of my crew thanks to the MycoAlarms fail. So, I only have one option available to me: rampage.

I waited VERY long for a time most of the crew is offline (this is HARD on this ship...Chun with RFS is like always online). Anyway, finally, I couldn't wait anymore. I made a guess of when Chun is offline, and then executed my rampage plan, which is to transfer into Derek and kill the pilots, since we have a hunter problem.

Well. Where do I start.

1. Derek only had 2 APs
2. My second attack gave me a Punctured Lung, which put a stop to my rampage
3. Chun secretly hidden a Pilot magebook, so my plan wasn't that good anyway

Not too long later, Chun woke up and RFS-ed me.

===My new human life had a rough start.===

I had Punctured Lung and 0 APs. I can't even drop things or lie down in a bed. I was losing 2 HPs every cycle from that injury, and I wasn't able to communicate at all (thanks to injury AND lack of talkie)!

By some miracle, the Medlab bed was empty, so I stayed awake in RL as long as I can just so I can do self-surgery (I had to wait in Medlab for 2 cycles to get 2 APs to lie down in bed, and then another 2 cycles to get 4 APs to do the surgery). I was down to 3 HP by the time I did the surgery, and I had just enough APs to use a Bandage, ran into BD where my talkie was, and beg the people I just tried to kill for some emergency healing.

It was pretty scary. And very humiliating. When I finally got my talkie back, I realised the entire human crew was laughing at my odd behaviour, not realising what I just went through. I spent the rest of the day explaining myself trying to regain a modicum of respect.

So eventually, Terrence and Derek started to plot our mutiny. They scraped together a coffee, the last jumpkin of the day and the last ration, Terrence fixed the hacked kit, and Derek hacked us back to Sol.
