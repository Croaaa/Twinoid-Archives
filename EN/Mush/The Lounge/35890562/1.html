[AP] Rise of the Tapeworm King

http://mush.twinoid.com/theEnd/2485

Alright, I'll start by saying that this was a great ship. Not only we dealt with MUSH menace with style (check below), but also we turned half of the universe in steaks. Breakage, yes we had some. Fires? You mean sparkles. Hunters? Check my shield and hull... oh, that's right, you can't because you just got killed by our pilots.
I think I can safely assure that by day 5 we had our researches and projects fully done, and the rest of the journey was only finding planets and doing expos (I'll thank our awesome astronavs for finding great planets).

Now, it all started one Sunday evening, when everyone woke up... kind of dizzy dazzled, but we all picked up quickly our senses. It didn't take long for the crew to organize and start working on research and projects. Naturally, given the activity-ness of the ship, the sneaky mushes hardly had any chance to make their naughtyness and soon gave in to our sharp hunting crew. Killed one, cured the other. Great human twist.

With our crew almost full, it was merely a matter of finding the starmap pieces. Soon after, we found the first, and got the Calculator.
Alas, despite our best efforts, fate had already decreed that it would not be joyful and pleasant, and on one planet our beloved Commander Jin Su stumbled upon more harm than good. We mourned deeply his loss, for a great one it had been, but we knew that there was only one way to honor his departure and sacrifice. To reach Eden at all cost.

And so, lead by the not less fierce, and newly appointed, Commander Chao, by day 9-10 we got the second one, and... LO! Behold! By day 11 the third starmap piece was in our hands.

Suddenly we found ourselves in front of an unexpected choice. Should be reach Eden or perhaps take our chances on reaching day 20 and beyond? After a serious votation, the crew decided to reach Eden.

So, we found and reached Eden. Yet, that was merely the ending of a great adventure that started about 11 days ago, during which bonds were created melted together. Some of us were less skilled, while others had a more extensive knowledge... yet in a way, I'd like to think that everyone in this ship was a golden companion. In this ship, where 16 unique individuals were placed, and only 14 of them would reach destination. Eden is now, but the memories will remain forever.
