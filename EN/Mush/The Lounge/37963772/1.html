Hallnote!

While we wait on maintenance to end, why don't we start a little forum game to pass the time?

I'd like to see what skills are everyone's most and least favorites! So lets do a Hurt and Heal to see what skills are best-liked!

The rules are simple! Each skill in the game starts with 10 points. Once per page, each unique Twinoid name may choose to either heal 1 skill (giving +1 points) and hurt another (giving -1 points), or choose two separate skill to hurt (giving -1 points each). When a skill hits 0 or 100 it is removed from the list and placed on the bottom or top of a ranked listing.

Please heal skills that you think are the best.
Please hurt skills that you think are the worst.

Since Mush skills are so wildly different than other skills, I'm going to have two separate tracks. One for Mush skills, one for Human skills. That means you can vote in *each* track!
