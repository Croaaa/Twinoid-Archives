The title of this AP is a tribute to our beloved jumpkins and their lovely side effect. We found out on this ship that when you’re in need of a planet with a specific thing, all you need to do is to get a pumpkin head and you shall find what you need. After 3 expos where our oxygen pump jammed (thanks to a mush traitor…) and Frieda wasn’t finding a good planet for us to go, I went to give it a try, ate a jumpkin and found the most perfect planet ever. Very safe, few food and artifacts zones and the precious o2. Some days later we were in urgent need of HC so Frieda decided to become a believed of the power of the pumpkinheads, she ate one jumpkin and of course she found us a wonderful planet full of HC.

But now for a small recap of major events

Chao’s death. - As Janice, I started by becoming incredibly happy to see an active Jin Su doing speeches very early on. As the crew started waking up, we were seeing some people that knew what to do but some others that were completely clueless like Chao that was almost finishing some useless project in nexus instead of oxygenated ducts. We all gathered to try to save the ducts and we were able to finish them even before everyone was awake. But Chao was marked as a possible griefer already since he was also holding Schrodinger and had no walkie. I tried to whisper to him asking if he was a new player and needed help but was met with silence. On day 2 he then decided to work on another random neron project instead of a more important one and we decided to kill him, fearing he would end up working on something like pneumatic distributor while plasma shield was up.

Stephen and the pilot book - We had 5 pilots at the start of the game since everyone that could have the skill, had it. And then when the game actually started, we got a pilot mage book. As soon as I finished saying that no one should read the book without the crew agreeing, Stephen (that wasn’t a chef!) read it and became immediately suspected. He then pulled some weight shooting hunters so we lowered our suspicions.

Paola’s death - Paola started the game being an active player, putting cameras in dorms and doing some organization threads. Then, after mush were assigned she was barely speaking, she went to the bridge where she had her sofa and did absolutely nothing in comms during the first two days. This behavior was screaming “MUSH” and we quickly killed her only to realize she was human. I never understand why such a big change in behavior…

Roland the mush - Roland seemed to be a newbie player that wasn’t being that much helpful. He got dirty on day 1 cooking, he got dirty on day 2 loading 1 fuel when we needed 6 and got dirty on day 3 (i think) cooking again and then announced he would be away for some days because he was traveling. We were already suspecting he could be mush but after his name was on Eleesha’s list together with mine (that was a negative on xyloph), Finola’s (that we trusted) and Terrence’s (that we also had no reasons to suspect he was mush) we were sure it was him. We had the natamy riffle and I had the pleasure of using it for the first time to get us rid of Roland.

She volunteered for a food test and while I usually don’t agree with mush testing, since it was such a weird situation and she was volunteering, I went to get food for the test. she then said she would do the test next cycle because she was out of ap… My riffle was getting itchy and I was refreshing the page several times waiting for her to something mushy… but I’m glad I had patience. After remembering that you don’t need ap to eat, she took a pill and a ration making me very relieved to confirm she was human.

Curing Hua As days went by and with one mush dead we still had the second mush to find. We had some main suspects due to their lower efficiency/odd behavior: Gioele first, Ian, Raluca. After day 4 I started going to the refectory every day and saw that Hua never went there. We thought she could be eating some of the fruit from expos but no one ever saw her eating (although apparently I wasn’t keeping a good track of fruit in icarus and she ate some there). So I started suspecting her. Then we had some terrible luck with expos, 3 jammed oxygen pumps (although Hua wasn’t in 1 of the expos), one expo where Frieda almost died (and that Hua launched while I was dealing with the Finola situation so they went on an expo with 3 people, few weapons and no diplomat…). Then at one time Frieda saw in the mycoscan that she had one spore that she had probably got in a trapped bravo bay. Gioele had passed by the bay before to go to a turrent and Hua had entered the bay and left right after for no apparent reason. To me all was evidence enough that Hua was mush and we decided to finish the serum fast and use it on her. One cycle before we cured her, I got scared that Jin Su was mush, he had eaten a jumpkin one cycle before and had no pumpkin head. No one knew if he had gotten it at all and got cured in cycle change or if he never had it at all. So we planned the cure of Hua without telling Jin Su about it and we were very happy when, after being cured, Hua told us there were no mush left.

Final Days After having no mush left, we entered in survival mode. We had plenty of oxygen, we were finding fruit and food in expos, hull and plasma shield were almost maxed but we started running low on fuel and some of the people became less and less active. We also lost Kuan Ti at one point, he had 6 hp left and asked for healing. He was on a different dorm so I told him I could heal him next cycle and he got a steep plate during cycle change. Gioele also died with a steel plate on the last day (but he was very inactive/uncommunicative anyway). We weren’t having that much luck with expos, even after having no traitor. We were suffering lots of accidents, not finding that much fuel and didn’t find any star map fragment. So we decided to go back to sol before we started losing more people due to lower activity.

Final Comments I had a great time on this ship, I never went to day 10 with a ship in such a good condition. The mush hunt was also fun and it was my first ship where we cured one mush. Special mention to Jin Su and Frieda, with whom I talked the most. Frieda was finding us new planets to go all the time and driving everyone to do what was needed. Jin Su did an excellent job, he was always there to move the ship when we needed him. Finola, Terrence, Kuan Ti and Chun also did a great job. Hua was very sneaky as mush and I can’t imagine being easy to be almost alone as mush for so long with an active crew like we were. I was very happy we cured instead of killing her because she was such a great help for the ship. Ian wasn’t really very communicative and we suspected him for a while because he didn’t seem to be working very efficiently but ended participating more as days went by. Eleesha didn’t talk much due to language barriers but after being appointed comms officer she did a great work there. Gioele I’m not sure what we was doing besides walking around the ship… Raluca was apparently being played by someone with some experience in the game but that was a terrible team player.
