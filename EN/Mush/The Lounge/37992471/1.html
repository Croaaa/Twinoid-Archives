We tried to stay stealthy, but over time, people either food tested themselves or got sick, and I knew that the process of elimination was going to narrow it down to my new friend and myself. So I did the logical thing, and flipped the nearest table (onto Janice, of course). I miscalculated my AP and didn't do nearly as much damage as I would have liked to. But I was mutated, so there was no talking my way out of the situation. Kuan Ti joined me in my rampage, and after that I'm not quite clear what happened, but the ship lasted a lot longer than I would have hoped.

I didn't think this ship was particularly amazing, but the crew had a few really cool members. Chun, before she died very early, seemed very involved and active. Kuan Ti and Eleesha were both very good for newbies. Paola was the first to die of starvation and then the thick tube burned in a fire before the sofa was built, but despite this, Eleesha did an excellent job at comms. And Kuan Ti was a very good first-time killer alien, and he demonstrated a good understanding of in-game tactics and rules after a very short time to learn them in as a mush. And Raluca was pretty awesome, even if she missed a few days due to a computer crash and came pretty close to starving to death.

I really would have liked to been with this crew as a human. But I expected more mush hunting. Also, we had a mute Finola, and so I was able to take advantage by taking credit for some of her research, making it look like I was spending more AP than I actually had. Chao took to the commander role early, and Eleesha took the comms officer role early, and virtually nobody else was doing any serious mush hunting.

Near the end, that last expo with the cristalite, was probably the mush's last good chance to convert anyone new. Kuan Ti and I both had a spore extracted and ready, and Kuan Ti took the mush Traitor skill, so we were really hoping for the cristalite mush trap, and then hopefully spiking our preferred target as soon as the expo finished. Aside from that, the only person who got spored was Terrence, and anyone who hugged the cat after Day 3.

Anyway, it ended for Kuan Ti and I on Day 6. I ran the microwave and wrecked the signal, and then went to mutate and attack Janice, and Kuan Ti joined me shortly after. I'm not quite sure what happened after that, but I died the next cycle at the hands of a crew mate, possibly Choa.
