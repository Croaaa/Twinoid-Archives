There are 4 types of mush players.

The ones that are constantly online, refreshing constantly to check crew actions ect, and are online at least 6 cycles a day.

There are the casual players that pop in from time to time throughout the day, but never stay tied down to the game.

There are the less casual players that are online once a day which is entirely fine for the character they play.

Then there are the inactives.

You have a choice to play out of all four choices (albeit it's against the rules to do the last one on purpose), just be a casual gamer if you want, its entirely fine. I had a bad laptop while I was waiting for my replacement and I was one of those constantly online people with the 5s refresh time, but once I got my replacement, my mush time lowered. That didn't make me a worse player, and sure I was definitely installing all the high spec games to run and gun and play them till I dropped. Sure I had less time for mush, and sure it wasn't as fun as the high spec games on my newly bought laptop; but Mush has something these high powered games do not.
