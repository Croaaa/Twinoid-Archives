Good evening, humans and vampires. This is your resident stowaway, and not your resident cook. For the last time, I am not a chef. It would be a grave mistake to ask me to prepare even a simple sandwich.

Anyway... I woke up one fine night on a strange ship, feeling completely frozen and surrounded by strange people. A young woman with pink hair, a grumbling technician with a cat in a foul mood, a man woman person waving enthusiastically at the ships firing at us from outside, a gardener wailing about his plants being burnt, an old woman wearing some kind of witch outfit. I also met a fascinating young woman with incredible hair and amazing energy, who immediately dragged me into bed (can't say that I complained, though).

As we were preparing an expedition on the unknown planet that we were orbiting, we learnt some bad news : two of us were Mush spies. We managed to launch a hastily organized expedition, with Kuan Ti holding the hacker kit, and during which Janice lost her walkie, thus becoming mute for five days.

Soon after, Jin Su raised the alarm. Eleesha, coming from the lab, had appeared dirty on the bridge. Her reasons were not convincing and, after a few discussions, I was sadly forced to execute my lover. That was our first and only sacrifice to Delzoup, the Goddess of Mush Hunters.

In the days that followed, we tried to discover who the second mush was, and if they had managed to convert someone, but they were proving elusive. One after the other, the crew members got confirmed. Some people starved, others were ill, and the Xyloph list confirmed five or six people. The mush hunting channel was getting more and more crowded as we kept inviting confirmed humans. Andie, Jin Su, Terrence, then Paola, Frieda, Derek, Ian, inactive Gioele, and finally Hua, Raluca and Kuan Ti. Eventually, only three suspects were left : Roland, Janice... and me, who was starting to feel uncomfortable as the only non confirmed human of the channel. Pheromodem had revealed nothing, so we decided to try our luck with RFS... but on who ? Since Ian was holding the serum and was asleep, we had time to discuss, including the possibility of forcing a food test (or worse, a Natamy test) on the remaining suspects.

You know, guys, discussing the possible murder of a suspect right in front of him is never a good idea.

At some point during the night, Janice gave me a fright by going to the lab, where Chun was staying, along with semi-inactive Derek who probably had full AP. Not too subtly, I grabbed a blaster, ran after her, and watched as she quietly relayed research, constantly keeping an eye on her skills and Derek's. Eventually, she went back to bed, and in the morning Andie was able to use RFS on her... and yes, she was our second mush.

She didn't confirm that there were no other mush left on the ship, though. Interestingly enough, Roland chose that moment to catch an illness, making me the last non confirmed human. I felt some stares and worried glances in my direction, and again demands for food tests were made, which I refused. Unfortunately, Jin Su caught me with a pumpkin head (I rushed to bed so as not to be seen, but it seems I wasn't fast enough), I must say that I was a little disappointed, because I would have loved to keep on the paranoia for a little while. But... oh well, we were officially mush free.

We dedicated the rest of our trip to finding the starmap fragments, which has rarely been as easy. It took time, but I think that nobody ever got lost on a planet, and we brought an amazing number of jumpkins from the planets we visited. And so, after one last peaceful expedition which gave us three artefacts including the third starmap fragment, here we are, about to start the final trip to Eden.

Thank you for that ship, everyone. With a special thought to Ian, who died tragically, another victim of the murderous Daedalus, and who would have deserved to come to Eden with us. And to Frieda, my old vampire partner. Another Eden with you !

The ship hasn't ended yet, and should go to Eden in about an hour or two. But as I should go to bed, I am already posting the AP thread and will add the link for the ship later. Will also explain the vampire story later, unless Frieda does it before me.
