Spazticus
A bug on Day 2 wiped out all of our rations. There was a camera in the room, and just a few cycles later, 30 rations were just...gone. They couldn't have been cooked, taken, or hidden. (I did already post about it in the Bugs thread, but that's a fail through no fault of anyone on the ship.) Murphy's Law seems to be in effect on this ship, in a way that not even our Mush could have taken credit for...
So we knew we had to get to a planet quickly just so we'd have food (as opposed to the usual first requirement for a planet, food). So we get a planet, we get to the planet... We go on an expedition! 10% chance of pump failure on the O2 zone... Bummer, but we got 4 fruit and 4 steaks (which were thrown in the freezer Stephen moved to the IB), and we weren't horrible need of O2 yet. Plenty of time to get it on the next planet.

So we need to leave orbit, Ian puts fuel in the tank, says it's ready to go. Hua posts that she had taken a patrol ship out and can't get back yet so not to leave yet. Jin Su sends the ship out of orbit and we lose our only pilot.

... I'm actually blanking on exactly just what happened with Jin Su after that before he was outted as a Mush and killed... Revealing that he had taken AnonyMush, and that there are at least 3 Mush on board by this point.

And Paola was also taken out, but as it turned out she was just a griefer and wasn't a mush.

Anyway, we got the MycoAlarms researched. (okay, recalling a bit more, like Jin Su moving one of the alarms, but don't remember the rest aside from a conversation about mush.) I moved one of the MycoAlarms from the lab to the MedLab (just dropped it, didn't hide it) where a few of us were. They left sometime after that. I decided to lay down on the bed, since while there wasn't a camera there, there was an alarm, and anybody who picked it up would be suspicious.

I check back on the game, look in the actions in the room... 6 minutes prior, Raluca had entered the MedLab. Which is suspicious since she really has no reason to go anywhere more towards the front of the ship than the central corridor (since it leads to the Refectory). But not nearly as suspicious or condemning as the BRRRRIIIIINGGGG... that went off right before she left. She didn't even notice the alarm before sporing me. (in her defense, there were still a lot of pills even if she looked at the shelving unit first) I promptly inform the rest of her Mush status and they take care of her.

It's day 4. We are really starting to get low on oxygen though, so we need to get to a new planet ASAP. Gioele and Frieda both provide planets with O2, Gioele's with 2 unknowns, and Frieda's fully analyzed (nothing truely disastrous). We go with Frieda's. Chao gets back on before we have newbie Chun use the hacker kit on the bridge (Chao wasn't that inactive, so I'm not quite sure why that was being pressed for since it can take a lot of AP to successfully use the hacker kit.) and we are off to the planet.

We get to the planet, start an expedition, and before I know what's happening, Stephen is shouting that Frieda is a Mush. As she had said there were too oceanic zones, where as there was only 1 and the other was actually a volcanic zone. So four of us (Finola (Me), Stephen, Ian, and Chao) are stuck on the expedition while Gioele goes after Frieda in the Bravo Dorm. In the middle of Gioele attacking Frieda, for no reason Kuan Ti starts attacking Gioele down to 1 health.

Meanwhile on the expedition, FATIGUE EVERYWHERE. First there was an accident on landing (Stephen), then there was an accident with predators(Chao), then 3-4 zones fatigued us. Everything chipping away at our health (Chao got down to 1 HP). We hit the volcano.... Thankfully nothing happened. But before we get back Stephen gets lost.

I run to the Lab to grab the Medikit (luckily I was full on AP), and run back to the Bravo Bay to heal Gioele and then back to the IB and heal myself and Chao. We quickly go back down to the planet (we still had 4 zones, including O2). We find Stephen and some fuel. We get to the O2 zone... And... Yeah... 10% chance of pump getting jammed... again... Well, that sucks, we had only a few O2 left.

We get back to the ship, I heal Stephen, and they go off and finish off Mush Frieda and Kuan Ti. I go grab fuel to put in the combustion chamber so we can escape the 12 hunters on our tail and hopefully maybe be able to get to a new planet... I for no particularly sound reason, check the fuel level before putting fuel in... I get dirty. I have 0 AP and 0 MP and am in the engine room... I also seem to have caught a cold from the expedition (which has a chance of eating my AP). And after they killed the Mush, I was down to 5 morale... Luckily Chao still had his mission to assign and gave it to me so I could get to the bravo dorm where Janice was and still have enough AP to take a shower. The cold was healed the next cycle too.

Janice comforted me for some more morale. On cycle 8 (which I was up really late to actually be awake at cycle 8...) I went to check the plants just in case to see if they were diseased, since we needed the O2 they'd give us next cycle (Day 5). Inactive Roland had just died that cycle from lack of oxygen. I head to bed. I wake up the next day, check MUSH, and find... I died the very next cycle after I went to bed. Apparently inactive Eleesha got back on just in time to send me to O2 deprivation instead of her... Aside from Stephen being the next to go, I don't know what happened after that. XD

And we still never found the soap or the drill...
