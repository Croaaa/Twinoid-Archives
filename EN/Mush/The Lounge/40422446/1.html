Sub Lieutenant Andie here! Good evening sirs and ma'ams!

Boy oh Boy! What a ride this was.

The early game went well enough. I had pushed out Antispore and constispore early to cripple the mush spore production. All had went well up until we went on our very first expo.

During the expo we lost Derek due to a bug. No sooner had Derek been "abandoned" then suddenly, mysteriously, someone ELSE died on the Daedalus. The Expedition team, consisintg of Jin Su, Kuan Ti, Myself, and Roland, were all biting our nails as we waited for that last countdown. None of us had walkies due to an int life zone (which stole my bloody compass -.- )

When we got back we found what had happened. Gold Hua, an alpha had our second shower before Stephen could reinforce it, stole the soap, Medkit, and apron, transferred into Frieda, killed her old body, and launched the Pasiphae.

Rather than give up, we decided to try and rush Magnetic net, pushing through a good four projects in the space of a day! That included, of all things, plasma shield, which went up incredibly quickly. Despite my reservations, Fire detector and Anomoly detector proved to be invaluable throughout the entire Journey. In the end, it proved to be a good idea to push through projects even though we never saw the Magnetic net because...

Ian, my wonderful lab partner, was Mush!

Likely taking a cue from his partner, he slimetrapped most of the crew, including Paola and myself, and took off with the wrench and an extinguisher. Further crippling us! But not without first trying his damnest to murder me in my sleep. Little did they know that I only had 2 HP after their attack! I got very lucky, and it spurred Paola and I to quickly fix the combustion chamber and hack the CC to abandon both of our alphas. Vital equipment or not! To counter the loss of pilots, I ended up going gold and eventually ending up as our primary pilot on the ship!

Of course, our woes were not over. Because we had both showers dismantled and no apron, research pretty much ended there. Denying us the cure. We quickly implemented a strict regime for showers. Everyone who was going to shower had a specific day to do so, and the people who showered were required to eat as much as they could before they did so, so they would not need to eat for several days.

Naturally Gioele ignored this and stole Paola's shower on day 4. After an argument, he took the hacker kit messing with bios and started tearing up items and eating in the Icarus bay. He was promptly murdered afterwards for his waste. Paola landing the finishing blow. The next few days were busy, as I had to struggle to regain AP, and several people had to fix their flagging morale. Injuries were difficult to handle as well, as the only place we could heal people was medlab.

That said, eventually I got the second sofa plan built in the refectory, in addition to all the coffee I could eat. Jin su remained clean through the mush attack, and became our primary planet scanner. Finding us many, many good planets (Roland, in Ian's body, remarked that he was like Frieda reborn). A few times our O2 or HC search was tight, but we always managed to find what we needed. I will not forget Janice, Stephen, Ian, and Kuan Ti. All four being major players in our expeditions! While Jin wasn't as active as he could have been, he was very responsive and good about moving us. Those few times he wasn't, we normally found someone to move the ship for us.

The hardest part of the later journey was keeping everyone at full health. Without the medkit things became very dangerous at times. We couldn't heal someone unless they were online and able to move to medlab. This, unfortunately, was difficult to explain to Raluca. She did not speak english well, and only reported her health and morale when she was in danger. This proved to be her undoing when, at 1HP/3Morale, nobody was online to heal her.

As day 12 drew to a close, fires had injured Janice and Kuan Ti, bringing them both extremely low on health. With the risk of losing at least two people next cycle, I decided that enough was enough and moved us to Sol before we lost too many more good players to the ship.

It was a pleasure serving, Sir's and Ma'ams! I would do so again. (Especially you Ioland!)
