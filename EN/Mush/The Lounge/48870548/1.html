In which the most notable thing that happened was a chain of events starting with a chitchat and ending with two unnecessary deaths, not to mention mass confusion. Here is to you all that stuck through it.

The journey started strongly enough - the first alpha was revealed quite soon due to some kind of mistake from Raluca. Defacing was used and terrence was spiked, but not converted. Our other alpha seemingly laid low, really low. way low.

After that, the ship settled into a general routine. Research proceeded at a fair pace and all eyes were open for the next mush move. Roland was inactive and was eventually sold to alien slave traders for extra oxygen - his only contribution to our trip other than complaining in the endscreen. Our Chun turned out to be a bog-standard noncommunicative, erratic and unhelpful type. Our Janice was inactive and, naturally, out of position to be even passively helpful. Ian was silent but did his work. Jin Su was minimally present in all respects. Standard fare, but the rest of the crew were fantastic. With the help of Raluca's corpse, we eventually got to the point where the major anti mush research was complete, mycoscanners up and running and spore extractors to remove the hitchhikers we had picked up one way or another.

Finally, at the dawn of a new days cycle, Terrence moved into bdorm to chitchat with Andie (our only real source of morale). Thirteen minutes later, Andie opened up on Terrence, explaining that the chitchat had revealed two Extract A Spore actions. In the process, Terrence lost his ears but was able to get out that Andie was lying about the chit-chat and food-tested himself before diving into medlab with Ian.

With apparent clear evidence that T was human, the lone active human felt there was only one other possibility: Andie was mush and lying about the chitchat results after failing to finish off the only technician. So Stephen moved in to attack Andie, who fled to adorm before being finished off a cycle or two later.

Human.

In the mass confusion that followed, checking and double checking the room activities between the players and with the knowledge that Stephen was still human from a death-inspired illness, the only explanation seemed to be this: Andie had transferred at the moment of death.

As Eleesha still had her skillbook skill, and as Stephen was apparently still human, there were only two candidates - Hua and Paola.

Both maintained their innocence, and Paola produced evidence of a snack bar she had intended to use for comms work that night. Eleesha made a decision, and Hua fell.

Human.


Well, shit.



It was then that Paola offered up this seed of an idea: *What does using the spore extractor look like in a Chit-chat log?*

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3O8J2...

Well, shit.


As it turns out, Terrence had two spores from the first day with Raluca business. We had researched spore extractor more or less for the express purpose of getting them out. Andie didn't make the connection. It did not occur to Stephen. Terrence was fighting for survival and did not think to mention that the events might be connected and in any case could not communicate after cycles end.

Extract A Spore

So two humans died for no good reason. It is with profound regret that we dedicate our successful mission and return to sol to them. Our remaining mush, Jin Su, never really did much. He had been (one of?) the main suspect for some time with increasing unease towards Andie and possibly frieda. RFS was completed, Frieda was chosen for stabbing, and with her eliminated as a suspect, Jin Su was smothered and we were mush free to wrap up.


So here we are, a cautionary tale and a pretty fun ship despite the awful mistake: Be very careful about reading into the results of a premonition/torture/chitchat, as at least one rather critical action turned out to be very ambiguous versus another.
