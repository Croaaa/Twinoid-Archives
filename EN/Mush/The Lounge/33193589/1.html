I was Hua in this game.

It's interesting how difficult the game stays even after the Mush are dead. Ian and Eleesha revealed themselves pretty fast, so by day three we had a clear ship.

While we did a good job working together and surviving, we were a bit less lucky; continuous and regular mechanical breakdowns means we had little of a ship to keep together. We ended up doing two expeditions, of which one the only real advantage was an oxygen refill, and the other stirred everyone into a panic after a risk of Mush infection...

In the end, we managed to repair PILGRED due to great dedication from Raluca, and albeit early, we made it back to Sol. We did not manage a cure, but I honestly do not think we would have survived at our situation.

Special thanks to:
- Chun, whose knowledge kept the ship together,
- Raluca, silent but dedicated,
- Janice, a great shrink with... interesting techniques,
- Roland, a new but highly skilled pilot (and rival).
