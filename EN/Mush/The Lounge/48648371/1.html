Phalanx300

Space Station 13. It has several codebases to work with but they are all basicly about a Space Station where several players are given antagonist roles, of which there are several. The normal crew aren't allowed to wreck havoc and the antagonists cause some destruction in order to fullfill their goals. The normal roles themselves can also be fun, anything from a Janitor cleaning the station up to a AI which can open doors but also flood the station with inflamable plasma.

Personally I recommend the TG servers, they have a nice mix of RP/Gameplay, which varies from server to server.

In order to play you need Byond installed, and you need to register a Byond account. You can do that here: http://www.byond.com/.

In order to play you can go here: https://tgstation13.org/ which will start the download once you select one of the two TG servers.

You should definately read the wiki as it can be extremely confusing if you are just starting out. The wiki you can find here: https://tgstation13.org/wiki/Starter_... .
