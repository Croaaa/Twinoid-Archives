Sentient Mushes (latest version here: https://www.dropbox.com/s/kypmjgvdctc... ) is a spin-off of this great game (which I never did play, but my friend inspired me with it ) which runs on the ZDoom ( http://www.zdoom.org ) engine, which is a source port of Doom itself, which if you don't know ( :O ) is a very classic FPS from 1993; source ports are changes in the source code of games that makes them more advanced, etc. ZDoom is a overhaul of Doom sporting more modding capabilities, like over 9000!

The use of ZDoom and the Doom engine also means this spin-off will be quite capable of maintaining the retro/classic feel of the original "amuscarian game", while adding a flavor of shooter to it.

You start as the antagonist though... Not sure how many people will this trigger

Currently, this is an addon for Chex Quest 3 ( http://www.moddb.com/games/chex-quest... ), which is another ZDoom game. That's because the spin-off, before hitting adult age, will yet need something to grow in...

It does also need some light to grow with photossynthesis. Anyone willing to help me with the lanterns?

Sorry, in most forums there is a edit button, but I couldn't find it; either way, I wanted to add that the story board is the following:

Sometime, a millenium after in Earth, you were in the middle of execution of your hideous "lightly" plan. However, you receive a call. Some alien race is attacking Earth, just as you though it would be safe to take over those four-limbed pricks! "There are aliens all around attacking us! They name themselves...", a tensious break in the call, "the Branx! We can do anything in exchan- *static*\" You have a new task. Save your race, and then save this race; remember, no corpse infection!

You try to escape to Radeo in a spaceship, disregarding the call (while killing or infecting all Branx in your way), however the escape is detected and the small escape pod is captured by the Branx sentinels!

You escape the Branx interrogatory, unlike the rest of the crew, who didn't even see you sneaking aboard, and you got it - this is really wrong! You find a Branx rebellion group and fight along them.

You find Chickenstein, but there is a giant cybernetic thingie guarding it - the Dominator!

You defeat the Dominator, but Chickenstein escapes in his way to Tamina.

In "Chickenstein's Nemesis", you intercept his spaceship, you hideously follow him and destroy it before it even touches Tamina!

(from this point onwards, the storyboard is just a sketch for Sentient Mushes II; we just finished two maps on Sentient Mushes 1!)

However, Radeo is destroyed right as you kill Chickenstein. It bursts in an explosion. You see a giant crater of lava disappearing with the planet. Some meteor hit it!

You receive a distress call from Aldebaran. A mysterious one.

Mid your journey, you find what will soon become your best friend in the story, Drake, a red dragon, very similiar to you, but slightly taller.

You decide to see what's going up, but it's a trap made by a giant cybernetic chassis with a cat in the top! 'It seems way too familiar', you think!

You defeat the cat. (lol)

You get to Earth, where you find out between the time's advanced industry, one of them sends a distress call by a Secretly Rebel Worker (SRB). "Hey mate, there's something chicky going on. I'm a rebel worker in this industry, but don't tell anyone; they are doing some giant military robot for no reason in this New Earth Government!"

You got to investigate, but as you approach, you and Drake listen beeps. "Uh oh!"

Dr Syntetic, the brain that started it all! The Branx attack, the evilination of that random cat.

You defeat him, and the Universe is saved now.
