I woke up, it was already day 1 cycle 8. The room was filling faster then Chaos trigger finger. The ship seemed to be full of nice people looking for a great time, and I was impressed. So the inactives list grew, from what we could tell, Chun, Ian, Frieda, Eleesha, Kuan Ti, and Jin Su were inactive. So this meant, no list, no research, no plants, no planets, no projects... Within a few cycles, Stephen reported that Eleesha was dirty, and 'I am going to murder her.' And what do you know? A few cycles later, 'Death Registered: Eleesha - Assassinated.' Meanwhile, in the Front Corridor, something is going on between Janice and Paola. Paola claims 'Janice hit me, she is mush!' And Janice says practically the same thing. Moments later, Janice is dead. Our resident mush hunter, Chao, investigates the scene. He finds explosive residue in the hallway, suggesting the use of explosives. He immediately issues a kill order on Paola. It only took a few shots and hits and Paola was dead. Scratch one mush! In the mean time, Chao orders Stephen to be quarantined in one of the Centre storage's until he proves himself to be human. He mentioned something about being sick, then casually when inactive. A few cycles later, Chao eliminates a fellow human. Chao proclaims in the discussion channel his mistake, and he insists that he will take the mush test to prove his humanity. He is also sick. He takes a pill and a ration and accidentally consumes the ration first. Gioele immediately assumes that this was indeed not a mistake, and punches poor Chao to death. Yet another human is dead. By this point, at least 4 people had died from depression. We even had a shrink book somewhere that could have prevented the chaos that ensued...
We were starting to get low on crew members, as I noticed a fire on the alerts bar. And I thought - A fire? This early? No, that can't be right. So I go rushing around the ship (wasting a lot of my AP) to find this fire, which was just next to the dorm I was in, Alpha Bay 2 was on fire. I announced in the discussion tab that it was possible that Roland was mush. Roland proclaimed his innocence as he lied back down next to me. Gioele: Well Roland wasn't mush, so I guess you are Terrence. Out of AP for now, he'll die later.
The next cycle, he died of depression. By now I knew my morale was going to be a problem, and I didn't think anyone else on the ship was going to care, so I took a morale pill and ate both bananas. The next cycle somebody else died of depression.. I felt kinda guilty. We were down to 5 crew. Myself, Finola, Raluca, and the inactives Ian and Frieda. Luckily for me (not so much her) Finola took the shrink book, and bolstered my morale up to 12. Later however, I put a biiig sus marker on Finola because a fire started and she was in the room when it happened. I requested she took the mush test but she was away most of the time.. A little bit later I was talking to Raluca in private chat, and asked her if she was mush, and she said no, and asked if I was mush, and I said no. I had to hop off at this point, and I come back 20 minutes later with 1 ! It was about to be day change, and I was asking around about morale, and Raluca only had 1 morale. Because Chun was dead, there was nothing we could do, and it was inevitable that she was going to die. It was really unfortunate too, I was hoping we could have repaired Pilgred and made a return to sol attempt.. We were running low on oxygen, and I knew the end was drawing near. It a last ditch effort to destroy the remaining mush, I took 3 oxygen out of the tank so there was 2 left, I hoped to kill the mush by exhausting our oxygen supply. Two cycles later, Ian dies of lack of oxygen, and Finola dies of depression... Then it was just me and Frieda. And the inactive Frieda was mush... Sounds pretty depressing! I hope your next ship goes better. Well here's a couple of things for you to know on future ships..
1 - When there's no 02 left, Alpha mushes do not die to lack of 02. Converted mushes can still suffocate. My mush partner and I once basically had to shower to death because we didn't want any more cycles to go on dragging our glory down.
2 - Pyro Mush is a very high level skill. If someone actually has it, it's very likely that they know how to use it properly to not get themselves discovered. It's generally safe to assume that fires are caused normally, which can happen early if your ship is unlucky.
3 - Raluca can still do the thing for :moral:, a guy just has to flirt with her first and she can initiate the thing.
