It was a very good game; made it do D10C5 before returning to Sol, which is the furthest I've ever gotten. Damn you!

It was an interesting game; our Chun was basically an axe murdered and tore a swathe through the ship. Highly entertaining.

Basically, what happened was that Chao and Jin Su flipped me early on Day 4; Chao had been boobytrapping shelves and I ran across two of the things, then Jin Su spiked me while I was poking around in the Refectory. Sloppy of me to be out of the dorms that long, I was wandering the ship looking through the logs. Jin immediately flicked on the Anonymush skill, and nobody was the wiser. Our Alpha Mush weren't subtle enough to be undetected, but they they worked fast, and they worked to a plan. They were more effective than everyone thought they were!

What I've learned, which I will be keeping in mind for next time, is to be extremely bloody wary of anyone who tells people where to find things. Also, when playing Eleesha, plan moves better. >_>

I had intended on collecting Paola, but decided to bide my time once I realized that the MycoAlarm research was done. Deploying that gave me a window on Raluca, but I didn't decide to make the call entirely until I knew that a lot of people had passed through her room; Jin spiked her once, then I hit her in Cycle 8 and five minutes later in Cycle 1, then immediately went Anonymush. My intention was to throw suspicion on her due to her not doing much of a job fixing Pilgred, but Chun killed her before I had the chance!

Anyway, after that I worked to make myself invisible. I've dropped occcasional contaminants in the rations, and spiked people when I could, but with three cameras and five mycoalarms in known locations, my opportunities for spikes were rare. Nonetheless I had Janice at 2 and Paola at 2, and a few people at one - had we made it to D10/C8-D11/C1, I was going to mess with every camera at once, kill Chun and flip two people, and die horribly as people worked me out. I am sad that I didn't get to cause that chaos, but with everyone being careful and hanging out under cameras or alarms, it made my life difficult.

Still. Love your work, guys, it was a good game.
