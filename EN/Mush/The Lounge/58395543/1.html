Well, season changes were required to reset the leaderboards and stir the competition (for that part of the player-base that enjoy such play-style). The problem is that MT couldn't simple reset the boards, they had to throw in some more unspecified meat to their viking soup.

Sometimes what they added was not thoroughly tested and had to be tweaked and re-tweaked, most of the time during the course of a season or two, which understandably caused some discontent with "new content".

I still believe, though, that the Shaman (as Profession, not as a Role) increased the amount of dedicated players and payers and, when compared to the amount of players that decided to quit because of the unfitting content, such increase was bigger than the losses we had.

The major complaint now is that we no longer get updates as we used to, so surely you guys are just one side of the bed sheet There is no way to contemplate everyone in this matter.

To me, constant addition of new things to a ftp game shows the love that the dev has for the game concept. Sure it will sometimes make players love or hate the changes (or sometimes both, in my case), but it shouldn't be a general rule to just boycott ftp games that are like that.
