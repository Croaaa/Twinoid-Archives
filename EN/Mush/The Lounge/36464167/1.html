Join me in the new birth of humanity, and the celebration of our Mush Lords."

I am Jin, in case you hadn't noticed.

So. This was an MMM ship, by the way, for all you readers. The fight begun D1C2, when Chao was the last person to unfreeze himself after shaking off the icicles of... unnamed substance. I gave a speech to fourteen people in Medbay, giving the largest speech I've ever given. Gotta say, it was one of Jin Su's few very inspiring speeches. It was at that moment that I knew I'd be , simply because there was no way I'd be human on such a fantastic ship of people.

Not long later, my fears were confirmed. I was an agent of Mush. But, Raluca was my partner. Raluca, dear dear Raluca. Physicist and Tech, she was a eureka. And it was the perfect Jin Su Raluca pairing that every Mush pair hopes for. So there was hope. Of course, like almost every MMM ship, this one was incredibly hyper active and one wrong move would spell death for an unfortunate Mush. Hence why my heart caught in my throat when Raluca told me she'd extracted her spore count for the day, but didn't get caught for it...

Day 1 passed mostly uneventfully. We planned for what we would do. We knew a Eden victory was impossible. Active Eleesha and a really fierce mush hunting crew would prevent that. Our best bet was rush Sol victory, and the entire time I was very scared that one of the crew members would ask me to eat a ration so I could go hunt pilots with more efficiency. Trying to dodge reasonable human requests while conserving AP for our final move was really difficult.

Day two came around, we got a starmap fragment from a lucky alien encounter, and steaks, and lovely stuff. PILGRED was already up at 30% around, so we were close enough to our goal to do it. I rested up in a bed until cycle seven (I slept in a lot longer than I intended) and had to move the ship. Our plan would begin Cycle eight.

Cycle eight came, I got out of my piano lesson, and I immediately charged down towards the rear of the ship under pretense of launching expo. I instead went to engine room. One minute felt like thirty minutes as I relayed for her three times, heart pounding in my throat the whole time because everyone on the ship was active at the time, and all it would take would be for someone to guard front corridor in time and our shot would be over.

No one stopped me on the way up though. PILGRED completed without a hitch, and Frieda was the only one who noticed something was wrong before I was in bridge room, and by then it was too late because I was already in front corridor. Arrived, gave the ending speech above, and so ends the first of the MMM ships.

I am really sorry about the abrupt ending to what would have otherwise been a fantastic ship, but it is the job of a Mush to do such things.
