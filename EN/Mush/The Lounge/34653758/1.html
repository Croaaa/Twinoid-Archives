In the meantime, Hua went and tried to fix a broken turret we'd asked people to drop a drone on and leave alone, and got dirty. A few hours later, Jin Su gets dirty kicking an inactive out of a bed. Not 10 minutes later, Janice gets dirty kicking an active out of a bed. Kuan Ti because he was dirty doing something he definitely shouldn't have done and his only skill was optimist. Chao because he'd been idling all over the place dirty from working on the wrong research. And those were just the most obvious of the options. Chun really rocked this ship. She relayed with Finola and they pumped out a ton of research. And then, she relayed with Raluca and they finished the plasma shield. And she was a sweetie to boot. Gioele was so impressed that he adopted her. Just kind of announced it as a fact and assumed she'd be okay with it. Thankfully, she was. ^^;

All in all, this was a fun ship. Returned to Sol day 6. Could have kept going longer, but we had the serum and the ship was crippled with the shower situation. So we put it to a vote, had a very clear majority, and went back to Sol.
