We're a D12C1 returned to Sol ship, surviving against all odds.

First Griefer

For starters, ship began in D1C7 and filled in D2C2. Sometime between that, Griefer Chao went around hiding everything important. Soap. Apron. Mush Sample. Blaster. Sofa BP. Both the Banana Plants. Pots. Other important things that I don't even remember anymore.

He also picked up Armor and Blaster, ate both bananas and a Ration. Just so he could try to kill me...by emptying his clip into me before replacing bullets with his fists. He came pretty close, but I survived because of pure luck. I had 2 HP left at the end of it.

Of course, being out of AP wasn't going to stop him from griefing, so he accused me of being a mush who spored in a turret (untrue of course). Fortunately, his particularly foul and potty mouth turned the crew against him. They trusted me over him, and chose to kill him. Janice made the killing blow.

First Mush

It wasn't very long after the griefer's attack when Mush Stephen suddenly threw a grenade into the Lab with Kuan Ti, Chun and myself inside. Kuan Ti died instantly. But he was an inactive, so we didn't really care. Stephen proceeded to pummel Chun into the ground, intending to kill our Nurse. He failed. Not that her survival made any difference. She didn't have Only Hope AND she was inactive!

Finola soiled her delicate little hands by plunging them into Stephen and ended him. My, there's more to that lady than meets the eye!

Second Mush

Our other mush was a lot more sneaky. I couldn't spot anything right away. For a few days, I wondered if the last much could be an inactive.

Then, a few red flags came up. Janice lied about starving. Well, MAYBE lying. No one really knew how soon a new player can start starving if they skipped tutorial. Janice complained about getting hurt lots by picking up objects. It wasn't proof, but it certainly raised her profle. to be continued....

Sleeper Griefer

Turns out, Paola was a sleeper griefer. At the start, she grabbed a plant an went inactive until it died. Then in Day 6 or something, she woke up suddenly and killed Gold Roland, our very active Pilot. She died of depression in the next cycle.

This was bad for us, because most ships don't survive long with only one pilot. So, active Jin Su read our Pilot Magebook and became our backup pilot to Hua.

Second Mush continued

Back to the second mush. I noticed some AP discrepency in Janice, but I couldn't be sure if she was semi-active or actually mush. It wasn't until we built Mycoscanner when we knew for sure. You see, I was infected with 2 spores...which was odd because I'm ALWAYS in Camera rooms!

Thus, I concluded that our second mush was a Trapper, and started examining the old logs to see who could've trapped me. Janice's name came up again. Using this new information, I sent Jin Su, Terrence, Frieda and Hua to check their infection levels, too...and sure enough, what they had was consistent with my hypothesis that Janice was a Trapper mush!

This created a dilemna. Chun didn't have Only Hope, and Jin Su was expected to spend all his APs on piloting. Killing Janice would end us, as we needed her shrink abilities.

Cat and mouse

So, we worked on a secret plan to cure Janice of her mushieness. It lead to some rather amusing exchanges while we were trying to trick each other. Some examples:

- Janice: I'm so clumsy - I got hurt picking up [some item]! Can I get healing?
- Jin Su: Healed you for 3HP.
- (In secret private chat) Eleesha: Don't heal her too many times...we don't want her to have the ability to secretly extract more spores!

Or another example:

(Janice still hasn't changed CPU Priority to Research for days, despite being asked a few times, and we desperately needed her to do it to research the mush cure!)

- Eleesha: Nah I don't think Janice is mush. I think she just missed this thread by accident. Anyway, let's try asking her again to change the BIOS setting and we'll see.
- Eleesha: Eep how embarrassing, posted in wrong thread!
- (In secret private chat) Eleesha: p.s. I intentionally posted in wrong thread, and I totally don't think she's human. I just wanted to "encourage" her to change the BIOS setting asap.
- (1-2 cycles later) Janice: Oh oops I somehow missed this! Sorry! It's changed now.
- (In secret chat) Finola: She actually did it.

While we were working on the mush cure, I even had to send still-clean Hua to intentionally trigger traps set by Janice because many of us (including myself) were really close to being converted by her numerous traps!

Needless to say, we completed the cure soon-ish and injected Janice with it. We pretended to not know Janice was mush right up to the very end, hehe. Fun times.
