We murdered 'MURICA-Chun quick, gaining us some human confirms via mental illness; rushed research and had an Andie skill debate ending in some sent gold days.


By D3, the hunt wasn't going to well. There were legions of semi-mute n00bs running around BD like there was a "free beer!" sign posted outside. Semi-mute-Kuan Ti has been holding the medkit since pre-selection so we couldn't even check for melt.

But just before we were about to knife him, Terrence had a breakthrough : Jin Su reported shooting hunters with only shooter points because he was out of AP directly after the 'MURICA-Chun rampage. Cold blooded would have generated 9 AP. We matched the chat logs with the patrol ship charges in the saved logs and Terrence killed him with one shot of the natamy. Ironically, Captain 'MURICA actually made this run easier for us. Thanks, 'MURICA!

Unfortunately, this didn't change the fact that Jin Su put two spores on Andie the day prior. We were well aware of this because of his movements but there was nothing to be done about it. In retrospect, knowing this, we should have barricaded Andie at the bridge or something.

Minutes after the death of his partner, Alpha-Kuan Ti broke cover and spiked Andie the last time. After a brief stand-off, Andie transferred into idle-Derek and murdered two of the non-n00b/idles. Terrence killed the two mush with the natamy and dedicated the kills to himself . Or something like that. I wasn't online so I only read on it after the fact. Elaborations from the rest of the crew would be appreciated

It's not hard to hide with junk actions before submitting to it, especially back before Janice comforted Andie to the max (it would have only revealed 6 actions)


All the idlers died of depression and we killed transferred-idle-Andie so pilot-Janice could get the title and do comms. With four crew left, we rushed PILGRED in one day; RFS in two after Janice printed the disk; did 5 really good projects; finished comms, upgraded some drones, built a refectory sofa with lizro and cheated some aliens on a pot deal, all over the next few days.
