Journey
So we were mush free at day 6 if I remember correctly and focused on ... well, on what?
We were lucky to find our first starmap piece on one of the first expeditions - but it should be the last we would find.
Although we had a great crew, Janice died quite early and Andie - R.I.P, Sub-Lieutenant - was pilot only, so our chances for finding more starmap pieces was not that good.

I myself did what I do always if the ship enters that paranoia-less phase: I tried to do my job and focused on RP - mainly with amazing Ian, also a bit with Stephen. Jin Su didn't seem to be willing to get involved that much.
