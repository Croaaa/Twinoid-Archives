Well... we made it!
Eleven full days, struggling against inactives and a pair of semi-active multi-teamers with command roles.

Our start was rockey, what with Ian starving so super-early. Hua, and later Raluca really stepped up to the plate as our fantastic botanists. I still kind of smile when I remember how poor Raluca accidentally dismantled the tubing she got out of the shower and the look on her face when we told her that the TUBE itself was needed for the bed.

Frieda totally deserved her glory rank on that ship. She was the one who really kept us going and alive even when half the ship was on fire. She was far more of a commander than Jin Su was, and my last actions totally reflect that. (He deserved that haymaker.)

Oh god, so many fires! The medlab AND the engine at the same time! Then a day later the NEXUS...then storage, multiple bays. We lost a fair amount of stuff until Raluca flooded the ship with post-its. Fear the little yellow army!

Raluca was fantastic with juggling a double duty of gardening and repairs. Terrance was always there to lend a helpful hand with his drones and tech skill, and despite how quiet he was, Roland was always on-point to down the hunters around the ship. Chun too was always there, ready to help with medkit in hand!

Sadly, our lack of any real NERON presence (I stopped updates around V5 to give Frieda the bridge sofa, and we never did manage to finish the sprinklers Y.Y) and almost no real morale boosters outside of fruit after day 7 really cost us in the end. With so many fires, so many dead, and Chun so very close to suicide, we returned home without the Serum. Hopefully we can finish it when we reach Sol. With the Mush in our midst dead and Chun very much alive, I think we can... but the journey itself is over.

It was an honor playing with you people. Thank you!
Loudmouth signing off!
