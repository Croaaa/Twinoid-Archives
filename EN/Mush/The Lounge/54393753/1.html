I've had this little idea for a little while, but I decided to type it out just now.
Why? It's 2AM, I'm bored, I won't fall asleep for half a day. Also my ship while very nice is only half full, and not yet fully active.
So how are we doing this?
You take 3 skills from the magebook pool, and 2 skills from characters in the game, other people playing this little RP, or make one and only one new skill for you (which you can replace with a character skill, or magebook skill). You then arrange these in level order. And describe where they come from, in passing if you need to.
You may also give yourself a trait to further describe you, if it has any particular advantage it should come with a bigger disadvantage. If one already exists that describes that affect then note it, but you can make one up if needed.
You "win" by making a skillset that describes how you might be useful on the ship, how you operate as an individual, and is balanced enough to not break the game. As judged by the other players of course.

Also you can optionally decide what chances of Gold Projects you get. You get to pick three, and split their chances. Or just one with 100% encounter. If you were to make one up it would not go above a 50% encounter chance. And remember good ones are a little rare.

Another optional inclusion would be alternate outfit, because reasons.

Here's the skills page to help you pick: http://mushpedia.com/wiki/Category:Hu...
For story reasons you get 6 months in the weird future this game takes place in. Plenty of time to get a couple spores in the event someone else is mush. Also enough time for some minor potentials to turn into minor skills.
