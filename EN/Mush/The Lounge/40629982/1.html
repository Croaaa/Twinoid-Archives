100 = Major Research Breakthrough!

Each part has a quality:
(d10)+(Modifier)
1-3 = Bad
4-6 = Decent
7-9 = Good
10-12 = Great
13-15 = Excellent
16-18 = Perfect
(Some research/kills with certain weapons add a modifier to the quality)

Reasearch Breakthroughs are the most important part. They unlock new things to research, then later build.

Each research has a quality minimum, meaning that only things that quality or above can be used to build that item, unlock that item, and research that item. A Major Research with the quality good could not be unlocked with James's Bad Major Research Breakthrough. Just as, if it requires 12 Scrap Alelianite, all of that would have to be good quality or better.

Each research is Major, or Minor. Minor Research is unlocked with minor research breakthrough, and major research is unlocked with major research breakthroughs. Each research requires the previous one on the tree to be completed.

Each research has certain skills required to research it, a goal, and the dice to roll for gain.

James is the only person who can go through the research breakthroughs. (And knows what to unlock next, ect.)
