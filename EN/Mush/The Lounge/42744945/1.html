Hello, comrades. I present you a beta-version of my custom scenario for Plague inc Evolved.
Their page, if you don't know about the game
This custom scenario focus on how mush took over the Earth before our team escaped. As editor has limited abilities, so I had to change something it game lore
- When you start, your infected humans are not real mushes yet. Like they have a spore or two, but never gets 3. Those spores can make other humans infected, but very-very slow.
- Taking one ability makes all infected humans turn to mushes. Still, they act like most horrible mushes you ever saw in game - press mutate button, extract spores with humans in room...and get killed.
- To make your mush agents useful, you do need to develop number of abilities just after turning them to mushes. For example - allow pheromon communication or make them splashproof.
- You will win if either all humans are killed or if everyone is infected and turned to real mushes.

Differences between this scenario and basic game:
Severity stat represent how much chaos mush actions cause
Lethality - how fast mushes die (mostly from being discovered)
Most aggressive actions like sabotage increase both. Still there are some exceptions, for example, being Hard Boiled helps to survive longer, but don't help not to be discovered. Unlike basic game, where you needed both to be high in most of game modes, here staying hidden can be a key.

How to play:
1. Download this scenario
2. Get Plague inc Evolved v 0.8
3. Unzip the file, put it in C:\Users\(your user name)\AppData\Local\Ndemic Creations\Plague Inc. Evolved\Scenario Creator\
4. Start Plague inc, select Single player-Custom scenarios-Local files-Mush the Prolog
