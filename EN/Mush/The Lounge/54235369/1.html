Hi all! Frieda here! I'm one of the early pilots keeping the hunters at bay, until we got our Plasma Shields, and then switched to planet scanning.

This has been a pretty good ship, with a strong planet scanning and expo team. We scanned planets like crazy, and every time we find a good planet, the expo happens like 1-2 cycles later, with all the right people rushing to IB. That was our greatest strength. We never did find where the mush hid our drill, but turns out, we didn't need it!

We were embarrassingly bad at gardening. To give you an idea how bad: we had our first jumpkin on D3 expo. Said jumpkin matures in 8 cycles on our ship. By the end of D10, we only just managed to have a full jumpkin garden (5 pots) because we keep forgetting to water them and plant them on C1, and as far as I know, none of us managed to enjoy eating a jumpkin fruit!

In the beginning, we had some, ahem, personality clashes between some people in the crew... specifically, Frieda and Jin Su, and some others as they took sides. It started when I was accused of being "not very useful" because I chose IT Expert as my skill (leaving the second slot empty). I got defensive about it and things got heated for a bit. However, fueled by a mutual desire to see the ship succeed, the tension was diffused pretty quickly and we got along great after that!

Now calm enough to explain our point of views, turns out, no one noticed I had helped around the ship earlier, so the "not very useful" comment was less about the IT Expert skill I picked, but more about how I spent my APs. Huh. A good old-fashioned misunderstanding. Nothing like a good drama to make a ship more interesting.

Most importantly, we really lucky. It wasn't easy finding the cristalite planets (we had to dump SO MANY PLANETS), but when we did, we got a map shard on all of them! Woo hoo!

I had lots of fun on this ship. Usually I prefer having more Mush Hunting, because I like the mystery and the paranoia, but when you have good people, it makes up for it.
