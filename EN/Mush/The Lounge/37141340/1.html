Preface

First of all, I was RPing a lot, so do not take things I said too seriously. Love you guys!

But then I turned into a monster, and decided that having half of the crew massacred is even better without a shrink, so I personally went Brutus on her. Sorry Janice!!!

So here is how it went down from my POV.



Chapter 1 - The grumpy human

I started as Raluca, restraining myself a bit first, but quickly being useful and organizing stuff as I always do - because it is most likely to stay human, and even if not, it gives you some cover.
Also, I like Raluca because you get to talk to people from her point of view.

These idiots just get in your way the whole time. Seeing them waste their time on such pointless trifles causes you to fear the worst for the future of humanity...


Janice, as mentioned, was very helpful. And I did not lie when I said that I could have rushed PILGRED and returned to Sol right away, had I turned Mush, because at that point is was already at 50%, thanks to her help. And she gave me hugs.

Same goes for Eleesha, who was immediately on the hunt with Chun and me. And Jin Su, who did his best leading the ship. Little did we suspect at this point that he was playing the good Mush.


That changed when Chao shot Frieda, allegedly on his and Hua's orders - shortly after she had proven herself 100% human via food testing!
However, while that was a major screwup, the rest of their (then investigated) Mush hunter chat looked like they really just failed to see the legitimacy of her plan not to pick a skill yet - even though, or maybe because, she had been rather vocal and obnoxious about that very fact.

Most of all, though, Chao managed to divert so much attention with his increasingly irritating behavior that the main question in the wake of this became whether or not we should kill him just to shut him up.

I certainly would have, after he pushed for that insanely risky expo, with six people going and Jin Su still on the bridge. I was so itching to stab him then! And everyone was OK with it too.

The only reason I did not was because, at that point, I was a Mush. And I needed my AP.



Chapter 2 - A new perspective

How did I become Mush? Well... after we decided to kill our wandering Finola, which Jin Su heard, both of them simply WALKED STRAIGHT INTO MY TURRET and spiked me. Which NOBODY NOTICED.
Of course I was not going to say it then, but man...

It was kinda ironic, because shortly before, I had expressed my regrets not to be born a Mush with the way things were going on this ship. (No research, no projects, and Chao...).

So now my call was heard, and we were going to have some fun with that!

Of course we could still have returned to Sol right then, not even needing to possess the captain for it. And it would have given us the most as well. But with Jin and me already leading the crew, there were soo many options...


The best of which was certainly that expo. How glorious that would have been!
Sadly, Jin Su had messed up there - or the crew was just too fast. In any case, both of us had called everyone to Icarus bay. THEN he tells me that he is now going offline for the next 7 hours.

So then, of course, the crew acts unusually fast for once. Icarus XL has 5 people in it within an hour, and I start that nerve-wrecking discussion as to why you should not launch without the commander just yet. Grudgingly, I spill out all the (valid!) arguments to wait for him to join, and all the (real!) ways the Mush, meaning us, could otherwise exploit this to kill all of you.
- Except that I did say all this not for fear that he would come online and abandon you, but for fear that he would NOT!

But then Hua listened to Chao's clamors over reason and lauched anyway. And since the captain was already Mush, I could not possess him and move the ship myself, either.

You were lucky that night.



Chapter 3 - Apocalypse

With that off the table, the O2 supply back form 10 to 32, and Jin having to leave for longer on D4, we basically decided to just murder everyone. So I got PILGRED to 95%, collected/built 2 grenades and did some interior decoration in A Dorm, with people's interiors. Jin Su joined in, I transferred into my trusted and competent friend Eleesha (sorry!) whom I had spiked before, and together we murdered Chao, Janice, Raluca (the real Eleesha), Chun, Gioele and Terrence.

Paola walked in during all of this, looked around, and left again. Then wrote something about the signal. The "coverup" to all this was Jin shouting "Terrence is killing everyone!" and later "I am Paola in Jin's body!"


Yet I think NOBODY actually checked what had happened in that room. Like that I had stabbed not only Terrence but also Gioele AS ELEESHA and had nothing to explain it.
Thanks to a vomit-inducing disease I had inherited from Eleesha, I actually got Roland to heal me up while we discussed how all of that was Jin Su's work, and what "we humans" should do about it.

The rest is history. Finola turned Mutant and stood in our dorm, which was a funny picture, being how everyone else was relaxing next to... it. Jin Su had "surrendered" his Armor to me as a sign of "goodwill" before Paola shot herself and thus solved the mystery, and he himself had taken Hard Boiled, so none of us Mush could really be killed by the 3 remaining humans. Even when not near Ian, who seemed blissfully unaware of all that was going on.
Back to full AP I got up, stabbed Hua to death on the bridge (that's what you get for launching that expo!!), and laid back down, still not sure whether that would be enough to give me away as the third Mush. Shouting "BATTLE ROYALE!" over the speakers and giving Roland a stab as well finally convinced him, but of course he couldn't do much.



Epilogue

As I am writing this, it is D6C4. The only people left on a broken and burning ship are Jin Su, Finola and myself (Mush), Ian, and a half-dead Roland. Somehow they have not died of depression yet, and we have just miraculously survived 9 Hunters firing at our 5 points of hull. That was strange, but this cycle will certainly be our last.

We did not realize our idea to go back to Sol as a Mush-only ship, as Jin is already gone by now, and Finola's current form does not go very well with keyboards.
But that is fine. Humanity is doomed, and I am spending my last moments indulging in apocalyptic poetry as they despair and die.


It was a fun ship after all. I hope those who may have raged at the "griefer" Raluca understand now, and that this little diary made up for your troubles.

Especially Eleesha, Jin Su and Janice - I am looking forward to seeing you in our next life.


Your "comrade",
Raluca-Eleesha
