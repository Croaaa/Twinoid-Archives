Hey there, my little mushroom friends, and my darling vampire commander.

As Soroosh said, the ship was quite active from the start, research went very well (we hated ourselves for that afterwards), we did a nice little expo... I started collecting the D2 logs and organizing them, and was about to read them in detail, when... right before day change, I saw Derek kick Ian out of the room. I was just about to run away when I saw Gioele spike me. I tried to run faster, but Derek caught me in the corridor, and as I was entering Bravo Bay, I was already mush.

In the mush channel, I was greeted by Derek, Gioele... and, surprise, Terrence, who had been converted a few cycles earlier by eating a ration brought by Gioele. Tsk. That's when things started to get really messy. Murder of Raluca, Derek's transfer to Janice, conversion of Frieda, me transferring to Stephen and killing my old body... The logs were a huge mess, with Gioele, Derek, Eleesha, Frieda, Ian, Terrence entering and leaving several times, Ian and Stephen being kicked out from the dorms, mycoalarms ringing, two defaced rooms, not to mention the others moves, Terrence and I going to the front of the ship, Frieda coming back with the cat... I don't think anyone could have understood exactly what happened, except maybe with Tracker and an aspirine. Ian tried to understand but couldn't do much. The other humans who were awake chose to stay in bed, and it wasn't until Jin Su woke up that they started to find out what had happened. At least the identity of the mush wasn't difficult to discover, since we were all huddled in Alpha dorm under a triple ceasefire.

At the next day change, Jin Su then Andie joined our little family, we got rid of a few humans, and Andie transferred to Kuan Ti, our inactive human. Then we converted Ian, who had bravely tried to investigate the situation among conversions and murders, and he became Roland. That poor Roland... He was a once-a-day player, and woke up one day in a messy situation, with a few dead humans and four known mush (five, in fact) protected by a ceasefire. He woke up the next day, still human, but had become Ian, completely out of AP, surrounded by dead human bodies and living mush. The following day, he was still Ian, but mush. All this confused him a great deal and it took him at least a day to ask what was going on in that ship and who was mush. We explained, he became Andie... and was pretty much useless until the end.

So there we were, eight active mush (Frieda unfortunately became idle, which was sad because she had been active and helpful), a mush cat (of course), and a little human pet, Ian, whom we tried very hard to keep alive. We healed him, checked him every cycle, comforted him... but in the end, his poor little human's subconscious must have panicked too much, and he committed suicide.

And so our brave attempt at reaching Eden ended, sadly, with two starmaps pieces and a slight chance to find the last one if we had been a little more lucky and if everyone had been fully cooperative. But the RNG being very angry at us (the gravity simulator was broken twice during the last day and the engine room was destroyed by a spike, then caught fire), and trying to run a ship without food (though we had plenty, including three or four jumpkins) or spores (half of us didn't have Phagocyte) was really difficult. After Ian's death, all we could do was finish Pilgred and go back to Sol.

A very fun ship indeed.
