(We had also bad luck with oxygen with the next planet but at least at the third and fourth planet with oxygen we could fill our tank.)

The search for the final mush
because of Eleesha's list we managed to kill our first mush, Raluca, at day 4 ( i think). day after day passed and there was no suspicious behaviour, we could not find the last mush.. some got ill in that time ( sometimes manipulated: stephen delivered me and janice bananas which were spoiled, i realized that after eating one ) and Gioele asked whether someone wanted to make a voluntary spore sucker test.
After that was done, i realized that Gioele was acting strange. He gave not so much advices, was not that active anymore and did mistakes, like unsuccessful trying to repair an oxygen tank although i said that i was about to go there (as technician ;-)). But it was weekend, so i thought that he has maybe less time at weekends... one time we had a fire and Gioele was just running to extinguish it, saying he didn't find one extinguisher. That was the situation which was most suspicious to me, i had the feeling that the extinguisher was there not a long time ago (but i was not sure)-when searching, i really found 2 hidden extinguishers... I opened a new conversation with only trusted persons and discussed mostly with Frieda whether Gioele is a betamush, we even thought about the possibility that Finola has infected him while he did the spore sucker test... unfortunately we dismissed that theory and went for the idea that Roland is our only and last mush, becaue every other crewmember was ill or did a test during our voyage so far...and we were able to researched the retro fungal serum.

Gioele showing his true face
when we got that serum, lots of people were gathering at the bravo dorm (also the drones ) and Roland was lying peaceful in the bed. Then there was a long discussion whether we should try that serum on Roland or not. Gioele was really against it and gave the advice to try testing Roland first with a spoiled banana. This is also a mystery for me until now: did you want to get cured or did you want to kill some valuable persons when the room was guarded or did you just wanted to slow us down? But we had a bad feeling and i think it was already day 10 with lots of bad events so we decided to just try the serum out on Roland.
Just when paola was inocculate the serum, Gioele attacked me. i was quick enough to escape the room (just before cycle change-could it be that you thought you have killed me at that moment Gioele, or did you just don't want to follow me?). so Gioele was obvous mush and we killed him. His final words were, that we are doomed and that he was it all along...

so it seems to me, that our first theory was right, Finola has infected Gioele, when he did the spore sucker tets with her Finola, how did you do it? he had 0 spores a day before i think. my only theory is that he had to use the shelve in the medlab often before the test and you restocked it with traps..

But we were not sure, so we wanted to wait, whether Finola was acting susoicious... and she was showering at least, but we waited again. unfortunately not very much time later Paola was standing in her room. and after that she died of depression :/ Now we were sure, it had to be Finola, and we immediately killed her.

The last days
we thought we were finally mush - free and that was right like i see now. we had lots of problems with fire (we had no fire detector :/). we did a great job, could also plant jumpkin trees, visited even crystalite zones for 2 times (unfortunatley no star map) and managed to survive until day 14, cycle 6. at cycle 5 of day 14 i wasn't able to hack the terminal to flee from the asteroids so we had only 5 hull left at cycle 6. now i was successful and we returned finally to sol.


I am really proud of our crew, that ship was really intense and it made lots of fun :-) i will write probably more later. That's all for now.
