Actually, soy goes great on mushrooms! What you do is toss soy sauce, a pinch of sugar, a little water, and some dehydrated garlic over mushrooms and stir fry. If the sauce is thin add a spoonful of flour-water paste (mix it up separate) to thicken. BAM! You have tasty mushrooms in brown sauce. Add thin-sliced beef to make it extra tasty.

You can use this recipe to make chicken and broccoli, too. Cook the chicken in the sauce first, remove and cut the chicken up while letting the broccoli cook in the sauce. Add the chicken back in to bring up to temperature. I make this dish pretty frequently and people love it. Serve it with rice or shred the chicken and serve with torillas or rice pancakes for faux "Mu Shu".
