http://mush.twinoid.com/theEnd/3885

Hello crew, this is Alpha-Mush-Commander Jin Su speaking.
First to say it was an absolutely awesome ship, lasting till Day 17 and might have lasted even longer if I hadn't this accident on my mobile with hitting "Extracting Spore" instead of just scrolling up the page while Frieda was in the same room. Also a shame Random Number God didn't provide us with the last Starmap fragment on this last expedition after having nearly 5 days scanning without a single crystallite zone.

But let me tell you how we managed to trick you all and nearly reached Eden from the beginning.

When I woke up with my comrade Roland I already had this mission from the collective to seek out for this place called Eden. So it was clear from the first day that our goal would be to be a sneaky part of the team and reach this new hunting ground.
Luckily I was in contact with the lovely Paola right before cycle change and due to the lack of an active Chun we found ourselves starting the "Mush-Hunting-Channel". I offered to do a food test any time and expected from Paola to stay clean all the time. Of course the food test was never possible for lack of AP or inactivity or other things.

Converting Raluca
Roland and me made use of all 4 spores in day one, but had to eat some of them to cover our mushy actions. For we extracted several spores right after cycle change, doing secret showers we still had enough spores ready to turn our first victim. Raluca was obviously the best victim for she was mainly inactive and standing in Engine room the first time. We spored her twice in day 2 and Roland could easily give her the last shot in beginning of Day 3, immediately going Anonymush. So short, so easy. After being converted Raluca never said anything again and didn't even move.

Getting rid of Eleesha
This one was the most nasty thing I ever planned. We first thought that our Eleesha was quite unskilled and mainly inactive - but she wasn't as we had to learn soon. For I was very helpful and actively "fighting the mush-menace" she trusted me and contacted me about suspicious actions. I told her of course being part of the secret Mush-Hunt-Team what Derek could confirm (he was proved being human by eating twice at this time). So I had her trust when she told me that Roland was clean when going to shoot hunters and dirty after coming back. His excuse of "checking and loading fuel" couldn't stand of course, but luckily Eleesha had no contact to the Mush-Hunt-Team but to me.
I didn't tell the Team, just told some other quite unintersting stuff she reported to me and so the active hunting crew thought Eleesha wasn't very valueable - some were even convinced she was mush herself. But it was absolutely obvious that we had to get rid of her.
So I planned and prepared a situation suitable for our final strike into relative security:
In day 4 we were in the situation of having 1 spore both. Earlier this day I already told Ian to stay out of the dorms because there might be some Mush-killing-action and we didn't want to lose AP. Then I told the Mush-Hunt-Group of Eleeshas suspicions about Roland (a whole day later than she reported it to me). For it was no 100% evidence and our plan could only work on day change I proclaimed I would interrogate Roland before we kill him, trying to trick him into lies. Of course Roland wasn't online till 1 hour before day change and I could manage to prevent Derek from killing Roland just out of suspicion. I told Eleesha in C5 this day that she should save up AP for we would go after Roland in D5C3. Poor Eleesha.
After doing a faked interrogation where Roland told me a wrong morale value and I telling him that that's not possible - exactly 60 seconds before cycle change - Roland entered the Dorm in which Eleesha and me were, removed the camera, spored her once and transferred into her. I immediately spored Rolands old body once, went out of room and after day change produced a spore and re-entered dorm. Roland (now Eleesha) did the same and spored old Roland the second time. I gave him the last shot and took Anonymush myself. Then I cried for help in all channels that Roland was after me and fled from the dorm. Eleesha, helpful as she was, attacked Roland immediately and Derek was a bit pissed that Roland was already dead when he entered dorm.
(The whole action took 90 seconds and was the most adrenaline-pumping thing I ever did in mush).
So we had our first confirmed dead mush.

Going into the shadows
Now it got very easy for us. There was some distraction from my side about spores upon me etc. but in the end it was obious that Raluca was mush when she didn't starve to death at the end of Day 5. So Derek finished her - glad he could kill someone - and we had our second dead mush at beginning of day 6. Of course Eleesha now wasn't able to print the list and the whole crew could breath up. I guaranteed that Roland didn't transfer into Eleesha and pretended that we had a secret codeword for this case - as I had it with Paola.

Boredom and Research
The days were now something between boredom and pain - at least for me because I couldn't stop being the always caring, glorious commander. All without any AP from food. Our Research team did damn good and due to Constatispore-Serum it was also no longer possible to get a little AP from sporing and Phagocyte. Long story short - I wanted to go on with something suitable for my evil mastermind. I first though about turning Paola, but that would have been a very big cripple of our team for she was the one in the end who held the ship together, being always everywhere. I also got a bit afraid of the activities of our Lieutenant Graham for Pheromodem would doom us if we would spike any other person - what I actually wanted to do. Also I had the feeling it wouldn't be appreciated by the collective to let this Andie-thing research an Anti-Mush-Serum.
So the next victim was selected easily - and so was the plan. I had some private talks with most of the crew members and it was quite easy to convince the very devote Lieutenant to do some physical action without a camera. Eleesha spored her once when she was standing in Icarus Bay so it only 3 nice little events from my side were actually needed. I could convince her to do it the last time at a cycle where our hyper-active Paola ususally wasn't this active. So it was easy to get her take Anonymush in time without anyone noticing.

The End
The end is told very quickly: For nearly all of our AP was needed to heal crew members, seek out crystallite planets and doing morale speeches it was not possible to extract many spores at all. We really tried hard to reach Eden, but in the end we had too less luck in finding crystallite planets.
There was still a little planning for the end because we wanted this to be a bit more than just hitting the Return to Sol-Button, so we delayed my final speech to D17C1 instead of D16C8, I convinced Hua to get to medlab so Eleesha and Derek had a dorm for they own (Eleesha spored Derek to level 2 the days before by being very nice to him) and Andie and Eleesha could easily turn him. At least they could if Eleesha didn't eat her spore by accident and Andie was too bloodthirsty - sigh....
The rest you all know: speech, little rampage, return to Sol.

Oh yeah - what about this spacecows? That's simple: I'm really afraid of space cows. I met them on one of my only 2 expeditions and only throwing a grenade on them saved my life. Really.

(Now OOC:
It was a really awesome ship but very exhausting and I am not sure if I'll try the sneaky way again soon. It's a pain to calculate every single AP, having excuses for everything in mind and especially not getting any benefits from food!
Outstanding thanks to all the people I had nice RP with and especially to Paola who was one of the main reasons we only lost 1 person till Day 17. Hope to hear from you.
I hope I see all of you actives again!)
