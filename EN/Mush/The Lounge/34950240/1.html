MY medlab.) I'll try to document everything that happened, but remember that this is from my point of view, I would love to hear from you MUSH people.

Stabby happy time ensues I couldn't finish him off, so I go over to Ian for protection and Terrence finishes him up. Sucker punched by a crippled man, the chief of security should be ashamed of himself...

Turns out that he was a human. Oh well, you can't win then all. The captain gives an speech about the importance of self-sacrifice so that he doesn't need to sacrifice himself. Good for morale... I guess?

After that great speech, we had a good discussion about booze and the properties of chryogenic fluid and it's potential application as licor. I tell you, that's the future! T'is not like we can get sick after we begin serving our great MUSH overlords, in any case...

That hit me hard (and knocked me to 1 morale), how could that be? Roland dead, Ian dead, Jin-Su a traitor... How could that be? They trusted you captain, I trusted you, you BASTARD! We were friends! I managed to reign my anger enough so that a few rational thoughts could surface. That could be an elaborate ploy, I didn't fully trust Paola yet... So I used my available AP to get to the hidden Hacker Kit and verify that indeed, no one had touched it. BLOODY BASTARD! (I am trigger happy and I start to see red fairly easily when someone kills the crew) I took a blaster (conveniently hidden right next to the hackerkit for just such an occasion) and took off after Jin-Su, sadly I did not have enough AP to make it past my medlab, so I try a desperate gamble: Taking a random pill.
