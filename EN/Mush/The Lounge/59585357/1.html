Now that I see the end screen, apparently they didn't even pull any spores, so they just got themselves killed for no reason.
Stephen was able to copy Shrink and then assume the Comms Manager role, replacing both our Mush at once.

I got to be one of my favourite characters, Kuan Ti, and finally managed to do some Astro after three attempts at Frieda ending before that point due to the Mush. This was after finishing PILGRED and working through the NERON Projects, and between my stints at repairing half the ship at once.

I'll admit the RNG was smiling at us for most of this ship. The only Projects I'd consider us having missing out on were Teslatron Reservoir and Magnetic Net; we found Jumpkins very early; and the three Intelligent Life zones we visited all gave Fragments! This was particularly pleasant because Finola's unusual choice of four skills not including Diplomat meant we were relying on our White Flag/Babel Module combo. We never really needed to use the Rocket Launcher, and unfortunately Frieda barely even got to wield the eponymous Friedom.

Our Piloting team under the command of Roland (a.k.a. Wolf), although officially Admiral Jin Su prevented very much damage to the hull and collected way more scrap than I said we needed. They also carried out my expedition plans effectively to keep our resources plentiful.
He and Ian - with a little help from me - kept the Jumpkins flowing out of our Heat Lamp Garden, while Raluca gathered our Coffee and helped maintain the ship with Chun and I.
Finola was quiet but hard-working, doing her rounds of Medic work and churning out all the Research topics we had unlocked, as was Chao with inventory, firefighting and expeditions.

Overall, it was a pleasure to fly on this ship, gain some distinctions and put another Eden under my belt. A nice bit of RP flavour to keep it interesting helped, particularly from Roland, but other jokes like Friedom and my almost failure to hit it off with any ladies and still getting syphilis made it fun too.
