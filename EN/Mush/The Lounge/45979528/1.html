Hey, guys, Chun here.

So, why such a name?
It was the first public for me with ALL the crew being active. Only Hua ended up braindead after first day. And people really didn't have much to do during first..mmm..maybe 8 days.
Funny fact: mushes lasted only for one(!) cycle. On d2c8 was the selection and in the middle of c1 both were dead already. I had an insomnia for some reason that night and when I checked out how the flight was going, I saw the report that Terrence dismantled the second shower 3 secs ago and hysterically pressing F5 helped me to trace all his rout after that. So I even knew where he hid the Wrench, hehe :3 Roland+me coordinately finished him pretty fast.
The second mush was Frieda (oh, that old lady was meeean, didn’t even miss her), but how she was killed is still a little mystery for me. There were couple of suspects beside her, so I didn’t want to hurry with the killing. And everyone went offline, which made the whole investigation kinda hard. But all of a sudden our Chao (who nearly never sad a word and never red the threads) all of a sudden goes to the Bridge and – bam – the second mush is dead. It appeared that she extracted the spore right there, in front of a camera and Paola, standing aside. But why did Chao so confidently go to the Bridge in the first place? Maybe it was cap’s joke about Frieda, telling the truth about if she extracted a spore today (or it wasn’t a joke?). God knows, cos Chao got his tongue cut and was silent for about a day, and even after being cured didn’t say much (well, actually, didn't say anything ).
Without all that investigations and paranoia part, the crew got very bored (here’re some proves: http://tinypic.com/r/1iognb/8 and http://tinypic.com/r/ae4q38/8) and since it was the very beginning of the flight, nearly nothing happened to help you feel useful. Fires? Hunters? Broken stuff? Well, all that was like a gift from almighty And btw it was the first ship for me where pilots KILLED asteroids instead of leaving them behind.
But life went on and situation kinda changed, we needed to coordinate better, but people continued doing senseless stuff and every expedition we tried to make gathered about a day Many were online only once a day and didn’t bother to read important threads (well, we made walls of text, that’s true, but at least 1-2 threads concerning your job is not so hard) before doing smth, as well as reporting important things. No shower made that even worse. But still we managed to find 2 starmaps and eventually the jumpkin (one was eaten just before I was going to hybridize it, still can't forget that horrible day ) and finish plenty of researches and projects. And I must give proper respects to the crew: with time the communications got better and better.
But all that sounds a little worse than it actually was The trip was fun and we even survived till d19c2 without losing anyone (which makes me really proud of our team). Sad that mr Clause spoiled everything by returning to Sol without any discussion about that, I kinda liked him before that. He was afk for rather long time and probably was on low health, so he deserted. I woke up on c2 to heal him and Paola just to find out that the trip has already finished. And that was only 6 cycles before d20 and before an expo to a planet with int life and shipwreck, so we had good chances of finding the last starmap and start a new life for humanity on Eden.

PS: sorry for being that meticulous, it’s just I was sure the ship had great chances to make it till d25 or so :3 And I hope it was a good experience for all of you as well as it was for me. I also hope, that Eleesha finally finds a proper man on Sol and Jasmine will grow into a stunning pilot-lady, as responsible and sharp-tongued as her parents are.
