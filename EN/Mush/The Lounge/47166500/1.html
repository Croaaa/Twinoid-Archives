Hello all, Jin Su here!

We managed to get to Eden because we were both superbly lucky and also we had a nicely competent & communicative crew. Things that I remember about this ship:

- My initial suspect was Eleesha because both O2 tanks broke in D2 or D3, and only Eleesha was in both rooms 2 cycles before they broke, so I thought it could had have been green jellied.

- But that changed quickly because Eleesha caught both Chao & Paola crossing Raluca in RAT... immediately we knew who the mush were & dealt with them promptly.

- Also, the BIOS in our Nexus inconveniently broke in D2 or D3. I wonder if Raluca maybe jellied it.

- On top of that, 3 fires broke out in D2 or D3.

- Also, we went through Nexus Projects ridiculously quickly, hoping to reveal Plasma Shields... but it turned out to be the very last project to be revealed. It was around D8 and we still had no shields!

You'd think that we're an unlucky ship at this point. But nope, we turned out to be an insanely lucky ship. Lemme explain why...

- We barely encountered ANY hunters in the entire voyage. Any that did appear were small enough to be dealt with quickly by the active pilots.

- That allowed us to trade all our inactives away! We had so much fuel & O2 from trades that we were never worried about them.

- Also, since Plasma Shields was the very last project to be revealed, we stopped the Nexus team and got Paola to upgrade Comms instead, unlocking the shields with only 10-12APs.

- We also found a Jumpkin on our very first expedition. And then 3 more Jumpkins on the 2nd expedition. We had a full Jumpkin garden with Heat Lamps & Extra Pots by around D8.

- Then, we found 2 map shards on our 3rd expedition, and another map shard on our 4th expedition! It was only D9 and we were already Eden-ready, but we decided to stay a bit for the golden ticket.

- By the time I hit the Eden button, we had 11 people alive. Not a single active on this ship died (except for the mush). Once, Paola had only 2 HP from random room events, though.

So yup, insanely lucky.

There is one unlucky event that my crewmates aren't aware of: just before I hit the Eden button, I decided to try solving the Mad Kube first. I started with full APs, drank 3 coffees, ate 9 Jumpkins, and 1 ration. That's around 45 APs spent on the Mad Kube. AND I STILL DIDN'T MANAGE TO SOLVE IT!
