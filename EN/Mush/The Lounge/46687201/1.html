As I promised, here are my thoughts on the crew (in random order):

Chao helped me extensively with the mush hunt, and kept helping the ship even after. His strategy of keeping low profile made him suspicious, which was hilarious for me, actually Even though he was unproven for long time, I came to the conclusion that his extensive help during the mush hunt would be unproductive and perhaps even against the rules for the mush team, so I trusted him. I have to say, his unexpected death was one of the saddest moments in Mush for me.

Frieda was clearly a cool player with love of good RP and it's a shame we had to kill her. She helped me before mush selection with item scanning, which maybe turned against her in the end, since it was suspicious that she cut all contact after mush selection. We were well prepared during that expedition after which we killed, though she still had some bad luck (she could find the Hacker kit and she was really unlucky attacking Janice).

Roland was a very dedicated and friendly comrade, who helped greatly to keep the ship safe from hunters, fires and even the mush and still had spare time to do some Astro and expeditions. I think I've never seen this character played so rich (and not just the Bravo Dorm/Bravo Bay routine).

Kuan Ti was an enigma to me at first. He wasn't very active on the chat and the projects were slow at first, which made him a bit suspicious. But later in game, I could see he's human, because of his dedication to the crew

I'm usually suspicious of inactives, and especially those who show some sporadic activity (thus showing they are not newbies). Usually leads to rampages... Thus, Giole was an obvious target to hunt down.

Eleesha was a wild card. She started her own mush hunt, but unfortunately, she was in minimal contact with me, and she kept sabotaging my efforts (she kept finding hidden items and publicly hiding them in Alpha Dorm, which made it easy for the mush). The fact that she had the Observant skill (which is actually useful for Mush) instead of Tracker was suspicious, too. I hope she can forgive me for framing her.

Janice was not as active as some other crewmembers, but everytime she was, it was pleasure to work with her We were unsure of her human/mush status at first, but when Frieda attacked her, she was pretty much confirmed as human, which was a big relief.

Kim "the Sniper" Jin Su was doing good job with his Commander duties, but sometimes he messed up in really hilarious ways! This almost got him killed more than one time - especially after his 19-hour long dirtiness.
