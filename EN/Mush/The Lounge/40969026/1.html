As planned, our ship returned with most of the Research and all Projects finished, earning our Gold tickets.

I was Kuaptain Ti, Obi-Kuan Tinobi or Head of the Kuancil of Elders depending on your preference, Leader of the ship by skill (managed to get the Politician distinction) and by action. As I've gotten into the habit of doing on my ships recently, I was giving out instructions and advice in every area of the ship to mostly-good results, at least where they were actually followed. Fortunately this ship was a mix of having a nice chatty crew to joke about with (D1C7 and C8 were crazy!) but also luck with Projects, planets and blueprints to be successful - usually I only get one or the other.

As mentioned in my final words, we had some players who made a lot of mistakes. Janice and Derek started out abysmally - I'm sure it was Janice who connected us to Sol with only five people awake - and if it wasn't for the latter helping kill Janice he would have been killed soon afterwards. We ended up killing Derek a few days later before he started wasting food, despite several tellings-off and lots of personal instruction from me he still couldn't make a whole day without cocking up and wasting his AP.
Jin Su wasn't quite as bad although it seemed to take a long time to move into orbit of a planet, get out of it, send a mission to Andie or launch an expedition. Despite my very clear instructions for every expedition, he managed to lose our Medikit near the start of the ship (the main factor in us not pushing for Eden) and continued to just take equipment with him regardless of what I said. If he hadn't been a Level 5 Gold proven human he'd probably have been killed, and I was still tempted to at times.

On the flip side, we had some new players who turned out to be amazing. Hua made some mistakes but was willing to learn from them and used the Welcome Thread to great effect, earning herself the Shooter book to boost her Piloting. She was unlucky to catch quite a lot of damage at once, but didn't head up to the Medlab for someone to save her before a room shake finished her off. Roland and Paola were in their first few ships but had bought Gold and knew what they were doing, both very effective.
Chun and Gioele couldn't be that active due to internet or work reasons, but when they did come on they were helping out when possible.

Raluca and I were initially suspects of Jin Su's given the slow NERON progress, but with Janice and Derek being stupid, Terrence Piloting, Raluca starting off PILGRED, Stephen inactive and Gioele helping with Research I didn't exactly have a large Nexus team to work with at the start. While fixing some low-base-chance terminals I double-ate in front of Chun so our "active and trustworthy people" chat ended up as the human one when Gioele shoved Stephen and Janice around.

With Derek seeming too useless to actually be Mush and Janice a dead human, our first real suspect was Terrence (who was Dirty a lot and had kept held of the Knife after an expedition), then Raluca along with him. Being fearful of a PILGRED rush, I was planning to use a Grenade on the two of them while relaying (I had developed Weapon Phobia from Janice's death) but while waiting several cycles in Icarus Bay, Eleesha wandered through with a weak excuse to be Dirty and not moving for an entire day despite some clear instructions in what to do, putting her in the suspect list. In hindsight, she was also in two of the three possible Green Jelly rooms - turns out the Coffee Machine and Comms Console were her, Nexus Core was random - at the right time (and even bluffed to me that she didn't understand how to investigate it). Terrence later reported one spore - which the logs say was actually from Raluca, not Eleesha - meaning it was probably Raluca and one of those two. Since Terrence offered to hand over the Knife (actually ninja-ing me asking him for it) we decided to go for Eleesha before she could rampage, although she just went to hide behind Ian without doing anything else. A quick bit of organisation took her out, then I started on Raluca... who had taken Hard-Boiled. Ralsputin took a Grenade, several punches, a few Blaster shots and then Andie stabbing her before she eventually fell, fortunately in the rather long time Raluca spent offline each day.

We then had our Oxygen crisis (same as my last long human ship) but again had the plants buying just another time to get to a planet to fix it properly. After that I grabbed the IT Expert book and took Astrophysicist, finding two really nice planets one of which was as good as intended but the other losing us Terrence in a Cristalite field. There were some close calls when people were slow getting to the Medlab, but no further deaths in the ship.

It was nice to feel appreciated at the end for my Kuaptaining, hope to hear from the rest of the crew here. Slightly peeved to be edged out on Glory only by Jin Su waking up before me!
