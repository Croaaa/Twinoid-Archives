Roland here!

This journey might only survive for 6 days, but boy does this journey is nerve-wracking!

So, we started off with a team of not-so-active people and mediocre people as well. Jin Su went dirty twice on the early stages of the game, and I contacted Eleesha about this. We assembled a team consisting of me, Eleesha, Chun, and Raluca. The Mush hunt is on.

But not really that great.

Derek is a newbie, and he proved to be a threat when he read the Botanist thread without telling. Then he hid a spacesuit on the rear corridor. Eleesha immediately killed him. Not the Mush.

Hunters are coming quite fast, and with Andie half-active and me participating in patrolling and investigating, I don't have enough AP to kill them. I managed to do it though, finishing a batch of 4 hunters, but the next cycle brought more and I ran out of AP.
That immediately took the toll and reduced the hull until 9. Problem number one.

The Mush hunt isn't going so well either. The Mush aren't really active in doing Mushy stuff, so Eleesha and I set up a list of suspects containing Ian, Kuan Ti and Jin Su. But they weren't doing any Mushy stuff at all. It got confusing, and with Chun being half-active as well and Raluca not knowing anything outside her zone consisting the rear turrets and the Engine Room, we're stuck.

First expo came in. I volunteered to go with Ian. Frieda was inactive at the time, and Jin Su was somehow always inactive when I forced him to go with me. We even were in the planet for a day, and to quickly finish it Kuan Ti offered to go. The expo went well, but immediately after going back to Daedalus... third mush!

It got hectic. Very confusing. We have to quickly flush out one of them to gain better leads. Paola joined in our Mush hunt team but she wasn't bouncing great ideas at all. Eleesha then killed Stephen after theory suggests that the Mush might be targeting somebody inactive, but he isn't the new Mush nor the old one as well. But then, Gioele sorted things out by himself and got Jin Su dead in his hands. Jin Su is one of the Mush!

It would seem like we got some progress on the hunt, but after that there was nothing again. Oxygen stocks ran low. Eleesha then killed another inactive. I started suspecting Eleesha and assembled another team consisting of the rest of the Mush hunt team and Gioele to maybe, figure out if Eleesha was the Mush all along and it was all my fault.

On Day 5, Eleesha set off with Gioele to kill Ian, who after got attacked by, confessed that he is the Mush. The oxygen stocks are only 6 at the time, and we all are pretty sure we would end up in the dark space, bodies floating around. There's still one Mush to uncover with Ian not dead at all and managed to sabotage the gravity thingy and the oxygen tanks. The oxygen got to 3 on the next cycle. Things are looking pretty grim.

Frieda confessed after that she is the second Alpha Mush after Jin Su, and she transformed to her ultimate nasty state on the Bravo Dorm to keep her from being assassinated. Yep, all of us are prepared to die with no AP left to do anything important.

Except Raluca.

She came up with a plan. First, she Geniused the Natamy Rifle for us to use to kill both Ian and Frieda. Then she will advance PILGRED as quick as possible with help on relays from the rest of the team. She said it is possible, and it would all be done within several cycles. We still have hope, but that is a slight chance of survival. So, why not, we took up the task. I took the Natamy Rifle to shoot at, but I ran out of AP when I got to Frieda. Gioele is there, so I left the rifle there.

Cycle change. Oxygen stocks were 0 before, so one of us was going to die. And boy, what luck. It was Frieda. One Mush down, only Ian left. We were having hopes of surviving this hell. I set off to the refectory to get me and Gioele some food to kill Ian later, using the microwave because the comms signal has been down since.

Then Chun came. As a true humanity's last hope (well, kinda like Daedalus's last hope), she went active, took the rifle, and killed Ian. No Mush are left! We kind of won stage one of Raluca's masterplan! The timing of Chun's activity is so convenient. Now we only need to finish PILGRED with Raluca.

Except you can't really finish it at that exact cycle Chun killed Ian. Raluca ran out of AP, and with full stomach, we'll have to wait for the next cycle for her to repair PILGRED the last time since she has the sofa set up in one of the turrets already. The two rations I brought to the Engine Room can't speed up the process, and Terrence, who happens to be the only second person to have the Technician skill, is... basically a moron, we really have to wait another cycle.

And there's no oxygen.

Gioele was the second commander, so he ran to the Bridge to wait for the signal of PILGRED's completion. Everybody else is holed up in the Engine Room to help relay PILGRED, but we can't get it to 100%. There's no other choice, the oxygen would have to take its toll one more time.

And we're hoping it isn't Raluca or Gioele who dies after. Else, the whole masterplan would fall apart. It was a tense 3 hours wait for all of us. We exchanged ideas, revisit the reasons why we fail to spot the Mush earlier, and the sheer epicness of the wait resulted in an agreement to make an AP. 3 hours passed so slow, and it was 5 AM for me at the time (I'm writing this at 6:37 AM, so no sleep so far).

Last 10 minutes, people were busy saying goodbyes. Chun and Paola are relaying the PILGRED one last time. People were saying prayers to the Random Number Generators and equipment in the Daedalus that the last person to die would have to be the imbecile Terrence.

Then, day change. Day 6, Cycle 1.

I found out that the plants are still giving oxygen and we suddenly have 1 in stock, but it doesn't get passed to on day change, meaning somebody would die whatsoever. It was Paola who died. Gioele was in shock, we were in a mix of happiness that none of the key factors in winning the game didn't die, but mourning the loss of Paola.

But we did it. Raluca fixed PILGRED. And here I am retelling the story. It's all thanks to Raluca and Chun. The autistic genius and the assault-rifle-wielding, hippy-killing, really-the-last-hope-for-humanity nurse.

(man that was long)


So, come out, you guys! I'll go sleep now...
