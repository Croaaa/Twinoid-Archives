After some awkward interactions where I'd started thinking they both love me and then realizing that I was being big-headed and in point of fact they both clearly loved each other while I was foolishly inserting myself in their way (i.e. a tea party where Roland had asked to speak to Kuan but I invited myself in and an injury after an Incident where Kuan thought we'd skip the tea and Roland ran off to aid Kuan) I was finally brought around to realize that Roland at the very least was actually pretty interested!

I woke to find him playing with my hair and all my obvious feelings gone or covered! We both felt like we were in a fog and weren't sure what was going on! Since Roland and I felt it 1st and we were both in the Medlab we wondered if there could have been something wrong with it (could pills decompose and release noxious confusing gases..!?).

This was A Big Problem because my Dad and Finola were going to get married the very next cycle and expected my participation! With my feelings all spun about again I couldn't trust that their feelings also weren't manufactured and they were about to make a terrible mistake... even though Fin was already pregnant.

I'd always been a little suspicious of Finola even though that totally went against her file and everything I'd learned about her from Ian after spending regular time with him in the garden under the heatlamps each day. I was concerned that having found the serum she'd also found some way to manipulate it and make my Dad fall in love with him. I thought I'd learn that she was no longer the Finola she thought she was (and maybe even was a Mush imposter!) by challenging him to talk to her about Tau-Ceti... but to my surprise she did still care and even brought my Dad around a bit to offering me a humanitarian job around it when we got back!

This made me feel my theories were groundless until yet another feeling change happened. Except it didn't hit Finola or my Dad and if Finola was behind this just to marry my Dad there's no reason she would make me ill and confused right before the crucial moment of the wedding when she knew full well it wouldn't go ahead unless I participated. My Dad had made that clear enough!

So then wedding went on hold a bit while Finola checked me and Roland out and this time she found... nothing. There was absolutely nothing strange about my system and this gave me yet another crisis of confidence. Was she lying? Was I just subconsciously so upset about this wedding I was making problems for it? Only Roland and I seemed to be having any problems so with the problem seeming to be me I made on last ditch effort to be absolutely sure with my Dad that his and Fin's love was true and then the wedding went ahead with my leaning on Roland for support in my confusion.

I promptly went to bed right after and passed out for many, many hours and when I awoke things were clearer than they had been the whole journey: my head was perfectly clear and I felt exactly like my old self again, the Mush had revealed itself and so had The Big Plot. We now understood the terrible experiment we had been in under with, worse, some of us still irreparably under it (dun dun dun)!


But what about Kivanc, Terrence, Kuan and Roland in my clarity? Well, I still cared for them all (all you need is love?!) but Roland seemed to stand out a little bit. After relaying how Kivanc and I feel about sharing love to see if this would be acceptable to Roland (it was new to him but he was willing to give it a shot) we once again returned to the Medlab for something of a happy ending (at least for us!).
