I have not touched the game for a very long time. Not since a string of mediocre and frustrating ships. I decided that I would try to jump with some TE in hopes to spark it back up again. It did not disappoint. Usually when you have a large group of TE on board a ship, one or two would end up mush. Surprisingly not! Didn't stop us from wondering about each other, even after the mush were eradicated.

So, for my Raluca, I wanted to do something a little bit different than the directly aggressive sort of RP. So I kind of made her crazy. She saw all these people who had skills she didn't and wanted to figure out how they obtained them. Since she was born with genius, she was sure they had inherited such skill from something.

It started with Eleesha where she died her hair orange and made a poor attempt at an afro. She even had a star ornament at the top of one of the spikes (poor afro, remember) that she had plugged into a wall to make it colorful. Like a Christmas ornament.

Then came Stephen and... Well, he loved that mustache and so did she. So she went around collecting of stray hairs of everyone and everything and slapped it to her upper lip with a strip of scotch tape. A rivalry ensued between them that we didn't get to resolve thanks to my crap long work hours.

Then there were Derek and Filandra (Frieda). Derek had his strength (pretty much all he had since he couldn't fireman fast enough). He had to teach Raluca how to build muscle. She took it literally and grabbed a hammer but eventually he taught her the ancient art of the "push-up". Before long she was beefy. As for Forza (Frieda), The only thing Raluca could see special about her was her metal-rimmed glasses. Raluca had inferior plastic rims. So Ral set about making a probably-not-safe metal frames out of scrap metal. But it wasn't enough. When Fanta (Frieda) had gotten lost one planet, Raluca saw an opportunity and immediately called dibs on the glasses themselves.

I didn't get to absorb any of the other crew, unfortunately. AFter the first week, my work started moving again and I just didn't have the time to really do much.

As for Janice, it was sad to see her go. The day before she was caught she had tried to push people into believing the BIOS settings had been changed but she was the only one to have possibly activated it. I figured, at the time, she was trying to incriminate me as I was the only one in the Nexus. Then the next day came around and she posted her message. For the first minute our first reaction was to believe it. Then Chun mentioned that she was pretty sure it wasn't a cycle change sort of ability. Which got me looking at it. Then I noticed that the punctuation didn't match up with the display picture in mushpedia. Eleesha then commented on how our message was all in caps but the original is not. At that point we *knew* she was lying. The issue was to figure out if it were a joke or not.

We played like we believed it while we checked to see what had been changed. Couldn't fly out in the patrol ships, efficiency on Research was still higher than normal (priority was set to it and not astro), and Plasma Shield was still kicking. I had asked Janice to see what was changed. It didn't really matter what she had said was changed, if she had even gone to check we'd have known she were Mush. After all, we had already came to the conclusion that she had forged that message. Sure enough, she comes online and heads to the Nexus to check BIOS.

We had decided that she was too dangerous to let live for RFS. Thanks to Paola's slow work on comms we had no reliable chance for the disk even though we had already suspected our not-starving tutorial Terrence as being Mush. So I obtained the knife and shanked her face.

The rest of the ship was a breeze, really. I got to teach Kuan Ti some things (He had suspected Chun to be mush early on, it was hilarious). It was definitely one of the smoother Eden ships I've had. Not as smooth as one ship which was pretty much a cruise liner but it's a close second! Thank you everyone for a great ship. =)
