Howdy, Raluca here!

First, thank you guys so much for the compliments. When I was thinking of how to approach being Raluca I was very iffy about doing the theme I went with. I had to be talked into it by three different people. I barely got many responses at first and I was kind of discouraged until I started getting random compliments via pm. Ok, so maybe only a few wanted to interact but at least the rest seemed to be enjoying it. Also you have no idea how many times I had to google meanings and hit up dictionaries. XD

My normal Raluca was the common Raluca. She hated people an felt superior to all of them. When the crew went into cryo something happened. Raluca spoke of a cloud of energy that the ship flew through that "opened doors" to her mind and split it apart. You take that however you want to, maybe they flew through a cloud of dense radiation that the ship couldn't completely protect against. Either way, she woke up... different.

She saw the universe through new eyes. Eyes that weren't normal for a human. She heard the situation and came to the conclusion that she would do anything for the crew... THen the nightmares of the dark entity on Eden (she referred to as the "land of false promises" ). She explained to the crew that some horrifying fate awaited them but she couldn't describe it.

As the ship went on she would do random things to try and learn or do something "nice" for the crew. Be it from making them aware how she sees them (then immediately touching their face) or inspiring the crew with poetry/music. She had even wanted to throw a party for them but it didn't end up happening due to my laziness.

As they got closer and closer to finding the rest of the map pieces she began growing more and more concerned. She had forseen the gathering of the last piece in a nightmare. The next day she demanded and forced herself to be on the expo to prove it. The angry Stephen who went down with them, striking the crystal from 10 feet from her and discovering the last shard. She knew she wasn't crazy, but the rest still weren't convinced.

The days continued going by and as she had the same nightmare over and over, she began to feel a connection to the dark figure. Perhaps it was after her? She came to the conclusion that she was a conduit to the fade (a fog between dimensions in which souls gather and bring dreams and nightmares to the living). Her only choice was to not go to Eden. She asked the crew to respect her choice for "when the time came".

Now we come the day before departure. Stephen pops an idea. He figured that a mush symbiote would be able to reconnect the damage done to her brain. He feared losing her. Raluca didn't know whether to be offended by him not accepting her as she was or grateful that he would do such a thing based on the firm belief that it would help her. She tried to warn them of the person she was before cryo but they continued to convince her. Feeling alone in her mission... Not a single member of the crew believing her... he began to doubt herself. If she left them now and ended up being wrong about her visions then they may have needed her. Yet if she was right and went with then the creature might get to her. She decided that she would be there for them, no matter what. Even if it meant facing the darkness she would be there with them! Stephen and Frieda sent her on her journey and made her family...

She had awoken for a third time in her life. Utterly frustrated with the memories she had of her broken self. What an insane loon. Then there were those apes who pranced around, claiming victory over the hard work of Stephen and Frieda. Her first interpersonal connection (Kuan Ti doesn't count because he was a kitty cat) and the crew spat on them. Why do they deserve their good will? Then there was that sex offender cow who manipulated the most generous person she's ever known. Raluca tried to explain to her family the atrocities and foolish waste of good time their views were but to no avail... Stephen accused her of being a monster... No... She was the only one thinking clearly, they saw to that. She'll do what they ask because she loves them... But eventually Eden would grow too boring... Too confined... Eventually she would seek an escape... But she wouldn't just leave her family... There was only one way... To bring the SDF to finish this charade. Raluca was the darkness she had feared the entire ship... The conduit to herself... The one whose promises proved false...

I had a lot of fun creating my character and developing her as I went. Stephen had discussed with me via OOC about the turning (OOC guys, it's great for building stories with people!) and at first I wasn't going to do it. But then I realized the luck of everything adding up leading to this one moment and it was perfect.

I adored all of you who put yourself out there to entertaining for all of us. Ian's constant aguing with NERON, Andie's adorably vicious immaturity, Hua's aggressive and random sexual exploits, Stephen and Frieda's awesomeness in how they worked with Frieda, Gioele had fantastic plans but life got in the way. I also loved having my random moments with Derek "The Maiden" Hogan and Paola. Also it's a shame Chun was so busy, her personality would've went great with Raluca's. Also can't forget adorable Cat Ti who had some pretty hilarious posts.

A lot of people were scared to really get going with the rp but remember that this entire thing is to help create fun for everyone as well as yourselves. Don't be afraid to try new things, we wont let it fall flat, we never do. After all, I was worried about my character at first but it went quite well! I jumped on it and I don't regret it. I hope you all try an RP ship again and really put yourself out there because I know you guys can be extremely entertaining!
