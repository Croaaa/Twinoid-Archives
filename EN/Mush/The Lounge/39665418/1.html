Eleesha here.

This was quite a great ship, which unfortunately didn't get to go to Eden, although we did try until the very last days, until that last expedition that was... well, that was quite frankly one of the most disastrous expeditions ever. One starmap fragment (finally...), but two people were lost (and found, thankfully, after much drama) and Kuan Ti died from an earthquake. That's when we decided to give up and just keep each other alive until we went to Sol on Day 20.

Other than that, we had an eventful trip, starting with a few days of mush hunting and secret plotting, which resulted in Paola getting killed early (poor Paola... she really was too obvious, getting dirty because "I thought the apron only worked in the garden"...) and in Jin Su getting cured without having infected anyone. Almost the whole crew helped to rush the serum and stick the commander with it. A great collective effort.

The rest of the journey was quite eventful too, marked by numerous expeditions (which didn't give us the starmap fragments we were hoping for, but did allow us to find a few artefacts, enough steaks and fruits to feed the whole crew for three weeks, and of course brought us the one jumpkin that allowed Ian to create his own little Eden, a garden of 10 bumpjunkins that gave us about 15 fruits every day ).

What else can I say ? We really had a great crew, and... a few entertaining specimens that made this trip even more interesting, like Raluca, who got killed because of her erratic behaviour (messing with the fuel, shooting at traders, taking the bridge sofa), Stephen who, very fittingly, starved to death in the refectory, Hua who was murdered after trying to kill Kuan Ti (whom she accused of being mush based solely on the fact that he had gone through Bravo Bay several times ), or Janice, who... well, who must have been useful somehow, since she managed to survive 20 days and none of us committed suicide, but who lived in her own little world.

All in all, a very enjoyable trip, and a random ship that made it to Day 20, which is quite remarkable. So well done, everyone, and welcome back to Sol.
