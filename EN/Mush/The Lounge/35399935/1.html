Genius Undeserved: Raluca or Stephen uses Genius to auto-complete a NERON Project already at 95+% completion. Medium difficulty.

Edgar is the One in the Hole: Mush only. Duct tape a single human (isolated) in either Centre Alpha or Bravo Storage Rooms, write a Post-it with the line "Edgar is the one in the hole" and put it in the room, leave the room, then sabotage the entrance to said storage room. Easily accomplished, but easy discovery.

Spontaneous Combustion: Toss three grenades in the same room during the same cycle. Difficulty depends on RNG.

They Were Useless Anyways: All six turrets are broken by cycle changeover. Difficulty depends on the current day.

Political Assassination: Kill Jin Su within the same cycle if he gives an Impassioned Speech to 8+ people in one room. Hard for it to happen, but easy to accomplish.

Cursed by the RNG: Attack the same person 5 times in a row, miss every single time, and contract a wound from doing so. Nearly impossible.

Blessed by the RNG: Deplete one turret's ammo supply, and hit with every shot. Very unlikely.

The Last of Us: As Janice, become the last (wo)man standing, then complete a NERON Project, Laboratory Project, and PILGRED, then return to Sol. Nearly impossible.

A Wee Bit of a Malfunction: Sabotage the NERON Core, Laboratory, or PILGRED Reactor while someone is using the terminal. Stupidly easy and stupidly dumb.

Big Brother is Preoccupied: Hide all five Myco-Alarms in one room. Then, take down (and then hide) every set-up camera by cycle changeover. Mush only. Very difficult.

Cruel Twist of Fate: Kill someone who attacks you and brings you down to 1 HP. The next cycle, a random breakdown kills you.

This is Why You Don't Drop the Soap: Die within ten minutes of dropping the soap. Easy to accomplish, but investigate the victim before attacking the person/Mush!
