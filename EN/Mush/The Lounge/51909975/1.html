There are some things to note about the Forum in general. For example: from what I've seen their are 8 subforums in Mush;


The casting room. (For finding a casting to join, advertise your own, or keep track of others like a creeper.)

Mush Advice. (In case you're feeling informative, or in need of information. The Q&A thread is nice if you need to know how something works, or you can ask the SDF about the rules on something specific.(Discussion of SDF points is strictly prohibited, to prevent system abuse.))

Mush Discussion. (Mush discussion is for more in depth analysis of game mechanics, it's also where MMM launches get discussed(It means Massive Mush Move, this is when a lot of people jump onto randoms at the same time).)

The Lounge. (This is where AP threads(After Party), Favorite conversations, and fail/win threads all go.)

High Court of Mush. (This is where the Judges and Mods discuss cases and rules, no you aren't supposed to see this one, the details about cases and rules are a close secret.)

Mod Forum. (As a judge I don't know where this exactly, but I have heard mention of it.)

Mush Officers Mess. (Sadly dead as is seems to be right now, this is one of the perks of buying Gold days. Basically nothing worth talking about here isn't better served being in a higher bracket.)

Casting Forums. (When you join a casting, and they have the forums unlocked this appears, Useful for organizing the team for jumps, or just hanging out with a more exclusive crowd.)


But if I missed anything here just mention it, I mean it'll be in the thread then.
