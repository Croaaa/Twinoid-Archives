here! I had a great time. For a random ship, we did really well. Hua almost screwed us over by dismantling the extinguishers within the first few days, but Terrence got our drones upgraded and the extra one from the NERON project meant we could put out 3 fires per cycle with them.

We were lucky to get Magnetic Return at the same time that Hua exposed herself as Mush by destroying our extinguishers. We rushed the project and were able to move the ship so that we didn't lose the Pasiphae. We were also lucky that she used her spores for Phagocyte and tried to kill Chun instead of sporing people because our Finola was somewhat inactive and research was slow. We almost had new Mush from an expedition to a Cristalite zone due to lack of progress on Mycoscan and the Super Soaper.

I underestimated the usefulness of drones before this ship. I didn't know that Firefighter was guaranteed to work each cycle and that it put out the fire as the "first action" on cycle change meaning that nothing burned up and the ship was not damaged in the cycle that the drone put out the fire. Basically, if you have a Firefighter drone in a room with a fire at cycle chance, it is just as good as putting it out earlier in the cycle. Better, since it costs no AP.

Would have been nice to visit more planets but Chao did an amazing job moving the ship just when it was needed and got people motivated for expeditions. Losing Chao and Paola on the same planet was hilariously bad luck, but the rescue mission went off perfectly! Ian was also great as a first time player, he picked up how to take care of the plants really easily. Janice was a good Shrink but it was also her first time and Shrink can be tricky. Paola was good at driving the crew on, but I think she could have eased off on the page-long chat messages - information overload! Gioele saved my ass with nick-of-time healing at least twice. I'd nominate Terrence our MVP because of the drones. Everyone else did really well but on the whole this was a pretty quiet ship.

On the Mush side, Hua got super unlucky that we had Magnetic Return pop up so early, since she could have stolen the Pasiphae after destroying the extinguishers. But that is not a very "active" tactic anyway IMO. Frieda was trying to play the long game and got even more unlucky - two steel plates to the head, and finished off by a room shake. However, we already knew that she was Mush because I healed her twice with the Pomade after the first plate and she was still injured - she had taken over 8hp of damage total. We knew it must have been from her showering, and we got lucky when she died to another plate and a room shake.

Thanks for the good run everyone!
