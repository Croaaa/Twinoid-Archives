Kuan Mush went and siphoned off the o2 from our tank while F/T Mush went and loaded 9 fuel in the engine when the oxy planet was just 3 fuel away. That should have destroyed the humans and it nearly did.

Luckily I woke up irl and looked in on the ship before I'd even had my coffee. I saw there was zero oxygen where there had been 4 or 5 when I went to sleep. OMG, my first thought was there was an oxy tank broken and as a tech I went to look for it. There was no Neron warning, so it must have been a mush who broke it. I found that Kuan had been in CBS for no reason and noticed Terrence was dirty in BB. I called a kill on Kuan, but didn't know for sure about Terrence yet. I just knew we needed to kill Kuan to get the o2 back and we needed to get to our planet for more.

So I went to load the 3 fuel we needed since no one had posted it was loaded. When I got to the Engine room I saw that Terrence had loaded 9 Fuel and called a kill on him too. I unloaded 6 fuel and just as I was loading the last of it back in the tank I saw we had jumped. I had gotten it out with maybe a minute or two to spare. FT Mush, this was I think your only mistake in a near perfect game. If you had posted saying you'd loaded 3 fuel, I wouldn't have gone to the engine room, discovered your ploy and unloaded the fuel. Just as deadly Gioele might have jumped us sooner and we'd have been toast. But really nice play.

I had the knife, almost full AP and a thermos of coffee and an energy bar available from the vending machine. I went and killed Terrence first since I knew where he was. Then I nearly blew it. I saw a death notice for Roland and also that Andie was on the move. I was in a blood frenzy at that point and not thinking as clearly as I should be and posted that Andie was a third mush to kill. I went to IB and saw s/he was there. I cut her/m once with the knife, and luckily my brain kicked back in for some reason. Sadly poor Janice died of asphyxiation even though we had o2, that was a real disappointment. With a final rush of team effort we got Pilgred from 16% to online status and made our way back to sol with about 5 or 6 humans still alive to tell the story.
