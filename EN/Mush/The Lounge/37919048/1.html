Eleesha was also a new player who seemed like she was going to keep playing at the time, except she went inactive with the Comms title for quite a while..I assume she had no AP when she stopped playing. We had a hard time getting Pilgred finished at first but we did scrounge up enough teamwork to get it done. I moved into the Refectory at this point to tap as much coffee as possible since my work schedule allows me to be active on all but one cycle long enough to hit the coffee machine. There was no point in any Research at the time because Wang was needed on the Bridge since, you guessed it, Frieda was brain dead (Along with Ian and kind-of Stephen). Hua was also quarantined for some reason, not sure what she did but it didn't hurt us too badly since we had three other pilots already.

-Finally-, Eleesha goes inactive so the Comms Officer title passes on to....Stephen. Joy. I'd already been riding on Stephen for roaming the ship and not saying anything, finally he replies to me that he's not new and didn't realize the title passed on to him, that he's normally very active but he's busy of late. Forgiveable, but he really should have mentioned that sooner, because I was very close to killing him. So he gets a few Xylophs done for us (Figures, the mage books were one of the last ones to come out) until he starves to death and the title finally goes to Janice, who's been very active and helpful. But it's already Day 8 or 9 at this point, all the signals are gone, none of them decoded..so there wasn't much for Janice to do really except get us Automatic Sprinklers and she was already stretched thing keeping comforts up. I helped her out there thanks to our Shrink mage book but without her we'd have all died days ago.

We nearly ran out of oxygen twice and barely managed to find decent planets before almost asphyxiating, finding 32 and 24 units a piece. Don't remember who found the first planet (Probably Kuan Ti) but I know Kuan Ti found the second one. Wang read the Biologist mage book (She had Bronze Medic) and together with me and Jin Su, we utilized 3 coffees, a mission, and a +3 AP -2 Morale pill (Which I offset with a comfort) to finish the Retro-Fungal Serum in one sitting. So that was awesome.

After going a good while without hunters, we jumped to the second planet with oxygen to find..43 of them waiting for us! We were down to 40 Hull left too, so..I was little afraid. Still, we pressed on, quickly raced through the expo, took out Aracks somehow in that mess and left most of them behind. 9 hunters left on the next cycle, but we drop down to 18 Hull. There wasn't much point in pressing our luck so we agreed to go home. Litttle bit early for the update but I'm sure we can wait a day before playing again.

So that was our ship, and I hope you readers feel a little more inspired in knowing that random jumps can still land you with a good crew that you'd love to fly 12 days together with. New players join and most of them idle out but we do get people like Terrence who stick around and make the game enjoyable, so thank you again Terrence and I hope your future ships are just as successful!
