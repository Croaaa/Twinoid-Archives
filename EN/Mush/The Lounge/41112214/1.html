nanomed Unbelievable. Theres a fact of premature return to SOL and it was Reported. (Btw Friedas post was the one I clicked "Report" on.) So how come SDF then won't find out and punish the guilty one?

It's because of how the programming for reports works. When you report 'someone', a specific case is created on that specific 'someone'. Any punishment given is targeted by the programming to the 'someone' the case was opened on. Through that case, almost everything your ship went through is visible to the judges - the judges get the information they need to determine who is guilty, who is innocent - but the programming only allows a judge to punish, or clear, the specific someone that was reported. Judges are not given the power to create a ticket while judging - the only people who can create a new case on someone is a player on the same ship at that time.
