Nepsy

It was D2C1 when I woke up as Finola . The ship wasn’t full yet but it didn’t take long for everyone to wake up. With Frieda’s help, I started doing my job immediately and we had antispore gas ready in the blink of an eye. The other anti-mush research followed quickly, which was great for the humans but didn’t make the mush very happy.

The missing extinguisher
Since their sporing was very limited since an early time, I spent the first day fearing a rampage but nothing seemed to happen so I kept being dedicated to spending my time between the lab and medlab. Until the day we got a fire and no one seemed to know where it was. I went looking for it, grabbed the extinguisher in rear corridor (the one in front corridor was grabbed by Chao) and was able to put out the fire. All seemed good except that Chao decided to idle in the engine room with Raluca for one cycle or two. And when he came back to the dorms, suddenly the extinguisher he was holding was found hidden in rear corridor. It didn’t take long for him to be killed but he was found to be human. People assumed he was being some mild griefer and no one seemed to care about tracking his steps and check if it was indeed him hiding the extinguisher…

Raluca’s Death
Life went on peacefully for the next few days, we got lots of o2 on an expo, we had fuel, plasma shield finished, mush didn’t seem to be doing anything since everyone that reported said they had zero spores. There also didn’t seem to be any mush hunt going on, I love mush hunting but since Finola has very limited mobility I wasn’t paying much attention to suspicious activity. But due to the lack of action I decided to take matters in my own hands. I briefly asked Eleesha about Raluca’s activity because Raluca seemed to be doing very little and was presenting some odd behaviors but Eleesha wasn’t keeping an eye on her. So I started a conversation with Gioele, the only confirmed human by then (negative on xyloph). At the same time I was starting to mush hunt, we suddenly had a rush of confirmed humans. Ian got sick on an expo (the same one where we lost Stephen and I got coprolalia thanks to it), Frieda got sick with slight nausea, Eleesha ate twice in the lab. So our mush hunt channel got bigger and the suspicious about Raluca were very strong, she wasn’t doing much but went out of ap/mp in a dorm, after showering without a soap and saying she was going to eat. She also didn’t seem very concerned about her morale even if she had spend at least a couple of cycles in a room with others. We decided to rush natamy riffle and Eleesha killed her. And she turned out to be mush.

We started discussing other suspects and the main suspects were Janice, because she wasn’t reporting much ap spent and some suspected Hua. Eleesha must have been high on adrenaline from killing Raluca and wanted to shoot Hua too with the natamy riffle. Luckily I was able to stop her and after taking a closer look at the chat I saw that Hua was actually killing several hunters and gathering scrap. We debated for a little to try to do the serum and gamble on using it on Janice but since the suspects about her were very little we decided to do pheromodem instead.


Terrence’s Rampage
We rushed pheromodem the cycle after Raluca was killed and I was shocked to find out that we had a perfect timing in killing her. Raluca and the other mush were planning to rush pilgred soon and return to sol. If we had waiting some more minutes they would have started the plan and we probably wouldn’t be able to stop them. As we were trying to figure who the second mush was and suggestions of being Terrence started to show up (because he would be the most efficient to work on pilgred with Raluca), neron announced that Jin Su was murdered. Terrence rolls into medlab immediately after, where Janice and I were. I figured he must have been the one killing Jin Su and was probably aiming for Janice after so I declared a ceasefire. Since Janice couldn’t be killed, he grabbed the armor that was carelessly in medlab and killed the very silent Chun.

Eleesha was away and still holding the natamy riffle, Terrence had the knife and the armor so while we were figuring the best way to deal with him, he spiked Roland and transfered into him. He didn’t waste time killing his old body and we were left without techs and Pilgred at 34%. While we waited, most people gathered in medlab to be protected by the ceasefire and we had a nice party there. While I went to sleep irl, Roland took off on a patrol ship (with the wrench and I don’t know if anything else). Next day we rushed the serum and were planning on moving the ship and cure him when he was forced to land (thanks to magnetic net).

Awfully Timed Downtime
While we were waiting for Gioele, a fire broke in the lab and then suddenly… downtime with a fire in the lab and one mush outside on a patrol ship… When the downtime ended, we found we only had 3 plants left, lots of broken things and that Roland had died due to injuries. We were able to recover from all the broken things, our hull was damaged but was still resisting but we had a problem with Pilgred… with no techs it would be very difficult to finish pilgred but then Kuan Ti gave us some amazing news… he said that on the next day he would have enough xp for tech!

Going Home
He and Paola were able to get pilgred to close to completion and then Eleesha, Frieda and I finished the last 12% on it. And it was on good time because we had 21 hull and 1 asteroid would hit next cycle. We were still not sure if Gioele would be online to either take us to sol or to go to an o2 planet (we had 5 o2 left at that point) but with 20min to go to cycle change he logged in but then… didn’t do anything. The asteroid hit us and left us with 1 hull! I asked Gioele to take us back to sol asap since a simple fire could destroy us and he did soon after.

Random Notes

And sorry for the long post, I do have a tendency to get carried away.
