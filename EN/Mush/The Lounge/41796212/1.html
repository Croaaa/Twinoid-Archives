Sigh. Where did we go wrong? I do not know. I would mainly say that people simply were not active or communicative enough, apart from a selected few. As you can see, both attempts to live failed. Our ship has died, not to the mush, but to our own incompetence. Hunters have shot trough our armor on day7, at a time when 3 people have already died to suffocation.
