Breakdown from my POV: Finola and I were selected as the Alpha Mushies. Janice figured her out about 5 minutes in and she was swiftly assassinated. That left me as one scared and lonely Mush with no other choice than to play nice with the humans. I advanced Pilgred for a couple days with Terrence, only having the opportunity to tag one person (Chao) without seeming suspicious. From here on out is where things just seem so darn ridiculous. As the oxygen reserves became critical, I saw the perfect chance to cut this voyage short. I managed to sabotage the oxygen reserves without anyone noticing then slipped back into my lair and awaited my death. NO DICE. Someone managed to fix the oxygen tank but not before Janice died. Again I had to lay low until another sabotage opportunity presented itself. In a couple days that opportunity showed up in the form of 14 hunters. Two broken fuel tanks, one busted combustion chamber, and broken doors in Rear Bravo Turret as well rear bravo storage set the stage for the ultimate battle in the engine room. Poor innocent little Ian stumbled in on me post-transformation into a guardian and quickly alerted the rest of the crew. Chun and Chao responded fairly quickly and proved to be an effective team. Chao was roughed up pretty bad and if not for the timely intervention of Roland, he might have perished. With a few shots of his trusty blaster, Roland put an end to my Mushy menace. However, this was too little too late for as the survivors attempted to repair the combustion engine and load it up, two asteroids collided with the Daedalus, putting an end to the hopeless crusade.

Overall it was pretty fun
