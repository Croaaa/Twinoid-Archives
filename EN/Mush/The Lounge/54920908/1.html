No we are not making real movie We are going to play sort of pen&paper in a system called Primetime Adventures. It's about making tv show. It wont be about those 16 characters we know. Instead there will be some survivors on Xyloph after it has exploded and they will be trying to survive Mush menace on their own.

The system is very easy, there are no dice rolls, all conflicts are decided very easily by drawing playing cards. Characters don't have any numeric attributes, only issue, edges, connection and some other details. The game is very light to play with main focus on those characters and to make "virtual audience" to enjoy whole show.

We will be using service http://roll20.net. Actual play it's not going to be regular session taking hours. It will be rather slow pace depending on time availability of everyone. We will be playing using chat on Roll20.

Contact me with PM if you are interested. We are currently 3 people ready to play, we could use 1-2 more.
