In the beginning:

There were a few interesting developments.

Giole understood that doesn't happen, and immediately pegged Jin Su as a mush. He was promptly killed, and was, indeed, a mush.

Our research team was on fire, and flew through research. I got the genome disk from Xyloph and they completed the pheromodem. We then knew that the final mush was either Ian or Hua, and that they had spiked Terrence. As Ian had been in the lab the whole time and was instrumental in speeding through the research we quickly concluded the Hua was mush.

We killed her and were mush free.

Giole organized our efforts and the entire crew worked as a team. We planned out maximizing efficiency to finish Plasma Shield and Pilgred. We planned expeditions and got extra resources. Including two jumpkins which, which are extra hydropot project, led to us creating a very AP productive garden.

This crew was amazing! This was a ship that could make it all the way to Eden!

Then tragedy struck. Our expedition team was built around Finola--she had diplomacy, so we didn't have to worry about combat. then she got lost. The combats that then happened killed Kuan-Ti and hurt the rest of the expedition team. On the first expedition they did not recover Finola.

As I understand it, a second attempt was made. The expedition team suffered some pretty hard damage. The thought that was with magnetic return the ship could be moved and Icarus would return with the team. That was not the case, and the expedition team was abandoned.

This was devastating to morale. To make matters worse, Chun and Janice had been inactive the whole game. They had been kept alive through rebel base morale boosts and scheduled healings from our medics to deal with starvation damage.

We decided Eden was no longer a realistic possibility but if we were going to return to Earth we'd return with all the anti-mush research we could have.

Through constant fires, hunter attacks, and damaged equipment we finished nearly all the research and returned to Sol.

Amazing crew, the best I've ever played with.
