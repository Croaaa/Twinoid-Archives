Our ship started at the MMM4 launch two weeks ago and ended today with a trip back to Sol.

We had an inactive Mush Paola, Mush Gioele who exposed himself by accident early on, an inactive Eleesha, and a Chao who died early to injuries and lack of communication.

For the next week and a half, the 12 remaining crew worked together to do 11 total expeditions, recovering 2 Starmap Fragments and various other artefacts (including 5 Alien Holographic TVs and 3 Water Sticks ).

After some bad luck with expeditions - we lost dear Raluca to the snowy wastes of Mantilabia III - the crew seemed to become demoralized and lost motivation, and even though we still clung to the hope of getting the last Starmap Fragment and finding Eden, our spirits were low.

Two weeks after setting out, the fires and steel plates were getting to be too much for us, as one after the other fell victim to the slipshod construction of the Daedalus and the ravages of constant stress.

On Day 15 we returned to Sol, only 6 souls strong, sad about our lost comrades but glad to still be alive.


Anyway, here! It was a great time for me, my first MMM ship, but I felt like it could have gone better with a little more involvement and communication. Share your experiences here!
