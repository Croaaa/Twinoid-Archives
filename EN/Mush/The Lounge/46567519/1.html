Jin Su and Gioele were slain.
Perfect timing too, because I had just spent all my AP, and Eleesha had determined with some certainty that both Jin Su and Gioele were most likely human...

So eventually Chao was killed and he was human, and this really put a dint in everyones morale (and ) so the hunt went on. A few hours later Paola contacted me with the Xyloph list which had Chao as a negative, as well as a few other people who were invited to the 'human chat'.

It was about Day 5, starving day when Eleesha gave me the run-down on the people that had been to grab food and who hadn't.
Everyone except Kuan Ti. I shrugged and awed for a while until we agreed that there was a good bet he was Mush. A few stabs later, followed by a few 'For Delzoup' cries showed we had 1 dead Mush. Roland got a mental disease too, and I joked that it could be Patryk because at a similar time he posted on the Q&A about faking a mental illness as mush. But he was cured by Janice a few cycles later and into the human chat he came.

Paola had constantly been contacting me every now and then concerning new pieces of information she had gathered or concerns she had. I always made a mental note before thanking her and closing the tab (incase somebody needed to contact me) and she had the impression I didn't trust her.
In all honesty, I didn't trust anyone.
I was fairly sure there had been no conversions (which I was right about) because the mush were playing sneakily, and I hadn't seen enough evidence to suggest that she was Mush so I some-what trusted her. She suggested that I start a small anti-much hunt (which I already had) and I think she got a bit frustrated when she hadn't been invited.

Anyway moving right along I had just finished another lehtargic run and I was completely spent when suddenly mutant Raluca barged into Bravo Dorm and slapped Eleesha once.
I screamed out to everyone saying so but nobody was online at the time. I took my chances, got up and stabbed once and got a critical hit, instantly killing her. With no death announcement I looked at the Nexus logs and learned that Janice had fiddled with the bios before extracting, spiking and transferring into Raluca. I told the crew we were probably Mush free now and a few cycles later Eleesha confirmed it.

So we were down to 8 people left. Hua, Roland, Eleesha, Finola, Ian, Frieda, Paola and myself. With 10 plants and oxy ducts we were pretty much good for O2 so Frieda could take her time while looking for planets. It was pretty much a casual road from there on out, I did a few heal runs (if there is ever a medic book for a lethargic Chun oooh boy) and we held out till D10C8 when we decided to head home shortly after Finola's death.

Overall, fun ship, unfortunate about what Chao did but we managed to hold on.
