wow. Such ship. This was my longest surviving ship, and I'm sad I couldn't be more active towards the end because my classes started again and I wasn't able to log more then twice a day... As such, I cannot make one of my traditional recollections of day-by-day happenings on the Daedalus and I will have to make this quick.

Ship slowly filling phase
I got to make friends with Chun, since she didn't know the game yet and asked for advice. Luckily for her, she did it before the MUSH were chosen, so she could actually trust whatever I said. I also warned her to get paranoid and to not trust anyone (including myself) after the last person woke up.

MUSH HUNTING PHASE

To put things bluntly... CHAO GIOELLE IS A BLOODTHIRSTY BASTARD WHO KILLS FOR NO APPARENT REASON, SAVE YOURSELVES HE'S OUT FOR BLOOOOOOOOOD!!! Or so I though at first. turns out he really knew what he was doing, had evidence and was just leading a personal crusade, not trusting any other person on the ship. It didn't help that his tongue got cut and he couldn't calm me down as I desperately tried to warn Chun against him. Sorry 'bout that. I will keep an eye on the omniscient bar on the topside next time. Needless to say, Chao Gioelle didn't like my actions (and it's never fun when the maniac psychopath who's holding a knife get's mad at you). But I got sick after witnessing a death so he couldn't do anything about it. HURRAY for diseases!

The MUSH hunting committee was created after that, with me, Chao Gioelle, Raluca and Chun. We failed to spot The corruption that had taken hold of Terrence until it was too late, and he murdered Chun before we could stop him.

SURVIVAL PHASE

The Daedalus is a sad gathering of metal parts held toguether by crazy glue and he was intent on killing all of us even more fiercely then the MUSH did. This was pretty much routine from this point forwards, blow up hunters, repair the ever-deteriorating Daedalus, despair at the decreasing level of oxygen, find a planet, go on expeditions, find fruits, find maps, find traps, find oxygen, find fuel, bean up, rinse and repeat. Oh, and Jiggle with morale problems.

Remarkable characters
Jin-Su: Commander and speech-writer, always making a countdown before jumping for some reason.
Frieda: Our Polish route-finder and emergency fixer. Also, hoarder of wrenches.
Finola: Angel in disguise, managed to do all the important research, found a cure, healed everyone and then... STEEL PLATE TO THE HEAD!
Gioelle: Our back-up pilot. Good guy but STEEL PLATE TO THE HEAD! (because the RNG is always hungry for more souls...)
Ian: Flower power and so on. Don't eat the creepnut.
Janice: Helping keep everyone alive, the happiness beacon amongst a world of struggle.
Paola: The one who ACTUALLY found worth-while planets.
Raluca: The one who shouts at everyone, but with whom no one gets mad because she's so darn competent at her job.
Roland: The main battle pilot and probably the person I trusted the most. I enjoyed the pilot tab, going to keep applying that idea for future games.
Stephen: Should REALLY have avoided the nexus at that turn.
Chao: Sherlock Holmes and firefighter. Actually quite cool for a psychopath
Chun: I wish you had survived longer =/
