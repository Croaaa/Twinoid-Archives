My first Eden, and Raluca's too, it seems.

That feeling when you are about to leave for Sol, because continuing might kill a precious crew member, but go on that last cristalite expo just in case. Cristalite zone brings some spores (typical!), everyone openly screams Sol in the main channel, and then there is that alien zone which gets us an artifact. A very precious one. I bet those aliens just steal the Starmap fragments and hide them from Mush. To give them out to virtuous people like Rinaldos and their crew.

And a jumpkin to celebrate. Wow.

We managed to jump right before running out of oxygen, leaking from both of our tanks. After all those dramatic expeditions, it was really rewarding.

Now we are far away from all the "space cows" and "tax collectors". We shall remember our late comrades - Janice, whose shrink certificate was clearly handwritten, passionate Jin Su, kind and loving Ian Soulton (I wish you saw that glorious jumpkin we got!)

I was happy with the activity level of this ship. And everyone tried to roleplay, even those with lower activity level, that really created the atmosphere. We had a new player who really embraced his character. Roland, keep it up! Like a hwak, which is like hawk but different :-)

I was your Andie, a naive young ...man, who knew no difference between real life and imagined stories. Went for a book-grade cool adventure, discovered irl it was all different.
Was a pleasure to serve under the Rinaldos. Gioele was one hardworking man, especially when you expect a billionaire to be spoiled. But this guy did not forget his young dreams and was so inspired and inspiring. Or maybe it's just coffee. As for Paola, don't even get me started on how she is a miracle of universe. I just was in love with her, which somehow came out from our initial catfights, she clearly despised a federal underdog.... But the journey proved her I am a MAN...indeed...

The space cows and tax collectors were the explanations Janice and Gioele gave to me about those hunters, trying to keep me under the belief that Federation is alive and well.

I wrote a diary (day 0 is glitched, ignore it), but it gradually went concentrated on my personal line rather than on ship events. But there are some long expeditional logs, in case you dig this kind of stuff, and the first half contains a bunch of our dialogues. Day 13 is entirely about Paola (and D7 is when my afffection started), with a Chun bit... Our Chun was lethargic by nature, not by a skill, and I made sure to get in touch with her via private. So, In case you wondered what kind of person our Chun was: a bloodthirsty one, and also a seducor, but I was faithful to my love.
