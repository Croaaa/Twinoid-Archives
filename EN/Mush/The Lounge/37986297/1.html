So, with the new update, 2 new characters are introduced, and i want everyone to have a group discussion on how these characters might be roleplayed, as this is a slightly large part of the community.

These are my personal thoughts on how the characters would act. Feel free to expand from here.

Derek: Overconfident. Whether it is through cockiness or heroism, Derek seems like the kind of guy who throws himself on grenades... in his made up stories of his own heroic adventures.
"Did i tell you about the time i got this scar in the war?"
He also seems like the type who doesn't like others around during his heroic acts, just so he can spice things up as he tells about them later.
"Stand back, comrades! I shall single-handedly take on this infectious mush scum!"
Overall, he seems like the cliche hero, who overestimates his abilities
He also keeps dropping hints around Paola, which causes strained relations between him, paola, and her father, Gioele.

Andie: Honestly, i haven't made any guesses about Andie's personality, though (s)he seems like (s)he would be very loyal to his/her superiors, and, if in command, act harshly.


Thoughts? Opinions? Additions?
