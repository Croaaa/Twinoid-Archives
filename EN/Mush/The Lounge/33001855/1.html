I thought for sure, by the way, that I'd get whacked for not giving the Hacking Kit to Chun. It wasn't actually a judgment call about her capabilities. It was that she was so inactive that the Mush would have no trouble knocking her off, then it would really be game over. I couldn't put both eggs in one basket.

The weird thing was, I decided not to eat in the Refectory. The AP gain, since I had to cook it myself, just didn't add up to a full try at the Hacking Kit.

Height of ironies, after begging all game for people to stand in the corridor, Ian and Kuan Ti were standing in the corridor! The labs were also full. So with no MP, I went to the labs. I made the terrible mistake of putting the Hacking Kit down, which was logged, but changed my mind and dropped the walkie-talkie to pick up the medkit and the hacking kit with my map. Finola had left the bed, and so I bedded down to recover AP.

A half hour before cycle change, Hua walked into the medlab.

I knew – the whole ship knew – that I had 0 spores to begin with. So this had to be a murder attempt. Hua also took the other bacta. So he was out of AP... no killing yet. I got up, was not murdered, so before he could, I just went right back to bed.

Exactly when the cycle changed, I let Hua push me out of the room. BOOM.

Chun was in the turret, and I thought about trying to get to Bravo Dorm, or maybe even circling to mosey to Alpha Dorm and its shower. Fortunately I didn't move, because Mush!Eleesha popped into the room. I could only go back into the room with Hua, and went right past him, thanks for the push out the door, Hua! LOL. I hung out in Center Corridor with Ian, and then Chun woke up and moved into Bravo Bay, and like magic.... I was in Bravo Dorm. And the Mush were nowhere near the Hacking Unit.

I did my best trying to save the rest of the ship but there were no updates about the Mush, or any other crew. The more ground I covered, the less the Mush would be able to find the Hacking Unit. Of course, I thought my human crewmates might realize I don't even have a walkie talkie, or even post-its, and personally ask me where I put it. But the ship was about to blow up, so I did reinforce the hull with my last AP, and then put out the engine fire, put fuel in the chamber, and then watched as someone checked another fire .... in the room with the scrap metal. Which could've repaired the hull. So the Hunters blew us up anyway.
