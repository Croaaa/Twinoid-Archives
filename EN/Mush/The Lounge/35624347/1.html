N_Faker

Lets start with Hua(1st dead mush). Within Day 1 she had taken the Hacker Kit and a Blaster from the Rear Storages and hidden them in Bravo Bay. She played innocent, not informing anyone. Killing a few hunters and moving out the Expedition gear to Rear Corridor as she picked them up.

After a few cycles she loaded 1 fuel 'for emergencies' and sabotaged the RBT/Engine Room door, 3 minutes into the cycle. The target had been confirmed. And was promptly killed within Day 2(I think). Chao extracted revealing information to boot, 'Extracted a spore' it said. He plunged his knife into her making a bloody mess of himself, and nothing but a bloody mushy pile of her. Tearing his tongue in the process, bloody murder does that.

Chao was mute, seeking treatment for his poor tongue. Finola(mush) was the answer, O' fine doctor. He informed Finola of his plight with a note from the ever handy Post-it note block. Rummaging through the pills in the Medlab until the note was placed visibly on the top. Having his tongue stapled back on, he got entranced by Finola. His Blood Queen, he would soon claim her to be.

Finola had been drenched in Chao's blood as she stapled his tongue back on. Ruining her dress and her usually stainless self. Walking down to the Bravo Dorm she showered, finally clean again she returned to her bed in Medlab. Only to be tortured gleefully by Chao. Her muffled cries of pain revealed nothing. She hadn't informed anyone where she got her blood stains from. Our Captain Jin Su had seen blood stained dress. Rushing to the Medlab he tried to beat her dead with his fists. Miss after miss he finally hit her once, bruising his hands on two hits to the wall in the process. Tired and expended he regained his senses. Healing Finola once as an apology.

Feeling assaulted and ripped from her sleep Finola went to the Lab and took the Blaster that was to be improved by her Anti-Mush researches. A Rifle had come to mind. But no. It was required for another task. Running to the Bridge she opened fire on our captain. Pew, pew, pew, only one shot connected. Tired and hurt she returned to her bed in Medlab, placing the Blaster under her pillow. She would be able to defend herself, for now.

HoboNixon

I guess I'll pick up where N_Faker left off. After Chao was all patched up and Finola & Jin Su's incident, I was invited in a chat with everyone who was deemed human. Our suspects for who the remain mush were the following: Jin Su for his violent outburst without stating a good cause, Finola for her refusal to do anything but be paranoid with a blaster, & Roland since he had only posted twice up until that point. At this point and time we were running low on supplies and had a planet all lined up to explore. We either needed to root out the last mush or go on the expedition with the hopes that the mush's schemes weren't going to tear down the ship in an instant. We opted to go on the expedition since we would be getting into a dire situation if we delayed it much longer.

This expedition is not listed on N_Fakers post, but it consisted of me (Janice), Finola, Jin Su?, and Chao. I'm not super positive on the landing party, but the important bit is that Finola was a part of it. Once we got our feet on the ground it turned out that Finola only had around 4 HP left and this planet wasn't exactly filled with butterflies and gumdrops. I imagine her plan was to try to loss as much equipment as she can from dying and maybe if we were unlucky enough have a few others die from the loss of man power. It seems like a sound enough plan from a mush who was starting to feel the pressure of a ever closing in crew. However the RNG gods seemed to not be on her side since she took the minimum damage from all the events and came back with 2 HP. Which then she was immediately tortured by Chao to reveal all the naughty item hiding she had been doing just before we left. With that information and the anti-mush serum not even started her fate was sealed.

After the final mush was put to rest and suspicions of beta mush were put to rest the ship progressed in a orderly fashion as you'd imagine. We completed all our Neron projects quite quickly and then proceeded to completed needed research such as ultra-healing pomade and meridon scrambler. Somehow we managed to stumble across the star map piece which changed our priorities to researching anti-spore measures and scanning for planets with crystalline sectors. After gathering two pieces of the star map we hit a huge dry spell in expeditions. Basically no matter how hard we scanned we couldn't get a planet with a good chance to hold a piece of the star map without something like manakrogs or volcanoes. It must have been 4-6 entire days without a suitable planet to explore and the team's morale was in the gutters. On day 13 we discussed whether or not to just head back to Sol and call it quits. After a bit of chatter we had decided that if by day 15 we haven't found a planet to go to then we'd give in and jump back to Sol.

I'm going to leave the rest of the story to N_Faker or any other crew member to tell due to the fact that most of it happened while I was offline. Also feel free to add more insight into anything I might have skipped over in this wall of text. I'm sure I've missed a few important things with my spotty memory.
