Greetings, comrades of the Daedalus! Kuan Ti here to provide you with an awful summary, as promised! Our ship: http://mush.twinoid.com/theEnd/1396

Ours was a mostly active ship. Chun jumped right in to take charge of the research team and Mush hunt right off the bat, and Finola managed to get quite a few Research projects out the door. Janice and I managed Nexus projects, Raluca stayed in the back turret, but closely monitored the PILGRED, and Ian had a bit too much fun with his bananas. Paola was a Rebel/Xyloph pro, Stephen proved very helpful in the beginning, and Gioele served as a second shrink at the start of the ship. Our Frieda did a great job scanning planets for us, we only lived long enough to visit one. Between that one planet and Ian's mad gardening, though, we had plenty of O2 the whole game. Terrence .... you know, in retrospect, I have no idea why no one suspected Terrence. He had Robotics Expert, but never once talked about his drones. Gioele was active up until just before he was turned to Mush, apparently, and worked with Chao and Chun on the Mush hunt. Hua and Roland kept us out of hunters, and I get the feeling the Eleesha did not have much experience with the Mush hunt. If I'm recalling correctly, our Jin Su was the only inactive, at the start. Last, but not least, the lived in Bravo Dorm along with our pink lemonade shower (which I, sadly, only got to enjoy once).

Poor Stephen was played well, no waste of food in the beginning, helpful throughout the ship ... but was the first human brutally murdered for delivering food when we started starving. Our clever Mush, you see, turned their first victim right around the same time as a ration was eaten, and he had the misfortune of being dirty from cooking, so it was decided that he was too suspicious to live. He marked our first death, killed by Chao under orders of Gioele, Terrance, and Chun. This started a rash of human slaughter, as we all turned against one another.

Though next was Jin Su, who starved to death. RIP. Your death was out of our hands.

After that, Janice took a gamble and killed Eleesha, convinced she was Mush. Then Chao killed Roland for odd behavior. Gioele, in turn, killed Chao for having killed two humans. Panic rippled the ship at that point, as it seemed rash decisions were being made at every turn with little to no evidence or discussion, and all of those killed were fellow humans. Chun, in response, set up an elaborate testing system to try and pinpoint Mush by hyper-monitoring point usage and activity.

During our testing period, a fourth Mush was born. There was a period of uncertain activity, then Chun died of depression. Hua was murdered for claiming to have given up on the ship early on, in spite of her excellent pilot skills. Then Frieda was murdered, Terrence mutated, and we hit the last legs of our journey.

The humans tried to take a last stand, but with MushIan, we were unable to take any of the Mush out. Janice went on a rampage after them, but ended up dying of depression. Raluca was offed next, and then only Paola and I remained. The Mush were trying to convince us to move the ship to avoid hunters, but we instead chose to sacrifice our ship and take a few pot shots at the Mush before giving in to the inevitable in our comfy, Alpha Dorm beds. I'm pretty sure Terrence died from injuries given to him by Paola in the end, there, too.


All in all, an awesome ship, and, as I stated on board, my first RP ship, so you'll all have a place in my heart forever. I have some scraps of our conversations at the end (which was, by far, the most fun leg of the voyage), which I'll put up a bit later on. Now it is time to give my fingers a rest and maybe get some coffee, though.
