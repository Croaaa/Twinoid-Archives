I have to admit, my most recent ship took the cake as far as memorable experiences went. I want to share it with you all, because of this:

(Please move this, if this is not the proper place for it. It seemed like the best fit, but I might be wrong!)


I choose to play as Ian, this time around, since out of the available choices, he was the one I had the most experience in and the most skills available to. It turned out that I was the second person awake on the first day, and ended up quite close to being through the entire day with only four-five people.

Also, I did my usual custom of posting "Banana Bread", as I always do.

Raluca and myself decided to make the most of the first day, scouting out all of the items and organizing them into one of two inventory threads (We each created one! But we subsequently used the spiffier, bold text one). I consumed a ration in order to keep going, having made a wrong turn and messed up my AP-MP levels, and unfortunately, became dirtied, but otherwise went about business as usual, before showering in the dorms. (We managed to get the Thalasso shower upgrade, too! Yay for vending machines). All of this occured in or around the Day 1, Cycle 8 time period.

Silhouette

After sleeping it off in real life, and heading to work, I come back online while working to find that everyone has awakened from the bliss of cryosleep, and nearly immediately, the whole world occurred in my slumber. Jin Su had immediately assumed command of the ship and was calling me out for being a liar in my Banana Bread thread, which consisted only of the word banana bread, and that I was dirty and suspicious because I had taken a shower in the dorms seven hours prior...

...The same shower that Raluca had immediately taken an axe to upon becoming a mush, immediately after I went to sleep, in front of everyone.

Scrolling through the messages, I found the expected threads. Stephen reported Raluca's mushiness, and the crusade began, culminating in the fungus' demise in or around the dorms. Some others were unexpected, as apparently Jin Su had continued his announcements that I was not to be trusted and Chun was close on his tailcoat eyeing me suspiciously. Especially after I had to take a second shower, since I forgot to put on the Apron after gardening.

This went on for a time, with Jin Su seemingly, relentlessly, after my head, despite my reassurances and the rest of my actions largely benefiting everyone around. Eventually, I earned the support of Terrence, and later Kuan Ti, who both appeared to be social and friendly once shown that I was dedicated to the cause of the Daedalus and it's crew. Chun eventually came around to reluctantly admit that I didn't appear to be a Mush, and Jin Su abdicated his position, begrudgingly, before turning to what I assumed was the next person he thought was Mush.

Silhouette

Around this time, we noticed our first two (and also very important) inactive players. Paola had marched around the ship and idled in the Laboratory before doing nothing for the rest of the day, would not speak in the discussion channel, and made no efforts to set up the Comms (Which led to me taking a Twinoid and promptly being suspected for that, as well!)

Janice, on the other hand, had stopped in the Central Corridor and never moved for the rest of the game.

Eventually, this became a problem, and the rest of us began considering ways of dealing with these two, as their titles were extremely important to our survival. As we were debating Paola's case (We decided Janice was less important, since we could use the Hacker Module to change BIOS settings), I happened back into the Laboratory, since we had moved my plants there, and noticed a little gem in the logs:

Gioele and Stephen had both entered the room, and began flirting with Finola, and promptly began to fight, with Gioele being the aggressor, picking up Schrodinger, and running away. Clearly, this had to be the only instance of domestic violence I had ever seen in the game of Mush. Upon his death, it turned out Gioele was not a mush, and therefore had either been the best roleplayer ever, beating a rival for the affections of "his woman", or he was a griefer, and we had to report him.

As we were all recoiling from the confrontation, our fears turned to Paola, once more, since the remaining mush had disappeared from the crew indicator on our Hubs. Anonymush we all shouted, laughing at the silliness of such a choice, before we attempted to verify whether she was or not, the plan being that she had to have some indication that she was a Gold player (Because she needed it to get the second slot!). As it turns out, there was no indication, and Chun attacked, anyways, but was unable to kill her.
On december 9, 2013
Silhouette

It was also around this time that Jin Su had begun his particular brand of "suspecting" Kuan Ti, because he had used a mage book to gain the Shrink ability, effectively replacing our inactive Janice. Supposedly, the argument started because Jin Su and Chun had wanted the latter to be the Shrink (Chun, that is), while Kuan Ti, and later Terrence and myself, thought that Kuan Ti had made the correct choice, since he was constantly near and in the dorms, and was a Neron tech, anyways, effectively replacing Janice, completely. The commander constantly insisted that he could be, or was, Mush, despite objections and resentment building because of this. Eventually, I attempted to persuade the commander to be less antagonistic to the crew, which... was foreboding, to say the least. Chun continued to back him up, and while the argument largely resolved itself peacefully, tensioned remained between the two parties.

The next cycle, and the Lab has burst into flames after the ship sustained hunter damage, burning a Hydropot, our Mush Review book, and breaking our Lab Camera, with Jin Su promising to use a mission to give someone AP to clear out the fire. After a bit of running, I managed to gather a fire extinguisher, and get to the lab, with exactly 0 AP to spare.

Jin Su's mission never came, despite multiple requests for assistance from myself and one from another user. Eventually, I picked up the Mush Sample to protect it from the fire at the cost of my health, and Terrence, who had been flying in Patrol ships to kill the Hunters, managed to come to the rescue, using my coffee I had left to put out the fire and fix the broken camera! Hooray!

Resting on our jubilations for a moment, we all enjoyed a brief moment of tranquility... before Jin Su came back online, and either through not seeing any of the previous actions or through completely unavoidable tunnel-vision, accused Terrence of behaving Mush-like and inactive.

It's at this point, I believe, that the Doom-Chord of the ship had been struck.

Jin Su also made another topic asking if I had been active and whether I was performing my duties, before also making insinuations that I was not communicating much with the ship, to which Kuan Ti, Terrence, and briefly Chun came to my aid and advocated my reliability and continued dedication to the ship, along with the multiple threads I had participated in prior to Jin Su's arrival, to which he abdicated again.

Unfortunately, at this point, Jin Su's post against Terrence had taken off, and flown all the way to the moon, with Chun getting involved at some point and taking the Commander's side. This led to Terrence becoming more and more hostile towards both of them, with Kuan Ti and myself both joining him in decrying Jin Su's unwarranted accusations and heavy-handed demeanor towards his crew, along with Chun's loyalty to him despite these actions. Terrence eventually broke, and dropped select obscenities, to which neither Kuan Ti nor I supported or participated in, though we did support the overall cause, which at this point had become that we did not see Jin Su's, and to a lesser extent Chun's, actions to be helpful to the ship, and found that he had not been accountable enough to his own failings.

It was at this time, amidst the arguing in the discussion threads, that Roland, disgruntled after never being able to find the Medlab bed unoccupied, and fed up with the crew, decided to grief and steal the Mush Sample from the lab, before flying out into space with the patrol ship and shouting Adios Muchachos!

We formed a group to attempt to combat this, immediately after I posted on the forums about it, and concluded that we should secretly pretend Roland had doomed us all, while planning to leave him behind. (The intent was to make him want to stay outside so we could leave him, and not allow him to come back inside to harm us further). Chun, still annoyed with Terrence from the earlier conversation, quit the conversation and immediately demanded, in public forum, that I drop the Apron and Blaster I was holding (I had explained that it was to keep Roland from coming back and stealing them, as well). At this point, I became fed up, and I declared that I was no longer interested in dealing with Jin Su or Chun, who refused to coordinate with us and held three of the most active and helpful crewmembers in contempt, despite only one being justified (Due to language and name-calling), and even that one was because he, too, had been subject to their heavy-handed accusations and, as they had come to be called, Witch-hunts that Jin Su continued to hold.

I declared that I was going to work on my plants, in silence, and dropped my iTrakkie, effectively cutting off communications except for those who came to whisper me. I still kept up with the conversations, for the rest of the day, by picking it up and reading through the logs, but I refused to speak to anyone about anything unless they were directly addressing me in a non-confrontational manner.

It was at this time that Kuan Ti and Terrence had effectively rose to challenge Jin Su and Chun over their leadership of the Daedalus and it's crew, with Kuan Ti insisting that the two take a more backseat role in the ship's operations, while Terrence continued to argue support for this cause, against their actions, and unfortunately, remained hostile, though he did attempt to extend an olive branch, once... admittedly with some passive-aggressiveness, which at this point was permeating the ship.

Over time, this resulted in Paola being outed as the Mush, and Jin Su being killed by Kuan Ti, in what was effectively a mutiny aboard the Daedalus. I never resumed communications with the crew, though I believe Kuan Ti had mentioned he was going to attempt to persuade me to after the Mutiny was a success. Chun had since hidden the Hacker module, however, and this caused us to lose valuable time in escaping the oncoming hordes of Hunters and Asteroids.

...Alas, we never had the chance. I appears that the dramatic clashes on the ship were insignificant to the universe around us. Had Jin Su or Kuan Ti prevailed in the mutiny, it appeared we were destined to be struck with so much damage in the next few cycles from Hunters, Aracks, and Asteroids, that the Daedalus was torn apart, unable to withstand the damage. We all died, as if we were just specks of dust in cosmic sand.



...And then everyone had reports filed on everyone. And we're all being docked with SDF points, apparently. =(

The End.
