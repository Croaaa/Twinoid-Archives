How we ended up with 9 mush to Sol

Phase 1
Day 2, ship starts. Jin and I selected as mush. After discussing our goals for the game, we agree that we wish to convert the ship, and that rampage is not to be considered unless all other options are closed to us.

I establish myself as an active, productive member of the crew. As a gold level 3 Terrence, I'm certainly a valuable member. Build the sofa, shoot some hunters, the usual. I also make my standard posts trying to organize the team.

I start arguing with Ian about the validity of doing mycoalarms as first research, end of day 2 I think it was. I would have said the same thing whether human or mush, and was very, very tempted by this conversation to prove my point... with the knife. It was cycle 8, Jin and I both had 2 spores on us, and Jin had mycelium spirit, phagocyte, infector and fertile... so 5 spores coming to us at day change, one of which would be free. And the knife lying on the shelf. We could have killed half the crew. But where's the fun in that?

Second day, Jin and I converted Kuan Ti, who turned out was once a day login, fairly new player, who had gone offline in front corridor. We'd gone on a quick expedition, and it was time to leave orbit.... problem: Jin Su miscaluclated his AP from turning Kuan Ti and was stuck on the bridge unable to leave orbit. He blamed it on a broken lateral reactor, claiming he couldn't leave orbit and was going to bed. He was immediately under heavy suspicion from Hua, Gioele and Paola. Next target: Paola, since she and I were the main pilots and trapping bays to get her seemed easiest.

When Jin woke, and decided to leave orbit, Hua also decided to go collect scrap in the pasiphae, and did so in the seconds between him checking a turret to see if anyone was outside and clicking leave orbit. Hua was dead, the pasiphae lost, and Jin Su more suspect than ever. Thankfully, Paola compared the time stamps on him leaving the turret and her take-off to confirm that yes, it truly did appear to be an honest mistake.

At this time, I could have simply stopped piloting and the ship would have blown up. I was the only one doing it. But we were determined to lead these humans to peace and prosperity.

Phase 2
With Jin Su under heavy suspicion and no idea how useful Kuan would or wouldn't be, I worked hard to establish my humanity. I faked gaining AP from eating a ration while piloting. I opened up a pm tab with the humans we assessed to be the biggest threat to our mission of bringing the humans peace- the Rinaldos. I suggested to them that while yes, it did appear that Jin Su was mush, I couldn't handle piloting by myself, and given that Janice was terribly unreliable, we needed his leader ability as well, so we should find the other mush and cure him.

I also began talking with Eleesha, who immediately shared her suspicions with me. I did my best to guide her, while not doing so too well... for instance, she was suspicious of Ian for not being at full health, even though he has never been to bravo dorm or dirty. I pointed this out. I gained her trust. I asked her when she was going to print the list, she wasn't sure, and took my advice to print it sooner rather than later, since we had little research and she had no clear suspects. Myself, newbie sprinter Roland, Eleesha, Frieda, Raluca and Chun were on the list. Eleesha immediately declared the mush on the list to be either Frieda or Raluca, probably Raluca, and wanted to kill her. I did my best to stall, since we wanted to convert as many as possible, and I hoped to do a convert-and-kill on Eleesha to get a list mush dead and buy us breathing room.

However, Jin came up with a better plan. A bold plan, one that wouldn't work on a ship with anyone active collecting logs, or with research being completed (at this point only antispore gas was done, if I remember correctly). We refined it, and right before day change, acted.

We readied 2 spores each. Jin went up to the bridge about an hour before day change. Knowing that Paola at least was online, I entered the bridge 30 seconds to day change, took down the bridge camera, and spiked her once. Jin Su delivered the other two spores, left the bridge, made two more right at day change. He came back and we turned Gioele as well. I put the camera back up, detoured to the lab to fix the apron which had been broken in a fire, and went back to bed. Kuan Ti and Paola took anonymush. We were now 5 and humans none the wiser. We held our breath and waited to see if any would enter the bridge and see the logs of my taking down and putting back up the camera with four of us there.... No one did.

Phase 3
From there on out, we didn't bother much with subtlety. Eleesha tried to kill Raluca and failed because no one would help her. I spoked to Eleesha and Raluca one on one to try to diffuse the situation. It worked for about a day, and then Eleesha got impatient and killed Raluca. Eleesha panicked at having killed a human, and food tested herself, so there went the idea of a convert-and-kill to pin her as list mush. Ian had also food tested himself, using bananas instead of planting them... during an O2 crisis no less. When questioned on this, he responded that he was saving the pots for alien fruits. As a human, I would have pointed out that you can graft onto young plants and his selfish play was actively hurting the ship. As a mush, I had bigger things to worry about, and simply decided that I'd rather shoot him with the natamy he was working on instead of converting him.

We ended up converting mostly inactive pilot Frieda and killing her, with Gioele going to far as to pick up the mycoalarm in the room she was in so w could turn her, then put it back, declaring in chat that it was useless in a room with a mush. And there was the dead 'list mush.' We could all relax a bit.

Attention then turned to Janice. She was mostly silent, wandering through bravo dorm and the refectory several times... possibly eating many rations without cooking them. She was also holding the knife at that point, and went idle in the lab for quite some time instead of a dorm, despite being asked at the beginning of the ship when she was in the medlab to always make sure she can get back to a dorm. Eleesha kept asking where her AP was going.... where indeed? I can only assume she was the one relaying research with Ian, but when Eleesha speculated, Ian never volunteered this information... I do not know whether or not Eleesha checked this for herself. I spoke with Janice one on one... she would not drop the knife for Eleesha, who she deemed paranoid, but she would, and did, for me.

Ian finished the natamy, Eleesha grabbed it and brought it to the dorms, ready to blast Janice with it. I suggested we ask Jin Su to do it to see how he reacted at the idea of killing our 'known mush.' It wasn't a suggestion I expected her to go along with, but I only needed to stall her until the end of the day, when we could awaken more. It was, quite simply, an attempt to prevent senseless deaths, and it worked. Eleesha ended up dropping the natamy in RC and going to sleep. I collected it.
