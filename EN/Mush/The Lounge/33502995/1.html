This was definitely a learning experience. If others on the ship had been more active, I would've suffered an early assassination. At one point I was literally accused of being a Mush, and even though I was an Alpha, I lied about it. Our Stephen, the other Alpha, had been through the Beta and was guiding me.

The first to die was Raluca, because our Raluca didn't know you had to be alone at the end of every cycle or lose morale.

Things people didn't notice: my taking the Mush sample from the lab to one of the dorms, several sporings in front of others, my killing Chun with a knife, and Hua's grenade toss that did practically nothing if I recall correctly.

I even played up Paola getting upset after Gioele died, even though as he was human and I was Mush I was thinking, "Yay, one less human."

All in all, great learning experience, but playing like that in any future ship would get me killed.
