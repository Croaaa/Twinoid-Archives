At this point, only one person hadn't been invited to the mush-hunting channel, and two others had been invited but weren't completely confirmed, though it seems highly unlikely that they were mush. But after two of them fell ill and I used the serum on Raluca just to make sure, we could finally be certain that Chao had indeed been our only alpha mush. All in all, a very fun trip, so thanks everyone.
