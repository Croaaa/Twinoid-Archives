The odds of us having one mush among us was something like 63%, the odds of two of us being mush were something like 12%. Depending on which team you play for, the RNG either loved or hated us.

But let's back up a little. First, we had Captain Crankypants (original JS) who started off by accusing me of holding a blaster because I had the audacity to pick it up from somewhere and bring it to the back of the ship. I had dropped it in BB, but that little bit of log was conveniently ignored so he could continue to yell insane things at me and various other crewmates.

Derek had been dirty since before mush selection and was asleep when the ship filled. He was in AD for that entire time and was asking about going to shower. Since I was clean and wanted to move to AD, I went to RC first so any spores would be logged, he switched dorms and I went to AD. Not to return to BD until much, much later. But somehow, that meant that we were both mush. Didn't matter how much we explained how game mechanics work, we were both mush working together and that proved it. Also, at some point, I apparently Did The Thing with Derek... I'm not sure what made him invent that, but it never did happen.

So anyway, Derek went on a bit of a rampage and the player behind it asked me in IRC if I knew what was going on. He said that Ian had thrown some grenades in lab and killed Stephen. This is where I become gullible the first time. I believed him. Because surely a TE person wouldn't lie OUT of game. But nope, totally lied. He admitted it quickly, but I had already called for Ian's death on ship. I quickly pointed out that I hadn't seen the logs with my own eyes and that someone else should verify (since I'd used all my AP fixing a damned door to get into lab) before doing anything. I'll let Derek tell his full story, but his rampage was rather effective morale-wise. Derek (one TE person) took out two other TE (KT and Stephen) among a bunch of morale deaths shortly after.

This left two of us. Me and Raluca. I had no idea Raluca was the other mush... she hadn't done anything remotely mushy (that I could tell) and wasn't even slightly on my radar. And continued that way for entirely too long. But we'll get there in a moment.

Since I got dirty fixing the door for lab, I finally went back to BD, showered and took a bed for a while. Then out of nowhere, Jin Su kills Raluca and I promptly flip my shit, screaming at him that he's fucking insane. This was my last TE comrade and how she's dead. And we've had another hit to everyone's morale. Most people are hovering around 1 or 2 morale at this point. I know I was down to 2 and I had spent one cycle with Janice early on. I checked logs to see what was going on and did notice that Raluca walked in, dropped a blaster, Jin Su immediately picked it up and killed Raluca. But now I'm being hit constantly and was down to 2hp and ran... and in the heat of the moment, the idea of a transfer didn't even dawn on me. Captain Crankypants was still the same old Crankypants I'd grown to know and hate.

Shortly after this, Terrence used NERON announcements to make a series of amusing posts about how to help people's morale. I'm not sure how half of the living crew continued to live, especially the idle Janice, but they did. And Hua finally got comms up and running and things were not so dire anymore.

The new Jin Su (Jinluca?) decided to RP the same kind of insanity that we'd been seeing from the original. He was getting more and more absurd, but to the rest of us, this wasn't at all out of line with what we'd seen before. I did notice one comment he made about being an "adamantium" player, which immediately made me go yell at someone in IRC (jokingly) that wasn't on ship because it was one hell of a coincidence that this unrelated person would use the same joke a day or so later. That alone should have raised my eyebrow more than it did, but this is gullible part 2. I fell for this aspect of RP. Then JS suddenly developed an actual personality that wasn't just yelling at people for random things. He was actually kinda funny. The he asked "how to steer ship Toward Eden" and I finally got it. I had no AP to deal with it, but got it.

Someone finally took him out and we were mush free. It took us a while to get PILGRED going (thanks to Paola not trading the inactive Janice when we had a chance and had asked her repeatedly), but we finally did and finally got back to Sol. It wasn't the best ship ever, but at least Jinluca made it amusing in the end instead of a miserable experience with Captain Crankypants.
