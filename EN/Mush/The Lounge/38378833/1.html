Paola agreed but changed her mind, because her only mush skill was phalogate, and Ian was playing it smart. She decided to spike the low-hanging fruit Jin Su, who was hanging out in the garden. Jin Su continued to do random things until he was killed many turns later - but I'm getting ahead of myself.

I tell Paola that they have figured it out and they're coming for her--Paola says "I'll just decode this rebel base to prove I"m human ." In the end, Frieda kills Paola without any resistance.

And then it happens - My friend Ian claims the kill in my name. "FOR DELZOUP!"

It was at this point I decided an AP became mandatory to explain why my buddy was killed in my name Sorry Paola!

So now I was left with the random Jin Su, but not all hope was lost. I had managed to trap Ian once when building the sofa, and again when handing off the medkit so he could heal Chun, who had gone afk for the long weekend (Thank you Chun!). Jin Su walked around being his random self, using the microwave, eating food, and pulling out a spore and spiking Terrence, momentarily ruining my plans to turn Ian. However, since Jin Su had spored in the room with Terrence his mushness was quickly discovered and he was eliminated.

At this point the humans declared victory, since Ian was certain that Paola ran out of AP trying to spike him directly. Ian asked our (new) Eleesha to print the list, but there was no follow up on that. While we were getting ready for the expo, I managed to trap and turn Ian.

Now there were two hidden mush. She had apparently told Chun - the list was the one with my name, NOT Paola or Jin Su on it, so very bad - but no further mention was made. Things were going swimmingly, until I woke up dead.

Derek had killed me, and then being at 0 moral, died himself.

At this point the journey was over for me, but I understand that Ian took his revenge...
