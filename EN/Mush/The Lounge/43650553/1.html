Again and again she punched the buttons on the terminal, but failure after failure only made her more and more furious. Her previously pale and beautiful face became a darker and darker shade of red, her features twisted into an unspeakable grimace of wrath. For a second, she glowed white hot, then melted into a puddle on the floor. She was struck by sudden divine intervention, for her anger was threatening to end the universe.

She was sleeping peacefully next to me in alpha dorm. I was going to deface the logs and spore her, then Andie would finish her off. Success!
