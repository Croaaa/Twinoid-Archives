We started D2C1. I'm not entirely certain when the ship filled up, but I was selected as human. Now, I can't exactly say that there was concrete reasoning, but from very early in the ship (Maybe as early as D4), I had a gut feeling that Ian was mush. I actually confessed this to a fellow TE member because I was pretty damn sure of it too. Fastforward several days and we'd cleaned up all our inactives, including Gioele, Stephen, and... Janice Things were still really vague, we hadn't cleaned up very many humans. We had a few solidly confirmed by the name of Chao (by vomit), Terrence (by expo), Paola (by accidental visible foodtest because someone walked in on her), and a few others that I forget. The rest we began to steadily rule out as we came to the conclusion that yeah their actions may have been stupid at times but probably not malicious. The Xyloph list really helped.

When it came time to print the list, a small group of us had narrowed it down to Eleesha, Frieda, and Ian. We needed Ian for a late game, sad to say it. When we printed the list and it originally showed Ian's name, I cleared Eleesha for deciding to even print it, killed Frieda, and said her name was the one on the list to hopefully buy some time for Raluca to get back on. When she did, she geniused the cure and stuck Ian immediately, and that's how we got mush free, around D7.

That began twelve days of our slog. I'm going to tell those of you who have never tried it, playing a long game without Janice is really, REALLY hard. As Jin Su, my job drifted between morale booster, expo runner, and pilot because I had shooter. I administered at least a dozen different speeches to try to keep everyone's morale high, and I had a pretty good hunter kill count. We were actually doing pretty well, but we found two cristalite zones that both gave us mush traps. Well, Eleesha and Hua died late into our game. Eleesha from trying to fix a door, and Hua from fatal depression. We found a third cristalite, but by then we were at critical fuel so we made the fateful decision to go for a HC planet instead of a cristalite planet, to try to prolong our game. Instead...

Well, we performed the expo, left the planet, and then Chun died from fatal depression. At this point we made the decision of whether to keep pushing or to just go back. We decided to try just a little more, I got a panic attack putting me at one morale, so I ate every fruit we had on board to put me back at four, and then... Kuan Ti died from injuries, putting me at three and Finola at zero. That cycle I decided to return us home to Sol, and sadly end our run. This crew was fantastic though, and it's really sad to be screwed over by RNG. But that's what happens sometimes.
