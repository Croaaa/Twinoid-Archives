Gioele here.

Eleesha and I started as Alpha Mush. Regrettably, my partner was not quite capable of evading attention and got killed very rapidly. I knew I had to act quicly to escape the same fate. So I strategized on what to do. Conversion of others I considered not an option. I was on my own with an alert crew. I would be dead probably before I even had my first victim. Demoralization was a path I quickly discarded as well, because both Jin Su and Janice were active. I turned my mind to the thought of fire. A beautiful, big conflagration, eating up all human life in the stainless steel shell of Daedalus. Soon, it became an all-powerful vision of what needed to be done.

First off, I made sure the two extinguishers were both hidden in an isolated location, centre bravo turret. My plan was for this room to be one of the first to burn, so that the extinguishers would eventually both break.

I made sure I had two spores and full AP on me. I chose Bypass, Phagocyte and Pyromaniac. The plan: put non-adjourning 6 rooms (CBT, Lab, Central Corridor, CAS, Nexus and Alpha Bay 2) on fire, override Neron's BIOS and allow non-pilots to fly and mess up the other settings in the process. I would pick up the wrench and the Mush sample and fly off with the Pasiphae.

The execution of the plan with exactly as planned with the added bonus of getting to put the drone on fire as well. As I executed right before cycle change, the fires immediately spread to another room.

The crew was vigilant, however. They quickly formed a plan to draw me back in to the Daedalus with their Magnetic Net by moving the ship. They made sure to check the BIOS (Janice), put in one fuel in the chamber (Raluca), move the ship (Jin Su) and guard the room (Janice). I was depleted of AP, so I could only watch in horror how they hacked me into bits with great efficiency. The joke was on them when it turned out I did not have the two coveted extinguishers on me, so valuable time was lost.

All the while, the fires spread like crazy and the crew decided to push for a minor victory by returning to Sol, rightly reasoning that all Mush must have been destroyed.

In the end, just before Pilgred was finished, there were 18 fires and lots of broken equipment. Andie found the two broken extinguishers, so they had somewhat more control over events.

@Jin Su - I really hope you enjoyed your first ship. You did a great job, captain!

I gave hearts to the active crew. I think I messed up your ship a lot, but you persevered anyway and pulled a victory from the brink of defeat. Very well done and I imagine that must have been a gratifying experience.
