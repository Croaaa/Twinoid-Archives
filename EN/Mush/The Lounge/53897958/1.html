The ship ! http://mush.twinoid.com/theEnd/9483

If someone had told me two weeks ago that we would survive that long, I wouldn't have believed it. Even surviving the first day was almost unexpected, with all those hunters trying to destroy us before mush selection. Frieda was idle, Terrence never picked skills and never talked, Roland did talk and pick skills... but he took Shooter and Sprinter, and then went inactive. Hua finally woke up, but she was a beginner who stayed outside after killing only one hunter.

It it hadn't been for a few of us who hacked the command terminal to move, the poor ship wouldn't have lasted long. And then luckily Jin Su woke up, I was able to copy Pilot from him, and things suddenly looked much brighter.

Of course we still had inactives, but we also had a good core of active people. The mush were killed early (sorry, Eleesha, I would have loved roleplaying with you, but walking dirty into that dorm with two active crewmembers was a mistake...), and Chao killed Kuan Ti before being killed.

In the end, only 9 of us remained, then 8 when we lost poor Jin Su during an expo. We missed you, captain... We had no tech, which could also have been a problem, but we still managed to repair Pilgred and to deal with repairs. And there were a lot of those. We might have been a poor small crew, but the RNG hated us more than the SDF. Oxygen tanks kept breaking even in the first days, not to mention other equipment. And those fires... argh, those constant fires without Fire detector...

My only small regret is not finding the last starmap, after being lucky enough to find two on the same planet. But it was still an awesome ship, with an amazing crew who fought and never gave up until the end.

Thanks, you guys, you were all great !
