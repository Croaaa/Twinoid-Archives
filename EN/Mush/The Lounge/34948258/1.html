Hello crewmates, Janice here Can't find words of gratitude to everyone on board, you all did incredible! I'm simply happy all 11 of us made it to Eden alive, things were getting harder on tracking down everyone's health after D12

I wanted to thank absolutely everyone on ship, and first of all our captain for his generous play and great leading skills!

Doing AP for non-native speaker is kinda hard, apologies in advance for any awkward & unclear words below; but I'm hoping to show some scenes from inner life of our voyage Dates or maybe even sequence of actions could be inexact, also sorry if I forgot some important scenes of our journey.

Janice's Diary

D1, D2
So, here we come - Chun grins a grin and says she's going to hunt mush, and she just can't sit in corner passively being Our Only Hope; not the best outcome for crew morale, on the other hand, both JinSu and me will be able to show ourselves cheering up everyone.
Raluca idles in Alpha Bay - what in Universe did she forget there? Eleesha doesn't seem to be fully unhybernated - she's not moving or talking - maybe Cryo system was bugged...
Captain activates ACTOPI protocol - love to see these tiny coloured dots on map

D3
Finola seems depressed; she did help us with early research, but then something happened with her, seemingly she gave up; maybe kidnapping affected her far too heavy. She's not responding or willing to do anything else. Eleesha dies unconscious. Raluca still stands in Alpha Bay, and Kuan Ti says he's reading his "How to Become Effective Technician" pocket edition, but it'll take him 12 days to complete, due to tiny fontsize. Terrence is only fond of robotics, and not about to help us repairing anything. We have no tech, and hope for a slight chance to get mage book from Xyloth.
Gioele suddenly recalls where he has hidden 2 spare cameras, and secretly tells me during one of seances that he's now very very fond of... coffee! Nothing I can help with about coffee addiction, alas. Maybe it'll help us some day.
I'm loudly asking dear Chun to read medic book... 4th time... Hopefully she agrees, she seems to be a little stressed about it, as if it could harm her.

D4
Frieda slips in shower quite of the sudden and breaks her... ugh, it's not neck - she was MUSH! Totally unexpected, at least for me. We're going to another O2 planet Kuan Ti found for us, and - what a luck! - bring Jumpkin with us. Ian starts searching for additional hydropots and hey! - here they are. Journey promises to become better; if only Paola gets us Tech book...
Local quarrel between captain and ship owner is settled peacefully. Usual thing to happen in real universe, no wonder
Chun reads medic book, our research moves a bit faster now.

D5
Xyloth books are Astrophysics and Botanics. As I had some secret sympathy towards MrSoulton, I've decided to help him doing the garden... he may appreciate it later on. Astro book destiny is being discussed, Gioele seems to be best candidate for it. All crew is exhausted working on Plasma shield

D6
After short discussion with us, Chao shoots at Raluca 3 times, and she's still watching at him and not falling... weird; one final shot reveals she was another MUSH; but many of us, me included, considers her to be infected by anonymush; time of mutual suspects; I'm quite sure in Kuan Ti, we were talking in PM right fromthe start, and also in Gioele, whom I was exlaining ways of the game for several days; finally, we've made open "Proven human" document to help us finding possible traitor; few of us who were ill or caught up eating too much are enlisted there.

Fire at bridge, our book is destroyed, noooooo.... thanks Nebulae, it was Commander's Manual Gioele reads wonderfully surviving Astrophysicist book, stuffs up with coffee and finds us Cristallite planet right away.

D7
Terrence suddenly finds himself capable of repairing and piloting!! Lost hope is arising! Later this day Roland, who was just running around the ship shooting from cannons beforehands, says he's now trained enough to pilot Patrol ships and shoot! Now we can withstand hunters. Need to mention Terrence's pack of 3 drones was slowly upgrading (he was planning to build 4th when we get more scraps), and 2 drones were already performing automated piloting. Magnetic net allows us to keep them nearby - smart drones just don't find it necessary to land themselves.
Expo to first Christalite planet - I'm lost among mirroring crystals. Our brave Commander launched rescue mission, and found me! We've got alien opener(!) and invertebrate shell to keep in rear turrets.

D8
New Gioele's planet, no Cristalite this time, but high chance to get lost in snow; I'm reluctant to go, and ask to find another one. However, we had to escape from hunters, and there was exact amount of fuel, so here we are, orbiting this cold unfriendly world. Oh well, let's go, Jin Su... noone else wants to... yes, compass is for you.
...
Hua rushes in, grabs compass, and without waiting for captain, launches expo. Of course, I'm lost in snow! But we're greatly awarded - we found Crystallite map fragment in Ruins!!!
Terrence rushes to rescue and saves me together with Hua; needless to say I'm done with expos, and tell openly to everyone that I'll ONLY GO TO PLANET WITH CRISTALITE FROM NOW ON!

Towards the end of the day Hua and Terrence decided to play an exciting game and measure between themselves: who's tapeworms are longer? Everyone is warned once again that there's absolutely no need to eat raw alien meat

D9,D10
Everyone is in Circle of Verified Humans! Mush menace is over Ian finally is generous enough to share jumpkins with crew - we have 8 jumpkin trees and 2 anemoles, and I'm happy to do garden with Ian... and not only garden 4th drone is built, almost all projects are complete, Paola gets us garden heating lamps, NERON is slowly finishing CPU overclocking with his usual 5%/cycle. And yeah, great news - PILGRED IS COMPLETE!

D11
Two days of mad scanning allowed Gioele to find us another Cristalite planet. No O2, oceanic, 2 low temp, alien life, caverns, and smth unimportant; 50% chance to get lost, deep inside myself I don't want to go, but promise is made, time to keep my word given. Ian and Kuan Ti join expo this time. Jin gets us on surface, and miracle began!
- cold zone - I'm lost
- MIA zone - I'm found
- cristalite zone - map fragment
- ocean - provision
- low temp - I'm lost again, 4th time already
- caverns - map fragmenr
- alien life - battle (now you'll SUFFER for leaving me in blizzard)
Then Jin Su launches another mundane rescue mission (3 out of 9 expos were rescue, and all crew seemingly became trained experts in finding me in wilderness)

D12
Supercalc research time, and we're quite busy surviving and repairing everything. Half of patrol ships, bridge and engine are affected with electric shock that destroyed equipment. Terrence and all 4 drones are madly repairing everything. Chao fights all fire on board, he's simply everywhere. Gioele scans another planet, but we just can't afford going there, Eden is awaiting!

D13
We're rapidly completing all undone glory research, including RetroFungal Serum, try to monitor everyone's health & morale, and majority of crew decides to go to Eden without risk of losing anyone of 11 survivors. Everyone's eyes are glowing and fingers trembling expecting salvation coming soon, noone is able to think clearly!

D14
Gioele hacks terminal with 2 coffee cups and hacker's kit, and while he's hovering his finger over Eden button waiting for us to complete last research, Jin Su comes online, takes over control terminal, and HERE WE ARE, in new paradise, ready to repel MUSH menace in whole universe! All team congratulates each other, and look forward for another GREAT ADVENTURE!!!
