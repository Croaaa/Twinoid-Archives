Hello folks, a handful of us just got off this ship here (http://mush.twinoid.com/theEnd/9099) and I had so much fun that I have to write about it.

I was Frieda or more like Feisty Frieda if you will...

The ship took forever to fill up and the connection to Sol had already been established by the time I woke up. So I parked myself on a bed in B Dorm under our lovely Janice's care. Our starting planet was an easy one: 6 cases, not really many threats so Jin Su and Andie decided to organize and expedition to it so I said I'd tag along. All in all, Mush select happens and I'm human lucky enough. So I go over to IB for Expo but no one's there and, given past experiences with waiting for an expo on the starting planet for ages and the nature of the planet, I soloed it. Dropped my trackie and took a spacesuit and off I went. It went well, I only got 2 illnesses from the 2 Forest zones and a bunch of Alien Steaks from the Intelligent Life encounter & whatnot. Now, the fun part came when I came back. I pick my trackie up to my "Planets" thread absolutely brimming with paranoid talk over me being Mush or griefing. That lead to a rather heated argument with both Kuan Ti and Andie until Eleesha and Raluca stepped in and we just sorta let things die down.


Now, since people clearly didn't like me, I grabbed the Armor and Knife because Chun was rather inactive and I got proven human due to getting 3 illnesses within a day (and vomiting all over the ship .-.). I then posted myself on the Bridge for the longest time until Jin FINALLY moved us after Paola FINALLY decoded the second base (lack of sofa and all made it harder). Things were quiet until the end of Day 4 when our lovely Terrence extracted two spores in CC next to an inactive Stephen. By this time we had Mycoscan and all that but no real Anti-Mush research done because most everyone was inactive. He then killed Andie and transferred into Janice (who had one spore on her) Then messed around our BIOS settings. I happened to be in B Dorm after having cured myself from my last illness and had taken a shower. Janice mush (former Terrence) was now sleeping next to me. Cycle change was coming and Raluca the Warrior came in and kicked her to a pulp after confirming that Terrence had transfered into Janice. Cycle change happened so I casually got up and stabbed the poor little soul, killing our first Mush. Soon after, things got back to somewhat normal. Everyone was suspicious of most everyone else.

On Day 5, towards the end of the cycle, our second Mush decided to act. He kicked Chun's behind, killed Hua, sabotaged an Oxygen tank and then sat there in CAS. People now knew that Jin was Mush, but no one had the resources to act on it. I had been sleeping on the sofa next to a Logistics Paola so I had enough AP to get out, ate a ration for good measure as soon as the cycle changed and then stabbed Jin in his fat heart until he turned into minced meat. That was us rid of Mush, but demoralized. The list had been printed after Terry Mush (in Janice's body) was killed and both Terrence and Janice were on it and so was I. But Jin wasn't.

I had uncovered a planet and the first couple of cases looked good, one of them being a MUCH, MUCH needed Oxygen zone. So the plan was to go on Expo in spite of the majority of the 10 cases being a mystery. I ended up falling asleep before I could move the ship and by the time I woke up, Kuan was stranded from trying to hack into the Command Terminal and Eleesha went to a Dorm. So I woke up and went to IB, ready for the expo. And I waited and waited and our Oxygen dropped to 2. Raluca came around for a couple of cycles with either her or myself leaving IB at cycle change to prevent her morale loss. Eventually, I got fed up with waiting for Kuan and Eleesha and I launched the expo with just Raluca and myself. We got really lucky, 16 Oxygen right off the bat, minimum damage and a bunch of tasty fruit. All in all, a huge success.


Our next plan was to rush PILGRED so everyone (except for me who was sleeping in B Dorm to recover some AP) pitched in and gave Raluca the mission that finished PILGRED. Cycle change was coming up as well so I swiftly took us to Sol. Funny enough, both Ian and Derek decided to join us at the end and my rush to Sol interrupted Kuan's attempt to wine and dine Eleesha.

I'll use the next post to say a few words to the few people who really impressed me on this voyage.
