The Honours List

Your Commander, Jin Su, here. I was really impressed with how enjoyable this game was; like my last one there were few Silver or Gold characters (just Terrence, Paola and I) but some experienced and active players nonetheless.

This was my first time as Commander and a Pilot, but I've spent enough time on the forums and Wiki to have gotten used to it easily enough. Fortunately we had another three Pilots so Hunters were never a serious threat and Scrap was reasonably plentiful - two Sofas, a Lizaro, an extra Drone, two Turbo upgrades, a Babel Module and a few hull repairs all built.

Unfortunately our Janice went inactive after burning through her AP with the MAD Kube, and by the time Stephen was eventually able to Solid her into the Dorm she starved or demoralised the next cycle. There was some trouble getting people to stay in the right place for my speeches but fortunately Gioele formulated the titular Rinaldo Co. Team Building initiative. Having spent the entire game flirting with everything in sight, Kuan Ti eventually got to do the Sofa tango with Raluca. Gioele also got Raluca to himself, then Eleesha not long afterwards.

Despite Andie's protests I had us risk the ~12% chance of hitting one of the two (out of twelve total) Seismic zones and getting a Death event. "Never tell me the odds", and our first loss was Ian. It was not in vain however, because Polyvalent Andie combined with our Babel Module bagged us a Starmap Fragment!

I found out that Eleesha was an awesome investigator from the professional state of her Mush hunting chat, having been added along with Terrence from expedition illnesses. Fortunately Eleesha had completed the MycoAlarms in time to catch Andie infecting Schroedinger - she was quickly knifed. As her death message warned, we were left without any Research bonuses so we didn't get much more done.
I also turned out to be a dab hand at Mush hunting, although as usual hesitation caused us harm: after I noticed the Bridge Camera had to have been Sabotaged and only Paola could have done it, we tried to get her to take out the inactive Derek or work on Comms first. Despite never going Inactive, she managed to not do very much of her job and strike while I was asleep. Chun was Demoralised to death and Gioele had killed Paola, but that was two human deaths according to the tracker. My first worry was that Eleesha had been transferred into, but seeing Gioele suddenly with the Solid skill (and then the logs showing he'd knocked over two Cameras) it was obviously a Transfer to take out another useful player. Stephen and I almost managed to take him out, but after a Mutation and running to attack Raluca, Frieda landed the killing blow before any more death occurred. Unfortunately she'd lost her tongue, and with the above-mentioned Speech confusion she soon suicided, followed by Roland the next day.

With seven of us left and no threat from Hunters or Mush remaining, the RNG cackled maniacally to itself: setting fire to the Bridge (twice), three turrets, the Lab with our plants, Front Corridor and Front Storage all in the course of two cycles. The Fuel tanks also broke about six times between them in only a few cycles time. Fortunately with some teamwork we managed to finish PILGRED and head back to Sol, but not before Stephen was shaken and plated to death two cycles too early.

Not that it is particularly important, but my last foray out in a Patrol ship before pushing the Sol button brought me from fourth to first in Glory! Our Top 6 are all in the Top 50 overall for this Season too (found most of the RP crowd that way).
