Eleesha here; this was a seriously turbulent ship. The crew was fairly passive and unexcited, and so looking for any signs of Mush fell entirely to me; a task I may have been overwhelmed by, because it resulted in the deaths of several innocents before we got a clear view of who the Mush might be.

The initial suspect, Roland, fell to the culling blade due to his erratic and strange behavior; he did a lot of strange newbie things while showing decent knowledge in other fields (such as knowing about the importance of trade ships and relaying). The crew agreed with me that it was suspicious, and so he was the first to fall... however he was just a human newbie. Sorry for killing you, Roland.

The second suspect was a result of two occurrences:

  • The Research Console unexpectedly broke. Viewing the logs made apparent that it only could've been a Green Jelly hit by one of three characters: Derek, Hua and Ian.
  • When asked to surrender his bed to me (a Gold character with 3 wildly important skills to the crew), Derek (as Shooter/Wrestler) straight up refused.

When my suspicion rose and I knifed him off the bed so that, if he was Mush, I could finish him faster later, he turned around to shoot me off the bed with his remaining Shooter point before the cycle changed. This prompted me to investigate his AP activity, of which I didn't notice much. Long story short, I collected all the evidence against him and presented it to the Mush hunt, who all agreed to kill him, and so he died just because he couldn't surrender a bed to a more important crew member.

After that, Derek was obviously eliminated as Green Jelly suspect, and Hua turned out to be too contributive to the crew to realistically be a Mush, as she constantly relayed researches, treated plants and shot down Hunters, keeping us alive. So, Ian was the only suspect left, which also aligned with the fact that he lying in bed all day and yet not doing anything whatsoever. As we were slowly making plans on how to kill him, Raluca snapped and went on a rampage in the Icarus Bay after an expo. Stephen and Paola eliminated her with some planning and good timing. And as we all went to sleep, Ian - as expected - went on a rampage and slew Frieda (who foolishly reported having 3 HP in a public thread) and, together with Jin Su, tried to kill Janice. I grabbed a Spaced Potato to slay them, and then all that was left was waiting for our inevitable demise, as we were being chased by dozens of Hunters, had barely any oxygen left, and didn't have the crew to sustain our journey any longer.

TL;DR: My Mush investigations led exclusively to innocent deaths, the Mush rampaged out of boredom, and barely anything ever got done on the ship except for comms and a couple NERON projects. It's a bit disappointing considering we've had a pretty good and active crew (props to Stephen, Jin Su, Kuan Ti, Paola and Frieda for making the ship lively), but I suppose it couldn't be avoided.

I apologize for my headstrong attitude, as I cannot help but take the reins in any game I play out of pure mistrust for other players' competence and activity. Sadly, the signs I took as evidence of mushiness were mostly evidence of ineptitude and un-efficient play. I have yet to learn how and when to rush for a kill, and when to wait for more evidence without risking a rampage. I also apologize for not role-playing Eleesha convincingly; I was going for a blaxploitation movie-kind of "groovy soul sista" character, but I didn't manage the role well enough to keep it up consistently. My cynical and cold character always manages to come out in the end.

Still, thank you for the journey! As short as it was, at least it was pretty interesting.
