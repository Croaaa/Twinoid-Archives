First days. Promising activity and 2 dead mushies.

I woke up early and I was pretty surprised. All of the awake ones were talking, and that wasn't just one and only "Hello-s"! Raluca answered on my "are you a new player"! Even Eleesha and Chun! Oh, what a relief, an active mush-hunting team, that's rare. But I was wrong, this didn't last for a long time.

Eleesha failed self-surgery, said her good byes and almost gave up, but we managed to heal her. Everything should be good, she lied in the Medlab and waited for Finola to patch her up. Unfortunately, she never woke up. I was really hoping, the she would return, I even asked to wait with trading, but I guess she just gave up on that ship. I was upset. Paola traded her for a fuel on D5.

Meanwhile, Chao was a typical Chao, torturing people in the BD. He was short-spoken and brutal, manly man of our ship.
Right after mush selection, 4lvl Ian told us he forgot to take an apron. I know, that people can do such a stupid mistakes, I did it myself sometimes. But not at the very first time and not right after the mush select. So I wrote Chun about that, told her to keep an eye on him. She wasn't online and I was going to sleep, so I added Chao and asked him to torture our Botanist afetr his visit to the BD. Chao showed himself as the manly man again. He told, he has no AP for that, because Ian is too pacifistic.
When I woke up (3 cycles after), both mushies were dead. Stephen and Ian. Have no idea, what happened.

Chun. Oh, Chun was such a fruit. She left the Lab right after the selection and slept in the dorms. I was talking with her privately, trying to explain about, you know, playing for your team or playing at all. She answered me that how dare I?! She's been working hard on our Projects! Oh, and that was a true. We lost Sprinklers and GPU, thanx to that work. Although I explained her about NERON and what should we work on - naaaah, just finish the wrong ones, confuse Raluca (both projects with the same percentage), sleep in the dorms, never collaborate. Chun died on D5 from injuries, have no idea what happened to her.


Middle of the game

So, there we were, 12 active and alive kids.

Neron team finally could finish the right projects, although they weren't so lucky. We had Plasma Shield only as our 4th or 5th project and we lost QS because of it.
Also, Neron team was a PILGRED team as well.

Our Pilots did really good job though, we had 100 armor all the time, even without Plasma. I honestly have no idea what to add there. They were our invisible help. Always there, protecting us. +Terry made us a bunch of Pilot drones and Hua visited almost every planet with me.

Paola wasn't so lucky with updates firstly, but she gave us Medic and Shooter books + we had Diplomat book at the beginning. Which I consider as a win. Medic for Paola, Shooter for Hua-Pilot and Diplomat for me as an ultimate expo-granny.

Chao became our Ian, she took care of our plants. And fires. And girls. I believe, he was the only one, who took care of the girls, by the way.

Researches were going slowly, because Finola was mostly alone. No Ian, no Chun. But she did well, we had our lenses and some other usefull stuff.

Captain. Oh, Jin was hilarious! He was a bit clumsy, sometimes made mistakes and rushed a bit and he had some problems with English, but he was so passionate! He was constantly saying that we should find Eden and last for the 30 days at least. He LOVED speeches and preferred them over other ways of getting some morale. He really cared about every of his crew members - "oh my god?! 10 morale?! speech! speech for you next cycle!", "oh my god, frieda and steel plate! heal her now!"

Gioele was drinking his coffee and scanning for Volcanoes and Mankarogs. Seriously, I believe we had only 1 planet without them from Gio. Also, he was always a bit distracted. "Where are we? What's that planet?". I guess, that's all because of coffee.

I scanned as much as I could and we had 1 expo every day. Can't say if I was lucky or unlucky with that. Almost every of my planets had O2 and Aliens (2 or 3 planets without it). Sometimes a bunch of Aliens. A bunch of Aliens and Shipwreck and Ruins. But Aliens were bad, they stole our Drill in my very first single-person expo and they gave us nothing but 2 Oils and 3 other useless artifacts. I repeat, we had A LOT of Aliens in a lot of expos. Greedy bastards. Anyway, there was no Cristalite. Well, I found one in the middle of the game and it gave me a spore.

We had no starmap till the Day 17. And no one (!!!) suggested to return to Sol! I'm so proud of that, it was one of the best things about this crew. Not a single whining person! The second thing I liked about you kids, that we all looked after each other. People always reported, if they saw a shake or anything and always tried to help. It was so cute, when you guys brought the Medkit from the dorms to heal me at the Bridge. It was D10 or so and I was just steel plated once, not. From the full HP, not critical. But you healed me anyway. I woke up after that, saw that activity and I felt like I was your grandma.

Later days
