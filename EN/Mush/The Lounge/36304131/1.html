Hello everyone !

Created first by Meowcate for the french Mush community, NRJ is a website which allows you to write down the everyday point of view of your character, secretly and in a role-play manner. Your identity is protected for the duration of your ship as no-one will be able to read your logs until the end of the ship.
Every day, a new page opens on new adventures. It's up to you to give them a place where they'll be able to live forever.

The greatest point of interest of NRJ is that all the individual logs from a same ship will form a big diary of this ship, where you'll be able to follow how everyone - humans and mushs alike - lived the same events. Once the Honours list of the ship is available, you'll just have to use the code of the game (theEnd) to link your journal with the ones of others players of your ship.

After a successful interest poll in the discussion forums, we decided to translate the app in english to bring you the possibility to use it more conveniently.

If you want to try the concept, you just have to connect at en.nrj-reloaded.fr. No new account creation required, NRJ uses a securised connection with Twinoid's API. NRJ is an approved Twinoid app, it has been verified and follows the rules fixed by the administrators of Twinoid.

Bear in mind that the app is currently being worked on, and is on beta stage. So please, if you come across a bug, a translation error, or anything that bothers you, we ask you to tell us by any means available, be it in this thread, or via PM.

Special thanks to Avistew for being a big help with the translation work.

For more information, please refer to this thread.

tl;dr : connect to en.nrj-reloaded.fr and have fun !

I suppose it's the same than the French version.
You first need to finish this journal.

Afterwards, go to the [Data] My Journals menu (not sure of the name of this menu since I have no access on the English version of NRJ)
Click on the Edit just under the journal you want to delete.
On this page, you'll have several options:

To delete a journal:
> neron: $burn(diary);

If you haven't written anything on the journal after the real end of your vessel, you probably won't have any empty entries. If you want to keep this journal for yourself but not publish it: > neron: \$hide(diary);

You'll find also on this page the link of your journal if you want to share it, you can modify the name of your journal and your last words and enter the Id of the vessel.
