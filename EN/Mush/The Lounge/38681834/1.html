Oh, hey. here, good to see you guys made it to Eden. It was my third MMM and first time I've ever been Mush on an MMM ship, and wow, what a pain in the ass.

There is a real feeling of "play to win" on MMM ships with the whole crew wanting to drag the ship on for maximum glory and duration, and of course I'm guilty of that too. Looking at it from the perspective of the Mush, however, it is frustrating and it'll shape how I play the game in the future. For instance, food tests are something I will avoid from now on. From Day 1 we had random crew members food testing themselves for instant confirmation of being human, and that is a really tough thing for a Mush to counter on a ship where you can be certain someone is tracking every AP you use.

Andie and I had a plan, and it was a good one, and it would have worked if I hadn't royally botched it at the critical moment. Andie did very well with his part before he had to give himself up. It was clear that many of the crew suspected Andie for being slow with research, especially with all the missions he got from Jin Su. Me being in the Mush hunt channel helped us to keep tabs on suspicions, but I had the feeling a few days in that a new chat had been started without me (having never confirmed myself human) and I was being fed baited info or left in the dark.

Now, hear my tale of woe and failure. Just before we died, we got two spores on Janice via traps. Andie read the Tech book and disassembled the shower, since he knew the humans were after him soon. After he acted, I needed to move fast, because my target was Raluca and I needed to transfer to her while she still had plenty of AP. Unfortunately for me, there were a lot of real life distractions occurring at the moment I needed to act, and where I should have taken the time to read things through, I jumped in without careful planning or thinking.

It was the first time since the patch for me as Mush and I wasn't sure if Transfer had been changed, so just before I acted, I checked to see how much AP it would cost to actually do the transfer. Of course, it's an aggressive action, and I checked while Ian was in the room, so it showed up as 2 AP! Convinced that I would have to save 2 AP to transfer to Raluca, I ran to RAS, infected Raluca with one spore, then used Apprentice to take her Technician ability so I could use the tech points to try to disassemble any other useful items like extinguishers. I ran to Bravo Dorm and blew both points on something, can't remember what. Moving to RC I saw the Blaster and noticed in the logs that Andie must have dropped it when he died, so I went to hide it - and accidently hit the Disassemble button instead! 3 AP blown. I was now down to 1 AP and thought I had ruined my chances to Transfer to Raluca. I went to Alpha Dorm and lamented a little before getting knifed to death by Chun.

Had I had the sense to Transfer to Raluca (as I could have at any moment right up until I died) I could have extracted another spore, went straight to the Dorm with Janice and converted her with a third spore. The crew would have lost all its Technicians and Raluca, and Janice would have been Mush. Maybe not a killing blow, but it would have made things hard.

Oh well, lesson learned. Plan ahead, think things through, and don't panic. I always get jumpy when I'm Mush, guess it takes practice to get used to it! Good ship everyone.
