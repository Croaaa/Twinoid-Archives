My biggest props go to Raluca who was always trying to help, Finola who was a researching MACHINE, Terrance who had swarms of drones flying around the ship, Janice who did lots of hugging to keep spirits up, and Roland who performed his hunter-killing duty like a champ. My biggest frustration was Hua who rarely communicated and, while she did get out there and shoot down hunters, liked to STAY out in her patrol ship for cycle after cycle. I firmly believe we could have probably managed at least one more expedition if we hadn't had to wait on her all those times. She also gave me a new respect for the Magnetic Nets project!
