On this beta ship, Paola and I (Stephen) went after the confirmed mush Finola, who hid with Ian hindering our efforts to kill her. I still had cooking points so I listened to the Mass Effect 2 End Run theme as I tried to head for the Refectory to give the others cooked meals as a fighting chance to take her down..only to not have enough MP. I remember saying something like, "Don't think you've won, woman. The others will get you! I'm coming, Hua!! (seconds before cycle change, there was previously this RPish joke going on that Stephen was infatuated with her instead of Finola ) Imagine my surprise when I found out the one I trusted and thought was going to kill her with me was the one who killed me herself, hehe.

On this recent ship, we got down to 5 crew members left including myself with one mush remaining who didn't do anything mushy the entire time so we spent forever waiting for the inactives to die expecting it to be one of them. The expo team died to a volcano so after I abandoned Eleesha, I used my last AP to entrust Paola with a mission to kill the mush and avenge us. Finola then came out and killed me, so that set up a final showdown between those two.

I think I've come off as rude in the past, but I really like the moments when you're positive you've determined the mush and their intentions. To me it's like those last moments in a cartoon where the investigators start detailing the specifics of the case where they deduced what the criminal's plans were and who they were all along. I get this big 'sense of justice' and come off as railing the mush player when I'm really just trying to achieve that kind of scenario. This ship comes to mind when I caught Paola temporarily removing the camera to convert Janice, who turned off the DMZ Inhibitor. I dropped everything with Stephen to go after them, getting a little too carried away as the 'heroic investigator'.

As far as wonderfully maleficent speeches, go, hehehe...in this ship I sent out this General Announcement towards the end, "Heellloooooo humanity! My apologies if you were expecting Paola, she's just gone 'off air', if you know what I mean! It's the end of the line and I thought I'd get all mushy with you guys. No, really! I'm being mushy! Of course, anyone who was active is now dead, so this is really just me talking to myself. Thank you all for playing right into our hands! You've been a most cooperative ship and I do hope you enjoy the suffocation!'

On this earlier voyage, I ended up becoming the ship's primary scanner. When the Mushrooms were detected, a quarter of the crew became brain dead, while the rest of us continued on. Raluca was becoming suspicious but her bout with decreased morale and evidence for it quickly subsided. Therefore, as we planned an expedition to Upsilon Cargino, I began to reprise my suspicions. The chief and commander, Chao, and Terrence, soon accused the pilot, Hua, of betrayal when she attacked Eleesha.

This is where things get creepy and awesome: Through the ship's lifetime, I developed close ties with Chao, Terrence, and Kuan-Ti since they were the most active. At the time when they killed Hua, my suspicions reached a near climax. I ended up lying through my teeth to Chao and went for the Icarus Bay to gather the armor and a blaster...

What followed: I looked and saw that there were a total of four mush. I calculated with my thousand year old brain that one of the people I trusted was a shroom. Suddenly, I found myself leaving the bay and discovering that I was cornered. Eleesha was in the Rear Corridor while Chao and Terrence were both in the Bravo Bay and Engine Room. I ran to the refectory and downed a ration to finish them off, but a great urge overwhelmed me: I turned into a mushroom! dat irony.

In the end, we controlled most of the ship's activities following the assassination of Janice (who we announced tried to kill me, but she really didn't) and led the ship to it's death at the hands of fellow Mushroom federation fighters.
turns out Chao, Eleesha, Terrence, and Kuan-Ti were the four mushes! I believe this was the best Mush victory I've been in . It didn't help that Kuan-Ti flaunted his ownership of the Daedalus haha.

"Gioele : Precisely. You are the only worthy foe on this ship. Gladiator you who are about to die I salute you!"
