I've had some debates in ships over how transfer works in universe. We know in game it switches who the two people are playing. In universe, I think it's different.
Popular consensus: It switches who the bodies are in. The minds switch into new bodies and have full knowledge of who they were.
In my opinion, the spores transfer bodies. The person that was mush becomes themself again. The new person is overtaken by the spores. They become mush themselves, while the old body is the human it was before infection.
I'd like to hear other opinions on this. Human skills are fairly straightforward, but mush skills are a lot more mysterious.
