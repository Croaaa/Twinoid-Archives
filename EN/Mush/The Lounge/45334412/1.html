It was impressive indeed I didn't believe that we will find Eden, let alone reach D21C1 with the skeleton crew that was down to 6 people. I was pretty pessimistic about the ship before D10, this is what I said:

StephenI suggest we just return to Sol on D10. This is just really bad, the plants are destroyed, we lost the fire detector, first planet with Cristalite was a mush trap and every single other planet has Volcanoes. I don't foresee any Eden under these circumstances.

The only thing that changed my mind was the first starmap fragment that we pulled from Caverns on D10. We decided to give it a shot because giving up is uncool.
When we found the third starmap fragment we decided that it's not enough to just reach Eden, we needed a real challenge to make it the most satisfying trip ever

The main reason why this trip was so successful is that I copied the Caffeine junkie skill from the inactive Gioele and read the magebook. In combination with my Creative skill I was having >50 AP per day, so me and Terrence were able to deal with pretty much everything by ourselves.

I was focusing on healing people, putting out fires and fixing hard to repair things like Gravity Simulator while Terrence was fixing oxygen tanks, doors and other things that have 99% with both tools.

Fighting fires was the most challenging thing on our ship, because we didn't have the Fire Detector. We lost it in the Nexus selection when it came with Plasma Shield.
After D15, we were having up to 6 fires at a cycle and usually we were unable to locate all of them so we had no choice but to ignore them and let them spread. We were trying to use the Anomaly Detector to check if anything was broken by fire, but most of the times it wasn't helpful at all.
