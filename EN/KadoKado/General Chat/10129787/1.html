Discover Popotamo!

A new online word game in the style of Scrabble.

Compete online against other players in various game modes by forming words on the gameboard.

There are two game modes:

Normal Mode: Start a game and play during the breaks throughout your day. Relax!

Rapid Mode: Pay attention! the gameplay is quick and exhilarating!

A Browser-Based Game: Popotamo requires no downloads or installations, play at home, at work or wherever you choose!

Popotamo changes as you play, you can improve your performance.

Win games and use your quids to improve your skills (Wisdom, Speed, Imagination) or to buy special in-game options which will improve your performance in future games!

Speed, Imagination and Wisdom are in-game skills which affect your performance during games.

 Options allow you to surprise other players and make the game more exciting!

Popotamo also allows you to communicate with other players!

Monthly Ranking: Receive honorary titles and win Popotamo Cups.

 Game Chat: Communicate with other players during games.

* Forum: Discuss tips, tricks and all things Popotamo with fellow players.

Click here to begin your adventure on Popotamo!
