My second computer is still on the ancient operating system Windows XP, with Chrome 49 and Adobe Flash Player 23, both of which were released in 2016 and were the last supported versions for XP.

Adobe programmed a hard shut-off date of 12 January 2021. You can try setting your computer's clock beyond that date and see if you can still load Flash content. If you have a fairly recent version of Adobe Flash Player (not necessarily the latest, as Adobe coded this a while ago), Flash will be blocked entirely.

Mine is unaffected because my Flash Player was downloaded a year before Adobe announced plans to phase it out.
