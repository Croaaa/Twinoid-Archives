In each level, you get 4 shots (with a white ball) to:
Hit the turtles to change them into point balls; and,
Hit the point balls to collect points (like KadoKado's points).
(The number of turtles is always 2 more than the level number. Also, if you're lucky, you'll find gold balls which each give you 2 additional shots for that level when you hit them.)

To successfully complete each level, you need to hit all of the turtles, but not necessarily any of the balls.
Starting on level 8, 1-3 black balls will appear. (The exact number depends on the level number.) The game will end as soon as the white ball touches any black ball.
Level 12 is the highest level.
Hitting all of the turtles in level 12 gives you bonus points.

When you've accumilated enough points, you use them to bid on in-game powerups, Carapass avatars, MT Passes, or 5 or 10 free games of Carapass. (Normally, only people who don't want MT Passes bid for the free games of Carapass.)
The in-game powerups are: Recharge, Nitro, and Magnet. Once you use them, you can't use them again until you go back to level 1. These are their effects:
Recharge gives you 3 additional shots for that play.
Nitro gives the white ball super speed for your next shot. (Be careful to not hit 2 point balls in a row when you use the Nitro, because the white ball will end up going so fast that the game will freeze up and get counted as a loss with 0 points scored.)
Magnet attracts all point balls currently on the screen to the white ball's current position. (When point balls hit it, it will move normally, but that won't change where the Magnet attracts the point balls.)
