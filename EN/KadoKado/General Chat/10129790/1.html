On may 26, 2010
[None]
Kashuat Sentinels:These are, like other Sentinels, unbreakable bricks (you can destroy them in the same way that other unbreakables). After contact with your drilling ball they fire 8 slow shots in your direction. If one of them hits you, you lose a life.
Guardians:These are destructible bricks which after contact with your missile, javelin or drilling ball fire one extremely fast beam in your direction. Effects of this beam are same like above. You can prevent Guardians from shooting by using G, T, O, Q powerups.
