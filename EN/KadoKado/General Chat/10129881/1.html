M-T is making another game site? Who woulda thunk it?

I highly dislike M-T's business strategy of flooding the market with half-baked "me too" product launches and abandoning old properties as soon as they show the slightest sign of plateauing or slowing down.

Instead of constantly distracting players with "new" games, M-T developers should focus on a few flagship titles, space out the updates, innovate, keep players engaged, and grow the community over the long term. Would World of Warcraft be half the phenomenon it is today if Blizzard had released a few updates and then given up on it?

I also don't understand why such a disproportionate amount of resources had been expended on Muxxu over the last few years. Yes, it's a game portal. Yes, it keeps up with the "social" emphasis that's currently trendy in the industry.

But Muxxu is hardly M-T's most successful product, and the amount of dev time and resources that have been diverted to Muxxu at the expense of other games is staggering. Is the expansion of Muxxu really worth killing off every one of M-T's classic games? NaturalChimie 2, released two years ago to positive acclaim, is slowly dying after updates ceased in May 2011. Someone in power at M-T is making some very strange decisions, to be sure.
