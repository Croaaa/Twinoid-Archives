[...]

The scoring is as follows:
1 point for a relation that makes sense (If you use multiple meanings for the same word, you can earn up to 2 extra points.)
0 points for a word starting with the right letter
-1 point for a word starting with the wrong letter

But anything can happen with the points, as they are under my complete control!

If someone posts a word starting with the wrong letter, continue from that letter (to prevent confusion). Obviously the alphabet will loop (otherwise this will be a real short thread), so A comes after Z.
