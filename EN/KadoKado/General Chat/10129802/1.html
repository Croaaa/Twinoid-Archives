This is how this game works:
ANS1, abmt, or JohnAtMC posts something, checking the "Post as anonymous moderator" box. Then, people who aren't mods guess who posted it. When someone guesses correctly, a mod edits the anonymous moderator post, by unchecking the "Post as anonymous moderator" box, and adding "Correctly guessed by: [name of 1st correct guesser]" to the end. After two wrong guesses, we'll reveal the moderator, and add "Not correctly guessed. " to the end.

We're going to keep score for this game.
+5 points for a correct guess
-2 points for a wrong guess
