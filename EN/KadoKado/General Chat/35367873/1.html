a) Principle of the game
Control the ninja to 3rd person , kill a maximum of Wizz - titis by throwing shurikens between banouniers .
End of the game :
When wizz - titi approaches to foreground ( all in front of you ) .

b ) Orders, points bonuses, enemies / malus , objects, weapons

*controls
Number of controls: 3
1.2 : "left" arrows / " right " move left / right.
3: Spacebar : launch a shuriken

*points
They get essentially killing Wizz - titis wearing banounes color .
The Wizz - titis who do not wear color banounes report 50 ( see "bonus" below) .

*bonus

- Yellow Banoune : 350 points at the bottom , the middle 150 points , 50 points ahead.
- Green Banoune : 500 points.
- Blue Banoune : 1,500 points.
- Red Banoune : 3,500 points.

- Pink Wing: + 50 points. Movements to the left and right are faster .
- Blue Shuriken : + 50 points. Increases the frequency of throwing shurikens .
- Red Shuriken : + 50 points. Shurikens become more powerful , more damage .

Malus
- Rotten Banoune (black with red tips ): + 50 points. It knocks the Wizz - titi for a short time but does not kill him. Sent on a Wizz - titi stunned, she wakes up.

*enemies
- Wizz - brown titi : The most widespread. The less resistant : 1 dies launch shuriken .
- Wizz titi - black with white edges Moderately widespread and moderately resistant : 2 dies throws shurikens .
- Black Wizz - titi : Rare . The most resistant . Died in 3 throws shurikens .
2) Advanced Help : techniques and tips - Written by :

- Hold down the bar to send shurikens seamless space.

- Sweep the screen moving from left to right ( or vice versa) to rake and kill as many wizz - titis effectively.

When you get the black banana remember not to focus on any single enemy. I hope this helps.
