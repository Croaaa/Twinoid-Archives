Ready for the craziest game of your life?

1. Type out a sentence.
2. Reverse it. (shown below)
3. You may start the sentences with a location where you do it. The location may be the same, or different, in each sentence.

(This shows how to reverse it.)

Sample one: In China, you find a party

Now reverse it: But no matter how fast you run, the party finds you.

(You can put what ever you want at the beginning as long as you put the reversed sentence after it.)

Again: In France, cows eat grass.

Reversed: But in NYC, grass eats cows.

Start typing and you'll learn quick.
