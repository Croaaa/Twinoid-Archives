Over the years, many players have expressed their desire for a site-wide, permanent ranking that takes into account all of a player's accomplishments since signing up. Although the Feather-Weight Ranking is the closest thing KK has to a general ranking, it measures extreme talent in a limited subset of games, and is also not permanent.

Therefore, I have released my own:
http://lueshi.info/kado500

On Day 14, 23:45 server time, an automated process searches every corner of KK for potential candidates. A numerical rating is calculated for each candidate based on his/her lifetime achievements on the site, using a mathematical formula that assigns arbitrary weights to each type of achievement (for example, 10 pts per , 3 pts per , etc.) Once the scores are tallied up, the candidates are ranked in descending order and the list is cut off at the 500th player.

If there is sufficient interest, I will release an updated list at the start of each new period. The rankings and associated player data are current as of this period (P146), but the candidate list was compiled based on player activity from the previous period (P145). (I've put a lot of thought into how to coordinate the timing, and decided it was fairest to give players the full two weeks to show up. However, it wouldn't make much sense to release updated rankings on Day 14, when the numbers would be immediately out-of-date for the following period. This is why the candidate list always trails the actual player stats by one period.)

Why Top 500? It's not my intention to be elitist, and I'd definitely like to have access to a larger and more inclusive list. However, because there are so many players, if I didn't limit the candidate search to a manageable scope, not only would the process be extremely resource-intensive, but the odds of someone being inadvertently left out would skyrocket. Ideally, Motion-Twin should be handling this instead of a third party, but they have their reasons for not wanting to do so.

That said, if you think you belong on this list and you're not finding your name on it, the first thing to do is to make sure you play this period. If you're still not on next period's list (P147), send me a private message and I'll look into it. If you've recently changed your name and/or your clan, they'll automatically be reflected in the next update.

Any suggestions or bug reports are welcome, too.

Enjoy.
