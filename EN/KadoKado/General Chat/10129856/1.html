I began playing KadoKado in Period 42, in June of 2009.

Now, almost two years and 50 periods later, I see that I've come quite a long way, more than I could have ever anticipated:

Table showing my level-up progress since Period 70
Graph showing my level-up progress since Period 42
I compiled these thanks to a VIP1 perk that enables access to previous periods' resumes (though you can only see brief summaries for Periods preceding 70.)

Once upon a time, I could not obtain a on any game except ABC Revolution, and earning a was an insurmountable task.

But I read Twinpedia for tips, watched DailyMotion from the best players, gained additional practice via Awako, and gradually became a better player.

I've set three goals for myself every period:
(1) Try to increase my star completion bar;
(2) Try to level up at least one game;
(3) Try to have fun.

I have fulfilled all of these objectives through Period 92 (this period is fulfilled thanks to my first-ever on Pioutch today ) I know that for (1) and (2), my streak will inevitably be broken at some point in the future. The star completion bar has an attainable limit and nobody has a in every game (or even 1/3 of them.) But it always feels good to set some objectives and try to meet them.

As for the third point, "having fun," it can often get extremely frustrating when the lucky RNG you need just never seems to arrive. Fortunately, it appears that I've taken care of most of the really luck-based games. The luck element is also why I tend to shake things up and play different games every day rather than persist on a single one. Maybe I lack the skills/luck to set a world record on a specific game, but hey, I'll be decent at all of them.

Before joining KadoKado, nothing I had played had tested my gaming abilities across so many different genres. I've gotten better at shmups and logic-based puzzle games perhaps as an indirect benefit of playing KK games.

In the next year I hope to reach 75% star completion, attain or above in all games, and earn my first . Here's to another great year of KadoKado gaming!
