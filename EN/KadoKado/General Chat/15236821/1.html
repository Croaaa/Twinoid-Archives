Win # 315 (maybe?) / # 52 : Electrolink, 139.750 pts

Although I'd been steadily improving my personal record over the past week (80K -> 82K -> 84K -> 94K), this score happened purely by chance.

If you're lucky, after you make your own LED combo, more blocks will fall down in such a way that another combo is automatically made for you. Usually you get a simple 2- or 3-LED combo.

If you're exceptionally lucky, the game makes a 14- or 15-LED combo (which I have never managed to produce on my own), and boosts your score from ~55K to ~110K.

Without this stroke of luck, I would have ended with 80-90K points, which still isn't bad. But I could have never expected something like this.

My star completion bar had been stuck at 80% for over a year. Feels really good to see progress
