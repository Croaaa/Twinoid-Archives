The ArtistiK contest is a competition to see who can make the best work of art in 4 categories: "image", "audio", "video", and "text". Each entry will be rated on a 5 star scale by each of the ArtistiK Judges, and the top entry in each category will win a MTPass! In addition, the 4 winners will be part of a second round of judging, in order to determine the best overall entry. The overall winner will receive an additional MTPass!

The rules for entry are as follows:
   1. Your entry must be about a specific KadoKado game, a combination of games, or the website in general. You may be disqualified if your entry requires a lengthy explanation to describe how it's related to KadoKado. Your entry should be able to speak for itself.
   2. You may enter multiple times, with these restrictions:
      a. Only one entry per category.
      b. The entries must be unique, you cannot enter in multiple categories with the same work of art.
   3. Your entry must be your own creation. If you plagiarize or have a friend/relative make your entry for you, you may be disqualified.
   4. You must agree to these terms. (Don't worry! I'm not asking for your soul or first born... or anything valuable, really.)

How do you enter, you say? Just post in here! I will then send you a confirming your participation. When you are finished with your work, just reply to that message. If you need to email your entry, reply to my message with your email address, and then send it to corosnark@gmail.com.

The deadline for entries is 11 PM (server time) on February 26, 2012.
