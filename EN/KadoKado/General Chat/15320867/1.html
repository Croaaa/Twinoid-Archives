When KadoKado was still an active website, the Forum Pass actually had it's use. It prevented new players from spamming the forums, and the site was active enough for people to want a Forum Pass.
At that time, the forums were also more useful. You could, for example, read about new games and discuss updates like when the Clan Missions were introduced.

Nowadays things are different. KadoKado has been slowly dying for a long time, the only thing giving it a small boost was the Twinoid integration. The players who come from Twinoid don't know anything about the usefulness of this forum (the game threads are pretty useful), and they don't want to spend their precious points on something like a pass to see the forums.

One of the main reasons for a lot of people to get addicted to games is the community. If Motion Twin wants to keep KadoKado at least a little bit alive, they should get rid of the Forum Pass to give the KadoKado forums some extra activity. If we're lucky a small community would form again and people would have a new reason to come back to this game, keep playing (and keep buying gems).
