Back in 2009-2010, there was a site called Namuhon that operated very much like Prizee. You played through a few crappy flash games with progressing levels to win codes (including MTPass) and other goodies. Of course, you could also buy tokens to progress more quickly.

For a long time, the webmaster kept hyping Namuhon V2, a relaunch of the site complete with new games and quests. Then things started getting a little odd: first, the big-ticket prizes were removed from the prize catalogue, then they stopped offering the codes. This was all done to ensure "a smooth transition to V2," or so the admin claimed. A few veteran players openly voiced discontent about not receiving their orders for game consoles and laptops.

In the summer of 2011, the V2 beta went up and sure enough, there were half a dozen brand new spanking games. Everyone was excited. For a few more months, updates continued on the beta site and developer's blog. Then suddenly, the original Namuhon (V1) was shut down entirely "to make way for V2." Except V2 never launched -- a year later, the site is defunct and the admin has vanished.

I won 3 MTPasses from Namuhon back in the day. But I'm so relieved I didn't deposit any real money.
