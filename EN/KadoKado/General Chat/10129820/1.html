Kind of hard to answer really because there's a lot to it, but I'll try...

Every day you get a certain amount of action points (kinda like gems here) that you can use to either go out into the desert and look for things, or spend on building defences etc. in the town.

Every night the zombies attack and if your town has insufficient defences, the town gets wiped out and you start all over again.

In the desert, you sometimes run into zombies. If there are too many, you're pretty much stuck there. You can try killing them but the chances are tiny that you will succeed. You can also try running away, but you will usually get infected and end up dying. Instead, you should call for help from your fellow townies and they can come and rescue you.

If there's no cooperation in your town to either rescue each other, or work together to build defences etc., you're pretty much screwed really.

There's a wiki here that might give you a better idea of what you can expect: die2nite.wikia.com/wiki/Die2Ni ...
