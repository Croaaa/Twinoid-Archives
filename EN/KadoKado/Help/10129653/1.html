By the way, this is an absurdly simple fix:

ST = score target
MSI = minimum score increment = the smallest score increase permitted by the game at any time

MSI x ceil(ST/MSI)

For example, Kavern = 59.061 pts
Kavern's minimum score increment = 100 pts
(each vegetable awards the player a score bonus that's a multiple of 100)

100 x ceil(59061/100)
= 100 x ceil(590.61)
= 100 x 591
= 59.100 pts
