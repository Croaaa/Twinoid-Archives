How do I take a screen-shot? :

Windows :
Once you have the screenshot you wish to capture displayed on your screen, click on the "Print Screen" button on your keyboard.

Then :
- open PAINT (start menu -> all programs -> accessories -> Paint).
- click Edit -> Paste.
- save your image on your computer (choose .jpg format for ease of use with the KadoKado website).

Mac :
- To capture a screenshot: "Apple" key + shift + 3
- Once you have the screenshot you wish to capture displayed on your screen, just press "Apple" key + shift + 4 and the mouse will turn into a cursor. Drag the cursor to form a rectangle around the screen and then release the keys, your image will then be saved to the desktop.

How do I upload a screenshot from my computer? :

- Go to imageshack.
- Click on "Browse" then choose the file on your computer that you want to upload.
- Click on "HOST-IT".
- Click on the last link which is a direct link to image.

How do I empty my cache? :

- Internet Explorer :
Tools => Delete history => delete files.

- Firefox - Method 1
Tools => options => "Private" tab => Cache => Empty the cache.

- Firefox - Method 2
Tools => options => "Advanced" tab=> "Network" tab => Cache => Clean now.
or:
Delete my preferences => tick "cache".

How can I display my history? :

The direct command is Ctrl+H.

- Internet Explorer :

- Firefox :
History => Display the side bar.
