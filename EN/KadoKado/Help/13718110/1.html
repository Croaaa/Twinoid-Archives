Reading your question, it sounds like you haven't discovered the SCORES tab.

It's on the right hand side, underneath the title for the game. When you pass the score required for the star, it will appear on your profile until the end of the period, where you will receive for it.

Don't sweat not being able to consistently get them. You're new to the site. As you gain practice with the different games you'll get better at them.
