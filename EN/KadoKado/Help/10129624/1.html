By the way, I make a temporary list of currently found clan options. Hope it can help.

Cool Game
Select a game which will be present inside all the future missions of this period

Cross step
Complete any selected mission step without playing.

Defense 120%
Increase one defense score (120%) during the clan attack period

Double Attack
Give the opportunity to a member to launch two simultaneous attacks instead of one during the clan attack period.

Douple points (yes, a typo )
The next mission will earn you clan two times it value when you will complete it

More time
Gives your clan more time to complete current mission

Next Mission
Cancel current mission and generate a brand new one

Trash Game
Select a game which will never be present inside all the future missions of this period
