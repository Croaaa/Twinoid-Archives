Coro is correct. Years ago, each tournament lasted 1.5 months and was divided into Transfer, Offensive and Strategic Periods. During Strategic Periods, clan wars were worth twice as many points. The old tournament system was retired in favour of Missions Mode, which generates far more income for M-T.
