Sorry for doublepost but after a LONG time I have found what was the problem.

There are many people experiencing this issue on new pc's with fresh systems and fast hardware.

Windows Aero is causing this bug, you have to deactivate it and switch to classic windows theme, after that the games will run as fast as on Xp
