coro

Well... I think the order of the games is based on popularity, so it may not always be the same. There is a high probability that the free games will stay at the top, though

Anyway, here's an unconfirmed list of free games:

Iron Chouquette, Alphabounce, K-Slash !, Interwheel, Ellon In The Dark, Kanji, Piou-Piou, Manda, Schizo Fuzz, Flushee, Kaskade 2, Opalus, Cooking Lili, Aqua Splash, Atlanteine, Xian-Xiang, Logic'O, Choco-Mouche

Now, I don't play all of these games on a regular basis, but I have a general idea of how "luck based" they are. I'm probably wrong about most of these, but hopefully this is a good start:

  • Totally luck based
    • Kaskade 2, Opalus, Flushee
  • Mostly luck based, the game laughs at your feeble attempts to do well
    • Ellon In The Dark
  • Mostly luck based, but you need some skills
    • Alphabounce, Xian-Xiang, Schizo Fuzz, Atlanteine, Manda (not too sure about this one)
  • Rewards skill, but requires good reflexes
    • Iron Chouquette, K-Slash !, Interwheel, Kanji, Piou-Piou
  • Rewards skill, but requires quick thinking
    • Cooking Lili, Aqua Splash, Logic'O (need good reflexes with this one), Choco-Mouche



Now, all games have a random factor to them. I just think the ones near the bottom are "more fair". You could have an unlucky play of Choco-Mouche or K-Slash, but you won't feel like it was the game's fault
