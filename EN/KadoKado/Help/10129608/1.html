If you mean change the above the date/time stamp for your messages, (where I have the brain robot,) to a different picture, then:

Click on your name at the top of the Kalendar, to go to your account settings page.
Then, click the "Change my avatar" button, and choose a small picture to upload. Select a '.jpg', '.gif' or '.png' image file. The permitted size is 64x64 pixels.
This file should not exceed 64kb.
