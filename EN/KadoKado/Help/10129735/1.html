The forum is a place where players can communicate in public. You can obtain information, clarify doubts and meet other players like yourself who share the same passion for Kadokado.

However, certain regulations are necessary in order to guarantee the main principles of mutual respect between all users. The moderation team is here to to make this happen. The moderators are at everybody's service and their aim is to keep a pleasant fun atmosphere in the forum.

The aim of these rules is to guarantee mutual respect, order, correct spelling and good manners between all players, moderators included.

Moderators are volunteer players at the service of Kadokado. Their role consists of helping players to understand the rules of Kadokado and the forum .

RULES

1 SPAM(massive sending of messages, repetition of smileys and/or words, etc.) is forbidden.

2 Bad spelling and SMS language are forbidden. If you're not good at spelling, you can install this:
Firefox addon: Spellchecking dictionary

3 Writing in uppercase letters is forbidden.

4 The advertising of web sites not belonging to Motion Twin is forbidden. However, advertising is permitted in a website which talks about Motion Twin or any of its games.

5 Being polite is mandatory: no insults, nor rude words. A "thank you" is always nice .

6 If you reply to a message, your answer must be useful and related to the main subject.

7 Account selling, as indicated in our "Terms and Conditions" page, is forbidden.

8 Asking for codes (sms, calls) is forbidden.

9 One single thread for clan is permitted for recruiting new members.

10 If you create a new thread, you must create it in the corresponding Forum section.

11 We regard all players who accuse another player or Motion Twin (the creators of Kadokado) of fraud, stealing or otherwise, as being offensive to the moral integrity or reputation of another person (article 2 in Terms and Conditions of the game). He/she will be banned or even expelled.

12 All questions must be consulted before in the Search box of the forum or in the "Frequently Asked Questions" and "Bug" sections. If a message has already been answered, the message will be deleted or blocked and the author can be banned.

13 A message can be deleted without previous notification if one or more of the present rules is not respected.

14 Write a clear and explicit title when you create a new message . Everybody will be able to know in advance what your message is about. This will help to improve the effectiveness of the Search box.

15 We ask you to write in English. This forum is for English speaking users but if you want to write in another language, it will be tolerated if you translate your text into English in the same message

16 In this forum trolls are not tolerated. We interpret as troll ,someone who posts controversial, inflammatory, irrelevant or off-topic messages, with the intention of provoking other users into an emotional response or to generally disrupt normal on-topic discussion. This also refers to those people who provoke flamewars (a prolonged exchange of deliberately hostile messages), making players angry and creating confrontation between them with the intention of confusing or provoking a reaction in players.

17 All forms of non-respect for the rules will be sanctioned with more or less severe measures without any previous notification.. The sanction can range from a single day ban to the deletion of the account.

18 These rules can be modified at any time.

19 Particiption in the forum implies the acceptance of these rules without exception.

20 Also, the forum is not a place where you can:

Question the tasks of the moderators or of the administrators.
Discuss the forum rules.
Publish reports about users who have committed theft, created multiaccounts, fraud, etc.
Ask for game tokens or modifications in the More Games section.
Organize massive petitions.

ATTENTION: If a player disagrees with the forum rules or with a moderator, he must discuss this issue by private messenger with the moderators and/or by the Frequent Asked Questions form (to get an administrator).

We thank you for your patience and respect. Yours faithfully,

The moderation and administration team
