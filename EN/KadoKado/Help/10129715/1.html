And of course, the last resort would be to make a new clan.

The French Technical Support says this (I've translated it, of course):

Is it possible to recover a clan whose leader is unreachable?

No.

Each Clan is attached to a specific person who may decide to transfer his rights to another player when he deems it necessary.

If the leader of a Clan simply becomes unavailable, this does not mean he will never return to his Clan, nor that he wants to be taken over by another Clan.

Consequently, neither the game options or the Support allow you to become leader of someone else's Clan. Our advice is simply to create your own clan!
