Ever since MT stopped updating the site more than a year ago, it has become increasingly commonplace for veteran players to deactivate their accounts and start over from scratch.

These accounts are easily recognizable: tons of s, #1s in every other game, leveling up dozens of games at the same time, and most importantly, membership in an elite clan.

Let's face it: no one gets into FISHERMAN, CSN, or another Tier 1 clan without at least a (in most cases, several s, s and records du site.) The only reason these clans would let in a "newb," no matter how promising he looks, is that he's actually a restarted veteran player.

Case in point (and screencap for future reference.) Nobody scores 117.250 on Mini-Race as a first-time . Nobody.

The problem with these players is that they become extremely disruptive at the upper ranks ( // ), and prevent honest players from making progress. They're also gaming the system to earn the one-time level-up s over and over again, even though (strangely enough) M-T's rules do not explicitly forbid this.

Look at Jaws0. He has a 624K Chakré Bouddha score in the level, when the second highest is "only" 179K. It's highly unlikely that a first-time level player can make a 600K score. Due to Chakré's low player turnout, only Jaws0 will advance to at the end of this period. The honest players lose out and have to wait for the next period (assuming there's not another restarter waiting in line.)

The restarters are all over the place. Rukawa's on at least his 3rd account; DragoTar is Gorbash reincarnate, etc. etc.

So what should be done about this? M-T can either slam an iron fist and disable account closures altogether, or (if they're willing to lose a miniscule proportion of revenue) actually give rewards to the players so they don't feel compelled to restart for extra .

My proposal would be something as simple as awarding 2000 for #1, 1000 for #2, and 500 #3. These prizes would be awarded at the end of every period, and only those who failed to win a prize from the featherweight championship would be eligible. M-T would lose no more than an additional 248.500 (< 50 EUR) per period, but there'd be a lot fewer restarters and the KK experience would be better for everyone.

Just throwing some thoughts out there. I know M-T will likely ignore this and do nothing as usual, but I'm staying optimistic for now.
