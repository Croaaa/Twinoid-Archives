If possible, keep KadoKado set to the language that matches the proper clan forum. Due to some oddities with the forum system, it will forget that you have access to your clan forum after a certain amount of time has passed. You need to remind Twinoid that you can read the clan forum by navigating to the forum page

KadoKado will also go back to your account's language whenever you log in. While you are guaranteed to get logged out after a period change, there are other situations where the site will automatically log you out. I know this will happen after you log into KK from another computer or you change any part of your Twinoid profile
