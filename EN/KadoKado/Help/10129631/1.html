Sorry, no there isn't.

Here's an easy way to send the same message to several people, one after another, but not at the same time:

1. After typing your message the first time, before sending it, copy the text from the text box, and then send the message to the first person.
2. Click Compose again, paste the text, type the name of the next person, re-type the subject, and click Post.
3. Repeat step 2 for each additional person.
