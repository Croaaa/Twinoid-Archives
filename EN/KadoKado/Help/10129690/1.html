starting this period, both parts of the tournament are mixed together so you can play missions while your attack is active. it seems that you can use mission bonuses right away on the atk/def system, except that some players wonder whether the "double attack" and "120% defence" have been removed ...

on the other hand, each tournament lasts only 1 period so the gains you would have earned according to your ranking are divided by 2. and you get remuneration for your clan atk/def and mission rankings separately.

the transfer period is a bit reduced so that player movements are impossible the last weekend of the period.

on that last weekend, only attacks started before noon and launched(finished) before 12.30pm will be acknowledged.
