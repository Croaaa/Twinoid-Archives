You can't transfer gift cards between Amazon sites at all

You'll have to find someone willing to not only buy an equivalent amount of gift card for .co.uk, but also someone willing to give you their shipping address
This is so that you can buy some items on their behalf (since gift card balances can't be transferred between accounts)

Or you can just deal with paying more for an item you want and import it through the .com site
Either option isn't great, but those are the only two
