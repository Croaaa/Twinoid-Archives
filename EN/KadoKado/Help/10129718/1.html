having trouble getting a high score on a certain game? here's some tips for some of the game i frequent:

schizo fuzz
after the first 100k, try sliding off the ramps a little more often and push the left/up arrow key to stay in the air as long as possible. after 150-200k, try to avoid the rotating gear, since it will not give you much air, and there is a good chance you will end up in a log. if you are high and there is a golden nut coming up, push down/left as early as possible straight down for you to catch the nut and bounce back up from the ground. don't try to go horizontally since you will end up in a log(if you are at over 150k).

kavern
don't bother collecting every vegetable since it will deplete your air supply fast. explore all rooms for air cans. try to avoid beaking in stone. anticipate your path before collecting vegetables in a room. if you are too far from a vegetable, move on. many times it's not worth wasting air digging 8 squares for a tomato. try to explore as many rooms as possible and only collect vegetables close to you or a group of high value vegetables.

binary
don't waste your moves. if you have 1 or 2 moves left, use them by getting closer other colors besides the ones you are currently matching. anticipate your next moves

cooking lili
this method is a little risky and you might lose a few gems trying it, but the end high score is worth it (usually). at the start of each game, rotate 2 ingredients, but don't match any until you have almost reached the top. then match ingredients at the bottom and enjoy your instant combos. only try this until the purple ingredient shows up. if you matched all in one go, and the purple ones still haven't appeared, then rotate (without matching) another 2 ingredients until the top and repeat the first step.

kanji
patience is key here. to do good you have to have a lot of patience. don't rush in to jump after the coin if there are many animals around. do it only when you see an opening and anticipate incoming fliers. don't stay too close to the edges since bees will often start flying at the bottom of the screen. if a boar is about to pass by, don't jump high. just gently tap the jump button, just enough to jump over him (requires practice since sometimes you tap too much or not enough).

opalus factory
always go for the gears. in your way try to collect the biggest formation of coins of the same colors. don't wander off going after big formations of coins if you don't have gears around.

pioutch
try to bounce the piou piouz from the edge of the drum, since this way they will bounce off further. don't use up your power push abilities early in the game. you will want to save 3 piou piouz after 10k rather than one at the beginning.

kill bulle
try to burst the smallest bubbles before bursting bigger ones or you'll end up with more than you can handle and the smallest bubbles are the ones that get you the most often

ours invader
always try to kill creatures starting from one side to another. that way the remaining creatures have more distance to go sideways and will last more time getting at the bottom.

red raid
if you have at least a jeep, make it go around the screen without stopping. that way the monsters won't have the chance to damage it, and the jeep can easily move and shoot at the same time. it also has a lot of resistance. don't waste your time with troops if you have a jeep or two.

for the paradice game you can apply the same principle as at cooking lili. try not to match any ice cubes and then when you finally do, you will have large combos. and like i said, it works best if you do it while you have three colors only.
