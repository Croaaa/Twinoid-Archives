The only suggestions I have (for the lag) are either:

1) Minimize the number of tabs you have open while playing a game. Even if you aren't looking at them, tabs open in the background can slow down your computer. Also, many sites have Flash-based ads, which can be a big factor for the lag

2) Try out another browser (if you can). Google Chrome has run well on computers I've installed it on, and it also updates it's built-in Flash plugin automatically
