You need to make them expand in 2-3 directions. If you can tunnel in 3 directions from the beginning you have a chance to make a really good score. Try to think one move ahead. When I get to the last 2-3 moves I calculate the total of each possibility before I continue.

750pts-the ones that flash,
875-directly behind the ones flashing,
1000,1125,1250...
2500pts-the ones that fall
