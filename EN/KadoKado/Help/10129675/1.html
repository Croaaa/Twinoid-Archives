Well, you've done one thing right (kinda), by posting a thread in the Clan forum.

Other than doing that, just find ways to look at a bunch of profiles (since I doubt you could persuade someone already in a clan to move, as of now). I have had the most success by interacting with the forum, and sending a to people I happen to notice aren't in a clan. Another place to look is the rankings for any game. From my experience, this is where you would go when your clan is fairly big, and you want to fill a hole in your defense. Do note that "my experience" is zero successful recruitments via that method.

Since you already have someone else in your clan, you've seen all there is to adding clan members. You can't force someone to join, so try not to be too pushy with people

One more thing, don't be shy if someone randomly applies to join your clan. I have two people in my clan that did this, and they have been very helpful! I wouldn't expect this to happen, but you never know
