Dear Clans,

After having observed the last round of missions and having also read various opinions about the new clan mode, here are some changes that have been made in order to improve the new system.

It is now possible to "reserve" part of a mission. Whilst observing the various stages of a mission you can now click on a reservation button which will indicate to other members of your clan that you are attempting that stage of the mission (don't forget to remove your reservation if you haven't succeded in completing the stage).

Please note that all clan members can remove the reservation of a mission stage if the given member wishes them to do so.

Please also remember to regularly refresh the page in order to check if any stages of a mission have been reserved by other clan members.

The target scores have been reset since the last clan tournament in order to avoid the setting of impossible target scores.

The number of stages in each mission have also been changed and will be now be dependant on the number of players in each clan and the level of progress in each mission.

From now on, only the completion of a mission (you can still have a joker) will win points. If you're not able to complete a mission, you will lose points for each stage which is left uncompleted.

Hopefully, you will now find that the missions are much more varied.

With regards to the question of whether it will be possible to accept a mission whilst a clan is banned, we will consider this problem following the first few rounds of the new period.

Good luck Clans!
