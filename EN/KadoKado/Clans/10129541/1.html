For a long time, through both the forum and the help section, we have been asked by players to develop the challenges which are offered by the clan mode.

It is therefore with great pleasure that we announce the new clan mode.

Our goal is to offer you a lively competition which is at the same time a true collaborative challenge where friendliness and self-help are fundamental.

Explained below is how the new clan mode will function:

The Championship of Clans is now divided up into 2 parts.

The first part is comprised of group missions:

During this period a clan must work together in order to complete a given mission. Clans will receive missions which comprise of many challenges, they will then have 24 hours in which to complete these challenges.

Each challenge succesfully completed during this mission will give you one point. If you manage to complete all stages of a mission within the given time your points will then be doubled.

On the other hand, if you don't succeed in completing all the challenges of the mission in the given time you will lose the corresponding number of points.

Once a mission is completed, you will then be given another. Each mission will become harder, with increased difficulty and a greater number of challenges.

How many missions can your clan complete? The challenge will be exciting!

During the group missions, you will also be able to modify your clan and recruit new members in order to develop your game plan.

Will you choose to recrute mercenaries in order to ensure victory?

The 2nd part of the tournament is a game of attack and defend in the best tradition of KadoKado.

To top it all off, this new system means that there will be a 33% increase in the number of Kado points won during clan mode.

We hope that these changes will delight smaller clans who are lacking a challenge whilst also pleasing the bigger clans who are eager to be challenged at their respective level.

Good luck!
