Since I'm posting in here anyway, I'll briefly explain the Karma system for my clan. This should save some time, instead of explaining it repeatedly

Each period has an Attack and a Defend requirement. For the Attack requirement, you must have successful to earn points. Each earns you 5 points. For the Defend requirement, you must successfully complete a or Mission Step. Each earns you 5 points, and a Mission Step earns you 1 point.

If you don't meet a requirement, you can lose Karma points. You start out with 3 points, and if you drop below zero... Well, nothing good will come of it!
