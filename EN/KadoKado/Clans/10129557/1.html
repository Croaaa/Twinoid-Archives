Chicken Chasers

About the clan
Chicken Chasers is an international clan with English as the main language. It was founded on July 7, 2008, with a group of friends, but has grown into a large clan with many players from all over the world.

Core Value
This clan is a relaxed competitor for the clan rankings, meaning we put having fun as priority and while we are having fun we'll see how good we can get on the rankings. We don't expect anyone to be able to reach certain scores at games, however we do put great value in activity and participation...after all, an inactive clan isn't much fun.

Recruitment
As long as we have space in the clan for new members, we are recruiting. Inactives will be kicked every 2 or 4 weeks, so even if there isn't any space, feel free to apply.
We are looking for active members, who are serious about participating in clan activities...such as completing missions or attacking/defending. It is required to speak English on the private forums.
Applications are accepted by subscribing AND sending a message why you want to join and what you have to offer to Tiezol.
We are open for merging smaller clans/groups into ours, however, there is no discussion about leadership.

What this clan has to offer
- An active and friendly environment.
- Private forums with several serious and chit-chat topics.
- Information about Games (how to play guides) made and gathered by the players of the clan itself.
- The Chicken Chase! (an internal competition at which members can participate to see who is best at a certain game).
- Top 100 rankings fights. A challenge for every player.

Wars
We will fight back if we are massively attacked, we always enjoy a good fight. If you are looking for an organized war, please message Tiezol.

This thread isn't just a recruitment thread, so feel free to talk about whatever, as long as it has to do with the clan .
