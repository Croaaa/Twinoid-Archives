Founded by Susimickey, Equilibrium is an English clan created at the start of Championship n°11 for all English speaking members looking to experience the fun of clan competition with other casual players without the need to meet certain requirements.

During Championship n°13 the clan merged with Lolotron's BubbaJubba clan, combining to become one of the strongest English speaking clans in kadokado.

A clan created as a democracy with all members having equal say in how the clan is run.

Our main goal is to have fun. We don't have requirements for how many you earn for the clan each period, as long as you stay active you are welcome in the clan.

One benefit of joining is that we have a (nearly) complete tips/tricks thread with tips on almost every game KadoKado has to offer.

If you are interested in joining either send me a message or apply here: Equilibrium

For such a relaxed atmosphere, we still have a pretty fierce competition to do really well-- we regularly end up in or very close to the top 200 and rarely move lower (if we do, we bounce up pretty quick). And this is of course without a full clan. Our number usually range from around 30-35, including inactives-- all we really need are a few more active players willing to improve and top 200 is in the bag, easy. The intra-clan competition is pretty interesting as the number keep shifting and there's always a fierce battle for the top spot-- but no one is crass about it; we don't steal reserved games or anything.
