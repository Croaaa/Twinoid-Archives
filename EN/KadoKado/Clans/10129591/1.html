Hello,

This thread is here to help both players who are looking for a clan to join and clan captains who are in search of players to add to their clan. Under no circumstances are comments to be made about players' profiles ahich are put here for consideration for joing a clan.

If in search of a clan, it is advisable for a player to note his skill level, his clan preferences, his playing time and any other information he/she may feel is useful.
_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

Here are the rules which must be respected if posting in this thread:

1 - Only players searching for a clan to join are permitted to post here.
Due to this, any type of message (advert for a clan.. recruitment.. etc..) will not be authorised.

2 - If a captain of a clan is interested in a player, he should contact the player only by .

3 - Once a player has been recruted by a clan, please indicate that in the thread that you are no longer looking for a clan to join. This will make it easier for other clans searching for players.

4 - The moderators reserve the right to delete any message which doesn't respect the rules of this thread. The author will not necessarily be informed of the deletion.

5 - The demand or exchange of codes are strictly prohibited from the forum and are punishable by a reprimand or even a banning from the forum.

6 - In order for this thread to remain useful for players and clans, it will be returned to 0 at the beginning of each tournament and moderated regularly.

(In order for this thread to be found easily, JohnAtMC made it a sticky thread. )
