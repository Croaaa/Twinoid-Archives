Synopsis

Kaskade is back!

Destroy the largest groups of same colour blocks to win the most points.

But be careful! You only have 20 clicks!

How To Play

Destroy all the blocks by clicking on groups of the same color with the left mouse button.

You have twenty clicks in order to reach your target score, use them wisely.

Tips

Keep track of the number of clicks you have used by looking at the counter at the top left of the game screen.

You can maximise your score by forming large groups of same colour blocks.
