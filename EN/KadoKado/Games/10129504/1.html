Synopsis

Help Julianus, the little Cloud, blow his bubbles without bursting them!

Avoid the Evil Picks and the Naughty Fans!

How To Play

Use the mouse to move Julianus.

Hold down the left mouse button to blow a bubble.

When all the bubbles have burst, the game is over.

Tips

Join bubbles to create bigger bubbles.

Live longer to get more points.

When a big bubble bursts, it divides into two, which makes it easier for Julianus to survive.

Use the keyboard arrows instead of the mouse if you prefer.

From my post:
Some small tips that might help:
1) Use the mouse to move, and the spacebar to blow (it works better for me, at least)
2) If you can juggle two bubbles at once, try keeping one on each half of the screen. This way, you don't have to move too far to get the bonus point balls. Also, if you have multiple bubbles, you can "sacrifice" one to get a point ball that's in an unsafe area

From iGenesis's post:
Clearly, there is a lot of luck involved:
The number and value of orbs for you to collect
The number of new bubbles that appear
The placement of picks and fans

Here is my strategy:
In the earliest stage of the game, when the picks are still very sparsely distributed, I try to keep as many small (size 1) bubbles on the screen as possible.

When the difficulty steps up (this usually coincides with the fans' appearance), I try to consolidate three size 1 bubbles to form a size 3 bubble, so that I can afford one hit against a pick without losing the game. I also keep 1 to 2 other size 1 bubbles on the screen until I can no longer manage them. If there are enough new bubbles coming in, I form two size 3 bubbles and maneuver them simultaneously.

In the final stage of the game (this is when the picks are all over the place like a minefield), I take my size 3 bubble and simply focus on survival. I only collect orbs when they're accessible and do not threaten survival.

The game's scoring system is directly tied to the size and number of bubbles on the screen at any given time:
A single "size 1" bubble on the screen gives you 5 points per unit distance traveled.
Every "size 1" or "size 2" bubble thereafter gives you 10 points per unit distance traveled.
Every "size 3" or "size 4" bubble thereafter gives you 15 points per unit distance traveled.
Every "size 5" or "size 6" bubble thereafter gives you 20 points per unit distance traveled.
Every "size 7" bubble thereafter gives you 25 points per unit distance traveled. (Larger bubbles truly aren't practical.)

For example, with 4 bubbles on the screen at once:
size 3 bubble (15 pts) + size 2 bubble (10 pts) + 2x size 1 bubble (2x10 pts)
= 45 points/unit distance
