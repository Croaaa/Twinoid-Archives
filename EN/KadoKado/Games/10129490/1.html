Synopsis

There is an infestation of flies which has contaminated a batch of chocolate from the Mummy Chocolate Factoy TM !

Save the chocolate!

Millions of children are waiting for the annual Chocfest, make sure to not disappoint them!

For each square of chocolate, separate uncontaminated blocks and don't touch blocks where there is a fly.

How To Play

Click on a square of chocolate to mark it.

Click once more to break it.

If there is a fly on it, bad luck! You lose a life...

Tips

There are numbers which indicate the number of adjacent flies.

You have to work fast, there's a time limit between each attempt!
