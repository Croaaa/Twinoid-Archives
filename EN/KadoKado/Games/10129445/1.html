Synopsis

The Robo-Rabbit patrol has kidnapped Chouquette! The disappearance of the Iron-Crocket's mascot means it would be disqualified from the Andromede interstellar tournament. Find and save Chouquette before it's too late!

How To Play

Move your spaceship by using the arrow keys.

Fire by using the space bar.

Fire a special weapon by using the CTRL or SHIFT key.

Tips

You cannot have more than 3 weapons. Some rewards will allow you to have more than three weapons at one time.

Be careful!: if you exceed the limit, your old weapon will be sacrificed.
