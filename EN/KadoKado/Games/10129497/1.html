Synopsis

Help Kanji save all the Piou-Piou falling from the Great Vortex!

How To Play

Use left or right keyboard arrows to move Kanji.

Use Kanji's cushion to bounce the Piou-Piou through the exit so that they don't fall with a crash...

If you miss a PiouPiou, you lose a life. When you lose 5 lives, the game is over.

You get points when a PiouPiou bounces towards the exit on the right of the screen.

Tips

Press the Space Bar to activate a Super Power and make the PiouPiou fly.

Your super powers are limited (moon icons at the top left indicate your super powers)

There are special Piou-Piou who give you an extra super power.
