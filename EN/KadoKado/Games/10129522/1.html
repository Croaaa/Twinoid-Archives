Synopsis

A red scolomorphe invasion has put the tropical ganglions in grave danger! You must absorb all scolomorphes and other cells in the game screen to totally avoid the risk of infection!

How To Play

Choose direction with your mouse.

Move the cell by clicking the right mouse button

When you see a cell which is smaller than your own, bump into it to absorb it and increase the size of your cell.

Each level is complete once all visible cells have been absorbed.

Tips

Warning! Avoid cells which are bigger than your own as they will absorb you if you bump into them!
