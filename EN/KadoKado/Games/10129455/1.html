Synopsis


Oursouin the little sea-urchin is living in your sink!

Help him destroy the Perverse Whiteners before they clean the sink!

How To Play

Use the arrow keys to move Oursouin.

Press the space bar to shoot.

Tips

You can bounce Oursouin's shots off the sides of the sink, so don't hesitate to use them.

Each turn, you will lose all bonuses but not the shield.
