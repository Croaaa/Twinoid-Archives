Synopsis

Piou-Piou has found a new job!

Piou-Piou is boss of the new production line in the Opalus Factory!

Help Piou-Piou to collect tokens for all Opalus players in the world!

How To Play

Move Piou-Piou by clicking your left mouse button in order to collect coloured tokens.

You can see specially requested tokens at your right. Collect these in order to win more points!

Be careful! the conveyor belt moves after each turn.

The game will end if Piou-Piou falls down.

Tips

Collect the screw nuts, Piou-Piou will throw them into the motor of the conveyor belt.

Each screw nut will slow down the conveyor belt.

You can see your screw nut stock on the digital screen at the bottom right.

Complete the special requests!

Each completed request immediately moves up the conveyor belt!
