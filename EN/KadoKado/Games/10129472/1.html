Synopsis

An evil general has established a dictatorship!

Tianan Man is our only hope.

How To Play

Group together all people you can in order to increase the size of your protest.

Use your mouse to move Tianan Man and his protest.

The quicker you reach them, the more points you will win.

Dodge the army tanks in order to survive.

Be careful! If Tianan Man or too many people die, you lose...

Tips

If you grab people quickly enough, you will be able to fill your star power gauge. When it's full, you will gain access to super protest mod which will result in more points and you will become invincible for a few seconds!
