Synopsis

Use your chainsaw to eliminate monsters and put together the best possible alignment of monsters.

Try to understand the dark aim of this mad game!

How To Play

Move to the right or to the left by using the arrow keys.

To cut up the monster above your chainsaw, press the up arrow key.

The chainsaw will automatically cut up all identical monsters situated side by side.

Attention: the chainsaw can't retrace its steps when it cuts monsters.

If there are more possible paths, the chainsaw will automatically choose the best one.

The game is over when you have made 5 times the same type of chain (blue, orange or green monsters).
