The big secret to this game is:

Moving your mouse

The car moves towards your mouse's position, so you need to position it correctly as you go around corners; then you don't slow down as much.

As well:
Perfect laps (no hitting cars or the lines), laps under 9 seconds, and laps under 10 seconds are very important bonuses that you can get.
