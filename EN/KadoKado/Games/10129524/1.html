1) Basics

Synopsis
El Tortuga, a veteran turtle general, has invented a revolutionary method to save his friends: the laser grid.

Unfortunately, his first attempts to cut up the field are being strained by his sworn enemies, including the squirrels who mistake giant tortoises for nuts, and Space Spice, the aggressive alien dog!

Game Principles

Objectives:
Move El Tortuga to draw a line. When the line touches two boundaries of the playing field, you will "claim" the portion of the playing field that you just outlined or separated from the remainder.

Each level has a target percentage of the field that must be claimed before you can advance to the next level. Level 1's target is 50%; Level 2's is 60%; and Level 3's is 70%.
