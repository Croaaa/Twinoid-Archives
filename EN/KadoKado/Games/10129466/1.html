Synopsis

Pioupiou is trapped inside The Mystical Grass Maze!

Help him find as many Eggs as possible to accelerate the Cyclopean Pentagenerator!

How To Play

Use the left and right arrows on your keyboard to rotate the Labyrinth.

Try to lead PiouPiou towards the Mutlicolor Eggs.

Then bring them back to the Cyclopean Pentagenerator at the center of the labyrinth.

When you run out of time, the game is over.

Tips

Don't lose your eggs: each egg placed in the Pentagenerator will continue to increase your score.
