Synopsis

The pets are very fragile: if you touch them they will explode into pieces! Maximize your points by using the least number of actions.

How To Play

You have a limited number of 10 shots.

Move the little monster with your mouse.

You can insert it on the right or on the left of its siblings.

When a group of 4 or more monsters explodes, new monsters will fall from the sky and replace them.

Tips

Destroy as many monsters as possible with one shot in order to receive more points.

There are two bonuses:

The apple gives you one extra shot and 1000 points.
The spiral gives you up to 5 extra shots.

To activate a bonus, make it disappear.

11450 -> 11100 -> 9900 -> 14050

This is the kind of analysis I do for each move. This example is only for Position #3 but I repeat the same process for all 14 possibilities.

I then rank all 14 possible positions from highest marginal score to lowest, but in the case of a close race, I do consider how badly the shot would mess up the board for future moves. In some cases it is wise to sacrifice a few hundred points in order to allow a potentially bigger combo down the road.

Another tiebreaker is the raw number of monsters exploded, on the potentially-faulty logic that clearing more monsters increases the odds of getting an apple or spiral. (However, based on what I have seen across most KK games, the relative frequency of good bonus items is pretty much set in stone when a / is expended and the RNG is determined.)

I'm not trying to be arrogant but I think this method is pretty much as foolproof as can get given the limited information we have in the game. We do not know exactly which monsters will replace the exploded ones so we can only try to do the best we can with the ones that are on the board.
