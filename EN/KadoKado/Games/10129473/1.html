Synopsis

Terrorist A.I has infiltrated our internet connection and sabotaged KadoKado's servers!

Join colored modules into groups of four in order to reset the processor.

How To Play

Use the left-button on your mouse in order to rotate the modules.

When four modules of the same color are joined together they will explode.

Be careful because you only have three turns in order to join them together.

After each explosion of a group of four you will have three new turns available.

Tips

After some explosions you will have access to new modules which will make the game harder.

Although they become harder, if you succeed you will receive more points than regular modules, so destroying them is a priority!

Finally nailed this game. The trick is to "look ahead" by up to 2-3 turns. Write out a proposed sequence of moves, e.g. {9, 10, 11} (which happens to be the only possible solution in this case), then simulate the rotations in MS Paint using the "Rotate 90 degrees" tool. Then, clear away the exploded modules and try to work out your next turn on whatever's left of the board. You always want to make sure that you can go at least one more turn after the turn you're about to make. If you can't, you've either screwed up fatally or you need to try a different move sequence. Or if you have spare moves, you need to use them to clean up the board further.

Also, try to avoid double/triple combos or deep chain reactions unless you're very early in the game. You don't get any extra points and you're wasting moves you could have used to clean up the board. Plus, there is a high degree of uncertainty in the colour distribution and spacing of newly-appearing modules.
