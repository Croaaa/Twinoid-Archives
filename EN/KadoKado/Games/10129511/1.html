Synopsis

The North Winds have frozen the famous Hokitika beach!

Fortunately, the Penguin Rescue Team is going to clean everything up.

Destroy every block of ice in order to help them defrost the coconut trees!

How To Play

Group 4 blocks of the same color together to make them disappear.

Use left and right arrow keys to move the blocks.

Press space bar or the up arrow to send a block.

If a column reaches the top of the screen, the game will end.

Tips

Some blocks are deep-frozen so you will have to blow-up adjacent blocks to heat them up a little.

Keep a look out for secret weapons!
