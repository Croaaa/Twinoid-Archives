Synapses
Game N°60 - Réflexion
_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

Synopsis

After waking up, hungover after a night of partying, Rick the punk has to put his brain back in order to be able to go to work.

Help him reconnect all his synapses.

How To Play

Control the red granulocyte by using your mouse.

Place it near a group of crazy synapses and click to start the chemical synthesis: all neighboring synapses will be captured by your chain.

When other contending granulocytes appear (blue, yellow, green or purple), make sure to collect the greatest number of synapses or you will lose the game.

Tips

Synapses caught at the end of the chain get you more points!

WORST. GAME. EVER. I'm not even joking.

It combines virtually every negative about KadoKado into one game.

Super-short? Check. Opalus takes 9 clicks to finish. Unless you're very good and very lucky, you'll probably die within 4-7 clicks. Compare a game like this to one that can potentially last for hours like Hypercube, and it makes you wonder why this site doesn't charge different prices for playing different games (for example, 0.5 for Opalus vs. 2 for Hypercube)

Extremely luck-based? Check. The positions and movement of competing granulocytes are completely random. Sometimes it looks like you got a good position, but all of the synapses go to the competing CPU-controlled granulocytes instead of yours. Plus, you start each successive level with a clean slate, leaving your previous win streak irrelevant.

Very unforgiving? Check. One error and it's game over. This can happen as early as Level 2, when you're barely 30 seconds into the game. Happened to me twice (out of 8 games.)

Obnoxious time limit? Check. This is especially evident when you reach the 5th level and beyond. You're not even stalling, just waiting for the other granulocytes to get into a more favourable position for you...and boom, 3-second countdown timer starts ticking. As of that weren't obnoxious enough, the numbers also cover up the screen so it becomes hard to see.

After 8 turns, my personal best is 57950 points, just a little short of the cutoff and good enough for the in a favourable period. Sadly, I won't ever be unlocking it here because it just plain sucks and is a terrible value for its 10000 price tag.
