Synopsis

Pick up or put down one of the pieces from the moving line.

Join it up with other pieces to form a square on the gameboard.

Remember that a big square gives you a lot of points.

This game will drive you insane!

How To Play

Press the left button of your mouse to pick up a piece.

Once you have decided where you want to put it, move the mouse and press the left button again to put in place.

Use the Space Bar to turn the piece around.

The aim of the game is to form squares with the pieces.

Once a square is formed, it disappears and you win points.

The points are awarded according to the size of the square. So the bigger the square, the better.

When there are no more pieces on the conveyor belt, you still have some time left to form your last square.

Click on the "End Game" link to record your score.
