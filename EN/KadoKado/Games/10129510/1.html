Synopsis

Flakra's pit has been invaded by water bubbles.

Cleanup this mess and destroying all of the bubbles!

How To Play

Use your left mouse button to click on the water bubbles.

One click makes a bubble get bigger.

When the bubble reaches its limit, it will explode and send out 4 water drops which will make adjacent bubbles bigger.

Burst a series of bubbles in order to win more points in one single shot!

Using the bomb will help you clean up but it won't give you any extra shots.

When you haven't got any more green balls, the game is over.

Tips

Try to destroy as many bubbles as possible with a single shot to win bonus shots and try to complete as many levels as possible.

Yellow bonuses can be destroyed thanks to the explosion of an adjacent bubble. They will give you 6 bonus shots.
