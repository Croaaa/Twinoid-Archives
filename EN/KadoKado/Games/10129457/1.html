Synopsis

Stop the circle from getting bigger by grouping marbles of the same color. When at least 4 marbles of the same color are grouped together, they will disappear. If the marbles touch the edge of the circle the game is over.

How To Play

Click on a marble to send it to the opposite side of the circle.

If 4 or more marbles of the same colour are grouped together, they will disappear and the circle will shrink in size.

Tips

Create big groups of marbles in order to win more points in a single movement.

I've been playing Logic'O for quite some time now, and here are my findings. I hope this can help you!

The addition of marbles works this way:
- As soon as you make a match, new marbles will be added
- When you click too many times without making a match, new marbles will be added
- When a new colour arrives, new marbles will be added.

Also:
- New colours arrive over time, your score doesn't matter. This is the reason why you also get new marbles when you are not doing anything, it's just the new colours being added.
- This also means there it no time limit on making a match. You can think as long as you want, this won't make new gems come.
- Of course, new gems will be added after a long time when a new colour arrives, so don't wait too long.

Tips:
- Speed is the key, the less colours there are on the screen, the smaller your ring will be. Most combo's can be made with the smallest ring, so try to make as many as possible while you still can!
- Taking gems away to make a match is quicker and more efficient than moving gems to the other side.
- When your ring is very big, you can make a match without new colours coming. Make another match very quickly afterwards to shrink your ring a lot!

That's everything I have to offer at the moment, if you have any more questions, I'll try and answer them.
