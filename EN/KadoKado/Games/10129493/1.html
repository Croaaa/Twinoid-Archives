Synopsis

Your are a pacifist equipped with a multicolor laser.

Protect Navy Z and Navy Y from destroying one another. Use your laser to prevent the massacre and to protect the security convoys!

How To Play

Move the laser with your mouse.

Change the colour of the laser by clicking the left mouse button.

Objectives

Protect each army from the other.
 Protect the security convoys.

The game will end when:

When one of the navies is devastated.
 When a security convoy is touched twice.

Tips

Don't move the laser too fast. It is sensitive to sudden movements...

Each missile has a color. You can only destroy it with a laser of the corresponding colour..

The neutral laser (black) is useful in very special cases.

The first canon of each navy has 3 energy points. It is the only one that has this characteristic.
