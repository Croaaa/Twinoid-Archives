Synopsis

Enjoy this cosmic billiards game!

Use your spaceship to send alien balls in the four corners of the universe.

Beware of the black holes!

How To Play

First of all, use your mouse to select the angle of your spaceship.

Click again to choose this angle and click once more to select the speed.

The number of tries is limited by your fuel (at the top). Each try will decrease your points multiplier.


Tips

For each alien eliminated you win a number of points related to the color of the alien, the current multiplier and bonuses (nice shots, more than one alien in one shot, etc.).

If you are not satisfied with the angle you selected press the Escape key to cancel it.

I'd like to add something.

To maximize your score, you have to be successful at bouncing your spaceship off of walls before you hit a "ball". Each bounce adds to your bonus at the end (1 wall bounce = x2, 2 wall bounces = x3, etc).

Also, to be successful, you need to hit successfully sink (get into a hole) multiple balls in a single shot.

For example, when I got my 36,000+ point record, I hit the red, orange, and yellow balls into a pocket on my very first shot.

Try and do that as often as you can, while keeping your ball out of the holes, as that will end the game.

And make sure to get the high valued balls as early on as you can, as you have a x7 multiplier at the start, which goes down by x1 each shot. (x1 is the minimum multiplier you can have.)

Lastly, make sure to sink all the balls, as there is a 5,000 point bonus for doing so. You have to have them all in by the end of your x1 multiplier shot, otherwise, you will not get the bonus. (Which is really important to a good score.)

Ball Values
1000 - Red
700 - Orange
500 - Yellow
300 - Green
200 - Dark Green
100 - Blue
50 - Purple
