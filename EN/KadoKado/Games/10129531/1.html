----------------- Welcome to the "Games" section -----------------


The "games" section is reserved for discussions about the games on the KadoKado website.

In this section, one can find; help for every game on the KadoKado website, videos, record scores, open discussions about KadoKado games and much more!

What threads will I find in here?
Here you will find only threads dedicated to the games on the KadoKado website.
Unofrtunately players do not have the possibility of opening new threads. But you are strongly encouraged to post in the threads which are already open.

For all questions relating to bugs on games, please go to the dedicated support website.


Stickies & Open Threads

Stickies

- Forum Rules: Make sure you read the rules and regulation of the forum before actively participating in the KadoKado forum.

- Official Game Guides: this sticky lists all the threads relating to help on KadoKado games. It is within these subjects that you can ask for help or give advice on any game on the KadoKado website.

- KadoKado Hall of Fame: summary of record scores on KadoKado games . To submit a record score, go straight to the thread "Record Scores".

- KadoKado Video Library: the best videos of KadoKado games. To submit a video, go straight to the thread "Video Submission"


Open Threads:

- Video Submissions: go here to submit a video which will be added to the "Videos" list.

- Record Score Submissions: go here to submit a record score which will be added to the "Hall of Fame".
