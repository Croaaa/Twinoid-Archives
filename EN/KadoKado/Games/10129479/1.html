Synopsis

The Storgz have invaded our colony!

We have only one choice: to escape!

Help the natives escape to the launch pads.

Be careful! Heavilly armed Storgz tanks have already been sent towards our position.

How To Play

The aim is to save the most inhabitants before you can transfer them to the launch pads of the escape pods.

When a pod is full it will take off into space and you'll get a bonus. An improved pod will arrive a few seconds later.

Take off : Press the up arrow key to take off from your launch pad.

Evacuation : Get close enough to the inhabitants so they can jump into your pod.

Be careful! The more passengers you transport, the heavier your pod will be.

Landing : Get to the nearest launch pad to deliver your passengers.

In order to land correctly you musn't go too fast or come down at too steep an angle.
