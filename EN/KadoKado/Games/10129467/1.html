Atlanteine
Jeu N°38 - Puzzle
_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

Synopsis

Help the cursed pumpkin escape from the Atlanteïne maze!
Use your brain to find the shortest path to the exit!

How To Play

Use your arrow keys to move the pumpkin.

To complete a level you must guide the pumpkin into the hole.

Be careful! the pumpkin will go forwards in the direction that you choose, if there are no obstacles in its way, it will fall into the abyss.

Tips

Finish a level quickly to receive bonus points.

Some obstacles can be moved only once per level.

You asked me about developers' thought process, of course i don't know how exactly they are thinking. I saw many incorrect, disputable or even just stupid decisions by so many different people before. Even if i would never do something that doesn't mean other people can't.

They believe randomness curbs cheating.

Let's use Puzzle-Manda as an example. It's basically impossible to earn a , or without picking up at least one rose orb (worth 15.000 points each.) On average, you'll earn about 1.000-1.500 points for completing a typical level with no coloured orbs. In other words, a single lucky rose orb is worth 10-15 regular level completions.

Now, even if you have the best automated Puzzle-Manda solver in the world, you can't get a good score without being exceptionally lucky on the coloured orbs. This randomness discourages cheating by minimising the advantage attained from using a solver.
