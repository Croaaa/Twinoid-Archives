Synopsis

Destroy the meteors with your starship Starfang DCX-40.

But be careful! it is illegal to destroy them! So you will also have to flee from the Space Police with your Hyperthruster.

Good Luck!

How To Play

Use the arrow keys to turn left or right.

Use the up arrow key to increase your speed.

Use the space bar to fire your main weapon and blast the meteors!

USe the down arrow in order to deploy your special weapon.

Tips

Special weapons are limited, so try not to waste them.

Don't stay too close to the borders of the screen as meteors can appear and destroy your ship before you have a chance to react.
