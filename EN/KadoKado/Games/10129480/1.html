Synopsis

Destroy precious stones of the same color whilst traversing the labyrinth of Opalus.

The more you increase the game space, the more points you will get!

How To Play

Click on one stone to destroy all adjacent stones of the same colour (these stones will blink when you hover over them).

Remember! only stones placed at the very edge can be clicked!

Tips

Try to spot groups of the same color!

You will then be able to start a chain reaction and destroy many stones with only a single shot.

This game is at least 90% luck-based.

The probability of getting a good board (one that'll get you a or higher) is only about 1 in 20

It appears that for the very best scores, token drops are most important. You can only go so far with chain reactions because the multiplier is much lower, at 125*N. You get a fixed 2500 pts for each token dropped.

When I got my , I dropped about 5 tokens on EACH of the last four moves. You can try to release a large chunk of tokens all at once, but the boards that'll allow you to do this are extremely rare.
