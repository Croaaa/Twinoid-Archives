Synopsis

Discover the mystery of this Chinese game.

Match symbols, shapes and colors to get the maximum points.

How To Play

Use your mouse to play this game.

Match similar counters by clicking on two which correspond.

The white line must not be blocked in order to match two counters which are not beside each other.

Tips

The more similarities which the two counters share,(color, shape and symbol), the more points you score.

0 similarity = 1 pt
1 similarity = 50 pts
2 similarities = 300 pts
3 similarities = 1000 pts

I find getting a spreadsheet, or piece of paper, and mapping out my 3 similarity options is my best bet, especially since this one isn't time based. Use your 1 similarity and 2 similarities matches to open up the routes for your 3s, since there usually isn't a pair side by side or on top of the other. Save your 0 similarities for the end. If it comes down to a 2 and 0 or a 1 and 1 option, go for the 2 and 0 for more points. This game is heavily based on luck, but with practice you can get better.
