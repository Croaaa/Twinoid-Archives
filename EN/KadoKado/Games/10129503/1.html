Synopsis

The Sokopoulos archipelago is at war!

Earn victory by destroying the blue army and taking control of its troops!

How To Play

At each turn you will have to place a group of 1 to 7 soldiers in the opposing field.

Click using your left mouse button on the field in which you want to send troops.

If a hostile troop beside you contains less soldiers, it will become part of your troops (red soldiers).

Every allied neighboring troops are thus reinforced. The weakest has the priority of getting reinforced first.

SCORE

Soldier = 50 points
Plain = 800 points
Beach = 600 points
Mountain = 400 points
Victory = 3000 points

Mountains can only take up to 4 soldiers but they give a bonus of +3 during fights.
