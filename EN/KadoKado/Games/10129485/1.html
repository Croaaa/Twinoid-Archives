Synopsis

Help Kanji the Ninja to survive the traps in the Infernal Zoo.

In ancient Japan, animals are not considered friends of man.

Avoid them by jumping and gathering Zen symbols.

How To Play

Help Kanji catch Zen balls.

To move Kanji use the arrow keys on your keyboard.

Jump using the space bar.

Tips

You can eliminate a bird by jumping on its back.

You can jump higher by holding down the space bar for a longer period of time.

The green Zen ball = 200 pts

The blue Zen ball = 500 pts

The red Zen ball = 3000 pts
