Cereal Punk
Jeu N°18 - Réflexion
_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

Synopsis

Rémi the punk has been condemmed to community work after having vandalised the highway.

Rémi has been sent to the granary in order to put all the seeds and cereals in order.

Put the seeds in order before you reach the time limit.

How To Play

Use left and right arrows on the keyboard to move Rémi.

Your aim is to put seeds of the same color together in the same column.

Use the up arrow key to pick up seeds and the down arrow key to drop them.

You cannot move different kinds of seeds at the same time.

Tips

Make your seed disappear near stones to crack them.

After three cracks the stones will explode.

Bubbles explode when you drop a seed or a stone on top of them.
