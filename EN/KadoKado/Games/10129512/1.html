Synopsis

Have fun with the Electrolytic Reaction Machine!

Link the green and blue LEDs to create chain reactions.

How To Play

Use your mouse to play

Click on a block to rotate it.

Make a path between the green and blue LEDs to win points.

Tips

The greater the number of LEDs, the greater the number of pointswon.

Be careful! Make sure to check the time lest on the clock as it is limited.
