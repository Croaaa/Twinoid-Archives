Synopsis

The Klinker Space Platform is experiencing a serious power failure! Link all the Chromatic Sensors of the same color to restart the engines and get a surprise!

How To Play

Click to draw a path to link sensors. Only sensors of the same color can be linked together.

Click on a sensor to erase a path. When all the sensors are linked, you will move on to the next level.

Tips

Two paths cannot cross. Try not to isolate a sensor.
The longer the path, the better: it means you can win more points.
