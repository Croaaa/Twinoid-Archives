Synopsis

Wiggle around to collect fruits and bonuses. Try to win the jackpot and avoid walls at all cost!

How To Play

Move the snake with the left and right arrow keys. Eat as many fruits as possible

Tips

Scissors cut a small part of your tail off.

Open the chest to discover its hundreds and thousands of suprises.

Better fruits will appear if you collect the wine grape.

The feather slows the snake down.

The potion permits the snake to move over its tail.

The stick creates an OGM at the center of the screen.

There is also a 'bell' - if you collect it, snake will return to it's original size from the beggining of the game. It is extremely useful when you catch it in about 300.000 points scores .

I think the most important thing in Manda is to find yourself a good path (for example - circle with some zigzags, covering the whole level) and always use it, catching fruits when they will come onto your path.

When you have a very long snake and you are likely to crash - try to collect potion. Then - collect as many fruits as you can when snake cannot crash in itself. When the potion's effect ends - the game ends too, but you will have a lot more points .
