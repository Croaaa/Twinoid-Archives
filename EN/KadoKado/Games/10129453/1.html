Synopsis

Pioupiou is a prisoner in a haunted farm. In order to escape you must help him swallow all the fruits.

How To Play

Swallow the fruits firsto group the.When you regurgitate them if in order they will disappear.

In Level 1, 2 fruits grouped together are enough to make them disappear. In Level 2, you need 3 fruits etc....

Your stomach has a limited capacity. If you swallow more than 6 fruits, you'll get a time penalty.

In order to get to the next level, you have to get rid of all the fruits on inside the game screen.

Tips

Be careful: one too many fruits will give Pioupiou indigestion!
