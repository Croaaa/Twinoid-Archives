Synopsis

The terrible hordes of Hive-y-Ghost-Blobs have invaded Shang-Xi plains.

Help the Bukoïen warriors to strike back and defend the invasion!

How To Play

Step 1:

Place your warriors one by one on the game board using the left button of your mouse until it's completed.

Step 2:

You'll able to destroy the Blob-Ghosts when you have more warriors than them on a horizontal or vertical line.

Launch an attack by clicking on a Blob-Ghost with the left button on your mouse.

Tips

If you succeed in isolating a group of blobs who don't touch the border of the game board they will be destroyed in a single attack.

But it can also be a wrong move for you.

If your warriors are too isolated, you will have problems in crushing the blobs.
