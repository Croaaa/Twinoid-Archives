Alphabounce
Game N°40 - Action
_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

Synopsis

Thanks to your metallic racket you can destroy all the blocks and launch devastating attacks using the rainbow letters! Don't let the ball escape from your control!

How To Play

Destroy 75% of the level bricks to progress to the next level.

Move your racket by using your mouse or the keyboard arrows

Make sure to keep your cursor inside the game window!

To launch the ball or activate a game option, use the left mouse button or the space bar.

Tips

Not all options are useful. Learn to recognise those which are useful and avoid those which are dangerous!

As a player who is at the level on this game as has gotten a score of 150k ish here's my advice.

Play the game constantly and don't lose. The more often you get though the first 8 levels (we all die at level 8) the more likey it is for you to see a whole bunch of pink bricks. Getting these is the only way to get high scores. If you die on level 3, you never know, on that go there would have been 100k of pink bricks waiting for you at level 5!

Use the [P] powerup not only to save your ball(s) but to aviod bad powerups - you know the situation when your ball is comming right down with a [I] powerup. Well, just click and let the beam save the ball while you stay well away from the [I].

Don't get the [U] powerup at the start of a level. As you'll get no more powerups, chances are you'll lose the ball before you finish the level. Remember, you get big scores from pink bricks, not lots of greens ones!

If you have [A] powerup don't ever get [K] with it. The bug mentioned above is quite common and if you've got the [A] powerup you're good to go anyway. Always get the [G] powerup when you have [A] as you'll get more points that way, but only if there's not many minerals left as with [G] ball you get fixed points for regular bricks and colored bricks.

Again, just play loads and pray for pink bricks.

Here's the [google.com] translation of the very helpful option list that was posted. I've tried to clean it up for English, but if anything is wrong, please correct. Hope others can find it helpful.

Kadoka AlphaBounce but not those of Alphabounce.com

A: Attraction, all balls accelerate to the pad when they fall (do not get any bonuses to pad as T, P, C, N,) except E and D

B: Shield: all blocks strengthen by one level.

C: Tail: all the balls stick to the pad (do not catch the beginning of the level unless you have the E)

D: Diminution: Reduces the size of the pad.

E: Extension: Extends the pad.

F: Fire ball clusters weakens blocks 2x.

G: Glace: Freezes blocks when hit. Any frozen block will be broken when hit again. (no glass to break grip)

H: Halo: The ball passes through any surface after hitting the pad. It will become solid again when returning.

I: Ivertir: Reverses controls pad (left becomes right, and right becomes left). Two I cancel each other out

J: Javelin: Launches a bolt of lightning that breaks everything on one line

K: Kamikaze: the ball usually hits the blocks

L: Laser: Release 2 beams on the sides of the pad

M: Multiball: Multiply the number of balls 2x.

N: Nerve: the pad shakes, but if you touch the side (not really clear about this one... I've never had an 'N').

O: Ouvir: Allows the pad move to the next level without breaking 75% of the blocks

P: Protection: throw a ray that makes the balls from escaping back to the game

Q: Quasar: sucks all the blocks from the middle

R: Rallentisement: Slows the ball

S: savetage actif: Brings the ball back if lost (only for a limited amount of time).

T: Temporalite: slows the balls and drop bonus

U: Unification: all blocks are green crystals

V: Wave: all common blocks and reinforced breaking weaken in 1

W: Whiskey: the ball moves to either side

X: Xemoves the effects of the ball

Y: the ball goes up very fast and very slow fall

Z: the ball accelerates


Cr Green: 250 points
Cr Blue: 1,000 points
Cr Red: 5,000 points

Cr (Blocks of color are hit points).
