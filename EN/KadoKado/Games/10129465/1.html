ABC Revolution
Game N°2 - Action
_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

Synopsis

Protect Teddy Bear against the evil mutant letters! Type the letters to make them disappear!

Make combos and boost your score!

How To Play

ABC revolution is a game of which relies on quick refelxes.

As soon as you see a word appear on the screen, use your keyboard to type each letter before they fall on poor Teddy Bear.

Tips

1 letter destroyed = 1 point.

Be careful! if you type a wrong letter or miss one, you will lose points.
