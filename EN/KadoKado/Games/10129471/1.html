Synopsis

Help poor Piou-Piou escape from the dangerous tomb!

How To Play

Use the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard to move PiouPiou.

Collect falling balls and avoid stones.

Tips

Green Ball = 200 pts
White Ball = 500 pts
Pink Ball = 3000 pts!

The more you climb, the faster the stones will fall.

The small white blinkers at the top of the screen will warn you when stones are about to fall.
