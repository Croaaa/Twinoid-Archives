Synopsis

Every year Kanji must clean his cave. Unfortunately this year the monsters are really aggressive. Try to neutralize as many as possible before running out of food!

How To Play

Use the arrow keys to control Kanji.

To attack a monster, you only need to move towards him and Kanji will attack it.

You can also throw shurikens using the space bar.

Be careful! you only have 3 shurikens at the beginning of your journey. You might find more on the road!

Tips

When you pick up an item, it is added to your inventory. Some items can be used by clicking on them, others have effects which will last for the entire game.

For each single action Kanji consumes one unit of food. If you run out of food, Kanji will lose one life per action. Unless you find more food he will die. and the game will end.
