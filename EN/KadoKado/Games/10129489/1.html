Synopsis

Take control of the Ultra Marines of the Famous Red Squad!

Exterminate all the giant insects from Sector 03.

Survive for as long as possible until reinforcements arrive.

How To Play

Click on a Marine to select it.

Click on a location on the map to make him walk towards it.

When they are not in movement, your Marines can defend themselves without your orders.

If you want to move several marines at the same time, hold down left mouse button and draw a frame around them. Once selected, your marines will appear with a green circle around them, this indicates that they are selected and under your control.

Tips

Press the Space Bar to invert your selection.

It's very useful to switch from one group of Marines to another.

Keep a close check on your Marines' health by holding down Enter. Make sure to keep the weakest marines away from the battlefront.

Something I've seen in videos others have made is that they keep the vehicle(s) in one selection, and everyone else in the other. This is probably done because the vehicles are much faster than the marines and are able to shoot while moving, which allows you to essentially trap the big beetles in one spot while you shoot them down. Basically you keep your marines off to the side while you lure enemies to an area where you can circle around them with the vehicles.

Also, when the blobs start to show up, it's probably best to send the marines to occupy it, as it just takes one hit from those to kill anything.
