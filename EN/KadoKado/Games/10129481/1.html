Synopsis

Take control of the Twin Spirit Squadron and it's two pilots Wallis and Futuna.

During a routine mission, Wallis' plane is shot down by the Evil Robert.

Go back in time with Time Machine Futuna qnd save Wallis!

Will you be able to intercept and destroy Robert in time?

How To Play

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control the ships.

Use ctrl, enter or space bar to shoot.

When Wallis' plane is shot down the first time, the level is reset and you play Futuna.

You will then see both planes on the screen as the "history of Wallis" is replayed.

If Futuna is destroyed during the game, all your enemies will be destroyed and you will play Wallis.

If Wallis is destroyed all your enemies will also be destroyed.

If you survive until the Wallis' death, Robert will appear glowing red.

Destroy him to win a Special Bonus and save Wallis!

Wallis will then follow Futuna.
