Synopsis

General Setcher's army has found process by which to transform stupid chimpanzees into bionic war machines.

You are 5th dan Jack Klimax, specialist in enemy infiltration.

Go on an undercover mission to the General's headquarters and look for the proof of these evil actions.

How To Play

To neutralise an enemy, you must immediately make contact with him.

Once you are in combat position, use the up, down, left and right arrow keys to defeat your enemy.


To go to the next building, you must first neutralise all your enemies.

A lot of smoke will come from Jack's kimono. Then press the space bar to leave the building.

Tips

Certain enemies will drop a precious gem before dying.

Recover 8 different gems to win a bonus point!

By the way, here's a simple guide from me. Hope it'll help newbies in judo.
I use my own word here, if someone comes up with better name, feel free to change it.

MOVES
While ↑ and ↓ is simply up and down, → and ← is front and back. It depends on Jack's direction.
→ is front, it's always the same direction of where Jack faces. If Jack faces left, then → is left, Jack faces right, → is right.
← is back, it's always the opposite direction of where Jack faces. If Jack faces left, then ← is right, Jack faces right, ← is left.

Front engage (E) : simply move towards them
Head engage (H) : just jump onto their head
Carry enemy (C) : E, ↓
Carry enemy 2 (C) : when enemy is laying on the ground, ↓
Hang enemy (G) : C, ↑
Drop enemy (D) : C, ↓

Front throw : E, →
Back throw : E, ←
High throw : E, ↑
High throw 2 : G, ↑
Scissor throw : H, ↓
Far throw : C, Space
Head Beat : G, keep pressing Space
Head Beat 2 : H, keep pressing Space
Air throw (pile driver ) : While enemy in the air, Space
Snap : While enemy is laying on ground, ↓ + Space

GEMS
Every enemy have a chance dropping a gem. It's not random, certain gem will appear after the enemy is killed by a certain move. Here is connection between the moves and the gems that will appear. Gems ordered from left to right.

Red : front throw
Orange : high throw / high throw 2
Yellow : scissor throw
Green : far throw
Cyan : head beat / head beat 2
Blue : back throw
Purple : air throw
Pink : snap

HEALTH
Jack's health has initially 10 bar, and these things makes it change.

health kit : +1 bar
hamburger : +3 bar
ninja's kick when engaged too long : -1 bar
soldier's punch : -2 bar
ninja's kick when meets Jack in the air : -2 bar
ninja's shuriken : -2 bar
soldier's shot : -2 bar
ninja's katana slash : -3 bar
soldier's bazooka : -5 bar
mine blast : -5 bar (unconfirmed, forgot it )

POINTS
Here's a brief about points in judo

Every punch in head beat : 5 points
Front/Back/High/Far throw : 20 points
Pick a gem : 200 points
Kill plain soldier : 400 points
Kill gun soldier : 600 points
Kill bazooka soldier: 800 points
Kill mine soldier: 800 points
Kill ninja: 1000 points
Clear a level : 1500 points
Complete all gems : 6000 points

Practice makes perfect.
