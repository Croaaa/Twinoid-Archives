Synopsis

Turn the Drakhan Wheel to eliminate the grapes of pearls.

Be careful! The Dragon won't allow pearls outside the gameboard!

How To Play

Turn the wheel with either the mouse or the left and right arrow.

When the wheel is correctly aligned use the space bar or the left button of your mouse to send a pearl.

When three pearls of the same color are grouped together, they will explode you win points!

When you empty the gameboard you win a special bonus.

Tips

If you isolate a group of pearls, they will explode and you'll will many points.
