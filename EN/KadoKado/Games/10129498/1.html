Synopsis

Kanji's Killer Bee strikes back!

This time you can help her, burst 100 colored balloons with your sting.

Wait until several balloons of the same color are aligned before bursting them and you'll win more points.

How To Play

Move Kanji's Killer Bee up and down using your mouse and click when you want her to move across the whole screen.

The aim of the game is to burst as many aligned ballons of the same color as you can.

Tips

When you spend too much time thinking, a black balloon appears. Don't sting it or else you'll die.

The counter (at the top right) displays how many balloons you still need to sting.
