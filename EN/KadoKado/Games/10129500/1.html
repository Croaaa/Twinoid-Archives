Synopsis

Papoyo, the wandering toucan, has lost his way during an unplanned migration to Sweden.

He is lost hovering above the port of Caen (France) and he's really hungry!

Help him dive down to find a delicious fish!

How To Play

Use the space bar or the left mouse button to make Papoyo dive into the water to look for fish. Release the button to make Papoyo resurface.

To reach the next level you have to eat all the fish. Try to catch several fish in one single dive to win a bonus!

Tips

Be careful: Papoyo doesn't really like getting wet. If he touches water, he will have to resurface quickly to dry out his wings.
