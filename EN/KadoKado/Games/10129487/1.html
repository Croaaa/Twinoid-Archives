Synopsis

Destroy the maximum number of fantasiosts by grouping them in a line of at least three of the same color.

How To Play

Launch the fantastiosts from your cannon by clicking on the left button of your mouse.

When three or more fantastiosts of the same color are placed side by side, they will explode!

If one single fantastiost gets to the end of the chain, the game will end!

Tips

Fantasiosts of the same color attract one another, so you can use this advantage to regroup the chains you split up.

Be careful! Pay attention to the black and white fantastiosts! They don't explode together but they can be ejected from the game by direct impact from another fantasiost launched from your cannon.
