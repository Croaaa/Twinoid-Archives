Synopsis


Help Kanji escape from his frightening Nightmare!
Kill your enemies, pick up as many ramen bowls as you can and don't waste time because Medusa is never far away...

How To Play

Move to the left or to the right with the arrow keys.

Use the up arrow key to jump on a platform.

Use the down arrow key to descend from a platform.

When Kanji is jumping, press the up key for a second time to throw Kanji's grappling hook in order to reach platforms which are further away.

Tips

Kanji canjump on his enemies to weaken them. However, beware of the enemies' stings...

During a superjump Kanji can still throw shurikens at all the enemies.
