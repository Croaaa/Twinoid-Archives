Synopsis

Beware of the flood! Help Karkra the little ink-stain escape from the Tenochtitlan's bathroom in the Aztec temple. Beware of the Great Quetzal ancestral mines!

How To Play

Click on your left mouse button or use the space bar to make Krakra jump from wheel to wheel.

Tips

You can take advantage of the walls to jump. Be careful not to slide down though as you will find yourself in the water!

If Krakar falls into the water, the game is not over just yet. Krakar can survive for a short period of time and might even be able to resurface.
