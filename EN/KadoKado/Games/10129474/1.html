Synopsis

The 69th Happy Brigade without warning, is being attacked by an experimental military weapon!

Help the Happy Tank to escape from the evil army and destroy as many enemies as possible. Flower power!

How To Play

Move your tank with the keyboard arrows.

Aim with your mouse and shoot by clicking with the mouse.

The goal of the game is to go venture as far away from your starting point as possible.

Avoid deadly missiles which will destroy your tank and end the game.

Tips

Each coloured circle gives you a bonus.

Be careful! Once you pass a warzone, missiles are launched. Be ready to avoid them.

The bonus options dropped by your enemies are crucial for beating your highscore.

Bonuses only last for a limited period of time and you cannot use more than one bonus at the same time.

Like all the games, ultimately your score is entirely dependent on luck. The fewer "circling TVs" stages you get, and the more time-stop power-ups you get, the higher your score will be.

Pick a single direction and only go that one way whenever possible. The more rings you pass, the higher your bonus will be. Try to end a combat stage at the edge of the screen in the direction you are heading in order to cover as much distance as possible.

During the first 3 combat stages, don't worry about how long it takes to kill the enemies - just focus on not getting hit. After the 3rd combat the timer starts. Try to get as far as you can between those first 3 stages - speed increase (happy face) is the best power-up to have for this section.

After the timer starts, you need to concentrate on killing enemies fast and surviving. The time-stop power-up is your highest priority. If that's not available, weapon power-ups are your second choice. Only take speed power-ups as a last resort. Same with armor - only worry about them if you are close to death.

When you get good at the game, it will almost always end when the timer hits 0. Staying alive until then is not that hard.
