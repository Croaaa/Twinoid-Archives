Synopsis

Kanji has infiltrated Bamboo country! Destroy the troublesome turtles by using your shurikens, your sabre or flatten them by simply jumping on them!

How To Play

Move Kanji by using the arrow keys on your keyboard. The up and down arrows allow you to jump up or down the platforms.

Press the space bar to throw "Shurikens" at your enemies.

If the enemies are close to Kanji, he will use his sabre and the attack will be a lot more powerful!

Tips

Kanji can jump on enemies to weaken them, but beware of spiky foes!

When Kanji is in the air, he can do a super jump if you press the up key another time.

When Kanji is doing a super jump, he can also throw a lot of shurikens at the same time!

My advice for this game: At the very beginning when it's all orange turtles, try to kill them with your sabre; it saves shurikens. Use shurikens to stop green and blue turtles from firing. Don't get too close to bats, their movements are somewhat unpredictable and if you're not very careful you'll probably end up its prey by getting close. The pinkish tribal guys have shields which can deflect shurikens if the shield is in your shuriken's path, they won't use it consciously but it's still a nuisance. I'm not sure if the shield deflects hits from the sabre though. Once there are many enemies visible on the screen no matter where you go, DO NOT STAY STILL! Use the super jump! You'll be able to throw shurikens at every enemy you can see at once. This uses up shurikens more quickly, but by this time you should have enough not to worry about the number.

Green gems give 200 points, blue give 1000 and red give 5000. A scroll is something like an extra life, if you are hit with a projectile you'll be substituted with a log and you'll respawn at the top of the screen. 3 small shurikens gives you 20 shurikens and a large shuriken give you 50 (I think). A flaming shuriken makes your shurikens more powerful (not sure how much more powerful, probably a factor of two), A sabre object makes your sabre more powerful (not sure how much more powerful, probably a factor of two), and a purple makes Kanji invincible for a few seconds, any enemy he crosses is instantly dead. I don't know of other items.
