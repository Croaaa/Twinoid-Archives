Synopsis

Kanji the Ninja is back with an all-new adventure!

Help him throw his grappling hook to destroy the bouncing bubbles and get the most points.

How To Play

Use the arrow keys to move Kanji.

Press space bar, to throw your Grappling Hook.

Each time you burst a bubble it will divide into two smaller bubbles.

Tips

Bonuses are rewarded in the Red Bubbles:
- the clock stops the time for a short period.
- The zen symbol gives you 5000 points,
- The super grappling goes right through all the bubbles in its way
- The shuriken divides all the bubbles on the screen
