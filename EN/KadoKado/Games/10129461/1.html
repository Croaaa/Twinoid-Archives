Synopsis

Fight "Gold-o-Bar", the infamous boxing champion!

Don't forget to hold your breath to recover your endurance!

How To Play

Press the space bar to hit your opponent.

Use left and right arrows keys to dodge his punches.

Hit him as often as you can before the end of the round.

But don't forget to stop moving in order to recover your endurance or the game will be over.

Tips

Dodging and hitting stright away is a good strategy.

Combine blows to build powerful combinations and increase your score.
