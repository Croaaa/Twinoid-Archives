Synopsis

The Evil Pokepi made Piou-Piou fall into the abyss and now he's trying to kill him under a waterfall of Popcorn!

Help PiouPiou defeat Evil Pokepi before the abyss is full of Popcorn

How To Play

Press the right arrow or left arrow key on your keyboard to move PiouPiou.

Press the space bar to activate the amazing "pullet-time".

Press the space bar a second time to jump.

Tips

Eliminate as many seeds as you can to avoid being covered by the Popcorn.
