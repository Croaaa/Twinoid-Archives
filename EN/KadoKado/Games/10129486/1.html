Synopsis

Accelerate, collect treasure and never return!

How To Play

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to play K-Train

Up arrow = speed up (faster)

Down arrow = speed down (slower)

Space bar = handbrake

Right or left arrow = Leave the train and move the driver.

Tips

Drive the train as fast as you can, pick up the treasures and make sure to stop off at the stations regularly in order to increase your reserves of coal.

Stop off at the station to get coal

Keep an eye on the small red train. If it manages to catch up with you, the game will end.

There are treasures in the desert. Don't pass them by.

You don't need to stop the train to get off.
