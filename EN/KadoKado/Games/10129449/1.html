Synopsis

Schizo Fuzz is envious of the Flying Squirrels. Maybe you can help him achieve his dream of flying?

How To Play

Press the Space Bar once start Fuzz's launch.

Press the Space Bar a second time to choose the power of his launch.

Press the Space Bar a third time to choose the angle and then launch Fuzz.

Fuzz can fly or dive. Just use the arrow keys.

Tips

Listed below are the functions of each obtacle Fuzz may come across and their respective funtions:

Grinder: Increases Fuzz's speed
 SpringBoard: Makes Fuzz go higher
Bush: Softens your fall but decreases your speed
 Log: Stops Fuzz = Game Over!
Helmet: Protects Fuzz against the next log so he won't be stopped
 Gold Nut: Additional points plus a power boost

Thoryan

For the first 100k points, stay low to the ground. The rotating gears are optimal for this.
Between 100k to 150k transition from staying low to the ground to trying to stay up in the air for as long as possible. The higher the score the more risky staying low is and the lower the score the more golden nuts you can easily pass by being too high.
After 150k always stay up in the air for as long as possible.
The best way to collect golden nuts is by bouncing off of the ground right before them.
At the normal speed, don't dive down for a ramp unless you're 80 meters up in the air or lower.
At the normal speed, don't dive down for a golden nut unless you're 95 meters up in the air or lower.
Getting to Paradise Level will take a while due to the luck of the game, but it's definitely possible to stack the odds in your favor if you play the game well. In other words, I don't believe Schizo Fuzz is nearly as luck based as many of the other KadoKado games.
