Bactery
Game N°14- Puzzle
_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

Synopsis

You are being attacked by bacteria!

They are reproducing fast and are eating your coloured balls.

Align three balls of the same color to build a stone ball.

Surround the bacteria with stone balls in order to stop their reproduction.

How To Play

Stop all the bacteria!

To do this, surround them with stone balls.

Once all the bacteria are surrounded, you win the game.

Build stone balls by aligning 3 stones of the same color beside each other.

To change their positions, click on two balls.

When 3 stones of the same color are aligned, they will turn into stone.

Tips

You can exchange any kind of element no matter how far away they are. Your score depends on the number of remaining balls and stars.

Bacteria = 0 points
Stone ball = 50 points
Coloured ball = 150 points
Star = 1000 points
