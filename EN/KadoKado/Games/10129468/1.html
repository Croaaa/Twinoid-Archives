Synopsis

Defend yourself from the all-directional onslaught of lava enemies. Extinguish them and survive for as long as possible.

How To Play

Move the Water Bubble with the arrow keys.

Press Space Bar to shoot at the monsters.

Pick up the bonuses.

Be careful! If a lava enemy or a shot hits you, the game is over.

Tips

If you keep the Space Bar pressed, your Water Bubble maintains the direction of the shot.

You can shoot & move diagonally by pressing two keys at the same time.

If you find it difficult to shoot diagonally, use one of the "control" (Ctrl) keys.
