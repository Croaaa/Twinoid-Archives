Cooking Lili
Jeu N°49 - Réflexion
_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

Synopsis

Help Lili make the best cake in the world by sorting out delicious ingredients!

Group identical ingredients together to earn points!

How To Play

Click between two ingredients using your left mouse button to swap their position.

Be careful not to let ingredients reach the top of the screen!

When 3 or more identical ingredients are grouped together, they will explode and release their flavour.

Tips

Some ingredients are frozen, in order to defrost you will need to explode adjacent ingredients.

Here's a compilation of info I gleaned off of the French help thread for this game. For the most part, these are just posts in there that have been machine translated, and edited by me (obviously ).

Tips for Good Scores
1 - At first, you get your ingredients up until they are just below the top line. You're trying to set up lots of small combos in order to maximize points. An average combo is worth about 12-15K points, a good combo can go up to 30K, and you're rolling with the big boys any higher than that!

2 - Then, you repeat the first step (pay attention to tall lines on the far edges) until the blue ingredients appear. At this point in the game, it is too risky to climb to the top line because of the increased number of ingredient types and also frozen ingredients.

3 - From this moment on, you should simply ensure that isolated columns do not form, ESPECIALLY on the sides. Try to make combos where frozen ingredients are, to allow more combo chances to become available.

Basic Scoring Info
A group of three ingredients is 70 points, a group of four is 150, etc. (The point values increase from here, but neither I nor the thread had more info than this )

Then you multiply that amount by the combo level: for example, a group of four ingredients in a 5x combo gives - 150 x 5 = 750 points.

The above applies to the red, green, and yellow ingredients. The purple ingredients are worth two times the points, and the blue ingredients are worth three times the points.

Hope this helps!

Via silverFX:
this method is a little risky and you might lose a few gems trying it, but the end high score is worth it (usually). at the start of each game, rotate 2 ingredients, but don't match any until you have almost reached the top. then match ingredients at the bottom and enjoy your instant combos. only try this until the purple ingredient shows up. if you matched all in one go, and the purple ones still haven't appeared, then rotate (without matching) another 2 ingredients until the top and repeat the first step.
