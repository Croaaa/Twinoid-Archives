Synopsis

Here comes the Famous mind-expanding-eye-dazzeling-D isco-puzzling: Travoltax !

Organise the falling blocks to make straight lines without holes.

How To Play

Pieces fall from the top of the game screen.

Organise them while they are still falling in order to obtain a line when they touch the ground.

Each straight line made will disappear.

Use the Up key to use the first option shown in your list.

Tips

Get the Black Marbles to obtain amazing game options.

Don't leave holes...
