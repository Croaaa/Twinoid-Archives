Synopsis

After severe indigestion, Manda The Snake is very hungry again. This time he will have to follow his nutritionist's wise advice in order to stay in healthy.

How To Play

Choose your starting point by clicking the left mouse button.

Use your mouse to move Manda.

Look at the prescription and follow it by eating the fruits in the correct order.

Manda cannot move diagonally.

He cannot crawl on a fruit he has already eaten.

If Manda gets stuck, click again to try a new path.

Tips

Be careful! Always check the pink time gauge to make sure you don't run out of time.
