Synopsis

Withstand the vicious attacks of the Evil Gems using your Magneto-Roto-Stamp before they take invade their entire grid.

Each time you pick up gems from the sides, a new one will appear. Destroy the gems in your possession before the grid is filled.

How To Play

Move your stamp to one of the sides of the game board.

Push the stamp into the side and then click the left button of your mouse to collect 3 gems.

Next push : try to push again a side with a gem the same color as the ones you have just collected:
When you collect 4 Gems of the same color, they dissapear.

Your stamp can collect more than 3 gems until you get 4 of the same color (as they disappear).
This means that you will have to decide between collecting more gems to make combos or only collecting a few in order to have enough space in which you can rotate your stamp.

Tips

Don't collect too many Gems or you won't be able to move anymore!
