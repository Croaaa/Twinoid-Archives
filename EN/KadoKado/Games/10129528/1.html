NEWS
Challenge MODE
We've been preparing a little surprise for you! If you take a look on the right-hand side of your games page, you'll see something all shiny and new: The Challenge Box!

This charming cocktail of confrontation, concocted by Yota and Mr HK will allow you to win gems, Kado points, or yet another novelty: packs of original smileys.

How does it work? Easy! We tell you which game has been selected for the challenge, the score to beat, and the date by which you need to do it, then it's down to you!


Oh yeah, another little bonus that you can all take advantage of: you don't even have to own the game to be able to take part in the challenges. Enjoy!
