Synopsis

Help PiouPiou explore the dark caverns! Collect vegetables to win points and air capsules in order to survive a little longer.

How To Play

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move him.

Use the down arrow to dig.

You have to collect vetegables in order to win points.

The bar on the right shows your air level. Once you have run out of air or if you are crushed by a falling stone, the game will end.

Tips

Collect air capsules in order survive.

More air is required for digging and breaking rocks.

Dig further to discover rare vegetables which are worth many points.
