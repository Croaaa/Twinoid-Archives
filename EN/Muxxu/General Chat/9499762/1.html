Hello!

Welcome to the RPG-like forum game called Amy's Big Adventure!

Overview
In the game, you are a girl named Amy who goes on an adventure from her home in California to Bordeaux, France to set out on a super secret project that Motion Twin gives her. Her job is to sneak into the Motion Twin building and get the code for Fever. (A French Muxxu game)

Object
Each time someone posts, they tell a sentence of what is happening in the adventure.

Example:
1st post: Amy wakes up in the morning.
2nd post: Amy hears a loud ringing sound.
3rd post: The ringing sound is coming from the telephone, and Amy gets a call from Motion Twin...

Rules:

1. Respect the General Forum Rules: No flaming, cursing, or anything prohibited in the list found here.

2. Make the adventure last: The mission can't be solved in 10 posts. This thread will probably last 100-200 posts.

3. Don't get too off-topic: Don't make the adventure last too long: You can leave the adventure for a few posts, but you are expected to get right back on track.

4. The adventure has to have a happy ending: Amy cannot die midway through the adventure, and she has to accomplish the mission.

Conclusion
Thank you! Remember to not get off topic for a long time!
