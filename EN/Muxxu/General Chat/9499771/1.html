Bark like a dog!!! Game.

Hi. Welcome to the official game thread - Bark like a dog!!!

How to play
1. You buy a dog and describe its looks, personality and its likes/dislikes.
2. Then, you attack the persons dog above you any way you like.
3. You post it.

Rules of the game:
1. Your dog must attack the person's dog above you using the dogs personality, likes/dislikes about that dog.
An example: My dog hates the look of the person's dog above me. They attack it.

2. When your dog attacks the person's dog above, they can either die or become injured.

3. You can create a new dog after someone else kills your dog or injures it. When you create a dog again, you will have to have a bill for treating your dog at the hospital/buying a new dog.

4. You can only have one dog in the game. When someone kills your dog you can buy another one.
5. You have money in the game. About $1,000 dollars.

6. Buying a new dog costs about $300 dollars. Treating your dog at the hospital depends on how much the other person's dog injured your dog. (From $100 to $400.)
YOU DONT HAVE TO INCLUDE THE BILL!!!
Thanks!
