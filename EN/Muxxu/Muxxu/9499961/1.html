From FR player perspective, here's a bit of review:

Studio Quiz: You got 10 questions, to answer them you need to type with your keyboard rather than choosing several answers, but as you're not alone you need to type the right one fast. As those questions were made from french people, they're quite centered around french culture. Thus you only need someone to put other subjects questions in english to do the work, but as SQ isn't a full MT game, deciding facts about changing it to a worldwide mode isn't their entire decision to make.

Majority: It's not at all the same game as SQ. You are in a room with 40 people, you have to choose between 3 answers. Those three, somehow, aren't the answers to that question, to answer correctly you need to have chosen the answer most chosen by the people in the room. The game finish when there's only two people at the end. What's the problem there if the answers aren't the right ones? Culture and education aren't the same from every people in every country, meaning you won't ever find a logic to this game, while packing french people with french questions, you won't be in disarray.

Motion Ball 2, Naturalchimie 2, Odyssey: Ask Epoq.
Tower Quest: Only hosted on Muxxu, ask Carpe Ludem.
About Intrusion: It's random generated texts, devs would have to create another random generating texts for English as syntax, grammar are not the same as French language.
