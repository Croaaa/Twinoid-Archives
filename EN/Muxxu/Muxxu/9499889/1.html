Whether it has came to your attention or not, I have the most friends of all the English server. Because of this, Iwill give you some tips on making friends so you can be popular too.

Find the people you already know.
As a Muxxien, finding people you already know in real life or from another Motion Twin game is a given. Chances are good they will accept yo with open arms.

Sift through the Fluxx and other's friend lists.
When you receive a message on the Fluxx saying, "x is friends with y," make friends with y. You could also sift through the friend lists of players witha lot of friends, making friends with the ones you haven't yet done so with. I recommend sifting through Wanze's friend list, as he has one of the most, if not the most, friends on Muxxu!

Make friends with the people in your group.
What??? You are in four groups and you haven't been friendly with any of your group-mates? Make friends withthe people in your group.

Read the forums.
Make friends with people you see in the forums. Read the French and Spanish forums, too!

Messaging Required for Friendship
If someone's status says "Ami? MP.", then it means that you must send them a private message telling them why you want to be their friend, and then they will accept your request. I never understood why some people do this.

Blocked Friend Requests
Some people just don't like becoming "friends" with people they do not know and never will know. Don't bother sending requests to them, as they will just reply back and say something like, "Do I know you?" Sometimes, when you request to be their friends, you get a message saying, "This player has rejected your request." Don't annoy them with friend requests, they will not like it and add you to their blacklist.
