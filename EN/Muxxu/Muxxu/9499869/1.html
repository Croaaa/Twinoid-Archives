Preface: This post is for Motion-Twin, but others can feel free to chime in! I mean no disprespect to the developers; I'm just trying to help out by posting my observations and suggestions!

— MyBrute: Some ability descriptions are vague; no official repository for ability details (including specific #'s and %'s). Third-party websites are in varying languages, and more often relate to the old MyBrute 1.0.
— MyBrute: Abilities have different names e.g. Chef / Cuisiner, Backup / Reinforcements. Why!?

— MyBrute: Suggestion: Cap Shuriken throws — infinity is just ridiculous.
— MyBrute: Suggestion: Adjusting the combo rate of low-level wolves — wolves are overpowered because of their insane combo rate (upwards of 4-5x); cap wolf combo rate to 1-3x until level 10. They'll still be a threat, just a bit more balanced.
— MyBrute: Suggestion: Lower the +initiative of Brutes with (more) pets — the more pets you have, the less likely (negative intiative) to start the fight first. Abilities like First-strike / Ballet shoes will negate these affects.
— MyBrute: Suggestion: Increase Chef damage (a lot) — 2 additional damage per hit isn't worth a Loss.
— MyBrute: Suggestion: Inclusion of prompts for receiving MyBrute trophies (and Muxxu achievements) — currently, there's no way to know you've unlocked a new MyBrute title or Muxxu achievement unless you back out of the game and check your Muxxu profile. Consider making an in-game prompt.

— Muxxu: Forums badly need moderation and more functionality (edit, delete, etc.)
— Muxxu: Currently, the only way to post a message on your Muxxu stream (profile) is to change your "quote". It would be nice if you could keep your quote, and also have a separate entry field for various status updates.
— Muxxu: Currently, the only way to post a message to a friends Muxxu stream (profile) is to "comment" on something. It would be nice if there was a better way to interact.

Otherwise, keep up the great work, Team!

While I agree that some weapons are overpowered, some effects like Chef (?) underpowered, and the shuriken are just... well... bad, I also think it's bad to change game mechanics and effects in-game / halfway through the game. That's like saying "Your AK-47 now shoots NATO rounds instead of 7.62mm ammo, and half of your grenades are apples - Abracadabra."
There will always be someone who isn't quite happy with his or her brute is behaving or how much damage a brute receives during a seemingly unbalanced fight.
But: If someone thinks that a weapon is OP, well, why not go for a brute with this weapon? I don't think a Ninja will be first Brutal Legend. If it's someone with a wolf, so be it - there are lots of wolves running around. I find the combo wolf/dog especially ugly - the dog diverts my net half the time, leaving my opponent's wolf free to attack. Hypnosis? Someone with a wolf having 2 wolves now, attacking me?? OP!
I recognize that disabling those possibilities would either be unfair to those who couldn't chose this anymore, while others still have it in their destiny, or it would be unfair for all those who have this combo and where it gets taken away.
Okay, this is an extreme example, but I just had to make it
Btw, wolf attack ratio loosely hangs on brute stats. It would be either complete dial-down like *0.8, or introducing potentially untested if-then-code.
