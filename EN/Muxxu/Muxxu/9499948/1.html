You can log in using either your email address or the login name you chose when you created your Twinoid account. You can't, however, log in with the name you use on the forums.

You now have two usernames: your login name which no-one else can see and which is used solely to log in to Twinoid; and your nickname which is the name everyone else will know you by and is displayed beside your posts etc. (DrP1ng, in your case). Your login name can not be the same as your nickname.

If you've forgotten what your login name is and/or you want to change it, go to your account settings.
