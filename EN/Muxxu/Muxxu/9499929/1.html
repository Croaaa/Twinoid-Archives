Twinoid seems to be an unnecessary extra layer to me. Sure you'll be able to access all your Motion Twin games with a single login, but couldn't that have been achieved by adding them to, or even just linking them to, your Muxxu account? Anyway, wasn't that supposed to be the whole point of Muxxu: multiple MT games accessed from a single login with the addition of social networking-style features? Why add another layer that does essentially the same thing?

I like that I now have the username that I originally wanted but, other than that, I don't see Twinoid as an improvement. It's hard to navigate, disjointed, clunky, and feels like it was rushed out without proper planning or testing. Links that used to work are now broken and formerly translated text has reverted to French. I expect things will improve but, for now, Twinoid looks like a step backwards.

It will be interesting to see what happens when MT gets around to incorporating MiniTroopers and the old MyBrute into Twinoid. The rules regarding multiple accounts in these games are less clear than in others, and many players, myself included, have more than one army or more than one brute. If players have to chose a single army and brute to link to Twinoid and are no longer able to access any others they have after countless hours of levelling them up, I can see these forums becoming awash with complaints...
