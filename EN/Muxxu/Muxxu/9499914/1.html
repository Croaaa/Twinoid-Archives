On a serious note, Motion Twin appears to be a company that makes a bombardment of mini-games, a few quick clicks and you're done for the day. Other Motion Twin games that aren't on Muxxu include:
die2nite.com, which gives you limited actions (in the form of AP) and a limited number of ways to replenish them, but is also a strategy survival game, so spending all your points at once is NOT always the best idea and checking back in several times throughout the day IS usually recommended. Use of the forums is also highly recommended (at least reading, you don't have to be a Chatty Kathy).
minitrooper.com which plays a lot like My Brute except you're building an army instead of just a single character. There's still your three fights per day, but you can also unlock a Mission option which gives you 3 chances to get a single success for a little extra XP (expressed as [T] or coin in that game) and the possibility of unlocking a Raid option with recruits (a little complicated, ask on the Mini Trooper forum for a fuller explenation).
naturalchemist.com is a pretty old one, the French version now has a second, upgraded version out but the classic is still available in English. Kind of Dr. Mario-esque in you match up 3+ shapes of the same shape/color and they combine to make a single, upgraded shape. Initially, you only get the one game per day, but give it a months time and you can unlock recipe's and work it to get more free plays per day, nearly unlimited if you're dedicated and play long enough.

There's several others, also, that I haven't started playing myself, a couple extra links available at motion-twin.com and a couple more still inside the Natural Chemist link above.

I can tell you, in my opinion, Mini Troopers is also a lot more fun if you can space out your fights and be active in the little mini-games and tournaments that are active on the forums.
