How do you waste food in kingdoms? Is it by harvesting when you are at max capacity?

exactly. any unit of food you harvest above your limit will count as wasted food.

example:

your stock: 50
your limit: 100
food your residents take each turn: 5
you harvest: 75
you wasted 20 food (75 - 5 - 50 = 20)
