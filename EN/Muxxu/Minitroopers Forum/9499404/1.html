Cripto

Setting a particular weapon as your favorite only really seems to determine what weapon the trooper enters with. After that, the favorite weapon doesn't really matter. Their artificial intelligence and perks determine how they use their weapons for the rest of the fight.
*
Sometimes the A.I even has priority over their favorite weapons and priority target. For example, my bazooka trooper has his favorite weapon set as infernal tube and his priority target as Doctor. But when a spy is deployed, he disobeys me. He takes the most appropriate course of action by coming in with his pistol and attacking the spy first even though a doctor is on the field and his infernal tube isn't sabotaged. Once the spy is dealt with, he takes out his infernal tube and fires at the doctor.
*
So basically, the A.I controls the majority of your trooper's actions on the field. The weapons that troopers normally switch out from after firing one shot are the ones with low capacity that need to reload after 1 or 2 shots. Troopers don't like to be saddled with the need reload after every shot since it's slow and makes them vulnerable. These troopers are smarter than you think.
*
Your best option is to make the infernal tube your trooper's favorite weapon. That way, he'd at least deal maximum damage before inevitably switching to his Ak47.
*
If you still intend on keeping the Lizaro Jungle as your favorite weapon, skills that might entice your trooper into not switching weapons could be 'loader', 'Nimble fingers' and 'Take Cover'. Otherwise, there's nothing that can be done.
