WHEN DOES THE FIGHTING START?
This tournament will begin when there will be 32 registered teams
An exemple of the tournament grid Here

HOW TO SIGN UP:
Reply to this thread. In your post you MUST include the phrase "I sign up", the name of your army, and a link to your HQ page. For example:

I sign up Zen Warriors http://zen-warriors.minitroopers.com/hq

HOW DO I SEND A CHALLENGE?
When you go to your daily fight selection there is an option to infiltrate another army by entering their name. This is where you put the name of your opponent. In each match it is the first person listed who should send the challenge to the other person.

HOW DO I POST A LINK TO THE FIGHT?
When you are watching the fight, highlight the URL and copy it. Then paste it into your reply on this thread. It will be helpful if you also include the match number. If you talk about the result then please use a spoiler tag. For example:
Match 3: http://zen-warriors.minitroopers.com/b/f view/17254

WHAT HAPPENS IF THE CHALLENGE IS NOT SENT ON THE DAY?
If someone fails to send their challenge before the end of the day then their opponent will advance to the next round by default. Please be careful not to use all your daily battles up by mistake, or to forget all about the competition! I'm sure that your opponent won't mind if you do, but the spectators want to see blood.

WILL I BE NOTIFIED THAT I HAVE TO SEND A CHALLENGE?
No. Muxxu won't let me send mass message mailings to people - you will just have to keep an eye on this thread to see what's going on. I'll try to post an updated fixture list as early as possible each day, but in the event that I don't you should be able to figure out who your next opponent is and who should send the challenge to whom by referring back to the initial fixture list and previous day's results.

ARE THERE ANY RESTRICTIONS ON MY ARMY?
No, you can upgrade as and when you like.
One army per person

Any other questions should be addressed to me in this thread.
If there are refereeing decisions to be made then I will make them - and my decision is final!
