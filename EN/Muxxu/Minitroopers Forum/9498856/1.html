Brown Coats versus Zealots
or
Anger beneath the Agaric

DRAMATIS PERSONAE:

The B
Huffolds . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . Heavy weapon operator
Brolone Chan . . . . . . . . . . . . . Suspiciously minded Scout
T-Bombeiber . . . . . . . . . . . . . Cannon fodder
Karlonnell . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . Seasoned Soldier
Dkarba. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . Communications Officer

The Z
Andaho the Spy . . . . . . . . . . . . Low down dirty sneak
Annette . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . Shotgun weilding maniac
Workshop . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . Close friend of Annette
Spacey . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . Sniper and grenadier
Adzman . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . Grenadier and rocketry
Createx. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . Ill-prepared Commando


ACT I

[Sunlight glances from the helmets of the assembled troopers. Foul play is afoot, as Andaho has sneaked into the camp of the Brown Coats with his shotgun and a machine gun. Dkarba is nowhere to be seen: the other members of the Brown Coats are standing in a group near to two mushrooms]

T-Bombeiber: Attend! The enemy draw near. This fragmentation grenade will welcome them to our field of battle most well I feel.

[enter stage right: the remaining Zealots, except for Createx]

Adzman: Aiiiie! I have fragments in places where they should not be! On, men, let us teach them a lesson they will not soon forget.

Andaho: The enemy are already nearer than you think, friend Bombeiber.
[Andaho fires a shotgun blast which catches most of the Brown Coats. T-Bombeiber falls to the floor. Before anyone can react Spacey throws a gas grenade which lands close to T-Bombeiber. Green gas billows and curls around the soldiers]

Karlonnell: Gas! Don't breathe it in! Too late, too late... be strong my friends. I will repay their grenade with one of my own!

Adzman: Aiiiie! More fire from above. It is more than one man can stand. [He dies]

Karlonnell: How do you like that, you weak and terrible creatures? You will learn not to come to our side of the mushroom patch. Learn or die!

Brolone Chan: Behind you! That's not Dkarba, that's an enemy spy! Watch out in case he shoots you! [Gunfire] Actually, you should have been watching in case I shoot you, but oh well.

T-Bombeiber: Uuuuugh [He struggles to his feet, but gives in to the poison gas and collapses into death]

[Spacey throws a flashbang which blinds Huffolds and Brolone Chan]

Brolone Chan: Did I get him? I think I got him. Damn this light, I can't see anything! Huffolds, stay close, stay close. Oh no, I hear the spy again!

[Brolone Chan fires towards the sound, killing the spy at last but injuring Karlonnell]

Karlonnell: You men, open your misted eyes and turn around - the enemy are BEHIND YOU!

[Huffolds spins to face the advancing Zealots, however Brolone Chan collapses to the floor and his weapons clatter about him]

Huffolds: Farewell, my faithful rifle. One last kiss for you before I die. [He also succumbs to the poison]

Karlonnell: Nuts. Time for some payback.
[Karlonnell fires a bazooka round directly at Workshop, blowing him into many tiny pieces]

Annette: Noooo! My friend, you are supposed to walk the battlefield beside me, not cover me from head to toe! This uniform does get so dirty, true, but this is just unsanitary.

[While Annette is distracted by the death, Karlonnell fires another round and the two comrades in arms join each other in the afterlife]

Karlonnell: Just you left over there now, soldier, what is your name?

Spacey: My name is Spacey and I am the last thing you will ever see. Say your prayers for you die this day.
[Spacey takes aim with his Sniper rifle and places a bullet delicately through Kalonnell's heart]

ACT II

[Dkarba approaches the mushrooms, muttering. He is carrying an assault rifle and has a revolver tucked into his waistband]
Dkarba: Five minutes peace, that's all I wanted. I can't leave these people alone. One day, oh one day they will regret it.
[He arrives on the battlefield and takes in the carnage quickly]
Dkarba: My soldiers! Dead! Oh, never again will the trouble me, how I will miss them! But what is this? A puff of blue smoke hangs over that tree, a sniper reloading I'll be bound. Now I will punish him.

[Spacey slaps more ammunition into his rifle and takes aim as Dkarba strides purposefully across the battlefield]

Dkarba: Ha! My bulletproof vest has protected me again, stand down sniper for I am come to kill you!

Spacey: Whoops. Should've gone for the headshot. No more ammo - chaaaaarge!

[Dkarba cuts him down in a hail of fire. Createx lumbers onto the field carrying the Zealot's prized Gatling Gun. He seems out of breath; it is a heavy weapon]

Createx: Now you die, Brown Coat scum! Eat lead!!
[A shot]
Createx: OK, you WOULD have died if I'd put more than one round into the gun. Not to worry, I have my trusty shotgun too.

Dkarba: Preparedness is all in battle. Join your friends.
[He kills Createx with ease]

Dkarba: Victory is sweet. War is hell. Dinner is ready.

Intermission: thought I'd have a go with http://caine.minitroopers.com/b/fview/28 405 as well.
This is the Cup Final match from the first knockout cup tournament.

Excitement here at the Caine/Klegg Clash. This fight has been a week in the making, now it all comes down to a few brief moments of frenzy.

Who will be the winner? Who will lose their life? Stay tuned and find out after these commercial messages.

CK-MAGELLEN, the gun you can TRUST! Make yours a Magellen.

And we are back! Both armies have taken to the field. Harko is quite literally jumping for joy at the prospect of the battle to come. That's not something you see every day! We see him take out his CK-Magellen and square up to the enemy. The first shot is fired! Bearsu takes the hit, it looks to be right in the groin. Nasty stuff, but he must have some kind of armour down there as he shrugs it off. This veteran sniper really is unshakable.

Cork making a move for team Klegg now, charging out to close the distance to the enemy. I'm not sure how he managed it but Harko gets the second shot of the battle off too! It kicks up dirt eight feet to Cork's left, though, pretty harmless. Cork today is wearing his signature edition bulletproof vest and has a rabbit's foot on a length of twine around his neck. More on this on our fashion roundup show at ten tonight, but now back to the action.

Klegg the saboteur has decided that Harko is too much of a threat and places a bullet perfectly between his eyes. MOS-TECK will be pleased to see him making such good use of their equipment. Harko is the first casualty.

Cycle Path starts to move forwards to support Cork, but Caine has other ideas. Popping a twinoid Caine pulls out a pump action shotgun and lets Cork have it. Cork returns fire but Caine hits him again, throwing him back towards the rest of Klegg's army. Looks like a head-wound and some damage to the arm... the rabbit's foot is looking a little the worse for wear as well.
It's worth notice here that Caine's favourite revolver has been sabotaged by Klegg. A busy night before the battle, obviously, stealing vital parts of the equipment. More worrying is probably the sabotage of Munitions Officer Flash's Gatling gun. Flash realizes that the gun is no good and takes out his backup weapon, a standard issue assault rifle.

Klegg's team are dismayed to see Cork die of his wounds, but can take some small comfort from the fact that trooper Caine had a reaction to the drugs he took and collapsed on the floor. He doesn't seem to be moving - I think it's all over for him.

Volga moves forwards for Caine's army, making the unit's other Gatling Gun look light as a feather. This weapon has not been sabotaged and could prove very costly to Klegg. We can just see that the ammunition is modified explosive rounds. Always a crowd pleaser.

Bearsu and Klegg watch anxiously, unsure of whether to fire. Communications Officer Cycle Path decides to take action and advances towards Volga while Bearsu lobs a gas grenade over his head towards the enemy.
Oh! It lands right at Volga's feet, a wonderful throw from the sniper. The toxic fumes seem to be affecting Volga slightly, he opens fire on Cycle Path but misses. The shells throw up great clumps of earth as they impact and then explode all around the UMP-weilding soldier, then Volga regains his composure and drags the stream of fire back on target. Cycle Path drops his weapon and falls backwards, dead before he hits the ground.

Flash moves forward to support Volga, then they both advance together oblivious of the sniper-fire from Klegg, which fails to find its mark.
Flash opens fire and takes Klegg out. Bearsu is next in his sights, down he goes before he can get a shot off.

Flash and Volga reload before the last member of Klegg's army, trooper Retrobution, can move up into position. Flash casually reloads and takes the reserve out too.

A bloody day, but the crowds loved it.
