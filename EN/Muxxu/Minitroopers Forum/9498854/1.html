Hacksaw
Comm. Officer can give your allies commands to boost their performance or give the enemy commands to hindrance them (can be used even when the comm. officer is in reserve).

Doctor can heal an ally (use to be determine with the current low HP's of low level soldiers; must be on the field to heal troops).

Saboteur can make it so enemy troops fight without their weapons (can be used even when the saboteur is in reserve).

Pilot gives increased chances of gaining a vehicle on level up.

Scout lets you deploy 1 extra troop on the field at a time (so 1 scout means you deploy 5 troops instead of 4, useable from reserve).

Spy deploys on your on the enemy's side of the screen instead of one of his troops.

Soldier gains +1 HP per level.

Munitions I'm not sure on, description reads he refills your troops ammo and doubles the use of their grenades.
