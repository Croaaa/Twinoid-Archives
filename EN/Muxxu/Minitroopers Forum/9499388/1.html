Second version of the Kill League A for those that could not participate in the other one. This version has slightly different rules in the scoring so make sure you read the Scoring details. This will also run for 15 days instead of 14.

Concept:
League competition where each army fights every other army.
Points scored for kills as well as for victory. An army may still score if it performs well even if it is overwhelmed by a superior foe.

Time frame:
League will run for 15 days. It begins after server reset at the end of 9th March. It concludes on server reset at the end of 24rd March.

Number of competitors:
Only the first 10 to sign up below will take part. If more than ten people wish to take part then anyone is welcome to copy and paste these rules into a new thread "kill league C" and gather more players.

Restrictions:
One army per participant. No restriction on army power rating.

Scoring:
EVERY fight you have against one of the participants can be scored and added onto your total. However, you can only fight the same army once every three days so that you don't pick on weaker armies to gain points. That means you should have fought all 9 other armies after every third day (there's three fights in a day). If you do fight an army more than once within three days, only the first fight made will count.

To claim your points, you must post the fights in the thread along with the outcome and how many enemy troopers you killed. I will regularly update the thread with the ranking from 1st to 10th, along with how many points each person has.

1) Fights will be scored 2 points for victory and 1 point for each enemy kill. Friendly casualties do not count.
2) You must be the challenger in the fight (army is on the left of the field)
3) Fights ending in MiSS1, MISS2, MISS3, BAT1, BAT2, or BAT3 do not count

Ranking:
Most points wins. In the event of a tie, greater number of wins will be ranked higher. In the event of a further tie, fights will be organized!

Prize:
Bragging rights.

Sign up below if you are interested and feel free to ask any questions.
