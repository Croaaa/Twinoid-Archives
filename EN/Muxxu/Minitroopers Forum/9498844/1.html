The leveling up is ridiculously slow, and "recruiting" someone really seems pointless because raids aren't all that rewarding, even at phase 14.

Why don't they make it so you can keep fighting as much as you want, if not that at least more than THREE times every TWENTY FOUR HOURS. It's just not something that will hold peoples attention. I think that recruiting people into actual armies that fight against each other (not just against raid NPCs) would be a lot more fun, and there should be more guys on the screen at once. Don't tell me a 20v20 battle of your army vs another wouldn't be awesome!

Why can't we form Alliances, make armies, have a chat system, and have factions put in place? Why can't we battle more, go through levels at our own pace, and even if it stays a short daily game, make it more involved with actual people. Let us be the "commander" of our army and add other commanders to a network we can send messages to and create "alliances" to form armies.

Anyone else have ideas/suggestions they think would make the game better? It has a fantastic framework to build on, they just need to make it happen!
