Right button at flash app, "Global Settings" (something like this, I'm not using english version of flash player), then in the "Storage" tab you must check "Allow sites to save information on this computer".

Bad news: this will prevent future issues, but your current army is already doomed not to have a password, because you hand't cookies enabled by the time you created it.
