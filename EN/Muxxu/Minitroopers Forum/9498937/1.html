+++ Strenghs +++

- Tactical Shield NEVER EVER protects your trooper when getting hit from the side or from behind
-BTW Pilots cannot use Tactical Shield when using a vehicle

Of course your trooper can drop or pick-up any Tactical Shield on the battlefield as he deserves :P
