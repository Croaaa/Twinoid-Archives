Tunkson
Sounds have to be created. Coders are needed for that. Coders rarely work for free. Over a multitude of games (how many games are there, around 8?), plus having multiple sites, that might also bite into server space and slow down performance if the servers aren't high-end ones, potentially forcing them to upgrade servers, could get expensive, especially since the financial ROI is miniscule.

I'm not saying this is the reason why there is no sound, but that's a reason why there may not be any. Even if they did decide on sound, it would be too stressful to determine what kind of sound to put in. Some sounds can actually turn the game into something it's not. If it ain't broke, don't fix it.
