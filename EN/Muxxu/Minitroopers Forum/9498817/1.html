Hacksaw
More soldiers means more firepower which means more chances of winning the fight and gaining more money.

Of course, if you don't get anyone to click your link and join you as a recruit it can take a long while to get the money needed to buy the recruits.

First person to join via your link gives you 100 money, 2nd-4th gives you 50 each (total that's 250 money with the first four recruits). That gives you enough to buy 4 troops (if you don't have any already) and level them up to 2 (can only level each troop once per day).

That's just my two cents, yesterday was my first day playing.
