Welcome to the first ever Knockout Cup Competition. Do you have what it takes to go up against the best, the bravest, the luckiest and the rest too? If so, sign up below!

RULES OF THE COMPETITION
This is a knockout competition with only one winner. Sign ups will be limited to two days, or until we get to 32 participants... the first Cup is small just while we're figuring things out, so if you want to take part then sign up quick.
After everyone has signed up I will do a random draw to determine the bracket and post the results in this thread.
There will be ONE match between each pair drawn to fight. The first person in each pair should send the challenge and also post the result and replay here in this thread. The next round of matches will begin the following day, in order to give people a little time between rounds for strategy adjustments.
We'll keep going until there is a winner!

SIGN UP BELOW:
Please post your army name below to sign up. Limit is one army per player. First 32 get to take part, or if we're short of numbers in two days time then I'll close signups and we'll get going with however many we've got.
