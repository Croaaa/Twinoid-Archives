I've noticed this a few times with my own army. I don't know if it's because mine just simply rocks (I'm sure it doesn't because Caine still whips my ass every time), but soldiers - or at least mine - seem to be immune to headshots.

Now, when I say immune, I don't mean they can't die from it, but their helmet protects from the first headshot. What would be a headshot on a soldier instead knocks off the helmet and then does 0 damage. All other classes are properly killed, since their helmets aren't helmets, but rather caps. Now what happens after the helmet is knocked off is another story, then it's open season on that forehead, but so far, I have yet to see any of my soldiers with helmet on die from a headshot.
(I don't mean shots towards the head as if you were randomly aiming at the head, I mean the sweet spot one-hit-kill type headshot where *headshot!* pops up, just like elsewhere *punctured lung!* or *gutshot!* would result in a one-hit-kill.)

Is there anyone out there who could confirm or deny this?
