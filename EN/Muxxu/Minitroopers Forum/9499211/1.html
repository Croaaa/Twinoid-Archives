Tunkson
I seem to remember someone saying that their soldiers inflicted more damage with Charge than non-soldiers did.

Yeah, that was me.

I didn't clarify damage because TJ didn't ask about damage. But as far as damage goes, the damage done is unrelated to remaining hitpoints as far as I can tell. What affects Charge damage, just like every other weapon used, is the area where the impact is made. Just like with everything else, limb damage does normal damage, head damage does double damage, and heart damage can go as high as 3 times if already injured (but then again, hitting an already wounded spot always does more damage). My squad chief is a very proficient user of charge, and has done up to 36 damage so far with one charge hit, whereas the most that can usually be done without hitting wounded spots or any other affects is 20 when hitting the head as soldier or 10 when hitting the head as non-soldier.
