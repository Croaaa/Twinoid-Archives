Anyone interested in a bit of multiplayer fun? A few of us have been taking part in lplplplp55's Big War idea for two weeks now. What's been going on is that we have a main army, and people make recruits for it. Everyone that makes a recruit gets input into the decision making process for the main army as far as upgrades and development goes.

Main thread: http://muxxu.com/forum/thread/1173572

At the moment we have two armies, Sharks and Jets. We've been making them fight each other; it's been quite entertaining.
What we are planning to do is have regular conflicts between the TOP recruit armies on each side, kind of like a massive fight.

Is anyone else interested in making a new team-army and taking part? You'd be two weeks behind, but if you started now then you should be able to catch up pretty easily and be competitive by the time we start with the big fights.
If enough teams are formed then we might even be able to have knock-out tournaments as well as regular pvp team fighting.

To take part you will need:
A team (three players seems to work well, odd numbers make voting on decisions more straight forward)
An Army signed up under one of the bot armies (lplplplp55 made our one for us, I'm not sure how the decision was made as to the starting equipment but we ended up with an assault rifle and a gas grenade)
A Thread on the forum for discussion
Fighting spirit

...and a few minutes spare each day to talk strategy and run the daily Raid fights to harvest your cash, of course.

Interested?
