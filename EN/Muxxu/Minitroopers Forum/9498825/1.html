From what I've been hearing you can have up to 3 armies per IP address. What I don't know is if you can run all three by yourself or if it's like Die2Nite where it's intended to let 3 different people in the same household play together.

As for the number of troops you can have in a single army, once you have 5 troops you get the ability to command those troops, and judging from the size of the shaded field versus the size of the troop ID card I'd say the Command page will allow up to 24 ID cards to fit.

Until I can afford the final troop my initial guess is a maximum of 24 troops. Time will tell.

Ipso-facto, I haven't seen an army yet with more than 6 troops, and those that do have 6 have an open line for 3 more recruits, so the minimum I've seen so far is 9 troops.
