Tunkson
I've learned from toying with recruits that making pilots for the sake of vehicle isn't worth it.

While yes, a pilot has a 25% higher chance of getting a vehicle, and yes, this does mean that the pilot will get a vehicle sooner or later, when, is anyone's guess. You could get a vehicle as soon as level 7, or it could not appear before level 12. And there are other professions that also have a chance of getting a vehicle, albeit with a lower percentage rate. If pilots came with any other perks (for example, munitions has two perks, one being refilling ammo, the other being double frequency of grenade usage, or soldiers who get more HP per level, plus having immunity to headshots), then one could consider using them anyway, but as it stands, if you're level 10 and still don't have a vehicle (which is possible), then you've basically wasted a crucial decision at level 6, since every other profession has mega profession-specific skills or traits by that time, but pilots don't.

So for recruits you control, sure, give pilots a spin. But for your main army, keep your hands off that profession. You could get a vehicle even if you're not a pilot.
