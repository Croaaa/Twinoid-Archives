Team-Jay

Charge attacks are based on the user's full HP. Their base damage will either be half of the HP, equivalent to or double the full HP. Other factors like helmets, existing injuries and headshots can add or take a few points from the attack's power.
There's a lot of evidence that soliders do a lot more damage with charge attacks, whether this is down to the fact that they have more HP is unknown.
Take my soldier for example, he has 26HP and usually does about 13 damage with a single charge attack, other times he does 26 and occaisionally he inflicts 52 damage (There's a video of it on the wiki, I believe). There's little or no variation in this trend.

Hope this clears it up.

Headshots are always twice the damage. If you hit the same body part twice, the damage doubles again. so if he already was hit to the head by a bullet and then your soldier charged at him, he will get 4x his normal charge damage.
