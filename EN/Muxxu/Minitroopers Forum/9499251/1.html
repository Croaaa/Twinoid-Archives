As Team-Jay said, the option appears when the trooper has more than 3 pieces of equipment, so it is not dependant upon your trooper having reached Level 10. It works in a similar way to choosing a particular look for your brute in the original MyBrute: you just keep clicking the button until it eventually randomly selects the combination you want.
