Yes it is.
Go to the "choice" page e.g. http://knife-fighters.minitroopers.com/c hoice
Then zoom in really close, by pressing ctrl and + together over and over.
Then you should be able to see your recruits, and also by selecting them you can access a link to the army they belong to.

Although you can change the order of your recruits this will not affect anything. Epic missions will always be in order strongest to weakest, while Raids will always be weakest to strongest.
