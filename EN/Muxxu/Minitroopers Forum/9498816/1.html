Hacksaw
With two snipers you're going to want someone who can fire in close combat to protect them. Gat's have much love, shotguns are good for splash damage against multiple targets, and assault rifles have a three-round burst which is pretty good for taking out a single troop.

In my opinion handguns fire way too slow to be used as a primary weapon but are otherwise tolerable for when your primary weapon(s) run out of ammo before being forced to resort to hand-to-hand combat. Personally, I'd go with a pistol as a sidearm for an assault rifle or shotgun user (if at all) and any of the guns in the previous paragraph as a "side arm" for a sniper or bazooka user, since they'll be spending most of their time aiming and enemies will definitely be close for serious splash damage with any of the above 3. But that's just my initial thoughts, though I can tell you it works nice for my one troop who uses the basic Sniper Rifle with a Gatling Gun as a "side arm". =)

As for Bazookas (the 6th and final weapon type), I'd advise against them (right now) since they're in the same boat as the sniper where they need protection to fire the weapon, meaning a speedy soldier or two can shut down your army in a hurry. Once you're able to produce cover fire (or if at least one of your snipers has a crappy sniper rifle and can switch quickly to one of the aforementioned guns, or both), then a Bazooka can deal some nasty damage, though I find it misses often enough to be considered a gamble. Right now I'd say it hits a little better than 50% of the time, but it's definitely a game changer; boss when it hits and focked when it don't.
