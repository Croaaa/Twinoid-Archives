Welcome to the Minitrooper Tricky Feather Belt!

This competition is open to any army with a power rating of 75 or below.
I do not want to go though the trouble of enforcing this, but I recommend that you use a army that is password protected.

This belt is very similar to the Featherweight Belt, except that before you can beat the person above you, you must roll the 10-sided dice.
You must follow a certain condition during your battle depending on the number you rolled.

If you roll a 1 : You can beat the army above you normally with no special conditions.

If you roll a 2 : You must use a 1-man army.

If you roll a 3 : You must beat the army above you at least twice.

If you roll a 4 : If any of your troopers use any kind of grenade, you forfeit the battle.

If you roll a 5 : If any of your troopers deploy in a vehicle, you forfeit the battle.

If you roll a 6 : All of your troopers must participate in the battle, assuming your troopers deploy in waves.

If you roll a 7 : If any of your troopers use any rocket-based weapon (such as bazooka, or infernal tube), or if any of your troopers use twinoid, voodoo doll, reverse attack, covering fire, propaganda, or charge, you forfeit the battle.

If you roll a 8 : If your army has more than 1 trooper, then at least 1 of your troopers must die during this battle, otherwise, if you use a 1-man army, you can beat the person above you normally under no special conditions.

If you roll a 9 : You must use an army with more than 1 trooper, and at least 2 of your troopers in that army must share at least 1 skill in common.

If you roll a 10 : You can post your army link and claim the belt without a fight, as long as the army has 75 power or less. If you dont want to do this, you can just beat the army above you normally under no special conditions.

If you roll a number and you do not think you will be able to follow that condition, you can just pass up your battle, and the person above you will still continue to hold the belt.

Only "Select an army base to infiltrate" replays count. Replays where there URL ends in BAT1, BAT2, BAT3, MISS1, MISS2 or MISS3 are not allowed.
If you notice that the current belt holder has upgraded and now has power rating above 75 then you can immediately post in this thread to claim the belt for yourself without a fight.

First person to post below with an army of power rating 75 or below will pick up the belt and get things rolling.
