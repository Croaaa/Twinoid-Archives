Hi all, this is the first edition of WORLD CUP MINITROOPERS (p.s. I would call it Minitroopers World Cup, it sounds more right...). This tournament is open to everyone and on all servers (english, spanish and french) This is a "cup" type tournament, that starts with pools of 4 teams (randomly chosen). The top two armies in each pool qualifies for the classic final phase with direct elimination. The number of games and turns will be determined once we find out how many teams sign up.


Rules and information:

- First, you must be active, I will not tolerate a miss or a delay of any match
- The infiltrate missions do not count.
- I will edit the first post of the topic (not this topic, this one: http://minitroopers.leforum.eu/t1614-Tou rnoi-Coupe-du-Monde-MINITROOPERS-1.htm) as often as possible, so you can know your opponent of the day.
- Do not hesitate to ask me questions if there is anything that you do not understand!



Registration:

- For this first edition, Not knowing the activity of .com and .es, registrations will be closed once a participant reaches number (for fr it would be nice if we reach 64 teams)
- Sign up giving me the link / HQ of your army on this topic
- You can enter multiple armies, no power limit.


Prizes:

1st: PT 50 + 350 recruits
2nd: 30 + PT 200 recruits
3rd: 10 PT + 100 recruits


The prizes are identical for each server. (The recruits will be spread over one or three armies maximum)
