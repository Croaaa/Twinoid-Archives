On february 14, 2012
Marcotonio
Spies can start on the opposite side of the field (AND negate one of the enemies' soldier to deploy in the first round, if they have more than 3 soldiers).
Scouts allow more soldiers to be deployed. Useful for making a powerful squad or deploying vehicles.
