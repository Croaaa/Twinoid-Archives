On october 20, 2011
dDShockTroop
There's a button on your hq which says "recruit a trooper" click on it, and it will give you a link which you can then promote/give out in any way you like. If another person creates an account using that link, their first trooper will be added to your "recruits". Your first recruit will unlock the "raid" mission, open up the "history" page, give you 100T and allow the possibility of the daily mission being the "epic" mission. Additional recruits will give you less and less T the more you get.

The raid mission is a series of computer generated waves of troops where all of your recruits will fight them. If a recruit dies, they will not carry over to the next wave. For every wave completed, you get 4T. At the end of every day, when the server resets the missions, your current raid phase will return to wave 1, and all of the recruits that died will revive and you can try again.

The "epic" mission can appear instead of the "infiltrate" or "exterminate" mission each day. In this mission, your army and all of your recruits will fight against a massive amount of computer generated troopers. (usually well over 30) You still only get 4T from this like any other mission, but you may be surprised to know that it is a much easier mission than exterminate later on if you have a lot of recruits.
