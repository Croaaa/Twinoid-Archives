FOUR LEAFED CLOVER CUP

This is a new competition to test your ability to build the best small army possible, within certain constraints.

Players will have three weeks to build their armies, then all players will fight each other in a randomized knockout cup to determine the winner.
The knockout cup will be best-of-three single elimination, with either player able to challenge (first come, first served) to avoid any problems with AWOL armies.

To take part in the competition an army must:
1) Have exactly 4 troopers
2) Have one each of the basic starting weapons: pistol, shotgun, sniper rifle, assault rifle
3) Each trooper must have a different skin tone (from the four on offer)
4) Each trooper must have a different hair style (from the many on offer) although hair colour can be the same
5) Each trooper must be LEVEL FIVE only
A player may only enter one army into the competition.

After the cup is complete, any army that took part will be allowed to enter a further seeded double-elimination competition, with seedings based on their performance. For the follow-up competition all four troopers may be upgraded to level six, but no higher.

The competition begins NOW.
The deadline for submitting an army is three weeks from now, i.e. Friday 5th October.

Players are encouraged to post their army links in this thread as early as possible and begin sparring with their potential adversaries.
