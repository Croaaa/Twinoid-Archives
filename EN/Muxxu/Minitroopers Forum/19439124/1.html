Welcome to the gauntlet run!
Start by looking over this thread,
try to follow the pattern of the other players here

Each post has 2 parts.
part 1, your gauntlet run.
part 2, the updated gauntlet list.


Part 1. Your gauntlet run.
Win or lose, post 3 replays showing any one army of yours attacking all 3 of the armies that are in the current gauntlet list.

Gauntlet run details.
- You may reuse replays from other games.
- Attacks do not need to be all on the same day or in any order.
- You may not use replays already used for other gauntlet runs or replays older then 27382983.


Part 2. Updating the gauntlet list.
Find the most recent gauntlet list in the last post.
Copy and paste this list into your new post.
Pick any one army in the list, replace it with yours and post.

Updating details.
- The army added must be the same army used in the gauntlet run.
- Do not add 'your' name into the list. The first words (or word) in the list is your 'army' name. The next player will copy and paste this into their infiltrate field.

Minitroopers standard gaming rules apply. http://tinyurl.com/br55a9w
