I welcome everyone to our first annual minitroopers awards. Here we will be giving honours to armies and troopers that deserve credit. The way this will work is that the awards will be divided into three parts: Nominations, voting and award giving. The nominations will be done in this thread but the voting and award giving will have their own threads for the sake of tidiness. The nominations are split up into 5 categories.


They are as follows:

Most durable soldier.

Most deadly spy.

Most efficient doctor.

Most frightening trooper.

Most strategic army.

Army with the most potential.

Most unique army.

Most underestimated army.

Most frightening army.


Additional info. You can nominate any army or trooper you want, including your own. You can also nominate the same army or trooper in more than one category. In the voting round, you are allowed to try and convince people of why you should win a particular category with fights. Feel free to ask any questions.


Let the nominations begin
