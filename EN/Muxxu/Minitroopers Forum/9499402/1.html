This is a sign-up thread for a tournament.
Rules are to be the same as for the Mad March Cup, so go see that thread if you want to see how things work.

Tournament will begin some time around the start of April and run for two or three weeks, depending on how many people take part. You'll usually have one match every couple of days, although things can progress more quickly if people do their fights early.

Just like the MMC, it will be a double-elimination event, with participants seeded according to their standing in the Continuous Championship.
No prizes other than kudos at the moment, although that might change.

No restrictions on power level for entry; you may enter up to two armies if you wish.

To take part post an HQ link to your army along with a comment along the lines of "sign me up!" and then you're in
