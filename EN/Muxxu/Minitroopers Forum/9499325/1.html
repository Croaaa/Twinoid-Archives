Doomys
Regular Grenade - It's like a one shot M25 Bazooka with no aiming time and no recovery. It can be quite useful. The problem is that the damage zone is only 2x2

Frag Grenade - The good thing about this one is that it counts as bullets. So if you have special shells, they can be used on the grenade.

Gas Grenade - Can potentially kill many troopers but takes time to act.

Flashbang Grenade - When blinded, bazookas won't be something you will have to be scared of.

Glue Grenade - This gives your army the time to strike! A well placed glue grenade and bazooka hit can be fatal to the enemy team.
Holy Grenade - My personnal favorite grenade. Although it might not kill all the troopers it hits, it has more chance of hitting many troops in 1 hit because of the 3x3 damage zone.

Shock Grenade - Much better than the glue grenade because the enemy army loses more than 200 initiative in time. Plus some troops switch to worse weapons instead of picking theirs back

Clown Grenade - Fun grenade. Usefull too because it distracts (non-smart) troopers to try to kill the clown.

Black Hole Grenade - My second favorite grenade. Great for killing enemies with alot of health. The problem is that it has a 2x2 damage zone.

Healing Grenade - This one is good because it's the only equipment that can let medics heal themselves. The only problems with this one are that if an enemy troop is in the healing zone, he will also be healed and I heard that if one of your troops has interception, he will throw back the grenade at the enemy!
