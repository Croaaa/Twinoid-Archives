This is a quick competition. Armies are grouped by power-rating as per the belt competitions, so things shouldn't be too unbalanced.

CONCEPT
Everyone that wants to play signs up in the power bracket that applies to their army.
When the competition begins, the object is to beat ALL the other armies in your bracket at least once.
First to do so (and post the proof) is the winner!
There will be one winner per bracket, and one overall winner for the fastest fighting army.

RULES
No BAT or MISS replays apply.
Only fights where you are the challenger may be used to claim.
Nobody new may join after the competition begins (although if it works we can always run another competition when this one is done)
You must post the replays in a CLEAR and EASY TO READ fashion, including the details of who each replay applies to and who you have beat/still need to beat. Just posting up a replay link with no details might even get you disqualified if the competition administrator is feeling tetchy.
An army may only be signed up in ONE power bracket.
No player may have more than one army in a single bracket, however they may have multiple armies across the brackets if they wish.

TIME FRAME
Sign-ups are open NOW.
Sign-ups will close when 2012 ends on the server (i.e. 23:00 GMT, or your equivalent time zone)
That gives you a couple of days only to sign up, so don't dawdle.
The competition will run until we have a winner in each bracket.

POWER BRACKETS
Flyweight (25 or below)
Bantamweight (50 or below)
Featherweight (75 or below)
Lightweight (100 or below)
Welterweight (125 or below)
Middleweight (150 or below)
Light Heavy (175 or below)
Heavyweight (no restriction)

HOW TO SIGN UP
If someone else has already signed up to your bracket then copy and paste the list, adding your own army link, army name and player name to the bottom.
If you are the first then write the title of the bracket, then add your details below.

STUFF
If any rulings are required then my decision will be final. Bribes are welcome.
Questions or comments should be posted in this thread, however changes to the format are unlikely until this competition is completed and the next one begins so don't be offended if your suggestions are kept on hold.

   Begin!
