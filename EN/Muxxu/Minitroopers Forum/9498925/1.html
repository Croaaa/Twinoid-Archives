An unsupported sniper is most likely toast.

Slow rate of fire and predictable but low damage means that they are seldom able to take out an enemy before the distance can be closed down. An opponent with a shotgun, rifle or pistol is able to get close and kill the sniper before the three sniper rounds needed to get a kill can be fired. An opponent with a bazooka has a good chance to get a shot off and one-hit-kill the sniper before taking fatal damage.
Also they are not much use against rodents once the distance is closed and the sniper is unable to fire.

Snipers can get critical hits, sure, but not often enough.

Now, with a few choice upgrades and some support from another trooper, they are much more useful.

Damage: 3 Range: 2-Max Reviews: x25 Aim: 150% Recovery: 65 Skills: heavy weapon, key most favorite location Capacity: 6 rounds refill maximum : 12 balls

Most people look at this gun and say ewww because the damage it does but look at its recovery rate its aim bonus and the fact that its a sniper rifle which can attack from range 2-max (most sniper rifles 3-max so they can get pretty close and you still be effective.) Also the fact that it has a 6round magazine means you can pop off multiple shots with it low recovery ensuring you get a fatal shot before they even get close.

If you add a combination of any of these abilities as well then you have a beast sniper who can hold his on even in a 1v1:
explosive bullets, hydroshock bullets, Toxic Shells, Cold Blooded (key),Heartbreaker,On Point, Charger, Trigger Happy, Voodoo Stick, Camoflouge,Dodge,Tuck N Roll, Unwavering.

If you get a decent combincation of those abilities you can have a sniper that can hold his own against any weapon.
