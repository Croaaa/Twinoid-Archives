Grenade deals damage to only a single target. Good for a big wound or possible kill in the early game, maybe more of a nuisance in the late?

Fragmentation Grenade does splash damage (so more liely to hit a target where the grenade can be a total miss) and can crit for 1 or more kills. Even if it doesn't, it wounds multiple targets and makes them susceptible to OHKO's even if the shots aren't criticals.

Flash Grenade blinds targets, which creates it's own breed of havoc. Not sure what the penalties are for being blind but in most games they include becoming easier to hit and more likely to miss their targets.

Gas Grenade poisons, which when combined with a Frag can mean rapid death for an entire squad. XD

Shock Grenade sounds like a back-up for when a Saboteur fails to disarm the enemy and a wasted action when they succeed. *shrug*

Sticky Grenades I've seen in action and I can't say they seemed all that spectacular. Honestly sounds like it should slow their movements or immobilize them for a turn instead of just dropping their init., but put them on a sniper or bazooka user and it may buy them those precious seconds to target and kill an enemy that needs to get close to use his weapons. *shrug*

But, seeing them in action, grenades don't appear to take that much time to throw, almost like a bonus action, so load up and enjoy.
