Hello guys!

There is a new season of our ladder for active players. Rules are the same as we had them previous season. I had no time to adjust rules, though I think they are almost perfect.

Rules

0. You can add/use your own armies only!

1. Don't break the forum rules and rules below!

2. Everyone is able to take part in. Make sure (by checking listed armies power) that your army is suitable to join.

3. If you are new you start at the bottom

4. If the link to your army is too long, then you shall use http://tinyurl.com/ service. Eg

wrong : http://kingdom-of-korea.minitroopers....

correct : http://tinyurl.com/KOFKOREA

5. You shall post FULL ARMY NAME and the POWER right after the link to your army (link and full army namve are not always equal!). DON'T forget to update the POWER. Eg

"1. http://tinyurl.com/KOFKOREA KINGDOM OF KOREA / 263"

6. To advance you must beat the army above you, post the replay and change your position in the list

7. If there is a double post please edit the most recent post to have the most up to date list (or post an up to date list).

8. If you have more than one army in the list you shall beat your own army that's above (according to clause 3) to move your second army further.

9. If you know that you won't be able to take part with any army, you shall mark that army with SKIP text. Eg

"3. SKIP http://tinyurl.com/WECOME WECOMEINPIECES / 257"
or
"3. http://tinyurl.com/WECOME WECOMEINPIECES / 257 SKIP"
or
"3. SKIP http://tinyurl.com/WECOME WECOMEINPIECES / 257 SKIP"

10. You can't mark your army with SKIP just for "a second". Armies of abusers will be removed from the list and may be banned until the next season

11. Once per 2-4 weeks I'll check armies that "got stuck". If those armies doesn't attack armies above or attack+beat them without posting a result then "stucked" armies will be marked with SKIP by myself.
