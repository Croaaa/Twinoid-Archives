A new game.

Concept:
League competition where each army fights every other army.
Points scored for kills as well as for victory. An army may still score if it performs well even if it is overwhelmed by a superior foe.

Time frame:
League will run for two weeks, or less if scoring is completed early. It begins after server reset at the end of 7th March. It concludes on server reset at the end of 21st March.

Number of competitors:
Only the first 10 to sign up below will take part. If more than ten people wish to take part then anyone is welcome to copy and paste these rules into a new thread "kill league B" and gather more players.

Restrictions:
One army per participant. No restriction on army power rating.

Scoring:
Each player must post ONE fight against each opponent in order to score. This fight may be chosen from ANY fight that takes place within the duration of the league, with the following qualifications:
1) You must be the challenger in the fight (army is on the left of the field)
2) Fights ending in MiSS1, MISS2, MISS3, BAT1, BAT2, or BAT3 do not count
Fights will be scored one point for victory and one point for each enemy kill. Friendly casualties do not matter.
To claim points a player must make a "scoring post" with the following format:
SCORING POST FOR PLAYER X
Player1: WIN 6 = 7 http://www.link-to-replay
Player2: LOSS 2 = 2 http://www.link-to-replay
Player3: ...etc.
TOTAL: 42 points 5 Wins
Where the army name of each enemy is listed, then win or loss, then the number of kills, then the total points for a fight, then a link to the replay. The last line of the post should be the total score and the number of wins.
Once posted the list may not be changed, however a partial list may be posted and then added to later.

Ranking:
At the end of the time period or when all players have selected their nine fights and made a "scoring post" then the league will be put into order. Most points wins. In the event of a tie, greater number of wins will be ranked higher. In the event of a further tie, fights will be organized!

Posting non-scoring replays:
It is permitted (and encouraged) to post replays in this thread as the competition progresses. However only those posted in a "scoring post" will be counted. 14 days of competition means each player will have a maximum of 42 fights available from which to choose their nine.

Sign up below if you are interested.

I'm thinking about making this an ongoing competition, if enough people are interested.
Here's what I'm considering at the moment - input would be welcome, especially from people that have been taking part in KLA.
Format would be 2 leagues, one for six-troopers-or-fewer and the other with no limits.
As many people as want to would be able to sign up and take part.
Each week you would put in a scoring post, with at most one fight against each other person in the league.
The league would be ranked per week, and also for the total score for the last 4 weeks. (not the total score forever, because then it'd be impossible for latecomers to join and be competitive)
Scoring would be as it is now: one point per enemy killed, and a bonus point for wining.

How does that sound?

Possible variations:
1) Scoring method would be kills minus losses. This would make it harder to get a perfect score by beating everyone.
2) Scoring fights not limited to one-per-opponent, but instead "X per week" or maybe "3 per opponent maximum, X per week total". This would allow you to choose several fights against one opponent that you can beat to cash in more points. This could lead to everyone picking on the "easy" army though, rather than encouraging everyone to attack everyone else - which is the whole point, really, to give people a reason to fight.
