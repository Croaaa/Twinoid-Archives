Alot of people don't understand what the Pilot helmet does.

"- Pilot, increases the chances of getting a vehicle by 25% every level-up."
This means if the pilot had 4% chance of using the helicopter without the skill, he will have 25% (0.25) x 4 = 1% more chance of using his helicopter after level 2. This means that by level 2 he will be at 5% chance of using the helicopter. For level 3, he will have 25% (0.25) x 5 = 1.25% more chance of using his helicopter after level 3. This means that by level 2 he will be at 6.25% chance of using the helicopter. same thing for levels 4-5-6-7-8-9-10, etc.
