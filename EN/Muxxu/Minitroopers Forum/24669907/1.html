In the spirit of having a wider choice of potential opponents to fight, here's another "challenge the person above" style game.

This one comes with a twist. When you challenge the person you need to post THREE things.

1) Your replay from the fight, win or lose

2) Your recommendation to the person you fought

3) Your type of recommendation for the person following

So, for example, if the person above you has chosen type "Book" you'd recommend a book to them, perhaps with a reason why. If you then chose your own type as "Food" then the next person to challenge would have to recommend you something tasty to eat, or perhaps something exciting and new to try.
If you can't think of something to recommend then you can't play!
