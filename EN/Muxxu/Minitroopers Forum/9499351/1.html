Hi there!

My opinions would be that for the first trooper Clown Grenade is the better option. As he has sprinter, coffee and rush the benefits from the motorcycle would not make that much difference. Zig-zag would be less useful as he will spend little time moving thanks to his speed, and fire as soon as he gets into range, so he won't get the benefit. Besides, clowns are scary. The enemy will be terrified.

For the second trooper the motorcycle would be a good option to have him into range and blast someone with his explosive shotgun. Tuck and roll is a great skill though so it's a tricky one. Depending on the rest of your squad I might go with motorcycle if you don't have many "forward" troopers.

Next time, use the [Official] thread about choosing skills.

Since you got Sprinter, I don't see much use for Motorcycle, and Zigzag will only be useful after you are in the middle of everyone. Clown Grenade might be a good thing to distract them while you do some close combat. Wait for someone's else opinion about this, because I don't know exactly the dodging properties of Zigzag, but you probably should choose this one or Clown Grenade.

About the other guy, Motorcycle will do great, he can start the battle by rushing to the enemies' side and shooting them down! Tuck'n'roll is always usefull, but I'd go with the bike.
