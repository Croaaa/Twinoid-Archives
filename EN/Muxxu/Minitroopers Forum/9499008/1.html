I wouldn't underestimate the ability of the munitions trooper. He may seem useless because all he does is refill ammo, but frankly, that's not true, the real bonus is that munitions troopers throw grenades twice as frequently.
One of my recruits is a munitions trooper and single-handedly carried the raid team 2 levels by spamming glue grenades and poison grenades (slow but deadly combination, since the poison grenade slowly razes LP and the glue grenade slows you down by a ton, by the time you get ready to fire, you're already dead, and no shot has even been fired yet).

So if you have a grenadier among your troops, munitions really is best for him.
