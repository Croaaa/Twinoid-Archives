Tunkson
You can't really rank them, Pause, because they each fulfill a different duty.

Nervous does not happen every time.
Trigger happy happens every time, and gives one extra shot.
Tail Gunner gives two extra shots, but only with bullets. (This means it won't work with 3-ammo bazookas.)

It depends on the trooper it may be assigned to. For example, for assault troopers, tail gunner may be best. For bazookas guys, trigger happy would be best. For "defenders", nervous may be better. It really depends on who is getting the choice on the skill.
