Pilots do have a higher chance of getting one as well.

But 25% higher chance doesn't mean 100% at any point in time. That's one of the reasons why pilots for main squads should never be chosen, because it's just not a guarantee that you will get a vehicle.

Imagine this:

Level 17 soldier: He'd have over 30 HP by now, I'm sure, and that's without health bonuses. This means he can take the strongest weapon in the game (infernal tube) without dying.

Level 17 comms officer: That's 16 communication points. From one guy. He alone can relay every order besides propaganda.

Level 17 medic: He'll pretty much be able to heal everyone and everything up to full health. Chances are, he'll also have savior and/or amphetamine shot.

Level 17 scout: Binoculars, sniper rifles, you name it, he'll have it.

Level 17 saboteur: 11 in sabotage. Nuff said.

Level 17 munitions: Oh, he definitely has at least two grenades by now, and perhaps also King of boules. Heck, maybe he even will have a more uncommon grenade by this time, like the clown grenade.

Level 17 spy: That's 150 initiative points he'll have when he spawns. Imagine that. There won't be any enemy army left when he gets done.


Instead, you have a level 17 pilot, and you have...nothing.
