*IMPORTANT* NOTE ON TIMES
To find out the server reset time Check the forum time at the bottom right of your screen underneath where it says "help!", or check the time-stamp when you make a post.
It is 10:10 on pop2
The server runs on European time, with the reset being at midnight. If you are not in the same time zone then you must be careful. For example "at the end of Monday" would actually be in the middle of Monday for someone in the USA.
All times quoted for deadlines in this tournament are based on server time. If you are not in the same time zone as the Muxxu/Motion Twin servers then please be extra careful.

HOW DO THE FIGHTS WORK?
Who sends the challenge
Either team may send the challenge. It is only the FIRST challenge that counts. In the event of dispute then the number at the end of the URL for reporting the fight will be checked, with the lower number having taken place first.
How to send the challenge
Click GO to start one of your daily battles. Enter the name of your opponent into the box marked "Select an army base to infiltrate:" and then click the Infiltrate button.
How to report a fight
Copy and paste the URL of the fight from your browser and post it into this thread. Please include the name of the fight from the fixture list (e.g. D, C, AE or similar) the names of both armies involved and who won. If you want to put the winner into a spoiler then that's fine, but please make sure you post who won. This will make it easier for everyone to follow the fixtures.
What doesn't count
Only challenges sent in the way described above are allowed. Fights that end in BAT1/BAT2/BAT3 or MISS1/MISS2/MISS3 are not eligible and will not be counted.

HOW OFTEN CAN I FIGHT?
Once both teams for a fixture have been confirmed, the next fight may begin at any time. It is possible that multiple fights can happen each day. There is no "start date" for any of the fights once the tournament is underway. The only constraints are that the FIRST fight is the only one that counts, and that fights must be reported before their deadline.

WHAT DO I WIN?
No prizes this time around, just pride at stake.

WHAT IF THERE IS A PROBLEM?
Please post in this thread. In the case of any dispute my ruling as tournament administrator will be final. If you have any concerns then let me know here and I will do my best to address them.

The tournament begins in a few hours so get ready and good luck everyone.
