Welcome to the Pauper's Belt.

This competition is open to any army, regardless of power rating.
Armies that are saving up their money will struggle to take part. To be successful you have to spend, spend, spend!

CONDITIONS OF PLAY
You may challenge the current belt holder as many times as you like. If you win then post the replay in this thread to make a claim.
Only "Select an army base to infiltrate:" replays count. Replays where there URL ends in BAT1, BAT2, BAT3, MISS1, MISS2 or MISS3 are not allowed.

SPECIAL RULES

1) You may only claim the belt by victory if you have 100 coins or fewer in the bank. You must include your cash balance in the post when you claim.

2) If the belt has not changed hands for seven days AND the current holder has more than 100 coins then they forfeit the belt and any other player with 100 coins or fewer may post their army link and pick up the belt.

That's all there is to it: get fighting. First pauper to post their army link below can claim the belt to start us off.
