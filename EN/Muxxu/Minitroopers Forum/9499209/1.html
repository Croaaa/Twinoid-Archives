Whether or not your troops get slaughtered depends less on their level and more on their setup compared with the enemy wave. It makes no difference if you have a cluster of level 3 troops or a cluster level 8 troops, when the rocket comes flying in, they all die the same.

That being said, the higher the troop's level, the higher chances that a few of your troops can prevent the above from happening.
