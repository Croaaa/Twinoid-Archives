I think I can shed some light into this:

I believe the number is a little vague, but the most number of participants that can be in an epic mission on your side is 32. Unlike raids, however, that go from lowest to highest level, epic missions take your own army, and then the 27 strongest recruits, and throw them into the ring against a number usually exceeding yours by 20. It doesn't matter if you have 30 recruits, or 300 recruits, only the 27 (28/26) strongest recruits under your army join you. I also have more than the amount of those that joined, but only the 26 strongest came along.

(The enemy team consists mostly of higher levels like 10+ for the first couple of waves, and then drops like the Walt Disney stock down to levels 1-5 after 2-3 waves. So in a way, it is balanced. It's just about having recruits that are strong enough to hold on to the bitter end, such as below.)
