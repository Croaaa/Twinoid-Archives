I was also wondering why I have 5 troops but only 4 of them show up in a battle?
On november 10, 2011
Tunkson
Because only 4 troops can show up at once without a scout. A scout allows one extra trooper to deploy along with the first four, meaning five deploy at the same time. But you need a scout to achieve this, otherwise only four will deploy, and in the event of the death of the first four troops, your fifth one will deploy (reinforcements).
