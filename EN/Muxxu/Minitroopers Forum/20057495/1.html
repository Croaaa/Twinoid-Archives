Xtremekiwi

Hello, and welcome to an idea that I've had to add some extra fun to the game. In this thread, you will see a number of challenges, some from me and then, if other's will want to, from other people.

Rules:

1: You must post a valid battle. The battle cannot be in MISS or BAT format.

2: The battle must fulfill the challenge requirements, otherwise it does not count.

3: No cheating.

As far as the challenges go, this is how they're gonna work:

Copy and paste the entire list to prevent confusion. If you completed a challenge, add your armies name next to the challenge you completed. If people want, though, we can do it where one person does the challenge and then it's removed from the board.

Also, just to make it fair, unless stated otherwise, you may NOT submit battles where you are over 40 power more powerful then your opponent.

Anyway, that's enough talk. Time for The Challenge Board!

1. Deadly Aim: Post a victory against any army. One of your troopers must deliver at least 4 killshots in a row.

2. Spread Fire: Post a victory against any army. One of your troopers has to kill at least three enemies with one attack. The attack may NOT be from a launcher weapon or a grenade. An assault rifle/machine gun weapon killing 3 enemies in one volley counts.

3. Counter-spy: Post a victory against any army. The enemy army has to have at least 2 spies, at least 1 with On Point and who must deploy on your side. You have to kill the spies without then dealing a single point of damage, even from friendly fire you deal.

4. Domination: Post a victory against any army. The enemy army has to have a higher power rating then your army. You may not take more than 9 points of damage overall (9 is okay, 10 is not). Healing in any way will not reset the damage limit.

5. Specialization Domination: Post a victory against the army "Dr. Frenetic". Your army must be specialized i.e. every trooper must have a specific ability. You may NOT use an army specialized in Smart, Twinoid or Frenetic.

6. Frenetic Determination: Post a victory against the army "Dr. Frenetic". Your army must be specialized in Frenetic. No power difference limit.

7. No Safety in Numbers: Post a victory against the army "Firstlab". Your army may not have more than 7 troopers.

8. Slow and Painful: Post a victory against any army. There must be a total of at least 75 points of damage dealt via poison on both sides.

9. Stun Punch: Post a victory against any army. You must kill at least one enemy with a melee attack (Punch, knife, stamp, faceboot, wrestler) while the enemy trooper is paralyzed.

10. Felling the Giants: Post a victory against the armies "Nevermore", "Caine", "971marSu". The victories must all happen within one day. If you're army IS "Nevermore", "Caine" or "971marSu", then you must beat the other 2 as well as the army "SilentPinoy" on the same day.

Begin, and have fun.

I wanted to create a challenge where you had to beat 9 top-end armies in 3 days, but I'm not that heartless.
