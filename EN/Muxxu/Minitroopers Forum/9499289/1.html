Tunkson
When dealing with main squads, the most important shot is the first one. Land the first one, and the result (1, 2, 3 dead) always turns the tide in your favor.

That said, On Point makes everything go faster. You can wipe out an entire squad in one shot with a bazooka (in particular the Infernal Tube) and On Point. And like TJ says, one shot is really all you need at times.

Between the M25 and the M1, I'd go for whatever does the most damage. The M25 does 6-12 damage, so occasionally, victims survive the shot (even more so when they have unshakable). So since rockets in main army skirmishes are pretty much a one-shot deal anyway, make as much damage as you can, as long as it has a reasonable firing time. Both of those bazookas fall under that category, making the M1 a better choice.
