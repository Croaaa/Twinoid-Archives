I originally didn't want to make a new thread for this, but the comms officer thread where I would have put this in happens to be locked. I have met the requirements and seen it activated on more than one occasion (twice in the same raid phase, to be honest).

I am just hereby confirming the requirements to use Propaganda:

1) At least 20 in communications. This means as many as 4 novice comms officers.
2) Any trooper. Doesn't necessarily have to be the comms officer himself.
3) The trooper must be within arm's length to the enemy trooper.

To those not in the know, Propaganda is the highest order a Comms Officer can relay. It converts an enemy trooper and turns it into a friendly trooper. It's a relatively rare order, given the high amount of communication required. Even when you do have enough in communications, the above, specifically point 3, needs to come into effect before using propaganda is an option. This makes Propaganda the only order that cannot be executed from anywhere on the battlefield.

(Note that if the enemy trooper has any sabotaged weapons, the weapons will not becomes fixed once the trooper is converted. Also note that it is possible to convert an enemy troop even if he is the only enemy still standing. In that case, converting said enemy troop results in an instant victory and the enemy-turned-allied troop runs off in victory alongside your guys.)
