Scouts are bad if:
You plan on leveling that trooper up a lot, as they don't have level-dependent bonus
Your army all stand in a clump and get hit by rockets, grenades, or heavy burst fire

Scouts are good if:
You have lots of high-initiative troops and can deploy more of them on the field to deal more damage quickly
Binoculars suits your army, and you are lucky enough to get it

Yesterday I got two troopers (4th and 5th) to level 6, and was given Comms. Officer and Scout for both.
I planned doing some kinda discussion up here before deciding, but I ended by choosing 2 x Comms. Officer.

Scouts are great for meatshield-like armies, outnumbering the enemy and getting in close combat. But I prefer keeping my team together, aiding each other. It's not very safe doing this when you got 7+ deployment, since a zooker can end the battle in one shot.
My plan now is to create a secondary team, which deploys afterwards, not altogether with the first one. So far I have only one man out of the first wave (a Heavy Machine Gun Saboteur), which pretty much gets only the remaining pistol/shotgun folks, since long-ranged soldiers get out of ammo while fighting my first wave. I don't know if I made the best choice (no tanks appeared so far, so I'm not regretting anything), but at least I've got some neat Comms. benefits now.
