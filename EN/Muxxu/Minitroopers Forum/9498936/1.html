AK47= [Damage: 2-3, Range: 2-7, Critical: x10, Bursts: 4, Aim: 100%, Recovery: 100
Capacity: 12 balls, refill maximum : 16 balls]

-- Vs--

UMP = [Damage: 3-4, Range: 2-6, Critical: x20, Bursts: 3, Aim: 100%, Recovery: 80
Capacity: 9 balls, refill maximum : 18 balls]

----------------------------------------

Burst x Damage
AK47: 4(2-3) = 8-12 damage
UMP: 3(3-4) = 9-12 damage
Winner: UMP

Burst x Capacity
AK47: 12 rounds X 4 round bursts = 3 attacks before reloading
UMP: 9 rounds X 3 round bursts = 3 attacks before reloading
Draw

Other comparisons:
Range: AK47
Critical: UMP
Recovery: UMP
Maximum Ammo: UMP
Aim: Draw

All in all the only thing the AK47 has over the UMP is that extra little bit on the maximum range. But that extra shot in the burst isn't anything to scoff at either when you consider it's an extra chance to hit, crit and kill should the first 3 shots all miss (althoguh successive shots apparently get a cumulative penalty to hit, or so the Compensater skill leads me to suspect).

If given a choice between the two, I'd take UMP for sure, but if the AK47 comes up before the UMP and without the option of UMP during the same level-up, then unless (one of) my other option(s) is something I can't live without then I'd definitely lean towards taking the AK47. (Parentheticals in this paragraph are for those troops with the Smart ability.)
