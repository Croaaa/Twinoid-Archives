Welcome to a "build a new army" knockout cup competition.

Players will have one month to build an army that meets the criteria listed below, then a single-elimination knockout cup (first challenge sent by either player is binding) will decide the winner.
The draw for opponents will be conducted 23rd February. The fighting will begin the first Monday of the following month, i.e. 4th March.

To take part you must post a link to your army in this thread on or before the 15th of February, which means you have two weeks to settle on an army that you're happy with. Any entries after this time will not be permitted to take part. Any army that does not meet the criteria listed below will be eliminated by default.

Criteria for army building:

Complete armies must have six troopers, with restrictions as below.
Total level count for all troopers combined must not exceed 36.
(e.g. six troopers at level 6, OR four at 6 + one at 5 + one at 7, or any possible six-trooper permutation)
Troopers must be given the names below, to make it easier to check compliance with the conditions.
The order of troopers and names does not have to match that given, so long as each name is included once and the trooper with that name matches all criteria.
If your only level-up options would break one or more of the criteria but you choose it anyway then your army will be eliminated by default, so plan carefully.

Trooper One: Gorman
Must only have a pistol as a shooting weapon. No other guns allowed! No rockets allowed! Knife is permitted.

Trooper Two: Drake
Must have a minigun/HMG/commanche as the favourite weapon.
No rocket-type weapons permitted.

Trooper Three: Vasquez
Must have a minigun/HMG/commanche as the favourite weapon.
No rocket-type weapons permitted.

Trooper Four: Hudson
Must have one of the following as favourite weapon: basic assault rifle, UMP, AK-47, FAMAS, Thompson
No rocket-type weapons permitted.

Trooper Five: Hicks
Must have one of the following as favourite weapon: basic shotgun, double-barrel shotgun
No rocket-type weapons permitted.

Trooper Six: Apone
Must not be a spy.

Interested? Then start working on your army now. Posting that you're taking part, even if you're not posting up a link to your army just yet, is encouraged.
If you want to make an army under one of my armies then I won't mind But you are free to recruit under whoever you like and wear whichever colour uniform you fancy.

Begin!
