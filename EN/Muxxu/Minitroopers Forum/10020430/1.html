This is the sign up thread for May's seeded double-elimination tournament.

If you want to know how things work then go check out the March Madness or April Clash threads, where you'll be able to see the rules of engagement as well as quite a few decent fights.
If, however, you're more interested in ACTION than reliving past glories then you'll want to be signing up below.

To sign up, post "I sign up!" and a link to the HQ page of your army.

Restrictions are: two armies per player maximum

Sign-ups will run until the server reset at the end of 28th of April, at which point they will close and seedings will be set. Seedings will be based on standing in the Continuous Championship at the time when sign-ups close.

So what you WANT to do is: sign up below, then spend the next 17 days trying to get as FAR up the CC rankings as possible, to improve your chances when the tournament gets underway.

Discussion on possible improvements to the way that the tournament operates should be posted in this thread too. Don't take offence if I disregard any suggestions - the rules at the moment are designed to give everyone a fair shot while still allowing me to do the admin in between my real-life commitments. Suggestions that complicate things don't stand much chance of being incorporated unless they are stunningly superb. I've got an open ear though, and will give everything due consideration.
Oh, suggestions from people that haven't signed up will be ignored
