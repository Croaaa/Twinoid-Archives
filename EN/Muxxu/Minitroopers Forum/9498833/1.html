Alright so if you could change anything about mini-troopers what would you? Personally there are a few things I would like to see change:
1. Allow us to do more then 3 fights a day. Make it like mybrute were you can do up to 10 if you continue winning and 3 if you loose. This would encourage people to play smarter with there armies and try different combinations in order to lockdown a good strategy for there army in order to get more wins and tokens. Only being able to fight 3 times doesnt give us alot of chances to experiment with our armies to see how we can use everyone effectively.

2. Get rid of the pupil system. The reason it was not put in the second version of mybrute is because people were cheating and using devices or programs to make tons of pupils for there guys and leveling up super quick. This is going to happen in mini-troopers and then the game is going to be ruined because those of us that play legit will always be fighting these armies with crazy levels and numbers of soldiers. This ties into #1 as well because if they did this they would need to up the number of fights we could do in order to allow us to get enough points to build our armies/ level up without it being at a slugish pace.

3. Have tournaments and rankings for armies to make things more competitive and fun. It would be cool to see other armies fight in tournaments and see what abilities people tend to lean towards ect. Also you would gain Tokens for wins and 1st, 2nd and 3rd place could get chunks of tokens (For example maybe 1st place wins 25-50 2nd 15-25 and 3rd 5-15) and would raise your rep for the winner.

4. Have an option to challenge other armies when you go to there page could say something like "Declare War?". Also building on this could have options when challenging other armies could wager money on it.
