KILL LEAGUE CHARLIE

Concept:
Rolling league competition. Points are scored for kills as well as for victories. An army may still score if it performs well even if it is overwhelmed by a superior foe.

Ranking:
There will be a weekly rank and a 4-weekly rank.

Commitment:
From five fights per week upwards, at player discretion. Using fewer fights per week is permitted but may affect your score.

Restrictions:
One army per participant. No restriction on army power rating.

Scoring:
You may choose from ANY match against other league members to score, provided:
1) You must be the challenger in the fight (army is on the left of the field)
2) Fights ending in MiSS1, MISS2, MISS3, BAT1, BAT2, or BAT3 do not count
3) You must not have claimed against that opponent the previous week.
4) The fight is from the current week
5) All fights are against different opponents
Fights will be scored two points for victory and one point for each enemy killed. The number of your own troopers lost does not matter.
To claim points a player must make a "scoring post" with the following format:
SCORING POST FOR PLAYER X
Player1: WIN 6 = 7 http://www.link-to-replay
Player2: LOSS 2 = 2 http://www.link-to-replay
Player3: ...etc.
TOTAL: 42 points
Where the army name of each enemy is listed, then win or loss, then the number of kills, then the total points for a fight, then a link to the replay. The last line of the post should be the total score claimed.
Once posted the list may not be changed.
Up to five fights may be claimed for each week.

What counts as a "kill" for scoring?
Only people that are "dead" at the end of the fight score.
If someone is removed from the field with Savior then you cannot score for them, as they are still fit to fight.
If you kill someone with survivor but they get up again, then you cannot score for them unless you kill them again.
If the enemy has a spy that does not deploy then you cannot score for them.
If the enemy has a jet which deploys then you cannot score for it unless you kill it!
If you kill someone with survivor and the fight ends before they use their skill then you CAN score for them.

Posting non-scoring replays:
It is permitted (and encouraged) to post replays in this thread as the competition progresses. However only those posted in a "scoring post" will be counted.

How do I join?
Post a link to your army HQ page along with the comment "I sign up!"
If you wish to leave the league then post a link to your army HQ page along with the comment "I quit the league!"
Players that leave the league will forfeit all their points and may not join again for a minimum of two weeks.

When must I have my scoring claim entered each week?
The deadline is the server reset at the end of each Saturday. Time will be reckoned by the time-stamp on your forum post. You may claim at any time during the week, however if you do not make a claim post in time then you will score zero for the week.

What if I have a question or a rules issue?
Post here in this thread and I will make a decision. My ruling is final in any matters of dispute.

How do I know who I can fight against?
I'll post a list of active competitors periodically in this thread. I'll also be posting the rankings for the league each week, once I've recorded the scores. This may take a day or two sometimes.

When does it begin?
Sign-up is active. Week one begins on Sunday April 1st 2012.
