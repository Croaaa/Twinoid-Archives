Hacksaw
You can see your last ten recruits in the History page.

You can see up to ten recruits to going to the page of any of your recruits and changing "/hq" to "/ranks" to see your top ten recruits (if the recruit selected isn't in the top ten he always appears at the bottom as an 11th).

Ranks page only helps, though, if your last 10 recruits are not among your ten most powerful.

But the only way to see all your recruits at once is to bookmark them as you get them.
