Ooh. I just realized I never mentioned a possible breakdown in the brackets.

What I had in mind was something like below.

Bracket 1: 100 +
Bracket 2: 75 - 99
Bracket 3: 50 - 74
Bracket 4: 5 - 49

The ultimate goal I have in mind is to separate the monster armies from the weaker ones so everyone has a chance to find success in the early stages of the tournament. I remember when I first started participating in the Knockout Cups and dreading going up against an army with a power differential advantage over mine of 20 points or more. When you reach monster sizes (which I consider anything above 110 or nine or more troopers), the rating isn't as meaningful.

When you are smaller, a difference of 20 points can mean the chances of victory being very slim for you if you're at the lower end of that power differential. For instance, with nine troopers, I was able to beat armies with 10 or 11 troopers in my Continuous Championship bouts. When I had five or six troopers, though, things tended to end badly for me if I faced an army with just an additional trooper.
