The Idea
This is a knockout tournament. Entrants will be seeded, which is to say put into ranking order before the start. This way the bigger armies won't meet until later in the tournament.
It is also a double-elimination tournament. This means that you have to lose twice to be knocked out. If you are beaten once then you will be matched against another army that has been beaten. If you keep winning then you can still win, however if you get beaten for a second time then you are out.
For a more thorough explanation of how this works you may like to check Wikipedia:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Double-elim ination_tournament

Who can take part?
Anyone can take part. You may enter as many armies as you wish, if you are lucky enough to have more than one army which you think is in good fighting order. There are no maximum or minimum power levels, no limits on how many or how few troopers your army can have.

How are the seedings to be done?
Firstly, entrants will be ranked according to their position in the Continuous Championship. The higher you are ranked the better your chances of getting a favourable draw will be, so it is in your interests to try and get as high in the CC as you can this week. Any entrants who are not in the CC will then be ranked in order of power (however CC members will be ranked above them regardless of power)
To determine the ranking in the CC I will check the last post in the CC thread before server reset on Saturday.

How long are sign-ups open for?
Sign-ups will close at server reset at the end of Saturday 25th February, or when we have 64 entrants, whichever comes first.

When does the competition begin?
After the server reset at the end of Monday 27th February.

When is the server reset?
Check the forum time at the bottom right of your screen underneath where it says "help!", or check the time-stamp when you make a post.
It is 05:56 on pop3The server runs on European time, with the reset being at midnight. If you are not in the same time zone then you must be careful. For example "at the end of Saturday" would actually be in the middle of Saturday for someone in the USA.

Are there prizes?
Yes. Doomys has kindly offered the winner a prize of four recruits and the runner up a consolation prize of two recruits. If anyone else wants to add to this and make it really worth fighting for then step right up!

How will the cup work?
I'll post up a fixture list before the cup begins. For each match, either player may challenge. It is the FIRST challenge only that counts. Each fight has a number, as seen in the URL, so we will be able to check up in the event of any disagreement.
Only direct-challenge fights will count. Infiltration missions are not counted.
Once the fight is over a link to the replay should be posted in this thread to confirm the result. You must also post the name of who won - the administrator may not have time to watch every fight each day!

When can the next fight happen?
Once both armies for the next round are confirmed they may fight immediately. You don't have to wait until the next day.

What if a fight doesn't take place?
Each round of fixtures will have a "latest due" date. If neither army has posted a suitable replay to claim the fight by that time then the match is scored as a double forfeit and neither team advances. In this case BOTH teams are eliminated, there is no second chance. Make sure you don't fall behind on your fixtures.

If I have another question what should I do?
Post it in this thread and I'll try to answer it for you as best I can. In the event of any problems or disagreements during the tournament my ruling will be final. I'm a pretty reasonable guy though so don't worry about that too much.

Where can I see the fixtures?
They won't be drawn up until sign-ups are complete, then they will be posted here. I will most likely make a new thread so that they can be in the first post and nice and easy to find.

Where do I sign up?
Right here! Post your army name and a link to your HQ page. If you want to brag about your chances then that's fine too.
Good luck to everyone!
