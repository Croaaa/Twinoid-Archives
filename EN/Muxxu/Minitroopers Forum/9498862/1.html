timpieh
Standard = what your trooper has been doing so far
Defender = stay in the back and use long range shots
Hunter = attacker, offensive, more aggressive, runs in and shoots 'em at shorter range

At least, that's what I think it does . So if you want to bring the fight to your opponent's side, use hunters, if you want your trooper to stay back and attack from afar, or wait on your side for the opponents to come to you, use defenders.
