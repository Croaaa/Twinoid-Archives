Just for a bit of fun, here's something to do next time your daily mission is Exterminate.
(this might encourage people to enjoy the fight against the furry hordes a bit more, rather than just going click-click-click-awwww at the three red boxes)

PART ONE: PREDICTION
Nominate one of your troopers as head rat catcher. This should be the trooper that you think will get the most kills.

PART TWO: ACCOUNTANCY zzzzz
Start the fight. Keep track of how many kills each of your troopers score. This may require pencil and paper.
Any rat that is made dead counts as a kill. If it then stands up as a survivor and dies again, that can be counted as another kill.

PART THREE: ANNOUNCEMENT
Post your totals for all your troopers on here so that the rest of us can nod wisely and pretend that we knew that was going to happen.
Mark up who you'd picked as your head rat catcher too, so we can see how good a judge of your army you are.
If you want to include a link to the fight too then by all means do so, even though it won't persist.
You can use any fight, 1 2 or 3, but only one per day as a maximum otherwise things will get out of control.

Anyone up for it? Form an orderly queue.
