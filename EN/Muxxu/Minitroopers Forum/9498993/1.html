green number with the wrench is the percentage of troops who have at least 1 weapon sabotaged. blue number is the communications points (from coms officers) the two numbers on the left are your total troops and the amount you will deploy each deployment (affected by scouts)

saboteurs don't necessarily sabotage all of someone's weapons, they can do one or two or anything. The "smart" ability makes you 50% resistant to saboteurs.
