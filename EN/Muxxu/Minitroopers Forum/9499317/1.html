MagicDragon
I guess its my turn to share my thoughts.

Hand Guns:
I happen to like the revolver most. A close second in terms of preference are dual pistols.

Shotguns:
I like the pump action shotgun the best. The automatic reloading feature makes it a deadly and effective weapon in the hands of a skilled trooper. It is my hope that my smart, frenetic spy gains it as a weapon.

Sniper Rifles:
I have to go with the CK Magellan. It's rate of fire is rapid and it is one of the more accurate weapons in the game. One of my recruits has it and he can take down multiple enemies in short order with it equipped. My second favorite behind it has to be the MOS-TEK. It has a high damage potential and its rate of fire isn't too shabby. In fact, I happen to think all the other sniper rifles are totally worthless in comparison.

Rocket Launchers:
The Infernal Tube is one I like. It isn't all that great a weapon compared to other, however when it hits it can take out multiple enemies easily.

Machine Guns:
I'd have to go with the mini-gun. I think it's a great weapon and in the hand of one of my spies, it can be a real killer. He's taken out multiple enemies (as well as allies at times, unfortunately) with a couple of bursts. It also has decent recovery.

Assault Rifles:
I happen to like the AK-47 and FAMAS the most of all the assault rifles. Of all the weapon classes, this the only one with a starter weapon that isn't completely fucking useless (I am looking at you pistol, shotgun and sniper rifle).

Hand to Hand Techniques:
I have to go with Faceboot. While the knife is a good weapon and immune to sabotage, I simply have to go with the graphic of this technique. I simply find it a bit of a visceral thrill to watch my troopers kick their opponents away like they're nothing.

Vehicles:
I have to go with the light tank. It's great whenever one of my recruits get to use it. I just wish I had one for my main army. The helicopter is another good one as well. I have no experience with the heavy tank so I can't comment on it at all.
