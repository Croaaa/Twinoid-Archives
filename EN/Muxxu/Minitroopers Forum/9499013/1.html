Look, I know you're just trying to be helpful and all, but this has nothing to do with the links.
Anything to do with a specific army is on the [armyname].minitroopers.com subdomain.
This army has a period in its name, which is an illegal character in root-level URLs. Spaces are somewhat similar, though they're escaped as dashes here. Periods aren't, apparently, so the army is inaccessible, by any means.
