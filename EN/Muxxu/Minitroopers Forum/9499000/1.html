Welcome to Recruit of the Liver.

In this thread we organise ourselves into pacts.
Each pact is made up of 3 players.
Each day the players in the pact create one recruit for each of the other 2 members in their pact.

This repeats each day until one of the players finds that they are not receiving the two recruits that they expected. The day that this happens the pact breaks.

When a pact breaks the players may return to this 'Liver thread' and look for a new pact.

- You may exit at any time,
- You may choose not to form a pact with specific players,
- You may find recruits from any other sources as well.

Pacts are intended to last more than one day. If you have any questions about pacts or about the liver structure please see... Recruit of the liver? : http://muxxu.com/forum/thread/949421
Try not to post questions in this thread, thanks
