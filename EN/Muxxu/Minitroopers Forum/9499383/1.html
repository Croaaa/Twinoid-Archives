Go to the order page, e.g. http://army-of-the-shar.minitroopers.com /choice
(if you don't have more than four troopers in your army then you'll have to type it in as the button won't appear)

When the page appears, zoom WAY out.
Hold ctrl and keep pressing - is one way to do this.
Eventually, when your troopers appear really tiny, your recruits will show up down the bottom. You can click on them to get a display of their gear, along with a link to their army.
