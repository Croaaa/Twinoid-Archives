A new belt competition, with a twist.
(so a MÃ¶bius belt, then?)

Here are the rules.

1) OVERDOG RULE
If the challenger has army power rating equal to or higher to the holder then they must LOSE the match to claim the belt.

2) WORTHY OPPONENT RULE
If the challenger has army power rating lower than the holder then they must post two replays, a WIN and a LOSS, to claim the belt.

3) CLARITY RULE
When claiming the belt the power levels of both armies at the time of the contest must be posted along with the replay(s).

4) PERSISTENCE RULE
Only infiltration fights count: BAT1/2/3/4/5 or MISS1/2/3 replays are not permitted.

5) OPPORTUNIST RULE
Any valid replay(s) may be used whether the claimant is on the left (challenge sender) or the right (challenge receiver), but must have reference numbers higher than the last fight used to claim this belt, i.e. they happened after the belt last changed hands.

5) COWARD'S WAY OUT RULE
The belt holder may choose to surrender the belt at any time, in which case the next person to post in this thread may pick it up.

6) TOO BIG TO FAIL RULE
If the belt does not change hands for seven days then the belt holder automatically surrenders the belt and a different player must pick the belt up.

First person to post a link to their army and their current power rating may pick the belt up, and we'll see how this works out.
Go!
