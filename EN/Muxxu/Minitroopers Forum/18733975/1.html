I don't think that taking a poll is a good idea. The people voting in the poll may have nothing to do with what they are voting about. Anyone can go into the thread and click on voting buttons without being part of that belt/tournament or without even playing minitroopers.

Here is my opinion about reusing battle replays.
Firsly, Karry did not break any rules.
Karry found a way to make the most of the win because the rules currently do not state anything about this.
However I think that we should state something about this in the rules for future players (regardless of what we state).

Personality I feel that battle replays should not be reusable.
I feel this why because of the 'cost' of the attack.
In this case HongKong had to spend two atacks taking these potions.
If HongKong's army had the power to take back both potions from Karry using only one attack then great, do that ...but to do this HK would need to be online at the right time.
When HK does come online Karry may no longer hold both potions, the cost of getting back both potions gos up. This would just be a technicality, it's not a sporting thing.

If we say... "may the best army win" then we should be trying to create a sporting environment.
However if we say replays are reusable then we are favoring the most active players over what could be the better armies just because these players are online at the right time. Active players would be favored over players who have busy jobs or maybe a family to take care of or what ever.

Also the alternative is really boring...
Imagine an active player refreshing their browser, looking over the same web page again and again waiting to see a usable combination of names/posts.
I would like to promote sporting behavior over active players.


karryF.E. Boxer Klitschko holds all the belts of all boxing organizations. When someone beats him in the match, he winnigs all belts from him. Yes but both fighters would have known that before the fight started. Winning all the belts was the intention not a coincidence.

Do I get the gold medal for winning the race? No, because I did not have a number on my back. Winning the race was not my intention unlike the boxing match.

If players really do want to allow battle replays to be reused then I would ask that we at least restrict it to the one game tag, [Belt]. If players were to combin a [Belt] win with any other ...say... a [Ladder] or [Cup] win then it would seem really weird to me.
