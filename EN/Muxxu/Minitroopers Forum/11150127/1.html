My favorite unit? It's actually not a spy, it's my Comms. Officer "Monk". There's always an air about him when he get's in, whether he's called in for back-up from Ranger's Talky-Walky or rushes in from a Reinforcements call. He comes in headstrong and rushes in rambo style and starts havoc. In being my most agile unit, there are so many moments where Monk is the hero by dodging bullets and taking on impossible odds. It's even more epic when Monk has no weapons and suddenly he kicks down the opposition. It's like Monk is a spy, but comes in mid wave and from my side.
-
My least favorite unit? Barbarian. I don't hate any my units by any means, everyone brings something to the table. It's just with Barbarian I expected something more. He's agile, but carries nothing but heavy weapons. He has useful equipment and good survival, but hardly does anything in the fray. Maybe I'll hope for a useful ability or vehicle on his next level up, coming soon.

My fave trooper is Roland, the first trooper in my army and it's CO. He is currently at lvl 13 (the highest lvl in my army atm). I use him as CK-Magellan sniper.


His full equipment and skills are: lvl 13 munitions trooper, CK-Magellan, Sniper, Wrestler, Hydroshock Shells, Thermos of Coffee, Smart, Cold Blooded, Eye of the Tiger, Nimble Fingers, Reverse Attack, Huge Calves, Sturdy, Light Tank;


My army specializes in eliminating rat infestations (And we offer good discounts too btw). Anyway, Roland deals massive damage with his CK-Magellan. With fast reloads and a little speed boost that he has he can move quite well in the back of the main force and deliver fast and accurate killshots. His best results in fighting those hairy rat buggers come when he works in conjunction with my other munitions trooper Beowulf, who carries a Rocket Launcher. They're usually the last two men standing.


Sometimes I wish I could clone them both and have an all munitions army with nothing but CK-Magellan and Rocket Launcher operators.
