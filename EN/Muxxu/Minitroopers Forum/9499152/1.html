Repost of a wall of text. :3
Army colours are not dictated by a recruiter, as each one offers 3 colours to choose from, the assortment being actually based on the recruiter's colour. For example, my olive-coloured army recruited navy (10), tan (10), and olive (6). Another navy-coloured army recruited purple (5), olive (4), and navy (2). As a matter of fact, if you examine several recruitment pages, any playable armies offer different colours for 5 troopers: the recruiter's colour in the middle, and 2 each of 2 adjustment tones to the sides. The colourations are ordered in a basic circling line, from left to right: ... -> Brick -> Orange -> Tan -> Olive -> Navy -> Purple -> Brick -> ... Non-playable armies, yes, offer only one colour. Although, it's more important to choose best starting skills, not colourations.
