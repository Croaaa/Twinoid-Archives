If you don't have 4 pieces of equipments and don't have the button to swap them, you can use a rude trick.
-> Go to the trooper's stat sheet.
-> Highlight the "Upgrade for <price>" button.
-> Copy the link.
-> Paste the link into the address bar.
-> Replace "levelup" with "equip".
-> Hit "Enter".
I have to say that it doesn't affect troopers with 2 or 3 pieces, as they can't be sorted in slots, but maybe it can help. Can't really tell if it's the equipment that isn't worn, or the skill that isn't actually acquired. Just try it and see if it works.
