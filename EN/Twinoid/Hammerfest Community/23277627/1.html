Queensalis Rockfaller Journey stands with the mechanics of Rockfaller from Arkadeo and is not a match-3 game. Candy Crush could pretty much be called a Bejeweled rip-off if you wanted.

The actual match-style gameplay isn't the same as Candy Crush, but the overall game is very obviously made to copy the style of King's games. The map, the three stars based on score, the way each level ends with all of your bombs going off, begging friends for lives, the life system itself that charges far quicker than most Twinoid games, paying to continue after you lose, it's all based on the King model. Even the name, "Rockfaller Journey" seems like it's emulating King's Saga games ("Candy Crush Saga", "Farm Heroes Saga", etc.). So no, Rockfaller isn't like Candy Crush as far as the core gameplay goes, but it is like Candy Crush in the same way that Diamond Digger Saga is like Candy Crush.
