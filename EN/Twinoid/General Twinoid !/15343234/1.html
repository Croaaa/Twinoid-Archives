Rise to the challenge this weekend!

From Friday @18h00 until Saturday @12h00, be the best on Binary
From Saturday @12h00 until Sunday @12h00, amaze with your aptitude for Alchimie
From Sunday @12h00 until Monday @12h00, fight for first place on Flushee

To take part, all you need to do is click on the icon on the Games page of KadoKado.
