I'll add a few of my own:

- Menus are not z-indexed properly, the borders on the Back/Reply/Follow buttons (and other buttons) have a higher z-index than the menus. This is probably due to the fact that the outline property is used. (Borders are better.)

- Back/Reply/Follow and other buttons have different backgrounds depending on where they are. This can be confusing to the viewer, and is an odd stylistic choice IMO.

- "Chercher utilisateur" is still in French, "Search" would be a proper replacement.

- Buttons appearing on hover is somewhat of a mixed case, some people like it and some people won't. It's not as accessible on older browsers as well, and the features (Share/Quote/Edit) might be missed by users.

- Twinoid updates constantly show "New" even if you have read them.

- On the Muxxu /go/ forum links, they don't function properly in terms of menus. Since most of the page is an iFrame, the top menus drop behind the iFrame.

Just some critiques of my own, thanks for reading!
