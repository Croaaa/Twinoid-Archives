Since I liked to language hop a lot in the past, I made something to easily toggle between them

I assume you prefer to have an English interface, so copy this link into your shortcuts bar
Clicking that link will redirect you to the same page, but in English
If a game has separate servers, then the link will redirect you to the equivalent page on the English server
There's an unfixable bug with forum links, so keep that in mind


And here's how language settings work
Twinoid itself has a language setting, but it's "soft"
Multilingual games also have a language setting, and it's "hard"
(Games that are available in only a single language or have language-separated servers also have a "hard" language setting)

The "hard" settings will override whatever your previous language setting was on the main Twinoid site (this is why it's a "soft" setting)
But you can add a language flag to any Twinoid URL in order to make the language setting a "hard" one
So say you're looking at a Twinoid group
http://twinoid.com/g/group-name would load the page with whatever language you were recently looking at, while http://twinoid.com/en/g/group-name would always be in English
