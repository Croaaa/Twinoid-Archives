It is confusing if you haven't been around long enough (or not paying attention), but let me explain


Motion-Twin is a Scop based in Bordeaux, France. They are the company that makes/made all the other things you mentioned

Twinoid is a set of centralized systems (accounts, forums, etc.) that MT developed in order to make it easier for people to play more of their games. Any new games from MT are highly likely to use Twinoid as a base, so you don't have to go through a manual registration process when they become available to the general public

The Nexus is a feed of stuff from you and all the people you add as contacts. I refer to the stuff on someone's Twinoid profile as their "personal Nexus feed", but that's just me. Nexus posts can be automatically posted by games (or Twinoid itself), or a person can post whatever they want. Custom messages can be flagged as "private", so you can discreetly mention something to only Twinoid friends

Muxxu is a game portal that was established before Twinoid. It is similar to Twinoid in certain ways, but games on Muxxu have a deeper integration with Muxxu's centralized systems (it's possible that Twinoid will become more like Muxxu in this aspect). Unlike Twinoid, Muxxu games have "region locking", so someone with an English account can only play English games. No new games will be added to Muxxu, as far as I can tell

The Fluxx was the precursor to the Nexus. Since it was a thing that was only on Muxxu, the posts were limited to events on Muxxu and it's games (plus custom messages)
