Hello, and welcome to the Twinoid forums! By reading this thread, you will learn what you need to do to keep the forums an enjoyable place for everyone.

These rules apply to both General Twinoid and the Coffee Shop. It may apply to game-specific forums, but only if these rules are explicitly referenced in the rules thread for that forum.

In addition, please read the Terms and Conditions! By creating a Twinoid account, you have already agreed to them.
Things you should do

Stay on topicIf you want to discuss something unrelated to the topic of a thread, make a new one! Respect other playersThe forums aren't a place for you to ridicule or tease players. Keep yourself in check whenever someone says something you don't agree with. Properly write postsThe forums are not a chat room, there is plenty of time to write out a proper response. Light use of well-known acronyms is acceptable. Write in EnglishThese forums are for English-speakers. If you are not able to write in English on your own, please use a translator before you submit your post. Use the search functionIf you want to talk about something, make sure that a thread about that subject doesn't already exist. Use the Q&A thread to ask simple questionsIn some games, there is an official thread specifically for asking questions. Sometimes, your question might spark an interesting discussion, but don't assume that. Making a thread for an easily-answered question clutters up the forum. Respect the administratorsMotion-Twin is a small company, so the administrators have a wide variety of tasks to deal with. epoq, your main English administrator, is fairly open to player interaction, so give him the respect he deserves! Respect the moderatorsThe moderators are volunteer staff, so don't give them too much grief! Moderators are very willing to listen, and help deal with issues. However, moderators don't have any powers in-game, so they can't help solve every issue you have. Things you shouldn't do

Act like a moderatorThis is not helpful in any way. If you aren't a moderator, don't clutter up a thread with a useless comment about what "should" be done.

If you feel a post is inappropriate, please block it by clicking on the red ! (this button is located at the bottom of a post when you hover over it). A moderator should deal with the problem. Have a chat on the forumsDo not use the forums as a chat room. If you want to idly chat about whatever crosses your mind, please use the Private Messenger or post on your Nexus. Post spam"Spam" is not just one kind of post, it's an umbrella term for several things that are unacceptable. Here are some examples of spam, please note that this is not a comprehensive list:

Excessive use of smilies
Making an excessive amount of posts/replies
Posting nonsense
Posting something completely unrelated to the topic/forum Revive an old thread (necroposting)Old threads do not auto-lock after a certain amount of time, so refrain from making a reply to a thread where the conversation ended naturally. In most cases, there isn't a good reason for you to reply to a thread after several weeks of silence. If a moderator thinks you needlessly revived an old topic, your reply may be deleted. Post private or personal informationIf you know sensitive information, do not post it in the forums. Some examples of this would be:

Verbatim chat logs
Information from closed beta tests
Personal information about a user Promote methods to exploit Twinoid or the games on itIf you have found a means to exploit Twinoid or a game on the network, please report it via the technical support site. Do not promote the use of the glitch, program, script, etc. that lets you do things in a way that the developers did not intend. Post advertisements or referral linksDon't make threads that are blatant advertisements for anything. Certain special threads in the Coffee Shop are partially exempt from this rule. These threads are designed to let users showcase their artistic talent.

Please note that this rule applies to Motion-Twin's games, as well! The only exception is MiniTroopers, but it is only a partial exception. Please read the next section. Post your MiniTroopers linkFor more detailed information, please read the sticky thread about this subject in the MiniTroopers forum. In short, do not post your MiniTroopers link in any forum but the MiniTroopers forum.
