If you have an internet-ready smartphone, you can now log in and access all the forums, newsfeeds and Twinoid messenger by simply entering the address we all know: www.twinoid.com

The games are not compatible at the moment, but at least this way you can keep in touch with the community and stay up-to-date when you're out and about.
