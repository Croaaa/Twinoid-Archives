This is why they (usually) lock when you are playing. For example, in AlphaBounce if you start a game, it'll lock and you can't open/click anything on the top bar.

If you want you could also do this:
Profile >> Interface Options >> Uncheck "floating" bar

Then it won't scroll down with your window, it'll stay at the top of the page, which might help on some games.
