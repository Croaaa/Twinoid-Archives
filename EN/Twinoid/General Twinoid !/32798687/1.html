Here is an edited machine translation of a section of the French T&Cs, which talks about your information:
7 . Confidentiality
In order to operate and provide the service, we collect certain information about you. We protect this information.

You are informed that your personal data may be disclosed to third parties to meet legal or regulatory requirements. In addition, information about you may be used to act in good faith when such access or disclosure appears necessary to protect the personal safety of employees of Motion Twin, customers or the public.

Personal data collected by Motion Twin can be stored and used in France or in another country in which Motion Twin has established its servers. No use of your data will be carried out for commercial purposes without the express consent of the User.

Some information about you may be disclosed to our service to execute a useful technique for the operation of the Service.

To provide you with the Service and improve it, we may collect certain information about Service performance, your use of the Service and your equipment.

Motion Twin is committed to the safety of the collection, use and retention of your personal information and data and agrees not to distribute, broadcast or transmitted in any form or release your information to affiliates, resellers, suppliers or other third parties.

In accordance with the French law nº 78-17 of 6 January 1978 relating to data, files and freedoms, amended by law nº 2004-801 of 6 August 2004 the French territory, the users have rights to access, oppose (art. 38), query (art. 39), and correction / deletion (art. 40) of personal data concerning them. You can exercise your rights by contacting us by mail, indicating your full name, email address and home address and proof of identity . Applications must be sent to the address indicated above.
