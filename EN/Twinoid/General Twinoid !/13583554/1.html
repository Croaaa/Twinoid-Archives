Sometimes there's misconceptions and confusion about things that noobs and sometimes even the pros have. This thread is here to solve those problems by telling you about things you may not know or have noticed. It can also double as a help thread, so if you have questions, post 'em here. I'd advise not to post entire detailed Survival Guides because those are boring. Instead, I advise to post a few sentence long tips, tricks, and answers to frequently asked questions.
I'll start.

The Workshop upgrades decrease the used for building cost, not the used for converting items.
Zombies die of loneliness or attrition. As noted in the Die2nite wiki, "The way attrition works is 1 zombie will die over night for every 2 zombies that were killed in the zone during the day, this allows you to clear zones more efficiently by leaving the last zombies to die from attrition."
Traveling for 11 zones in the desert without drinking water causes you to become Thirsty.
Traveling for 11 more zones while thirsty will result in you becoming Dehydrated.
Traveling another more 11 zones while Dehydrated will cause you to die.

MadDonut

If you ever just have to build something, at least use any extra to convert items at the workshop to make up for it.
Shunning is generally a bad idea unless they are killing people or making the town badly suffer from their actions on purpose. Shunning will decrease your town's overall daily since that player can no longer work at the Construction Site or convert at the workshop. Shunning should only be issued by the second or third offense.
Always consult the Die2Nite Wiki whenever you need to choose between items to bring to town since you have limited space or they are heavy items. Also, some items can become more important than others depending on the town's situation.
