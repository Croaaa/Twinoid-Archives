My tender-livered cohorts, we have arranged something rather special for you all! Here it is - the latest on Season 5, from the World Beyond!

A New Profession

The All-powerful Necromancer, who uses the bodies of dead citizens:

  • He recovers the AP of any players dying that day.
  • He can modify the career choice of any Hero currently in the same town.
  • Using a heroic action, he is able to teleport (one time only) to any spot in the desert.
The Survivalist

The survivalist can no longer trust his pocket guide 100%, he now has a chance of finding poisoned water or poisoned food. Will you take the chance?

When is Season 5 going to start?

Season 5 will begin on the 1st of May 2012, and will last 8 months.
