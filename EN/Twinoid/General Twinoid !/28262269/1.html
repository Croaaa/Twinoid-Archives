AnglophoneWhat has happened to the KadoKado clan / page? Why can't you access the twinoid profiles of any of the users who have attacked you? What exactly is the funciton of the golden star beside their names?

Like npfan said, it's been like that for a while

The reason for the stars is related to an update to "mod posts". I'll try to explain

Twinoid lets moderators post anonymously, either as a generic moderator*, or as part of an official group. Twinoid is designed to associate names with a user ID, but the "anonymod" posts don't have a user ID. Twinoid now considers any instance of a name without a user ID to be a mod, and shows the star icon instead of the red contact icon. In certain games (like KadoKado or Die2Nite), there are places where user IDs are stripped from regular users' names. As a result, normal people look like mods

* Do note that in certain forums, there is a special user account that is set to be the generic mod account. Since this special user account does have a user ID, the star icon isn't shown next to its name. A future update might change this behavior, but it's unlikely to happen
