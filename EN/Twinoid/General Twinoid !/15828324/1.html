Version 2.5 of Twinoid is online NOW! Here are the broad strokes of what we've done:

  • Visual upgrades to Twinoid pages and announcements. Don't forget to refresh the page or hit "ctrl"+"F5" if there's anything that doesn't look quite right.
  • Mini-surveys: You can create your own mini-surveys on your nexus.
  • Improved content sharing for social networks: In addition to more user-friendly buttons, we have our very own URL shortener "twd.io", to allow addresses (URLS) to fit in your tweets more easily.
  • Mobile version: We've made huge improvements to the way Twinoid displays on mobile devices.
  • Moderation: You can block inappropriate messages (obscene or aggressive content, etc.) in forums and on the nexus thus informing our moderators that they should intervene.
  • 2 New Twinoid Emoticons: If you look carefully you'll know which ones they are!

These are only some of the changes we have made, we are testing the update and making minor changes and fixes over the coming hours. The complete list can be found here .
