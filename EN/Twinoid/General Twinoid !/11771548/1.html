Quick tip for roaming the deserts.

Run around, then eat food, then drink. Not the other way round - every 11 steps you take you drop a level of thirst.


0-normal
11-thirsty
22-dehydrated (drinking bumps you up to thirsty
33 - Death by terminal dehydration.

It's also worth noting you drop a thirst level over night, unless you also have the 'Refreshed' status (gained by drinking whilst thirsty or normal).

Good luck, and don't die2nite.
