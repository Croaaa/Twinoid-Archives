Meadow

Yes, Mush has in one day 8 cycles. One cycle is 3 hours so one day would have 8 cycles.

You hav two different "points". One is "action point" and one is "movement points". If you move ofcourse you use MP. But if you do not have MP you will convert on AP to MP 1:3 ratio (1 MP consumed in movement process, net gain 2 MP). Every cycle if you are standing without bonuses/penalties you would get one AP and one MP + 1 XP.

If you're resting in a bed again without any bonuses/penalties you would get 2 AP+1MP+1XP.

AP is used to perform actions such as repairing, cooking, shooting aliens, hitting someone etc.
