What are the requirements for validation, besides the requirements listed in the three bullets right here: https://twinoid.com/developers/doc ? And if I want my app to be validation-ready, how can I submit my app for validation?
Yes, external apps do need to be validated.

Unfortunately, the current validation procedure has only been explained in the French forums. A group of volunteers known as the "Guilde de l'Architectoire" has been selected by the admins to validate the apps. They are identified by the title "Architecteur" in this list. I am one of them.

To submit your app for validation, you can either create a new topic for your app in the "❱❱ Développement" forum or send a private message to the group "Guilde de l'Architectoire" (you can write in English, do not worry about that). The request must include the client_id of the application so that the validators can check if you have filled in the fields correctly (URL, description, icon, etc.) and try your new app.

Once validated, your application will appear in the list of external applications and will be usable by everybody.
