If you're in the US, here's what you do:

  • Go to the payment selection screen
  • Pick the option at the bottom
  • Select your purchase amount and click Continue
  • On the next screen, there should be a tab that says "# more choices!" ("#" being a number... it varies)
  • You should see a Paysafecard button
