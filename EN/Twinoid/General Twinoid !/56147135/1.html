Speed is hands down the worse aspect to upgrade on MyBrute. Just slightly increase your attack turns, but as there's many counter skills around, attacking more doesn't mean dealing more damage.

The key of a winning brute is high agility, which means more dodging/counter-attacking. The rest is just picking the right weapons (or even a single one), Martial Arts and a few other skills.
