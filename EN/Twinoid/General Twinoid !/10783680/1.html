Gym Uncommon Blueprint
Work yourself and get in shape with top of the line exercise routines such as The Fat Cat Dumbbell Crunches, Guard Dog Cardio Routine, and Corpse Punching Bags Drill.
By using 2 AP, you become Fit.
"After all the straining, sweating, and panting, your ready to hit the showers."
Allows Heros to regain Healthy Body.
Unlocks 'Workout' option.

Requires: 10 Twisted Wooden Planks ; 4 Metal Supports ; 2 N&B

Fit Health Status
No pain, no gain. With your reflexes heightened, your ready to take on the world.
Increases your base AP to 7. (Meaning that instead of being to refilled to 6 when you eat or drink, you'll refill to 7)
+10% chance to kill a zombie with a weapon. (Charge counts)
+7% chance to not use 1 AP.
"(Normal action message) Instead of using the usual # AP, your training has allowed you to pace yourself properly, saving you 1 AP!"
+5% chance to kill a zombie with your bare hands.
Showering afterwards gives you an extra 1 AP. One time only.
"Ah, the refreshing feel of water over your strained body has refreshed your pores. You've gained 1 AP!"

Requires: Workout at Gym.

Mike's Gym Ruin
The birthplace of muscle junkies. Lucky me to find it.
You can find: Mobile Phone, Dumbbell, Punching Bag, Groundsheet, Antibiotic Steroids, Moldy Ham Sandwich, Mini HI-Fi (Broken), and Incomplete Cafeti√®re.

(Each Dumbbell and Punching Bag in the bank slightly increases Fit, Trained, and Healthy Body effectiveness.)

Kung-Fu Dojo Rare Blueprint
HI-YA! Prepare yourself, grasshopper, to become a warrior of war and mind.
Gains you the Trained status instead of Fit by using 2 AP.
Unlocks 'Train' option at the Gym.
All melee weapons increase it's effectiveness.
Decreases Gym use to 1 AP.
NOTE: You can still just use the Gym, but you must use the Gym first to use the Kung-Fu Dojo.

Requires: 10 Wooden Beams ; 2 Metal Supports ; 3 N&B ; 3 Rotting Logs ; Gym must be built.

Trained Health Status
You feel like a ninja from the movies. All your senses are heightened, your much more strategic, ready to defend yourself and your land.
Gains you 4 BP (Battle Points. Like Construction Points, but for fighting.)
Lasts until you use all your BP or the day ends.
Allows you the chance to kill up to 3 zombies.
"In a dazzling awe of jump kicks and chops, you managed to dominate # zombies!"
Gains you 1 bonus Control Point.
Pretty much a overhauled version of Fit.

Requires: Train at the Kung-Fu Dojo.

Allows for people to safely travel through the zone for the next 30 minutes.

FOR THE CROW! ARGGH!

"After nailing in some loose planks and scooting heavy objects around, this place isn't looking to bad."
