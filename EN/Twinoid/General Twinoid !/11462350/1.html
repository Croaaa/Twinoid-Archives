On july 31, 2012
Morte745

I was changed to English version when Twinoid came out and came back to French months later.



What happened

No changes and you can continue to play as usual:
Die2nite, DinoRPG, Naturalchimie.

Will change only the language, everything else is kept:
Alphabounce, KadoKado, Galaxy55, Fever.

Have independent progress, will start from zero after language change, you can continue from where you started once you return to original language:
MyBrute, MonsterMotel, Kingdom.
Snake: Cards are kept through languages, enciclopefruit stats are not.
Kube: Worlds are different, everything else is kept(inventory, buyed items). All stats except kubes picked/placed are split.

French only games:
Intrusion, Odyssey, Motionball2, Majority, Studio Quiz, TowerQuest.

Twinoid site languages can be changed by inserting /fr/, /en/, /es/ or /de/ after "twinoid.com".


What's better, in my opinion

I can understand French, but me talking it is a bit stroggonoffical, so I play French and use English forums. No problems at all. French games are more active and normally receive more attention from developers.

Would be really appreciated if French-only games are open to everybody, but with a "international board" forum like MyBrute.

Odyssey, Intrusion and French Kube are really worth of a language change, great games, even SQ is great, but is difficult to get local French culture questions.
