how to change password
On june 12, 2012
JasonBourne

1) Click on your name "SweetJuliet" in the top right corner of your screen, on the right hand side of the blue Twinoid bar.

2) From the dropdown menu select "Modify my profile"

3) Click on "Change my password"

4) Enter you old password and then your new password twice.

5) Then click the lovely orange button "Change Password"
