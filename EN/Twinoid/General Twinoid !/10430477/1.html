Alright, so I got to thinking from the byproduct of being drunk, high, and bored. I'm thinking out loud here, but I came up with a list of perks for certain distinctions. All of these would be gained by unlocking the last achievement in each distinction and can only be used once per game. Some I couldn't think of (I did this spur of the moment.)

I think there may be some good ones in here though. All of these can only be used once per town and are not expected to be overpowered. Just like how Armageddon Witness gave us "Break Through" and good food, it would be good (well, in my opininion "good") to award such actions when someone unlocks the titles.

I think this gives residents who can't/won't pay for the game incentive to continue to play. Which makes more people play from word of mouth and a different set of people that may pay to play.

Just my thoughts and everyone may not agree. Also, some of these "suggestions" I couldn't think of so any advice/suggestions would be welcomed.

I love this game and will continue to play it, but I think it'd be cool if residents could perform original actions. Maybe it's just the old school rpg class style that has brainwashed me.
