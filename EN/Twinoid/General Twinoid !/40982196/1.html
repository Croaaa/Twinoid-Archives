MandyWhat Games in Arkadeo can give me lots of points?

What you look for is Kadeo prizes <- those

There are 6 ways to get kadeos on Arkadeo:

  • 1) Kadeos "hidden" in games:
    Each game you play there will be hidden kadeos to collect. This happens in both score and level up mode.
  • 2) Jackpot:
    On Level Up mode if you reach certain levels of the games you will win some kadeos too. Finishing all levels to get the best Jackpot (or not, depending on Jackpot fluctuation.)
  • 3) Kadeo prize for League position.
    At the end of the Arkadeo season you will get a kadeo bonus depending on what position you are on the League. Higher ranked leagues award players better.
  • 4) Kadeo prize for Mission accomplished:
    On the solo mission mode you will get some kadeos by completing all stages of your current mission.
  • 5) Clan mode:
    • Kadeo prize for Clan Mission accomplished:
      If you are part of a clan and a Mission was accomplished then everyone in the clan will receive some kadeos. If your clan manages to stay at top 10th place on Missions you will also receive a bonus.
    • Kadeo prize for Tactical Mode clan rankings:
      At the end of the season if your clan figure on an awarded position of the Tactical mode leaderbords you will also receive some kadeo reward.
