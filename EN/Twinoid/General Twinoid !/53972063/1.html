There were several different issues :

  • From the 18th or 19th until the 22nd or 23rd, the servers for images, scripts, style sheets and other static contents were unavailable for players located in several European countries or regions including parts or all of Germany, Austria, Switzerland, Italy. For players outside these countries, everything was working fine. This was an issue with the CDN used for serving the static files. Users from the affected countries got the error "Connection reset by peer" when trying to load these files. The result was that the layout of the forums and most games was severely broken and had lots of blank spaces.
  • On Monday the 25th in the morning, a database server was down for a few hours. The games were still working, but the forums and many parts of Twinoid were not accessible. Those who were logged out could not log in again.
  • During the last few days, there were some issues with Die2Nite, probably related to some changes done by the admins while trying to fix the issues on the French server hordes.fr.
