SnapSo uh, why isnt residents near the top?

Well to begin with is the obvious reason of residents have no abilities at all (class, standard or earned). Two of which can be extreme game changers: pro camper and heroic return. With no pro camper players your end game camping will be far shorter then anyother town and without heroic return (or second wind for that matter) exploring the far corners of the map will be near impossible. Not to mention other base abilities such as 4cp for easier late game asing, heroic rescue for earlier game longer exploring or even the ability to escort which saved many lives in the scout town as im sure it did in all the towns.

putting all that aside I do believe a resident town could do FAIRLY well if all 40 players were experienced. However, the way the resident event seems like its going to be set up is going to be a disservice for the resident town that does get to compete in cott3. As of right now it seems that the towns for the resident event will choose their citizens either at random or by column as shown on oo. So although the towns will do better then a normal random town full of residents they will still be basically random towns.

Although the way the towns seem to be organized now seems to be the most fair way to pick towns so they all can compete against eachother in the resident event, it has also most likely secured the winning towns last place spot in cott3.
