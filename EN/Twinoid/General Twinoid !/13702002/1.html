If you want to practice the game, here is a copy of the game that has all the in-game options unlocked. Note: the option that converts the death balls has not been offered in the auctions

In-game options are usable once per "run". In other words, your options are renewed once you lose a game and return to level 1

Descriptions of the options:
Recharge - adds 3 shots (unused shots do not carry over)
Nitro - supercharges the power of a single shot
Aimant (magnet) - attracts anything that isn't a turtle to your ball

Avatars, extra games, and MTPasses are credited once, only the options listed above will renew themselves
