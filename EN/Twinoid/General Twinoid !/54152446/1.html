Has anyone every played Space Station 13?

I have a go at it recently, and it was really fun. Being a small person in a much larger machine was an interesting concept, and one I think would be awesome if it was modernised and had quality of life changes to it, and maybe even a little graphical improvement.

Could even add a progressional element to it by making your character level up through playing one of the various roles, and gaining skills within that role, travelling from Station to Station and undertaking various generated missions and goals.

My game idea for the day.
