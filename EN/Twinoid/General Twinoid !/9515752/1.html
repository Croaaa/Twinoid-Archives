Twinoid v2.0 is on-line!

Discover all the new features:

  • New forums: All Muxxu games now have new Twinoid forums (more modern, easier to use, even more practical!)
  • Private wall: share your experiences and your thoughts with your Twinoid contacts on one page! Follow your friends' updates and somment on their victories and defeats.
  • Game statistics: you can now see at which games your friends excel at a glance. Twinoid game stats are displayed directly on your Twinoid profile! Some statistics allow you to earn distinctions: icons, images, titles (complete or partial).
  • Community titles: create an entirely custom title by assembling the prefixes and suffixes you have unblocked in-game, or by choosing from the "complete" (show-off) titles.
  • ... and lots moar stuffs!!
