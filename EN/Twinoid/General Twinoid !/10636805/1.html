I saw in the updates section that when Twinoid 3 comes out we will get groups! Yeah! Except that muxxu groups cost 200 (muxxu tokens)! I don't want to spend money on a group when playing is still very fun without them. Let's post suggestions for solutions to this problem. Here are mine:

1. Make groups free.

Pros: No problem with cash.
Cons: No revenue for MT. Mass Spamming.

2. Bring groups over from other games (such as DinoRPG)
Pros: Totally fixes the problem of cost (other than muxxu)
Cons: Many games don't have groups.

3. Turn points into a "currency." So you can use points you earn in games (can be viewed at your profile) into a currency that can be used for groups.
Pros: Removes payment problem.
Cons: Points are fairly easy to get so spamming problems still exist.

4. Make Muxxu tokens easier to get for free.
Pros: MT still might earn revenue. You don't have to pay.
Cons: Affects other parts of games.

I'm for number 3 or 4. Other suggestions are welcome, or just vote on a previously posted solution.
