I know a lot of people are looking for options to play DRPG on their phones. I have found ones that I use for both apple and android. The android browser "FlashFox" is free and supports full movement and playing. Obviously it is a small screen but it is what it is; it also has small ads at the bottom of the browser.

On apple, I use "photon" which, while it does cost a couple bucks, supports full flash on a tab at a time and can be used to stream sports from online sites and everything. It is one of the top 20 paid-app downloads for a very good reason. Highly worth the small investment. I haven't found anything else free on there that works.
