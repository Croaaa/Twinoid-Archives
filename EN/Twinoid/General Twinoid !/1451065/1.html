I get that sometimes when trying to recruit an army for a friend (read: recruit of the heart" thread on Muxxu forums). Best guess is since most ISP providers recycle addresses, Mini Troopers "thinks" you already created armies when you really didn't.

If you have more than one computer in your house, try making one on a different computer. One of them might slip past (since it'll have a different MAC address (I think it's called, whatever the code that identifies a specific computer on a LAN)) and let you create an army. Otherwise, it's just pot luck waiting for MT's cache to clear and finally let you make an army again.
