nicus9

My reviews on all the games I think Evoland is the only game you could somewhat have spoilers in so I blocked those out. All the others should be fine. If you think otherwise... well deal with it


Yeah The Balance (karma game ) looks pretty cool. Good artwork and cool song in the background. Wish there was a little more to it though (I understand there were time constraints lol, just saying now that it is over I wish there was more to it). At least give a different ending based on whether you make the world good, evil, or balanced. Maybe even ones if you managed to kill all of them or none of them I think the art of it is what made me really like it though.


Strike Of Rage was ok. Nothing real ground breaking about it. There were a few cheap moves on both sides: the enemy could knock you over (like with bombs) and if there were more enemies then they could keep hitting you and you aren't even able to move, lost like 90% of my health like that at one point. You could walk towards someone, punch a couple times, then jump through them and repeat and rarely would they get a hit on you. Also it was actually better for you if you didn't use your full combo. Knocking over enemies often gave them an advantage whereas if you just did 1-2 punches over and over again (a small pause between hits) then you could sometimes stun-lock them so they couldn't move.


Beetlefield I'm just terrible at It doesn't matter which side I choose, that side always loses. I played like 20 times and only won once. From what I could tell though is basically you had "Random bugs vs Random bugs" mode and then "One you saw and saved vs Another one you saw and saved" mode. If that is really all there is to it, it is a little disappointing just because I wish there was more to it (time constraints I know).


Evoland was probably my favorite out of em. Kind of funny at times. At times it seemed too easy (just hold down spacebar and walk around lol) while other times it was a little annoying (wtf my sword went through you, how did you kill me first?!). Once you got a health bar it wasn't as bad since it was no longer one hit kill (at least for you lol). I'm glad he hid some stuff around so it wasn't completely straight forward. I apparently missed 6 treasure chests with gold in them so I may go back and try to find them. I didn't feel like checking every tree and rock my first playthrough. I only wish the direction you pushed the rock didn't matter, just a time saver lol. I'm just glad he added sound and most of all improved the graphics, it really would have given me a headache if it stayed like it was at the beginning. Oh and also make the little pop ups last longer, I couldn't read some of them fast enough.


Bact Guerrilla I'm kind of meh about. Probably the main reason I disliked it was I spent a good 5 minutes hitting the ESCAPE key to shoot and couldn't figure out why the heck my bullets weren't working <_< At the very least take 10 seconds to proof read the controls you leave the player After I finally figured out the real shoot button I started a new game and won in about 15 seconds. The movement was kind of hard to get used to, although honestly I wouldn't say I ever got completely used to it but I at least wasn't shooting off in the wrong direction entirely.


Evolv I just got fed up with The first game I played my weapons kept going on backwards or sideways so I died quickly. After that point whenever I started a game I would spawn surrounded by enemies and would die in about 3 seconds before I could even really move. I've played similar games to that but that is the first one I've seen where you add the enemies to you during battle and it rotates around until you pick it up. So it was interesting but also why I didn't like it just because I had back luck with getting the weapons to work right


Promoted I'm not sure what to think of. This one I know wasn't finished at all but I'll still critique it just to be fair lol. First off a quick two second direction would be nice so you don't automatically fall behind or lose because you aren't certain what to do:

First level: "Catch the sleepers!"
Second level: "Time closing the correct blinds at the right moment!"

I spent the first 30 seconds or so on the second level moving the boss around in his chair trying to catch or avoid the arrows. Took me a minute before I finally hit the spacebar.

Anyways:

The first level kind of made me say "WTF?!" at first but after that it was alright. By the time I realized what was going on I had fallen behind so I figured I would try to lose and start over, only to realize even if you empty the meter the game keeps going (pressing space bar as fast as you can is freakin hilarious though ). Also you can lose that level and the game still says you won it (you can go to level 2).

The second level was just plain disturbing. I mean seriously? I'm not even going to go into this one, there is so much wrong with this level <_<
