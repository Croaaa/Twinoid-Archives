I'm sure everyone has a story behind their username, so let's share


I've been using coro (and variations of it) since April 20, 2003. I was registering on the Something Awful forums, and decided that my current handles sounded too stupid (when you're paying money, you tend to strongly consider any decisions you're making)

Not sure how "coro" popped out of my brain, but the more likely reason is that I thought of "Coronado". Some of my relatives are Coronados

When it came time to register for Gmail (back in 2004, when it was invite-only), I couldn't get "coro" on there. So, I put "corosnark". Snarks were my claim to fame at the time, cute little things I would draw for no particular reason... I named them by thinking of what kind of sound they would make

Much, much later, I made a website called abe. It was a place for people in The Soupalineans to share info on what they were called on various websites. There, I started a gimmick account called "FAKEcoro", and roleplayed as an antagonistic person against myself. abe eventually faded away...

After a while, FAKEcoro changed a lot. Eventually I deleted my original KK account and re-registered as FAKEcoro. At first, FC was still a gimmick account (I'm sure some people on here remember what I was doing). Then, I got tired of doing that, and started acting normally again

On Twinoid, I decided to just use "coro". Mainly because of how I registered so early (I knew that no one else would have taken the name)

FAKEcoro is now what I use as an artist name, since I feel it's an appropriate name for a "band" consisting of one person


And there's an excessively long story about why I have my name, and why some people refer to me as "FC"
