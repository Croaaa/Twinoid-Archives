Here's my ideas on sound and distance for roleplay elements so you can add them to your weapon info. These are probably realisticly inaccurate, but whatever.
Close - Roughly 0-5 feet away.
Short - Roughly 5-20 feet away.
Medium - Roughly 20-50 feet away.
Long - Roughly 50-100 feet away.
Far - Roughly 100-150 feet away.
Very Far - Roughly 150 - 200 feet away. (no weapons should pass this point)
Silent - Not hearable.
Quiet - Heard roughly 0-5 feet away.
Loud - Heard roughly 5-20 feet away.
Very Loud - Heard roughly 20-50 feet away.
Extremely Loud - Heard roughly 50-100 feet away.
Ridiculously Loud - Heard roughly 100-150 feet away. Momentarily deafens Close range players.
Screeching Loud - roughly 150 - Heard 200 feet away. Momentarily deafens Close-Short range players. (no weapons should pass this point)

You don't necessarily have to write down how far away from each player you are, but you can hint it in your role-play. If you're not sure, flip heads or tails.
