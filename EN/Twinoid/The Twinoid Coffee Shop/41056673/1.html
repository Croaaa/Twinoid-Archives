This is a really easy game to do in your free time, a bit of a break from RPGs or playing with RNG.

Basically everyone who wishes to sign up does so, simply by stating they want to play.

Then I will specify a challenge, of which will lead to one person being eliminated, then another different challenge, also having a single elimination.

Example
Game 1- Everyone rolls a d100 and the lowest is out.
Game 2- A Rock Paper Scissors tournament, loser is out.
Game 3- Each player guesses a number, and a d100 is rolled. Farthest away loses.

You get the idea. The games will go until only one man stands, who is then put into the hall of fame.
