Wow we're at the 3rd thread already! Read below if you're new.

====================================

Some Adventure Thing was a forum RPG created by Dragonice a long time ago. It eventually died down after 4 full 1000-post threads, but seeing that the forum currently doesn't have any RPG with a full level-up/battle system (Soul Fray is inactive) and Dragonice suddenly reappeared, we've decided to restart it with improved consistency/balance/story line.

In the world of Somewhere (yes that's the world's name because I'm bad at names), there are 5 known civilized races: Human, Elf, Dwarf, Goblin, Dragonkin. You're a new adventurer of one of these races. You don't really have any aim (like most RPG adventurers); you just want to explore and get stronger, but of course you can make one for yourself.

All info of this game is/will be put into this Google Spreadsheet. You can pick one of the race and an available starting class for the race.

After that, pick a name, fill in the character sheet and you're free to go!

Some explanations:
Location: you start at the capital of your race. The map is on the Spreadsheet.

= HP: base is 5, -1 because Dragonkin
= MP: base is 5, +2 because Dragonkin
= level: you start at level 1, and you level up whenever you gain enough
= experience, you need 100XP to level up from 1 to 2, and more at higher levels
= skill points, you gain one each level up. You can use one to gain a new skill from your class or level up an existing skill.
= Arions, the currency of Somewhere. You'll need it. You start with 10 Arions.

You start with a (pathetic) pocket penknife in your inventory. The icon shows that it's a weapon. You most likely won't have this icon, so use another instead. You can only equip two 1-hand weapons or one 2-hand weapon at the same time (obviously). Weapons are 1h unless otherwise stated. Inventory has 7 slots unless you're a goblin, then you get 8.

Skills consist of spells and passive traits. You start with a level 1 skill from your class. You can pick any one of them, but I'd prefer a spell or otherwise your MP has no use. The number inside the square brackets is the level of the skill.

====================================

You can perform one of the three actions each day (according to server time): Travel, Scavenge or Rest.

You can choose to travel to one of the 8 adjacent grids (including diagonally). There's a very high chance you'll encounter an enemy while you travel. If you survive the fight, you'll successfully arrive at your chosen destination, which may contain Arions or items laying around, an NPC, and/or a dungeon entrance, etc.

If you choose to scavenge, it's guaranteed you'll get an item (read: not Arions) out of the location's search table, but you lose the chance of gaining . You cannot scavenge at a starting point.

If you choose to rest, you regain HP and MP up to half of their max value. (rounded down)

If you die for whatever reason, you respawn at your race's starting point with your reset to 0. (Level is unaffected)

====================================

Player interactions:

When two players meet at the same location, they can choose to travel together. The encounters will be more difficult, the will be evenly shared, and the loot will be distributed by RNG.

One player can also challenge the other to a duel (with a stake or not). Both players will begin a battle fully healed, and everything will be restored to the pre-duel state after the duel (except the staked items). No consumables can be used in a duel. Both players must agree for the duel to start.

If you don't want to issue a challenge, you can also choose to assault the other player. In this case, only the assaulted will be fully healed, and deaths will result in losing and being sent back to respawn point. The winner can take half of the and one item that the loser has. All consumables are allowed.

====================================
