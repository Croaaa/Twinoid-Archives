taylork2

Well like in all games whether you die or not depends on what difficulty you are playing on, how much preparation you do before hand, and how carefully you play. If you WANT to die then put it on a harder difficulty and go do something stupid without thinking it through lol

And there are no previous games of Skyrim, there are previous games of The Elder Scrolls series however lol

As for the storyline, if you have played any of them you can appreciate how complicated and how much detail they put into the story. I would recommend looking up the games on wikipedia or something and read the story there. There are plenty of summaries if you google it.

Here is the very first result that popped up for me
http://www.ugo.com/games/the-elder-sc...

One point that I think is fairly important is that all the games take place in the same "reality". You can think of each game as taking place in a different region then the others but it is still the same world (so in real world terms, one game could take place in Canada while the next might take place in Mexico but it is the same reality unlike other games that completely reboot for each game)

So if you want you can go read the wiki for the stories (I can't do them justice) or go to youtube and watch someone play through the game or find a story summary there, or, my favorite idea, play the games to learn the stories first hand. They are good games after all lol
