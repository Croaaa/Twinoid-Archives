Mafia is a game, similar to mush, but much different as well. There are two sides to a normal game, the town, and the mafia. The mafia is a group that has been hiding within the town for decades, and chooses now to strike. The day is divided into day and night phases. In day phases, everybody in the game can speak in the thread. This phase is used for discussion among the townspeople(both the mafia, and the regular townsfolk), and they can kill somebody through a lynch. Lynches are done through voting. You vote for the person you want to lynch by saying Vote: Who you want to lynch in bold. Once a majority is reached, the day ends. You may still speak in the thread until the moderator(me), confirms the lynch and that the day is over. Day phases last a maximum of two weeks, and if the deadline hits before a lynch, there will be no lynch. At night, the thread is silent. Nobody may speak but the moderator. The mafia can talk in a separate method, apart from the thread. The regular townspeople have no night powers, and can't do anything. However, some townies(and even mafia members) have special abilities to be used in the night. These are called power roles. I will tell you your role at the beginning of the game, so you will know who you are. You can tell me who you want to use your role on, and if it involves investigation, I will tell you the results. Night segments last up to five days, ending earlier if everything is decided. If there's any interest in playing, I'll host a game here and you guys can see what it's like. I'm shooting for about 9 players, as a setup I know has 9 players, and it will be easier for me to choose roles while still being balanced.
Current Volunteers: Dr. Winner, DragonIce, Syvaris, Miner101x, Derpenstein. Join us, and we can rule the galaxy!
