What is e-Sim?

e-Sim is a world simulation game in which you become a citizen in the country of your choosing. Once you have your citizenship you can engage in a whole plethora of activities, including (but not limited to): working for a company, owning one or several companies, joining or leading a political party, running for congress or the president of your country, participating in battles and waging war with other nations, and buying/trading stocks or running your own stock company.
