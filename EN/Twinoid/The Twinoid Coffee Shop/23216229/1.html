MadDonutMadDonut wandered about the cafe, acting as if the mild mannered coffee shop dining area was a formal ball. He strutted with a strong confidence as he makes his way towards Saucy.

"Kind sir," MadDonut asked in a very posh voice, "would you mind if I accompany you at this table?"

"I do mi-" Saucy began, but was rudely interrupted as MadDonut sat down and he was forced to put down his book.

"I am The Almighty Pastry, otherwise known as MadDonut. What is your name?" MadDonut asked.

"What's the use of names nowadays? Heh. But I am SaucyemmettC," Saucy answered. "You can call me Saucy, if you want."

"Might I ask what is your occupation?" MadDonut questioned.

"Well, hehe, I'm a Resident," Saucy replied with a yellow grin.

"Oh no, mister Saucy. I don't mean 'What is your job?' I mean 'What do you do, what strives you forth, what is your reason for living?'" MadDonut explained with mighty hand gestures.

"Well, I enjoy many... sinful things that a sophisticated man such as yourself would regret to know," Saucy said, sarcastically remarking on the madman's contrasting appearance and actions. "The Raven King demands my poor fleshy body to do dirty work."

"Don't fret, mister Saucy. I have heard many terrible tales in my days of travel," MadDonut stated. "I don't think I will succumb to despair to hear of your most likely marvelous views of life!"

"Well, I'll tell you of one hobby of mine, hehe," Saucy said, leaning in closely to MadDonut. "Cannibalism."

"Oh yes!" MadDonut said with a surprise of enjoyment in his voice. "Oh such wonders is the flavor of fresh meat oozing upon one's tongue. A delight indeed. How do you like it? Running, screaming, begging, pleading, desiring, hating, loving, killing, crying, flailing, scared, sacred, scarred, sad, happy, confused, or something else? Or even a combination? I enjoy it if the cattle is sentient, obedient, and ignorant. Easy to get, but still rewarding."

The cafe quieted down after MadDonut loudly discussed the ways of eating another person alive.

"Well, I li-" Saucy began.

"Oh deary me!" MadDonut exclaimed. "Look at the time! Just look at it! I'm afraid I'll have to end this conversation with a cannibal. It was a wonder meeting you mister Saucy, or should I say meat? Ha! Anyways, I must run. I believe I have just been shot and killed."

MadDonut stood up, whistled to his chicken, and walked out of the shop, leaving a small brown note on the floor.
