Alright, alright. Here's most of what I have so far. Just a few stones of a castle. All titles, places, etc are work in progress.

City of the Dead
Setting: Futuristic dystopian metropolis.

Main groups: Bottlecap Collective, Distributive Army

Important locations: Cloud Nine, The Metro, The Hive

Plot: A monstrous plague has ran rampant across the entirety of the global. Small havens have sprouted in this infection, dotting themselves across the world. The most thriving survivor communities have specialized air transports that pilot over miles and miles of tainted landscape to trade their supplies. Neopathia, your current city of residence, is doing quite well despite a massive hivemind teeming within city limits. Up high on top of the massive Cloud Nine skyscraper is the main HQ of the Distributive Army. Multiple ports are scattering alongside the higher section of the skyscraper, with many trade and resupply ships flying about. The main job of the Distributive Army, as their name implies, is to share the wealth and strength of the city to the suffering inhabitants below. Sadly, little to no supplies ever make it to the small pockets of humanity below. In anger, the lower city residences often lash out at the D.A. (Distributive Army). The most popular resistance group by far are the Bottlecap Collective. Currently, both sides are evenly matched. The Bottlecap Collective would be in better strength if not for their situation of being surrounded by quarantined zones and the lack of proper weaponry and other general supplies. The D.A. would wipe them out quickly if the infection made it too treacherous to venture to the sub-levels of the city. Stuck in their corners of the ring, the two groups battle out in small firefights spread out across the city.

Info about the infection: There are four primary classifications of the infection, two of which are infected creatures. Undead, Freaks, Mutants, and Parasitic.
Undead: Slow moving, independent of any hive minds. Headshot required to kill.
Freaks: Fast moving, living versions of the Undead with sharp claws in place of nails. Any mortal wounds are fatal. When killed, typically resurrects as a clawed version of Undead.
Mutants: Mutants come in many forms. Three of the main forms being Titans, Winged Beasts, and Packs. Titans are large, muscular beings with the only purpose of destruction. Winged Beasts are flying versions of Freaks. Packs are the most feared of all, however. Packs are large four-legged creatures that work in pairs. Their abilities are the terrifying combination might of a canine, jungle cat, grizzly bear, and carnivorous dinosaurs. They're highly capable creatures with supreme instinctual powers and deadly, long claws capable of ripping the armored doors of a tank in a single swipe.
Parasitic: The most primitive version of the infection, though in a large mass become a serious threat. Hiveminds are only found in seriously infected areas. Small clusters often eject highly-infectious spores into the air while large clusters can sprout long tendrils, some tentacles being large enough to tears entire buildings down.
