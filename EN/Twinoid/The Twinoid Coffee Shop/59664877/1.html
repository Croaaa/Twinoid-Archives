What I enjoy about Die2Nite is the constant clashing between being a team player and surviving by yourself. You cannot rely solely on yourself, but at the same time, when stuff hits the fan, you gotta fight for that LMS.

Most people don't quite get this and get personally offended when it happens, but only being out for yourself in Die2Nite is a completely fair option. It is not against the rules to lie, cheat or steal in any way you can imagine. It's all about survival, and if you successfully poison the last ration of water and make the Guardian in a Safehouse drink it, you just improved your chances immensely.

That is what I like the most about the game - having to think ahead, tip-toeing around the Bank and improving your chances day by day, and finally, when it all goes down and chaos begins - being the only one who prepared ahead of time enough to survive the inevitable doom of the town.
