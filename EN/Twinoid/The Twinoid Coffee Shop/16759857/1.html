In Australian Rules Football, each of the 18 AFL clubs have a song, like an anthem. They are both played at the start of each game and the winning team's song is played several times at the end of the game and then sung by the players in the change rooms.

It would look quite bizarre to people from overseas but to us it's a really important part of the game
