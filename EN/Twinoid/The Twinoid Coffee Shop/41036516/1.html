Fighting & Combat:
To fight an enemy, go to a certain area. Depending on the day of the week and/or the events, they will change. Check daily for these.

Every attack that hits deals 1 damage.
When you win a battle against a creature that is your ranking or higher you get 1

To attack, say a target and then flip a coin. A heads is a hit.
Combat is turn based.

Also, a Season lasts a random amount of days.
At end of season, all of your items are sold for 50% price, and then all of your gold is added up and added to your guild's bank. If you do not join a player guild, it goes to your Race's bank.
You then also get back 25% of what is in your guild's bank. (10% if race bank)

Creating a guild is free. It just needs a name.

Money in the bank also unlocks secret classes and bosses.

Also, between seasons you may change class, as you will always reset to Rank 1. (Rank = 1 + items bought)
