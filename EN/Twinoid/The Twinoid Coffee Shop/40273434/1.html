Hi all... so with a few RPG's coming up around here - Heroe's Journey, Epsilon:Genesis, Come Together RPG, I've decided to make called, as you guessed,

VexRPG.

It's a twist on RPG's such as Final Fantasy or Bravely Default - it's more like MT's own Kingdom, My Life as a King or My Life as a Darklord. (Look the last 2 up if you want to.)

Story: You are a prince who inherits a weighty legacy from your father in the kingdom of Vex. When the other kingdoms hear about your loss, however, they see this as the most powerful Tactician stepping aside to let the inexperienced prince try and manage a Kingdom.

Basically, instead of getting your own hands dirty, you send your minions/army/bodyguards into the fray. There is no penalty for death of a soldier, BUT after ALL of them die, the enemies may enter the castle, and you, the prince, are very inexperienced in swordplay.

Although each soldier can't level up individually, each garrision/barracks of soldier levels up. So, you may have a Lvl. 3 Barracks with strong soldiers and a Lvl. 1 Barracks with weaker soldiers. To level up, you must reach a certain amount of Exp and then pay a fee. To level your Prince up, you don't need exp, but the sum is much heftier.

There are also 4 personalities/atrributes; Picky lets you recruit less soldiers, but they are stronger. Fair allows you to recruit more soldiers which are weaker. Greedy is a balance between Picky and Fair, but receives more gold. Courageous is a balance between Picky and Fair, but it makes your prince a little stronger.
