Instructions:

1) Go to Wikipedia.org and hit random. The first random wikipedia article you get is the name of your band.

2) Go to quotationspage.com and hit random. The last four or five words of the very last quote of the page is the title of your album.

3) Go to flickr and click on “explore the last seven days”. Third picture... no matter what ...it is, will be your album cover.

4) Use This to put it all together.

5) upload results here.

6) Post results.


Good luck!
