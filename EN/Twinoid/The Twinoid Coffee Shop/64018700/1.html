Description and History:
You are playing as a young man who works for the Shishū Gādo. The Shishū Gādo is the government's secret police, orphans are trained in cruel furtive combat training since childhood and serving only to the Emperor, Emperor Akio. The Emperor was so strict that he closed the country. No one comes on and no one comes out except for the Dutch. They can trade in the southern parts of the country. Now that the country isn't at war, the population increased. Roads, schools and businesses were built. People started doing new arts, they made books and studying western inventions. 10 years later, The Spanish Armada led by Commodore David arrived in Japan and threatens the Emperor to open the country or face hostilities. The Emperor realised that their technology and weaponry couldn't repel the invaders. So in the mid-year 2854, they accepted the unequal treaties imposed to Japan from the Western Powers. This treaty has made Japan unstable in politics and the economy. Dissatisfaction towards the Emperor rise. The Leader of Shishū Gādo disobeyed Emperor Akio and overthrew him. Then Emperor Akio's brother, Hikaru, becomes Emperor. At dawn, Hikaru takes action and wrote a letter to Master Raiden to expel and kill key officials and foreigners. This is where MC's adventure begins.


Character's Information and Story:

You are playing as a young man who works for the Shishū Gādo. The Shishū Gādo is the government's secret police, orphans are trained in cruel furtive combat training since childhood and serving only to the Emperor, Emperor Akio.
The MC will be given The Mechanical Box of 16 Blades. 10 Blades for killing, 5 blades for torturing and 1 for committing suicide for failing a mission. He trained with the Shishū Gādo for 15 years and the results were really outstanding. 2 days later, a letter was sent from Emperor Akio to Master Raiden. Raiden gathered the MC and other comrades to the hall. He unscrolled the scroll and said "Execute Order 56" and Raiden dismissed.
