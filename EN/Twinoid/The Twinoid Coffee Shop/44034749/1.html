Eventually, Gregory spotted a white RR sign leaning off to the side of the road and a pair of railroad tracks. Approaching the railroad tracks he saw mangled barbed wire and a toppled sandbag wall on the other side with very militaristic signs on each side. Both signs read as such:

WARNING
THE US ARMY HAS CONDEMNED THIS CITY HAZARDOUS
ANY ATTEMPT TO ENTER THIS CITY IS PROHIBITED
TRESSPASSERS MAY BE FIRED UPON
TURN BACK NOW

As aggressive and serious looking as the setup was, the apparent wear and damage to the basic blockade made it clear that no military personal were still around. With an uneasy shrug, Gregory stepped over and across the barricade, careful not to get caught on the still menacing wire. It was clear to him now that he must be entering the city on one of the primary roads, considering the amount of attention left behind.

Unknown to Gregory, he and Andrea were potentionally on a collision course. As she made her way into the forest off to his left, he slowed his pace as he proceeded. Gregory began noticing the telephone poles that meshed almost perfectly with the trunks of the real trees. It was an interesting display civilization mixing with nature. Gregory began to idle out, making it more possible for him to be unaware of Andrea and anything else lurking in the forests..
