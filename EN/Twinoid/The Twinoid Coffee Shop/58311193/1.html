Zombie Survival

Rules
1.You are on a map of 25x25 squares. Each square takes One Ap to travel. Once your Ap runs out you are stuck in that area until tomorrow. In order to restore Ap on that day you most drink water,eat food,alchol or take drugs, which restores 4Ap. An can only be done once.
2.In each square you can only search a location four times and thats it except when a examinable object is there. Once you interact with the object you get one random item.Examinables can be in any location. The zone starts with fresh new items the next day. Only one building is discoverable in each zone. But all items in that location disappear the next day.
3.Each Zone has a chance of a random number zombie encounter. Combat will be explained in its own section. You can use a random name picker with the zombies as well to tell what items players find. An how many zombies they encounter. They can only encounter one zombie. An the amount of items is picked by a random number generator or dice but they can only be four(4) Or Six(6).
4.You have food and water bars each bar indicates if you are hydrated or hungry. Your thirst bar is three and your hunger bar is seven each lasting the days they represent. So long as you keep your water bar hydrated. You won’t die instantly on the next day. Which is heads your dead and tails your alive. If your alive that means you have one bar left before you die. Hunger tells how well you do in battle. If your hunger is low at the last bar of seven you can’t fight and will lose until your hp runs out. An you’ll die the next week.

Combat
Each weapon has dmg and that is it besides weight. Weapons also have a hit and miss mechanic. Heads is a hit and tails is a miss.No breakage or weapon deterioration.
The first to start is picked by random with a name picker(Player or enemy).

So with the dmg range of the weapon(or strike from a zombie) minus health.
If player or zombie is wearing Armour e.g kelvar this is also heads for hit and tails for miss. So witg the range of dmg caluate dmg-Armour(0 or absobtion)-Minus player heath. If there is a miss or hit that is.

Each turn is one at a time.
If a player runs out of hp they die. They can also escape which is heads for escape and tails to failure to escape.
Stats e.g Dmg:1-20 Hit Or Miss: Heads or tails. Defense:50/50
Inventory
