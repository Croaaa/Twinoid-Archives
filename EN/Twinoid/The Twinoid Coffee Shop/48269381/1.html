Deep underground lies a small catacomb, build from metal. A map of science that is only written about and never acted upon. From these labs experiments took place once upon a time. These experiments were not great advancements of technology however. They were of the human body, and if one could play God. These experiments left its victims changed, and unnatural. The Changes, as the victims had called them, did not spare anyone of any age.

Many people were taken right from their lives, kidnapped. Some were stolen from prisons. Some belonged here, some did not.

However, the tests were abandoned when The People found out. A debate upon how ethical this was, and if it should continue was short, and the entire lab was abandoned, left to rot. Those who were experimented on were not cared about, they were put into a cryogenic state, surviving off the remaining generators. They were just as disposable then as they were when they first arrived at the labs.

But now, they awaken. The generators have ran out of power, and those who had lives so long ago as regular people, then as test subjects, then as creatures.

You find yourself inside a glass tube, it is dark outside of it.

This RP will have a lot of creative freedom, for all of you will partly be GM's. I am here mostly to monitor and/or help things along if needed. You are free to create your own NPC's, monsters inside (or even outside the lab if you so choose to leave it), and to have your own events happen. I wish for you all to think like authors.
