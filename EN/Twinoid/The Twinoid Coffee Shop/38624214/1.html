Part one of my rant about Legend of Korra:

So, let us start it off with the series... Well, starting off.

Every character you loved is dead, leaving only Katara. And Katara... Well, she wasn't really the reason you watched the show unless you were a teenage boy in a really really weird age at the time.
The only thing that really made her a cool character was her arguments and nagging with Sokka. And Sokka is dead. Just like everyone else. That was such a fucking bitchslap from the creators.

Now, instead of all those nice, loveable and charismatic characters we had... You give us a sassy bitch with a big ole pair of bewbs. Oh, and start it off with KORRA KNOWING 3 OUT OF 4 BENDING STYLES. WHAT. THE. FUCK.
I watched that episode a while ago and don't remember how old she was, but Aang didn't even know he was the avatar for 12 years. And he grew up in an environment designed to train benders. Seems like Korra pulled magic out of her ass without any training or masters to teach her how to even use basic water bending.

Next up- the plot. For 'The last air bender' we had the whole 'Taking down the firelord' thing. And now? I didn't bother watching past the first chapter, but I'm going to guess we are going to see a bunch more petty villains.

Yeah, I called that Amon guy petty. Why?
Well, let me go through this guy in 3 parts: His force, his goals and- what was the biggest issue- his abilities.

So, his force- a bunch of non-benders. There is a large force of trained, experienced benders more than capable to deal with the rebellion and kill Amon. Seriously, they could have just killed Amon so many times and with such ease it actually hurts. In a fight between an angry civy that got a week of training at best, and a soldier with the ability to BEND FUCKING METAL (For fucks sake, Toph was the only one to accomplish this during the first series if I remember correctly. Now, just because she went: 'oh, yeah, you can do that' earth benders just went ahead and did it?) we know who would win.

Next issue- his goals. Once again- didn't bother following along too much. The story was slow, boring and uneventful, and when something happened it was either purely moronic or just lazily written.
As far as I got it, the whole 'equality' thing was done to... do a thing with Amons brother? Okay. Yeah. He does that, the rebellion eventually gets oppressed by, I don't know, the rest of the nations forces. Then what? Amon's just one man with a small force. No huge lord with a massive army and resources like the firelord.

And finally- his powers.
I just... Bloodbending does NOT work like that. You can say 'oh, u dunt kno dat' all you want, but I really do.
Bloodbending allows for a person to control someones body. Period.
No bending blocking. And certainly no 'blocking out the bending I know you can do and not the one that you still haven't learned, and al- OG MY LORD, YOU CONVENIENTLY FIGURED OUT HOW TO DO AIRBENDING, NOOOOOOO'
In 'The last airbender' there was a certain fighting technique we all recall that used 'pressure points' and 'weak spots' on the body. That gave Sokka the plan with that drill thing at Ba Sing Se. That pressure points thing allowed for temporary movement and, in fact, bending impairment, so you'd think blood bending could also accomplish this. Temporarily.
And even then it would impair ALL bending and ALL movement.

So, what would've been a better plot? One that we were left off at the end of the 1st series, a question, cliffhanger and quest that was still ahead of our characters, especially Zuko?

Skin care products.
Finding Zuko's mom.

I'll be sure to make another rant, for there is no way to express how disappointed I was in the series. I loved the first ones, really did, but this is a disgrace.
