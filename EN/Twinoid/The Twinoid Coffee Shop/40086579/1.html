While the peacekeepers defend the innocent and weak great magick raves the land as the overlords of power in the land fight eachother for supremacy, the state does not interveene unless it is a speciall occasion, certain ceasfires that affect all the fighting in the whole state.

Roumors spread of the state paying the most powerfull of the warriors (and peacekeepers) to be able to make warriors from their genes.

while the state seems poor, individuals that want to stop the fighting are inventing defense sistems andeffective wepons, these still pull the city ahead in science, not too much but it's something, the state just pays the other states to give them science... roumors are also spreading of a revolution...
