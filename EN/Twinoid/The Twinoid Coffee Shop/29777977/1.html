You haven't given your reasons for leaving. Is being honest to your boss completely out of the question? If you get caught lying, you will probably get sacked or at least get a warning, and you could lose the reference. There's no guarantee that you'll get the job you're going for...

If you take too many sick days, I'd start asking for doctor's notes. Some companies will ask for one every time, regardless of your history. Some won't ask for proof at all unless you take several days off in a row. I don't know your company so can't really advise.

Try to arrange the interview in your lunch break or outside normal hours. Do you have any holiday due? You can always make appointments for other things and simply don't turn up... or cancel as soon as you have proof.
