Once upon a time, in another world where steam power was mode of transportation, there existed several Old Kingdoms of whom bickered amongst themselves.

Burning each other down to the ground, 5 nations of considerable strength and power arose, of whom would ruled the world between them.

  • Eruch, whom arose out of the ashes with an iron fist and gentlemanly conduct. With its great military, intelligent commanders, and powerful air force it ruled several parts of the world. SE
  • Dalmace, of which mostly arose out of the lower-class population. They established themselves as a new world order and a republican empire in the new era of empires. N
  • Orlin, a nation of merchants who had used their immense fortunes to buy themselves an empire, trading territories like they were money. SW
  • Pulias, a faction shrouded in mystery, and of which came out of the shadows of the Old Kingdoms. Using black-mail and great planning, it would carve out land for its empire out of what little remained of the Old Kingdoms, and even more from the wasteland frontiers. W
  • Vedria, which was created by scientists, and what few remained of the royal class. Its entire philosophy would be based off reason and the advancement of science. It existed for science, and only for science. E
============================================================

One day, a group of people had an idea, The Grand Idea. The 5 nations would cooperate together to create this Grand Idea, creating one grand city for this Grand Idea.

However, something went wrong, something had come to their world from another dimension, and it's effects were devastating.

The dead, now living, swarmed through the 5 nations, destroying them and toppling civilization.

Only a few survivors remain, those lucky enough to have survived the apocalypse.

One of these survivors were you, and so here you are, having traveled a great distance, to arrive within the grand city, untouched due to the disappearance of all its inhabitants and the undead rushing into the city.
