Your race may be a...

Human:
Average humans. No special traits, starts with each stat 100. The underdogs.

Dwarf:
Hardy goldlovers and master traders. Has high durability and heavy weapons skill, and great charaisma, but lower other stats.

Mana Golem:
Golems made of mana and elements. High durability and magic power, but bad with weapons. Very low charisma.

Sun Elf:
Elves of the sun. Good with healing and fire magics. High charisma, but lower durability. +Good

Shadow Elf:
Outcast elves of darkness. Good with attacking magic. Very low durability, but great magic and speed. +Evil

Half Demon:
Demon offspring with another race. Can take a beating and very charming, and great wtih fire, but bad with small weapons and ranged weapons. +Evil

Gnome:
Bretherin of dwarves, only with a love of song and tale. Best charisma, and good durability, but lower other stats.

Guardian:
Magical beings made of mana and people. High magic power and weapon skill, but bad attack magic and charisma. +Good

Aquir:
People of the sea. Great with ranged weapons and small tools, but lower magic skills, except water. Can stealth easily.

You can make a class with symbols. (Or parenthases names if you have no symbols) Choose a total of 3 symbols. You can repeat for added skill. 4096 combos!
