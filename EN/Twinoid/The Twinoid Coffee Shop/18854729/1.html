This game is called Pass the Parcel, and as we know have surprises in them.

Rules■ A mod will start the game and pass the parcel onto someone that is online at the time.
That person will then have the choice to wait and pass the parcel onto someone else that is online or too wait until later on in the day. (Only the person that is named in the thread can pass it along).
■ You can only pass the parcel onto the same person 3 times during the day.
■ Later during the day/night a mod will come into the thread and stop the game, and the person that has hold of the parcel at that time will be told what is inside the parcel. It could be good it could be bad… who knows?

So for example, I will start the game and I will say "I pass the parcel to A "

No-one except A can then pass it along (write in the thread).
