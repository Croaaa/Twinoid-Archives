The year is 16539. Three years have passed since a EScorp prisoner defied and left the Karbonis belt without permission. He joined FURI, and managed to locate Terre, the human homeworld. He started a slander campaign against EScorp, speaking out against their evil practices. People didn't are much. It was EScorp which gave them an easy life. However, prisoners picked up his transmissions. They heard freedom, and many left to join FURI. EScorp offered better though. They offered freedom, real freedom, if they helped stop FURI.

The Karbonis area is disputed. FURI want it all to be a safe and nice place to live. EScorp want Terre to be a nice place to live for the rich, regardless of the cost. FURI have begun expanding their liberation efforts, stopping the oppression of EScorp. They've been holding the mineral-rich planets, defending them with diplomatic patrols, destroying the unmanned EScorp drones. EScorp is ready to start a war. FURI is ready to stop a war.

That's where you come in.
