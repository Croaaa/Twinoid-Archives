Welcome to the most exiting game of Twinoid Coffee Shop Mafia.

The Rules

If you have never played a game of Mafia here are the basics:

The game consists of two factions The Town and The Mafia. The goal of the town is to survive, track and finally lynch all the criminals trying to take over they peaceful city while the mafia is constantly killing and disposing of the townspeople.

The game consists of two phases: Day and Night. During the day this thread will be open for discussion and if a majority consensus is reached - the lynching of a fellow citizen. The Night is where all the secret mafia meeting and investigations begin whose results can be seen by everyone during the day.

The Setup

This game will consist of 9 players whose roles will be the following:

3 Citizens. Helpless observers caught in the battle.
1 Gunsmith. One time per game during a game can make a gun and hand it to another player, the gun can be fired during the day by the player killing the target and having a 50% chance of revealing the shooting.
1 Cop. During the night can investigate a player and find out to what faction he belongs to.
1 Bulletproof. Can survive a single assassination attempt by the mafia.
1 Mafia. A simple mafia goon.
1 Hooker. During the night can 'hook' a single player preventing him from doing anything that night.
1 Lawyer. Can choose a fellow criminal and make him appear as an innocent to the cop during that night.

Do time zone problems days will take 48 hours and nights 24.

During the day this will be open for all the players to chat and votes on lynching. Also please leave your votes Like this at the end of your and take note that you can change your choice later during the day overwriting the old one.

During the night discussion can only take place in PM's and all the roles have to report their actions to me (That being who mafia kill, who the cop investigates so that I can report to him the answer, and who the gunsmith gives the gun to).

Anyway feel free to ask any questions about this and feel free to play Epicmafia in preparation. And of course I need 9 players so feel free to volunteer.
