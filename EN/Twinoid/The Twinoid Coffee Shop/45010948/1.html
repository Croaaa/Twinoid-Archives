Welcome to the world of Monster Hunter.

BACKSTORY BELOW!

Let me tell you a tale of a group of Hunters, who sought out to fight the things that have threatened us for centuries, since the first tribe.

Monsters.

From larger than usual insects that can inject you with paralyzing venom to giant, world destroying dragons of legend, it's a wonder how we're still alive to this day, gentlemen.

But there's a simple reason.

Because for every monster, no matter how strong, how fast, how intelligent, how awe inspiring, there is an answer. The Hunter's Guild.

Founded almost eighty years before this story AND before now, (year 322) to fight the monsters that threatened the population, by a group of wary travelers in a hut, with sharp stones, spiky stones and big stones.

Travel forward a few years and that hut has been destroyed. Destroyed for the new Hunters Hub, a...Well, a hub for hunters. Complete with a blacksmith, merchant, chefs, houses, hotels, anything a hunter needed was there, besides the materials the blacksmiths needed for crafting.

Hunters from all walks of life were there. No, still there; The Hunters Hub is still up and running, of course. It's not like a pack of Rathians and Rathalos came by and destroyed the place...Probably.

But that's all fluff! You're here for the story of the Hunters of Jumbo Village, aren't you?

Then, again, let me tell you the tale of a group of hunters...

Now, our hunters in question.

BUT WAIT! WE HAVEN'T TOUCHED ON RACES YET, DERPY!

Hush now, valiant hunter, I was getting to that.

The races are...

Felynes!
These are literal cat people. Felynes are significantly smaller than other races, barely reaching a human's knees. What they lose in strength and height, they make up for in speed, agility and adorableness. Most tribes are reclusive, but some have integrated themselves into society.

HUMANS! The most common species in the world, and the most average.

Wyverians! Wyverians are very similar to humans, except their longer ears. However, as they age, they grow extremely tall, until they become elderly. Then it reverts and they become extremely short. There are few exceptions.

Shakalaka! These creatures are as short as felynes and all of them wear masks at all times. Their rite of passage from child to adult involves a shakalaka finding their own mask rather than wearing a big acorn.

With all that done, CHARACTER SKELETON TIME-

BUT DERPY WHAT ABOUT WEAPONS?!
FINE.

Great sword: Giant swords. Or for shakalaka's and felynes, normal swords that in comparison are HUMONGOUS. You can't run around wielding these, since these are heavier than YOU. Despite that flaw, however, if you can wield it well, it deals massive amounts of damage.

Lance and shield: pretty much a big stick with a sharp end on it and a shield. Slow but the defense is top notch.

Gunlance: It's...A bowgun...With a lance...Combined...With a shield stuck on too...I don't even.

Sword and shield: Combining mobility and defense at the expense of power.

Dual blades: Think a sword and shield with two swords and no shield. Fast, but not powerful.

Bow: Well...It's a bow. You shoot things with it and you use arrows with it. Self explanatory.

Bowgun: Firearms reverse engineered from ancient technology, these are similar to the rifles of the real world, but they don't deal much damage and are expensive to get ammo for. Speaking of ammo, these shoot out bone canisters, filled with explosives, fire, water, whatever. Bowguns have a light and heavy variant. Light bowguns are weaker but allow you to move about more. Heavy bowguns are like sniper rifles; You don't want to be moving much when wielding one.

Giant Hammer: Self explanatory, moving on.

Long sword: Two handed weapons that are quicker than a great sword, but don't deal as much damage.
