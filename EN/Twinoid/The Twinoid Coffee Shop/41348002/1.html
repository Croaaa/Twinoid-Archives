Hello Ladies and Gentlemen!

The original The Open Story.
Versatio
[STORY GAME]
[ROLE-PLAYING]
[SHARED AUTHORSHIP]

I love the open-ended collaboration of forum adventures and story games. Everyone can be a part of the creative process, and no one can predict how the story will unfold. But many of these stories seem to fizzle out and die at some point, because their authors get busy and move on. So I'd like to try something different. Let's write a story together, but with decentralized editorial power shared amongst all of us. Here is a set of rules that will allow this story to take on a life of its own.

Each participant (including me) will play the role of a single character. We will all share the roles of NPCs and the Narrator. I will be a facilitator, helping to keep the process organized and moving forward. But I'm not in control - the power is shared equally by all players, and anyone can perform the tasks of facilitation if they understand the rules.

Dialogue and actions of player characters are automatically accepted into the story. Players cannot control another player's character. But any player can control the Narrator and NPCs, to guide the story in any direction they wish.

When a player character attempts an important action that might fail, a d100 dice must be rolled to determine whether it was successful. But first we have to determine the character's chance of success for that action, so don't roll the dice yet! Please describe the action you want to attempt, and declare the percentage chance you believe you should have, based on your character's biography.

Player characters will be considered NPCs after the player has been inactive for 3 consecutive days. Players can resume control of their character after they return from being inactive.

We'll begin with some narration to set the scene and maybe introduce some NPCs. Then you should try to guide the narration to a point where you can introduce your own character. Feel free to give any back-story for your character, but remember you can always add more details later, even when you're attempting an action that depends on it. No need for numeric attributes, just give general descriptions, such as "very strong" or "dumb as rocks". Let's just say it's a Fantasy/Sci-fi cross-over universe, to keep it really open-ended. Magic and technology, orcs and aliens, studded leather and laser blasters - anything goes.

I'm not going to re-write anything, so your contributions will become part of the story as-is. But don't worry about perfect English or great writing, just try to be clear about your meaning and have fun!
Yes.. I have edited it to somewhat fit with Modern TOS rules as well, but it is the original in spirit, if not in physical form.

With that said, let us begin.

A searing flash and a thunderous blast
erupted the Senate floor.
The rubble and flame, and the bodies they claim,
obstructed the grand entrance door.

The screaming that followed was echoed and hollowed
by shock through the senator's ears.
The peace had been broken without a word spoken,
at last, after so many years.

They cowered and ran, without any plan,
no time to think or debate.
But who was behind it, and why were they blinded
by ever such violent hate?
