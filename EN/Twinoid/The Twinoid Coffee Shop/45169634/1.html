Inspired by Town of Salem and EpicMafia, I have finally decided to start up the common Mafia game.

Here are the rules:
Before each game, a number of people can request to join. The game will begin when between 7-11 join, and each person will then be assigned a role from the following, top priorities first!

Human Roles
Investigator - Each night, they may investigate someone. They will learn the possibilites of that person's role.
Sherrif- Can investigate one person each night. They will learn the person's alignment.
Roleblocker- Can visit one person each night. That person will not complete their nightly action.
Doctor- Can visit one person each night.
Vigilante- During the night, they can shoot someone. If they were a towny, you will hang yourself over guilt the next night.
Lookout- Can choose someone to watch at night. They will learn who visited that person.
Bodyguard- Can guard someone each night. If that person is visited by any mafia, both The Bodyguard and the Mafia will die.


Mafia Roles
Godfather- Can kill someone each night. Is immune to night attacks, and appears as a towny to the sherrif and investigator.
Framer- Can frame someone each night. That person will appear as a Mafia to the Sherrif and Investigator.
Consort- Equivalent to the mafia Roleblocker.

Neutral Roles Jester- Get yourself lynched, at all costs.

Every day phase, you can vote to lynch someone. If the majority of the town votes to lynch someone, then they will go up on trial. Be sure to bold your votes.
Vote- Name
If someone goes on trial, they are allowed One Post to defend themself. After that, each town member may vote Guilt or Innocent.
Vote- Guilty/Innocent
After the day phase, the night phase begins.
During the night phase, everyone can do their nightly action.
The town wins when all the Mafia are dead.
The Mafia when when they outnumber the town.
The Jester wins when he is hanged.

To join, just say so and when we have 7-11 i'll send you all a PM. I'll be on in 2 days to start the game.
