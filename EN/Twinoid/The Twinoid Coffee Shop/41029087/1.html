October 25, 3040. Another excursion into the universe. Civilisation is found. Short creatures with huge facial hair. Strong for their size. Peace treaty is formed.

December 12, 3042. Relations between Dwarves and Elves grow strained.

January 3, 3045. Dwarves declare war on Elves.

February 10, 3045. Humans get raided for supplies by Elves. War is declared.

Present day, July 30th, 3050. Three way war still rages. Everyone feels their foes wrath. Humans are versatile with advanced ballistics and bulky, slow mechs. Elves are silent assassins, as their opponents have known before death. Elemental ammo and arrows, cloaking technology, that is their realm. Dwarves are the most technological and explosive. They have high power explosives, mechs and lasers.
