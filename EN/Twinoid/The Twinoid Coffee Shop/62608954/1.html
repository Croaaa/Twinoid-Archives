Exposition Begin

So, this is a CYOA (I think) where our character has no eyes, living in normal society, can somehow still see, until he’s been whisked away from the government to go to a special college and whatnot.

Now, the mechanics of this is a little complicated. You know how a game takes a bit to load all the objects in said game? It’s like that, but with your 5 senses.

Your eyes (or lack thereof) need time to adjust to your surroundings, and you have to wait for a few seconds, afterwards, you can see abstract blocks hat represent said objects.

Your ears are pretty sharp actually, except for the fact that you can only recognize sounds you’ve heard from yesterday. Such as the noise of birds, car engines, etc. And for some reason, you’d know the exact thing that made the noise.

Your nose can only stay smelling for a limited amount of time, 5 minutes max. Afterwards you smell nothing.

You’ll never know the texture of an object until you touch it, so you have to grope it just a tiny bit. This isn’t necessary all the time.

And you won’t know what things taste like until your tongue makes contact, luckily, this is the only one that stays permanent.

So, yeah. Pretty complicated ain’t it?

Exposition End
