Preview

In this game you have a small kingdom and an army.
You have to build a city and attack other cities.

Instructions

Chapters
-Buildings and upgrades
-Resources
-Army
-Attack and defense

Bulding descriptions

Your City
This is your city icon.
Cranny
Holds 100 units of each resources.
Farm
Farmers produce food here for your soldiers.
Rally Point
Your village's troops meet here. From here you can send them out to conquer, attack, raid or reinforce other villages.
Warehouse
The resources Wood, Iron and Food are stored in your warehouse. By increasing its level you increase your warehouse's capacity.
Citizens homes
Your citizens live here.Upgrade the level of your houses to have more citizens.
Hills
Workers produce iron and wood here.The hill also give an defense bonus for your army.
Fountain
Raise the morale of your soldiers.(Attack bonus every upgrade)
Scheme of buldings
City
Can't be upgraded.
Cranny
Level 1:100 units of each resources are safe from enemy attacks.
Level 2:200 units of each resources are safe from enemy attacks.
Level 3:300 units of each resources are safe from enemy attacks.
Level 4:400 units of each resources are safe from enemy attacks.
Level 5:500 units of each resources are safe from enemy attacks.
Farm
Level 1:100 food produced every turn.
Level 2:200 food pruduced every turn.
Rally Point
Can't be upgraded.
Warehouse:
Level 1:200 units of each resources are stored in the warehouse.
Level 2:400 units of each resources are stored in the warehouse.
Level 3:600 units of each resources are stored in the warehouse.
Level 4:800 units of each resources are stored in the warehouse.
Level 5:1000 units of each resources are stored in the warehouse.
Citizens homes
Level 1:100 citizens will live in the city.
Level 2:200 citizens will live in the city.
Level 3:300 citizens will live in the city.
Hills
Level 1:100 metal and wood produced in the hill, 10 % bonus defence.
Level 2:200 metal and wood produced in the hill, 20 % bonus defence.
Level 3:300 metal and wood produced in the hill, 30 % bonus defence.
Fountain
Level 1:10 % bonus attack.
Level 2:20 % bonus attack.
Level 3:30 % bonus attack.

Resources
-Food:produced in the farm and used to feed your army.
-Wood: Produced in the hill and used for buildings.
-Metal: Produced in the hill and used to train soldiers.
Army
-Soldier:Costs 1 metal, eat 1 food per turn.Can kill 1 soldiers.
-Archer:Costs 2 metal and eat 2 food per turn.Can kill 2 soldiers.
-Cavalier:Costs 3 metal and eat 3 food per turn.Can kill 3 soldiers.
Attack and defense
How to attack?
First declare war.
For example: Player 1 declares war to Player 2
Then send your troops to the enemy city.
For example: Player1 sends xx soldiers to Player 2
How to defend?
Example: 10 soldiers kills 2 soldiers.Losses:2 soldiers.
How the attack bonus work?
Example: an 10 % bonus will kill 10 soldiers
How the defense bonus works?
Example: an 10 % bonus will kill 10 soldiers.
Turns
Every player get an turn every day.
You can build a building in a turn, produce resources in a turn and attack/defend in a turn.
