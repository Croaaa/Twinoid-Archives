A disease has struck. It renders roughly 70% of those infected with a fever, making them weak. The other 30% have had their DNA meld with that of the disease, making them display serious anomalous behavior. A small number of people have displayed an immunity to the disease, some continuing to spread it, some not carrying it at all despite constant exposure.

The result?

Orion city is in carnage. a quarantine has been set up, nobody gets in or out. What remains of the police force tries to control the rampage of Mutagens flexing their new abilities, to little effect. Would-be 'superhero' vigilantes are stepping up, deciding to use their new strengths for 'good'. This doesn't always work out well for Orion City, as those who have the disease can do little as their homes are ransacked and robbed.

Rewind a week.

Orion City is fine, it's a normal day. The first infections are starting. You are a civilian of Orion City. Will you be infected? Will you mutate? Will you use your immense power for good, or for evil?

That's up to you to decide.
