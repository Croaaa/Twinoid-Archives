[Role play]
[Story Game]
[Multiple Authors]

Based off of the Mush forums game, but not everyone plays mush and not everyone wants to be limited to The Daedalus.

Rules:
-The Story can take any arc, any angle.
-Double posting is strictly forbidden.
-When someone disagrees with another's idea for what ever reason, they may call a veto.
-It takes three supporters for a veto to succeed.

-Characters-
Each user can only have one PC, or player character. All of the players will agree on the traits of NPCs, and also agreed on their actions. While creating a character, there is no template, as this is a multiple author game, but be sure to be detailed and cover what you need to. The game will officially "start" some time after the thread is posted, then the players will decide how many NPCs to include, if any.

-Events-
An event is a sort of turn, something that happens or when something takes place. All of the players must agree on the preferences, and players can edit other players ideas. Examples:

Player 1: What happens to the plane? Let's roll a d100. On a 1-50, it crashes.
Player 2: Sounds good, but on a 25- a character dies!
Player 3: How about 20-?
Player 2: Sure.
Player 4: How about on a 100 it goes super fast due to winds?
Players 1,2,3: Okay then.
Players 1,2,3,4: Now we wait for player 5.
Player 5: Okay I support your ideas, let's roll the dice.

Starting Off
The hardest part off this will most likely be the actuall storyline beginning. I suppose all the players that may or may not exist can agree on something, or always flip a coin.
