"Ring-a, ding, ding, baby!"

Moneymouth was quick to respond, but not quick enough as the pungent smell smacked him in the face and grabbed his hand and shook it.

The squatter gingerly smiled, shaking his hand. "What'ya looking for, bacon?" The man had a filthy face with a fat nose stuck in the middle of two wide eyes.

"Something, maybe a someone. Perhaps a someone with a special something.

"What about something with a someone, ah?"

Wasting time. He needed to move on to search for the jewel. "Do you know where I can find a guy, long brown hair, masculine?" The squatter scrunched up his face.

"No idea what you just said, but I think I reckon that a group called the Vikings sped past here hours ago."

"You reckon? Where can I find them?"

The squatter laughed, trying to respond through breathless laughter. He gave up and went to his friends who started laughing their socks off, though they had no socks.
