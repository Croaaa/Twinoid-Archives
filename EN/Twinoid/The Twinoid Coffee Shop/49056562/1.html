I've tried to start this a few times before, but now i'm convinced that I can stay organized and run a succesful games.

Here are the rules:
Before each game, a number of people can request to join. The game will begin when between 7-11 join, and each person will then be assigned a role from the following, top priorities first!

Human Roles

Villager - No special abilities besides a vote and the ability to scumhunt.
Cop - During the night can investigate someone, will learn the alignment of that person.
Doctor - Can protect someone at night. They will be saved from dying that night.
Vigilante - Once per game, can kill someone at night.
Oracle - Goes on one person once per night. If the oracle dies, that person's role will be revealed to everyone. Can not be roleblocked.
Roleblocker - Can roleblock one person each night.

Mafia Roles
Godfather- Can kill someone each night. Is immune to night attacks, and appears as a towny to the sherrif and investigator.
Framer- Can frame someone each night. That person will appear as a Mafia to investigative roles.
Consort- Equivalent to the mafia Roleblocker.
Hooker - Roleblocks one person each night.

Notice that not all roles will be included in each game.

Neutral Roles Jester- Wins if he is lynched.

Instead of playing a "trial" voting setup, we will be playing with majority rules.
Once everyone has voted, whoever has the most votes on him or her will be lynched. I have to ask the dead people to not post in this thread anymore.
After the day phase, the night phase begins.
During the night phase, everyone can do their nightly action.
The town wins when all the Mafia are dead.
The Mafia when when they outnumber the town.
The Jester wins when he is hanged.

To join, just say so and when we have 7-11 i'll send you all a PM. Then game One will begin.
