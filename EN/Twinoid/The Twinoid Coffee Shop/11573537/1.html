OK, new rules!

The main hills are where instead of rolling an 100 die, you roll 3 8 dice if you want to attack or defend. Example: 834
There are 4 main hills. If you rule all 4 you win the game and given the title, 'Lord of the Hills'. Then, a new game is started.

For side hills, you only roll a 100 die. There are 6. If at any point in the game, you have all 6, you get the title, 'Knight of the Hills'. There can only be 5 knights at one time. If there are 5 knights and someone else obtains 6 hills, the 6 knights are 'The Royal Guard of the Hills' and there is a new game. If you are the first knight when this happens, you cannot be a knight in the next game. Instead, you become a 'Defender'.

If you are the first defender at the end of a game, you can not play in the next. ALL side hills must be ruled before anyone attacks the main hills.
