Your main goal is to find water in three days or die. An find food for a week or die.

# Moving around he map
The map is 25x25 map like non-distant towns.

You start anywhere on the map. You have 4Ap(No way to restore it and thats it.) Until next morning.

You move across the map with these directions: North,Sourth,East and west.






# Combat

The player fights first.

Use the according dice to the according weapon.
You have 5hits before you die.
Each zombie has a different number of hits before it goes down.

Each weapon has a hit,a miss and a break. A hit is the side of the dice the weapon is supposed to hit a target.e.g 5.
An a miss is below that 1-4,An a break is at 6.

Certain weapons defy this e.g for a very rotting blunt bat it is a 1 that is considered a hit. Anything else is a break.

You can only fight one enemy at a time in an area.

certain Weapons require ammo. An are useless without it.

# Encountering Enemies

Enemies are rare.

Each Area is a 20sided role. Specific enemies at certain sides.

#Escaping
Escaping has a chance of injury leading to infection and death.

Eyes-You are blind(Game over)

Hands-You cannot use your weapon or pick up weapons.(Only bandage,medicinal plant ,water and food )

Foot-No penalty but your still injured.

Head-No penalty but your still injured.

Chest-No penalty but your still injured.

You can only loot a location five times.Regardless of a ruin. The next day zone will be replenished. An any items on the ground disappear.


# Inventory
You only have five slots that is it. No way to increase it. You also crafting and use the item if its on the ground.
