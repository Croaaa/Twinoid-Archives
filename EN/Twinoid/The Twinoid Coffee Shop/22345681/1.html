BRIEF HISTORY
It is year 3047. 566 years ago, a horrifying calamity destroyed the world. No one remembers what caused the calamity for sure, there are too many theories, almost all sounding insane and impossible.

SOLAR SYSTEM
Earth: Has become completely uninhabitable, poisonous gasses, mutated creatures, swamps covering the entire landscape. What could have caused this?
Moon: 1st thing to be colonized after earth. The human race had to find a new home soon: there were too many people, and not enough room or materials to hold all of them.
Pluto: Last planet standing. Other attempts to colonize on the other planets in our solar system ended in complete failure. Solar panels, which became very useful after the calamity, are less effective. Its only solid supply of power is a rare crystal- the antarctist.
MARS: A planet taken down by an unidentified virus. A base camp still remains intact on it.
SHIPS
Interceptor: A wall of large, spherical ships called the "Interceptors" have been placed tightly around an area in space, called the "Forbidden Sector". The purpose of these ships is to stop anything trying to get past it.
BattleCruiser: A large ship, used in war against the Zeta aliens, colonization, mining operations, salvaging and fighting the "Cosmic Buccaneers.
Sentry: A small and agile ship, operated by a single man. Used to scout the universe. Patrols the borders of the "Forbidden Sector".

FORBIDDEN SECTOR
The sector was declared forbidden in the 13 of March, 2798. Humans, seeking a new home, had entered this new sector. They inhabited a planet called "#012".
A strange species of dangerous aliens had attacked from the planet
"#057".
Somehow during these events, most planets on the sector were destroyed. Not a single person from planet #012 had survived, and possibly the events of those days will remain a mystery forever.
FORBIDDEN SECTORS PLANETS
#012: A planet which was home for the human race, until tragic events, resulting in the destruction of the planet, and the start of war against the Zeta aliens.
#057: Home of the Zeta aliens. It is surrounded by a strange, red, unexplainable force field.
CREW
Every BattleCruiser requires a crew. These jobs are REQUIRED on a ship.
Medic: Performs simple and complex medical treatments EXACTLY according to protocol. (coming soon!)
Soldier/s: These are required to defend against attacks from "Cosmic Buccaneers" and Zeta aliens
IT specialist: Is required to help work with the BattleCruisers difficult terminals
Pilot: Steers the ship. makes sure the ship gets where its supposed to.
Captain: Sets the course, makes sure everyone is accounted for. His in charge of the missions.
