DarkTheRaven

In terms of MMO's the only one i've played recently is Elder Scrolls Online. Which was fun for about a few weeks, the PvE isnt bad but the PvP leaves much to be desired

Basically the PvP is a large map with 3 factions fighting for control of castles, these factions are:

Daggerfall which has not many players but they're all high quality (also has a very good economy if you want to play a crafting character)

Ebonheart which is a lot of decent quality players (and a lot of australians)

And The Aldmeri dominion, basically the lowest quality players IMO, never did much in PvP except when they had so many players in one place the game servers crashed (rather often)

The main issue with the PvP was Ebonheart had so many people from Australia (who play on the North American server) that every night when the americans went to sleep they would take over a PvP server and use it to farm materials, then in the (American day) Daggerfall would take it all back, rinse, repeat about 10 times a day. The other issue is that once the Ebonheart guys had a server they would show up every night to take it over again.

In summary: If you just want a game to PvE with some friends, or to make a merchant character the game is all right, though the combat system is rather broken (blocking does nothing, hits are delayed by about 1/2 a second) But assuming people still play it the economy in the daggerfall faction was quite nice for a merchant character, and the game is a nice way to make some friends even in the PvP mode

Another note: even in the PvP map you can play PvE, it also drops rarer loot and whatnot

Another issue: While I believe they implemented a counter measure, when a character reached max level you went into veteran mode, and then you got these points to assign to certain things like DPS, magic resistance, etc. when I last played there were people who would make a veteran character extremely high level with about 500 points to assign, then give them to a lower level character (veteran characters cant PvP with non-vets) so there'd be level 5's running around dealing 5k damage per hit
