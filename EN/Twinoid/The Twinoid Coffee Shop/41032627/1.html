In the year 2837, the Earth has been nearly destroyed. A combination of Plague, famine, pollution, and war has left our planet a desolate wasteland. The Earth is nearly hopeless, but there is a chance. A large group of humans have united, at a last stand for humanity. They have assembled a civilization, and are attempting to rebuild society. Only time will tell if they are successful.

Welcome to The Last Town!

In this game, you each play one of the last humans of Earth. You will face many challenges that will test your ability to survive. Each Day will pose a new challenge, which may be handled appropriately. Each character may have a class, chosen by the player.

Classes
Alchemist: Can combine liquids, create potions.
Doctor: Can heal players, use drugs.
Technician: Can create defenses, use metal.
Tamer: Can tame animals, control monsters.
Shooter: Can operate turrents, handle guns.
Demolitionist: Blows things up.
Searcher: Can excavate to find things.
Wiseman: Can make predictions and understands magic.
