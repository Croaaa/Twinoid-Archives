It's year 2560 and the humans are very advanced in technology. They have reached out and colonized another solar system. Earth on the other hand is a complete wasteland and human capital is Mars (city of Kalmor). Humans have made contacts with other species.

This technological advancement came with a prize - humans are ruled by a dictator named Roger Kilpu. Roger is the 4th dictator of the Human Star Federation. The first three showed how arrogant and selfish a ruler can be.

About Hakoiu, this trip would be it's 5th expedition, serving more than one hundred years already. It has 5 major divisions: research division, medical division, civilian, engineering division and the security force. Player can join any one of these. I don't know how to make someone a captain, but there is a head for each division: research overseer for research division,chief medical officer for medical division, head of personnel for civilian, head technician for engineering and Chief for security force. Ship needs to be refitted for the trip (each player has his part in this).
