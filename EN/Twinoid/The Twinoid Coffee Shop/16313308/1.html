Ok, this is a fairly complicated forum game I played on another forum, so I decided to run it here. I assume nobody will join and it will not take off, as it is very hard to explain/comprehend. Here goes the rules:

Basics:

Everyone posts here if they want to join, and I compile a list of names.
Following this, they PM me with their 'Alias'.
As the game goes on, people try to guess what name goes with what Alias, and can then try to identify a username with it's alias. Upon getting correctly identified, the player loses.
The winner is the last player not identified.

You are probably wondering "But how would you guess who they are?" That is where the meat of the game comes in. Rounds:

Each round, players vote on a specific Alias to 'Pressure'.
If a player gets 4 or more pressures, then they must give a hint which would reveal their username. The hints may be as vauge as you like. Example, one of my hints may be: "Was declared dead for a month, but came back.", which would relate to my forum ban.
A player may also use a 'Super Pressure', or SP, to get 3 pressures that round instead of one. These may be used as you wish. However, you also get one pressure put against yourself.

Ok, that is how the rules work. If you would like to play, post below.

Tips:
Working in an alliance with other players can help focus your pressures.
Be careful with SPs. Remember, you are also putting pressure on yourself.
