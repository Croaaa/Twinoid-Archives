The simplest rpg ever:
Flip a coin.
If heads you level up.
You start at Level 1.
First to level 10 wins.
If you get tails and are Level 5 or higher you lose a level.
If you win the thread explodes.
The thread will explode in 2 days if nobody wins.

I made this thread because I was bored.

New mechanics:
You're level is now what it is at.
You may flip coins equal to your level.
Each heads is a hit and +1 score to you.
The boss has 75 health.
Each tails means you lose a health.
You have 3 health per level you are.
If you run out of health you are killed and can no longer fight.
