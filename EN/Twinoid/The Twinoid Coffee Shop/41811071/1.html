This is a reboot of an old game of mine, Dungeon Explorer, which has about 10 players.

Backstory:

In a far away realm, ancient evil lurks. But the world lives on, in this land of old and ancient dungeons. Miners, collect ores to give to blacksmiths to forge new items to give to platoons of soldiers, set to guard the towns from the evils that lurk, but the evils are also collecting objects, refining them, and creating masses of monsters!
In a land locked in turmoil, who will win? Who will live on, and who will be reduced to just stories?


The Basic Idea:

Each player inside of Dungeon Lands controls 1 or more groups of a certain class. The goal is to have the combat classes claim dungeons and towns and fight, refine and build classes create items for combat classes, and collect classes to collect new items for the build and refine classes.
As the game goes on, the map will expand and change as players fight. Each group of a class controlled by a player has a certain number of them, dependent on level.
Players may choose what faction to join to enter the fight!


Heroic Classes:

The heroes side contains the 'good guys'.
Collecters:
-Miner - Starter Group Size: 6 - Energy: 3 - Job: Collect ores
-Hunter - Starter Group Size: 4 - Energy: 4 - Job: Collect wood and meats
-Scavenger - Starter Group Size: 5 - Energy: 3 - Job: Collect herbs and items from battle

Builders and Refiners:
-Builder - Starter Group Size: 4 - Energy: 4 - Job: Builds towns, upgrades, and buildings
-Blacksmith - Starter Group Size: 3 - Energy: 5 - Job: Refine ores and wood into usable items
-Alchemist - Starter Group Size: 4 - Energy: 3 - Job: Turn herbs into potions, and turn herbs and meat into meals

Soldiers:
-Foot Soldier - Starter Group Size: 7 - Energy: 4 - Health: 2 - Damage: 1
-Rouge - Starter Group Size: 6 - Energy: 4 - Health: 1 - Damage: 1 Ranged
-Mage - Starter Group Size: 5 - Energy: 2 - Health: 1 - Damage: 2 Ranged



Monster Classes:

The monsters side contains the 'bad guys'.
Collecters:
-Soul Thief - Starter Group Size: 4 - Energy: 3 - Job: Steal souls from fallen soldiers
-Magic Chanellers - Starter Group Size: 5 - Energy: 4 - Job: Collect mana
-Recruiter - Starter Group Size: 5 - Energy: 2 - Job: Make allies with monster clans

Builders and Refiners:
-Wizard - Starter Group Size: 3 - Energy: 3 - Job: Conjure monsters using souls, and conjure items using mana
-Trainer - Starter Group Size: 4 - Energy: 4 - Job: Turn allied monster clans into true dark warriors
-Worker Minion - Starter Group Size: 8 - Energy: 2 - Job: Build dark towers, upgrades, and monster dens with mana and souls

Soldiers:
-Minion - Starter Group Size: 8 - Energy: 2 - Health: 1 - Damage: 1
-Undead - Starter Group Size: 6 - Energy: 3 - Health: 1 - Damage: 1 Ranged
-Trained Monsters - Starter Group Size: 3 - Energy: 3 - Health: 3 - Damage: 2



How to Play:
To start, select a new class. You must then give yourself a name, and the leader of your new class group a name. Each point of health, energy, and damage is per person in that group. Each group has 0 P to start, and gains 1 by doing its job. Every 10XP is 1 level. Each level, you may increase the size of your group, or give your class a research point for all people of that class to share. Research points may be spent for all people to gain buffs at certain points in time. Notice, that while actions are secret, events are not. This forum is to post events. To overall start, post all this info to me. I'll tell you more of how to play that class in message. Notice, you may have 3 class groups at once, but they must all be hero team, or monster team.

Good luck!
