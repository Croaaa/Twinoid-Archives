In a distant land, far far away, there is a world that doesn't stick to the rules. Wizards class with terrorists, dinosaurs chase around dragons, and kings and queens fight dictators and presidents. The world is chaotic, and is a mix of countless different cultures. It is called The World of Idris. and there is no other dimension like it. Most people are commoners, who have no idea what to make of the place they live in, and often just work with whatever they have to understand. But then there are a few people, who seem to stand out above the others. Mighty Wizards, Warriors, Soldiers, and Mythic Beings work together to make Idris better, or hope to conquer it for themself. There are an endless amount of options, so better start looking.

Classes
Wizard- Use your magical studies to your advantage.
Soldier- Currently or recently in the army, trained in gun combat.
Knight- Raised in the medieval Kingdom, uses swords to beat enemies.
Mafia Boss- Leader of a criminal organization, trained in the art of Mafia.
Alchemist- Can make potions and brews.

Or you could design your own class with powers/perks, just keep it reasonable.

Races
I'm not going to make a list of these, really you can choose. Examples Human, Demon, Dwarf etc...

Rules of Combat
As this is a RPG, there is bound to be some sort of battling happening often. This is how things work: You roll two d100, the first being your roll, the second being your targets roll. Here are a few attack mods that may happen:

Suprise Attack: +10
Better Weapon: +10
Superior Weapon: +20
Target Distracted: +10
Outnumbered:+-5 Depending on conditions, for each figure more than the opposers.

Others may be added later into the game.

Rules of Skill Rolls
As above, with a few more modifiers.

Trained in Skill: +20
Untrained in Skill: -10
Familiar With Skill: -+0
Distracted: -10

Player Interaction
I'm gonna let you guys go free with this. You can meet up, attack each other, form organizations and get NPCs to join, whatever you want. Since different characters may be off different allignments, you may fight each other, but we'll get to PvP rules when it happens.

World Exploration
Idris is a very expansive world, and no one can truly map it all. Whenever the players walk a certain distance, they will make the minimap expand and include more details. The hub of the world is a city called The Citadel, and this is where all the cultures combine, as well as where all players begin their Journey.
