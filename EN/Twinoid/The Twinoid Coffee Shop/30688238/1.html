This RPG is turn based, everyday, you may talk, shop many times you want, but you can move once per day.

Classes
There are several classes to choose for your character:
Ice Mage
Fire Mage
Thunder Mage
Shooter(Ranged)
ShovelMan
Rogue
Engineer

Some classes can do special actions, the ShovelMan can search for items once per day, the Rogue can steal stuff from NPCs(Dangerous action, you can be banned from the village), the Engineer can build machines.

Stats
Health - The amount of damage your character can take, if it reaches 0, you die.
Damage - The amout of damage you deal, requires a weapon.
Defense - The Amout of damage blocked, reduced, requires armor or shield.

Moving
The world is not explored, you can move north, south east or west, but you don't know what lies ahead unless the area has already been explored. You will aways begin in the Spero Village.

Skills
There are several different skills, you learn them by reading skill books, or learing them naturally.

Fighting
When you move to a different area, there's a change of finding a monster and fighting it, I'll tell you the results of the battle.

Mage Weapons
You can choose between spell tomes or staffs, there's no difference.
