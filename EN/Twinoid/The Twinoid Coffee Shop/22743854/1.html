((Name: Hatchet
Race: Death/Robot
Age: 112
Weapons: A scythe
Special abilities: one poke and you're dead))

She walks forwards the the blazing sun, the heat waving off her titanium alloy body as she continues. Steam shoots from her back, making her black lips crawl to a smile, her teal eyes shooting open and glowing brightly. She grips her scythe harder, looking at the blood-stained blade of mixed metals and rusting bolts keeping it all together. Her creator would never approve of this, and nor would her so called "brothers".

But alas, she had to. If her clone, Tomahawk, didn't, she did. Death would certainly put them on his personal list, or each other on their death note lists. That, or they would end up with more souls to handle than wanted, or needed in that matter.

Her black dress swayed a bit in the small winds of the Arizona desert and she looked around, checking to see if anyone was around. She was invisible to her surroundings, so possibly whatever couldn't see her she couldn't see, unless on her list. But she decided to keep that way until called out or needed.

The town started to form beyond the high noon horizon, and she sighed steam, her clockworks whirring and making her laugh. Finally, what she had been searching for. She patted the golden plated corset around her stomach and smiled, hearing the soft clinks of her dresses' knee plates, the gold and silver bands, hit against each other. In an instant, she knew they would be gone, but smiled any how.
