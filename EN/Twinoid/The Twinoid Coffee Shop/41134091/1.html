Attacking.
Attacking has the basics of the S.A.T. Mechanism with one main adjustment
Instead of rolling an ht for a hit or miss you now roll a d6 which determines the quality of attack

6: critical hit; half of attack damage added to total (if you have a 4s wep it'll boost to 6)
5: normal attack
4: normal attack
3: light attack (half damage dealt)
2: miss
1: miss
